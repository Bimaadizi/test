Eboanaflimoth , also known as Ebonflame , was a red dragon working with Cult of the Dragon .

History

Hating Lathander with a passion, the dragon sent her cultists to attack the Morninglord's temple in Tilverton around 1370 DR .

By 1374 DR , Ebonflame was transformed into a dracolich .
