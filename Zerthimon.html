Zerthimon was a gith who fought in the wars for his people's liberation from enslavement by the mind flayers . After philosophical disagreements with the insurgence leader Gith over the fate of the liberated people, he became a prophet and a leader of the splinter group who would call themselves githzerai , as they split from their cousins who had chosen to follow Gith, the githyanki .

Rumors & Legends

Githzerai legends told that Zerthimon was in a state of eternal meditation within a realm of spirits, from where he regularly sent visions to guide his people. They also prophesied the return of Zerthimon in the form of a powerful guiding spirit.
