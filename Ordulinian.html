Possessions

Ordulinian was the proud owner of a cloak of non-detection .

Activities

Despite being a spellcaster of divine magic , Ordulinian was a regular at the Sorcerous Sundries in the Heapside district of the city.

Relationships

He thought very little of Arkion and Nemphre , two feuding necromancers that lived in the city.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Ordulinian sought to stop Arkion and Nemphre in their petty contests that involved stealing trinkets from one another and bodies from the sewers that ran beneath the city. He hoped to do so without the use of violence if possible.
