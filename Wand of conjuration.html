A wand of conjuration was an enchanted wand that allowed the wielder to recognize any conjuration spell .

Powers

By simply grasping this wand the wielder was able to recognize any written or seen conjuration spell. The wielder could expend one of the wand's charges to cast unseen servant or monster summoning . The wand could also be used to conjure a curtain of pure blackness that absorbed all light. The curtain could attain a maximum size of 600 ft² (56 m²) but needed to reach from floor to ceiling and wall to wall wherever it was placed. A prismatic sphere could also be created with the wand, with a single color of the sphere appearing at a time from red to violet.

Notable Owners

Adnan al-Raqi , a notorious sha'ir from Huzuz , carried a wand of conjuration .
