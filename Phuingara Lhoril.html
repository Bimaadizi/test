Phuingara Lhoril was an inhabitant of Myth Drannor in the 3 rd century DR .

Relationships

She was the lady of house Lhoril .

History

In 241 DR , she joined a party at Satyrdance Pool , where she revealed that she feared for the continued existence of Cormanthor . Albeit she was ridiculed at first, her friend group eventually came around, and they agreed on some plan to steer their husbands towards keeping Cormanthor from complete destruction.
