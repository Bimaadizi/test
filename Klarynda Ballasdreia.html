Klarynda Ballasdreia , nicknamed The Lady of Masks , was a successful perfume merchant who resided in Athkatla 's Temple District in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Klarynda Ballasdreia was a well-known socialite, breaker of hearts, and a schemer of Athkatla's high society.

History

By the late 14 th century DR, Klarynda Ballasdreia ran a successful business dealing in perfumes and beauty masks as well as being a savvy investor. Her large mansion was a shrine dedicated to the goddess of fortune, Tymora , before being sacked by the city in the Year of the Groaning Cart, 1267 DR .
