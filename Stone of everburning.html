A stone of everburning was an enchanted stone that radiated constant heat.

Description

This magic item was quite unremarkable and appeared to be a regular stone.

Powers

A stone of everburning radiated constant intense heat at all times. Touching the stone would result in instant burns to the skin.

History

Some stones of everburning were believed to last for thousands of years. They were used in Mulhorand to power steam engines when immersed in water.
