Gloves of healing were magical gauntlets that allowed the wearer to heal others and eradicate poisons from their bodies. They were often worn by clerics and paladins of benevolent deities .

Usage

These gauntlets were commonly used among members of the Order of the Radiant Heart , who would bestow them upon their novice members who had not yet learned how to cast divine magic . It was considered an honor for those outside of the paladin to be gifted a pair of these magic gauntlets.
