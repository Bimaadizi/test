Pontswain was a small coastal town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Pontswain was located at the end of Corwell Road in the kingdom of Corwell in southern Gwynneth. The town sat along the southern coast of Corwell Firth near the edge of Kimball Moor .
