The Golden Teacup was a club in Tarmalune , one of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond .

Background

The Golden Teacup was one of the most frequented "darren," a Tarralune drinking, gambling, and wenching club. It was also the most elegant of the darrens active around 1479 DR .
