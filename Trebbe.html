Trebbe was an archwizard and one of the most prolific spell creators in Netheril .

Abilities

He was a specialist in magic that protected arcanists, enabling spell duels to last much longer.

History

Trebbe was born in −2292 DR . He established Shadowtop Borough , a Netherese enclave for magical research in −2267 DR . It was here he created the Crown of Horns , an evil artifact.

Death

In −2237 DR , a spellcasting accident triggered while Trebbe was working on the crown led to his death.
