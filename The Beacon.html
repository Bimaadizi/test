The Beacon was a pleasant inn in Thentia . It was named for the globe lit by a continual flame mounted on its roof.

Location

The Inlet was on a main intersection in south-eastern Thentia, not far from the docks.

Interior

The Beacon's bedrooms were soundproofed to ensure a good night's sleep.

Services

The Beacon particularly attracted boisterous travelers and adventurers who desired a comfortable and friendly place to relax at. It offered food, drink, and bedrooms, and all three for the night were provided for 8 gp . The innkeep Moavar enjoyed telling tales of his time on the sea, both true and false.
