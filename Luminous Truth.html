Luminous Truth was magical staff found in Eberron during the mid-1480s DR of Faerûn .

Description

The wooden stave had forked prongs at the end.

Powers

Luminous Truth carried the power of the Silver Flame , and burned foes with fire and radiant light. It also improved the power of fire and light-based spells cast by the wielder.

History

The staff was made for the priests of the Church of the Silver Flame and their allies. It was awarded to loyal adventurers who entered the Spinner's Prison and faced the Spinner of Shadows .
