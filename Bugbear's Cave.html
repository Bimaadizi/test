The Bugbear's Cave was a pub that stood in the south-western part of Hillsfar .

Services

The Bugbear's Cave offered several types of meals, catering to people of varied means. An average meal could cost up to 4 gold coins .

History

As of the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , the tavern was a crowded establishment, filled with gossips. The barmaids were happy to entertain visitors with stories and gossip for a generous tip. In 1358 DR, a dwarven fighter named Granite visited the Bugbear's Cave during his investigation into Hillsfaran stolen the jewels.
