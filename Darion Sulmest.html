Darion Sulmest was a cobbler inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Darion was the main point of contact for the Order of Cobblers & Corvisers circa the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR . He held the title of "Spokesman of the Order" and worked out of both the Cobblers' & Corvisers' House , which was their headquarters along Soothsayer's Way in the Trades Ward , as well as his personal business, Sulmest's Splendid Shoes & Boots on the High Road in the North Ward .
