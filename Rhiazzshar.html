Rhiazzshar was a wood elf druid of Silvanus and a member of a tribe in Wildhome in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Rhiazzshar had amber-colored eyes and mahogany skin. She smelled of freshly crushed pine needles.

History

In 1479 DR , when the wild elves became interested in Duvan 's immunity to the Spellplague , Rhiazzshar pretended to show affection for him so that she could convince Duvan to participate in experiments to determine the limits of his abilities. They became lovers for a time but Duvan rejected her when he realized she was using him for her own purposes.
