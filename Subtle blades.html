Subtle blades were enchanted daggers used by rogues and assassins . They could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

Especially when dual-wielded, subtle blades could be used to make attacks that appeared to be at once both feints and strikes. Their enchantment meant that they were particularly effective for disarming an opponent.
