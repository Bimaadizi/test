History

Around the year 1369 DR , Randal, Atrion, and some other Freedom Riders led an expedition into Shraevyn's Tomb . Following an initial battle with the Zhentarim , the entire group was either captured, or killed and turned into undead by the shadow Gothyl , save for Atrion. He managed to escape the chaos of the battle and return to Shadowdale .

After he carried himself back to town, Atrion sought out the great wizard and sage Elminster , at his tower . He found the scribe Lhaeo but managed to convince him to form a rescue party for the commander and resistance leader.
