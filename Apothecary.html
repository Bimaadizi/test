An apothecary was a profession in many settlements of the Realms. Apothecaries saw to the medicinal needs of customers and sold bottled potions and salves as well as treated and washed wounds or provided splints to those with broken limbs. Apothecaries sold herbs in addition to medicines.

Almost every market town had at least one, and several villages along trade routes did as well. Apothecaries were almost always on the staff of small shrines.
