The Hungtse River was one of the largest rivers on Toril , spanning over a thousand miles across both the Hordelands and Kara-Tur .

Within the territories of the Hordelands, the river was often referred to as the Upper Hungtse River .

Geography

The Hungtse originated in the A-Ling Shan mountains from glacial runoff from the Glacier of the Dragon . It flowed west to east, crossing the large expanse of the Katakoro Plateau and many provinces in Shou Lung before ultimately emptying into the Celestial Sea near the metropolis of Karatin .
