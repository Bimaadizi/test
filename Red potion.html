The red potion was a rather unique draught that was offered for sale by a traveling merchant during the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

The actual solution was rather peculiar in appearance.

Powers

The imbiber of this potion experienced a number of changes to their physicality and mental faculties. They were struck down with extreme weakness, to the point where they could only carry a few pounds on their person. The potion also left them with very poor judgement and lack of common sense.

The primary benefit of the red potion was that it enhanced a person's intellect, to a degree that was beyond genius-level. It also offered them significant protection from magical spells and spell-like effects.

Both of the potion's effects lasted for a full 24 hours.

History

The red potion was offered for sale by a merchant at the Nashkel carnival in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .

Reputation

This potion could be considered a great boon for sorcerers and other arcane spellcasters .
