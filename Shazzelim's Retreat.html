Shazzelim's Retreat was an inn found in the Amnian city of Esmeltaran during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The inn was built near the center of Esmeltaran.

Services

Shazzelim's Retreat provided excellent service, especially its food. It was named after the scimitar mounted over the bar, allegedly Shazzelim , though it was actually a mundane imitation with a few glamors cast upon it.
