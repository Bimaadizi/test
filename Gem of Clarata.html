TypeMagical gem

The Gem of Clarata was a mysterious gem of legendary power. Reputedly, it could grant its user the powers of healing , inter-planar travel , far-seeing , and telepathy , among many others. As of 1368 DR , it was believed to be possessed by Tiatha Hawksong in Leuthilspar on Evermeet .
