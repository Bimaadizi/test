Angelo Dosan was a corrupt Flaming Fist officer who worked for Sarevok during the Iron crisis of 1368 DR .

Personality

He was a man who was greedy for greater wealth and power, but was not foolish enough to cross those he knew would be a true threat.

Relationships

Angelo was also the father of the fearsome warrior Shar-Teel , from whom he was estranged.

Within the Flaming Fist, Angelo had a number of loyal soldiers that chose to serve him over the Baldurian council, namely the soldiers Benjy and Dilos .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , briefly ascended to the rank of commander of the Flaming Fist following the assassination of Scar and incapacitation of Duke Eltan . While he had the power, Commander Angelo agreed to turn over command of the Fist to Sarevok Anchev during his coronation as Grand Duke .

Angelo accompanied Sarevok when he was exposed as a scheming villain in front of the Baldurian nobility . They fled to the temple of Bhaal in the Undercity along with Sarevok's other cronies, where they were slain by Gorion's Ward and his party.
