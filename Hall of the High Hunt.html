The Hall of the High Hunt was a temple of Solonor Thelandira in Evereska .

Geography

It was built into the western slope of Moondark Hill .

Structure

The beauteous temple was built as an open-air pavilion set within a circle of elder shadowtop trees. By the late 15 th century DR , a series of imposing marble minarets formed the architectural counterpoint to this natural glory, themselves linked by vine-covered archways.

At the center of the hall was the 'Singing Spring of Solonor Thelandira', a fountain of silver waters formed from a mountain spring originating beneath Moondark Hill and meandering through several natural caverns before reaching the surface.. The air in and around the temple held the lingering scent of sweetwater.

Activities

In addition to its role in the service of Solonor, the Hall of the High Hunt served as the meeting place for Evereska's Hill Elders , the council of senior elves that oversaw their city's protection and guided its citizens.

Due to the restorative properties of the water that arose from the 'Singing Spring', the temple became a place of healing for Evereskan elves that became injured or ill.
