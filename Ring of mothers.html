The ring of mothers was a unique elven enchanted ring from ancient Myth Drannor .

Description

This ring was a metal band decorated with images of beasts .

Powers

The ring of mothers allowed the wearer to cast charm monster spell at will. The ring had four charges of the spell.

History

In 1369 DR , the ring of mothers could be found worn by soulless orogs , indoctrinated into serving a greedy dark naga who dwelt within the First Cellars of the Elven Catacombs underneath Castle Cormanthor . Along with all other treasures, the ring was scavenged from the ancient elven graves.
