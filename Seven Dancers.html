The Seven Dancers were a set of unique golden statuettes dedicated to Sune , the Lady Firehair.

Description

These precious items depicted goddess Sune and were studded in rubies , opals , and sapphires .

History

As of the late 14 th century DR , the Seven Dancers were among the major treasures recovered from the numerous cliff caves along the Horn Shore on the Tethyr Peninsula . The statuettes were found between the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR and the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .
