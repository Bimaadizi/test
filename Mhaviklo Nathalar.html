Mhaviklo Nathalar was an aging famed actor who performed at the famous Crown Aflame theater in Athkatla in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Even in his later years, Mhaviklo was described as handsome and magnificent.

History

"Lord" Mhaviklo Nathalar's long career included many paid speeches for noble families, many heart-broken lasses, becoming the leader of several acting troupes, and even achieving the title of a guildmaster of an actors' guild somewhere far away from Amn . As of Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Halvaruth was still performing at the Crown Aflame but was semi-retired. His renown and height of acting skills brought many amateur actors under his tutelage in the late 14 th century DR.
