Fesric was a Zhent soldier at the Citadel of the Raven in 1367 DR .

In the wake of the disaster that had destroyed Zhentil Keep , Fesric became the leader of a group of like-minded Zhents who wanted to break away and transform the Citadel of the Raven into the base of a separate power. Kandar Milinal considered him a possible problem for the future, and he considered arranging an "accident" to befall Fesric.
