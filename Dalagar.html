Dalagar Longwalker was a king of Andlath , a kingdom east of the Iltkazar Range in the Lands of Intrigue in the 3 rd century DR .

History

Between 205 DR and 281 DR , Shoon IV forced Dalagar, magically or politically, to create a trade route from Ormath to Athkatla , now known as Tethir Road .
