Johan Kist was a talented Red Wizards of Thay and the inventor of the Nomad's rings , active some years before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Johan Kist was still a young and promising Red Wizard when he created the first of the Nomad's rings . His apprentice attempted to claim the invention after stealing the ring . The thief was quickly apprehended by Johan and in retaliation, he created a spell that caused its target's flesh to peel away in small strips. As of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Johan Kist kept the torturous spell to himself, never sharing its magic formulas.
