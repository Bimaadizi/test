My Battered Tankard Filled was book penned by Dauthin Maer , master merchant of Baldur's Gate , in the mid-14 th century DR .

History

My Battered Tankard Filled was a collection of Dauthin Maer's musings on various topics, including his thoughts on love and relationships. It was published in the Year of the Bridle, 1349 DR .
