Dagnabbet Waybeard was the general of Mithral Hall in Luruar .

History

Dagnabbet served as general under King Connerad Brawnanvil in 1484 DR during the Darkening , a time when Mithral Hall was under threat from the orcs of the Kingdom of Many-Arrows . She successfully repelled the first attack and prevented great losses because of her shrewd reasoning.

Around 1486 DR Dagnabbet was the first Queen of Mithril Hall.

Relationships

Dagnabbet was the daughter of Dagnabbit and the granddaughter of Dagna Waybeard .
