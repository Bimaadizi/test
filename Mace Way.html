Mace Way was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Mace Way was located in southern Zhentil Keep within the Foreign Quarter . The street was found in the far western section of the quarter running roughly north to south in between Teshwave Street and Twilight Street near the River Trail Gate . Center Street connected near the midpoint of Mace Way.
