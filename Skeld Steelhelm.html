Skeld Steelhelm was a dwarf adventurer from Mithral Hall who joined a group of fellow dwarves aiding Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a companion of Gord Roundshield of Citadel Felbarr , Taggot Ironshroud of Sundabar , and Tori Hammerheart of Citadel Adbar .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the four dwarf companions came to Neverwinter to offer their aid to the beleaguered city. Skeld helped to coordinate their efforts where it was needed, but Gord charged off into the Tower District to wage a one-dwarf war on the Many-Arrows orcs occupying the area. Skeld asked a fellow dwarf adventurer to check up on Gord and ask him to return.
