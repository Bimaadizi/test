Touor was a lover, paramour or husband of lady Laurlaethee Shaurlanglar . He had died years prior to 241 DR .

History

In 261 DR , Lady Laurlaethee Shaurlanglar recalled the warmth of her late Touor during her casting of blood dragon . Touor had died years prior to that point.
