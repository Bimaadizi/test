The Hall of the Treasury was part of the complex of the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

Since the wealth of the Grand Caliph was stored here, it was heavily guarded. In addition to gold dinars , the treasury housed priceless works of art, valuable gems, and other relics.

Defenses

Twenty mamluk guards circled the Hall of the Treasury at all times. A pair of noble dao were stationed within the actual vault. Priests of Jisan were on hand to monitor and dispense funds from the treasury.

Magical traps abounded within the Hall of the Treasury. The exact location and nature of these traps was only known by the Grand Vizier, Alyana al-Azzazi , and the Keeper of the Treasury, Ala'i ibn-Hasim .
