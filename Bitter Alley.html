Bitter Alley was a small alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Bitter Alley branched south off of Fish Street in Waterdeep's Dock Ward , connecting with Snail Street to the east and Presper Street to the south. Two Flask Alley branched off of the western side of Bitter Alley.
