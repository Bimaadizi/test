A ring of teleportation was a magic item that allowed the wearer to teleport .

Description

These rings were fitted with a deep blue gem worth 500 gp .

Powers

The user separated the gem from the ring and placed it in a location of their choosing, allowing them to teleport without error to that location by expending one of the ring's charges. If the location of the gem was unsafe in any way then the ring expended four charges but nothing happened. The gem could be moved to a new location at any time.
