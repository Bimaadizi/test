Slade was an early knight of Neverwinter and the ninth of the Nine knights who served Lord Halueth Never . They were the basis for the Neverwinter Nine .

History

Slade was the last to join the Nine, and on his first day of service, he died in battle. A statue of Slade and his sword later stood guard in Halueth's tomb.
