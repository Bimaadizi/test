Luther's Protector was a staff enchanted for the use of dwarven monks of Myth Drannor , named after the decorated hero, Magister Luther .

Description

Luther's Protector was a simple staff in its appearance carved out of beechwood .

Powers

Luther's Protector possessed the +3 enchantment and improved the wielding monk's initiative in battle.

History

In 1369 DR , Luther's Protector could bee found in the Deep Halls of Dwarven Dungeons in the scaly hands of one of the marauding lizardfolk led by a mysterious sorcerer Malgi Hi , looted from one of the ancient tombs of the dungeon.
