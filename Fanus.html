Fanus was a small town found within the Cities of the Pantheon of interior Zakhara .

Geography

Fanus was found near the center of the southwestern peninsula that jutted into the Golden Gulf . The nearest cities, Hudid and I'tiraf , were both roughly 100 miles (160,000 meters) away.
