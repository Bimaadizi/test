Naneatha Lharilstar's residence was a row house and the home of Naneatha Lharilstar located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The home stood three stories tall.

Location

Naneatha Lharilstar's residence was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the south side of Gem Street across from the House of Gems . The rear of the house faced Hillock Court .
