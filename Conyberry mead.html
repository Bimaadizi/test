Conyberry mead was a sweet alcoholic beverage flavored with fruit , honey , and spices that was brewed in the town of Conyberry on the edge of the Neverwinter Wood .

Powers

Conyberry mead was known to have beneficial effect on imbiber's perception.

History

In the mid-to-late-15 th century DR , Conyberry mead was sold by the Tarmalune Trade House in Neverwinter on the Sword Coast North .
