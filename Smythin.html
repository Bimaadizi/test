Smythn was a man who lived on the Sword Coast during the 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Smythin was captured by the Cult of the Absolute and taken to the Shattered Sanctum . There he was prepared by the goblin warrior Druk , to be thrown into the sanctum's dungeons and fed to their giant spiders .
