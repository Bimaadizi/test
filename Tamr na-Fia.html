Tamr na-Fia lived in the Tower of Adnan al-Raqi in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Relationships

Tamr was the favorite student of Adnan al-Raqi , who looked upon her as his own granddaughter.

Activities

Tamr disappeared after she was sent to the court of one of the genie lords at the request of Adnan to find answers to the Grand Caliph's sterility. He kept her private quarters ready for her in case she ever returned.
