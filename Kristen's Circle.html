Kristen's Circle was a circular intersection in the Holyhouses neighborhood of Ravens Bluff .

Location

This junction was intersected at its northeastern and southwestern corners by Fire Lane , whilst intersected from the parallel directions by Evensong Ride . From its northern and southern ends, it was intersected by De Villar's Ride . From the east it was connected to a small, unnamed path that led to Manycoins Way , whilst from the west another unnamed path connected it to Blacktree Boulevard South .

Notable Locations

History

Sometime in the late 14 th century DR , Kristen's Circle was the startling site of a murder. Early one morning, two City Guardsmen discovered the corpses of four priests chained together at the junction's center. They were identified as each being acolytes of a different faith: Helm , Lathander , Tempus , and Tyr . Pinned to one of the bodies was a note that read, "Gods sleep while Checkmate stirs". The incident was reported the next day by the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter broadsheet , though the City Watch were withholding details at that time.
