The Earring of Prince Mamoon was an item that could be found in Zakhara , the Land of Fate.

Description

These pieces of jewelry were hoops of jet coral covered with intricate calligraphy.

Powers

These earrings were fashioned by Prince Mamoon of the marids . He created them for his students of marqab , a school for sha'irs in Gana . Anyone who presented one of these earrings gained acceptance into the school.
