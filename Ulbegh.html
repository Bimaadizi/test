Ulbegh was an inhabitant of Scornubel in the mid-14 th century DR .

History

Late in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Belgon Bradaskor made a remark to Tornar that the noblewoman who had slain Ulbegh last summer, strongly implying 1356 DR , was his source for the report that Ornrim had been replaced by a shapeshifter. Tornar knew it was Tessaril Winter .
