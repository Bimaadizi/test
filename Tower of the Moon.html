The Tower of the Moon was a mosque dedicated to Selan found in Muluk within the Free Cities of Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Description

The Tower of the Moon had a spiral top complete with an observatory used to monitor the stars. The mosque stood within a beautiful garden in the northwest corner of Muluk.
