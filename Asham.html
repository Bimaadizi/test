Asham , who was also called Mad Asham by those who witnessed him wandering the streets babbling aimlessly, was an inhabitant of Qudra in north Zakhara in 1367Â DR .

Activities

At one point Asham was a skilled sha'ir . He could usually be found on the streets of Qudra, grabbing strangers and warning them of the "whispering doom."

History

Asham was once a magical advisor to the Dauntless Mamluk Society . Long ago while on a raiding party with the Dauntless, Asham's group disappeared within the Haunted Lands . He remained missing for six years until one day he stumbled back into Qudra as a tattered mess. Asham's memories had be erased by unknown sources and no available magic, including that of skilled genies using wishes , was able to restore him.
