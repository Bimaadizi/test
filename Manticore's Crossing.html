The Manticore's Crossing was a crossing on the Way of the Manticore in Cormyr .

It was the site of a battle between the army of King Galaghard III and the undead forces of the Witch Lords in the Year of the Thirsty Sword, 900 DR . The Cormyreans were victorious, but the undead would rise again.
