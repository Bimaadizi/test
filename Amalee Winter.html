Lady Amalee Winter was a member of the noble House Wyvernspur of Cormyr who lived in the late 10 th and early 11 th centuries DR .

History

Amalee Winter married into House Wyvernspur sometime before the Year of the Orator, 980 DR . Her husband was Gould Wyvernspur and with him she sired two sons. The older son, Gerrin Wyvernspur , was born in 980 DR. He became a hero fighting red dragons in the attack on Suzail in the Year of the Dracorage, 1018 DR . Gould's and Amalee's younger son was Finder Wyvernspur who would eventually become a renowned bard , one of the founding members of the Harpers , and subsequently, a deity .
