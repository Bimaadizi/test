Olyrnn was one of the noble elven houses of Anauria in north Faerûn , one of the survivor states of ancient Netheril .

Activities

Wizards of House Olyrnn that lived in the city of Anauril were significantly involved in the crafting of the its famous steel weaponry. They would imbue powerful enchantments on the blades that were formed by smiths that gave the "City of Swords" its moniker.

Members

Along with House Nyntynel , Olyrnn was one of two families that composed Anauria's elven nobility.

History

During the Era of Upheaval , some scarce remnants of the family's heraldry could still be found among the ruins of Anauria, buried beneath the sands of the great desert of Anauroch .
