Teldin Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr in the mid-to-late 13 th century DR . Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles , he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty and was the son of his predecessor, King Vance . His eventual successor was his second son, Rieger .

History

Teldin assumed the throne in the Year of the Leaping Frog, 1266 DR , after the death of his father. He ruled for only five years before he died of heartstop in the Year of the Shattered Wall, 1271 DR .
