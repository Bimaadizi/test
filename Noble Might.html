Noble Might was a heavily enchanted scepter wielded by Siamorphe , the Divine Right.

Description

Noble Might was forged from the purest adamantine alloy.

Powers

The scepter served as a +5 enchanted weapon during combat. Additionally, it possessed all of the combined powers of a Netherese blast scepter , a rod of rulership , and a staff of curing .
