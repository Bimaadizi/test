Orlathon Lunemast was a human cleric and a priest of Selûne in the city of Tsurlagol in the Vast in the mid–14 th century DR .

By 1367 DR , Orlathon was High Priest of The Rising Moon temple of Selûne in Tsurlagol, in charge of nine subpriests. He was still in charge by 1370 DR .
