Soothsayer's Way was a secondary avenue in Waterdeep that began in the city's Trades Ward and spanned westward into the Castle Ward .

Geography

This road ran in a straight line west from Way of the Dragon , this Trades Ward portion of it being paved in cobblestone. Once it crossed through Snail Street , the road was paved in hard-packed dirt and gravel . As it continued on, Soothsayer's Way became the southern terminus of Soldier's Street , the northern terminus of Belnimbra's Street , and passed through Cook Street . Finally, the road ended in a dead-end before Castle Waterdeep .
