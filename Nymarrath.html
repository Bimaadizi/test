Nymarrath was an aged druid living in Elventree in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Nymarrath took care of the Silver Shadow Cave in Elventree.

Relationships

Nymarrath had good dealings with the drow wizard Murssvayas Dhuurniv of Szith Morcane .

History

Nymarrath supported Murssvayas in his effort to depose the fire giants of Maerimydra .
