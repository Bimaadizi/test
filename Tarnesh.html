Tarnesh was a bounty hunter mage active in the late 14 th century DR .

Possessions

Tarnesh was armed with a simple quarterstaff and his spellbook contained simple spells such as mirror image , horror , and magic missile .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Tarnesh put his sights on Gorion's Ward , Abdel Adrian. A hefty bounty of 200 gold coins was placed on Abdel's head. The wizard tracked his mark down at the entrance to the Friendly Arm Inn . Tarnesh was unlikely to have survived the confrontation.
