Giant sea horses were a large version of their smaller kin.

Description

They looked like large sea horses of flamboyant color, with a long body and a curled tail.

Behavior

Giant sea horses were naturally shy creatures and try to avoid contact with surface creatures.

Combat

Giant sea horses defended themselves by headbutting an opponent. They could be trained to use their strong tail as a weapon in melee combat.

Ecology

Diet

These creatures were a herbivorous species.

Habitats

Giant sea could potentially be found in nearly all but the coldest of oceans.

They could often be found in the seas and ocean around the Moonshae Isles .

Relationships

Tritons and giant sea horses had good relationships. They also were sea elves ' favored mounts and could be trained in combat. Locathah were also known to occasionally capture and train them.

Giant sea horses were sometimes found as courtiers or servants in the palace of Kalbari al-Durrat al-Amwaj ibn Jari .

A giant sea horse could carry up to 450 pounds and drag up to 2,250 pounds.

Summoning

A giant seahorse could be summoned with the Summon monster II spell .
