Quarra was a skilled wizard and member of the Black Gauntlet , an adventuring company based out of Starmantle active during the 13 th century DR .

History

Quarra was with the Black Gauntlet when they slew the mighty green dragon Chathuulandroth in his lair. She met her end later at the hands of a demon that was improperly summoned. Before her death Quarra rented the tome Unique Mageries to Elminster .
