Dob's Loss was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Dob's Loss was located in the southwest section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . The road branched west off of the northern tip of the Street of the Singing Dolphin , ending at the Field of Triumph on the Lions a short distance away. Seawatch Street and the Street of Whispers merged to connect with the western end of Dob's Loss.
