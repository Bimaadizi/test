Xundra Sharmhand was a prominent wizard who lived in Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR .

Personality

Xundra was quite secretive, perhaps due to her proclivity to practice the dark art of necromancy .

Activities

Xundra used her powers to control a veritable army of skeletal rats, bats, snakes, cats, and dogs.
