History

At some point, Rance joined a group led by the elf named Vail , along with Achen Dell , Cailan , and Faizah Tin . The group assaulted the famous mage Ramazith Flamesinger at his tower in Baldur's Gate , but were petrified by the gaze of a basilisk .

The stone statue of Rance, along with those of his companions, were sold to the Baldurian nobleman, Felonius Gist , and kept at his family's estate in the city.
