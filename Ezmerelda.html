Ezmerelda was the aunt of Rab Bitnez , the garlic -obsessed wizard from Liam's Hold , who authored the 101 Garlic Recipes cookbook.

History

Ezmerelda always claimed that Rab Bitnez's mother laid down in a garlic patch to give birth to him. It was believed that this resulted in the gnome 's life-long obsession with the pungent plant.
