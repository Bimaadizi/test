Angrath Woodwise was one of servants in Lord Marsten 's hunting lodge.

History

Angrath was the chief hunter for Lord Marsten until his master was killed some time before the Tyranny of Dragons and the lodge was occupied by Cult of the Dragon agents under Talis Kasterel . She decided to use Angrath and the other servants as her own. However, Angrath was demoted to head groom and his position went to Trepsin . Angrath greatly hated the troll .
