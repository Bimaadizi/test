Negus (Prince) Kwalu was the son of King Osaw I and a member of the barae , the seven "super-paladins" sworn to protect the holy city of Mezro in Chult before its destruction during the Spellplague .

Description

Kwalu was a handsome man. He had square features and deep eyes. He often dressed in a tobe of purple that had green triangles around his heart.

In the 1360s DR, Kwalu was over 100 years old, yet, like all barae , he was immortal.

Personality

Kwalu had a very intense personality and took very seriously his role of protector of Mezro. He admired true bravery.

Possessions

Kwalu never went anywhere without being armed. His weapon of choice was usually his kerrie , or war club. It was pitted from having cracked so many Batiri skulls.

Relationships

The youngest of the barae , the young girl named Fipya , looked up to Kwalu as if he were her older brother.
