Barbed darts were a very large darts that were both fletched and barbed.

Description

They measured 2​ to ​3 feet (0.61​ to ​0.91 meters) in length and weighed around 5 lb (2.3 kg).

History

Around 1372 DR , they were often used by lizardfolk .
