Nalabar was a minstrel from the city of Selgaunt in Sembia in the 14 th century DR .

Personality

Nalabar had keen wits and a sharp tongue.

History

Around 1370 DR , Nalabar was one of the more well-known itinerant minstrels wandering the Vast .
