The Tricky Thief was a a magical miscellany and curio shop in the hamlet of Skull Crag in the Storm Horn Mountains of Cormyr in the 14 th century DR .

Location

The shop was located on the southern side of the village.

Services

The Tricky Thief was filled to the brim with weird arcane paraphernalia, including stinging magical stones and inflated odd sound-making goat bladders .

History

In the mid-14 th century DR, The Tricky Thief operated at odd hours with long lunch breaks, it catered to curious travelers of the High Road .
