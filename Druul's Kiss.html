Druul's Kiss was an enchanted curvy-braided dagger from the ruined city of Myth Drannor .

Powers

Druul's Kiss possessed a potent +5 enchantment and allowed the wielder to cast the poison spell . This magic came at a price to the wielder, as Druul's Kiss dramatically reduced the owner's charisma.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , members of the Cult of the Dragon occupied the ruined city of Myth Drannor. One of the Cult's Overseers stationed in the depths of the Elven Catacombs wielded Druul's Kiss and the sceptre of Leogans . The Overseer answered directly to Kya Mordrayn , the Wearer of Purple who helmed the operations in Myth Drannor.
