Shestandeliath Parvida was a dragonborn member of Clan Shestandeliath in 1486 DR .

Relationships

Parvida was the sister of Zaroshni and the cousin of Ereshkin .

History

Parvida was one of the few known members of the Liberators of the Steel Sky .

On Nightal 15 of 1486 DR, Parvida was killed, alongside her fellow companions of the Liberators, by a maurezhi granted to the God -King Gilgeam .
