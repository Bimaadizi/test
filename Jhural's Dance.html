Jhural's Dance was a festhall located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The festhall stood three stories tall.

Location

Jhural's Dance was located in the northwest corner of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the southwest side of Swords Street .
