Chaladoana was a drow infiltrator working in Scornubel during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Chaladoana took orders directly from Daerdatha , and had three apprentices.

History

Chaladoana was physically present during the ball at Halonder Eldeglut 's manor in Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , where the merchant and his wife Iyrevven Eldeglut were supposed to be subject to a "taking", that is, they were to be taken to Chult as slaves and supplanted by other drow infiltrators.
