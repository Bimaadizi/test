Alice Althea was a woman of means and a client of Sorcerous Sundries in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Alice was a wood elven woman with fair skin tone with greenish tints. She had light short bob hair and was dressed in dark green finery.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Alice found herself contemplating a purchase of a freshly reanimated zombie from Sorcerous Sundries. The undead was created by the shop's in-house duergar necromancer Cordrane the Recent . However, Alice was unsure if the price of a zombie servant was worth it.
