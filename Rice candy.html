Rice candies were a type of chewy candy from Shou Lung . They were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .

Description

Flavored with flower essences and exotic fruits which conferred them a tart taste, rice candies had a chewy and gelatinous texture. Candies purchased through Aurora's came in packages containing mixed assorted flavors.
