The Roadkeep , which was once called Castle Narnbra , was a small gate castle located in the city of Yhaunn in 1358 DR .

Location

The Roadkeep was found on the northwest perimeter of the city where Tildaryn's Ride and the East Way entered Yhaunn. It sat within the greater complex of the Road Gate .

Activities

In addition to serving as a defensive structure, the Roadkeep was also a guard barracks, jail, treasury, and it even contained several apartments for select visitors..
