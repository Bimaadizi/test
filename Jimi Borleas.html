Fist Jimi Borleas was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Jimi was a gold dwarvish man with a light skin tone, blue eyes, and a clean-shaven face. He wore a set of chainmail armor as part of the Flaming Fist uniform, decorated with the organization's emblem on a tabard . He was armed with a halberd and was ready to use it to punctuate his stern and cruel attitude.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Borleas answered a complaint from Battrick Degronce in the Lower City. The gnome complained about the stench of decay around Philgrave's Mansion . Fist Borleas tried to convince Battrick that the stench was nothing but a clogged sewer and that he would file a report requesting the sewer's eventual cleanup. However, Battrick was correct in his suspicions that the stench was coming from the mansion. It was the lair of Mystic Carrion and his undead servants.
