A Demon Grinder was a bulky, armored infernal war machine .

Description

The Demon Grinder was a colossal six-wheeled vehicle that featured imposing iron jaws affixed to its front and a massive metal ball suspended at its rear.

Abilities

Any creature or non-magical object devoured by its iron jaws would be pulverized and expelled through pipes situated at the sides of the vehicle. Additionally, the rear metal ball could be utilized for demolishing structures or other objects. It could carry up to 2,000 pounds (910 kilograms) in cargo.
