Plate armor (also known variously as plate mail, field plate, full plate, or simply plate ) was a type of armor used on Toril . It made up a wide variety of different heavy armor designs that covered the entire body.

Description

Plate armor consisted of shaped, solid plates that covered various parts of body. It was supplemented by chain armor that covered any gaps, and beneath the metal was a thick layer of padding. The whole suit was held together with numerous straps and harnesses to distribute the weight.

Many of the pieces that made up a set of plate had their own distinct names, such as the couter (elbow guard), pauldron (shoulder guard), rerebrace (upper-arm guard), vambrace (forearm guard), and gorget (throat-plate).

Plate was often the heaviest type of armor available, sacrificing mobility and agility for superior protection.

Variations

Plate armor was among the most expensive and heavy armor a warrior could buy. The different types each had their own advantages and drawbacks to their designs.
