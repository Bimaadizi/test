Tofu was a type of food that was produced from soybeans .

Description

Specifically, tofu was made up of protein from soybeans that had been congealed. Those that made tofu were called "tofu makers". Tofu makers were known to make a living in Kara-Tur , such as in Hiwasa village, which had three tofu makers.

Tofu came in different forms, such as dried sheets. The versatile protein could also be made into bean curd noodles , which were fine and lengthy; they were also characterized as both transparent and had a satisfyingly chewy texture. In addition to sheets and noodles, tofu was served as slabs in vegetable broth .
