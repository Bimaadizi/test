Hamate was a ghoul servant of Mystic Carrion , a mummy lord who resided in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Hamate was slain and risen as an undead servant sometime before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . Mystic Carrion, who performed dark magic serviced for the wealthy clients from the Upper City , kept Hamate and other undead as his minions in Philgrave's Mansion in the Lower City. The manse was a mysterious and suspicious landmark that attracted attention due to the putrid stench of the dead that emanated from the building.
