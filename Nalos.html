Nalos of Pumanath was the triton centurion of the sixth squad of Abydos and a member of the Nantarn Council .

Personality

Initially during the Twelfth Serôs War , Nalos was skeptical about his mission and his position but, by 1370 DR , he had come to appreciate the shallow waters. His only concern was his brother's situation after the latter's acquisition of Claw of Xynakt ; however, his contacts among the dukars reassured Nalos.

Activities

Monthly Nalos reported to his emperor via hippocampus courier on the progress of the Council.
