History

In the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , Goulart and his fellow adventurers recovered a collection of scrolls and documents from the remains of a dragon that had died in battle on the edge of the Wyvernwater . They soon discovered that inscribed upon the scrolls were the true names of quite a number of the Red Wizards of Thay , having been collected by agents of King Azoun IV .
