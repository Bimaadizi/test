The Barber Shop of Gorar al-Aksar was a prominent fixture of the Grand Bazaar of Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Description

The walls of the barber shop were festooned with astrological charts and graphs. Gorar al-Aksar owned a unique smoking efreeti bottle that he kept on display near his work area. In addition to being a delightful collector's piece, the bottle proved that at least a few of Gorar's lavish tales were true.

Activities

Gorar's shop was always full of customers. The talkative barber gave them their horoscopes whether they wanted one or not.

History

As of 1367 DR, the shop had been in the Grand Bazaar for 40 years.

Inhabitants

Gorar owned and operated this establishment.
