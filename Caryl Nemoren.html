Caryl Nemoren was a young guardswoman from the village of West Harbor in the Mere of Dead Men circa 1374 DR .

Description

Caryl had green eyes and short cut blonde hair. She acts with put-on confidence and tried to look intimidated even if her shaking hands betrayed her youth and agitation.

History

Caryl Nemoren was one of the villagers put in charge of guarding the entrance to the rebuilt walled village of West Harbor circa the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR . She was hoping for a group of adventurers to come along, sending them to Mayor Buckman who needed such a group to aid the settlement, threatened by a duo of black dragons .
