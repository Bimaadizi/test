Bags of molding were magic containers with seemingly useless enchantments woven into them.

Powers

These bags' enchantments caused all food placed inside to grow mold and spoil immediately.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a bag of molding was displayed among splendorous enchanted rewards for those willing to partake in Akabi 's Wheel of Fortune game in the Circus of the Last Days during its stay in the Rivington neighborhood of Baldur's Gate .
