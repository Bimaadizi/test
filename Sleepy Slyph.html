The Sleepy Slyph was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tavern stood two stories tall.

Location

The Sleepy Slyph was located in the far southeast corner section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the southwest corner of the intersection of Snail Street and Rainrun Street .
