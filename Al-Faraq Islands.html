Al-Faraq Islands were a large archipelago found near the center of the Crowded Sea within interior Zakhara .

Geography

The northern reach of the archipelago was found approximately 30 miles (48 kilometers) off the coast of the Cities of the Pantheon and the town of Fanus . The islands stretched southeast from there into the Crowded Sea for another 180 miles (290 kilometers). A smaller arm of the archipelago also ran parallel to the southwest coast of the Cities of the Pantheon.
