Beldora was a secret Harper agent in Bryn Shander sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

Activities

Beldora, posing as a homeless waif, gained coins by helping to tend other folk's campfires. Secretly, she listened to all conversations around the fires and — thanks to a sending stone — once a day reported the relevant ones to her contact Thwip Ironbottom of Hundelstone .

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR , Beldora was at the marketplace when giants attacked the city.

Personality

Beldora liked to tell lies to people, believing that this made life more interesting. However, she wanted to protect poor people.
