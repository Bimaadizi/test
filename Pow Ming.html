Pow Ming was a wizard who lived in Yartar sometime after the War of the Silver Marches , in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Pow Ming acted as security chief of the Grand Dame , a riverboat housing a gambling hall. She patrolled the cabin and monitored newer customers.

Personality

Pow was a truly neutral individual. Her goal was to join the Arcane Brotherhood and used her time at the Grand Dame to meet influential people that could help her in this objective.
