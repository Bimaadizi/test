Rugglar Tossarim was a cooper inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Rugglar was the Guildmaster for the Coopers' Guild during the early 1370's DR. He worked out of the Coopers' Rest , their headquarters located on Pressbow Lane in Waterdeep's Dock Ward .
