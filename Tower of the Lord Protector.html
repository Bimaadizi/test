The Tower of the Lord Protector was a two-story keep in the center of Triboar in the mid-to-late 14 th and late 15 th centuries DR . It was the residence of the ruler of the town, titled the Lord Protector.

Structure

The keep was made of stone, was of simple design, and leaned noticeably eastward. Over the entrance hung the lord protector's banner, displaying three black boars running towards the head of the banner on a blood-red field. It got quite dusty.
