Avarin was an infiltrator from the Cult of the Dragon at the Damaran Gate of Bloodstone Pass in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

On the night of Mirtul 2 , in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Avarin and his comrade, Fodel , opened the Damaran Gate for the Bandit Army of Vaasa . After Fodel was found out by a paladin of Ilmater , the two cultists got into a fight with the paladin and other soldiers. Right before Damaran reinforcements, including wizards , could slay them, an arriving Malazan incinerated these forces instead, allowing the two traitors to open the gate.
