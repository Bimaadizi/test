Bladuid was an elder gold elf high mage from Evermeet that served as the aide to Lord Laerm Ryence , co-commander of the Swift Cavalry as of the late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Like other gold elves, Bladuid had a particular disdain for humans and half-elves .

History

Bladuid attended to Lord Ryence during the campaign against the phaerimm in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR . Bladuid and Ryence were among those that survived the initial attack by the phaerimm as their group crossed Serpent's Tail on Nightal 28 of that year, though their fate was unknown after the subterranean aberrations let loose their most terrible war magic.
