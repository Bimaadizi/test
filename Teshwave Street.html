Teshwave Street was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Located in southern Zhentil Keep within the Foreign Quarter , Teshwave Street was a small road in the western Foreign Quarter that passed between Mace Way and the main intersection with Zhent Avenue in front of the Force Bridge . Teshwave Street also connected with Center Street and Twilight Street at the intersection.
