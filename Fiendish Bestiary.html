Fiendish Bestiary was a book on the topic of Hellish zoology that saw print sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Content

Fiendish Bestiary attempted to record the fiendish fauna and many creatures that battled in the Blood War . The chapter on devills took the majority of the book's page count.
