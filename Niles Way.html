Niles Way was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Niles Way was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . The road originated on Snail Street , branching west from its north section where it ended on Hillock Court near Castle Waterdeep . Niles Way also connected with Watchmen's Way and Guards Court .
