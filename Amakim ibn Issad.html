Amakim ibn Issad was the first king of the great city of Muluk in the Free Cities of North Zakhara .

History

Amakim founded Muluk during the fifth century DR . As of 1367 DR , his blood line had ruled Muluk for over 900 years. A statue dedicated to Amakim was found in Muluk.
