Abilities

As a shapeshifter druid, Voltine was afflicted with lycanthropy but had complete control over her transformation.

Possessions

She carried on her person a wand of polymorphing .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Voltine caught a mage by the name of Gervisse lurking around a building in the Upper City of Baldur's Gate, spying on two twin children, Laerta and Louise . She would transform herself in order to check in on the children and protect them from the old spellcaster.
