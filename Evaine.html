Evaine was a wizard in Phlan .

Relationships

Evaine was an acquaintance of Shal Desanea .

History

Evaine lived in a small tower near the city of Phlan. Her duty was to protect the city from the evil of the pool of darkness . She lived with her cat familiar Gamaliel .
