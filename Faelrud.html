King Faelrud was a king from the nation of Veldergauttland in the Whamite Isles . Faelrud represented Veldergauttland in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
