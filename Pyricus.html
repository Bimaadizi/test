Pyricus was an elemental prince in service to the primordial Maram of the Great Spear . In the Year of Deep Water Drifting, 1480 DR , he was freed from the Anchor of Chaos and tried to use subjugated barbarians from the Ride to fully destroy the anchor and free his master but was defeated by a band of adventurers hired by Lord Protector Anivar Daoran of Phlan .
