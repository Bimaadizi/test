Leera's Alley was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Leera's Alley branched slightly southwest off of Gut Alley in Waterdeep's Dock Ward , connecting with Drawn Sword Alley a short way to the west just south of Watchrun Alley .
