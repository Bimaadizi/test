Aman Al-Raqib was astrologer and advisor to Hetmann Alham Pasha Burak . He sought adventurers to fulfill Martek 's prophecy.

Description

The man had thinning hair, flaring eyebrows, and wore robes, with a pouch at his belt.

Personality

Aman was more than willing to share what he knew about the prophecies and legends of Martek, as well as rumors he knew. However, he had a scheming side, and would conspire to use his influence with the Hetmann to have adventurers forced into a quest if he felt he could not persuade them to undertake it willingly.

Activities

Aman was looking for adventurers to find the Star Gems of Martek and acquire his Sphere of Power. He had sent a letter to Elminster for that purpose alongside with a teleportation device.

Relationships

He was astrologer, advisor, and friends with Hetmann Alham Pasha. A mute giant, Fuigarm , was his bodyguard.
