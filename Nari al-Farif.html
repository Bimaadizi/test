Nari al-Farif was an inhabitant of Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Activities

Nari was the Dean of the College of Fine Arts . She often attended lectures by her instructors to ensure their competency. She would not hesitate to remove those whom she deemed unfit.

Nari was fluent in every language taught by her college.

Relationships

Nari was the niece of the head of the House of Al-Farif , a prominent merchant family in Huzuz.
