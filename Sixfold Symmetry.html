The Sixfold Symmetry was a mercenary organization within the Sevenfold Mazework of the Great Dismal Delve .

Organization

The Sixfold Symmetry was made-up of 500 crysmal mercenaries.

Activities

The activities of this group was unknown, but they were never seen outside of the Sevenfold Mazework.

Base of Operations

The Sevenfold Mazework within the Great Dismal Delve served as base of all dao military organizations.
