Iletian Blackeagle was a merchant living in Ashabenford in the late 14 th century DR , the leading partner of the Black Eagle Coster that operated in Mistledale . Iletian was a noted swordsman, journeying in an adventuring company with his business partners before they retired.

As a side business Iletian brew his own beer, Blackeagle brew , a raw and fiery ale that was extremely popular in the city of Glister .
