Angus Blackstone was an earl who lived in the Moonshaes in the 14 th century DR .

Possessions

He kept a pack of moorhounds , one of whom was called Warlock .

Relations

Blackstone's son and heir, Currag Blackstone , was killed in mysterious circumstances in 1365 DR .
