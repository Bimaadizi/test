Meraedos Fine Furs was a furrier shop in the North Ward of Waterdeep operated by Shalrin Meraedos in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Location

The shop was situated on the west side of the High Road near Tarnath Street .

Structure

The two-story building was a typical Waterdhavian row house.

Services

Meraedos Fine Furs specialized in expensive and fashionable fur creations, especially caps, vests, and cloaks with secret pockets. The proprietor also offered perfuming of furs.

Rumors & Legends

Rumor had it the shop was protected against intruders by a mysterious guardian. Some said one of Meraedos's furs was actually a living creature, either a great cat or some manner of furry cloaker .

Inhabitants

The proprietor, Shalrin Meraedos, was a high-ranking member of Waterdeep's Solemn Order of Recognized Furriers and Woolmen .
