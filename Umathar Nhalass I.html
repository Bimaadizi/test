Umathar Nhalass I , who was nicknamed the Horselord , was the first Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule Raumathar .

Activities

In −900 DR , Umathar I completed his assimilation of the Raumviran tribes of the Endless Wastes of Northeast Faerûn , forming the nation of Raumathor with the capital at Winterkeep . He reigned as Arkahn until his death in −867 DR .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Umathar II .
