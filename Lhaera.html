Lhaera was an inhabitant of the Dalelands in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

As of 1357 DR , she wasn't young anymore.

Personality

She was a kindly host.

Possessions

She wore patched clothes and owned at least one cauldron. Rathan Thentraver had blessed her house in the name of Tymora .

Relationships

Her daughter's name was Imraea .

History

She was visited by Rathan and Torm of the Knights of Myth Drannor one day in early Flamerule of 1357 DR , to bring her daughter a kitten, Snuggleguts . She had been healed by one of them recently, almost certainly by Rathan specifically.
