Kang Bay was a bay between the two branches of the Koryo Peninsula in Koryo in northeast Kara-Tur .

Locations

It held a number of islands, large and small, the greatest of which were Quelpart and Kanko . Kanko was actually a volcano that emerged from the sea.

Anticlockwise around the bay lay the Koryoan settlements of Tu Pe and its floating city; Xi Hulang on the mouth of the Hoparatu River , which flowed into the bay; Koje ; Xai Chung ; and Bejung , closest to Kanko.

The bay bordered the Sea of Kozakura .

History

In ancient times, when the Kanko volcano rose up out of Kang Bay and erupted, it was said that the very skies over Koryo caught fire. The land was devastated; the city of Old Bejung , closest to Kanko, was engulfed in poisonous gas that killed all the people, before the city itself was obliterated in a matter of seconds and buried in ash. According to legend, the entire Han clan sought shelter in the vast Dang Caverns .
