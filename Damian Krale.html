Damian Krale was a worshiper of Shar who was tasked by the Mistress of the Night to unite cells dedicated to her worship throughout the Western Heartlands . The ultimate goal of his endeavours was mostly a secret and only a few of the leaders of the cells knew what it was.

Physical appearance

Krale was a massive man with a booming voice, both of which served to enhance the force of his impressive personality.

Possessions

In battle Damian wielded Shadowstone , an enchanted heavy mace that was also a minor artifact created by Shar.
