Goldengulp Brewery was a brewery located in the Chauntean farm-complex known as Goldenfields .

Location

The Goldengulp Brewery was located in the center of Goldenfields, near the residential buildings.

Structure

The brewery was established within a two-story estate.

Adjoined to the rear side of the structure was a large watchtower referred to as the Dragon's Spire . It was so named for a bronze dragon that once perched upon its peak.

Services

Goldengulp was known for the production of its namesake beer . It was served at the local Northfurrow's End inn.

Inhabitants

About 60 individuals worked in the brewery, as of the late 15 th century DR .
