Arls Beatorh was a vintner merchant from the city of Athkatla in Amn who lived some years before the 15 th century DR .

History

Sometime before the 15 th century DR, Arls Beatorh infamously met his doom inside one of his own vats of wine . Even though presumably he died from drowning, some believed that Arls had some "help."
