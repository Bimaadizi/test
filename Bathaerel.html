Bathaerel , also known as rustbanish , was a purplish powder made from rocks found in the Abyssal layers of Rauwend and Voorz'zt .

Powers

When mixed with water, potatoes , and green plants and placed in a bag with rusty metal items, it would remove the rust from the iron , although it could not replace metal that had been already lost.
