Versveshardinazar , also known as The Opaline Terror , was the dragon ruler of the Dead Stone Mountains of Shyr , Abeir , in the centuries before the Spellplague .

History

Versveshardinazar was the owner of Haizverad , before the dragonborn earned his freedom. Versveshardinazar was interested in magic items, and used the remains of the primordial Petron to procure some for himself.
