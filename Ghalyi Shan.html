Ghalyi Shan was the easternmost mountain within the A-Ling Shan range in the Hordelands .

Geography

This impressive mountain was part of the A-Ling Shan range. It formed the eastern border of Yarlhang La .

Description

Ghalyi Shan towered over all other nearby mountains standing 19,000 ft (5,800 m) high.

Inhabitants

Ghalyi Shan was inhabited by a me-zu oni named Gyawa Zangpo , sentenced to live at the small hermitage at the mountain until he humbly carried 10,000 travelers to the top of the pass.
