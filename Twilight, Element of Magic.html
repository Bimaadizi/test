Twilight, Element of Magic was a powerful magical staff found during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

Twilight was a powerful aid to any spellcaster, letting them channel far more magical power than they could normally, with particular benefit to evocation and enchantment magic, and the use of pure arcane force. The staff's powers could be further augmented in combination with a planar focus of erudition , planar focus of prowess , or planar focus of subterfuge .

History

During the struggle with Lolth , heroes from Eberron invaded her domain to foil her plans with Ana Brabener and the Demon Weave in order to save their home and Faerûn . Among the prizes of Elminster 's victorious expedition was Twilight , which was a spark of magic destined for a studious mind.
