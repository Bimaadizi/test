Isundoun Impaerlath was a dwarven blacksmith inhabiting Saerloon in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Isundoun's claim to fame was the special enchanted axes of hurling that he forged. He was a well-known adventurer in his earlier days before he fired up his forge.
