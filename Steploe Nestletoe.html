Steploe Nestletoe was a halfling member of the Cellarers' & Plumbers' Guild in Undermountain in 1479 DR .

Personality

Steploe was an eternal optimist.

History

In 1479 DR, he was with his crew in the city's sewers when their lights suddenly went dark and he was struck on the head. Steploe woke up in Undermountain as a prisoner of the goblin and kobold followers of the mad half-elf mercenary Zarr . However, he believed he knew of a sure escape and he could learn something from this experience.
