Body sleighs were tools sometimes used by rangers or woodsmen throughout the colder regions of Faerûn .

Description

A typical body sleigh weighed just 8 pounds (3.6 kilograms).

History

These items were used to easily transport slain game animals, or even injured comrades, across the snow.

Body sleighs were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 8 gold pieces .
