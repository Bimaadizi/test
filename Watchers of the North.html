The Watchers of the North were a line of tors marking the northern edge of the Forlorn Hills .

Geography

The row of hills were the northernmost of the Forlorn Hills.

The western end of the Watchers bore a valley where the Floating Tower stood in the 3 rd century DR .

History

The Watchers of the North had that name from at least the 3 rd century DR .

Notable Locations

The temple known as Firehammer Hold , also known as Torstultok, was located underground, beneath the eastern Watchers.
