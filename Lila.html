Lila was a traveling musician, performer, and a bard on the Sword Coast North circa 1374 DR .

Description

Lila was a short and slender elven woman with large brown and chestnut braided hair. She wore a green performer's garb. She was a cold and aloof demeanor that strangely contrasted her vocation.

History

In the 1374 DR , Lila, a flautist , was a part of a roguish band of traveling performers that included Venn who played a lute , William – a tambourinist , and the troupe's leader, a flaming dancer named Azahr . Lila and the band spent some time performing at the Crossroad Keep as it was being reinvigorated by trade and commerce after a devastating Shadow War . The band's leader was happy to be making coin once again and planned on staying at the keep for a little while. However, that time was cut short after Azahr sole a precious artifact – Gauntlets of Ironfist , from the keep's seneschal. Lila accompanied her bandmates to Azahr's cabin hideout in the Neverwinter Wood . Khelgar Ironfist sent a group of adventurers to retrieve the precious stolen magic item; a brawl was ensured.
