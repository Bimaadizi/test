TypeFancy stone

Rubellite was a variety of tourmaline that was pink, red, or reddish-purple in coloration.

Usage

History

In the late 15 th century DR , untapped veins of rubellite could be found around the wild tundra of Cold Run . During that time it was commonly being used in the manufacture of titansteel equipment, magical rings , and sparkling, multicolored panes of glass.
