Rairun ( pronounced : /ˈ r ɛər ʌ n / RARE-unn ) was a druid in the interior Faerûn area in 1357 DR .

Activities

Rairun wandered the Inner Sea area trying to protect forested land from human activities, working with elves in the Dalelands , with the priests of Chauntea in Voonlar and Sembia , and with druids all around the area. His best idea was to plant trees like farmers planted grain and vegetable crops; this idea was used in some Dales and in the north of Sembia.

Relationship

Rairun had a feud with the satyrs of the area. He had tutored most of the druids of the area.
