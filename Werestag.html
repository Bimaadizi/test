A werestag was a type of lycanthrope that could take the form of a stag or a stag-like hybrid.

Description

Werestags were able to take different forms. The first was the animal-form, where the lycanthrope could transform into a stag. The other form was the hybrid form, where the lycanthrope would appear as a shaggy humanoid with the head of a stag.
