Just Barrels was a shop in the Central Trade Lanes of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR .

Structure

Just Barrels was a one-story building in poor condition.

Services

The shop produced barrels and kegs of every size, with small barrels priced at two gp and larger versions at three gp. The shop also offered specialized “security” barrels equipped with hidden compartments and false bottoms for smugglers or the particularly cautious. These custom items sold at a minimum of twice the cost of a standard barrel.

Inhabitants

Just Barrels was owned by “Lucky” Shrubfoot.
