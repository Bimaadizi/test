My Journeys Around the Sea of Fallen Stars was a book written by Nelve Harssad of Tsurlagol , first published in the Year of the Pointed Bone, 1298 DR , and reprinted in the Year of the Sword and Stars, 1332 DR .

The book was in three volumes. It detailed the author's experiences while traveling on and around the Sea of Fallen Stars , and provided a great deal of lore about the area.

Around 1372 DR , a copy could be found in the Tower of Tomes of the Leaves of Learning , a temple to Oghma in Deepingdale . The library at Candlekeep may have kept the original.
