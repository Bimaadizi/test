Borwald Firehammer was the chief advisor of Horgar Steelshadow in Gracklstugh during the Silence of Lolth .

History

Borwald was one of the most loyal followers of Horgar and was his chief advisor and marshal of the army. In 1372 DR , after seizing power, Horgar instructed him to prepare the army to march upon Menzoberranzan , as planned with Nimor Imphraezl .
