Dunstable's Dreams was a renowned language reference book. Politely called Dreams and casually called Dunstable , it was well-respected and much-referred to in the Heartlands of the Realms .

Description

The book was only the size of a human hand, but was extremely thick. A newer edition came with sturdy leather slipcovers with four thong catches, intended to help protect the spine of the book from cracking so swiftly.
