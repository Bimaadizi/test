The Syndicate of Celestial and Righteous Lawmakers was an association of warriors and paladins devoted to Amaunator , the Keeper of the Eternal Sun, because of his influence over law.

Organization

The Syndicate had 70 members as of the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , the vast majority of whom were paladins.

Activities

Members of this group infused the outward face of their shields with continual light to blind those opposed to the law.
