Drago Dreadloch was a bloodforge mage in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars ( 648 – 657 DR ).

Drago was supported via the Great Sea and was a strong foe of the leader of the Legendary Campaign . Snatching a surprise opportunity, the Legendary Campaign leader established a foothold on the Realm of Tides , and used it as base to strike at Drago in bloodforge battle.
