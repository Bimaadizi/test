Gwyon Ironhoof was the old and wise king of the centaurs of Evermeet circa 1367 DR .

Personality

Gwyon was a loyal and wise leader. He kept secret his wanderlust.

Relationship

Gwyon had good dealings with the elf Queen Amlaruil Moonflower .

Possessions

When in battle, Gwyon wielded a magical +1 bow and an enchanted +1 luck blade broadsword .

History

In his youth, Gwyon fought in Faerûn together with the Mindulgulph Mercenary Company .
