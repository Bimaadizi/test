Jaco Tirst was a dwarf inhabiting Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Activities

Jaco was the head guildmaster of the Goldsmithers' Guild . As head guildmaster he was in charge of ensuring the guild operated smoothly in their production of gold items.
