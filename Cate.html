Cate was the sister of an eccentric alchemist who lived in her old houseboat tethered to the Fisher's Wharf in Eastway in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

By the Year of the Purloined Statue, 1477 DR , Cate had already been long dead. Her sister Drusilla still cherished Cate's memory. The alchemist kept Cate's remains in a terra cotta urn decorated with a cameo of her face. When Drusilla made tea , she served a set of clay mugs for the sister, placing them in front of the urn. The alchemist made tea with honeycomb like Cate liked it in life.
