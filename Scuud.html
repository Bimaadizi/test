Scuud ( pronounced : / s k uː d / skood ) was a type of tuber vegetable found in the Realms.

Description

Scuuds were white, fleshy, and on average grew to the size of a human 's fist. They tasted like a cross between a potato and a radish .

History

In 1358 DR , a group of goblins and orcs living within the Dungeon Level of Undermountain had scuuds stored with other food for animal feed.

Usage

This vegetable was typically used for similar purposes as potatoes.
