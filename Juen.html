Juen was a member of the Adventurer's Guild of the Crossroad Keep and a member of Daerred 's adventuring group in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Juen was a halfling woman who wore an arcane spellcater's garb.

Activities

Juen and her companions claimed to had trained in the art of adventuring under the illustrious Knight-Captain, also known as the Kalach-Cha . When Juen's troupe leader, Daerred, was granted permission to open his guild in the mercantile fortress of the Crossroad Keep, she joined the rest of her traveling companions. Together, they dreamed of opening the greatest adventuring school on the Sword Coast , passing along all things they learned from the Knight-Captain to the next generation of heroes. Circa the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Juen could be found in the Adventurer's Guildhall, offering teamwork training to seasoned and aspiring adventurers alike.
