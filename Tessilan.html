Relationships

Tessilan worked with two other merchants, Glayde and Faltis .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Tessilan and his fellow businessmen were targeted by the scam artist Silke Rosena . She arranged to make a purchase of gems from them at the Red Sheaf inn, while telling others a different story. Silke told Garrick and some others that Tessilan, Glayde, and Faltis were thugs coming to collect payment for a missed performance at Feldepost's Inn . She hoped to have Tessilan's group mugged in order to steal the gems and get away all the richer.
