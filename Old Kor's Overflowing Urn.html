Old Kor's Overflowing Urn was a shop in the Upper Trade Lanes of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR .

Structure

Old Kor's Overflowing Urn was a one-story building in fair condition.

Interior

The interior was permeated with the acrid scent of smoke and combustion, and the walls bore the blackened marks of countless accidents past.

Services

The shop specialized in selling oil, pitch, tar, and resin in bulk, typically by the barrel. Prices began at 10 gp and varied based on the material and quantity.

Inhabitants

Old Kor's Overflowing Urn was owned by Old Kor . It was not uncommon for Kor to confuse orders because of his failing memory. As such, seasoned customers were in the habit of inspecting the contents of their barrels before finalizing a purchase.
