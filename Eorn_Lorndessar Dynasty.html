The Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty ruled over the city-state of Westgate from the Year of the Dracorage, 1018 DR , until the Year of the Cockatrice, 1248 DR . The dynasty began when Sarvyn Eorn was named King of Westgate through the great praise of the city after King Tarnhart Campion II died without an heir.
