The axes of the slicing storm were a pair of unique enchanted throwing axes crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Saarvin in the late 15 th century DR . They were just two of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

Laeral crafted these axes to complement Saarvin's gift for being able to maneuver himself on the battlefield to turn his enemies' defensive positions into liabilities.

Powers

When thrown at an enemy, the axes of the slicing storm had a chance of also attacking the target's nearby allies.
