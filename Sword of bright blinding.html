The sword of bright blinding was a unique enchanted sword crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Halbenet Soladonel in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

Silverhand crafted this one-handed sword to be wielded by Halbenet specifically, and said that its enchantment was inspired by their gift of calm on the battlefield that would amaze even their enemies.

Powers

It was a mighty, glowing blade that could stun those it struck.
