Mellomir was a wizard who lived in Arabel in Cormyr in 1367 DR .

Activities

In addition to being a powerful wizard, Mellomir was a sage specializing in history, prophecy, and divination.

He accurately predicted the fall of Bhaal and Myrkul during the Time of Troubles , as well as the return of the Ring of Winter .

Due to various kidnap attempts, Mellomir cast a unique personal protection spell warding his body against many forms of attack.
