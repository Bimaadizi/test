The Horned Lord tribe was an orcish tribe that inhabited the High Forest in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Homeland

The orcs lived in small villages and underground tunnels in the High Forest, located around two days' journey from the forest's edge.

Customs

Like many of the High Forest orc tribes, the Horned Lord tribe worshiped Herne the Wild Hunter .

Abilities

The members of this tribe possessed many of the forestry skills typical of rangers .

Relationships

The members of this tribe were often at odds with human , elf , and half-elf rangers.

History

In the Year of the Horn, 1222 DR , the Horned Lord and Tanglethorn tribes gathered in the Blackhorn Horde and overran the settlement of Lord Ornigar Tchazzam in Starshadow Vale . This was to avenge the plundering of the orcish burial mounds in the vale.
