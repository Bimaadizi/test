Pallar the Obedient was a human man of the city of Procampur in the Vast .

He was a priest of Torm and was High Priest of the House of the Hand temple by 1358 DR , a position which he still held by 1367 DR . He had 26 subpriests and 48 lay-worshipers.

Priests of Torm ran Calispar Delgorth , a poison-dealing priest of Talona , out of the city early in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR . When Calispar later dealt with the Shadowkind assassins in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , he asked them to assassinate the high priest.
