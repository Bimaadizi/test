Jimmy was a human pirate from the isle of Brynnlaw who sailed the Bleedin' Mary in the Sea of Swords in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Jimmy was a large and jolly man with sun-kissed skin and shiny bald head.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Jimmy could be found drinking in the Vulgar Monkey dock-side tavern. Like so many fellow pirate customers of the rowdy establishment, Jimmy was an avid shanty-singer. I Flounder an' Den I Lobster , a seafood-pun-filled shanty was his favorite to drunkenly sing.
