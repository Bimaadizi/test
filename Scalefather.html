The Scalefather was a powerful lizardfolk shaman that rose prominence on the Pirates' Skyhold earthmote in the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

Wielding a massive spear and metal shield, the Scalefather attacked his foes with ferocity and cunning, utilizing his great strength and connection with divine magic . He could conjure forth roots and plans from within the earth to hold his enemies in place, while throwing projectiles that exploded with poisonous gas.

Activities

The Scalefather led the tribe of lizardfolk that dwelled within Vilebog swamps, west of Skull Fortress . He maid is lair in the aptly-named cave, the Scalefather's Hollow .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Company Yargo and the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge ventured to Pirates' Skyhold in part to recover what they could from the crashed skyship , the Magister's Hope . During their foray into the swamp, the adventurer delved into the Scalefather's cave and slew him alongside his personal guard.
