The Rusty Nail was the largest inn to be found in the dock section of Hillsfar around 1367 DR . Since it was located outside of the walled city it was perfectly legal for the inn to cater to adventurers .

Location

The inn was situated on the coast of the Moonsea near the main docks.

Services

A bed and meal at the Rusty Nail cost 6 gp per night but that price did not include ale .
