Shiau Chen was the proprietor of the Lily Pad Inn in the Xiousing district of Marsember .

Chen ran the inn with the help of his Cormyrean wife Cornelia Chen and his children. He was once a member of the xenophobic society known as The Chuen but was kicked out when he took a non- Shou wife.
