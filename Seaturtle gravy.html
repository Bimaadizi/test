Seaturtle gravy was a savory condiment found in the city of Waterdeep on the Sword Coast North .

Description

Seaturtle gravy was thick and brown in color and closely resembled beef gravy . It was a popular condiment that was served with pan-fired quipper fish as evenfeast at the Yawning Portal .
