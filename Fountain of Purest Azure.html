The Fountain of Purest Azure was a fountain located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The fountain was found within the Foundry district in the south central section of the city. It lay along the eastern side of the Riqaq Canal south of the Street of Stelae and north of the Naranj Canal .
