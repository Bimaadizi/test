An avenging hammer was a magical warhammer that only the physically strongest could wield.

Powers

In battle, the avenging hammer was functionally equivalent to a lucern hammer +2 . Upon striking an opponent wearing metal armor (such as chainmail or plate mail ), there was a slight chance that the hammer would destroy the armor, shattering it into pieces and rendering it completely useless.
