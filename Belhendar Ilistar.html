Belhendar Ilistar , who was nicknamed the Stout , was the second King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Belhendar inherited the throne when his father, King Farnath I , died in the Year of the Baleful Song, 663 DR . He ruled until the Year of the Wandering Sylph, 687 DR .

Relationships

He had two sons. The younger son, Thartryn , would succeed him to the throne.
