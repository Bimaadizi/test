Falconer was a dwarf in Daggerford around 1370 DR .

Activities

Falconer was an unusual dwarf in peace with nature. He worshiped Chauntea and was able to communicate with animals, which always filled his house.

His home was located in central of Daggerford along Fields Lane and Farmer's Road next to the market.
