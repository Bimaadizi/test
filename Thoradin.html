Thoradin was an inhabitant of Luskan , Sword Coast North , in the late 15 th century.

History

Thoradin once befriended Larethar Gulgrin , and eventually saved his life.

In the late 1480s DR, he was working as a drug mule, smuggling traveler's dust , a highly illegal substance, in Luskan.

Around 1487 DR , he reached out to Larethar after many years apart, asking to meet him in the Cutlass inn in Luskan.
