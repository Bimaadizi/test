Savra Parhek was an Amnian son of the Parhek dynasty.

Relationships

Savra was the daughter of Prince Malek the Coinmage , and the twin of Rhinn . Their mother died in childbirth, and they had a younger half-brother Parav by their stepmother.

History

Savra grew up in Waterdeep as part of the exiled royal Parhek family. They were located by her cousin Prince Rohav , and she was believed to have been killed in 1305 DR by his agents.
