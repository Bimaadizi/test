A gorgon plate was a suit of enchanted full plate mail crafted by the drow of the Underdark under Old Shanatar . It was different from most armor crafted by the dark elves, in that it could withstand being exposed to light when it was brought upon the surface world of Toril .

Powers

This +4 set of full plate armor offered its wearer extra protection against acid and fire, both mundane or magical in nature.

Creation

This armor earned its name from a unique ingredient used in its crafting, the blood of a gorgon .

Notable wearers

Matron mother Ardulace Despana of Ust Natha was known to wear a suit of gorgon plate armor.
