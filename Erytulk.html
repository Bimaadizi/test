Erytulk was the shaman of the Kuro goblin tribe in Chult .

Relationship

Erytulk was the younger brother of Irtana .

Personality

Erytulk believed himself the most powerful Batiri shaman of all Chult. However, he was greedy, always wanting more power and wealth. Secretly, Erytulk envied his sister and desired control of the village but knew this was an impossible dream.
