Viola Silvertongue was a musician and minstrel, part of the Circus of the Last Days troupe in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Viola was a colorful human woman with fair skin and light pink hair. She wore red metallic cosmetics and was armed with a rapier .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Circus of the Last Days returned from the Feywild and opened its gates to the inhabitants of the Rivington district in Baldur's Gate . Viola Silvertongue entertained guests by performing alongside Calliope Blingsdorf , who believed her be a true wonder.
