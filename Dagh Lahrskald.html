Dagh Lahrskald was the leader of the city of Ruathym , the largest settlement on the island of Ruathym in the Trackless Sea , as of the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

He was an old warrior by that time, but commanded a fearsome force of barbarians , shamans , and witches .

History

He pledged his loyalty to the Storm Maiden after witnessing her defeat his three most skilled berserkers in combat by herself. As of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , she had tasked him with uniting the various settlements across the island of Ruathym, using force as necessary. She promised future raiding and pillaging across the seas once he had succeeded.
