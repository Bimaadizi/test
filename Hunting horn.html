Hunting horns were musical instruments in the Realms popular among bards .

Description

These small curved horns produced a sound that could be heard several miles away. They were usually made from wood, horn, shell , or brass .

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 20–35 gold pieces depending on the material used in their construction.
