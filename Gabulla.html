Gabulla was a goblin shaman living near Julkoun around 1485 DR .

History

Around 1486 DR , Gabulla was possessed by the pit fiend Baazka and, under his manipulation, convinced her leader Shorg to invade the village of Julkoun, following the suggestion of one of the Red Wizards of Thay . Later, she was defeated by an adventuring party.
