Musings on the Realms was a book written by Oren bel Dannar , the sage of Triel . The year of its publishing was unknown.

As of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , a copy of the book could be found in both the Leaves of Learning in Highmoon , and in Candlekeep .
