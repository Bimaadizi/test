Race(s)Aarakocra

Donakkis Storage Company was a warehouse business on the planet Coliar that provided cargo storage to visiting spelljammers and served as a trade base.

Organization

Donakkis Storage Co. was an aarakocra establishment, owned by the large and powerful Donakkis family. The family owned twelve such bases, all located in the southeast hemisphere of the planet. One of these trade bases was located on the major port island of Athanar .
