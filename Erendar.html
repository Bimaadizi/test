Erendar was an undead mummy in Myth Drannor , who existed from at least the Weeping War up to the late 14 th century DR .

History

The undead creature served as a powerful tomb guardian in the south wing of Ildrannath's Tower . It was eventually disturbed by Elves of Cormanthor intruders, who sought the Key to the city's mythal .
