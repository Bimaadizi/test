The Fellowship Warehouse , also known as the Fish Warehouse , was a warehouse in Waterdeep that was owned by the Waterdhavian guild known as the Fishmongers' Fellowship .

Location

This warehouse was located along the Dock Street , in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep . It was located near the Smokehouse , which was also owned by the Fishmongers' Fellowship.

Structure

This warehouse was four-stories tall.
