Urko was a kobold in northwest Faerûn .

Urko was the leader of the kobolds who attacked Hilltop in 1372 DR on orders from Tymofarrar . After being attacked by gnolls , Urko and his men sought refuge in a nearby elven crypt, only to find it infested with undead elven warriors and giant spiders . Terrified, they barricaded themselves in an empty room, but were soon approached by an unnamed adventurer who wished to retrieve the mummy hand the kobolds had stolen from Drogan Droganson .

Yazka and Kipper were Urko's lieutenants.
