Saerin Fierne was a resident wizard at the Chantry of St. Sammaster for the Cult of the Dragon in the late 14 th century DR . He had achieved the rank of Keeper of the Sacred Horde.

Description

He was 5 feet and 10 inches (1.8 meters) tall.

Personality

Though formidable, he was willing to flee combat if he had to.

Abilities

He was a very tough guy, in addition to being a mighty wizard.

Possessions

Of note, he carried a superior ring of protection and a quarterstaff .

Activities

He carried out the responsibilities of a Keeper of the Sacred Horde for the Cult of the Dragon.

Relationships

He was second-in-command to Maezra , a Wearer of Purple within the cult cell.

History

In the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Saerin Fierne was left in charge of the Chantry of St. Sammaster by Maezra, the leader of his cell, when she set out to bribe Nevalarich into joining the Cult. During her absence, Zhentarim forces descended on the chantry; during the conflict, he may have entered into battle with the invaders, or even crossed adventurers sent after them by Whistkins . He planned to escape if he had to, however.
