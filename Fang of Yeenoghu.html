A Fang of Yeenoghu was a gnoll that had been been possessed by a demonic spirit, a gift bestowed upon them by the demon lord Yeenoghu . They were considered the Lord of Savagery's most favored servants.

Abilities

Whenever a Fang of Yeenoghu slew another creature, the victim's corpse could in a sense birth new gnolls. Any hyena that feasted upon the remains of a Fang's prey would initiate a horrific transformation. The hyena's body would burst open, and a fully-grown mature gnoll would emerge from its viscera. This frightful metamorphosis reflected the moment when the first gnolls were spawned by Yeenoghu himself.

Combat

Fangs of Yeenoghu often wielded 'cudgels of bloody teeth', massive greatclubs studded with teeth that seeped blood.

Society

Yeenoghu's chosen garnered further favor from the demon lord by capturing mortals as slaves and dragging them down to his domain in the Abyss . They often served as shamans that presided over rituals dedicated to the Prince of Gnolls.
