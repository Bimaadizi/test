Lorthio Bukbukken was a duergar alchemist in Gracklstugh in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationship

Lorthio had dealings with the corrupt Gorglak .

Activities

Lorthio was an alchemist of Clan Bukbukken and a member of the Gray Ghosts .

History

Circa 1486 DR , Lorthio was waiting for a delivery from Droki .
