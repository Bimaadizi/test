RaceMuskox

The mystic musk ox was a magical , legendary muskox that laired within the maze of King Mertwig in the Vast .

Abilities

Any intruder this musk ox attacked in its maze lair would shortly thereafter be teleported outside of it. The creature was also somehow connected to an onyx figurine of a muskox belonging to the royal family, which would grant whoever wielded it protection from the muskox.

History

How the mystic musk ox came to be or how it came to lair within Mertwig's Maze was a mystery.
