Vanthorm was an adventurer inhabiting the Savage Frontier during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

While exploring the Fallen Lands circa the Year of the Dragon, 1352 DR , Vanthorm once witnessed a massive beholder command hobgoblin servants to capture various monsters to be used for a bizarre breeding program.
