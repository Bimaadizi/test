Ratlarbig was a fortress that overlooked Lennahc Hsilgne in the Whamite Isles . Although it was located within the territory of Zagrus , it was not necessarily aligned with that nation. Ratlarbig took a tax of gold from all traders who sailed on Lennahc Hsilgne.

Ratlarbig was a key location in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
