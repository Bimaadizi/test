That Which Watches was a death knight sentry that guarded the entrance to the Murder Tribunal deep in the Undercellar of Baldur's Gate's Lower City in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

That Which Watches wore a set of intricately decorated plate mail , blackguard's gauntlets , plate boots , and a horsetail helmet . The creature's armor was lines with gold . It carried a longsword .

Abilities

That Which Guards shared abilities and vulnerabilities of other undead creatures. It could lang smiting strikes of great power that could leave the targets staggered.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , That Which Watches was placed to stand guard of the entrance to the Murder Tribunal alongside That Which Lurks and That Which Guards . The creature spoke in rhymes with an archaic turn of phrase.
