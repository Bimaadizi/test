The Benevolent Old Fat Wise Man was a mysterious and notably generous individual from Far Vandmeer in the Whamite Isles . He made occasional appearances during the wild conflicts between various nations to the north. The man arrived suddenly and philanthropically donated gold to those who were losing the wars. His visits took place at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
