Sorntalar's Rest was the an inn located in the Amnian city of Crimmor . It was famous for offering a quiet place for guests desiring to rest up.

Location

The inn was located in the Wheel Ward , near the western city walls.

Interior

Atmosphere

Tapestries were hung from the thick walls, and halls were carpeted in order to dampen excessive noise, and provide a respite from the constant noisy bustle of the streets. The owner attached a great importance to the quietness of her establishment, going so far as to ask noisy guests to leave.
