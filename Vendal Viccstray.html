Sheriff Vendal Viccstray was a strongheart halfling warrior and commander of the militia in the village of Shining Falls in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Based in the town armory, which part of the Granite Tower in the center of the village, Vendal led the village's militia. He was aided by Sergeants Hethat Deegrell , Jren Prencertal , and Nemmet Viccstray and forty guards. Since Deogol Fengrath ruled with a light hand, he left the day-to-day running of things to Vendal.
