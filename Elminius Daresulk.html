Elminius Daresulk , Bard of Chondath , was a well-known individual in the Vilhon Reach in the late 14 th century DR .

History

As of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Elminius Daresulk was a vocal advocate for redrawing maps of the Vilhon Reach according to the political boundaries that often varied greatly from published maps of the region. Cultural and politically inaccurate maps led to trouble for adventurers who visited the region from afar, unaware of fragile political relations between the Free Cities and nations of the Reach.
