Ziraj the Hunter ( pronounced : / z ɪər ˈ ɔː dʒ / zeer-AWJ ) was a half-orc agent of the Zhentarim , operating within the city of Waterdeep during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

A taciturn man, Ziraj would only kill a target if asked to by one of his friends.

Abilities

Ziraj was a master archer and a skilled assassin .

Possessions

He carried an oversized bow , with which he fired equally oversized arrows .

Relationships

Ziraj was a member of the Doom Raiders adventuring company. He and his former companions composed the leadership of the Zhentarim of Waterdeep. After Ziraj saved the life of Fala Lefaliir , they became friends. Ziraj frequently visited Fala, who kept a room reserved for him.

History

Ziraj was once an adventurer but together with his adventuring companions he decided to join the Zhentarim. In 1489 DR , Ziraj and his allies were sent to establish a Zhent foothold in Waterdeep.
