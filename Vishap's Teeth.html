The Vishap's Teeth was a landmark located in the High Desert of Zakhara .

Description

It was surely a striking feature. Four immense pyramids of dark granite composed the structure.

Rumors and Legends

Most believed the Vishap's Teeth to be a natural landmark, but there were those who thought they were actually the teeth of a sleeping wingless dragon or vishap .
