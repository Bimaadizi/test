Kraanfhaor's Door was an ancient elven magic college in Talthalaran on the High Moor . It was one of the most ancient colleges, dating to millennia before the Descent of the drow . In the Year of the Haunting, 1377 DR , Q'arlynd Melarn found a clue to its location inside a kiira found in the High Moor.
