Muthoth was a necromancer of the Red Wizards and a follower of the lich Szass Tam in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Muthoth had sharp, haughty features. He had no fingers on his right hand, having lost them in a fight with the bard Bareris Anskuld .

Relations

He was often accompanied by his fellow necromancer So-Kehur .

History

Muthoth and his companion So-Kehur played a key role during the battle at the Keep of Sorrows by killing many opposing warriors with their dark magic, and raising them as undead to add to Szass Tam's forces.
