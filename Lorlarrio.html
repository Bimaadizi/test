Lorlarrio was a duergar linguist and a member of the Society of Brilliance during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Lorlarrio was fascinated by the long vowel shift in the Middle Kobold language .

Relationships

Lorlarrio pondered Professor Limeleech 's true opinion of her.

History

Lorlarrio wrote a guide for language teachers called The Pronunciation of Common for Monstrous Species: A Guide for Pedagogues .

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Lorlarrio lived in the Lodge of the Society of Brilliance and was actively working on a translation dictionary aimed at bridging the communication gap between the warring species of the Underdark .

She tried to find someone speaking the Classical Meenlock language to conduct an interview with for her dictionary.
