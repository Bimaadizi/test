Mount Okk was a mountain in the Alpuk region of the Great Glacier .

Geography

Mount Okk was a tall mountain, up to 12,000 feet (3,700 meters) in elevation. Its height made it one of the tallest mountains within the Lugsaas Chain . Within a fissure at the base of the northern side of the mountain was the dangerous and mysterious Ice Castle .
