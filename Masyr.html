Masyr ( pronounced : /ˈ m ɔː z ɪər / MAW-zeer ) was a wizard and businessman of Zhentil Keep in 1358 DR .

Activities

Though he owned both the Green Sash Tavern & Pleasure House and the Green Rings Trading Company , Masyr was always short on coin. As a skilled invoker , he tutored students in order to supplement his income.
