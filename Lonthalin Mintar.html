Lonthalin Mintar was a human wizard in Baldur's Gate in the mid–14 th century DR .

Activities

Lonthalin, like many of his fellow minor mages, worked from a stall in the Wide , an open market of Baldur's Gate, and was a fixture there around 1366 DR . He specialized in spells that provided temporary cosmetic adornments, such as fantastic hairstyles, tattoos, scents, and changes in color and making revealing costumes cling. He could make disguises for a fee of 25–100 gp .
