The Carpenters' Lodge was the guildhall for the carpenters and other wood-workers in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The structure stood four storys tall.

Location

The guildhall was located in the northwest corner of Daggerford at the junction of Wall Street and Horse Way .
