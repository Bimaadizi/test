Amrythale Goldsheaf was a woman of Heldon in Athalantar in the early 3 rd century DR . She was the mother of Elminster Aumar .

Relationships

Her father was a forester. She married Elthryn Aumar , a prince of Athalantar.

History

She married Elthryn Aumar in the Year of the Steelfall, 210 DR . They had a child, Narrune Aumar , in the Year of Spoiled Splendors, 211 DR , but she went missing, leaving only blood on her crib. They had another child, Elminster Aumar, who was born in the Year of the Awakening Magic, 212 DR .

Amrythale Goldsheaf was killed in the razing of Heldon by Undarl Dragonrider and his black dragon steed Anglathammaroth in the Year of the Flaming Forests, 224 DR .

Rumors & Legends

It was said she must've bewitched Prince Elthryn somehow to get him to marry her.
