Shock darts were small enchanted thrown weapons that dealt lightning damage to their targets.

Powers

These darts were small enough to be easily concealed. Upon closer exemption, carved lines of power could be seen on each of the missiles. When they reached the target, these darts released a burst of electricity.

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , shock darts were used by Aurilian druids who resided in the secluded village of Andora , deep in Icewind Dale 's wilderness.
