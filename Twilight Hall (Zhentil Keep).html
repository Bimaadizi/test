Twilight Hall was a temple dedicated to Cyric located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

The temple was located in south Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the Foreign Quarter .

Description

Twilight Hall was a short structure made from black granite . The front double doors were made from copper and Cyric's holy symbol adorned them.

Tombs filled with notable worshipers of Cyric, including Lord Chess after a special arrangement was made with Khelben Arunsun in Waterdeep , could be found in the subterranean section beneath the temple.

Defenses

The entire temple was warded with protection from magic and protection from good spells . Arcane magic and magic items of any kind would not function within Twilight Hall, though divine magic worked normally. This reflected Cyric's utter hatred of Mystra .

Activities

Religious services were held twice daily an hour before dawn and an hour after sunset. A special service was held once per week at midnight, and extremely special events were held during solar eclipses.

Inhabitants

Fornault Blacksun was High Priest of the temple in 1369 DR . He was assisted by 16 acolytes.
