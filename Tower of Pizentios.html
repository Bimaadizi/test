The Tower of Pizentios was a singular large spire found on the northeast region of the island of Sahu in the Ruined Kingdoms of east Zakhara in the early 1400s DR .

Description

The Tower was roughly mushroom-shaped, similar to the towers found in the ruins of Ereshkigal on the opposite side of the island. The Tower was situated near a small lagoon where several storage sheds could be found.

A door in the basement of the Tower led into the Underdark and the Garden of Eternity .

History

As its name implied, the Tower was the home of the necromancer Pizentios in 1367 DR .
