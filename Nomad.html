A nomad was a type of psion who specialized in the psychoportation discipline of psionics , honing their skills in propelling and displacing both creatures and objects across spacetime. They had no equivalent among the specialist wizards .

Culture

Among the greater paths of psionics, psions of the nomad discipline often took interest in the agile ways of the elocater .

Abilities

Nomads almost exclusively utilized powers that fell within the purview of the psychoportation discipline. Their specialization in this discipline allowed egoists to unlock a suite of powers that were unknown to other psions. The most skilled of nomads honed their study of the discipline to such a degree that they could achieve movement through time .
