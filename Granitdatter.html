Fist Granitdatter was a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Granitdatter was a dwarvish woman with pale skin, blue eyes, long strawberry blonde hair and a clean-shaven face. She wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist – a set of chain mails and a tabard adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia, with a large greataxe strapped to her back.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Granitdatter was stationed just outside one of the gates between the Lower City of Baldur's Gate and its more affluent Upper City . Her and the fellow Fists' job was stopping rabble from entering the Upper City, allowing only those with much coin through. The Fists managed a slow-moving queue of rich and poor – city folk trying to return home or just find safety behind the second set of walls.
