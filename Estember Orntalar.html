Estember Orntalar was a priest of Oghma of the Domes of Reason temple in the city of Procampur in the Vast .

Circa 1369 DR , Orntalar held the position of High Loremaster Librarian at the Domes, but was engaged in an aggressive power struggle with fellow priests to succeed the missing Patriarch, Cullen Kordamant , and become leader of the Orthodox Church of Oghma . Orntalar was apparently set to win this position.
