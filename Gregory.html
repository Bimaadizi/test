Gregory was a human bouncer who kept peace at the Black Eye tavern and festhall in the city of Westgate in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Gregory was the oldest and the most hulking of the three brothers: Little Davey , the Black Eye's proprietor, and Marv , a fellow bouncer. Gregory was 7 feet (2.1 meters) tall and looked capable enough to reduce an ogre to a bloody pulp.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Gregory, armed with a club , guarded the entrance to the Black Eye tavern along with his younger brother Marv. Few could best Gregory in a brawl, however Mantides , a disgraced drunkard paladin managed to do just that in 1374 DR.
