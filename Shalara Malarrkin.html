Shalara Malarrkin was an apprentice wizard inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

She married her long-time tutor, Kappiyan Flurmastyr . Shalara resided with Kappiyan in a comfortable home in Waterdeep's Southern Ward .
