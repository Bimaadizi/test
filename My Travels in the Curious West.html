My Travels in the Curious West was a travelogue book written by Samatcho Ahonu and published in the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR .

Contents

This book detailed the experiences of an inhabitant of Kara-Tur traveling in Faerûn .

History

A copy of My Travels in the Curious West belonged to Manshoon XII , a clone of Manshoon , around 1370 DR . The book was stored in the mage 's library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep .
