History

Throughout the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , Trystan helped the sage Elminster correspond to his fellow scholar and member of the Avowed , Gorion , by delivering his incoming letters. The following year , Trystan received one more letter from Elminster, asking about information regarding the Bhaalspawn . Trystan had the unfortunate duty of informing Elminster that Gorion had left Candlekeep shortly before, along with his ward Abdel .
