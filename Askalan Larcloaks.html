Askalan Larcloaks was a gnome wheelwright in Immerford in Cormyr .

History

In 1480 DR , Askalan and other wealthy citizens went to protest to Lord Lothan Durncaskyn about an unseen thief and murderer. He protested about the detention of Lords Cornyn Risingbroke and Yarland Amflame inside Castle Irlingstar .
