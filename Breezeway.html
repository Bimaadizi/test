The Breezeway was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

The Breezeway was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . The road branched southeast from an unnamed courtyard, turning south through a small neighborhood. Endshift Street joined with the northeast side of the Breezeway.
