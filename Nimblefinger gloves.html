Nimblefinger gloves were enchanted gloves that boosted the wearer's finger dexterity and fortified their sleight of hand skills.

Description

Nimblefinger gloves were crafted out of the softest finest doe leather that offered warmth and protection to the wearer. The gloves were designed specifically to fit a gnomish hand perfectly so that they felt like the wearer's second skin.

Powers

Any creature who wore the gloves had their sleight of hand skills slightly boosted, however, small folk, like hin or dwarves also had their overall dexterity slightly boosted, while gnomes received both boons with their overall dexterity boosted even further.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Rhonda Swiftfootsdottir , a family member of Glint Gardnersonson , possessed a pair of these enchanted gloves, possibly giving them to Glint as a gift.

A pair of nimblefinger gloves was sold by the merchant Bumpnagel of Clan Ironhand , a deep gnome clan of artificers from Baldur's Gate , in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
