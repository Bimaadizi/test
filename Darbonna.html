Fist Darbonna was a member of the Flaming Fist and a healer who served the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Darbonna was a human woman with tan skin, bluish-green eyes, and shortly-trimmed brown curly hair. She wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist – a set of splint mail and a tabard adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia, with a large hammer strapped to her back.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Darbonna was stationed just outside one of the gates between the Lower City of Baldur's Gate and its more affluent Upper City . Her and other fellow Fists' job was stopping rabble from entering the Upper City, allowing only those with much coin through. The Fists managed a slow-moving queue of rich and poor – city folk trying to return home or just find safety behind the second set of walls.
