Alard Belaerl was a tavern owner in Waterdeep around 1367 DR .

Activities

Alard managed the Sleeping Snake tavern in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep , where it was possible to watch dancers perform. He also sold the services of his dancers as spies to various thieves.
