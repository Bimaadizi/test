The Leaning Man Inn was an inn just outside the city of Westgate in 1367 DR .

Description

The sign for the Leaning Man Inn depicted a standing old man. The sign was tiled at a sharp angle, giving the establishment its name.

Location

The inn was located beyond the city's West Gate .

Activities

The Leaning Man Inn was a quiet place that promised a secure night's sleep. Its accommodations were fair and the prices were affordable.
