Birta Grougg was a dockworker from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Birta was a large female half-orc with grayish hide and dark hair gathered in twin buns.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Birta Grougg worked in the Gate's docks alongside Yves . They were known to borrow wine bottles from the shipments they worked on and, during breaks, sip the alcohol while pretending to be fanciful sommeliers, claimed that they detected notes, smells, and flavors of various foul things in the wine.
