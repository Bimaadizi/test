Murtlen was an unhappy commoner from the City of Coin, Athkatla , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Murtlen was a fair-skinned human male with red hair.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Murtlen could be found shopping in Athkatla's Center District , in Waukeen's Promenade . He frequented shops and stalls opened on the elevated wooden walkways, including stalls run by Maheer yn Kadar el Saddith and Quataris . Murtlen ranted at passersby folk about them surely having been cheated by these merchants and accused their goods of being shoddy and of poor quality.
