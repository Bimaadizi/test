Sundeth Gossyl was a cleric of Tymora in the Moonsea until 1356 DR .

History

Growing up in Sembia , Sundeth in her adult days went to Moonsea to spread the words of Tymora and was soon known as a fighting-priestess involved in many adventures.

In 1356 DR, she sacrificed her life to destroy a rogue wyvern that was trying to destroy the Citadel of the Raven .
