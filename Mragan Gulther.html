Mragan Gulther was a farmer in Red Larch circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . Mragan grew hornapples in an orchard just north of the town off the Long Road . The orchard could be found by turning east at Nurnath 's big, green chicken barn onto the cart track that led to Old Angrath's Smokehouse . The orchard occupied one side of the road and ran for almost a mile. On the other side of the road was pastureland owned by Indurr "Warsword" Stalnstern .
