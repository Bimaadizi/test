Queen Besmra was a ship in the Cormyrian navy in the 14 th century DR .

Etymology

The Queen Besmra followed the standard naming customs of official Cormyrean naval ships, being named for one of the nation's rulers. In its case, the ship was named for Besmra Obarskyr , the 24 th Obarskyr monarch of Cormyr.
