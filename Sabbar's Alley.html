Sabbar's Alley was a small back alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Sabbar's Alley was located in the northwest section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . The alley branched east off of the Street of the Singing Dolphin , terminating at a two dead ends behind some buildings just south of Aureenar Street .
