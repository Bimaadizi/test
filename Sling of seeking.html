The sling of seeking was a magical weapon with improved accuracy and damage over a normal sling . Some versions of this weapon provided infinite ammunition.

Description

This weapon significantly improved a user's accuracy and damage when hurling bullets at a target. It turned any type of standard sling bullet into the equivalent of a magically enchanted bullet. An improved version of this enchanted sling didn't require any sort of ammunition and could endlessly fire slightly enchanted bullets.
