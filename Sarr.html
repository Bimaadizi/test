Sarr was a tiny island in the Pirate Isles on the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Geography

Sarr was found in the southern region of the Pirate Isles due south of Mirg Isle and Telfar .

Description

A single ruined lighthouse sat on Sarr's eastern tip, crumbling and laying half-buried in the sand.

Inhabitants

The island was exposed to harsh storms approaching the Pirate Isles from the south, causing most pirates to ignore it.

Rumors & Legends

Unbeknownst to the pirates, a magical artifact called the Beacon of Light was buried within the ruins of the lighthouse.
