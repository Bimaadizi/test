Snow Tiger helms were enchanted protective cowls made out of heads of snow tigers and worn by the Snow Tiger lodge of Rashemi berserkers .

Powers

Each of these cowls offered the wearer a magical +2 enchanted armor and dramatically boosted their physique constitution. Additionally, Snow Tiger helms made their wearers immune to magical and ordinary fear .

History

By the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Snow Tiger helms were used by some of the Rashemaar berserkers stationed in the Ashenwood 's Lake of Tears Garrison , a Rashemi fort led by a wychlaran Dalenka . Odesya , the fort's requisitions officer, offered one of these items for sale to the fort's allies.
