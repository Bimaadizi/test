The Firloch was a lake found on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The lake was located near the center of the Winterglen just north of Citadel Umbra in northeast Gwynneth. The Firloch was longer than it was wide, covering approximately 24 square miles (62 square kilometers).
