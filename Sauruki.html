Sauruki was a member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave at the Scarlet Moon Hall .

Possessions

In battle, Sauruki dressed in a chain shirt and fought with quarterstaff .

History

During the Elemental Evil crisis in 1491 DR , Sauruki was sent by his masters to infiltrate the Cult of the Eternal Flame 's camp in order to spy on their Rite of the Wicker Giant . Around this time, Sauruki joined a group of druids known as Comnall , Murcadh , and Ragnad .
