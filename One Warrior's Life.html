One Warrior's Life was a book penned by the famed Lion of Neverwinter , Azlundar , in the late 13 th century DR .

History

The biographical collection of musings on life, honor, and a hero's journey, One Warrior's Life , was published in the Year of the Sighing Serpent, 1289 DR and a copy of the tome was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
