Farsea Marshwine was a variety of red wine , produced in the Farsea Swamp of Cormyr . It was exported as far as the Western Heartlands , the lands along the River Chionthar , and the city of Baldur's Gate , as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Farsea Marshwine was a sweet and bitter red. Some described its watery taste as if it's been thrown overboard. This wine was sold in thin and long tan-colored bottles.
