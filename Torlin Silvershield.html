Torlin Silvershield was one of the Grand Dukes of Baldur's Gate in 1479 DR .

Personality

Torlin was a consummate actor with great control over his voice. His main concern was the best interests of the city, even over the will of the church of Gond. Torlin had no interest in money or status, but thought that the ends always justified the means.

History

Torlin was the leader of the greatest noble family of Baldur's Gate and the high priest of the grandest temple. He helped defend the city from Valarken 's coup and helped to establish the Parliament of Peers . In 1482 DR, Torlin was involved in the crisis the culminated in the resurrection of Bhaal . Eventually he became the Chosen of the resurrected Bhaal. Torlin was later captured by the Red Wizards of Thay , who transported him to the Doomvault for experimentation and transformed him into a wight .
