Hrothgar was a human adventurer from Easthaven within the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale .

Description

The soldier was a powerfully built man who wore heavy armor, like plate mail . He wielded a magical axe of great power, which made him more wise and charismatic.

Personality

Hrothgar spent many years traveling and collecting trophies from legendary beasts, such as a gorgon and a mind flayer . He was known for helping anyone in need and had found a strong place in the community of Easthaven.

History

In Eleint , 1281 DR , Hrothgar put together an expedition to travel to the aid of Kuldahar in the Spine of the World .

Death

Hrothgar was killed when the expedition was attacked by frost giants in Kuldahar Pass . He was buried in a peaceful glen outside the town of Kuldahar. His death was treated as that of a hero in gratitude of the adventurer coming to Kuldahar's aid when his home – Easthaven, was facing strife of its own.
