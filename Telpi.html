Telpi was a language spoken by the inhabitants of the Dragon Coast and the Pirate Isles , from Proskur in the north to Nathlekh in the south, and from Iriaebor in the west to the Pirate and Whamite Isles in the east.

Telpi was the only East Thorass language , but it included three dialects— Dragonsmere , Pirate pidgin / argot , and Whamite . The dialect of the pirates of the Sea of Fallen Stars was strongly influenced by other languages of the region. They used it as a sort of secret language for carrying on their illegal activities.
