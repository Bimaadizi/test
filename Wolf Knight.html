The Wolf Knight was brigand who once ruled over the Keep of the Far Hills in the Western Heartlands .

History

Once a follower of Angarn Surfyst , Wolf Knight betrayed and slew the bandit leader, before seizing the Far Hills ' fortress as his own. Unfortunately for the unnamed lord, his tenure as ruler of the keep was short-lived.

The Wolf Knight was himself killed by the sorceress Sarunn Thoon , who posed as a timid captive and prospective lover.

Rumors & Legends

Although his name was lost to the ages, the Wolf Knight appeared as part of Sarunn's tale, as immortalized in The Witch of the Far Cold Hill .
