The Baths of Drusus were a ruined bathhouse in Medinat Muskawoon .

Structure

A greenish stone building 200 ft (61 m) by 120 ft (37 m), it was now open save for the walls and vaulted ceiling on the northeastern end.

Inhabitants

Two wraiths remained in the building; there were four golden links hidden in the drains.
