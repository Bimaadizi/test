Brother Benitus was a male human and a Cleric of Helm . He was a prelate of the Golden Fortress in Eshpurta , Amn , in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR through at least early 1370 DR, the Year of the Tankard He, along with younger priests of each faith, oversaw the chapels to Helm , Tempus , Torm , and Tyr that existed within the Golden Fortress.
