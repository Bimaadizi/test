Papyrus pads were items in the Realms often used by wizards for writing.

Description

Papyrus pads were made from high-quality cross-grained papyrus stock pressed in Mulhorand and sewn along one edge by skilled craftsmen from Unther and Chessenta .

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 15 gold pieces per 25 sheets.
