Zochimaloc was the ruler of Tulom-Itzi in 1361 DR .

Personality

He was very wise and knew much concerning the coming of the Golden Legion in Maztica , but was very enigmatic in speaking about it.

History

He ordered the kidnapping of Gultec , a young Jaguar Knight from Payit , and transported him to his city. There, Zochimaloc tutored Gultec, who, thanks to this training, learned to use all of his hidden powers. Just before the Night of Wailing , he encouraged Gultec to go to Nexal .

Zochimaloc died destroying the Darien 's army of giant ants .
