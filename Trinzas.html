Trinzas was a goblin warrior and a member cult of the Absolute in late 15 th century DR .

Description

She had yellow-green skin and wore a crudely-made set of chainmail , adorned with metal plates and animal bones.

Possessions

In battled, Trinzas wielded a giant greataxe .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a few adventurers and Halsin , the archdruid of the Emerald Grove , attempted to infiltrate the cult's fortress, the Shattered Sanctum . Trinzas' fellow cultists managed to capture the druid, and turned away the adventurers that accompanied him. Afterwards, Trinzas and other cult warriors remained on high alert.
