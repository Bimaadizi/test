The Lathkules were a family of Waterdeep ennobled in the mid–13 th century DR . They survived through to the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

The family business was based around jewelry, prospecting, gem mining, and gem cutting.

Possessions

Lathkule Villa in the North Ward was their main residence.

History

The family was ennobled in the Year of the Cockatrice, 1248 DR .

At some point, the Defenders Three foiled one of the plots of the patriarch, Nimor Lathkule, against Lord Maernos , a fellow noble.

In the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , Maerjanthra Shalace 's jewelry store and workshop was attacked, and its owner apparently slain. Her child, Narnra Shalace , suspected the Lathkule or Artemel families of having ordered it, though she never found out the truth.
