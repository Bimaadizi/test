Gerain Huntcrown was a member of the Huntcrown noble family of Cormyr , an Oversword of the Purple Dragons and a member of the War Council in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He had a narrow shoulders, leaned on a wooden cane, and had a habit of spitting as he talked in his gruff voice.

History

During the War Council, he was wary of the Shadovar threat and dismissive of the then Regent Raedra Obarskyr 's plans. He later had a genuine meeting with her, remarking on her successful approaches, and lamenting his distant relative, Lindon's choices.
