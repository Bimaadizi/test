Borold was a guard in Waterdeep in the early 14 th century DR .

Relationships

He was married to Nalitheen . They had two daughters, Boroldira and Naleetha .

History

He was slain two summers prior to Eleint of 1321 DR , suggesting it was before summer of 1321 and 1320 as well , in a campaign in which pirates of Amn , led by Mirt the Merciless , pillaged a cargo of silver bars belonging to the Lords of Waterdeep .

Legacy

His wife Nalitheen , still with two daughters, became destitute, and took to street work to make ends meet. She later met Mirt the Merciless, whom she almost knifed.
