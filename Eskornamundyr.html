Eskornamundyr was the dragon emperor of the realm of Eskorn , Laerakond , in the world of Abeir , centuries before the Spellplague .

History

Eskornamundyr was killed by rebel forces led by the legendary hero Aunglass Roaryl centuries before the Spellplague happened. After his death, Eskorn became a human-dominated realm.

Legacy

The realm of Eskorn was named after him.
