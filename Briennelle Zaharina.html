Briennelle Zaharina , or Brie-Zara , was the half-drow daughter of Drizzt Do'Urden and Catti-brie in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Catti-brie gave birth to their daughter Briennelle Zaharina—named for both of her grandfathers, Bruenor Battlehammer and Zaknafein —during the war between all the houses of Menzoberranzan outside and beneath Gauntlgrym in the Year of Dwarvenkind Reborn, 1488 DR .

Description

The child had auburn hair, like her mother, and her eye color was like her father's, except a deeper, jewel-toned purple. Brie-Zara's skin tone was somewhere between her parents' coloring, like a pink flower at dusk in hue.
