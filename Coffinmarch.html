The Coffinmarch was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Coffinmarch was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward . The road branched east off of the High Road and ended at the entrance to the City of the Dead on Zendulth Street . Both Spindle Street and the Winter Path intersected the Coffinmarch.

Notable Locations

Dunblast Roofing Company sat on the south side of the eastern end of the Coffinmarch direcly in front of the City of the Dead.
