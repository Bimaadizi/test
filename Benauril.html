Benauril was an outcast wizard from Halruaa who was active circa the mid-to-late 12 th century DR . He was notable for creating the creatures known as dark trees .

Activities

Benauril's research involved necromancy and a variety of other, similarly distasteful forms of magic .

History

From within the Aerilpar Forest , Benauril spent a great deal of time trying to create his own breed of treant -like servants that would heed his will. He invested a great deal of time into his work, and was initially quite pleased with his progress until a failed experiment produced the first dark trees, which rebelled and killed him.
