Erilon Amadar was the leader of the Fallen Justice of Unther in the late 14 th century DR .

Abilities

Erilon Amadar was a scout , and a powerful spellthief as well.

Activities

As the leader of the Fallen Justice, Erilon Amadar spearheaded their battle against old foes of Ramman , such as the followers of Hoar and Gilgeam .

Relationships

He had twelve other spellthieves under him as part of the Fallen Justice.

History

Erilon Amadar was one of thirteen scouts who became drenched in Ramman's blood at the time of his death during the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR . By 1372 DR , they had formed the Fallen Justice, fighting against Ramman's old foes.
