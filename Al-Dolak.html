Al-Dolak was the captain of the guard in Mobius Tower . He had been implicated in the ongoing assassination attempt on the tower.

Appearance

Al-Dolak's corpse was huge, and still stank. As a ghost, however, he did not have a smell.

Powers

As a ghost, Al-Dolak's touch could age their target by decades.

History

Al-Dolak had been implicated in the assassination attempt in the tower: he helped carry it out by calling an inspection of the guards just as it happened.
