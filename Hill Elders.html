The Hill Elders was the ruling council of the elven nation of Evereska circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .

Organization

It consisted of warriors, nobles and mages presided over by a High Elder.

Base of Operations

The Hill Elders held court in the High Hall of the Hunt in Evereska.
