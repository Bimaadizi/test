Strom's Discussions of Poetics was a philosophical book written by Storm Silverhand and published sometime before the destruction of the city of Phlan in the beginning of the 14 th century DR .

History

A copy of Strom's Discussions of Poetics was left in Mantor's Library in the city of Phlan as the city fell into ruins during a Rage of Dragons of the Year of Thunder, 1306 DR .
