J Orchard was a Gondian artificer from the city of Baldur's Gate , who died some years before the late 15 th century DR .

History

J Orchard died sometime before the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR and was buried in the Lower City Graveyard , next to the Grand Mausoleum . The artificer's gravestone had a single inscription: "J Orchard. Artificer of Gond, crafter of odd sockets." J's grave may had been accidentally desecrated, and their body animated as a zombie by Nina Dortmell , a talented child with a gift, who was trying to resurrect her recently deceased brother.
