Jeyrelle Fey was one of the few female members of Bregan D'aerthe in the early 15 th century DR .

Description

Jeyrelle was a beautiful woman with amber eyes.

Skills

She was a priestess of an unknown goddess that was not Lolth .

History

In 1488 DR , Jeyrelle, together with Braelin Janquay , acted as support for the resurrected Zaknafein for a mission for Bregan D'aerthe.

At Bregan D'aerthe's meeting with House Hunzrin in Waterdeep 's sewers, she remained behind.
