Galdel was a small city located in central Halruaa .

Description

Galdel was a quiet town nestled on the southern banks of Lake Halruaa .

Inhabitants

Several dozen expert electrum smiths lived in Galdel. They were capable of crafting wondrous items that were suitable for enchanting.
