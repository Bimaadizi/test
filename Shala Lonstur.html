Shala Lonstur was a famled halfling explorer who operated out of the city of Baldur's Gate sometimes before the 14 th century DR .

History

Shala was an accomplished seafarer who charted many routes that laid west from Baldur's Gate across the Sea of Swords . Unfortunately, her deeds were overshadowed by humans who sailed the high seas.
