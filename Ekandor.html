Ekandor was the leader of a team of Red Wizards who hunted the wild mage Neera .

History

Ekandor and his fellows hunted Neera for a long time leading up to the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR . They finally got to her in the town of Beregost , but were turned away when Neera encountered Abdel Adrian and his adventuring party.

The Red Wizards tracked Neera across the Western Heartlands to the hidden enclave of another wild mage Adoy , hidden in the Cloud Peaks . Ekandor met his untimely end in a final battle with Neera and her allies.
