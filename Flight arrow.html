Flight arrows were arrows that, as the name implied, were designed with range in mind. They were often used when hunting.

Description

Flight arrows were lighter compared to "common" arrows. They were usually made from birch or ash trees and measured between 30 inches (76 centimeters) and 40 inches (100 centimeters) in length. Twelve flight arrows could be purchased for 3 silver pieces .
