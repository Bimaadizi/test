Winter wolf gloves were warm enchanted hand coverings that carried magical properties of winter wolves ' pelt they were made of.

Powers

These gloves of soft white fur granted the wearers resistance to mundane and magical cold. Additionally, the gloves slightly improved the wearers' charisma.

History

Circa Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , Oswald Fiddlebender crafted various magic items of clothing using pelts of winter wolves that followed the Cultists of Auril plaguing Icewind Dale . Some of other items included winter wolf cloaks , winter wolf hats , winter wolf scarves , and winter wolf boots .
