Klarz was a hobgoblin sea-captain on the Trackless Sea in the late 15 th century DR . He was the captain of the Koalinth .

History

Once, Klarz and a ranger member of the Emerald Enclave were marooned together on an island in the Korinn Archipelago . It was plagued with monsters and the ranger saved his life, a debt that Klarz promised to repay.

After 1485 DR , Klarz repaid his debt by providing passage to Enclave-affiliated adventurers traveling in search of the Morkoth . The Koalinth anchored in a cove some miles north of Waterdeep .
