Doncastle was a small town located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Description

The village's buildings was concealed within the treetops of a forested valley.

Geography

Doncastle was situated within the folds of the Dernall Forest along the western bank of the Swanmay River in the county of Stagford . The town lay just east of the Drowned Forest at the end of a trail that branched off from the High King's Road .
