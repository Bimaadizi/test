Elklund Oromanche was one of the many Oromanche siblings of a prominent Mosstonian merchant family in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Every member of the Oromanche family was described to have "dark, lush good looks," which made the family lush with rumors of and well-publicized trysts. The family was known for being flamboyant and grand individuals.

History

By the late 15 th century DR, the Oromanche clan was one of the most successful merchant organizations in Tethyr and the town of Mosstone. Maerele Oromanche was the matriarch of the clan and the oldest sibling. The rest brothers and sisters were much younger including Odyle , Imraphar , Olora , Emruelle , and Haladeira . Most contributed to the family's mercantile activities. Elminster of Shadowdale had a warm relationship with the family, but his attitude towards Elklund Oromanche was notably cold.
