The Dark Gateway , which was also called the Upper Temple , was a shrine dedicated to Ibrandul , the Lord of the Dry Depths.

Location

This secret shrine was located in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Activities

The shrine was connected to the Deep Temple of Dark Hope in the Underdark via a two-way gate .
