Bandor Village was a small but influential mining settlement in northwest Netheril .

Geography

The village sat below the eastern Barren Mountains, southwest of the Bay of Ascore .

Trade

Bandor's people mined rich veins of gold and silver from the Barren Mountains , and traded it to nearby large settlements including the dwarves of Ascore , the flying enclave of Sanctuary , and the Rengarth of Vandal Station . The metals were then traded elsewhere in Netheril across the Narrow Sea .

History

The village was founded in 1423 NY (−2436 DR) , and spent its whole existence as a mining community. It was a place where common miners could strike it rich, but they contended with constant cave-ins, underground monster attacks, and lethal fights with other miners. It came to a sudden end in 2328 NY (−1531 DR) when a lethal plague killed every member of the community and passed on to nearby Vandal Station, where it killed another 4,000 before burning out.

It was reported that the plague originated from an interaction with winged humanoids who inhabited the nearby mountains .
