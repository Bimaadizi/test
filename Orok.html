Orok was a runaway boy of Blue Bear Tribe in Zelbross .

History

Orok abandoned his tribe because his siblings bullied him. From then on, he hid in a lair in the ruins of Zelbross.

Personality

Orok, like all Uthgardt , distrusted magic .
