History

Valurian started out as a member of an adventuring company operating out of Iriaebor , but they were ambushed by a group of bandits who slew everyone but Valurian, whom they took prisoner instead. While deciding her fate, they themselves were attacked and wiped out by the Blacktalons. With her friends dead and lacking better prospects, she joined up with the mercenary company, working as a scout for the organization.
