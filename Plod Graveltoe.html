Plod Graveltoe was a deep gnome in Mulmaster .

History

Plod led a svirfneblin expedition into the Mulmaster sewers. He and his men were attacked by some black puddings but could have been saved by some adventurers working for Selfaril Uoumdolphin .

Relationship

Plod had good dealings with the Emerald Enclave .
