A sign of Tyr was an enchanted defensive bracer of the Tyrran faith in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

A sign of Tyr was a single leather armlet with a golden disc attached to it. The disk was decorated with Tyr's holy symbol , proudly announcing the wearer's devotion to the God of Justice.

Powers

The enchantments imbued into a sign of Tyr covered the wearer and their allies with an aura of protection that made it harder for foes to land a successful strike on the affected creatures.
