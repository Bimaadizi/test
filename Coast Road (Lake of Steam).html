The Coast Road was a paved trail along the north banks of the the Lake of Steam in south Faerûn . It was rarely ever featured on maps made by northerners.

Description

The Coast Road was narrower than the more prominent Golden Road .

Geography

The trail began where the Lake Way ended in the west at the city-state of Ankhapur . Coast Road cut east along the shore of the Lake of Steam, terminating at the city-state of Saelmur .
