Mama Booga was an orc bandit and shaman of the Clotskull orc tribe in the 15 th century DR .

History

Around 1485 DR , Mama Booga and her boys were hired by the Red Wizard of Thay Arvik Zaltos to steal an egg from the lair of the black dragon Thoss Fyurnen . Later, Booga and her boys awaited the Red Wizard at the ruined Krandon Manor near Daggerford .

Relationship

Booga had seven children who were half-orc , half-dwarf , like many Clotskull orcs.
