Heartward Hall was a temple of Sune located in Helm's Hold during the late 15 th century DR .

Location

The temple was located on the outskirts of the Heartward market in the center of the city. The marketplace took its name from its proximity to the shrine.

History

Originally constructed a mere shrine, Heartward Hall was reformed as a temple shortly before the Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR .
