Darts of pinning were large needle-shaped enchanted darts designed to restrain rather than kill.

Powers

Darts of pinning were enhanced with a +2 enchantment and held magics that paralyzed the darts' targets. Once a dart of pinning reached its target it struck them motionless as if affected by the carrion crawler brain juice or hold monster spell.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , darts of pinning could be found in Thar and the Moonsea region and wielded by agents of the Zhentarim . Some of these daggers also belonged to Marcos Flass during his time in the town of Termalaine in Icewind Dale .
