Droot was a type of non-alcoholic beverage found in the Realms.

Description

This beverage was made from nut and root distillates, much like the types that were usually added to bad beers to make them more palatable. It was similar to root beer , a beverage that was unknown on Toril but common on Earth .

Availability

In Waterdeep this beverage was known to be sold at Mhaalauvur's .
