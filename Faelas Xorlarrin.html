Faelas Xorlarrin was a drow wizard of House Xorlarrin in Menzoberranzan in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Faelas was a competent Xorlarrin wizard. In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , he was a master of Sorcere and a coded letter was written to him about how Lolth 's current plan supported the idea of wizards joining the Ruling Council .

In the Year of the Iron Dwarf's Vengeance, 1485 DR , Faelas and his cousin and fellow Sorcere master Jaemas Xorlarrin accompanied the demon army led by Marilith and Nalfeshnee . They witnessed the defeat of the two demon lords at the hands of Drizzt Do'Urden , and Jarlaxle urged them to report the news to Matron Mother Zeerith Q'Xorlarrin .

In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Faelas was not chosen to join the reformed House Do'Urden .
