Nasty Boy was an allosaurus who competed in the dinosaur races of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 th century DR .

History

As of the start of the death curse , Nasty Boy was notable for having a famous trainer. However, bookies were giving it only 1:3 odds of winning at the races. Nasty Boy was present at the races on the day of the inaugural Na N'buso's Haka .
