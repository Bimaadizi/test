Bellar was a member of the Zhentarim that worked out of their hideout in the caves beneath the Risen Road in the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

He utilized a longbow in combat and appeared quite confident in his skills as an archer.

History

Bellar was present at the Zhentarim hideout when their leader Zarys ordered for its demolition in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
