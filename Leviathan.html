The leviathan was a huge whale and child proxy of the Earthmother of the Moonshae Isles . It was regarded as a demipower within the seas of the Isles.

Description

A great black-skinned whale, the leviathan was larger than any whale naturally found on Toril . Fully 360 ft (110 m) long, the beast's tail was 60 ft (18 m) wide and the creature had a mouth full of razor-sharp teeth.

Abilities

The leviathan was mortal and could grow old and die like any other natural creature . Though it was immortal in a sense, its spirit would be reborn in time after it died.

History

The behemoth slumbered for centuries until awakened by the Goddess in 1345 DR .

The leviathan was killed by several poisoned poles attached to the ships in which Kazgoroth , disguised as Thelgaar Ironhand , led an army of northerners against the Ffolk of the southern Moonshae Isles.
