The Warstaff of the Wind Dukes was an enchanted wizard 's staff that channeled the power of the Wind Dukes of Aaqa .

Powers

Warstaff of the Wind Dukes allowed the wielder to tap into the elemental magics of the Wind Dukes and enhance their magical abilities.
