Imraea was a kid, and an inhabitant of the Dalelands in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

She was small and dark-haired.

Personality

She was a whirlwind, but quite polite.

Relationships

She was Lhaera 's daughter.

History

During early Mirtul of 1357 DR , Imraea was visited by Torm and Rathan Thentraver , who brought her a kitten, then named Snuggleguts , and nine gold pieces for kitten-related expenses in the future.
