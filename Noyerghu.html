Noyerghu was a Thayan gnoll around 1485 DR .

History

Noyerghu was a gnoll from Thay who accompanied the Red Wizard of Thay Thegger Grynn in 1485 DR in taking control of the Phylund Lodge . Thanks to possession by the pit fiend Baazka , Noyerghu replaced Flubnak as chieftain of the local gnolls and led them in raids against the caravans from Daggerford . However an adventuring party invaded the lodge to stop the gnoll's raids and confronted and killed Noyerghu.
