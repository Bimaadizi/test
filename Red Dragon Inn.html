The Red Dragon Inn was an inn in the city of Luskan on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 th century DR .

Location

It was located on the northern bank of the River Mirar .

History

This was the seat of government for Captain Deudermont in Luskan in the Year of the Haunting, 1377 DR .
