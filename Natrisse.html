Natrisse was a female human of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR .

History

Natrisse was a priestess of Lathander , who worked along Brother Merring , before he went to West Harbor .

Gameplay

The Kalach-Cha and the companions could obtain a quest from Natrisse to bring Brother Merring a tithe box. Natrisse would warn them not to open the box, else they'd feel the wrath of Lathander. If they did, the box was destroyed as well.

Note that unlike most merchants she is affected by friendly spells and bard songs. (her Appraise skill can get buffed if she is within spell range, raising her prices)
