The Street of Glances was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Street of Glances was located in the northwest section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . It ran roughly east to west, originating on Westwall Street in the west and branching east to end at the Heroes' Walk . The Street of Glances was intersected by Stormstar's Ride , Morningstar Way , Gorl Street , Staghunter's Way , Sighing Maiden's Walk , Telchar Lane , Sniff Alley , the Skulkway , Ivory Street , the Street of Whispers , and the Street of the Singing Dolphin .
