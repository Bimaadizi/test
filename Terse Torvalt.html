Terse Torvalt was a member of the Guild from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Terse was a halfling man with dark brown skin, green eyes, and curly brown hair. He wore an archer 's leathers dyed in the Guild's green colors, hid his face behind a Guild-green bandana, and carried a bow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Terse Torvalt was tasked with standing guard at the secret entrance into Baldur's Gate's Undercellar , where the Guild's Guildhall was hidden. Whilst a half-orcish enforcer Tusgront manned the main entrance, Terse was ready to dispatch any unwanted guest from a nearby rooftop.
