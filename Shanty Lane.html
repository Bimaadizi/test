Shanty Lane was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

Shanty Lane was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . The road branched east off of Chop Street , eventually terminating at a dead end of unnamed alleys.
