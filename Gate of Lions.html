The Gate of Lions was found in the city of Muluk in the Free Cities of Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Location

The Gate of Lions was located on the western wall of the city. It opened to a garden right before the Royal Lagoon within Muluk and led to the docks.

Description

A pair of lions was carved into the framework of the gate, giving the portal its name.

Inhabitants

The Gate of Lions was a fortress as much as it was an entryway into Muluk. It housed 200 soldiers.
