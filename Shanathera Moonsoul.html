Shanathera Moonsoul was a high priestess of Selûne in the Temple of Silver Stars in the mid-14 th century DR .

History

Sometime between 1368 DR and 1370 DR , Shanathera passed away at an elderly age. This was followed by a period of mourning among the members of her temple.
