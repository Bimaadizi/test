Brigitte Bannal was a mage and ruler of the Realm of Lands in the Utter East during the time of the Bloodforge Wars ( 648 – 657 DR ).

Brigitte understood how to use the complex terrain to her strategic advantage, making nature effectively a fortress. At last, the leader of the Legendary Campaign came to the Realm of Lands to overcome these defenses and defeat her in bloodforge battle, as part of a plan to unify the war-torn Utter East through conquest.
