Crughth was an orc shaman inhabiting Oman's Isle within the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

He was a ferocious and scornful warrior who reveled in bloodshed.

Abilities

He was able to call upon ancestral spirits to attack his foes, and he could imbue his weapon with spiritual energy to enhance its speed and deadliness as well as to slow his enemies' movements.

Possessions

He wielded a battleaxe and wore hide armor .

Activities

Crughth was well-respected among the giant rulers of Oman's Isle because of his divine and primal power. He planned to taint the moonwell found in the Old Stone Wood . If Crughth's plan succeeded, the giants of would be able to transport raiders to any of the other moonwells scattered throughout the Moonshae Isles.
