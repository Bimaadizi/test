Lureene Dundragon was a Zhentarim agent in Phlan in 1489 DR .

History

Lureene was possibly involved in an expedition to Throstulgrael 's lair. The Zhent 's objective was to recover a set of trade maps of the Moonsea from the dragon's hoard.
