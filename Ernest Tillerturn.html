Lady Ernest Tillerturn was a patriar of Baldur's Gate sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Ernest had delicate features and long silky brown hair. She wore a minstrel 's garb and a large beret, topped with a large decorative feather. She was depicted playing a lute , yet appearing disconsolately bored.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a portrait of Ernest Tillerturn painted by Oskar Fevras was kept in the attic of the Jannath estate of Baldur's Gate. That year, it became possessed by wailing poltergeist unwittingly summoned by the painter. Other copies of the same lute-playing depiction were seen across the city of Baldur's Gate.
