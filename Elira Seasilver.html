Elira Seasilver was a noblewoman of House Seasilver and the grand duchess of Marsember during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Elira could be cold, dismissive and emotionally manipulative.

Personality

She had a particular distaste for apples and anything apple-flavored.

Relationships

Lady Elira and her husband Grand Duke Farnig Seasilver had a single daughter, named Arietta . Elira thought little of her daughter's independent streak and believed her duty was to family first, over her country or her people.

History

On Uktar 2 of the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Lady Elira was captured by Yder Tanthul during the evacuation of Marsember , in the conflict between Netheril and Cormyr . She was rescued a short time later by the combined efforts of her daughter and house guards, guardsmen of the Marsember Watch , and three chosen of the gods; Kleef Kenric , Joelle Emmeline , and Malik el Sami . The grand duchess was then sailed to safety by watchman Jang , presumably to her extended family in Westgate .
