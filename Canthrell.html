Canthrell was an elongated island within the kingdom of Norheim in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Canthrell was part of the Norheim Isles , located near to Norland . It lay between the Sea of Moonshae on its southeastern shores and the Trackless Sea to the northwest. Canthrell was approximately 25 miles (40 kilometers) long, but only 5 miles (8 kilometers) wide at its widest point.
