Trenahess was a human village in Cormanthor , about 80 miles (130 kilometers) west of Myth Drannor , on the banks of the River Ashaba .

The green dragon Verthandantalynx , also called Verdant Cloud, lived in the area and the people of Trenahess worshiped him, nicknaming him "Little Verthie". The villagers offered up visitors and treasures to Little Verthie, who defended them from monsters and other communities.

History

On Uktar 13 , the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Verthandantalynx was affected by the Rage of Dragons and attacked the village, laying it to waste.
