Ooaurtann , or Ooaurtann of Westgate , was a slaver active in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In 1373 DR , when the Harper Roldro Tattershar wanted entry into Lady Joysil Ambrur 's home in Marsember and was refused by her servant Elward , he name-dropped the slaver Ooaurtann, trying to be threatening.

Rumors & Legends

Per the Harper Roldro Tattershar, Ooaurtann had at least one trade-agent in Marsember . The Harper gave indications he strongly believed that agent to be Elward, servant to Lady Joysil Ambrur.
