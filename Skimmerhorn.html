Skimmerhorn was a ranger in the 14 th century DR who hailed from the small town of Secomber .

History

Around 1358 DR , when traveling in the High Forest Skimmerhorn came across and investigated the Endless Caverns . He would later report having found evidence of illithid activity within them.
