Mulsantir Illistine II was the second King of the Illistine Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Mulsantir II inherited the throne when his father, King Mulsantir I , died in the Year of the Festering Heart, 440 DR . He ruled until the Year of the Rolling Heads, 452 DR , when he was slain by a wizard of the Shoon Imperium called Myntharan "the Magus" who claimed the throne and ended the Illistine bloodline in Westgate.
