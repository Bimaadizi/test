Cymon the Skald , better known as Cymon the False , was a famous wandering gnome singer, storyteller, and songwriter active in the Moonshae Isles as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Cymon made use of illusions and glamours to continuously change his physical appearance, and it was this ever-changing nature that earned him the moniker of "False". It was uncertain whether he was even truly a gnome.

Personality

Cymon was playful and musical. He was quick with a sly wink or a mischievous grin.

Possessions

He carried a unique stringed bandore , which served as a calling card for him.

Activities

Cymon spent his days wandering the whole of his homeland of Sarifal , and always with a song on his lips.

He was known as a composer of danceable music, and had written recognizable pieces suitable to be played by small orchestras.
