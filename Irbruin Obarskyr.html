King Irbruin was born in the Year of the Defiant Stone, 127 DR the second son of Dorglor I . He was the 13th Obarskyr monarch and a king of Cormyr , beginning his reign in the Year of the Pirates' Port, 145 DR at the death of his uncle Embrold . He and his firstborn son Faerlthind were slain together in Year of the Weary Warrior, 169 DR , and the throne passed instead to his second son Moriann .
