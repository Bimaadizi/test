Valetta was a dragonborn priest of Gond at the House of Inspired Hands in Waterdeep in the early 1490s DR .

History

In 1492 DR , Valetta was in charge of taking care of the nimblewright Nim . Following the detonation of a fireball in Trollskull Alley that killed the gnome Dalakhar , Valetta discovered that a nimblewright of Nim's own creation was involved in the attack and decided to strip Nim of all his construction materials.
