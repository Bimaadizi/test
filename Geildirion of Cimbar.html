The Geildirion of Cimbar was a book of secret lore from Myth Drannor .

History

The book was held in a tower in Myth Drannor and guarded by a beholder mage until the party of Quenthel Baenre , with the help of Jezz the Lame , retrieved it in 1372 DR for Tzirik of House Jaelre in exchange for more information about the Silence of Lolth .
