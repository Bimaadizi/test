Winko Battleblade was a dwarf of Mithril Hall in the late 15th century DR .

History

During the late stages of the War of the Silver Marches in 1485 DR , Winko and his young cousin Rollo were among the dwarves that marched from Mithril Hall to battle against the orcs and drow .
