Shurrs was the viper familiar of Bishimzon , a wizard member of the Cult of the Dragon circa 1369 DR .

Description

Rexin was a tiny magical beast .

Abilities

Rexin could swim and, like other vipers, possessed a venomous bite. Rexin's venom, once in the target's bloodstream, dealt damage to constitution. Like other familiars , Rexin shared an empathic link with her master. Handragath could channel magic and cast spells through his familiar . Rexin could speak to his master and other snakes.

History

In 1369 DR, Shurrs resided in Darnarest's tower alongside Bishimzon and a small Cult of the Dragon slaver operation. Bishimzon, under Handragath 's leadership, was capturing local villagers and able-bodied adventurers to send them through a portal in the tower's basement to Myth Drannor , where they were doomed to work for the cult or be murdered and turned into undead servants. Around two tendays into the Cult's slavery operation, a group of adventurers searching for missing townies, destroyed the outpost in Darnarest's tower.
