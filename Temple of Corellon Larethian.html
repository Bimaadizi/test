The Temple of Corellon Larethian was a temple in Leuthilspar on the island of Evermeet .

Structure

The temple was dedicated to the worship of Corellon Larethian , head of the Seldarine . The building was set in its own grounds and featured two spires. Inside was a large statue of Corellon Larethian.

History

As of 1369 DR , the temple was run by High Priest Rathal Evanara , who was assisted by two acolytes.
