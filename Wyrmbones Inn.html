The Wyrmbones Inn was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The inn stood three storys tall.

Location

The Wyrmbones Inn was located in Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the west side of the High Road across from the Coffinmarch .

Inhabitants

A group of highborn Waterdhavian ladies called the Bloodred Crown adventured out of the inn's basement.
