The Striking Talons of Memnon was an adventuring band from Calimshan , active in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

By the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the Striking Talons of Memnon were believed to had been dead for a decade, perished in the Weldath forest of Tethyr . The band's name, however, was remembered thanks to stories of the accumulated treasures buried nearby the Hunter's Tree .
