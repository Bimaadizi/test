TypeTree, fruit

Kumquats were a type of tree-grown citrus fruit found in the Realms.

Availability

Kumquats could be found sold in Halruaa and Neverwinter .

History

In the 14 th century DR , as part of a stage performance at the Ale & Hearty in the city of Schamedar , the wizard Wiglaf used his magical abilities to conjure a great sky-reaching crop of vegetables that included kumquats.

Trivia

It was traditional for those performing the epic poem Neverwinter Nights to balance bowls full of kumquats atop their heads while doing so.
