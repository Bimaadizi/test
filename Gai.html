Gai was a Shou human from Kara-Tur and a Harper agent operating near Hillsfar in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa 1486 DR , Gai was watching a puppet show in a tavern near Hillsfar, while his superior Olisara Lightsong hired an adventuring party to investigate some cases of kidnapped farmers in the area, which was possibly related to the Red Plumes .
