OccupationSage

Astergullph Daeryn was a sage in the town of Dhedluk in Cormyr . He was the author of the book Aster's Journal . In one passage of the book concerning the humble origins of many adventurers , he wrote that wielding a sword in small, apparently peaceful surroundings was no less heroic than attacking mighty cities or summoning powerful magic against a dragon .
