Fairest Maiden was a rowdy tavern ditty overheard in the city of Samargol , Samarach in the late 14 th century DR .

History

By the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Fairest Maiden was a popular song performed across the taverns in the jungle nation of Samarach, including the inn of Leira's Trick of Samargol.
