History

In the Year of the Hippogriff's Folly, 241 DR , the White Raven patrol, led by Iymbryl Alastrarra , was completely eliminated by ruukha (hobgoblins) . Iymbryl, the last survivor, was rescued by Elminster Aumar but died of his wounds, bequeathing upon him the mission to return his kiira to his family in the city.
