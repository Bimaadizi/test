Titanium was an uncommon metal known of in Faerûn .

Properties

Titanium weighed but 280 pounds (130 kilograms) per 1 cubic foot (28 liters), making it one of the lightest metals one could find.

Usages

The impure ore of titanium, ilmenite , could be used to make the white pigment titania .
