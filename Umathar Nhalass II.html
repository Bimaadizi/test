Umathar Nhalass II was the second Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Umathar II inherited the throne when his father, Umathar I , died in −867 DR . He ruled until his death in −821 DR .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Umathar III .
