The Strait of Alaron was a large channel connecting the Sea of Moonshae with the Sea of Swords in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The strait passed between the islands of Snowdown and Gwynneth in the southern Moonshae Isles. It continued north past the entire length of Gwynneth's eastern shores before passing by the southern end of Alaron to meet the Sea of Moonshae east of Oman's Isle . The Stait of Alaron was quite large, measuring over 200 miles (320 kilometers) in length with an average width of 25 miles (40 kilometers).
