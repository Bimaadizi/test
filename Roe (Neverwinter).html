Roe was a male human Watch lieutenant of the city of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR .

Activities

Roe was the lieutenant of the Watch.

History

Roe was Neverwinter Watch's lieutenant. He served the Watch with a great loyalty. If the Kalach-Cha sided with the watch, Roe would eventually die in a fire at the city guardhouse made by Moire .
