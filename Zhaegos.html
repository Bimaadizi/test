Zhaegos was a laborer inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Zhaegos worked at Crommor's Warehouse located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward on the Reach . He served as a point of contact for the fence Ulmar the Watchful .
