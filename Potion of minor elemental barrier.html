Potions of minor elemental barrier , were magic concoctions that protected the imbiber from fire and ice.

Description

These potions were bottled minor elemental barrier spells . When consumed, it gave the imbiber resistance to extreme cold and heat.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , potion of minor elemental barrier could be purchased from eccentric gnome alchemist Oswald Fiddlebender who briefly aided the Ten-Towns against the menace of the Legion of the Chimera .
