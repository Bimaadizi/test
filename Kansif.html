Kansif was a half-orcish wizard - evoker , a worshiper of the Absolute , and a guide, stationed in the Moonrise Towers during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Kansif was a half-orcish man with a strong body and a hide of muddy gray. He had short brown hair and wore a set of enchanted thermoarcanic gloves .

Abilities

As a mage, Kansif specialized in fire magic, dispensing firebolts and fireballs . He also wielded the cloud of daggers and cure wounds spells .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Kansif was among those who joined the new religion that sprang in the Western Heartlands . He was stationed within the Moonrise Towers – the fortress controlled by the Absolute's warlord – Ketheric Thorm . Kansif was among the loyal Cult of the Absolute 's guides that helped pilgrims reach the Moonrise Towers amidst the deadly shadow-cursed lands .
