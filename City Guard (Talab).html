The City Guard of Talab served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Questing".

Organization

The composition of the city guard as of 1367Â DR was 1,000 regular city guards, 100 clerics , and 900 mamluks of the Parched Mamluk Society . An additional 3,000 soldiers and 2,000 cavalry from the Sword of the True Gods were also stationed in Talab.

Activities

The sole task of the mamluks was to protect Talab's water supply. The Pantheist cavalry guarded the route into the Cities of the Ancients region.
