Pelmuth's Draw was the name given to an alley in Scornubel in the 14 th century DR .

Location

Pelmuth's Draw branched off from Delsart's Drive . It led to a courtyard, where one of the doors was blue, leading to a warehouse.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Dove Falconhand went through that street when looking for the trader Sarltan during her investigation of a drow infiltration on that year.
