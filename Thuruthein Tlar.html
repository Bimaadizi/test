History

Thuruthein trained under Orth Lantar.

In the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , he, his master, and other apprentices including Prestym and Iyrit , went to Myth Drannor to search for magical treasures. There, however, he met the Rangers Three .

However, the sudden attack of a malaugrym convinced Tlar to join the Rangers in their battle against the monsters.
