Doomlord Esprelus Morammon was a worshiper of Kelemvor inhabiting Tulmon along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Esprelus was a thin man with a grim countenance.

Activities

He presided over Skullspire , a temple to the Lord of the Dead in Tulmon. He often walked the streets during the dead of night.

History

Esprelus was an adventurer earlier in his life.
