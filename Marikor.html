Marikor was a settlment in Turmish that bordered the Orsraun Mountains .

History

In the Year of the Wyvernfall, 512 DR , the young nation of Turmish clashed with an orcish horde of the Candlekairn clan that descended from the Orsraun Mountains. This conflict became known as the War of 512 in which Shaus , Marikor, and Dauntshield cities were wiped off the face of Turmish. Of them, only Dauntshield was rebuilt after the Turmish army defeated the invading force in the last days of summer of 512 DR .
