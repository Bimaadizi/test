Cordula Eltan was a granddaughter of Duke Eltan , member of the Flaming Fist , and an official of the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Cordula was a human woman with chestnut hair gathered in a tight bun, a light skin tone, aquamarine eyes, and a stern demeanor.

Abilities

As a wizard , she was armed with an enchanted +1 quarterstaff and had a collection of deadly spells ready: lightning bolt , cone of cold , otto's irresistible dance , confusion , and others. She was also trained in the use of medium and heavy armor , wearing a set of plates that did not restrict her movement when casting magics.

Cordula was always accompanied by two bodyguards: Pollo the Muscle and Wanda Tullermoor .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Cordula Eltan served the city of Baldur's Gate under Counsellor Florrick , taking over her role were something to happen to the elf . She prided her family's history, and was fiercely loyal to the Dukes, including Duke Ulder Ravengard . When Duke Ravengard was abducted by the Cult of the Absolute , he sent out a word of the looming cult and its dangers that reached Cordula. Desperate to save the city from a war, she could've passed The Legend of Ansur to the adventurers, also known as the Absolute Adversaries , if she trusted them to defend the city and a confrontation with Wyll Ravengard had not turned bloody. Cordula Eltan believed that the legend of Ansur was true and the dragon could rise one time and only once time if called to defend the city of Baldur's Gate.
