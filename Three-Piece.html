Three-Piece was a member of the Guild from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Guild reinstated tethered knife fights to its members delight. The fights were held until the first blood. Babyjowls and Three-Piece had at least one fight and were scheduled for a rematch sometime during the Absolute crisis in Baldur's Gate. Bursar Uktar collected bets and dispensed winnings.
