Uryiax was a powerful red dragon who killed the Company of the Red Raven adventurer group with his flaming breath on 12 Ches 1355 DR . The event was recorded on a commemorative plaque in the office of Registrant General Lord Partic Thistle in the Royal Court of Suzail .
