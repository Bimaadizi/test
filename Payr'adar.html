Payr'adar was a puppet member of Zhentil Keep 's Council of Lords in 1368 DR .

History

Payr'adar hailed from Chessenta and arrived in Zhentil Keep with a caravan full of exotic women at his service. Quickly, he started a very lucrative festhall and with mutual vote Payr'adar was elected to the Council. Secretly he was a member of the Zhentarim .

He was one of the few lords to survive the 1368 DR assault on the Keep by Cyric 's monstrous army

Payr'adar's estate was located in the north section of Zhentil Keep's Inner Quarter .
