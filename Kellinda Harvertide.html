Kellinda Harvertide was a halfling cleric of Yondalla at the Hearthcircle in the village of Shining Falls in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Living in a cottage near the grove, Kellinda and her two young acolytes provided magical healing for donations.
