Lylar Emveolstone was a noble inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Lylar was the patriarch of the Emveolstone noble family of Waterdeep. He lived within Emveolstone Villa on the Street of the Singing Dolphin in Waterdeep's Sea Ward .

Relationships

His son and heir was Lylar Emveolstone II .
