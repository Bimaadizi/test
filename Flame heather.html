Flame heather was a rare plant found only in the mountains of the Nakvaligach region of the Great Glacier .

Description

Flame heather grew in clusters of two to four plants, growing to a height of approximately 1 foot (30 centimeters). Its delicate, feather-like leaves were red and yellow.

Uses

Flame heather took its name from its exceptional flammability. A single cluster could burn throughout the entire night, delivering ample heat to warm a medium-sized snowhouse .
