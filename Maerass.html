Maerass was a small town located in the nation of Halruaa during the 14 th century DR .

Geography

Maerass was located in central Halruaa along the southern bank of the River Maeru just upriver from where it joined Lake Halruaa . The town sat northwest of Zalazuu and the Bay of Azuth .
