Clicking Heels was a hamlet on the southern slopes of the Spine of the World , northeast of Mithral Hall .

Description

It consisted of a cluster of houses surrounded by a piled stone wall.

Inhabitants

Its population was two hundred humans in 1370 DR. The settlement was home to one unnamed cleric who possessed little in the way of healing magic.

History

Tred Mc Knuckles and Nikwillig stumbled upon Clicking Heels when Tred was injured, and its inhabitants allowed them to rest there for more than a tenday.

Clicking Heels was later attacked and razed by a force of frost giants and worg -riding orcs in 1370 DR , about a tenday after the departure of Tred and Nikwillig.
