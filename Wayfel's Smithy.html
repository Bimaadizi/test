Location

The smithy was located in the northwest corner of Daggerford at the junction of Wall Street and Horse Way .

Services

The only thing lower than Wayfel 's prices was the quality of his work. He did not come close to competing with the fine pieces forged at Derval's Bright Blade .

History

Some time around the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR Wayfel was hired to construct a tower that overlooked Daggerford's River Gate. Unfortunately, he failed to anchor the structure to the solid rock beneath the Delimbiyr River . When the river flooded, the tower collapsed.

Wayfel was not offered any construction contracts after that fiasco.

Lore

When referring to the blacksmith Cromach , locals said his work was "as shaky as Wayfel's best."
