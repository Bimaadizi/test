Nelburn Frust was an inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Nelburn was a dark-skinned gold dwarf man with a clean-shaved face, black hair, and blue eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Nelburn Frust procured a spell or an incantation of dubious authenticity, presumably from the nearby Devil's Fee magic shop. Regardless of the spell and the promises of luck it could deliver, Nelburn struggled greatly with recitation.
