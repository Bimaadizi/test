The Codsbay was a small part of the Sea of Moonshae located on the northern coast of the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The bay was located along the Strait of Oman at the mouth of the Codsrun Creek were it emptied into the sea at the Codsbay Mire .
