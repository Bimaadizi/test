The efreeti tyrants were the ruthless leaders of the efreet .

Organization

The Sultan of the Efreet, Marrake al-Sidan al-Hariq ben Lazan , ruled over all of the noble efreeti tyrants.

Activities

Efreet were obsessed with conquest and expanding their sulfurous empire, earning them many enemies along the way.

Base of Operations

The City of Brass within the Elemental Plane of Fire served as the seat of power for the efreeti tyrants.
