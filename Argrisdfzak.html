Argrisdfzak was a demon of the fifth layer of the Nine Hells who was summoned to a child's 5 th birthday party.

History

The demon was born in the Year of the Jeweled Aerie, 744 DR . Ulysses the Not-so-Smart summoned the demon to his son Harry's 5 th birthday party.

For unknown reasons, there was a statue in memory of the demon on the second level of Undermountain as of 1372 DR , which related the above information. The inscription on the statue described the party where Argrisdfzak attended as very memorable.
