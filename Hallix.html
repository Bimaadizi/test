The Hallixes were a family based in Neverwinter .

Activities

Like other wealthy Neverwintan families, the Hallixes maintained a family mausoleum in the Neverdeath Main Graveyard . This mausoleum contained a secret connection to the crypts of the Waterclock Guild .

History
