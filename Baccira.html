Baccira was a soultaker ghul-kin living in Hawa in 1367 DR .

Description

Like all ghul-kin, Baccira used illusions to mask her true appearance, preferring to appear as an attractive dark-haired woman.

Activities

Baccira was a member of the Cult of Istishia , serving as a lesser priestess.
