Beef Sharburg was an exquisite and expensive dish that originated from Sharburg in the Dalelands .

Description

Beef Sharburg was a complex dish, served with a side of Marsember beets and followed up with a bowl of eldritch berry custard .

History

Even though originated in the Dalelands, Beef Sharburg was served in more exclusive and expensive inns, such as Chateau Elite in Daggerford circa 1372 DR .
