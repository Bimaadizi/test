Teresiel was a prisoner at the Fane of the Eye under Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

In 1491 DR , Teresiel was chosen among many others to be part of a diplomatic delegation to Mirabar . However, at Beliard in the Dessarin Valley , they were ambushed by the Cult of the Black Earth . Teresiel was captured and transported beyond the River Dessarin . There, in the Sumber Hills , they were attacked by the Cult of the Howling Hatred , but Teresiel remained in Black Earth's custody.

Some time after being transported to the Fane, however, she was ceded to the Cult of the Eternal Flame , who put her in a jail in their section of the Fane until some adventurers infiltrated the temple and freed her.
