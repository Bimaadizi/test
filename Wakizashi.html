A wakizashi ( pronounced : / w ɑː k ɪ z ɑː ʃ i / wah-kih-zah-shee ) was a short sword commonly used in Kara-Tur .

Description

The wakizashi was basically a short katana , sharing almost identical design and forging process. A wakizashi's blade was between 2‒3 ft (61‒91 cm) in length and 1 in (2.5 cm) in width.
