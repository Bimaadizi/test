Faerdaleeyn was a member of the Ala-min tribe within the Lake of Steam region during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Though scarred from many battles, Faerdaleeyn was still considered handsome in a rugged way. He had deep black eyes that often intimidated strangers.

Activities

Faerdaleeyn was the elder of the Ala-min as of the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR . He inherited leadership upon the death of his father, Zalaster .

Unbeknownst to him, an agent of the Harpers named Frederique hoped to use Faerdaleeyn and his tribe to attack a group of Banites who travelled through their lands.
