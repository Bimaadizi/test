King Rhadik Torlath II "the Sleeping King" was a long-lived Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty, known as the fifth Wizard-King of Amn.

Relationships

Rhadik was the fourth son of of King Jhanos II , and his sole son was Imnel IV .
