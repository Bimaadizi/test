Mhaalauvur's was a jug store in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep .

Location

This building stood at the northwestern corner of The Boomgarden , where it intersected with Fillet Lane .

Services

Mhaalauvur's sold a wide variety of liquids within jug vessels the size of a human head. These included axle grease, cooking oils , scented washing soap flakes, slicewash (liquid disinfectants), and droot .
