Serag Kull was one of the captains of the military force known as the Red Plumes .

Description

Serag stood around 6 ft (1.8 m) tall with a broad build. He had a scar on his neck that he received from a Zhentarim soldier.

Personality

Never to be out-manipulated, Kull was a cruel, sly and dangerously smart individual.
