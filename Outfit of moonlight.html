The outfit of moonlight was a fine set of clothes found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The clothes were imbued with the mysterious aspect of the moon , and sheltered the wearer from hostile spellcraft while making their own spells more flashy and attention-grabbing.

History

The outfit was found in the House of Gems when the Elves of Cormanthor raided it for the phylactery of Droic Orgul .
