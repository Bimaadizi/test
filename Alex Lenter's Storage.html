Alex Lenter's Storage was a warehouse located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Location

Alex Lenter's Storage was located in the central section of the Dock Ward on the southwest side of the intersection of Trollcrook Alley and Zastrow Street .
