Garras' Keen Sight was a unique magic ring , etched with intricate elven filagree, that belonged to elven archer Garras in the days of Myth Drannor 's glory.

Powers

Garras' Keen Sight possessed powerful enchantments, especially helpful to archers. The wearer of the ring was given uncanny power to hit any target with a bow, increased the wearer's dexterity, and the wearer could cast the cat's grace spell once a day.

History

Before the Weeping War , the Keen Sight ring was used by the archer-defender of Myth Drannor Garras, aiding his skills and career. After his death in the Dwarven Dungeons , the ring came to be possessed by a mysterious monument, the Great Stone Head , found in the Nightingale Court of the ruined Myth Drannor. In exchange for one of the eidolons , small totem statues depicting various gods, the monument, dispensed one of the powerful magic rings. One of those rings was Garras' Keen Sight .
