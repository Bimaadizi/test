Belagon was one of the best generals of Mephistopheles .

History

Belagon was one of the generals of an army of devils that invaded the Shadowfell in 1484 DR . The devils clashed with an undead army led by Drasek Riven . In order to lure out Mephistopheles himself, Drasek confronted Belagon and killed him in combat.
