The Vale of the Reaver was a valley located in the Vilhon Reach named for its occupancy of devout followers of Garagos , the Reaver. Compared to the oldest House of Steel , the Vale was the largest or most prominent center of Garagathan faith as of the mid-14 th century DR .

Geography

The valley was located near the headwaters of the Thornwash River between the Thornwood and the western edge of the Cloven Mountains . It was located north of Saelmur and Ankhapur and south of the Deepwash .
