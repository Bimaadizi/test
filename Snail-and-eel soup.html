Snail-and-eel soup was a soup dish made by the lizardfolk of the Vast Swamp of Cormyr , made from snails and eels .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the Sharptooth tribe brewed snail-and-eel soup in a hot spring at their encampment in the swamp. The chieftain Kessessek offered this concoction to adventurers investigating Sharran activities there as they parlayed.
