Volland Moss was a young boy that lived with his family on a peat farm in the Twilight Marsh during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Volland was one of five children born to Millivent and Haldred Moss . He siblings included sisters Alleena and Kithian , brother Quayle , and baby Bo .

History

During the 1480s DR , the Moss family farm was ravaged by goblin raiders. Volland and most of his siblings were believed to be taken prisoner by the goblins, while only his mother and baby Bo managed to get to safety.
