An amulet of inescapable location was a cursed magical item worn on a chain or as a brooch.

Description

It appeared to be an amulet that prevented scrying , detection, and the like, and even magic such as detect magic would confirm that this was the case.

Powers

When worn, an amulet of inescapable location would make the wearer far easier to scry or otherwise detect by doubling the range and ease at which all location attempts were made against the wearer.
