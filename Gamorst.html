Gamorst was the leader of the Tribunal of Evernight as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

As a member of the Tribunal, he was a powerful ghoul and a follower of Orcus .

Personality

Gamorst was succinct and impatient. He immediately demanded tribute in exchange for the Tribunal's time, and he allowed for no interference in their decisions.

Abilities

He had the ability to destroy even a lich with a mere point of his finger.

History

As of the late 15 th century DR, the undead residents of Evernight held Gamorst responsible for the unpopular rule that allowed the Tribunal to extend their protection to mortal visitors to the city.

When the lich Zarifrax attempted to slay a mortal to whom Gamorst had extended the Tribunal's protection, Gamorst swiftly destroyed him and claimed his phylactery .
