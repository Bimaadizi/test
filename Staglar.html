Lord Staglar was a member of the court of Cormyr in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

He was quite portly, obese even.

History

As of Ches 14 in the Year of the Sword, 1365 DR , Andemel Graeven believed him to be at the same revel as he was at the time.
