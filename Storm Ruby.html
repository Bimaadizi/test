Storm Ruby was a variety of dessert fortified wine created by Thamalon Uskevren , of the noble Uskevren family of Selgaunt , Sembia , in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Storm Ruby was a sweet, tart, and full-bodied wine.

In the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , Thamalon Uskevren sought to trade with Nuldrevyn Talendar of House Talendar , discussing a deal to ship Storm Ruby and other Uskevren wines to southern Amn , Tethyr , and Calimshan , along with other Sembian trade goods. After tasting Storm Ruby, Nuldrevyn Talendar appreciated its excellent taste.
