Twilight Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Twilight Street was located in southern Zhentil Keep within the Foreign Quarter . The street was found in the far western section of the quarter running from Slingshot Way and the River Trail Gate to the main intersection with Zhent Avenue near the South Force Gate . Twilight Street connected with Center Street and Teshwave Street at the same intersection.
