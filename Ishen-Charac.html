Homeland(s)Tymanther

The Ishen-Charac was a bigger type of the heavier breed of horses that were often used as warhorses. They were commonly found throughout Tymanther , where they were prized and cherished by the dragonborn .

Description

The Ishen-Charac were big, powerful wide-backed horses, specifically breed to be able to bear dragonborn riders.

History

A few dragonborn clans were known for breeding the Ishen-Charac, among them clans Shestandeliath and Clethtinthtiallor .

Dragonborn didn't use their Ishen-Charac for war until the Invasion of Tymanther of 1479 DR , when the Lance Defenders implemented their first cavalry units on horseback.
