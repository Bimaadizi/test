Noanar was a Netherese arcanist of the Silver Age .

Description

The Fire Warden was a talented arcanist who specialized in fire-based magic .

History

Noanar was born in 1505 NY (−2354 DR) south of Netheril and became a wanderer of north Faerûn . After much travelling, the arcanist settled in Canlespiere . In 1535 NY (−2324 DR) , the archwizard Ioulaum bestowed upon Noanar the title of "Fire Warden". Noanar later developed much of the well-known fire-based spells that were used around the Realms . The first of these was fire trap , which was developed in 1540 NY (−2319 DR) . This was followed by wall of fire , fire shield , delayed blast fireball , and finally, fireball . Noanar practiced his spells on ogres around Canlespiere. However, he was killed in a skirmish with these ogres in 1559 NY (−2300 DR) .
