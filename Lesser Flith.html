A lesser flith was a form of enchanted starmap.

Description

A lesser flith built by Delmuth Echorn occupied the upper half of a room with a domed ceiling. It was made of gemstones, presumably suspended in midair through magical or otherwise means.

Powers

When stars were named in a lesser flith , their representation glowed with light. Even if the gemstone corresponding to a star was missing, the point in space would glow; almost certainly.
