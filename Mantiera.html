Mantiera was a human druid who enjoyed shapeshifting into the form of a seal .

Description

In her seal form, Mantiera had brownish-black skin. The only telltale mark of her true nature was the red medallion fastened around her neck.

Personality

She preferred taking the form of a seal primarily because she liked the feeling of water on her fur. She liked swimming alone, but would occasionally travel alongside friendly seal families.

Abilities

She was unusually dexterous, making her difficult for foes to land a blow on.

Possessions

Mantiera owned a medallion of thought projection .
