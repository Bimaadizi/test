Rorsorn was a highknight of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

During his last raid, he was in the same group as Kelgantor , Tethlor , Rorskryn Mreldrake , and Jusprar .

History

Rorsorn was one of the highknights that took part in the raid on Elminster Aumar 's refuge at Tethgard in the Hullack Forest after the old sage had stolen the Royal Gorget of Battle , on Mirtul 5 of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR . He was not named among the known survivors afterwards.
