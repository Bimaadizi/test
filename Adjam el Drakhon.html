Adjam el Drakhon was syl-pasha of Calimport during the Third age of Calimshan .

Relationships

Syl-Pasha Adjam's son was one of the sultans of Calimport.

History

In −1161 DR , the famous wizard Ali el Samesaj recued Adjam's granddaughter from kidnappers. As a reward, Ali was made court wizard of Adjam's son.

During the entirety of Adjam's reign, the empire was at war with the beholders of the Alimir Hive , led by Qyraaptir the Bloodeye .
