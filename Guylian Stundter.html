Guylian Stundter was a halfling farmer in the Moonsea in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Guylian regularly sold his products to Reyill Werned . He was a charismatic being, always smiling.

History

His partnership with Reyill was very good for Guylian's business over the years.

Circa 1486 DR , Guylian was drinking with his halfling friends and Reyill at the Plodding Plow , in the village of The Stop .

Relationship

Guylian was friends with Narder Ulthub and Roge Applewink . He had good dealings with Reyill Werned.
