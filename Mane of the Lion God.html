Mane of the Lion-God was a type of enchanted cloak created and worn by the followers of Nobanion , the titular Lion God of the Gulthmere Forest .

Powers

These Nobanionite cloaks were imbued with the Lord Firemane's divine magics increasing the wearer's defense when fighting creatures venerating chaos. Additionally, the wearer became unaffected by normal and magical fear .

History

Manes of the Lion-God were worn with great pride by the druids of Nobanion who warred against lycanthrope servants of Malar , Nobanion's enemy. These clocks served as reminders that there was order even in nature. As of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Nobanionite religion started spreading outside the Gulthmere Forest, along with the church, Manes of the Lion-God started appearing in regions other than the Vilhon Reach . One such cloak was being sold by a druid merchant who did business on the Market Triangle in Westgate.
