The Amaratha Stream was a flowing body of water found in the grassy steppes of the Ride .

Geography

The stream flowed south from the White Peaks and wound southwest for miles before ending at a point just east of the Rogan's Sprint trail.

Etymology

Amarathas were a variety of light green jewels unique to Faerûn .
