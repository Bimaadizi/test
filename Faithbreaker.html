Faithbreaker was a magical warhammer associated with the cult of the Absolute .

Description

While the handle of the hammer had the typical notches that were associated with the rank of its hobgoblin owner, Faithbreaker featured another, more notable marking. Its leather handle was stamped with a brand in the shape of a hand.

Powers

The warhammer possessed a +1 enchantment . It allowed its wielder to channel the power of the Absolute .

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , it was in the possession of the True Soul leader Dror Ragzlin .
