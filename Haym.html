Haym was a Netherese arcanist who oversaw the Mythallar Housing of Eileanar in Netheril shortly before its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Haym was partially responsible for guarding the Housing and monitoring the output of the enclave 's twin mythallars , with Dewez and Salix taking up the duties on their own work shifts. All three were assisted by the arcanist Cowper whenever necessary.
