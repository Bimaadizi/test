Narthil Keep was a fortress near Narthil in the 3 rd century DR .

Location

It was north of Narthil .

Structure

The fortress could be described as "a pile of rocks", but had a gate.

Activities

In the Year of Bloodflowers, 234 DR , Aunsiber , the Lord Steward that oversaw the town during the occupation by Athalantar , lived in that keep.

Defenses

There was at least a nominal amount of forces at the keep.
