Ysgarda was a page of Candlemas , the High Steward of Delia , in the Netherese Empire circa −700 DR .

Description

The girl had deep blue eyes and short black hair.

Personality

Ysgarda acted very flighty and nervous around powerful figures, such as Candlemas.

Possessions

When working for the High Steward, Ysgarda wore a white and black outfit.

Relationships

Her father managed the dovecotes in Delia for Lady Polaris .

History

During the Shadowed Age , the nine-year-old page informed Candlemas that members of the Beneficent Traders' Guild of Dalekeva , also known as Oberon's Hold, wished to speak to him about a growing problem. The traders begged the Netherese to protect them from the orc and human army of the One King who had destroyed the neighboring city of Tinnainen .
