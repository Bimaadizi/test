Dwarven Celebration Brew was a special type of dwarven alcoholic drink, often called "Liquid Courage" .

Powers

Served in tall foamy steins, Dwarven Celebration Brew gave its imbibers a rush of heroism making them stronger and more valiant.

History

In the mid-to-late-15 th century DR , Dwarven Celebration Brew could be found in the city of Neverwinter , popular among its adventuring population. These invigorating drinks were also served at the Beached Leviathan tavern.
