Admiral's Way was a dockside street in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate .

Description

Admiral's Way was located in the Eastway , a single city block south of Sailor's Crossing plaza. The Low Lantern festhall was docked at the eastern end of Admiral's Way in the 15 th century DR .

History

In the summer of the Year of the Purloined Statue, 1477 DR , on Kythorn 19 , the Sailor's Crossing plaza became a scene of a grizzly murder, located just within earshot of Admiral's Way. A puking human male was reported being on Admiral's Way shortly after the murder took place, but the detective missed the opportunity to interrogate the likely witness.
