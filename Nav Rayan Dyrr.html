Nav Rayan Dyrr was a very talented scout of Bregan D'aerthe in the early 14 th century DR .

History

In 1313 DR , Nav Rayan Dyrr was the head scout of a Bregan D'aerthe mission to Ched Nasad headed by Beniago and Zaknafein Do'Urden . They were to follow a House Hunzrin expedition. In 1329 DR Nav was secretly among the members of the expedition led by Dinin Do'Urden against the surface elves and witnessed Drizzt Do'Urden killed a child; immediately reported the news to Jarlaxle .
