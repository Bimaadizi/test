Yosh al-Nasr , or the Eagle, was a Maztican Eagle Knight living in Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

His real name was Yax Cautli .

History

While living in Maztica with his sister, Yosh sailed across the Trackless Sea with the hopes of seeing Faer√ªn . His ship was blown off course and wrecked during a violent storm, yet he and his sister were able to survive by clinging to a piece of flotsam, though they were separated at sea. Yosh was eventually rescued by sailors who transported him to Qudra on the shores of Zakhara . He was able to narrowly avoid becoming a slave.

Activities

Yosh wanted to discover the fate of his sister, Taxatl , who was in fact a member of the Royal Harim of Huzuz . He used his unique skills to earn work as an elite bodyguard while he searched for his sister.

Possessions

Yosh owned some bizarre, at least by Zakharan standards, equipment including a maca and a matching set of feathered armor and headpiece.
