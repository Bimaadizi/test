Amulets of the Harpers were enchanted necklaces imbued with protective magics.

Description

Amulets of the Harpers were delicate pieces of jewelry inlaid with several gemstones .

Powers

The necklace gave the wearer the ability to cast the shield spell once per day. Additionally, it imparted wisdom onto the wearer, which protected their mind.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , an amulet of the Harpers belonged to Talli , a Harper quartermaster who followed the druid Harper hero Jaheira into the Shadow-cursed Lands .
