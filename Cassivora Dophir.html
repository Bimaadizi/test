Cassivora Dophir was a Gur monster hunter from the tribe led by Ulma that traveled along the Sword Coast and Western Heartlands during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Cassivora had a stern look and even tougher personality. She wore a nose ring and a set of crude leather armor with a leather circlet on her head. Cassivora carried two scimitars .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Cassivora Dophir's monster hunter tribe found itself targeted by the vampires of Baldur's Gate during the conflict with the Cult of the Absolute crisis, when the Gurs camped in the Rivington district of the city, along with a small army of refugees displaced by the conflict. To the Gurs' horror, the tribe's children were abducted by the vampires under orders from Cazador Szarr . Cassivora could be found near tribe's funeral bonfire sternly commanding visitors to announced themselves to Ulma.
