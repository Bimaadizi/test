Etienne was a mage who worked aboard the Crane during the mid-to-late 15 th century DR .

Some time after the Year of Splendors Burning, 1469 DR , Etienne joined the sailors of the Crane as part of its crew. She became romantically linked with the ship's captain, Harp Levesque , in a tryst that ended poorly. Under some unknown circumstances, Etienne left the ship in tears, never to sail with them agian. According to crewman Boult , she was "chased off" by Captain Harp.
