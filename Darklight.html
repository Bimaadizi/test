A darklight was an undead creature of the Quasi-Elemental Plane of Radiance .

Mortal spirits that departed their bodies at death, but did not become petitioners and then became lost on their way to its final rest , might arrive at the Plane of Radiance. Here, they were filled with radiant energy and became free-willed undead beings, called darklights.

They appeared as humanoid-shaped specters of blackness with bright, shining eyes of ever-changing colors.
