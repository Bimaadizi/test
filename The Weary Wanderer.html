The Weary Wanderer was a gruff and unwelcoming inn in the hamlet of Skull Crag in the Storm Horn Mountains of Cormyr in the 14 th century DR .

Services

The Wanderer offered lodgings in several private rooms as well as the common room. The inn's proprietress offered free lodgings in the common room to adventurers and caravan guards, despite thinking them to be nothing but riffraff, while rooms were reserved for individuals with coin. The Weary Wanderer did not turn away customers; humans , dwarves , and even oni mages were all allowed patronage, as long as they could pay.
