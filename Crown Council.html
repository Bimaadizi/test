The Crown Council was the ruling council of Relmaur , Laerakond , as of 1479 DR .

Background

The Crown Council was established by High Crown Harangor Urlingscar to allow all the prominent Relmorn dragonborn clans, Clans Urlingscar , Belarrak , and Garandorn , to participate in the government of the kingdom.

Members of the Council were called Crown-swords. There were six members in the Council, two from each clan.
