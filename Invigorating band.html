Invigorating bands were enchanted circlets, used in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

When worn, invigorating bands produces constant tingling sensation around the wearer's temples. This magic granted them improved initiative in battle.

History

In the 15 th century DR, invigorating bands were in wide use across the Sword Coast North area, namely the Sword Mountains , the city of Neverwinter and its markets, among goblins of the Neverwinter Wood , and the ancient ruins of Illefarn .
