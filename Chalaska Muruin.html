Chalaska Muruin was the unofficial ruler of Bargewright Inn during the Elemental Evil crisis in 1491 DR .

Activities

Muruin was the master of the gate guards of the Inn and acted as unofficial ruler under orders of a cabal of plutocratic business owners, most of whom were in the pockets of the Zhentarim .
