The Guardians of the Three Fires were an elite group of soldiers in the City of Brass in 1367 DR .

Activities

The Guardians of the Three Fires served to protect the Mystical Legate of the Hidden Imam and the temples under his control.

Base of Operations

The Guardians were operated within the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire . They were active primarily within the Furnace ward of the city.
