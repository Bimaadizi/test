Darastriverthicha ("Dragon Mountain", in Draconic ) was an ancient dragon kingdom during the Dawn Ages ( −30,000 DR to −24,000 DR ). It warred with the giant kingdoms of the day. It was ruled by Caesinsjach , a mighty green dragon .

Among its vassal states was Darastrixhurthi , a nation of kobolds ruled by Kurtulmak , located in what is now the Shoal of Thirst .

History

During the first Rage of Dragons , circa −25,000 DR , Caesinsjach and her fellow dragons destroyed Darastriverthicha and all its vassal states.
