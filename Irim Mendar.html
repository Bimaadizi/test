Irim Mendar was a merchant from the city of Samargol , Samarach , circa 1374 DR .

Description

Irim Mendar was a charismatic and eloquent merchant who dressed in green finery and demeaned his competitors' goods in order to prop up his own merchandise.

History

Circa the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Irim Mendar had a stall full of trade goods located in the Openpalm Bazaar in Samargol. He dealt in a wide variety of items, from the finest clothing to enchanted arms and armor.
