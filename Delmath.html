Delmath was an acquaintance of several guards in Shadowdale in the mid-14 th century DR .

Relationships

While being spied by Malark Himbruel in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , one guard related unto the others that he'd witnessed the spellfire wielded by Shandril Shessair . While describing her exertion, he compared her face to the one that Delmath himself made while lifting a heavy barrel.
