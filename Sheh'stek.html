Sheh'stek was a lizardfolk from the Vast Swamp in Cormyr in 1479 DR .

History

In 1479 DR, Sheh'stek was one of the lizardfolk that came through a portal in the Vast Swamp and arrived in Undermountain . He was sent to explore a portion of the dungeon and was captured by the vampire Dayan . Sheh'stek was left in his cell to die.
