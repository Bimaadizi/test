Garno was a vengeful slayer of clergy of Loviatar who lived some time before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Some years before the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Garno's family was put to the torch by malicious members of the Church of Loviatar . Driven to disillusionment, Garno swore to bring vengeance on the evil clergy. He claimed many Loviatan lives thanks to wielding Arbane's Sword of Agility . It was believed that a deity of chaos helped the sword to reach Garno's hand, reveling in havoc caused by the vengeful man.
