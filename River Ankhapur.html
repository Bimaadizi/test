The River Ankhapur was a small waterway found near the Lake of Steam .

Geography

The river originated near the center of the Ankhwood north of the eastern half of the Lake of Steam. It flowed southwest and west for 200 miles (320 kilometers) before emptying into the sea approximately 50 miles (80 kilometers) southeast of Ankhapur .
