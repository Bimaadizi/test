Mystra's Mantle was a temple to Mystra in Haramettur during the mid–-8 th century DR .

Activities

The priests were capable of some castings.

History

The sage Tabarast attested in the Year of Laughter, 761 DR , that the priests at Mystra's Mantle in Haramettur were not great casters, and also, that they'd told him of matters regarding a Chosen of Mystra , the One Who Walks .
