King Uth Myrmoran I was a human of Illuskan descent who constructed Uthtower and became the self-proclaimed king of a small port settlement on the Sword Coast North in the Year of the Risen Towers, 146 DR . The realm was named Uthtower after the newly built seat of power.

Relationships

Uth I's consort was Queen Sarlani and their eldest son and heir was Ornoth Myrmoran I .

History

Uth I was a Lord of Tavaray until he was exiled. He moved to a small port settlement in the region south of Leilon and, in 146 DR, he built Uthtower on a rocky hill near the coast. He brought a company of Shoon mercenaries with him, and proclaimed himself king, intending to build a great realm from this first settlement. He had brought with him a great deal of personal wealth from Tavaray and used this to pay off anyone who was opposed to him seizing power.

Uth I began to feel the effects of age around 158 DR and allowed his eldest son Ornoth Myrmoran to run the government. He died a decade later in the Year of Scattered Stars, 168 DR .

King Uth I's and Queen Sarlani's crypts were located below the ruins of the Uthtower in what eventually became the Mere of Dead Men .
