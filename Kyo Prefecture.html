Kyo was a prefecture in Kara-Tur , made famous by the creation of Hideo's kamas many years before the late 14 th century DR .

History

The simple farmer folk of the Kyo prefecture were forced to take up arms in defense of their homes against marauding pirates, but lacking martial weapons. The man named Hideo was the mind behind the use of simple farming implements and created the first Hideo's kamas .
