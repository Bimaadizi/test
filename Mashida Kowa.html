Mashida Kowa was a korobokuru samurai and retainer of the Goshukara clan in Nakamaru in the 1770s on the Wa Calendar .

Activities

Because of both his skills and his heritage, many considered Kowa an ill-begotten upstart. Regardless, he tried to live according to human customs and was very sensitive about racist offenses.

History

Kowa was born and raised in the wooded mountains outside Nakamaru but for his qualities he was adopted by the Mashida clan . With them, he trained as a samurai and a very promising future.

In Wa Year 1777 (1359 DR) , Oka Yoshinaka and Kowa clashed with the Gamo brothers Gamo , Mitsutoshi , and Mochikuni .
