Hua Li Te was a powerful abbot in Shou Lung in 1357 DR .

Activities

Hua Li managed the Pure Thought Monastery , the largest monastery in Arakin .

Personality

Thanks to his age, Hua Li remembered well the time of the great religious wars and was determined to never live through a similar time again. For this, he was very severe with monks or sohei of his monastery that fought with other sects. Thank to his cunning and skill, Hua Li was still the most powerful of his monastery.
