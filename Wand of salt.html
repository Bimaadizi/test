A wand of salt was a rare enchanted wand that wracked a victim with intense cramps. Only two of these wands were known to exist.

Description

These items were made from a strange substance called saltsteel . The surface of the wand was overlaid with sand and sparkled.

Powers

The beam issued from a wand of salt assaulted victims with intense cramps and thirst that caused severe pain until they could drink a large amount of water. The wand could also be used to make a plot of land measuring 50 feet (15 meters) wide and long infertile.

History

These items were used by wicked followers of Tiamat .
