Daruk was a human bard of the late 15 th century DR and a friend of the Ikemmu merchant Tatigan Carrlock . He was said to be beloved by Shar .

History

In the Year of Deep Water Drifting, 1480 DR , Daruk traveled with a caravan from Ikemmu to Rashemen . Along the way, Daruk helped defeat bandit and winter wolf attacks.

Abilities

Daruk had a special connection to Shar and was able to channel some of her power. He was able to infuse some of Shar's power into the shadar-kai Ashok to help him defeat a winter wolf attack.

Personality

Daruk had a cocky demeanor and often annoyed those around him.
