Shylo's Slicer was an intelligent shortsword in the possession of Hogan Underwood , who once used the alias of Shylo Slick.

Powers

Shylo's Slicer was not just a very powerful sword . It could teleport up to 600 pounds once per day, and it detected when passages sloped. It was also more than fairly intelligent.
