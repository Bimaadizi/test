Stlarakur was a priest of Bane in the mid–14 th century DR .

Personality

He was unaccustomed to hastening anywhere.

History

Stlarakur traveled with a caravan from Scornubel to Waterdeep in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , which Shandril Shessair joined and he was tasked with retrieving her spellfire . However, while searching for cover during an ambush, he was slain by Marlel . The Zhentarim observers Hlael Toraunt and Korthauvar Hammantle confirmed his death later.
