The stone heart was a magical stone similar to an Eberronnian warforged docent , recovered from the Demonweb Pits in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The stone heart appeared to be the petrified heart of a fiend and radiated a feeling of magical power, but had a truly mysterious origin and purpose.

Powers

The stone heart could be used as a docent , imbuing the warforged with physical toughness and resilience.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Cormyrean forces entered the Demonweb Pits and the Domain of Lord Grulemith . In the process of reaching Lord Grulemith , the stone heart may have been recovered from the hezrou Shufarix or the beast Tsirrik .
