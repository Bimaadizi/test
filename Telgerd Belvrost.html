Telgerd Belvrost was a member of the Belvrost clan of mages .

History

As of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , he was one of the best-known and most influential members of the Belvrost clan in Reddansyr . Besides being the town wizard, he was a toymaker, the owner of the Belled Boot .
