Rurik was a dwarven man that lived on the Sword Coast for a time during the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

He wielded a pair of daggers in battle and made liberal use of poison on his blades.

Relationships

For a while, Rurik traveled alongside the fallen paladin of Tyr Anders , the wizard Cyrel , and the ranger Trynn .

History

Rurik and his companions spent some time guarding a tollhouse on the Risen Road in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , when it was attacked by a band of vicious gnoll cultists . They managed to turn away the bestial raiders, while Rurik himself killed the largest of their force, but could not save the lives of the innocent toll takers.
