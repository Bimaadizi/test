Lamp Street was a short, curving avenue in Waterdeep that spanned the length of the city's Castle Ward and had a small segment in the Trades Ward .

Location

This road extended west off of Andamaar's Street , corduroy-log paving, and curved southward until it terminated at the High Road . From there the road continued westward, where it was paved with packed earth and gravel pavement, intersecting through the main avenues of the Street of Bells and Street of the Sword , the alleyway Elsambul's Lane , and finally terminating on the eastern side of the main avenue Warrior's Way .
