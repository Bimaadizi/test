The Black Lord's Caress was the unique and ancient enchanted Banite light flail that belonged to banelich Shrazgul .

Powers

This flail held a +4 enchantment and was imbued with negative energy . The weapon's hammer could easily smash through bones with its massive critical hits.

History

Banelich Shrazgul infamously wielded this dreadful weapon against the enemies of the Church of Bane and even the fellow Banites when the lich deemed them guilty of supposed slights against him. Shrazgul named the weapon the Black Lord's Caress in pious attempts to honor his dark deity. However, that did not stop the god from imprisoning him when the banelich became wayward and hard to control. In 1135 DR , the Black Lord's Caress was considered lost after its owner's disappeared from the Realms.

The weapon resurfaced in 1373 DR , found under the town of Voonlar , inside its deep catacombs dedicated to recently resurrected god Bane.
