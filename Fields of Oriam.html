The Fields of Oriam were plains south of the Bay of Chessenta in Chessenta .

Geography

They formed a savannah of rolling grassland with long grasses and occasional pine barrens, but the soil was poor, being too dry and acidic for farmland.

Inhabitants

Hardly any human settlements were to be found here, partly because of the limited agriculture but mostly because the area is home to cockatrices .

Locations

Ancient Untheric ruins dotted the savannah. The most notable among these is the so-called Lost Temple of Ptah , which was said to still hold holy tablets of magnificent power and wisdom.
