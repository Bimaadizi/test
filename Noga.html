Noga was the language of the ancient kingdom of Nog in the Ruined Kingdoms .

Description

Compared to Midani , Noga had a cadence that seemed strange to those not fluent in it.

Speakers

As late as 1367 DR , Noga could still be heard amidst the bazaars of the mainland Ruined Kingdoms, though Midani was the official language by that time. Despite this, most scholars considered it a dead language.

Related Languages

Noga was in the Midani languages subgroup, being related to the Midani tongue spoken in Zakhara . This placed it also in the Untheric group of Rauric languages.
