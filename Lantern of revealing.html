Lanterns of revealing were enchanted hooded lanterns that allowed the user to dispel invisibility .

Description

Lanterns of revealing were regular hooded lanterns in appearance.

Powers

With a spoken command word, these lanterns were lit with magics that purged invisibility off of all things and creatures that were illuminated. Lanterns of revealing required oil to burn and could sustain its flames up to six hours fro one pint of it. These lanterns illuminated 25 feet (7.6 meters) - 30 feet (9.1 meters) radius and created dim light for 30 feet (9.1 meters) more. With lowered lantern hood, its light only reached 5 feet (1.5 meters).

Creation

Lanterns of revealing could be created by experienced wondrous item crafters and the use of invisibility purge spell.
