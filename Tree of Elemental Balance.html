The Tree of Elemental Balance was a unique tree located in the Protector's Enclave of Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR . It was tended to by Archdruid Morningdawn of the Emerald Enclave . The Tree of Elemental Balance could be used with the power of elemental water, earth, fire, and air to create a elemental weapon from an Awakened Heart .
