Khalab was a small fishing village located in Murghôm .

Location

Khalab sat on the western banks of Gbor Nor .

Inhabitants

Though the village was unremarkable, some considered the long trip to Khalab well worth the effort with the hopes of training under Salid Dhuzma , a bard of great renown.
