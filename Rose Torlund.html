Rose Torlund was one of the owners and proprietors of the Rose and Hammer inn, located within the town of Deadsnows . She shared ownership, as well as innkeeper duties, with her husband, Hedrick "the Hammer" . She was known for her good-humored disposition and welcoming attitude she displayed to her guests.
