The Nowhere Inn , as it was called by its inhabitants, was a campsite located on the Infinite Staircase that provided services and lodging for travelers along the planar pathway.

Description

The "inn" was located on a particularly wide landing of the Stairway that connected to several flights of stairs going up and down. It was a small agglomeration of tents and other temporary structures. The smell of cooking and the campfires could be picked up from quite a distance away.

Trade

Several of the campsite's permanent and temporary residents traded goods with travelers and offered information about some of the nearby destinations. It was also possible to hire guides or to set up parties to travel together along sections of the Staircase.

Defenses

The community of the Nowhere Inn was very close. Threats by visitors against a resident tended to end in violence.
