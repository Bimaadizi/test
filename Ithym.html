Ithym was a thief with the Brave Blades in the 3 rd century DR .

Personality

He was a little sarcastic, which seemed to be common in the band.

History

During the Brave Blades' delve into Ondil's Tower , Ithym found at least one secret door. Later, during a battle against a trap that held a red dragon , beholder , and a mind flayer in abeyance, Ithym helped fling projectiles into the beholder. He also helped explore the area beyond the trap, a circular room with no end of balconies on its sides, though he realized soon that all of the balconies, including the one they came from, led into the same room.

After they made their way into the lich's chamber, Gralkyn smashed one of the windows; in response, a force squeezed him and Ithym out of it, and hurled them into the ground, where they died.
