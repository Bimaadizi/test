The bracelets of flourishing fauna were a set of magical bracelets found in Spiderhaunt Wood during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The bracelets specially sharpened the teeth and claws of druids that wild shaped . They also granted a form of true sight to the wearer.

History

In ancient days, elven druids wore bracelets like these as a sign of their devotion to nature and the animals within it. At least one set of bracelets eventually found their way to the hoard of the fang dragon Nylerian , who laired in the Spiderhaunt Wood with a group of ettercaps . They may have been recovered by the forces of the Elves of Cormanthor during the Elven Crusade around 1374 DR .
