Lokoshin was the female Azuposi human spokesperson for the settlement of Kin Elbhaz in the Pasocada Basin in Maztica in the 1360s DR .

Personality

She had led the community for a decade and was strong-minded. She favored change and put in place numerous small projects to maintain this state of change. She was distrustful of strangers, but harbored curiosities about any she encountered.

Description

Lokoshin was around 5 feet 5 inches (1.65 meters) tall and weighed around 140 pounds (64 kilograms). She had graying hair and a weathered face, with sparkling, youthful eyes.

Equipment

Lokoshin used a club in times of combat.
