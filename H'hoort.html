H'hoort was a troglodyte in the Wormwrithings in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

H'hoort was the mate of Chieftain S'slaar .

History

Circa 1486 DR , S'slaar had a disagreement with his war chief H'slaat about the ownership of a magic sword . Soon a civil war started between S'slaar's and H'slaat's loyalists. H'slaat ordered warriors to capture H'hoort, hoping to bargain her for the sword.
