Orc double axes were a type of orcish poleaxe .

Description

This weapon was two double-bladed axes mounted on either end of a stout shaft. There were two wide grips on the haft, about midway between the center and each axe head. The blades were narrower than those on a greataxe to reduce the weight, which was considerable. The toe of each axe blade could be extended to a sharp point, and the ends of the haft could be spiked also. The average orc double axe weighed around 15 lb (6.8 kg).

Weaponology

They were not designed to be used as throwing weapons.
