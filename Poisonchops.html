Poisonchops was a giant spider from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Poisonchops, along with other giant spiders, drow , and duergar , all were slaughtered in the home of Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica . The murder spree was performed by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal .
