An animating door was a type of dweomered door that would temporarily bring to life any corpse that was brought through its threshold, as per the spell animate dead . Depending on the level of decomposition of the deceased person or creature, it could be brought back as a skeleton or zombie version of its previous self.

Creation

Animating doors were created with use of the animate dead spell.
