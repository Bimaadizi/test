Velki was a Reaper of Bhaal who resided in the ruined Temple of Bhaal in the Undercity of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Like many other Reapers of Bhaal, Velki was wrapped in dark cloth, a mask and a hood hid her facial features, leaving only her brown eyes exposed.
