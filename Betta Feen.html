Betta Feen was a human barmaid at the Ball and Chain Tavern on the docks of the Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Betta had coppery red hair in two braided pigtails. She wore a short shoulderless white dress with a dark gray corset and sandals while working.
