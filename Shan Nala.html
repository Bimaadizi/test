Shan Nala was the highest peak of the Raurin Alta mountains of the Raurin desert.

Geography

Shan Nala stood on the far eastern end of the mountain range near Howling Gap and Solon . It overlooked the peaks of Leopard Shan and Jorhat Shan on the plains below.

Description

At an elevation of 13,951 ft (4,252.3 m), Shan Nala towered above the other peaks of the Raurin Alta.
