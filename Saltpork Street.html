Saltpork Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

Saltpork Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . Saltpork Street began and ended on the Fieldway , branching first in the west just north of Twelvedog Court and reconnecting in the east near the High Road . Prestige Lane intersected Saltpork Lane's western section.
