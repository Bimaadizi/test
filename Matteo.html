Matteo was a shopkeeper in Phlan in 1367 DR .

Description

Tall for a half-elf at nearly six feet, Matteo was well-muscled. He wore his reddish-black hair shaved on the sides in a mohawk with the tail descending past his shoulders.

Activities

Matteo was a weaponsmith located in the residential district. He was very talented but slow in his work.
