Staves of corrosion were enchanted staves that exuded acid .

Description

Staves of corrosion were simple-looking wooden quarterstaves from 6 feet (1.8 meters) to 9 feet (2.7 meters) long. They were sometimes carved out of stout oak and impeded with jade stones.

Powers

In battle, staves of corrosion exuded acid when hitting a target. Some stronger versions of the item existed, bearing potent damage and aiming enchantments.

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , staves of corrosion could be found in the Icewind Dale region of northwest Faerûn . Stronger versions of these items saw usage in the areas surrounding Neverwinter in the 15 th century DR .
