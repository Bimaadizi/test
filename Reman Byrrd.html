Reman Byrrd was a human wizard and brother of Ramen Byrrd , active in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Reman stood 5 ft 6 in (1.7 m) tall and weighed around 150 pounds (68 kilograms). He had willowy blond hair and blue-green eyes. Overall he was very similar in appearance to his brother Ramen, with people finding it difficult to tell the two apart. They even had similar speech patterns.

Abilities

Reman and his twin brother Ramen shared an uncanny empathetic link. The twins were able to share ideas and thoughts between them without uttering a single word.

He was capable of casting the spells blindness , burning hands , charm person , clairvoyance , deafness , detect invisibility , ESP , feather fall , friends , flame arrows , identify , improved phantasmal force , knock , levitate , Melf's minute meteors , phantasmal force , secret page , sleep , spectral force , suggestion , summon swarm , unseen servant , ventriloquism , and wizard mark .

Possessions

Reman typically wore pale colors such as peach, beige and ivory, to contrast with the dark colored clothes that his brother preferred. Though sometimes he swapped outfits with his brother to confuse others.

On his person, Reman Byrrd wore his magical bracers of defense and was armed with a staff of striking . The brothers also owned a spelljammer ship.

History

The most notable event in Reman and Ramen's lives was their father's mysterious disappearance that was punctuated by the arrival of a spelljammer ship at their home. The twins took this opportunity to turn the magical vessel into an amusement ride.
