Alec III of the Carrathal Dynasty was a High King of Callidyrr in the early 11 th century DR . Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles , he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal royal family of Callidyrr and was the grandson of his predecessor, King Erfle . His son and sole heir was Bayle , who would go on to be his successor. Bayle's eventual successor, Yolanda , was Alec III's grandniece.

History

Alex III assumed the throne in the Year of the Flamboyant Coif, 1012 DR , after the death of King Erfle. He ruled for eighteen years before dying in a fire in the Year of Warlords, 1030 DR .
