Oorok was an exceptionally brutal ogre who commanded others of his kind within the Dragonspear horde , in the Year of the Wyvern, 1363 DR .

Description

What Oorok lacked in intelligence and courage he made up for in size and severity.

History

During the Second Dragonspear War , Oorok commanded the ogres that were part of the division known as The Storm . They fought in the final battle just outside of Dragonspear Castle .
