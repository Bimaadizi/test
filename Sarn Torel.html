Sarn Torel was a large fortified town of Athalantar in the early 3 rd century DR .

Description

Sarn Torel had a castle along the Unicorn Run. It was a large town.

Defenses

The city had castle walls overlooking the Unicorn Run.

History

In Hammer of the Year of the Flaming Forests, 224 DR , a group of twenty armsmen and twelve mages were sent out of Sarn Torel by mutual agreement between the local swordmaster and the Magelord Kadeln Olothstar . These men quickly rooted out the rebellious Knights of Athalantar fortified at Lawless Castle .

Ildryn Thallin had a tower in Sarn Torel, where contingencies went haywire upon his death in the Year of the Chosen, 240 DR , causing much chaos.
