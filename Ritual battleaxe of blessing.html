Ritual battleaxes of blessing were dwarven enchanted ceremonial axes used for burial by the dwarfs of Myth Drannor 's Dwarven Dungeons .

Description

Ritual battleaxes of blessing often appeared unused and lacking any signs of being used in battle. The axe itself had a harp double blade, perfectly capable of being wielded in combat.

Powers

These battleaxes had a +1 enchantment as well as gave the user the ability to cast bless .

History

In the days preceding the Weeping War , ritual battleaxes of blessing were laid along with dwarven warriors in the burial coffins as a part of the final rites ceremony. By 1369 DR , some of these enchanted battleaxes could be found in the coffins left behind in the Dwarven Dungeons of Myth Drannor, left behind by the restless undead the fallen heroes became.
