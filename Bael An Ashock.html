Bael An Ashok was the proprietor of the Grand Emporium in Neverwinter during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Bael spent much of his time offering advice to adventurers that were new to the city. He aided the efforts of Lord Neverember , helping facilitate novice adventurers with the accompaniment of traveling companions.

History

Circa the Year of Deep Water Drifting, 1480 DR , Bael became in some secret matter regarding the tieflings of the city. To this end he began recruiting tiefling adventurers to complete some specific missions..
