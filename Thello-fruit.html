TypeFruit

Thello-fruit was a variety of fruit endemic to the warm land of Luiren in the Shining South .

Description

This fruit had a taste comparable to kiwis , but its juices had a sort of thick and viscid quality like that of okra .

Usage

The hin of Luiren commonly used thello-fruit to make the condiment harelveauplum sauce .
