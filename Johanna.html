Johanna was a commoner who resided in the village of Imnesvale in the Umar Hills of Amn in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Johanna was a fair-skinned human woman who wore tan-colored commoner's clothing.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the village of Imnesvale faced a slew of disappearances and locals did not hesitate to blame the legendary Umar Witch . Johanna and her husband Ander spent much of his free time in the Umar Inn . Unlike her husband, she believed that the features spiriting way Imnesvale's villagers were recently-arrived ogres who could be found in the Umar Hills. She did not trust the creatures even after it was revealed they meant the village no harm. Johanna through that the adventurers who negotiated with the ogres were to be their next meal.
