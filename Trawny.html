Widow Trawny was an inhabitant of Hultail in Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the late 15 th century DR, Widow Trawny rented out two back rooms in her home on the southern border of Hultail to a young and beautiful herbalist named Sulimra who had relocated from Turmish . From Widow Trawny's home, Sulimra grew herbs and ran her medicine-making business.
