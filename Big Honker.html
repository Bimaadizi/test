Big Honker was a young racing tyrannosaurus rex in Port Nyanzaru in Chult in the late 15 th century DR .

History

As of the start of the death curse , Big Honker was a crowd pleaser and a favorite to win in the dinosaur races . Bookies gave it 7:1 odds of winning. Big Honker was present at the races on the day of the inaugural Na N'buso's Haka .
