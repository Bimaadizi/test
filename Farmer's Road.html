The Farmer's Road was a road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Farmer's Road in northcentral Daggerford left the northern arm of Wall Street at the Farmer's Gate and cut south, then east between the Happy Cow and River Shining Tavern , and south once more to Duke's Way . The Farmer's Road was linked to Daggerford's market by its intersection with Market Way north of the River Shining Tavern. Its segment between Market Way and the Farmer's Gate was sometimes referred to as Field's Lane.
