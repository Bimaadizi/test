Nlaea was an inhabitant of Myth Drannor in the 3 rd century DR .

Abilities

She had a mantle, holding the spells unseen servant , magic missile , locate object , and strength .

History

In 261 DR , she served Alaglossa Tornglara at the time of the raising of the mythal . She reacted badly at first, frightening her mistress, but soon stabilized.
