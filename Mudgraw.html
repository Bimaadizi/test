Mudgraw was a tortle druid and guide who lived in Ahoyhoy in the late 15 th century DR .

History

He had led expeditions to Shilku and the Heart of Ubtao . Two years later, he led an expedition to Omu . This was three years before the death curse .

Mudgraw had only one visit to Omu. He accompanied a group of treasure hunters from the city of Athkatla . They were ambushed by yuan-ti and Mudgraw was forced to abandon the treasure hunters. He hid in the ruins of a shrine to the god Ubtao . In the shrine, he found a holy symbol of Ubtao and since then wore it around his neck believing that Ubtao had assisted him in escaping the city.
