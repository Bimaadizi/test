Gostaraj , also called the Reaver King , was a mercenary lord and a priest of Garagos commanding the House of Steel . He was the King of Westgate from 900 to 927 DR .

Activities

Around age thirty-five, Gostaraj seized the throne of Westgate from the Prince Templars in a bloody rebellion in the Year of the Thirsty Sword, 900 DR . He ruled until his death of sixty-two in the Year of the Red Rain, 927 DR .
