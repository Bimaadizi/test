Koler was a Netherese arcanist who taught at the Inventor's Conservatory in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Koler taught alongside the other seven resident teachers, made up of Arconi , Dolci , Belair , Ballarini , Agassaz , Staufehaus , and Clayton .
