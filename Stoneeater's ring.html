The Stoneeater's ring was a curiously-named enchanted piece of jewelry.

Powers

The wearer of the Stoneeater's ring could cast the stone to flesh spell once per day.

History

The sages who discussed the Stoneeater's ring admitted that the item had history or note, however, they refused to elaborate for one reason or another, claiming that it was "irrelevant and distracting."

As of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , the ring was available for sale at the Grange , a pacifist trader who refused to sell armor and weapons in the city of Elmwood , Moonsea region.
