Merelder Manor was a large mansion located in the Uptown District of Ravens Bluff during the 14 th century DR . It was the Tentowers home of the well-liked Liontower noble family.

Nearly as large as the imposing Boldtalon Manor on Waelstar Way , the expansive mansion was guarded by 20' (6.1 m) tall golden lion statues, who sat upon the estate's fore-facing stone walls. The manor was fully complemented with its own stables and personal carriage house.
