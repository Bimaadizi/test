The Breakneck Resort was a ski resort located in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale in the late 14 th century DR .

History

By the late 14 th century DR, the Breakneck Resort was run by Henry Coldwater . The slopes of the resort were dangerous enough to leave only orcs and only the most proficient skiers unharmed. At one point, a hin smuggler from Luskan was accepted as an apprentice to Henry Coldwater. Jaccob became an apprentice tour guide and was said to have had the potential to be the best tour guide in the Ten Towns, despite the region's lackluster tourism. Jaccob's life ended abruptly as the Breakneck Resort proved its name to the newcomer halfling.
