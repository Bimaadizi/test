Elmira was an elderly resident of Neverwinter who lived in the city's Blacklake District during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

She saw through the rhetoric proclaimed by the "revolutionary group" known as the Nashers , seeing them as little more than another gang that preyed upon the less fortunate.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Elmira saved up what money she could to hire adventurers to deal with the Nashers in her neighborhood. She wanted them to take back the goods that the Nashers stole from her fellow less-fortunate Neverwintan residents.
