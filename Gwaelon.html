Gwaelon was an elven house of Myth Drannor .

History

In the Year of the Hippogriff's Folly, 241 DR , the heir of House Gwaelon alongside half a dozen eleven house heirs, attempted to assassinate young Elminster Aumar and Symrustar Auglamyr , a Chosen of Mystra . The two escaped leaving behind a statuette charged with magic. When one of the young elven lords and warriors stuck the statuettes, a fearsome explosion occurred near the Narnpool . The Gwaelon heir was killed in the explosion.
