The Order of the Silver Lance was a mercenary company based in Ormath in the Shining Plains and active in the Sea of Fallen Stars around 1358 DR . They were a corp of medium cavalry.

Membership

They employed many paladins , fighters , and rangers .

Contracts

The Order took 100 gold pieces per day. They worked only for non- evil patrons and were always honorable and trustworthy.

Leadership

Their leader Thorivald the Grey was a famous paladin.
