Windstone was one of the Palace families who served the Crown of Cormyr in the 15 th century DR .

History

By the late 15 th century DR, Windstones were career officiers for multiple generations. Like other officers and chamberlains, the Windstone family resided at Cormyr's Royal Court south of Castle Obarskyr . Lady Jalessa Windstone took office of the Lady of Graces in the Year of the Third Circle, 1472 DR .
