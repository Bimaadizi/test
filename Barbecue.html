Barbecue was a type of fire-roasted smoky and tangy food consumed in the Northwest Faerûn , North Faerûn , and The Vast .

Description

Spit-roast barbecue was a type of food prepared by the Zhentarim -allied fire giant who inhabited the Flaming Tower outpost in the Dalelands . They cooked animals whole, slowly rotating the carcasses over barbecue pits.

Availability

Pork and chicken barbecue was served at the Painted Boat Restaurant in the city of Ravens Bluff .

Barbecued meat was a common dish in the city of Neverwinter during the annual Lliira's Night and Midsummer festivals.
