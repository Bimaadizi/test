The spellskins of Mhairshaulk were magic items associated with the ancient sarrukh realm of Mhairshaulk .

They were reputed to lie within the Crypt of Anthilar , the legendary secret lair of the lich Anthilar , Archmage of Untisczer , which was hidden in the mountains west of the Tashan Gap in the Tashalar . Hoping to recover the spellskins , many adventurers searched for it in the 14 th century DR, but few, or none, were ever seen again by 1373 DR .
