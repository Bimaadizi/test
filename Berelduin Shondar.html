Activities

By 1358 DR and through 1367 DR , he was the High Watcher of the Helm's Shieldhall temple in Elturel , ruling over twenty-one priests and eighty-five followers.

He also led patrols of the Hellriders , as many as the High Rider Lord Dhelt did.

Personality

Patriarchal and stern, Berelduin ran the temple with an iron hand. He was belligerent and aggressive in defense of the Helmite faith.
