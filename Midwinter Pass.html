Midwinter Pass was a mountain pass found in the Hordelands and east Faerûn .

Geography

Midwinter Pass was found in the southern region of the Giant's Belt Mountains . The pass connected the plains of the Eastern Shaar and the Beastlands with the southwestern corner of Raurin . A short stretch of plains separated the pass from the Aerilpar Forest .

History

Midwinter Pass derived its name from the perpetual cold that permeated the pass regardless of the time of year.
