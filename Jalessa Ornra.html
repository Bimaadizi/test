Jalessa Ornra was the butcher of Red Larch during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Activities

Jalessa managed a shop in the village, where she lived together with her husband, the town's constable Harburk Tuthmarillar .

History

In 1491 DR , after the Believers and their crimes were uncovered, Jalessa was most likely chosen as the new mayor of the city.
