Eravien Haund was a half-elf and a noble member of the Lords' Alliance in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Eravien was of both moon elf and Tethyrian descent, but lived in Waterdeep .

Around 1486 DR , , Eravien was the Lords' Alliance delegate to King Bruenor Battlehammer of Gauntlgrym to discuss the madness spreading in the Underdark , despite not believing personally in the threat. He also knew that a deep-cover agent of the Lords' Alliance in the Underdark, Khelessa Draga , had recently gone silent.

Personality

The half-elf was a charming and sly courtier, able to be immediately size up any social situation. However, he focused too much on socializing, distracting him from important information.
