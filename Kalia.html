Kalia was a high-ranking officer of Muktar in the Turmish in 1358 DR .

Activities

Kalia was a member of the Champions of Muktar . She was also a high priestess of Myrkul . Kalia commanded the Maneuver Brigade of the Muktar's army.

History

In 1358 DR Kalia and his fellow Champions arranged the trap inside Fort Blood planned by the self-styled king of Muktar , Hetep , to eliminate an adventuring party well-known in Golconda . Later she commanded her brigade in the Battle of Wilma Pass .

Personality

Kalia liked showing her face to the sacrificed victims she made to her god.
