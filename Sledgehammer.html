Sledgehammers , or sledges , were heavy, practical hammers commonly used as tools in the Realms .

Description

Sledges were large, heavy, well-balanced hammers.

Usage

Sledgehammers were widely used in construction and masonry.

Sledges were used with wedges to split wood. Cording mallets were a one-piece version of a sledge and wedge that used an axe -like head for chopping wood.

Striker's sledges were used in blacksmithing for hammering metal during heavy forging.

Sledgehammers could be used as melee weapons, but they were too slow and cumbersome to be practical in anything but an emergency. The maul was a martial-focused version used for breaking through heavy armor . Tomb tappers used sledges as their primary tool and weapon, and were strong and large enough to use them effectively.

History

Ondeth Obarskyr 's iconic sledge was placed with his body on his funeral pyre in the Year of Opening Doors, 26 DR . The scorched metal head of that sledgehammer was later kept in storage among other Cormyrean treasures.
