Milosh the Impartial was a merchant who lived in Helm's Hold during the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

He sold a variety of goods useful to adventurers such healing kits that could help mend injuries, or those that helped them better understand magic, religion or the natural world. Milosh even sold items that aided in the art of thievery or helped uncover secrets found within ruined dungeons.

History

During the Siege of Helm's Hold circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Milosh became stuck outside the city's main entrance alongside several other prominent citizens, including Investigator Rannigan and the Helmite priest, Brother Satarin .
