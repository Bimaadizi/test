The Shield of the Order was a unique enchanted shield associated withe the Order of the Radiant Heart .

Powers

The shield held a powerful +5 enchantment and bolstered the inherent mental and physical defenses of its wielder.

History

The shield once belonged to the brave knight of the Radiant Heart, Peragath the Valiant . He carried it during his final battle on Toril , against a horde of tanar'ri that were invading the prime via a portal linked to the astral plane . It was lost for quite some time when Peragath lept through the portal in pursuit of the demonic forces.

By the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the shield came into the possession of the fire giant Yaga-Shura , who carried it with him during the siege of Saradush .
