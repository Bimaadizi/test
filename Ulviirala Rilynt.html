Ulviirala Rilynt was the matron mother of House Rilynt'tar of Ched Nasad .

Description

The priestess of Lolth did not take the full name of her house as her surname, but only part of.

History

In 1370 DR , Ulviirala had a seat on Ched Nasad's ruling council as the eighth matron mother. Two years later, she believed that House Melarn had committed treason. Along with four other matron mothers, she summoned a black guardian spider that destroyed much of Ched Nasad.
