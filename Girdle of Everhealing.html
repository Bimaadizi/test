A girdle of everhealing was an enchanted item that healed hurts.

Powers

Such girdles allowed the person strapped in to endure many harms, including acid and poison , without dying. Presumably, it healed their injuries, or granted regeneration.

History

The Chosen of Mystra Elminster Aumar was regularly tortured with poison and acid while strapped into one such girdle between 761 DR and 764 DR .
