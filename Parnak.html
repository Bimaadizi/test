Parnak was a dragon turtle living in the Golden Gulf in Zakhara 1367 DR .

Activities

Parnak accosted a pair of merchant vessels sailing the Pearl Way and demanded tribute. Insulted by the tribute offered, he decided to destroy both ships and left them on the bottom of the Golden Gulf.

Rumors and Legends

The Reaver was blamed for the two missing ships that Parnak destroyed.
