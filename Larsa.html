Larsa was a skilled wizard inhabiting the city of Messemprar in Unther during the mid–14 th century DR .

Personality

He believed most people were generally good, a trait that often left him blind to the nefarious planning of others.

Activities

Larsa was a member of the Northern Wizards , one of two groups of spellcasters found within Unther. He was close friends with the Northern Wizards' leader– Shurlash .
