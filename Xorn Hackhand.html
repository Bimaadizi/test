Xorn Hackhand was the personal herald of Sthavar , the Lord Magistrate of Suzail , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , he accompanied Sthavar at a royal feast held by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Keep .
