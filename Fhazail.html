Fhazail was a human bandit and steward.

Description

Fhazail was a fat man. He wore several rings of gold around one finger. He also wore yellow and orange silks which clung around his corpulent body.

Activities

He betrayed the White Shield Company to Graal , an orog , who also served as his master. Graal hated him because he would give up too easily and bow before anyone.
