Hammestone Street was one of the two streets that formed the southern border of The Shutters neighborhood in the Crow's End District of Ravens Bluff .

Geography

Hammestone Street began at the Ravens Bluff Sanitation Facility , located in the Pumpside neighborhood, and moved west in a straight line, crossing Helhavryn's Walk , Landleen Lane , Burning Shield Lane , Stonekeep Concourse , and ended on Mraedara's Street where a forested area took over the eastern side of the district.
