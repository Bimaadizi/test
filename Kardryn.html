The Kardryn , which was also called the Swords of Justice , was the city watch of Yeshpek in the Lake of Steam region of south Faerûn .

The word "kardryn" translated as ratcather in an ancient local dialect.

Activities

All members of the Kardryn carried iron stunning bars which were used with brutal efficiency to restore order, typically when fights broke out in taverns. Their uniforms consisted of leather armor dyed purple. All members of the Kardryn received martial training from Yeshpek's standing army–the Yevven Riders .

Organization

The Kardryn utimately served the Khaldomaun of Yeshpek. They numbered 1,200 strong during the late 14 th century DR . They were led by a pair of equal ranking members– Haslet Quarren and Dalanthla Dreist .
