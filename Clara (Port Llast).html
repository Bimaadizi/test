Clara was an inhabitant of Port Llast in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Circa the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Clara worked at the Alliance Arms Inn was a waitress. She was in town then shaming undead creatures started appearing on the streets of Port Llast late at night. However, danger didnt stop locals from drinking, including old Eommar , and Clara needed to make coin .
