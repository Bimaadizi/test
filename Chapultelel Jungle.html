The Chapultelel Jungle was the untamed tropical forest that separated the mainland of Maztica and the Lopango part of the continent.

Description

The Chapultelel Jungle grew on the eastern shores of a deepwater bay, part of Patzcoatl , the Western Ocean and bordering a huge swamp to the east that hugged the Gulf of Qotal . A single large river ended in the bay and ran from the drylands north the jungles. Chapultelel bordered the westernmost part of the great House of Tezca dessert and Mount Xochipas .
