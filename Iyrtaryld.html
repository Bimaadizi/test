Iyrtaryld was a Red Wizard of Thay in the mid-14 th century DR .

Appearance

He had an upthrust spike of a beard on an otherwise bare chin.

Personality

He was ambitious and malevolent.

Relationships

He was part of a cabal together with Thaltar Glaervar , Dlamaerztus , Norlarram , Quaerlesz , Olorus , and six other Red Wizards .

History

In 1369 DR , Iyrtaryld was smoking at a meeting with several of his fellow Red Wizards, where he demonstrated an illusion of his newest magical development, the Hungry Mouth : a spell to snatch anything that wasn't nailed down into a portal, bringing it to Thay with them. His plan was to eat whatever couldn't be coerced to work. While he got some feedback on it, the table at which they sat was hurled upwards by a colossal entity made of stone which smashed its fist down on him, killing him instantly. That was the Simbul 's doing.
