King Sorentah Croam was the second King of the Croam Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Sorentah inherited the throne when his father, King Hygaarth , died in the Year of the Reluctant Hero, 95 DR . He ruled until the Year of the Halfling's Dale, 135 DR .

Relationships

He had three children. His son Dalious would inherit the throne.
