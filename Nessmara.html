Nessmara was a lamia noble who briefly ruled the independent city of Westgate from the Year of the Lamia's Kiss, 615 DR , to the Year of the Ensorceled Kings, 616 DR . She slew the androsphinx King Lyonarth and took the crown.

History

Nessmara discovered the fabled Maguscepter . Her bloodline lived on within the lamia nobles who ruled the ruins of Ilimar .
