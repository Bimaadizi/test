Leechhome was a swamp located in the northern region of the kingdom of Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Leechhome was found between two freshwater lakes–the Lake of the Leech in the north, and Lac Sielya in the south. The swamp was approximately 10 miles (16 kilometers) from the shore of the Sea of Swords . The swamp covered roughly 50 mi² (130 km²).
