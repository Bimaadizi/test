High Hand of Ice Malakhar Rhenta was the high priest of the House of Auril's Breath in Glister in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

Activities

Malakhar's Storm Circle consisted of 14 other senior priestesses. Together they ran the great temple and planned which weather magic they would cast.
