How to Track an Illithid was a hand-written book penned by Golbraith Stredivas , friend and confidant of Duke Abdel Adrian , published sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Content

The book collected numerous methods used to track down the dreaded mind flayers . Each of the methods was well detailed and tested, with each endeavor's outcome painstakingly documented by Golbraith.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the original tome of How to Track an Illithid could be found in the home of Golbraith Stredivas in Baldur's Gate 's Lower City .
