Darlakan was a gofer in the kitchens of Castle Obarskyr in Suzail in the late 14 th century DR .

History

During the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , in preparation for the Council of the Dragon , the Master of Revels Khaladan Mallowfaer sent Emmur and Darlakan to fetch wagons and bring new chairs and stools from Zorsin's Dock . He later sent Halance Tarandar after them, to make sure they didn't break everything.
