Prior Maxtilar Rhebbos was a Tempuran cleric in the 14 th century DR .

History

Maxtilar lived as a member of the Tempuran clergy of the House of Heroes temple in Waterdeep from 1357 DR and through 1368 DR .

In 1363 DR , Maxtilar sponsored a ship alongside the local clergy of Sune , known as Goddess' Glory . This ship was meant to deliver a load of precious statuary to the Temple of Beauty , but it mysteriously sank just south of the city. Unbeknownst to them, the sinking of the Goddess' Glory was caused by Kerritas Reefsilver , a member of the Cult of Ao , and two merfolk . They pilfered the statuary to place within a small temple to Deep Sashelas , situated in an underwater reef just outside of the Deepwater Harbor .
