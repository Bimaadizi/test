Vatilan was a shadow demon of the Abyss that ventured into the Nine Hells in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In 1494 DR , Vatilan was part of a group of demons led by a nalfeshnee called Trantolox who was searching for the Bleeding Citadel . During their journey, Vatilan discovered Crokek'toeck , the lost pet of Yeenoghu , buried inside the Scab . Vatilan remained there to direct five dretchs to liberate Crokek'toeck.
