Potion of endurance were magic concoctions that increased the drinker's stamina.

Powers

Potion of endurance could be created with a variety of different recipes but all of them had the same effect when consumed. The potion enhanced the imbiber's stamina and overall health but it possessed no healing magic.

History

Potion of endurance were widely available for purchase from traders and wizards of the Sword Coast in the 14 th century DR .

Circa 1373 DR , potions of endurance were a part Zhentil Keep garrison's standard equipment and could be found in the satchels of simple guardsmen, cavalry, and skymages. During the same period of time potions of endurance could be found in the wizard stronghold of Telkoun's Tower .
