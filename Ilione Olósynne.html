Ilione Olósynne was the third Mage-Queen of the Olósynne Dynasty who ruled the nation of Aglarond .

Activities

Ilione reigned from the Year of the Broken Blade, 1260 DR , until the Year of the Watching Cold, 1320 DR . She inherited the throne where her brother, Halacar Olósynne , was killed by agents from Thay . She died of plague in 1320 DR .

Relationships

Ilione's mother was Ulae Olósynne .

The Simbul was known to be Ilione's apprentice.
