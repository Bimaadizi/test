The Warders were the city watchmen of Thentia .

Activities

The Warders were responsible for keeping the peace inside Thentia. They normally patrolled the city in groups of 8. They also directly protected the Watchlord's Tower with a garrison of 10 veteran members, and kept at least 20 Warders patrolling the city's docks at any time due to its constant and high levels of activity.

Posessions

The Warders were equipped with leather armor and clubs or short swords .
