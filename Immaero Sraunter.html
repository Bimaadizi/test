Immaero Sraunter was an alchemist in Suzail in Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Around 1479 DR , Manshoon managed to discover his hidden secrets and enslaved Sraunter to do his bidding. He ordered the alchemist to release into the city five death tyrants .

Later, Manshoon magically sent Sraunter inyo the besieged Stormserpent Towers .

In 1480 DR , he was dealing with Lord Danthalus Blacksilver .

Later, Manshoon decided to possess Sraunter and use him as his main body. In Castle Irlingstar , Manshoon was scrying while using the body of Jarlin Flamtarge but at last Rorskryn Mreldrake discovered him and using his new magic power made Manshoon's head explode. However, it was Sraunter who died and Manshoon just transferred himself into the body of Ondrath Everwood .
