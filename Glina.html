Glina was the student of the Neverwinter Academy of Magic .

Biography

Glina was a wizard of The Academy, who entertained herself with gossips. Despite her father, Jochris , being the headmaster of the Academy, she was Qara 's enemy. When Qara burned the stables at the Academy and went off, Hetha and Glina tracked her to Sunken Flagon inn.

Gameplay

When the Kalach-Cha and the companions persuaded the City Watchmen to work with the thieves or stay with the Watch, they would encounter Qara trading insults with Hetha and Glina. The Kalach-Cha could've resolved the matter, causing Hetha and Glina to walk away.
