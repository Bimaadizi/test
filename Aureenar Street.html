Aureenar Street was a minor, corduroy-log paved, curved street in the northern tip of the Sea Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This street was situated south of the Street of Lances and curved parallel to it.

It ran from east to west, going down southwest off of Trollkill Street and intersecting Stormstar's Ride . Aureenar Street then gradually curved northwest, passing through Morningstar Way , Murlpar Street , and the Street of the Singing Dolphin , until finally terminating into Westwall Street .

Along the way, Prayer Alley ran south off of Aureenar Street and a narrow passageway ran north from it into Shank Alley .
