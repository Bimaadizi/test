House Alenuath was a moon elf house on Evermeet .

History

The house was established by the legendary Haramara Alenuath, who reputedly killed the red dragon Skarra with a single arrow shot from her bow.

Membership

Its members were typically rangers , hunters, or others who spent a great deal of time alone, which their sun elf neighbors thought unusual. The house members were often to be found scattered across Evermeet on their adventures.

As of 1368 DR , the house was led by Thalanil Alenuath .

Base of Operations

Although the house had a small manse in Leuthilspar adorned in the family's colors, it was often unoccupied for extended periods. It was cared for by the mostly moon elf servants. As they could more-or-less do as they liked during these times, they thought the Alenuaths to be good employers.

Possessions

Haramara's bow was a non-magical bow that had great significance in the house. It was held by the house's leader.

The house's colors were gray and green.
