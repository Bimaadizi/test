Luthness was a gallery owner of expensive artwork in Innarlith on the Lake of Steam in the mid–14 th century DR .

Possessions

He was in possession of very expensive sculptures.

History

In the Year of the Wave, 1364 DR , he sold a Kozakuran sculpture called " Small Evil Deity Crouches in the Running Stream, Mindful of Its Breathing " to Willem Korvan , who was trying to get it to impress Phyrea , the daughter of Inthelph , his patron. Luthness sold the sculpture for 45,000 gold pieces . The sculpture had been crafted by Akira Tanaka .

Marek Rymüt , a clandestine Red Wizard in Innarlith, was also a customer at his store.
