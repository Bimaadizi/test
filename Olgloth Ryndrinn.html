Olgloth Ryndrinn was a kobold inhabiting the Ice Mountains in northwest Faerûn in the 14 th century DR .

Activities

Olgloth was the leader of the Drinn , a large tribe of kobolds numbering nearly 4,000 strong. He organized attacks on Citadel Adbar , but usually just dealt with any miners who wandered into his territory.
