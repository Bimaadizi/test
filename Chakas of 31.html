The Chakas of 31 were a group of prominent chakas in the Shining Lands in 1367 DR .

Organization

Chakas of this group were usually the most successful merchant organizations within the Shining Lands. Many members of these various chakas were also associated with the Council of Merchants , granting them influence with major decisions effecting the Shining Lands.

Base of Operations

The Chakas of 31 were based out of Heldapan in central Durpar .
