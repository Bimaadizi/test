Doisin Kivna was a gnome member of the Silver Sails Trading Company of Neverwinter in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Doisin could often be seen dressed in navy blue finery. He had slightly crossed eyes and appetite for coin .

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , following the end of the deadly Wailing Death epidemic, Doisin manned the Silver Sails Trading Company's warehouse in the Docks District of Neverwinter . The gnome supervised arriving and departing cargo as well as offered Silver Sails's goods for sale. Doisin was working at the warehouse when sailors from the Midnight Rose were stricken by terrifying visions of all-consuming oceans while loading up an ancient statue excavated in the Anauroch .
