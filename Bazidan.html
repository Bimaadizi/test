Bazidan , also called Bazidan of the Flames was an efreet pasha who lived in Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Activities

Bazidan served in Zakhara as a representative of the Sultan of the Efreet, Marrake al-Sidan al-Hariq ben Lazan . Bazidan controlled his own stronghold, complete with a staff of other efreet and noble efreet as well as a contingent of slaves of various races.
