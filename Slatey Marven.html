Slatey Marven was a commoner from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Slatey was a human woman with a sun-kissed skin tone, big brown eyes, and black hair gathered in tight cornrow braids.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , the half-orcish artist named Splatters Doolug spent his days painting murals on the Old Wall . His art faced criticism from some passersby like Slatey Marven, who thought Doolug took criticism poorly. She believed that artists were too soft and that their egos crumbled with a single negative comment, instead of taking the criticism as feedback to grow and improve.
