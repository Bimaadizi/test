Wariton Minlow was a nobleman from Hlondeth , an independent city-state in the Vilhon Reach , active circa 1020 DR .

History

For a short period of time in the early 11 th century DR , Wariton Minlow was the leader of an influential noble family of Hlondeth and a contender for the city's seat of power. The family's claim to power was cut short in the Year of Smoldering Spells, 1020 DR , following Wariton Minlow's death. The nobleman did not leave a heir, and Hlondeth's throne was claimed by House Extaminos .
