Rhammas Thann was a noble of Waterdeep in the midâ€“14 th century DR . He was the husband of Cassandra Thann and thus the patriarch of the Thann family, although in his later years, he retired from an active role in family affairs.

Personality

He was a hardworking and honorable man who was overshadowed by his wife, Cassandra.

His son, Danilo Thann , remarked several times that Rhammas in particular disapproved of him and his foolishness.

History

In 1315 DR , Rhammas convinced his father, Erktos I , to stop the family's involvement in the slave trade.

Relationships

Rhammas was the eldest son of Erktos I.

He once had an out-of-wedlock daughter named Lilly , who was killed by the Knights of the Shield .
