Fire daggers were a type of common enchanted simple weapons often found in Icewind Dale .

Powers

These simple-looking daggers possessed a +2 offensive enchantment as well as increased the wielder's defense. Each his with this dagger had a change of lighting the target on fire.

History

Circa 1281 DR , several of these magic daggers could be found in Easthaven of the Ten Towns and in the depths of Dragon's Eye on the Spine of the World .
