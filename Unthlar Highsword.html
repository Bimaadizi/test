Unthlar Highsword was a Zhentilar veteran in the mid–14 th century DR .

Abilities

A veteran Zhentilar , he could throw knives quite well.

Possessions

He wielded a "deathfang" dagger , which was extremely poisonous .

History

In the fight at Irondrake Rock between Shandril Shessair and the Zhentarim forces of Gathlarue in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Unthlar Highsword hurled his deathfang at the spellfire wielder after the death of Gathlarue. His throw was parried by Mirt ; moments later, Narm Tamaraith 's strongest spell blew up the top half of his body, slaying him instantly.
