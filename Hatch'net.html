Hatch'net was the master of Lore and a trainer at the Academy within the hall of instruction at the Melee Magthere in Menzoberranzan .

Personality

All drow warriors had to go through fifty days learning the heritage of the drow culture, and Hatch taught hate and distrust, helping to create some of the Underdark's most vicious killers.

History

In his early training in the Melee Magthere, Drizzt Do'Urden was trained by Hatch'net at the Academy, but could do nothing to turn Drizzt away from his morals and values he placed within himself. Hatch'net was still a master of the Academy in 1479
