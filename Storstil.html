Storstil was a caravan drover in the Sword Coast during the mid-14 th century DR .

Personality

Storstil was known to grimace at Narbuth 's ramblings. He was sour a sour man.

Description

Storstil often wore a distinctive tunic, dun in color with red trim. The man was slim.

Possessions

He owned a pipe and smokeweed.

History

Storstil worked as a caravan drover in Orthil Voldovan 's caravan. He was part of the caravan when they headed from Scornubel to Waterdeep in late 1357 DR , and were joined by Shandril Shessair .

While relieving himself and trying to get a smoke in, Storstil was slain by an unknown assailant who reduced his head to ash with an intense flame.

Rumors & Legends

Thorst , another caravan guard, was inclined to believe Shandril was his slayer. Narbuth , yet another fellow, thought Narm Tamaraith was instead.
