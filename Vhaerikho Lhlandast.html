Vhaerikho Lhlandast was a bootmaker from the town of Hultail in Cormyr , active in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Vhaerikho was a dandy halfling who possessed the skill to make deadly-accurate imitations of other prestigious shoemakers' work, aided by a group of gnomes from his workshop.

History

By the late 15 th century DR, Vhaerikho Lhlandast had fled his home in Saerloon , a city in Sembia , accompanied by his gnomish crew. They allegedly fled from a scandal or some other trouble, but none were willing to discuss their mysterious past.
