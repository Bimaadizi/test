Lansiblin was a street seller in Hastarl during the early 3 rd century DR .

Activities

He sold pies, normally squirrel-and-green pies , from very early in the morning; there was a corner where he normally was.

Relationships

Two repeat customers of his' were the thieves Farl and Eladar the Dark; the latter was secretly Prince Elminster Aumar of Athalantar .

History

Lansibilin sold pies to Farl and Eladar on the morning after they put together the Velvet Hands thieves' guild. Elminster thought there was a little less rat than usual on his'.
