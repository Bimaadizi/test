The Street of Smiths was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Street of Smiths was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . The road ran east to west, connecting the much larger Way of the Dragon with the High Road .

Notable Locations

Several small unnamed alleys branched off of the southern side of the Street of Smiths.
