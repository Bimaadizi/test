Fastidious Fungi was a book penned by J. L. Corrins and published sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Content

Fastidious Fungi included some information on hags , presumably the pearlspore bell mushrooms that could be used to return a slain hag to the land of the living. However, that lore was thought to be pure speculation by some.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a copy of Fastidious Fungi was studied by Mayrina after forming the Anti-Hag Support Group , in search of ways to stop hags' sinister and malicious plots.
