The Pole was a prison located in the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Pole was located in the eastern section of Almorel adjacent to the Dwarven Mir .

Activities

In addition to housing Almorel's convicted criminals who weren't executed at the Hanging Place , the Pole served a ground where duels were settled.
