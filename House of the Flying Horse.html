The House of the Flying Horse was an inn that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The inn was located on Roguerun Alley in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep.

History

The inn was used by smugglers to fence stolen property. Sometime before the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , a spell duel occurred here between rival wizards , destroying the House of the Flying Horse in the process.
