Justina Sittas was a human rogue and Waterdhavian tax collector in the mid 14 th century DR .

Description

She was a beautiful woman clad in elegant clothes as behooved to her station.

Possessions

She owned a bag of holding that could hold up to 1,500 lb (680 kg).

Activities

Justina walked the piers of Waterdeep every day, collecting the harbor dues from the ships anchored there. Those who did not pay and would not set sail, would have 2-8 constables come to give them an incentive to pay or leave.
