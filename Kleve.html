Kleve was a death slaad in the Underdark in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Circa 1486 DR , Kleve acted as the loyal bodyguard of archmage Vizeran De Vir , thanks to a control gem the drow possessed. He was always at his master's side and obeyed all his orders.
