Ander was a commoner who resided in the village of Imnesvale in the Umar Hills of Amn in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Ander was a human man with long hair and light skin who wore olive-colored commoner's garb.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the village of Imnesvale faced a slew of disappearances and locals did not hesitate to blame the legendary Umar Witch . Ander, along with his wife Johanna , spent much of his free time in the Umar Inn but avoided interacting with strangers out of fear.
