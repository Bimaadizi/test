The Mage's Lover was a book penned by Níroth Donbor sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

History

A copy of The Mage's Lover belonged to Manshoon XII , a clone of Manshoon , in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR . The book was stored in the mage 's library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep .
