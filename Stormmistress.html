Stormmistress was the High Priestess of Umberlee in the Foreign Quarter of Zhentil Keep in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Activities

Stormmistress was in charge of a newly erected temple called the Jagged Rocks . She was assisted by six other priestesses and helpers.
