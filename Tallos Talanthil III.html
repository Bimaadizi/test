Dhalarth Talanthil was an Arkhan of the Talanthil Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Dhalarth inherited the throne when his older brother, Mornarth II , died in −215 DR . His name was changed to Tallos III upon becoming the Arkhan. He reigned until −199 DR .

Relationships

Tallos III's son and sole heir was Mornarth Talanthil III .
