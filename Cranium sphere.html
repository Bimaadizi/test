The cranium sphere was a magical necklace found in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The pendant of the long necklace was a tiny brain encased in an orb.

Powers

The cranium sphere broadly fortified and bolstered the bearer physically, and allowed them to cast nightshield on themselves three times per day.

History

The strange necklace could have been found in Darstil's Tower in Wheloon Prison , after the mad wizard Darstil Vurusk was slain by the Riverside Rats .
