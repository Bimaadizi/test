Belween was the second bastard son of Berellip Xorlarrin .

History

In 1484 DR , in preparation for the drow war against the Silver Marches , Belween, under the orders of Tiago Baenre and Ravel Xorlarrin , disguised himself as the orc prince Lorgru , who had been kidnapped previously. So disguised, Belween killed the king of Many-Arrows , Obould XVII .
