Shoor Street was a hard-packed dirt and gravel paved road in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This road curved northwest out of Drover's Street , crossed through the High Road , and terminated on the northeastern end of Beacon Street . Along the way, it acted as the northern terminus of Blackmul Street .
