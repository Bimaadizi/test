Ogruhl was a dragon tortoise that lived in the Anauroch desert for thousands of years, dating back to the times of ancient Netheril .

Relationships

During his time in the desert, Ogruhl developed a trusting and cooperative bond with a band of desert chwingas . The little elemental spirits would bring Ogruhl small parcels of food and water over time, and the great tortoise provided them with safety and shelter within the little caves in his massive shell.

History

Centuries before the Era of Upheaval , Ogruhl was imprisoned by a particularly callous Netherese mage and magically bound to one of the empire's terrestrial cities. Once the city was abandoned by the Netherese , Ogruhl was left to languish along in the desert.

Eventually, Ogruhl was discovered by the group of chwingas with whom formed a mutually beneficial relationship. Unfortunately it remained bound to the ruins of its former home city, even as of the late 15 th century DR .
