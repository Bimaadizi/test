Shessra , also known as Death's Whisper , was a warlock obsessed with uncovering the secrets of ancient Netheril .

Description

The half-elven woman only spoke in soft, hissing whispers.

Abilities

She was known for using magic to warp and manipulate the minds of other people, or drawing out secret lore from the long-since-deceased.
