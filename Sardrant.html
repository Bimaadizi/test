The sardrant was an animal found in warm climates in Calimshan and Tashalar .

Description

The sardrant appeared much like an armadillo , with natural plating covering much of its body. It was shaggy and slow moving.

Ecology

The sardrant was an herbivore. It was edible by humanoids .
