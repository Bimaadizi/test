Kiwis were uncommon exotic fruit exported from Snowdown 's North Coast.

Description

Famous for its exquisite taste, kiwis had thin brown skin covered with unusual fuzz.

Availability

Kiwis originated from Snowdown and were considered a rare delicacy outside the Moonshae Isles . These fruits were imported from their native region and could be found in some locations and establishments in the Western Heartlands .
