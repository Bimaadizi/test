TypeMagical stone

A wild magic stone was a rock with magical properties which presumably related to wild magic .

Description

One wild magic stone was a small, sparkling pebble.

Powers

A stone employed in Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR brought down an antimagic field . It had a further radius of effect; the mage who employed it made sure to remain outside its radius.
