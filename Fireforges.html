Fireforges was a realm of industrious dwarves in east Faerûn , home to the Arforge , Hulhammer , and Tamstone clans.

Geography

The realm lay in the roots of Mount Thulbane , extending beneath parts of Threskel and Chessenta . The region was highly volcanic, with warm and humid caverns, magma rivers, and dangerous gas pockets.

Trade

As of the 15 th century DR , the clans of Fireforges mined widely and aggressively, coming into conflict over mining claims with Khaelud to the south. The clans took advantage of the volcanism to power their smelting and forging, producing great amounts of metalwork that they exported.
