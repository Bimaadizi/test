The Ba'etith was an organization founded by the sarrukh in −34,000 DR .

Organization

The purpose of the Ba'etith was to study the primitive forms of magic utilized by the various races. They were responsible for the creation of the Assikhath , as well as that of the Nether Scrolls .

Members

The Ba'etith comprised sarrukh, batrachi , and aearee .
