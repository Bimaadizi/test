Fulta was a halfling criminal in Phlan in 1489 DR .

Activities

Fulta was a freelance criminal in Phlan with a group of her fellows acting as her guards.

History

In 1489 DR, Fulta and her guards were hired by Soares to help silence an adventuring party hired to recover some stolen books but they were defeated and killed.
