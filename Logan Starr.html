Logan Starr was a human rogue and one of the senior constables in Ravens Bluff sometime in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He was a hardworking man, and worked tirelessly pursuing truth and justice in the great metropolitan city.

Possessions

On duty he would wear a set of elven chainmail , and carry an enchanted longsword +2 , alongside at least one potion of healing .

Activities

Logan was as one point the foremost investigator in Ravens Bluff, having solved numerous cases. Highly respected amongst his fellow members of the city guard, he was very much less so amongst the city nobility.
