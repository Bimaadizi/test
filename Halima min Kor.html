Halima min Kor was a human ethoist within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Activities

As the High Priest of the Palace, Halima led the religious services of the entire palace. Many of the palace servants, who did not have time to venture to the Golden Mosque , worshiped here. She held the position for 40 years as of 1367 DR.

In addition to her religious duties, Halima commanded the security forces of the Vault of the Word of the Loregiver during emergencies.
