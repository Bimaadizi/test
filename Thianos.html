Thianos , nicknamed "Ironskull" , was a male dwarf cleric of Moradin who lived in the town of Llorkh in the Savage Frontier in the late 1350s DR .

Following his takeover of Llorkh in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Lord Geildarr Ithym named Thianos an outlaw and ordered the Lord's Men to find him.

He was considered aged at the time.
