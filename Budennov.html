Budennov was a small town located along the shores of the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Geography

Budennov was found along the southern shore of the Lake of Mists northwest of Strelsi . The Clearflow River and Dead Dwarf Bridge were located due west of Budennov. It was located approximately 35 miles (56 kilometers) north of the Golden Way .
