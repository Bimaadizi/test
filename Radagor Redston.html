Radagor Redston was a dwarven merchant who traded with Hal Stoutheart 's Tethyamar expedition during the 1480s DR .

Description

Radagor had a neat white beard and moustache, and had shaved the sides of his head.

History

Radagor joined the Tethyamar Mining Outpost not as a miner, but as a general goods trader. He provided food, tools, and other supplies to the more adventurous members of the expedition, and could make a tidy profit without risking himself.
