Harajin was a fallen angel in the Labyrinth in the Northdark in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The gods condemned Harajin and seven other fallen angels to remain as stone statues in the depths of the Labyrinth for unknown crimes.

Powers

Harajin was constantly screaming in horror, afflicting people in the vicinity.
