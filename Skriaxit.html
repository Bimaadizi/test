Skriaxits , also known as blackstorms and living sandstorms , were malevolent air elementals . They were retributive spirits summoned from the Elemental Plane of Air by ancient gods .

Skriaxits could whip up powerful sandstorms that can ripped the flesh from their victims within minutes.
