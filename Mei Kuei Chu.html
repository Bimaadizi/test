Activities

Mei Kuei was set on a quest to preserve her clan's honor: three generations previously, a human burned a sacred bamboo grove, killing her aunt, and her uncle killed the man in retribution. The local magistrate condemned all the Chu clan, and they ran away. Mei Kuei worked to prove her aunt was actually murdered and show the charges against her uncle were unjust.

Personality

Mei Kuei was focused on her mission.
