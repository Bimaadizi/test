The seal of tranquility was a unique enchanted disk crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Joppa in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

Silverhand crafted the seal to represent Joppa's monkish discipline.

Powers

The disk was light enough to float, and could neutralize rough waters.

In combat, it had a similarly calming effect on the spirits of Joppa's enemies, making them much more likely to make mistakes.
