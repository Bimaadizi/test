Janthasarde Ilbright was a liaison officer of Silverymoon in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Appearance

She was short and quite busty.

Personality

The woman's enthusiasm could be described as "perky".

Activities

As a liaison officer, she coordinated between the Silverymoon administrators and the palace guests.

History

In 1369 DR , Janthasarde Ilbright, acting in her role as a liaison officer, was the second-to-last person to meet with Garthin Muirtree on the day of his murder, reviewing their upcoming meetings. Before that point, she'd met with him many times.
