Narlgathra was a venerable red dragon . On Tarsakh 5 1373 DR , Narlgathra appeared from the ruins of Myth Drannor and attacked Tangled Trees , killing most of its inhabitants.

Activities

Narlgathra laired in the abandoned temple to Aumarathar the Greater Dragon. Inside existed a gate to the Thunder Peaks
