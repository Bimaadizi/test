Broland Osstra was a young farmer that lived in Nightstone during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

He was known as an amiable and kindhearted individual.

Relationships

Thelbin lived with grandmother, his mother, his uncle Brynn , and Brynn's husband Thelbin .

History

The young man escaped disaster when cloud giants assailed Nightstone by dropping boulders from their cloud palace some time in the later 1480s DR . While his family's home was partially damaged, Thelbin and his uncles were fortunately unharmed.

To escape the falling rocks, many of Nightstone's townspeople fled their homes and took refuge in a nearby network of caves. Broland's mother and grandmother were tragically killed when the refugees encountered a band of goblins within the caves.
