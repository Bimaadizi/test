Agrathan Hardhammer was a dwarf cleric of Dumathoin and a member of the Council of Sparkling Stones of Mirabar in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He had soft features for a dwarf, and a long white beard. As the highest-ranking dwarf in Mirabar among the humans , he was also the voice of the dwarven population not only to the council, but to the Marchion as well. He often wore robes. He worked in the mines some time before 1270 DR before taking up the priesthood.

History

Agrathan once devised a plan against Mithral Hall in which inferior charcoal would be swapped into their stores to hamper their industry.

By 1370 DR , he was apparently the only dwarf on the council. During the rioting in the Undercity of Mirabar over the imprisonment of Torgar Hammerstriker , Agrathan revealed to Shingles Mc Ruff that he had known about the imprisonment and had done nothing, prompting Shingles to assault him, beating him to the ground.

Agrathan played a large role in Mirabar's time of unrest between its dwarven and human populations from the very beginning. The majority of his time was spent trying to act as a voice of reason between the strongest proponents of the conflict: Torgar Hammerstriker and Marchion Elastul Raurym . He also faced great inner turmoil during the conflict, having to come to terms with and decide whether his loyalty was to his race or his city.
