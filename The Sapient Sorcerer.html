The Sapient Sorcerer was a magic shop in the city of Ravens Bluff that was owned and operated by Olcrannon Fossa in the 14 th century DR .

Location

This shop was located in the neighborhood of Tlasbras in the Southside District of Ravens Bluff. It was on southwestern corner of the intersection of Stonekeep End and the southern edge of Whisper Lane .

Description

Structure

This shop was a one story building. It looked to be rather shabby, made in the rubble masonry style.

Interior

The interior of the store was rather musky and had many cobwebs. Behind the counter was a sleeping cot that the owner rested on.

Atmosphere

The shop had a gloomy atmosphere.

Services

The Sapient Sorcerer sold a variety of supplies for mages . Including inks , quills , vellum , inkstones , different types of scrollcases , scrolls containing minor spells like comprehend languages or neutralize poison , pebbles enchanted with continual light , healing potions , crucibles, and general material components .
