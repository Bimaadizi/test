Tuathal Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles at the end of the 5 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High King Tristram . His successor was his son, Carrig Cymrych III .

History

Tuathal assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Soaring Galleon, 483 DR , after the death of his father.

He ruled for sixteen years before he was assassinated in the Year of the Wager, 499 DR .
