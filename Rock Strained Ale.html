Rock Strained Ale was a variety of ale created by the dwarvish alesmith of Ravens Bluff named Bowser in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Rock Strained Ale was a great-tasting libation with high alcohol content. An important part of its creation was being "washed" through the rocks of Bowser's homeland. It was down during the ale's fermentation process. This often left Rock Strained Ale contaminated with pebbles. Because of that, it was recommended to never chug this drink.
