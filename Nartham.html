Nartham was a member of the Cult of the Black Earth at the Temple of the Black Earth in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Personality

Nartham was a violent and short-tempered fellow, always looking for an excuse to kill.

History

In 1491 DR , Nartham, his bulette mount, and four hobgoblins patrolled the main gate of the temple. However, some adventurers infiltrated the temple and confronted and defeated Nartham and his minions.
