Hunting shortbows were a variety of simple enchanted bows that were used against monstrosities .

Description

Hunting shortbows were strong and thick ash - wood weapons.

Powers

These bows were imbued with a ranger 's magic that felt like a ghost 's wisp in the shooter's hands. The wielder of this shortbow could place a hunter's mark on a creature once per day. Additionally, the weapon made it easier to shoot a monstrosity, and it possessed a simple +1 magic of guidance and damage boost.
