Patatak was a small village in the Alpuk region, in the Novularond , within the Great Glacier .

Geography

Patatak was located in the southern half of the Novularond.

Inhabitants

The residents of Patatak were languorous and lethargic. Their daily routine was little more than bathing in the sun and sleeping. Instead of hunting, they raised rabbits for their meat.
