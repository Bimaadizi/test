Anukiru was a famed swordsmith from Kozakura , who lived many years before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Anukiru made a name for himself by being a master weapon maker. His fame stretched across the entire Kozakura. The swordsmith's fame earned him a sword commission from the Shogun of the time, who wanted a grand sword that would symbolize his reign. Infuriated, the Shogun beheaded Anukiru with Steel Blossom . However, the Shogun's life was soon violently cut short, while Anukiru's name lived on, still remembered in the late 14 th century DR, thanks to Steel Blossom .
