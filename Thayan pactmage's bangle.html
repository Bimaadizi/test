The Thayan pactmage's bangle was a magical bracelet found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The pactmage's bangle greatly empowered the abilities of a warlock and their pact, while also magnifying spells that used physical and force-based damage.

History

The bangle was a powerful item used by professional Thayvan warlocks, brought to Myth Drannor by Droic Orgul 's expedition. It may have been claimed by the Elves of Cormanthor during the Elven Crusade .
