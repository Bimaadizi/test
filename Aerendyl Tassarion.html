Aerendyl Tassarion was an inhabitant of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

Aerendyl took part in the hunt for Elminster in 241 DR , where he was killed. His father, Lord Tassarion, named him as a reason for his conspiracy against Coronal Eltargrim .
