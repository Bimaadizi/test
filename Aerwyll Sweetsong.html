Aerwyll Sweetsong was an elven bard in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Aerwyll had long black hair pinned back and light skin. He wore a fine outfit decorated with sun imagery and carried a lute .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Aerwyll joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their base camp at the ruins of Myth Drannor . He served as a trainer for prospective bards at the camp.
