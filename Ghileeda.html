Ghileeda was a maid in the Swinging Sword tavern in Red Larch and secretly a member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Activities

Ghileeda worked at the Swinging Sword tavern. She also spied on citizens, under the orders of her fellow cultist Justran Daehl .

History

In 1491 DR , Ghileeda noticed some adventurers hired to find a diplomatic caravan missing near the city. They probably uncovered her secret.
