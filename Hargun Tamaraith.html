Hargun Tamaraith was the father of Narm Tamaraith .

Abilities

His son, Narm Tamaraith , believed he was a ranger until he came down with the shaking fever .

Activities

Hargun dealt in smithwork and weaponsmithing.

Relationships

Hargun married Fythuera , who traded as his equal. They had one child, Narm.

History

As of 1335 DR , 22 years prior to 1357 DR , Hargun and Fythuera's child, Narm, was born. They had been married for some time prior. While Narm was still a baby, they traveled to Triboar and then to Waterdeep , up and down the Sword Coast in search of commerce opportunities. In 1346 DR , 11 years prior to 1357 DR, they were slain in Baldur's Gate while in the crossfire of a duel between Kluennh Tzarr and Algarzel Halfcloak . Hargun's last act was to hurl Narm into the river, so that he would survive the fireball.

Legacy

His son Narm sought to become a master of the Art in order to avenge his parents.

Rumors & Legends

Per Elminster , Hargun and his wife may have been Harpers .
