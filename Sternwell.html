Sternwell was the uncle of Kroyce Tefoco , the spouse of Oliver Tefoco , the proprietor of the Beehive Goods and Wares general store in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute threatened Baldur's Gate's safety, Kroyce Tefoco took the youngest kids, Hemjax , Maggie , and others to the town of Beregost , to live with Uncle Sternwell, away from the threat of war. Sternwell cleaned up his shearing shed for the family lo live in.
