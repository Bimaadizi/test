Sewyrm was the name for a type of lizard that dwelt in large numbers in Waterdeep 's sewers.

Description

Sewyrms were a mottled "lettuce green" color. They were about the size of a mastiff dog .

Combat

The tail lash of a sewyrm was worse than its bite.
