An ankylosaurus ( pronounced : / æ ŋk i l oʊ ˈ s ɑːr ʌ s / ænk-ee-lo-SAR-us listen ), sometimes called a macetail behemoth or a rock-skin —by the inhabitants of the Malatran Plateau —was a type of dinosaur .

Description

Ankylosaurs were quadruped herbivores. Their bodies were covered in a thick plate armor.

Combat

If threatened, ankylosaurs used their knobbed tails to defend themselves against predators. Some varieties had spiked tails instead.

Ecology

Habitats

Beyond the Prime Material plane , some could be found in the Forbidden Plateau .
