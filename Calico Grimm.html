Calico Grimm was a sea captain in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Calico was the captain of the swift ship Joen's Heirloom with Bonnie Charlee as first mate.

History

In 1488 DR , the Joen's Heirloom was designated as the flagship of the Luskan navy under Bregan D'aerthe in the fight against the fleet of Brevindon Margaster .

Calico discussed with Bonnie, Kimmuriel Oblodra , and Wulfgar how to better fight the Margaster fleet.

During the battle the Heirloom was sunk and Calico was presumed dead.

For truth he was sliched in half by Asbeel .
