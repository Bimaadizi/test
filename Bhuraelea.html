Bhuraelea was an elven sorceress of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

Bhuraelea was selected to protect Mythanthor by the Srinshee in 241 DR after the death of the human armathor , Elminster Aumar . While guarding his tower, she was ambushed and felled by a number of elven mages bent on slaying her charge. Her body went missing during the fight; Elminster, then a ghost , hoped it was because the Srinshee had whisked her away for healing.
