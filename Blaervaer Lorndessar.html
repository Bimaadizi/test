Blaervaer Lorndessar , who was also called the Reluctant , was the fifth King of the Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Blaervaer inherited the throne after King Roadaeron 's brief reign after his return from exile in the Year of the Prancing Centaur, 1162 DR . Blaervaer ruled until the Year of the Bone Helm, 1191 DR , and died in the Year of the Midday Mists, 1195 DR .

Relationships

He had two sons, Roadaeron II and Maeraedryn , both of whom would hold the crown during their lives.
