Rags Deelarma was a beggear and a refugee from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Deelarma was a human woman with a light skin tone and blue eyes. She kept her brown hair cut short.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in the Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate , displacing countless refugees in the process along the way. Rags Deelarma was one of the refugees forced from her home. She found shelter in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate at Nortale's Hostel . To survive, she begged for coin in front of the Counting House . One of the bank's wealthy clients, Vrindula Mardassus , liked to flaunt her money in front of Deelarma, counting coin but not showing sympathy to the beggars.
