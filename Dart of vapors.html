Dart of vapors was a type of dart used by the drow .

Powers

When a target was struck with this dart, it conjured a cube-shaped cloud, 20 feet (6.1 meters) per side, centered on its impact point, which could be any target, not necessarily a living creature. The effects of this cloud mirrored those of the spell stinking cloud in all respects.
