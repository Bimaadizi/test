Tamar Harpell was a young apprentice wizard inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Tamar was a student of Blackstaff Academy within Blackstaff Tower in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . Originally raised in Longsaddle with his family, the Harpells , Tamer was sent to Waterdeep to study magic .
