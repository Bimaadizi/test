The Storm's Front Tavern was a tavern in Waterdeep's Sea Ward .

Structure

The tavern was decorated with a nautical theme so it looked like a faux Dock Ward tavern.

History

In 1480 DR , the Storm's Front Tavern was burned to the ground during a confrontation between the deva Jinnaoth Ir'Gadohn and the dark angel Sathariel .
