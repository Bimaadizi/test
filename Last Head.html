The Last Head was a cape found on the island of Moray within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The cape was located at the southern tip of Moray, jutting south from the Orcskull Mountains for 10 miles (16 kilometers) into the Trackless Sea where the Strait of the Leviathan began. The Ossian Reefs curved around the waters south of Last Head, and the sea and fog concealed treacherous rocks.

The peninsula itself was covered in foreboding woodland and was dominated by a wide hill at its center, with a small clearing at its top.

Notable Locations

History

In the Year of Deep Water Drifting, 1480 DR , a warband of the Black Blood tribe led by Bloodeye seized the lighthouse and then moved to attack Langmuir.
