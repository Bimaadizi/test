Peris Whallnsor was a female human and a Battleguard of Tempus with the title of Battlelady.

With Trusted Sword Jak Errins soon to retire, she was contending to take over the leadership of the Towers of the Vengeful Hand in Eshpurta , Amn , in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR through at least early 1370 DR, the Year of the Tankard . Her chief competitor for the position was Lance of the Lord Niskam Tresdaap .
