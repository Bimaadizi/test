Phiraz of the Naturalists was a wizard and a sage who lived in Scornubel .

Activities

Phiraz was an expert on otyughs . He would often purchase live monsters or relatively intact carcasses.

History

At some point in the 14 th century DR , Phiraz wrote up a report regarding Otyughs for the city of Scornubel's Commissioner of Public Sewers. Later, around the late 1360's DR, Phiraz was conducting a study of life on the High Moor .
