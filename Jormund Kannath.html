Jormund Kannath was a dwarven resident of Eveningstar during the 1480s DR .

Description

Jormund had blonde hair and a large forked and braided beard.

History

When Arman Argentine arrived in Eveningstar to take recruits for the Tethyamar mining outpost , Jormund took note but wasn't particularly interested for himself.
