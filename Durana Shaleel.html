Durana Shaleel was a member of the Council of Seven Burghers in 1368 DR .

Activities

A seafarer in her youth, Durana was a successful importer and exporter. Often she went by the wharves to speak with fishermen and sailors. She ran the Harrowcoaster Coster and associated warehouse .
