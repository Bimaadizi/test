Shri Neerune Hlaund was the matron mother of House Hlaund of Ched Nasad .

Description

Shri Neerune was a competent priestess of Lolth , but was the weakest on the council.

History

In 1370 DR , Shri Neerune had a seat on Ched Nasad's ruling council as the twelfth and final matron mother. She conspired with other matron mothers of the city in 1372 DR and summoned a monstrous gargantuan spider , which ended up destroying the city.
