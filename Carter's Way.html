Carter's Way was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Carter's Way was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . The road originated on the Specterwalk where it branched south cross Coach Street and eventually end at a deadend in a neighborhood south of Coachlamp Lane .
