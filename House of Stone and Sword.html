The House of Stone and Sword was a metalsmith shop located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 th century DR before that city's destruction.

Location

The House of Stone and Sword was located in north Zhentil Keep within the Common Quarter . The shop was located in between Wizards' Way and Fortress Street .
