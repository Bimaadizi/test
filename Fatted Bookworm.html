The Fatted Bookworm was a business in the Lower Trade Lanes of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR .

Structure

The Fatted Bookworm was a two-story building in deteriorating condition.

Services

The Fatted Bookworm served as a unique purveyor of rare books , scrolls , and high-quality writing materials within Skullport. The shop was renowned for its collection of tomes, parchment , vellum , and expertly bound volumes, all offered at premium prices.

Inhabitants

The Fatted Bookworm was owned by Velara Lillianyth , a human mage . Velara’s reputation ensured steady business, regardless of the presence of her occasional shop clerk, Amryyr Yauntyrr . Amryyr, a drow wizard and thief , utilized the shop as a base when in Skullport, dealing in rare texts, trading valuable information, and facilitating discreet item transfers between Skullport and other parts of the Underdark . Velara, who outwardly expressed little interest in Amryyr’s activities, allowed him to operate within her shop due to an obligation to his associate, Malakuth Tabuirr , a drow slaver . When Amryyr was present, the Fatted Bookworm occasionally handled highly coveted items, including rare magical texts, arcane scrolls, and, on rare occasions, spellbooks .
