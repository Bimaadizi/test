Madruen Morgaunog was a well-known spellscarred .

History

Between 1385 DR and 1396 DR , he fell accidentally into the Plaguewrought Land ; he walked back out a day later, having gained ironlike skin that was impenetrable to almost any weapon, even ballista bolts.

Rumors & Legends

As his story spread, Ormpetarr became a popular destination for spellscar pilgrims.
