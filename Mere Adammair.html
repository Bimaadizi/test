The Mere Adammair was a small marshland located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The mere was located in southwestern Gwynneth between the Dwyvach Salt Flats and the western end of the Llyrath Forest . Kimball Moor bordered the Mere Adammair to the north.
