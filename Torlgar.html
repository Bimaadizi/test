Torlgar was the warden of the Sanatorium in Helm's Hold during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Like many in Helm's Hold, the imposing goliath was afflicted with a spellscar . After becoming involved with the cults that arose in Helm's Hold, Torlgar allowed himself to be transformed into a foulspawn , a process that turned parts of his flesh entirely inside-out.

Activities

Torlgar's original duties included watching over and protecting the Helmite priests as they administered healing to the potentially harmful patients of the Sanatorium. Over time he started to serve as Rohini's personal bodyguard as well.

Relationships

He was wholly devoted to the Prophet Rohini, to the extent that he allowed himself to be experimented upon, eventually undergoing the process of being transformed into a foulspawn berserker . Unlike several others who sacrificed of themself for the cult leader, Torlgar never surrendered his soul to Rohini.

History

Torlgar was ultimately slain by the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge during the Siege of Helm's Hold , circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .
