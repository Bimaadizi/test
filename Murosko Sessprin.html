Murosko Sessprin was a pirate who sailed the seas of Faerûn during the late 15 th century DR . He was the original captain of the Uncivil Serpent , before it experienced a change in leadership.

History

Around the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , Captain Sessprin died when his first mate, "Dead-Eye" Cadavrus led a violent mutiny aboard the Serpent .
