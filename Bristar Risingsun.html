Bristar Risingsun was a half-elf wizard inhabiting the city of Ammathtar in northeast Dambrath during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Bristar was the consort of the Duchess of the North, Tralia Falrith , following the demise of her previous consort, Skield . He was not a native of Dambrath.
