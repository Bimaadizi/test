Dilthari was one of the Empyrean Knights stationed in Stardeep . She accompanied the Keeper of the Cerulean Sign Telarian in 1375 DR during the outriding into the Underdungeon of Stardeep, looking for a different passage into Sildëyuir ; while investigating a skeleton on a ruby throne atop a pile of petrified bodies, she was attacked by the suddenly animating relic lich . She died instantly, and her body fossilized before it hit the ground.
