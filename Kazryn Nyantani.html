Kazryn Nyantani was a senior member of the Avowed and one of the Great Readers of Candlekeep , as of the late 15 th century DR .

Great Reader Nyantani was considered a master if two fields of study that were well-separated from one another. They possessed a vast knowledge about the natural world of Toril , and was an expert at navigation by use of the celestial bodies .
