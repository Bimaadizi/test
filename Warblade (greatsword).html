The Warblade was an enchanted greatsword that originated from the barbarian tribes of the North .

Description

The sword was crafted during a raging battle. After it was forged, the blade was dipped in the blood of courageous fallen warriors.

Powers

The Warblade possessed a +4 enchantment that was said to have bestowed by barbarian gods themselves.

History

As of the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the sword was in the possession of Ribald Barterman , a merchant in Athkatla .
