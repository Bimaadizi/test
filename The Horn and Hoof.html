The Horn and Hoof was a noisy tavern in Longsaddle in the Savage Frontier in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Services

It had a great assortment of drinks served at incredibly high prices.

Inhabitants

It is run by an retired warrior named Malavos Drunn , who keeps order with the threat of force.

Atmosphere

Violence is still common however, with the dead or beaten bodies simply being tossed outside.

History

The reason the tavern has survived for as long as it has is most likely due to its lack of competition.
