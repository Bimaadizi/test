Almaestaddamir Auldcastle , commonly called Almaes , was an alchemist who lived in Ashabenford . He was known for crafting and selling such items as tanglefoot bags , smokepowder , sunrods , as well as various acids and antitoxins.

History

Almaes once offered to build a smokepowder-keg -throwing device to combat the drow for Haresk Malorn , but he politely (and likely wisely) declined the offer.
