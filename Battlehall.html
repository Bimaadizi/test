Battlehall was a shrine devoted to Tempus , the god of war, located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the midâ€“14 th century DR before that city's destruction.

Location

The shrine was located in Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter in the section of the city located on the south bank of the River Tesh . Battlehall sat upon Zhent Avenue near the center of the Foreign Quarter.

Description

The building resembled a small fortress. Spears were wedged onto the uppermost battlements, each sporting a humanoid invader's head upoint the point.

Activities

Given his worshipers enjoyment of battle, many of Tempus' followers within Zhentil Keep were members of the Zhentilar and made their way to Battlehall. These were usually lower ranking soldiers since the Zhentilar rarely promoted Tempus' faithful given their lust for battle and sometimes wreckless behavior purely for the thrill of combat.

Inhabitants

Following the majority of Zhentil Keep's destruction in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Battlehall's high priest, or Battlemaster, was Bladreth Crackbone . Bladreth was assisted by eight other priests .
