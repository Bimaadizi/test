Maernos Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Maernos .

Location

The villa was located in the west section of Waterdeep 's North Ward at the intersection of Delzorin Street and Copper Street . The rear of the villa sat on Sidle Street .

Description

The five buildings that composed the villa compound were three or four stories tall.
