Mharartel was a barony in Chessenta during the late 13 th century DR .

Geography

Mharartel was northeast of Pandrik .

Geographical Features

During the late 13 th century DR , Mharartel was lightly forested rural lands, with woodlots, and pastures. There was at least one altar to Lathander . As of the turn of the 16 th century, the land had turned into a part of Sebakar in the Vast Swamp , south of the River Adder .

Government

As a barony, there was presumably a baron in charge.

Trade

Tarnsaera brandy was made in the barony, and there was horse breeding.

History

By the turn of the 16 th century DR , Mharartel was long-vanished.
