Vaerluth was the easternmost goblin realm in the Rauvin Mountains in Luruar .

Geography

Vaerluth was located in the caverns and foothills of the eastern Rauvin Mountains.

Government

Vaerluth was ruled by a succession of kings named Vaerluth; in 1372 DR , the realm was on its 36th King Vaerluth. Succession of each king came through the murder of his predecessor.

Inhabitants

The goblins of Vaerluth were considered the poorest of the goblins in the Rauvin Mountains. They dwelt in huge-spider-infested caverns and foothills, which gave them immunity to the spider venom. The goblins boiled the spider webs to make soup.
