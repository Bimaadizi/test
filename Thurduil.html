OccupationWizard

Thurduil was a mage instructor in the late 8 th century DR .

Relationships

Thurduil had been the first magic instructor of Alustriel .

History

Between 767 DR and 778 DR , Thurduil had been Alustriel's instructor for eight years, hired by her uncle, Thamator . Her tutoring proceeded under a different wizard later, Gaerd .
