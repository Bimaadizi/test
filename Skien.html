Skien was a small village located on Oman's Isle within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Skien was located along the western coast of Oman's Isle along the Old Stone Wood . It lay just a few miles north of Trondhelm .

History

The village was reduced to ruins after giants passed through the region following the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR .
