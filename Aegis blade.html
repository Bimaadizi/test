An aegis blade was an enchanted sword meant to be wielded by a swordmage .

Description

Any heavy or light blade could be enchanted to become an aegis blade .

Powers

Aegis blades were magically precise and sharp weapons, especially when a target was struck in a vital area. When wielded by a swordmage, it allowed them to utter a word of power to mark all foes within 15 feet (4.6 meters) with their aegis of shielding or aegis of assault .
