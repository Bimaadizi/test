Sul was a zealous human worshipper of the Absolute who completed his pilgrimage to the Moonrise Towers during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Sul was a common human man with bronze skin, dark brown curly hair, and hazel eyes. He had a mustache and a goatee and was dressed in a simple maroon tunic .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Sul was among those who joined the new religions that sprang in the Western Heartlands . Like many others, Sul took on a pilgrimage to the seat of the Absolute's power – the Moonrise Towers – the fortress controlled by the Absolute's warlord – General Ketheric Thorm . When a group of True Soul heroes arrived at the Moonrise, Sul cheered for the moon-witch's arrival.
