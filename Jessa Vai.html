Jessa Vai was a officer within the Flaming Fist of Baldur's Gate during the mid-14 th century DR . For a brief time, she was assigned stationed in the town of Beregost .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Officer Vai was assigned to Beregost to deal with the bandits that were assaulting caravans and travelers along the Trade Way . She paid a 50 gp bounty for every bandit scalp that was brought to her during her stay. Vai specifically recruited Gorion's Ward to assist her with this endeavour.

Once the bandits were considered to have been dealt with, she returned to her superiors in Baldur's Gate and delivered her report on the situation.
