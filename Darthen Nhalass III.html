Darthen Nhalass III , who was also nicknamed Fiendbane , was the tenth Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Darthen III inherited the throne when his father, Rhendar I , died in −633 DR . He ruled until his death in −610 DR . Darthen III invaded Narfell in −622 DR and again in 610 DR. He was slain in battle during the second invasion along with his firstborn son.

Relationships

His secondborn son and heir was Umathar VI .
