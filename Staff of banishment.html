Staves of banishment , also known as wands of banishment , were a type of magical short staff created to rid the Prime Material plane of powerful other-worldly creatures.

Description

These light staves were usually crafted from shadow-wood .

Powers

The staves possessed a limited number of charges of the spells banishment , dismissal , repulsion and slow , which could be cast by the wielder upon utterance of a command word .

History

This type of staff was conceived by the young mage Savengriff , apprentice of Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun . He crafted just over a dozen of these powerful staves and distributed them among his friends and colleagues in the North , including Khelben. He later lost one of these while exploring the Dungeon of the Crypt with the Company of Crazed Venturers .

When Elminster Aumar examined one of these staves, he discovered its command word was "Baerlaguth" .
