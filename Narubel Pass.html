Narubel Pass was a dangerous and mostly forsaken mountain pass found on the Chultan Peninsula .

Geography

The pass was located in the central region of the northern arm of the Sanrach Mountains .

Description

Narubel Pass connected the independent coastal city of Narubel in the north with Samarach in the south.

Activities

The pass was rarely used as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , as trade between Narubel and Samarach was forbidden. Dangerous creatures awaited any travelers along the pass when they reached the Samarlogh .
