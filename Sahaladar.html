Sahaladar was the largest floating city on the world of Azallah .

Geography

Sahaladar was built on a floating island that was located above the watery depths of Azallah.

History

The inhabitants of Sahaladar peacefully shared their world with the water-breathing species known as the Kilm .

At some point in time, the city was invaded by Morag and her army of Old Ones . These armies were successfully stopped by the Hero of Neverwinter confronting Morag in the Source Stone in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
