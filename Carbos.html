History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Carbos was hired to murder the foster child of the sage Gorion , Abdel Adrian . He managed to gain entrance into the library-fortress of Candlekeep but was unable to kill the young man.

At some point over the next year, Carbos traveled to Athkatla .
