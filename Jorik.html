Jorik was a human who owned the Weeping Willow Inn near the Mere of Dead Men on the Sword Coast North .

History

Jorik came to the Mere of Dead Men and found a lone building. He discovered it had once been part of a village that had been destroyed by the King of Shadows , so he re-established it as a tavern.

When the Kalach-Cha and Khelgar Ironfist came to the inn in 1374 DR , Jorik asked them to defeat the invaders and if they did he would trade with them and give information about Neverwinter .
