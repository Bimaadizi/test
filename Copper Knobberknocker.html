Copper Knobberknocker was a gnome tinkerer and devotee of Lathander , who lived in Icewind Dale during the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

He wore a big fuzzy hooded coat that made him look like a bear cub.

Relationships

For some time, Copper served as the assistant to the sage Macreadus at his isolated residence referred to as the Black Cabin . While the two friends were working on a powerful magical device called the Summer Star , their lack of success with the project, combined with living conditions that were less than ideal, caused them to bicker all the time.

History

Some time around the Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR , Copper and Macreadus got into a particularly bad argument and the gnome left the cabin for the Ten Towns .

While staying at the House of the Morninglord in Bryn Shander , Copper sought out some adventurers to bring supplies and food to his friend and mentor.
