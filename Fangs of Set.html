The Fangs of Set was an organization that tried to rule Mulhorand from the shadows, leading the nation along a path of Set 's design.

Members

They were an adventuring party gathered in 1357 DR by Seti , the living incarnation of Set . It was composed of four members, Hodkamset , Nekiset , Hamsetis and Suliyar , each one commanded a variable numbers of minions.
