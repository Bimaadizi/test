Tym Waterdeep Limited , Tyme-Waterdeep , Tyme Waterdeep, Ltd. or simply TWL , as it was known among booksellers, was a large publishing business located in the city of Waterdeep .

Structure

The publishing company was a large, converted warehouse. The building was four floors high.

Inhabitants

The building was owned and run by Justin Tym in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Tym Waterdeep Limited was famous throughout the Realms as the publisher of all of the works of Volothamp Geddarm , including all his famous guide book, the ever-popular Cormyr: A Novel and Evermeet: A Novel by Greenwood Grubb . In the 15 th century DR , in association with the Fellowship of Carters and Coachmen they published Volo's Waterdeep Enchiridion .
