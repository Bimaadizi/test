Jhamanthra Tsathree was the proprietor of Tsathree's Comforts , a shop on Golden Serpent Street in the North Ward of Waterdeep .

Activities

Sometime around the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Jhamanthra invented a new scented facial wash she named Waterfall Kiss and began selling it in her shop. Unbeknownst to her (she claimed), her secret formula caused skin to glow a bright green color hours after certain cosmetics were applied to the same area.
