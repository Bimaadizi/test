Geography

The city lay on the coast of the Wizards' Reach on the Sea of Fallen Stars just south of the Yuirwood .

History

Nethra was initially founded by the empire of Unther following the great wars between Narfell and Raumathar , but over the passage of time Unther lacked the strength to hold it and Nethra eventually declared independence in the Year of the Eclipsed Heart, 504 DR .

Later, under the name of Nethrar , it was the capital of the kingdom of Galadorna until its fall in the Year of Mistmaidens, 764 DR .
