A weasel was a small furry mammal.

Description

Like ferrets , these animals were small, slender-bodied predators. The various breeds of weasel ranged in length from 5​ to ​16 inches (0.13​ to ​0.41 meters) on the lower end, measuring from head to tail, to 1​ to ​2 feet (0.3​ to ​0.61 meters) on the larger end of the spectrum.

Behavior

Weasels could be extremely aggressive but chose to attack smaller prey.

Sub-Species

Ecology

Habitats

In the land of Kara-Tur wild weasels could be found in its temperate forests, plains, and swamps. As well as in its more arctic regions.

Relationships

These animals could be trained and domesticated. And some wizards were known to have them as familiars .

Weasels were a favored animal of the deities Garagos , Garl Glittergold , and Thoth .
