Medrash was a musician and entertainer, part of the Circus of the Last Days troupe in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Medrash was a dragonborn with a red dragon ancestry. He had flaming red eyes and wore a fanciful bard 's garb. He was a proficient lyre player.

Abilities

As a red dragonborn, Medrash could breathe out a burst of flames. He also could cast the faerie fire spell .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Circus of the Last Days returned from the Feywild and opened its gates to the inhabitants of the Rivington district in Baldur's Gate .
