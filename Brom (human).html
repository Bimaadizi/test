Brom , was a hired guard who worked at The Track in the city of Westgate in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Brom was a human man with a pale skin tone, brown eyes, red hair, and a red bushy beard.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Brom was employed by The Track's proprietor to guard the gambling den. The man enjoyed spending his off-duty time in the establishment's kitchens with the complimentary meals.
