Raag Hammerstaad was the Northlander king of Norheim in the mid–14 th century DR . His insignia was a blue whale .

History

Raag ruled Norheim from his lodge at Hammerstaad . He departed for a voyage to Tethyr circa the Year of the Snow Winds, 1335 DR , shortly following a visit to his lodge by Elminster .

Alongside Grunnarch the Red , he supported Thelgaar Ironhand in his attempt to invade the southern Moonshae Isles in the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR . Raag was killed in the final battle of the Darkwalker War by the wolves of the Pack .
