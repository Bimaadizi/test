Flame Daryn Falburn ( pronounced : /ˈ d ɛ r j n ˈ f ɑː l b ɜːr n / De-ryn-FALL-burn ) was an officer in the Flaming Fist that served the city of Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

She was quote tall with striking red hair and vibrant green eyes.

Personality

Daryn was a direct, and no-nonsense individual. She was wholly dedicated to her duties within the Flaming Fist, and cared little for making friends. While sympathetic to the plights of the less fortunate, she remained cynical that they could receive meaningful aid.

Activities

Her offices were located in the second story of the Basilisk Gate , which separated the Lower City district of Eastway from the Outer City neighborhood of Stonyeyes .

History

When scores of refugees fled Elturel in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Flame Falburn made numerous attempts to help them find refuge in Baldur's Gate. She was one of the few officers of the Fist still dedicated to keeping order within the city. At the same time, she was working with a group of adventurers to investigate a series of gruesome murders.

After two initial killings, Falburn enlisted the adventurers to uncover the truth about yet another killing in Bloomridge neighborhood of the Lower City . She directed them to work with Manip Tradran Foremantle , and warned them that Manip Benn Hithlin could be an agent of the emerging cult of Zariel . Together they uncovered the whereabouts of an actual devil named Gharizol had been summoned within Baldur's Gate.
