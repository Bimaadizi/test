Dark Mere was a swamp in the Spine of the World mountains in northwest Faerûn , 30 miles (48 kilometers) north of the small town of Blasingdell .

Geography

This swamp stood at the foot of the Stone Tooth , a peak of the southern Spine of the World range. It surrounded a network of tunnels known as the Sourscale Warrens .

Inhabitants

Kobolds from the Sourscale Warrens were known to leave slain creatures in its brackish waters to "pickle" them for a while before eating.
