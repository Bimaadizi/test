Smoke was one of the three ninja in the service of Daisake Mayazato in the town of Aru in Wa .

History

Smoke trained in the ninja arts in the Warazama clan together with Moonlight and Snake , until that clan was destroyed by internal dissent. He and his fellows entered into the service of Daisake Mayazato to repay him for previous services.
