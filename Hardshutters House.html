The Hardshutters House was the Roofers, Thatchers, and Glaziers Guild 's guildhall in the city of Suzail , Cormyr .

Location

The Hardshutters House stood on the northern end of Suzail's open-air Market , facing south, where Garth Street reached the Market plaza.

Interior

Early in his career, Ullvor Mharivven commissioned a custom spell that animated glass shards collected in Hardshutters House's attic. With a single opening of the guildhall's attic, its master could release a whirlwind of sharp glass onto thieves and unwanted guests. The shard storm was similar to the effects of the blade barrier spell .

Services

The Hardshutters House offered membership to roofers, slate-masons, shingle-cutters, thatchers, thatch-cutters, glaziers, " sandglass " makers, and glass stainers.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , Ullvor Mharivven was in change of the guildhall.
