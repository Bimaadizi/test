Nalevac was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age .

History

Nalevac developed three spells during their life. The spells they invented were color spray in 1802 NY (−2057 DR) , infravision in 1854 NY (−2005 DR) , and lastly, rainbow pattern in 1895 NY (−1964 DR) .
