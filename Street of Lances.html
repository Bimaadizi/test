The Street of Lances was a street in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

The road was located at the very north of the ward just south of the Trolltower , running west to east from Westwall Street to Trollkill Street . Along the way it passed through the Street of the Singing Dolphin and Morningstar Way . This street also acted as the northern terminus of Stormstar's Ride .

Inhabitants

The street was walked by broadcryers who peddled their broadsheets to passers-by.
