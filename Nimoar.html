Nimoar , "the Reaver" , was the leader of a human tribe of barbarians in Northwest Faerûn . The tribe was one of many to attack a small settlement on the Sword Coast North , but this tribe was the one that succeeded, leading to Nimoar's tribe taking over the settlement and naming it Nimoar's Hold , but this would soon be changed to Waterdeep , the name used by sea captains docking at the harbor.

Possessions

Nimoar owned a heavy magic steel shield known as his blazing bulwark . This spiked flaming shield was carried into battle during the First Trollwar of the Year of Fireslaughter, 932 DR , where it served well against the regenerating giants .

History

Nimoar was involved in the Orcfastings War , in which the orcs from the Sword Mountains attacked and besieged Nimoar's Hold. Nimoar survived the war, but died of old age in 936 DR .
