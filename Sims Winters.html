Sims Winters was the son of Beartrid Winters and Kalas Winters , who worked at the Wolf's Pelt Inn in the Icewind Dale community of Targos circa 1369 DR .

Description

Sims was a fiery 13-year-old boy with short-cut black hair and his family's big brown eyes. He was 5 feet and 8 inches (1.7 meters) tall and weighed 127 pounds (57.6 kilograms).

Personality

Sims looked out for his family, especially his mother and sister. He jumped to their defense at a hint of insult or confrontation, trying to prove his manliness to his father.

Possessions

Sims always carried a dagger gifted to him by his father Kalas for his 13th birthday.

History

Around Tarsakh in 1369 DR , Sims lived with his family in the Ten Towns .
