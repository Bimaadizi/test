Nura al-Hamiir was a dwarven inhabitant of Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Personality

Nura possessed a sharp wit and keen eye, earning her a notorious reputation for being able to single-out smugglers among the throngs of people who passed through the Pilgrims' Gate .

Activities

Nura was the captain of the Pilgrim's Gate in Huzuz. She enjoyed her job and excelled at it, often taking the time to personally welcome visitors into the City of Delights.
