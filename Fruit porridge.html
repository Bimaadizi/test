Fruit porridge was a common simple variety of porridge found in the Western Heartlands in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

This dish of oatmeal or barley was a chunky mixture overwhelmingly flavored with bananas and served topped with berries.

Availability

Fruit porridge could be found being served in homes and eateries of the city of Baldur's Gate and inns alongside the Risen Road and the Chionthar Valley . The dish was also known to be consumed by the githyanki , namely the inhabitants of Crèche Y'llek in the late 15 th century DR .
