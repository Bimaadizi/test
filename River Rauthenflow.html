The River Rauthenflow was a waterway in northern Mulhorand known for possessing an exceptionally fast current. The Rauthenflow connected Gbor Nor in the Hordelands with the Alamber Sea .

Geography

The River Rauthenflow moved swiftly west from its origins in Murgh么m to empty into the Alamber Sea at the falls of Rauthgor . The region surrounding the waterway was arid, and the only river crossing was a bridge located at Rauthil .

Inhabitants

The area surrounding the River Rauthenflow was inhospitable to most habitation. Despite this, various monsters called this region home. The falls of Rauthgor were known to be frequented by a notable population of mermaids .

History

The entire length of the river was navigable due to efforts from the ancient empires of Mulhorand and Imaskar . These massive projects added canals around cataracts, fortified the soft banks with stone, and cleared rapids of large rocks.

The Rauthenflow was a vital source of income for Murgh么m since they applied a tax to any vessel sailing its waters.

Due to the high volume of river traffic, pirates were virtually unheard of on the Rauthenflow.
