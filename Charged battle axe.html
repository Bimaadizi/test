Charged battle axes were enchanted weapons imbued with lightning magic.

Description

These battleaxes were of superb quality with magic glow pulsing through tiny rune etchings that spiderwebbed their surface.

Powers

Charged battle axes possessed +2 enchantment that made the weapon's edge more deadly and guided the wielder's hand towards the target. Additionally, the axe had a cointoss of a chance of dealing lightning damage to the target and a three out of every twenty successful slashes stunned the target.

History

In the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , charged battle axes could be found in the ruined labyrinthine dungeons of Dorn's Deep in the Spine of the World . Another such axe belonged to Doogal , a thuggish trapper from the town of Lonelywood of the Ten Towns .
