The Harbor Tower was one of the garrison buildings staffed with Purple Dragons in Suzail , the capital city of Cormyr .

Location

Suzail's Harbor Tower rose above the waters of the Royal Docks at the Citadel of the Purple Dragons , overlooking Dragonmere . The Harbor Tower was the westernmost tower of said fortification.

Description

The tower's garrison was equipped with war horns that signaled danger or responded to other war horns blaring from the castle, gates, or other fortifications of the capital. The towers of the Citadel, including the Harbor Tower, were considered to a part of the Royal Docks and were under the direct command of the Royal Admiral of Cormyr, Admiral Hazen Kelafin , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .
