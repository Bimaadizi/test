History

She joined the armed forces of Waterdeep while her brother Arran Ashatiel studied as a businessman. However, Arran was killed in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR under the orders of Sarevok Anchev . Totally devastated, Adras joined the crusade of the Shining Lady , in order to take vengeance against Sarevok one day.

After Sarevok was killed by his Bhaalspawn sibling, Adras turned her ire on the surviving godchild.
