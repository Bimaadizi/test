Dannath Lisosar was a a carpenter and shopkeeper inhabiting in the city of Waterdeep in the 14 th century DR .

Activities

Dannath owned a fine woodworking shop called Patient Fingers Finework located on Sleepers' Walk in the Trades Ward . In addition to running her own shop, Dannath was the main point of contact for the Guild of Fine Carvers . She held the position of Second Master Carver within the guild.
