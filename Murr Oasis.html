The Murr Oasis was an important watering place found on the southern fringe of the High Desert in the Pearl Cities of west Zakhara .

Geography

Situated atop a landrise, the Murr Oasis overlooked the Realm of Bleeding Trees to the south. It was the first oasis any caravans traveling between Ajayib and Gana would reach when leaving either city. The oasis was equidistant, 150 miles (240 kilometers), from both Ajayib or Gana.
