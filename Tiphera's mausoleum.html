Tiphera's mausoleum was a lavish tomb in the Raurinshield mountains where Tiphera tir-Yseldre had been interred.

Location

The mausoleum stood at the site of Tiphera's victory against the blue dragon Sturykkazynarr .

Activities

A three-day rite at the location could be used to awaken the final set of powers of the Wyrmbane Helm .

History

The mausoleum was built on the orders of King Andizen after the death of Tiphera. She was interred there with her helm, the Wyrmbane Helm , but it disappeared soon after.

Inhabitants

The mausoleum had become infested by powerful undead by the year 1372 DR .
