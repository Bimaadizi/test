The Obould dynasty was a line of seventeen orc kings ruling the Kingdom of Many-Arrows , starting in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR . [ citation needed ] It was preceded by the Broken Arrow tribe, whose king was Obould I . It was succeeded by the usurper warlord Hartusk in the Year of the Awakened Sleepers, 1484 DR . [ citation needed ]

The Obould dynasty ended when Obould XVII was assassinated in 1484 DR by drow , and a usurper, Hartusk, took the throne. [ citation needed ]

After Hartusk was killed in the War of the Silver Marches , Lorgru , son of Obould XVII, returned from his exile and reclaimed the throne of Many Arrows. [ citation needed ]
