Aleema Meddlebrook was a herbalist from Neverwinter who traveled along the High Road between Neverwinter and Luskan during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Aleema had deep tanned skin and dark brown hair.

Relationships

Aleema was old friends with Miranda , the occult supplier in Luskan.

History

Some time around the Year of the Star Walker's Return, 1490 DR , Aleema joined the merchant's caravan of Tollin and his crew going from Neverwinter to Luskan, accompanied by members of the Order of the Burning Dawn . The trip was somewhat of a disaster, as several members of their group went missing, and members of the caravan's group were attacked by mercenaries, an assault that was set up by the treacherous Martumal .
