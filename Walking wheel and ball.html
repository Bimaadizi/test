A walking wheel and ball was a type of item in the Realms popular among bards and acrobats who performed balancing acts upon them.

Description

These specialized wheels were made from thick steel in order to support weight. The ball was usually a granite sphere.

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 4 gold pieces for a wheel and 7 gold pieces for a ball.
