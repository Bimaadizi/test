Corvus Nightfeather was a kenku assassin and spy and the owner of Nightfeather's Circus of Wonders of the late 15 th century DR .

History

Corvus was trained at the Rookery of Tears . He ran the Circus of Wonders, a circus traveling through southern Faerûn for a number of years.

In 1479 DR , he led the rescue of Marod el Arhapan 's heir, Cephas , from enslavement on the Island of the Free . He took Cephas to Almraiven , where one of his employers, Acham el Jhotos , intended to enspell Cephas with deadly magic to kill Marod. When two members of the circus were abducted and taken to Calimport , Corvus traveled with Cephas and other friends to attempt a rescue. Corvus was imprisoned by his other employer Shahrokh and stripped of all his magical items. Corvus escaped Calimport when Cephas destroyed Marod's palace and gladiatorial arena.

Activities

In addition to his circus, Corvus earned his coin by acting as a spy for various factions in southern Faerûn .

Description

Corvus had black feathers.

Personality

He seemed to have a soft spot for outcasts such as the goliath Tobin .

Abilities

Corvus could shadowstep and mimic the voices and hand writing of other people.

Possessions

Corvus's most powerful magical item was an extradimensional space he accessed through his chest feathers. He possessed one of the two books necessary to complete the Ritual of Return to free Calim and Memnon from their imprisonment.
