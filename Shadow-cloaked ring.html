Shadow-cloaked ring was an enchanted ring imbued with shadow magics.

Description

Despite its gilded appearance, shadow-cloaked ring never gleamed as if constantly in shadow.

Powers

The ring's shadowthief enchantment increased all damage the wearer did to targets obscured by shade or darkness.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , shadow-cloaked ring was found on a powerful shadow mastiff who led a pack within the Shadow-cursed Lands of the Western Heartlands . The same pack that hounded True Soul Korliss leading to the cultist's death of hunger.
