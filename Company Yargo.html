Company Yargo was a mercenary company and one of the Five Companies of Yaulazna . As of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , it was headed by captain Shil Yargo .

History

The halfling family that led Company Yargo descended from Yargo , who acted as the then-earthbound Yaulazna's pirate king before the Spellplague .
