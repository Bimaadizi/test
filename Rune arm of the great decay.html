The rune arm of the great decay was a rune arm -like armlet found in Spiderhaunt Wood in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The rune arm strengthened the acidic abilities of artificers brave enough to use it, and could shoot blasts of magical acid directly.

History

The ancient elven armlet was gradually soaked in the power of decay and destruction over time, and became a powerful implement in its own right. It ended up in the hoard of the fang dragon Nylerian , who laired in the Spiderhaunt Wood with ettercaps around the time the Elves of Cormanthor and Elven Crusade reclaimed Myth Drannor .
