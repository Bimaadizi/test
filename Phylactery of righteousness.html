Phylacteries of righteousness were magical items that could be found in Zakhara , the Land of Fate. They were only carried or used by holy slayers .

Description

A phylactery of righteousness was actually a collection of sacred writings that were worn on the user's arm.

Powers

This item provided heightened combat skills against the "Unenlightened" or anyone who had offended the user's god.
