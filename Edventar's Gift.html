Edventar's Gift was a unique ring of free action .

Powers

The wearer of the ring was immune to effects, objects or other impediments that limited or hampered their movement. Similarly, their movements could not be enhanced by in any way, such as by certain spells .

History

This ring was named after Edventar , a scavenger that played no small part in the defeat of the "Pirate Queen" Yenandra Hazm'cri . He was gifted his namesake ring by a group of aquatic elves he counted among his friends.

One suchring, original or a replica, was worn by Purvis , the assassin who was hired by the speaker of Lonelywood in the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR .
