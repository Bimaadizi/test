History

Demir's family was struck with tragedy in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , when Mayrina's husband Connor died rather suddenly. Bearing Connor's unborn child and devastated after her loss, Mayrina left for the Sunlit Wetlands to make a bargain with the old hag that lived in a shack within.

Demir and Johl went after their missing sister and managed to confront the old woman of the swamp Ethel , before she made it back to Mayrina who was waiting within her home.
