O'Roarke was a Ffolk bandit inhabiting the Dernall Forest in Callidyrr in the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR .

History

O'Roarke and his henchmen used a special snare trap to accost small boats sailing down the Swanmay River through the forest. He once even trapped and encountered Elminster of Shadowdale using this trick.
