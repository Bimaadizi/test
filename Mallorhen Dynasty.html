The Mallorhen family was a noble house of Tethyr that briefly held the throne in the 9 th century DR during the greater Ithal-Strohm-Bormul period.

History

Tibor Mallorhen came to the throne by marrying King Strohm V 's eldest daughter, who did not inherit it on her father's passing in the Year of the Slow Herald, 832 DR . Instead, her spouse Tibor became king, followed by their son Samyte . Though they had several sons, the dynasty was cut short by the assassin Uthaedeol the Blood-drenched , who killed the king and his heirs in the Year of the Hunted Elk, 841 DR . With the dynasty extinguished, the throne passed to Strohm's younger daughter, Alisande , who started the Queens' Dynasty
