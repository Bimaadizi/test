The goggles of the berserker were a set of magical goggles found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Description

These heavy goggles had red crystal lenses, befitting those who saw red.

Powers

These goggles specifically aided barbarians and berserkers , making them even more destructive and deadly once they started swinging. It also made them slightly incorporeal to avoid physical attacks, protected them against spells that would instantly slay and snuff out their life force, and gave some resistance to negative energy .

History

The goggles of the berserker were found after the Elves of Cormanthor raided the Irithlium , where Thramont the Watchful and the Red Wizards of Droic Orgul had set up a stronghold for their operations.
