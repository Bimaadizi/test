Oskler Mhandyvver was the proprietor of Mhandyvver's Poultry in Red Larch around 1366 DR; a poultry shop offering meat, eggs, and chicks.

Oskler hired younger boys in order to protect his poultry runs by killing predators such as foxes , hawks , owls , and weasels .
