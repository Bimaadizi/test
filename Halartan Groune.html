Halartan Groune was a wizard from a tiny hamlet northeast of Daerlun named Drelt , circa 1360 DR .

Description

Halartan was obese from too much good living.

Personality

Halartan loved food and wine , to the point of gluttony.

Relationships

Groune was a business partner with Endreth Molipher , a former pirate , and two other wizards, Orbel Mhaerouzan , and Gorstal Hammers .

Activities

Halartan agreed to bring monsters that he subdued and captured to Molipher on his island sanctuary of Storna in the Inner Sea . He also provided magical assistance to ensure the success of Molipher's Dark Dragon Hunts by implanting dreams of heroic monster-slaying while clients slept, which reinforced the spiel they were fed so that they believed they had actually defeated a dangerous creature.

Possessions

As part of the agreement, Groune was allowed to use the island as a hideout or a place to store items he didn't want easily found.
