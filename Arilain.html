Lady Arilain was a Netherese archwizard that lived during the Shadowed Age .

History

The female arcanist controlled the Opus enclave , a great flying city in the empire of Netheril . In 3518 NY (−341 DR) , the talented archwizard discovered that the great Karsus was developing a new extremely powerful spell . The spellcasters of Opus managed to divine the effects of this spell, which would become known as Karsus's avatar . Arilain warned the other arcanists of Opus, but personally set out to approach Karsus himself. However, after meeting with Karsus, she was never heard of again.
