The Athora was a gigantic alien rock located under Thaymount , which greatly enhanced magic spell and magic items . It was considered one of the major reasons behind the power of the Red Wizards of Thay .

Powers

In addition to its innate abilities to empower magic, the Athora was a weave anchor , helping Mystra stabilize the Weave .

History

The Athora was created by the Ba'etith . It was located in the Assikhath , a cavern deep under Thaymount guarded by powerful protective statues.
