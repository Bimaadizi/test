Farnath Ilistar IV was the eighth King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Farnath ruled as Westgate's King from the Year of the Lizard King, 783 DR , until his death in the Year of the Leaping Lion, 834 DR . He was suspected of killing his brother, King Thartryn II , to take the throne.

Relationships

His second son who would succeed him to the throne was Pendernath .
