Description

Bernard was a rather rotund individual.

Activities

In addition to offering rooms for rent and drinks on tap, Bernard had a number of magical items for sale at the Copper Coronet.

Relationships

Bernard was an ally of several members of the Harpers , among them the druid Jaheira and her fellow agent Belgrade . He was somewhat wary of the Athkatlan Harpers who operated out of the Galvarey estate near the docks.
