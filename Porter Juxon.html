Porter Juxon was a human commoner from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Porter was a human man with light skin, gray eyes, and black curly hair. His black mustache and mutton chops were properly groomed and shaped.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the threat of the Cult of the Absolute , Porter could often be seen arguing with hin friend, Bliggerillo , over the Baldur's Mouth gazette's reliability and its claims that the walls of the Gate would protect its citizens. Bliggerillo showed no confidence in the broadsheet 's propaganda, unlike Porter. The human believed that the broadsheet would never lie to the entire city.
