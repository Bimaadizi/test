Hezebel was an erinyes who was trapped within Ammon Jerro 's haven in the Sword Mountains , for the purpose of empowering the warlock .

She was in a constant feud with several of the other imprisoned demons and devils , but broke free when Baalbisan convinced Shandra Jerro to spill her blood to undo the magic that bound the fiends to the haven.
