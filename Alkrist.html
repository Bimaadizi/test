Alkrist was a monk of the Avowed as well as one of the eight Great Readers of Candlekeep during the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

He was considered to be Candlekeep's most knowledgeable individual on the topic of militarism and politics. He had expertise knowledge on many of the battles in Toril 's history.

Activities

As one of the Great Readers, Alkrist was partially responsible for the day-to-day duties that were required to maintain Candlekeep's daily duties.
