Horned helms were enchanted helmets in common use in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

Those individuals who donned horned helms became prone to surges of uncontrollable aggression and, in turn, received boost to their ability to dispense punishment.

History

In the 15 th century DR, horn helmets could be found among bandits and heroes of the Sword Mountains , in the city of Neverwinter , raiders of the Mere of Dead Men , and ruins of Illefarn .
