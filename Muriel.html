Muriel was a monk at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose .

History

In 1491 DR , Muriel and some other monks were kidnapped by cultists of the Cult of the Howling Hatred , who planned to use them in a new plot to access an elemental node . In a room of the node's complex under the monastery, she met an adventuring party hired by Grandmaster Perrywinkle Shin to find the missing monks. The adventurers saved her from poison gas.
