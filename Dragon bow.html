Dragon bow was an enchanted longbow rumored to be imbued with the spirits of Chultan dragons .

Powers

Dragon bows were created to honor the great dragons who once lived in Chult. When used in combat, every shot made with a dragon bow was empowered by dragon spirits, making it a powerful enchanted item. Additionally, these bows enhanced the user's individual powers and abilities.
