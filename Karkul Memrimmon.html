Karkul Memrimmon was a Zhentilar warcaptain of the Eastern Stonelands company in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

He was an enormous man.

Personality

Per Gathlarue , he was too rowdy for any tavern.

Abilities

He favored gauntlets for fighting.

Possessions

He wielded spiked gauntlets.

Relationships

Gathlarue, a Zhentarim sorceress, avoided him. Simron was a member of his company.

History

In late 1357 DR , Karkul Memrimmon was sent to ambush Shandril Shessair , moved from his post in the Stonelands of Cormyr towards Hullack Forest . Aided by an upperpriest of Fzoul's, they crossed the Moonsea Ride north of Gnoll Pass , and then rode nightly to the Immer upstream. From there, they got in position to ambush her.

During the ambush, Karkul Memrimmon's eyes got burned out, and Simron, one of his underlings, escaped, though they almost succeeded in capturing Shandril. Before he could get his eyesight restored, Karkul Memrimmon was slain by Delg Ironstar , a companion of Shandril's.

Fzoul Chembryl later confirmed him dead.
