Caligarde was a mage who studied the arcane arts under the Seven Wizards of Myth Drannor , survived the fall of the city and went on to become a spell inventor of note.

Description

Known for his arrogance, sarcastic wit and impatience, Caligarde was mischievous in his search for magical knowledge.

History

After the destruction of Myth Drannor, Caligarde developed a hatred for elves , to the point where he hunted down every elven mage he caught off-guard. During this time, he took on many human apprentices who shared his ruthlessness.
