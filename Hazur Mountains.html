The Hazur Mountains were a small mountain range found on the Chultan Peninsula of southwest Faerûn that served as the border between Tashalar and Thindol .

Geography

The Hazur Mountains bordered the Shining Sea in the north and curved south for approximately 200 miles (320 kilometers) before reaching the coast of the Great Sea . The Dolsel Gap and River Dolsel split the mountain range in its southern tip.
