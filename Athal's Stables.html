Athal's Stables were a stables in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

These stables were located in the Waymoot neighborhood at the end of Coachlamp Lane , in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

Athal's Stables was a two story tall building.

Services

Mounts and gear related to them, such as bits, bridles , and saddles , could be purchased here.
