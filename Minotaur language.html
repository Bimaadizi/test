The Minotaur language was the racial language of minotaurs .

Speakers

Beyond minotaurs themselves, Minotaur was a common secondary language among some specialty priests , such as the Authlim of Iyachtu Xvim and the Dreadmasters of Bane .

Phonology

Minotaur had some similarities to the Ogre language , to such an extent that proficiency in either tongue could allow one to quickly pick up the intricacies of someone speaking the other.
