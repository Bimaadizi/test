Westhaven was a small village located on the island of Pandira within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Westhaven was found along the northern side of Pandira along the coast of the Sea of Swords .

Inhabitants

Most of Westhaven's population was composed of ruffians and dangerous individuals. This small port was a known base for pirates.
