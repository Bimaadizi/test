The Pasocada River was a prominent river found within the Pasocada Basin of northern Maztica .

Geography

The river wound through much of the Pasocada Basin, originating in the east in an unnamed mountain and flowing west through the Long Canyon before turning south at the Raven Falls and Great Raven Pueblo to eventually empty into the Eastern Ocean . The main arm of the Pasocada River stretched for approximately 300 miles (480 kilometers). The Opallin River branched from the Pasocada River near Opallinoc and flowed south into the lands of the Dog People , wolf tribe , and Rockjaw clan of desert dwarves .

Notable Locations

Settlements found along the banks of the Pasocada River included the Great Raven Pueblo, Lomaraj , Yapoza , Opallinoc, Nozoma , and Esh Alakar .
