Special bags were used by wizards in the Realms for a variety of purposes within their research labs.

Description

Bags were available in a wide variety of sizes for storing items, including water-tight options and those suited for fermenting.

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 1 copper piece to 1 silver piece depending on the size and type.
