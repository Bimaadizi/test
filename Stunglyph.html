A stunglyph was a powerful handglyph weapon favored by the Geomancers of Kadar used to stun their enemies.

History

Zakharan sages were unsure of the exact method used to create these wondrous handglyphs, but believed an ancient precursor to the create soundstaff spell was involved.

Powers

When activated a stunglyph discharged a wave of sonic force that blasted anything in its path with powerful magic. The cone of force measured 60 feet (18 meters) in length and spread to a width of 20 feet (6.1 meters). Anything caught in this area was hit with an effect similar to the power word, stun spell.

Most stunglyphs had between 2—8 charges and could not be recharged.

Description

These items could be found in a variety of shapes and sizes.
