Tobias Timble was a vintner and a deep-cover vampire in Westgate around 1374 DR .

Activities

Tobias, on the surface, was the proprietor of Brother Tobias Winery , a small winery in the city. In truth, he was a vampire member of the Night Masks , with the task of protecting the entrance to the Lair of the Night Masters .
