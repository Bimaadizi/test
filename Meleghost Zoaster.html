High Phantasmage Meleghost Zoaster was the ruling governor of Samarach as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Activities

Originally from Nimbral , Meleghost left his homeland to rule Samarach. Rumors abounded that he had no intention of returning home. Some believed he was under the influence of a sect of Cyricists who settled in Samarach following the Time of Troubles .
