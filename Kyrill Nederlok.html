Personality

He preferred Winter Wine over other types.

History

Kyrill Nederlok first joined the Flaming Fist as a mercenary at the group's founding in the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR . In time, he rose through the ranks and was promoted to marshal around the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR .

During the War of the Shining Crusade of 1368 DR , Marshal Nederlok was tasked with leading the Fist soldiers that contributed to the army of the Coalition .
