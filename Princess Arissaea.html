Princess Arissaea was a mythical character who commonly appeared in songs around the Sea of Fallen Stars for hundreds of years by the late 14 th century DR .

Description

In her stories, Princess Arissaea started life as a sheltered innocent, before becoming a slave, and then a pirate, and later slew a dragon . Ultimately, she returned to her conquered realm and liberated it, becoming its queen and finding love and happiness along the way.

History

In Turmish in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , a popular singer and actor known as the Songbird was drawing audiences with her new cycle of ballads revolving around Princess Arissaea. However, after every show, a few people who were in her audience were found to have vanished.
