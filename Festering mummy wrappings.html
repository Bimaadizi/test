The festering mummy wrappings were an unpleasant piece of necromancy -imbued cloth bandages found in the Stonelands during the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

When worn as a belt of sorts, the wrappings were a powerful aid to an undead , pale master necromancers , or some other creature that benefited from negative energy . The wrappings improved their ability to heal with negative energy, to resist purifying light, and violently reacted to those that struck against them with an effect similar to inflict wounds .

History

The bandage wrappings were once used by an ancient mummy , later found in the treasure pile of the red dragon Daeronnax , the master of Clan Daeronnax . Given the proximity, he may have stolen them from his neighbor, the Netherese mummy Laeron .
