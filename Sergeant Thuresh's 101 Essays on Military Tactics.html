Sergeant Thuresh's 101 Essays on Military Tactics was a book of military knowledge penned by Sergeant Ugrand Thuresh of Waterdeep sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

History

A copy of Sergeant Thuresh's 101 Essays on Military Tactics belonged to Manshoon XII , a clone of Manshoon , in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR . The book was stored in the mage 's library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep.
