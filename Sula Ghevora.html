Sula Ghevora was a middle class inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Sula was a human woman of tan skin tone and dar brown eyes. She wore quality clothes with blue hues being favored, and golden jewelry.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, the gates between the Lower City of Baldur's Gate and its more affluent Upper City were closed, monitored by the Flaming Fist mercenaries and allowing only the wealthy and current residents of the Upper City through. Sula Ghevora was among those Baldurians who sought entrance into the Upper City, lamenting that people like her could barely afford to rent a basement in the rich upper-crust neighborhood.
