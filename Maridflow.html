The Maridflow was a river in eastern Calimshan .

Description

The river began at Azure Falls and flowed south from the Alimir Mountains to the bay of Almraiven , which opened into the Shining Sea .

The Maridflow had a high mineral content yet did not have a large population of fish or shellfish. The shallow water and the reflective stones of the river bed made the waters shimmer and gleam.

History

The river was named for the the marids Haj and Luar who were slain here in a battle against the noble djinni Calim 's forces in −7687 DR .
