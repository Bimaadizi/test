Measuring cups were a type of cookware container in the Realms, used to measure the volume of liquid or dry ingredients.

History

Sometime in the 14 th century DR , the cargo ship The Gentle Lady was delivering a shipment of farmer and merchant goods to a small coastal community in the Dragon Reach . Among the goods being carried by the ship was a crate full of cookware that included eleven tin measuring cups.
