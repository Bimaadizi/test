Brother Eardon was a wandering priest of Lathander during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Activities

Eardon wandered northwest Faerûn , using his powers to help villagers as a messenger or priest.

History

In 1491 DR , Eardon was at the Swinging Sword tavern when some adventurers were hired to find a diplomatic caravan that had disappeared near the city. He confirmed that he had seen the caravan a few days before in Beliard .
