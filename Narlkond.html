Narlkond was a Sharran priest during the mid–8 th century DR .

Description

He had noticeable ritual scarring on his forearms, and when in ritual, he wore a mantle of shards between his legs, drawing blood with every step.

Activities

He was a senior priest at the House of Holy Night .

Relationships

Dread Sister Avroana wasn't as confident as Narlkond was in his scheme.

History

In early spring of the Year of the Awakening Wyrm, 767 DR , Narlkond had magically sent for many other priests of Shar after trapping an avatar of Mystra — Dasumia , the Queen of Galadorna —and discovering that through mass ritual human sacrifice, he could entrap the avatar and siphon away its power to grant his own priests the spells of wizards . They agreed to supply him with Sharran faithful.
