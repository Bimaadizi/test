Eiellûr was an ancient kingdom of green elves created during the First Flowering in −21,000 DR .

History

Eiellûr was drawn into the War of Three Leaves against Thearnytaar and Syòrpiir by the actions of assassins and spies from Ilythiir in −17,100 DR .

Eiellûr entered the second Crown War by allying with Thearnytaar and declaring war against Ilythiir after Ilythiir destroyed Syòrpiir with fire in −11,600 DR . The The Sable Wars , which began in −11,450 DR , were a series of battles during the Second Crown War. Aided by a few allies from Keltormir and Shantel Othreier , Eiellûr and Thearnytaar invaded Ilythiir , aiming to reform or conquer the dark elves before they destroyed any more realms. Over half their forces were wiped out by the dark elves' corrupt magic.

Around −11,400 DR , the kingdom was isolated from its allies and destroyed with fire by the dark elves of Ilythiir. The Ilythiiri were aided by treacherous green elves seeking peace through appeasement.

Over the course of time, the ruins of Eiellûr vanished under the forest canopies of the Winterwood .
