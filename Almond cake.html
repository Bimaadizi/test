Availability

Almond cakes were baked in the City of Splendors, Waterdeep . One of the bakeries that specialized in this sweet treat was Ackrieg's Bakery in the late 14 th century DR . Almond cakes were considered a traditional fare in Waterdeep.

The pastry was consumed as far south as Calimshan and as far east as Thymar , Tymanther .

History

In the late Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Baldur's Mouth Gazette could have published a recipe for the "Almond Cakes of Avernus ", the courtesy of a new cafe opened by some of the surviving Elturian refugees, six months after the Absolute crisis ' resolution.
