Radman was a Netherese citizen that lived in the 32 nd century NY .

Relationships

Radman was the father of Karsus . His partner gave birth to the legendary archwizard -to-be in 3163 NY (−696 DR) .

History

When his son, Karsus, created his floating Netherese enclave , he named it after his father. However, as time went on, people didn't refer to it as 'Radman'.
