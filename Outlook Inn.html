The Outlook Inn was an inn found just outside of Selgaunt 's walls.

Location

This large establishment was located just outside of Selgaunt's west gate along the Way of the Manticore .

Activities

The Outlook Inn was a new and clean establishment as of 1358 DR . Since the inn was located outside of Selgaunt's walls, they had no problems catering to adventurers when compared to the vast majority of inns and taverns found within the city proper.
