Dalabrac Bramblefoot was a halfling member of the Fire Knives in the late 15 th century DR .

Relations

Dalabrac was an important leader of the Fire Knives in Westgate and he had many dealings with the pirate Anton Marivaldi .

History

In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Anton recruited Dalabrac and his men to break into a temple of Amaunator in order to free the Chosen of Lathander , Stedd Whitehorn .
