Terondarg Del'Armgo was a drow and one of the most able scouts of House Barrison Del'Armgo in Menzoberranzan in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , following his house's attack against the resurrected House Do'Urden , Terondarg conducted a scouting mission. He later made a report to the matron mother Mez'Barris Armgo and high priestess Taayrul Armgo , using Drow Sign Language concealed by his cloak for privacy.
