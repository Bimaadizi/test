Mhair's Tower was the tower home of the wizard Mhair Szeltune in the 14 th century DR .

Location

This tower stood on the east side of Spindle Street , in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This tower stood five stories tall. The structure was conical in shape and had an oval front door made of copper and mithril .
