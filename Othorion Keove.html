Othorion Keove was an elven warrior.

Description

Othorion wore full elven mail armor.

History

In 26 DR , Othorion strongly opposed Iliphar Nelnueve 's decision to allow Faerlthann Obarskyr to become the first king of Cormyr .

In 900 DR , Othorion was the last survivor of Iliphar's court and returned the court to Cormyr. He claimed that he had come to hunt. His arrival was on the eve of Cormyr's Battle with the Witch Lords and during the battle, Othorion's elves arrived to aid Galaghard III 's humans, swinging the battle in their favour and bringing them a victory. Othorion stated that he had felt like hunting orcs and goblins . Othorion gave his approval of the kingdom of Cormyr after the battle.
