Uldorn was a thief of Hastarl and member of the Moonclaws gang in the early 3 rd century DR .

Relationships

Uldorn was a member of the Moonclaws .

History

Uldorn was one of the thieves from the Moonclaws that attempted to kill the Velvet Hands gang in an ambush shortly after its formation. The leader of the Velvet Hands, Farl , and his closest associate, Eladar the Dark (prince Elminster Aumar of Athalantar incognito) were targetted by a trio of Moonclaws that included him; Elminster slew them all.
