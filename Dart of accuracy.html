Darts of accuracy were small enchanted thrown weapons that flew unerringly towards the target.

Powers

Darts of accuracy were milled with extreme precision, as balanced as the finest of arrows . Each of these darts was imbued with a powerful +5 enchantment that helped the missiles to fly towards the target and increased their damage.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , darts of accuracy were sold at the Market Triangle of Westgate .
