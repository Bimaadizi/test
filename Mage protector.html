Mage Protectors were tower shields with enchantments of the highest order. They were created by the Imaskari for their elite warriors to better protect their assigned artificers and magewrights.

Powers

Mage Protectors carried a protective enchantment of the highest grade, and granted awesome resistance against magic that affected the mind. They also boosted the wielder's strength, nearly doubling it; unfortunately, this came with a great loss of intelligence.
