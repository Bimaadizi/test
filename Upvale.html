Upvale was the area between Tall Trees and the Far Forest in the North .

Geography

Upvale was part of a larger region known as the Delimbiyr Vale .

History

This region, along the Delimbiyr , used to be an area with many farms and pastures settled by humans , but subject to frequent raids by orcs . It eventually fell under the control of Hellgate Keep until that location was laid to ruins.
