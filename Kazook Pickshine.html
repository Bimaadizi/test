Kazook Pickshine was a notorious deep gnome alchemist in Blingdenstone in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Kazook helped create the spell gems used in the city's defense.

History

In Mantol-Derith , Kazook met the duergar merchant Werz Saltbaron and the two started a gem business behind their superiors' backs.
