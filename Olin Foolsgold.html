Olin Foolsgold was an alchemist who lived in the Cormyrean village of Darkenshield in the mid-14 th century DR .

Olin created the Formula For Vengeance , a ritual which could summon vengeance upon the perpetrators of a crime.

When Darkenshield was attacked by the Twilight Brigade during Gondegal 's war in 1352 DR , he hid in the Village Meet with many of the villagers. When it became clear there was no escape, he distributed a poison to the young and elderly that painlessly slew them, then died fighting the raiders.
