The Sheik used to alk to her while he sorted out things in his mind. Sothal was present while the Sheik had an audience with the adventurers who ultimately released Martek .

She was aware of several things regarding the oasis. She also believed the reason why Tolnus Granicus of the Sandvoyagers' Guild had not been seen for some time was that he had been the victim of foul play.
