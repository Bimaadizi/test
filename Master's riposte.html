Master's Riposte was a longsword found in the Storm Horns of Cormyr in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The longsword had a wide steel blade and an ornamented hilt.

Powers

The Master's Riposte enhanced the bearer's ability to dodge threats and immediately counterattack into an opening.

History

The sword was recovered by Cormyrean fighters after destroying the forces of General Grablandurr at Tumblerock Pass in the Storm Horns.
