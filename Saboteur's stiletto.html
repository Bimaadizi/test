Saboteur's stilettos were enchanted daggers created to be used in assassination and subterfuge.

Description

This bronze stiletto was covered in a never depleting coat of bubbling acid .

Powers

The blade was unaffected by its acid, remaining shining and sharp. The weapons possessed a +1 enchantment and burned its targets with acid upon contact.

History

In the 1374 DR , a saboteur's stiletto was wielded by a yuan-ti pureblood agent Luaire Farwander of House Se'Sehen . The spy used the dagger against a group of adventurers hired by Sa'Sani , the leader of one of Samargol 's merchant companies. The stiletto was taken off Luaire Farwander's dead body.
