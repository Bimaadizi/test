The Shaldizar Inn , which was also simply called the Shalda , was an inn located in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan in the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The inn was located in the northeast section of Suldolphor.

Description

A massive golden scimitar served as the inn's exterior sign. The interior was filled with gaudy decorations and furnishings including floating staircases, enchanted musical chimes , and fireveil hangings. The air was scented thanks to a combination of perfumes and spices tossed into the fireplace.

Activities

The Shalda was one of the most popular places to be seen in Suldolphor in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .
