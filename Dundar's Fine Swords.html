Dundar's Fine Swords was a weapon shop that specialized in swords located in Tilverton in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Location

The shop sat in the south-eastern part of Tilverton on a side street off the Street of the Sorceress .

Inhabitants

The shop was owned by a retired fighter named Dundar . His sister Andalara helped finish the blades and make scabbards when she was not out hunting.

Services

Around 1366 DR Dundar had expanded his craft and started selling axes , scythes , saws , and other bladed implements, eventually changing the shops name to Dundar's Fine Blades . He was sometimes assisted by his sibling Andalara, who finished weapons and made scabbards. Dundar also provided weapons training for aspiring warriors, drawing on his own significant skill and experience.
