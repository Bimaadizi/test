Hunters' Alley was a narrow, hard-packed dirt and gravel paved, alleyway in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This road ran in a straight line east from Way of the Dragon , across from Soothsayer's Way . Midway through the passage, it acted as the southern terminus of Burdag Lane .

History

Hunters' Alley was named for two young, courageous brothers, who grew up to become a sage and an aspiring duke.

Reputation

This alleyway was generally a safe passage to journey through.
