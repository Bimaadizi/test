Powder of good taste was a type of magical powder used for culinary purposes.

Description

This powder had a white hue and it was packaged in packets.

Powers

Placing a single pinch of this powder on any 1 lb (0.45 kg) of food, no matter its condition, would cause it to taste delicious. Though the appearance of the food would not be altered.
