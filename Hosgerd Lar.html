Hosgerd Lar was a contractor who connected hirings with adventurers in need of help in the town of Eveningstar in the 15 th century DR .

Description

Hosgerd was a dwarvish woman with a light skin tone, short blonde hair and clean-shaven face. She wore dress adorned with furs.

History

In the 15 th century DR, Hosgerd Lar could be seen dealing with adventurers from the world of Eberron , who set out to investigate the nearby ruins of the Haunted Halls .
