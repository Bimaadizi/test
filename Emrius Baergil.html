Emrius Baergil was Captain of the Riders of Mistledale , in Mistledale , the Dalelands , beginning in 1477 DR . He was promoted to Captain at the age of 22.

Personality

Emrius was described as quick-witted and never forgetting a face or a name. He was known to be a mediocre soldier but an excellent diplomat and investigator.

History

During his time as Captain of the Riders, Emrius covertly trained young boys and girls in order to use them as information gatherers, reporting anything suspicious to the Riders of Mistledale.

Relatives

Emrius was a descendant of Athror Baergil , brother to famous Captain Darrask Baergil .
