Ulan Zev was an albino Red Wizard of Thay necromancer residing in Illefarn around 1480 DR .

History

Ulan Zev was busy doing magical research using as a laboratory the infirmary of Ironaxe Hall.

Personality

Ulan was irremediably evil. His dream was to become a lich together with his twin and serve Szass Tam for the eternity.

Relationship

Ulan was one of the four apprentices of Arvik Zaltos . He had an imp familiar always at his side and was very close to his twin Luuthgar Zev .

Powers

Ulan and Luuthgar had a strange power: they had a very strong psychic bond and always knew each other's health. Most incredibly, if one of the twins died, his spirit could inhabit his brother's body like a phylactery and the surviving twin could grow a new body for the other from his own body.
