Igrayne Elonis was an elven priest of Mystra in the 14 th and 15 th centuries DR .

Description

She had light skin, gray eyes, and short backswept white hair. She favored a simple dress with a low-cut bodice.

History

Igrayne was once a priestess of Mystra, but after her goddess's death in 1385 DR she was left aimless.
