The Untheric languages group was one of the two major branches of the Rauric languages family and included languages spoken in both Faerûn and Zakhara .

Subgroups

The Untheric languages included the Untheric language spoken in Unther and also those of two language subgroups, the Midani and Alzho languages . Untheric was strongly influenced by Imaskari slave argot, while the Midani and Alzho languages were shaped by the languages of genies .

History

By the late 15 th century DR , the Untheric group had risen to become the third most active language group in Faerûn .
