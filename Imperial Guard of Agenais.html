The Imperial Guard of Agenais was a group of soldiers from Agenais in the Whamite Isles . They served Inwhar III and empress Zelina in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
