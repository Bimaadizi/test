TypeVehicle

Minecarts , also written as mine carts or mining carts , were a type of wheeled vehicle that rain on rails, used for moving ore and other materials procured in the process of traditional mining.

Availability

The dwarves that inhabited the Fire Caverns manufactured minecarts for trade.

History

In the 14 th century DR , a dwarven woman introduced an amusement ride to the Jack Mooney & Sons Circus that utilized minecarts as the ride vehicle. On its first day of operation three guests were injured by the ride, leading the Circus to permanently close the ride.
