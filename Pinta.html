Pinta was a member of the Shadow Druids of the Cloakwood Forest during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

She wore a simple leather harness and trousers.

Activities

While acting in secret within the Emerald Grove , Pinta took on the animal form of a large rat .

Possessions

She preferred to utilize a shortbow in combat.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Pinta joined Wren and their superior Olodan on a mission to infiltrate the Emerald Grove south along the Sword Coast . Together they worked with Kagha , a trusted leader within the Emerald Enclave who ascended as archdruid in the absence of Master Halsin . They aimed to protect the grove from a growing horde of goblins and other monstrous creatures, that threatened druid enclaves across the region.
