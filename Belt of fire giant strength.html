A belt of fire giant strength , also referred to as a girdle of fire giant strength , was one of six varieties of belts of giant strength . It conferred its wearer with the strength of a fire giant .

Description

At least some of these belts were crafted from the beard hair of fire giants.

Powers

The belt magically granted physical strength equivalent to that of a fire giant to its attuned wearer. If the wearer already possessed or exceeded such strength, the belt had no effect.

Notable Owners

The minotaur Torogar Steelfist , who served in Avernus as Arkhan 's bodyguard, wore a belt of fire giant strength .

Quite fittingly, the fire giant Bhaalspawn Yaga-Shura kept a girdle of fire giant strength in the lair beneath his keep in the Marching Mountains .

Ambrose the cavalry commander of king Halvor II , wore a belt of fire giant strength .
