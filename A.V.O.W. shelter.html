The A.V.O.W. shelter (an acronym for Animal Victims of War) was an animal shelter in the Tent City outside of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Working in conjunction with Marigold's Menagerie , this facility sought to heal animals injured during The Three Womens War and find new homes for animals that lost their owners.

History

This facility was established in 1370 DR , following the Three Womens War, with the help of generosity from adventurers .

Inhabitants

This facility was operated by Mindy Mufflefoot & Victoria Shadai .
