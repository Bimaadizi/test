Harloch was the largest lake located on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Harloch sat near the center of the island between Andover Heights in the west and Maloren Sods in the east.

Notable Locations

The village of Harloch was located on the lake's eastern shore on Snowdown Ride .
