Danyig was a skilled blacksmith who lived in Manass in 1367 DR .

Activities

Danyig was easily the most skilled blacksmith within the entire city. Not only could he forge beautiful Shou -style weapons and armor, he could also forge nearly any unique item if given a copy to work from.
