Bliggerillo was a hin commoner from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Bliggerillo was a lightfoot halfling man with a light skin tone, curly head of light brown hair, and blue eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the threat of the Cult of the Absolute , Bliggerillo could often be seen arguing with human friend, Porter Juxon , over the Baldur's Mouth gazette's reliability and its claims that the walls of the Gate would protect its citizens. Bliggerillo showed no confidence in the broadsheet 's propaganda, unlike his friend. He believed that the gazette's monopoly on the news in the city had became a problem.
