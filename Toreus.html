Toreus was a garrison turned trading city in Chessenta .

Trade

This city was known for its assertive business strategies, and went as far as trading with marauding pirates and criminals. The other cities in the region disdained such tactics, as Toreus appeared to openly defy the laws of Chessenta; even a Red Wizard enclave was allowed to operate within. Coins not minted in or accepted in other places in Chessenta were accepted in this settlement and could be used to purchase practically anything.

History

Though Toreus started out as a lone garrison for Mordulkin soldiers, it grew into the third biggest city of the confederation of Chessenta in the 15 th century DR .

Inhabitants

The inhabitants of this settlement were somewhat diverse, as Toreus allowed in and even welcomed those from the most despised nations across Toril .
