Hsiung chi Kang was a samurai in Shou Lung in 1357 DR .

Activities

Hsiung was a young noble indulging in flirtation, strutting, drinking, and gossiping. In truth, however, he was an agent of the Imperial Secret Police , using his cover to gather information.
