The fey semblance amulet was a magical amulet from the Feywild created some centuries before the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The fey semblance amulet was made using gemstones that, in reality, were kidney stones of some unknown archfey .

Powers

The amulet's wearer had their intelligence, wisdom, and charisma fortified greatly, similarly to the qualities of many fey that resided in the Feywild.

History

The fey semblance amulet had been "passed down through the centuries" until it ended up in the hands of Mayrina of the Anti-Hag Support Group who sought to end the sinister child-abducting plots of the hag who called herself Auntie Ethel in the city of Baldur's Gate in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . The fey semblance amulet could've been passed down as a reward for ending the hag's life.
