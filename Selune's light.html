Selune's light was a divine gift found in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

When held, Selune's light aided priestly and light-based spells, as well as bolstering health.

History

The moony item was found after the Sharran leader of the Night Brigade , priestess Storna Harnwood , was slain by the Riverside Rats with the blessing of Selûne .
