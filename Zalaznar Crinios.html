Zalaznar Crinios was the ruler of Cedarsproke in the Gulthmere Forest in 1370 DR .

Activities

Zalaznar was a totally independent leader, not affiliated with the Emerald Enclave . He sometimes disagreed with the Enclave's politics but remained neutral instead, showing dissent without weakening the religion.

History

In 1391 DR Zalaznar was transformed into a mighty treant for his service to nature, taking hold of the druid circle in Cedarspoke .
