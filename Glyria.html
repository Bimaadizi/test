Glyria was a Death's Head of Bhaal who resided in the ruined Temple of Bhaal in the Undercity of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Glyria was a rusted metal and leather -clad human who hid his facial featured under a cowl – uniform of the Bhaalists . He had dark brown eyes and his upper body was covered in tattoos of designs and skulls.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Glyria killed and slaughtered his victims in service of Bhaal , the Lord of Murder and his Chosen – Orin the Red . His desire to kill was an overwhelming bloodthirst. Thorough servitude to Orin, Death's Head of Bhaal Glyria supported the Cult of the Absolute that threatened the city of Baldur's Gate.
