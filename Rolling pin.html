A rolling pin was a cooking utensil used to knead dough. It was considered a common household object and could be used as an improvised weapon.

History

Sometime in the 14 th century DR , the cargo ship The Gentle Lady was delivering a shipment of farmer and merchant goods to a small coastal community in the Dragon Reach . Among the goods being carried by the ship was a crate full of cookware that included three rolling pins.
