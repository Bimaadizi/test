Highsun Liqueur was a druidic alcoholic drink found in the Western Heartlands , the lands along the River Chionthar , and the city of Baldur's Gate , as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Highsun Liqueur was brewed using various herbs picked during summer solstice – Kythorn 20 . Druids believed that this liquor cleansed the imbiber's spirit from the previous season's sins and sickness and was said to aid digestion.
