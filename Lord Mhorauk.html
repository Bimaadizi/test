Lord Mhorauk was a nobleman of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He came off as a younger lordling, implying he was younger than other lordlings.

Personality

He came off as impatient.

Relationships

He was known to the bodyguard of Lord Haelwing , Oskrul Meddanthyr .

History

Lord Mhorauk was at the Old King's Favorite eatery on one morning when Lord Haelwing got very drunk, and his wizardly bodyguard, Oskrul Meddanthyr, started drinking recklessly and talking about many subjects. He was not very courteous with the wizard, but got him to admit he didn't know who trapped the Nine into blueflame items .
