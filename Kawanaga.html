A kawanaga ( pronounced : / k ɑː w ɑː n ɑː g ɑː / kah-wah-nah-gah ) was a weapon unique to the continent of Kara-Tur .

Description

The kawanaga served as both a versatile tool and a weapon. It consisted of a length of rope at least 8 feet (2.4 meters) in length, featuring a grappling hook on one end and a weight on the other. Its functionality extended to climbing and combat, allowing for striking or entangling opponents with the hook or weight. Ninjas favored this weapon for its multi-purpose nature and ease of concealment, making it a popular choice among their arsenal.
