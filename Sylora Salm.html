Sylora Salm was a sorceress of Thay who served Szass Tam .

Relationships

Salm was Dahlia Sin'felle's rival.

History

She tricked Dahlia in Gauntlgrym which ended in the release of the fire primordial. She was tasked to construct a Dread Ring in the ashes of Neverwinter 's destruction from the eruption of the fire primordial.

Death

Sylora died at the hands of the mummy Jestry after being defeated by the trio Drizzt Do'Urden , Dahlia, and Artemis Entreri in the Year of the Reborn Hero, 1463 DR .
