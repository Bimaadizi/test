History

She remained in Talis to await the child wizard of the Talfirc prophecy .

When the Fellowship of the Dreaming Dragon passed through Talis in 1364 DR , she told them about the Talfirc prophecy and gave the mage Morhion Gen'dahar a magical ring set with a violet stone, and a book, On the Nature of Shadows . She was thought to be defeated by Shadowhounds, but no one could say for sure.
