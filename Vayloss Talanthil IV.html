Vayloss Talanthil IV , who was also called the Flamebringer , was the tenth and final Sorcerer - Arkhan of the Talanthil Dynasty who ruled the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Vayloss IV inherited the throne in −154 DR when his older brother Tallos IV was slain in battle during an assault on Dun-Tharos in Narfell . He reigned until −150 DR and died during Kossuth 's final assault of the Great Conflagration that utterly destroyed both Raumathar and Narfell.
