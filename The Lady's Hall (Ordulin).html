The Lady's Hall , which was also called Luck House by the non-devout, was a temple dedicated to Tymora located in the city of Ordulin in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Inhabitants

The temple was run by High Priestess Duatha Lindar . Duatha was assisted by 16 other priests and 24 followers.
