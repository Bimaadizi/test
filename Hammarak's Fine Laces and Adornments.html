Hammarak's Fine Laces and Adornments was a shop that sold feminine garments in the City of Splendors, Waterdeep , in the mid and late 14 th century DR .

Description

Hammarak's Fine Laces and Adornments was located in the third building west of Tchozal's Race , on Calamastyr Lane 's southern side. The shop took over its building's first floor with rental floors above. The third floor above Hammarak's was rented out to an all-female drinking and dining club called The Lace Harp .
