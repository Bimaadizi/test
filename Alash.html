Alash was a ghost who haunted the infamous Haunted Halls near the village of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the 15 th century DR .

Description

In his undead state, Alash retained the appearance of his mortal self. He was a light-skinned human man with sharp features, stubble, and long blond hair. He wore armor of the Zhentarim and wielded two hammers in battle, even as an incorporeal spirit.

Activities

In the 15 th century DR, Alash could be seen haunting the depths of the Haunted Halls, having perished long before. His equipment suggested that he was one of the Zhentish agents who once worked under Whisper in the mid-14 th century DR.
