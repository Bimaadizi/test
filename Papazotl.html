Papazotl ( pronounced : / p ɑː p ɑː ˈ z ɑː t ʊl / pah-pah-ZAH-tul ) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult . He was a primal spirit that took the form of an eblis .

Personality

Papazotl was conniving, shrewd, and imperious. He refused to listen to others, yet expected them to obey him.
