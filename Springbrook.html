The Springbrook was the river that flowed from the Elder Spires at the center of Ilighôn and ended in a shallow pool of the Springbrook Shallows .

Description

The Springbrook was born in the Elder Spires, in a plateau at the island's center. From the Oakenhall plateau, the Springbrook cascaded majestically off the mountains and ran west, approximately 6 miles (9.7 kilometers) until growing into the Springbrook Shallows.
