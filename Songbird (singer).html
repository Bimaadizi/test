The Songbird was the stage-name of a talented half-elf singer and actor popular in Turmish in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

She was known for her incredible beauty and grace.

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , the Songbird was drawing audiences with her new cycle of ballads revolving around the mythical Princess Arissaea . However, after every show, a few people who were in her audience were found to have vanished.
