Tharos was a kingdom that existed within the Great Dale from −987 DR to −946 DR .

History

Tharos was established when Nentyarch Thargaun Crell of the Crell Dynasty rose to power, naming the kingdom in honor of his father Tharos Crell . The fortress, and seat of power, of Dun-Tharos was established within the kingdom in −970 DR . Tharos later became Narfell when Thargaun assimilated all of the surrounding Nar tribes in -946 DR.
