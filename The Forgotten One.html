The Forgotten One , whose name was lost, was a hermit and wiseman who lived on Mount Pojah in Tabot .

The Forgotten One was one of only five wisemen recognized by the monks of Tabot around Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) as being a true oracle, being capable of predicting the future and raising heroes from the dead for good causes.
