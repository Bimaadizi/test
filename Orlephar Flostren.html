Orlephar Flostren was a native of Chancelgaunt who fortified the location that would eventually become Zhentil Keep .

History

Orlephar turned the lawless, and often raided, caravan camp that would be Zhentil Keep into a fortified stronghold. Flostren later sold his stronghold to twelve merchants.
