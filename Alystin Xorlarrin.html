Alystin Xorlarrin was a drow of House Xorlarrin and a twin daughter of the archmage of Q'Xorlarrin , Tsabrak Xorlarrin .

History

In 1484 DR , during the War of the Silver Marches , Tsabrak appointed Alystin and her twin brother Alak Xorlarrin to find and take control of the Fell Fortress in order to create an undead army. However, an adventuring party hired by the dwarf mystic Axelcrantz confronted and defeated them.
