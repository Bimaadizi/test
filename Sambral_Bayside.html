The Bayside was one of the districts of the city of Sambral .

Geography

The Bayside was the second largest of the city, and included the docks. The temples of Valkur and Umberlee were located in this district.

Inhabitants

The Bayside was home of the fishermen, pearl divers, sail makers, net weavers.
