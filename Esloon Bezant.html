Esloon Bezant was an exiled Red Wizard of Thay in Waterdeep in the early 1490s DR .

History

Esloon had been exiled from Thay a few years before and settled in Waterdeep, where he started a small criminal organization in the Dock Ward .

In 1492 DR , the Zhentarim courted him in order to try to add his gang of thugs to their ranks. However, Jalester Silvermane , under the orders of the Lords' Alliance , wanted to hire some adventurers to ruin this alliance.

Personality

Esloon was too smart to get caught doing something illegal.
