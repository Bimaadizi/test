Aedyn Graymantle was a moon elf ranger and author of Far from the Misty Hills who lived in the 15 th century DR .

Activities

The innovative ranger was known for braving the wilds of the North , as well as being a guard (both for caravans and as a bodyguard) and a guide.

History

Graymantle hailed from the Hidden Realm of Evereska and took on multiple professions throughout her lifetime. She later became an author and published the treatise known as Far from the Misty Hills .
