Fist Kyberos was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Kyberos was a middle-aged human man with a tanned complexion, brown eyes, and graying short but wavy hair. As an artillerist of the Flaming Fist, Kyberos was adorned in a set of leathers , decorated with the symbol of the Fist, and carried a heavy crossbow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , shortly before the armies of the Absolute attacked the Gate, Fist Kyberos was assigned to guard Lord Enver Gortash during his coronation as the city's Archduke. He considered the assignment to be his duty.
