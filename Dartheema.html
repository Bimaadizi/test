Dartheema was a young woman inhabiting Waterdeep , the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Tzarrakyn of the Company of Crazed Venturers married a merchant's daughter named Dartheema. Dartheema perished in childbirth less than a year after Tzarrakyn died while facing a beholder . The child was named Tzarrakyn the Younger in honor of his father.
