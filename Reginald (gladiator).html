Reginald was a human fighter and slave of Baeloth Barrityl .

Description

Reginald fought with two katanas , and wore chainmail and a helmet . He carried a potion of healing .

History

Reginald and his party were taken from a "shabby saloon" to the Black Pits . They were killed by the Champion of Baeloth's Pits and their party in the Black Pits arena.
