Radellus was a mage at some point before the turn of the 9 th century DR .

Personality

By the end of his life, Radellus had wearied of Amn's intrigues.

History

At some point before the turn of the 9 th century, Radellus decided to retire to a tower east of Sreve , and slew the previous owner, Justal Iroon , for it. During the battle, a local young boy of the village, Kurtal , snuck in and made out with three of Justal Iroon 's spellbooks, which he then used for practice. Radellus flung Justal Iroon's smoking body the length of Sreve's main street; it was then whisked into the millpond, killing all its fish.
