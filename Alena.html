Watchful Sister of the Earth Alena , who was also politely referred to as Sister Alena , was a priestess of Chauntea inhabiting Mistledale in the Dalelands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Sister Alena lived was a member of Mistledale's Council of Six where she represented the Abbey of the Golden Sheaf . Her fanaticism with regards to Chauntean rites and worship often lead her to clash with her fellows on the Council of Six.
