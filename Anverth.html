Anverth was a rock gnome living in Gnomengarde in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Like many other inhabitants of Gnomengarde, Anverth could reliably be found napping in the dormitory.

Abilities

Anverth, like all other gnomes in Gnomengarde had some training as a wizard in addition to his innate magical abilities.
