Sadrul was a sword belonging to the elven forager Feraih . After her death, it was bequeathed upon Kiril Duskmourn in 1374Â DR . It bore a minor enchantment.

Powers

Per Essam , the sword bore a minor enchantment, and it was the sharpest in all of Al Qahera . In actual combat, Kiril Duskmourn used it to slice a foe's steel scimitar in half.

History

Sadrul originally belonged to Feraih . After she returned from the Palace of the Purple Emperor , some curse she carried with her slew her, even though she'd done nothing other than look at it from outside; Essam gave her sword to Kiril Duskmourn , who was headed for the same palace, so that she may avenge Feraih if she came across the source of the curse.

Trivia

Per Essam, the sword was so sharp that Feraih once used it to part a man's dignity from his self-esteem (this was a joke).
