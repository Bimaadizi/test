Diyab was a divine spellcaster and devotee of the Bhaalspawn Sarevok who lived in the Realms during the midâˆ’14 th century DR .

Activities

Diyab worked under Zhalimar Cloudwulfe alongside his fellow agents Aasim , Alai , Gardush , and Naaman . Together they sought to advance Sarevok's plans and goals over those of the Iron Throne .

Possessions

He wielded a +1 warhammer and carried with him a number of potions and oils.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Diyab and his allies were sent by the Iron Throne to the city of Baldur's Gate to advise Thaldorn in his negotiations with Emissary Tar , who represented the Baldurian Council of Four . Right before the meeting, the Diyab and the others were defeated by Abdel Adrian and his companions.
