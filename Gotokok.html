Gotokok was an Iulutiun village known for its annual weapons trade fair.

Geography

Gotokok was in Alpuk , alongside the stream linking the Lugalpgotak Sea to Risuak Lake .

Trade

Every year, the tiny village of Gotokok hosted a sukkiruchit specifically for the trade of weapons. Arctic dwarves were known to travel from Novularond to this fair.
