Description

Bitter-ale was typically stored in kegs. It was notably dark in color and had a thick, strong, and unpleasant flavor. Its foul taste and potency could make even the stoutest of northern gold dwarves grimace.

History

Circa the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , many barrels of bitter-ale were stored in a small duergar stronghold under the Spine of the World , as Barud Barzam 's duergar were preparing to reclaim the Black Raven River , which long before itself was a duergar fortress in ages past.
