A pearl of the sirines was a magic item capable of granting wondrous powers related to the sea to its wielder.

Description

Regardless of their magical enchantments, these beautiful pearls were worth at least 1,000 gp .

Powers

This item granted the wielder the ability to breathe underwater as if they were breathing clean air and swim at an incredible rate while underwater, and also protected the wielder from a sirine 's poison.

To unlock and understand the powers of the pearl of the sirines , the wielder needed to clutch the pearl tightly and perform any action related to the pearl's powers.

Notable Owners

Khalil , a half-elven sha'ir who sailed the Great Sea near Zakhara , carried a pearl of the sirines .
