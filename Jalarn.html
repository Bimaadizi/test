Jalarn was the seneschal to Mrilla Malsander in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He had strong shoulders, and graceful, long strides.

Personality

Jalarn had a tendency to look over Mrilla Malsander to the crest of her family on her tall seat, which infuriated her, though his stance and bearing were otherwise beyond reproach.

Possessions

He had a silver rod of office.

Activities & Relationships

He served under Mrilla Malsander, for whom he showed constant contempt in numerous ways.
