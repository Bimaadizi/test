Kaggen Entreri was a young Warlock Knight in Vaasa in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In 1480 DR , Kaggen and his platoon were sent north of the Lugsaas Chain mountains to Frohm's Rest in order to investigate a relic of the Witch-King Zhengyi .

Personality

Kaggen was a natural leader and a forceful swordsman.

Relationship

Unknown to everyone, including himself, Kaggen was the last heir of the Dragonsbanes , being a secret son of Brianne Dragonsbane .
