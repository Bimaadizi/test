Adimond Kroskas was the leader of a Cult of the Dragon cell in Neverwinter circa 1480 DR .

He was enslaved by Valindra Shadowmantle , who also gained his ring of dragons . However, he agreed to her project of resurrecting the legendary dragon Lorragauth as a dracolich . However, the problems stalling the project put him under a lot of stress, being caught between the elven lich and his Cult subordinates.
