The Hr'a'cknir bracers were a set of magical bracers of githyanki origin. They channeled energy from the Astral Plane and offered numerous spell -like powers to their wearer.

Description

The bracers were crafted from "planar silver ". They allowed the wearer to tap into "latent astral energies" that permeated the Weave .

Powers

At will, the wearer of the Hr'a'cknir bracers could conjure a mage hand at pace much quicker than most spellcasters. Several times per day, they could also mentally move, manipulate, or forcefully launch objects or people, up to a range of 60 ft (18 m).
