Helms of the Horselords were enchanted Tuigan helmets found in the Hordelands and some select specialized export stores in Faerûn .

Powers

These helmets were simple in appearance and weighed 2 pounds (0.91 kilograms). They possessed several minor enchantments. Wearers of Helms of the Horselords were immune to ordinary and magical fear , gained slightly improved concentration, and gained a gift of almost uncanny intimidation.

History

Helms of the Horselords started appearing in Faerûn after the failed Tuigan invasion helmed by the self-proclaimed Emperor of All the World Yamun Khahan in the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR . Most Tuigan warriors wore simple fur caps or helmets looted off the enemy bodies, yet some were given magical Helms of the Horselords . Commanders of the Grand Army of the Tuigan were the lucky owners of the enchanted helmets that instilled terror in the hearts of enemies and follow troops alike.

One such helmets was available for purchase in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR from Okuzo's Imports store in Westgate .
