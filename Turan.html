Turan was one of the Blades of the Old Man as of 1374 DR . On the night of Tarsakh 25 , they became aware of an intruder in Sentinelspire . While they were looking out for the intruder, they came across Lewan and Ulaan . On the orders of Weilus , they tried to get them to return to their room; however, this led to a fight. Though at first he managed to hold Ulaan, she broke his hold and stabbed him; by the time he got up, they became swarmed by killoren , which slew him.
