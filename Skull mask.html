Skull masks , also known as white skull masks were magic masks crafted to resemble masquerade face-wear and provided the wearer with several beneficial enchantments.

Description

These enchanted masks were crafted to resemble a humanoid skull, white in color, with black paint around the eyes.

Powers

White Skull Mask transformed the wearer's face to appear like an actual skull. It granted the wearer increased morale and improved the defenses against diseases , fear , and paralysis , as well as making them immune to abilities and spells that drained life force. Additionally, the wearer was able to recognize any creature's true nature, whether living, dead, undead, or inanimate.

The mask did possess one negative effect, the undead were attracted to the wearer of the mask and prioritized attacking them above all other opponents.

Creation

White Skull Masks could be created by anyone proficient in crafting wondrous items. The creation involved casting of the following spells: deathwatch , detect magic , detect undead , negative plane protection , and remove fear .
