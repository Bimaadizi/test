Center Street was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Located in southern Zhentil Keep within the Foreign Quarter on the southern bank of the River Tesh , Center Street was a small road in the western section of the Foreign Quarter that derived its name from running parallel in between Teshwave Street in the north and Twilight Street to the south. Center Street originated on Mace Way and merged with the major intersection to the east with Zhent Avenue .
