De'Arnise signet rings were enchanted rings worn by the members of the Arnise noble family in Amn and bounded to them by blood.

Powers

These signets were imbued with a variety of protective magics. It shielded the wearer with a +2 defensive enchantment, gave them resistance to magical and humane flames, and improved chances of recovering or sharking off effects, afflictions, and increased reaction.

History

De'Arnise signet rings were given out to members of the Arnise family when they reached adulthood. In the 14 th century DR , one such ring was gifted to a one-time companion of Abdel Adrian , Nalia de'Arnise , by her father as she became an adult. She never parted with the ring.
