Rhiiman the Glorious (real name Rhiiman Obarskyr ) was the fifth king of Cormyr . He reigned from 66 DR until his death in 79 DR . He was the first to push back the borders of the forest, and killed the last great dragon of the Wyvernwater .

Relatives

His namesake was his uncle , who abandoned Cormyr to go back to Impiltur when his father Faerlthann 's mother ( Suzara Obarskyr ) got tired of his grandfather Ondeth . He was the youngest of Faerlthann's four sons but was third in their succession to the throne after he outlived Imlon , Eskruis , and his nephew Bryndar . He was succeeded by his last remaining brother, Embrus .
