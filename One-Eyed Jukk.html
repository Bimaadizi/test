One-Eyed Jukk was a fence of stolen goods inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

One-Eyed Jukk worked out of the Bloody Fist tavern located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward where Presper Street and Shrimp Alley joined Snail Street . He dealt exclusively in stolen weapons, armor , and fine metalwork.
