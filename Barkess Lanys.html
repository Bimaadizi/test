Barkess Lanys was one of the founding members of the Knights of the Sword Coast during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Barkess founded the Knights of the Sword Coast with his close friend Vhonna Deepdell in the Year of the Crown, 1351 DR . Their goal was to rid the Sword Coast of evil whenever they came across it during their travels. Barkess remained with the Knights until his death in the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR .
