Wiri Fleagol was a slave of the fire giants in Ironslag sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Wiri was captured by fire giants and taken to Ironslag where she worked as a miner together with many other gnomes and dwarves , overseen by salamander guards.
