Roadaeron Lorndessar was the second and fourth King of the Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Roadaeron became King in the Year of the Old Giant, 1111 DR , when his granduncle King Sarvyn "the Old" died. He ruled until the Year of the Twelverule, 1117 DR , before his throne was usurped by his Mage Royal Alzurth . Roadaeron lived in exile for over 40 years before reclaiming his rightful throne right before his death in the Year of the Prancing Centaur, 1162 DR .

Relationships

He had seven children during his life. His third son Blaervaer would rule as King.
