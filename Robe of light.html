Robes of light were enchanted robes that protected its wearers from darkness.

Description

These soft yellow robes were weaved out of the finest silk .

Powers

These aptly named magic pieces of clothing were imbued with a permitting aura of light that illuminated the area of 15 feet (4.6 meters) around them.

History

Robes of light gained popularity on the Sword Coast , in the cities like Neverwinter and Daggerford in the late 14 th century DR . One of these robes was sold by Boonmoppet the imp from the Western Farmlands .
