Cattl was the shaman of the Nexalan people of Maztica and the grandson of Tecco , the Prophet of Zaltec , who lived in the late 11 th century DR .

History

After Tecco, the Prophet of Zaltec, received divine commands from the holy pillar of Zaltec to find the southern valley of their future and from there to conquer the world, the Nahuat tribe wandered the lands for many years. The exact number of years the people who would become the Nexala wandered the True World in search of the prophesied land was unrecorded by historians. But in the Year of the Restless, 1099 DR , Cattl, Tecco's grandson, led the tribe to the Valley of Nexal . The lush green valley became home to the Mazticans, eventually becoming the Nexalan empire of the True World.
