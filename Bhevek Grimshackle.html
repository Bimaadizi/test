Bhevek Grimshackle was a halfling jailer at Grimshackle Jail near Phlan in 1489 DR .

Personality

Bhevek and his brother were only interested in money. He was filthy and bedraggled like his brother.

Activities

Bhevek and his brother Ukharim Grimshackle opened Grimshackle Jail, where they took criminals whom they ransomed to the highest bidder.

History

In 1489 DR, Bhevek permitted, for some money, an adventuring party to interrogate Gray Patriots 's member Rathene Fel . Afterward, the adventurers dealt with a planned prison break.
