Frostbrand , or frost brand , weapons were designed to fight creatures with an affinity for fire.

Powers

These weapons were particularly effective against creatures with a tie to the Elemental Plane of Fire . Magical flames could be extinguished half of the time by using a frostbrand . If ambient temperatures fell below freezing, the frostbrand was capable of emitting light just like a torch .

Frostbrands also provided protection as a ring of fire resistance .
