Haelond was an abandoned mining outpost located in the mountains of Gontal .

History

Haelond was a mining outpost run by the Elkannath family of the Twelvefold .

In 1469 DR , Haelond was abandoned when it was overrun by mysterious monsters that swarmed out from the Underdark .
