Heldon was a farming village in outlying Athalantar until the Year of the Flaming Forests, 224 DR , later the site of an armed camp.

Description

The city had a smithy, a mill, and at least two towers of different heights.

Geography

To the south of Heldon was a curve of the Delimbiyr River . At a higher elevation than the settlement proper, there was a meadow. A high ravine separated it from Heldon, and there was a cave on the end of that ravine. West of Heldon were the Horn Hills .

Trade

Sheep were raised around Heldon.

History

In 210Â DR , Prince Elthryn Aumar married Amrythale Goldsheaf of Heldon. Of note, their son Elminster Aumar was born two years later.

The village was razed by Undarl , riding the red dragon Glathkarlarrogh , on behalf of the Magelords of Athalantar , in the Year of the Flaming Forests, 224 DR ; he was there to slay Elthryn and his kin, and slew everyone in sight for good measure. There was only one survivor: Elthryn's son, Elminster, whom Undarl left for dead early on.

After its destruction, a palisade wall was built around a new armed camp at some point on or prior to Year of Bloodflowers, 234 DR .

Notable Locations

There was a shrine to Chauntea south of Heldon.

Of note, there was a meadow atop the village, and a cave hidden somewhere under that meadow, known to almost none outside Elminster.
