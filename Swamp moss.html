Swamp moss was an aptly named species of medicinal moss that grew in the Mere of Dead Men that was used by inhabitants of the hamlet of West Harbor in the 14 th entry DR .

Abilities

Swamp moss's healing properties were quite potent for a simple plant. When imbibed, the moss acted as a weak potion of healing .

History

Circa the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Brother Merring of West Harbor used swamp moss to heal the hamlet's inhabitants.
