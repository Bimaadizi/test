Torin Crysst was a refugee child who found shelter in the Rivington neighborhood of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Torin was a human boy with a light skin tone. Blue eyes and short blonde hair. He usually wore a simple brown tunic .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate , displacing countless refugees in the process along the way. Torin Crysst and his relatives found their way to the Rivington district outside the city walls, but, like all other refugees, they were refused entrance.
