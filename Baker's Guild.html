The Baker's Guild was an organization of united, skilled bakers that operated within the city of Waterdeep . They were an extremely wealthy and influential guild that was renowned for the high-quality goods they produced. . The guild had the interests of other businesses looking for investments, which increased the influence of the guild and individual bakers with other guilds and merchants).

Their livery consisted of white cloaks and hats highlighted with a light blue chevron on the left shoulder or brim.

Activities

In addition to the tasty breads and pastries the guild produced, which formed the majority of many Waterdhavians' diets, they garnered the attention of a great many thieves from throughout the city.

As a result, the guild hired dedicated "runners" to ensure their orders were safely delivered to their intended destinations. In addition, these private escorts acted as bodyguards and even chased down anyone who attempted to intercept their wares. While they had no "official" authority within the city, they were often given relative free reign by the City Watch .

Membership

Guild membership cost 10 gp , with an annual fee of 5 sp per month. Entrance into the guild had to be approved upon application by the Master and the "Master Bakers" (the ten most-senior members of the guild).
