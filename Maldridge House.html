Maldridge House was a stately old mansion located on Mac Intyre Path in Ravens Bluff in the late 15 th century DR . The mansion was best known as Jack Ravenwild 's temporary residence after he rescued Seila Norwood from enslavement by drow in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR . The mansion burned to the ground that same year during a drow attempt to recapture Jack.
