The Bellows of Milil was a giant magical pipe organ built into the main temple of Milil in Athkatla .

Description

More than a simple musical instrument , the Bellows was built into the structure of the temple. Playing it caused the whole building to resonate, and some of the sounds it produced could be heard as far as the harbor.

This organ was so large that various creature dwellt within, such as wind walkers and volts . Priests of the clergy, who were the only persons allowed to touch it, also walked into the instrument for cleaning and maintenance purposes.

Rumors

Although most information about the Bellows were kept secret by the temple various rumors circulated regarding the presence of safe rooms and teleportation portals inside it, as well as the presence of strange creatures within it.

Rumors also pretended that a wind dragon roamed the pipes and rooms of the organ.
