The Tethir Road was a trade road in the Lands of Intrigue that was one of the oldest structures in Tethyr and Amn . The road connected Murann to Ormath .

History

It was built by King Dalagar "Longwalker" of Andlath from 230 DR to 239 DR to expand trade south of his kingdom.

From the Year of Slaughter, 1090 DR , this road also acted as a border between Amn and Tethyr.
