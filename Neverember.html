Neverember was a noble family that lived in Waterdeep and Neverwinter in the 15 th century DR .

Residence

The Neverember family lived in both cities of Waterdeep. and Neverwinter .

History

According to some conflicting records, Nasher Alagondar , king of Neverwinter in the late years of the 14 th century, had a bastard son named Vers Never , who left Neverwinter and settled in Waterdeep, marrying Mirtria Ember and founding the Neverember family.

Around 1468 DR , the Neverember family rose to prominence when Dagult Neverember became first the Open Lord of Waterdeep and then subsequently the Lord Protector of Neverwinter.
