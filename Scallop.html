Scallops were a type of edible bivalve mollusk.

Usages

In the city of Neverwinter , scallops could be found cooked wrapped in bacon .

Scallops were served at Sharkey's Bar & Grill in the city of Ravens Bluff , priced at a single silver per six scallops. Another seafood eatery in the Bluff that offered scallops was the Painted Boat Restaurant . A single serving of scallops was sold for ten coppers .

A giant variety was known to exist and their shells were used to craft large trays.
