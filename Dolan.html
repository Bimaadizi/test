Dolan was a no-good trapper, brother to Doogal and Digby , from the town of Lonelywood in Icewind Dale in the late 13 th century DR .

Description

Dolan was a human male with light skin and light chestnut hair. He was somewhat dim-witted and had some sadistic tendencies. Just like his brothers, Dolan overindulged in drinking "fire water" but was often cut off because when drunk, he was a "crazy bas-tard."

Possessions

He wore a set of simple leather armor and carried a shortsword .
