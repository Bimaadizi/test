Ssessimyth was a powerful kraken in the Sea of Fallen Stars in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Somehow, Ssessimyth entered into symbiosis with the mythal of the ruined Netherese enclave of Sakkors . When the Princes of Shade Rivalen Tanthul and Brennus Tanthul restored the flying city, Rivalen managed to enslave Ssessimyth. On Uktar 11 of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Rivalen ordered him to attack the docks of Yhaunn in order to distract the city's guards.
