The Song of Kaudies was a powerful song that became imbued with magic if performed by a bard .

Content

When performed, the song's magic had a cointoss of a chance to negate effects of sound-based spells and abilities such as silence , great shout , command , and others.

History

By the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , The Song of Kaudies was performed by some of the most experienced bards. It was heard being performed in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale and the village of Kuldahar .
