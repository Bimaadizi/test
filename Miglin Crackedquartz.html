Miglin Crackedquartz was a deep gnome in Blingdenstone .

Activities

When not acting as a guide, Miglin wandered the city making, notes about the caverns and passages.

History

In 1479 DR , Kargien Dissengulp sent Miglin to act as a guide for an adventuring party he'd hired in tunnels toward the Wormwrithings .

Personality

Miglin was a gruff and acerbic individual. He was more interested in gemstones than people, even offering to the people that employed him reports about the various properties of every rock he saw.
