The Dale Council was a group of six elected councilors, headquartered in Highcastle , that governed High Dale within the Dalelands . They were chaired by a 7th member, known as the High Constable , who was elected by the members of the council.The well-organized council was responsible for law and order within, and defense of, the dale; trade regulation; and relations with other nations.
