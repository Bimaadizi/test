Akota was a mysterious country somewhere beyond the western shores of Zakhara .

Activities

Akota exported gold , slaves , wood, and exotic goods to the Land of Fate through the city of Akota to the city of Tajar .

It had its own language called Akotan , which was also spoken in Zakhara's western settlements that were conducting trade with exotic Akota.
