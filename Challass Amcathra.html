Lord Challas Amcathra was the patriarch of the noble Amcathra family of Waterdeep throughout the late 13 th and over half the 14 th centuries DR .

Description

Challas Amcathra used potions of longevity to grant himself and his wife unusually long lives, achieving the milestone of becoming a centenarian.

Relationships

He had six older brothers, all of whom died to one unfortunate circumstance or another.

Lord Challas was married to Lady Miri , and the father of nine children as well as the grandfather of nineteen grandchildren. Most notable of his sires were Arilos , his eldest son and heir apparent, and his youngest Mourngrym ,who went on to become lord of Shadowdale .

History

During his youth, Challas Amcathra was one of the more promising adventurers that traveled the Sword Coast . But around the Year of the Talking Skull, 1293 DR , he was forced to abandon that lifestyle and take on the role as lord of the Amcathra family.

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the aged Challas and Lady Miri traveled to Shadowdale to visit their son Mourngrym. Unfortunately, their visit preceded the invasion of Shadowdale by the Zhentarim Occupying Army . The Waterdhavian nobles were imprisoned by the Zhents within the dungeons of the Twisted Tower of Ashaba for a time.
