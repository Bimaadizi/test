Borth was a small coastal town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Borth was found along the coast of the Strait of Alaron in the northeastern region of the kingdom of Corwell in southern Gwynneth. Borth sat just north of Kingsbay .

History

Borth fell into ruin following the conquest of Gwynneth and expulsion of the humans by the fey of Sarifal . By the late 15 th century DR , it was occupied by escaped Ffolk slaves , and was one of only two such free human ports on the island (the other being nearby Kingsbay ).
