Karr Nevrim was a half-orc rebel and a cell leader of the Nashers in Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Karr had a taste for fine liquors and owned a bottle of Ferguson-Mosiondz Single Malt .

History

Following the defeat of Nashers second-in-command Karzov in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Karr's cell continued to hold out from their base in the sewers below the old Merchant Square in the Tower District . He had underhanded dealings with the Many-Arrows orcs who had occupied the district that threatened the rest of the city. When Captain Janros learned of this (via reports carried by a slain courier), he dispatched an adventurer to invade their sewer base and slay Karr.
