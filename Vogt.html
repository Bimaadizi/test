History

In the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , Vogt and the Company of the Dawn took on the job of escorting some friends and acquaintances of their leader Thurbrand through Spiderhaunt Wood . The journey was devastating to their company, leading to many of their deaths, leaving only Vogt, Thurbrand, and Isaac as survivors.

After arriving at their destination in Shadowdale, the three remaining members of the company returned to the city of Arabel in Cormyr .
