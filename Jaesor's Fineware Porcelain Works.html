Jaesor's Fineware Porcelain Works was a shop located in Neverwinter in the 14 th century DR .

Location

Jaesor's was located in the northwestern end of the Blacklake District , next to Dannar's Mechanical Marvels in the intersection between Hantor's Lane at the Street Of Storms .

Description

Jaesor's was a shop specializing in porcelain works. This shop was popular among the well-known families of Neverwinter in the late years of the 14 th century.

Prices

A custom-paint plate cost 20 gp , while a matched-set of 20 cost 45 gp.
