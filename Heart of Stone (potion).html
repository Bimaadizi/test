Heart of Stone was a variety of potion formulated by the green hag known as Auntie Ethel , from within her cavernous lair beneath the Gnarled Teahouse of the Putrid Bog .

Description

The fluid of this potion continually separated and amalgamated from itself in a rhythmic fashion. Small flecks of malachite were found within.

Powers

Any being that drank this draught became highly resistant to both mundane and magical poisons .

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , only one bottle of the Heart of Stone concoction was known to exist, found in Ethel's Ancient Abode .
