Nascent Owens was a zombie servant reanimated by Cherish Hollow , the author of The Amiable Art of Necromancy , sometime in or before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Nascent Owens was turned into an undead sometime before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . His mistress, Cherish Hollow, believed that the shambling creature derived pleasure from giving her backrubs, serving tea , and eating the brains of her enemies. Despite Nascent remembering nothing of lis life nor having any sentience, Cherish believed that the zombie was a worthy company in her loneliness.
