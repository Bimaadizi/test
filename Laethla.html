Laethla was a cook at the Eel Revealed in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

She was known by name to Arclath Delcastle .

History

In Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR Laethla was testing a new condiment, and she asked Arclath for his opinion. He liked its peppery spiciness, and thought it should result in plenty of drinks being ordered.
