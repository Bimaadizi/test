Fist Eostre was a mage member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Eostre was a human woman with long curly dark hair, black eyes, and a pale complexion. She wore a wizardly robes uniform of the Flaming Fist and a circlet , and carried a simple quarterstaff .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Eostre was stationed in the Basilisk Gate 's barracks and prison, serving under Blaze Liara Portyr and Manip Seyama . She was tasked with cataloguing requisitioned produce, which was always, to her frustration, "organized by a rabid worg ."
