The seal of guidance was a unique enchanted token crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Saarvin in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

It was a silver chip into which Saarvin had carved a notch to commemorate each of his daring hunts into and beyond the Spine of the World .

Powers

The magic of the seal of guidance could be used to draw the attention of Saarvin's enemies, which he could use to his advantage.
