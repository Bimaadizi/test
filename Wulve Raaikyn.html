Wulve Raaikyn was a halfling thief inhabiting Waterdeep , the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Wulve was a recent addition to Waterdeep as of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR . He came to the city posing as a textiles trader, but really only wanted to pocket some gold and magic items before returning east with a wagon full of turnips . Wulve often targeted sleeping adventurers and had multiple escape routes planned in the rare event that he was caught picking one of their pockets.
