Gower was a male dwarf scout living in Waterdeep during the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR .

He was an acquaintance of Durnan and Khelben and guided them, plus Elminster Aumar and the mortal Kelemvor , to a passage to the Fugue Plane located in Undermountain .
