Norlarram was a Red Wizard of Thay in the mid-14 th century DR .

Personality

He was a habitual smoker, who was strongly critical of the intellect of his fellow conspirators.

Possessions

He had at least one comically large (foot-long) cigar, and owned at least one object shaped like a petrified, cupped human hand that served him as an ashtray, but would serve in other functions in a pinch.

Relationships

He was part of a cabal together with Thaltar Glaervar , Dlamaerztus , Quaerlesz , Iyrtaryld , Olorus , and six other Red Wizards .

History

In 1369 DR , Norlarram was smoking at a meeting with several of his fellow Red Wizards, where Iyrtaryld demonstrated an illusion of his newest magical development, the Hungry Mouth . While he got some feedback on it, the table at which they sat was hurled upwards by a colossal entity made of stone which smashed Norlarram, killing him instantly. That was the Simbul 's doing.
