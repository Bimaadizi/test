Eiruidin Ondaru was a Pasha from upland Calimshan in the mid-13 th century DR .

History

Eiruidin Ondaru had taken a boy, Aralagath Tarsil , for a favored pet, whom he kept collared and leashed until his own murder by his court wizard , Ongraunnathan , in 1275 DR . After his death, the wizard used magic to make him appear to be alive, and reigned in his name until he was unmasked in 1286 DR .
