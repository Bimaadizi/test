Bytter nuts were a variety of nut that could be found in the jungles of Chult .

Usage

The nuts could be crushed to form a compound that had healing properties when applied to an open wound. It was useful for treating unconscious patients.

History

The druid Arvo of Ravens Bluff noted the healing properties of bytter nuts in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .
