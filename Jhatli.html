Jhatli was a boy in Nexal .

History

In 1361 DR , he was one of the survivors after the Night of Wailing . He and other young boys decided to act as scouts for the column of refugees, checking the advance of the Viperhands army. Jhatli alone survived to report the rapid advance of the monsters.
