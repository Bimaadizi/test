Mari bint Sadita ran a small stall within the Grand Bazaar of Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Personality

Mari was quiet and her pleasant attitude made customers feel at ease.

Activities

As a newer addition to the Grand Bazaar, Mari only had a small stall, which consisted of a carpet loaded with the coffee and tea she sold. Her quality wares attracted more and more customers in the few weeks since she had set up shop. She offered free samples to prospective customers.
