Templar's Justice was magical cold iron dwarven waraxe of the Church of the Silver Flame found in Eberron during the mid-1480s DR of Faerûn .

Powers

The waraxe was filled with holy and good energy and blasted foes with radiant light energy. The cold iron made it more effective against demons and some other types of monsters.

History

The axe was said to deliver the wrath of Tira Miron , champion of the Silver Flame . It was given to adventurers who faced the Overlord Spinner of Shadows in the Spinner's Prison before she escaped.
