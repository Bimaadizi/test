Dawnmaster Welkinglory was one of four devoted leaders of the Rosymorn Monastery in the Western Heartlands , who died before the late 15 th century DR and the monastery's fall to the githyanki invaders.

Description

Dawnmaster Welkinglory's stained-glass depiction showed a humanoid with dark skin, a pious look, and Lathanderian garb. The depiction was shown wielding a masterfully crafted ceremonial sword .

History

Dawnmaster Welkinglory was one of four high-ranking members of the clergy who served at Rosymorn Monastery sometime before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . The other three were Seed , Stockhold , and Vaseid . All four were depicted on a circular stained-glass mosaic in an upper central chamber of the monastery.
