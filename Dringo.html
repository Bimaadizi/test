Oyster Boy Dringo was a messenger hound caretaker who worked for the Sword Coast Couriers in the Rivington neighborhood of the Outer City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Oyster Boy was a young male rock gnome with a light skin tone, blue eyes, and curly light chestnut hair.

History

Oyster Boy Dringo was fired to man the kennel outside the Sword Coast Couriers office at some point before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . He was put to work under an angry dog trainer named Mar'hyah , who verbally abused Dringo and physically the Couriers' three dogs: Scratch , Gustav , and Zola . Mar'hyah's disdain for Dringo was such that she requested Postmaster Danzo Arkwright to dock the boy's pay. The adventuring band known as the Absolute Adversaries , who might've rescued and adopted Scratch after his courier master was slain, may have had confronted Mar'hyah over her cruelty.
