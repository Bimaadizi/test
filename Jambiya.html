Jambiyas were sharply curved daggers common to Calimshan and Zakhara .

Etymology

The word for "dagger" in Alzhedo was jambiya , yet when most people used the term "jambiya", they were referring specifically to the style of curved dagger found among Calishites and Zakharans .

Description

A jambiya was a sharply curved dagger, almost like a hook. They tended to leave long, narrow wounds in an opponent.

Popularity

Nearly every Calishite male bore a jambiya at his side.
