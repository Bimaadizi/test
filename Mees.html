OccupationSteward

Mees was the steward of Escanor Tanthul that lived in Thultanthar during the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Mees was quite skilled at understanding the wants of Prince Escanor with only the slightest hint or gesture.

History

Mees, Rapha , and the rest of Escanor's attendants were present with the Prince of Shade when he met with Galaeron Nihmedu , Vala Thorsdotter , Aris , and Malik el Sami at Villa Dusari on Mirtul 9 in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
