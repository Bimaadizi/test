Jherald's General Store was a trading post found in the village of Blackfeather Bridge in Featherdale .

Activities

Jherald's store was actually a trio of warehouses that served as a general store and trading post in the dale. Jherald provided an impressive array of goods and services, but charged steep prices. Travelers in a pinch had little alternative options unless they wanted to press further down the road to Ordulin .
