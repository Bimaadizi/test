Reinhar VIII was a king of the Arkaiuns of Dambrath .

Relationships

The man who would rule as Reinhar VIII was the first son of King Reinhar VII . He was the father of Reinhar IX and at least four other sons.

History

Reinhar VIII was born in the Year of the Druid's Wrath, 717 DR . He took his father's throne and name upon the latter's death in 750 DR . Reinhar VIII died in 801 DR , passing the throne to his son.
