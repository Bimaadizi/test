Vorinar Gall was a Red Wizard of Thay that joined Droic Orgul 's raid on Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

History

Vorinar searched the Burial Glen of Myth Drannor for the remains of Earynspieir Ongluth , accompanied by Ezkir Taromor and a number of undead minions and living mercenaries, coming into opposition with the Cormanthor Elves .
