Old Pharaoh's Ruin was a variety of expensive magical spirits named after the Pharaohs of Mulhorand , available in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The enchanted drink was on the menu at the Yawning Portal tavern of Waterdeep, stashed away in Durnan 's cabinet. One serving of Old Pharaoh's Ruin was priced at ten gold coins .
