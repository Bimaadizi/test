Jarg was a mercenary of the Zhentarim that operated out of their hideout within caves deep beneath the Risen Road on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

He was exceptionally confident and even cocky about his skill as an archer .

Possessions

Jarg wielded a pair of hand crossbows , one in either hand.

History

When the Zhentarim hideout was ordered to be demolished in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Jarg was among those setting up explosives.
