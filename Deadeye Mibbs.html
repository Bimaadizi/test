Fist "Deadeye" Mibbs was a member of the Flaming Fist and who served the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

"Deadeye" was a human man with light skin, light brown eyes eyes, and brown wavy hair. He wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist – a set of leathers adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia, typical for crossbowmen of the organization.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , "Deadeye" Mibbs was stationed just outside one of the gates between the Lower City of Baldur's Gate and its more affluent Upper City . His and the fellow Fists' job was stopping rabble from entering the Upper City, allowing only those with much coin through. The Fists managed a slow-moving queue of rich and poor – city folk trying to return home or just find safety behind the second set of walls.
