Shoce was an early knight of Neverwinter and the fifth of the Nine knights who served Lord Halueth Never . They were the basis for the Neverwinter Nine .

History

Shoce survived battles against three dragons , but died due to the treachery of a fourth dragon. Afterward, a statue of Shoce and his sword stood guard in Halueth's tomb.
