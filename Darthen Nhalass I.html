Darthen Nhalass I was the fifth Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Darthen I inherited the throne when his brother, Umathar IV , died in −780 DR . He ruled until his death in −749 DR .

Relationships

Darthen I was the secondborn son of Umathar III . His firstborn son and heir was Shendar .
