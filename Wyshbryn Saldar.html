Wyshbryn Saldar was a wererat spy of the Riverside Rats gang, who dwelt in the Wheloon Prison of Cormyr during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

As a wererat, Wyshbryn had black fur.

History

Wyshbryn lived in Wheloon Prison with his fellow Riverside Rats until they were forced from their hideout by the Night Brigade under Storna Harnwood , who turned it into a Temple of Shar . After being driven outside the prison, Wyshbryn spied on the Night Brigade's foul Grand Arcanist Darstil Vurusk , who performed horrible experiments upon helpless prisoners, and eventually sent an attack to kill him and end his madness.
