Acid tipped dart were small enchanted thrown weapons that dealt corrosive damage to their targets.

Powers

These darts were small enough to be easily concealed. Upon closer exemption, carved lines of power could be seen on each of the missiles. When they reached the target, these darts released a burst of acid .

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , acid tipped dart were used by druid Myrvek of Auril who resided in the secluded village of Andora , deep in Icewind Dale 's wilderness.
