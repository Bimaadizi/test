A short sword , (or shortsword ) , was a light, piercing melee weapon.

Description

This sword was a smaller version of the longsword , but longer than a dagger .

Tactics

The short sword was designed for thrusting and piercing. It was a common weapon for use in the off-hand when dual-wielding.
