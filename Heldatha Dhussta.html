Heldatha Dhussta was a female human and a Painbearer of Ilmater . She was the Revered Mother of the House at the Towers of Willful Suffering in Eshpurta , Amn , in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR through at least early 1370 DR, the Year of the Tankard
