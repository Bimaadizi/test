Ambaeril Moonmistarr was a powerful wizard inhabiting Saerloon in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Personality

Ambaeril was a gentle individual who mostly kept to herself.

Activities

She was often seen in the company of faerie dragons or pegasi . Ambaeril aided those in need and fought evil at every opportunity.
