Erned Stoutblade was a member of the Order of the Gauntlet during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

In 1491 DR , having heard that the Iceshield orcs had attacked many farms in the Dessarin Valley , he decided to go to the Dellmon farm to help Kerbin Dellmon to fight them off. With the lucky arrival of some adventurers, the Dellmons managed to defend the farm.
