The University of Definitive Archwizardry was a prestigious magic school in Eileanar that taught all magical fields with excellence.

Services

Unlike other institutions, the University did not specialize in a single field of mythal but instead taught all three. It became regarded as the best school of magic in Netheril , as well as the most expensive. The archwizard Baden served as the Dean of Teachers, and taught variator magic alongside Aleman . Cheberk and Tudor taught inventive spells, while Oudinot and Daa handled mentalist students.
