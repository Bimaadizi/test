Baldur's Grape was a variety of quality red wine , produced in the city of Baldur's Gate , as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Baldur's Grape was a quality beverage with a powerful velvety taste with a pleasant tart finish. This wine was good enough to grace the Grand Duke 's dinner table. Baldur's Grape was packaged in ornate oblong green glass bottles.

History

Bottles of Baldur's Grape could be found across the city of Baldur's Gate as of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
