Alana Silvershield was a noblewoman from the wealthy Silvershield family living in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century.

Personality

Relationships

Alana was the younger daughter of Torlin Silvershield and Evelyn Silvershield .

History

Alana was born in the Silvershield family and lived in the Silvershield estate in Baldur's Gate. In the Year of the Narthex Murders, 1482 DR , Alana was seven and she was living in the estate's nursery with her brother Entar .

She had children , but some of them left Baldur's Gate in the 1470s DR and there was no information about where they had gone or what had become of them.
