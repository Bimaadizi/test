Personality

His fierce reputation matched his bristling red beard. He appeared to have a limitless amount of energy as he bustled about his tavern.

Activities

Felzoun owned a tavern named after himself called Felzoun's Folly which was located in Waterdeep's Trades Ward . Unbeknownst to most, Felzoun served as a link to the fence Haerlit Thomm .

Possessions

He always carried a pair of throwing axes hidden beneath his apron .
