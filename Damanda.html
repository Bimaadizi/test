Damanda was a Talontar blightlord vampire operating in the Rawlinswood as of 1373 DR . She was the right hand of the Talontyr , whom she served directly.

Description

She was a thin woman in form-fitting black armor, so tightly articulated that it might as well be a second skin. Her nails were midnight black, matching her lacquered armor.

Abilities

Being a vampire, she was capable of extracting energy or vitality with her attacks, and of using a gaze that crushed the volition of the living. Furthermore, she could summon to herself an aura of biting, stinging vermin. Her vampiric strength granted her the ability to tear down walls in a pinch. She had some meager capability to cast arcane spells, allowing her to trigger some magical items.

History

In 1373 DR , she was assigned to escort Fallon and Araluen to The Close , after Anammelech perished. Fallon missed the hour of their meeting, so she sought him out in Under-Tharos . They finally met outside one of the paths to Under-Tharos; after Araluen 's touch annihilated her underling Absalme , she decided to keep Fallon alive, so that he could lead Araluen.

After taking refuge in an old hermitage for the day, the team led by Marrec caught up with them. After her insect armor was slain by the priest's petrifying gaze and Fallon betrayed them, slaying Lex , she fled together with Bonehammer by using a scroll that Lex had created ahead of time. Even in this refuge, she was soon cornered by the adventurers, and after they destroyed Bonehammer, she fled to The Close, together with the Rotting Man.

She aided him in the fight against Marrec's party by activating a lingering control over Ususi Manaallin , which persisted until Elowen slew the vampire with one of the powers of the sword Dymondheart .

Relationships

She had four vampire underlings of her own, named Absalme, Bonehammer, Ezekial and Lex.
