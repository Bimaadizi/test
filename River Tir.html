The River Tir was a river in Rashemen that served as a crucial trading route for the Rashemaar people.

Description

Like the great River Rasha , the Tir was a long and broad-bodied waterway that vital for transportation and the facilitation of commerce in Rashemen. The flow of the river picked up tempo and became more rapid as it neared the town of Taporan .

Geography

The River Tir originated in the High Country mountain range in eastern Rashemen and went southwest until it joined with the River Rasha and emptied into Lake Tirulag .
