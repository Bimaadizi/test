Xianthrope was a beholder inhabiting the Shining South during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Xianthrope lived within the Granuin Forest in Luiren , land of the halflings . The beholder used its consider power to hold dominion over many of the other monsters inhabiting the forest.
