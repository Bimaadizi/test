Bez was a goblin warrior and follower of the Absolute that lived in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

She had a particular fondness and interest in the giant spiders that Dror Ragzlin kept in the depths of the Shattered Sanctum.

History

Bez was present at the Shattered Sanctum in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , when the leaders of the Shattered Sanctum began searching for individuals aboard a crashed nautiloid they believed were True Souls of their goddess.
