Amrik Vanthampur ( pronounced : /ˈ ɑː m r ɪ k ˈ v ɑː n θ ɑː m p ɛ r / AM-rik VAN-tham-per ) was the second youngest son of Thalamra Vanthampur of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Amrik was born to Thalamra Vanthampur and her second husband.

By the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , Amrik ran a moneylending business in the tavern known as the Low Lantern .
