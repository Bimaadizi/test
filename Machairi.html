Machairi was an invading horned devil in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

History

The devil was pulled to Myth Drannor via a random portal caused by the malfunctioning mythal . It proceeded to menace the northern part of the city near Shaundakul's Throne and Ildrannath's Tower .
