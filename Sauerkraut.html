Sauerkraut was a type of savory dish found in the Realms .

Description

This dish consisted of cabbage that had been thinly sliced and fermented in salt , lending it a taste that was pleasantly sour.

Availability

Jars of sauerkraut, weighting 1 pound (0.45 kilograms), were sold across the Realms via Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .

In the city of Hillsfar , sauerkraut was served with roast meat , mashed potatoes , and cheap wine .
