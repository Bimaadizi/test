Dien Nyu was a fortress in Laothan in Malatra .

Geography

It stood in the north of Laothan, at the edge of the jungle and on the border with T'u Lung , facing Chedoru .

Description

It constructed of wood and was the largest border fortress of its kind in Laothan. Its defenders were soldiers of the Nuyen clan.

History

Dien Nyu was constructed in the year 594 on a local calendar on the orders of Nuyen Sing , with the aim of discouraging raiders.

Circa 1357 DR , the rebellious Prince Nuyen Phi was covertly recalling the soldiers from Dien Nye in readiness for his planned confrontation with Thok Lian , the monarch of Laothan.
