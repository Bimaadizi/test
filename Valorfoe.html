Valorfoe was a unique and fearsome enchanted warhammer of the ancient dwarvish make, forged long before the 14 th century DR .

Description

This masterfully-crafted weapon was carved with magic runes. A single sentence was written on it in the tongue of the ancient gray dwarves : "Tis Not Death That Is Worthy of Dread, But The Fear of Death."

Powers

Valorfoe was created to break the enemy's spirits and resolve in battle in addition to crushing their armor and bodies. With each successful strike, Valorfoe had a chance of inflicting magical fear onto its targets. In addition to this power, the warhammer bore a +2 enchantment that strengthened the weapon's blows and guided the wielder's attacks towards the enemy.

History

Valorfoe was an ancient weapon even by dwarvish standards. The markings of its creator were long-lost to the history of the duergar by the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR . By the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Valorfoe ended up in the paws of the leader of ice trolls of Icewind Dale , who allied himself with Iceblood , a mysterious blue-skinned shaman who served godling Arctus and sought to lay waste to the Ten-Towns .
