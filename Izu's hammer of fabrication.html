Izu's hammer of fabrication was an enchanted hammer wielded by a loyal Harper ally named Izu , sometime before 1373 DR .

Powers

The hammer of fabrication possessed a powerful +6 enchantment, making it a devastating weapon, especially for a light hammer . Additionally, Izu's hammer dramatically improved the wielder's mastery over crafting armor and weapons as well as magically imbued them with knowledge of lore. However, the weapon's magic made sure the hammer could only be wielded by a Harper.

History

Izu was a blind master smith who died in the Silver Marches sometime before the late 14 th century DR . As the blind smith spent years of his life making enchanted armor and weapons for the Harpers, his heirs gifted Izu's hammer of fabrication to a young Harper scout the smith was especially fond of. Sometime later, that Harper went missing, along with the hammer, while mapping the Upperdark .

By the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Izu's hammer of fabrication found its way to the city of Melvaunt on the Moonsea . The hammer was kept by the Gondar priests of The Purple Portals .
