Altar cloths were religious items in the Realms carried by clerics who used them at altars.

Description

Altar cloths were typically white and made from linen , velvet , or silk and some were decorated with gold brocade.

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 15 gold pieces for one made from linen, 30 gold pieces for velvet, 35 for silk, and 40 for gold brocade. Smaller versions suitable for altar cases cost 3 gold pieces.
