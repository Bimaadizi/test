Ulchabhán Mór was a large owlbear that prowled the foothills of the Storm Horns in Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The beast was found in the wooded foothills in the mid-1480s DR, enjoyed clawing trees, and had a particular taste for venison .
