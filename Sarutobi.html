Sarutobi was a renowned martial artist from Kara-Tur who lived long before the late 13 th century DR .

History

Sarutobi was already a famed fighter by the time he was to fight a duel with a stone giant . To aid the martial artist, a wealthy merchant of Shou Lung commissioned the creation of enchanted bracers , later to be known as Sarutobi's Stone Breakers . According to the story, Sarutobi passionately declined the gift, claiming that he needed no help in crushing the giant's skull. The martial artist survived the duel and won.

One day, Sarutobi stepped through a gate and disappeared into the planes , leaving a son behind. The merchant gifted the magic gauntlets to Sarutobi's son, who later went on a life-long quest to find his missing father. He never did.
