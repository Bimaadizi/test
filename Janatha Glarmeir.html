Janatha Glarmeir was a noblewoman of Hastarl in the early 3 rd century DR .

Relationships

She was an elder of the Glarmeir family.

History

Once Nanue Glarmeir and Peeryst Trumpettower were married in Year of the Black Flame, 229 DR , the elders of their families were downstairs from the bridal suite. They heard some suspicious sounds coming from upstairs as the newlyweds were attacked by the Moonclaws gang of thieves, but largely assumed that it was just their marital activities.
