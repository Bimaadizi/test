Jorsh was the eldest son of a cattle rancher who was abducted and enslaved by the Cult of the Dragon in 1369 DR .

Description

Jorsh was known for his charismatic personality.

History

In 1369 DR, Jorsh was one of the unfortunate villagers captured by the Cult of the Dragon's slavers. Alongside Tren , Wenta , and Ristim , the villagers were taken to the Cult's hideout in the ruined Darnarest's tower . Most of the abducted folk were transported to the ruins of Myth Drannor to work in the Cult's excavation project in the Polyandrium . Even though Jorsh's fate remained unknown, the Cult's nefarious plan was stopped by a group of adventurers sent to search for the missing townies as well as the group known as the Veiled Ones .
