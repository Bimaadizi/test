Tlannatar Wrathtree was an inhabitant of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

Tlannatar Wrathtree participated in the hunt of Elminster Aumar in 241 DR . Though he was concerned that it involved entering Castle Dlardrageth , where Elminster was hidden, he was overruled by the others. During the hunt, he slew a number of his own companions with a spell he cast upon Elminster, which was reflected.
