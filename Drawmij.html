Drawmij was a great mage from Oerth who was known to have visited Toril . He was known by a few inhabitants of the Realms , and his spells were used throughout.

Description

The Oerthly wizard's known spells in the Realms were Drawmij's instant summons and Drawmij's marvelous shield .

History

In the 14 th century DR , Khelben Arunsun thought of the "great Drawmij of Oerth" when imagining a spell that was made to penetrate magic fog. In the same century, Drawmij visited Raven's Bluff , where he told stories to the public.
