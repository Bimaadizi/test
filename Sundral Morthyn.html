Sundral Morthyn was a devotee of Mystra and the author of The Way of a Wizard , active in the 14 th century DR .

History

Sundral penned the The Way of a Wizard in the Year of Singing Shards, 1044 DR , in which he claimed that most mortals did not understand the way of the Goddess of Magic. Sundral's works were preserved by the great library of Candlekeep . The Way of a Wizard could be found among the shelves of Candlekeep in the late 14 th century DR.
