Preceptress Eressea Ambergyles ( pronounced : / ɛ r ɛ s ˈ s i ɑː ˈ ɑː m b ɛ r g ɪ l ɛ s / er-ess-SEE-ah AHM-ber-giles ) was the ranking religious official at the The House of the Lady in Shadowdale .

History

When Erressea was younger, she was a member of the Knights of Myth Drannor . She retired from the Knights after her death and resurrection in 1354 DR , and ministered to the faithful of Tymora in Shadowdale until, at least, 1374 DR .
