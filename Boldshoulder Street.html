LocationNeverwinter , Sword Coast North

Boldshoulder Street was a street of Neverwinter in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Boldshoulder Street was unevenly cobbled, and assiduously used by wagons and horses, implying it was a major way.

History

The conspirator Auvrarn Labraster slipped on some dung on Boldshoulder Street one night while drunk and impersonating Blandras Nuin on the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , precipitating the events that led to his cover being blown.
