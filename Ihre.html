Ihre was a Netherese arcanist and cleric who lived in Karsus Enclave before 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

History

Ihre came to the aid of his adventurer friend Yousouf when he was cleaved by an ogre , mauled by a mountain lion , and frostbitten by the cold. Ihre managed to keep him alive and restore his health, but most of Yousouf's toes had to be removed before they became gangrenous .
