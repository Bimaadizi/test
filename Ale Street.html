Ale Street was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Ale Street was located in Zhentil Keep's Common Quarter in the eastern section of the city. The road connected Beholder Street with the Street of Sorrow . Black Street intersected Ale Street near its midpoint.
