Relationships

Along with her comrade Prebek , Sanasha served the mad necromancer Xzar .

Possessions

Sanasha adorned herself in a mage robe of cold resistance and was armed with a simple quarterstaff . She carried several spell scrolls on her person.

History

As of the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , the trio were conducting experiments to create hideous little creatures that resembled goblins . Both young mages were jumpy and wary of intruders, apparently, at least on one occasion, Xzar sent intruders to Prebek's house in the Wave District as a test. Unbeknownst to the Zhents, they were being investigate by the Athkatlan Harpers under the leadership of Galvarey . Prebek, Sanasha, and their "creations" may have been ordered dead by Harper Rylock .
