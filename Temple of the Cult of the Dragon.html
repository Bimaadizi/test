The Temple of the Cult of the Dragon was a secret location used by the Cult of the Dragon found in the city of Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR .

Inhabitants

The temple was run by High Dragonservant Faerlaur Onthim . He was assisted by six other priests and thirteen followers.
