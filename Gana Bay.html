Gana Bay was nestled in the southeast corner of west Zakhara in the Pearl Cities in 1367 DR .

Description

Gana Bay measured approximately 30 miles (48 kilometers) wide by 60 miles (97 kilometers) in length, forming a roughly rectangular shape.

Geography

The bay offered natural protection to the important trade city of Gana from the storms of the northern reaches of the Crowded Sea . It was filled with run-off from Al-Sabiya River .
