Halavin Fey-Branche was the Matron Mother of House Fey-Branche in the early 14 th century DR .

History

The fall of House Do'Urden in the Year of the Wanderer, 1338 DR , led to the ascension of House Fey-Branche from the ninth house of Menzoberranzan to the eighth, granting Halavin a seat on the ruling council.
