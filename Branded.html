The Branded , or abd Wusum , were located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire .

Activities

The efreet mamluks of the Branded guarded the Sultan's personal harim. There were 150 members of the Branded. All were known to be discreet and observant individuals who were immune to bribery attempts.

Base of Operations

The City of Brass served as the base of the Branded.
