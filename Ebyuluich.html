Ebyuluich was a small village in the Alpuk region within the Great Glacier .

Geography

Ebyuluich was located on the southern shore of the Lugalpgotak Sea .

Inhabitants

Ebyuluich was famous for its gaming festivals, which occurred on a monthly basis. Popular were the gambling games such as tinguang .
