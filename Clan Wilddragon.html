Clan Wilddragon was an extinct noble house of Eskorn .

History

Clan Wilddragon was one of the dynasties that ruled Eskorn centuries before the Spellplague . This clan was very popular in legends and songs, and Eskorn was called the "Wild Dragon Realm" after it.

It was believed that Clan Wilddragon was totally exterminated by a rival clan, although some sages and legends claimed that a few descendants of the clan still survived in the 15th century DR , ignorant of their own lineages. Those surviving Wilddragons were accomplished adventurers and outlaws. According to legends, when the Wilddragons returned to the throne, Eskorn would become a prosperous land.
