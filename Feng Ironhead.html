Feng Ironhead was a half-orc shopkeeper in Red Larch during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Personality

Feng was a genius in his field: he could pick what looked to be an ordinary weapon or piece of armor that in truth was extremely durable. However, he was a bad businessmen, often selling good items for cheap prices, but he didn't care.

Activities

Feng ran a shop in the village.

History

Feng was a sellsword and caravan guard until 1489 DR when he settled in Red Larch, opening his shop.
