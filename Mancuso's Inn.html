Mancuso's Inn was a rental lodgings business in the city of Neverwinter in the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

The inn was located not far from the Neverwinter Square , one of the oldest locations in the Jewel of North. A sign affixed to the wall over the door invited customers in.

History

Mancuso's was established sometime before the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .
