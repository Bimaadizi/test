Zomir was a Calishite human bounty hunter active in 1357 DR .

Activities

He was one of a trio of bounty hunters hired by Baron Abon Duum of Manshaka to recover or slay the escaped slave-gladiator Vajra Valmeyjar . In 1357 DR , they went to the Selûne's Smile in Waterdeep and used a poison dart and a club to subdue Vajra and kidnap her, though even beaten and drugged she put up a fight.

Conner , Timoth Eyesbright , and Salabak eventually caught up with the band, distracted them with the illusion of a dracolich , and slew them with arrows, rescuing Vajra.
