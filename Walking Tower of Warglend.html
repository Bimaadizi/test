The Walking Tower of Warglend was a motile tower during the mid–8 th century DR .

Location

As of 761 DR , the Walking Tower was located near Tower Tor .

History

In the Year of Laughter, 761 DR , the sage Tabarast of Moonshorn Tower reported that, amongst other signs from the Gods, the Walking Tower of had Warglend moved for the first time in a thousand years, since approximately −250 DR , and left its place in Tower Tor, moving instead to the middle of a nearby lake.
