Maxam was the consort of Storm Silverhand in the mid-14 th century DR .

Personality

Storm Silverhand considered Maxam's intelligence and kindness to rival her own, for whom adventuring was in the blood.

Activities

Maxam was an adventurer .

Relationships

He was Storm's man.

History

Maxam was an orphan. After they got together, Maxam went to live with Storm Silverhand in Shadowdale , where he remained for a decade, though they were adventurers throughout that time. He perished in the summer of 1355 DR , during a battle with tanar'ri at a gate opened by illithids .
