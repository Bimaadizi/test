Rivergate Way was a small street found in the city of Westgate .

Location

Rivergate Way was located in the smaller section of Westgate found on the eastern bank of the River Thunn . It was connected to the River Bridge and provided the only means of crossing over to the major sections of Westgate other than by boat.
