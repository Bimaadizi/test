Martem Trec was a Waterdhavian boy in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In 1492 DR , Martem was an eyewitness to the detonation of a fireball in Trollskull Alley in the North Ward . He saw his halfling friends die in the explosion and the subsequent fire.
