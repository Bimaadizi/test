Quiornim was an old gold dragon in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

For many years Quiornim was trapped alongside a noble djinni on another plane. They worked together throughout the long years to free themselves.

Abilities

Besides his fire and chlorine breath attack, he was also able to cast wizard spells of up to the 4th circle, and clerical spells of the first.
