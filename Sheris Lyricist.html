Sheris Lyricist was a noblewoman and the leader of the adventuring company - the Lady's Lament from Waterdeep , active circa 1310 DR .

Description

Sheris was a charismatic, pampered, and spoiled young lady from the City of Splendors. Her main passion was music, and she worked hard to be a skilled musician.

History

Several years before the Year of Storms, 1310 DR , Sheris's pampered life of luxury came to an end when she found out about a marriage arranged by her father. Unwilling to comply with her noble family's wishes, Sheris fled home and took on the only job she could - becoming a bard . Eventually, Sheris organized an adventuring company she named the Lady's Lament. Her adventuring troupe grew as several individuals joined: a half-orcish couple Geldin and Khatja , a cleric of Ilmater named Laeris who offered his wise advice to compliment Sheris's charisma, a drow sorceress Vraedna who denounced Lolth , and a hin rogue Kylie who used his shiny personality to put a smile on the faces of his teammates.

In 1310 DR, the Lady's Lament, along with many other mercenaries, traveled from Luskan to the besieged city of Targos in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale .
