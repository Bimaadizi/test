Bear King Günvald Halraggson ( pronounced : /ˈ g uː n v ɑː l d h ɔː l ˈ r ɒg s ʌ n / GOON-vald hawl-ROG-sun ) was the ruler of the Tribe of the Bear during the late 15 th century DR , in the years following the War of the Silver Marches .

Personality

Günvald was a merciless and stalwart king who did his best to bring honor to his reign.

He didn't understand the "civilized" folks of the Ten Towns and stayed well away from their influence.

History

For some time, Günvald led the Tribe of the Bear with his fellow chieftain Wolvig Barrundson . After Wolvig fell under the influence of the Aurilian Chosen known as the Ice Witch and perished, Günvald became the tribe's sole leader and Bear King.
