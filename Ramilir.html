Ramilir was an acolyte of the Avowed that lived in the great fortified library of Candlekeep during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Ramilir was a driven and scholar and forthright man that received much gratification from his work in the halls of Candlekeep. Despite his dedication to academia, Ramilir could be careless when given too many tasks at the same time.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , accidentally pulled the cursed book title The Scrivener's Tale from the vaults of Candlekeep and offered it to a group of adventurers by mistake. While this was an honest mistake, Ramilir's error led to a member of the group suffering the curse of the scrivener's mark , that gradually caused them to transform into a petrified being of shadow glass.
