The wand of petrification was a unique wand owned by the half-drow mage Madame Narcosa .

Description

This wand was made of thin, rusty iron . Set at its tip was a fossilized shark 's tooth measuring 2 feet (0.61 meters) in length.

Powers

When activated, this wand emitted a dark gray beam out to 40 feet (12 meters) that caused those struck by it to become petrified . The wand was only usable by mages.

Other wielders

The Red Wizard Belathin wielded either the same wand or one like it sometime in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .
