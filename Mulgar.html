History

Mulgar was a guard for Orthil Voldovan's caravan in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , when it was joined by Shandril Shessair and Narm Tamaraith . During the ride from Scornubel to Triel , he was briefly assigned to quiet a horse somehow or else kill it, with Tarth's help. He was also assigned briefly to keep watch on Shandril, where he had a quick outburst after she threatened to kill them for keeping her away from Narm after a bandit raid injured him. He helped cool the situation afterwards.
