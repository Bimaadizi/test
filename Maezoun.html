Maezoun was a small town located in the nation of Halruaa during the 14 th century DR .

Geography

Maezoun was located in southern Haluraa along the coast of the Great Sea . The port town was situated at the western coast of an unnamed peninsula that jutted into the Bay of Taertal near the island of Rulasuu .
