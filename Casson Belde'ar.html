Casson Belde'ar was a child who lived in the city of Baldur's Gate in the mid–14 th century DR .

Personality

He had a proclivity for causing trouble and getting caught for it.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Casson and his friend Varci Roaringhorn snuck into the Water Queen's House in the city. Both of the boys were caught, however Casson could not run away as fast as Varci. He was apprehended by the priestesses of Umberlee and killed for trespassing upon holy ground.
