Yeul Gustaverg was an half-elven tailor and seamstress for hire from the city of Neverwinter , active in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Yeul Gustaverg was born to a human seamstress mother and an elven tailor father who ran a clothing shop in the city of Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR. Yeul hoped to inherit her father's business when the time would come, using the seamstress skills taught to her by the human parent.
