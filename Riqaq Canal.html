The Riqaq Canal was a prominent canal located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The canal passed through the center of the city, originating at the Edible Bazaar within the Pyraculum where it branched southeast. It turned due south, forming part of the southwest border of the Plume , before ending on the north side of the Naranj Canal at the border of the Foundry . The Riqaq Canal passed beneath the Street of Stelae near its center.

Description

As with all canals within the City of Brass, the Riqaq Canal was filled with lava from the Sea of Fire as well as oil which could be set alight.
