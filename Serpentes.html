Serpentes , which translated to "nation of serpents", was an ancient kingdom founded by Sseth , the World Serpent, in -304 DR .

Geography

The kingdom reached from the Peaks of Flame in the west to the Mhair Jungles in the east, encompassing what would later be known as Chult , Thindol , Samarach , and Tashalar .

History

Many of the human cultures inhabiting the regions that would become known as Serpentes were enslaved and transformed into histachii . Sseth disappeared beneath the Peaks of Flame several centuries after the founding of Serpentes, leading to the kingdom's rapid decline by 10 DR .
