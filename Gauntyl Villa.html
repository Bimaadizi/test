Gauntyl Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Gauntyl .

Location

The Gauntyl Villa was situated along Ivory Street , across from Eltorchul Villa and next to the Temple of Beauty , in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This building complex consisted of two high quality buildings, one that was two stories tall and another that stood a single story tall, one being the mansion proper and the other a guesthouse. Like many Waterdhavian villas, it was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden.
