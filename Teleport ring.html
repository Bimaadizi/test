A teleport ring was a small, brass finger ring that enabled instantaneous travel.

Powers

A teleport ring was a small, brass finger ring that allowed its wearer to teleport without error as per the arcane spell . The only difference was they did not allow for travel across the planes .

History

These rings were once more common in Faerûn but were very rare circa 1369 DR . Prior to this period, teleport rings were often gifted by the Zhentarim to wizards in the organization who had achieved an expertise level of 7 th or higher.

The ring of a Zhentarim mage, Gathlarue, was filched by her assistant Tespril prior to their confrontation with Shandril Shessair in 1357 DR .
