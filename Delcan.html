Delcan was a laborer and a delivery man of the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Delcan was a pale-skinned human man with blue eyes and shaved head. He wore simple garb of a wraparound tunic, brown pants, and a hat.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Delcan was employed by Baldur's Mouth and could be found outside the broadsheet publisher's office picking up deliveries. The man had a foul attitude thanks to customers' incessant requests to purchase the gazette. His temper often boiled over as he preemptively shouted at folk that he did not sell the broadsheets .
