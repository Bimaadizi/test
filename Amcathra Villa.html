Amcathra Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Amcathra .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's North Ward in between the High Road and Ilzantil Street just north of Tarnath Street .

Description

Amcathra Villa contained two buildings within the walled estate. The main building dwarfed the second building. They stood two and four stories tall.
