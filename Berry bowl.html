Berry bowls were a simple type of gnomish highsunfeat dessert from the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast .

Description

Berry bowls were just that – bowls filled with a variety of berries, depending on what was in season. The berries were drizzled with sugar sauce.

History

Berry bowls were the only alternative to the chocolate crab cakes at a gnomish out-of-the way café in Ravens Bluff called Le Po Teete Café in the late 14 th century DR . More adventurous customers preferred strange but delicious chocolate crab cakes.
