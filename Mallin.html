Mallin was a lich and one of the lovers—and skulls—of Tashara of the Seven Skulls .

After Azuth defeated Tashara, her seven skulls split apart, and went in many directions. The skull of Mallin took residence in a shattered tower in Myth Rhynn , where he collected and studied magic . He sent visions via magic and spread rumors of great treasure to lure adventurers to him, so he might steal their magic.
