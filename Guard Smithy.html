The Guard Smithy was a civic building located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood two storys tall.

Location

The Guard Smithy was located in the far southern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward just northeast of Soldier's Street near its intersection with Soothsayer's Way .
