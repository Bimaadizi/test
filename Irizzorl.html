Irizzorl was a silver dragon wyrmling , living in the tower of the wizard Chazlauth Yarghorn 's Waterdeep residence, sometime after the War of the Silver Marches in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Irizzorl was considered to be a companion to the wizard Chazlauth. While living in his tower, the dragon often enjoyed terrorizing the several cats that the wizard kept in the tower alongside them.
