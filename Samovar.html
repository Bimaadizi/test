Samovars were vessels filled with boiling water intended for serving tea . They were important in Rashemaar culture, where they were used in tea ceremonies .

Description

A samovar appeared as an urn-shaped vessel with a spigot. A tube of burning charcoal kept water stored within at boiling temperature.

While typically crafted out of metals such as tin , some samovars were instead made of porcelain .

Some of these vessels were crafted to accommodate a separate teapot on top, which would be filled with particularly strong tea meant to be diluted with the samovar's boiling water. In Zakhara , samovars of this kind were sometimes also used to serve coffee .

Notable Samovars

A marilith-shaped samovar was one of several madness -inducing magical items spread by Graz'zt during his stay in the Deep Wastes .
