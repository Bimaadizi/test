Darrun was a horse belonging to the adventuress Kendra in the mid–14 th century DR . It was trained for battle.

Description

It had a dark gray coat.

History

Kendra rode Darrun through her travels in Cormyr in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , from Wheloon to Ghars on Eleint 16 and even into the Vast Swamp on Eleint 17 . When she returned on the evening of Eleint 18 , she fought a rogue hydra from horseback, and gave Jasper a ride back to Benelaius 's cottage. Kendra rode away on Darrun toward Anauroch on Eleint 21 .
