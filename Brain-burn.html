TypePoison
Available fromWaterdeep , Sword Coast North

Brain-burn was a deadly poison that ruined any chances at magically drawing information from a dead body.

Powers

Those struck with brain-burn died immediately and, furthermore, their corpses could not be interrogated magically .

History

The moneylender Caethur employed brain-burn on the weapons he used to assassinate, including those he employed to kill a number of his debtors in early spring of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR . Amongst those killed by brain-burn were Kamburan , Hammuras , Aldurl Nael , two others, and his own two bodyguards.
