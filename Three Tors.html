The Three Tors was a rock formation found near Aithelar in the kingdom of Callidyrr on Alaron in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Three Tors were found just south of Aithelar along the southern edge of the Southflow Marsh . The rocky hills formed a small diagonal barrier extending inland from the coast of the Sea of Moonshae .
