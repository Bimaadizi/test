Fergus Gunderson was an old farmer that lived in Rothé Valley Village during the early 15 th century DR .

Relationships

He was believed to have been related to the young woman Cella Gunderson , and her uncle , the village's mayor some 50 years after Fergus' time.

History

On one occasion, Fergus made a bet with a traveling wizard named Rucksam , the specifics of which had been lost to time. To win the bet, Rucksam created a tunnel in the valley, linking Fergus' home village with nearby farms. As a result of his loss, Fergus had to pay the wizard's bar tab every time again he visited the town.
