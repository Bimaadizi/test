Plaguegrass was grass that had been touched by the Spellplague and which grew only within the borders of the Plaguewrought Land .

Description

Plaguegrass had blue crystals growing on the stalks.

Usage

The grass was a key ingredient in an elixir that blocked the effects of spellscars and the Spellplague, such as was devised by the alchemist Gregor in the late 15 th century DR .
