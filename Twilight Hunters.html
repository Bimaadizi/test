Twilight Hunters was a tavern located in the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The tavern sat in the eastern section of Waterdeep's North Ward on the west side of the northern end of Nindabar Street just one building south of Saerdoun Street . The rear of the tavern faced an open courtyard.
