This unnamed shadow priest was an antagonist of the unnamed graveyard near the Mere of Dead Men on the Sword Coast North .

History

This shadow priest was located in the unnamed graveyard. He raised zombies and skeletons to boost Black Garius ' army. He also captured Tann and his men. However, the Kalach-Cha was employed to investigate and defeat the shadow priest.
