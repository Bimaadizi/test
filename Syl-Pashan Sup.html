Syl-Pashan Sup was a type red Calishite wine , available in the Western Heartlands , along the River Chionthar , and the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

This red wine had a bold and loud meaty taste. It was said to hit the tongue of the drinker like a raging bull .
