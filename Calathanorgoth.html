Calathanorgoth , also known as The Old One , was a powerful black dragon dracolich who lived in the High Moor in 1370 DR .

Relationships

The dracolich lived in the ruins of Orogoth with his offspring: the venerable mated pair Wastirek and Vilholin , their son the adult Woklef and the young Aswidorg , and the mature adult female Cheleen .
