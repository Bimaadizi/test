Elder Grashtio was one of the mages from Estagund who joined Aniolon Gruanthe on his exodus in the Year of the Slaying Spells, 976 DR , ultimately founding the city of Lliiress . Shortly after the city was founded, he attempted to disable the Evise Jhontil which the blue dragon Gerinvioch had used to trap the city's inhabitants. He died in the attempt.
