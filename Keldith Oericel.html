Keldith Oericel was one of the captains of Leuthilspar 's infantry who joined Seiveril Miritar 's crusade.

In 1374 DR , he replaced the dead Celeilol Fireheart as leader of the Leuthilspar contingent and was one of the captains summoned by Seiveril to plan the Cormanthor War .
