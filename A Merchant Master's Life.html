A Merchant Master's Life was a book written by Asargrym , a merchant of Baldur's Gate , and published in the Year of the Blue Flame, 1327 DR .

Description

A Merchant Master's Life was a collection of life lessons and musings by the master merchant.

History

A copy of the book was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
