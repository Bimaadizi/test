The Troll Hills were a series of hills along the coastal area of the Western Heartlands . .

Geography

Flora & Fauna

The area was covered in oak trees, and the hilltops were dotted with patches of razorgrass . The Troll Hills were also home to tree-worms , a species of flatworms that tasted terrible to everyone but trolls. Besides them and other insect species, these hills were largely devoid of birds and small woodland creatures, having been wiped out by the forest's monstrous inhabitants.

Inhabitants

Trolls were the most predominant creature in this region, with many bands being led by giant two-headed trolls . Many of them and allied creatures lived in caverns deep beneath its peaks.

Several competing troll kingdoms were found here.

Important Locations

Rumors & Legends

Local rumors claimed that a drow city once stood beneath these hills, but that the drow destroyed all access to it once the area became overran by trolls.
