The Winter's Cradle Tavern was a tavern in Easthaven within the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale .

Structure

Located in the northeastern part of Easthaven, the wooden building looked ramshackle on the outside. In front of it was a sign that bore the name of the tavern alongside an image of a key.

Interior

The tavern was a comfortable place with a fireplace burning to keep the cold northern chill from the bones of any adventurer.

Inhabitants

The tavern was owned by Grisella who sold Winter Wine , meads , frost giant yellow snow melts , Grisella's Dale ale , Luskan stout , Cormyrian brandy , and knee cracker cider . The tavern was often patronized by the dwarf Hildeth Highhammer and the soldier Hrothgar .

History

In Mirtul , 1281 DR , the cellar of the Winter's Cradle had an infestation of beetles .
