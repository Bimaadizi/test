Lucian Pancrates was an apprentice scholar who lived in the city of Neverwinter during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Lucian studied under the master spellcaster Tualo the Bright .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Lucian was present at Tualo's house when the learned master performed a ritual with the use of a tome called Transmogrifications of the Spellplague . The ritual went awry however, as the book had been altered by the malevolent moon elf Rhazzad , opening a portal to the Far Realm . Lucian survived the encounter, but Master Tualo was not so fortunate.
