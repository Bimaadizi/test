Darthym was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He prided in speaking softly, pleasantly, and parsimoniously.

Activities

As of 1373 DR , Darthym was a member of the retinue guarding Vangerdahast 's sanctum near Mouth o' Gargoyles .

Relationship

He reported to Huldyl Rauthur .

History

One day after spring began in 1373 DR , there was an attack on the sanctum of the retired Royal Magician of Cormyr , Vangerdahast, directed by Harnrim Starangh . In the aftermath, Darthym reported the damage to Huldyl Rauthur, his superior, somewhat losing his composure.
