RaceT'ien lung

T'ien Lung was the name given to a celestial dragon (also called a t'ien lung ) of the 2 nd millennium before Dale Reckoning .

In −1943 DR , T'ien Lung killed Emperor Kujawa in battle, which led to the collapse of the empire of Anok-Imaskar .
