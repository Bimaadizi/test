Lakh varnish was a variety of varnish used in a variety of crafting methods for creatiing weapons , armor , and treating dinosaur leather .

Description

Lakh varnish was a powerful liquid created from terebinth and lakh resin . It was primarily used to finish wood and create lacquer.

When mixed with Tears of Ubtao , lakh varnish formed a magical substance called living varnish , used to create masterwork items in Chult . Other uses for lakh varnish were hardening and treating bronzewood , treating spruce wood for the construction of bucklers , strenghtening wooden barricades, crafting tlahuitolli , feathered teotlanextli , feathered ilhuilli , obsidian miztonhiyo , wootz scythes , and lacquering dinosaur leather.
