Sarmeir was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 th century DR .

History

After spring began in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Harnrim Starangh made a quick raid near Mouth o' Gargoyles on the sanctum of Vangerdahast , then retired from his post of Royal Mage . After he slew three War Wizards and escaped, the War Wizard Huldyl Rauthur ordered Darthym , who'd just reported the losses, to wake Sarmeir and have the two of them stand guard in the armory, where two people had been slain.
