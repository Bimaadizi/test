Somidorr Danthan was a half-elf man and smuggler of the city of Tantras in the Vast .

Somidorr made a number of shrewd decisions in his illicit trade, and by 1358 had become very rich and dangerous. He desired to increase his own power with the acquisition of magical items , and didn't care who he took them from or how.
