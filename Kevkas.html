Kevkas was a patron at the Cutlass in Luskan during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Around 1490 DR , his companion Janstine attempted to cure Mary of poison with the help of the Champion of the Burning Dawn . Kevkas was of no help, being too caught up in the romance of the unconscious maiden.
