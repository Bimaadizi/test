Makal of the Pine was a devious druid who plagued orcish loggers some years before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Sometime before the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Makal used his druidic magics in creation of a bludgeon named Gnasher . The club was imbued with the spirit of Makal's fierce wolverine animal companion . This powerful and dangerous weapons remained in the hands of the druid and on several occasions was used against orc loggers who threatened Makal's forest.

Makal of the Pine was presumed dead by 1369 DR, when the Gnasher was wielded by the Shadow Druid named Dalok who plagued the town of Trademeet .
