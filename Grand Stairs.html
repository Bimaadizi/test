The Grand Stairs were part of the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 th century DR .

Description

The stairs formed the entrance into the entire cave face. They were carved from the bedrock and at one point were lined with statues. As of the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , all of the statues had been shattered with their broken remains laying on the ground surrounding the statues.
