Thavaun was a Red Wizard of Thay , who was involved in a takeover of Triel during the mid-14 th century DR .

Personality

He was somewhat arrogant.

Abilities

He was capable of disguising himself as someone else with magic.

History

At Triel , Orthil Voldovan began acting strangely, in Beldimarr 's view, speaking stiffly and taking too long to recognize him. Thavaun had magically disguised himself as Orthil Voldovan ; Shandril found out about this just as she began her immolation. However, Sharantyr , once she arrived at the caravan, quickly realized it wasn't him.

He was slain by Arauntar in the aftermath of Shandril's immolation.

Rumors & Legends

Somehow, he had been whispered of as far as Scornubel . He was known to the Harpers either in that way or through different means.
