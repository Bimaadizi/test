Luther was an ancient beloved Magister hero of Myth Drannor who died long before the advent of the Weeping War .

Possessions

Luther owned a unique enchanted ring of the protected , which served him in life and was buried with the Magister after death.

History

Magister Luther perished many centuries before his final resting place in the Elven Catacombs was disturbed by the Cult of the Dragon and their drow House Freth allies in 1369 DR . His body was laid to rest within one of the decorated crypts of elven heroes of the City of Song, alongside Arianna the Archer, Erik Sliphand , Sindar , Bannoc , and Shyla . Their sarcophagi were corrupted by the wild magics unleashed during the Weeping War. The Magister, and other heroes he shared the tomb with became cursed with the undead existence as wraiths , forever guarding the artifacts they were interred with.
