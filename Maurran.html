Kithguard Maurran was a shadar-kai rogue of the mid–14th century DR .

History

Serving Kithlord Thieraven , he commanded a group of shadar-kai defenders at the Lost Refuge in the Vast Swamp in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR . He was likely slain when adventurers in service to Mystra assaulted the fort.

Possessions

Equipped similarly to all his people, Maurran wielded a well-made spiked chain and composite shortbow and wore +2 studded leather and a +2 gal-ralan .
