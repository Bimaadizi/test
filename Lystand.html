Lystand , also known as Lystand the Pick , was a thief in Skullport in the mid-14 th century DR.

Description

Lystand operated Delver's Folly . With her background in designing and bypassing intricate security systems, Lystand specialized in crafting traps that were as lethal as they are difficult to disarm. Her poison gas and dart traps, in particular, were infamous for their complexity.

History

Before moving to Skullport, she was an adventurer in Waterdeep .
