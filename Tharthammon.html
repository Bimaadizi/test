Tharthammon was a pit fiend of Avernus in the late 14 th century DR who was slain by Nergal .

Personality

He was grim and close-mouthed.

Description

He was unusually portly and large, even among his fellow fiends.

Relationships

He was frightening enough that even the dukes did not talk much to him when he gave them a dark look.

History

As of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Nergal had slain Thartammon, and would use his shape to avoid discovery if needed.
