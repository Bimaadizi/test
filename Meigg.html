Meigg was an enclave of Netheril that was notable for its involvement in the Seven Sigils War .

History

The flying citadel was created by the Netherese domain of Rdiuz in concert with their attempted invasion of the Outlands through the Cat's Gate in 3370 NY (−489 DR) , leading to the Seven Sigils War and a century of conflict in both planes.
