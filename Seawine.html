"Seawine" was a name given to a potent liqueur produced at the Nasaqh Estates on Mount Sar , in the Sword Coast North region.

Reputation

The liquor made the Nasaqh Estates famous in the surrounding areas. In particular, it was popular in Waterdeep among wealthy merchants.
