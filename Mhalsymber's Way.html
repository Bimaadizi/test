Mhalsymber's Way was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Mhalsymber's Way was located in the south section of Waterdeep's North Ward . The road ran north to south, connecting Golden Serpent Street in the north with Sammarin's Street in the south near the northwest corner of the City of the Dead .
