The Breaker's bauble was a bit of junk jewelry worn by Agarta the Breaker in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

Something about the bauble made the wearer hit harder in combat and break past armor more easily.

History

The armor scrap was carried by Agarta the Breaker, an extremely aggressive and bloodthirsty member of the Night Brigade gang of Wheloon Prison . It served as a reminder that no defense was perfect and could always be broken.
