Bregg was a mercenary thug and a partner of Cohrvale , from the River District of Athkatla in Amn in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Bregg was a pale-skinned dwarvish man with a chestnut beard. He preferred to wear hooded black rogue 's leathers.

Possessions

As a fighter , Bregg wore a set of studded leather armor and carried a simple short sword .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Bregg and his human partner Cohrvale worked for someone called Mr. F. At one point, the thuggish duo came across Abdel Adrian and his traveling companions in front of the Copper Coronet in the slums of Athkatla. Cohrvale showed short fuse and demanded the adventurers to get out of his way. Bregg attempted to diffuse the situation, seeing how the adventurers were well-armed and Mr. F didn't like his men slaughtering people on the streets of Athkatla. Whether Abdel Adrian taunted Cohrvale and provoked him into battle, or avoided confrontation was unknown.
