The Bay of Norland was a prominent bay located on the island of Norland within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The bay was located near central Norland's eastern shore on with the Sea of Moonshae just northwest of the North Cape .

Description

Reef formations sat at both the north and south ends of the bay, creating a safe passage in the middle for ships while also helping to protect the bay from storms.
