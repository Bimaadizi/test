Bentan was a devoted evangelist of Ilmater who traveled the Sword Coast sharing the dogma of the Crying God during the midâˆ’14 th century DR .

Personality

Like others of his faith, Bentan believed it was his duty to take on the suffering of others and endure them himself.

He believed that those who gave themselves to greed or lust were evil people, as were sorcerers and other practitioners of arcane magic .

Activities

The old man traveled the countryside attempted to convert others to the Ilmatari faith . He told people to cast away their wicked ways and material possessions in order to fully devote their lives to Ilmater's cause.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Bentan was seen traveling in the area around the Firewine Bridge .
