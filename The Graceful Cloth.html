The Graceful Cloth was a type of enchanted monk 's garb from Port Nyanzaru in Chult .

Powers

The Graceful Cloth was enchanted to constantly bestow cat's grace onto its wearer, and it possessed the nimble as a cat magic that made the wearer's reaction quicker and more nimble and boosted their jumping distance.

History

Sometime before the late 15 th century DR , The Graceful Cloth was worn by Roahton Mobar , one of the monks of the port city of Nyanzaru , who fought against the Headless Serpent, Secret Venom gang. The cloth later found its way into the hands of Lady Esther , an adventurer in service of the Society of Brilliance of Baldur's Gate .
