Private Mather was a soldier of the Neverwinter Guard during the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Private Mather was injured by one of the Many-Arrows orcs while making a delivery in Neverwinter's Tower District . He was on his way to deliver a chest of components to M'tari Prin'tal of the Seven Suns Trading Coster at her shop in Protector's Enclave , but then he took an arrow in the knee. He beseeched an adventurer to aid him.
