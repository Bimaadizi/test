Pim Tung was a town in Tabot .

Geography

It lay in southern Tabot, along the river and the road between Pabex and the twin cities of Motra and Joya , with forest in the south and the plains to the north.

Government

The effective ruler of Pim Tung around Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) was Lady Holang , the favorite wife of Baron Mukow Jian , who, had no actual political power under Tabotan law. The local dzong-pon (sheriff or district official) was her puppet, while the warriors of Pim Tung were her men.

The sign of Pim Tung was a burning arrow, which marked the representatives of the town.

Defenses

The warriors of Pim Tung were adorned with the burning arrow and tied their scabbards to their belts with a distinctive red cord. Holang's men were common in Motra and Joya and bluntly interrogated anyone who passed through Pim Tung's lands.
