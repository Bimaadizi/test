A tantan , known as a tambourine on other worlds, was a type of percussion instrument.

Description

Tantans consisted of jangling hoops that were used to emphasize rhythms in ensembles and to enhance campfire dances.

Availability

Tantans crafted by the masters of the plains of the Hordelands and traded through Semphar were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .
