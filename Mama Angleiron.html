The woman simply known as Mama Angleiron was the mother of the master smith, an aspiring writer, and the proprietor of Rivington General , Gyldro Angleiron in the mid-to-late 15 th century DR .

History

Mama Angleiron was the mother of Master Gyldro and some unrevealed number of his siblings. Despite Having a close relationship with his Mama, Gyldro was a lonely and sensitive soul. By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , he developed a case of melancholy that worried Mama Angleiron. She tried to encourage the master blacksmith via petter to seek professional help. His depression likely was the result of loneliness and a broken heart. Despite her pleads, Gyldro seemed be resistant to the idea.
