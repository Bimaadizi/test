The Tower of Argûl was residence of wizard Argûl Marammas of Suzail in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

Argûl's abode stood to the west of Suzail's Horngate next to the city's northwest wall.

History

The tower was the destination for those seeking magic scrolls from Argûl Marammas's collection or custom-scribed by the wizard.
