Ormantor was one of the bodyguards for Marlin Stormserpent in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He was immense and very muscular.

Personality

He usually said nothing, but thought his lord's caginess was kind of stupid.

Activities

He worked as a bodyguard for Marlin Stormserpent .
