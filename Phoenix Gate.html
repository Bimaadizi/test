The Phoenix Gate was an interior gate located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The gate lay within an interior wall joining the Plume and the Char districts. It was found in the eastern wall of the Plume south of the Maidan .

Description

The gate resembled a permanent fortress complete with a garrison of soldiers.
