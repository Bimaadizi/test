The Shop of Faded Carpets was found in the city of Kadarasto , owned and operated by Cholk min Kado in 1367 DR .

Description

This humble shop sold ancient rugs , carpets , and tapestries .

Rumors & Legends

The Shop of Faded Carpets was a front for a massive spy network carefully orchestrated by Cholk. His contacts with over 200 local beggars were able to reach anyone in practically any location within the city.

The shop was also used to sell magic items looted from nearby ruins and to smuggle illegal goods out of Kadarasto.
