Ermlaut was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Ermlaut was a human man with a light skin tone, dark brown eyes, and curly blonde hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Ermlaut attended a performance of some showy magics by three spellslingers, on the Murl's Rest plaza in front of Sorcerous Sundries . Ermlaut enjoyed the show but wondered whether these street mages would be better off on the battlefield, fighting the Absolute .
