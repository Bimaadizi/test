Inthar was a ruined fortress whose origins were unknown and that was avoided by almost all sailors.

Location

This fortress sat atop a rocky sea crag, east of Ruathym and south of Axard . It was roughly 35 miles (56,000 meters) south of the ruined fortress Rethgaard .

Structure

During the night time, eerie green lights would shine within this fortress, silhouetting shadowy shapes, and faint whispers could be heard from afar. A one-way portal in the river Sargauth exited into the depths of this fortress.

History

Some time prior to 1361 DR , an illithid named Vestress banished a drow banshee in Ascarle to this fortress by means of a portal.

Rumors & Legends

Some tales spoke of there being a hidden shaft within the fortress that led straight down to Baator .
