Hupe Brightbell was a halfling innkeeper near the Quivering Forest near Phlan in 1489 DR .

Personality

Hupe was a painfully meek little man. He helped people but only because he was scared of what they might do without his help. So he was kind but people often took advantage of his kindness.

Activities

Hupe run the Leaky Bucket inn with his wife Grinna Brightbell in the Kabel's Hill 's community in Quivering Forest just near Phlan.
