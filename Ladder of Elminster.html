A ladder of Elminster was a special kind of ladder used by some performers.

Uses

Popular with tumblers and acrobats , these ladders were balanced so that a dextrous individual could climb up one side and down the other while the ladder was standing upright without leaning on a solid object. These ladders were classified into various skill levels for beginners at 5 feet (1.5 meters), masters at 8 feet (2.4 meters), and "mages" at 10 feet (3 meters).

Prices

A ladder of Elminster could be purchased through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 5 silver pieces —8 gold pieces depending on its size.
