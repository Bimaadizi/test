Sheila Kree was a pirate and Captain of the ship Bloody Keel active around Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR . Her partners included Jule Pepper , Le'lorinel , Bellany , and many others. She resided in the Golden Cove with her ogre allies. She befriended Chogurugga and Bloog , the two head ogres living in the cove.

Sometimes between Year of the Prince, 1357 DR and Year of the Staff, 1366 DR , she took in her crew the moon elf Ellifain Tuuserail .
