Serelis Greenleaf was a ranger in the Quivering Forest near Phlan in 1489 DR .

Activities

Serelis usually acted as a mediator between the Kabel's Hill 's community and the elves of the forest.

History

Serelis lived in the Quivering Forest near Kabel's Hill. He was possibly involved in the case of the disappearance of Halvin Graingle and six other villagers inside the forest when an adventuring party went to ask for his help.
