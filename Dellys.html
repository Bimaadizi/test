Dellys was a news hawker who worked for the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Dellys was a dark-skinned human boy with brown eyes. He wore a quality leather jerkin and a hat while selling the broadsheets .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Dellys could often be found peddling the Baldur's Mouth Gazette across the street from Beehive Goods and Wares in the Lower City alongside Pharnilla , with whom Dellys took breaks. When he was interrupted whilst on a break, Dellys claimed to be a member of the Gazetteers' Guild and stated he had rights and whatever business the person had would have to wait.
