Gnome hooked hammers were a type of two-handed martial hammer , specially designed by gnomes for use by members of their race.

Description

Gnome hooked hammer had relatively short shafts, with a hammer on one end and a sharp-pointed hook (curved only slightly more than a pickaxe ) on the other. On average they weighed around 6 lb (2.7 kg).

Weaponology

Due to their size, these hammers were well suitable for fights in cramped spaces. The hooked end of these hammers could be utilized to trip opponents.
