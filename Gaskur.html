Gaskur was a bodyguard and servant to Marlin Stormserpent in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He was quite nondescript.

Personality

He was not one to second-guess his master.

Possessions

Gaskur was not known to own any property.

Activities

Gaskur did all that Marlin required of him, and being a bodyguard was only a small part of his service.

Relationships

He worked for Marlin Stormserpent . He was no stranger to kicks from nobles who found him not obsequious enough. As far as Marlin knew, he had no kin nor family in Suzail , but actually he had nieces, and these nieces had cats.

History

By 1479 DR , Gaskur had served Marlin Stormserpent for six or seven seasons.

Gaskur accompanied Marlin Stormserpent in his foray to Dragonskull Chamber of Castle Obarskyr , where he stayed out the door while Marlin experimented with the control of Blueflame ghosts using the Wyverntongue Chalice and Flying Blade . Later, he ushered in the lords Delasko Sornstern , Broryn Windstag , and Kathkote Dawntard when they were looking to see Marlin Stormserpent after their murder of the officer of the Crown, Delnor , while drunken in the Dragonriders Club . Gaskur was attending to Marlin right up until the moment where the latter was approached by the two Blueflame Ghosts, Relve Langral and Treth Halonter , after completing their appointed task: slaying Seszgar Huntcrown and fetching his head. Marlin resolved to tell Gaskur to clean up after them, and to rake the corpse's bones out of the furnace.

Unknwon to anyone, the evil archmage Manshoon had resolved to slay Gaskur for one of his intrigues: he was going to send Talane to murder him. Gaskur's body was brought to Marlin afterwards; according to the watch, he was murdered on a roof, and the word " talane " scrawled on that same roof with his blood before he was kicked onto the floor.
