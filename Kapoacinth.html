A kapoacinth ( or : kopoacinth ) was an aquatic creature related to the gargoyle . They could not fly but instead swam, using their wings to propel them forward.

Personality

Much like standard gargoyles, a kapoacinth delighted in causing pain to other creatures. Most often humans , merfolk , and sea elves .

Society

Homelands

Kapoacinth typically lived in shallow waters, often lairing in undersea caves. A group of them were known to inhabit lairs beneath the city of Ascarle near the Purple Rocks .
