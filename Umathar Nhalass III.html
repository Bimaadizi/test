Umathar Nhalass III was the third Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Umathar III inherited the throne when his father, Umathar II , died in −821 DR . He ruled until his death in −799 DR .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Umathar IV .
