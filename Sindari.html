Sindari was a poisonous herb native to Thay .

Uses

This herb was used to make a poison .

Effects

It was a powerful poison. It showed no effects for 24 hours, and then, after the time had elapsed, the victim would go into violent seizures. Death was expected in a couple of minutes.
