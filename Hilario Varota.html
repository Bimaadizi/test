Hilario Varota was a middle class inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Hilario was a male rock gnome light brown wavy curls and amber eyes. He wore a fine quality doublet of bright colors.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Hilario Varota could be found in the Lower City , gossiping with his dwarvish friend, Megthanet . Megthanet expressed her disgust with the food at a local eatery, the Elfsong tavern, and the two discussed the death of Duke Belynne Stelmane . Hilario was adamant that only the locals knew knew the story of her death, and not the Baldur's Mouth Gazette.
