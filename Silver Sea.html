The Silver Sea was the ancient ocean located off the western coast of the super-continent of Merrouroboros during the Days of Thunder and before the Sundering of Abeir-Toril .

Notable Locations

The Aearee Enclaves , from whence the Aearee creator race originated, was located in the middle of this sea.
