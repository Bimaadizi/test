Mickey was a fiendish flesh golem created by the night hag Mad Maggie , and a member of her Knucklebones Gang in Avernus .

Personality

The fiendish construct was described as being endearing but rather foolish.

Appearance

Mickey was formed from various devil , yugoloth ,Â and demon body parts. He was particularly large and was often dragged on a leash by Mad Maggie.

As of the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , Mickey had a piece of bone devil buried in his leg. Since he was unable to speak, he only grunted in pain when he walked or periodically hopped around in a little dance.

Relationships

Mickey was subservient to his creator Mad Maggie. He was often ridiculed by the redcaps and madcaps in the Knucklebones Gang.
