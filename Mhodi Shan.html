Mhodi Shan was a prominent peak of the Katakoro Shan mountain range.

Geography

The peak was found in the southeast corner of Khazari in the branch of Katakoro Shan that formed the southern border of the country.

Description

Easily the highest peak in Khazari, Mhodi Shan rose to an elevation of 19,251 ft (5,867.7 m).
