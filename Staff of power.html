A staff of power was a powerful magical staff .

Description

A staff of power was usually straight with a precious gem on top.

Powers

A staff of power has a number of potent abilities, including casting magic missile , making enemies enfeebled , creating a continual flame , allowing the user to levitate and be protected by a wall of force and globe of invulnerability , holding monsters , and using offensive spells such as lightning bolt , fireball , and cone of cold .

A staff of power could also be willfully broken to release all of its charges within a 30-ft area. Upon being broken, the staff had a 50% likelihood of transporting the wielder to another plane, or else the wielder would be destroyed by spell energy.

A staff of power could also be used as +2 quarterstaff .

Belt of power

A variant had been crafted by the Simbul , known as a belt of power , which operated similarly but in belt form.

Creation

According to Hasuraman the Muddled, a staff of power of the variant popular around Neverwinter could be created with the use of the feeblemind spell and a pair of bodak teeth, within the confines of a proper wizard's laboratory. Such staves could also be created with quartz and a Giant fire beetle 's belly, using the fireball spell according a process described in the Imaskarran Tome of Fire .
