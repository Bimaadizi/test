Tinker was a gnome caught by bugbears in Mount Dread during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He had a secret project that required a silver spring.

Possessions

He had found a silver spring he needed.

History

During the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , Tinker was in Mount Dread looking for a silver spring when he got captured by bugbears . If adventurers came across these bugbears, they'd catch 'em arguing over what to do with him. If the adventurers prevailed over those bugbears it is likely that they let him escape.
