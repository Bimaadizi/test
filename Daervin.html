Daervin was a Highknight in the mid–14 th century DR .

Relationships

He served alongside other highknights, Forold and Regrar amongst them.

History

Daervin was part of Azoun IV 's retinue as of late 1357 DR during the king's trip to Eveningstar, to meet with Tessaril Winter . In that trip, while carrying a roasted bustard , Daervin witnessed a malaugrym impersonating King Azoun reverting to its normal shape, and attacking both Tessaril and the king; he quickly hacked away at it with his sword, but the creature crushed and decapitated him before fleeing.

He was buried in a gorge near Eveningstar afterwards.
