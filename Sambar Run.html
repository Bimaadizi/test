The Sambar Run was a small straight that separated the islands of Lantan proper and Suj on the Trackless Sea .

Geography

The Sambar Run was narrow, stretching for approximately 100 miles (160 kilometers) from west to east between the islands. Several cities and towns were located along either shore of the straight.
