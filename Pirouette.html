Pirouette was a tiefling who led particularly notable thieves' guild some time during the mid-to-late 15 th century DR .

Pirouette was known to have committed numerous crimes during their career. Pirouette was eventually apprehended by the authorities and sentenced to a lifetime imprisonment within Revel's End .
