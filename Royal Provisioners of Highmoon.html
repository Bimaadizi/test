The Royal Provisioners of Highmoon was a provisions and supply store found in the city of Highmoon in Deepingdale just on the north side of the East Way . They offered quality goods for the caravans passing through the city, at the reasonable price of a 10% markup. These supplies included provisions, camping supplies, tack and harnesses and tools especially for traveling merchants .
