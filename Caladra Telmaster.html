Caladra Telmaster was a town guard that lived in the settlement of Idyllglen .

History

Caladra was present in Idyllglen when Yeenoghu assaulted the settlement with his demonic horde. [ as of when? ] Caladra and the other guards bravely defended their fellow citizens. They were driven temporarily mad and gained the urge to consume human flesh, when they became covered in demon ichor during the battle.
