Merchant Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Merchant Street was located in Zhentil Keep's Common Quarter in the southwest corner of the city. The road ran west to east roughly parallel to Border Street and the outer southern wall of the city. Merchant Street branched from the southern tip of Nool Street and passed east through the city before ending at the wall of the Harbor District near the North Force Gate . Merchant Street was intersected by Beggars' Way , Dixon Street , and Brigand Way .
