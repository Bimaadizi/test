Brain Food was an eatery in Waterdeep in the late 14 th century DR . It was one in a chain of eateries and was primarily, infamously, known for being ran by a mindflayer .

Location

This eatery stood along Ship Street , next to the festhall Velvet Tentacles , in the Dock Ward district of Waterdeep.

Services

Brain Food was primarily known for serving fish balls .
