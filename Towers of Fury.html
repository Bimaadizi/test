The Towers of Fury was a temple complex in Calimport dedicated to the worship of the Gods of Fury .

Location

The Towers of Fury was located in the the eastern half of Samilar Drudach , part of Evyrtaan Sabban of Faiths Ward , in Calimport.

Structure

The Towers of Fury consisted of four towers, each five-stories tall. The complex was surrounded by the wall that shared sides with the drudach walls.

History

The Towers of Fury was constructed in the Year of the Worm, 1356 DR , by Syl-Pasha Rashid el Djenispool after centuries of previous temples being destroyed by the irritation of either gods or mortals.
