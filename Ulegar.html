Ulegar was an Uthgardt sailor aboard the Vigilant , a ship that traveled between the Sword Coast and the Chultan Peninsula circa 1374 DR .

Description

Ulegar was a gruff middle-aged human male. His neck and backs of his hands were decorated with tribal tattoos . During the ship's voyage, the tattoos' origins often sparked interest.

Personality

The Uthgardt man disliked docking in Samarach , calling the city of Samargol a den of cowards.

History

In the 1374 DR , Captain Lastri Kassireh and the Vigilant sailed from the Sword Coast to the Chultan nation of Samarach. Onboard the ship were several passengers, including Volothamp Geddarm . The voyage was cursed with bad luck and numerous strange occurrences. Food supplies were mysteriously spoiled, freshwater barrels – ruptured, one of the crew – Kellen got drunk and drowned, Volo almost got poisoned, new ropes became frayed.

Ulegar's life ended when the string of strange occurrences culminated in a deadly storm. The Vigilant's shipwreck was washed ashore not far from the city of Samargol . Most sailors perished in the storm, while others were captured to be eaten by the Batiri .
