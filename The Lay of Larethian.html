The Lay of Larethian was a painting portraying the deity Corellon Larethian created sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The painting depicted the elvish god Corellon not as a beautiful blonde male, but as an elven lass with dark hair, lounging naked on a maroon muslin.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , The Lay of Larethian painting could be found hanging in the Szarr Palace in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate .
