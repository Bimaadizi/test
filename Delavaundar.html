Delavaundar was a member of the War Wizards of Cormyr , active in the mid 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Spur, 1348 DR , Delavaundar, alongside Marlegast , was assigned by Vangerdahast Aeiulvana , the Royal Magician of Cormyr, to dig for information on two of the newest members of the Knights of Myth Drannor . Then, the troupe bore the name of the Swords of Eveningstar, and its newest additions included "Pennae," or Alura Durshavin of Arabel , and Martess Ilmra .
