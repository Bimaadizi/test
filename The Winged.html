The Winged was a fallen planetar that dwelled on the Prime Material plane , in the nation of Thay on Toril during the late 14 th century DR . She was once a servant of Torm , the God of Duty, before her downfall.

Personality

The Winged was a guarded and personal individual who seldom spoke to anyone other than Dennaton , the operator of a gladiatorial arena in Thay, and members of her mercenary company, the Planar Hunters . She always ensured she was paid in full, and exactly on time according to her schedule.

Possessions

Among her other possessions, the Winged wore a single, unique gauntlet that granted her exceptional power in combat. The powerful armored glove could be worn by no one else but her.

Relationships

The Winged led the mercenaries known as the Planar Hunters . Among its members were the admaantine golem Bannor Kaxazel , an efreeti by the name of Dormamus , the bard Joker , the Red Wizard Pol Pyrrus , and a fire salamander called Thassk Kun .

By all appearances, the Winged and her mercenaries worked under the employ of Dennaton. In truth, they were employed by Szass Tam , the Thayan zulkir of necromancy , during his campaign against his rival Aznar Thrul .

Activities

For some time during her existence, the Winged fought as a champion in the Blood War . It was not clear if she fought for the baatezu of the Nine Hells , or somehow aligned herself with the demons of the Abyss .

During her time on Toril, the Winged and the other Hunters served Dennaton by capturing powerful combatants and fearsome beasts and perilous entities that would serve as gladiators in Dennaton's arena. They also acted as enforcers, to ensure the fighters remained subservient between their matches. Among the powers subdued by the Winged and her mercenaries were illithids , fiends from the lower planes , the dragon Yllaxxia and her offspring, and even two children of the god Bhaal , Enegar and Kastulor .
