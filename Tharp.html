Tharp was a warrior in the early 3 rd century DR , and an adventurer of the Brave Blades .

Appearance

He had a deep voice.

Personality

He spoke very little.

Relationships

He was a member of the Brave Blades adventuring band. He was also a spy for Undarl Dragonrider .

Possessions

He had a helm and armor.

History

At some point before him joining the Brave Blades, Tharp had become an informer for Undarl Dragonrider .

During the Brave Blades ' delve of Ondil's Tower in Marpenoth of Year of Much Ale, 233 DR , Tharp was one of their members. At the door to Ondil's tomb, after realizing that the lich Ondil was still able to cast spells and was slaying them with those from a distance every time they tried to open the door, he tried to go the other way; the doorway opposite the tomb's entrance was also trapped, and his bones were ripped out of his body, spraying up and out. He died as a result.

Aftermath

Upon Ondil's defeat, Undarl Dragonrider materialized into the tower and attacked the two remaining Brave Blades, revealing Tharp had been his spy.
