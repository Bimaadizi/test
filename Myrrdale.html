Myrrdale was a small town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Myrrdale was found in eastern Gwynneth in the kingdom of Corwell . The town sat among the eastern foothills of the Cambro Mountains near the hidden kingdom of Synnoria .
