The River Thent was a river located in the Moonsea North region of north Faerûn .

Geography

The river originated from Lake Thentar in the Great Grey Land of Thar , and flowed southward into the Moonsea lake.

The Sword Trail crossed the river at a point just west of the independent city of Thentia .
