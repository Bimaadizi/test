The staff of icy touch was an enchanted staff used in combat. It could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The staff of icy touch fired a ray of frost , but had a good chance of spontaneously releasing two additional rays, each targeting a different enemy of the wielder.
