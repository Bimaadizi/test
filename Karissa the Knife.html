Master Thief Karissa the Knife was a skilled rogue and senior officer of the Night Brigade gang in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Karissa had pale skin, gray eyes, and short black hair.

Personality

Karissa was quite greedy and aloof. She was happy to lair in her isolated hideout and wait for visitors or invaders to struggle their way in to find her.

Possessions

Among Karissa's treasure were her special goggles , the poniard of pain , echoes of night , tunneler's toolkit , and packrat's rat pack .

History

As the best thief in the Night Brigade, Karissa operated a thieves' guild of sorts in Wheloon Prison. Once secure in her position, she set up an opulent mansion for herself and blocked it off from any regular access. The only way to get in was to traverse a lengthy series of underground tunnels and chambers, filled with traps and monsters that she laid out for visitors. When the Riverside Rats came for their revenge against the Night Brigade, they sent allies ahead to bypass the hazards and face the deadly rogue. Surviving such threats as a ballista and otyugh , the invaders finally found Karissa in her lair and slew her.
