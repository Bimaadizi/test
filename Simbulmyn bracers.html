Simbulmyn bracers were enchanted arm-guards worn by the Simbulmyn of Aglarond .

Powers

The Simbulmyn served as the witch-queen of Aglarond's, the Simbul 's personal guard and spies, and as such, they needed powerful defensive items that steeled their minds and bodies. Simbulmyn bracers possessed a +2 defensive enchantment, granted them indomitable will, and magical toughness, and improved their reaction in battle. Additionally, the wearer received resistance to negative energy . Simbulmyn bracers were designed to be used by humans , elves , and half-elves exclusively.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , a pair of the Simbulmyn bracers fell into he hands of Mother Feodorra , a charm and magic item peddler from the Rashemi city of Mulsantir .
