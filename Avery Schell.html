Avery Schell was a cleric of Deneir and one of the headmasters of the Edificant Library in the midâ€“14 th century DR . He was the substitute father of Cadderly Bonaduce .

History

Avery became a father figure to Cadderly after Cadderly's father Aballister Bonaduce left under the shame of killing his wife, the mother of Cadderly.

In the Year of Maidens, 1361 DR , Avery was savagely killed by the mage Bogo Rath during an attack by the Night Masks while visiting Cadderly in Carradoon, with his student Kierkan Rufo leading him to his murder.
