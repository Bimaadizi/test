Yarlhang La , or the Trying Pass , was a prominent mountain pass located the Hordelands .

Based upon maps of the region, Yarlhang La was the general location where the A-Ling Shan and Katakoro Shan mountain ranges converged.

Geography

Located on the northwestern side of the mountain range, Yarlhang La was the only pass through the steep rock surfaces. The Old Caravan Road wound through the pass on the way toward Kushk .
