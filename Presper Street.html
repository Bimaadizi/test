Presper Street was a winding street in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep .

Location

This road ran from east to west, stretching from Ship Street to an intersection with Snail Street and Shrimp Alley , formerly known as Shrimp Street. At its midpoint, the street acted as the northern terminus of the Street of Six Casks .
