Greymace was the majordomo for the noble Seasilver family of Marsember during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Greymace had a hooked nose with eyes that were set close to one another. He wore fine robes of velvet, emblazoned with the sigil of House Seasilver.

Personality

Befitting his rank, Greymace had a commanding air that was evident every time he stepped into a room.

History

On Uktar 2 of the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , the city of Marsember was assaulted by shades during Netheril 's invasion of Cormyr . Greymace was able to accompany Grand Duke Farnig Seasilver aboard the Wave Wyvern during the conflict, sailing away from the city. Unfortunately, the Duke Seasilver was slain shortly thereafter, though Greymace's fate was unknown.
