Presio's dagger was an enchanted Talonite weapon wielded by Presio , the necromancer - cleric of Talona in 1281 DR .

Powers

Presio's dagger was enchanted to keep a keen edge, giving it an equivalent of a +2 enchantment. It was also magically infused with poison that continued damaging its targets following a successful strike, gradually sapping the target's strength.

History

Presio's dagger was a ceremonial weapon of the Talonite faith, given to Presio by her church sometime before the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , after attaining the rank of the Most Debilitating Holiness. The dagger's ceremonial nature was obvious, considering Presio's denomination did not allow her to use bladed weapons.

In 1281 DR, Presio's dagger was in its mistress' possession during her time in the Dragon's Eye , Spine of the World , working for Yxunomei , to destroy Kuldahar and conquer Icewind Dale . After Presio's death, the dagger was picked up by the heroes of Icewind Dale and eventually, in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , ended up in Marcos Flass 's collection of magic items during his work in Termalaine of the Ten-Towns .
