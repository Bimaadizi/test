Ashakar's Accessories was a showroom-style shop in the city of Ravens Bluff that sold all manner of small trinkets, accessories, and other unique aquatic tchotchkes during the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The shop was located on the southern end of the Front , within the Silverscales neighborhood of Ravens Bluff's Harbor District .

Interior

The showroom of the business featured quality decor and dim lighting.
