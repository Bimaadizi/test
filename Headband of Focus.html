The Headband of Focus was a unique circlet associated with the faith of Nobanion .

Description

The headband was made of gold -colored silk . Along the length of the band were stylized depictions of lions .

Powers

It bestowed upon its wearer improved skill in unarmed combat. Additionally, it allowed them to attain divine strength, speed, and fortitude , for a limited duration of time.

History

The Headband of Focus was crafted by a devour follower of Nobanion. During its crafting, they focused upon Lord Fireman's teachings and entered into a state of meditation. Upon its completion, they realized the headband had unique properties, bestowed upon it by Nobanion himself.

By the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , it had come into the possession of the unscrupulous fence Waizahb .
