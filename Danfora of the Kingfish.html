Danfora of the Kingfish was a drow from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Danfora had long white wavy hair and she wore a circlet and a set of light armor .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a large group of duergar , giant spiders , and drow, was slaughtered in the home of a Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica . The murder spree was performed by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal . Danfora's butchered body was found in a cellar underneath Lavernica's home, near a slaughtered drow woman, a grotesque display – an effigy representing Sendai , a drow Bhaalspawn who died a century earlier.
