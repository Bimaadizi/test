Skoda Valanaster , nicknamed Bookwyrm , was the First Reader of Candlekeep for a time during the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

An experienced spellcaster , Skoda had access to a variety of spells up to and including 6 th level.

Activities

As First Reader, Skoda dedicated their efforts to continually increase the number of tomes and other articles of lore that were counted among Candlekeep's ever-expanding collection.

Relationships

First Reader Skoda served under Janussi the Keeper of the Tomes, and was aided by the efforts of the eight Great Keepers of the Avowed .
