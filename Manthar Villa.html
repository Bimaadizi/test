Manthar Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Manthar .

Location

The villa was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward at the border with the North Ward on the northwest corner of the intersection of Shield Street and Delzorin Street .

Description

The walled compound contained a large central building and several smaller surrounding structures. Each building stood between one and three stories tall.
