Weave whispers were a phenomenon of the Weave in which utterances were repeated as a side effect of uncontrolled magic .

Origin

Weave whispers were snippets of audible voices, and, as of 1372Â DR , most of them were from long prior.

Activation

The hauntings accompanied a number of triggers, such as the shattering of long-extant magic fields, wild magic effects, traversing portals , spellcastings in particular places, the death of a powerful archmage, or sometimes even in response to prayers to Mystra or Azuth .

Known Manifestations

According to Elminster , the Dragon Wood was known to harbor such hauntings.
