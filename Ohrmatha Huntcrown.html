Lady Ohrmatha Huntcrown was the head of the noble Huntcrown family of Cormyr in the 14 th century DR .

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Lady Ohrmatha attended King Azoun Obarskyr IV 's birthday feast, hosted by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Keep . She was accompanied there by Lord Krell Huntcrown .
