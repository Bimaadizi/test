The River's Heart was a shop that sold magical items and other curiosities.

Location

This magic item shop was located just west of the north gate of Neverwinter .

Services

The River's Heart had several items available for purchase, such as enchanted arrows and battleaxes . It also sold magic scrolls as well as the powerful dart of the hornet's nest .
