Alec I of the Cymrych Dynasty was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the early-to-mid 7 th century DR .

Relationships

He was the first son of High King Embro II and the elder brother of Samuel Cymrych . His successor was his young son, Rowland Cymrych .

History

Alec I assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of Burning Skies, 632 DR , following the death of his father.

He ruled for only eight years before being mauled to death by a bear while hunting in the Year of the Fanged Beast, 640 DR .
