Chever was a Netherese archwizard and well-renowned mentalist .

Personality

Chever was an arcanist obsessed with discovering new things and retaining the knowledge of them.

History

Chever was born in 1894 NY (−1965 DR) and established the Netherese enclave Opus at 39 years old. Upon Opus, he ordered the construction of universities, theatres, and other academic buildings. Chever was also interested in spell creation, and developed contact other plane in 1945 NY (−1914 DR) and simulacrum 11 years later. Driven by his obsession with acquiring knowledge, he used his first spell to repeatedly contact extraplanar beings. Because of this, he was driven insane and jumped into a sphere of annihilation in 1960 NY (−1899 DR) .
