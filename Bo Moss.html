Bo Moss was the youngest child of the Moss family of the Twilight Marsh . Baby Bo was born sometime during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Bo's parents Millivent and Haldred Moss had four other children, all of whom were older. Bo's siblings included sisters Alleena and Kithian , and brothers Quayle and Volland .

History

During the 1480s DR , the Moss family farm was attacked by goblins from the Foulwater tribe. During the attack, Millivent managed to scoop baby Bo to safety and leave the farm in time before being captive. The other members of the family were not so fortunate.
