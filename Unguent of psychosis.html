Unguents of psychosis , were magic bright yellow ointments, popularized in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

These unguents were in common use among rogues who coated their weapons with the salve. Those who suffered contact with a weapon coated in unguent of psychosis suffered crippling psychotic damage from greatly amplified hearing that became an excruciating torture.

History

In the 15 th century DR, unguent of psychosis could be found in use around the Sword Mountains , areas surrounding the city and in the markets of Neverwinter .
