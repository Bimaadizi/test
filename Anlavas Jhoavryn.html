Anlavas Jhoavryn was a merchant in the mid–8 th century DR .

Activities

He dealt in brasswork and cheap sundries, but worked in bulk.

History

The merchant hailed from somewhere south of Nethra , across the sea.

During the late Year of Mistmaidens, 764 DR , his throat was cut while at Nethra. Lord Elminster Aumar , then the Court Mage of Galadorna , made inquiries of the local guard captain, Rhoagalow , who admitted he suspected Limmator to be behind his death.
