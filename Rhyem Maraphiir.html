Rhyem Maraphiir was a moon elf and the father of the wizard Ashemmi .

Relatives

Rhyem had two children with the sun elf Chelryn Sarynnryllar : Ashemmi (born Shemithil Maraphiir) and Malphys Maraphiir .

History

Soon after Ashemmi's birth in Silverymoon , the family moved to live with the wood elves in the Neverwinter Wood , and were taken in by the Ilbaereth house.
