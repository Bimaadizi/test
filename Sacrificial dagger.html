The sacrificial dagger was the personal ritual dagger of Priestess Vicala Szind of Sschindylryn in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The blade had a curved point and many barbs angled backwards, ensuring that it would cause massive tearing when pulled from a wound. It bore a sickly yellow glow from the virulent enchantments upon it.

Powers

The dagger had lethal and gruesome enchantments , including a vampiric negative energy bite that drained the life force of the victim and transferred it to the wielder. It also exuded a terrible poison that weakened the target rather than killing them too quickly, and possessed a powerful +6 enchantment that guided the wielder's hand towards the target and increased the weapon's natural damage.

History

The Priestess Vicala had the blade during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar during the Darkening , when she attacked the Midler farmhouse and took the Midler family as sacrificial prisoners. During her time around Eveningstar , she used the blade to sacrifice many innocent farmers.
