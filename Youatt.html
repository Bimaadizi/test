Youatt was a veterinarian inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He lived in a small house located in central Daggerford on the northern perimeter of the drill field. Youatt specialized in caring for typical pets such as small mammals and reptiles. He worked out of his home.
