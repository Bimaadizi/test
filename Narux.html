Narux was a half-orc worshiper of Talos and a member of the anchorites of Talos in the late 15 th century DR . Along with fellow anchorites Flenz and Yargath , he was responsible for overseeing rituals dedicated to Talos , god of storms, at the Circle of Thunder in southern Neverwinter Wood .

Abilities

As an anchorite of Talos, Narux was a shapechanger —able to adopt the form of a boar —and a divine spellcaster able to invoke his god's power to cast lightning bolt .

Possessions

Narux lived in a cave on the northeast side of the Circle of Thunder, and had few possessions save for those his fellow anchorites also shared, such as a pet boar. However, at some point he managed to acquire an enchanted shield , which he hid inside his cave by burying it beneath the floor.

Activities

Narux and his fellow anchorites conducted rituals at the Circle of Thunder in order to please Talos and to create storms. They also revered and would attempt to summon Gorthok the Thunder Boar , a primal spirit in service to Talos. These rituals often entailed blood sacrifices of animals or humanoids , followed by dancing around the victim's corpse while wearing a rotted boar's head as a mask.
