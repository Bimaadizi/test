Rhendar Nhalass I was the ninth Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Rhendar I reigned from −662 DR to −633 DR , inheriting the throne upon the death of his father Umathar V .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Darthen III .
