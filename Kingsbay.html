Kingsbay was a natural bay located between the islands of Snowdown and Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Kingsbay was located off of the coast of southeast Gwynneth along the Strait of Alaron . The bay's eastern side bordered the island of Snowdown.

The bay was a major fishing ground.
