Chief Tokan was the leader of the dwarven Orothiar Clan in Bloodstone Barony as of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Activities

Tokan normally spoke of ancient events like they'd happened yesterday.

Possessions

He carried with him an enchanted dwarven thrower hammer that was revered by his clan.

History

In 1357 DR, Tokan and his tribe met the adventuring party of Gareth Dragonsbane . Following the Bloodstone Wars , Tokan became the clan's representative on the Council of Bloodstone that governed its namesake barony .

Later in the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR Tokan and his people had joined the Alliance of Belt Watchers alongside a group of local halflings and centaurs .
