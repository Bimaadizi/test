Varro , who was nicknamed the Fat , was a Rashemaar innkeep inhabiting Immilmar in Rashemen in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

Activities

He owned the Blackstone House inn . Varro enjoyed spending time with foreigners to Rashemen, and catered his inn to their needs. He kept Blackstone House stocked with a fine selection of wines and ales , in addition to an impressive menu featuring cuisine from a dozen nations.
