The Inn of the Sea Bass was located in Huzuz , the City of Delights, along the banks of Al-Sarif River .

Description

This was a small inn that afforded its inhabitants a splendid view of the Caliph's Harbor .

Inhabitants

Keyfa al-Parid lived at the inn so she could be close to her duties at the harbor.
