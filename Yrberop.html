Yrberop was a myconid and leader of the Circle of Sporers in Neverlight Grove in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Yrberop was easily seduced when Zuggtmoy 's will twisted most of the Circle circa 1486 DR . It began creating more and more myconid sprouts.

Personality

Because of Zuggtmoy's influence, Yrberop always swayed to a music that only it could hear, and those in tune with the myconid could hear a faint echo. Yrberop was excited for Phylo 's plans to spread the will of Zuggtmoy throughout the Underdark . Due to its madness, Yrberop forgot all conversations after they ended.
