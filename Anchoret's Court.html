Anchoret's Court was a small courtyard that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Anchoret's Courty was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . It sat at the southern end of Anchoret's Alley just north of the Specterwalk .

Description

A public well sat in the center of the courtyard.

Notable Locations

The home of Kapplian Mulmastyr stood on the eastern side of Anchoret's Court near Caravan Street .
