Austrus Flambrant was a noble in Cormyr and the lord of Flambrant House.

Relationships

Flambrant was close to Lady Jalassra Dawningdown .

History

Around 1480 DR , Lady Dawningdown disappeared from Suzail and Flambrant believed the culprits to be Mirt and Lady Rensharra Ironstave . He managed to find them near Lord Lothan Durncaskyn of Immerford and went asking for answers. Mirt confronted him and knocked him down.
