Leanwall Alley was an alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

Leanwall Alley was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . It ran in between Chop Street and the northern exterior wall of the city. Leanwall Alley branched east from the Northyard and ended where Shanty Lane deadended.
