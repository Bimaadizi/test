Markovel Stonestorm was a maker of harps and owner of Stonestorm's Harpery in the city of Silverymoon in the Silver Marches in the late 14 th century DR . Around 1372 DR , Markovel was a regular client of Chesmyr Morrowynd , a maker of harp strings, and they met to do business in Swordsfall House .
