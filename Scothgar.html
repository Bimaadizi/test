Scothgar was a Northlander human man and a prince of Rogarsheim on the island of Norland in the Moonshae Isles in the 7 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Nineteen Swords, 621 DR , he was due to marry the Ffolk Princess Bridget of Callidyrr , but she and her whole royal entourage vanished on the voyage to Norland . Ffolk and Northmen each blamed the other for the disappearance, which worsened relations between them.
