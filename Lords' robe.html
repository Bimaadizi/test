A Lords' robe was an enchanted piece of clothing given to all Lords of Waterdeep . It was one of three articles, referred to collecitvely as the Lord's ensemble .

Description

These robes were dark black and voluminous.

Powers

The magic of a Lords' robe disguised the shape and height of the Lord who wore it, making all Lords appear to be approximately 6 feet (1.8 meters) tall with an unknown gender. A true seeing spell could pierce the illusion , but even in that case the physical nature of the robe made it difficult to distinguish physical features.
