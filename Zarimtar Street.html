Zarimtar Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Zarimtar Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . It originated on Sul Street where it branched west to end at a large intersection that included the Sutherlane , Feather Street , Darselune Street , and Haltovar Street . Mendever Street crossed Zarimtar Street near its midpoint and Cloaksweep Alley connected with it just north of the Courtyard of the Well .
