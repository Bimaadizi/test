Vlotho the Blue was a half-elf gambler who lived in the city of Harglast , in the Windrise Ports of Laerakond , as of 1479 DR .

History

Vlotho was a known gambler and gossiper in Harglast, and he usually dealt with more obscure information as well. He was also a skilled player of Liar's Dice , a popular dice game in Harglast. Vlotho was a regular customer of the Leaping Hart Tavern .
