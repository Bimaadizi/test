Araeralee Summerstar was an apprentice wizard inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

She was a compassionate woman.

Activities

Araeralee was a student of Blackstaff Academy within Blackstaff Tower in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

History

She was an orphan who grew up in Blackstaff Tower.

In 1369 DR , she was one of the apprentices on watch when Qilué Veladorn attempted to enter Blackstaff Tower from a hidden entrance. Rather than slaying her, she decided to rouse Laeral Silverhand , to the mocking of her fellow apprentice on watch.
