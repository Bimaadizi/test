The cloak of the crow was an enchanted cloak worn by Dahlia Sin'felle .

Description

The cloak of the crow was a high-collared black cape that could transform its wear into a giant crow .

Powers

The cloak of the crow was also reputed to affect changes to its bearer. When worn by Kensidan , the cloak of the crow made the man appear more bird-like, giving him an awkward, stiff-legged gait and a long, hooked nose.

History

The cloak of the crow was worn by Kensidan the Crow, of Ship Rethnor . In the Year of Knowledge Unearthed, 1451 DR , the cloak of the crow was worn by Borlann , the grandson of Kensidan. Following the death of Borlann, Sylora Salm became the wearer of the cloak of the crow . In the Year of the Reborn Hero, 1463 DR , Dahlia Sin'felle took possession of the cloak of the crow after her and her companions Drizzt Do'Urden and Barrabus the Gray defeated Sylora Salm.
