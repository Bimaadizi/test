Auntie Ethel's charm was an enchanted talisman made out of a rot-smelling bone.

Powers

Whoever held this charm felt they were strangely powerful. When the charm was snapped in two, it unleashed a collection of spells on the owner: bear's endurance , bull's strength , cat's grace , ' eagle's splendor , fox's cunning , and owl's wisdom .

History

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the kindly herbalist woman who inhabited the Riverside Teahouse in the Sunlit Wetlands – Auntie Ethel , was a green hag who wore a human guise. One of these bone charms was a creation of her hag magics. Ethel was willing to part with the charm as a gift to those adventurers who would not attempt to take from the hag the young woman named Mayrina and her unborn baby.
