Nulra Blacksaddle was a member of the Knights of the Shield who lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Nulra worked under the cambion Kaddrus , who himself was an aide to lady Lutecia Hhune of Baldur's Gate .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Nulra and two other knights of the Shield accompanied Kaddrus on his mission to reclaim the Shield of the Hidden Lord .
