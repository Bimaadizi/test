Palaghard Obarskyr II of the Obarskyr dynasty was a king of Cormyr in the 13 th century DR , reigning from the Year of the Wall, 1227 DR to the Year of Bright Dreams, 1261 DR .

Relationships

Palaghard was born the second son of King Dhalmass Obarskyr and Queen Jhalass Huntsilver in the Year of the Waking Wrath, 1214 DR . He had two sisters, Pelara and Elliira , as well as an older brother, Proster , who died at the age of 9 in 1222 DR .

Palaghard's queen was Alendue Wyrvar . They had three sons: Lhannas (who died at the age of 22 in 1258 DR ), Azoun (who would become King Azoun III), and Salember (who would be regent after Azoun's death).
