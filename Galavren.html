Galavren was an early knight of Neverwinter and the second of the Nine knights who served Lord Halueth Never . They were the basis for the Neverwinter Nine .

History

A statue of Galavren and his sword stood guard in Halueth's tomb. It bore the message "The only who remembers the promise left unfulfilled."
