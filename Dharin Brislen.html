Dharin Brislen was the husband to Leepak Brislen and father to Blossom and Bunny Brislen , all inhabitants of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Dharin was a human with a tan skin tone, hazel eyes, and bright chestnut hair gathered in a bun. He wore a simple workman garb.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Dharin attempted to ship his wife and daughters away from the Gate. He attempted to negotiate a passage to Waterdeep with Captain Sleam , a smuggler with her own boat and crew. Sleam asked for too much money claiming it was a life and death situation for her crew too. Dharin could not afford to pay for four seats so he resorted for staying behind. It was unknown of the family escaped the Gate before the battle of the Upper City commenced.
