Aithe High Street was a the main road that passed through Aithelar in Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles in Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR .

Location

Aithe High Street passed through the center of town, running north to south. It intersected many of Aithelar's other streets including Bottle Lane , Whitefish Alley , Eel Street , Rope End Lane , Finn's Alley , Aerynn's Street , Blackdorn's Way and Blaedar Street .
