Bubbles Tefoco was the oldest daughter of Oliver Tefoco , the proprietor of the Beehive Goods and Wares general store in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Bubbles was a daughter of Oliver and Kroyce Tefoco , mother of Dorimara , and a sister to a number of siblings. The older siblings were: Dariel Tefoco , who served the Flaming Fist , Thegglis , and Crowell , who were training as caravan guards. The youngest siblings Hemjax , Maggie , and others still lived with Oliver and Kroyce as of the late 15 th century DR.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute threatened Baldur's Gate's safety, Bubbles lived outside the Lower City walls in the Stonyeyes neighborhood and gave birth to Dorimara, who became the first granddaughter of Oliver Tefoco's. As the city became closed off in fear of the cultists, Bubbles received a letter from Oliver Tefoco with several coins he sent over for her to buy a cute hat for the newborn. The grandfather could not wait to visit but was worried about the closed-off city gates and burdening his daughter, who had just given birth.
