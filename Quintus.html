Quintus was a Netherese arcanist who oversaw the Alchemical Seminary in Eileanar shortly before its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Personality

Quintus was a kindly old man.

Activities

Quintus was an expert transmuter and had learned the techniques for turning lead to gold . He spent his time managing the Seminary and its roster of teachers, including Hakewill , Cujas , Thibauneau , Fries , Delacroix , and Colombier . He was extremely good at running the school, and had turned it into the foremost institution in the enclave regarding alchemy and the related magical fields.
