Pursai was a town in Laothan in Malatra .

Geography

It stood amidst the jungles of northern Laothan, halfway along a trail linking Sayan and Nalamh .

Economy

In the midâ€“14 th century DR , Pursai grew rich on the spice trade, particularly cocoa , pepper , saffron , and vanilla . Spices were harvested in the surrounding lands.

Legends

A killer stalked Pursai. Local spice harvesters were attacked at random and brutally murdered, but the killer disappeared before he could apprehended. Dubbed "the Old Man of Pursai", he was thought to be a mad hermit or even a ghost . Although a reward was set, no one had yet been able to capture him as of around 1357 DR .
