The Shannyth River was the only named river found on the isle of Moray within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Shannyth River originated near the center of the Orcskull Mountains in southern Moray. If flowed north for 10 miles (16 kilometers) before reaching the Breasal Marsh . The river formed the western border of the marsh for 25 miles (40 kilometers) before joining Lac Dynnegall on its southern shore. The river exited the lake on its north shore, joined an unnamed river from the Trollclaw Range , and continued to flow northeast before reaching the coast of the Sea of Moonshae near Caer Moray .
