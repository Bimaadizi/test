Sulchra's was a festhall of some repute in Suzail , the capital of Cormyr , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

Sulchra's Festhall stood on Coachwheels Way , just north from where it came to a moot with Swordstars Lane . Across the street from the establishment stood the Thousandheads Trading Coster yards.
