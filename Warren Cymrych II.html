Warren Cymrych II was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid-to-late 7 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty , and had been named in honor of the first Cymrych monarch . He was the first son of his predecessor, High King Samuel , and his successor was his own first son, Tobert .

History

Warren II assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Waning Sun, 651 DR , following the death of his father.

He ruled for twenty-three years before he died of natural causes in the Year of the Nomad, 674 DR .
