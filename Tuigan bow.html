Tuigan bows were enchanted shortbows crafted by the Tuigan people of the Endless Wastes steppes.

Powers

Tuigan bows were masterfully crafted specifically to accommodate mobile Tuigan horseback riders. The process of the bows creation was a closely guarded sacred, but these weapons possessed a +1 enchantment and could be shot at a dazzling speed, thrice quicker than any ordinary shortbow.

History

Tuigan bows found their way into the hands of the inhabitants of the Sword Coast following the Grand Army of the Tuigan invasion of the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR . In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , one such bow was in the hands of the Beastmaster who worked in the illicit fighting pits in the Copper Coronet in the City of Coin, Athkatla .
