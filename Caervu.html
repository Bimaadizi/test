Caervu was a village located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Caervu sat along the east coast of the island on the Strait of Alaron , just east of the Cambrent Gap .

History

Caervu fell into ruin following the conquest of Gwynneth and expulsion of the humans by the fey of Sarifal . It remained a ruin as of the late 15 th century DR .
