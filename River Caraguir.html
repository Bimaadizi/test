The River Caraguir was located in Dambrath .

Geography

The River Caraguir originated in the Gnollwatch Mountains , flowing south across Dambrath before emptying into the Bay of Dancing Dolphins .

Notable locations

The city of Luenath was located on the west bank of the river.
