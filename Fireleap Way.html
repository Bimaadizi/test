Fireleap Way was a long and straight street in Ravens Bluff that crossed the city almost completely, from east to west, beginning in the Foreign District and ending in the Tentowers neighborhood.

Geography

Fireleap Way's western end was located in the Foreign District, by the Coker Wharf Company 's docks. From there the street ran east, turning into Daren's Ring circle with the Ring Park in the middle, turning back into a straight line just before the gate out of the Foreign District. Next, Fireleap Way passed the moot with Westwall Way North and Fire Lane , further east it crossed Raphiel Road , Blacktree Boulevard North , De Villars Ride , Manycoins Way , the moot with Macintyre Path and Evensong Ride , followed by crossings with O'Kane Court , Waelstar Way , and Lamorgyr's Ride . Then, Fireleap Way crossed De Villars Creek via the Old Dragonbridge , and continued on east past Torngel's Ride , Stonekeep Concourse , and finally ended on Bouldergrinder's Way.
