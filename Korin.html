Korin was a bard and a regular patron of the Beached Leviathan , one of the most popular taverns of Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

Activities

Korin was known as the "chanteyman" of the Beached Leviathan because he always performed in the tavern. Korin was a friendly fellow, happy to tell stories and lead drinking songs and sea tunes. On occasion, he sang a dark dirge about a man who lost his family to devil cultists that showed real talent.

History

At some point before 1479 DR , Korin's family was murdered by Ashmadai cultists operating out of Neverwinter. Korin went to live in Neverwinter in the hopes of tracking down the cultists and get revenge.
