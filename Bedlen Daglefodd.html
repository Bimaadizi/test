Bedlen Daglefodd was a humble svirfneblin who lived in a small village in the Underdark .

Possessions

The deep gnome owned and fought with a one-handed axe , darts , and a buckler . He also donned a set of chainmail armor .

History

Drow patrols near Ust Natha cast spells upon Bedlen when he was wandering outside of his village around the year 1368 DR . He somehow became trapped in the second-to-right chamber of the Facet sphere , where his soul was kept for a few days. He may have been freed by Gorion's Ward , by request of Bedlen's father, Therndle .
