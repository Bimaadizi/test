Skie Silvershield II was a noblewoman from the wealthy Silvershield family living in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century.

Personality

She became the authoritative and unyielding matriarch of her family in Baldur's Gate.

Relationships

Skie was the elder daughter of Torlin Silvershield and Evelyn Silvershield .

She secretly observed her son Ralagondar 's actions through her spies, among whom was the doppelganger Seldrorn . Despite Ralagondar's attempts to conceal his wrongdoings, his amusing behavior thoroughly entertained her, and she was quickly becoming a devoted admirer of his deeds.

History

Skie was born in the Silvershield family and lived in the Silvershield estate in Baldur's Gate. Her family's substantial wealth allowed them to allocate several thousand gold pieces for the acquisition of an operatic chandelier, complete with whispering candles , for her cotillion ball.

Skie's first marriage with Ostrand Beldaerhallow was arranged when she was five, but he passed away mere months later. She was married again to Atelburt Maerbraunt later that very year.

Her husband Atelburt was eager to have offspring immediately, so he used a powerful and contemptible spell to accelerate her aging. From around the Year of the Third Circle, 1472 DR , she gave birth to a son, Ralagondar, and two daughters, Paerevelle and Elnantra . Her forced aging didn't lead to any development of love within this marriage and they grew apart even more. When she was near 18 years of age in 1492 DR , she could be described as "elderly".

Around the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , her second husband Atelburt died. By this time, some of the influential family members had left the Gate and Skie became the leader among the Silvershields still living in the city.
