Nym Mlezziir was the Battlemaster of the Eastern Marches in Sshamath in the year 1370 DR and 1372 DR .

Nym was one of the primary military commanders of the city, assigned to supervising the patrols in the tunnels and the caverns that included the Gauth Grottoes and the ruins of Oghrann . He was renowned for fighting with dwarves and nighteyes and it was said that he had slain at least two gauth alone.
