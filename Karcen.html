Gravedigger Karcen was a member of The Guild from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Karcen was tall and strong human man with dark skin and curly brown hair. He wore a breastplate and carried a longsword .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Gravedigger Karcen served Nine-Fingers Keene as a muscle and could be often found in the Undercellar where The Guild's Guildhall was hidden. During the Absolute crisis , Karcen stood alongside Nine-Fingers during the attempt of Zhentarim takeover of the weakened Guild. The conflict's outcome was unrecorded, and neither was Karcen's survival.
