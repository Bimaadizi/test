Gureth the Obedient was an elf warlock in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Gureth had pale orange hair and dark green eyes. He wore an ornate leaf-styled green robe and could be seen floating off the ground in a ring of flickering energy.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR during the Elven Crusade , Gureth joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp on the outskirts of Myth Drannor . He served as a trainer for other warlocks who passed by.
