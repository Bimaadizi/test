Harps of the Beastmaster were enchanted instruments, created to be used by bards , rangers , and Harper scouts.

Powers

These harps found wide use by the sea wolves and sea-faring adventurers for its ability to charm both beasts and men. Each harp of the Beastmaster allowed the wielder to charm person or mammal via its enchanted music three times a day, and dominate animal once a day.

History

After the Wailing Death of 1372 DR , harps of the Beastmaster could be found in several locations of the Nelanther Islands, namely sold at the The Monkey's Cutlass of Spindrift Town by Grogbeard Mc Pherson .
