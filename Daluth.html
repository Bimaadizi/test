Daluth was a Sharran faithful during the mid-8 th century DR .

Description

He was a bald man with purple and black robes. He spoke with a thick drawl.

Possessions

He looted a plethora of magic from Karsus' Keep .

Relationships

Daluth was one of Elryn 's dreadspells. He was not greatly loyal in extreme situations.

Abilities

As a dreadspell, he wielded borrowed arcane power.

History

Daluth was one of the dreadspells sent with Elryn to find and slay a Chosen of Mystra near Westgate two tendays into the Chaos of the Art in the 797 DR . During the first leg of the trip, he had Uldus Blackram lead the way, while a knife to the man's back. During the first leg of their trip, Uldus Blackram was killed by Ilbryn Starym , an elven wizard they chanced upon.

Once they found the ruin, Karsus' Keep, Daluth helped loot it of valuables. The team of dreadspells then sought to bring down the whole ruin on top of two people they spotted, a self-styled god Azuth and the Chosen of Mystra they sought, by using all their looted magic to hurl destructive spells at them. Once their looted magic failed against Azuth, despite bringing down the ruin, they fled.

When Dread Sister Klalaera appeared and herded them back against Azuth, they were stopped partway by Umbregard and Starsunder , two mages that appeared suddenly. During the battle, after being injured by a beam that bared the flesh of his shoulder and the Dreadspells subsequently being reduced to just two, Daluth struck with a wand against Klalaera; upon death, her body to become animated by the power of Darklady Avroana . He was killed by a bolt of dark flame sprouting from her mouth, which left hardly anything of him.
