Muleskull Tavern was a tavern that doubled as the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Dungsweepers' .

Location

This building was located along Ship Street , in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This building stood two-stories tall.

Reputation

The Muleskull Tavern was among some of the more notable and recognizable buildings in the Dock Ward, considered by locals to be a landmark.
