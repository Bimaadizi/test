Borlann was the leader of Ship Rethnor and one of the High Captains of the city of Luskan in 1450 DR .

History

He was the last lover of the Thayan elf Dahlia Sin'felle but in that year Dahlia decided to terminate their relationship, and as always, she killed Borlann after the separation.

Possessions

Borlann was the grandson of Kensidan and possessed his Cloak of the Crow , a magic cape that could polymorph a person into a giant crow, which was later taken by Dahlia.
