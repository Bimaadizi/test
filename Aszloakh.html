Aszloakh the Hooded was the finhead saurial who was in charge of The Fool's Altar dedicated to Finder Wyvernspur , located in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Aszloakh's moniker was given to him by inhabitants of the Gate because the saurial always wore a hooded weathercloak when outside on the city streets. He had a deep and rumbling voice and had many years of service to the Church of Finder Wyvernspur under his belt. Personality-wise, Aszloakh always remained patient and calm, even in the face of danger. The finhead saw humans from a friendly, humorous, yet cynical lens. He expected to be lied to and betrayed.

Possessions

The Hooded one carried an enchanted staff , but its powers were unknown to most, even Elminster Aumar .
