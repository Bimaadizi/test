Sarvyn Eorn , who was also called the Old , was the first King of the Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Sarvyn came into his throne by widespread acclamation of the general public in the Year of the Dracorage, 1018 DR , after King Tarnhart II died without an heir. Sarvyn ruled for many years until Year of the Old Giant, 1111 DR .

Relationships

His grandnephew, Roadaeron Lorndessar , would succeed him to the throne.
