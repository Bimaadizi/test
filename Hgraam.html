Stonespeaker Hgraam was the leader of the stone giants of Gracklstugh and a cleric of Skoraeus Stonebones in the late 14 th through to late 15 th centuries DR .

Personality

Hgraam was a capable leader for his people, known for his intelligence, diplomatic skills, and martial prowess. However, he was strict and demanding, but never cruel and his word was his bond. He did not much like the Council of Savants , nor did they like him. Hgraam despised the new ruler Horgar Steelshadow for his enormous ambition and the rumors of his involvement in the death of King Tarngardt

Activities

Hgraam served both as chief and shaman of the Clan Clairngorm in Gracklstugh.

History

In his youth, Hgraam went to the mystical library of Gravenhollow .

Circa 1486 DR , he sensed that some great evil had infiltrated the Underdark, but wasn't aware of the threat's danger or extent.

Relationship

In the late 1400s DR, he had some apprentices, among them Dorhun and Rihuud .
