Erich Inshiland was the Grand Mairshar of Tasseldale in early 15 th century DR . He followed the reign of Elizzaria Whitehand , having been groomed by her at an early age as she prepared to step down.

History

In 1420 DR , Grand Mairshar Inshiland led the dale in battle to resist the invasion of Sembian mercenaries from Yhaunn . Their forces lost unfortunately, and the dale fell under the rule of Sembia.

Personality

During his youth, Erich was known for having a cheerful disposition, which grew into a charismatic personality as leader of Tasseldale.
