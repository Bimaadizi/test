Jaxanaedegor was an ancient green vampiric dragon who lived on Mount Thulbane in Threskel .

Appearance

Jaxanaedegor was described as having scales of alternating light and dark green and yellow eyes. He was so big that he even towered over blue dragons .

History

Around 1365 DR , Alasklerbanbastos sent Jaxanaedegor and some lesser dracoliches to devastate Mourktar where Regent Kabarrath Telthaug had ignored the will of the dragon-king. In 1479 DR , he was Alasklerbanbastos's lieutenant during Threskel's war with Chessenta . He was also a taaldarax of Brimstone 's xorvintaal .

In one of the most important battles in the war, he switched sides and betrayed Alasklerbanbastos.
