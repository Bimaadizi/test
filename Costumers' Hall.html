Costumers' Hall was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Order of Master Taylors, Glovers, and Mercers .

Location

Costumers' Hall was located along the High Road and Spendthrift Alley , in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This guildhall stood two-stories tall.

Interior

The second floor of this building was used as the guild's meeting rooms.

Services

This guildhall offered clothing services, in the form of costumes, as well as props.

Reputation

The Costumers' Hall was among some of the more notable and recognizable guildhalls in the Trades Ward. Like other guildhalls in the Trades Ward, it was considered by locals to be a landmark.
