Irwynne was a clever elven adventurer in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Irwynne had long sandy hair, and dressed in a green robe.

Personality

She had a very analytical mind, and was always planning ahead.

History

In between their adventures, she visited the Ball and Chain Tavern on the docks of the Wheloon Prison alongside her friends Marbone , Gorli , and Bash .
