The Amphail gray was a breed of light horse exclusively bred in village of Amphail in the Sword Coast North .

Description

They were known for being particularly loyal and hardy steeds. Aside from their namesake coloring, they were fairly average in appearance.

Price

These horses fetched a fair price at market and typically sold for 225 gp a head.
