The Healing House of Lathander was a temple to Lathander in the city of Scornubel .

It was the city's only temple, and people came from all over the city to seek healing from its priests.

Inhabitants

As of the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , the temple was run by High Priestess Josura Hlammel .
