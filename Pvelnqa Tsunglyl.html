Pvelnqua Tsunglyl was a member of the derro Council of Savants of Gracklstugh in 1370 DR .

Personality

Pvelnqua was a traditionalist who openly called for a Uniting War against an enemy like Blingdenstone or Menzoberranzan . He believed that the derro had become too soft and a war could restore their ancient glory and cull the weak. Secretly, he believed that derro could even win this war and conquer vast parts of the Underdark . His areas of expertise were derro cultural traditions, speleology and the military history of the Underdark.

History

Pvelnqua was a recently elevated member of the Council in 1370 DR.
