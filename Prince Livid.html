Prince Livid was a massive gray ooze follower of the Pudding King in Blingdenstone in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The Pudding King, an insane deep gnome fascinated by oozes , subjugated a black pudding he called Princess Ebonmire and a gray ooze he called Prince Livid. Princess Ebonmire and Prince Livid always remained close to the Pudding King.

Circa 1486 DR , after a great battle between the Pudding King's ooze army and all the factions of Blingdenstone gathered together, Prince Livid was killed just before its master by a party of adventurers hired by some deep gnome factions after escaping from drow slavers of Velkynvelve .
