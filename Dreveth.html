Dreveth the Gray was a male human diviner who resided in Hillsfar and would occasionally help adventurers.

Abilities

As a highly experienced wizard who specialised in divination magic , Dreveth could cast some of very powerful divination spells , if needed.

Activities

Dreveth the Gray was occasionally known to lend help to adventurers in need of his aid. However this help was generally only given out to those he deemed to be serving the cause of good.

History

In 1372 DR , Dreveth the Gray resided in Hillsfar where he would aid adventurers that he considered to be undertaking goodly deeds.
