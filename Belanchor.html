Belanchor was a port town of the Sea of Fallen Stars , completely wiped out by the 14 th century DR .

Geography

Belanchor was located in the eastern Sea of Fallen Stars, possibly along the Wizard's Reach or the north coast of Chessenta .

Trade

It was a trading port of some importance.

History

The port was still extant in the early 3 rd century DR , but had been wiped out after a dragon took it for its lair, and then battled a rival in a fight that turned the entire town into so much rubble to be swallowed by the sea.
