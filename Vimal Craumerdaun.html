Vimal Craumerdaun was a human male and the head accountant for the Craumerdaun family in Eshpurta , Amn , in early 1370 DR, the Year of the Tankard . Due to the treatment of arcane spellcasters in Amn, Vimal kept his wizardly skills a closely guarded secret.
