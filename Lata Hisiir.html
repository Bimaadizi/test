Lata Hisiir was a half-elf trader who inhabited the Shining Lands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Lata specialized in the sale and trade of gemstones and jewels. She wandered the Shining Lands, always on the lookout for a good deal and was honest in her dealings with the price of her stones. She had a keen eye for her craft and knew exactly how much each gem was worth. Lata was allied with the Seltarir chaka . She usually kept this information private, prefering instead to build a reputation for herself based upon her skills.

Possessions

She carried a shortsword .
