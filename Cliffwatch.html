The Cliffwatch was a public walkway in the North Ward of Waterdeep that partially spanned the length of the edge of the steep cliff of the same name . The southernmost portion of this road was also referred to as the Watchway .

Description

There were no rails or walls separating this walkway from the steep 100 feet (30 meters) drop of the Cliffwatch.

Location

This street stretched from north to south, running parallel to Saerdoun Street and Zendulth Street — as well as meeting the two of them at an intersection — until terminating alongside Malkur's Ride at the Beaconmarch , right before the Watchway Tower .

Reputation

This walkway provided pedestrians an ample view over the Cliffwatch, looking over the beautiful vista of the countryside east and south of Waterdeep.
