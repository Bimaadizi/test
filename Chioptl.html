History

In the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR , her tribe's elder, a jaguar lord follower of Azul , was granted a vision by the god Tezca , telling of the location of the Cloak of One Plume .

In order to become the elder's bride, Chioptl led a group of warriors to Nexal with the mission to recover it.

However, in Nexal, the tabaxi were captured by the orcs of Chief Jeordos . All the warriors were killed but Chioptl escaped thanks to the intervention of orcs following Chief Michitl .
