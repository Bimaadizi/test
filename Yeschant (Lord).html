Lord Yeschant was an inhabitant of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

Lord Yeschant participated a covert meeting with the people of other houses, conspiring against Coronal Eltargrim , on 241 DR after the hunting of Elminster Aumar . He named the death of his son, who had participated in the hunt for Elminster, as a reason for his participation.
