Krak al-Zinad , or the Keep of Fire Striking Steel , was a castle located in the City of Brass in 1367 DR .

The Keep was located in the Cindersweeps ward of the city.

Activities

Krak al-Zinad housed soldiers that protected the harbors of the City of Brass. Additionally, all of the marines, and others who used specialized seaborne weaponry, were trained here.
