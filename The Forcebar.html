The Forcebar was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Forcebar was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . The road originated on the High Road where it branched due east, ending on Tezambril's Street after a short distance. Anchoret's Alley branched south off of the Forebar's western side.

History

The road was originally named as it was designed with defense in mind. If Waterdeep was ever invaded the plan was to block strategic points on roads with rock magically transported from the Ethereal plane .
