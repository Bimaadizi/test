Fyodor was a monosyllabic bugbear entertainer, part of the Circus of the Last Days troupe in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Fyodor was a tall and muscular bugbear with brown hide and red hair. He wore a colorful jester 's collar.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Circus of the Last Days returned from the Feywild and opened its gates to the inhabitants of the Rivington district in Baldur's Gate . Fyodor was present at the circus when it was revealed that the Cult of the Absolute had murdered one of the troupe, the clown called Dribbles , and replaced him with a murderous doppelganger .
