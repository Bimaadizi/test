Frub was a kobold from the Dragon's Rest cloister in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Frub had limitless energy and relished asking questions about the activities of the cloister's inhabitants.

History

Circa the Year of the Grinning Halfling, 1481 DR , Frub shared with three other kobolds a small monastic cell in the Dragon's Rest cloister cut into the cliff face. Each of the four slept on individual hammocks. Just like all the other kobolds, he worked at night because of their sunlight sensitivity.
