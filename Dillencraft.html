Dillencraft was a Netherese arcanist who was the Dean of Teachers at the Institute for Sorcerous Imagery in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Dillencraft managed and taught alongside the other six resident teachers, made up of Driander , Galanti , Sylberg , Coletti , Maleville , and Holstmann .
