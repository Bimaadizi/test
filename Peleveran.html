Peleveran was a city built into the side of the Landrise , just north of the River Shaar . There was a hidden temple deep in the ruins that was rumored to house a portal to the Nine Hells .

History

In 1018 DR , Peleveran was destroyed in a battle between two factions of the Cult of the Dragon , one of which had fallen to the worship of Gargauth .
