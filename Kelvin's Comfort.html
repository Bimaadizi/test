Kelvin's Comfort was a tavern in Bryn Shander .

Location

The tavern was located next to Bryn Shander's market square.

Services

The tavern was famous for its signature drink known as Flamebeard's Firebrandy . It also served mead from Good Mead and liquors imported from Mirabar .

Inhabitants

Kelvin's Comfort was owned by shield dwarf Ogden Flamebeard sometime after the War of the Silver Marches . A frequent patron was Baric Nylef while he searched for Dwarven criminal Worvil Forkbeard , who was known to be addicted to Flamebeard's Firebrandy.
