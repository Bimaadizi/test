A wheelbarrow or pushcart was a type of common hand-propelled tool and cargo mover found in Faerûn .

Description

Wheelbarrows were a simple sort of tool used for moving loads about. They could be used for carrying anything from loads of grain to injured people.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue offered fine duskwood wheelbarrows for sale, with a front wheel made of iron by Berduskan wheelwrights. It could safely carry 400 pounds (180 kilograms) of cargo.
