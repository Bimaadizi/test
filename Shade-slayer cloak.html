Shade-slayer cloaks were a variety of enchanted rogues 's cloak that made their sneaky attacks deadly.

Description

When worn, a shade-slayer cloak felt rough and coarse against one's skin, however, the moment the cloak entered shadows, it softened to the texture of the finest silks .

Powers

The cloak's wearer could dispense especially deadly and well-aimed attacks from the shadows with a greater ease, making even the worst assassins into the deadliest opponents.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a shade-slayer cloak belonged to the youth merchant named Sticky Dondo , a member of Fetcher's Brats and the infamous criminal organization of Baldur's Gate – The Guild .
