Maresta Rhanbuck was an apprentice wizard inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

She was a half-elf with moon elf blood.

Activities

Maresta was a student of Blackstaff Academy within Blackstaff Tower in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . She adopted the role of a motherly figure among the other students, and helped Laeral Silverhand run the daily operations of the tower.
