The Dhaermos Warehouse was a storage warehouse in Waterdeep that was privately owned by Lhund Dhaermos in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Location

This warehouse was located along Belnimbra's Street , in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This warehouse was five stories tall.

Services

Within this warehouse Lhund would fence exotic creatures and plants, as well as a wide variety of slaves. All of these were sold for 30% of their new-made market price. He acquired his stock from a man along Wharf Street by the name of Hulfast .
