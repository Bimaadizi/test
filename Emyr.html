Gauntlet Emyr was a Flaming Fist soldier who served Baldur's Gate and the surrounding lands during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Emyr was a dutiful soldier who held true to the belied that order must be maintained for the betterment of all.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Emyr and several other Flaming Fist soldiers encountered nearly a dozen people that recounted fantastical stories related to the Companion and the city of Elturel . They said the false sun changed from its normal radiance to a black sphere that released countless shadows that enveloped the city, causing it to disappear.

Emyr ordered their soldiers to take these people into custody until they could glean more information about the situation.
