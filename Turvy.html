Turvy was a deep gnome wererat living in the Underdark with his twin sister Topsy sometime in the late 1480s or early 1490s DR .

Personality

The gnome was very close to his twin Topsy. He was constantly blabbering and muttering darkly and his sister translated for him. They feared that someone would discover that they were novice wererats who couldn't easily control their instincts.

History

Topsy and Turvy lived in Blingdenstone . Sometime in the late 1480s, they were infected with lycanthropy .

Not much time later, during a trip to collect some mushrooms, he and his twin were captured by drow slavers of Velkynvelve .
