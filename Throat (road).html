The Throat was a small roadway found in the Moonsea region between Zhentil Keep and Yûlash .

Description

The Throat ran south from Zhentil Keep, crossing the River Tesh before turning southwest to connect with Yûlash and the Bowshaft Way .

The Throat connected with Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter at the Throat Gate .
