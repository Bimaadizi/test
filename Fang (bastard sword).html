Fang was a unique bastard sword imbued with corrosive magics, crated sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Fang was enchanted to produce never-ending acidic venom . This substance was not poisonous but burned anything it touched.

History

In the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Fang was left within one of the guest rooms of the Luskan Arms inn in the town of Targos , in the far-northern region of Icewind Dale .
