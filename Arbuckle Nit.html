Arbuckle Nit was an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Arbuckle was a customer of the Sword Coast Couriers , a mail and parcel delivery service that serviced Baldur's Gate. When the Couriers started experiencing mail disappearances alongside with its pigeons , Arbuckle Nit was among the people affected. He was missing broadsheet deliveries, however, it only mildly irked the man.
