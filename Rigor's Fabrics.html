Rigor's Fabrics was a shop in the Central Trade Lanes of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR .

Structure

Rigor's Fabrics was a two-story building in fair condition.

Services

The shop produced sturdy textiles such as burlap and canvas at average prices with good-quality workmanship. While most of the inventory was supplied by the owner's offsite connections, she did occasionally produce specialized fabrics on the premises, charging twice the standard rate for these custom orders.

Inhabitants

Rigor's Fabrics was owned by Sharia Oriest and her daughter, Villianne .
