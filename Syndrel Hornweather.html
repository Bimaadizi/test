Syndrel Hornweather was the queen of Athalantar and wife of Uthgrael Aumar in the late 2 nd century DR .

Description

Eyewitnesses called her beautiful.

Activities

She was the "mistress of all of Uthgrael's stag hunts".

Relationships

She married Uthgrael Aumar, and together they had seven children, known to history as the Warring Princes of Athalantar.

History

Syndrel Hornweather married King Uthgrael Aumar in the Year of Dissolute Drow, 188 DR , becoming queen of Athalantar . She died eight years after, in the Year of Yearning Elves, 196 DR .

Rumors & Legends

It was not clear from the records whether Queen Hornweather had elven blood or was in fact a half-elf .
