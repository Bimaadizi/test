Dalin Evenbough was a halfling merchant aboard the Vigilant in 1374 DR .

Description

Merchant Evenbough was a fair-skinned slender hin with cautious personality. When pressed to defend himself, Dalin wielded a shortbow , staying away from the immediate danger of the front lines.

History

In the 1374 DR , Captain Lastri Kassireh and the Vigilant sailed from the Sword Coast to the nation of Samarach on the Chultan Peninsula . Onboard the ship were several passengers, including Dalin Evenbough and Volothamp Geddarm , who journeyed to meet with a wealthy yuan-ti trading company leader—Lady Sa'Sani . The voyage was cursed with bad luck and numerous strange occurrences: food supplies were mysteriously spoiled, freshwater barrels ruptured, one of the crew, Kellen , got drunk and drowned, Volo almost got poisoned, and new ropes became frayed. In reality, the ship was sabotaged. Dalin Evenbough was rightfully worried for his life aboard the ship.

While most of the crew's lives ended in a deadly storm, Dalin survived. The Vigilant's shipwreck was washed ashore not far from the city of Samargol , and Dalin, Volo, and several other passengers ended up defending themselves against flesh-hungry Batiri .
