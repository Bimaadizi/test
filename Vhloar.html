Vhloar was the second king of the sahuagin kingdom of Aleaxtis .

History

In the year −443 TS (−513 DR) , Vhloar succeeded the first king of Aleaxtis, T'Raam . His reign would go on for over 468 years, lasting until the year 25 TS (−45 DR) .

Under his reign, the kingdom of Aleaxtis engaged in a century long war with the merrow that inhabited the same body of water as them, the Alamber Sea . The war finally came to an end in 73 TS (3 DR) . Having won against the merrow, Vhoal claimed more than half of the Alamber Sea for Aleaxtis.
