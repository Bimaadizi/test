The farseer of Illusk was a magical telescope used by the Academy of Stargazers in the 14 th century DR . It was built on the top level of Castle Naerytar 's keep. While the castle was occupied by the Cult of the Dragon , Rezmir used the telescope to observe Voaraghamanthar in the dragons lair, through wich she discovered the dragon's secret twin. To protected the telescope room, Rezmir convinved four Gargoyles to stand watch over it.
