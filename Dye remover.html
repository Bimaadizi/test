Dye remover was a commonly used substance that stripped all dyes from metals, leather, and cloth, used in the Western Heartlands in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Commercially sold dye remover found throughout the Chionthar Valley and the city of Baldur's Gate was a secret recipe that included a universal solvent . The substance was first created by a certain hedge witch long before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
