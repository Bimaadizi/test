Azargatha Nimune ( pronounced : / ɑː z ɑːr ˈ g ɑː θ ɑː n ɪ ˈ m uː n i / ah-zar-GAH-tha nih-MOO-nee ) was a wizard in Deepingdale in 1357 DR .

History

Azargatha once defeated Randal Morn of Daggerdale in a wrestling match during a Shieldmeet .

Activities

Azargatha owned a shop where she sold small toys made by her and by Elven Court elves .

Personality

Azargatha was a woman who was strong both in character and body. She loved wrestling.

Relationship

Azargatha was a Harper or a Harper ally. She often was in the company of Harper rangers.
