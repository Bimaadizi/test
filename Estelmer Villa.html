Estelmer Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Estelmer .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's North Ward on Vhezoar Street . The villa also touched Sulmor Street , Brondar's Way , and Hassantyr's Street .

Description

Three buildings were arranged in an "L" pattern within the walled compound. The buildings were two or three storys tall.
