Flying zombies were a unique creation of Ongraunnathan , a Calishite mage.

Abilities

Unlike normal zombies, flying zombies were capable of magical flight.

History

Ongraunnathan created flying zombies from slaves he screened for magical talent. They were ultimately turned against him by a rival sorcerer between 1286 DR and 1294 DR ; after slaying him, they were turned against Aralagath Tarsil , another of his slaves who stole his spellbooks, but they were destroyed by mages after chasing him into a crowded market.
