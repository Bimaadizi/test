Activities

Barrodar owned Barrodar's Fantastic Fabrics in the western section of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter . The role of successful businessman was a mere facade. Barrodar was actually the top fence within Zhentil Keep following the city's destruction in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR . Allied to no one, he served his own best interests and only dealt with someone he knew.

History

He lost his first business during Zhentil Keep's destruction but bounced back quickly. Within just three days, Barrodar took control of an abandoned shop and started his new venture.
