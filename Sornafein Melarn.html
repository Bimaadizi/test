Sornafein Melarn was the house patron of House Melarn in 1479 DR .

Personality

The drow was intelligent and handsome, with great charm. He enjoyed his current situation.

Abilities

Melarn had a beautiful voice and was skilled with a number of stringed instruments.

History

Sornafein was a musician in his early life until a performance circa 1390 DR when he met Zhindia Melarn , who fell in love with him at first sight. He soon became the first house patron of the new House Melarn. His ability to mitigate the matron mother's fury made him the most popular male of the house.
