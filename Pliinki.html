Pliinki was a mad derro savant and a member of the Gray Ghosts thieves guild in Gracklstugh in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Pliinki believed that the Gray Ghosts' obelisk was hidden in its location by Diirinka herself and that rebuilding it would grant "unimaginable power" that she could use to conquer the city.

History

She was tasked by the guild's leaders with the mission of rebuilding the obelisk, assisted by a spectator . Circa 1486 DR , she was waiting for the delivery of a fragment from Droki .
