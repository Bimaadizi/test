King Argosh , who was nicknamed the Scaled , was the half-fiend ruler of Impiltur for several years in the 8 th century DR .

Relationships

Argosh was the son of Duke Errendil Tarrik , a prominent noble of Impiltur and secretly a fiend worshiper. Argosh was the direct result of a dalliance between his father and an other-wordly being.

Activities

Argosh claimed the throne of the kingdom after the death of King Forvar II in the Year of the Dowager Lady, 726 DR . He held the throne for several years until the Year of the Twisted Horn, 729 DR , when he was slain during the Fiend Wars .
