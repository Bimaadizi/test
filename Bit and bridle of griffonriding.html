A bit and bridle of griffonriding was a magic item that made it much easier for the user to ride and handle their griffon efficiently.

Description

There were different variations of this item including amulets , brooches, medallions, necklaces, scarabs, and periapts.

History

A bit and bridle of griffonriding was part of a collection of other magic items collectively referred to as the griffontack of Belabranta .
