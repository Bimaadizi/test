Fallorain's Plate was a set of enchanted plate mail , worn by Captain Fallorain of Calimshan , sometime before 1368 DR .

Description

This set of armor was a combination of chain armor and plates of hard steel that shielded vital areas of the wearer. Metal plates were placed to protect chest, abdomen, and groin areas.

Powers

Fallorain's Plate held a simple +1 enchantment.

History

This plate mail belonged to Captain Fallorain, the leader of Calimshan's 12 th cavalry brigade. The commander wore this enchanted armor to the Battle of the Spider Swamp , where he and his comrades met their doom battling some evil whose name was lost to history.

With time, several copies of this enchanted plate mail were created.
