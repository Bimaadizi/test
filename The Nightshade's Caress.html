The Nightshade's Caress was a shop in the Upper Trade Lanes of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR .

Structure

The Nightshade's Caress was a dilapidated two-story building.

Services

The shop sold rare and dangerous botanical substances. One could purchase herbs , fungi , and plants with paralytic, narcotic, or lethal properties. The shop's inventory included a wide array of toxins and venoms extracted from deadly creatures such as giant centipedes , wyverns , and giant toads .
