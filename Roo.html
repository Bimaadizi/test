Sharp-eye Roo was a goblin and a member of the Cult of the Absolute that dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

As were many of her cultists, Roo was a truly devout follower of the Absolute . She wanted nothing more than to be designated as one of her True Souls .
