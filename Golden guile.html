Golden guile was a powerful fiendish bit of jewelry found in Eberron .

Description

The necklace had golden links with a matching charm.

Powers

Golden guile greatly improved the wearer's charms and glibness, to the point that they could distract and confuse enemies in combat just with speech. It also partially sheltered the wearer with twilight shadows that caused attackers to miss more often.

History

The necklace was worn by a rakshasa of the Lords of Dust at their safehouse in Stormreach , only for the Church of the Silver Flame to attack during their investigation into the Spinner of Shadows . Despite the cultist Marideth 's efforts, she could not protect the cell nor the jewelry from the crusaders.
