Bergun Hall was a courthouse and records hall located in the city of Daerlun in Sembia in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

Bergun Hall was located at the terminus of the Street of the Dragon and Barrel Lane . The building stood in the northwest corner of a large open courtyard called the "Place of Banners."
