The Mistcliff was a steep, volcanic cliffside located along the northwestern coast of Chult . It was situated between the Mistcliff Mountains to the south and the Shining Sea to the north.

Description

It extended 1000 feet (304 meters) out of the crashing waves to the jungle above. It was over 200 miles (320 kilometers) long.

Inhabitants

The cliff was home to several large groups of aarakocras along with populations of pteranodons and quetzalcoatluses .
