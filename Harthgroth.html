The Harthgroth were the largest of the Nar tribes of Narfell from the 14 th to 15 th centuries DR .

Organization

When the tribe was led by the veteran warrior Thalaman Harthgroth , they could rally 4,000 riders to any cause.

Relationships

They viewed outlanders as potential sources of material wealth via trade, rather than targets for robbery to violence.

History

The Harthgroth were considered the ascendant tribe of Bildoobaris as of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .
