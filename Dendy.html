Corporal Dendy was a human fighter who lived in Neverwinter during the late 14 th century DR .

Relatioships

Dendy was among the instructors who taught the Hero of Neverwinter at the Neverwinter Academy .

Dendy believed that Herban might be a strict disciplinarian, and didn't want to risk getting in trouble for chatting while on duty.

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Dendy was a corporal of the Neverwinter Guard . He worked as an instructor specializing in melee weapon combat and assisted instructors Herban and Hewwet . He usually sparred with the students of the Academy and conducted combat trials.

Dendy harbored hopes of crossing paths with Aribeth de Tylmarande at the graduation ceremonies, but his life was ended in the attack on the Academy.
