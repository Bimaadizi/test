Alimon's Mental Academy was an educational institute of magic in Netheril .

Location

The school was located in Seventon , Netheril.

Services

This academy taught mentalism , one of the Fields of Mythal . The owner and tutor was Alimon .

History

Enollar the arcanist enrolled at the institute in 1317 NY (−2542 DR) . He quickly became the top student, and left with a wealth of knowledge to travel around the rest of Netheril.
