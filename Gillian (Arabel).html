Gillian was a teenager that served in the Company of Dawn mercenary company for a time during the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

The young woman had sun-tanned skin, straggly brown hair and blue eyes.

Abilities

She was very agile and adept at climbing trees without giving notice of her movements.

History

Gillian was killed during her company's ill-fated journey through Spiderhaunt Wood during the Time of Troubles in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR . She was the first of her fellows to die, her body impaled by the made bladed legs of a giant spider .
