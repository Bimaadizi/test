Buldos Durinbold was a noble inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Buldos was the patriarch of the Durinbold noble family of Waterdeep. He lived within Durinbold Villa on Vhezoar Street in Waterdeep's North Ward .
