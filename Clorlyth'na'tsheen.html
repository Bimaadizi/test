Clorlyth'na'tsheen was a drow settlement in the Underdark .

Description

The city was populated by drow inhabitants who paid homage to Lolth , who was known as Araushnee to them.

History

Clorlyth'na'tsheen's history appeared to remain undocumented, yet it disappeared long prior to the 14 th century DR .
