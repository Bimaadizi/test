Aquatic bolts were enchanted projectiles used by aquatic humanoids , sailors, and pirates of the Nelanther Isles and the Sea of Swords .

Powers

These bolts were designed and enchanted to be as deadly underwater as on the surface. When an aquatic bolt hit its target, the projectile's tip released a small but deadly burst of acid . Additionally, these bolts were especially effective against reptilian creatures.

History

Aquatic bolts were in common use in the late-14 th century DR . Notably, Captain Allendry of the Midnight Rose outfitted his crew and offered the bolts for sale.
