Karrundax the Red was a young red dragon that made her lair within the River of Flame that flowed within the deepest reaches of Mount Hotenow .

Activities

The young red dragon claimed an earthmote that floated within the caldera of Mount Hotenow as her lair, but also maintained one in the caverns that extended out from the subterranean River of Flame .

Relationships

Karrundax formed an alliance with the fire giant Gommoth after he formed the cult of Maegera within Mount Hotenow. She allowed them to utilize a portion of her lair in exchange for the protection of her brood, requesting that the fire giant mercenary Pyraphenia serve as her personal guardswoman.

As a token of camaraderie, Gommoth bestowed upon Karrundax the Hand of Maegera , an elemental that he and his cultists created.

History

Some time around the late 15 th century DR , Karrundax' brood of eggs hatched, spawning at least five red wyrmlings .

Karrundax and her offspring were slain by the Heroes of the Sleeping Dragon Bridge in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , around the time of the dissolution of the cult of Maegera.
