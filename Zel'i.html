Zel’i was a githyanki child living and training in Crèche Y'llek in the Western Heartlands during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Zel'i was raised in the githyanki military culture at Crèche Y'llek, likely under the guidance of crèche caretakers and elder warriors devoted to Vlaakith . Despite her young age, she showed early aptitude with psionics and magic , namely the cantrip mage hand .

When the Absolute Adversaries arrived at the crèche, they would find Zel'i and her friend Ra'jak using their magic to toss around a locked traveler's chest . Unbeknownst to them—or possibly known and ignored—the chest contained a gremishka . The visitors might've either played along, interrupted them, or obtained the chest, possibly releasing the creature.

Personality

Zel'i's actions reflected the intensity of githyanki indoctrination, but her laughter and mischief revealed her youth. Zel'i was bold, curious, and enjoyed toying with outsiders. She referred to a stranger as an istik (a Gith insult for non-githyanki), but with a childish, mocking tone. Her sense of superiority was inherited from her people, but her behavior mimicked innocence more than cruelty.

Relationships

Ra'jak was Zel'i's close companion and fellow githyanki trainee. They were nearly inseparable and always seen together.
