Adobe was a skill and craft used by the Azuposi people of the Pasocada Basin of Maztica .

Description

An Azuposi skilled in adobe could make a strong plaster out of combining the right amount of mud, water, and gravel. The resulting product was used to make walls and overhanging roofs. Someone particularly skilled in this practice could construct a wall measuring 20 feet (6.1 meters) long by 5 feet (1.5 meters) tall in a single day.
