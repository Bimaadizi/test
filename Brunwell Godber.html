Brunwell Godber was a mysterious human Selûnite in the Wheloon Prison of Cormyr during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Brunwell had back-length pure white hair as a young woman.

History

When the Riverside Rats sent a team to destroy the Night Brigade and their leader Storna Harnwood at her Temple of Shar , Brunwell loitered outside the temple gates and greeted the attackers. She chatted pleasantly and gave some innocuous yet portentous advice, which preceded Selûne taking a subtle hand in sabotaging Shar that night.
