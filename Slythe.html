Slythe was an assassin that operated out of Baldur's Gate during the mid–14 th century DR .

Personality

Slythe appeared to take pleasure in the terror of his victims.

Possessions

The skilled assassin possessed the Shadow's Blade , a unique short sword of backstabing .

Activities

He conducted his business out of the Undercellar of Baldur's Gate.

Relationships

Slythe worked alongside his wife and fellow killer-for-hire, Krystin .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Slythe and Krystin were hired by Sarevok Anchev to slay three Dukes of Baldur's Gate . They managed to kill Entar Silvershield before regrouping to kill Duchess Liia Jannath and Duke Belt at Sarevok's coronation. Unfortuantely for the duo, they were confronted in the Undercellar and themselves killed before they could complete the job.
