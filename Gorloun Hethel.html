Gorloun Hethel was a veteran member of the Purple Dragons in Suzail during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Around 1479 DR , Hethel and four more Dragons acted as escorts for the War Wizard of Cormyr Ellard Gauntur in his pursuit of the renegade Rorskryn Mreldrake . They caught up to Rorskryn as he used a portal to Marsember , while in the shape of a woman; Ellard Gauntur figured out that it was Rorskryn transformed from a stain on her clothes, and they pursued through the portal. Then the evil vampire archmage Manshoon sent the death knight Targrael after them to hide Mreldrake's trail.

Targrael found Hethel at Ildool's Veil atop the King's Tower in Marsember; he was coming out, asking for Ellard Gauntur to come and see something, when he saw the man, just slain by the death knight. Hethel gave her a brief fight, but was overcome almost immediately.
