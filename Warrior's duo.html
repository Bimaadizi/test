A warrior's duo was an enchanted set of equipment consisting of a short sword and a shield with matching protective magical properties. This equipment could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The blade of the warrior's duo hit harder than a mundane blade, and when it hit particularly hard, the duo released protective magic that bolstered the defenses of not only the wielder but their allies as well.
