Jula Bingle was a gnome alchemist in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Jula had blue-gray hair styled in a side cut, short on one side and long on the other.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Jula joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their base camp at the ruins of Myth Drannor . She served as a trainer for fans of alchemy at the camp.
