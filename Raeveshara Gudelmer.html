Raeveshara Gudelmer was a member of the Gudelmer merchant family and the oldest daughter of Albaerlus Gudelmer who resided in Athkatla in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Like her sister, Raeveshara Gudelmer was a smart, astute, and charming woman.

History

The sisters lived with their father, Albaerlus, in the family mansion, famously haunted and beautiful Dancing Dolphins House in Athkatla's resplendent Gem District . The Gudelmer sisters were well-known in Athkatla's upper classes through their social status and professional life. Raeveshara's best skill that brought her successes and coin was acting.
