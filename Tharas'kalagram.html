Tharas'kalagram , also known as Kalag , was a red dragon that lived in the Forest of Wyrms in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The dragon was about 80 feet (24 meters) long. He had scarred scales.

Personality

Tharas'kalagram played a game with the red dragon Rylatar'ralah'tyma .

Lair

Kalag's lair was in a cave in the side of a small volcano in the Forest of Wyrms. Two rotting green dragon carcasses lay outside of the cave.
