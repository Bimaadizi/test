Maracrath was a village located in Impiltur in the Northeast Faerûn , off the coast of the Easting Reach .

Geography

Maracrath was known as one of several overnight stops for caravans that traveled along the Merchant's Run , part of the Herald's Road that stretched between Impiltur and the Cold Lands . Cairnpur was the first caravan stop northwest of Ilmwatch , west of Even's Gulf , the northernmost part of the Easting Reach, followed by Maracrath. The next stop northwest along the Merchant's Run was the settlement of Guidodale , which stood on a crossroad where the road from Timbertown joined Merchant's Run.

Defenses

Maracrath received protection from Ilmwatch's garrison contingents that traveled with merchant caravans between the Cold Lands and the Easting Reach.
