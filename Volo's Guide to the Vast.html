Volo's Guide to the Vast was a book written by Volothamp Geddarm . It was a travelogue about the lands of the Vast . It was written over 1358 to 1360 DR , but went officially unreleased.

Nevertheless, around 1372 DR , one copy was kept in the Tower of Tomes of the Leaves of Learning , a temple to Oghma in Deepingdale . Another copy was in the holdings at Candlekeep , and the original was held by someone in Ravens Bluff .
