The Race was a stretch of water located off the southern edge of the Tethyr Peninsula in west Faer√ªn . The sea was named for the strong winds through it or for the frequent sight of ships fleeing the pirate bands that prowled the area.

Usage

In the 14 th century DR , the Race and Asavir's Channel were used to reach the wealthy ports of the south, but such shipping was harassed by pirates based in the nearby Nelanther Isles . Once through the Race, merchant ships came under the protection of the Calimshan navy.

History

In the Year of the Worm, 1356 DR , the Companions of the Hall traveled through the Race after escaping the pirate Pinochet 's fleet and landed in Memnon .
