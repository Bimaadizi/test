Vance was a toll collector who lived and worked on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He had fair skin and gray-tan hair he kept cropped short.

History

Vance died in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , when the cult of the Absolute raided the tollhouse at which he worked. He and his fellow Marten were abandoned by the guards sworn to protect them, but were lost their lives. Vance leapt out of the tollhouse's window in an attempt to flee, but did not survive the landing onto the ground below.
