The Lhespenbog was a small wetland found in the western Shaar near the border of Lapaliiya .

Geography

The bog was located along the southeastern shore of Lake Lhespen at the northeastern edge of the Shaareach . The Lhespenbog was bordered to south and east by the Rathgaunt Hills . The wetland was known for its sprawling reed -fields. One of the species of these reeds was study enough to be used by an eastern weapon maker Meng Aiedo to create enchanted staves – Aiedo wither-sticks , found as far north as the city of Neverwinter .
