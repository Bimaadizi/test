The Monks of Nabbach was a monastic order from the Monastary Nabbach in Agenais . They represented Agenais in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
