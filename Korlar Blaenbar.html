Korlar Blaenbar was a thief in Hastarl during the early 3 rd century DR .

Personality

Korlar was quieter than his sibling Klaern Blaenbar .

Activities

The Blaenbar siblings were thieves.

Relationships

He was the sibling of Klaern Blaenbar and Othkyn Blaenbar .

History

Korlar Blaenbar was a thief in Hastarl together with his siblings Klaern and Othkyn. They participated in a meeting in either the Year of the Black Flame, 229 DR or Year of the Wailing Dryads, 230 DR , where the thieves Farl , Jhardin , Rhegaer , Chaslarla , and Eladar the Dark founded a new gang; though Klaern volunteered to lead it, they refused to have him and his siblings as members.

The other two Blaenbar brothers were seen dead by Eladar afterwards; Farl explained his rationale for having the Blaenbars killed. The certainty of his words all but certainly included Korlar among the dead.

Rumors & Legends

Farl believed that the Blaenbar siblings might've already joined the Moonclaws, or would tell that rival gang about the formation of the Velvet Hands, which led to their killing.
