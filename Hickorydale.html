Hickoyrdale was a small village located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Hickorydale sat in the shadow of the Dernall Forest on the southern edge of the Drowned Forest . The village was located along a trail that branched off of the High King's Road in the county of Stagford in central Callidyrr.
