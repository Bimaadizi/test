Quinlan's endless quiver was one of many strange and mysterious magic and mundane items found in Torleth Mindulspeer 's curio shop circa 1372 DR .

History

In 1372 DR, Quinlan's endless quiver could be found among the "antiques" within Torleth's Treasures of Gillian's Hill . The magical quiver was propped up at the back of the store on a simple wooden rack with a white piece of parchment attached to it. The price written on the tag was set to 500 gold pieces.
