Donar Heremet was a thief inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Possessions

He wore an enchanted suit of leather armor , and wielded a short sword and a dagger of homing . The equipment, as well as his stealthy training, came from Essimuth Lanys .

Activities

Donar enjoyed playing pranks on people that were usually harmless such as hanging a bucket of water over a door. His antics frequently got him chased from the Safehaven Inn .

Relationships

He had two sisters named Edrianna and Ariel .
