The robe of sunlight was a magical robe in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Description

The robe was a light yellow, and heavily decorated with sun designs and motifs.

Powers

The sunny power of the robe empowered the strength of the wearer's spells, and protected them from incoming magical threats.

History

The robe was found by the forces of the Cormanthor Elves during the Elven Crusade around 1374 DR , after they found Kelgoth Darksong and fought Zun'thar the Unbound in the Speculum .
