The trapsmith's crossbow was a repeating heavy crossbow found in Wheloon Prison during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The crossbow was very large and heavy, mainly made out of steel .

Powers

The trapsmith's crossbow was specially enhanced shoot extra fast for its size, and imbued every shot it took with a special debilitating poison .

History

The crossbow was recovered by Cormyrean forces from the interior of Wheloon Prison after fighting with the prisoners and Shadovar forces during the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon .
