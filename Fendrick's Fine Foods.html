Fendrick's Fine Foods was the restaurant in the small town of Freedale in the Dalelands that claimed to be owned by the best chef within a hundred miles, Fendrick .

Location

Fendrick's Fine Foods was nestled between Cordac the Leatherworker's store and the town's General Store owned by Gart Stonenose ..

Structure

The building that housed Fendrick's Fine Foods was a small one-story yellow-tan painted building.

Services

Fendrick's Fine Foods was renown for being the best, if not the only eatery in town. The establishment catered to clientele tired of hardtack adventurers ' rations and served well-crafted meals, with the dish known as the " chicken surprise" being the chef's specialty. Some foodies traveled from across the Dalelands to try said dish. A standard dining experience cost just a few silver coins .

Inhabitants

In the mid-14 th century DR , the owner of the restaurant and its cook was Fendrick himself. He took his trade seriously and cared for cooking more than anything else in his life. His staff consisted of a junior cook named Boris , and a serving lass Clara .
