Ziplocbagration was a bishop from the nation of Inter Mierionydd in the Whamite Isles . Along with the archbishop Bucket , Ziplocbagration represented Inter Mierionydd in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
