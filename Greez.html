Greez was a warlock who devoted himself to the Absolute , and lived within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Greez had grey-brown skin and decorated himself in red body paint. He wore a leather tunic decorated with bones and feathers. His leather good was surrounded the the fractured skull of some terrifying creature.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Greez and other goblins celebrated the cult of the Absolute 's terrible, yet successful, raid on the Waukeen's Rest inn. Following their victory, Priestess Gut offered many of their ranks the mark of the Absolute .
