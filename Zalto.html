Duke Zalto was the ruler of the fire giants sometime after the War of the Silver Marches circa the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Zalto was a warmonger, dreaming to lead his kind at war in the glory of Surtr and Annam . He would sacrifice his family to achieve said goal.

Activities

Zalto could usually be found in Ironslag 's assembly hall playing with his two hell hounds Narthor and Zerebor .

Relationships

Zalto had also managed to forge a non aggression pact with the nearby yak folk .

History

Zalto was one of the giant lords at time of the shattering of the ordning .
