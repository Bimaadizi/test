Jivin Mizzrym was a warlock member of the Dead Eyes , a criminal group from the Lower City that named itself for the grim trophies they collected from the enemies in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Jivin was a deep gnome male with a charcoal-black skin color, dark eyes, and wavy white hair. He wore a long brigandine coat and handled a simple dagger in battle.

Abilities

As a warlock, Jivin could sling a variety of spells , including acid splash , eldritch blast , misty step , shatter , and fear .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Dead Eyes set out to perform a job that would end up setting the gang against the Guild and its leader, Ninefingers . The Guild did not take competition lightly and sent thugs to take care of the Dead Eyes. The band survived and relocated to a new lair.
