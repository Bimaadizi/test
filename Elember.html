Elember was a lord of Tavaray on the Sword Coast North in the early 3 rd century DR .

History

In the Year of the Flaming Forests, 224 DR , when introduced to someone whom he believed to be Herald Huntinghorn of Tavaray , the Mage Royal of Athalantar , Ubriien Orlyn , assumed him to be an envoy of Lord Elember.
