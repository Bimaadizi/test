Belathin was a human Red Wizard who moved to Suzail in order to remove himself from the cutthroat culture in Thay and amass power in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

An intelligent manipulative person, Belathin used clever words and powerful charm spells to manipulate his group of followers. He grew up in the Thayan power politics of the Red Wizards, but realized that in order to amass power for himself, he needed to get away from that place, thus his absconding to Suzail.

Possessions

He wore a set of bracers of defense , and a ring of protection , defending himself with a wand of petrification .
