Stonegate House was a rental villa in the city of Waterdeep in the 15 th century DR .

Location

This villa fronted the western side of Snail Street , nine doors north of where that road met Shesstra's Street , in the Castle Ward of the city.

Structure

Stonegate House was known to have at least three floors.

Interior

The villa featured a dining area, a kitchen, a sitting room, bedchambers, bathing rooms, and areas for the storage of one's wardrobe.

Inhabitants

On the third floor of this villa, a suite of rooms were uneasily rented jointly by seven men who all aspired to be wealthy. These men all used the rooms at their exposal to conduct secret meetings as well as to entertain their various upscale contacts and clients.
