Heleen was an opportunistic tiefling delinquent who resided in Skullport , under Waterdeep , circa 1490s DR .

Description

Heleen had sharp fangs, result of her lower planes tiefling descent. She always carried her trusty dagger with her , even though her unarmed strikes were just as deadly.

Personality

Even though Heleen and her buddies had adversarial relationship with the local pirate crew of the Molly Roger and were ready too aid someone in trouble, one thing they valued the most was coin, or promise of bounty.

History

Sometime in 1490s DR, Heleen, along with her shady friends Ssslasssh and Fundunsun resided in Skullport and frequented the tavern not far from the docks called the Blind Fish . The trio encountered the halfling thief who was on the quest to retrieve Laeral Silverhand 's baby griffin from Xanathar's Thieves' Guild . They fished the halfling out of Sargauth waters and took him to the Blind Fish to dry off. The thief unwisely asked questions about the Xanathar which prompted the shady friends to attack the thief. Xanathar offered bounty for anyone who showed curiosity in the crime lord. The little halfling managed to escape the trio with the aid of his wit and dexterity... and the orc bartender's heavy club .
