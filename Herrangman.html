Boyar Herrangman was a human noble inhabiting Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Activities

The Herrangman estate was located in northcentral Almoral along the shore of the Lake of Mists. The compound was walled off from the rest of the city along a small peninsula.
