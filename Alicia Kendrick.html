Alicia Kendrick was the High Queen of the Moonshae Isles .

History

Born in 1346 DR , Princess Alicia was the eldest daughter of High King Tristan Kendrick and the druid Robyn Kendrick . Groomed during her early years by the kingdom's archmage Keane , Alicia became an adventurer in 1365 DR .

During the Year of the Sword, 1365 DR , Alicia was briefly granted the powers of an avatar by the Earthmother , in order to defeat the dracolich Gotha , a servant of Talos who attacked the Moonshae Isles.

She died unexpectedly in the Year of the Halls Unhaunted, 1407 DR , and was widely believed to have been poisoned by agents of Lionel Carrathal . Her death prompted a series of insurgencies in Callidyrr , which were swiftly quelled by the Callidyrr dragoons .
