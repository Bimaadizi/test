A dastana was a type of arm-guard used by fighters in the land of Chult .

Description

A set of dastanas consisted of a pair of hinged plates that fully encircled the user's arm. They were typically accompanied by mittens made of either cloth or mail.

History

In the late 15 th century DR , artisans in Port Nyanzaru created a number of high quality dastanas for use by guardian fighters .
