Irlin Stonestable was a noble in Cormyr and lord of the Stonestable noble house.

Personality

Lord Stonestable was grim-hearted, but steadfast.

Relationships

Per Arclath Delcastle , Irlin Stonestable ran with a circle that included the lords Broryn Windstag , Delasko Sornstern , Mellast Ormblade , Kathkote Dawntard , Sacrast Handragon , and Marlin Stormserpent . He was one of the more formidable lords there.

History

He was with Marlin when Broryn Windstag's posse got kicked out of the Dragonriders Club , and went to Stormserpent Towers afterwards. Stonestable was also present when Marlin Stormserpent spoke to them of the blueflame items , and told them a magical, largish hand axe in the possession of some noble was one.

In the calamitous aftermath of the Council of the Dragon days after, he met with Broryn Windstag at Staghaven House , where the rest of their faction slowly turned up. Lord Stonestable was incredulous at the word on the street being that Cormyr was on the verge of civil war. While he was there, the moot was joined by Traevyn Illance , Marlin Stormserpent, and briefly visited by Osbur .
