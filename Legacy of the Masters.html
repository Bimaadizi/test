The Legacy of the Masters was a unique set of gauntlets of weapon expertise that originated from the kingdom of Threskel .

Powers

The gauntlets allowed the wearer to attack with greater precision and force.

History

Throughout their history, the Legacy of the Masters were owned by many different warriors, many of whom died in particularly gruesome ways. One of the owners found death in the depths of the Fell Wood , west of the mountain town of Kuldahar . The gauntlets were stashed inside a hollow tree within the woods, surrounded my restless undead of creatures lured to their deaths by will-o'-wisps , as of the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR .

By the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR they had come into the possession of Meilum , the self-declared greatest swordsman of the Sword Coast .

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the gauntlets could be found in the city of Baldur's Gate .
