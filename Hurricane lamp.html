A hurricane lamp was a type of lamp with a glass chimney to protect the flame against the wind.

Description

The hurricane lamp design included a glass cylinder around the flame, which served to protect it from being blown out by wind, be it accidentally from a breeze or some sudden rapid motion from the bearer, or intentionally, even if from a magic effect such as a gust of wind spell.

A variety of adorned hurricane lamps with a dwarven -made mechanism to raise and lower the wick was available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .
