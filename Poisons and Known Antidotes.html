Poisons and Known Antidotes was a book cataloging poisons and antidotes published before 1269 DR .

History

A copy of this book was found in the Accursed Tower of Icewind Dale in 1369 DR by adventurers searching for the Diary of Damien Morienus . The book was hidden in the secret treasure room inside the tower's library. That copy of Poisons and Known Antidotes was turned into a trap for greedy looters. The insides of the book were hollow with a giant spider hiding inside in temporal stasis. Once the book was opened the spider was enlarged and animated to attack the thieves.
