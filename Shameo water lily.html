Shameo water lily was an aquatic flowering plant found in the shadows of the Sleepy Lake on the Malatran Plateau in Kara-Tur .

Description

The lily's pulpy roots were foraged by the Nubari Zantira tribe of humans that dwelt in the floating villages on Sleepy Lake. The roots were juiced and then fermented, creating the local speciality colorless and bitter alcohol simply called shameo , one of the major exports of the Zantira.
