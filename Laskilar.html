Laskilar ( pronounced : /ˈ l æs k ɪ l ɑːr / LASS-kill-ar ) was the captain of the pirate ship the Stirge during the late 15 th century DR . He and his crew operated out of the Jahaka Anchorage , in the bay located on the western coast of Chult .

Personality

As debonair as he was confident, Laskilar preferred the thrill of treasure hunting over raiding and pillaging other ships at sea.

Possessions

Laskilar owned a cape of the mountebank .

Relationships

Laskilar maintained communication with a number of affluent patrons in Port Nyanzaru . The first mate of his ship was the half-orc Heel .
