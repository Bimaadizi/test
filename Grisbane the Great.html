Grisbane the Great was a renowned nobleman and a knight from Baldur's Gate on the Sword Coast who lived sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

History

During his glory days, Grisbane the Great wore an enchanted belt that, with time, was passed along to his grandson, Sir Griswald . In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Griswald wore his grandfather's belt to the siege of Castle Exeltis in attempts to rescue his beloved "princess" Soliana .
