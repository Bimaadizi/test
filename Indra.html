Indra was an enormous wolf kept by the Zhentarim mercenaries that operated within an underground hideout found deep beneath the Waukeen's Rest inn on the Sword Coast for a period during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

She seemed somewhat scared of the dark, or at least unnatural darkness.

Relationships

Indra loyally protected her person Vol .

History

The wolf Indra accompanied Vol as she helped prepare the Zhentarim hideout for evacuation and eventual demolition in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
