Angus Hammerfall , better known under his pen name Artimal Penfall, was a dwarven scribe, playwright, and a war correspondent sometime before 1372 DR .

Possessions

Angus Hammerfall's enchanted helmet called Penfall's Pot that traveled with him around the world.

History

Angus Hammerfall had a rich and fulfilling career of a scholar and a scribe. He undertook multiple voyages by sea and land in the line of his work, he traveled to the areas of conflict to document the happenings, often in the heat of battle from the middle of the battlefield. His crowning achievement was that of a playwright as he penned a comedy well-loved by Volothamp Geddarm himself, called My Dinner With Goblins .
