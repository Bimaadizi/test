Tlaverlt Chaserperi was a member of the Chaserperi clan of wizards , and a likely candidate to become the patriarch of the clan in the late mid-14 th century DR .

Activities

He was a shipmaker, and also produced olive oil .

Relationships

The clan matriarch, Chancereene Chaserperi , was considering him to replace Faereldan Chaserperi by the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .
