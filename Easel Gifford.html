Easel Gifford , commonly called Easel of Amn , was a human paladin of Torm hailing from Amn and the leader of Code Helm in Realmspace in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Based in the third ring of Glyth , Easel led Code Helm in launching attacks on illithids , both on the planet below and against their spelljammers .

Possessions

Easel wore at all times a suit of full plate mail forged from platinum and polished to perfection, and also had a ring of protection +3 .
