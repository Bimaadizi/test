Painbliss Hall was a temple dedicated to Loviatar located in the city of Westgate in 1358 DR .

Location

Painbliss Hall was located on Westlight Walk in the northwest section of the city.

Inhabitants

The temple was run by High Whipmistress Spyrytara Xalass . Spyrytara was assisted by 27 other priestesses and 34 followers.
