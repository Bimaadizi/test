History

In 1370 DR , Zariel could often be found inside the Yawning Portal , giving out blessings to the brave adventurers entering the mega-dungeon of Undermountain . She also offered her divine magical services for the returning survivors of the expeditions in exchange for a donation to the Church of Tymora . She aided the lone hero sent into Undermountain by the Blackstaff and the Lords of Waterdeep to investigate the recent kobold activities and thefts that all originated from the upper levels of the dungeon.
