Zinjir al-Muqi , also known as the Twelvefold Master or the Shackle-breaker , was a wizard from Hiyal in 1367 DR .

Activities

Zinjir was able to overcome the powerful magic used upon holy slayers designed to keep them from breaking their oaths of silence.
