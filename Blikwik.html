Blikwik the Beast was a creature of unknown type from the Land of the Beastly Brigands in the Whamite Isles . Together with Badmuddin the Bald, Blikwik represented the Land of the Beastly Brigands in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
