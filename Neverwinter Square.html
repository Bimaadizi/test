Neverwinter Square or The Great Plaza of Neverwinter , was one of the oldest features of the City of Skilled Hands, Neverwinter , with an ancient arbor at its center in the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

Neverwinter Square was a plaza, or a strake, in the area that would be later known as the Protector's Enclave , near Castle Never . History

The Great Plaza of Neverwinter was built around a small arbor, a public park that dated back to the city's earliest days. The plaza still existed in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .
