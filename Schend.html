OccupationWriter, scholar

Schend was a writer and scholar who penned the Mythal Theory , along with Melka , sometime before the fall of the Hand of the Seldarine citadel on the Spine of the World .

History

Schend lived and published his scholarly works sometime after the Hand of the Seldarine's construction circa 900 DR and its fall in the Battle of Seldarine's Hand . The book on the nature of mythals was considered a prolific work and was taught after as late as 1281 DR .
