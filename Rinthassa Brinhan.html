Mage Arcane Rinthassa Brinhan was an influential wizard inhabiting Saerloon in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Rinthassa was a devout follower of Mystra and one of several leaders of the Tower of Mystery temple in Saerloon.
