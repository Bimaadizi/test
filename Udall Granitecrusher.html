Udall Granitecrusher was a dwarven warrior.

Description

A heavily muscled man, Udall had a short, brown beard with no mustache. He clad himself in chainmail and leather, and wore a metal helmet .

Abilities

He was noted for the force with which he could swing a weapon.

Possessions

Aside from his mundane suit of chain mail, Udall owned several pieces of magical equipment: a shield +4 , an axe of hurling +5 , a pair of gauntlets of ogre power , a ring of fire resistance , a gem of seeing , and a bag of holding .

Activites

Udall was fond of adventuring, and spent most of his spare time practicing combat with his friend Vasos Flameslayer .
