Dornsar's Ride was a small road found in western Sembia connecting the small towns of Kulta and Huddagh .

Description

Dornsar's Ride passed northwest from Huddagh to Kulta. In addition to connecting the small towns, the road also connected the larger Way of the Manticore and Dawnpost .
