Madame Darkness was a fortune teller in the Moonsea in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Madame Darkness was the fortune teller in Doctor Jubal's Carnival of Curiosities . Her visions were always vague but after twilight her responses had a hint of menace.

History

Madame Darkness escaped the Underdark circa 1486 DR and joined the Carnival. She sensed something strange from the new mirror maze attraction.
