Amori De'Spri was a resurrected noble in Undermountain in 1479 DR .

History

In her past, Amori was a noble in Waterdeep . She was buried in Undermountain after her death, together with her husband Julain De'Spri .

In 1479 DR, the vampire Dayan using a strange resurrection machine he had found resurrected Amori. However, because she returned to life thanks to the life forces of kobolds and rage drakes , Amori's mind was temporarily addled and she often displayed kobold and rage drake mannerisms. Amori disliked her new life and wanted to return to her death with her Julain.
