Sarcelle Malinosh was a Neverwintian noble and wild magic sorcerer in the late 15 th century DR . She lost the ability to conjure magic during an expedition to a distant plane .

Description

Sarcelle's responses to questions were very cryptic, almost like predictions. Nevertheless, she strove to manage this habit, aware of how irritating it was.

History

In the late 1490s DR , Sarcelle and other three nobles were kidnapped by group of Vecna cultists and taken to Neverwinter 's graveyard, Neverdeath . She was then confined in a crypt that had been converted into a cell.
