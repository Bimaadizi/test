Fire flails were a type of common enchanted blunt weapons found in the glacier above the Dorn's Deep .

Description

Fire flails appeared to be mundane bludgeons with sturdy iron -reinforced wooden hafts, and a metallic bludgeons attached to the haft with a chain. The bludgeons were small metal spiked balls. The entire surface of these weapons was carved with glowing arcane runes.

Powers

Apart from a potent +3 enchantment was also made it significantly easier to land a hit, fire flails also had a coin-toss of a chance to dead scorching magical fire damage to its target.

History

Circa 1281 DR , enchanted fire flails could be found in the Wyrm's Tooth Glacier above the Dorn's Deep, an ancient dwarven underground stronghold on the Spine of the World .
