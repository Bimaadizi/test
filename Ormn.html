Ormn was a bear that dwelled at the Emerald Grove along with his master, Archdruid Halsin .

Personality

He had the bear form of low self-esteem and did not view himself as particularly strong.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Halsin departed the Emerald Grove for the nearby Shattered Sanctum to investigate a strange happenings at Moonrise Towers that saw the implanting of mind flayer tadpoles into the eyes of humanoid creatures and their conversion to the mysterious Cult of the Absolute .

Ormn was despondent over his master's departure. He spent all his time catching fish at a small stretch of beach near the grove, unable to pick up Halsin's scent despite the return of those belonging to his traveling companions.
