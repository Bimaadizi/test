Pratcus was a dwarf priest of Damara in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Pratcus had the typical sturdy build of a dwarf with muscular arms. He wore a sleeveless studded leather vest.

Abilities

Pratcus could enact all kinds of priestly magic, such as casting the bull's strength spell.

History

He died during the fight at the replica Castle Perilous , impaled on an iron spike trap, in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .
