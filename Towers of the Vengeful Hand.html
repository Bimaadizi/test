The Towers of the Vengeful Hand was a temple complex devoted to Tempus and located in the center of Eshpurta in Amn in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The temple stood in the very center of the city.

Inhabitants

As of 1367 DR , there were ten underpriests serving about 150 adherents and a large number of worshipers among the 28,000 garrisoned troops, instructors, trainees, and support personnel of Eshpurta.

As of 1370 DR , high priest Trusted Sword Jak Errins was getting on in years and two of his underpriests, Battlelady Peris Whallnsor and Lance of the Sword Niskam Tresdaap , were vying for the politically powerful leadership position.
