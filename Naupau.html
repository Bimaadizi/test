Naupau was a small city in Sossal .

Description

Located north of the Great Glacier , along the coast of the Yal Tengri , Naupau was the only known trade city of the nation of Sossal.

Naupau had a number of small trading outposts along the Yal Tengri, as well as a decent number of trade ships.

All outside visitors to the nation were confined to the walls of the city.
