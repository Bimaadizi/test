Jerrod was a barbarian shaman who fought against the archmage Arakon and united the Reghedmen and the Uthgardt tribes against the common foe.

Possessions

Jerrod wielded an enchanted mace , in later centuries simply known as Jerrod's mace .

History

Some time before the late 13 th century DR , wizard Arakon attacked Icewind Dale with a army of mercenaries. Banding together the Reghedmen and the Uthgardt tribes fought back until the archmage opened a gate to the Nine Hells . The legendary Tribe of the Bear warrior Hreidgar of the saga of Wandering Sky was among the first to join Jerrod's army and participated in the great battle against Arakon's summoned fiends .

After seeing a vision of Tempos , Jerrod sacrificed himself by leaping into the portal to Baator , sealing the passageway and creating monument that came to be known as Jerrod's stone .
