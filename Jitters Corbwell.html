Jitters Corbwell was an addict from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Jitters was a pale gnome with blond hair and blue eyes. She wore green frock finery atop a brown very and a ruffled tunic.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Jitters Corbwell found herself addicted to an illicit substance found in Baldur's Gate called silkroot . She found a dealer in the Lower City , in an alley near the Basilisk Gate . The brightly acid-color-scaled dragonborn was a member of the infamous Guild , but instead of silkroot, he peddled aloe ointment , as simpletons could never know the difference between the two substances.
