Imir Castdie was a hill giant member of the Zhentarim and the leader of the giant and humanoid forces based near Darkhold , to which they held allegiance.

Description

Imir had a white head of hair, with a graying beard and arm hair. He typically wore the giant-sized plate mail armor that the Zhentarim provided him and his giant forces, though felt its required helmet was uncomfortable.

Personality

Imir was known to be smarter than the average hill giant and a hard drinker.

Relationship

Imir answered to and respected the Pereghost , highly regarding his honesty and valor. . However, he secretly dreamed of one day commanding Darkhold himself.
