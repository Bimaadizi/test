Jumble's Fresh Fish , also locally known as Old Rak , was one of Suzails fishmongeries in the 14 th century DR .

Location

Jumble's Fresh Fish was a small shack-sized shop located on the eastern side of the canal that connected the Basin with the Dragonmere lake on the southern side of Suzail. It stood to the northwest of Silverscales Street . Jumble's Fresh Fish was attached to its immediate northern neighbor, a warehouse shop of Flar Oldbottle's Nets, Ropes, Cables, Hawsers, and Cords .
