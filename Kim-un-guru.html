Kim-un-guru was a korobokuru wu jen in Kozakura in 1357 DR .

Activities

Kim-un-guru took care of the Korobokuru Shrine , dedicated to Raven the spirit god. He often openly voiced his opinions, wandered drunk, and embarrassed others in public.

Personality

Kim-un-guru liked drinking, singing the "old songs," and dancing. He played the role of the barbaric outsider, being intentionally outrageous and insulting. He became a popular figure in the area, doing things that locals could not because of their social rules.
