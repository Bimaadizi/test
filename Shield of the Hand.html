Shields of the Hand were masterfully crafted tower shields found in the Seldarine's Hand citadel on the Spine of the World .

Description

Shields of the Hand were carved with elven runes and forged using the strongest ore from Dorn's Deep .

Powers

These shields offered supreme protection from enemy arms and were very effective against missiles and ranged weapons.

History

After completion of construction of Seldarine's Hand circa 900 DR , the inhabitants of the citadel lived in a close alliance with the dwarves of Dorn's Deep. The troops of the elven citadel were outfitted with the best armor and weapons created by the crafters of both races. By 1281 DR , the Seldarine's Hand had been ruined for centuries and came to be known as the Severed Hand. After the citadel's fall, several shields of the Hand remained in existence and could be found inside the ruins until the Severed Hand was transported to Limbo in 1306 DR .
