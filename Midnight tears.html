Midnight tears were an ingested poison that only took effect at the stroke of midnight.

Acquisition

The bodies of two hellwasps could be mixed with tar to create a vial of midnight tears.

Effects

If not neutralized before midnight, midnight tears caused great damage to the imbiber.
