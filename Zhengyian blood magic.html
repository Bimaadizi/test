Zhengyian blood magic was a magical practice in Vaasa in the 15 th century DR that involved tapping into traces of the dark power of Zhengyi the Witch-King contaminating one's blood. This contamination generally came about after a person's exposure to a Zhengyian relic , but it could also be inherited from a contaminated parent. It was akin to a form of sorcerer origin .

Notable Zhengyian Blood Mages

As of 1479 DR , Zhengyian blood mages were most common among the half-orcs of Palischuk . The Warlock Knight Mansard Kanaoth was a Zhengyian blood mage.
