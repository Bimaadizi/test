Shinen-Gaki ( pronounced : / ʃ i n ɛ n g ɑː k i / shee-nen-gah-kee ) were evil lesser spirits native to Kara-Tur . They were the least dangerous type of gaki .

Description

Shinen-gaki, unlike other gaki, was not humanoid. Rather, they were small floating balls of flame. The flame was either blue, red, white or yellow. They had a dark circular area near the middle of its body that acted as the eyes. Shinen-gaki were able to form tendrils of flame that were 5 ft (1.5 m) in length.

Biology

Like all gaki, shinen-gaki were the reincarnated spirits of wicked people, in this case soldiers. Soldiers who committed treason or an act of cowardice were at risk of becoming shinen-gaki.

Combat

Shinen-gaki used fire to trap a target. They exploited a humans natural curiosity, who were lured to the spirit to by its light. It then created a circle of fire around its victim with its tendrils.

Abilities

Like all gaki, shinen-gaki had the innate ability to duplicate the effects of become invisible and polymorph spells at will, and passwall three times daily. They were immune to all charm, fire, hold and sleep spells, as well as drowsy insects . They were vulnerable to any spell that affected spirits and water-based spells. Shinen-Gaki were able to fly and slowly regenerated damage that was inflicted upon them.

Society

Shinen-gaki were solitary creatures but were sometimes found in small groups. They were able to understand Kara-Turan languages but were not able to speak.

Lairs

Shinen-gaki were not known to have any form of lair.

Homelands

Outside of Kara-Tur, shinen-gaki were known to occasionally be found in the the Hordelands , though in its western regions only rarely.
