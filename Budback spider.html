Homeland(s)Khopet-Dag

Budback spiders were vermin from the Khopet-Dag mountains in the Hordelands .

Description

These spiders were about the size of a human thumb.

Behavior

Budback spiders liked to bite whenever they were annoyed. They were poisonous , but their poison was not harmful, not even if dozens of them were involved. It took a whole swarm of them biting for their poison to incapacitate a man. However, they did not swarm naturally, though magic could compel them to form such a swarm.

History

On the night of Tarsakh 15 , in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the druid Berun used budback spiders to incapacitate several assassins .
