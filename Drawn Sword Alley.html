Drawn Sword Alley was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Drawn Sword Alley was found in Waterdeep's Dock Ward . The alley branched southeast off of Watchrun Alley and then turned due south at the intersection with Leera's Alley . It terminated at Gut Alley just one building south of its intersection with Nelnuk's Walk .
