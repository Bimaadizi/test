A bow and arrow set was a type of toy designed for children to play pretend and emulate rangers , as well as means by which to teach children proper etiquette regarding ranged weaponry. Despite being supposedly harmless fun, it was generally advised that adults supervise children playing with them.

Description

Each set consisted of three bowstrings , five arrows whose tips were padded tips by affixed bean bags , and a small bow . These bows were crafted with a very resilient willow stem, had a 5 lb (2,300 g) pull, and weighed around 2​ to ​3 pounds (0.91​ to ​1.4 kilograms).
