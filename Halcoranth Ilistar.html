Halcoranth Ilistar was the tenth King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Halcoranth inherited the throne in the Year of the Black Book, 849 DR , when his uncle, King Pendernath , died of plague. He ruled until the Year of the Fell Firebreak, 886 DR .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Thartryn III .
