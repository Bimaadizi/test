Joril's axe was a huge two-handed battleaxe that belonged to Joril Frostbeard of the Wyrm's Tooth Glacier on the Spine of the World .

Description

Joril's axe was a giant weapon with a thick 6 feet (1.8 meters) haft topped with a double-bitted axe blade. Created to be used by frost giants , Joril's axe was unwieldy. However, a skilled warrior could handle using this axe in battle, dealing devastating damage to their opponents.

Powers

Joril's axe was enchanted with several types of magics. In addition, it possessed a +3 enchantment and increased the user's constitution in exchange for dexterity reduction.

History

Circa 1281 DR , Joril's axe belonged to Joril Frostbeard, a mighty frost giant leader, who lived with his clan in a cavern within the Wyrm's Tooth glacier atop the Spine of the World.
