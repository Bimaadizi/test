Crunch was a Gondsman constructed sometime in the 14 th century DR by the inventor Trobriand .

Possessions

Crunch was outfitted with a +1 mithral chain shirt and a +1 greataxe .

Activities

Crunch acted as an assistant, bodyguard, and friend to his creator Trobriand.
