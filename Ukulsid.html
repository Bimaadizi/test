Ukulsid was a dread warrior gnoll and the military commander and governor of the fortified town of Surcross in Thay as of 1479 DR .

Activities

As the military commander of the massive garrison in Surcross, Ukulsid was in charge of the forces stationed there to protect Thay's border with Thesk .

As the governor of Surcross, Ukulsid met with his subjects and meted out justice from the Hall of Petition within the town's central citadel, known as the Tombstone . However, few Surcross inhabitants ever came to petition him owing to the fact that he was prone to kill any who annoyed him, and to then have their corpse reanimated to serve the town's garrison.

Relationships

Ukulsid received his orders from Szass Tam himself. He permitted Valindra Shadowmantle to use his city as her headquarters only because this was the will of his master.

History

During the Shadovar–Thay War , Ukulsid was in charge of Surcross as it came under assault from the flying Netherese citadel of Kolthunral .
