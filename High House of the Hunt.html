The High House of the Hunt was a small temple dedicated to Malar , the Beastlord, in Zhentil Keep in 1358 DR . It was one of just a handful of temples dedicated to the Beastlord throughout the Eastern Heartlands .

Location

The temple was located in eastern Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter .

Inhabitants

This temple was led by Huntmaster Baerdeth Malagar . He was served by 11 other priests and nearly a score of fellow worshipers of Malar.

History

The temple was destroyed, along with most of Zhentil Keep, in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .
