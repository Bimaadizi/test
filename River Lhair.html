The River Lhair was a small waterway found in the far southwestern region of Halruaa .

Geography

The River Lhair originated in the southern stretch of the Lhairghal Mountains near the Mhair Jungles . It flowed southeast for approximately 100 miles (160,000 meters) before reaching the Great Sea . The town of Lhair was located on its eastern bank where it met the sea.
