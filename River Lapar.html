The River Lapar was found in the heart of Tashalar on the Chultan Peninsula of southwest Faerûn .

Geography

The River Lapar originated on the southern end of the Lapal Sea . It flowed south for approximately 100 miles (160 kilometers) through dense jungles before reaching the Great Sea and emptying into the Lapalgulph . The river served as the southern border between the Black Jungles in the west and the Mhair Jungles in the east.

Inhabitants

Yuan-ti from House Jennestas patrolled the jungles around the River Lapar.
