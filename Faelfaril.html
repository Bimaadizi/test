Faelfaril was the proprietor and innkeeper of Faelfaril's Inn in Bryn Shander in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was fat and getting old as of the late 1370s DR. His inn was of poor quality.

Personality

Faelfaril kept apprised of local news, and was a good source of information about travelers passing through town and about the Ten Towns ' knowledge of the activities of the Reghedmen barbarian tribes.

Relationships

We was acquainted with Drizzt Do'Urden , who would consult him for local information.
