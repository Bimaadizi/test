Quarrel was a half-orc assassin in the service of Tyranthraxus .

Description

Quarrel was a slender and powerful female humanoid with black hair, jet colored eyes, and a piggish nose.

Personality

Slim and beautiful, spoke with a rough voice and could not escape her orc heritage. She hoped Tyranthraxus would reward her for killing Shal Bal , Tarl Desanea , and Ren o' the Blade with transforming her orcish nose into a human one.

Possessions

In battle, Quarrel armed herself with her favorite dagger and wore dark cape over her chain mail . As a secondary weapon, Quarrel equipped herself with a small scimitar .

History

In 1340 DR , Quarrel and her assassin guild searched Sembia for ioun stones . Two weeks later, she was hired by the Possessing Spirit to either persuade the heroes of Phlan to join the Lord of the Ruins or die.

Death

A day later on the 28th of Flamerule 1340 DR, Quarrel met the heroes in the Laughing Goblin Inn but they refused to join Tyranthraxus, so she threw her poisoned dagger at Shal and fled. During a short melee in the inn, Quarrel had her skull crushed in under the hooves of Shal's horse familiar , Cerulean .
