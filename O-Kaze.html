O-Kaze was a Phoenix clan ninja in Nakamaru around 1357 DR .

History

During the plot of Goshukara Sadamoto to ruin peace talks between Funada Mitsuhide and the Big Head yakuza gang, O-Kaze was assigned by the Phoenix ninja to kill Jirozu no Nakamaru , framing Funada clan retainers in the process.
