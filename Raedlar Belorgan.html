Raedlar Belorgan , or simply Red , was one of five young men who attempted to assassinate Princess Tanalasta during the Abraxus affair in 1369 DR . He was killed in the attempt.

Rumors & Legends

Raedlar was rumored to be a titled Baron of Forgemarket (a market crossroads in the Wyvernshores ).
