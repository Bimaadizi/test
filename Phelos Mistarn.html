Phelos Mistarn was a priest of Shaundakul , the Rider of the Winds, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was an old man with a grim attitude.

Activities

Phelos lived in Hillsfar in Moonsea region. He was one of only a handful of known priests of Shaundakul following the end of the Time of Troubles in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .
