Citadel Tralkarn was the seat of power of the durthans in Rashemen during the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The fortress was located within the Erech Forest in northern Rashemen.

Activities

The durthans were able to construct Citadel Tralkarn in secret without alerting the hated hathrans .
