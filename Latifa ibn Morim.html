Latifa ibn Morim was a half-elf sand mage within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Activities

Latifa served as one of the Grand Vizier's assistants. Her prime responsibility was security within the palace guard.
