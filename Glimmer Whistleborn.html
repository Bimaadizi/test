Glimmer Whistleborn was a reporter employed by Baldur's Mouth in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Glimmer was a fair-skinned female rock gnome with red pixie-cut hair and big brown eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Glimmer Whistleborn worked on her regularly printed column for the Baldur's Mouth Gazette . During the Absolute crisis , the gnome could be found working within Baldur's Mouth's offices in Heapside of the Lower City . She expressed her discomfort with the offices being guarded by the steel watchers , being unable to concentrate under their disconcerting gaze. One of her column's topics of note was The Absolutist Next Door, in which Glimmer recounted eight warning signs one's neighbor was a cultist of the Absolute . However, as the gazette was tasked with printing propaganda for the new Lord Enver Gortash , Glimmer's work likely bore no truth. Despite that, she received a letter from "A Loyal Reader," who thanked the gnome for her column and claimed that their uphill neighbor displayed all eight warning signs. The reader promised to watch the "traitor" neighbor and report him to the Flaming Fist upon the first witnessed (or perceived) crime.
