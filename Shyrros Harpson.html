Shyrros Harpson was a Sorlyn cleric who represented the Church of Milil in Crimmor during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , a duty he shared with his half-brother Tahrun .

Description

Shyrros was a studious poet, who created many popular songs and ballads with the help of his sibling. Together they tended to the faithful of Milil in the Theater of Joy , which was dedicated to the deity from highsun until dusk.
