Solshuss the Bard was the writer of a number of love songs at some point prior to the mid-14 th century DR .

History

During her training of Narm Tamaraith , Jhessail Silvertree attempted to distract him by pontificating at length about a number of topics; she mentioned them tangentially the love songs of Solshuss the Bard.
