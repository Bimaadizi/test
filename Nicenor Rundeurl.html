High Priest Nicenor Rundeurl , nicknamed Nicenor o' the tithes , was a priest of the Hall of Diamonds , a temple dedicated to Waukeen , in Yhaunn in 1358 DR .

Activities

Nicenor was the leader of Waukeen's faithful in Yhaunn. His congregation within the temple consisted of 23 other priests and 6 followers.
