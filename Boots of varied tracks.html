TypeMagical item

Boots of Varied Tracks were ordinary-looking boots that allowed the wearer to alter the tracks they left behind.

Powers

The wearer could change the size of their footprints to ones as small as a halfling 's to as large as an ogre 's, shoed or barefooted. Each set of boots also had four extra types of tracks it could leave, ranging from common animals such as wolves and horses to basilisks and hill giants .
