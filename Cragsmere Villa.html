Cragsmere Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Cragsmere .

Location

The villa was located in the west section of Waterdeep 's North Ward . It sat on the southeast corner of the intersection of Copper Street and Vondil Street at the border with the Sea Ward .

Description

Three separate buildings were found within the walled villa compound. A garden and small pond were situated toward the center of the villa. The buildings were two or three stories tall.
