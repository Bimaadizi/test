Gulthmeran Reserve was a variety of wine , produced on the southern Dragon Coast and the northern Vilhon Reach . It was exported as far as the Western Heartlands , the lands along the River Chionthar , and the city of Baldur's Gate , as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

This high quality yet harsh-flavored wine was in high demand among those of discriminating taste palettes. It was sold in small 0.4 pounds (0.18 kilograms) round green glass corked bottles.
