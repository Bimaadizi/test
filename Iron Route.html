Description

While its name suggested otherwise, the Iron Route was little more than a hard-trodden trail.

Most of the time, Iron Route was considered to be generally safe by locals, at least by Moonsea standards.

Geography

The road ran across a stretch of the northern shore of the Moonsea lake , spanning the entirety of the Stormy Bay . It lay just south of the Giant's Cairn and the untamed steppes of the Grass Sea .

History

In the 1480s DR , the Gray Patriots began carrying out raids beyond their usual targets of rich nobles from Phlan, instead striking against caravans moving along the Iron Route. These raids infringed upon the territory claimed by the Claws of Tiamat , a sect of the reborn Cult of the Dragon that employed kobolds for the acts of robbery they committed on the trade route.
