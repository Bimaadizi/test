Skyvald was a storm giant king in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Skyvald's daughter, Princess Irie went missing. Skyvald dispatched a cloud giant named Burruld to search for her. It was with this end in mind that Burruld piloted his cloud castle over Waterdeep in 1491 DR .
