Abyad Abd al-Bas was a famed mamluk of the Valiant Mamluk Society from the Free City of Qadib in north Zakhara .

Description

Abyad had a streak of white running through his dark hair.

Activities

Abyad was a legendary warrior among the mamluks of the valiant. Many strove to attain his level of notoriety.
