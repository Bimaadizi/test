Nerys Dawnhelm was an elf paladin in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Nerys had light skin, gray eyes, and neatly-styled short black hair. She wore a suit of half plate armor and carried an engraved longsword and shield .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Nerys joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their base camp at the ruins of Myth Drannor . She served as a trainer for other paladins at the camp.
