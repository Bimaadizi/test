Devout Wasp was a goblin raider and follower of the Absolute that dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

Wasp was among the goblins granted the mark of the Absolute .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Wasp and other goblins from the Shattered Sanctum launched a savage raid on the Waukeen's Rest inn, which was situated along the Risen Road . They managed to kill many Flaming Fist soldiers and capture some Baldurian nobles , including as Wasp put it, "some duke" , all of whom were taken to Moonrise Towers .
