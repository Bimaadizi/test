King Jarund Elkhardt ( pronounced : / j ɑː ˈ r uː n d ˈ ɛ l k h ɑːr t / yah-ROOND ELK-hart ) was the leader of the Reghedmen Tribe of the Elk barbarians during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Physically, King Jarund was an imposing man of great stature with a face hardened by many years. In contrast, he ruled with an exceptional care and wise decision-making.

History

In the Year of the Narthex Murders, 1482 DR , the king's son Jarund Twice-Born was slain by a bear . Jarund the elder struggled to name an heir to his rule.
