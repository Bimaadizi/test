Highrock was a small mining town located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Highrock was found in the far northeast corner of Callidyrr within the county of Fairheight . The town was within the Fairheight Range near the Farfair River close to the border with Gnarhelm , making it the northernmost settlement in Callidyrr.
