Surrell was one of the richest mind flayers on Glyth and a leading breeder of humanoids for illithid consumption.

Possessions

Surrell was the owner of the 12 Skulls Ranch , a humanoid breeding ranch with a herd of over 12,000 oortlings located in one of the few surviving forests on the surface of Glyth.

Activities

On his ranch, Surrell conducted experiments with selective humanoid breeding. He worked tirelessly, underground by day and on the surface by night, fine-tuning the oortling race so their brains tasted better for mind flayers. The products of his work made him famous across Realmspace and even known in Greyspace . His herds were sold at a high price to other mind flayers, but were considered a delicacy.

Relationships

Surrell's success drew the envy of other breeders from the planet.

History

Selective breeding of oortlings by mind flayers using techniques such as the ones performed by Surrell was one of the causes of the decline of the oortling race. Although a few oortling colonies still existed that remained untouched by mind flayers, the ones who were captured had their curiosity and creativity bred out of them, leaving behind a broken race of docile, frightened, and cattle-like people.
