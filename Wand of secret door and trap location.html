Wands of secret door and trap location were magic items used to reveal the locations of both hidden doors and traps .

Powers

The effective range of these wands was a radius of 15 feet (4.6 meters) around the wielder for revealing secret doors and double that distance for traps. The wand would pulse with energy when pointed at a secret door or trap. It could only be used to detect doors or traps for each charge expended, but the wand could be recharged.
