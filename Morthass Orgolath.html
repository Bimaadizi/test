Morthass Orgolath , who was also called the Cloven , was a tiefling Nentyarch of the Orgolath Dynasty of Narfell .

Activities

Morthass ruled as Nentyarch from −475 DR to −434 DR .

Relationships

Morthass was the secondborn son of Horthaun . His brother, Jelethar inherited the throne upon his father's death in −482 DR , and Morthass came to power when his brother disappeared in −475 DR. Morthass's sole heir was his son Nardrad .
