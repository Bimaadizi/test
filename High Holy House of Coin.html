The High Holy House of Coin was a temple dedicated to Waukeen , the Lady of Trade, built in the late 15 th century DR in the city of Scornubel .

Location

The High Holy House of Coin stood in the northeasternmost side of Scornubel. The temple's rear wing hid the temple yard, framed by the stables on the yard's other side.

Activities

The High Holy House of Coin's daily operations were headed by the Preceptress. Priestess Jhalanavress Tanthavur held the rank in the late 15 th century DR. She ensured the building, its piping, walls, good, all were in good shape, supervised the temple's lay staff and underpriests, as well as purchased goods and supplies, including thael leaf to be mixed with pipeweed by the clergy and added to the temple's incense .

History

The High Holy House of Coin was successfully financed and constructed in the late 1490s DR.
