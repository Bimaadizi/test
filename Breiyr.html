Lord Breiyr was a frontier noble of the Sword Coast North in the 3 rd century DR . He was the Lord of Morlin Castle .

Description

He was stout and aging, with a ruddy face. His hands were knobbed and scarred from numerous breakings in his youth.

Personality

The man was appreciative of good food and had a rough, direct manner, but he was not above attempting to be diplomatic.

Abilities

In his youth he'd been a mighty warrior.

Possessions

Of note, he owned Morlin Castle .

History

When he was younger, the Lord of Morlin had fought many foes, such as warbands of orcs , hobgoblins , and even giants . He had since married, and widowed. He had also made his daughter the steward of Morlin.

On Eleint 11 of the Year of the Flaming Forests, 224 DR , Breiyr received visits from both Arthlach , Axelord of Westdelve , and Falaeve , Highlord of Siluvanede ; the two took a quick dislike to each other, which threatened to spill over during dinner. During their evening feast, Axelord brought up the rumors that the fires last spring had been set by sorcery, which the elven lord rejected. Then the Herald Huntinghorn arrived at the castle, and Breiyr swiftly invited him to the dinner.

The herald confirmed the rumors as far as he knew, and brought up the Magelords of Athalantar as the certain culprits. As the elven lord attempted to cast a spell, the dwarven lord responded swiftly and slew him. The elf melted into the form of a man, recognized by Breiyr's Knight of the Gate as Ubriien Orlyn , Mage Royal of Athalantar . The herald revealed himself to be Myrjala Talithyn , a local sorceress, and took her leave. Lord Breiyr then agreed with his daughter that Athalantar was probably going to be a difficult neighbor.
