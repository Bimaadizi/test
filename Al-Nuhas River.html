Al-Nuhas River , also called the River of Copper , was a small river found in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Geography

Al-Nuhas flowed east from the center of Al-Yabki Mountains for 75 mi (120 km) before reaching the city of Sikak on the coast of the Golden Gulf .
