Saer Preciosa was a patron of the Elfsong Tavern from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Preciosa was a half-elven woman of the wood elven ancestry with a light woody or bronze skin tone and muddly blue eyes. She wore her blonde hair braided and lightly dyed in lavender.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Saer Preciosa was a frequent customer of the Elfsong Tavern in the Eastway neighborhood of Baldur's Gate. She attended the "Laff Riot" show at the Elfong, a stand-up comedy performance hosted by Harvard Willoughby in Elfsong's side room with a stage. Preciosa, a well-dressed woman herself, thought Willoughby to be a snappy dresser apart from the man's monocle . She hoped the word around town about the comedian was true and he was funny enough to distract her from the looming threat of the Cult of the Absolute .
