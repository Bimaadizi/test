Lord Alexi Dauntinghorn was a member of the noble Dauntinghorn family of Cormyr in the 14 th century DR .

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Lord Alexi attended King Azoun Obarskyr IV 's birthday feast, hosted by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Keep . He was accompanied there by Lady Tremayne Dauntinghorn .
