Lord Llewellyn was the ruler of the coastal town of Llewellyn in Callidyrr in the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR .

Activities

Llewellyn was constantly at odds with his neighbor, Lord Kythyss , to the south. The two bickered about who had the rights to fish the fertile waters of Llewellyn Harbor . They even brought the matter to the attention of the High King.
