Xalyth , also known as Nal , was a female drow trader and fencer of gems and spell components in Menzoberranzan in 1370 DR .

History

Her fortune came from her relations with nine suppliers, from Ammryyr of Skullport , a male drow worshiper of Vhaeraun , to Ubelein Shubelith , a duergar of Underspires nicknamed "Fatreek".

Personality

Xalyth was fearless and fairly trustworthy. She had a sharp tongue, but was slow to anger. Her nickname was formed from the initials of "Not A Lady" .
