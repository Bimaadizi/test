TypeMedicine

Merenthe was a medicine used to bring sleep while under conditions of extreme pain.

Powers

Merenthe allowed its user to sleep, even if they were in pain.

History

Merenthe was a part of Orthil Voldovan 's provisions during his run from Scornubel to Waterdeep in the late Year of the Prince, 1357 DR . He kept it in a red flask.
