Wavemother's cloak was an enchanted Umberlant cloak found in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

This blue cloak was adorned with Umberlee 's holy symbol and on the inside, it hid Little jellyfish-shaped pockets.

Powers

When worn in battle, Wavemother's cloak granted the wearer the damp aegis – the magic that covered the wearer in a protective layer of wetness that made them resistant to fire and unable to be lit aflame. However, it made the wearer vulnerable to electricity and cold damage.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Wavemother's cloak belonged to Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queen's House in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. She was willing to reward the cloak to adventurers who would investigate and avenge the death of Holli Dylford , one of the clerics of the Water Queen's House.
