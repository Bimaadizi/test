Riskin was a species of darting tiny brown-feathered birds found in the King's Forest of Cormyr , notably in the vicinity of Dathyl .

Ecology

These chirping birds were small enough to fit in an adult human 's hand. Riskin were partial to delicious cavanter buds that grew in the King's Forest.

History

In 1348 DR , a small flock of riskins intruded on a morninefest of Florin Falconhand and Narantha Crownsilver , fruitlessly trying to snatch a heaping leaf-full of their favorite cavanter buds gathered by Florin.
