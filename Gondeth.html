Ghondeth was a skilled wizard inhabiting Westgate in 1358 DR .

Personality

Gondeth was a calm and shy individual.

Activities

Gondeth sold magical scolls and cast spells for a fee, but he never went adventuring. He worked out of his shop called Gondeth's Mageries located at the intersection of Southgate Market Street and Westgate Market Street .
