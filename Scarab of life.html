A scarab of life was an enchanted piece of jewelry that could raise the dead.

Description

The scarab was a jeweled beetle set in an ankh .

Powers

When worn by a priest 's brow, the scarab of life enable them to cast cure critical wounds once per day and raise dead once per week. Both powers required uttering a command word . With each use of this item's powers there was a one in ten chance that it would lose its magic .
