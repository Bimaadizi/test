The Weed Rocks were a pair of rocky islands in the Trackless Sea .

Geography

The two Rocks were located west of the Nelanther Isles . They were due west of the island of Nemessor and due south of the island of Carcathen .

The southern Rock was known as Ulduth .

Inhabitants

The Weed Rocks were notable for their population of seabirds .

Ulduth was a known haunt of the white dragon Arveiaturace .
