Gesh was a member of the elite dragonborn guards maintained by the High Lady Ordalf of Sarifal in the Moonshae Isles prior to his betrayal and death in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

He was a loyal follower of his captain, Shamasar . He fought in tandem with his comrade, Haroon .

Abilities

He was a fearsome warrior, willing and able to take on overwhelming odds. His dragon breath was of boiling acid .

History

In the late 1460s DR, Gesh was one of five dragonborn guards accompanying Shamasar and Valeanne in a plot to smuggle Princess Amaranth out of Sarifal. As they neared their destination of Crane Point , they were attacked by a company of drow , and Shamasar sent Gesh and Haroon to cause chaos and slow the enemy. Gesh, Haroon, and another dragonborn managed to halt the advance of the drow across a half-mile-wide (0.8 kilometer) plain between the Innes and Ulls lakes before they were overrun and slain.
