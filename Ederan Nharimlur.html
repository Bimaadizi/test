Ederan Nharimlur , who was also nicknamed Catseye , was the third High Mage of Silverymoon .

Activities

Ederan served as High Mage from the Year of the Lost Lord, 719 DR , to the Year of the Shattered Tome, 784 DR , when he died of old age.

Relationships

He married the elven princess Elénaril of Myth Drannor . His half-elf daughter Amaara would succeed him as High Mage.
