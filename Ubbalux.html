Ubbalux was an imprisoned barlgura in Avernus , first layer of the Nine Hells , in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Once, archdevil Bel defeated Ubbalux and imprisoned him inside a circle of obelisks, telling him that the secret of escaping was inside the runes inscribed into the obelisks. From then on, Ubbalux studied the runes but with no fortune.

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Ubbalux was posing as a human wizard , one whom he had previously devoured, hoping to manipulate the next visitors the circle in order to gain their help.
