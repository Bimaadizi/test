Portrait of Lohse was a painting depicting a spell -weaving woman, created sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The woman in the painting was pictured enveloped by waves of purple magic. Her blue and brown dress and red hair, streaked with white, were wildly flopping with the wild magic. Apparently, this picture captured a bard , mid-dance, and the painter captured something special about the woman's eyes, as if there was more than one soul behind them.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Portrait of Lohse was found within the druids ' library of the Emerald Grove in the Western Heartlands .
