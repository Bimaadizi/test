Delyarna a wealthy citizen of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Pompton was a high elven woman with a pale skin tone, light blue yes, and blonde hair braided and gathered in a bun. She a quality jerkin.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Delyarna and her neighbor, Pompton Norvath , lived in the Lower City , strolling the streets near a shady part of the town with abandoned buildings where the notorious Guild had one of their Guildhall entrances guarded by the gang's thugs and snipers. Delyarna wished that area was more secure via steel watcher patrols and lamented the buildings being abandoned by people fleeing the incoming armies of The Absolute .
