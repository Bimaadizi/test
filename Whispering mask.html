The whispering masks were a collection of magical wooden masks used by the hag Auntie Ethel to control the guardians of her inner sanctum.

Description

The wearer of the mask was bombarded with incomprehensible whispers and a putrid stench that overwhelmed their senses and thoughts, forcing them under Ethel's control. While this effect could be resisted for a time, it took a strong sense of willpower.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Ethel had enthralled four individuals into her flock by means of the whispering masks , naming them the Masks of Regret , Servitude , Terror , and Vengeance .
