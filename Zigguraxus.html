Zigguraxus was the home plane of the Untheric pantheon , manifesting as a vast mountain with its peak eternally covered in mist under a starry night.

Great Wheel cosmology

From the viewpoint of the Great Wheel cosmology , Zigguraxus was solely the divine realm of Gilgeam . It was located on Avalas , the first layer of the Outer Plane of Acheron .

History

When the pantheon was dissolved in 1358 DR after the deaths of Gilgeam and Ramman , the plane disappeared from Toril 's cosmology.

In 1487 DR , the returned god Enlil rebuilt the plane for some purpose.
