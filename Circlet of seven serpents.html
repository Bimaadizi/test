A circlet of seven serpents was a type of wondrous circlet .

Description

These circlets were made of serpentine stone and resembled seven serpents intertwined, with each devouring the tale of another. Tiny emeralds were embedded in place of their eyes and their detailed scales were set apart by a lattice of silver .

Powers

Once per day, the wielder of a circlet of seven serpents could cast effects similar to the spells charm monster and true strike . They also would be capable of casting effects similar to the spells mage hand and open/close at will from a range of 25 feet (7.6 meters).

History

The first circlet was crafted by unknown bard, though many believed that they were of Jhaamdathan origin.

Notable Users

Since the founding of the kingdom of Sembia , they were typically known to be used by nagas in the Eastern Heartlands .
