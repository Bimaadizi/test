Lazlo Lunk was a high-ranking officer of Muktar in Turmish in 1358 DR .

Activities

Lazlo was a member of the Champions of Muktar . His weapon of choice was his cursed sword. His companions gave him a wide berth in battle. He was also the commander of the Heavy Brigade of Muktar's army.

History

Lazlo acquired his cursed sword in a game of chance.

In 1358 DR , Lazlo and his fellow Champions arranged a trap inside Fort Blood , planned by the self-styled king of Muktar , Hetep , to eliminate an adventuring party well-known in Golconda .

Later, he led his brigade in the Battle of Wilma Pass .

Personality

Lazlo continued to use the cursed sword because it suited his battle-hungry personality.
