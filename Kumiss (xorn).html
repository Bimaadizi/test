Kumiss was a xorn ruffian inhabiting the Great Dismal Delve on the Elemental Plane of Earth in 1367 DR .

Personality

Kumiss was a brute in every sense of the word, relying on brawn over brains to get what he wanted.

History

He was a runaway slave from dao masters.
