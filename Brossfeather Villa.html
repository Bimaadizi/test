Brossfeather Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Brossfeather .

Location

The villa was located in the north section of Waterdeep 's North Ward where Anteos Lane met Brassfeather Lane just north of Whaelgond Way .

Description

The villa contained four separate buildings arranged around a central garden. The buildings stood two or three stories tall.
