The Measure House was a temple in Calimport dedicated to the worship of the god Anachtyr .

Location

The Measure House was located in Masal Drudach , part of the Bakkal Sabban of the Faiths Ward , in Calimport.

Structure

This building stood six stories tall and was topped with a minaret that stood another six stories tall.
