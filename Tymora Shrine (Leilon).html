The Tymora Shrine was a temple to Tymora located in the town of Leilon .

Location

The Shrine was located on the Leilon town square.

Services

In addition to serving as a place of worship, the Shrine also served as the only place to purchase potions in Leilon during the town's reconstruction in the late 15 th century DR . Its inventory was inconsistent and restocked once a tenday. Generally, the Shrine was well stocked with potions of healing , and often carried a few vials of potions of climbing , growth , water breathing , and heroism .

History

The original shrine of Tymora was one of the first major temples in Leilon, and stood in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR. When the town was rebuilt by Dagult Neverember in the late 15 th century DR, a new shrine to the goddess was the second temple to be erected. with the first temple being the rebuilt Lathander Shrine (which had paid to be first). The Shrine was overseen by Puck Caribdas , who also sold potions out of the temple.

When Leilon was besieged by the Cult of Talos in the Battle of Leilon , acolytes of the temple aided in the defense of the town and the townsfolk took care to protect the Shrine.
