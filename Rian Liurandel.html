Priestess of the High Moonlight Rian Liurandel was a Selûnite priest and the leader of the House of the Moon in Bloodhand Hold (later known as Waterdeep ) during the 8 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Lost Wayfarers, 757 DR , during her time at the House of the Moon, Rian was informed of the charges laid against the New Moon Pact by High Moonmistress Brima Chintamn of Bright Lady's Tower in far away Chancelgaunt in Sembia . Believing that the Pact had indeed fallen to the heresy of believing Shar and Selûne the same being, she supported the charges and condoned the extermination of the Pact.
