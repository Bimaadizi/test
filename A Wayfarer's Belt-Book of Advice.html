A Wayfarer's Belt-Book of Advice was a book penned by sage Rasthiavar of Iriaebor in the late 13 th century DR .

History

A Wayfarer's Belt-Book of Advice was published in the Year of the Many Mists, 1282 DR . One notable part of the book talked about a rare magic-eating creatures known as balhiir. A copy of the book was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
