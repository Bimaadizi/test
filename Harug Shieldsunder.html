Harug Shieldsunder was a dwarf and member of the Cellarers' & Plumbers' Guild of Waterdeep .

Personality

Harug was called "most cantankerous dwarf in the city," but was considered one of the guild's best tunnel workers.

Relationships

Harug and Laraelra Harsard were good friends. Harug was the uncle of Dorn Strongcraft .
