Jester's Green was an inn and the namesake of a small town located just north of Suzail in Cormyr .

History

Sadly, the inn was destroyed long ago in a fire when the innkeep cheated a traveling Red Wizard of Thay who launched a meteor swarm at him. The innkeep perished along with his establishment.
