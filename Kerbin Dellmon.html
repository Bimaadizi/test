Kerbin Dellmon was the patriarch of the Dellmon family and a farmer during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Activitivies

Kerbin managed a great farm in the valley.

History

In 1491 DR , the Iceshield orcs attacked many farms around the Dellmon property. However, Kerbin refused to abandon his farm and go to Yartar . So he remained with his workers and family to defend it. With the lucky arrival of some adventurers, he was able to defend the farm.
