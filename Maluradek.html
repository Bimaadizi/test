Maluradek was one of the noble houses of ancient Anauria .

Members

It was one of three human houses that composed Anauria's nobility, along with House Nemrin and the royal House Thardresk .

History

Circa the Era of Upheaval , some traces of House Maluradek could be found amongst the ruins of Anauria, scattered across the Anauroch desert.
