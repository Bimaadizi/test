Whitethistle was a shrub plant that grew in Neverwinter Wood on the Sword Coast North .

Description

Whitethistle was a low-bearing sprawling shrub with green leaves and small red berries. The plant was notorious for giving the runs to anyone who consumed it.

History

In 1374 DR , a traveling gnome bard Grobnar Gnomehands composed a catchy song about whitethistle of the Neverwinter Wood.
