Jott was an agent of Thylius Caramitru in Mulmaster .

Activities

In 1491 DR , Jott and his brother Rolo were the main agents of Thylius, running for him the Yellow Breath Chapter House . However, an adventuring party hired to find some missing noblewomen entered the house and defeated Jott.
