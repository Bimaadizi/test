The Sprite language was the racial language of sprites , a type of fey .

Speakers

Beyond sprites themselves, Sprite was a common secondary language in Faer√ªn among forest gnomes , lightfoot halflings , rock gnomes , and tallfellow halflings . Beyond demihumans , Sprite was also commonly spoken by their fellow fey classified as sprites , the atomies , grigs , and pixies . Non-sprite fey known for speaking the language included brownies , dobies , dryads and hamadryads , and gorse faeries. Beyond sylvan creatures, the Sprite language was commonly known by faerie dragons .

Sprite was also fairly common as a secondary language among most druids , including those devoted to certain nature deities. These included the druids of Chauntea , druids of Eldath , druids and druid/ rangers of Mielikki , the druids of Silvanus , and the druids of Sheela Peryroyl . Other forms of specialty priests in the Realms for whom Sprite was a commonly known secondary language were the Eldathyn peacemen .
