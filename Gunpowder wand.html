TypeFirearm

Gunpowder wands were some of the first handguns known outside of the island nation of Lantan .

Description

These firearms were plated in silver and carried up to two shots. Unlike other Lantanese firearms, which used the substance smokepowder , as their name implied gunpowder wands operated on gunpowder that had been divinely enchanted into a non-inert state by high-ranking priests of Gond with their deity 's blessing.
