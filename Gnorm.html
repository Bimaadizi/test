Gnorm the gnome was the proprietor of the Dragon's Jaws tavern and inn in Suzail .

Personality

Gnorm was exceptionally friendly and was known to be the life of any party, but he had a tendency to tell really bad jokes. He greeted regular patrons by name and always made a point to welcome newcomers to his establishment.

Abilities

Gnorm was a retired adventurer, and he was skilled at axe -, knife -, and halfling -throwing, being the inn's champion in all three competitions.
