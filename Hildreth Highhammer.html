Hildreth Highhammer was a dwarven adventurer from Easthaven within the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale .

Description

The dark-haired dwarf woman dressed in a blue tunic and brown leathers .

Possessions

Even when drinking at Grisella 's Winter's Cradle Tavern , Hildreth carried an axe .

History

In Eleint , 1281 DR , Hildreth joined Hrothgar 's expedition to aid Kuldahar in the Spine of the World .

Hildreth was killed when frost giants attacked the expedition in Kuldahar Pass .
