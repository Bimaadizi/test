The Bronze Phalanx , also called the Nutcrackers , was part of the standing army of the efreet in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire .

Organization

The 1,200 azer of the Bronze Phalanx were the siege engineers for the Sultan's army.

Activities

The Sultan of the efreet usually deployed the Bronze Phalanx against the djinn or rebellious noble efreeti .

Base of Operations

The City of Brass served as the base of the Bronze Phalanx.
