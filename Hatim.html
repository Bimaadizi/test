Hatim the Righteous was a paladin inhabiting Phannaskul during the mid–14th century DR .

Activities

Hatim's outspoken rants against the corruption of Phannaskul's nobles gained momentum with the commoners of the city. He attracted the attention of powerful enemies who searched for a solution to their "problem."
