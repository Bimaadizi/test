Asper was an inhabitant of Waterdeep and the wife of Mirt around 1373 DR .

Description

Asper was a lithe and petite beautiful woman.

Personality

She was soft-spoken but daring and merry in nature.

She followed the teachings of Lady Luck, Tymora .

Activities

Asper was Mirt's near-constant companion, but during her free time, she adventured with the Gray Hands , aka Force Grey , so often she was treated as an honorary member.

Mirt often sent or accompanied her on missions to Skullport , where together, they battled the beholder Xuzoun and awoke the dragon, Vulharindauloth .

Her preferred weapon was a bow.

History

Mirt rescued her when she was a babe from a sacked city and raised her. He considered her his little girl even though he later married her.
