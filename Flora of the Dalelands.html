Flora of the Dalelands was a botany book that detailed many plants native to the Dalelands in north Faerûn .

Content

Among the plants described in the book was Kelemvor's Kiss , a type of poisonous shrub found deep in the forests of Shadowdale . This description was hand-written into the margins of a page.
