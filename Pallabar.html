Pallabar was a rock gnome living in Gnomengarde in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Like many other Gnomengarde inhabitants, Pallabar preferred not to talk about his home's troubles with outsiders.

Abilities

Like all the other gnomes in Gnomengarde, Pallabar had some training as a wizard in addition to his innate magical abilities.
