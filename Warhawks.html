The Warhawks were an adventurer company who operated circa 1369 DR .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the Warhawks attended a party hosted by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Keep in Suzail . The occasion was King Azoun Obarskyr IV 's birthday, and Lord Thistle hoped to improve the standing of adventuring companies in Cormyr by having them present themselves as a gesture of fealty to the King.
