Methegrist was a fortress in the Moonwood that served as the headquarters of the Moonwatchers , a small group of paladins devoted to Helm who had appointed themselves defenders of the Moonlands . The fortress was a ruin as of the 15 th century DR .

History

The imp Fortenesti infiltrated the Moonwatchers and caused them to turn on each other. The Moonwatchers fractured and Methegrist fell into ruin.
