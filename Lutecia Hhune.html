Lutecia Hhune was a matron of the mercantile Hhune family of Baldur's Gate , who lived in the city during the 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Lutecia had little in the way of family in her golden years. With a deceased husband and no children of her own, the only family she knew was a few siblings to whom she did not speak.

She was well-served by her personal valet Kaddrus , a cambion that took the visage of a particularly attractive younger man. She believed him to be quite capable in carrying out her wants and orders.

History

In the early 1490s DR , Lutecia sought out scholars at High Hall in order to track down family members who she would find more amenable to being heir to her family's city estate.

When the Shield of the Hidden Lord was stolen from the family crypts, in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Lady Lutecia tasked Kaddrus and his lackeys with its retrieval.
