The Abyss of the Abomination was a temple dedicated to Moander , the Rotting God.

Location

This subterranean temple was located deep within the earth beneath Yûlash . A deep shaft hidden within a warehouse provided the only access to the temple.

Description

The mouth of the pit was surrounded by large fangs.

History

Following Moander's destruction, the temple and Moander's faithful were tolerated by the Red Plumes of Hillsfar by the use of well-placed bribes.

Activities

The citizens of Yûlash used the pit to dump refuse in exchange for paying a fee to the cult.
