Ganark was a bugbear chieftain who led his fellow bugbears in Mrolgyet's Legion in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Ganark and his troops were disciplined. He was known to share his allotments of food and treasure with his tribe while also assuming more than his fair share of danger in battle.

History

In the battle between Mrolgyet's Legion and the Lords' Alliance on Harvestshield Mountain in the late 15 th century DR, Ganark led his troops to support the gnolls led by Hnalguk. He was defeated in combat by a group of adventurers from Waterdeep , causing his disciplined line to buckle and forcing the Legion to spread their troops out more than they wanted to.
