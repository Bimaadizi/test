The Great Horsehoe Chamber was the dominant feature of the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 th century DR .

Description

The chamber was located on the left side of the great chamber level of the temple. It measured 30 feet (9.1 meters) wide by 45 feet (14 meters) long. A large statue of Hung Te Ping dominated the center of the chamber. A rock sat before the statue and was believed to have been struck by Hung's horse . A distinct aura of well-being could be felt within the Great Horseshoe Chamber.

Inhabitants

The oni who occupied the temple in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , avoided this chamber.
