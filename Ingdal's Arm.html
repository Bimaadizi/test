Ingdal's Arm was a human settlement in Aglarond . It was the westernmost settlement in Aglarond, and was not far from the edge of the Yuirwood . It bordered Altumbel .

Description

In 1368 DR , Ingdal's Arm was a quiet fishing town with a population of around one thousand, made up of roughly half humans and half half-elves . The adventurer known as The Masked One made a home in Ingdal's Arm.

History

It was settled in 756 DR .

The settlement was the site of the Battle of Ingdal's Arm in 1065 DR and of the subsequent Peace Talks of Ingdal's Arm . Fennibal "Thunder" Bale was a hero of the Battle of Ingdal's Arm, who famously charged into the enemy infantry, single-handedly pushing them all back. Fennibal sacrificed his life in the heroic deed allowing his side – the half-elves win the battle and eventually gain independence.
