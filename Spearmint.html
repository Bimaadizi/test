Spearmint was a type of plant of the mint family that produced a herb of the same name.

Usage

Spearmint could be brewed into a tea . Spearmint cleansed other tastes from the mouth. The halfling beer belbuck had a similar taste and effect to spearmint.

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Teseryne Truesilver of the Delimbiyr Vale offered spearmint tea and shortbread to her guests.
