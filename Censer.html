Censers were religious items in the Realms often carried by clerics who used them like portable braziers to burn incense .

Description

Censers were commonly made from brass , silver , or gold and decorated in a variety of fashions. Most could hold up to 6 oz (170 g) of incense at one time.

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 1 gold piece to 5 gold pieces depending on the material.
