Saerloonian Glowfire was a wine exported from Saerloon . It was also sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue , and available as far west from Sembia as Baldur's Gate .

Description

This renowned wine had a slight luminescence, adding a mystical quality to the wine. Neither sweet nor dry, this aromatic wine conjured images of refreshing summer breezes. Its flavor evoked the taste of pears .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , A Modest Guide to Sword Coast Wine offered Saerloonian Glowfire as much praise as it showed disdain for mulled wines such as Daggerford clarry and zzar .
