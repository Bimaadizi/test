The Gnarlroot cave was a cave system found within the Blighted Grove in the Feywild .

Geography

Geographical Features

The tunnels of Gnarlroot twisted and turned in the ground beneath the forests of the Feywild.

Flora & Fauna

The caves were filled with a wide variety of fantastic plant life. These ranged from shriveled black scrub brush and massive thorned roots that wove throughout the cavern's walls, to eye-catching flowers, such as the enormous violet lotuses and luminescent mushrooms that dotted the cave floors.

History

Circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the red cap blighter named Grimak Nailbiter used a variety of poisons created by fomorian giants to poison the trees found in the grove above the cave, by targeting the root system within.

Under the direction of Lady Merrisara Winterwhite , the elven military leader Pelendethas Everstide strived to eliminate Grimak and his corrupted influence that blighted the grove.

Around that same time a particular leprechaun sought others to help "teach a lesson" to the malevolent dark fey that dwelled within the cave.

Inhabitants

Gnarlroot cave was inhabited by all manner of evil fey creatures, such as red caps and quicklings , along with monstrous creatures, like spiderlings and various types of trolls.
