Haklisstyr was a wizard from Selgaunt in the early-to-mid 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Ilthond carried a scroll of a spell akin to teleport , which he had purloined from Haklisstyr after knifing him with a poisoned dagger in the back, strongly implying he had been his apprentice. Though Ilthond was young, the date of the event was unknown.
