RegionAthalantar , Western Heartlands

Upshyn was a landmark of Athalantar , almost certainly a town, in the early 3 rd century DR .

Geography

Upshyn was close to a crossing of sorts, almost certainly a bridge, for the Unicorn Run . It was also a high-traffic area, where guards could conceivably be posted.

History

In the Year of the Loremasters, 228 DR , Elminster had his doubts as to how to cross the Unicorn Run. Helm Stoneblade advised him on how.
