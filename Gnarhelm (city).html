Gnarhelm was the capital city of the kingdom of Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Gnarhelm was located near the center of the kingdom at the southern end of Salmon Bay . The city was situated on the rolling plains that dominated much of the land just east of Salmon Bay. The region was the only major agricultural center in Gnarhelm.
