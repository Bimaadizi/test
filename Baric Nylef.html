Baric Nylef was a knight of Tyr that lived in Bryn Shander some time after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

Baric was a member of the Order of the Gauntlet .

In the late 15 th century DR , Baric went to Bryn Shander to capture the notorious criminal Worvil Forkbeard , rumored to have hidden in the Ten Towns . He posed as a unemployed caravan guard drinking at the Kelvin's Comfort tavern. Baric was at the tavern when giants attacked the city.

Personality

Baric was a true devotee of Tyr. He waited for the day when he would meet his god. In the meantime, he felt that he must clean the world from evil.
