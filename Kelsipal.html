Kelsipal was a master trader and merchant inhabiting the Shining South during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Kelsipal lived in the city of Pyratar in Var the Golden . He was an influential member of Chaka Turamir .

Rumors & Legends

He worshiped Mask in secret and operated a group who wished to see their god take over the entire country.
