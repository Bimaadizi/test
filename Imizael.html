Imizael was a innkeeper in Phlan during the Tyranny of Dragons .

Personality

Imizael was an efficient tavern-keeper, always attentive to patrons' needs. However, she did not like to talk with customers, responding to polite questions and directing all customers wanting speak with her to the waiter, Markoth .

History

Imizael was born to a mother obsessed with elves , even giving her a clearly elven name, despite the fact that they were completely human .

Imizael managed the Laughing Goblin Inn in Phlan, helped by Markoth. In 1489 DR , she became involved in the Cult of the Dragon 's coup in the city, as the tavern became the headquarters of the forces fighting against the cultists.
