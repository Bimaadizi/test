Clorde was a human who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

Activities

Clorde was merchant, but this was a cover for his true profession: he was a member of Bregan D'aerthe operating out of Neverwinter. His wagon had the habit of "breaking down" in important locations where Clorde could gather valuable information for his employers.

History

Clorde survived the first attack of the " Lost Heir " of Alagondar and his plaguechanged minions of 1479 DR however he was most likely killed by the monsters during the "Lost Heir"'s second attack.
