Xamlyn Margaster was a wizard of the Margaster family in Silverymoon sometime after the War of the Silver Marches of 1485 DR .

Activities

Xamlyn resided in a tower in Silverymoon. Her duty for the family was to keep a watch on a nearby secret stash of magic items.
