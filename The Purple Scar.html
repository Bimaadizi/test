The Purple Scar was a festhall of note in the Cormyrean capital city, Suzail , opened in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

The Purple Scar stood to the south of open-air market plaza and to the southeast of the Field-Gates , facing the waters of the Dragonmere . Before the mid-14 th century DR, the Purple Scar was a smithy-shop owned by Atham One-Eye .
