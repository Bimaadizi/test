The Hall of the Water Buffalo was a shrine dedicated to Yampa found within the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 th century DR .

Description

The Hall was located in the center of the upper galleries level of the temple. The Hall was roughly 20 feet (6.1 meters) long. A pair of chambers were found along the hall, each measuring 15 feet (4.6 meters) wide by 15 feet (4.6 meters) long. The rearmost chamber contained a statue of Yampa the water buffalo.

Inhabitants

A pair of oni lived within the Hall of the Water Buffalo as of the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .
