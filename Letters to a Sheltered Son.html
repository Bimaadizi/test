Letters to a Sheltered Son was a book written by Oblut Thoim of Teziir in the early 14 th century DR .

History

Letters to a Sheltered Son was an autobiographical collection of letters published in the Year of the Striking Falcon, 1333 DR . A copy of the book was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
