Lady Jasmine was the head priestess of Selûne and a priest of Sehanine Moonbow who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

Description

Lady Jasmine had snow-white hair and opalescent eyes.

History

Lady Jasmine witnessed the fall of the shards of Selûne on Neverwinter and recognized the event as a sign from Selûne. She later contacted and tasked a group of adventurers to retrieve the gems. When the gems were brought back to her, Lady Jasmine used them to purify the Shard of Night , reconsecrating the place to Selûne.

Lady Jasmine may have aided a group of adventurers to solve a series of mysterious murders in Neverwinter, when a plaguechanged aboleth of the Abolethic Sovereignty went insane and began to causing the citizens of Neverwinter to go mad and kill one another.
