Sedina Wolpaw was a scout in the Dessarin Valley during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

Sedina hailed from Triboar . In 1491 DR she was in Westbridge , where she could have helped some adventurers defeat a remnant cell of the Cult of the Black Earth in that city.
