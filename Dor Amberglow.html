Dor Amberglow was a human adventurer active in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Dor had short ginger hair and white skin. He wore a brown tied shirt.

Abilities

While seemingly not a wizard , Dor was able to cast arcane spell and was immune to offensive psionic powers. He was known for his competent teamwork with other adventurers during battles.
