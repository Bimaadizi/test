Tinnir was a secluded village found in the nation of Rashemen .

Description

While most of the village's structures were whitewashed and typical of those found in the region, there were some buildings that resembled those found in west Faerûn .

Geography

Tinnir was hidden away in a small forested valley on the southeastern shore of Lake Tirulag , just beyond the site of the Golden Way .

Trade

Tinnir received some visitors from among the fisherman that made their catches in Lake Tirulag.
