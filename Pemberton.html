The Pembertons were a rich noble family from Highcastle during the 14 th century DR.

History

At some point decades before 1370s DR, Harrold Pemberton married into the noble family of Mildred Pemberton. The fact that Mildred never stopped bringing up even into the couple's advanced years. Harrold was ashamed of his family's origins and kept it a secret from most.

In 1372 DR , Harrold and Mildred traveled to the city of Daggerford and cold me seen bickering inside the luxurious Chateau Elite in the Money Quarter .
