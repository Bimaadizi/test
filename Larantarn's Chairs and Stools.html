Larantarn's Chairs and Stools was the shop of the master carpenter and furniture-maker Ommagol Larantarn of Sundabar , circa 1365 DR .

Specializing in, as his shop's name states, wooden chairs and stools, Ommagol was so skilled in his craft he could craft a chair, and immediately follow up with an exact duplicate, without referring to the original.

Ommagol's shop featured heavy tapestries that hung upon the walls, and scattered across the floor. As he would turn chair legs on the lathe , Ommagol would fling them over his shoulder while drinking wine and singing.
