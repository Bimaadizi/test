Onshall Goldcloak was a wicked wizard apprentice who inhabited the city of Waterdeep during the 1350's DR .

History

Onshall was apprenticed to a particularly vile mage named Melinter . He would meet his master, along with other apprentices and members of their gang, in a small courtyard in Waterdeep's Dock Ward . The courtyard would later become known as Melinter's Court because of these meetings.
