Titys was an old fence living in Hillsfar around 1367 DR .

Description

Titys was tall and had graying hair.

Activities

Titys owned Titys's Emporium , which was a well-stocked general store, but he also ran a side business buying anything without question from adventurers . He was a reliable source of information regarding anything about the Red Plumes or other aspects of the area.
