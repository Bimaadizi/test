Wombats were tunnel-digging marsupials .

Description

Wombats appeared somewhat similar to beavers or small bears , with flat noses, bristly whiskers, rounded ears, short legs, and particularly heavy front claws. Their bodies appeared powerful, and they were covered in coarse fur.

History

Some believed that wombats had been brought to Toril from another world through portals .

Notable Wombats

An island off the coast of Orlil was home to a population of intelligent wombats divided into small settlements, each with a population of no more than 50, give or take the odd platypus . Sometimes, these wombats were known to enroll in Orlil's College of Bards .

Two intelligent wombats, Byrt and Lugg , became known outside of their island home as companions of Artus Cimber starting in the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR .
