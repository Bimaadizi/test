The Wall of Coins was a temple devoted to Waukeen , the Coinmaiden, located in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan in the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The temple was located in central Suldolphor near Emerald Dragon Way .

Description

Since Waukeen's silence following the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR , the Wall of Coins was practically abandoned, filling with dust and darkness. A famed floating altar made from pure gold lay within the temple.
