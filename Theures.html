Theures was a Dragonsoul of the Cult of the Dragon that took the lead on their operations in Icespire Peak during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a direct subordinate of Wyrmspeaker Galvan .

History

In the 1480s DR , Theures was directed to take the lead on acquiring mithral from within the Clan Icehammer 's mine at Icespire Peak within the Sword Mountains . While the valuable metal was much desired by the cult for the construction of their lightning jars , as well as construction efforts elsewhere, Theures did use some of what they acquired as an offering to the white dragon Merothrax .
