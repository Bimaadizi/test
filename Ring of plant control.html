TypeMagical ring
Value2,500 gp

A ring of plant control was a magical ring that imbued wearers with an effect similar to a potion of plant control .

Powers

So long as a ring's effect was concentrated upon, the wearer could influence plants, fungi , molds , and plant creatures like shambling mounds into performing any activity that wasn't self-destructive. The area of control for normal plants was a square with 20‑foot (6.1‑meter) sides and it had an effective range of 90 yards (82 meters).
