Tortilla was a flat bread made of coarse-grained mayz . It was traditionally baked in Maztica and was available for purchase in Faer√ªn through Aurora's Emporium .

Description

Tortillas were baked on hot stone griddles and could be consumed plain, with spices , wrapping meat or beans, baked with cheese or fried in oil .

Tortillas purchased through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue were shipped from the Temple of Helm at Ulatos . Part of the profits was donated to the temple in order to assist the indigenous population with medical care and education.

A particular recipe involved cutting tortillas into triangles, frying them in oil, and then dusting them with ground pepper .
