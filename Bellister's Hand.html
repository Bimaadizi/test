Bellister's Hand was a store in Waterdeep .

Location

This store was located on the major intersection Waymoot , close to its warehouse Bellister's House , in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This shop stood two stories tall.

Services

This business sold general goods. It was also possible to find stolen goods here.
