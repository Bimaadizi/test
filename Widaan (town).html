Widaan was one of the nine towns of the sahuagin kingdom of Kurrimal and its predecessor Aleaxtis .

Geography

This town was located within the Alamber Sea .

Government

Like all nine of the towns of Kurrimal, this town was governed by a prince of the same name, Widaan .

History

In the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , when the kingdom of Kurrimal was founded as the successor of Aleaxtis, this town's name was changed to that of the prince put in charge of governing it.
