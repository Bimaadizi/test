Lurkan the Reaver was a "mercenary lord" who operated throughout the Sword Coast , and later the Eastern Heartlands , during the 14 th century DR .

History

In Eleint of 1357 DR , Lurkan traveled from his home in West Faerûn , east toward the lands around the Sea of Fallen Stars . Rumors of his relocation stated that he intended to sell his services between the various city-states of Zhentil Keep , Mulmaster , Hillsfar and the Forest Nation of Cormyr , who were all involved in major conflicts at the time.
