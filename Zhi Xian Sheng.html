Zhi Xian Sheng was an abbot of the Caves of a Thousand Gods in Kora Shan in the Hordelands during the mid 14 th century DR .

Activities

As of the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR , Zhi Xian Sheng was the eldest and wisest, and by default the most powerful, abbot within the Caves of a Thousand Gods.
