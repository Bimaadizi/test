A belt of hill giant strength , also known as a girdle of hill giant strength , was a magical belt that granted an individual tremendous physical might.

Description

These belts were rather plain-looking in appearance, made of thick leather and metal studs. They were considered among the least powerful of belts of giant strength .

Powers

When worn, the belt imbued the wearer with the strength—albeit not the size or the girth—of a massive hill giant .

History

As of the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , one of these belts could be found in Ribald Barterman 's Adventurer Mart in Athkatla .
