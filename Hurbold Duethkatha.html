Hurbold Duethkatha , or Scar , was a commander of the Flaming Fist mercenary company during the mid–14 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a good friend of Duke Eltan the founder of the Flaming Fist.

Activities

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Scar worked with Abdel Adrian to discover evidence that the Seven Suns was involved with the Iron Throne . At the same time he was investigating a series of disappearances that originated from the city's sewer tunnels.

Commander Scar was killed a short time later and his persona was taken over by one of the doppelgangers working for Sarevok Anchev .
