Alea Dahast was a female elf who led a raiding party on a human camp in Cormanthor in −75 DR . The purpose of the raid was to kill the camp's inhabitants and destroy the settlement, but Alea and her party rescued the Netherese wizard Baerauble Etharr in the process. The two would later become lovers.

Known as the "High Lady of Art", she helped raise the mythal over Myth Drannor .

Alea would later become the Spell-Major of the Akh'Faer and a High Mage from 490 DR .

She fought in the Weeping War and died on Flamerule 11 – 12 of 714 DR in one of the last battles of the war.
