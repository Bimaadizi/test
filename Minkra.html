Minkra was a notable sage in the nation of T'u Lung around Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) .

History

Minkra lived in the great city of Balanzia , a century prior to its destruction. There they operated an observatory in the eastern reaches of the city, across the street from the home of the Fendo Clan . Along the same road, to the west of their home was the city market and to the east was the Bonaku Boarding House .
