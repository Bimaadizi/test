Xicvizt , or Rogue Thought , was a rogue mind flayer from Ryxyg .

History

During the late 1480s or early 1490s DR , Xicvizt sensed a strange presence in the Underdark . Investigating, he found the demon lord Graz'zt . Soon he disconnected from the illithid hive-mind and went rogue, starting to worship Graz'zt and becoming a warlock . He retreated into a lair and allied with a group of githyanki .
