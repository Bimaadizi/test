Mithykyl was an illithid who was under command of the Bhaalspawn Sendai circa 1368 DR .

History

When Gorion's Ward was close to reaching Sendai's inner chamber of her enclave, Mithykyl was appointed as the last bastion of defense. He was in charge of several ulitharids , vampiric illithids , and elder umber hulks . In the end, Mithykyl and his minions fell, and the ward gained access to Sendai.
