Al-Suqut Mountains , or the Tumbling Mountains , were found in west Zakhara .

Geography

Al-Suqut Mountains separated the western "border" of the Pearl Cities from the Great Sea . They were situated just south of the High Desert .

Description

The mountains were strung along the western coast of the southern portion of west Zakhara, sitting in a roughly linear north to south position. The mountains created a foreboding coastline of sheer plunging rocky cliffs.

Inhabitants

Al-Suqut Mountains were believed to harbor mad wizards , unenlightened tribes, and sea-faring brigands.
