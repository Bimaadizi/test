Warchief Fiella Markin was the ruler of Hethar in Dambrath .

Personality

Due to her lack of priestly skills, Fiella was a fiercely determined individual who was not afraid to take risks in order to accomplish her goals.

Activities

Fiella was loyal to Duchess Merial Luenath and Queen Yenandra .

Relationships

Fiella was the sister of Grenai Markin and Herald Markin .
