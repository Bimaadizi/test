Myrian Beechwood was the provisional lord of Scardale in 1368 DR .

Activities

Myrian needed to subject most of his actions to the Parliament of Advisors , composed of representatives of the occupying powers.

History

Once Myrian was an adventurer priest of Waukeen . After Lashan Aumersair 's demise, he did so much for the poor people of the dale that the occupying forces offered him the provisional lordship. To the surprise of all, himself included, he remained in office until 1368 DR, despite growing tired and angry from the conflict in the Parliament.
