Esselyn Xharn was a town guard who lived in the settlement of Idyllglen .

History

Esselyn was present in Idyllglen when Yeenoghu assaulted the settlement with his demonic horde. [ as of when? ] Esselyn and the other guards protected their fellow citizens during the battle. However, in the conflict they were covered in demon ichor , which caused temporarily madness and gave them urges for cannibalism.
