The Jorhat Shan were a pair of tall mountains located in the Endless Wastes .

Description

Located between Solon in the Raurin Desert , and Ra-Khati in the Katakoro Shan mountain range, sat the twin mountains of Jorhat Shan.

The Jorhat Shan, which meant "Two Brothers Mountains", was considered one of the major landmarks of the Endless Wastes. The mountains themselves rose 14,389 ft (4,385.8 m) above sea level and were known to be barren and rocky, with few trees or plants growing on their slopes.

Notable Locations

Located on the Jorhat Shan was an ancient fortress known only as the Fortress of Jorhat .

Most people hurried past this location, due to rumors of the site being haunted by those killed there.
