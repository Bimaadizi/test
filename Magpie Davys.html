Magpie Davys was a member of the Guild from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Guild reinstated tethered knife fights to its members delight. The fights were held until the first blood. During the Absolute crisis ravaging Baldur's Gate, newcomer Magpie Davys was scheduled to fight Hans "Leather" Feather . Bursar Uktar collected bets and dispensed winnings.
