Dekanter was a famous explorer miner, whose endeavors commenced off the Silver Age of Netheril .

History

In the −2758 DR , Dekanter discovered great wealth of valuable minerals beneath the Greypeak Mountains for the empire of Netheril. Among the riches found beneath the mountains were veins of iron , silver , gold , platinum , and mercury . The mines of Dekanter were formed in his name, and dwarves were enlisted to extract the minerals for the empire's use. Thus began the Silver Age of Netheril.
