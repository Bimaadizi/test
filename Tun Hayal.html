Tun Hayal was the owner of the Merchant's Home in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

Though he owned the most splendid carvanserai in all of the City of Delights, Tun was rarely found within his establishment. He preferred to leave the operation of his business into the hands of trusted servants.
