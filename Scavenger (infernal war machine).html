A Scavenger was a bulky infernal war machine built with a clawed arm used to haul damaged machines or other debris worth salvaging from the battlefield.

Description

Attached to the back of the Scavenger was a massive swinging crane with an iron grappling claw fastened to the end of a winch and a 50‑foot-long (15‑meter) chain.

Abilities

The grappling claw of a Scavenger could be used to catch objects and creatures alike. The claw's operator could choose to release whatever the claw was holding. It could carry up to 4,000 pounds (1,800 kilograms) in cargo.
