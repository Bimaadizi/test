Avro Quarrel was a human resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Avro had light skin and shoulder-length orange hair.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR , , Avro trained under the supervision of a Purple Dragon with his hoe , learning how to wield a weapon.
