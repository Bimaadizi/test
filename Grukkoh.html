Grukkoh was a bugbear who lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Grukkoh made his way to the village of Moonhaven , after it was taken over by the cult of the Absolute . During his stay, he took some time to enjoy the company of the ogress Buthir . They went to the village's barn and had a rousing, and noisy, roll in the hay.
