The Black Viper was an inn found in the city of Saerloon in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Though less popular than some of Saerloon's more notable inns, the Black Viper's accommodations were good and the pricing was reasonable. The atmosphere was usually quiet as the inn was well-policed by efficient bouncers that even included a few wizards .
