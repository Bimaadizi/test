Captain Pripyn was a human Purple Dragons officer who was deployed in Tilver's Gap in the 14 th century DR .

Abilities

Pripyn was extraordinarily strong for a human.

Activities

Around 1358 DR , Pripyn commanded a cavalry patrol on the roads near Tilverton , alongside other squads commanded by Captain Thardock and Captain Orlberl .
