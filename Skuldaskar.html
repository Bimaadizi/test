The Skuldaskar was a forest in the Western Heartlands between Berdusk and Elturel .

Geography

It lay somewhere between Berdusk and Elturel . The Skuldask Road went through its heart.

Flora & Fauna

Per Elminster Aumar , he had seen bears , forest-cats and sleep-spiders in the forest, as well as an overturned wagon surrounded by gnawed bones. The flora included shadowtops .

History

Elminster Aumar was attacked by bandits while riding across the Skuldaskar during the first frosts of the Year of the Chosen, 240 DR , going from Elturel to Berdusk . He found many of the bandits' weapons to have an emblem of a serpent ready to strike. As the bandits struck his horse down, he walked the rest of the way, stopping at the Herald's Horn .
