Imryth Tower was the home and place of business of the cleric Audara Imryth in the city of Westgate in 1367 DR .

Location

The tower was located at the end of Twoturrets Lane in the southwest section of the city.

Activities

Audara sold potions from her tower, but this was just a front to keep her role as a Harper agent a secret.
