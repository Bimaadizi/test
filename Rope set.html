A rope set was a tool in the Realms used to climb surfaces.

Description

Rope sets included a variety of grapples and pulleys with 600 feet (180 meters) of elven -made triple-stranded silk rope . A set weighed only 5 pounds (2.3 kilograms).

History

Rope sets were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 40 gold pieces and included a satchel .
