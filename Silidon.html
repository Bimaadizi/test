Silidon was a sorcerer from Isle Broddick in the Whamite Isles . He had an apprentice . Silidon represented Broddick in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
