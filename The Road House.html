The Road House was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Fellowship of Carters and Coachmen .

Location

This guild hall was located on Carters' Way , in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This guildhall stood two-stories tall.

Reputation

Like other guildhalls in the Southern Ward, this building was used as a landmark by locals.
