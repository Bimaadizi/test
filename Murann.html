Murann , known as the Sailors' City , was a port city and the second-largest city of Amn until the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , when it was conquered by the Sothillisian Empire and became the capital of the newly established kingdom of Muranndin .

Geography

Murann was located in Tarseth Bay along the Sea of Swords .

Trade

Murann was located at the western end of the Tethir Road . The city's economy was based on sea trade, including shipbuilding and whaling. By the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Murann had well-established transoceanic trade with Maztica .

Defenses

The city was defended by the Harbor Guard (3,000 strong).

History

Murann was established in the Year of the Awakening Magic, 212 DR .

In the Year of the Wanderer, 1338 DR , the Murann Spice War occurred between the Ophal and Ulvax family holdings.

In the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR , the Coast Plague infected Murann, as well as other settlements in the area, killing 20% of the population.

In 1369 DR, three ships of the Vemmil clan returned from Zakhara .

In Eleasis of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the armies of Sothillis and Cyrvisnea attacked and laid siege to Murann. By Midsummer of 1371 DR, Murann fell to their forces.
