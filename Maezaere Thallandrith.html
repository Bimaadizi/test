Lady Maezaere Thallandrith was a noblewoman of the late 14 th century DR .

History

In early spring of 1373 DR , Lady Maezaere Thallandrith was one of the guests of Lady Joysil Ambrur at Haelithtorntowers in Marsember in an evening in which the lady retired to her chambers early, dismissing her guests. Later that evening, she met with a host of other people, which included the Red Wizard Harnrim Starangh .
