Vuth Steelire was the Battlemaster of the Akh'Velahr during the Fall of Myth Drannor .

History

Vuth was a key strategist during the Weeping War , the mastermind behind the Northdark Skirmishes , and the hero of the Mythal's Mettle Campaign in Alturiak of 712 DR .

In Hammer 29 – Midwinter of 713 DR , thank to their strategies, Battlemaster Vuth, Captain Hachaam Selorn , and Clan Chief Jhax Narlgold defeated the nycaloth Gaulguth and an entire division of the Army of Darkness was obliterated.

Personality

Vuth was an arrogant and brash dwarf with no patience for prejudice or politics. He truly inspired his troops and was an honorable individual.

Possessions

Vuth wielded one of the Baneblades of Demron , Mhaorathil .
