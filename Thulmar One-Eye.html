Thulmar One-Eye was a warrior and trainer based in Port Llast on the Sword Coast North in the late 13 th century DR . Notably, Thulmar was a wielder of Namarra , the Sword That Never Sleeps.

History

Around the 1270s DR, Thulmar One-Eye provided training in the combat arts to the adventurer Flar of the Company of the Gryphon . To pay for it, Flar gave Namarra to Thulmar.

Thulmar One-Eye wielded the Sword that Never Sleeps until dying in an event known as the Cold Winter . Afterward, Namarra was received by the renowned Stormraven , the Proud Queen, Mistress of Port Llast.
