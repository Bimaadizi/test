Jhalass Huntsilver was a queen of Cormyr and the wife of King Dhalmass Obarskyr , who ruled from 1210 DR to 1227 DR . She was the mother of King Palaghard II .

Relations

A member of the Huntsilver family, Jhalass was the wife of King Dhalmass Obarskyr. They had four children: Proster (born 1213 DR , died 1222 DR , aged 9), Palaghard (born 1214 DR ), Elliira (born 1218 DR ), and Pelara (born 1220 DR ).

History

After the port city of Marsember rebelled, King Dhalmass seized it back in the Year of the Wall, 1227 DR . However, just after the reconquest, both Dhalmass and Jhalass were killed in mysterious circumstances when their caravan back to Suzail was ambushed. Assassins from the Fire Knives were blamed for the death of the king and queen, and the organization was banished from the kingdom.
