Majarra Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Majarra .

Location

The villa was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep 's North Ward on the south side of Sulmor Street where it was intersected by Ussilbran Street .

Description

The walled compound contained three buildings arranged around a central courtyard and garden. The buildings of Majarra Villa were three or four storys tall.
