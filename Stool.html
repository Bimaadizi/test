Stool was a myconid sprout living in Neverlight Grove in the Underdark in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa 1486 DR , it was captured by Sarith Kzekarit , who took it to the drow outpost of Velkynvelve .

Personality

Stool's main concern was to return to its fellows. If it grew attached to humanoid people, it acted like a curious and enthusiastic younger sibling. At the hands of the drow, it felt lonely and frightened.

Powers

Thanks to its spores, it could communicate telepathically with other creatures and could also act as bridge between two creatures without a common language in order to communicate.
