The theurgy of spring was a powerful magical ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The ring embodied the equally balanced power of the sun and moon , and channeled the full power of the spring season and its storms. It gave a broad benefit to the wearer's ability to avoid all sorts of danger, but its most obvious trait was the empowering of lightning, electricity, and air -based spells. A charge of electricity built up in the ring all the while, eventually lashing out to shock foes with a deadly blast of lightning.
