King Haedrak I of the House of Ithal-Strohm-Bormul was the fourth king of the Lions' Dynasty of Tethyr .

Relationships

Haedrak was a first cousin to Kortal , the previous king. He had at least five sons.

History

Haedrak was born in 988 DR , took the throne from his cousin upon the latter's death in 1022 DR , and passed the throne to this fifth son Coram on his own death in 1059 DR .
