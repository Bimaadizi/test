Maheer yn Kadar el Saddith was a traveling merchant and master instrument maker who traveled the Realms in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Description

Maheer was a portly fellow with bronze skin.

Personality

As befitting his career as a merchant, Maheer was a particularly friendly individual.

Abilities

While he had to proficiency with weapon smithing, Maheer was skilled in the art of crafting musical instruments, particularly musical horns . He was even known to have been able to work with and further enhance horns of Valhalla .

Activities

Traveling across the lands, Maheer primarily dealt in mundane and enchanted weapons. He specifically had numerous weapons made from iron found in the Storm Horns of Cormyr .

Relationships

He was acquainted with the expert Athkatlan blacksmith Cromwell .

History

As of the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Maheer had established himself at the open marketplace of Waukeen's Promenade in Athkatla. Previously, he was selling his wares and abilities in the far-eastern nation of Rashemen .
