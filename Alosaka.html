Alosaka , who was also called the Germinator , was a great spirit of the folklore and myth of the Azuposi people of the Pasocada Basin of northern Maztica .

History

The Germinator was called into being by the song of Sus'sistinako , the Spider Woman. As a spirit of creation, Alosaka helped make all of the flowers, animals, and other life forms found within the Pasocada Basin.
