Tanalanthara Mytersaal , who was also nicknamed She-Wolf , was the sixth High Mage of Silverymoon .

Activities

Tanalanthara's time as High Mage was rather short, serving from the Year of the Scratching Claw, 877 DR to the Year of the Curse, 882 DR . She deposed the Warlord Lashtor to claim her rule.

Relationships

Her brother Yril claimed the title of Warlord after Lashtor was deposed.
