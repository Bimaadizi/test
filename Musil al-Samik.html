Musil al-Samik was a broker who lived in the Pilgrims' District of Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

Musil acquired fabulous manors for wealthy pilgrims who traveled to Huzuz. Over the years he developed relationships with many members of the court.

History

Musil initially traveled to Huzuz in 1347 DR as a pilgrim and priest of Zann . He discovered a talent for real estate and decided to pursue this new profession.
