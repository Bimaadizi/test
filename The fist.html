The fist was a magic +1 chainmail gauntlet made from adamantine , created by dwarves .

It was covered with spikes and protected the wearer from chill metal and heat metal . Once a day, the wearer could make a powerful punch that knocked over opponents.

It was worth around 23,000 gold pieces.
