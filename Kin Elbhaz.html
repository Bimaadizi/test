Kin Elbhaz was a pueblo settlement in the Pasocada Basin in Maztica . It was known for its good fortune.

Description

It always received above-average yields from its crops and rarely suffered from any diseases or pestilence more serious than the common cold .

Population

It had a population of 500.

Rumors

There were theories among jealous outsiders that the villagers or their ancestors had made a pact with evil forces in order to ensure their prosperity, and that there must be a terrible price to be paid for this. In fact, the prosperity was thanks brownies living nearby, who aided the villagers by driving off pests and watering the crops and so on, in exchange for unlimited grain they took from the pueblo's store via a secret passage.
