Gothal Street was a minor street in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

Gothal Street ran between the thoroughfares Calamastyr Lane and Julthoon Street , and was crossed by Trader's Way .

Notable Locations

Links to Sewers

An entrance to the sewers of Waterdeep could be found where Gothal Street met Calamastyr Lane.
