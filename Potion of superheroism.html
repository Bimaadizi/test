A potion of superheroism was a stronger version of the potion of heroism .

Powers

When ingested, the potion granted an increase in life energy akin to the potion of heroism , boosting the combat prowess of anyone who had yet to reach legendary levels of fighting skill.

These potions could be used as the material component for the Tenser's transformation spell .

History

One was kept in the Devouring Chambers , within Thaymount 's Citadel .

Production

Lapis lazuli was an adequate base for such a potion. They may also have been made with a lock of a great hero's hair, or by priests with access to the charm sphere .
