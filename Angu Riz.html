Angu Riz was a wu jen and seer in the nation of T'u Lung around Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) .

History

Riz lived in the great city of Balanzia , a century prior to its destruction. There they lived within a number of towers in the far north-western reaches of the city, just south of the mansion of Rhot Chu Asivna .
