Lehigh was a small village located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The village was located along the High King's Road on the northeastern fringes of the Dernall Forest . Lehigh was just 8 miles (13 kilometers) due south of Caer Callidyrr in the county of Talffolk .
