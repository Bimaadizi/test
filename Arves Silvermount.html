Arves Silvermount was a client of the Sharran House of Grief and an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Arves sought the services of the House of Grief aid. The Mapping of the Heart revealed Arves's "melancholy heart." The ritual was paid for in full and Arves was referred for Unburdening .
