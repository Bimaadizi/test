Wilton Mortaire was a sergeant in the Zhent army that occupied Daggerdale in 1369 DR but was also an honorable man.

History

Wilton Mortaire was a member of the Zhentarim and became a sergeant in the Zhent army that occupied Daggerdale in 1369 DR. When resistance fighters started the last battle against the invaders, Mortaire and some of his men retreated into a warehouse.

Adventurers with the resistance had the option of attacking and slaying Mortaire and his fellows or convince him to go out and talk.
