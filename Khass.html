Khass was a male deity worshiped by barbarians of the Eastern Shaar .

Description

The deity was a cloud god with multiple limbs, and palm trees were said to be his fingers.

Worshipers

Khass was a frustrating god to worship, because he would ignore his worshipers' pleas for rain for months, and then send heavy downpours to drench them for weeks at time.
