Lobo Denysse was an unsavory gnomish barbarian mercenary from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Lobo was a light-skinned strawberry-blonde gnomish woman. She wore hide armor , hid her face behind a red mask, and carried a massive axe .
