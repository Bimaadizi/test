Thusk Tharmuil was a powerful archmage who lived in Glister in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . He was often credited with keeping the town safe despite its hostile location in Thar .

Description

Thusk was not merely Glister's most powerful wizard , but one of Glister's claims to fame in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR. The man himself was 5 feet and 8 inches (1.7 meters) tall, appearing as a portly man in his 50s with a balding head and a bushy grey unibrow. He preferred to dress simply, such as in a gray robe with black slippers. He did, however, wear a silver -rimmed belt which held a jeweled dagger .

Personality

Thusk was talkative and jovial, and was generally a kind and sympathetic individual. He was gracious to those who shared his zany sense of humor and could make him laugh. He enjoyed eating, drinking, joking, singing, and telling stories, and had a deep, baritone singing voice.

Possessions

His tower was built into a hill on the east side of Glister.

Activities

Thusk was ostensibly retired, although he still took an active interest in the peace and safety of Glister, and kept an eye on the orcs and ogres of Thar as well as the activities of evil factions like the Zhentarim and the Cult of the Dragon . He would go so far as to recruit and pay adventurers passing through town to solve the regions problems. When sizing up an adventuring party's worthiness, he would invite them to a sumptuous dinner of rich food and wine to swap tales and tell jokes. If he liked the adventurers, we would treat them to a dessert of pudding and coffee .

Thusk was a protector of Glister and an important source of its regional power, responsibilities he shared alongside the town's temples: the Hall of Luck , the House of Swords , and the House of Auril's Breath . He bred special wolves which attacked orcs and goblins but left the humanoid inhabitants and guests of Glister unharmed. His efforts were credited not only with keeping the town safe from local monsters, but also from monsters out of Vaasa .

Relationships

As of the late 1360s DR, Thusk had a dutiful apprentice named Menzel and a beleaguered cat named Sasha .

Thusk kept in touch with other wizards in order to stay abreast of news. He also had many connections across the Moonsea from his days as an adventurer , and knew that he could field an army of mercenaries if Glister's need was ever great enough. His closest allies were his old companions: Ghoran Bhinduth , Multhran Brokenshield , and Jhaness Lhasprair . He had implied that he had an admiration for—or perhaps a friendly rivalry with— Elminster .

He took a hard stance against the Zhentarim and any plans or projects they had within Thar.

History

In his adventuring days, Thusk led a party known as The Company of the Cat . He retired to Glister at some point prior to the Vaasan War and was nominally credited for being the reason that Zhengyi the Witch-King of Vaasa did not attempt to march his forces across the Galena Mountains into Thar.

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Thusk aided a young refugee from Vaasa to find a band of adventurers to help her rescue her parents from dragon -riding brigands, whom Thusk correctly surmised to be members of the Cult of the Dragon. Unbeknownst to the archmage, however, the teenage girl was in fact a steel dragon named Steelheart .
