Poutine was a type of food found in Baldur's Gate and the Western Heartlands .

Description

Poutine was a type of food found throughout the Western Heartlands along the shores of the River Chionthar . It was a savory dish consisting of fried potato strips topped with cheese and gravy . A poutine usually sold for 20 gp as of the late 15 th century DR .
