Aklar Delkash was a human man and merchant operating in the Vast in the late 1360s DR .

History

Aklar was a trader in quality furs and woven rugs . He planned to sell these in the city of Procampur , and stopped at the hamlet of Sevenecho on the way. There he stayed at the The Worried Wyvern inn .

On the 2nd Eleint in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Aklar learned that a local well had been contaminated with a magic -resistant poison . Frightened, he made a quick departure.

Description

Aklar Delkash was a dark-skinned man with black hair and bushy eyebrows. He had beady, somewhat shifty-looking dark brown eyes. He was 5 feet and 4 inches (1.6 meters) tall and weighed 180 pounds (82 kilograms).

He dressed flamboyantly, in brightly colored fine silks . This was at odds with his generally dour attitude.

Allies

He had hired four mercenaries to protect and assist him on his journey.
