Lord Consul Povalo Lurian was a noble of House Lurian in the city of Procampur in the Vast in the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

As head of House Lurian, he was involved in the family's gold -smithing business. He also held the title of Lord Consul and sat on the Nobles Council , as well as several other councils for the betterment and management of the city, including the Council of Justice.

Relationships

His wife was High Lady Jasmine Lurian and they had some children.

History

He was head of the family by 1370 DR , when he was aged 55.
