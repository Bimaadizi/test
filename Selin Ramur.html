Selin Ramur was the marchion of Mirabar sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

Activities

Selin was the nominal ruler of Mirabar and a key member of the Lords' Alliance . However the true power in the city was the Council of Sparkling Stones and Selin ruled only over foreign policy.
