Bexron was a glabrezu in Maerimydra .

History

As of 1491 DR , Bexron and two bar-lgura watched the entrance to the Undying Temple but in truth they relaxed with a good smoke and boasted to each other.

Personality

Bexron was a ferocious demon but the madness spread by demon lord Graz'zt had changed him into a lazy and somewhat friendly creature. Bexron now looked for new experiences to luxuriate in.
