The Tower of the Eye was a temple of Helm in the Temple District of the city of Procampur in the Vast in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Wandering Wyrm, 1317 DR , the High Guardian of the time wrote about the Plague of Dragons and the dragon that spread the diseases around the Inner Sea , telling of theories surrounding them and events in other lands. The current Thultyrl recruited the temple corps to help defend the city. The High Guardian's writings were kept in the Tower's ecclesiastic archives.

In Marpenoth of the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , when Procampur was struck by the featherlung plague, clerics of Helm treated the sick within the Temple District.

Organization

By 1357 DR and through 1367 DR, it was led by High Guardian Endra "Watchever" Mathlyn , with 18 subordinate priests and 24 lay-followers.
