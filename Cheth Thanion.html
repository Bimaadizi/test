Cheth Thanion was an innkeep inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Cheth was a large man who was easy to get along with. He had a knack for always remembering a face and never forgot when someone owed him money.

Activities

Cheth owned the Wandering Wemic located at the corner of Satchel Alley and Diamond Street in Waterdeep's Sea Ward .
