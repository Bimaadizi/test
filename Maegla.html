Maegla was a shopkeeper in Red Larch during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Activities

Maegla ran a clothing shop with her husband, Helvur Tarnlar . She was the true business mind in the shop.

The Tarnlars had at this time four children, two boys and two girls, aged between 7 and 10 years old, who were known to play in and around the ravine by Lance Rock outside of Red Larch.

Maegla, as well as her husband, were members of the Lords' Alliance at this time.
