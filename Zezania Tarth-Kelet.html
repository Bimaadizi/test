Zezania Tarth-Kelet was a Chultan human woman from Tashalar . She was a priestess of Shar and a member of Demarch's Folly , an organization dedicated to learning shadow magic .

Abilities

Zezania was a wizard and cleric of Shar who studied divine magic , arcane magic, and shadow magic , and was able to use the Shadow Weave . She combined these diverse paths of study as a mystic theurge , and also developed as a child of night .

History

Interested in shadow magic and curious about the Shadow Weave , members of Demarch's Folly sought out someone who could use both. They discovered Zezania. She agreed to join their organization and provided guidance in their efforts.
