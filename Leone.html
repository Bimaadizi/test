Leone was a warrior inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Leone's residence was located in Waterdeep's Castle Ward at the southern end of the Street of the Sword where it joined Waterdeep Way . The south side of his home sat on Gem Street .
