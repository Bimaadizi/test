Aqua fortis , also known as nitric acid , was a highly corrosive liquid substance known in the Realms.

Properties

This substance was typically yellow in color, but could range to orange-brown or clear. Though highly corrosive, aqua fortis was not potent enough of an acid to melt through gold and platinum . However, in a sufficiently concentrated form, it could inflict immediate injuries to a victim, especially if ingested.

Crafting

Aqua fortis could be distilled from alum and nitre using oil of vitriol .
