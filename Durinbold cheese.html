Durinbold cheese was a type of sheep 's milk cheese found in the Western Heartlands and the Sword Coast North regions of Faerûn as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The Durinbold family's cheese was distinctly flavored. This creamy cheese had a nutty taste and was soaked in honey . It was preserved by being wrapped in chestnut leaves and was cut into wedges when sold.
