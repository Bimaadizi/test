Sumcha was a human thief who claimed to be a merchant prince when he moved to Velprintalar in Aglarond around 1358 DR . His real occupation was as a burglar in Sembia , but he was forced to flee to Aglarond after stealing goods belonging to Zhentil Keep . He adopted Velprintalar as his new home, building and living in Sumcha's Tower with his family and servants.
