Jaxsen was a sorcerer member of the Xanathar's Thieves' Guild in Waterdeep in 1375 DR .

Activities

Usually Jaxsen lived inside the Yawning Portal inn, keeping an eye over all the adventurers attracted by the dying message of Halaster Blackcloak .

Personality

Jaxsen worshiped an evil god and joined the Xanathar's Guild because saw the beholder crime lord as the ultimate tyrant.

Relationship

Jaxsen had an eyeball familiar called Xiv , which he used to keep in contact with the other Guild members. Xiv was regularly under an invisibility spell.
