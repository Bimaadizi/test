The City Guard of Ordulin served as the main line of defense and police force for the city.

Organization

Captain Raithspur was the leader of the city guard in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR . He commanded 2,000 guardsmen. Guardsmen were armed with variety of weaponry including halberds , maces , long swords , and daggers . All guardsmen wore plate mail .

Activities

The city guard occasionally served as the standing army for the city, but they mainly policed the streets.
