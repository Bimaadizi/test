Shendar Nhalass was the sixth Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Shendar inherited the throne when his father, Darthen I , died in −749 DR . He ruled until his death in −700 DR .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Darthen II .
