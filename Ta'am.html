Ta'am was a small town found near the center of the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara .

Geography

Located approximately 30 miles (48 kilometers) north of Gana along Al-Sabiya River , Ta'am was located at an ideal spot for caravans departing Gana north to Tajar along the western slopes of Al-Yabki Mountains . Ta'am was located along the fringes of the fertile river valley fed by Al-Sabiya River.
