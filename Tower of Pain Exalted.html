The Tower of Pain Exalted was found in Zhentil Keep in 1358 DR . This temple was dedicated to the worship of Loviatar , the Maiden of Pain.

Inhabitants

Ulamyth Quantor served as the High Priest, or High Torturelord, of the temple. He and his 14 fellow priests preached the word of Loviatar to a congregation of 26 believers.

History

The temple was founded in the Year of Pain, 1245 DR during a time when Zhentil Keep was more welcoming to some of the oppressed religions of the south.
