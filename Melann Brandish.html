Melann Brandish was a cleric of Chauntea at the Bounty of the Goddess temple in Archendale in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Melann had long dark hair, tied away from her face. She wore a leather jerkin and a brown cloak for protection while traveling. She was never found without her wooden holy symbol to Chauntea around her neck on an amulet. She had a small, crude mace with a wooden haft and a lead -covered head.

Relationships

Melann had a single sibling, her brother Whitlock Brandish .

History

Circa Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Melann and her brother went on a quest to try and lift a deadly curse that was cast on their family by Chare'en .
