High Scrivener Phylipas Crauntias , who was also nicknamed the Pompous , was the high priest of the House of the Tablets , a temple dedicated to Deneir , in Yhaunn in 1358 DR .

Activities

Phylipas was the leader of Deneir's faithful in Yhaunn. His congregation within the temple consisted of 5 other priests and 4 followers.
