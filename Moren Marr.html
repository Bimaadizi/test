Moren Marr was a Dark Justiciars of Shar and a member of the Gauntlet of Shar in the 10 th century DR .

History

Justiciar Marr was among hundreds of Dark Justiciars and Selûnites who died in a bitter battle between the followers of the two goddesses in the Year of the Dusken Ride, 986 DR . Moren Marr was chopped in two during the battle, and the casualty was recorded in a long log of the dead penned by Malus Thorm of Reithwin .

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Moren's death was long forgotten, being just one of an endless list of the dead, stored in Malus Thorm's House of Healing in the shadow-cursed town of Reithwin.
