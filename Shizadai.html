Shizadai was a samurai retainer of the Michimori clan in Maeshi Province in 1360 DR .

History

Shizadai was ordered, together with his older brother Shizuoka , to lead a company of bushi to assist an adventuring party hired by Michimori Uemon to retrieve the Seven Swords . He may have helped them battle Korimori clan troops led by Sakigahara and Himeji .
