The tunneler's toolkit was a belt of thieves' tools held by Karissa the Knife , Master Thief of the Night Brigade in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The tools in the belt were extremely useful for dealing with locks , traps , and other mechanisms, and gave a passive boost to searching and athletic leaps. It was well-regarded by aspiring tunnel rats and sneaky scamps alike.

History

The tunneler's toolkit was in the possession of the thief Karissa in her lair until she was slain by the vengeful Riverside Rats gang.
