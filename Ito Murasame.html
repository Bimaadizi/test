Ito Murasame was an undead shikki-gaki in Ito-jo in 1360 DR .

History

Ito Murasame was the lady of the castle who died in the castle's burning in 647 DR . She became an undead shikki-gaki. Finally, in 1360 DR, an adventuring party hired by Michimori Uemon to recover the Seven Swords fought and destroyed her.
