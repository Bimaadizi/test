Huld Belabranta was a noble inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He lived within Belabranta Villa located in the Sea Ward at the intersection of the Rough Road and Mendever Street .

Huld was a rarity among the wizards of Waterdeep in that he was one of a small handful of individuals who were not affiliated with the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors .

Relationships

He was the patriarch of the Belabranta noble family of Waterdeep. Huld was killed by a Succubus Fiend of corruption he was having an affair with, and in the moment of his death he was supplanted by a greater doppelganger called Rhelgen . Since some years prior his death, it was noted that his character had somewhat changed, from a cheerful and generous person to a darker, amoral and greedy wizard.
