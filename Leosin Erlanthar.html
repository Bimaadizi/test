Leosin Erlanthar was a monk from Berdusk , the master of Nesim Waladra . and a member of the Harpers who researched the reformed Cult of the Dragon under Severin Silrajin during the late 1480s DR .

History

Leosin noted the unusual actions of the Cult of the Dragon and decided to investigate the matter. In Greenest , he permitted the cultists to capture him in order to examine them more closely.
