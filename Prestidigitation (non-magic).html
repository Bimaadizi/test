Prestidigitation involved a variety of objects in the Realms popular among bards and performers who used them to appear to perform magic tricks.

Description

Items used for prestidigitation performances included marked playing cards, hollow-topped hats, pocket scarves, foldable canes, flowering rods, and retractable knives .

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 3–4 gold pieces .
