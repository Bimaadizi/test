Varimbra was a serving maid at the Eel Revealed in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The serving staff at the Eel Revealed uniformly wore high heels, garters, and short, twirling skirts. They had suns painted on their skin.

Relationships

Varimbra was known by name to Arclath Delcastle .

History

In Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Varimbra was working as a serving maid at the Eel Revealed on the day that the customer and young nobleman Arclath Delcastle was sought by the War Wizard Glathra Barcantle , who was investigating his relationship to two murdered men, Belnar Buckmantle and Halance Tarandar .
