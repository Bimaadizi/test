Herban was a human fighter who lived in Neverwinter during the late 14 th century DR .

History

Herban was a stern and disciplined combat instructor at the Neverwinter Academy who worked tirelessly, educating all students in the art of combat tactics against various monsters . Corporals Dendy and Hewwet assisted him in his teachings. The Hero of Neverwinter was among his students.

Despite his exceptional skills, he tragically perished during the assault on the Academy in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
