Lookout Grush was a goblin warrior who joined the cult of the Absolute of the Absolute in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Grush wore crudely-made plate armor affixed over simple cloth garments.

Activities

After joining the Absolute 's cult, Grush was assigned to be a lookout at the Shattered Sanctum .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Grush began watching over the cult's torturer, a particularly malicious goblin named Spike .
