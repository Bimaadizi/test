Files were tools in the Realms used to cut through metal or wood.

Description

A typical set of files included a variety of shapes and sizes. Iron was a commonly used metal for files, with many having wooden handles that were connected to the metal by some form of glue .

History

Files were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .
