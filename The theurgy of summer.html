The theurgy of summer was a magical ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The ring embodied the blazing power of the sun and summer . It helped the wearer survive all kinds of danger, and gave great boons to the casting of fiery spells.

History

The ring was found in Castle Cormanthor when the Elves of Cormanthor performed an attack on the Red Wizard lich Droic Orgul , who sought to loot the ruined city.
