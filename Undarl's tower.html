Undarl's tower was a tower belonging to the mage Undarl in northwest Faerûn during the early-to-mid 3 rd century DR .

Location

The tower lay along the Delimbiyr River , just a few miles west of Secomber , or Hastarl in ancient times. It was built on top of a rocky outcropping.

Structure

The keep had open balconies both facing outside and inside, over the courtyard, with many major towers having flat roofs and being half-round, with the round side facing outwards. The base of the walls had plinths. The structure had six interior floors and three underground levels. The bottom-most level was too damp to use. All levels but the ground level, broken by the courtyard gate, made complete circuits around the structure.

However, most of the aboveground structure had been reduced to a jutting stone outcropping by the late 14 th century DR , with only a basement laboratory preserved properly, with strong magic.

Inhabitants

Shadow creatures inhabited the surrounding area, due to a leaky portal .

History

The tower belonged to Undarl in its day, but had since become a ruined keep.
