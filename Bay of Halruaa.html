The Bay of Halruaa was a bay located in south central Halruaa along the Great Sea .

Description

The Bay of Halruaa opened up the central portion of southern Halruaa to the Great Sea . It connected with the Halagard Channel to connect Lake Halruaa and all of Halruaa's major rivers and tributaries.
