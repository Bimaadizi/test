Varden was a male human thief living in Scardale during the Time of Troubles .

History

Varden was a successful thief in Scardale Town until in 1358 the god Bane , after the Battle of Shadowdale , ordered the Zhentilar armies to conquer Scardale in order to then threaten Tantras .

Varden was conscripted into the Sembian Resistance , composed of all forces in the dale fighting the Zhent forces. During a mission, they met the heroes Midnight and Adon . Understanding the importance of their mission, Varden helped them in many ways until they reunited with Kelemvor Lyonsbane and boarded a warship to Tantras to fulfill their mission. Varden went with them but when attacked by Bane's assassins on the sea, Varden was killed.
