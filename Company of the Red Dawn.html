They were recruited and sent by Artor Morlin , the Baron of Blood, to kill Noreyth. Noreyth, his servant Dhusarra yr Fadila el Abhuk , and two other Malarites were ambushed on the Storeroom Level of Undermountain . The company killed the two other Malarites, but Noreyth and Dhusarra escaped.
