Boots of false tracks were magical boots whose tracks misrepresented the wearer's race.

Description

The boots could only be worn by humanoids .

Powers

A pair of boots of false tracks could, at the wearer's choice, leave tracks that were characteristic of a different humanoid race, as long as the chosen type of creature was roughly the same size as the wearer.
