Corinth was a resident of Innisfall who joined the Shining Crusade in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .

Description

He was not nearly as devoted to the crusade's cause as some its more zealous soldiers.

Possessions

Corinth was a skilled archer and carried with him his unique longbow that bore his family's crest.

Activities

While serving in the crusade, Corinth was a scout in the Blinding White battalion.

Relationships

He had family that lived in Innisfall and was friends with his fellow crusader Damarr .

History

When the Shining Lady led her crusade along the Sword Coast , the army eventually came by the town of Innisfall. In order to save his family, Corinth warned the people of the town to flee before the soldiers arrived.

When the army arrived at Dragonspear Castle , Corinth was accused of betraying the cause by his fellow crusader Valis . While Corinth denied any wrongdoing, he was put before Officer Leopas for trial.
