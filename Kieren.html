Kieren was a Chosen in the Doomvault dungeon in 1486 DR .

History

Kieren was one of the many Chosen captured by the Red Wizards of Thay and transported to the Doomvault dungeon for experiments. More specifically, he was the chosen of Ilmater . Kieren was freed from his chains by the heroes of Daggerford .
