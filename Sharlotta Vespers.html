Sharlotta Vespers was a former lieutenant in House Basadoni in Calimport .

Later, after Jarlaxle and his mercenary band, Bregan D'aerthe , expanded their operations beyond the Underdark and took over House Basadoni, Sharlotta served as figurehead for the guild.

She was killed by Rai-guy Bondalek , a drow wizard / cleric , who was plotting against Jarlaxle to take over Bregan D'aerthe.
