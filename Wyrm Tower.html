Wyrm Tower was the tower of the archmage Shandrath in the early 3 rd century DR .

Location

The tower lay in Athalantar .

Defenses

The building was defended by the power of its archmage, Shandrath .

History

Shandrath took the tower for his own at some point in his old age. The tower itself was destroyed in the Year of the Ghosthunt, 222 DR , during a combined attack by many of the Magelords of Athalantar .
