Black's Finest was a variety of wine produced in Tantras by the Black Family of Merchants in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , Black's Finest was brought to the year-end celebration festival in the town of Mossbridges in the Vast by Carl Morthinson , a representative of the Black Family of Merchants. Carl trusted in the wine-tasting and grading competition's in which Black's Finest was enrolled to be fair. The wine produced by this noble Tantran family was sold at five silvers per tankard, and only judges were allowed free tasting.
