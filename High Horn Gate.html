High Horn Gate was the western gate into the city of Arabel in Cormyr .

Description

The gate was flanked by two towers. A detachment of Purple Dragons guarded the gate, who could be quite persistent in their questions of visitors.

Location

Mother Lahamma's House and the Night Wolf Inn were just outside High Horn Gate, and it was at the latter where Cyric once stayed as a mortal.
