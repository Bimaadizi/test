Eloise Thurman was an inhabitant of the Brampton neighborhood of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Eloise Thurman attempted to report suspicious housecalls late at night among the neighbors as well as people failing to make offerings to Umberlee , claiming that these activities were proof of the Cult of the Absolute 's activity. Eloise made the reports to Liara Portyr 's garrison, where they were dismissed.
