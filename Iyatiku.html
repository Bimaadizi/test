Iyatiku , who was also called the Corn Mother , was a great spirit of the folklore and myth of the Azuposi people of the Pasocada Basin of northern Maztica .

History

The Corn Mother was called into being by the song of Sus'sistinako , the Spider Woman. As a spirit of creation, Iyatiku helped make all of the flowers, animals, and other life forms prevalent within the Pasocada Basin.
