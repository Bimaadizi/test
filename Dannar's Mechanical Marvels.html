Dannar's Mechanical Marvels was a shop in Neverwinter in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

Dannar's was located next to Jaesor's Fineware Porcelain Works , at the intersection between Hantor's Lane at the Street of Storms in the northwestern end of the Blacklake District .

Description

Dannar's Mechanical Marvels was a shop specializing in really expensive gnomish , Lantanna , and dwarven clockwork devices. Examples were self-striking, wind-up, push-button flint boxes and electrum jewelry boxes inlaid with pearl and displaying animated adornments, like small clockwork dragons that chased their tails around a pop-up vanity mirror . These tended to wow visitors.
