Vials , or phials , were small glass containers that were used to hold liquids and powders. They could be used in laboratories to conduct experiments or to transport these substances.

Description

A typical vial could hold up to 4 fluid ounces (120 cubic centimeters) of liquid. Outside of the laboratory, vials were most commonly used to store and transport acids , antitoxins , perfumes , poisons , and potions .

Availability

A variety of vials were available for sale through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue , along with matching corks and other laboratory glass containers produced in Urmlaspyr by the glass-blowers guild .
