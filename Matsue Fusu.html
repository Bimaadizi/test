Matsue Fusu was a samurai retainer of the Niwa clan and overseer of Gomen in Kozakura in 1357 DR .

Activities

In Gomen, the largest fishing village of the Tara-gawa Basin area, he led a company of five samurai and fifteen bushi . Fusu oversaw a strategic area, bordered by a shoen of the Hori clan and one of the Tsu family .
