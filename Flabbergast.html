Flabbergast was a wizard who served Acquisitions Incorporated as a mojordomo in the late 15 th century DR .

Relations

He was rarely seen without his familiar , Mister Snibbly .

History

Little was known of Flabbergast's history, although his family was said to come from Neverwinter , and that they had helped to erect the Dolphin Bridge there.

At some point in his life, Flabbergast had worked for the rival company Dran Enterprises , and their boss Portentia Dran .

He later left to serve Acquisitions Inc. instead, and was there by 1496 DR .
