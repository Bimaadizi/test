The Respected Mamluk Society , also known as the Respected , was a military organization in Zakhara .

Activities

This large mamluk organization had a long and proud history of serving Muluk , the City of Kings. They were bitter rivals with some of the mamluk organizations from Qudra , including the Dutiful Mamluk Society .

Organization

The Respected were led by General Oman Abd al-Hazim

Base of Operations

The Respected operated out of Muluk in north Zakhara. They had a presence in all of the Free Cities of Zakhara .
