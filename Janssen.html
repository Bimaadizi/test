Janssen was a retired bard inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Though not quite a sage , Janssen was a valuable source of information regarding anything about the High Forest region. He could usually be found at the Lady Luck Tavern , raising his drink and boisterously starting a new round of song.

He lived in a house in the south section of Daggerford on Wall Street near the barracks.
