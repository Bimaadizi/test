The Dragon's Rest was a barracks in the city of Arabel that was owned by the king and used as a guesthouse for official visitors, instead of having them housed in the palace .

Location

The Dragon's Rest was located in the north of the city, across from the estate of House Marliir and the Lady's House , the local temple to Tymora .
