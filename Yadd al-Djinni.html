Yadd al-Djinni , or the Hand of the Djinni , were rocky jungle islands of the Djinni's Claws southwest of mainland Zakhara .

Geography

The islands of Yadd al-Djinni were very slender and covered with dense jungle.

Inhabitants

Civilized people stayed far away from these islands since they were inhabited by a variety of monsters and undead , including the rare su-monster .

Rumors & Legends

Some speculated that the undead that could be found on this island were offshoots of the undead that inhabited the island of Kaff .
