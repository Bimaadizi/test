Windswept armor was an ancient dwarven set of enchanted heavy armor.

Powers

Windswept armor was a set of perforated plate mail armor with a magical +2 enchantment that improved to +4 when confronting gargoyles , commonly found in the ruins of Myth Drannor .

Like other sets of heavy armor, windswept armor , when worn by an arcane spellcaster, resulted in a 40% chance of spell casting failure.

History

In 1369 DR , an ancient set of dwarven windswept armor could be found on the Halls of Light level of Dwarven Dungeons of Myth Drannor, in possession of a group of Lizardfolk .
