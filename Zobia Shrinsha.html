Zobia Shrinsha was a tavern owner who inhabited the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

She was a shy and quiet woman whose demeanor reminded her patrons of a little girl. Her passive nature actually concealed her true terrifying nature as Zobia was a skilled wizard armed with magic items .

Activities

She owned A Maiden's Tears that was located on a small unnamed alley that branched south off of Lion Street in the northeastern section of Waterdeep's North Ward .

Relationships

Zobia maintained close relationships both with the City Watch and the Lords of Waterdeep .
