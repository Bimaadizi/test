Shammath's Pool , or Slammath's Pool , was a pool in Myth Drannor .

History

The pool dried around the Year of the Smirking Knaves, 160 DR . In the Year of Soaring Stars, 261 DR , the mythal of Myth Drannor was laid, reaching as far south as Shammath's Pool.
