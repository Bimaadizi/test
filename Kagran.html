Kagran was a member of the cult of the Absolute who lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR . Along with the elf Narvass , Kagran was one of the few non- goblin members that dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum .

Abilities

Kagran was capable of blessing his allies with a boon from his deity.

Possessions

He wielded a mace in battle.
