Stilard was a Red Wizard of Thay , apprenticed to Azmyrandyr in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

He was gaunt, with a scarred face.

Personality

Stilard was somewhat perverse and the most disloyal of his fellow apprentices.

Relationships

He was apprenticed to Azmyrandyr , and served by one doppleganger .
