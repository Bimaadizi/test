Brian's Street was a minor avenue of hard-packed earth and gravel in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep .

Etymology

This street was named for Brian the Swordmaster , one of the Masked Lords of Waterdeep.

Geography

This street ran from west to east. At its western end, Brian's Street ended at the Way of the Dragon , while at its eastern end the street terminated at the High Road .

The street was met at the south by Mouse Alley , which connected it to Blackcloak Alley .
