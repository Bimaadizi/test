Ogrum's Siegebow was a unique and powerful enchanted heavy crossbow once wielded by one- orc army Slogg Ogrum .

Powers

Despite Ogrum's Siegebow being slightly harder to aim with, missiles that left the weapon packed a mighty wallop, similar to that of a powerful +6 enchantment. Additionally, the crossbow generated an infinite supply of bolts of fire .

History

Ogrum's Siegebow was a mystery, and its origins were unknown. The first time the weapon appeared was in the Year of the Sword, 1365 DR , when Slogg Ogrum singlehandedly besieged the town of Priapurl in the Eastern Heartlands . When the orc was defeated with the help of the Mindgulph Mercenary Company , Ogrum's vast arsenal of weapons was uncovered, however, no one really uncovered the reasons for the orc's siege of the town.

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Ogrum's Siegebow was sold in Westgate , among countless unique and rare weapons and armor on the city's Market Triangle .
