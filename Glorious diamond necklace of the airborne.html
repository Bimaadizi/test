The glorious diamond necklace of the airborne was an enchanted amulet from Kir Sabal of Chult in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The necklace of the airborne was a piece of diamond -encrusted jewelry.

Powers

The enchantments of this necklace made the wearer light as air and strong as wind. Additionally, the amulet enhanced the wearers' arcane magics.
