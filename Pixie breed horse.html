Pixie breed horses were a cross-breed of fey and equine from the Blade Kingdoms of the Vilhon Reach .

Description

Pixie horses were a quick and nervous breed, easily distinguished from other horses by their two feathery antennae that grew up from their brows.

History

Along with the huge metallic Gens D'Or , pixie horses were breeds only seen in the city-states of the Blade Kingdoms. Circa the Year of the Falling Moon, 1217 DR , these creatures were used by heralds of the city-state of Sumbria . One such herald was sent to negotiate peace following the disastrous battle in the Valley of Umbricci in the early fall.
