Aziz-Tagem was a crime lord on the isle of Bawa in 1357 DR .

Activities

Aziz-Tagem, a man of mixed Bavanese and Bawani ancestry, ruled over the still small yakuza of Bawa. He was a master organizer and a very prudent leader, hoping to expand his network without interference from the authorities. He usually directed the bold yakuza to explore the wilderness.
