General Matick was a legendary figure of the Netherese Empire , who invented many of the most popular spells used throughout the Realms . His known spells were: General Matick's missile , General Matick's shield , and General Matick's armor . He was also a rare arcanist of the martial arts and the arcane, like the later rebel Adrian Freeman .

Description

The general was not only a skilled arcanist and inventor , but also a great warrior . Unlike most mages of the era, Matick was both especially strong and robust.

Personality

General Matick was said to be relentless and aggressive, particularly towards elves , whom he disdained.

History

Matick was born in 2238 NY (−1621 DR) of the Golden Age of Netheril . He quickly worked his way up the ranks in the army of Seventon and repelled several raids of goblinoids . An extremely successful commander, General Matick had the lowest ally-to-enemy troop loss ratio in the entire history of Netheril. As well as eliminating over 35,000 goblins, Matick was said to have killed over 25,000 orcs , 5,000 halflings and elves, and 3,000 trolls . The general invented the well-known spells, the 1st-level magic missile , shield , and mage armor . At the Pass of Humaithira , General Matick died in a last stand against a horde of trolls in 2298 NY (−1561 DR) .

Matick officially introduced magic missile in 2268 NY (−1591 DR) at age 30, shield in 2279 NY (−1580 DR) at age 41, and magic armor in 2284 NY (−1575 DR) at age 46.

Rumors & Legends

Some sages did not believe that Matick had invented his wildly popular spells, but instead had them commissioned.
