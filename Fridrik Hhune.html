Fridrik Hhune was a member of the Hhune family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Fridrik Hhune perished the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate. Fridrik's tombstone was commissioned to be carved by Candulhallow's Tombstones in the Lower City . His freshly-carved grave marker read: "Hid last connection with this realm, severed."
