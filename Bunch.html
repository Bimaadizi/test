Bunch was a berbalang who served as an assistant to Vargo , the skull lord who controlled Cube 1717 .

Description

Unlike most of the inhabitants of Cube 1717, Bunch was unaffected by the pixelating curse , due to its special extraplanar make-up.

Personality

Bunch had a tendency to flee when confronted.

Abilities

As a berbalang, Bunch could conjure a spectral duplicate of itself. It used its spectral duplicate while hiding its true body.

Possessions

Bunch had a large collection of bones, most of which had been pixelated by the pixelating curse. In its pile of bones, Bunch also owned a ring of free action .

Activities

Bunch spent its time collecting bones on the surface of Cube 1717, and counting them in its lair inside the cube. It used speak with dead to interrogate the skulls, then wrote its notes on the other bones. For Vargo, Bunch operated a reception room inside Cube 1717, where it pretended to induct visitors as new arrivals into an afterlife in Acheron . This was a trick, and Bunch actually used this to test each visitor's intelligence.

Relationships

Bunch worked for Vargo, the leader of the creatures affected by the pixelating curse. Vargo sent it to monitor any visitors to Cube 1717, to assess their intelligence, and to send them to it.

History

Bunch arrived at Cube 1717, seeking to collect bones from its dead. In order to access the bones, it willingly agreed to serve Vargo.
