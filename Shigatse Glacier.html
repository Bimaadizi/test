The Shigatse Glacier was a monstrous glacier that sat just south of Ra-Khati in the Katakoro Shan mountain range.

Geography

The glacier measured 90 mi (140 km) in length and was 30 mi (48 km) at its widest point. The Shigatse's yearly runoff fed the Muren Tso on the Katakoro Plateau to the east.
