The Scab Eaters were a tribe of goblins that inhabited the Orsraun Mountains in the mid–14 th century DR .

Possessions

Their members typically wielded spears and rode upon the backs of dire wolves .

History

In 1358 DR , the Scab Eaters were one of three goblinoid tribes—the other two being the hobgoblin tribes the Back Crackers and Spine Snappers —that were rallied together by chief No-Nose of the Bloodier Teeth tribe to fight in the Battle of Urml , an assault on a human village of the same name .
