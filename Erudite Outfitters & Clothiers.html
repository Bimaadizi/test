Erudite Outfitters & Clothiers was a modest clothier and tailor shop that did business with the Seekers and the Avowed of Candlekeep , the library-fortress on the Sword Coast .

Location

Erudite Outfitters & Clothiers stood in the Court of Air of Candlekeep, across the courtyard from the House of the Binder , next to the Emerald Door that separated the fortress from its courtyard.

Services

Feldmar Bisset III , the shopkeeper in the late 15 th century DR , catered to the Seekers and the Avowed who sought to purchase new scholarly robes or patch and mend their old garb.

In addition to the tailoring service, Feldmar gave classes on simple stitching and patchwork to reduce the amount of simple work that the shop got from the monks.

Lastly, Feldmar's hobby was raising silkworms in the shop's atrium. He harvested silk from the worms to weave his own precious cloth.

History

By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Feldmar Bisset III, a Gondar acolyte, and his family had carved a place for themselves amongst the Avowed, making garments on request.
