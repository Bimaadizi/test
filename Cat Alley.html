Cat Alley was an alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Cat Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . The alley originated on the Street of Bells where it branched west to pass through a small neighborhood east of the High Road . A smaller alley called the Cat's Tail branched off the south end of Cat Alley.
