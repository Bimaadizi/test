King Dynnegall was the ruler of the Kingdom of Moray on the island of Moray in the Moonshae Isles during the early-to-midâ€“14 th century DR . He was the last of his line.

History

Dynnegall assumed the throne of Moray in the Year of the Snow Winds, 1335 DR , after the death of his father in battle against Northmen at Trondhelm .

In the Year of the Bloodbird, 1346 DR , King Dynnegall and his heirs were all slain by assassins led by Razfallow on the orders of Cyndre . His daughter survived just long enough to describe the assassins, whose attack went off-script when they were forced to kill dozens of guards while hunting down an infant member of the family who had been hidden in the attic by a nursemaid.

The village of Dynnegall was named in his honor.
