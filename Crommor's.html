Crommor's was an inn and tavern found in the city of Saerloon in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Location

Crommor's was located near Saerloon's docks.

Activities

This establishment tended to attract unsavory folk. Though the accommodations were fair and the prices were cheap, brawls were a frequent occurrence here.
