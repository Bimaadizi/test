Mrs. Barnabas Hinkel was an inhabitant of the town of Ghars in Cormyr in the mid–14 th century DR .

History

Mrs. Hinkel borrowed a book on local history, including that of the bandit chieftain Fastred , from the Ghars library in the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR .

She died the following year, in the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR .

Seven years later, in 1367 DR , Jasper found as part of his investigation that her withdrawal had been the most recent of the books discussing Fastred.
