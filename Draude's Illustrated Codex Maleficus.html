Draude's Illustrated Codex Maleficus was an evil and forbidden book from the old days of Myth Drannor .

Description

Like other forbidden tomes from Myth Drannor, Draude's Illustrated Codex Maleficus was a heavy tome bound in iron . It was wrapped in fabric and locked with iron chains . When opened, the tome was filled with disgusting and dark illustrations.

Powers

This book was imbued with vile and evil enchantments . This magic allowed the owner of the book to cast the repulsion spell four times a day and increased their reaction in battle. However this power came at a price as the Codex Maleficus drained constitution from its wielder as long as it remained in their possession.

History

The Illustrated Codex Maleficus was locked away inside a long-abandoned wizard 's laboratory in the First Cellar of the Elven Catacombs underneath Castle Cormanthor . The book was bound and chained to be destroyed but with the advent of the Weeping War , the evil tome was left behind in the ruined city. In 1369 DR , the book still lay within the hidden lab.
