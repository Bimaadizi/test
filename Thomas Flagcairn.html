Thomas Flagcairn was the Mayor of the city of Sapra on Ilighôn , off the coast of the Vilhon Reach in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Thomas was a humble blacksmith of Sapra. As of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , he was also elected the Mayor of the town. He ruled alongside the six members of the council of elders. Thomas's mayoral position was a part-time vocation, and he continues honing his craft while meeting the council once a month unless a crisis required an emergency gathering.
