Shortsword of First Blood was an enchanted shortsword found in the Underdark circa 1492 DR .

Description

Shortsword of First Blood was a golden masterfully-crafted weapon with a superbly keen blade.

Powers

This sword held the enchantment known as breaking the unbroken . It made the sword's attacks, aimed at creatures previously unscathed, more powerful.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , shortsword of First Blood was in possession a dead duergar, part of the raiding squad who massacred deep gnome fluorite miners and attacked a peaceful myconid colony in the vicinity of the Moonrise Towers in the part of Underdark underneath the Western Heartlands .
