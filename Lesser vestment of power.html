A lesser vestment of power was a magic item that provided a number of useful magical abilities to the wearer.

Description

These items usually appeared in the form of a set of robes .

Powers

Foremost, simply wearing a set of lesser vestments of power made the wearer more difficult to harm with weapons and more likely to resist the negative effects of spells , poison, etc. The robes also continuously granted the wearer the benefits of protection from normal missiles , feather fall , and a ring of fire resistance .
