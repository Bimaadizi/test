Mifru was a high-ranking member of the Cult of the Howling Hatred at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose .

History

In 1491 DR , Mifru was the right-hand of Stormgale in their second plan to access the elemental node under the Monastery of the Yellow Rose . They kidnapped some monks, planning to use them as a sacrifice to open a gate to the Elemental Plane of Air .

At last, however, an adventuring party hired by Grandmaster Perrywinkle Shin to find the missing monks ruined their plan and Mifru was defeated.
