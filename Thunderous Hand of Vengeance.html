The Thunderous Hand of Vengeance was a temple dedicated to Hoar , the Doombringer.

Location

The temple was located in the city of Akanax in Chessenta .

Activities

The Thunderous Hand of Vengeance was one of two temples vying for the highest position of power within Hoar's clergy. The priests from the temple were stricly loyal to the king of Akanax. They ran both the city watch and the courts with an iron fist. Some of the higher ranking priests also held positions of power within Akanax's army.
