Acid-etched hand crossbows were enchanted crossbows popularized in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

These magic hand crossbows coated all bolts shot out of it with corrosive acid . Additionally, these weapons possessed +2 or +3 enchantments to the damage and magics that guided the missiles towards their targets.

History

In the 15 th century DR, acid-etched hand crossbows were in use in the city of Neverwinter , sold at the Mercenary Arms Depot , and found among savage inhabitants of the Old Owl Well during its occupation by orcish bandit bands.
