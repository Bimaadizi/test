Glorious dawn was a magical ring blessed with Amaunator 's love found in Cormyr in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The ring was a simple golden band that shone brightly in the light.

Powers

Glorious dawn protected the wearer from entropic negative energy , and could prevent them from being slain instantly by especially powerful effects. It further protected the wearer by flashing a bright, burning light at attackers.

History

The ring was awarded to brave adventurers who had gone into Wheloon Prison in aid of Fordwin and his team, overcoming the Sharran priests Stanwicke Gall and Tulla Chymbal in the process.
