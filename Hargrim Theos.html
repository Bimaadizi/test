Hargrim Theos was a Doommaster of Beshaba who headed the Temple of Beshaba in Bezantur , Thay during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He was a wicked man who found joy in inflicting misfortune on others.

Activities

Hargrim was an efficient leader. As the highest ranking member of Beshaba's clergy at the Bezantur temple, Hargrim was perhaps the only one privy to all of the labyrinthine temple's secrets.
