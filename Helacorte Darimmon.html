Talatha Vaerovree was a spellslayer and a short-lived Magister in 1385 DR . She was the last Magister until the Second Sundering .

Activities

She was an accomplished spellslayer.

History

Helacorte accepted the mantle of the Magister from a manifestation of Azuth in the Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR , shortly before the onset of the Spellplague . She spent less than a tenday as the Magister; she went insane upon the death of Mystra , and then her head exploded.

Rumors

Per Elminster , Helacorte was offered the mantle by an Azuth who knew Mystra 's death was imminent, so that Helacorte's activities would cease.
