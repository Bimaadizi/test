Tezambril's Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Tezambril's Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . The road originated in the north on Robin's Way where it branched south to eventually end in a neighborhood in front of Anchoret's Court . Tezambril's Street also connected with the Forcebar , Tulmaster's Street , and Rednose Alley .

Notable Locations

New Moon Swords was located along the east side of Tezambril's Street near Rednose Alley.
