Jaccob was a halfling smuggler and thief turned tourist guide in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Jaccob was a 3 feet and 6 inches (1.1 meters) tall hin who weighed 67 pounds (30 kilograms). He had brown hair and hazel eyes.

Abilities

The halfling used clubs , daggers , and slings in battle. He was proficient in appraisal, rope use, and could write and read in Common .

Possessions

Jaccob's possessions included lock picks , charcoal , aniseed , a hand lamp , hollow boots , a false scabbard , a mouse cart , three pet mice , and an enchanted longtooth dagger.

History

Jaccob made a name for himself working as a smuggler to and from the northern city of Luskan . His career ended in earning a large bounty on his head, forcing the halfling to flee north to the frigid Ten Towns. Some claimed he had been the second halfling to ever visit the Ten Towns. Jaccob found a fleeting temporary home while apprenticing under Henry Coldwater as a tourist guide at the Breakneck Resort . Henry saw potential and initiative in the newcomer. However, Breakneck Resort proved its name to the amateur skier . Only the most proficient skiers and orcs returned from the slopes unharmed. A short time later, Henry Coldwater hosted a eulogy for the dearly departed Jaccob the halfling.
