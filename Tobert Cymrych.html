Tobert Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid-to-late 7 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High King Warren II . His successor was his own first son, Ernest Cymrych .

History

Tobert assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Nomad, 674 DR , following the death of his father.

He ruled for five years before being slain in battle against firbolgs in the Year of the Scarlet Sash, 679 DR .
