Dathguld Guldar was a noble lord of Westgate in the early and mid-14 th century DR .

Relationships

He was the head of the Guldar trading family.

He was strongly believed to be the father of Torm , one of the Knights of Myth Drannor , bore to him by a local prostitute , Netha . Torm confirmed this himself to Elminster .

He had another illegitimate son, Oswald Guldar . They had no loyalty to each other.
