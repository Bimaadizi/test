Lady Althea Mecatoris was the matriarch of House Mecatoris and one of the merchant lords of Sambral , one of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond , as of 1479 DR .

Description

Althea was a tall, thin human female with steel-colored hair.

Relationships

Althea was the mother of Davien Mecatoris .

History

In 1479 DR, Althea was one of the merchant lords, the rulers of Sambral.

That same year, Althea helped a band of adventurers to destroy a cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame that operated in Sambral.

In 1480 DR , Althea allowed the adventurers to use one of her merchant ships to sail to Imdolphyn when the true identity of Pyrultimus was discovered, in order to put an end to the cult for good.
