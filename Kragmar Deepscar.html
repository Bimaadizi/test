Kragmar Deepscar was an orc warband leader of the kingdom of Many-Arrows in the late 15 th century DR . He served under commander Vansi Bloodscar in their occupation of the River District of Neverwinter in the late 1470s DR.

Possessions

Kragmar wore a personalized Belt of the Many Arrows . This girdle granted him limited regeneration abilities in addition to the powers of a regular Belt of the Many Arrows .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Kragmar and fellow warband leaders Drogthu Elfeater and Lurg Bloodspear were in charge of coordinating attacks against the Neverwinter Guard . Finally, Scout Gaelen of the Guard sent an adventurer to infiltrate their barracks through a secret passage and eliminate all three of them.
