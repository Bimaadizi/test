Hawkhill was one of the " border baronies " of Amn , situated in the foothills of the Troll Mountains . It served as the headquarters for the Baron's Blades , the personal bodyguards of baron Uldonner Erendrin .

Description

Just north of this location was the barony of Vaeranth .
