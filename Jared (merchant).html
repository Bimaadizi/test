Jared was a merchant from the Sword Coast , active circa 1368 DR .

Personality

Jared had an uppity and abrasive personality.

Possessions

The merchant owned a pair of boots of the north .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Jared somehow ended up on a river crossing the west from the town of Nashkel , pursued by a hungry polar bear . The merchant was desperate to find aid against the beast and offered gold and his pair of boots of the north as the reward.
