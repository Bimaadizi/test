The Swilissaire Slicers was a subgroup of the Swilissaire Swipers of the pirate nation of Swil in the Whamite Isles . The Slicers served Swil in a series of screwy scraps between several states and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
