Vrakk was a Zhentilar general, and noted war hero in Zhentil Keep . He was bodyguard and co-conspirator with Fzoul Chembryl in the creation of The True Life of Cyric in 1368 DR

History

In 1360 Vrakk led the orc army that the Zhent send to joined the allied armies led by Azoun Obarskyr IV in fighting against the Grand Army of the Tuigan in Thesk . In 1370 DR , Vrakk was the second-in-command of the Zhentarim army in Zhentil.
