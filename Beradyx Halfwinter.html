Beradyx Halfwinter was a human wizard and transportation mage for the Aurora's Realms Shop, Slut Street Catalogue Counter in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Called a "fat and lazy lackspell wizard" by Elminster and other mages, who were contemptuous of his lack of power in the Art , Beradyx preferred a life of "spellhurling for hire" in the safety and comfort of a city. He cast spells only for legal and non-combat purposes. In particular, he used the teleport spell to transport goods to the Aurora's Realms Shop.

Personality

He loved fine wines and ales and cheese , which showed in his waistline.

History

When he visited the shop in the mid-1360s DR, Volothamp Geddarm was unable to learn Beradyx's identity.
