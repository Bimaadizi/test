The Broken Harp was a tavern in the Amnian city of Esmeltaran during the 14 th century DR .

Location

The tavern was built right on the western edge of Esmeltaran next to Lake Esmel .

Services

The Broken Harp provided relatively poor service for its middling prices. It was a popular meeting spot for anyone who considered themselves an enemy of the Harpers .
