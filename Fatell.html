Fatell was a member of the Cult of the Eternal Flame in Mulmaster .

History

Fatell was a lumber merchant and a member of the Brotherhood of the Cloak and also a fire cultist in Mulmaster. In 1491 DR , a fire destroyed his firewood shop. He and a few fellow surviving fire cultists were investigating the ruins of the shop when they were surprised by some adventurers hired by Cora Peytir , who uncovered and defeated Fatell. Lieutenant Grady Moll of the Soldiery , an agent of the Lords' Alliance , was also investigating him.

Relationship

Fatell, as a lumber merchant, had dealings with Cora Peytir.

Personality

Fatell was very cunning and had a very good knowledge about the inner workings of Mulmaster's political structure.
