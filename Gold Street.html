Gold Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Located in the Inner Quarter in central Zhentil Keep, Gold Street branched west from Raven Street near the center of the quarter and passed northeast to terminate at the wall of the Common Quarter . Gold Street crossed Flostren's Memory and the Dark Road .

Notable Locations

The residences of Lord Ssorg and Magistrate Osconivon were located on Gold Street. The High Hall and Detention Center sat on the eastern end of Gold Street. The Zhentilar maintaned a barracks and armory on Gold Street.
