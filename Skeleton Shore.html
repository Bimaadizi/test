The Skeleton Shore was a coast line along the Dragonmere in the Dragon Coast .

Geography

The shore ran from Teziir in the west all the way to Westgate .

Geographical Features

The most notable feature of the Skeleton Shore were the Cliffs of Karthaut, atop which sat the Karthaut Lighthouse . This seemingly innocuous tower was the domain of Aeroth , a Wearer of Purple for the Cult of the Dragon , and the lair of the green wyrmling Eldrisithain .
