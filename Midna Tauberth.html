Midna Tauberth was a priestess of Shar and an adventurer in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR .

History

Midna was once an adventuring priestess of Shar and a member of the Fine Fellows , an adventuring company from Daggerford .

In 1492 DR , Midna and her fellows descended into Undermountain. There, in the Dungeon Level after Kelim became lost, Halleth Garke accused Midna and her two remaining companions of withholding treasures from him, so Midna and the other two beat Halleth to death and threw his body into a pit. However, Halleth rose as a revenant in order to have vengeance. Unfortunately, he was unable to get out of the pit without help. If Halleth managed to have his vengeance, he finally died for good.

Meanwhile, Midna decided to settle for a time inside the Arcane Chambers in a ruined laboratory where she managed to control the nine unseen servants living there.

Personality

Midna searched a diplomatic solution only if she feared for her life.

Relationship

The unseen servants obeyed Midna because they mistook her for their creator, a failed apprentice of Halaster .
