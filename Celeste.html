Celeste was a cleric of Selûne living in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

History

In 1479 DR , Celeste took part in the Siege of Neverwinter . She rescued Xuna from being killed by an undead horde outside of the city walls before she herself was rescued by a counter-attack led by Sergeant Knox . Together, the three of them defeated a powerful undead warrior before meeting Valindra's army at the head of a force of Neverwinter's defenders.
