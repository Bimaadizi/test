Shadow dragon scale was a variety of armor made from the scales of a shadow dragon .

Description

The scales of the armor linked together in a manner that offered practical protection and graceful aesthetics.

Powers

This armor offered the wearer significant protection from acid .

History

The dwarven smith Cromwell of Athkatla was capable of forging shadow dragon scale armor.
