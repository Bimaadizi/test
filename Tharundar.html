Tharundar was a Red Wizard of Thay in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was thin, sharp-looking, and had a long beard.

Relationships

Fellow Red Wizard Harnrim Starangh answered directly to him. Tharundar actively encouraged Harnrim calling on his wisdom, and told him it was a good thing.

Abilities

Harnrim considered Tharundar his superior in wisdom and power.

History

After the War Wizards raided the Rightful Conspiracy 's meeting in Marsember in the early spring of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Tharundar was consulted by Harnrim Starangh prior to a meeting with the Lady Joysil Ambrur of the same city, inquiring about any intelligence collected on that woman.
