Ahlmer's General Store was a shop in the city of Procampur in the Vast in the late 14 th century DR . It was owned and run by Ahlmer .

Location

It stood in the Port District .

Services

The prices were low and among its goods were fresh-baked biscuits for which the store was famed.

In Alturiak in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , a strange well-dressed gentleman found wandering the streets of Procampur identified himself as "Lord Sellwin Wagglebottom , Deputy Assistant Commander in charge of Perishable Supply Requisition for the Armies of Ravens Bluff " and asked for directions to Ahlmer's General Store.
