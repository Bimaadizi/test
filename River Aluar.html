The River Aluar was a small waterway found in northern Halruaa .

Geography

The river originated around Mount Alue in the Nathaghal Mountains of northern Halruaa. It flowed south, meeting the High Aluar near the towns of Eru and Orhamphal , before emptying into Lake Halruaa several hundred miles to the south.
