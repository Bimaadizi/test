Lamia Crytrapper was the head of the Crytrapper family, a major Amnian family, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Lady Crytrapper wielded great influence in Crimmor , as she knew of secrets hidden by many important people. She invested in small businesses, focusing on those run by women , and more particularly those created by widows. She was also known to regularly sponsor minstrels .

Personality

Lamia was a kind and cheerful old lady who loved the countryside. Despite having her main residence in Athkatla , she spent most of her time in Crimmor.
