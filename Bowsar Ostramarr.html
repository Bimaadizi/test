Bowsar Ostramarr was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He liked to spend time at The Downed Falcon , a tavern.

History

One of the Waterdhavian thief Narnra Shalace 's first assignments as a spy for the Royal Magician of Cormyr , Caladnei was to eavesdrop Bowsar Ostramarr, among others. Bowsar was not numbered among the women she was tasked with investigating, strongly implying he was a man.
