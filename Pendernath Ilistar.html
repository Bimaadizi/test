Pendernath Ilistar was the ninth King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Pendernath's reign began when his father, Farnath IV , died in the Year of the Leaping Lion, 834 DR . He would rule until his death from plague in the Year of the Black Book, 849 DR .
