Ito was a Flaming Fist soldier who served the city of Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

He did not think highly of Wyll , the adventurer known as Blade of Frontiers. Ito believe Wyll was not trustworthy and no real hero.

History

Ito was among the Flaming Fist soldiers stationed at or nearby the Waukeen's Rest when it was raided by the cult of the Absolute in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . He along with other soldiers, including Gauntlet Yeva , gave their best efforts to rescue Councilor Florrick from the inn after it was set ablaze.
