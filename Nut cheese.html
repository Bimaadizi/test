Nut cheese was a type of cheese mixed with nuts produced in many cities of the Savage Frontier , including Silverymoon , Mirabar , and Sundabar . A selection was available from Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .

Description

Each Northern city produced its own variety of nut cheese. If ordered from Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue without specifying a desired origin, a customer would receive an assorted lot.

Nut cheeses were hard and rich, and mixed with ground hickory nuts, walnuts , and chestnuts . Once mixed with the nuts, the cheeses would be left to ripen.
