Abilities

Peter was trained in handling swords of all sizes and had access to several divine spells, such as rigid thinking , hold person , spiritual hammer , and entangle .

Possessions

The adventurer was protected by a suit of chainmail , a horned helmet , a medium shield , and was armed with an enchanted +1 morning star . He also carried a bottle of oil of fiery burning and a vial of potion of explosions .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Peter's adventuring party, led by Kirian visited the area east of Beregost known as Mutamin's Garden . There, Peter, Kirian, Lindin , and Baerin antagonized travelers, fellow adventurers, and "rustic locals," provoking them into combat.
