Storntil was a Hastarl thief and a member of the Moonclaws gang in the early 3 rd century DR .

Description

He was older and heavier than fellow thief Farl .

Personality

He took things he did seriously.

Possessions

He was known to use poison on his knives in a fight.

Relationships

As a member of the Moonclaws , Storntil was under the command of Isparla . Farl believed Isparla was his mistress; whether it was true or not, she thought fondly of him.

History

After the ambush that the Velvet Hands put for the Moonclaws on the gates of Athalgard in response for their attack on them, the Hands escaped only to somehow end up crashing through a roof into a room surrounded by Moonclaws. One of them was Storntil, who tried to square off with Farl; though he had the upper hand in combat, Storntil was slain after another of the Hands, Tassabra , managed to sneak in a strike into his ribs despite being busy fighting for her own life. Storntil was soon after stabbed through the eye by Farl.
