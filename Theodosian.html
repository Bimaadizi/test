Brother Theodosian was a Helmite cleric who lived in Helm's Hold during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Unlike so many of his fallen brethren, Theodosian survived the initial occupation and attempted reclamation of Helm's Hold circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR . He accompanied Captain Dunfield to the Last Redoubt war camp formed by the Neverwinter Guard and assisted in the efforts to retake the city . He served as a healer at the camp, and aided in the efforts to bless the remains of his allies who fell on the battlefield.
