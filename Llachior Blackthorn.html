Llachior Blackthorn was the Duke of the Duchy of Cape Velen , and a prominent figure in the court of Tethyr in the midâ€“14 th century DR . He was also appointed to the position of lord royal durbland, which controlled the trade and money of Tethyr.

Description

A burly and boisterous man, the duke was missing two fingers from his left hand and walked with a limp after taking serious wounds in a fight with Prince Yusuf Jhannivvar in the Storm Siege of Myratma .

Personality

He would have preferred adventuring to the reconstruction and planning of Tethyr's economy. He was also described as loud yet kindhearted by those that respected him. His less-friendly peers thought him weak and scatterbrained.

Activities

Along with his duties as duke and as the royal durbland, Duke Blackthorn was known to personally train the guards of new mints that were started under his reign as the durbland.

Relationships

He had a close relationship with the count of Firedrake , Ondul Jarduth

He was married to the Lawantha Silendia as the two recuperated from wounds sustained during the First Siege of Myratma.
