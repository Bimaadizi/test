Dale cheese was a variety of cheese produced in the Great Dale in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Dale cheese was a hard cheese, filled with chopped up olives .

History

Dale cheese was popular among the merchants and traders on the Sword Coast , all along the Trade Way , and as far north as the city of Luskan .
