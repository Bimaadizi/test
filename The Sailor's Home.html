The Sailor's Home was a modest inn in Velprintalar , Aglarond , in 1368 DR , kept by Mharus .

Its patrons were seafarers and the less wealthy merchants. A room was two silver pieces per night, while a supper of fish, beef, or poultry was one copper piece .
