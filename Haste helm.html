A haste helm was a variety of magical leather helm that incited a sense of urgency within the wearer.

Description

The leather helmet resembed others of its kind, but was decorated with three braids that extended outward from its crown.

Powers

A haste helm granted the wearer increased alacrity, along with the urge to take quick action whenever danger was present.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , one haste helm could be found within a locked chest in the ruined village of Moonhaven .
