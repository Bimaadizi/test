Borgo the Scrounger was a quartermaster officer in Droic Orgul forces when he attacked Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Borgo had pallid skin and a shaven head with extensive tattoos . He wore a suit of black plate armor and carried a pair of flaming curved blades.

History

Borgo served the lich Droic in his headquarters at Castle Cormanthor during their raid on Myth Drannor. He may have fallen in battle defending the base when the Elves of Cormanthor attacked the Red Wizards , destroying Droic and his phylactery .
