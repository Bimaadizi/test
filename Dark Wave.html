The Dark Wave was a small group of holy slayers that were operating out of Hawa in 1367 DR .

Activities

The Dark Wave served the Cult of Istishia , against their will. They carried out assassinations when ordered.

Base of Operations

Hawa, on the island of the Genie's Turban within the Corsair Domains , served as the base for the Dark Wave.

Relationships

All members of the Dark Wave were under the influence of a ghul-kin named Faizah and her Waters of devotion .

Members

The Dark Wave only had three members: Setara bint Aasim , Shadha , and Sahlah .
