The Sugarcane was a seedy tavern in the city of Lheshayl , Shining Plains , circa 1370 DR .

Description

The Sugarcane was located near the poor area of Lheshayl.

History

At some point before the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Adamar Session , Bard of Starmantle , visited the Sugarcane during his twelve-year adventuring trek across the Vilhon Reach . The establishment received a notable notion in the guide published by Adamar.
