Cordio Muffinhead was a dwarf priest from Mithral Hall in the late 14 th century DR .

History

He treated King Bruenor Battlehammer when he was struck by magic and put into a coma in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .

The reincarnated Regis disguised himself as Cordio in Elturgard on the Trade Way to Triel in 1481 DR .
