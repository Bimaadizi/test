Fjellafar was a frost giant skald from Svardborg who lived centuries prior to the 14 th century DR .

History

Fjellafar wrote sagas that continued to be sung far and wide by giants circa 1356 DR . These chronicled centuries of victories and the challenges of living in the Sea of Moving Ice , such as what the area's frost giants hunted for among the ice during summer and winter.
