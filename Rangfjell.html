Rangfjell (meaning " Honor Mountain " in the giant language ) was an ancient giant -ruled kingdom in Faerûn during the Time of Giants of the Dawn Age ( −30,000 DR to −24,000 DR ).

History

Rangfjell was one of the giant kingdoms that warred with the ancient dragon kingdoms during the Dawn Age.
