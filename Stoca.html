Stoca was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age .

History

Stoca developed six spells in total, all within an eight-year period. The spells they invented were shape change in 2061 NY (−1798 DR) , plant growth in 2062 NY (−1797 DR) , animal growth and fly in 2065 NY (−1794 DR) , water breathing in 2067 NY (−1792 DR) , and lastly, enervation in 2069 NY (−1790 DR) .
