Historian's regalia was a variety of enchanted robes worn by the archeologists of Neverwinter in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Historian's regalia was a thick and warm set of robes made to help historians survive cold northern climates whilst on a quest for knowledge.

Powers

Apart from the benefit of warmth and some light protection, these robes boosted the wearer's accuracy and attention to detail, aiding the wearer in battle, helping them land well-aimed and deadly attacks, as well as helping them during excavations and scholarly pursuits.

History

In the late 14 th century DR, historians of Neverwinter were equipped with these enchanted robes during their excavations of the Ostorian relics across the Savage Frontier and in the Sea of Moving Ice .
