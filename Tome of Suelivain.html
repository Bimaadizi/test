The tome of Suelivain was a book written by Suelivain , a shade of Thultanthar . In it he included his notes on shadow magic .

History

Suelivain was dwelling in Thultanthar when a shadowstorm struck and he lost the tome of Suelivain and several other possessions. These were spread across the Plane of Shadow .
