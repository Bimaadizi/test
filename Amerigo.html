Amerigo was a human soldier in the Golden Legion during their invasion of Maztica in the 1360s DR .

Description

Amerigo had tan skin and a thin, black beard. He wore a heavy leather cape over his clothes, and a plumed Amnian helmet atop his head.

Personality

He had no qualms about conquering Maztica, and went about his work with a strong sense of duty and loyalty, which in turn earned him many loyal friends. Amerigo was rather ambitious, and worked hard hoping to gain promotions.

Possessions

In addition to a series of polearms , Amerigo owned a suit of leather armor and a ring of protection .
