Ghevel Coalwhever was a duergar boatman in Gracklstugh during the Silence of Lolth .

History

In 1372 DR , Ghevel was hired by the drow party of Quenthel Baenre to travel the Darklake . When duergar authorities tried to arrest the drow group, fearing they had discovered the plan for the Siege of Menzoberranzan , Hallistra Melarn magically compelled Ghevel to flee the city. The drow abandoned Ghevel and his boat when they had escaped. It is unknown what happened to him.
