Lorin was an elf man who became trapped within the lair of the hag Auntie Ethel , some time during or before the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

The unfortunate man was afflicted with seeing horrific illusions. From his perspective, he saw truth where others only saw lies. In actuality, he was forced to witness his own death, seemingly at all times.

Relationships

He was both a husband and a father.

History

At some point in his life, Lorin was presented with two paths he could take. He approached Auntie Ethel at her home and requested the power to see his own fate. The hag granted his wish, but with a distinct twist: Lorin was filled with visions of his own death. He remained trapped in the bowels beneath the Gnarled Teahouse as of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
