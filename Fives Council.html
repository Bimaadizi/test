The Fives Council served as the ruling body of government of Yhaunn in 1358 DR .

This organization derived its name because each table of a council session seated five people.

Activities

Aside from dealing with current events topics, the Fives Council was responsible for electing the Nessarch , or mayor.

Organization

Composed of wealthy property owners and merchants, the Fives Council was open to those who arrived early to claim the 75 seats. Seated individuals were permitted to vote on current topics.
