Unger Farshal was the proprietor of the Grinning Lion tavern in the North Ward of Waterdeep in the early 1370s DR . He secretly worked for the Two-Cities Consortium , operating the drug markets weekly between Waterdeep and Skullport . He had been known to obtain bloodroot for Orlpar Husteem , a Waterdhavian noble.

Description

Unger was a bald, close-mouthed and sinister person.
