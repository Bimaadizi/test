Tavern Dancing My Way to Wizardly Might was book penned by Jerenneth Ghaluin of Neverwinter in the mid-14 th century DR .

History

Tavern Dancing My Way to Wizardly Might was an autobiographical book of musings by Jerenneth Ghaluin. Some of the ruminations put to paper were the author's thoughts on love and companionship. It was published in the Year of the Bridle, 1349 DR .
