Lucio was a Netherese cleric who worked at Dundaden's Armory in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Lucio worked alongside Macchi under Dundaden . Although not as skilled as the legendary Dundaden, he was still an expert at forging armor.
