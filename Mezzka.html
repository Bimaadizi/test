Devout Mezzka was a goblin priest of the Absolute that came to reside in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Unlike other devotees of the Absolute, Mezzka opted not to decorate his clothing with bones and garish feathers, opting instead for simple leather garments.

History

Mezzka was present at the Shattered Sanctum following his group's horrific but successful raid on Waukeen's Rest in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
