A staff of arcane blessing was a magic staff associated with the goddess of magic Mystra that could be found in the Realms circa the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The staff emanated an aura of calmness and relaxation.

Powers

The wielder of a staff of arcane blessing was granted Mystra's blessing , a boon that bolstered their spellcasting ability, prowess in combat, along with their natural defenses.

They were also granted the ability to bestow a lesser blessing upon their companions or others.
