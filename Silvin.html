Silvin was a hawk who served as the companion of the wemic Kreston in the 14 th century DR . The two shared an empathic link with one another and Silvin was affected by Kreston's druidic magic provided they were in close enough proximity.

As Silvin could in a manner communicate with Kreston, he acted as a scout for the Golden Mane pride, providing them with reconnaissance from the skies.
