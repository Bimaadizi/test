Luskan stout was a dark malty brew from the city of Luskan . This stout was so thick it could be considered an entire meal in one tankard.

Powers

Luskan stout possessed some energizing and invigorating qualities.

History

In 1281 DR , Luskan stout was imported into the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale and was served by Grisella at her establishment - the Winter's Cradle Tavern of Easthaven as well as sold at the The Whistling Gallows of Lonelywood .

In the mid-to-late-15 th century DR , Luskan stout was available in the city of Neverwinter , notably, at the Beached Leviathan tavern.
