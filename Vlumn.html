Vlumn was a wizard operating in the High Ice of north Faerûn in the mid–14 th century DR .

Activities

Vlumn was engaged in some sort of scheme to create ice golems the size of mountains..

History

Late in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Elminster related to Storm Silverhand that he was prevented from helping protect Shandril Shessair (who was at that moment fighting at Irondrake Rock ) by the urgency of stopping, amongst other things, the schemes of Vlumn for the creation of unfathomably large ice golems.
