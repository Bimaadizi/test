Gauntlet was a battle horror (an evolved kind of helmed horror ) that led the Doom Brigade under orders of the beholder Vaxall .

Relationships

Gauntlet was one of the main minions of Vaxall. It led the Doom Brigade , a company of ten helmed horrors.

History

In 1370 DR , Gauntlet and his troop tried to stop the awakening of the Knights of the Crescent Moon , considering them a threat for its master. but unsuccessfully.

Possessions

Gauntlet possessed a helm of telepathy that permitted it to give orders to the helmed horrors and was equipped with Guardian , a magic sword enchanted by the lich Jhaniloth Puiral .
