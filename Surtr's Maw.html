Surtr's Maw was a mountain found on the island of Llorgh in Norheim in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Surtr's Maw dominated the southern half of Llorgh, touching both Northwind Strait and the Sea of Moonshae . The mountain covered approximately 50 mi² (130 km²) of land.
