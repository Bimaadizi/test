The Speedy Lightfeet were a unique part of boots that sparked with lightning energy. They were created by infamous inventor, Yrre the Sparkstruck .

Description

While they appeared worn and frayed, the Speedy Lightfeet crackled with electricity whenever they were moved or adjusted.

Powers

Whenever the wearer of the Speedy Lightfeet ran at a quickened pace, they could in a manner charge the boots with lightning energy. While this current was running through the wearer, they became invigorated and could better perform any athletic feats.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the boots remained stashed away within a chest found within the windmill of Moonhaven .
