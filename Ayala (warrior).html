Ayala was a wood elf warrior who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Ayala was the twin sister of Sylvara .

Equipment

Ayala had a magic sword .

Activities

Alongside her twin sister, Ayala led a band of elves that operated out in the Blacklake District . As of 1479 DR , the sisters' goal was to become stronger so they could join a band of Bregan D'aerthe that was operating near Castle Never .
