Drow Spinnan Trisagion was a mysterious enchanted book of the Lolthite faith.

Description

This large tome was decorated with web emblazonry and weighted 2 pounds (0.91 kilograms).

Powers

When Drow Spinnan Trisagion fell into priestly hands, the book shared its formidable divine powers with them. Curiously enough, the book could be used by clerics of any faith, unrestricted to the worship of the evil Spider Queen. The cleric in possession of Drow Spinnan Trisagion gained impressive resistance to arcane and divine magics and had their mind was fortified against any sorcery aimed at bending one's will. The owner also gained increased awareness, allowing them to spring into battle with efficiency. Lastly, the book's user could cast restoration spell.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Drow Spinnan Trisagion was brought to the ruined city of Myth Drannor by drow inquisitors of House Freth . The drow house forged an alliance with the Cult of the Dragon that lorded over the ruins and were granted control of the deep Elven Catacombs underneath Castle Cormanthor . The inquisitors created a small shrine to Lolth and placed Drow Spinnan Trisagion inside a sacrificial spider totem. The shrine was defended by a smattering of House Freth warriors, a High Priest, an inquisitor, and two trained arraccats . Any group of adventurers strong enough to defeat the drow party and inquisitive enough to open the totem was sure to be rewarded with the powerful clerical tome.
