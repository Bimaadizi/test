Brohn was a member of the Targos Guard , active in the early 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , the town of Targos in Icewind Dale became besieged by the Legion of the Chimera . One of the Legion's surprise attacks herded a troop of goblins by magical means through underground tunnels into the Targos docks, where Brohn and his partners, Reig Redwaters and Honest Jon , stood guard. Reig and Jon managed to survive, but Brohn was killed by a goblin arrow that pierced the man's throat.
