One-Legged Larry was a pirate from Luskan during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

One-Legged Larry was bald, with a big grey beard, and an eye-patch over one eye. He often hung around the Cutlass , and his drink of choice was Waterdhavian Fire Ale .

History

Some time around 1490 DR , Burkins tricked the Champion of the Burning Dawn into believing that One-Legged Larry had recently acquired a new peg leg in the form of a powerful artifact during an overseas raid.
