Thartryn Ilistar I was the third King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Thartryn I began his rule in the Year of the Wandering Sylph, 687 DR , when his father, King Belhendar , died. Thartryn ruled until the Year of the Toppled Throne, 710 DR , when he was deposed and killed by the cambion Iyachtu Xvim who then assumed the role of King.

Relationships

Thartryn I died before siring any offspring. He had one older brother.
