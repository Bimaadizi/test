The intricate rings were a set of metal rings that were crafted and enchanted by Coriolis Rillevay for use in battle. It could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The rings hovered just above the wielder's head, and could be directed to strike at a nearby enemy, although sometimes the rings seemed to choose additional targets at random.
