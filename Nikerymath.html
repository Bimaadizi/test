Nikerymath was an ancient elven kingdom. In −255 DR , in response to Jhaamdath's murdering of many elves over their need for wood to build their ships, the High Mages of Nikerymath destroyed the realm of Jhaamdath by summoning a gargantuan tidal wave which also reformed the coastline of the Vilhon Reach .
