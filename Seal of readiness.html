The seal of readiness was a unique enchanted disk crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Shevarith Kendia in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

The seal was a cold, metal disk that served to remind Shevarith to be like the winds of her native Rashemen : ready to strike in any direction.

Powers

The seal of readiness empowered Shevarith's ability to apply her arcane studies to retaliate swiftly against her attackers.
