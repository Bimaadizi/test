Lorgru was an orc and a prince of the Kingdom of Many-Arrows in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Awakened Sleepers, 1484 DR , Lorgru was the eldest son and heir of the king of Many-Arrows and, like all his dynasty, a moderate who favored peace with humans . However, Tiago Baenre and Ravel Xorlarrin abducted him and replaced him with a drow assassin who killed the king of Many-Arrows.
