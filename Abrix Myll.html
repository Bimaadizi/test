Abrix Myll was a Zhentish merchant and agent who worked in Thentia in 1367 DR .

Activities

Abrix traded in perfumes and spices, and shared the Zhentish Merchant Quarters with fellow purveyor and agent Oryk Kalain .The two of them were responsible for hiding and responsibly spending the hoard of 16,000 gp that the Zhentarim stole from the local temple of Waukeen in 1345 DR .
