Thlorrog was a mind flayer living in Gauntlgrym in the Underdark in 1484 DR .

History

Thlorrog was the leader of a illithid sept that kidnapped many being in order to use as guinea-pigs for their experiment until the Underdark explorer Soren Ravaris send an adventurer to defeat them and to kill Thlorrog.
