The Shattersoul River was a river located in the freezing reaches of north Faerûn .

Geography

The river originated in the frigid Titan Peaks that sat atop the Great Glacier . It flowed south off the great ice wall until it joined with the River Pelauvir , before the current emptied into Mirror Lake .
