Wand of magus was an enchanted wand from the ruined Castle Cormanthor of the elven city Myth Drannor .

Description

A wand of magic was an ornate rod that shimmered with magical power.

Powers

These ornate wands allowed the wielder to summon a beam of searing light . On average, a wand of magus held four chargers of the spell at a time. Additionally, these wands' enchantments granted the wielder significant sagacity at the coast of lowered physique constitution. These wands could only be used by clerics , paladins , and rangers .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , a wand of magus could be found locked away in an ancient study of the Silverspear Armory in Castle Cormanthor. Along with the wand, the chest contained the magic death's head ring , several healing potion s, and a ' potion of bull's strength .
