Dunatis was a minor interloper deity of mountains and peaks who originated from another plane of existence .

Description

The avatar form of Dunatis was that of a rather ordinary looking human male.

Personality

Dunatis was allegedly one of the most approachable members of his pantheon, as he found himself rarely called upon due to his portfolio. He was even known to prefer spreading the word of his faith himself.

Abilities

Dunatis was immune to charm , paralysis, and petrification effects.

The most notable power of Dunatis was to flatten large mountains into flat plains and vice versa. This effect occurred instantaneously and was often utilized to destroy an enemy's fortifications. He could also manifest boulders in thin air and throw them with great accuracy at targets up to 1,000 yards (910 meters) away.

Possessions

Dunatis owned a shield and set of armor , both with a +4 enchantment , that he always donned when entering combat.

History

In the kingdom of Bakar , a survivor state of the Imaskar Empire , a mighty city known as Medinat Muskawoon housed temples to gods of faiths that the populace's ancestors brought over from their original worlds. Among these temples was one dedicated to Dunatis.

Realm

Dunatis lived within the Outlands , in the divine realm of Tir na Og . His own divine realm within Tir na Og was a vast mountain on its fringe, known as the Pinnacle. Though just a single mountain, the Pinnacle's terrain varied considerably across its surface, from nearly flat areas to sheer, steep rocks. Trees covered the slopes of this mountain and wild animals could be found roaming freely about it.

Worshipers

Dunatis was known to be revered by some rilmani .

Rumors & Legends

This deity claimed to have been the one responsible for the creation of the Spire that stood at the center of the Outlands.
