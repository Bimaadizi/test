Kassa was young female weretiger inhabiting Jumlat in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara in 1367Â DR .

Personality

Kassa was a quiet individual, but her weretiger side expressed all of her pent up anger. She was completely unaware of her lycanthropic status, blacking out any time the weretiger emerged.

Description

Kassa was 14 years old in 1367 DR.

Relationships

Her older sister, Daria , was a member of the Sultan's harim .

Rumors & Legends

In weretiger form Kassa was actually the murderer known in Jumlat as " The Darkness ". The Darkness began to attract quite a following in Jumlat, but Kassa believed it would bring danger to the poor.
