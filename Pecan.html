Pecans were an exotic variety of nut in the Realms that grew as the fruit of trees of the same name, which were a species of hickory .

Availability

Beyond Toril

Usages

History

To celebrate its sixth anniversary in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the Downunda Patisserie in Ravens Bluff whipped up a large menu of special dishes. One of these dishes were cinnamon rolls topped with pecans and raisins . The bakery sold these in groups of a half a dozen for just one silver piece .
