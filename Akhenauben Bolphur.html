Akhenauben Bolphur was a famous crafter inhabiting Yeshpek along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He was straight to the point and did not haggle with customers. The price he set was final.

Activities

Akhenauben was known for his work with silver , specifically decorated bowls and flagons that were personalized for each individual customer. He was also a spokesmen for Yeshpek's crafters, representing them in matters of local law and trade.
