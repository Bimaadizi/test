Golias was a dwarf fighter and adventurer of the Puppetmasters company circa 1369 DR .

Personality

Golias was a stubbornly loyal and fearless warrior, who was fanatically devoted to his leader Brenna . He would grimly fight to the death if necessary.

Possessions

Golias specialized in his +3 dwarven thrower , and protected himself with +4 chain mail and a shield .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the Puppetmasters came up with a scheme to win an easy prize for the company. They planted a letter and treasure map leading to the dungeon of Barrenstone , allowing another adventuring group to excavate and clear out the complex. When they emerged from the dungeon with the treasure, the Puppetmasters attempted to rob their victims, only to be driven off and receive nothing for their troubles.
