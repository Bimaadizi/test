Seal of Illusk was an enchanted golden disk engraved with the seal of the ancient city of Illusk .

Powers

When held, the seal of Illusk glowed with strange radiance and felt warm to the touch. The glow created an illusion that the image of Illusk's seal was dancing in eerie light. Just possessing the item affected the owner, making them feel stronger, more confident, and more brazen. However, it came at the coast of their tact. The disk was invaluable to those using their skills for diplomacy or intimidation or just those seeking to be physically stronger.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the seal of Illusk was among the possessions of Rynn Shepherde , an agent of Luskan sent to take over the town of Port Llast . Rynn offered the seal as a reward for those willing to help Luskan cement its hold on the port city allied with Neverwinter .
