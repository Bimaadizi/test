Geography

The city lay on the coast of the Wizards' Reach on the Sea of Fallen Stars just south of the Yuirwood .

History

Taskaunt was initially founded by the empire of Unther following the great wars between Narfell and Raumathar , but over the passage of time Unther lacked the strength to hold it and Taskaunt eventually declared independence. Taskaunt was destroyed in a plot hatched by Hargid Tenslayer and his allies during the Salamander War of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .
