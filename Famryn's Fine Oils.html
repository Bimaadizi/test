Famryn's Fine Oils was an oil -making business in Baldur's Gate during the 15 th century DR .

Services

Famryn's produced various high-quality oils in the gnome -owned and human -operated workshop. It was linked by merchants' map to the Arthane Armory in Waterdeep , which bought "fire oil" to apply to newly made longswords and other weapons before sale.
