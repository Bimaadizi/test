Khlemmer's dock was a dock along the Chionthar in Scornubel during the 14 th century DR .

Location

It lay along the Chionthar .

Activities

There were weights at the dock.

History

In 1369 DR , several drow under the command of the drow sorceress Daerdatha hurled a blindfolded and tied-up Qilué Veladorn into the Chionthar at Khlemmer's dock, after loading her with weights to make her stay down.
