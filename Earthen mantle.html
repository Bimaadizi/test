The earthen mantle was a magical cloak found in the Underdark in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The cloak was quartered with white and red, with a diamond in the center. It innately smelled like wet dirt and fresh-cut grass .

Powers

The earthen mantle greatly improved blasts of evocation and force-based magic, and oddly improved the wearer's balance.

History

An earthen mantle could be found in the treasures of Mistress Laerion'vyr 's drow outpost in the Stonelands .
