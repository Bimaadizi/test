The Silversgate was an archway portal in Myth Drannor that connected the city to the High Forest region of the North .

Location

It was located on Kurvhan's Way , in the Old City district of Myth Drannor.

Structure

The gate was a tall archway made of stone and silver .

Activities

The portal transported anyone who passed through the archway to the Silverymoon Pass . It originally opened up directly to the city of Ascalhorn , but the destination was changed to allow easier access to Silverymoon itself.

It was guarded at all hours by members of the Coronal Guard to regulate passage and ensure nothing dangerous came through the other side.
