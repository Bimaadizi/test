Vendetta Kress was a tiefling businesswoman who lived in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Usually she sold wine and spirits for the Oathoon patriar family. She had a web of contacts in the city, including every tavern owner in the city.

History

In the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , Vendetta was abducted while walking in the Lower City by Dead Three cultists. During her captivity, she was interrogated about her masters' security measures. During that time she witnessed the interrogation and eventual death of Effinax Zalbor and overheard rumors about a possible means of escape.
