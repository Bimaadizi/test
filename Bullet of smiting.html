Bullets of smiting were heavy enchanted sling missiles that dealt powerful bludgeoning damage to the targets.

Powers

Bullets of smiting were significantly more dense and heavier than regular sling missiles. When one such bullet was launched out of a sling, it accumulated extra weight, gaining greater velocity, smashing into the target as if it were smashed with a mace .
