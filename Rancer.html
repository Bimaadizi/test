Brawler Rancer was a goblin cultist who followed the Absolute during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Rancer had orange skin, decorated with light blue body point, and black hair. He wore a leather harness and cap.

Possessions

In battle, Rancer wielded a crudely-made goblin scimitar .

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Rancer dwelled within the cult's stronghold of the Shattered Sanctum .
