Zarondar Riautar , who also went by the first name Zorondar and was nicknamed the Nimble , was a shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He was always scowling and wary of others, seldom speaking unless spoken to.

Activities

Zarondar owned Riautar's Weaponry , a weapons shop located on the High Road in Waterdeep's Trades Ward . He was also the public spokesman for the Fellowship of Bowyers and Fletchers .

Skills

He was incredibly skilled in the use of the light crossbow .
