Baron Herokimal was a ruler of Ulgarth in the Shining South during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Herokimal ruled the city of Kelazzan on the western coast of Ulgarth. He was a strict but fair ruler. Herokimal lived within a manor inside the city.
