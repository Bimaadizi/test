Glavel was a monk at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose .

Activities

Glavel was a monk in the Monastery of the Yellow Rose and a member of the Order of the Chisel in the Disciples of St. Sollars the Twice-Martyred . His duty was to act as a brewer and cook for both the monastery and the Guest House. Secretly, he was also a member of the Cult of Howling Hatred .

History

Glavel was one of the cultists who infiltrated the monastery in 1491 DR . However, he and Olara were discovered by Lana . Despite being left for dead after their meeting, Lana survived and in Mulmaster reported her discovery to the Order of the Gauntlet , the Lords' Alliance , and Zor Garwyl Gos , who hired an adventuring party to investigate. When the adventurers arrived at the monastery, they confronted Glavel.

Relationship

Glavel was the lover of Olara , a fellow cultist.

Personality

Glavel at heart was a selfish sociopath. He joined the Howling Hatred only because they encouraged his hobbies. Glavel was a very good liar and would quickly change sides if his life was in danger.
