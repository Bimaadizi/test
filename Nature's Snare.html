Nature's Snare was an enchanted staff found in the druidic enclave of the Western Heartlands in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Nature's Snare was a wooden staff of wriggling thread-like vines wrapped around its shaft.

Powers

The wielder could unleash its fly trap enchantment upon a successful strike, ensnaring the target in place. However plant creatures and beasts were unaffected by this weapon's magic.

History

This staff was stashed within a trapped chest in a hidden cavern passage outside of the Emerald Grove in the Western Heartlands circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
