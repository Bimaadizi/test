Uthred was a young warmage from Impiltur . He was a part of the attack on the enclave of the Cult of the Dragon in Tarsakh 12 , and participated also in a second desperation attack after the rout on Tarsakh 16 , where he perished.

Description

Uthred was still relatively young, but affected long blonde whiskers, presumably to look like more of a mighty wizard.

Abilities

Uthred had been the one to conceal a group of archers from the Cult's forces during the rout. In the process, he cast several spells, including presumably invisibility sphere , fireball , ice storm , and blink . Later, he employed a spell, likely detect magic , to determine whether there was any activity in Vorasaegha 's barrow mound. In the final fight against the dracolich that protected the Cult's enclave, Uthred was seen using levitate and fireball .

Debriefing

After Quelsandas cast the aggravate dracorage spell in the Gray Forest , routing the forces of Impiltur on behalf of the Cult of the Dragon on Tarsakh 12 , Uthred was one of the warmages supported the retreat of a group of archers by rendering them invisible and providing cover with spells. After they regrouped thanks to the intervention of Taegan Nightwind and Jivex , Uthred employed divinations to study Vorasaegha 's mound. He was initially skeptical of her recruitability, but Taegan insisted. Later, Uthred was key to a ritual to conceal the entire company in preparation for a final assault on the Cult's enclave. He was slain by the dracolich guardian of the enclave with a breath of corrosive acid.
