Othmar bin Kalath was a sha'ir who lived in Rog'osto of the Cities of the Ancients in Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Relationships

Othmar was served by a loyal efreeti named Xamus .

Activities

In addition to being an accomplished sha'ir, Othmar was a qadi of Rog'osto.
