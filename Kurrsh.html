Kurrsh was a village located near the Lake of Steam .

Description

The village was composed of a cluster of wattle-and-daub buildings. The people of the area mostly consisted of farmers.

History

In the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR , the village was the site of a meeting between Phyrea , the daughter of Senator Inthelph and Ivar Devorast , the chief engineer of the construction of the canal to connect the Lake of Steam with the Nagaflow . It was a secret meeting in which Phyrea was delivering a supply of saltpeter for the creation of smokepowder for the canal construction. The saltpeter came from her father's estate of Berrywilde located near Innarlith .
