Eirgel was a page in the High Palace of Silverymoon in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Appearance

He was but a boy.

Activities

He was a page.

History

In 1369 DR , Alustriel Silverhand called on all her pages to determine whether the trader Oscalar Maerbree was lying about not having met with the recently murdered tradelord of Neverwinter , Garthin Muirtree . As they arrived, they caught him saying something rude to her. One of them, named by the Lady as Eirgel, challenged him to a duel. Alustriel talked him down.
