The Tale of Curran Strongheart was a song that inspired courage in others.

Content

When the ballad was performed by a bard , it became imbued with their magics. Those allies who heard the song became brave. This negated magical effects such as fear and fortified their courage in the face of even the most terrifying foes.

History

By the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , The Tale of Curran Strongheart was common among bards of meddling skills. It was heard being performed in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale and the village of Kuldahar .
