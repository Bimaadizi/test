History

When the elven wizard Ilbryn Starym passed through Ripplestones in early-to-mid-spring of the Year of the Awakening Wyrm, 767 DR , he tried to ask Baerdagh and Caladaster Daermree , who were just outside the Fair Maid tavern, about the whereabouts of Elminster Aumar . They did not respond, but a man at the door, introducing himself as Jalobal, blabbed instantly and told him all about the Dead Place where Elminster had gone, east of Ripplestones. Despite Jalobal's warnings, Ilbryn just showed them the wounds on his side, and then went off. Jalobal went back in to tell everyone about it.
