Melshar's Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Melshar's Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . It originated in the north on Julthoon Street where it branched south, passed through the western edge of Trader's Way , then turned southwest to end on Calamastyr Lane .
