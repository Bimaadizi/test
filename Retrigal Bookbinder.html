Retrigal Bookbinder was a scholar who lived in Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The sage had a greying beard.

Possessions

Retrigal enjoyed wearing turquoise robes.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Retrigal ventured into Neverdeath graveyard to investigate Lord Neverember 's claims of inheritance to the throne of Neverwinter. While parsing over some old records, Retrigal was assaulted by the risen undead and forced to seek the aid of a nearby human adventurer at Doomguide's Watch .

He eventually found the tomb of Vers Never , but it was guarded by a particularly powerful undead guardian.
