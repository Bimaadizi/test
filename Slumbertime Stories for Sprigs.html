Slumbertime Stories for Sprigs was a book written or compiled by Anastra Syluné Silverhand (writing as "Syluné of Shadowdale ") in the 1330s DR . It was an anthology of tales intended for children.

Availability

A copy was kept in Candlekeep by 1372 DR .
