Bene was a commoner human from Athkatla , the City of Coin, in Amn who lived in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Bene met his demise in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR . He, along with several other men, was captured by a drow druid named Pai'Na who built a massive spider nest in ancient tombs underneath Athkatla's Grave District . Bene and other peasants were consumed from the inside by Pai'Na's spiders.
