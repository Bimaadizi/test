The Street of the Learned , or the University Road , was located in Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Description

This street was so named because it provided the most direct route for students commuting to the ferries that crossed Al-Sarif River to the great university, Al-Bidir Sallah . The Street of the Learned was adjacent to the Street of the Ajami in the Craft District , passing northeast though the district to exit the city and connect with the River Road and the ferries.
