Buckle Street was a hard-packed dirt and gravel paved road in Waterdeep that started in the city's Trades Ward and continued downward into the Southern Ward .

Geography

This road ran from north to south, running south off of River Street and cutting through Sahtyra's Lane before entering the Southern Ward, wherein it ended in a dead end.

In the Trades Ward, Buckle Street acted as the eastern terminus of Salabar Street and Wagonrace , while in the Southern Ward it acted as the western terminus of Tornsar Alley and Dark Alley .

Notable Inhabitants

A number of merchants lived along this street, including Fylgard Onister .
