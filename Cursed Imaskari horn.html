the cursed Imaskari horn was an Imaskari relic unearthed from the ruins of Solon by the slaves of Ambuchar Devayam .

Description

This horn was made from black ivory.

Powers

When blown, this horn would cause an ancient black dragonne to arrive within ten minutes, looking for its long-lost master. On arrival, it would attack whoever blew the horn instead.
