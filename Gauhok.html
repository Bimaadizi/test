Description

The dwarf had a thick black beard. He was the son of Steelfist , the dwarven representative of the Council of Sparkling Stones . He was exceptional at many things, especially combat; he was remarkably strong and talented.

Possessions

Gauhok fought with full plate mail , a helmet , and a battleaxe .

History

Circa 1358 DR , Steelfist offered his son, Gauhok, the opportunity to adventure with the famed Heroes of Ascore , at the heroes' discretion.
