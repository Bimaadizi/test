Lord General Ulgrym was the commander-in-chief of the Zhentilar and a member of the Zhentarim in 1370 DR .

Relationships

Ulgrym was a member of the Council of Lords of Zhentil Keep and the leader of the Zhentilar.

History

In 1357 DR Ulgrym commanded the Zhent military forces in Teshwave under Guthbert Golthammer .

At the onset of the Time of Troubles Ulgrym was a captain of the Zhentilar.
