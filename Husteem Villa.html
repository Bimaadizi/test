Husteem Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Husteem .

Location

The villa was located in the western section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward on the northeast corner of where Delzorin Street joined the Street of the Singing Dolphin . The northern part of the villa also touched Sighing Maiden's Walk .

Description

The walled compound contained three separate buildings. The largest building sat on the west side of the villa. Each building was three or four stories tall.
