Eskrel Starbridge was a Highknight of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Appearance

He was stern-looking.

Relationships

During the Tethgard raid, Eskrel Starbridge was part of the same squad as Merlar and Therlon . Later, he assembled a force of his own for a mission to Shadowdale, looking for Elminster. The force included a number of Highknights including Baerengard and Narulph , and the War Wizards Lemmeth and Mereld .

As head of the Highknights, his Clerk of Vigilance was Talonar Winter .

Possessions

He carried a teleport ring that belonged to Filfaeril Obarskyr , until he yielded it unto Storm Silverhand .

Activities

He was a veteran Highkight by 1479 DR , when he became the Head Highknight. Per Storm Silverhand, he was one of the few that could be trusted to do much of anything.

History

Eskrel Starbridge had been part of the raid on Tethgard in Mirtul 5 of 1479 DR against the fugitive wizard Elminster Aumar . During the raid, Storm Silverhand , an accomplice of Elminster's, snuck up on him and knocked him unconscious with longsleep herb before the confrontation. Thus, he survived the raid.

After the raid, Eskrel became the new head of all highknights. Acting on information from Rorskryn Mreldrake , he decided to go seek out Elminster in Shadowdale , personally. He came across a doppelganger pretending to be him, and then across Storm Silverhand , whom they initially mistook for the Old Mage, until they saw her bare chest as she introduced herself as the Marchioness of Immerdusk . She convinced him to yield up his teleport ring .

He was unable to return in time for the Council of the Dragon . By the time he returned to Castle Obarskyr , Arclath Delcastle , a minor nobleman, was being chased by the death knight Targrael . He got commanded by Glathra Barcantle to capture Arclath, Amarune Whitewave , Storm Silverhand , and Elminster Aumar . Later, Talonar Winter and him were the ones who reported the death of everyone in Staghaven House to Glathra, including Rorstil Hallowdant , who wasn't supposed to be there.

Eskrel was among the retinue protecting King Foril Obarskyr I during the Festival of Handras , when the Wizards of War became aware of a plot to draw out the blueflame ghosts and anticipated a dangerous situation for the King. Foril insisted in a different tactic, where the ghosts would be lured out of the palace; they succeeded on that.
