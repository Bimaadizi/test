Selara was a large boat from the town of Lonelywood in Icewind Dale , in the late 13 th century DR .

Description

Selara was a sizable hand-built fishing boat that could fit up to seven humanoids on board and safely transport them across the frigid Maer Dualdon lake.

History

Selara was built by Thom Farold of Lonelywood in the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR . The boat was named after Selara, Thom's late wife and the boat would be used by Thom and his friend, Young Ned , to fish in Maer Dualdon.
