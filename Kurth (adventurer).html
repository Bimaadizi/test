Kurth "Bandit-Slayer" was an adventurer of the Vast .

History

Kurth was an adventurer who earned a reputation for killing bandits, hence the nickname. He founded a settlement that was named for him, Kurth , which was a large village by the 1360s DR .

Personality

Kurth was grim and watchful, much like the town. However, Kurth was friendly to the local dwarves and loved by them in turn.

Possessions

Kurth wielded an axe.
