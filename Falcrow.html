Falcrow was the captain of the Sancarla during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Sometime prior to the late 15 th century DR, Falcrow was the captain of the merchant vessel Sancarla , but doomed his crew by attempting to trade with a monster who slaughtered them all. Waking up later, seemingly unscathed, Falcrow and six crew members set sail and fled the monster's stone fortress. During that time, Captain Falcrow was contracted by the merchant Ridrien Karsten .

Around 1490 DR , Captain Falcrow was lairing in a mausoleum within a Luskan cemetery, but was defeated by the Champion of the Burning Dawn and their party.
