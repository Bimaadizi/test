Stronk was a fomorian living in the Wormwrithings in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationship

Stronk somehow formed a bond with the purple worms .

Activities

Stronk acted as a caretaker for the eggs of purple worms in the area.

History

Circa 1486 DR , he was killed by an expedition led by some adventurers , who'd been hired by Bruenor Battlehammer to investigate the threat of the demon lords , who were in need of a purple worm egg for a rite to banish the demon lords.
