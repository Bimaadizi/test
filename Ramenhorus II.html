Ramenhorus II was the eighth pharaoh of Mulhorand .

History

Ramenhorus II was born in 895 DR . He ascended to the throne of pharaoh of Mulhorand in 922 DR , after the former pharaoh Horustep II had died in battle against the Red Wizards of Thay during a half-hearted attempt to prevent the nation from seceding from the Second Mulhorand Empire . Ramenhorus II would die later that year during the same ongoing conflict.
