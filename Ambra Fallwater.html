Ambra Fallwater was a maid working in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR . She was described as being rather plain-spoken.

History

Some time around the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Ambra began working for the Vanthampur patriar family at their estate . She was hired after the family's previous housekeeper was let go when they broke a vase.
