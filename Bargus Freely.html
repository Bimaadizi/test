Bargus Freely was an inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate who died some years before the late 15 th century DR .

History

Bargus Freely perished sometime before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR and was buried in the Lower City Graveyard , near the cemetery arched gate and the Durinbold Mausoleum . The gravestone was etched with a single inscription: "Here lies Bargus Freely. He should've lookt before he leapt," suggesting the man's cause of death.
