Manycherries wine was a popular wine served in the Realms and usually consumed by nobles.

Availability

Manycherries wine was poured in tallglasses and served during nobles' and influential individuals' events in the City of Splendors, Waterdeep . Visitors from Amn in the Lands of Intrigue appreciated the drink as much as the northerners.
