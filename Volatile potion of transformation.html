Volatile potions of transformation were magic potion that received a recreational use in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

Volatile potions of transformation possessed magics that altered the imbiber's appearance for a short period of time, seemingly at random. Among the forms the potion resulted in were various animal forms, such as wolves , pigs , or chickens .

History

These volatile potions were consumed recreationally during the annual celebration of the Masquerade of Liars in the city of Neverwinter as early as of the mid-to-late 15 th century DR.
