Bhaerusk Montalar was the proprietor of Montalar's Happy House‎‎ eatery in Suzail , active in the late 14 th century DR .

History

By the late 14 th century DR , Bhaerusk was married and had four daughters. His entire family worked in Montalar's Happy House, along with the wife's two sisters. They operated the busy eatery and lived on the floor above the dining hall. Bhaerusk rented out apartments on the third floor and the cellar. Some said that he rented the place out to anyone with some dubious background, no questions asked.
