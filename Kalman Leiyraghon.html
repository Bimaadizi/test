Kalman Leiyraghon was a scion of Leiyraghon House in Melvaunt in 1374 DR .

Activities

Kalman wandered through all the taverns in Melvaunt.

History

In 1374 DR, he met the dwarf mercenary Dorn Crownshield at The Rusty Bucket tavern. However, secretly, he met with his friends Argens Bruil , Elaint Marsk , Kara Calaudra , and Oreal Nanther planning to go on an adventure, with the support of Dorn.

They found a "treasure map" (in truth, a trap devised by Bremen Leiyraghon ) and went on adventure in Thar , only to be captured by the orog Daazlag and his orc minions.
