Vedina Stareye was an inhabitant of Ravens Bluff , active in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Vedina was described as a woman with a "face of a pock-marked road and a voice of like fingernails on slate ."

History

Vedina Stareye was a dock frequenter or a dock worker who lived in the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast in the late 14 th century DR. Little was recorded of her life; however, the woman's name graced the pages of the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter broadsheet . Jacinth "Jackie" Moonspring , the Society Editor, connected Vedina to a notorious womanizer named Don Juan Montery , who, apart from her, also entangled with several other notable women within just a single month: the half-elven scout named Opal Starlight , a handsome paladin of Tyr , Yvonne Chantel , and the fellow Knight of the Golden Rooster named Ladyhawke .
