Umbarl Darlhoun was a War Wizard of Cormyr .

History

In 1480 DR , Darlhoun was on duty in High Horn 's garrison. However, Lord Sunter , the garrison's head, one day found Darlhoun's body dismembered.

Personality

Darlhoun was a cheerful individual, always nice, sympathetic, and diligent.
