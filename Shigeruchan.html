Shigeruchan was a monk of Fochu Peninsula in Wa .

Activities

Shigeruchan was the spiritual leader of the village of Gugedo in Fochu Peninsula. He liked wandering the wilderness composing a lengthy poem to the Path of Enlightenment .

History

In 1358 DR , Shigeruchan met Osari Minhiro and a foreign adventuring party. He insisted that the adventurers were possessed by demons and need to cleanse them through his poem.
