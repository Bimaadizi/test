Jenzar was a warrior and shopkeeper inhabiting Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Like his brother, he was quite lazy.

Activities

Jenzar was one of the owners of Reliable Guides , a shop that hired out guides to take adventuresome individuals through the ruins of Zhentil Keep following the city's destruction in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .

His shop was located on the Throat outside of the Throat Gate of the Foreign Quarter .

Relationships

His brother, and co-owner of Reliable Guides, was Goartet .
