Lady's Lament No. 4 was a variety of expensive magical spirits found in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The enchanted drink was on the menu at the Yawning Portal tavern of Waterdeep. One serving of Lady's Lament was priced at ten gold coins .
