Rylatar'ralah'tyma , also known as simply Rylatar , was a red dragon dwelling in the Forest of Wyrms in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Rylatar was around 80 feet (24 meters) long. Her scales were crimson-colored and her wings were a deep indigo color.

Personality

Rylatar claimed to not be fond of anything, but she liked to play games and hunt.

Possessions

Rylatar had a silver ring in the shape of a winged dragon eating its own tail, which had the power to change her shape into the form of a human or elf . She used the ring to take the form of a human woman named Ryla Dragonclaw .
