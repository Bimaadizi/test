Percival Gallard Woodehouse , also known as Pig , was a famous chef in Skullport .

History

Percival was a famous chef in Waterdeep until Denver Gilliam hired him to work at the Traitor Picks . Under him, the tavern earned a good reputation.

However, Percival was convinced to assist Volothamp Geddarm in one of his trips into the Underdark . During his absence, Denver fired and replaced him. In 1370 DR , back in Skullport, Percival was without a job.
