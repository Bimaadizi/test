Boorvald Orcbane was great chief of the Uthgardt Tree Ghost tribe around the time of the War of the Silver Marches of 1485 DR .

Activities

Orcbane earned his name from the raiding parties he led, which targeted settlements of the Iceshield orc clan.

Relationships

The chief had quite the prolific family. He had six sons and three daughters, all of whom were considered friends of the elves of the High Forest . These friendship were secured by gifts in the form of elven oathbows , one of which was granted to each of Boorvald's nine children.
