Chess Variations of the Masters of Old Impiltur was a book on the game of chess in the kingdom of Impiltur .

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , at the conclusion of the abraxus affair in Cormyr , Giogioni Wyvernspur spent half a night studying Chess Variations of the Masters of Old Impiltur to have a chance of beating King Azoun IV at chess.
