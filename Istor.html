Istor was a wolf kept by the Zhentarim operating within the organization's hideout found within a massive cave found beneath the Waukeen's Rest inn during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Istor was set to guard an pathway in the rear of the Zhentarim hideout that led down into the depths of the Underdark .Istor's keen sense of smell made suspicious of the illusory wall that obscured the entrance to this pathway, though as a wolf he did not fully comprehend the discrepancy between his senses.

History

The massive wolf was tasked with guard duty as the Zhentarim planned to demolish their hideout in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , to avoid detection by the Flaming Fist .
