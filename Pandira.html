Pandira was an island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Pandira was one of the Korinn Archipelago's smaller named islands. It was found in the northern region of the archipelago surrounded by many smaller islands. Pandira was rather unremarkable save for the settlement of Westhaven .
