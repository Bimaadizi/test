Shaengarne's Tooth was an alcoholic drink from the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale .

Availability

The potation was sold as early as the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR at The Salty Dog tavern in Targos . Need after the ice waters of the Shaengarne River , the drink was one of the most expensive offerings at the Dog. It served for ten gold pieces at the bar by its portly proprietor Gohar who was happy to share the town's latest rumors with paying customers.
