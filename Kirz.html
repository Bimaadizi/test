Tracker Kirz was a member of the cult of the Absolute who dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Kirz had mottled green and yellow skin. She wore a crude leather harness, loincloth, and a cloth-and-leather hooded mask.

Possessions

She carried with her a crude wooden bow .

Relationships

Kirz held great respect for her fellow cultist Crusher .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Kirz was among the Absolute's raiders celebrating their successful raid on the Waukeen's Rest inn.
