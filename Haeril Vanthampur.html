Lady Haeril Vanthampur was a member of the Parliament of Peers from the noble patriar Vanthampur family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Lady Haeril Vanthampur was already a member of the Parliament of Peers. When the Absolute crisis arrived to the Gate, she was noted by the Cult of Bane as one of the eight top patriars of the Parliament, vulnerable to bribery, blackmail, or direct threats.
