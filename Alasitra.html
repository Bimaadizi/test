Alasitra was an abjurer and one of three minor wizards stationed at the enclave run by the young Red Wizard Hinnar .

Description

Alasitra's body bore a magical tattoo from the Red Wizards that made her abjurations more difficult to resist.

Abilities

Alasitra was tough, dexterous, and intelligent and was trained in alchemy , spellcraft, and the scribing of scrolls.

Possessions

Alasitra carried a dagger and a small crossbow of superb craftsmanship.

Alasitra's spellbook contained 23 different spells.

Activities

Alasitra spent most of the day's hours in the compound's main barracks scribing spells with other wizards.
