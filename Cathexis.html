Cathexis was the wizard responsive for creation of Lord Alex's boundless beer stein sometime before 1372 DR .

History

Cathexis crafted the never-ending magical stout stein for his good friend Lord Alex who loved to partake in tavern revelry. Eventually, Lord Alex's boundless beer stein ended up among junk in Torleth's Treasures store in Gillian's Hill .
