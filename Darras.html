Sir Darras of Suzail was a human cavalier in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

His hard focus on adherence to his order's code and focus on honor above all, opened his path to the higher ranks. Born to middle-class parents, he had to work harder and be of better moral fortitude than those from higher classes in his order. He felt and made, himself responsible for all those who traveled with him.

Possessions

He wielded an enchanted longsword .
