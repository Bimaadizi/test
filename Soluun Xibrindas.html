Soluun Xibrindas ( pronounced : / s oʊ ˈ l uː n z ɛ ˈ b r ɪ n d ɑː s / soh-LOON zeh-BRIN-das ) was one of the top agents of Bregan D'aerthe in Waterdeep during the early 1490s DR .

Activities

Soluun, together with Fel'rekt Lafeen and Krebbyg Masq'il'yr , was tasked by Jarlaxle with carrying out dirty deeds in the city. Soluun preferred to act during the day. At night, Soluun's hobby was killing solitary elves and half-elves in the Dock Ward . Soluun resided on the Scarlet Marpenoth ship, where he prayed to Lolth .

History

The family's house was destroyed and only Soluun and his younger brother Nar'l survived. They decided to join Bregan D'aerthe .

Personality

Soluun was a fanatical bully totally loyal to Bregan D'aerthe and Jarlaxle. He considered his brother weak for his decision to study magic . From his earliest days, he hated surface elves and half-elves and when he had free time outside his missions Soluun always tried to kill some solitary elvish individuals.

Possessions

Like his fellows Fel'rekt and Krebbyg, Soluun was equipped with Lantanese firearms .
