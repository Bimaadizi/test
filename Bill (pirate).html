Bill was a dwarvish pirate from the isle of Brynnlaw in the Sea of Swords in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Bill was a male dwarf with dark hair and a horned helmet . He wore armor and carried a large shield , armed with a simple blade.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Bill sailed the Bleedin' Mary and could be found drinking in the Vulgar Monkey dock-side tavern alongside the rest of the ship's crew. He cheerily made terrible sea-related puns and made fun of landlubbers.
