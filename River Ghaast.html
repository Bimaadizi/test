Geography

The River Ghaast originated in the western region of the Shalhoond . It was fed by many unnamed tributaries within the forest as well as one reaching through Ejen Horo into the Great Amber Steppes . The river flowed south for several hundred miles before reaching the northern banks of Gbor Nor .
