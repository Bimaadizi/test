Lord Rapha was one of the Shadovar of Thultanthar that served Prince Escanor Tanthul , circa the late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was a large, intimidating shade with red eyes that glittered like rubies .

Activities

Rapha was trusted enough by Prince Escanor to served in his place in the case he was injured or otherwise incapacitated.

History

In Tarsakh of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Rapha was among the Shadovar present at the splicing of the shadowshell , a barrier of shadowstuff the Shadovar formed over the Greycloak Hills to trap the recently-released phaerimm within. When Escanor was injured during the splicing, Rapha look over lead of the operation and helped bring the injured Prince of Shade back to Thultanthar.
