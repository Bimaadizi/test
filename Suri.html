Suri was an adventurer and wererat active in Waterdeep during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Suri was a girl with brown hair who wore a silver sun-shaped pendant.

History

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Suri and her sister Perdita adventured through a secret crypt hidden beneath the City of the Dead . Misled by fake treasure maps distributed by Bregan D'aerthe , the young sisters reached the lair of Artor Morlin , a powerful vampire . There, Perdita lost her life and became a ghost , while Suri fought a wererat and subsequently contracted lycanthropy .
