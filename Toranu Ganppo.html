Toranu Ganppo , also transliterated as Toranu Gang-po , was a fortress in Koryo . Its location was lost by the 14th century DR .

The fortress was said to hold many treasures, among them the magical chalice called the dancing hart .

In 1357 DR , a band of adventurers headed by Glei Yuindo returned to Pan and it was rumored they had discovered the lost fortress. However, before they could tell their story, they died of a mysterious sickness.
