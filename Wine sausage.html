TypeFood
OriginOak-Father's Boon , Tethyr

Wine sausages were a delicious and strongly mint -flavored sausage dish available in the town of Mosstone in Tethyr .

History

Wine sausages were served at the Oak-Father's Boon inn in Mosstone. In the late 14 th century DR , Volothamp Geddarm mentioned the dish along with bowl of hares as some of the best offerings from the Boon.
