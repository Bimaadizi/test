Elbereth was an elf fighter and the prince and later king of Shilmista in the mid–14 th century DR .

Relations

He was the son of King Galladel and a descendant of Dellanil Quil'quien .

Description

Elbereth was almost 6 feet (1.8 meters) tall and weighed approximately 130 pounds (59 kilograms), with striking black hair and piercing silver eyes that were "almost eerie".

Abilities

Elbereth was an accomplished sword-fighter and archer, being talented with both longsword and longbow . He was equally good at archery from horseback as he was on foot.

Personality

He was courageous in combat and fought vengefully against the elves' enemies.

Possessions

He owned a +3 longsword of sharpness , the blade of which glowed with blue flame.

He had a powerful steed named Temmerisa .

History

Upon the invasion by forces of Castle Trinity in the Year of Maidens, 1361 DR , Shayleigh urged King Galladel to perform the ancient ritual of Daoine Teague Feer . But he rejected the notion, thinking it a waste of time and that it had no chance of working. Shayleigh told Elbereth and he also called for it. His father begrudgingly allowed Elbereth to do it. Elbereth successfully performed the ceremony, causing the trees to aid the elves of Shilmista in its time of crisis.
