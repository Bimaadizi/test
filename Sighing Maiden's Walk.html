Sighing Maiden's Walk was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Sighing Maiden's Walk was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . It originated on the the Street of Glances where it branched southwest to end on the Street of the Singing Dolphin . Sighing Maiden's Walk was intersected by Ivory Street , Sniff Alley and Staghunter's Way .
