Drom was a male goblin of Duskwood on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR .

History

Drom was one of the goblins who inhabited the caves near Duskwood .

Gameplay

He would warn the Kalach-Cha not to take the Glowstone . If ignored, Drom would attack them.
