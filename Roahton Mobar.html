Roahton Mobar was a crime-fighting monk from the port town of Nyanzaru in Chult , active sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

History

Roahton was known for his flawless stances that were broad and strong. The monk was one of three fighters, including Strigga Morkoscavich and Kimigha Poji , who opposed the Headless Serpent, Secret Venom gang of Port Nyanzaru some years before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . Each of the monks wore one of the sets of enchanted clothing. Roahton's garb was known as The Graceful Cloth .
