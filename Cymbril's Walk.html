Cymbril's Walk was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Cymbril's Walk was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . The road orignated on the Street of Bells , branching west for several blocks through the Street of the Sword and Warrior's Way before turning north to merge with Alnether Street on the Street of Silver across from the eastern end of Keltarn Street . A small alley called the Prowl connected with the northern side of Cymbril's Walk and Elsambul's Lane connected with its eastern side.
