The Underdark was a tavern in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Interior

The Underdark tavern had a secret passage that led to Undermountain in the back of the establishment's basement. The passage was hidden behind a movable rack of shelves filled with supplies in the far back corner. Behind the shelves was a door locked with an iron bar that led to a long staircase shrouded in darkness that eventually led to Skullport .
