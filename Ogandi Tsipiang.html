Ogandi was the Prince and ruler of Khazari and lived within the city of Skarou .

History

The Prince was forced to offer terms of peace to the invading Yamun Khahan after he successfully captured the city of Alashan . Ogandi later attempted to reorganize the power structure of Khazari with hopes of consolidating power to better combat future invasions.
