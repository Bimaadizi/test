Illyth Fleetwood was a noblewoman of the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast in the late 14 th century DR . She lived outside the city at Woodenhall manor, the home of the Fleetwood family. Despite her noble status, Illyth was an accomplished scholar and had an extensive personal library. She was married and delighted in activities such as the Game of Masks . She was also fast friends with Jack Ravenwild .
