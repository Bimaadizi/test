Fonlim was a gnome wizard who lived in Neverwinter during the late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Like almost all the other students of the Neverwinter Academy , Fonlim was fond of Lady Aribeth and was grateful that she didn't blame all the wizards the spreading of the Wailing Death and other events in Neverwinter and.

Fonlim had doubts about ever reaching graduation, and the memory of Herban 's angry shouts during basic training continued to haunt his nightmares.

Relationships

During the graduation ceremonies in 1372 DR, Fonlim was chatting with other students, Kiki and Deelin .

History

Deelin was a student of the Neverwinter Academy. In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , she finished her studies and was one of the eight students, among whom was the future Hero of Neverwinter , present at the graduation ceremony of that year. During the ceremony led by Lady Aribeth, the Academy was attacked. He fought against the mysterious mages in the Graduation Chamber. It is not known if Fonlim survived the attack.
