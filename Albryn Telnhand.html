Albryn Telnhand was a gnome leatherworker who lived in the Dock Ward of the city of Waterdeep in the 13 th century DR .

History

Circa 1226 DR , Albryn invented a factory machine known as the pressbow . Though some disputed this claim, saying that the machine had been around since much earlier and that Albryn had merely refined it.
