The Gospoda-Veche was a large government building located in the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The building and surrounding compound was located in northeast Almorel along the Golden Way .

Activities

In addition to serving as a meeting hall for the Gospoda , the Gospoda-Veche was also one of Almorel's main armories.
