Sha'Quessir Mirabella was rumored to be a human warrior offered acceptance into the elven knights of Myth Drannor .

Possessions

If the rumors were true, Mirabella would have worn elven plate , fought with a longsword , and rode a moon-horse like all other knights.

History

Centuries ago, Sha'Quessir was a human knight at Myth Drannor in the Elven Court .

Rumors and Legends

Gold elves of Evermeet disputed this claim since all elven knights were pure-breed elves .
