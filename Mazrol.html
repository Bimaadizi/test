Mazrol was a former priest of Vhaeraun .

History

Mazrol was one of the many priests of Vhaeraun who joined the Church of Eilistraee after 1375 DR .

Mazrol however actually agreed with Valdar Jaelre 's idea that it was Vhaeraun who had killed Eilistraee and was playing a deception plot.

In 1379 DR , Mazrol, alongside Valdar and others, plotted to help Ghaunadaur 's followers attack the Promenade of the Dark Maiden . However, he, together with Naxil and Jub , was put inside the Pit by the priests of Ghaunadaur who were trying to awaken the avatar of their god. The ooze god consumed Mazrol.
