Nameless was the tressym familiar of Tsarra Chaadren .

Description

Nameless was jet black, with one blue eye and one green.

History

From 1364 DR , Nameless was the familiar of Tsarra Chaadren, who became the second Blackstaff . "after ten years of bonding".

The Spellplague merged Nameless and Tsarra's husband, the human Raergar , forming the Nameless Haunt . The location where they were merged was on the eastern side of Mount Waterdeep . Their merging created a permanent cat's head mark on the cobblestones, which remained no matter how many times they were replaced.
