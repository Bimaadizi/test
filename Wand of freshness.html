Wands of freshness were a type of wand that operated similarly to the spell freshen .

Description

These wands were always brightly colored and usually decorated with etchings of flowers.

Powers

Expending a charge of this wand would cause any rancid or spoiled food within a 1‑cubic-foot (0.028‑cubic-meter) to become permanently fresh and delicious once more.
