Rupture demons were barely sentient, sludge-like demons that were the weakest of the spawn of Juiblex .

Description

Rupture demons were oily and sludge-like creatures.

Behavior

Rupture demons were barely intelligent, possessing little sense of self and essentially no sense of self-preservation.

Abilities

When a rupture demon died, the energy released by their death healed a nearby demon.

Combat

Rupture demons attacked anything that attacked them, using pseudopods as weapons.

Ecology

These fiends lived as scavengers in the Abyss , trailing behind larger demons and feeding on the remains of their victims. Some scholars theorized that the rupture demons' aiding of other demons via their deaths was part of some scheme wrought by Juiblex.
