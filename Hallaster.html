Hallaster was a messenger sent from Kuldahar .

Activities

The messenger was sent to ask the people of Easthaven for information about strange disturbances in the surrounding Spine of the World , such as giant sightings and goblin raiders.

Relationships

Hallaster was a friend and student of Archdruid Arundel of Kuldahar.

History

In Eleint , 1281 DR , Hallaster traveled from Kuldahar, but was wounded and died before the doors of the Temple of Tempus in Easthaven. His dread news motivated the priest Everard and Hrothgar to plan an expedition to Kuldahar in the Spine of the World.
