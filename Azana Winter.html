Azana Winter was the lord of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Azana sought champions from adventurers passing through Eveningstar to rid the town of its drow enemies, asking them to work with Purple Dragon Constal Adamor Thellik , and War Wizards Korrex Dunhaven and Xarathas .
