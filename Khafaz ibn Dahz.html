Khafaz ibn Dahz was a trader and caravan master in Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Description

Khafaz had handsome features and appeared well groomed. Though in his mid-40s, his lean frame made him appear much younger.

Personality

Khafaz was a devious man. He often blamed others for misfortunes.

Possessions

His basic equipment included leather armor , a dagger , some darts , and a short sword . Khafaz owned a valuable brooch shaped like a camel's head and two silver bracelets. He also carried a flask that had two doses of a potion of healing .

History

Khafaz learned his skills as a teenager on the streets of Tajar . He eventually saved enough money to purchase ships to carry goods to Huzuz and send caravans across the High Desert . He was very knowledgeable with the geography of the High Desert.
