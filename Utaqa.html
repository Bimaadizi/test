Utaqa , located at the mouth of Al-Zulma River , was the northernmost of Zakhara 's Free Cities . Laying almost 200 mi (320 km) from its nearest neighbor, Hafayah , Utaqa's citizens felt on the edge of civilization, and took pride in their independence from authority.
