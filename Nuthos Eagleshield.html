Nuthos Eagleshield was a noble inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Nuthos was the patriarch of the Eagleshield noble family of Waterdeep. He lived within Eagleshield Villa on Feather Street in Waterdeep's Sea Ward .

Relationships

Nuthos' consort was Glenn Eagleshield and his eldest daughter and heir was Arundel .
