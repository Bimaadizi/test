Mulnis Lenz was a caravan leader and warehouse owner in 1359 DR .

History

In 1359 DR, Mulnis led a caravan from Waterdeep to Cormyr with a stop in Teziir . Secretly, he was transporting a gift from the Lords of Waterdeep to King Azoun Obarskyr IV .
