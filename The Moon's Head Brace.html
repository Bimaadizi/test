The Moon's Head Brace was an alcoholic mixed drink from the city of Ravens Bluff , served at the Silvermoon Playhouse in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

The Moon's Head Brace was white in color and tasted of wild mushrooms . It was strong enough to send the imbiber's head spinning.
