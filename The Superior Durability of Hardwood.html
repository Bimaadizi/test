The Superior Durability of Hardwood was a book that promoted the craftsmanship of Odd Valros , a coffin -maker from Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Contents

Odd Valros's book advertised the deluxe coffins made of hardwood . It claimed that this superior wood helped preserve corpses and protected them from vermin such as rats and maggots for thrice the time other materials did.

History

A copy of The Superior Durability of Hardwood could be found inside Candulhallow's Funeral Arrangements , or Candulhallow's Tombstones, in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
