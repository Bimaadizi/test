Althidon Alastrarra was an ancestor of the Alastrarra of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

Althidon had perished about two centuries prior to the Year of the Hippogriff's Folly, 241 DR , roughly in the year of the Year of the Barbed Wind, 50 DR . The casements of Alastrarra were Althidon's work, and the finest in Cormanthor as of 241 DR.
