Skittle , also known as the Great Collector or the Merchant of Mysteries among his furry kin, was a simple rat who prided itself for collecting great artifacts from the Flaming Fist prison and the barracks in the Basilisk Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Skittle was an unassuming male rat with brown coat.

Possessions

The vermin merchant collected various spell scrolls and thieves' tools , as well as a key from one of the Counting House secure vaults, and a single elixir of universal resistance .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the rodent Merchant of Mysteries inhabited the Flaming Fist prison in the Basilisk Gate of Baldur's Gate. The rat scurried along the nooks and pipes of the prion and collected items it thought to be artifacts. Those capable of speaking with animals could convince the Great Collector to part with some of its erasure for coin .
