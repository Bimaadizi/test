Alatian was the apprentice of a reclusive lich and arcane researcher Estera , sometime before 1372 DR .

History

During his apprenticeship, Alatian scoured countless graveyards on behest of his undead master. Wearing Alatian's Spell Shroud , folk who witnessed him in the daed of night often mistook the apprentice for a ghost , thanks to the Shroud's eery blue glow. Eventually, Alatian was rewarded for his tireless work with eternal life as a lich.
