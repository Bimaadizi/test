Windove was a powerful white stallion named after his speed by Akabar Bel Akash circa 1357 DR .

History

In 1357 DR , Akabar Bel Akash purchased Windove, Lightning , Lady Killer , and High Roll from Dimswart Manor in Cormyr .

Windove loyally served Akabar, taking him to Suzail , the Shadow Gap , Shadowdale , and Yulash , where the heroes were forced to abandon their mounts while pursuing the Abomination of Moander through Cormanthor .
