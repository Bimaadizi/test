King Dorglor was the eleventh Obarskyr monarch and a king of Cormyr . He was born in the Year of the Quirt, 110 DR , the second son of Sacrast Obarskyr and began his rule after the death of this brother Daravvan in the Year of the Biting Frost, 124 DR . His reign ended when he took his own life in the Year of the Impassable Chasm, 134 DR .
