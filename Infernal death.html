Infernal death was a rare magical poison sometimes used by Holy slayer assassins of the Flamedeath Fellowship in Zakhara .

Description

This liquid was derived from the ichor of fire elementals .

Uses

Infernal death was a slow-acting poison that caused death within 12â€“48 hours. This poison was highly reactive and was administered through spicy dishes where it could be ingested. Victims who resisted the poison's deadly properties still developed a debilitating rash that lasted for several days.

The only means of curing someone affected by this poison was by casting neutralize poison and protection from fire with a ten-foot radius simultaneously. Thus, two clerics were needed to cure the victim.
