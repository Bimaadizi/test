Aizel Treemaine was an elf wizard in Waterdeep in 1479 DR .

History

In 1479 DR, Aizel was hired by the Red Wizard of Thay Varriel to explore on her behalf a necromancer 's laboratory inside Undermountain . However, she joined the doomed Rutherford Urmbrusk 's party and disappeared.

Just after Rutherford entered a secret passage, Aizel was captured by the vampire Dayan , who used her for one of his experiments. She was placed inside a helmed horror guardian. To power it, a person had to die inside the armor and Dayan chose Aizel as a sacrifice. She was slowly dying from starvation and only Dayan could free her from the armor without killing her.
