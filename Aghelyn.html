Aghelyn was a Knight of Athalantar turned to banditry in the early 3 rd century DR .

History

They were slain by an Athalantan patrol in the Year of the Loremasters, 228 DR , which chanced upon the entrance to Lawless Castle just while Aghelyn was on watch duty; they were the one confirmed casualty on the spot.
