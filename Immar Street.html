Immar Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Immar Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's North Ward . Immar Street originated on the High Road at the border with the Sea Ward , branching east and southeast to end on Selrin Street near the eastern outwer wall of the city. Immar Street was intersected by Whaelgond Way , Thunderstaff Way , Windborne Way , Tower March , Pony Way , Black Dog Alley , Phull Lane , Sashtar Street , Watch Alley , Stallion Street , and Brassfeather Lane .
