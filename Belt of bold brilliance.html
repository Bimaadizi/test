The belt of bold brilliance was a belt found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The belt boosted the wearer's wits and intelligence, as well as their quick reflexes. It gave wide-ranging assistance to most things, especially healing magics.

History

The belt was originally of dwarf make, and may have been delivered to Myth Drannor as a gift. Whatever the origin, it ultimately ended up in the ruined House of Gems . It may have been found by the Elves of Cormanthor during the Elven Crusade in 1374 DR when they searched for the phylactery of the Red Wizard Droic Orgul there.
