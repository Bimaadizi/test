Telmyz was a priest of Vhaeraun .

History

In 1377 DR , Telmyz was one of the six nightshadows chosen for an assault led by Kâras on the main temple of Kiaransalee .

Together with the conjurer Mazeer , he was polymorphed into an insect when a sudden flood hit them, them killing Telmyz.
