Elixirs of luck were potions favored by the likes of rogues and assassins in combat. They could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

An imbiber of an elixir of luck felt a boost to their confidence and was demonstrably more likely to land a lucky or fatal blow.
