Sulmor Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Sulmor Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's North Ward and also crossed into the eastern section of the Sea Ward . The road ran east to west in between Sul Street to the east in the Sea Ward and Saerdoun Street to the west near the outer wall in the North Ward. Other roads intersecting Sulmor Street included Shield Street , Copper Street , the High Road , Ilzantil Street , Vhezoar Street , Brondar's Way , Whaelgond Way , Ussilbran Street , and Nindabar Street .
