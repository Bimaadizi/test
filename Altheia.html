Altheia was a young courtesan from the Wave District of Athkatla in Amn in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Altheia was a beautiful young woman with blonde hair and dressed in blue garments.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Altheia, still a very young woman, found herself in financial strain. Her upper class family faced hardships after the death of Altheia's father and her mother was unable to provide, leaving Altheia desperate for coin and selling herself in the Wave District of Athkatla at night.
