Rylett Mc Ginn was a busy elven wizard member of the Sorceller's Encapsulate in Daggerford circa 1372 DR .

Description

Rylett Mc Ginn appeared quite youthful, but like many other elves, his mannerisms betrayed his true age and maturity. His pale skin almost made him look sickly. He was dressed in opulent wizardly robes and had long red hair.

Personality

Rylett was responsible and overworked with the guild's mundane tasks and his personal arcane research. Rylett displayed annoyance bordering with disdain towards fighters and warriors preferring the company of fellow spellcasters.

History

In 1372 DR , Rylett Mc Ginn could often be found in the Sorceller's Encapsulate's guildhall's main chambers busy with his mundane tasks while directing people to the guild's library. He did not, however, let non-wizards access the guild's arcane knowledge and felt extremely irritated with constant interruptions and requests to enroll in the school from the folk he deemed unworthy.
