The Hall of Five Arches , also referred to as the Tall Hall to its enormous size, was the guildhall of the Builders' Guild in the city of Ravens Bluff .

Location

This building was located along Moorland Ride , in the Sixstar neighborhood of the city's Uptown District .

Structure

This large building stood a full six stories tall and was T-shaped. It was purposefully designed by the members of the Builders' Guild with the intent of showing off their architectural skills and their guild's wealth. To that end, throughout different parts of its exterior one could find relief carvings of roaring lions and soaring ravens . Its overall architectural style was what those on some worlds would refer to as baroque .

Interior

The massive entrance to the building was met with a large array of high buttressed arches and statues in its central hallway, leading to a tall desk at the far end where the guild's receptionist sat. The buildings halls were divided into rooms for various departments of the guild, such as Complaints, Public Relations, and Safety.
