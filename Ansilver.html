Ansilver the Locksmith was a shopkeeper in Waterdeep around 1367 DR .

Activities

Ansilver managed a shop in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep , where he created custom locks. Ansilver was a wealthy landowner having many holdings in the South Ward of Waterdeep and in farmlands east of the city.

Personality

Ansilver was always alert. He kept his status as a cleric of Gond hidden from everyone.
