Tamara Cymrych was High Queen of the Moonshae Isles for a brief time in the mid-to-late 3 rd century DR .

Relationships

She was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first daughter of the dynasty’s legendary namesake, Cymrych Hugh . Her mother was his second wife.

She was the sister of both her immediate predecessor, High King Warren I , and of her successor, Carrig I .

History

Tamara assumed the throne of the High Queen in Callidyrr during the Year of the Magedirge, 259 DR , after the death of her brother, High King Warren I. At the time, Cedric Riker ruled in her family's ancestral seat of Corwell as its regent, and would do so for her entire reign.

She ruled for only four years before dying of poison in the Year of the Worn Pages, 263 DR .
