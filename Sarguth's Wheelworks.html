Sarguth's Wheelworks was a wainwright shop found in Essembra in Battledale as of 1368 DR .

Description

The shop was found in the southern portion of the town right off of the main road.

Activities

In addition to making wheels, Sarguth's used an assembly-line approach to quickly repair and refit wagons , including mounting new wheels for the customer. For a hefty fee of 25 gold pieces per hour, customers could request their project be pushed to the front of the line while they waited.

Inhabitants

A fat old man named Sarguth owned the establishment.
