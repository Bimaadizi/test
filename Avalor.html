Avalor was the Treespeaker of the elves of the Wealdath , the great forest found in the realm of Tethyr .

Personality

Avalor was a powerful yet judicious leader, who instilled trust in those he worked with.

Abilities

He was quite adept at creating elaborate illusions that fooled the senses of others.

Relationships

The sagacious elf had one known child, a daughter named Liel , who was a powerful druid and force of nature in her own right.

Avalor served as a member of Queen Anais ' privy council. This appointment was seen as detestable by those in Tethyr that maintained a distinct and vicious hatred for the elven people.

History

While it was not known for sure, some suspected that Avalor was responsible for the Children's Massacre of Tethyr in the Year of Splendors Burning, 1469 DR . One survivor of the attack , who managed to remained anonymous saw that the killers had the pointed ears of elves.

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Avalor heard about a supernatural attack in Cardewton , the colony in Chult in which Liel was staying. Avalor was convinced she was murdered by her husband Declan Cardew . To get some closure, Avalor reached out Liel's former lover Harp Levesque , and the two met at the Broken Axe pub. Avalor hired Harp and his crew to uncover her whereabouts in Chult , or in the worst case scenario, bring back her remains.
