Facemaker, Tastemaker was a book penned to promote Facemaker's Boutique , a shop in the city of Baldur's Gate the late 15 th century DR .

Content

The book contained a collection of various fashionable and resplendent drawings of designs: from embroidered capes and wide-brimmed hats decorated with exotic materials and daring patterns to unique-looking gowns in fashions alien to the Gate.

History

Copies of Facemaker, Tastemaker , from under Figaro Pennygood 's quill , could be found in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR in Facemaker's Boutique located in the Lower City near the Manor Gate .
