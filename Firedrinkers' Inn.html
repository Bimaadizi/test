Firedrinkers' Inn was an inn found in Yhaunn in 1358 DR . This famous establishment had a pristine reputation among the traveling folk of the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Services

Firedrinkers' was the finest and most expensive inn in Yhaunn. It was fitted with luxurious furnishings that made it a popular resting place for those who could afford the expenses, which was matched by fine dining either brought directly to a patron's room or served in the ground-level dining room accompanied by music and dance.
