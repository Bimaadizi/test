Relona was the owner of an alchemy shop in the market district of Luskan in the late 15 th century.

Description

Relona had shoulder-length dirty blond hair.

History

Around 1487 DR , Relona sent her apprentice to fetch samples of the oozes that formed in the Luskan sewers.
