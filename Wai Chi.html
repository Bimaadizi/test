Wai Chi was a Shou scribe on Akari in 1357 DR .

History

Wai Chi was the scribe of Governor Ko Lei Kho . In 1357 DR, he guided Tai Ko town an adventuring party hired by Ko Mei Kho to stop the ochimo . Later that night, the ochimo appeared in the town and Wai Chi gave him a scroll with information about the adventurers. Despite this, the ochimo killed him and absorbed his soul.
