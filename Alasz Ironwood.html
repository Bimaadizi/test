Alasz Ironwood was a sarcastic horse breeder and trader in High Dale .

History

Alasz was once a high-ranking Zhentilar warrior before he settled in High Dale. When in 1358 DR a secret Zhent occupation army occupied the dale, Alasz was a member of the Council of the High Dale .
