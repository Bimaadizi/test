Reshresma was a priestess of Eilistraee during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Reshresma was known to participate in certain rites to Eilistraee within Ardeep Forest .

Relationships

Reshresma served Eilistraee under the guidance of Qilué Veladorn .

History

During one night of 1369 DR , Reshrema was one of the priestesses dancing under Qilué Veladorn's guidance in an Eilistraeean rite which was interrupted by the arrival of the Harper Dove Falconhand , who sought Qilué for a task in Scornubel . Reshresma was the first to scream at the interruption.
