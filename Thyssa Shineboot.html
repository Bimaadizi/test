Thyssa Shineboot was a lifelong Baldurian and an inhabitants of the Lower City in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Thyssa was a hin woman of middle age. She had a pale complexion splattered with freckles. Thyssa had blue eyes and hid her hair under a cloth fillet.

History

Thyssa Shineboot grew up in the city of Baldur's Gate. In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Thyssa found herself among those Baldurians in a crowd who witnessed Bastian d'Zinn , a man of means, accosting the child named Rhaneld for accidentally hitting him with a rock while playing. Bastian believed that a child playing on a street was a sign of the city's crime-ridden downward spiral.
