Bertrand Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr in the latter half of the 11 th century DR . Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles , he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty via his marriage to Yolanda Carrathal . The pair had multiple children, and Bertrand's eventual successor was their third son, Edwin .

History

Bertram ruled in Callidyrr first as king at the side of his wife, who became monarch in the Year of the Frozen Kingdoms, 1052 DR , and then as High King following her death in the Year of Three Faces, 1089 DR .

His solo reign lasted for six years before he died of natural causes in the Year of the Dawndance, 1095 DR .
