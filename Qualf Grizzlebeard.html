Qualf Grizzlebeard was a barfly in Skullport in 1379 DR .

Activities

Qualf was a barfly but secretly worked for the Gray Hands , gathering information about criminal organizations in Skullport.

History

In 1379 DR, Qualf was captured together with some elves by some Unyielding Sword mercenaries working for the slaver Lanador .
