The Knowledge-Hold was a library located in the city of Baldur's Gate as of the late 15 th century DR .

Services

The library served as a repository for historical knowledge and unique articles of written work.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Knowledge-Hold was infiltrated by the Cult of the Absolute . The cult managed to brainwash and then kill head librarian Hayley Kroe , using her sacrifice to summon a strange portal to some unknown locale. Fortunately, the cult's initial efforts were thwarted by the Heroes of Baldur's Gate . The adventurers managed to hold off the cult until the Watch could arrive, without disrupting too many priceless works.
