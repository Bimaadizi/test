Chalavon Narthiir was a male companion who worked at the Dryad's Dreams of Mosstone in Tethyr .

Description

Chalavon was slender and graceful, a strong contrast for Ardogh Stonegrip , a fellow courtesan and a mountainous man.

History

Chalavon was one of the male escorts who worked at Dryad's Dreams in the late 14 th century DR , along with several men and women who offered their services and companionship at the only festhall in Mosstone. Chalavon's skills laid in his wit and sharp mind who could out-speak a sage. He was a talented chess player who rarely lost at the game.
