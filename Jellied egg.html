Jellied eggs was an appetizer dish from the Red Wizards ' nation of Thay .

Description

Jellied eggs were served on a bed of pickled rice as a meal starter in Priador 's capital â€“ Bezantur . The dish was quite delicate and the jelly was green in color while the eggs inside were still soft with blood-red runny yolk. Each egg was encased in a green oval-shaped jelly. The appetizer was sometimes served before a main dish such as peppered gnolls' tongues .

History

As of the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Aznar Thrul , the Zulkir of Evocation and the Tharchion of Priador considered peppered gnolls' tongues to be a delicacy while jellied eggs reminded him of delicate flesh easily parted with a knife, similarly to how he experimented on his slaves' flesh.
