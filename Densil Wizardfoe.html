Densil Wizardfoe was an adventurer in Cormyr with the Magebanes company in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Densil had a strong distrust and disdain for magic items and spells , and refused to use or work alongside them.

Abilities

Lacking magical enhancement, Densil was a strong and powerful warrior on his own merits.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Densil and Floryn Spellshatter attended a party at Thistleflame Keep , held by Lord Partic Thistle for King Azoun IV 's birthday. They stayed the night in the Keep and were briefly considered suspects after Lord Thistle's apparent murder, but their refusal to use any magic ruled them out.
