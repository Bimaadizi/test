Luthland cheese , also called sharpsair , was a type cheese crafted in Luthbaern within the Border Kingdoms .

Description

Luthland cheese was a crumbly white cheese. Its flavor was very sharp, similar to that of hot mustard. It was very popular in Luthbaern and Luthbaernar consumed the cheese cut into cubes and covered in mustard .
