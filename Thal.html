"Thal" was the assumed identity of a doppelganger in Shadowdale during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

In his usual guise, Thal was barefoot and had bright eyes.

Activities

Thal spent some time as a hand at the Old Skull Inn , and sometimes led travelers into ambushes under the pretense of taking them to see Elminster Aumar .

Relationships

Thal worked with a number of other doppelgangers.

History

When in early spring of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the Bloodshields came to Shadowdale , Thal was asked to lead them to Elminster, whom they sought. Thal led them to a cave where another doppelganger pretended to be Elminster, and a number of other doppelgangers had an ambush prepared; the Bloodshields did not survive.

A later attempt to ambush a number of War Wizards with the same trick failed, however, and Thal was held for questioning by War Wizard Lemmeth .
