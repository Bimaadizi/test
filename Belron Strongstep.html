Belron Strongstep was a commoner from the Rivington district of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Belron was a gold dwarf with a dusky complexion. He wore his dark brown hair under a skullcap and facial hair shortly-trimmed. He had dwarvishly bushy eyebrows and hazelnut eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Belron Strongstep could often be seen conversing with a fellow commoner Arne Jacobson on the streets of Rivington, discussing the Cult of the Absolute and the refugee crisis that affected the Outer City .
