The Qadim Hills were a grouping of hills in the Troll Mountains of Amn .

Geography

These were the western hills of the Troll Mountains, beginning north of Trollford and running until the Greenfields . The hills were relatively steep, and some of the waters composing the Esmelflow began atop them.

Inhabitants

Goblins used to inhabit these hills, and they warred against the trolls of the mountains.
