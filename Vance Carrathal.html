Vance Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr in the mid–13 th century DR . Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles , he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty and was the first son of his predecessor, King Cedric . His eventual successor was his own son, Teldin .

History

Vance assumed the throne in the Year of the Blue Dragon, 1243 DR , after the death of his father. He ruled for twenty-three years before he died of natural causes in the Year of the Leaping Frog, 1266 DR .
