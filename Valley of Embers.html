The Valley of Embers was a barren lowlands situated within the jungles of Chult . The valley floor was covered in lava flows that spewed out of the Peaks of Flame . The sky above was choked with clouds of ash and dotted with blazing cinders.

Notable Locations

Inhabitants

The predominant creatures that could survive within the valley were mud and steam mephits .
