Turmeric was a flowering plant, related to ginger , that was harvested for its thick fleshy roots that were used as a type of spice .

Availability

Throughout the Realms, turmeric was distributed via Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue and was valued at 25 gold pieces per 1 ounce (28 grams).

The main location in the Realms that exported turmeric was Anauroch .

Usage

Turmeric roots were known to have some medicinal properties.

The Bedine women of Anauroch used turmeric, along with tanbark , as makeup and sunscreen, while the wider population of the Realms used them as dyes . While their price was relatively low, their availability was intermittent.

Turmeric was a common spice used in Turmish .

Well-prepared travellers in South and East Faerûn often carried a variety of spices and flavorings, including turmeric.

Turmeric could be mixed with dwarven deepfire brandy to make a substance that surface-dried as a clear coating (such as on metal), but when it dried completely after a few more hours, it disintegrated to ocher-colored turmeric powder once more. It could be used to surreptitiously mark items and those who handled them by the bright stains it left behind.
