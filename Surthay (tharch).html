The tharch of Surthay was a region of Thay north of the Thayan Plateau , just beyond the First Escarpment . It served as a strong, military bulwark against the forces of nearby Rashemen , as well as the launching point for invasions into the cold, enigmatic lands of northeast Faerûn .
