Jhorrgel , who was nicknamed the Black , was the master of the Old Lodge in Thasunta in Rashemen in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR .

Activities

Jhorrgel was a renowned Rashemaar berserker who had mastered the fighting styles of three other berserker lodges before joining the Old Lodge. Given his fierce reputation, Jhorrgel was surprisingly perceptive and patient.
