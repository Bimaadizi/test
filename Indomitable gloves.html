Indomitable gloves were enchanted items that bolstered the wearer's defenses. They could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The gloves granted the wearer an uncanny ability to block attacks and defend themselves. The wearer could reliable halve the number of injuries they might have otherwise accrued in a battle.
