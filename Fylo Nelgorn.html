Fylo Nelgorn was a Zhent diplomat in Nesmé sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

Fylo was a priest of Bane who led the Zhentarim delegation in the reconstruction of Nesmé in the late 1480s or early 1490s DR . Secretly, however, Fylo went to deal with the fire giant Gundahella . Gundahella promised the liberation of a Zhent agent prisoner inside Ironslag fortress in exchange for support from the Zhents in stopping good groups like the Lords' Alliance for interfering with research on a fragment of the Vonindod .
