Khyssoun Ammath was a High Mage during the Fall of Myth Drannor .

History

Khyssoun was born in −323 DR .

In 667 DR , Khyssoun was chosen as one of the members of the early Council of Twelve .

He fought during the Weeping War and in a decisive battle in Ches 3 – 5 of 712 DR at Dlabraddath , Khyssoun followed the Spell-Captain Paeris Haladar , leading four companies of Akh'Faer and Akh'Velahr to attack the Army of Darkness , believing he could end the war with a decisive victory. Instead, they found a trap and were all killed, including Paeris and Khyssoun. However, the Army also suffered a great loss.

Relationship

Khyssoun was the Elder of the Ammath clan and cousin of the Lord-Speaker Puorlass Ammath .
