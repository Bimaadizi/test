Flails of the Vortex were enchanted weapons wielded by air elemental myrmidons when they were summoned to the Prime Material plane .

Powers

Flails of the Vortex' carried powerful +3 enchantments that also discharged powerful lightning strikes onto their targets with each successful swing. These weapons also had a chance of stunning their targets with a successful strike.
