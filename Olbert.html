OccupationMage

Olbert was a mage at some point before the 8 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Laughter, 761 DR , the sage Beldrune , working at the Moonshorn Tower , opined about a certain Olbert's triple-delayed blast fireball spell. Beldrune noted that Olbert claimed to have created that spell by combining the work of Iliymbrim Sharnult , Lhabbartan , and a third person whose name he could not recall at the moment. This indicated that Olbert was aware of both their works.
