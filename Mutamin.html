Mutamin was a gnome mage , possibly mad, who resided in an area known as Mutamin's Garden north of the halfling village of Gullykin .

Possessions

Mutamin wore an enchanted adventurer's robe and was armed with a plain quarterstaff . He usually kept two potions of healing , a scroll of horror and a scroll of stinking cloud handy.

Activities

The gnome had trained the local basilisk population to attack and petrify wandering humanoids, but not to eat them, as Mutamin wanted to keep the statues of their victims as "art".

History

Mutamin was said to have died in 1368 DR at the hands of adventurers.
