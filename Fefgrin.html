Fefgrin was a seller of maps in the Delimbiyr Vale in the Savage Frontier in the late 14 th century DR .

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Fefgrin may have sold a map of the road between Loudwater and Shining Falls to an adventurer for 25 gp .
