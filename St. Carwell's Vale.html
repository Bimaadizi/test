St. Carwell's Vale was a tiny village hidden in the Hamlar Hills of Amn .

Geography

The village was hidden in a grassy vale amid the western Hamlar Hills towards the coast, northwest from Mount Speartop .

Notable Locations

A wood and stone sanctuary with a mineral water spring lay in the middle of the village, a shrine to Eldath overseen by Baniol the Peaceful . St. Carwell's Hospice was a small hospital operated by Baniol's friend Rhynda , a priest of Ilmater . Between the two of them, the Vale was a refuge for any who sought it.
