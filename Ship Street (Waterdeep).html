Ship Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Ship Street connected Fish Street with Dock Street in Waterdeep's Dock Ward . It branched south from Fish Street to meander south toward the waterfront and end on Dock Street. Other roads connecting with Ship Street included Aline's Way , Keel Alley , Odd Street , Presper Street , Shipwright's Square , Shrimp Alley , Siren Street , and Sternpost Street .
