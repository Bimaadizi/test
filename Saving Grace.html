Saving Grace was an enchanted shield that was long associated with the followers of Bane .

Powers

As of the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the shield possessed a +3 enchantment .

History

While Saving Grace was once a very powerful shield its powers waned following the death of Bane during the Time of Troubles .

At some over the next decade, the shield found its way to the abandoned temple of Amaunator located beneath the city of Athkatla .
