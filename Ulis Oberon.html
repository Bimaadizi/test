Ulis Oberon was a member of the Oberon patriar family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the Absolute crisis of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Ulis Oberon was found dead, alongside the bodies of Malghar Caldwel and Flaev Linnacker , within the territory controlled by the Guild and guarded by its thugs, near the entrance to the Guildhall in the Lower City . All three patriars were nothing but charred remains after the Guild failed to collect the trio's debts.
