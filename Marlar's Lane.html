Marlar's Lane was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Marlar's Lane was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . The road branched west off of the smaller Aka Way , running parallel to Tharleon Street to the south before terminating at a dead end.
