History

Tresk came to be sold into slavery when he was betrayed by his apprentice. Tresk had also previously betrayed his master.

He was imprisoned, brought to Mantol-Derith in the Underdark by Hadrogh Prohl , and sold to Xandra Shobalar in 1348 DR . He was kept by her in captivity in Menzoberranzan for two years. During his time in captivity, he learned a great deal about the magic of the Underdark.

Description

Tresk was muscled, tall, and had a sallow complexion. He had green eyes and a narrow nose. He was bald except for a long thick grey beard and had tattoos on his head that served as magical runes. He was sixty years old in 1348 DR.

Relatives

Tresk's father's father was from Rashemen . His mother was a Mulan .
