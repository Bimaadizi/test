Tenrax was the seldom-seen leader of the Nugha tribes in T'u Lung in 1357 DR .

Activities

Tenrax was the kio ton mu (witchdoctor) leader of the horsemen of E'sang Yi in the hill country south of Chunming in Ausa Province . He performed ancient rites and possessed ancient and dangerous knowledge. Tenrax spoke for the Nugha at the court of Wai .
