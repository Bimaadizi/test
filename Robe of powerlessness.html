The Robe of Powerlessness were a set of cursed robes that robbed the wearer of their strength and smarts.

Description

Looking like any other set of enchanted robes , they only revealed their cursed nature when donned.

Powers

Once someone had donned the robes, usually due to the belief that they professed a boon to the wearer, the cursed activated and stripped the wearer of their strength and intelligence. They would forget all spells and other magical knowledge, and while the robes were easily removed the curse would linger forever unless the spells remove curse and then heal were cast and in that order.
