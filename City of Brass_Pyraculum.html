The Pyraculum was a district within the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire .

Description

This section of the City of Brass held the City Market, which was larger and more upscale than the bazaar of the Avencina district. Many of the major canals of the city passed either through or by this district, including the Great Canal , Munhani canal , the Naranj canal , and the Riqaq canal .

Trade

Out of all the markets within the City of Brass, the bazaars within this district offered the most diverse array of high-quality goods.

Inhabitants

Many craftsmen and traders were found in the Pyraculum, as well as artisans of many races.
