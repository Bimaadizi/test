Halfana al-Yamini was an oathbinder genie who worked for the Everlasting in Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Activities

Halfana was summoned when eight rafiqs prayed to Hajama , and the lamp of devotion was filled with rich, and possibly magical, incense and lit. A da'i set the conditions of an oath for a new supplicant, and Halfana sealed the obligation.
