Irbryth Authamaun was a human sage inhabiting Waterdeep in the late 14 th century DR . He knew just about everything there was to know about Waterdeep.

Activities

Irbryth specialized in the lore of Waterdeep, with a special emphasis on the city's broadsheets and history.

Home

He lived in a house in Waterdeep's North Ward , on the north side of Sashtar Street opposite the Thann Villa . This house served as his home and office.
