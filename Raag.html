Raag was a member of the Reghedmen barbarian tribe of the Tribe of the Wolf in the 13 th century DR .

History

Raag lived some years before the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR . His son, Angaar , was among those who joined the barbarian King Wylfdene of the Tribe of the Great Wyrm in his crusade agains the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale .
