Candlekeep Chandlery was a shop in Baldur's Gate that sold beautifying products and candles, both mundane and magical in nature. The shop was located in the Seatower district of the Lower City .

Services

Beyond cosmetics and soaps, the establishment's most famous product was its whispering candle . It was crafted with a small fire mephit place within its tallow. As the candle burned, the mephit repeated back any phrases or noises it heard while the candle was being made.

These candles were quite popular among a variety of folks, including the Baldurian patriar , who used them for their parties, as well as groups like the Watch , the Flaming Fist and the Guild , who used them for discreet communication.
