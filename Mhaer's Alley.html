Mhaer's Alley was a hard-packed dirt and gravel paved, short alleyway turned street, in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This street ran north off of Spendthrift Alley and terminated on Wall Way .

Notable Locations

Mhaer's Alley was host to a great variety of shops across both of its sides, all along its length. Including one of the greatest concentrations in Waterdeep of shops that sold cord, rope , thread, wire , and trimmings for clothing.
