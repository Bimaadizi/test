Rikmo Naquander was a Master Thief of the Shadowmasters of Telflamm in 1370 DR .

Personality

Rikmo had great attention to detail. He concentrated on understanding the workings of the city-state's government and considered getting onto the merchant council in future. Rikmo was ambitious but friendly to his friends. He was wise enough to understand that for now Keshna Finlothleer was a better leader for the guild.

History

Rikmo joined the guild in 1358 DR and was soon appointed as an asset in the dealings between the guild and the merchant council.
