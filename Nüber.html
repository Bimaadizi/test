Nüber was a rather annoying man who lived on the Sword Coast during the mid—14 th century DR .

Description

His boot size was six rods and a jackdaw.

Personality

He had the habit of repeatedly asking inane questions and sharing non sequitur statements about his life.

History

On one occasion, Nüber saw a rider on a horse .

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Nüber went searching for Kayler in order to join the Shining Crusade with the hopes of getting a new pair of boots. He made it at least as far as Dead Man's Pass .
