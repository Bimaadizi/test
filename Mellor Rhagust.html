Mellor Rhagust was a weaver inhabiting Waterdeep , the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Mellor was the public spokesperson for the Most Excellent Order of Weavers and Dyers circa 1357 DR . He worked out of the House of Textiles , which was their headquarters on Nethpranter's Street in the Trades Ward .
