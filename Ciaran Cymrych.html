Ciaran Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid-to-late 8 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the son of his predecessor, High King Declain . His successor was his cousin, Ardan Cymrych .

History

Ciaran assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Snow Sword, 762 DR , after poisoning his father.

He ruled for eleven years before he was killed by an angry mob during the Festival of the Spring Equinox in the Year of the Aurumvorax, 773 DR .
