The Inn of the Downstream Run was an inn and tavern located in the Northbank of Silverymoon , near the River Rauvin and the Moonbridge , in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Location

The inn stood on the southern end of Brightbuckler Street , just one door away from the riverbank.

Interior

If a visitor were to mention one thing of note about the Inn of the Downstream Run, they would surely mention the driftglobes that floated above the common and dining rooms, shedding their lights at the dinners. The magic globes changed colors when commanded by their mage creator or the inn's staff. However, the Downstream's driftglobes also radiated warmth and emitted certain scents that were controlled to enhance the guests' experiences.

Services

The Inn of the Downstream Run offered its guests food, drink, and lodgings.

History

In the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , the Inn of the Downstream Run offered special rates to the guests visiting the city and attending the Festival of Mielikki , held annually in Mielikki's Glade .
