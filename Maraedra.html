Maraedra , also known as "Mother Maraedra", was the keeper of a brothel called The Willing Smile in Suzail , Cormyr , in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Rather than alluring, she was motherly.

Relationships

She was acquainted and on good terms with Elminster Aumar .

History

Maraedra allowed Elminster and his relative, the coinlass Amarune Whitewave , to enter the Willing Smile in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , providing them with a table where they could converse.
