Sunset dagger , as well as its sibling, Sunset stiletto , were powerful artifacts , daggers imbued with the power of the light, found in Barovia within the Domains of Dread in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The Sunset dagger was empowered with piercing rays of sunlight to battle the creatures of the dark. When a Sunset dagger and a Sunset stiletto were wielder together by a capable rogue , both artifacts, as a set, increased the damage dealt and boosted the wielder's healing. Whilst the wielder was in Barovia, the weapons also increased their damage resistance and boosted the speed of movement. Additionally, all the magical abilities listed above were boosted even further during the sunset and night hours.

History

These artifacts might have had been recovered from Castle Ravenloft , after a band of adventurers faced Strahd von Zarovich in battle. Initially, the artifacts were dimmed, and their sunrays-given power had to be unlocked.
