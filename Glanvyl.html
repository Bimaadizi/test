Glanvyl was a relatively unknown wizard who lived in Scornubel during the mid 11 th century DR .

Possessions

Glanvyl's workbook was his most notable possession. It contained several common spells in addition to various notes and experiments conducted by the mage. His workbook even included a druid spell which was probably copied erroneously.
