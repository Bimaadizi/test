Belvedere Huntsilver was a Cormyte nobleman of House Huntsilver in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Belvedere was an aged human man with white hair and muttonchops. He carried a cane with a large knob at the end.

Personality

Belvedere was a gruff and severe man, concerned heavily with the reputation of House Huntsilver, but admitting that Oriphaun's health came first, as did fighting the enemies of Cormyr.

History

In the late 15 th century DR, Belvedere Huntsilver, accompanied by the footman Antone Corluk , came to Eveningstar to find his wayward nephew Oriphaun Huntsilver , who had taken up the lifestyle of a treasure hunter and adventurer . He hired a team of competent-seeming adventurers to find and help his nephew, despairing as they followed Oriphaun through Wheloon Prison , the troubles in Druid's Deep , the battles on the High Road , and finally into the Storm Horns where Oriphaun clashed with Archmage Amskar .
