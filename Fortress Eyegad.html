Fortress Eyegad was a re-claimed fortress, located in the Spine of the World , that was inhabited by the Skortchclaw tribe during the 14 th century DR .

Activities

The Skortchclaw orcs used a group of enslaved goblins to mine mithral beneath its structure.

History

It was originally constructed and occupied by dwarves , but by the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR it had been taken by the orcs.
