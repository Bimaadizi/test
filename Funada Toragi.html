Funada Toragi was a kuei in Nakamaru around 1357 DR .

History

In life, Toragi was the half-brother of Funada Kazuo and the uncle of Funada Mitsuhide and Funada Toyoo . He was falsely accused by Kazuo of a plot against the shogun and executed. In truth, he had agreed to serve the Goshukara clan . However, he rose as kuei in order to prove his innocence.

Personality

Toragi wanted desperately to have a body again in order to prove his innocence.
