Mishann , also seen as Mithann , was a cleric of Amaunator in Bryn Shander in Icewind Dale in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Mishann was an adventurer from Cormyr . When she became too old to fight evil, Mishann retired to Icewind Dale. However, she did all she could for good-hearted people of Icewind Dale.

In the Year of the Awakened Sleepers, 1484 DR , facing multiple threats to the dale, Mishann recruited adventurers to perform some missions for her.
