A kupuk sled was a type of sled used on the Great Glacier .

Description

A kupuk sled was constructed similar to a dog sled . However, it was wider, at about 3​ to ​4 feet (0.91​ to ​1.2 meters) wide and shorter, at about 5​ to ​7 feet (1.5​ to ​2.1 meters) in length. It was pulled by a single kupuk and could transport loads of up to 1,000 pounds (450 kilograms).
