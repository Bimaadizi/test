Ephemerals , also called ephemeras , were a class of outsider native to the Plane of Shadow . It was a diverse group of creatures in form and abilities, with few traits in common beyond their origin and composition.

Description

Ephemerals had no unifying form beyond the fact that all were partially or wholly composed of shadowstuff .

Personality

Both dusk beasts and ecalypses were generally neutral in outlook, while umbral banyans were predatory and vicious enough to typically be evil.

Ecology

Ephemerals were native to the Plane of Shadow , but were often found on the Material plane as well, either from slipping through open portals or from being deliberately brought over as guards, mounts, or hunters.
