Glenraugh was a settlement located in Sarifal on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles as of the late 15 th century DR .

Geography

Glenraugh lay in Myrloch Vale along the route from Karador to Crane Point .

History

In the late 1460s DR, a plot was devised by Lady Valeanne to smuggle the nine-year-old heir of Sarifal, Amaranth , out of the realm by first taking her to Crane Point. Leaving Karador before dawn with the girl in tow, the conspirators rode their horses as hard and fast as they could, stopping along the way only in Glenraugh for food and fresh mounts , including a pony named Flower for Amaranth.
