Nimbul was a bounty hunter sent by Sarevok Anchev to collect the bounty on the head of Gorion's Ward in Nashkel .

Description

The bounty hunter had a somewhat odd personality. He was a minor mage, and could cast basic spells .

Abilities

Nimbul knew how to cast the spells horror , magic missile , and mirror image .

Possessions

Nimbul owned a Topsider's Crutch , a pair of Senses of the Cat boots , and a scroll of find familiar . He fought with throwing axes and an enchanted short sword +1 .
