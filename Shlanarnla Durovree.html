Shlanarnla Durovree was the ninth ruler of the Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate , and Westgate's first and only Queen.

Activities

Shlanarnla was engaged to King Ilphael . The throne passed to her when he died without naming an heir in the Year of Many Monsters, 1233 DR . Queen Shlanarnla would rule until the Year of the Struck Gong, 1236 DR , when she abandoned the throne.
