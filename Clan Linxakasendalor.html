Clan Linxakasendalor was one of the dragonborn clans of the nation of Tymanther .

Description

Members of Clan Linxakasendalor used copper owl -shaped piercings in their faces as their distinctive clan piercings.

History

Clan Linxakasendalor had a prominent role in the defense of Tymanther during the invasion by the ash giants in 1479 DR .
