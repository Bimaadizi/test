The White Peaks were a range of mountains located in the Moonsea North region of North Faerûn .

Geography

The mountains divided the barren, desolate Tortured Lands in the north, from the grassy steppes of the Ride to the south.

Geographical Features

The most notable mountain of the White Peaks range were Whitehorn , the peak that lent its name to a local mining town at its base, and Kossuth's Ire , the long-dormant volcano.

Two notable streams flowed down from the southern slopes of the mountains, the Amaratha Stream , and the Crescentflow that snaked into the Border Forest .

Trade

Circa the mid–14 th century, Melvaunt sponsored the constructions of mines in White Peaks.

History

In the Year of Deep Water Drifting, 1480 DR , the planar fissure to the Elemental Chaos opened up, casing Kossuth's Ire to erupt after years of no activity.
