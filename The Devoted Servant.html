The Devoted Servant was a magical katana crafted in Wa for the shogun 's most loyal samurai .

Description

It had a golden hue and a blade wrapped with red cloth.

Powers

It was a +1 katana that inflicted additional damage of a divine nature.

History

By unknown means, one of these swords surfaced in Faerûn circa 1374 DR .
