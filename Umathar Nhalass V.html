Umathar Nhalass V , who was nicknamed the Blade of the Plains , was the eighth Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Umathar inherited the throne when his father, Darthen II , died in −691 DR . He ruled until his death in −662 DR .

Relationships

His secondborn son and heir was Rhendar I .
