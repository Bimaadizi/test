Wildroot was a variety of flora that could be found in the jungles of Chult . It was known to have medicinal properties.

Ecology

Wildroots could be found growing in groups of up to six.

Uses

The juices of a wildroot could neutralize poisons and infections when introduced to the bloodstream. Usually, this was accomplished by rubbing the root directly into an open wound. Each root contained sufficient juice to clean one injury.

History

The druid Arvo of Ravens Bluff noted the curative properties of wildroot in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Wildroots were grown in the gardens of Nangalore and could be found in Omu .

As of the late 15 th century DR, a whole wildroot sold for 25 gold pieces in Chult. Their sale in Port Nyanzaru was governed by Jessamine , the merchant prince who oversaw the sale of local plants.
