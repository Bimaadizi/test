The Gallery of Angels was a cavern deep within the Labyrinth . It housed eight fallen, petrified angels .

Description

The ceiling of the cavern was some 60 feet (18 meters) high; the floor was uneven and broken. A living cave, it had abundant stalactites and stalagmites dripping water. The statues were in various poses of fear, suffering, or longing: some covered their faces, some clawed at them, some gazed at the ceiling longingly. All were dotted with lichen and mineral deposits.

Geography

The cave was located approximately 48 mi (77 km) away from the cavern that housed the Maze Engine .

Inhabitants

The angels were Anaya , Baatral , Harajin , Lorabelios , Nemevon , Silnia , Tamiel , and Zarod .
