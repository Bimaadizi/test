Baerimel Dunnath was a wizard in Thentia in 1367 DR and in 1373 DR . She was deputy of Sureene Aumratha , of the House of the Moon .

Description

As of 1373 DR , Jannatha and Baerimel were petite and impishly pretty.

Activities

Together with her sister Jannatha Goldenshield , they used to play tricks on people for fun, such as pretending they were waifs and wandering into adventurers' camps to see what sort of treatment they got, as of 1367 DR . As of 1373 DR , they assisted Sureene Aumratha and the mages of Thentia in decoding Sammaster 's notes and ferreting out the traitor in their ranks. That same year, they investigated Kur-Tharsu in Thar over rumors of increased activity around the stronghold. This prevented them from meeting the Hero of Daggerford .

Relationships

She was the sister of Jannatha Goldenshield, and the cousin of Sinylla Zoranyian .

History

Baerimel Dunnath helped Taegan Nightwind by casting lie-detection spells on the other mages aiding the effort to decrypt Sammaster 's notes on the Rage of Dragons in 1373 DR . She also helped in the final battle against Phourkyn One-Eye 's impersonator.
