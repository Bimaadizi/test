Tirdir was an inhabitant of Athkatla who became a victim of adduction in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Tirdir was a human man with light skin and light brown hair.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Tirdir fell victim to Am-Si and his cronies: Camitis and Reti , who began kidnapping and extortion racket in Baldur's Gate. Tirdir was kept hostage until his family paid the ransom, after which Am-Si knocked the man out and buried him in a freshly dug grave in Athkatla's Grave District . Sethle , a gravedigger, was on Am-Si's payroll and allowed the criminals to dispose of the abductees, never to be found. By chance, Tirdir was found, and his life was saved. He was able to give the investigators some clues about who was responsible for the crime, along with a piece of red cloth ripped from the kidnapper's clothes. The victim was terrified for his and his family's lives as the kidnappers would return with a vengeance if Tirdir's survival were known.
