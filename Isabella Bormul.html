Lady Isabella Bormul was a silver merchant and a member of the noble patriar Bormul family of Baldur's Gate , active sometime in the 14 th or 15 th century DR .

History

Lady Isabella Bormul was a supporter and patron of the Counting House in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. She made significant donations to the bank, including seven cartfuls of Amnian silver.
