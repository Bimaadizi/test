Shadowdark ale was an ale brewed in Shadowdale with a dark brown cloudy bubbling appearance and a yellow foamy head.

Description

It had a light, bitter taste.

History

It was first served at the Old Skull Inn but was later sold in many areas of Faer√ªn , including Highmoon 's Rising Moon inn, The Silver Taproom in Essembra , and even the City of Splendors, Waterdeep . The Friendly Arm inn on the Sword Coast and the Nashkel Inn in Amn served Shadowdark for a single gold coin .

Jasper purchased a pitcher of Shadowdark for himself, Bortas , Rob , and Will at the Swamp Rat tavern in Ghars , Cormyr , on Eleint 17 in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR . It was the most expensive drink at the Swamp Rat. Among other places in Cormyr where one could buy a frothy tankard of Shadowdark was Marsember 's The Wight on a Waterdragon .
