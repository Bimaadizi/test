Black Dan was a pirate from the nation of Klocckbarn in the Whamite Isles . Black Dan represented Klocckbarn in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
