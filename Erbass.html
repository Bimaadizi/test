Erbass was a noted warrior of Raumathar during the waning years of the ancient empire. He took part in a number of the climactic battles during the Great Conflagration between the Raumvirans and the Nar , before fleeing east into the depths of the Yuirwood forest.

Possessions

During the last battles between Raumathar and Narfell , Erbass claimed Galathos , a prized blade forged at the height of their empire, for his own.
