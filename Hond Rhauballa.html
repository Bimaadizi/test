Shadowlord Demarch Hond Rhauballa was the high priest of the House of Spires and Shadows , a temple dedicated to Mask , in Westgate in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Hond was the leader of Mask's faithful in Westgate. His congregation within the temple consisted of 9 other priestesses and 21 followers.
