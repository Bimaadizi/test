The Asberyth was the seat of government in Melvaunt around 1367 DR . All city government offices operated out of this building, and monthly Council sessions were held here.

Location

The Asberyth was located in the east central section of Melvaunt.

Defenses

The government building was protected by 24 city guards at all times.
