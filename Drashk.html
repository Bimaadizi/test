Captain Drashk was a sea-captain in Waterdeep sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

Activities

Drashk was a member of the Zhentarim who sometimes acted as captain of the Lost Cause . He had an imp as advisor.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR , the Zhentarim ordered Drashk to travel in search of the Morkoth .
