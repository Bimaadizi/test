Tyche Pink was a type of blended wine , found in the Western Heartlands , along the River Chionthar , and the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Tyche Pink owed its color to a mixture of red and white wines, both were very fragrant. This blended wine was considered to be somewhat a low-class libation and nobles of Baldur's Gate only consumed Tyche Pink as a last resort.
