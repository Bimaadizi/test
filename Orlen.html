Orlen was a male human of the village of West Harbor on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR .

History

Orlen was a respected old farmer who kept his crops in good health. However, in 1374 DR , his crops were not doing good and Orlen couldn't report the problem to the druids , who would tell him what the problem was.

After the Kalach-Cha became the Knight Captain, Orlen was invited to come to the keep, to tend to the fields.
