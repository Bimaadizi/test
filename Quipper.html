A quipper was a general term for a wide variety of fish similar to piranhas that shared the trait of sharp teeth and gathering in large schools when hunting their unsuspecting prey.

Species

Ecology

Habitats

Quippers were well adapted to many environments, but typically found in freshwater. It was not uncommon to find schools of quippers even in underground lakes.

They were common in rivers throughout Chult and in the headwaters of the Styx in Pandemonium . During the time of Netheril , schools of quippers lived in the southern Channel Lock , but never migrated into the deeper Narrow Sea , the reason unknown. Giant quippers could be found in the River Dessarin .

Uses

Quipper and chips was a popular dish served at the Yawning Portal in Waterdeep in the 1490s DR .
