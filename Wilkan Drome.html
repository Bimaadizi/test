Wilkan Drome was a servant of Amaunator in the Cormyrean town of Eveningstar in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Wilkan had light skin, blond hair, and wore a brown robe.

History

After the House of the Morning was destroyed in the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Wilkan joined Azerie Germond at the ruins of the temple. While Azerie exhorted the faithful, Wilkan acted as a quartermaster of sorts, supplying them with potions of shield of faith and death ward . He also offered up rarer relics to the worthy, including the star of day , breastplate of the shining sun , signet of the shining sun , and the symbol of the shining sun .
