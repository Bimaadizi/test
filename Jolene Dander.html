Jolene Dander was a dealer in potions , spell components , and various alchemical reagents in the town of Eveningstar in the 15 th century DR .

Description

Jolene was a diminutive hin woman with curly red hair and pale skin tone. She wore a leather apron and a dress.

History

In the 15 th century DR, Jolene Dander could be seen dealing with adventurers from the world of Eberron , who set out to investigate the nearby ruins of the Haunted Halls .
