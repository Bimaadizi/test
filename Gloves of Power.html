The Gloves of Power were a set of blessed gauntlets .

Description

These gauntlets were blessed by Gut , a goblin priestess.

Powers

This pair of gauntlets was said to aid anyone who had the mark of the Absolute on their skin.

History

The Gloves of Power appeared to originally be owned by Za'krug , a goblin boss.
