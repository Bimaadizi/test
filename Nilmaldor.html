Nilmaldor was a Knight-Gallant of Ascalhorn .

After his death, Nimaldor's spirit guarded the Ascalhorn crypt in which he was buried, and even fought and defeated a wraith at one point, although this weakened him greatly. No longer able to guard the crypt, it was soon overrun by rats and giant spiders .
