Description

Although a pureblooded Northman , Laric was shorter and of darker complexion than his fellows, and walked with a slouch. His eyes were black and unfeeling. He wore a red cloak .

History

Under Laric's superb leadership, the Bloodriders came to be known as the most elite mounted warriors among the Northmen.

In the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR , Laric accompanied Grunnarch the Red to Oman's Isle to the council before attacking the Ffolk cities in the southern cities of the Moonshae Isles . There, he and his Bloodriders came under the influence of Kazgoroth and served in the Darkwalker War . He was killed by Kamerynn .
