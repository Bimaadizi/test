Flicka was a magical and powerful dagger that could be thrown at enemies.

Description

Flicka was owned by the adventuring wizard and self-proclaimed greatest mage in the Realms , Kirath . This unique weapon was found among his remains in the reaches of Xanathar 's outer sanctum. The powerful weapon had a +5 enchantment.
