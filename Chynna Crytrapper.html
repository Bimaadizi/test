Chynna Crytrapper was a priestess who represented the Church of Lliira in Crimmor during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR

Description

Chynna was a woman with white-blonde hair, known for her impish behavior. She tended to the faithful of Lliira in the Theater of Joy , that was dedicated to the deity from dawn at night.
