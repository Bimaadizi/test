Crazy Celvan was a mad inhabitant of the River District who could be found in the area's rooftop walkways in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Celvan was a pale-skinned male gnome with gray hair.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Crazy Celvan dispensed cryptic yet prophetic limericks at passers by only to disappear a moment later with a giggle. The strange little man once had a run-in with Abdel Adrian , a Bhaalspawn , and his companions.
