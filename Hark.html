Hark was the leader of a group of goblins sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

Hark and his fellow goblins lived in the Ardeep Forest . In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR the elves managed to expel them from the forest.

Hark relocated the goblins to a cave near the Nightstone village. Thanks to the cloud giants ' bombardment, Hark managed to capture the fleeing villagers.

Personality

Hark was a reasonable person. He always preferred to bargain than fight.
