Hannaah Befling was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Hannaah was an aged human woman with a light skin tone, and hair silvered with age cut in an elegant bob. She wore a simple blouse and a corset .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Hannaah Befling was a customer of the Facemaker Fashion boutique. On the day the shop's proprietor was attacked by the Bhaalist assassins, Hannaah impatiently waited for her appointment in a queue outside of the shop.
