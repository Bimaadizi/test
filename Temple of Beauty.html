The Temple of Beauty was a temple of Sune in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

This temple stood in the Sea Ward district of the city, next to Gauntyl Villa .

Structure

This was a rather lavish temple, that stood three stories tall. It was constructed of marble , gold , and silver . It contained many statues of the goddess Sune, as well as statues depicting some of her most comely worshipers throughout the history of Toril .

Services

Like many temples in the city, this structure offered healing services.

History

In the Year of the Wyvern, 1363 DR , the clergy of his temple sponsored a ship known as Goddess' Glory alongside Maxtilar Rhebbos of the House of Heroes . This ship was meant to deliver a load of precious statuary to the Temple of Beauty, but it mysteriously sank just south of the city. Unbeknownst to them, the sinking of the Goddess' Glory was caused by Kerritas Reefsilver , a member of the Cult of Ao , and two merfolk . They pilfered the statuary to place in a small temple to Deep Sashelas , situated in an underwater reef just outside of the Deepwater Harbor .

Inhabitants

In the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , this temple was led by the High Priestess Ssaeryl Shadowstar .
