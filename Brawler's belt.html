Brawler's belts were enchanted belts created by the members of the Order of the Dark Moon to improve their deadly hand-to-hand combat abilities.

Powers

There were two types of these belts: regular brawler's belts and much more powerful greater brawler's belts . Both types were enchanted to shield their wearers from bludgeoning damage; however, the greater versions of these belts were significantly more potent.

History

Brawler's belts were created by the Dark Moon sect to give its members an unfair advantage over other monastic orders in the annual hand-to-hand combat challenges. By the late 14 th century DR , there were quite a few of these enchanted belts scattered around the Sword Coast , being found in Neverwinter 's shops and around adventurers' waists, as well as in the temples of Hillsfar .
