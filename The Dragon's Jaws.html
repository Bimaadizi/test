The Dragon's Jaws was popular tavern found in Suzail in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Location

The tavern was located in the heart of the city along the Promenade right across from the royal court.

Activities

The Dragon's Jaws was a famous place to hold meetings and business dealings. A fair amount of gossip could be learned here as well. Both the food and wine selection were praised by Suzailans. The tavern was owned by a gnome named Gnorm .

History

The tavern acquired its current name when a wizard from Thay met their end here after picking a fight with a gold dragon disguised as a woman.

Rumors & Legens

Secret tunnels and passageways connected the tavern with the court and palace.
