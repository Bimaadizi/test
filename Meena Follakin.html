Meena Follakin was a healer and priest of Amaunator during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Meena was a human woman with a bob of white hair. She wore a suit of chain mail decorated with the symbols of Amaunator.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Meena worked at the Eveningstar War Hospital in treating the wounded and sick. She assisted fellow priest Anazar Narroun in investigating the strange events around Peron Uthe 's return to Eveningstar, and directed aid to the hospital where Peron had transformed into a red musk zombie and was spreading the monstrous infection to others. After the hospital situation was addressed, the investigation moved onto Uthe Lodge , where the healer Eolynn Arva had spotted an unnatural overgrowth of vegetation.
