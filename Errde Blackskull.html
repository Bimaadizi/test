Errde Blackskull was the duergar commander of the Stone Guards in Gracklstugh in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Errde's main concern was the growing corruption in the city's governments. Errde knew even her guards were ineffective in uncovering it.

History

Errde concentrated her efforts on capturing the derro courier Droki , who she believed was a crucial pawn in a conspiracy of the Council of Savants .
