Lorabelios was a fallen angel in the Labyrinth in the Northdark in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The gods condemned Lorabelios and seven other fallen angels to remain as stone statues in the depths of the Labyrinth for unknown crimes.

Personality

Despite its situation, Lorabelios had hope.
