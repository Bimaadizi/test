Owlroost Head was a hill in the Zehoarastria region of Amn and the namesake of the Owlroost Head Inn in the town of Purskul .

Description

Despite the inn's location, Owlroost Head hill stood many miles east of Purskul.

History

The hill's name was used as a password to enter the inn's clandestine meetings of the Many-Fanged Serpent secret society. With time, the password became the inn's name, and the Many-Fanged Serpent was thought to had been dissolved by the late 14 th century DR .
