Arghel was a swordsman of Athalantar under Helm Stoneblade during the early-to-mid 3 rd century DR . He spent some time as a bandit.

Activities

Arghel spent a number of years as a bandit during the rule of the Magelords .

Relationships

He was the brother of Baerold , a fellow bandit.

History

In the Year of the Black Flame, 229 DR , during the month of Hammer , Arghel was part of an ambush on some Athalantan soldiers, in a bandit team composed of himself, Baerold, Sargeth , Engarl , Nind , and the disguised prince Elminster Aumar . They slew a dozen armsmen in the cold.
