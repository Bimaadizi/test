The cloak of the forest's blade was a woodsman's cloak found during the reclamation of Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

It was made of dark green-brown patterns that resembled leaves.

Powers

The cloak aided those that fought in melee combat, helping them strike faster and knock their foes off-balance.

History

The cloak was once used by the elven rangers of pre- Weeping War Myth Drannor as they patrolled the Cormanthor for threats. It was eventually found in the hoard of the fang dragon Nylerian , who laired in the nearby Spiderhaunt Wood while preying upon elves.
