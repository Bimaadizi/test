The Fanebar was a corduroy-log paved, short alleyway in the northern section of the Sea Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This alleyway ran from northeast to southwest, coming off of Trollkill Street and terminating at Skulls Street . Towards the middle of the Fanebar a narrow passageway opened off its eastern side into Rook Alley .
