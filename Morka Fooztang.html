Morka Fooztang was a hermit and wiseman who lived on Noko-Ji Peak in Tabot .

Fooztang was one of only five wisemen recognized by the monks of Tabot around Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) as being a true oracle, being capable of predicting the future and raising heroes from the dead for good causes.
