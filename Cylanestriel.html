Cylanestriel was an elven ranger and celebrated hero that ventured the Realms circa the late 15 th century DR .

History

At some point during the 1480s DR, Cylanestriel was abducted by the Cult of the Dragon and taken to their fortress at the Well of Dragons . She somehow managed to free herself from imprisonment but was seriously injured during her escape.
