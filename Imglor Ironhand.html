Imglor Ironhand was a slayer-for-hire in Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

He was excellent at assassination, and at making things resemble accidents.

Activities

He was a high-rolling slayer-for-hire.

Relationships

He had been hired by Manshoon , who used spells to share in, see and hear what he saw and heard.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the vampire archmage Manshoon hired Imglor for a considerable sum in order to find the master of the Blueflame ghost revealed during the disastrous Council of the Dragon . The man spied on Lord Harkuldragon , whom Manshoon believed might be holding one such. Imglor was eavesdropping on them plotting the murder of Khaladan Mallowfaer through an open window from a nearby roof when they witnessed the sorceress Cymmarra murdering Harkuldragon and his mage Sarrak .
