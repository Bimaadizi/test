Aeryll was a ranger of the Yuirwood in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

He was barely a year out of his Jalesh Rudra , hardly more than a boy according to Chereth , when he participated in torturing Kheil on the night of Mirtul 2 , Year of the Sword, 1365 DR .
