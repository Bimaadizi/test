Sourdough was a type of bread from the Pirate Isles , and other coastal sites around the Sea of Fallen Stars . It was available for purchase in Faerûn through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .

Description

Sourdough was made by setting aside part of a previous batch of dough to leaven the next batch. The resulting bread had a heavy consistency and a hard crust.

A loaf of sourdough could also be hollowed out and used as a bowl in feasts.

History

Sourdough bread originated in the Pirate Isles but rapidly gained popularity in the Sword Coast and the Western Heartlands in the 15 th century DR . As of that time, the town of Ayakar in Turmish had become famous for its sourdough production.
