Whyte's morningstars were enchanted morning stars originally used by Whyte's Legion .

Description

These weapons were made of some bright metal . They were surrounded by a glow.

Powers

Whyte's morningstars possessed an innate +1 enchantment. They also inflicted electrical damage and allowed users to parry with greater ease.

History

As of the late 14 th century DR , Whyte's morningstars were used in the Sword Coast North .
