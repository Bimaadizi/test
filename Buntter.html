Buntter was a builder and city wall repairman from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Buntter was a tall half-orc with tan brown hide, brown eyes, and red hair. He wore a simple worksman's tunic .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Buntter could be found in the Lower City, working on the city walls alongside Tamrin and Paddo Garrard .
