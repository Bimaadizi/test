The Well of Despair was a mythical enchanted spring found within the Weeping Desert in the Haunted Lands of interior Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Powers

The cold dark waters of the Well were believed to provide visions of the future to any who drank from it. However, as the Well was located within a mausoleum of undead rom , anyone wishing to drink from the well needed to deal with the giants first.
