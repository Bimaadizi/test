Ribsmasher was an apparently mad human male monk of Tempus .

Personality

His apparent madness was simply the excessive zeal of a monk in the service of Tempus.

History

Ribsmasher was locked up in the ogre Ghellu 's Riverguard Keep in 1374 DR . Upon being released by the Kalach-Cha and other adventurers, Ribsmasher attacked his jailers with his bare fists.

Later that year, Ribsmasher was again a captive, this time of a cult of Shar . When freed once more by the Kalach-Cha, he traveled to Crossroad Keep . Once there, he made available his abilities to passing adventurers and any rib-related smashing opportunities they might be able to offer him.
