The Jar was a small courtyard that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Jar was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . The courtyard was found on the south side of Tilman's Lane

Notable Locations

The Old Monster Shop was located on the Jar.
