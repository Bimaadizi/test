Gustofsen Eller was the most renowned planar expert in the Realms in 1357 DR .

History

In his youth, Gustofsen witnessed his entire family slaughtered by cultists of Kali , a goddess of another plane .

Around 1357 DR, Gustofsen proclaimed he had killed the goddess Kali in her layer on the Abyss . An adventuring party was assembled in order to accompany Eller to verify this.

Personality

Gustofsen was obsessed with defeating Kali. He was a somewhat arrogant individual.

Relationship

Gustofsen taught his second cousin Inata Eller .
