Bristar (called Velethuil by its elven inhabitants ) was a village of moon elves in Deepingdale , and one of the largest community of elves in Cormanthor during the late 14 th century DR . They had a strong alliance with the Dalesmen, lending their archers to the dale's militia in their shared fight against the drow .

Etymology

The name "Bristar" is a contraction of the name "Brightstar". Brightstar was the name of the clan of elves living there. The elven name for the settlement comes from their former leader, Velethuil, who was tragically killed by a forest buffalo when scouting the region to found the village.

Activities

The elves of Bristar were particularly protective of Lake Eredruie , a sacred pool of water whose waters had healing properties to elves and half-elves .
