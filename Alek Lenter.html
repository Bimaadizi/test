Alek Lenter was a member of the Xanathar Thieves' Guild in Skullport around 1370 DR .

Activities

Alek was a fence in Waterdeep before he was recruited by the Xanathar for his operations in Skullport . Alek, however, secretly spied on the Xanathar's Guild for his friends in the Shadow Thieves , who wanted know all that happened in "their" city. Alek hoped to use this situation to gain as much money as possible. If he was caught, he would be killed.

Possessions

Alek owned a dagger of venom .
