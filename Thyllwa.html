Thyllwa was an ancient dryad that resided in the ruins of Myth Drannor 's Throne of Thought around the late 14 th century DR .

History

The unfortunate dryad and her tree was in the courtyard of the temple to Labelas Enoreth , which suffered chronological fluctuations . She was quite hostile to all comers.
