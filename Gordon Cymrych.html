Gordon Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid–6 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High King Colin . His successor was his daughter, Miranda Cymrych .

History

Gordon assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Unstoppable Ogre, 549 DR , after slaying his own father, who had become infected with lycanthropy .

In the thirteenth year of his reign , Anhaern Rhyllgallohyr , Captain of the Sisters of Synnoria on Gwynneth , died and returned to life as the "Shee" (short for " Banshee Rider").

Gordon ruled for twenty years before dying of illness in the Year of Tumbled Bones, 569 DR .
