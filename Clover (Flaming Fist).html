Fist Clover was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Clover had a fair complexion with mousy-gray hair gathered in a ponytail wrapped around her head. She wore a set of leather armor as partof her Flaming Fist uniform and was armed with a deadly heavy crossbow . Clover had a flirtatious personality.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Clover was stationed in the Basilisk Gate 's barracks and prison, serving under Blaze Liara Portyr and Manip Seyama . She spent her time patrolling the barracks grounds during the Absolutist crisis.
