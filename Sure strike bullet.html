Sure strike bullets were a type of enchanted sling missile that made it hard to miss the targets.

Powers

Seemingly simple in appearance, sure strike bullets were imbued with magical currents that made them feel lighter, more balanced, and very likely to hit their targets. Each bullet was enchanted with a true striking spell, equating it to a powerful +5 enchantment.

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , sure strike bullets were used by Aurilite Stormsisters and postulants who joined the Legion of the Chimera to bring destruction on the Ten Towns and Kuldahar of Icewind Dale .
