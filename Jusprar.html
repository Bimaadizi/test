Jusprar was a Highknight of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Jusprar was a Highknight .

Relationships

During the Mirtul 5 raid on Tethgard , Jusprar was part of one of the teams, guarding the rear of Rorsorn , Kelgantor , Rorskryn Mreldrake and Tethlor .

History

Jusprar was one of the highknights under Kelgantor during the raid on Tethgard where they attempted to capture or kill the fugitive wizard, Elminster Aumar . They were not counted among the known survivors after the raid.
