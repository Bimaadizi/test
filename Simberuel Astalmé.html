Simberuel Astalmé was one of the last princes of Ascalhorn .

History

In 882 DR , Prince Simberuel Astalmé led a group of human refugees from the fallen kingdom of Ascalhorn. Attacked by vrock demons in the Turnstone Pass , the dwarves of Citadel Sundbarr came to the rescue. During the battle, Astalmé gave his life to save the Forgemaster of Sundbarr and, in gratitude, the dwarves offered the humans a home in the abandoned surface portions of the citadel and over time build and founded the settlement of Sundabar .
