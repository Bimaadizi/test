History

Kennair was inspired to take up the venturing life when a group of adventurers traveled through Beregost.

In the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , Kennair cleared out some kobolds near the hamlet of Ulgoth's Beard . With the gold he earned, Kennair bought his father a new plow.

Sometime in the year that followed, Kennair lost his life while traveling beyond Beregost. His father deeply mourned his death and wound up spending much of his time drinking at Feldepost's Inn .
