Seven Whirling Stars were enchanted shurikens also simply known as the shurikens of Mystra , favored weapon of Mystra , the Lady of Mystery.

Powers

Shurikens of Mystra possessed an impressively powerful +7 enchantment that made them deadlier and greatly aided the wielder's aim.

History

As of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , shurikens of Mystra could be purchased from the cleric of the Three named Katya at the Temple of the Three in Mulsantir .
