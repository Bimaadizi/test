Three-Seeing Eye was a tasked guardian genie who inhabited the fortress of Krak al-Niraan in 1367 DR .

Activities

Three-Seeing Eye was tasked with guarding the treasure room of the keep, preventing anyone but Jamal al-Yindannim or Yodfah al-Muluki from touching a single piece of gold . Any intruders would be politely warned that they would be destroyed if they attempted to touch anything within the treasure room.

History

The genie was a gift from the Bonfire to help protect the fortune of the Brotherhood of the True Flame .
