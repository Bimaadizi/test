Sea chips was a seafood dish from Ravens Bluff .

Description

Sea chips were made from cod that was pressed into small, flat pieces, then fried in oil , not unlike frites .

History

Sea chips were served to great acclaim at the Shark's Finn tavern in Ravens Bluff, alongside other seafood dishes such as cod stew and deviled crab , thanks to the fugitive cook Caleni .
