Rhegaer was a thief with the Velvet Hands in the early 3 rd century DR .

Appearance

There were taller barrels than he was.

Activities

Relationships

Rhegaer became a founding member of the Velvet Hands .

History

Rhegaer was one of the thieves at the founding meeting of the Velvet Hands in the Year of the Black Flame, 229 DR . On a night not long after, he was the first Velvet Hands thief killed by the Moonclaws .
