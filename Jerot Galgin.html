Jerot Galgin was a Neverwintian aristocrat and necromancer in the late 15 th century DR . He secretly led a cult devoted to Vecna .

Relationships

Jerot concealed his life as a cult leader from his friends and family. He believed that keeping these details hidden from those close to him was another way to honor Vecna, the god of secrets.

Jerot had a loyal nothic assistant named Maszundrin who spoke Common .

History

Jerot lived all his life in Neverwinter . He secretly cultivated a profound devotion to Vecna and amassed extensive knowledge on necromancy over the years.

By the late 1490s DR , Jerot and his cult kidnapped Eldon Keyward , Indrina Lamsensettle , Sarcelle Malinosh , and Umberto Noblin , believing they possessed valuable secrets that could be drained and sacrificed to his dark god. He kept them in the Waterclock Guild's Crypts , in Neverdeath . In response to these kidnappings, Lord Dagult Neverember dispatched adventurers to rescue the victims.
