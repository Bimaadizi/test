Daglow's Inn was an inn of middling quality in the city of Neverwinter in the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

The inn was located not far from the Neverwinter Square , one of the oldest locations in the Jewel of the North. The inn's name was placed on a sign affixed over its front doors, inviting customers in.

History

Daglow's was established sometime before the 1357 DR .
