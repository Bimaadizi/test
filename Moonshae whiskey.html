Moonshae whiskey was an expensive alcoholic drink exported from the Moonshae Isles .

Availability

The whiskey was sold as early as the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , in the city of Targos of Icewind Dale , at The Salty Dog bar. The drink was among the most expensive items on the menu, sold for fifteen gold pieces by the tavern's portly proprietor Gohar . As of the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR , pricy Moonshae whiskey could be found as far south as the city of Calimport in Calimshan .
