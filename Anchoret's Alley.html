Anchoret's Alley was a small side alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Anchoret's Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . It originated on the Forcebar where it branched south through a small neighborhood, eventually ending on Anchoret's Court just north of the Specterwalk .
