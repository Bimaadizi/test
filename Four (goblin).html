Four was a goblin child that lived in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Four was quite belligerent even for a child, let alone a goblinoid one.

Relationships

She looked up to her mother, believed she was "tough as worg -meat" even before their lives were blessed by the Absolute .

History

Four was present when her companion Eight 's parents were killed at their camp's raid of Waukeen's Rest in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . She consoled her friend and avenge his parents' death by helping kick and punch the corpse of the man that killed them.
