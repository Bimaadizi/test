Larnaeril Darakh , who was also called the Fiendwitch , was a vile human woman who readily submitted herself to the lusts of the demon lord Orcus .

Relationships

The child born of Larnaeril's coupling with Orcus, a son named Jesthren , was born in −431 DR . Orcus' progeny would end the line of Orgolath Nentyarchs in Narfell and begin the Darakh Dynasty in −399 DR and continue to rule until the fall of Narfell in −150 DR .
