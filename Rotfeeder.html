Rotfeeder was a giant fire beetle that lived in the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

History

The giant bug resided in the hidden Temple to Moander around 1374 DR , accessed by a portal found in Myth Drannor during the Elven Crusade . Around this time, a cult of Moander had attempted to create a new avatar vessel for their god in the temple.
