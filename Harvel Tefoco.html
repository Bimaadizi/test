Harvel Tetoco was one of the children of Oliver Tefoco , the proprietor of the Beehive Goods and Wares general store in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate , who joined the Flaming Fists in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Harvel was a son of Oliver and Kroyce Tefoco , and had a number of siblings. The oldest of them was Bubbles Tefoco , who lived in the Stonyeyes district of the Gate with her family and the newborn Dorimara . another older sibling was Dariel Tefoco who served the Flaming Fist . Thegglis' brother Thegglis was a fellow caravan guard, and the youngest siblings Hemjax , Maggie , and others, still lived with Oliver and Kroyce as of the late 15 th century DR.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute threatened Baldur's Gate's safety, two of the Tetoco children, Blunce and Harvel, applied to join the Flaming Fist Company. According to the letter signed by Manip Seyama of the Basilisk Gate barracks, both men, Blunce and Harvel, were approved and commanded to immediately report to the Seatower of Balduran to receive gear and equipment training.
