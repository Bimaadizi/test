Bailey was one of the employees of Lady Jannath's Estate in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Bailey was a rude human servant with an olive-hued skin tone, dark blue eyes, and a head of chestnut hair.

History

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Chamberlain Mercer, the man in charge of Lady Firelia Jannath 's staff, dismissed all servants from the mansion on the mistress's orders. Mercer placed Bailey, who was tasked with shining silverware , Furbleston , Cyrilla , Jooli , Phubbs , and Chef Marjoram on temporary leave with the pay of half-wages and allowance to seek temporary employment somewhere else. The chamberlain issued a statement that this dismissal was done at no imputation of malfeasance and would be ended upon notice.

Bailey May have had been fired for his attitude in 1492 DR.
