Sherlen Miller was the commander of the militia and the constable in Daggerford around 1485 DR .

Activities

Her dual role meant Sherlen had to use two different aspects of her personality: as a militia commander, she trained her fellow warriors; as constable, she had to mediate disputes and take care of the duke's interests.

Personality

Sherlen considered it a great honor serving in the same role as her namesake Sherlen Spearslayer . She preferred her role as commander, feeling she did not suit the diplomatic role she had as constable.
