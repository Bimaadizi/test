Belnar was a person believed to be able to challenge the power of the Obarskyr royal family of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Rumors & Legends

In Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , while preparing to sneak into the Haunted Wing of Castle Obarskyr in Suzail , Marlin Stormserpent briefed the retinue he'd hired for the task by claiming, amongst other things, that the haunted wing held people who'd become a threat for their ability to challenge the Crown. When asked about Belnar, he claimed that one, among others, was there.
