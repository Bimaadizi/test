The Brown Mushroom Mercantile Company of Menzoberranzan was a fellowship created to counter House Baenre 's profits from trade in the city.

Organization

The fellowship was one of two competing merchant houses in Menzoberranzan. The company was run by House Fey-Branche , which later gave some of its power to House Barrison Del'Armgo .
