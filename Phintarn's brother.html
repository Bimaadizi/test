The brother of Lord Phintarn "Redblade" of Llorkh in the Savage Frontier owned the Worried Wyvern , the only inn in the town in the 1350s DR .

Not long after Phintarn was murdered in a Zhentarim takeover shortly before the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , his brother died mysteriously one night, and his inn was razed the very next day.
