Geography

The city lay on the coast of the Wizards' Reach on the Sea of Fallen Stars just south of the Yuirwood .

History

Lasdur was initially founded by the empire of Unther following the great wars between Narfell and Raumathar , but over the passage of time Unther lacked the strength to hold it and Lasdur eventually declared independence. Lasdur was destroyed in the Salamander War in a plot hatched by Hargid Tenslayer and his allies in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .
