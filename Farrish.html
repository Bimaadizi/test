Farrish was a priest of Lathander in Freedale during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was an old man.

Personality

Farrish was described as kind and gentle.

Abilities

He could cast spells .

Possessions

Of note, he had chain mail and a mace .

Activities

He cast spells for money at the Temple of Lathander to help with the upkeep. He would not do this for the malevolent or false, however.

Relationships

He was the master of Lordan the Pure, and would have a place of succor and healing for him as long as Lordan remained in good standing with their shared beliefs.

History

Farrish had built the Temple of Lathander in Freedale on his own, over the years.
