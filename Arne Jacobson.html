Arne Jacobson was a commoner from the Rivington district of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Arne Jacobson was a human man with a pale skin tone and a shaved head, hidden underneath a commoner's hat. His skin and commoner's garb were often dirty.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Belron Strongstep , gold dwarvish commoner, could often be seen conversing with Arne Jacobson on the streets of Rivington, discussing the Cult of the Absolute and the refugee crisis that affected the Outer City .
