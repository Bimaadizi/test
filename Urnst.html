Urnst was a potter from Kuldahar in the Spine of the World .

Description

The white haired human wore a brown tunic and grey pants.

Personality

The potter was a fearful man who stuttered .

History

In Eleint , 1281 DR , Urnst spoke of fleeing to Baldur's Gate once the weather got better after villagers were kidnapped by the lizardfolk of Dragon's Eye .
