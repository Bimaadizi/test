Tildaryn's Ride was a small roadway found in eastern Sembia that connected Ordulin in the west with Yhaunn in the east on the coast of the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Description

Tildaryn's Ride ran in nearly a straight line from west to east, extending where the East Way terminated in Ordulin.

History

The roadway's namesake was Antur Tildaryn , the man who built it. Antur was a businessman and an investor who rose from rags to riches in Yhaunn long before the 14 th century DR .
