The Fellowship of the Mithral Mountain was an order of priests dedicated to the god Geb of the Mulhorandi pantheon , active around 1370 DR . The majority of adventuring priests of Geb were members of this order.

The fellows were charged with discovering the location of a legendary mountain supposed to contain large mithral deposits.
