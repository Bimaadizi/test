Lord Randolf Vammas was a senior member of the noble patriar Vammas family of Baldur's Gate and a member of the Parliament of Peers in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Randolf Vammas was a human man with an olive complexion and red hair. He had his red beard well-trimmed and wore purple finery embroidered in gold and a wide-brimmed hat.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate, Lord Randolf Vammas was a Parliamentarian of the Gate and voted in support of crowning lowborn Lord Enver Gortash as the city's Archduke. Lord Vammas was present for Gortash's coronation that took place in the audience hall of Wyrm's Rock . Following the coronation, Lord Vammas bragged about a number of business propositions he had submitted to the new Archduke while in conversation with a fellow Parliamentarian, Lady Winstra Hullhollyn .
