Devout Cry was a lesser priestess of the Absolute that lived in the cult's fortress known as the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

She had a more calm and almost understanding demeanor, which stood in stark contrast to the other goblin cultists.

History

Cry was present at Dror Ragzlin 's ritual , when the hobgoblin warlord. attempted to interrogate a dead mind flayer brought back to the Shattered Sanctum in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . The aberrant creature was aboard a nautiloid ship that crashed near the River Chionthar , a vessel that carried an item of great importance to the Absolute.
