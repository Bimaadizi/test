Kasidikal was a mature adult black dragon and a member of House Orogoth .

She laired in the ruins of Orogoth on the High Moor of the Western Heartlands , just north of the Serpent Hills .

Relationships

She was the great-granddaughter of the black wyrm dracolich Calathanorgoth the Old One, the daughter of the black wyrms Vilholin and Wastirek , and the sister of the black dragons Woklef , Cheleen and Aswidorg .
