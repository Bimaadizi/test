Carina was a lieutenant of the Sisters of Synnoria , warriors of the Llewyrr .

Description

Carina had a high, almost musical voice.

Relationships

As part of the Sisters of Synnoria, she fought under Brigit 's command and had Maura and Aileen as companions.

History

Death

Carina died in the battle of the road of Corwell slain by Laric , captain of the Bloodriders .
