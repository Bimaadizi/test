Karlott was a cleric of an unknown religion, on a quest for atonement in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was banished from his home due to an indiscretion bad enough to warrant rejection from his fellow cultists, and was forced on a quest for atonement to his god. Should he fail in this, the powers he wielded would be lost to him.

Possessions

Korlott wore a robe of blending and a ring of protection +4 while wielding a staff of command .
