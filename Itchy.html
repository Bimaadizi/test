Itchy was one of two crawling claw undead pets kept by Lucretious , the proprietress of of the Circus of the Last Days in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Itchy was an amputated reanimated hand of a humanoid creature. It had pale skin, and its fingers were decorated with many rings .

Abilities

Like other crawling claws, Itchy had the usual strengths and vulnerabilities of an undead creature. It was unaffected by poison and possessed a blindsight .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Itchy and another fellow crawling claw named Scratchy could be found in Lucretious's tent at the back end of the area in Rivington of Baldur's Gate , where the Circus of the Last Days performed for a short period of time, before moving on across the planes.
