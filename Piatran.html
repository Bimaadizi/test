Piatran was a merchant inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He created and sold clothing, with cloaks being his specialty. Piatran could even obtain enchanted cloaks through his contacts at the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors , but he only did this for select customers vouched for by Madame Garah , Elistar Thistlebuck , or Nindil Jalbuck . His shop within the Adventurers' Quarter was called Piatran's Clothiers .

Possessions

Piatran wore a scarab of protection .
