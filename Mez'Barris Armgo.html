Mez'Barris Armgo was the matron mother of House Barrison Del'Armgo , second house of Menzoberranzan , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Mez'Barris Armgo was unusually tall and powerful. She was also brilliant and had a vicious cunning. She was a fierce warrior who loved single combat.

History

Mez'Barris became matron of her house prior to 1361 DR and remained in that position in 1372 DR .

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Mez'Barris held nominal command of the Army of the Black Spider .

Circa 1486 DR , she controlled the largest number of trained wizards and mercenaries-for-hire in Menzoberranzan.

Possessions

Mez'Barris had a pair of enchanted gloves that gave her great strength. Her weapons were magical maces named Qu'lith ("Blood") and Qu'uente ("Guts").

Relationships

Mez'Barris had five daughters. Uthegental Armgo was her patron.
