The Guts and Garters was an inn in Skullport .

Geography

The inn was located off Illithid Way in the Lower Heart district of Skullport near the Feathered Rat , the Thrown Gauntlet , and the Bindle's Blade inn.

Interior

In 1370 DR , the Guts and Garters was an inn in fair condition, but the rooms were of poor quality.

In 1492 DR , the inn was in run-down condition, with windows almost opaque with grime.

History

Around 1370 DR, the inn was owned by Bolton Brimwell , who rented private rooms to those who had coin to spend.

By 1492 DR, it was run by a sullen, soft-spoken tiefling called Quietude and was Skullport's last remaining inn. Quietude spied on guests for the Xanathar . The inn contained a secret door leading to a staircase that descended 20 feet to a magically lit rough-hewn tunnel that extended 300 feet west and connected to the Xanathar's secret dungeon lair.
