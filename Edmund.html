Edmund was a warlock who lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

Through his pact with a cambion , Edmund acquired powerful warlock powers.

Relationships

He was in love with a woman named Miriam who unfortunately got involved with the criminal known as Maggie Two-Fingers .

History

After Edmund and Miriam fell on hard times, Edmund was visited by a cambion, who offered him a way out of their dire straits. The half-fiend offered Edmund infernal powers in exchange for something in return.

Armed with the eldritch powers of a warlock, Edmund received a significant payment from a mercenary group in exchange for promise of work. He paid off Maggie Two-Fingers in full, before heading off to Icewind Dale with the mercenaries. He wrote a letter to his beloved, but unfortunately it was picked up by harpies .
