The Sword of Balduran was the magical longsword wielded by the legendary explorer Balduran .

Powers

This +2 longsword bestowed upon its wielder partial protection from magical spells and abilities.

History

Baldur's sword, along with his shield and armor , were stolen from a museum in the city of Baldur's Gate . At some point it came into the inventory of Deidre , a merchant from Aurora's Magic Emporium , who did business from the Adventurer Mart located in Amnian the city of Athkatla .
