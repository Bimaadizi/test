Naleetha was the daughter of Borold and Nalitheen in the 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Both she and her sister, Boroldira became orphaned two summers prior to 1321 DR , and were raised thereafter by their mother alone, Nalitheen . They got acquainted with Mirt the Merciless in that year.

History

Either she, her sister, or both survived into 1372 DR .
