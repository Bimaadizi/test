Estimyra was a writer and a War Wizard of Cormyr , active in the early-to-mid 14 th century DR .

History

By the Year of the Dragon, 1352 DR , Estimyra had spent twenty years serving as a War Wizard with her latest known post at the High Horn keep in the Storm Horns . She published a collection of autobiographical musings titled Twenty Winters a War Wizard in 1352 DR. By the late 14 th century DR, a copy of her work was stored within the fortress-library of Candlekeep .
