The Prayer Stone was a large granite slab in the western part of the town of Amnwater , in Amn .

Description

The Stone was once part of a full stone circle , and acted as the only permanent temple in Amnwater. It was a multi-faith temple, and prayer to any deity was permitted, provided it did not disrupt others.
