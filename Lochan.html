Fist Lochan was a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Fist Lochan was an dark-skinned human woman with brown eyes. She wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist: a helmet , a set of chainmail , and a tabard adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. Lochan wielded a halberd .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Lochan was stationed in the Rivington district of Baldur's Gate, beyond the Wyrm's Crossing fortress. During he Absolute crisis , Lochan, had to deal with Baldurian protesters, who zealously expressed their disdain for the refugees, who flooded the city, displaced by the moving armies of the Absolute. One of the protestors who gave her hard time was Saer Grotpoll , who railed the crowds, claiming that the refugee vermin horde was a danger that could over overwhelm the city and that Baldur's Gate was for Baldurians.
