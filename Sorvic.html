Sorvic was a member of the reformed Cult of the Dragon under Severin Silrajin .

Possessions

When working, Sorvic usually kept around 20 gp on his person.

History

Sorvic was a cultist and, alongside Maelgot , one of the bodyguards of Talis Kasterel , at his master's side in the Cult's occupied hunting lodge in the Greypeak Mountains .
