Alanna was a cleric from the Temple of Waukeen in Neverwinter in the late 14 th century during the years that followed the Shadow War .

Description

Alanna was an attractive woman with red-chestnut hair. She wore her church's robes and a golden circlet .

Personality

Alanna was a cheerful and pleasant cleric who warmly greeted visitors to the Temple of Waukeen.

History

Alanna arrived in Neverwinter with her church shortly after the Shadow War of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR by Lord Nasher Alagondar 's invitation. Once the temple opened its doors in the Tyr's Eye , where the city's mages' academy once stood, Alanna worked at the front of the chapel, warmly welcoming guests and answering any questions they might've had.
