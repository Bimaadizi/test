A samisen ( pronounced : / s ɑː m i s ɛ n / sah-mee-sen ) was a stringed instrument common in the land of Wa .

Description

A samisen was similar to a guitar , lyre , or lute . It was plucked.

Reputation

Among all the instruments found in Wa, none brought more respect to a musician than the samisen.
