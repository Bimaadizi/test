A wand of metal and mineral detection was a magic item that revealed the location of metals.

Powers

The effective range of these wands was 30 feet (9.1 meters). A wand of metal and mineral detection would pulse in the wielder's hand and point toward any detected source of metal or mineral. The wielder could also choose to concentrate on finding only a specific type of metal or mineral such as gold or diamond .

History

A wand of metal and mineral detection lay lost and forgotten deep in the old mines of the Dungeon of Death in 1370 DR as the situation that dark place culminated.
