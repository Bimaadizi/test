Zarghar was a playwright of some renown who authored a "grand play" called And So They Met the Gargoyle sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

History

As of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , "grand play" gained popularity in Athkatla of the Lands of Intrigue . The famous Temple District theater Crown Aflame added Zarghar's Gargoyle to its repertoire to the delight of theatergoers of Athkatla.
