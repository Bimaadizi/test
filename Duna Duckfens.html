Duna Duckfens was a duergar from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Duna wore a simple set of chainmail armor .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Duna Duckfens, along with other duergar, giant spiders , and drow , was slaughtered in the home of Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica . Duna's body was left broken and decapitated on the cabin's floor. The murder spree was performed by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal .
