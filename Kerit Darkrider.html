Kerit Darkrider was a Sembite adventurer and member of the Gold Coins company in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Kerit attended King Azoun IV 's birthday party at Thistleflame Keep in Suzail alongside his companion Garit Strongarm . They stayed the night at the Keep and were suspected of being involved in Lord Partic Thistle 's apparent murder, but proved innocent.
