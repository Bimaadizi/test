Halacar Olósynne was the fifth king of the Olósynne Dynasty who ruled the nation of Aglarond .

Activities

Halacar inherited the throne when his mother, Ulae Olósynne , died in the Year of the Killing Wave, 1257 DR . He only ruled for a few years until he was slain by agents of Thay in the Year of the Broken Blade, 1260 DR .

Relationships

Halacar's younger sister was Ilione .
