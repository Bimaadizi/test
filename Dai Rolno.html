Dai Rolno was a Morninglord of Amaunator that visited the Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR during the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon .

Description

Dai was a light-skinned human woman with orange-red hair tied into two braids. She wore a set of sun-decorated chainmail , and carried a buckler and mace -like holy symbol of Amaunator.

Personality

Dai saw Wheloon Prison as a failure of justice, especially for the innocents trapped inside.

History

Dai Rolno visited Wheloon alongside the other Morninglords Adara Semple , Karie Leddle , and Maribe Banro . There, she met Purple Dragon knight Faline Abbot , and petitioned her to officially investigate and send assistance to the Wheloon natives at Harvest Hall .
