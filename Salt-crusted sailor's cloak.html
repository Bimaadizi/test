Salt-crusted sailor's cloaks were enchanted cloaks used by sailors and pirates of the Nelanther Isles in the Sea of Swords .

Powers

Whether the sea salt 's effects of minor magics weaved into the clothing, salt-crusted sailor's cloaks increased the wearer's protection against all known diseases .

History

These sailor's cloaks were in common use in the late-14 th century DR . Notably, Captain Allendry of the Midnight Rose outfitted his crew and offered these items for sale.
