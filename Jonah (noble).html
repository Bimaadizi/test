Jonah was one of the members of the wealthy high society of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

At some point before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Jonah set his eyes and amorous ambitions on Lady Firelia Jannath , one of the city's patriars and a notable patroness of the arts. He was among suitors who fought for the lady's hand in 1492 DR , when her lover, Oskar Fevras , was presumed missing or killed, in reality, captured and enslaved by the Zhentarim smugglers.
