The Grottoes of the Nine Sages were a series of nine caves within the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 th century DR .

History

Each cave was once a shrine dedicated to one of the Nine Sacred Sages of the Pathâ€”these being Chou Zhiangpen , Ta-wan , Tai Hsueh-chi , Li Chih-wu , Yao Tsung-i , P'eng Khaisan , Hung Te Ping , Hsiao Tzu-chi , and Pieh-chi . As of the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , the caves were overrun with goblins .

Description

A statue of each Sage stood within their respective cave, usually facing the entryway.
