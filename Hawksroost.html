Hawksroost was a village found in the Moonsea North region of North Faerûn , circa the late 13 th century DR .

Geography

The village lay on the border of Thar southeast of the Ride , less than 50 mi (80 km) south of Ilinvur . Before it was left to ruin, it was fairly accessible by the nearby Ilinvur Trail and Naed Creek .

History

Sometime after the Year of the Claw, 1299 DR , Hawksroost was destroyed in a series of ogre attacks.

Inhabitants

Hawksroost was once the home of Daern , a warrior who fought alongside the Ilmatari priest Flaergon of Glister and figured highly in the tale of the Tome of Torment .
