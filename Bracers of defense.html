Bracers of defense were protective magical items.

Description

Typical bracers of defense found after the Second Sundering were hollow with an intricate golden pattern.

Powers

The power of the bracers could only be accessed if the attuned wearer had no armor and no shield , but did work with other kinds of kinds of magical protection. Their magic worked by increasing the chance of attacks (magical or otherwise) to miss the wearer.
