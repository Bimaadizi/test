Broargar "Windcloak" Iringil was a Impilturan wizard and instructor of the Art who lived in the city of Lyrabar during the 14 th century DR .

Activities

Iringil was the founder as well as one of the head teachers of the Towers of the Wind , a well-known magical academy located within his home city. He was quite proud of the school he had established.
