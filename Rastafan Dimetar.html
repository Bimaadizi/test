Rastafan Dimetar was a courtier of Cormyr in 1357 DR .

History

In 1357 DR, news arrived in Suzail that runaway princess Alusair Obarskyr was seen near Tilverton . Rastafan was one of the many envoys sent by King Azoun Obarskyr IV to the Dalelands in order to recruit an adventuring party to find Alusair.
