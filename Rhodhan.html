Rhodhan was a cleric from Pangaea who served as the second-in-command of the multi-planar Chemcheaux business for Prismal the Outrageous .

Description

Rhodhan was a handsome man with blue eyes.

Personality

Rhodhan had an abiding respect for the law, while maintaining a deep empathy for those less fortunate than himself. He was also extremely pacifistic and refused to carry a weapon or use offensive spells, resorting only to competitive arm wrestling as a last resort.

Abilities

Rhodhan was an all-round surpassing man, with exceptional physical strength and fitness as well as sharp mental faculties and a wise countenance. He was skilled in the fields of animal handling and training, ancient history, healing and herbalism, heraldry, and spellcraft. On top of all that, he was fully literate. His divine powers involved the spheres of animals , creation , divination , the elements , healing , and protection .

Possessions

Rhodhan wore a robe of protection +4 , a ring of protection +3 , and a pair of boots of speed .

Relationships

Rhodhan had a wife and seven children. His eldest daughter had three children of her own.
