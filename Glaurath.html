Glaurath , who was nicknamed the Great , was a warrior and the fourth King to rule the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Glaurath inherited the throne when the previous King, Mather , was slain by black dragons in −301 DR . He reigned until −291 DR . He served as Captain of the city guard before his rise in power.

Relationships

Glaurath was a close and personal friend of King Mather.
