Halfoles were a style of building prevalent in Luiren that were used by halflings .

Structure

Halfoles were located underground. Their size and layout varied greatly. Halfoles were used for both domestic and commercial structures.

History

Most of the rich halflings of Luiren preferred to live in a halfole compared to any opulent above-ground residence.
