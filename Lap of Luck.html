The Lap of Luck was a shrine of Tymora in Scardale Town , and the only religious building that survived the Second inter-dale war in 1356 DR .

During the occupation of the city, the shrine was run by the cheerful and popular halfling priestess Gelli Parabuck . Despite her favorable reputation, many Scardalesman felt the Goddess of Luck had abandoned their land.
