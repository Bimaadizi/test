Indesm was a noble house of Cormyr .

Bases

They were situated in Arabel .

The Indesms owned the hold of Hawkhar , which lay north-east of the Wyvernwater and northwest of Firefall Vale .

History

The Indesms supported the Lost King Gondegal in his brief insurrection in the Year of the Dragon, 1352 DR .

Following the death of King Azoun IV during the Abraxus affair , the Indesm family preferred to have Vangerdahast as regent, rather government by a ruling council, as did many families with country estates.
