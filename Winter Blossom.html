Winter Blossom was the lemur familiar of the Shou matriarch Lady Feng .

Description

The black fur on the lemur's face formed the shape of a mask around its black eyes. His hands had similar black fur, and his long tail had ringed stripes of white.

Abilities

While Winter Blossom held a special connection to Lady Feng, he could only with others by a series of chitters and growls.

History

When Lady Feng was abducted by the black dracolich Hethcypressarvil in the Year of the Wave, 1364 DR , Winter Blossom was left along in her apartment at the Ginger Palace . The poor creature was discovered by the Harpers agent Ruha , who nicknamed it "Chalk Ears". After the Feng was rescued by the combined efforts of Ruha and the lady's son, Prince Kao Chou Tang , Winter Blossom was reunited with her mistress.
