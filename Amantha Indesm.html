Amantha Indesm was a noblewoman of Cormyr and a Harper in the late 14 th century DR .

History

One early spring afternoon in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Amantha Indesm was among the guests at Haelithtorntowers for a meeting with Joysil Ambrur . When she was about to leave, she embraced her host before departing. A fellow guest, Noumea Cardellith , noticed that she'd cast a spell on Joysil at that point, and warned their host once Amantha was out of earshot. Joysil explained that Amantha did that every time, and then consented to have the spell broken.

Description

At the meeting, Amantha Indesm wore an emerald gown with bare shoulders. To Noumea, she had both the smoldering eyes of a tigress and the tinkling smile of an innocent.

Abilities

She had the ability to cast at least one spell , or else somehow lay spells on people quite discreetly.

Activities

She was a regular at Joysil Ambrur's meetings.
