The Nature domain was a set of spells that were granted by deities whose portfolio included the natural world, such as Silvanus . Clerics who chose this domain were granted free access to these spellsâ€”they could cast them at any time without having to prepare them in advance.

Deities

Spells

Novice clerics of this domain could cast animal friendship , speak with animals , barkskin , and spike growth . Those of some experience were able to use the plant growth , wind wall , dominate beast , and grasping vine spells. Master clerics of the Nature domain were skilled enough to cast insect plague and tree stride . In addition, they were able to charm animals and plants .
