Ladles were a sort of kitchen utensil used in cooking and serving liquid foods.

Description

Sharing some similarities with spoons , ladles were mainly used for measuring and serving liquids from pots like soup and stew . They were made from seasoned wood or metal, with a long handle connecting to a bowl -like cup that often had a notch, spout, or lip that allowed liquids to be carefully and accurately poured from it.
