Garion Mendocio was a human adventuring swashbuckler active in the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

Garion was a pompous and loud man who often underestimated his opponents.

Possessions

As a swordsman, Garion wielded a pair of enchanted weapons : a +3 rapier and a +2 stiletto . Other magic items in his possession included boots of striding and springing and gloves of missile snaring .

History

Garion was raised in a traveling circus where he was taught the swashbuckling skills. These swordsmanship skills remained in use and helped the man for many years after he left the circus life.
