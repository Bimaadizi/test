Maeraddyth Starym was an inhabitant of Myth Drannor in the 3 rd century DR .

History

On 261 DR , Maeraddyth Starym barged in on the Starym scrying on the court when Llombaerth Starym attempted to slay Coronal Eltargrim and failed. Though he cried for the Coronal's blood, he was talked down by Uldreiyn Starym .
