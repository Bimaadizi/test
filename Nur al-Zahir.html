Calipha Nur al-Zahir was a member of the prominent House of Zahir in Zakhara , the Land of Fate.

Relationships

Nur was the mother of Grand Caliph Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir and married to his father Achmed al-Assad . She was also the sister of Rahmah .
