The Temple of Garagos was a place of worship dedicated to Garagos in the city of Bezantur , Thay , that also served as an inn known as Santhro's Rooming House in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The temple was located just inside the western wall of Bezantur—the so-called "City of a Thousand Temples"—just north of the city's west gate . This gate made the temple convenient to caravans arriving in town from Thasselen and Thay's coastal cities.

Structure

The temple was located inside a rundown tenement building.

Services

As an inn, rooms could be rented from the Temple for one gold piece per month. Meals were only served to customers who asked nicely.

Activities

As a place of worship, the Temple catered to a small group of Garagos's faithful. Despite the congregation being small, they were enthusiastic about their god.

History

As of the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , the Temple of Garagos was one of only a few temples remaining to the god in all of Faerûn (once worshiped throughout the Inner Sea ), and even within the City of a Thousand Temples, the Reaver was not a popular god as he was widely believed to have been killed by Tempus . This forced High Priest Santhro to fight against these perceptions, and ultimately to convert the temple into a boarding house in order to make some money.
