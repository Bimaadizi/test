The Mountain of Skulls was a temple of Cyric found within the Cloud Peaks of Amn circa the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The temple was built within a nearly inaccessible cavern, 1 mile (1.6 kilometers) into a gaping gorge, some 10 miles (16 kilometers) east of the Fangs .

Structure

A skull-shaped opening formed the cave in which the temple was founded.

Interior

The Mountain of Skulls earned its name from the massive mound of skulls that lay within its center. This horrific display was treated as an idol by the local Cyricists.

Activities

The temple was overseen by Watchful Skull Tynnos Argrim , founder of the order of assassins known as the Flames of the Dark Sun . Argrim's priests were seen as heretics by the Cyricists of the Twin Towers of the Eternal Eclipse .

History

The temple was once dedicated to the god Bane , before being claimed by Cyricists during the Amnian Banedeath in the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR .

In the summer of the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Watchful Skull Argrim slew the leader of the Dark Redoubt within the temple's premises and forced its clergy to fall under his own leadership.

The Mountain of Skulls endured the decades of languish experienced by the Cyricist faith, and remained standing as of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .
