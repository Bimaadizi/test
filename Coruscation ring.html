Coruscation rings were enchanted bands that allowed the spellcasting wearer to illuminate targets with radiating orbs .

Description

These simple rings ' counters bore engravings that projected provocative lights.

Powers

The arcane radiance enchantment weaved into these items added additional effects to the wearer's damage-dealing spells . All targets that were damaged by the wearer's spells were immediately illuminated by radiating orbs above their heads that showered them in bright light, making them visible, vulnerable, and exposed. The radiance lasted for twelve seconds.

History

One such item was hidden behind a secret wall in the Last Light Inn , presumably before the Sharran shadow-curse consumed the lands around the Moonrise Towers in the Chionthar Valley . That coruscation ring was still hidden at the inn in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
