Relationships

He was the heir of Olgarth Oldbridle .

History

After the death of Olgarth Oldbridle in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , his heir, Elbert, received a missive from him by his servant Osbur , a warning about Sembian intrigue in Cormyr. He had Osbur transmit the warning to Traevyn Illance .

Later, he had people seek out Arclath Delcastle , inviting him to a meeting at Arkanon Nalander 's. They caught up to him while he was on his coach.
