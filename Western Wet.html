The Western Wet was the sister road of the Eastern Wet , both located in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate and encircled the city's wharves and docks on the Gray Harbor .

Location

The Western Wet stretched from the Seatower of Balduran in the Seatower district, passed Black Eel Street and Glaezel's Dock , continued on to cross Caundorl Street and Hethkantle's Jetty , and ended by the Water Queen's House .
