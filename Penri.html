Penri was a korobokuru forest barbarian adventurer in Miyama Province in Kozakura in 1357 DR .

History

Penri born in the Bear Clan in northern Kozakura. The Bear korobokuru were at war with the Kozakurans until 1357 DR when the humans won and the korobokuru had to escape into mountainous and snowy forests, struggling to survive.

Penri was chosen to go in search of allies and he decided to travel to Miyama.
