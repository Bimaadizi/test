Shagambi ( pronounced : / ʃ ɑː ˈ g oʊ m b i / shah-GOM-bee ) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult . She was a primal spirit that took the form of a kamadan .

Personality

Shagambi was wise and virtuous, yet she showed no mercy to evildoers .
