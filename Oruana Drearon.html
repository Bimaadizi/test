Oruana Drearon was a member of the Sharran monastic order of the Dark Moon and its representative in Westgate 's Dark Lady's Promise temple in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Oruana was a woman of pale skin and long obsidian hair. She had fierce lavender eyes and was wrapped in dark-colored robe .

History

Circa the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Oruana Drearon was one of the members of the church of Shar who served High Priestess Velisha and was her personal aide, responsible for the temple's maintenance and well-being.
