Silnia was a fallen angel in the Labyrinth in the Northdark in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The gods condemned Silnia and seven other fallen angels to remain as stone statues in the depths of the Labyrinth for unknown crimes.

Powers

Silnia was tormented by terrible nightmares, which could affect other beings in the vicinity.
