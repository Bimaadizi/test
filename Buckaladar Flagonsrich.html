Buckaladar Flagonsrich , or Buck Flagonsrich , was the Guildmaster of the Distillers' Guild in Purskul of Amn in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Buckaladar was a haughty snob of a halfling , a winemaker of great skill, pallet, and terrible temper. With a single sip of wine, the halfling could identify the grape used in the wine's pressing, vintage year, and country of origin.

Possessions

The Flagonsrich family, along with several other notable hin clans, owned most of Purskul. Buck's home stood along the Orc Road in the Zehoarastria region of Amn. The Flagonsrich Estate included a great garden and vineyards in the back.

History

In the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , before the Feast of the Moon , the Knights of the Shield worked on restoring Amn's monarchy, manipulated by Rhinnom Dannihyr , the Grandmaster of the Shadow Thieves . Buckaladar Flagonsrich was a strong supporter of the agenda and led to Buck's nomination to the Knights of the Shield.
