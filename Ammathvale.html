The Ammathvale was a small open valley separating the Lluirwood in the north from the Southern Lluirwood in Luiren .

Geography

This region stretched west to east between the Trader's Way and the city of Ammathluir spanning roughly 100 miles (160,000 meters). An unnamed road branched off from the Trader's Way to pass through the Ammathvale, connecting the rest of the cities of Luiren.
