Ozebaw the Unwholesome was a dretch demon of the Demonweb Pits .

History

During the Darkening and the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR , Ozebaw lurked in the Demonweb Pits. It may have run across adventurers from Eberron who sought a way to Toril .
