The River Maeru was a small waterway found in eastern Halruaa .

Geography

The river was formed from water leaving the southern edge of Lake Maeru and an unnamed tributary that originated in the Muaraghal Mountains in eastern Halruaa. It flowed west for approximately 200 miles (320,000 meters) before reaching the southeastern rim of Lake Halruaa .
