Glasgirt's Hall was a room in the palace of Silverymoon during the 14 th century DR .

Location

The hall was within the palace of Silverymoon .

History

During the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the trader Oscalar Muirbree used that room as a bedroom throughout his stay at Silverymoon.
