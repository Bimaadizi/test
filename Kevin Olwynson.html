Kevin Olwynson was the baron of the city of Dralpur in Ulgarth around 1367 DR , known as a crafty leader.

He was a former ranger who once wandered the realm of Ulgarth, the Utter East , and the Dustwall Mountains , before he became baron. He secured the loyalty and skills of the 500 warriors who defended the town by paying them well with gold .
