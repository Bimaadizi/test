The Skykillers were a disbanded mercenary company based in Chondath around 1358 DR .

History

They fought during the war against the arcanaloth Yrkhetep at the yugoloth 's side. They were destroyed by the Windriders at the Battle of Five Crowns .

Membership

The Skykillers were hippogriff -mounted cavalry.

Leadership

Their leader, Sir Lehakin , was a mortal enemy of Bren Wingblade , leader of the Windriders.
