Yornar's crescent was a magic item associated with the church of Mielikki .

Description

It was just a simple piece of worked leather, weighing 1 pound (450 grams), with a very aged and weathered appearance.

Powers

With the appropriate command word , the holder of the crescent could detect poison or produce a light as often as they needed. In addition, they could enchanted natural weapons and transport through trees once a day each.

History

It was of course named for the legendary ranger of Mielikki known as Yornar the Tracker , who was the first bearer and namesake of a holy Mielikkian spellbook — Yornar's Trail Companion . Like that book, the crescent changed hands between Mielikkians many times. Moreover, many versions of it were made.

Creation

Together with skill at item enchantment , one needed the spells detect poison , faerie fire , greater magic fang , and tree stride .
