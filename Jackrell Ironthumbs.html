Jackrell Ironthumbs was a reluctant client of the Sharran House of Grief and an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Jackrell sought the services of the House of Grief aid. The Mapping of the Heart revealed Jackrell's "prying heart." The ritual was paid for in full but Ironthumbs had an adverse reaction to the Mapping and fled, abandoning the Unburdening .
