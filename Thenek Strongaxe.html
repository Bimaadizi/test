Thenek Strongaxe was a dwarven bard and court musician of Mithral Hall in the mid 14 th century DR .

Description

Singing with a gravelly bass voice that few non-dwarves appreciated, he blended singing with storytelling to a near hypnotic effect. When not performing in the court of Mithral Hall, he was often found in the forges where he would be lending a hand to the smiths. In times of war he would wield his hammer against Mithral Hall's enemies instead of in the forge, still singing dirges of death.

Personality

He was often smiling when working helping out in the forges.

Possessions

He wore a mail of terror while wielding either his enchanted hammer or his dwarven thrower .
