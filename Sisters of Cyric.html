The Sisters of Cyric were a Thayan cavalry regiment active in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . Every member was an evil priestess of Cyric who rode into battle on a black unicorn .

Activities

The Sisters were prominent even among the already infamous black unicorn cavalry of Thay. They served Aznar Thrul , the Zulkir of Evocation and Tharchion of the Priador .
