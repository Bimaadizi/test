Shiver-strikes were a type of enchanted bolt , originally crafted by elite guardsmen in eladrin kingdoms.

Description

These bolts were largely constructed of plain steel .

Powers

Designed for combating groups of enemies, these bolts would violently split apart upon impacting a target. This sent shards of steel spraying at anything nearby.
