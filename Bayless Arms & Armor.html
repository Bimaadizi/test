Bayless Arms & Armor was a weapon and armor shop in the city of Neverwinter in the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

The merchant's offices stood not far from the Neverwinter Square , one of the oldest locations in the Jewel of North.

History

Bayless Arms & Armor was established sometime before the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR . The proprietor had a humble selection of mundane weapons and armor available for a reasonable price.
