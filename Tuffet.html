Tuffet was a bear that dwelled in the Emerald Grove during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Tuffet was a lazy bear, seemingly without a care in the world.

Activities

It appears that Tuffet was tasked with guarding the mechanical wooden elevator that allowed direct access to the druid's grove from the cliffs above. Tuffet could be directed away from their responsibilities with a gentle, and carefully-placed nudge.
