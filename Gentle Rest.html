The Gentle Rest was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The inn stood five stories tall.

Location

The Gentle Rest was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the High Road and Spoils Alley .

Inhabitants

The inn's owner in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , was Torst Urlivan .
