Rihuud was a stone giant living in Gracklstugh in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Rihuud was one of the apprentices of Stonespeaker Hgraam , leader of Clan Cairngorm . Circa 1486 DR , during a rite of "communing with the stone", he went mad from demonic energies unleashed in the Underdark and went on a rampage in the streets. Fellow apprentice Dorhun was tasked with taking him back alive to their quarters.
