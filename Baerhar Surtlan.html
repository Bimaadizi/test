Baerhar Surtlan was a shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Baerhar owned a shop called Surtlan's Metalwares that specialized in selling metalwares. His shop was located on River Street in the Trades Ward . Baerhar was also the public spokesman for the Guild of Trusted Pewterers and Casters .
