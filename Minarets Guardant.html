The Minarets Guardant was a temple in Calimport dedicated to the worship of the god Helm .

Location

The Minarets Guardant was located in Daid Drudach , part of the Bakkal Sabban of the Faiths Ward , in Calimport.

Structure

This building stood four stories tall. Each corner of the building was topped with a minaret, which stood six stories tall.
