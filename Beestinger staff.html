The Beestinger staff was an enchanted monk 's quarterstaff that belonged to the famed hin adventurer and a member of Acquisitions Incorporated Rosie Beestinger in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

Rosie's Beestinger staff had an empowering effect on its wielder.

History

The Beestinger staff was procured by Rosie Beestinger's friend and teammate, Walnut Dankgrass , and, eventually, ended up in the halfling monk's hands.
