Menthan Calcoler was a General of the Zhentarim who had grand military plans to conquer the Dalelands sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

History

General Menthan Calcoler was remembered for devising a plan to march Zhentil Keep troops into the Dalelands, all outfitted with custom-made cloaks of protection versus good . However, his dreams of conquest were never realized as he was assassinated by a Harper spy. Menthan's successor never implemented the plan and the commissioned magic cloaks were sold off by the wizards who crafted them for the Zhents.
