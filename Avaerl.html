Avaerl of Sembresh was a Zhentarim wizard .

History

In 1358 DR , after Elminster Aumar had foiled a Zhentarim plot to conquer High Dale , Manshoon sent Avaerl to track and kill the Old Mage. In truth, the High Lord of the Zhentarim was skeptical of Avaerl's chances and also sent Zalarth Bloodbrow to follow Avaerl and intervene if needed.

Avaerl found Elmninster and Sharantyr at Spellgard . He confronted them but was easily killed by Sharantyr.
