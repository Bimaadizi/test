Rhiiman Obarskyr was the brother of the first king of Cormyr , Faerlthann Obarskyr .

Relatives

Rhiiman's father was Ondeth Obarskyr and his mother was Suzara Obarskyr . His older brother was the first king of Cormyr, Faerlthann Obarskyr.

Background

Rhiiman came to the land that would become known as Suzail with his parents and his older brother in 6 DR . By 12 DR , Rhiiman's mother Suzara had had enough and left his father to return to Impiltur , taking him with her.
