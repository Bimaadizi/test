The Lair of the Night Masters was the secret lair of Manshoon and his vampire associates during the time he led the Night Masks in the guise of Orbakh .

Geography

The lair was located in Westgate 's sewers and could only be entered through the secret back entrance in a trapped tunnel.

Structure

The lair had several coffins which were fakes designed to fool thieves and to cower lower-ranking Night Masks. A trapped teleporter led to Orbakh's audience chamber.

History

The main entrance had been destroyed around 1391 DR by the Eye of Justice .
