Imam Reya al-Affif was the leader of Hakiyah 's faithful in Sikak in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Activities

Imam Reya was renowned for her distrust of strangers and her rousing speeches.

Relationships

Reya was the sister of Sultan Magar al-Azim .
