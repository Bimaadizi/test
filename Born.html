Born was a creature that claimed dominion over Dragonspear Castle in the Western Heartlands around 1479 DR .

Born led the undead forces that emerged from the Nepenthe River under the castle. They slaughtered the living inhabitants and destroyed the shrine to Tempus , before shrouding the castle in a constant fog and repelling efforts to retake the castle. Born raided the graves of lost cities on the High Moor to expand his army of undead.
