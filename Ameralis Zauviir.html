Ameralis Zauviir was a drow cleric who had a connection with the Watcher's Keep in the mid–14 th century DR .

Abilities

Interestingly enough, Ameralis possessed some inherent resistances that were typically found in demonic beings. She also had some form of natural regeneration .

Possessions

She wore a suit of full plate armor along with a piwafwi .

Activities

Ameralis and her companions served as one of the three groups connected to the fifth level of Watcher's Keep, which held the final seal that kept away the being known as the Imprisoned One . The group was summoned to the keep when the heart key was used to unlock the seal.

Relationships

Her companions included the demons Nalmissra , Xei Win Toh , Y'tossi , and the Huntress , along with an unnamed hive mother .

History

Ameralis and her allies were killed in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , when Abdel Adrian and his companions bypassed the final seal of Watcher's Keep and reached the Imprisoned One.
