The Blue Baldric was a beauty shop and nightclub located in the city of Waterdeep during the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Blue Baldric was located in the northeast section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward along Bazaar Street , being the middle building of a block that was bounded by Bazaar Street, Lamp Street , the Street of the Sword , and Warrior's Way .

Services

During the day the Blue Baldric operated as sort of beauty shop during the day and a club at a night. During daylight hours they additionally offered "pampering" in the form of scented bathes alongside servings of wine .
