The Whispering Timber was a powerful wood woad that lived in the forested foothills of the Storm Horns during the late 15 th century DR .

History

The local satyrs in the region knew a ritual to summon forth the Whispering Timber, using a fox tail among other ingredients. Like the local dryads , the Whispering Timber was hostile to most non- fey due to the destructive presence of Archwizard Amskar 's forces.
