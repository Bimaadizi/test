Caustic band was an enchanted ring that granted the wearer the offensive power of acid .

Powers

Caustic band infused the wielder's weapons or fists with magical acid – this malefic excretion was released with each successful attack onto the targets. The ring's central big green gem released a faint sound of sizzling when it was adjusted on the wearer's finger.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , one such ring belonged to Derryth Bonecloak , a dwarvish potion maker who ran a shop in Baldur's Gate and collected fungi in the Underdark in the late 15 th century DR .
