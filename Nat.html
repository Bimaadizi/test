Nat was a street urchin from Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Nat was tall and skinny for her age. She was deaf and communicated with her friends by using a sign language that she invented.

Possessions

She carried a wooden toy sword .

Relationships

Nat led the group of street urchins that included her friends Jenks and Squiddly . The three of them call themselves the Ragamuffins.

History

In the late 15 th century DR, Nat and her friends were unwittingly entangled in the city-wide hunt for the Stone of Golorr , sometimes innocently helping or hindering the efforts of adventurers in retrieving it.
