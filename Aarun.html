Aarun was a Harper in Icewind Dale in 1485 DR .

History

Aarun was a member of the Harpers based in Ten Towns . In 1485 DR, he grew suspicious about Vaelish Gant and his minions. Aarun discovered that Vaelish's minions were wererats of Ship Rethnor and contacted fellow Harper Emryn Dawe in Luskan , requesting silver weapons. Emryn promised to send them in the next shipment but, in the meantime, Vaelish discovered Aarun's true nature. He faked a letter from Mithann , leading him into an ambush, where he murdered Aarun.
