Khaalvandar was a grand villa and guest residence owned by the royal Irithyl family of Cormanthyr .

Location

It was located in the Old City district of Myth Drannor , immediately east of Castle Cormanthor .

Structure

The lowest of the manse's four levels was built over 90 ft (27 m) above ground level, within the trees that loomed over the the Talltangle .

Interior

Khaalvandar was lavishly decorated with all manner of art and decorative sculptures. It housed the Lord-Speaker's library, that featured works that dated back to the ancient elves of Uvaeren .

Activities

The mansion was often used a secret meeting place between the Myth Drannan High Mages and the Srinshee .

Defenses

It was known to be protected by some of the most potent and powerful protective wards in all of Cormanthyr.
