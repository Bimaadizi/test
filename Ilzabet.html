Ilzabet was an erinyes that served Mahadi of the Wandering Emporium in Baator circa the late 15 th century DR .

History

The rakshasa master of the Wandering Emporium could be found on the Avernus in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , following the city of Elturel being plunged into the Nine Hells . Ilzabet had already been bound to Mahadi by an infernal contract at the time. She was ready to be summoned by her master if a need arose.
