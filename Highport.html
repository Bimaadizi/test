Highport was an island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Highport was located just northeast of the island of Caftenor . Highport was also less than 10 miles (16 kilometers) off the coast of the far northern tip of Gnarhelm on Alaron . A large unnamed mountain peak dominated much of the southern half of the island. A small forest was found in the northern half.
