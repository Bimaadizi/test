Kadila adh Vitendi was a Calishite Sun Soul monk inhabiting Waterdeep during the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Activities

Kadila was a Righteous Sister within the Order of the Sun Soul , a monastic order originating in ancient Netheril who followed Amaunator and later Lathander , Sel√ªne , and Sune . In addition to the Common tongue, Kadila spoke Draconic , Ignan , Alzhedo , and Loross .

History

Kadila was born in Teshburl in Calimshan as an illegitimate child of the sultan Mond el Vitendi . Raised in a harem as a jhasina , or harem slave, she was later sold by her father to a distant cousin in Memnon . The caravan she was traveling with was attacked by bandits en route to Memnon. Kadila fled during the confusion, working in taverns and inns during her travels and eventually settling in Waterdeep. She had a chance meeting with Hanor Kichavo and was immediately drawn to the ways of the Sun Soul monk.

Possessions

She wielded a silver light mace in combat. She owned several magic items including a pair of bracers of armor , an amulet of mighty fists , gloves of dexterity , and potions of barkskin , cat's grace , and cure light wounds .
