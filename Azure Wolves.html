The Azure Wolves were a mighty mercenary company active east of the Sword Coast in the mid–15 th century DR . They were so renowned that any mercenary active in the region knew their name even a decade after they had ceased activity.

History

The Azure Wolves—and their leader, General Varnoth Wender —were widely feared. Their fame peaked around the Year of the Plagued Lords, 1471 DR , after which the injured and exhausted General Varnoth left the company to live a life of atonement at Dragon's Rest on Stormwreck Isle .
