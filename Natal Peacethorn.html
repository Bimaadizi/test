Natal Peacethorn was Janesta Leafgrace 's friend. On 1375 DR , while studying standing stones that showed the path to a Causeway , they found the mist between the stones dissipating and the path open for the first time. Entering, they saw the Gate of Stardeep . The day after, their encampment was attacked by Empyrean Knights , leaving his friend Janesta as the only survivor. During the attack, she saw him dragged out of his home.
