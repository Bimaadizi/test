Aunglass Roaryl was a legendary hero of Eskorn , Laerakond , and the founder of the Roaryl dynasty.

History

Several centuries before the Spellplague of 1385 DR , Aunglass Roaryl led a rebellion against the dragon emperor Eskornamundyr . Thanks to his efforts, the rebels were able to overcome their dragon master and gain their freedom.

Legacy

He was the founder of Clan Roaryl, the ruling dynasty of Eskorn as of 1479 DR .
