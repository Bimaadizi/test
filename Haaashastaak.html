Haaashastaak was a demigod of lizardkind who was worshiped on Toril by beast cults .

Worshipers

Haaashastaak was primarily worshiped by intelligent reptiles, though some humanoids also showed it reverence.

Rumors & Legends

Some sages in the Realms speculated that Haaashastaak was nothing more than an aspect of Sebek , the Untheric god of crocodiles , though a more popular theory among sages was that the Master of all Lizardkind was an aspect of the Chultan deity Ubtao .
