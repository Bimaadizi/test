Personality

Unlike other sirines that were often high-spirited and exuberant, Sil was rather defensive and ready to defend her tribe's home at the slightest provocation.

Abilities

She could make herself invisible and had the power to make others go into a furious rage and attempt to slay anyone nearby.

Relationships

Sil led a small tribe of sirines that included at least two others of her kind.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Sil and her tribe relocated to a beach on the Sword Coast just north of the Seawatcher lighthouse, claiming it as their "living-land". As they claimed the territory the sirines managed to sink the boat of a local fisherman .
