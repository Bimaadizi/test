Helmfast Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Helmfast .

Location

The villa was located in the south section of Waterdeep 's North Ward on the southeast corner of where Catchthief Alley met Golden Serpent Street .

Description

The walled estate contained three separate buildings arranged around a garden. The buildings stood three stories tall.
