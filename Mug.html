TypeDrinking vessel

Mugs were a variety of large drinking vessel in the Realm. They were typically used to drink hot or warm beverages, such as coffee or tea , and served as an improvised weapon in many a tavern brawl.

Description

Mugs were made in a variety of materials, including bones, shells, clay , wood, ceramics , glass, and metals such as brass , pewter , or most expensively silver . They were always thick and heavy, with most sporting a handle.
