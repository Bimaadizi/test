Potions of bless were magic potion infusions popular among adventurers for being their "portable priests" .

Powers

Potions of bless granted the imbiber a brief boost to their morale in the face of fear and a slight improvement in combat skill that increased their chance to strike a blow or aim true. .

History

Potions of bless were popular among adventurers and were widely available for purchase from traders and temples of the Sword Coast in the 14 th century DR .
