Illinge Hardraw was an apprentice to Zunroun Ilber in the early 8 th century DR . He killed his master on the Night of Fourteen Magisters , but did not survive him for very long.

Personality

He was cold-blooded, and calm.

History

On the Night of Fourteen Magisters of Eleasis 4 , 709 DR , he slew his master after the latter became Magister , before he regained consciousness from his spell to bind his own clones to his command. He became Magister, but while he gloated, he was scried on by Belmer of the Six Thunders, who slew him with a spell.
