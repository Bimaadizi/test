Great Chief Syken Nightblaze was the leader of the Gray Wolf Tribe during the latter half of the 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Syken had one daughter, Envir Sykensdottir , a strong leader in her own right.

History

Sometime after the War of the Silver Marches of 1485 DR , Skyen was killed by a band of adventurers that came from the northern city of Neverwinter .
