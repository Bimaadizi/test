Miirsar was a small fishing community of the Lost Kingdom of Asram .

Geography

It was situated on the shore of Lake Miir .

Trade

The town was known for its highly-skilled shipwrights and great number of fishers, who brought in catches of blue-scaled brench and spicy crabfin .
