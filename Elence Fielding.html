Elence Fielding was a merchant of the village of Imnesvale in the Umar Hills in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Elence was an elf who wore wizard 's robes. She had a fair skin and brown hair.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the village of Imnesvale faced a slew of disappearances, and locals did not hesitate to blame the legendary Umar Witch . Elence sold her goods, including mundane armor , weapons , some jewelry, and an occasional potion of fire resistance . However, her business suffered from strife, and the supplier mercantile company hadn't been bringing goods in.
