Staech ( pronounced : /ˈ s t eɪ tʃ / STAY-ch ) was a type of topical paste used by dwarves of both sexes in Faerûn .

Powers

This paste allowed one to inhibit the growth of hair. In order to work, the area treated with this paste had to be seared in an open flame.

Creation

The ingredients necessary to make this paste were highly secret.

History

Staech was developed long before the 14 th century DR by a dwarf of the same name.
