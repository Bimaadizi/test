Maernos family crest was the enchanted pendant that belonged to Charissa Maernos circa 1374 DR .

Description

This regal-looking amulet was adorned with the crest of the Maernos noble family of Waterdeep .

Powers

Maernos family crest was imbued with charisma-boosting enchantments that made the wearer arrear more regal even if they were dressed in rags.

History

Sometime before 1374 DR, Maernos family crest belonged to Charissa Maernos' mother. Eventually, the enchanted amulet was passed along to Charissa, a young paladin who traveled to Westgate in search of revenge against the Night Masks who ruined her family.
