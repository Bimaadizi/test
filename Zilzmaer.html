Zilzmaer was a drow warrior of House Faen Tlabbar , the fourth house of Menzoberranzan until the Silence of Lolth .

History

Zilzmaer was one of the guards of Faen Tlabbar's complex. In 1372 , Nimor Imphraezl of the Jaezred Chaulssin infiltrated the complex. On the way he killed Zilzmaer.
