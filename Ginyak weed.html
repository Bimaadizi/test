Ginyak weed was a plant found in Calimshan in western Faerûn .

Description

When the weed was burned, the smoke gave off an acrid smell. Ginyak weed was a valuable but unsavory trade commodity exported out of Calimshan and freely sold in the alleys of Caer Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles .
