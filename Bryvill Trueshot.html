Bryvill Trueshot was an elf ranger in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Bryvill had long blonde hair and light skin. She wore green leafy clothing including a tunic , breeches , and archer's hat , and had a huge copper bow .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Bryvill joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp outside the ruins of Myth Drannor . She provided training and guidance to prospective rangers that passed through the camp.
