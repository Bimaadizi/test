Sharabirt was a wizard from Calimport in Calimshan , of the 13th century DR .

History

Sharabirt accepted Glaragama Dunrohiyr as an apprentice. Sharabirt later died, apparently of natural causes, and Glaragama switched the apprenticeship to Rrestel of Athkatla .
