The Adamantine Palace was the seat of power for the monarchs who reigned over the dwarven kingdom of Ammarindar , located within the royal city of Splendarrmornn .

Description

The entire palace was constructed from the extremely rare, and nearly indestructible black alloy, adamantine . It featured spectacularly engravings, crafted from gold and silver , inlaid with shimmering gems .

History

Following the collapse of Ammarindar in 882 DR , the royal capital of Splendarrmornn was plundered by fiendish tanarukks in 1220 DR . During the sacking of the city, many of the decorative inlays and gem stones were stripped away from the Adamantine Palace, though some signs of the dwarves' wealth still remained.
