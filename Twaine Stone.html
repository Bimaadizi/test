Twaine Stone was a jackalwere and member of the Black Blood tribe inhabiting the island of Moray within the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Although capable of subterfuge, in battle he fought with feral and rabid ferocity.

Abilities

He had the ability to daze or even put to sleep up to two individuals with his mere gaze.

As a jackalware, he could assume the form either of a human or of a large jackal . In his human guise, he favored the falchion .
