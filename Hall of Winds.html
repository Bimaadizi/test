The Hall of Winds was a tower located within the complex of the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Description

Aptly named, the Hall of Winds was literally filled with raging winds that circled constantly within the tower structure. The Hall of Winds stood 80 feet (24 meters).

Activities

This tower housed the djinn who served the Grand Caliph .

Inhabitants

In addition to the djinn, sylphs and aerial servants were present to infuse the wind with pleasant scents anytime the djinn were in residence in the tower.
