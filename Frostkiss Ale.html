Frostkiss Ale was a type of aged ale found in the Western Heartlands , along the River Chionthar , and the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Frostkiss was named after the cold goddess Auril , the Frostmaiden, and was cold and sour to taste just like the deity's demeanor. This ale was aged for several years before being consumed.
