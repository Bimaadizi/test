Sardi al-Danafi was an inhabitant of Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Personality

Sardi was a fair man, though the occasional bribe padded his pockets.

Activities

Sardi was the harbormaster of the Trade Harbor in Huzuz. He answered to Keyfa al-Parid .

He insured that any ships carrying goods for his family were docked quickly at the best locations in the harbor.

History

He was the youngest scion of the House of Al-Danafi .
