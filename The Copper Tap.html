The Copper Tap was a tavern in Calimshan , famed for its award-winning brews in the late 15 th century DR .

Services

The Copper Tap was a locally popular establishment that brewed its own type of award-winning ale .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , sailor PJ Stubbs , a friend of Bosun Gannet of the Blushing Mermaid in Baldur's Gate , found himself in Calimshan and decided to visit The Copper Tap. The discussion he quickly regretted, claiming that its award-winning brew tasted like "flowers had a wank on his tongue." PJ hated the taste so much that the sailor complained about it in a letter sent to Bosun Gannet. Even ten pints of ale failed to wash the taste off.
