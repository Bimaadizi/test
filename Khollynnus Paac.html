Khollynnus Paac was a textile merchant and the leader of the largest Cowled Wizards cell in Amn in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Khollynnus had skin of olive hue and strawberry blonde hair.

Abilities

She was proficient in conjuration magic and had access to such powerful spells as Abi-Dalzim's horrid wilting , chain lightning , circle of death , pierce magic , ruby ray of reversal , spell turning , maze , simulacrum , and many others.

History

Khollynnus led the first cell of the Cowled Wizards in Amn. Her group's secret base was hidden underneath the Jann Lane . Its entrance was hidden in the Pride of Athkatla market, behind a column, always under the surveillance of the Cowled Wizards and Sulrajack , a charismatic merchant allied with the organization.
