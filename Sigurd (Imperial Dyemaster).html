Sigurd was the Imperial Dyemaster and the only manufacturer of Drake General dye in the late 15 th century DR .

History

By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Drake General dye was created exclusively by Sigurd and his apprentice Aurora . This red and gold dye was considered to be extremely rare and valuable and encountered in the Western Heartlands , West Faerûn , and the city of Baldur's Gate .
