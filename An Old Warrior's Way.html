An Old Warrior's Way was book penned by Dathlance of Selgaunt in the late 13 th century DR .

History

An Old Warrior's Way was a tome that collected musings and life lessons of a warrior from Sembia , Dathlance. It was published in the Year of the Blade, 1275 DR . A copy of the book was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . Another copy was kept in the War Library of the Abbey of the Sword in Battledale as of the late 14 th century DR.
