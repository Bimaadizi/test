Vauv was a type of a nut -based dessert made by the Bedine folk of the Anauroch desert in north Faerûn .

Description

Vauv was one of many types of desserts found throughout the Realms , similar to the Earth 's halva . Vauv was a very sweet treat made out of crushed dates , roasted and ground tharra , honey syrup, and wild rice flour . The mix was pressed into small handfood cakes and could keep through most months of the year apart from the hottest periods of time.
