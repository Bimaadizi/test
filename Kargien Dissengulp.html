Kargien Dissengulp was a deep gnome burrow warden in Blingdenstone .

History

Kargien was a burrow warden who had escaped from Blingdenstone in 1371 DR .

Circa 1439 DR . he led the first expedition back to the ruins of the city and started reconstruction.

In 1479 DR , he was busy trying to clear the city from all hostile factions and so hired adventurers.

Personality

Kargien liked to be the chief.
