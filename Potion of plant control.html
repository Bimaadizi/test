A potion of plant control was a potion capable of influencing plants.

Powers

The imbiber of these potions could influence plants, fungi , molds , and shambling mounds , causing them to perform any of their normal activities with the exception of any self-destructive behavior. The area of control for normal plants was a square with 20 ft (6.1 m) sides.

Notable Owners

Fennis , a winemaker genie in Zakhara , carried several potions of clairaudience that he used to produce fabulous vintages.
