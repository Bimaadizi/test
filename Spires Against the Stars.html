The Spires Against the Stars was the most holy site of worship for the faithful of Beshaba , the Maid of Misfortune.

Location

This hilltop fortress was found in eastern Tethyr north of Saradush .

Inhabitants

The Spires was run by Nails of the Lady Dlatha Faenar in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

History

The fortress was once the seat of power for a noble family, but was later overrun by vampires .
