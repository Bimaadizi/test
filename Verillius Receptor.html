Verillius Receptor was a High Inquisitor who served Zariel , the Archduchess of Avernus , in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Verillius often took on disguises and false shapes when she was traveling on her hellish mistress's orders. Her true form was unknown, but it was said she was a gargantuan creature, mind-scarring to behold.

History

By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Verillius was officially entitled to visit the House of Hope and audit the collection of artifacts displayed in Raphael 's library. The very same collection contained the Orphic Hammer that may have been stolen from the Nine Hells by the Absolute Adversaries .
