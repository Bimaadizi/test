Nada , also known as Juto , was a yakuza in the town of Aru in Wa in the 1770s on the Wa Calendar .

Possessions

In battle, Nada used a ninja-to .

Abilities

He was proficient in games.

History

His profession was something of an open secret to the people of Aru, but none knew where he lived or operated out of.

In the Wa Year 1775 (1357 DR) , Nada was ordered by the local yakuza family to discourage an adventuring party hired by the daimyo , Benju Matsutomo . Calling himself Juto and contriving to bump into them in the street, Nada would idly tell the adventurers that bad karma would come to them if they continued their investigation. It was not meant as a threat, but as a helpful warning.
