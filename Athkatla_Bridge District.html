The Bridge District of Athkatla was one of the city's poorer quarters, situated on the southern reach of the Alandor River , just south of the bridge that spanned its waters.

It was home to a fair number of low-end businesses, tiny hole-in-the-wall shops selling second-hand or illegal wares. Many of these catered to the merchants and travelers who made their living along the caravan routes.

Description

The district had narrow, winding streets lined with rowhouses of some of the poorer Athkatlan citizens. Among these were the city's laborers, street-side cart vendors, shop assistants and the like.
