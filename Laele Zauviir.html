Laele Zauviir , most knew as Streea'Valsharess Zauviir , was the high priestess of Lolth in the city of Sshamath in the year 1370 DR .

History

In 1379 DR Streea'Valsharess together with her nephew Guldor Zauviir and T'lar Mizz'rynturl devised a scheme to gain more power in the Conclave of Sshamath but failed.

Later they discussed about why their plot failed. T'lar killed Guldor and vanished and the blame for the killing fell to Streea'Valsharess. After T'lar killed also Streea'Valsharess and took control of her temple.
