A sash of vitality ceaseless was a magic sash worn about the waist that granted extra vitality to the wearer when it was most needed.

Description

The sash had a red and white color pattern, with red representing "harm" and white representing "healing".

Powers

While wearing the sash of vitality ceaseless , individuals recovered more rapidly, but only if they were badly injured.

Notable Owners

The Cult of the Dragon located at the Well of Dragons possessed a sash of vitality ceaseless .

Rumors & Legends

It was said that the true power of the sash of vitality ceaseless could only be tapped by a deva . For instance, if a deva wore the sash in conjunction with certain other items crafted by their ancestors, the objects were said to grant further powers such as a resistance to fire . Devas who came into contact with such items often came to feel that they had possessed the same item in a prior life.
