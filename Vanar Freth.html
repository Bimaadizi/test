Vanar Freth was a drow wizard of House Freth who dwelled within Spiderwatch Keep , deep within the bowels of the dungeon-complex known as Undermountain .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Vanar and two of his fellow drow infiltrated the domain of Arcturiadoom . They sought to steal powerful magic and aid their house's conquest of the deeper levels of Undermountain. During his excursion, Vanar was accompanied by a shadow demon he had summoned.
