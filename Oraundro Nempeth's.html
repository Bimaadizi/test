Oraundro Nempeth's was a shop in Khôltar , south Faerûn , circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Location

Nempeth's was located on the east side of North Way near Handrornlar, the northern gate through the walls of Khôltar, the Iron City. It was sandwiched between Munsrum's Ready Ladle to the north and Harhallo's Warmhearth House to the south. Across the street and a few strides north was Jamrado's Thanetalium .

Structure

This building was made of stone and the shop had a relatively narrow front facing the North Way.

Services

Oraundro Nempeth's sold various binding cords , rope , twine , wire , and a few sizes of chain . It also carried metal rings used as fasteners and wall-mounts.
