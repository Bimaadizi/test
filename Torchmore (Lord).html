Lord Torchmore was a nobleman of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

History

He and Lord Halvaeron came to Manshoon 's attention in Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , as lords who supposedly led factions but whose intrigues and supporters were largely unknown.
