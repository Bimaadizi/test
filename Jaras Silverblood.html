Jaras Silverblood was a Huntmaster of Malar , and the leader of the Company of the Hunt , a group of outlaw-worshipers of the Beastlord from the Winterwood , during the late 14 th -century DR . He was known for his particularly calculating, and overbearing nature.

History

Jaras was wanted in Turmish for the murder of an assemblyman.

Possessions

Among Jaras' possessions were a helm of brilliance and a pair of gauntlets of ogre power . He also always kept several vials of poison on his person.
