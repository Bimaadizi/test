Azak Throm ( pronounced : /ˈ ɑː z ɑː k ˈ θ r oʊ m / AZ-ack THROHM ) was a Red Wizard of Thay diplomat in Mulmaster in 1491 DR .

Personality

Azak was a cunning and calculating individual. He had an immense ego but was tempered by incredible patience.

History

Azak worked at the Thayan embassy in Mulmaster as a diplomat. In truth, he was a spy of Szass Tam , which was suspected by the Hawks , Mulmaster's secret police, who always surveilled him.

In 1491 DR, he happened to be involved in a mysterious arson attack on Tiega Gos , whilst meeting with her nephew, the mad painter Andrik Gos . An adventuring party hired to unveil the truth interrogated him.
