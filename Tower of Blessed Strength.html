The Tower of Blessed Strength was a public garden located in the city of Dhaztanar in Semphar during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Tower of Blessed Strength was located within the Furdah Rustauk District in south Dhaztanar. The tower sat along the Silk Road where it entered Dhaztanar from the east.

Description

The tower was built into the main wall surrounding Dhaztanar.
