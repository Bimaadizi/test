Malango Durlurleth was a successful merchant inhabiting Yeshpek along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Malango was pudgy but still handsome.

Activities

His tongue and wit seemed to be three moves ahead of everyone he dealt with. Malango was at the center of any important trade deal occurring within Yeskpek.
