Pale Oak was a type of enchanted staff wielded by the most devote members of the Church of Silvanus .

Powers

Each of the Pale Oak staves was imbued with the divine magics of Silvanus , the Oak Father to be wielded by the Faithwardens , the Silvanite defenders of nature. The staves allowed their wielders to cast the Faithwarden's vines spell . The wielders were also unaffected by the spell and could move freely through the summoned plant growth.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Kagha of the Emerald Grove in the Western Heartlands could reward helpers to the Grove's cause with a Pale Oak .
