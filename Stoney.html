Stoney was a mountain giant that lived in the northern part of the second level of Undermountain in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Some time before the year 1372 DR , Stoney found himself living in a corridor on the second level of Undermountain. When Drogan Droganson's pupil ventured into Undermountain the same year, it is possible that they met and the adventurer slew Stoney and looted his body.

Possessions

At some point in time, Stoney acquired several magic items , including one of the Stone Cold Clubs .
