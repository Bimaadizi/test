Elmariel Emmarask was a member of the Emmarask noble house of Cormyr and a War Wizard in the early 7 th century DR . He was a trusted friend of Luthax .

History

Elmariel found a magical creature in the ruins of Netheril . He conspired with Luthax to kill King Draxius Obarskyr , but their plot was foiled in the Year of the Empty Hearth, 629 DR , when Thanderahast alerted the king. Amedahast found the conspirators and detonated her magical staff, killing Elmariel, Luthax, herself, and the others in the room.
