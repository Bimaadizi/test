A ring of research was a magic ring that aided the wearer when conducting research.

Description

These rings were made from gold or silver and were fixed with a single gemstone .

Powers

When worn, a ring of research allowed its wearer to quickly find the correct page in a book , or place on a scroll , where the answer to their question could be found provided they had access to a sufficient library that contained the knowledge they sought.
