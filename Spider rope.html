Spider ropes were a variety of enchanted ropes that were popular in the Dalelands in the 14 th century DR .

Description

These stout ropes were mundane in appearance and in their unactivated state measured no more than 10 feet (3 meters) in length.

Powers

Twice per day, a spider rope could be verbally commanded by a wielder to increase its length and attach itself to an inanimate object. Spider ropes could increase their length to up to 200 feet (61 meters) and would remain at that length indefinitely until their owner commanded them to return to their original length.

Spider ropes attached themselves to inanimate objects, no more than two, by tying their ends around the targeted object(s). If one were to ever tie a spider rope around something manually though, the rope would swiftly untie itself.

If enough damage were inflicted upon a spider rope it would disintegrate into a fine, gray powder that lacked magical properties.

Notable Owners

Most guilds in the Dalelands possessed one or more spider ropes .
