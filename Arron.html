Arron was a merchant who lived with the druids of the Emerald Grove in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

While he was a businessman and a merchant, Arron was a rather caring individual.

Possessions

Among the various goods Arron had for sale were an amulet of elemental torment as well as the Rain Dancer quarterstaff , and the Dragon's Grasp handaxe .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the threat of goblin attacks threatened the residents of the Emerald Grove. Arron was of the opinion that the tiefling community they harbored should not be outcast into the wild, left to fend for themselves.
