The Fellowship of the Next Mountain was a military order devoted to Shaundakul , the Rider of the Winds.

History

The order formed following the Time of Troubles in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Organization

The Fellowship of the Next Mountain was composed of rangers and windwalkers . These individuals usually operated alone, scouting trails through unknown parts of the Sword Coast North and Moonsea North .
