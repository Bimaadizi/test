Ashtukzu I , who was also called the Mulan , was the first King of the Mulan Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Ashtukzu was a mulan worshiper of Gilgeam who once lived in Unther . He was sent by the God-King to conquer and absorb the city-state of Westgate. He accomplished this in −27 DR when he took the city and ousted the doppleganger posing as King Ryndarth Soleilon II . However, once he had established a firm grip on the city as King he declared Westgate to be independent of Unther.

Relationships

His nephew was Thunn , who would later betray him and take the throne in −8 DR .
