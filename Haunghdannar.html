Haunghdannar was a small realm of seafaring dwarves located on the Sword Coast during the Age of the Proud People . They remained vigilant against the beasts of west Faerûn , but were eventually driven mad by the time they spent at sea. Their nation had rather undefined borders.

The emblem of Haunghdannar was a left-facing fish over a mountain, encircled by a seven-pointed star.

Notable Locations

History

The nation was founded on the coast of the Sea of Swords , in the year −4974 DR , by a group of dwarves who were believed to have a much larger, more prosperous dwarf kingdom. They were believed to have been armed with a mighty arsenal of magic weapons , granting them strong defense against the goblinoids and leucrottas in the area. Around 300 years later, they completed construction on the fortress of Sonnmorndin on Arauwurbarak island, north of the Moonshae Isles .

In the year −3389 DR they sent out a fleet of ships, very few of which returned. It was believed the sea had driven the dwarves mad and, degenerating, the realm dwindled over time until it was overrun by the orcs , trolls , ogres , and bugbears of the coastlands.

It was believed the survivors of Haunghdannar eventually became the Madbeard berserkers that raided Northlander ships from their island home in the Uttersea .
