A philter of persuasiveness was a magical substance that boosted charisma.

Powers

When imbibed, this philter boosted the imbiber's charisma and ability to persuade others. Additionally, the imbiber could make a suggestion to all creatures within 90 ft (27 m) once every ten minutes.

Notable Owners

Shahpesh , a moralist from Zakhara , carried a philtre of persuasiveness .
