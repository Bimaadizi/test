Lemere Soleilon II was the third King of the Soleilon Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Lemere II reigned from −74 DR until his death in −68 DR . He inherited the throne from his father King Lemere I .

Relationships

Lemere II married Lady Lharida Ithal of Tethyr . His firstborn son and heir was Ryndarth I .
