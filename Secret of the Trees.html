Secret of the Trees was one of the specialty dishes of the Dryad's Dreams , the only festhall in Mosstone , Tethyr .

Description

The Secret of the Trees was a sophisticated and mouthwatering dish. The Secret consisted out of frothed roasted garlic , toasted walnuts , and local wild mushrooms from the Forest of Tethir , served on sweet stewed peppers .

History

Secret of the Trees was served at the Dryad's Dreams in the mid-to-late-14 th century DR . The dish was praised by Volothamp Geddarm in one of his published travel guides.
