Lundrago Mursimmur was the publisher and editor-in-chief of the Neverwinter Nights chapbook in the city of Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

By 1480 DR , Lundrago was still a young and exceedingly energetic man, known by many as "brightwits." He was a cheerful fellow who appreciated the beauty of today, caring more about enjoying life than climbing a social ladder or making enemies by digging deep for scandalous dirt on the broadsheet 's subjects.

Relationships

Lundrago had six female high-society friends with whom he attended revels, feasts, grand openings, marriage ceremonies, and other socialite events, later penning columns on these events for Neverwinter Nights .

History

By the Year of Deep Water Drifting, 1480 DR , Lundrago Mursimmur ran a group of a dozen employees, called "haelhards", who were entrusted with printing broadsheets via a hand-cranked printing press .
