Lyrwood was a tree whose wood was used in the creation of magical items . It was extinct in the wild as of 1374 DR .

Description

Arrows made of lyrwood were so black they resembled lines of darkness.

Availability

The Nentyarch in Yeshelmaar claimed the lyrwood in the arrow shafts he gifted to Anom , Cirid and Fallon was harvested from the Shadow wood of the Mystic's Dreams, and could no longer be easily made as of early 1373 DR . The Imaskari vengeance taker Iahn Qoyllor wielded a stiletto whose handle was constructed out of lyrwood during his quest to apprehend Ususi Manaallin in early 1374 DR ; as of then, it was a tree of the ancient world, which only grew behind the Great Seal, in Deep Imaskar .
