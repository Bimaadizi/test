High Lady Jasmine Lurian was a noble of House Lurian in the city of Procampur in the Vast in the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

As head of House Lurian, he was involved in the family's gold -smithing business. He also held the title of Lord Consul and sat on the Nobles Council , as well as several other councils for the betterment and management of the city, including the Council of Justice.

Relationships

Her husband was Lord Consul Povalo Lurian , the head of the family, and they had some children.

History

She was the High Lady by 1370 DR , when she was aged 52.
