Sardamon Thorland was a lord from the Forest Kingdom of Cormyr who was one of the nation's mining magnates in the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

Lord Thorland owned at six or more highly profitable iron and silver mines on the western edges of Cormyr.

History

The Thorland blazon commemorated the family's triumph over dwarves and claiming their first mine, centuries before the 15 th century DR. Sardamon's family grew to be reclusive, keeping their hands clean of Suzail 's politics.
