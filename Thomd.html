Thomd was the innkeep for the Wanton Wyvern inn near Hullack Forest , Cormyr , in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Description

Thomd was a tall, battered-looking man.

Personality

He was unkind to Baergasra and insisted that they'd never let a leper inside, because the place was clean.

Activities

Thomd was responsible for letting people into the Wanton Wyvern late in the evenings and for barring the gate.

History

Late in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Thomd opened the Wanton Wyvern one evening to a group of travelers including Shandril Shessair , Narm Tamaraith , Mirt , and Delg Ironstar . After they were ambushed by local Zhentarim bullyblades, Narm required healing from Baergasra. Though Thomd tried to evict her in the aftermath, she turned out not to be an actual leper, but a Harper in disguise. He drew her a bath.
