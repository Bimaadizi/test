Tesh Street was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Tesh Street was located in Zhentil Keep's Common Quarter in the southeastern section of the city. The road branched west off of the Street of Sorrow and terminated several blocks later at the eastern gate to the Harbor District .

Notable Locations

A Zhentilar guardpost was located along Tesh Street.
