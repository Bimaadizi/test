Eenoro vine was a species of plants found on the Malatran Plateau in Kara-Tur .

Usages

The main known use for eenoro vine was the creation of a special medicinal oil known as oil of the eenoro vine or shan sao balm . The substance was an oily green concoction. When the oil was applied to the skin of a creature, it healed some of its broken tissue, cured poisons , toxins, and healed diseases .
