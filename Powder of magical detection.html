TypePowder

Powder of magical detection was a type of magical powder.

Description

This powder was extremely fine and consisted of minute, crystalline granules. They typically came in packets that contained around eleven to twenty pinches of the powder.

Powers

Whenever a mere pinch of this crystalline powder made contact with a magical item , regardless of its size, it would spark and flash with a prismatic array of colors. Users could not discern from the powder the nature or intensity of an item's enchantment , only that the item in question was magical.
