Princeps Kovik was a chain devil warlord in Avernus in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Although particularly cruel, Kovik was a capable leader who had gained the trust of its subordinates.

Possessions

The Princeps rode a Devil's Ride infernal war machine .

History

Kovik once commanded the 8th Infantry Legion of the Nine Hells, nicknamed "Terror Incarnate". After a crushing defeat in battle against the demon lord Kostchtchie on the banks of the Styx , Kovik blamed his superiors. The chain devil commander gathered the surviving members of the 8th Legion and renounced the infernal hierarchy, forming the marauder gang known as the Eighth Remnant .

Activities

Kovik's ultimate goal was to gather enough military strength to drive the demons out of Avernus forever. Many disenfranchised devils sympathized with this goal, and so flocked to join the Eighth Remnant.
