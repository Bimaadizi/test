The Hunabar ( pronounced : /ˈ h uː n ɑː b ɑːr / HOON-ah-bar ) family was a notable part of the nobility of Waterdeep , first gaining nobility status in Year of the Moonlight Tapestry, 1170 DR .

Organization

As of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , prominent members of the house included patriarch Haskar , consort Amonra , and Haskar's eldest son and heir Haskar Hunabar II .

Activities

The Hunabar family were most notable for importing and trading in textiles and fashions.

Base of Operations

The Hunabar Villa was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep on the southeast corner of Hassantyr's Street and Ilzantil Street , and consisted of two-story buildings.

Possessions

As of 1368 DR , Horth Hunabar had in his possession a dagger of homing , a magical dagger specifically crafted as a throwing weapon that would teleport back to its scabbard.
