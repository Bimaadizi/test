Speaker Amalrae was a member of an intrigue in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Abilities

She could cast spells .

Activities

Amalrae was part of the nameless cabal headed by Halaster Blackcloak .

Relationships

She was part of a cell within the nameless cabal under Halaster, and this cell contacted him directly on at least one occasion. Her cell had eighteen people, fifteen of whom were Red Wizards , including Azmyrandyr and Roeblen , and two of whom were Sharran priests.

Skloon was her rival, whom she hated.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , after the killing of the Red Wizards Azmyrandyr and Roeblen, Amalrae attended a meeting with the rest of their cabal; there, she learned that Thaltar Glaervar , a third Red Wizard linked to the other two by the magics of Halaster Blackcloak, had fought the Simbul and prevailed, taking a wand of hers as a trophy. Before more could be discussed, the wand proved to be the Simbul herself shapechanged; she soon turned herself into an energy field that eliminated all of them.
