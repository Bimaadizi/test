The Stirge was a pirate ship that operated out of Jahaka Anchorage during the late 15 th century DR .

Crew

The Stirge was captained by the ostentatious pirate, Laskilar . The role of first mate was served by the half-orc Heel . IT had a crew of eighteen Chultan and Tethyrian pirates, along with a priest of Umberlee named Caldos Hellinskorn .
