Unkh ( pronounced : /ˈ ʌ ŋk / UNK ) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult . She was a primal spirit that took the form of a flail snail .

Personality

Unkh was indecisive, self-absorbed, and incapable of making decisions, yet she was also contemplative and understanding.
