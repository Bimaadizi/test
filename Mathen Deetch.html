Mathen Deetch , was a child who lived in the town of Moonhaven in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Sometime before the Moonhaven was attacked and ruined by the goblin raiders of the Cult of the Absolute in the Year of the Scarlet Witch, 1491 DR , Mathen was among the four children who had gone missing. Their names: Mathen Deetch, Rochelle Kirk , Marcus , and Maggie Terrens . The missing children's notice was still hanging on a wall as the village laid in ruins in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
