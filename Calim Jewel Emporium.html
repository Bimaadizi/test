The Calim Jewel Emporium was a jeweler's shop located in Little Calimshan within the Outer City of Baldur's Gate .

Services

In addition to being the home to perhaps the most well-regarded Baldurian jeweler of the late 15 th century, the emporium served as a forum for local agents of criminal organization known as the Guild . The emporium was owned by the Guild Kingpin of Little Calimshan, Rilsa Rael . She used her establishment as a headquarters for guild members and to host public events for the people of her neighborhood and the rest of the Outer City.
