S'slaar was a troglodyte chieftain of a tribe in the Wormwrithings area in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa 1486 DR , he had a disagreement with his war chief H'slaat about the ownership of a magic sword. Soon a civil war started between S'slaar's and H'slaat's loyalists. Unless others interrupted them, in the last battle H'slaat would kill S'slaar and become the next chieftain.
