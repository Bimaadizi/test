The Diamond Gate was one of the entrances into the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The Diamond Gate was found along the western section of the north wall of the city in Keffinspires .

Description

The gate resembled a permanent fortress complete with a garrison of soldiers.
