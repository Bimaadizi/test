Andar Beech was the High Artificer of Gond in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR . He led over one hundred Gondar priests and acolytes within the grand High House of Wonders in the city's Temples District .

High Artificer Beech took over the post after the former head priest, Torlin Silvershield , was revealed to be an abomination of the returned deity Bhaal .
