Chop Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

Chop Street was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . It was one of the larger roads found in this section of the Field Ward, running east to west in between the High Road and Gawenknife Street . Chop Street was also connected with the Fieldway , Stench Alley , and Leanwall Alley .
