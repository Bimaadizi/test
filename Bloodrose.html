Bloodrose bushes were a decorative plant from Teziir .

Description

The bloodrose was a darker rose bush with blood-red flowers.

Cultivation

These roses were at first only known to have been grown by a conclave of clerics near Teziir , then gifted to the Lord of Suzail . In the Year of the Wailing Winds, 1000 DR , they started growing in the wild in the Battle of Bones ; though all of the roses died that year, enough were taken to propagate the type.

Usage

Bloodroses were pricey to cultivate because of how delicate they were, but that made them all the more valuable.
