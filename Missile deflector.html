A missile deflector shield, also known as a shield +1/+4 vs. missiles , was a variety of body shields that carried a basic item enchantment for greater protection, especially against projectiles.

Powers

These shields had a protective enchantment effective against most attacks, which was four more times effective against projectiles or hurled weapons. Additionally, they carried a small chance of negating attacks from the magic missile spell , though only if the missiles came at the wielder's front-side.
