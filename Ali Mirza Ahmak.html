Ali Mirza Ahmak was an inhabitant of the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Activities

Ali was a djinni spy who masqueraded as a smoke merchant within the city. Rumors abounded constantly throughout the city that the city guard would arrest anyone with conjured charges that they were spying, making Ali a nervous man anytime the guard was in sight.
