Lauhren was a priestess of Lliira inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Lauhren spent her entire young life within Waterdeep and longed to experience the adventure of the open road.

Possessions

She owned a rope of entanglement and some bracers of defense . These items were gifts from a friend who wanted to encourage Lliira's faith in Lauhren.

Relationships

She was in love with a young man named Jan Hunabar .
