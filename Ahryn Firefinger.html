Ahryn Firefinger was the leader of a dozen or so Red Wizards within their enclave in Mulmaster .

Relationships

Ahryn's responsibilities were to ensure that their magical item trade remained profitable. He maintained a relationship with Tharchion Dmitra Flass to ensure they were working towards the same end.
