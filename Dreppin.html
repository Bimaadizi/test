Personality

He was a rather timid man who was just fine living his life within the secure walls of Candlekeep. He was weary of the dangers of the Sword Coast and believed them too risky to venture leaving his home.

Activities

Dreppin spent his time caring for the livestock of the keep.

Relationships

The monk was acquainted with a number of other residents within the keep including the guardsman Hull , the youth Abdel Adrian , as well as its regular visitors like Phlydia the mage .

History

Dreppin was especially nervous about the iron crisis that plagued the Sword Coast in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .
