Highluthholt was a temple to Mielikki in the High Forest destroyed in the early 13 th century DR .

Location

It lay amidst the forest some day's journey east of Secomber .

History

In the Year of the Waking Wrath, 1214 DR , Highluthholt was attacked and torn apart by a flight of seven wyverns that carried off a number of treasures, including the holy spellbook Yornar's Trail Companion . The culprit was suspected to be a villainous wizard who used magically controlled wyverns in a number of robberies and raids around the time. The name and fate of this wizard were unknown, and the Companion remained lost for years.
