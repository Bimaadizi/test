Naaman was a roguish spellcaster who aligned himself with the Sarevok , the manipulative Bhaalspawn who rose to power during the midâˆ’14 th century DR .

Activities

Naaman worked alongside numerous companions including Gardush , Aasim , Diyab , Alai , and their leader Zhalimar Cloudwulfe . While acting under the authority of the Iron Throne , the group sought to advance Sarevok's agenda throughout the Sword Coast .

Possessions

He wore a pair of bracers of defense as a means to protect himself. He carried a variety of weapons on his person, including numerous types of daggers and a myriad of magical scrolls.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Naaman and his group were sent by the Iron Throne to Baldur's Gate as advisors to the agent Thaldorn , to aid in his negotiations with Emissary Tar and the city's Council of Four . This meeting never occurred, as Sarevok's half-brother Abdel Adrian and his companions thwarted their efforts.
