Vartown District was an upscale yet shabby district in Vaelan , Durpar . Warian Datharathi initially rented a flat there during his return to the city on the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR . He enjoyed some counter-culture performances there, satirizing the members of the most important chakas, including his own, during the two days of his stay.
