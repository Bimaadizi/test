Larrana Vestel was a human explorer and guide who operated throughout Gontal , mainly in the Glaur Barrens .

Activities

As of 1479 DR , Larrana was renowned in Gontal for her skills as an adventuress and for being brave enough to explore the Glaur Barrens. She charged rather high fees to her customers, but her skill with the bow and her knowledge of Gontal's lands and dangers were worth the coin. Larrana was also one of the few individuals in all Laerakond who had first hand knowledge about the Dominions of Nehu .
