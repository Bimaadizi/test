Zocon was a human warrior and Jaguar Knight who fought for his tribe and god in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

As with most of his tribes Jaguar warriors he was able to shapechange into a jaguar . He was very skilled with the stone weapons his tribe members, like Lactun , made.

Personality

A fierce and brave warrior, he never thought twice about flinging himself into the fray, fighting for tribe and zaltec .

Possessions

Clad in jaguar skin armor, he wielded shield and maca .
