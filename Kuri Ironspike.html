Kuri Ironspike was a human fighter in Maztica .

History

Ironspike was originally a warrior from Amn who'd fought wyverns and gnolls in the Snowflake Mountains . After venturing to Maztica, he entered into the service of Lord Drakosa but secretly worked for Captain Alanza Da Nosta .

In 1362 DR , he was tasked with accompanying an adventuring party hired by Drakosa to find the Cloak of One Plume . In truth, in exchange for a passage back to Faer√ªn , he would deliver it instead to Da Nosta.

Personality

Ironspike was always ready, believing all situations to be possible traps and all creatures possible dangers. He believed the Mazticans were like children in need of strong leadership, or were savages little better than orcs .
