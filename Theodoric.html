Theodoric was a cleric of Tempus in the Chondath in 1358 DR .

Activities

Theodoric was the patriarch of Tempus in Chondath and a scholar specializing in the old Chessentan Empire .

History

In 1358 DR , the arcanaloth Yrkhetep manipulated him, making Theodoric insane. Totally mad, Theodoric used the Staff of Skulls given to him by the yugoloth to raise an undead army and lead them to battle.

Theodoric faced in battle the Company of the Singing Dawn , the Llandrydd's Steel , and Enhanen 's men.

Personality

Theodoric suffered schizophrenia. In order to use the staff, he had developed a second personality: an evil cleric from the time of the Chessentan Empire.
