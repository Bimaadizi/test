The Ziggurat of Eternal Victory was a massive pyramid temple dedicated to Gilgeam , the Supreme Ruler of Unther .

Location

The temple was located in the Palace District of the capital city of Unthalass .

Description

The Ziggurat of Eternal Victory a step pyramid with sixteen tiers. The entire surface of the pyramid was covered in gold , bronze , and other metals and the top was crowned with blue brickwork. The temple stood 800 feet (240 meters) tall, making it one of the largest man-made structures throughout Faerûn .

History

Much of the Ziggurat of Eternal Victory was sacked and burned following Gilgeam's death in the mid–14 th century DR .
