Khayar Ironfist was a member of the Ironfist clan on the Sword Coast North in the 1374 DR .

History

Khayar was an older dwarf, who went to explore the Ironfist stronghold. However, he was captured by bugbears .

Khelgar Ironfist and the Kalach-Cha could have encountered Khayar while he was imprisoned by the bugbears. Once the bugbears were defeated, Khayar would be freed and the Kalach-Cha could have gained influence with Khelgar, so long as there was no mention of Khelgar deserting the clan.
