Daried Selsherryn was a noble sun elf bladesinger .

History

He once lived in Cormanthyr in an area around what would later be called Mistledale . There, his family had an estate ringed with a garden and protecting wards and guardians were created to protect the residence and the tombs of the family line.

He and his family later Retreated to Evermeet .

In 1374 DR , he was a troop leader of an elven resistance force overlooking the River Ashaba in the vicinity of his abandoned home and came to revisit it. The estate held a family moonblade that had lost its powers.
