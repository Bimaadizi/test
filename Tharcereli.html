Tharcereli was the ulitharid Creedmaster of the Loretakers Creed of Oryndoll in 1370 DR and 1372 DR .

Personality

Tharcereli had an abiding hunger for knowledge but never shared this knowledge with non-illithids.

History

Tharcereli was a member of the city's Elder Concord during the Time of Troubles and Ilsensine gave it tendril rings of Ilsensine . As the leader of the Creed of the Loretakers, Tharcereli was the third most powerful individual of the city, behind only the Encephalithid and the high cleric of Ilsensine.
