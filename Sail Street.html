Sail Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Sail Street originated in the Dock Ward where Dock Street met Fishgut Court . The road passed west into the Castle Ward near the waterfront to end at the junction with Coin Alley . Sail Street was intersected by Lion Street , Tarnished Silver Alley , Lackpurse Lane , Sakiir's Street , and Belnimbra's Street .
