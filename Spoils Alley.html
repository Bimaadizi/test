Spoils Alley was an alleyway of hard-packed dirt and gravel in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Description

This alleyway was heavily crowded with barrels, crates, and the like.

Geography

This alleyway curved due southwest off of the southernmost end of Quill Alley , then terminated in a dead-end.

Activities

Spoils Alley was primarily used as a delivery route for the backs of shops, but was also used to conduct shady meetings. And prior to its dissolution, members of the Thieves Guild of Waterdeep would gather there to divvy up their takings.
