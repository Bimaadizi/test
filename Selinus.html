History

Just after the start of the War of the Silver Marches , Selinus discovered that his leader, Varen , was a member of a ring of horse thieves who stole Riders' horses. Despite having no clear proof, he accused Varen. This caused a schism among the Riders.

Personality

Selinus was young, idealistic, and proud. He always saw the Riders as a bastion of virtue. Selinus believed himself the only person able to restore the honor of the Riders.
