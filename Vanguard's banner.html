The vanguard's banner was an artifact from the frigid region of Icewind Dalem used to inspire combatants in battle.

Powers

The vanguard's banner was a crude gonfalon decorated with an image of an upright sword . Every friendly combatant was filled with inspiration and bravery, affected by the banner's quiet and deep power.

History

The vanguard's banner was in use in the late 15 th century DR in the Ten-Towns during the black ice crisis of the Year of the Iron Dwarf's Vengeance, 1485 DR .
