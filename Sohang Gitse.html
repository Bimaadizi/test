Sohang Gitse was a gambler and a yakuza in Shou Lung .

History

In 1358 DR , at the komite in Suijeng , Sohang Gitse took bets on any and all contests in the tournament. Although technically a criminal, he was scrupulously honest about the gambling he managed. Sohang had a vast network of informants to help him set odds for each fight. He had gathered a great deal information on specific fighters, judges, and the tournament in general, which he would share with anyone for a price.
