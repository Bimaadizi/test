Rezron was a dwarvish rogue and student of the adventurers ' Neverwinter Academy in Neverwinter during the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Rezron was a classmate and part of the same graduation class as the person who would become the Hero of Neverwinter .

History

Rezron had been one of the Neverwinter Academy students for some time by the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , during the deadly outbreak of the Wailing Death . During his graduation ceremony, alongside the Hero of Neverwinter, he met a venerated local hero: Aribeth de Tylmarande . Rezron was honored to meet Aribeth, thinking her to be quite awe-inspiring for a half-elf . Just moments later, the infamous attack on the Academy took place. It took the lives of many students and instructors. Rezron was among the defenders who stood against the attackers. It is unknown if he survived the battle or was felled by an enemy sword or spell .
