Adom Kimball was a King of Moray in the Moonshae Isles during the early-to-mid 12 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Petulant Dragon, 1135 DR , Queen Eleanor of Callidyrr appointed King Adom as regent over her realm following the death of her husband, King Edwin Carrathal II . He served as regent for six years before Edwin II’s sole heir, Adrian Carrathal , was old enough to be crowned king in the Year of Eyes, 1141 DR .
