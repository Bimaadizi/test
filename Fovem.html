Fovem was a thief and a seller of black market goods from the city of Athkatla in Amn in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was a human man who worse tan and brown thieves garb and leathers .

Possessions

Fovem offered items at reduced prices. His stock included several types of weapons and armor, including dangerous potions of explosions .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Fovem peddled his illicit goods in Athkatla's Wave District , just outside the Galvarey Estate , secretly a Harper safehouse. He also offered information on the city and its goings-on for a price of 50 gold pieces . Fovem was not affiliated with the Shadow Thieves of Athkatla nor the new emergent mysterious rival guild; however, he claimed that the Grandmaster of the Shadow Thieves sat on the Council of Six and Renal the Bloodscalp ran the guild's daily activities.
