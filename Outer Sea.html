History

During the Twelfth Ser么s War , a number of ports along the Outer Sea were attacked by the forces of Iakhovas . The war came to an end when the Whirlpool Gate of Myth Nantar was used to expel Iakhovas from the Inner Sea and into the Outer Sea.

Inhabitants

The Outer Sea shared many of the same sentient races as the Inner Sea, most especially aquatic elves . Other races included merfolk , morkoths , and sahuagins . However, they exhibited a number of sociological and physiological differences from their Inner Sea counterparts.
