Galoomp the Bookkeeper was a book merchant from the City of Coin, Athkatla , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Galoomp was a portly man with fair skin.

History

Galoomp's book stall could be found in the grand market known as Waukeen's Promenade in Athkatla's Center District in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR . Among more mundane tomes, Galoomp carried a selection of mage 's spell scrolls .
