The Effects of Drought Upon the Farm Economies of the Eastern Realms was a heavy tome on the topic of agriculture, written and published sometime before mid-14 th century DR .

History

A copy of The Effects of Drought Upon the Farm Economies of the Eastern Realms was found in the massive book collection of Klavikus , a demon in the service of Zhengyi the Witch-King of Vaasa in the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR . Klavikus's copy was cared for and carefully marked with silken bookmark.
