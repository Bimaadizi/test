Simon Broven was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate and a former Flaming Fist mercenary in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Simon was a human man with light brown hair, eyes, and a light skin tone.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , healer Sylbert spent his days at the Harbourview Park in the Lower City, helping Simon's recovery after either suffering some trauma. The man lked to come to the Harbourview Park because of the silence he could find there. Even with Sylbert's help, he still had long road to recovery ahead.
