Rawleigh Gallaway was the individual who ran the Gallaway Trading Coaster and traveled through Lonelywood , in the early 14 th century DR .

Description

In his young days, Rawleigh was a fair-skinned human man with dark brown hair.

History

In the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , Rawleigh Gallaway ran caravans for his father's trading coaster and ended up being stuck in the town of Lonelywood when Bremen's Run became cut off by barbarian forces of chieftain Wylfdene . Subsequently, Rawleigh, along with other family members, took over Gallaway Trading Coaster after Rawleigh's father, Ilmus Gallaway , was unable to be in charge due to his age and love of Cormyrian brandy . In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , Rawleigh still ran the family business. As a "one last favor," Rawleigh requested his trusted adviser and employee, Jemeliah , to lend his expertise and sharp mind to Deirdre Gallaway in Targos , who strove to prove herself as a capable businesswoman and merchant.
