Dreylil Andrus was captain of the palace guard of King Yarin Frostmantle of the Damara circa 1484 DR .

Following the assassination of King Frostmantle, he worked with Court Wizard Red Mazzie to deflect suspicion from Queen Concettina Delcasio in order to ensure a peaceful transition to her rule.
