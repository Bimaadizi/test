Stonemason Gergerann was a duergar craftsman of Clan Flameshade that took up residence in Grymforge for a time during the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Clan Flameshade were hired by the Cult of the Absolute 's Sergeant Thrinn to help Nere with the excavation of Grymforge with the aim of staking a claim on the Adamantine Forge . Gergerann helped Kith in his studies of the ruined architecture of the underground fortress.
