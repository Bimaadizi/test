Eddard Silvershield was a young nobleman from the wealthy Silvershield family of Baldur's Gate .

Relationships

Eddard was the son of Grand Duke Entar Silvershield .

History

At some point during the iron crisis of 1368 DR , Eddard traveled with a caravan to his home city of Baldur's Gate. However, the caravan was assailed en route by bandits and Eddard was killed.

The dwarven mercenary leader Kagain led a search for Eddard's whereabouts, as his outfit was responsible for the caravan's safety, though the young noble was already dead for some time.
