History

Quallo was a relatively normal man until he came under the influence of the intelligent sword known as Lilarcor . After that he took to living within Athkatla's sewer system, watching over the sword's resting place.

He knew the secrets to perform the ritual that would reveal Lilarcor , and allow it to be retrieved by another individual.
