Runepowder and the Modern Gnome was a tome that contained a treatise on the recreation of the mythical runepowder , penned sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Contents

This extremely wordy tome collected lore and gnomish legends regarding the mythical and deadly explosive substance called runepowder. It also presented a possibility of the lost substance's recreation by the modern gnomish masters. The book also mused on such seemingly random topics as the space-time environment and the thought-behavior-action philosophy.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a copy of Runepowder and the Modern Gnome belonged to Gyldro Angleiron the Smith and the proprietor of Rivington General in the Rivington district of Baldur's Gate.
