The Prince Templars assumed rulership of the city-state of Westgate when King Thartryn III , and all heirs of the Ilistar Dynasty , died from plague in the Year of the Burning Tree, 890 DR .

Organization

This group served as the holy council of Westgate. The high priests that composed the ranks of the Prince Templars formed an oligarchic theocracy. The Prince Templars would hold power in Westgate until the Year of the Thirsty Sword, 900 DR , when they were overthrown by the mercenary lord Gostaraj .
