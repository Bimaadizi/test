Thara Olósynne was the first Mage-Queen of the Olósynne Dynasty who ruled the nation of Aglarond .

Activities

Thara ruled Aglarond jointly with her twin sister Ulae Olósynne . They inherited the throne upon the death of their father, King Philaspur .

Relationships

Thara died without producing any heirs.
