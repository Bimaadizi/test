Olap Tamewater was an engineer son of Jorun Tamewater who worked in Targos in the early 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , the Ten-Towns and the town of Targos became besieged by a goblin army that served the Legion of the Chimera . Jorun Tamewater, an experienced engineer, and his son Olap Tamewater, constructed a crane to transport lumber up the cliff from Targos's docks to the outskirt of the town to build defensive palisades.
