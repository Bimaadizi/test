Mirt's Mansion was an opulent villa and the home of Mirt the Moneylender located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The villa stood three stories tall.

Location

Mirt's Mansion was located in the southwest corner of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the northwest side of where Coin Alley met Tarnished Silver Alley .
