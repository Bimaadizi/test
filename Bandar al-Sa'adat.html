Bandar al-Sa'adat was a town located on the isle of Masud Jazayir in the Nada al-Hazan archipelago within the Crowded Sea south of mainland Zakhara .

Organization

The town could field a militia of 100 soldiers who were adept at defending against pirate raids. Several wizards offered magical assistance to the militia in times of need.

Activities

Sea trade was a major source of income for the town and also caused its population to fluctuate.

Inhabitants

This town was ruled by Mahmud Ben Aziz . His brother Karim Ben Aziz was captain of the marketplace guards. Much of Ben Aziz's family held other positions of note within the small town.

Masudi islanders had a love of words, opting to speak in colorful ways to alleviate boredom. Outsiders to the island often stuck out just because of the way they spoke.
