The Ticklebelly Hills was a grouping of rocky hills and cliffs found in the Moonsea North region of North Faer√ªn .

Description

The Ticklebelly Hills comprised rocky mounds, large crags and sparse vegetation. The landscape was relatively difficult to traverse, at least for humanoid travelers.

Geography

The hills were situated along the southern slopes of the eastern Dragonspine Mountains , north of the Grass Sea and the Giant's Cairn .

Flora & Fauna

Bulettes were known to dig their tunnels beneath the earth of the Ticklebelly Hills.

History

In the Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR , a shooting star fell from the skies into the hills, causing unease among the local wildlife and forming a massive crater .

Notable Locations

Inhabitants

As of the late 15 th century DR , orcs of the Burning Banner tribe were observed to be active throughout the hills.

Stone giants and their saber-toothed tiger pets were known to wander the hills and the surrounding lands.
