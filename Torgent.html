History

In the Year of the Harp, 1355 DR , Torgent was guarding a passage from the home of Shyrrhr to the Palace of Waterdeep when Mirt tried to enter with Asper . Mirt was allowed to pass, and asked for the location of Piergeiron Paladinson , to which Torgent replied by warning him the Open Lord was not himself lately, and had taken up his full armor and helm in the Inner Audience Chamber .
