The Knotted Rope Tavern was a tavern in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep in the late-15 th century DR .

History

Some time following the Spellplague , a memorial was set up in the tavern dedicated to all the captains that had lost their lives to seawraiths in Deepwater Harbor . This included an image of a captain by the name of Scrimshaw .
