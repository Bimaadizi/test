Sharna was the oldest and preeminent of Vhaemas the Bastard 's children.

History

Sharna was born in 1340 DR to Vhaemas and Eaja Kamaar .

She participated in the Twelfth Serôs War following her father at Iakhovas 's side. Sharna and all her warriors never saw the redemption of her father and his rejecting of Iakhovas.

Personality

Sharna was full of hate for her grandfather. She was a capable leader for her people.
