Zilchyn Q'Leptin , or simply Zilch , was a drow wizard from Menzoberranzan in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Zilchyn was a kleptomaniac. This behavior increased with the arrival of the demon lord .

History

Circa 1486 DR , he stole some spell components and was discovered. He fled the city, losing his spellbook , and had an encounter with a black pudding that gave him many scars over the left part of his body. Zilchyn finally arrived in Mantol-Derith , where he hoped to begin a new life.
