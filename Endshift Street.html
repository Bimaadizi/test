Endshift Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

Endshift Street was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . The road branched off of the northeast side of the Breezeway , ending at a dead end at a small unnamed courtyard after several buildings.
