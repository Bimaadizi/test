Farnath Ilistar was the first King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Farnath was a skilled wizard under the tutelage of the Handweaver . After Nessmara , the lamia noble who killed King Lyonarth , was revealed and slain in a battle to the death that also claimed Lyonarth, Farnath was named King in the Year of the Ensorceled Kings, 616 DR .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Belhendar "the Stout" .
