Beaufort was a Netherese priest who worked at the Mythomian Warehouse in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Beaufort worked for Adrian Freeman at the warehouse, serving as a security guard for the premises alongside Whichcoot .
