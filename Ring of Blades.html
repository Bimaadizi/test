The Ring of Blades was a tavern of Hastarl in the 3 rd century DR .

Location

The tavern was somewhere in Hastarl .

Services

People drank there.

History

Between 228 DR and 229 DR , Farl , a local thief, invited the outlaw Eladar the Dark, who was secretly Prince Elminster Aumar to drink at the tavern. Once there, he got him drunk to know him a bit better.
