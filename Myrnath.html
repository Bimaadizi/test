Myrnath was a high elf that was abducted by a nautiloid in the 1490s DR.

Description

Myrnath had white-yellow skin and weighed 110.2 lb (49.99 kg). He wore blue trousers and brown leather boots .

History

Some time during the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Myrnath was taken aboard a nautiloid. Myrnath's brain was used to give birth to the intellect devourer known as Us .
