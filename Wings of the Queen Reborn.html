The Wings of the Queen Reborn was a cathedral dedicated to the dragon goddess Tiamat . It was the center of operations for the Church of Tiamat within the Cold Lands during the latter half of the 14 th century DR .

Location

The temple was located in a cavern within the dungeon complex beneath the ruined Castle Perilous in Vaasa .

Activities

The main focus of the temple's operations was to take revenge on the lands of Damara and the faithful of Bahamut after King Gareth Dragonsbane and his companions slew an aspect of Tiamat in her own lair at Bahamut's direction during the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR . This goal was of such great personal importance to Tiamat that she would frequently send an avatar to oversee the temple's activities.

From the Wings of the Queen Reborn, the Church of Tiamat launched operations throughout the Cold Lands to undermine Damara both directly, by bringing chromatic dragons together on their doorstep, as well as indirectly, such as by infiltrating Cult of the Dragon cells. By the 1370s DR , the temple complex had become the site of an ambitious and profane crossbreeding project to create dragonspawn , monstrosities not seen since the Dragonfall War . These creatures were then sent far and wide to do Tiamat's bidding.

Defenses

Aside from the faithful of Tiamat, the temple was protected by dragons and dragonspawn, not to mention the inherent dangers of attempting to reach the temple through the treacherous ruins of Castle Perilous.

History

In the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR , shortly after Gareth Dragonsbane's victory over Zhengyi and Orcus and his planting of the Tree-Gem of Bloodstone , Tiamat took notice of the Tree-Gem and sent her avatar to Castle Perilous and summoned flights of chromatic dragons of all colors from across the Cold Lands to prepare to help her take revenge on the hero and his realm. The temple was created soon after, overseen by a cult known as the Handmaidens of the Undying Queen . Among other things, this cult sought out the eggs and remains of metallic dragons to feed to their goddess.

Within a decade, the various chambers beneath Castle Perilous had been cleared sufficiently to begin housing a growing force of cultists, and the old cruel laboratories of Zhengyi had been repurposed to begin making dragonspawn.

Rumors & Legends

Around the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the Cult of the Dragon heard rumors that the temple was home to a multi-headed dracolich . The Cult suspected that this was a trap by the Church of Tiamat meant to trick or control them, and so rather than send their own agents to parlay with this rumored dracolich, they began circulating the rumors further in hopes that adventurers would find the truth for them. In fact, these rumors spoke of the avatar of Tiamat herself.

Inhabitants

The temple was maintained by the Handmaidens of the Undying Queen, led by Hesthera Draketalons . It was also inhabited by chromatic dragons who had heeded Tiamat's summons beginning in 1359 DR , and over the next decade and a half, it would become populated by growing numbers of dragonspawn and cultists.

The most notable inhabitant, however, was the avatar of Tiamat herself which frequented the temple.
