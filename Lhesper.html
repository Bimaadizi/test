Lhesper was a ruined town on the shore of Lake Lhespen in the western region of the Shaar .

History

Lhesper was founded in the Year of Whispering Stones, âˆ’373 DR , by refugees from Lapaliiya who were fleeing plague. The city remained independent until th Year of the Wailing Mothers, 297 DR , when the army of Qysara Shoon V marched across the Shaar conquering everything in their path. The city would not regain its independence until the Year of Silver Holly, 437 DR , and again in the Year of Unleashed Fears, 451 DR .

The town was destroyed by a pack of rampaging gnolls in 7th century DR . Around the year of Wild Magic Yuan-ti began to move into the area, searching for some mysterious buried object.

Activities

As of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Lhesper was taken over by members from the Coiled Cabal who were searching for the Emeralds of Merrshaulk .
