Dak Zarre was a mercenary leader in Chondath in 1358 DR .

Activities

Dak led the Blood Hawks mercenary company, composed of many Turmish warriors. They acted as military forces in Chondath.

History

In 1358 DR, Dak Zarre was called to Hlath to a meeting of prominent citizens gathered to discuss the barbarian army from Kara-Tur that menaced the city. He was hired to work under Pohidge Plam .

He took part in the defense of the city.

Personality

Dak was able in tactics but preferred to follow orders, avoiding discussions about the command structure. He and his men were quite courageous.
