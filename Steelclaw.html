Steelclaw was a hairless cat , the feline slayer of illithid tadpoles from the Moonrise Towers during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Steelclaw was a female hairless cat who thought herself to be a dangerous killer and a powerful foe. She referred to humanoids as "the furless ones."

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Steelclaw could be found just outside the walls of the Moonrise Towers in the deadly Shadow-cursed Lands . The cat traveled through lapping waters on leaking ships to get to her new hunting territory.
