Barlowe was a male human of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR .

Activities

Barlowe was the runner of the smuggler's warehouse .

History

Barlowe was a member of the thieves guild, who oversaw the transactions of the smuggler's warehouse.

Gameplay

If the Kalach-Cha and the companions sided with the thieves and have transported weapons to the warehouse, Barlowe would give them a reward before the Watch came to attack the thieves.

If the Kalach-Cha and the companions sided with the Watch, then Barlowe would attack them in the warehouse. Once killed, they could take the papers, which contained codes of the suppliers.
