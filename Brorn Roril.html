Brorn Roril was a retired swordcaptain of the Purple Dragons in Suzail , Cormyr , in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He was fat.

Personality

He was louder and prouder than the rest of the regulars at his favourite tavern.

Activities

While retired, he became a regular at The Goose of Doom , a tavern.

History

After the massacre at the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Brorn Roril ran into The Goose of Doom to proclaim the Obarskyrs dead, and civil war as having begun. As the Obarskyrs were alive, he was mistaken in so doing.
