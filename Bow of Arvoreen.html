The Bow of Arvoreen was an enchanted red shortbow wielded by an aspiring halfling paladin , Mazzy Fentan , in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The bow was imbued with divine magics and possessed a +2 enchantment and could only be wielded by a halfling.

History

This bow was gifted to Mazzy Fentan several years before the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR by her deity – Arvoreen , The Vigilant Guardian. The bow remained in her hands as she set out to stand against the Shade Lord and his pet dragon Thaxll'ssillyia , who infested an ancient ruined temple dedicated to Amaunator with darkness.
