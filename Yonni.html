Yonni was a Cashguard in the employ of the Counting House of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Yonni was a human woman with tan skin and gray eyes. As a Cashguard, she wore a set of uniform breastplate armor and a helmet . Yonni was armed with a two-handed heavy crossbow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Cashguard Yonni was tasked with guarding and patrolling the private docks that belonged to the Counting House in the Lower City , alongside a number of fellow Cashguards and the city's steel watchers .
