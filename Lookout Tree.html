The Lookout Tree was an elven tree in the Dalelands in the mid–8 th century DR .

Description

It was a shadowtop tree, with a near-endless spiral staircase. Up top, the leaves were large enough to act as cushions; for those who were guests of such elves as Starsunder , the leaves would mold themselves into chairs if needed.

Defenses

Those who did not walk up the stairs would startle the tree on contact, and it would defend itself.

History

In the Year of the Awakening Wyrm, 767 DR , during the Chaos of the Art , a mage from Galadorna , Umbregard , visited Tangletrees looking for answers regarding Elminster Aumar , one of the Chosen of Mystra . He was met by Starsunder, who brought him to the Lookout Tree to converse.
