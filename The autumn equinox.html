The autumn equinox was a magical ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The ring balanced the power of the sun and moon , channeling the full energies of autumn and the equinox . It helped the wearer avoid all sorts of danger, let them strike more rapidly in combat, and further empowered those with deep knowledge and attunement with nature.

History

The ring was found when Elred Maglan and the Elves of Cormanthor searched the Burial Glen for the deceased Earynspieir Ongluth .
