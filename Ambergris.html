Ambergris was a substance extracted from the stomaches of whales , giant whales , leviathans , and narwhals . It was a precious trade commodity used for creating various aromatics and perfumes .

Description

This gray, waxy, oily and highly flammable substance was secreted by sick whales. In its voluminous fresh form, ambergris was sickeningly yellow and emitted a profound stench. However it could be sold for 1,000 to as much as 20,000 gold pieces in a big city.

Availability

Ambergris was sold thorough Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue , priced at 1 gold piece per 6 ounces (170 grams).

In Waterdeep , the City of Splendors, ambergris could often be found among perfumes, stored in silver and glass vials, even though it was a scarce substance, imported through the city's Dock Ward .

The city of Velen in the Lands of Intrigue was a major exporter of various products of the sea, including fish , shellfish , and whales . Oils , pearls , and ambergris were among these trade goods.

The sailors of the Sea of Fallen Stars often hunted the intelligent whale bards of Serôs for their ambergris.
