Drunster's Well was a well east of Soubar , dry by 1370 DR .

Characteristics

Dry by 1370 DR , the well had been blocked with a cairn of stones to keep animals from wandering inside.

History

After the well ran dry, other, deeper wells were dug west of its location.

East of it lay one of the refuges created by Maldiglas Turntower during his time as the Magister .
