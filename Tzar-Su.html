Tzar-Su was a malicious spirit in Shou Lung in 1358 DR .

History

In 1358 DR, Tzar-Su unleashed a gorgon he had created from the Qui Ren family's plowing ox inside the House of Qui Ren . He unleashed the monster because he was offended he not personally been invited to the inn (he was a spirit who took offense at all manner of slights, real and imagined). However, some adventurers and the wu jen Ko Ho San were on hand to deal with the gorgon and Tzar-Su.
