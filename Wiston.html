Wiston of Yartar was a master apothecary in Daggerford around 1370 DR .

Activities

Wiston invented and sold many potions and herbal remedies in his shop , Wiston Apothecary , which was located on Tymora Alley and Wall Street in north Daggerford near the Farmer's Gate .
