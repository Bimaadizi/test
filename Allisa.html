Allisa of the Mists was a powerful human paladin and a "mistrider" or "mistwalker," active in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Allisa was a talented combatant and a devoted follower of her deity . While she used her strength to fight, she never hesitated to shield members of her church from harm. She was known for her gentle and caring personality that contrasted with her strong exterior. She spoke little.

Possessions

Allisa was known to wear a full set of enchanted +2 plate armor as well as a magical +3 shield which bolstered her in combat. Additionally, she wielded a scimitar of speed and a helm of telepathy , giving her the advantage in any fight.

Abilities

Allisa of the Mists gained her nickname for her uncanny ability to pass through any mist, along with her unicorn mount, into any of the Domains of Dread she chose. She was even more rarely defeated than she spoke.

Activities

The paladin was known to arrive always with the morning mists.

History

At some point, Allisa's trusted mount was a unicorn named Skuldreir . Later, the creature was replaced by another unicorn named Cissl who found the paladin on its own accord and the duo were inseparable ever since.

Rumors & Legends

Sages had debated that the mysterious heroine may have been a minor avatar of Lathander . A few claimed she must've been tied to the lands of Ravenloft .
