Consolation of the Dark was an introductory Sharran text, penned in or before the 15 th century DR .

Content

Consolation of the Dark claimed that amongst other gods, only Shar was honest and direct, not dispensing shallow promises and false claims. Shar's promise to bring the Eternal darkness was a promise of curing all pain. As such, all followers of Sharran dogma were to bring pain and suffering in order to help others understand their deity and that her promise of darkness was a promise of kindness.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a copy of Consolation of the Dark was found within the library of the Cloister of Sombre Embrace , a secret Sharran sect underneath the Lower City of Baldur's Gate .
