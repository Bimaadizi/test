Samirson Yarth was one of the best assassins during the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR .

History

Samirson was a very efficient killer living in the city of Urmlaspyr in Sembia . He was so deadly that the god Bhaal praised him.

Yarth was in the act of killing a man when the god Bane called to him the souls of all assassins and Samirson died immediately.
