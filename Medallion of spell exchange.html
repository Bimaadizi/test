A medallion of spell exchange was a magical amulet that allowed a spellcaster to exchange one spell for another once per day.

Description

Originally created by Pax , an apprentice of Mordenkainen , these medallions were the result of heavy manipulation of the spell Mordenkainen's lucubration until they could exchange spells one-for-one.

Powers

The medallion of spell exchange allowed the wearer to exchange one spell per day from the 6th circle or lower with another that they had access to.
