Chukka was a kenku mechanic and member of the Knucklebones Gang in Avernus in the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

Chukka lost its weapon, before replacing the lost weapon with a silvered pike .

Personality

Chukka was animated and irritable.

Relationships

Chukka and Clonk , known together as "the Magpies", were members of the Knucklebones Gang and were loyal to Mad Maggie .

Activities

Circa 1492 DR , they repaired infernal war machines in Fort Knucklebone .
