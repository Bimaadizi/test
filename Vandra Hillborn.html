Vandra Hillborn was the leader of a community of dwarves that relocated to the city of Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR . She famously led an expedition into the depths of Mount Hotenow to explore an newly-uncovered dwarfhold from ancient Delzoun , in search of an entrance to fabled city of Gauntlgrym ,

Relationships

Vandra worked with a number of dwarven scholars, including Darney the geologist, the historian Thrimbul , and a surveyor named Baera .

History

Sometime during or before the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Vandra Hillborn was elected as the leader among a large dwarven population that formed in Neverwinter. She helped her people set out on the Hillborn Expedition, a journey into Mount Hotenow to explore Fireguard Fortress in order find clues about Clan Hillborn 's ancestral home. During the expedition they encountered the forces of the mad fire giant Gommoth , and helped how they could to stop the cult's disastrous scheme to reawaken the primordial Maegera .

When members of the expedition unfortunately became separated from the rest of their dwarven brethren. Vandra worked with the Hero of the Sleeping Dragon Bridge to find the members of geologist Durney's team and gather information about the mines that were previously lost.
