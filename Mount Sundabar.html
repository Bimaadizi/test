Mount Sundabar was a mountain found in Sossal in the Cold Lands of northeast Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Geography

Mount Sundabar stood in the northern end of the Icerim Mountains in southeastern Sossal. The mountain peak jutted west from the rest of the Icerim Mountains and lay southwest of the settlement of Sundice .

History

When the Ice Kingdom of Dareth first fell to the White dragons of Hoarfaern the surviving dwarves, alongside their rescuers from Clan Shattered Shield of Citadel Sundbarr reestablished themselves in this mountain and named it Sundabar after the latter clans former hold in −327 DR . It was sundered four years later in −323 DR by the vengeful wyrms of Hoarfaern using mighty magical items of elder origin, that sheard the very top of the mountain off and left a great plateau. Some time after the sundering of the mountain, a group of Avariel nomads happened upon the desolate and once-more uninhabited mountain and set up a redoubt for their race, remaking the peak in Glassteel .

Notable Locations

The entire missing mountain top had been replaced by the great Aerie of the Snow Eagles by the Avariel as a citadel and temple to Aerdrie Faenya

Inhabitants

The Avariel lived on the slopes, and peak plateau of the mountain, at least as late as 1372 DR .
