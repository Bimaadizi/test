Crawford Cordragon , known as the friendly dragon, was a dragon from the Whamite Isles . With his cordial nature and power of flight, Crawford Bucket was open to allying with any side in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
