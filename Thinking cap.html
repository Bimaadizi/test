First appearanceHordes of Dragonspear

Thinking caps were a species of giant fungi that grew underground.

Description

These fungi were not toxic to ingest, though they had a taste reminiscent of chlorine .

Ecology

Habitats

One of the known locations of these fungi was beneath the Misty Forest .

Usages

If removed carefully, the caps of these mushrooms could be used as makeshift rafts .
