Ibn was first mate under Captain Hrolf on the Ruathen pirate ship Elfmaid .

Description

Ibn had fair ruddy skin, blue eyes, and a red beard. He was nearly six feet tall.

Ibn hated elves, particularly Liriel Baenre , whom he tried to kill several times.
