Heldul's Rest was a waystop on the north side of the Way of the Manticore halfway between Wheloon and Dreamer's Rock . It was home to a small keep called Stonewatch with a garrison of Purple Dragons and War Wizards .

History

Around 1479 DR , the local commander Ornrion Lhornan Drakehar performed an investigation into the nearby rancher Nars Thormil , who had a suspiciously large supply of thimdror oxen .
