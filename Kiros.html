Chief Kiros was the leader of the centaurs of Guard in Bloodstone Barony in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He had a grizzled, slow-talking, but powerful personality. He was always terribly formal and precise.

Activities

Kiros was a kind of sage about history, warfare, and nature. He served as the leader of Guard's Council of Elders, which included War Chief Valon and Elder Shinar .

History

By 1358 DR , Kiros had led his village in joining the Alliance of Belt Watchers with alongside the halflings of Waukeshire and the dwarves of Clan Orothiar . That year, Kiros and his fellows met Gareth Dragonsbane 's party. Later, he joined the council that ruled the Barony.
