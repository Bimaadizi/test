Jorunn Nightbury was a rather shy horse breeder that lived and worked in the city of Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR . He was employed by the Belt patriar family, whose wealth was attributed to the sale of quality steeds.

By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Jorunn had accumulated a great amount of debt through excessing gambling.
