Baratathlaer , also called Tayyib-kher , was a male ancient brass dragon who made its lair somewhere in the Scimitar Spires in Anauroch . He was known to be still alive by 1373 DR .

The Bedine consider him to be a good-natured protector of the Scimitar Spires and he has been known to hold-up an entire tribe's travel by engaging in long conversations with their elders.
