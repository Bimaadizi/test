The Wheloon Watch House was a watch house located in the city of Wheloon in Cormyr . It stood at the intersection of Wheloon Way and the Way of the Manticore .

The watch house was a base for the Wheloon Watch and also served as a barracks for the local Purple Dragons . Also a part of the building was the Wheloon Jail and an armory. Around thirty Dragons manned the watch house at a time, either hearing public complaints, guarding the jail, or sleeping.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , some prisoners of the Wheloon Jail, those who chose to repent and perform holy service in exchange for their sentences being commuted, were recruited to serve as temple guards for the new Temple of Mystra .
