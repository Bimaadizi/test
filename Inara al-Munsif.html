Inara al-Munsif was a princess of Hudid in the Pantheist League of interior Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Personality

Inara inherited many of her mother's good traits. She was polite and preferred to lead by example.

Activities

Educated and determined, Inara chose to follow her mother's path as a moralist . She rose steadily through the ranks of the church and later served as the caliph's Chief Vizier. She was the caliph's legs and voice throughout the city of Hudid.

Inara was tasked with determining the validity of accusations that Balanite heretics were operating out of Hudid.

History

Inara was born in Huzuz .

Relationships

Inara was the daughter of Caliph Sajah al-Munsif . Jinara was her sister. She was involved in a secret romance with Haji bin Marat , a heretical Balanite .
