Cormac was the husband of Brigid , a lady of of means from the Rivington district of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Cormac was a foppish man who preferred the color green. He wore a brown vest over a swamp-green frilled tunic and a large green feathered beret. Cormac average human features, shaved head, hazel eyes, and fair skin.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the city of Baldur's Gate was under a looming threat of the army of the Absolute and was flooded by refugees from the Western Heartlands fleeing from the cultists. During the time of crisis, crime was on the rise, and the criminal organization known as The Guild was getting bold and aggressive. Brigid's husband Cormac was attacked by the Guild's thugs, mugged, and beaten. Brigid was outraged by the crime wave and was prepared to give a stern talking to the Flaming Fist mercenaries of Rivington where the attack took place.
