Sadik el-Huari was a Zakharan coffee trader in Yeoman on the plane of Bytopia .

Description

He was a dark-skinned man.

Personality

Sadik was a haggling enthusiast, and would reward skilled customers with free cups of coffee.

Activities

He sold coffees and teas from a variety of planes , including popular varieties such as "Krigala Supreme" from the Beastlands , "Mountain Majesty" from Mount Celestia , as well as "Devotion" and "Purity" from Elysium .

His tent in Yeoman's Bazaar was strewn with pillows and low tables, and was a popular site for business dealings among the local elite, including Mayor Lizabet the Seer .

For business reasons, Sadik displayed the symbol of the Order of the Planes-Militant , but was not himself a supporter of that organization.
