Phosphorus was an alchemical substance commonly used as a material component for spells .

Properties

In its pure form, phosphorus was a white, waxy-looking substance had to be kept submerged away from air.

Availability

In 3520 NY (−339 DR) , the Elemental Store of Karsus Enclave sold pure elemental powdered phosphorus for 3 gp per 1 ounce (28 grams).

Aurora's Emporium sold phosphorus for 1 gp per 3 oz (85 g), intended for use by priests and wizards as reagents.

Usages

The Netherese use phosphorus in the production of explosives.
