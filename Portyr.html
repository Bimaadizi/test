Portyr ( pronounced : / p ɔːr ˈ t ɪər / por-TEER was a wealthy and influential patriar family of Baldur's Gate .

Activities

The Portyrs made their coin in maritime shipping, investing, and trading.

Base of Operations

The Portyr estate was an unassuming manor in the city's Temples District .

History

After Grand Duke Valarken and the Band of the Red Moon 's failed coup in the mid-15 th century DR , Dillard Portyr, the only duke to survive, was named Grand Duke of Baldur's Gate. He reformed the Parliament of Peers , granting the institution more power over the day-to-day business of running the city. As Grand Duke, Dillard Portyr was known for being easily manipulated and conflict-averse. He ceded the title of Grand Duke to Ulder Ravengard in the late 15 th century.
