The Myrloch , also known as Lake Myr , was a large freshwater lake located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Myrloch was located at the heart of Myrloch Vale in central Gwynneth. The lake was easily the largest freshwater body of water found within the Moonshae Isles, covering approximately 1,000 square miles (2,600 square kilometers). The Myrloch filled the basin between the Cambro Mountains in the east with the Highlands to the south and west.
