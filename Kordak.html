Kordak was an evil mage during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Kordak was selfish, bloodthirsty, and deceitful.

Abilities

As a wizard he knew a number of spells: charm person , magic missile , stinking cloud , shield , invisibility . Notably, none of those spells controlled undead. He was also slighly better defended than one would expect, presumably due to fairly good agility.

Possessions

He had a sack with 1,500 silver pieces and an ioun stone that improved his physical defenses, presumably a dusty rose prism .

Activities

As of the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , he was trying to set up a secret hideout.

Relationships

He had two zombie servants and a pet snake .

History

In the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , Kordak was in a dilapidated tower within the caverns of Mount Dread , where he tried to set up a secret hideout. If adventurers came across him, he may have tried to charm one of them into helping with a nearby black bear , then ambush them with his zombie underlings and magic after they succeeded, or should they refuse.
