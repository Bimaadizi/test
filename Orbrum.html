The Orbrum was a book on magic known in the 8 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Mistmaidens, 764 DR , the sorceress Dasumia commanded her apprentice Elminster Aumar to fetch the Orbrum from her side table in a well-warded room, the Blue Chamber, in her own Castle of the Lady , along with the other books Prospaer on Nameless Horrors and the Tome of Three Locks .
