Springstep boots were enchanted boots that boosted the wearer's movement in battle.

Description

These elegant and nimble leather books had a bounce and spring back with each step of the wearer.

Powers

Springstep boots possessed a minor enchantment that slightly boosted the wearer's movement speed every time they dashed in combat. The boosting effects ended if the wearer was restrained, entangled , or hindered by similar means.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a pair of springstep boots was stored in the treasure room within the ruined Shattered Sanctum in the Chionthar Valley . Presumably, the boots were among treasures collected in the Cult of the Absolute 's raids on nearby communities.
