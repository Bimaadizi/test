Pien-Hung was a shukenja in Kozakura in 1357 DR .

Activities

Pien-Hung was a traveling shukenja who fell sick and asked for hospitality at the Shrine of the Setting Sun and remained there for a while.

Personality

Pien-Hung returned to robust health thanks to the local sea air and peaceful surroundings. He was a quiet and respectful individual, emanating an aura of cheer and good nature.
