Keltar was a member of the Cult of the Black Earth at the Fane of the Eye under Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Keltar believed Gorx was an emissary of Ogrémoch .

History

In 1491 DR , Keltar was at the Fane when a gate to the Elemental Plane of Earth disgorged Gorx. Keltar quickly pledged himself to what was, in his eyes, the holy envoy of Ogrémoch. He did all Gorx demanded of him, but remained to patrol the Black Earth Hall. However, some adventurers infiltrated the fane and confronted him.
