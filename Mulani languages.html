The Mulani languages group was one of the two major branches of the Rauric languages family and included languages spoken in East Faerûn by the Mulan people.

Subgroups

The Mulani languages were further subdivided into the Mulhorandi and Muhjuri branches.

History

The Mulani languages derived in part from a slave argot of one or more of the Imaskari languages . Over the millennia, the Mulani tongues remained more similar to their Rauric roots than did the Untheric languages .
