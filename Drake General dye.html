Drake General dye was a rare and expensive dye found in the Western Heartlands and the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Drake General dye was valued at fifty gold pieces per bottle. It was created exclusively by Imperial Dyemaster Sigurd and his apprentice, Aurora . This flame-red and gold dye was advertised for those adventurers who valiantly rush into the world of treasures and adventure.
