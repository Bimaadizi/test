TypeFood

Cheddar was a type of cheese .

Description

This white-yellow cheese was often formed in the shape of wheels or blocks. It could be heated up, and emitted a potent smell. Cheddar was sometimes paired and cooked with tomatoes .

Reputation

The great sage , Elminster , often enjoyed eating cheddar, especially when conversing with reputable mages , such as Mordenkainen .
