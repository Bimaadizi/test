Trende Cafe was an eatery located in the affluent Merchant's District of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , the wizard named Samman was swingled by a group of criminals. These people, Marella , Elizza , Moamar , and Rundell , were members of the warlord Myrkyssa Jelan 's army that waged war on the Vast just a year prior. Samman posted a bill that he sought a special magic wand . The criminals arranged a meeting and sealed the deal at the Trende Cafe.
