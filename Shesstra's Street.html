Shesstra's Street was a road paved in hard-packed dirt and gravel in the city of Waterdeep , that stood on the uneven boundary line that separated the city's Castle Ward , Dock Ward , and Trades Ward . Shesstra's Street acted as the southern border of Castle Ward and Trades Ward, while also being the northern border of the Dock Ward.

Geography

This road ran from east to west, cutting through Snail Street as it spanned between Book Street and Gut Alley .

Just north of Blackstar Lane , an unnamed, dead-end alleyway opened off of Shesstra's Street.
