Alima bint Khayri was a halfling corsair living in Al-Azra'Wadi in 1367 DR .

Personality

Alima's cheerful personality led many to underestimate her. She was a savvy negotiator.

Activities

Alima served as the ruler for Al-Azra'Wadi.
