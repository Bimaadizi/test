Cassyt was a cleric of Kelemvor in Phlan during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Cassyt's principal character trait was a sense of humor that was dry and a little warped. Once being sunny and talkative, she later demonstrated a grim determination.

Relationship

She was good friends with Aleyd Burral . Later she had dealings with the Order of the Gauntlet

History

Cassyt was a young worshiper of Kelemvor living in Phlan, working under Yovir Glandon . However, she spent most of her time studying books instead of patrol the graveyard's catacombs. Recently, by the Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR , she with some adventurers were instructed by doomguide Glandon to investigate the Graveyard, where were signaled undead activities and disappearance of some Kelemvorite. This adventure helped her grow.

During the Cult of the Dragon coup in the city, she helped the resistance forces. She guided them through the sewers in order to get Lord Regent Ector Brahms safely out of the city.

In the Year of the Scarlet Witch, 1491 DR , Cassyt was in Mulmaster where investigated about possible corruption of the resurrected Aleyd At last she tried to resonate with Aleyd but her old friend killed Cassyt on surprise
