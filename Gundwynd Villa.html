Gundwynd Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Gundwynd .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward on the northheast corner of where Mendever Street intersected Vondil Street .

Description

The four buildings within the walled estate were arranged around a central garden with a building sitting in each corner. Each building was two or three stories tall.
