Yangor was a bearded devil who was on Avernus for some time during the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .

History

Along with his fellow Narg , Yangor served as a bodyguard for the chain devil Zartar . The pair watched over Zartar during a pilgrimage to the site known as Sundered Chains , as the chained devil sought to claim more power from the god Asmodeus .
