Kochetnik was a Raumviran who lived in Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Activities

Kochetnik was rumored to be the best sailor living in Almorel. His house was located along the waterfront in the far northeast corner of Almorel.
