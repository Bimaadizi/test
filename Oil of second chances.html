Oil of Second Chances was a unique type of oil , always in high demand, and produced by the Church of Tymora .

Powers

Oil of Second Chances was a special brew that could remove curse and grant good luck to the person who used the substance. Apart from the oil's primary use - removing curses , the effect similar to that of the bless spell lasted for thirty-six seconds, while the effects similar to those of the luck spell - for eighteen seconds. Thus, in order to tap into these beneficial magics, the user needed to spend at least six seconds applying to oil.

History

This magic oil was produced in abundance by the clerics of Tymora . However, it always swiftly sold out, leaving many adventurers who missed out on the latest batch furious. These oils were so effective that even clerics of other faiths kept a reserve for when their divine spells weren't effective enough. Churches that opposed Tymora, like the Church of Beshaba , sought for a way to duplicate the Oil of Second Chances but in a way that bestowed curses instead.

In 1281 DR , Oil of Second Chances was available for purchase from Quinn Silverfinger , a cleric of the Golden Lodge in Lonelywood .
