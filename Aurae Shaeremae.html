Lady Aurae Shaeremae was an elf of House Shaeremae in Cormanthor in the 3 rd century DR .

History

In 241 DR , she attended a revel where Elminster substituted Coronal Eltargrim Irithyl . Her husband, Qildor Rewen , spoke for the two of them. She gave a few words of her own during parting.
