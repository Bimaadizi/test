The Lightfinger Tempest was a notably large and powerful water weird that served as a protector of the Lightfinger Estate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The Tempest was one of many water weirds bound in servitude to the Lightfinger family of Waterdeep . In particular, it served as a guardian of one of the early courtyards in the Estate.

When the Estate became overrun by devils in the late 15 th century DR, the Tempest attacked a group of adventurers sent to investigate the disturbance by Open Lord Laeral Silverhand . These adventurers defeated the weird, releasing it from its servitude and allowing its waters to drain away.
