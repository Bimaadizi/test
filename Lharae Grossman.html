Lharae Grossman , who was nicknamed the Lithe , was a thief and inhabitant of Tilverton in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

Lharae was quite beautiful and witty.

Activities

Lharae was a member of the Rogues of Tilverton , though most of her time with the group was spent infiltrating the Cormyrean garrison and the local clergy of Gond in order to gather valuable information.

Relationships

Her father, Artur Grossman , was the leader of the Rogues.

History

In the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , her father Artur had disappeared and was presumed dead. She took up leadership of the guild from that point.
