Naertho was an inhabitant of Myth Drannor in the 3 rd century DR .

History

In 241 DR , he was working as a servant of Ihimbraskar Evendusk . After a very interesting moment between Lord Evendusk and his wife, Naertho was given a bottle of tripleshroom sherry for him and the other servants.
