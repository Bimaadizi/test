Sherry Feldstone was a human druid and adventurer in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Growing up in Mistledale, Sherry was always fascinated by the brooding splendour of the forests, which led her to become a druid.

Possessions

She wore a pair of bracers of defense , a ring of protection , and a ring of animal friendship . Her ring of animal friendship allowed a greater number and power of beasts to be influenced by the item than most such rings, passively influencing animals within 10 feet (3 meters). Additionally, Sherry's ring of animal friendship granted the wearer ability to speak with animals under the ring's influence.

Activities

She had adventured near the ruins of Myth Drannor , and twice she had attempted to prevent gargoyles from moving into the area, both times unsuccessful.
