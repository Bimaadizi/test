Shadowroot sacs were small, faintly glowing, bulbous fungus growths found in the Shadow-cursed Lands surrounding Moonrise Towers in the Western Heartlands in the late 14 th and 15 th centuries DR .

Usages

Despite their overwhelmingly putrid stench, shadowroot sacs were harvested for their alchemical properties. The sacs and their secretions were refined into a vitriol that possessed a peculiar effect on those who smelled it. This vitriol had a smell of cloying sweetness but provoked bile rising in one's throat. The same smell seemingly sharpened the senses of those who smelled it.

Shadowroot vitriol could be combined with certain types of ashes to brew elixirs of viciousness .
