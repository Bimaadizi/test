Retorts were scientific items in the Realms often used by wizards to distill liquids.

Description

A retort consisted of a basin with a tube attached to the top that ran diagonally downward.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold retorts, ranging in price from 1 silver piece to 5 gold pieces depending on the size.
