Amberfly was a citrus liquor distilled on the southwestern shores of the Sea of Fallen Stars and sold throughout the Sword Coast in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The liquor had a slightly bitter taste due to being made using rinds of small citrus fruits that resembled oranges . Amberfly was typically bottled in vessels of opaque green glass .

History

Amberfly was popular among the wealthy of Suzail in Cormyr , and merchants and traders on the Sword Coast, all along the Trade Way , and as far north as the city of Luskan .
