The tavern's walking stick was an enchanted quarterstaff used at The Whistling Gallows inn of Lonelywood in Icewind Dale in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The staff's wielder had their charisma and physical strength boosted. Additionally, it allowed the wielder to cast bull's strength , cat's grace , eagle's splendor , endurance , fox's cunning , and owl's wisdom spells once per day each.

History

In the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , the tavern's walking stick could be found on the second floor of the Whistling Gallows in the town of Lonelywood, in one of its guest rooms.
