Uder Mordin was a duergar companion of Carlig and Finderlig , as well as a trader.

Description

Uder Mordin tried to avoid excitement and conversation, especially near the drow city of Ust Natha . He informed Gorion's Ward that a mage, Vithal , was imprisoned to the north of their trading site, and that he could be freed by using a spell, such as Freedom .
