Daggerford's Salvation were magic rings that commemorated the defeat of Gorgomorz , Daggerford 's Devil by Duke Edwin Daggerford circa 1270 DR .

Powers

Daggerford's Salvation gave the wearer increased defense via improving their parrying and deflection skills and improved their resistance to fire magic and effects as well as fear.

History

Daggerford's Salvation , like Edwin's elixir , was created in celebration of Duke Edwin's triumph over the terrible and murderous Gorgomorz circa 1270 DR. During the Gorgomorz crisis, a big part of Daggerford's militia were crippled by horror at the sigh of the terrible devil. In order for this to never happen again Daggerford's Salvation rings became standard issue for the city guard..

In 1372 DR , Daggerford's Salvation were available for purchase from the Moonglow Tower , the temple of Lathander in Daggerford.
