Finnfaran was a salamander who moonlighted at the Underdark Arena beneath Cormyr during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Finnfaran served as a gladiator in the magma-filled Underdark Arena of the red dragon Aptoryx , alongside Scintilla and the Brimstone Archon .
