Hemeth was a duergar arms dealer from Gracklstugh in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa 1486 DR , the dwarf heard that the village of Sloobludop had started a civil war, so he went there in order to sell his weapons to both factions. However, Hemeth was captured by cultists, led by Bloppblippodd , who wanted to sacrifice him to a new god named Leemooggoogoon , the Deep Father (who was, in truth, the demon lord Demogorgon ).
