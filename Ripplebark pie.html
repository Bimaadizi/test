Ripplebark pie was a variety of Duergar pie made using the flesh of unappetizing ripplebark fungi .

Availability

As of the late 15 th century DR , expensive 2 pounds (0.91 kilograms) servings of ripplebark pie were sold by Gorstaf , a rude duergar baker from the city of Gracklstugh in the Underdark in northwest Faerûn . Despite its exotic ingredients, ripplebark pies appeared to be quite ordinary and appetizing.
