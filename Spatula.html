Spatulas , referred to as osaye in Wa-an , were a type of tool found across Toril , including the ancient Calim Empire , the Vast , and on Wa .

Description

Spatulas were defined by their large, flat heads that allowed them to carry food and other substances.

Usage

Spatulas were commonly used as cookware across the Realms .

In Wa , a special metal-tipped osaye could be used to serve powdered incense during an incense ceremony.

Spatulas were also used as a tool for spreading non-food pastes and plasters , similar to trowels .

History

Sometime in the 14 th century DR, the cargo ship The Gentle Lady was delivering a shipment of farmer and merchant goods to a small coastal community in the Dragon Reach . Among the goods being carried by the ship was a crate full of cookware that included eighteen spatulas.
