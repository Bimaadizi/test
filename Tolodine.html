Tolodine was a Netherese arcanist who developed several wind-, fog-, and cloud-based spells .

Description

This proficient arcanist was an astute student of Sadebreth , the priest -arcanist of Mystryl . Unlike his mentor, Tolodine specialized in inventive spells.

History

Tolodine was born in 1444 NY (−2415 DR) . The inventor concentrated on developing spells from the winds , though the spells he developed were simpler to formulate than those of his tutor, Sadebreth. Tolodine developed six spells from 1473 NY (−2386 DR) to 1500 NY (−2359 DR) ; these would become known as gust of wind , wind wall , cloudkill , death fog , stinking cloud , and finally, the epic Tolodine's killing wind . He lived a short life compared to his mentor, and passed away from old age in 1501 NY (−2358 DR) .
