Lashivian was a necromancer working for the Cult of the Dragon as a Wearer of Purple during the late 14 th century DR .

History

During the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , he convinced Arveiaturace to become an agent for the Cult. However, by the next year, he still hadn't managed to convince her to become a dracolich .
