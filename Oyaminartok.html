Oyaminartok ( pronounced : / oʊ j ɔː m ɛ ˈ n ɑːr t ɒk / oh-yaw-meh-NAR-tock ) was a goliath werebear that was skilled at fishing.

Description

Oyaminartok's werebear form represented a humanoid polar bear , though her huge arms almost reached her feet. The werebear's hair was styled into thick braids and decorated with metal beads. She wore furs and a murky green garb that was fastened by a leather belt .

Personality

Though she had a fearsome look to some, Oyaminartok was rather kind, and willing to help adventurers.

Possessions

She carried a fishing rod , as well as a lot of fishing tackle and the fish she had caught (particularly knucklehead trout ).
