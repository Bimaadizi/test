Leader(s)Valsharess
Race(s)Drow

The Red Sisters were an elite band of assassins that the Valsharess formed to help her with her plans for taking over the surface, starting with Waterdeep in the early 1370s DR . The Red Sisters assassinated a lot of important people in Waterdeep who were seen as a threat to the Valsharess. In the end, Mephistopheles , the ruler of Cania , killed the last of the Red Sisters.
