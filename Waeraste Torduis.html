Waeraste Torduis was an under- priestess of Lolth from Sschindylryn during the 15 th century DR .

Description

Waeraste wore a stylish spider -themed corset and skirt, with her hair up in a ponytail.

Abilities

Waereste could cast chaos hammer , fear , cometfall , divine punishment , hold monster , poison , symbol of pain , and unholy blight .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Waeraste guarded the entrance to the Sschindylryn Ziggurat .
