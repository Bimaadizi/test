Braziers were simple devices that could be used to burn materials (such as incense ) , make a light source, or to house burners to provide a low but constant source of heat.

Description

6 ft (1.8 m) temple braziers, 4 ft (1.2 m) chapel braziers, and field braziers were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue . They sold gold , silver , and bronze variants of each. . The catalogue also sold special bronze braziers that came with matching burners and bronze braziers that stood atop tripod stands.
