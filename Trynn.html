Trynn was a halfling ranger and agent of the archdevil Zariel who posed as a paladin of Tyr , the God of Justice.

Possessions

She carried with her a bow and demonstrated great skill with the weapon.

Relationships

Rurik traveled alongside her fellow agents, human paladin and leader Anders , the wizard Cyrel , and the dwarf named Rurik .

History

Sometime in the year Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Trynn was tasked by Zariel to bring the tiefling Karlach Cliffgate back to the Nine Hells after she escaped the archdevil's service by boarding a Nautiloid .

Lead by Anders , Trynn and her fellow agents caught up with the tiefling somewhere along the Risen Road and managed to injure her, but could not kill her. The group stopped by a nearby tollhouse along the River Chionthar to corner their mark and were subsequently attacked by gnoll cultists loyal to the Absolute , though Trynn and her companions managed to slay them.
