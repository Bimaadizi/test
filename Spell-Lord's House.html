The Spell-Lord's House was a temple to Azuth in the city of Calimport .

Location

The Spell-Lord's House was located in Fikra Drudach of Hapij Sabban within Quill Ward .

Structure

The temple was a beautiful six-story structure. Its most notable feature was its eastern wall, which contained a massive, 50‑foot-diameter (15‑meter) rose window , with panes composed not of glass but of permanent illusory colors. These colors constantly swirled about.

Interior

The Spell-Lord's House contained one of the oldest monasteries to the High One in Faerûn and one of the best libraries in the city that was not privately owned.

History

The temple was first constructed at the end of the Sixth Age of Calimshan but had been rebuilt at least four additional times.
