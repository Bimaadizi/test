Simon the Illusionist was a human illusionist of the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He was a spell stealer, preying on other illusionists and stealing their spellbooks.

Possessions

He wore a pair of bracers of defense , and fought with and enchanted dagger +2 and a wand of magic missiles .

Relationships

He worked with Exeter in return for getting his hands on Henry the Shadow Masters spellbook, which Exeter had exaggerated the value of.

History

During the events of Exeter's vengeance upon Wemick and the Nellie Thursday Home for Experienced Adventurers retirement home, he would duel Henry and lose to the much older illusionist.
