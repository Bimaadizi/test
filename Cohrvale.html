Cohrvale was a mercenary thug and a partner of Bregg , from the River District of Athkatla in Amn in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Cohrvale was a human man with a light complexion.

Possessions

As a fighter , Cohrvale wore simple chainmail and a helmet . In battle, he wielded a battle axe and a medium shield .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Cohrvale and his dwarvish partner Bregg worked for someone called Mr. F. At one point, the thuggish duo came across Abdel Adrian and his traveling companions in front of the Copper Coronet in the slums of Athkatla. Cohrvale showed short fuse and demanded the adventurers to get out of his way. Bregg attempted to diffuse the situation, seeing how the adventurers were well-armed and Mr. F didn't like his men slaughtering people on the streets of Athkatla. Whether Abdel Adrian taunted Cohrvale and provoked him into battle, or avoided confrontation was unknown.
