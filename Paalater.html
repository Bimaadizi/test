Paalater was a noble family of Amn that held the Baron and Baroness titles as of the late 14 th century DR .

History

As of the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the Paalater family included at least three members: Baroness Paalater, her husband, and their daughter, young debutante Alicia Paalater . The young Paalater woman was enamored with Count Claylan to disdain of Lady Lasalla , who experienced Claylan's amorous ways of using naive youths.
