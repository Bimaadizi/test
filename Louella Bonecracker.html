Louella Bonecracker was a burly dwarven bouncer at the Bubbling Bulwark in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . She was responsible for guarding the inner private door which led into Thistleflame Keep , while Dwak guarded the main public door.

Relationships

Louella rather liked Annalise , one of the barmaids. She disliked the adventurer Garit Strongarm for his repeated rowdy behaviour and harassment of Annalise.
