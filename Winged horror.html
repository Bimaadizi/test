Winged horrors were a variety of flying ghouls that were encountered throughout the Shadow-cursed Lands in the Western Heartlands during the 15 th century DR .

Description

While they resembled ghouls in appearance and structure, winged horrors were smaller and weighed less than their land-bound cousins.

Behavior

These creatures were commanded by Absolutists to swoop down and seize important assets or kidnap individuals.

Abilities

They could fly at a rate of 600 ft (180 m) per minute.

Combat

Like other ghouls, winged horrors could paralyze foes with their bite. Certain races such as elves and other undead were immune to this effect.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the ex- Flaming Fist soldier Marcus led several winged horrors in his planned abduction of Isobel , for General Ketheric Thorm .

Notable Locales

A pair of winged horrors preferred to perch atop the battlements of Moonrise Towers .
