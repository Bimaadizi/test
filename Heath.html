Sergeant Heath was a soldier of the Neverwinter Guard stationed at Helm's Hold for a time during the late 15 th century DR . He served under Dunfield , a mercenary captain that led the operations during the occupation of Helm's Hold.

History

Sergeant Heath fought in the reclamation of Helm's Hold in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , following its occupation by devil -worshiping Ashmadai forces. He worked with fellow guardsmen to rescue Lieutenant Nessfettle and her squad, after being forced to barricade themselves in a building to stay safe from the fiends that roamed the city's streets.
