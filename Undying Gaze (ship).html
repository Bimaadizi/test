The Undying Gaze was an independant Cormyrean ship in the 14 th century DR .

Etymology

The Undying Gaze followed the standard naming customs of independant Cormyrean sailing ships, being named for a weapon belonging to one of Cormyr's rulers. In its case, the ship was named for Undying Gaze , the eye-studded war shield of King Draxius the Neverdying .

Activities

Like other independant Cormyrean ships, this vessel was used in the nation's ongoing efforts to combat pirates.

History

The Undying Gaze operated in the Sea of Fallen Stars around 1370 DR .
