Pharnilla was a news hawker who worked for the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Pharnilla was a pale-skinned human boy with dark brown eyes and long strawberry blonde hair. He wore a quality green tunic.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Pharnilla could often be found peddling the Baldur's Mouth Gazette across the street from Beehive Goods and Wares in the Lower City alongside Dellys , with whom Pharnilla took breaks. He didn't like taking breaks, thinking them boring and lamented not being able to read his own stock to pass the time.
