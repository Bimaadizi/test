Tales Told by the Warm Fireside was book penned by Korin of Neverwinter in the early 14 th century DR .

History

Tales Told by the Warm Fireside was an autobiographical book of musings and ruminations on lessons learned and philosophy of dealing with one's dark times. It was published in the Year of the Blazing Brand, 1334 DR .
