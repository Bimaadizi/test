The Silver Herd was a group of twelve unicorn stallions that often accompanied the appearance of an avatar of Lurue .

History

Lurue would always be at the head of the Silver Herd, leading them on spirited runs. The unicorns would stampede against any enemies threatening a follower of the Unicorn Queen in their presence.

The Silver Herd appeared before a group of adventurers , who would later become the Knights of the Unicorn , outside the elven castle of El'lahana Raikeil .
