Graffen Disteaf was a member of the Sembian High Council in Sembia in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Graffen was a stately but old individual.

Relationships

Graffen was a fervent supporter of fellow councilor Mirabeta Selkirk .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , after the death of Kendrick Selkirk , Highspeaker Dernim Lossit summoned the Council on Marpenoth 10 in order to elect the new Overmaster. The two main candidates were Countess Mirabeta Selkirk and Endren Corrinthal of Saerb . Graffen, alongside two other councilors, proposed the candidacy of Mirabeta. After the Council descended into chaos following the accusation against Endren of having killed Kendrick, many started to fight and Graffen received a hit on his head.
