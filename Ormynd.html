Ormynd was a noble family from the land of the Red Wizards of Thay before the 11 th century DR .

History

The House Ormynd was the first owner of the enchanted weapons later known as the battle axe of Mauletar . In the Year of the Tightening Fist, 1074 DR , people of Thay rose against oppressive Zulkirate regime. This became a short but bloody civil war. Members of the House Ormynd were fleeing the country when they were betrayed by close allies. The family friend and bodyguard, named Mauletar sacrificed his life, allowing the family to escape.
