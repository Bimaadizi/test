Sinveri Trollkin was a quarter-drow half-elf woman who served as the general of the armed forces of Ulgarth around 1367 DR . She hailed originally from the land of Dambrath , but had turned renegade.

Sinveri was a capable leader and intelligent. She was a devout follower of Tyr , god of justice and warfare.
