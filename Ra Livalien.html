Ra Livalien was an elven solicitor that lived in Silverymoon in the late mid-to-late 15 th century DR .

History

In 1379 DR , while visiting friends in Waterdeep kidnapped and enslaved by Lanador . During his disappearance a 500 gold piece reward was put out for his safe return.
