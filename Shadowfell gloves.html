Shadowfell gloves were a variety of magical gloves woven from Shadowfell thread.

Description

These were supple black gloves.

Powers

Once per day, the wielder of shadowfell gloves could cause a harmful arcane spell they cast to deal that harm through necrotic energy .

Reputation

Shadowfell gloves were highly prized by warlocks and wizards alike.
