Gerrell was one of the Blades of the Old Man in the fortress of Sentinelspire . He was a part of the team accompanying Sauk to look for Kheil and secure the Erael'len in the Northern Shalhoond in Tarsakh of 1374 DR , conspiring to kill the Old Man of the Mountain . He was a westerner, with round eyes and pale skin; he helped Sauk ambush Berun . He was slain by Berun on Tarsakh 16 with a sword through the throat.
