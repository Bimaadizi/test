Honeyed buns were a type of local bread dish from the city of Arrabar in the Vilhon Reach .

Description

Honeyed buns were small-in-size handfoods covered in sweet honey , making them a cheap treat and popular morningfeast among the common folk of Arrabar.

History

Circa the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , blind aging commoner Nimra Skolotti and her young daughter Mirolyn Skolotti liked to start their day with a single fresh honeyed bun from a local bakery in the Living District they loved in. A simple pleasure, the women split a bun as they took care of daily chores.
