Delzjhava was a Netherese archwizard .

Description

Delzjhava inhabited the frontier of Netheril .

History

In 2693 NY (−1166 DR) , another arcanist , Decampus , wandered Netheril. Upon reaching the frontier, the two engaged in a spellbattle. Delzjhava won the battle and Decampus was slain.
