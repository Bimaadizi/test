Sirhan al-Din was a janni and member of the Sword Dancers in 1367 DR .

Description

Sirhan stood over 6 ft (1.8 m) tall. He had dark eyes, chestnut hair, and dusky skin.

History

Sirhan was a resident of Sarahin from the time the jann first built that great fortress. He was completely loyal to the Sword Dancers.

Activities

Sirhan served as master of the hounds. He managed to capture and train two cheetahs who followed him wherever he went.
