Heartsong Harps was an instrument shop specializing in harps in the city of Silverymoon in the Silver Marches in the late 14 th century DR . It stood on Goblinwood Rise in the city's Northbank . It was owned by Arilyn Dathlue and used strings made by Chesmyr Morrowynd circa 1372 DR .
