Dundar was a blacksmith and inhabitant of Tilverton in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Dundar owned Dundar's Fine Swords where he in fact, sold fine swords . By the late 1360's he had expanded his craft to include saws, axes, scythes, and other bladed implements, and renamed his shop to Dundar's Fine Blades . He also provided training for novice fighters in weaponplay.

Relationships

Dundar's sibling, a Harper named Andalara , helped him in the shop by finishing blades and constructing scabbards when she wasn't hunting.
