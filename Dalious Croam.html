King Dalious Croam , who was also called the Errant King , was the third and final King of the Croam Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Dalious ruled from the Year of the Halfling's Dale, 135 DR , to the Year of the King's Destiny, 137 DR , when he abdicated the throne in favor of the first of many Croamarkhs who would rule Westgate for over a century.

Relationships

Dalious was the third son of King Sorentah Croam .
