This building was a row house in the Hook Ward of Calimport in the 14 th century DR .

Location

This rowhouse was located in the Piqaz Drudach of the Osiir Sabban of the Hook Ward.

Structure

This building stood five stories and featured a cellar.

Services

The first floor of this building was occupied by the shop of a ropemaker . Starting in 1370 DR , its small cellar apartment housed a shrine dedicated to Ilmater and the Ilmatari saint Asref .

Inhabitants

The cellar of this rowhouse was occupied by the Ilmatari priest Brother Dolmas , who tended to his personal shrine and operated a mission out of the small apartment.
