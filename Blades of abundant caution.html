The blades of abundant caution were a pair of unique enchanted short swords crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Naomlen Girdaur in the late 15 th century DR . They were just two of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

Silverhand conceived of the blades as honoring Naomlen's gift of patience in combat.

Powers

Those struck with the blades of abundant caution were compelled to take time to consider the fight and their fate, thus forcing them to slow down and become vulnerable.
