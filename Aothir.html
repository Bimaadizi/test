Aothir was a dwarf who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

Description

Aothir was a portly, red-bearded dwarf.

Activities

Aothir was an armorer who owned a stand in the market square of the Protector's Enclave . He sold armors made by his friends from Sundabar , his hometown.

History

Aothir proclaimed himself to be an heir of Delzoun , and in his younger years he traveled the North in search of fabled Gauntlgrym . However, at some point he retired from his quest and opened an armory in Neverwinter.

Aothir survived the attack of the " Lost Heir " of Alagondar and his plaguechanged minions of 1479 DR not only once, but twice.
