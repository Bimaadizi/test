Shorah Hevrun was a human girl in Elturel in the late 15 th century DR .

History

When Elturel was transported to Avernus , first layer of the Nine Hells , in the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , the Hevrun family were killed in the transition, leaving Shorah the only survivor. Four ghasts entered the house to devour the dead while Shorah hid in a cupboard.
