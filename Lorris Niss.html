Lorris Niss was a half-elf acolyte at Candlekeep on the Sword Coast in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Lorris was middle aged.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Loris was reading The Price of Beauty with her friend, a fellow acolyte named Falthrax Loderr , when he went missing. Lorris grew worried when she hadn't seen him in two days. Encountering some curious adventurers , she explained what had happened and enlisted their help.
