Enchanted papyrus blades were magic katanas of impeccable crafting skill and devastating dweomers weaved into, wielded only by the most accomplished individuals.

Description

When a person held these exotic swords , they immediately noticed their artful design, graceful curve, and deadly sharp edge. Enchanted papyrus blades were a pinnacle of smithcraft.

Powers

Each of the enchanted papyrus blades held a collection of powerful enchantments . When the blade was wielded in battle, it magically increased the defenses of the wielder, gracefully guiding their hand in defecting attacks. This property was equal to a +3 enchantment to the welder's defense. The wielder also affectively became a master of katana blades, gaining proficiency in battle dodging, battle movement speed, greater focus while wielding the blade, the ability to cleave their enemies with monstrous force, and improved chances of the wielder landing especially lucky slashes on their opponents.

Immediately offensive magics weaved into these katanas was a +5 enchantment to punishment dispensed. Each hit generated magical heat that scorched the opponent, and its keen edge could land especially devastating slashes.

On top of that, enchanted papyrus blades were imprinted with several spells that the wielder could unleash. Haste could be activated once per day, fear could be cast twice per day, and lastly, Balagarn's iron horn could be unleashed once a day.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , a battle mage named Maevhaun wielded deadly spells and an enchanted papyrus blade as he competed in Westgate 's Quivering Thumb Arena .
