Michitl was one of the three leaders of the Azatl settlement in Nexal in 1362 DR .

Activities

Michitl ruled over a territory of Azatl. He reinforced its walls in Amnite fashion, making his palace a true fortress.

Personality

Michitl knew he was the weakest of the three leaders of Azatl and tried to find any means of increasing his status.
