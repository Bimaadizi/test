Spirecoast was an ancient Netherese outpost built in the Sword Mountains during its early period of expansion.

Geography

The outpost was located within the foothills of the southern Sword Mountains, west of what would become the Westwood .

History

Spirecoast came about during Netheril 's Silver Age ( −2758 DR to −2208 DR ), when the Terraseer directed Netheril to explore its western frontier. Reported among the first promising sites in 1491 NY (−2368 DR) , alongside Old Owl Well and Kryptgarden Falls , Spirecoast served as a caravan stop, watering station, and fortified watchpost amid Netheril's westward expansion. Secured with warriors and overseen by arcanists , it was equipped with crystal balls that were used to spy on the surrounding area, especially the nearby elves of Illefarn . The outpost was also fitted with a mythallar that powered quasimagical items , but the need for expeditions to venture further and further afield caused the creation of some of Netheril's first proper magical items that could be used anywhere.
