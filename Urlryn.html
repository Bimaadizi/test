Urlryn was a drow of Menzoberranzan in the late 14 th century DR and one of Houndaer Tuin'Tarl and Syrzan 's rebel group.

History

Urlryn once trained at Melee-Magthere under Ryld Argith and was proficient enough that Ryld remembered his name many years later.

During the Silence of Lolth in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Urlryn joined Houndaer's group desiring to start a slave revolt. The plot was discovered by Ryld Argith and Pharaun Mizzrym . When they escaped from the conspirators' building, Urlryn and his fellows hunted them, but Urlryn was killed by his former master.
