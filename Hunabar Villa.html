Hunabar Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Hunabar .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's North Ward at the intersection of Hassantyr's Street and Vhezoar Street . The western side of the villa compound also lay along Ilzantil Street .

Description

The walled compound contained two buildings, both of which were located on the west side of the villa with the east side being composed of open gardens. Both buildings stood two storys tall.
