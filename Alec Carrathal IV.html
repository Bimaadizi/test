Alec IV of the Carrathal Dynasty was High King of Callidyrr at the turn of the 14 th century DR . Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles , he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal royal family of Callidyrr and was the first son of his predecessor, King Benjamin . His eventual successor was his third son, Reginald , who would go on to be the last Carrathal to sit the throne.

History

Alex IV assumed the throne in the Year of the Black Hound, 1296 DR , after the assassination of his father. He ruled for thirty-six years before dying of natural causes in the Year of the Sword and Stars, 1332 DR .

Two years prior to the end of his reign, the Great Druid of the Moonshaes , Brianna Moonsinger , was killed while trying to stop the return of Kazgaroth .
