Potions of restoration were invaluable magic potions that restored lost life energy, popular among adventurers dealing with powerful undead and clerics of Lathander .

Description

Potions of restoration were usually dispensed in small crystal phials. The liquid itself appeared murky when inside the phial but began to gently glow when brought close to a living creature.

Powers

Potions of restoration came in three power levels, regular, lesser restoration , and greater restoration . When consumed, these potions restored the imbiber's life force and ability drained by the creatures of the negative energy .
