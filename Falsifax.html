Falsifax was an odd fire reaver that prowled the High Road of Cormyr during the late 15 th century DR .

History

The demon was seen on the High Road west of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar . It had attacked a traveling merchant and stolen the key to his locked chest, then attempted to flee with the key in its mouth.
