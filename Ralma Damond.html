Ralma Damond was a former member of the Company of Eight in Tethyr in 1358 DR .

History

Ralma was one of the original members of the Company in 1344 DR . In time, she fell in love with her companion Nestrun Farclutch and in 1352 DR they abandoned the company to settle in Pailstone to form a family. They took care of a sanctuary for the company in the northeastern arm of the Forest of Tethir .
