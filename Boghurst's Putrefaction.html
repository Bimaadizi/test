Boghurst's Putrefaction was an enchanted dagger found within the Dwarven Dungeons of Myth Drannor .

Description

Boghurst's Putrefaction was a slender dagger with a longer than average blade.

Powers

Boghurst's Putrefaction was a regular dagger that possessed the power to bestow curse on its targets. The dagger had seven charges of the curse. Another notable quality of the dagger was that it attracted undead creatures to its foul magics.

History

In 1369 DR , Boghurst's Putrefaction could be found in the deeper level of the Dwarven Dungeons, the Halls of Stone . The dagger's container attracted a small horde of restless undead over time. Dungeon delving adventurers needed to defeat a skeleton lord and its army of undead in order to retrieve the accursed item.
