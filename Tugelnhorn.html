Tugelnhorn a laborer who worked the docks of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Tugelnhorn was a gold dwarven woman with a bronze skin tone, brown eyes, and brown hair. She kept her face clean-shaven.
