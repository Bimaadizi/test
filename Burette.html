Burettes were glass devices used in laboratories to measure liquids.

Description

Made by strictly supervised glassblowers from the guild in Urmlaspyr , a burette was individually graduated by an alchemist to make certain it would properly measure fluids.

Prices

This item cost 15 gold pieces through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue and included a bronze stand and cork stop-cock.
