Boray Gulditch was an elven kineticist who hunted goblins in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

A powerful kineticist, and proficient telepath and egoist she dedicated her life to cleanse the Goblin Marches of goblins.

Possessions

With a mace in hand, and a pair of bracers of defense on her arms she went about her grizzly task.
