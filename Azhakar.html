Azhakar was a talking bird who lived in Zakhara , the Land of Fate, in 1367 DR .

History

Azhakar was found living with a singing tree during the time of Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir 's great-grandfather. As of 1367 DR, he lived in the gardens of the Palace of the Enlightened Throne with the same singing tree.

Activities

Azhakar would frequently provide answers to important questions posed by the Grand Caliph . Others might be lucky enough to get an answer as well if Azhakar believed them worthy.
