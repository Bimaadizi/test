Star spawn hulks were a type of star spawn , creatures created by Elder Evils to act as their agents on the Prime Material plane .

Description

Star spawn hulks were shaped similarly to ogres , with a transparent skin revealing layers of muscles. They had pale, lidless eyes, too many teeth, and too small a nose.

Behavior

Star spawn hulks were rarely seen without a star spawn seer nearby to command them.

Abilities

Star spawn hulks possessed the ability to redirect the effects of any psychic attacks made against them to everyone within a 10 feet (3 meters) distance.
