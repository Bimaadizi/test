Shimmer was a magical cloak found in the ruins of Tethyamar during the 1480s DR .

Powers

The cloak possessed powerful attractive energies, aiding the wearer's enchantment magic and making them generally a much more popular person. Something about it worked even in perfect darkness, as it drew interest like a siren 's song.

History

The cloak was found among the drow who invaded Tethyamar in search of the jewels of ardor .
