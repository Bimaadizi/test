NicknamesDoohickey

A Fist of Gond , otherwise referred to by layfolk outside of the Church of Gond as a doohickey , was a complex contraption developed by Gondsmen in the late 15 th century DR . They were designed to aid Gondsmen in combat, acting as momentary combatants.

Creation

Constructing one of these devices required, in part: an axle , cogs , grommets , springs , sprockets , and some form of paper for its three radiating blades.

History

These devices developed out from attempts by Gondsmen to innovate caltrops .
