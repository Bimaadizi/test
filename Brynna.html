Brynna was a young woman from the Western Heartlands who joined the Cult of the Absolute during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Brynna and her companion Andrick were followers of the True Soul Edowin . They were wholly devoted to the man who they viewed as some form of chosen .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Brynna, Andrick and Edowin were tasked with fighting a group of "fugitives" of the Absolute, individuals that were imprisoned aboard a mind flayer Nautiloid that crashed near the River Chionthar . Unbeknownst to Brynna and Andrick, their true goal was to retrieve a mysterious artifact for the Absolute.

Unfortunately for the trio, Edowin was mortally wounded by an owlbear and left to die in the forest.
