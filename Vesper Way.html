Vesper Way was a road in the Uptown District of the city of Ravens Bluff .

Location

Vesper Way stretched from Blacktree Boulevard in Torchtown to Mac Intyre Path in Sixstar . Some referred to this uppermost portion of the road as Vesper Way North . Between these two roads it was intersected by De Villars Ride , Manycoins Way , Moorland Ride , and a number of nameless alleys.
