The Right Pashas was one of the crews―or local street gangs―that operated in Baldur's Gate during the 1490s DR .

Base of Operations

The Right Pashas held territory in Little Calimshan , a neighborhood in the Baldurian Outer City .

Activities

The crew worked in direct opposition to the the Guild of Baldur's Gate, and the Kingpin of Little Calimshan, Rilsa Rael . The Right Pashas viewed the criminal organization as outsiders to their neighborhood. They sought to rout her agents from the neighborhood in an ongoing gang war that broke out in the district each night.

Not all of the crew's activities were violent however. Some more wholesome members chose to spend their time selling brownies , to raise money to brighten the lives of Little Calimshan's children.
