Grundlgornn was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Grundlgornn was a tan-skinned beardless dwarf with short and dark curly hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Grundlgornn found himself listening to a curbside preacher of Talona , Umbrenne , spewing out his beliefs of the cycle of life and death being carried out through pestilence. Despite not being interested in the Talonite faith, Grundlgornn was terrified of the priest's promises of plagues .
