Eel Street was a small road located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The road was found in southern Waterdeep within the Dock Ward . Eel Street ran north to south in between Lackpurse Lane in the north and Belnimbra's Street to the south.

Notable Locations

A tavern called the Grey Griffon was located on the west side of Eel Street where it connected with Lackpurse Lane.
