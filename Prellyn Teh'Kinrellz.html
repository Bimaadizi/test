Prellyn Teh'Kinrellz was a drow member of House Teh'Kinrellz in Ched Nasad in the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Prellyn was the main enforcer of the Matron Teh'Kinrellz in the ruins of Ched Nasad. She acted as an overseer to Q'arlynd Melarn , also using him for sex.

History

In the Year of Risen Elfkin, 1375 DR , Q'arlyn subtly learned from Prellyn information about a medallion of Eilistraee he had found while scavenging in the rubble.
