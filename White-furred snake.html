White-furred snakes , also known as snow serpents , were a species of constrictor snake native to cold regions.

Description

As their name suggested, these snakes were covered in white fur, highly adapted to the sub-arctic climate. Their hairs were barbed so as to gain better traction on snow and ice. They grew to be up to 100 feet (30 meters) long, and were warm-blooded.

Behavior

White-furred snakes would lie in wait for prey along snowy mountain paths. They attacked with a powerful bite, but the danger lay in their ability to constrict and crush their prey.

Ecology

White-furred snakes hunted in arctic hills, mountains, and plains, and could also be found in rough terrain and ruins.

Homelands

They were endemic to the Bloodstone Lands of Damara and Vaasa .

White-furred snakes could also be found on the world of Nehwon , and huge versions were rumored to have been seen in Pandemonium .

Uses

Their fur could be used for a wide range of unusual functions, and fetched a hefty price quite unlike the skins of the ordinary giant constrictor snakes.
