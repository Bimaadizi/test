A Far Realm battery was a device that could store energy―including raw magic ―that had been extracted from the Far Realm .

Description

It resembled an amethyst crystal, set within an elongated cylinder crafted from glass and metal . They measured about 2 ft (0.61 m) long and 1 ft (0.3 m) in diameter.

When activated, it would emit a 3 ft (0.91 m)-long beam of light.

Powers

The Far Realm batteries could be used to power permanent portals , and other devices that utilized one or more forms of energy, including powerful explosives.

History

Some time during or after the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Red Wizards of Thay established a hizagkuur -reinforced Far Realm battery factory in the ruins of Xorvintroth , beneath the snowy wastes of Icewind Dale .
