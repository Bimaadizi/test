The Sign of the Melancholy Dwarf was an expensive inn in the North around the 14 th century DR .

Services

This inn served a variety of food. Including potatos , yams , various sweetmeats, and wines .

Interior

In the back of the Melancholy Dwarf were privacy booths with curtains.

History

At some point a respected dwarven sage , Birgit of the Iron House , invited a group of adventurers to meet her at this inn in order to discuss hiring them for a quest.
