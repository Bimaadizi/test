The Temple of Lolth was the second largest temple in the metropolis of Cathyr in Dambrath .

Description

The temple sat on one side of the queen's palace. It had flying buttresses shaped to resemble spider's legs, and its main entrance was carved to resemble the spider's head and mouth.

Inhabitants

The Temple of Lolth in Cathyr was led by High Priestess Chaladra Sse'blis .
