Magusta was a powerful female malaugrym that was involved in the hunt for Shandril Shessair in 1357 DR . At the end, she managed to get close to the girl posing as her dwarf friend Delg Hammerhand but was killed by Torm of the Knights of Myth Drannor , whom Elminster had sent to find and stop the malaugrym.
