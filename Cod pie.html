Cod pie was a dish of cod fish baked in a pie known in Faerûn in the mid–14 th century DR .

History

Jasper ordered a cod pie along with a Golden Sands Orange at the Bold Bard in Ghars , Cormyr , in Eleint of 1367 DR . He regretted the cod pie the next morning.
