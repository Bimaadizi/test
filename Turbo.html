Turbo shells , also known as green snails , were valuable seashells that were used for adornment.

Usages

Giant rare specimens that had been polished to a pearly sheen were prized for their own sake, while smaller common ones were cut and polished for their mother of pearl . Those that had a certain "fire luster" provided nacre instead.
