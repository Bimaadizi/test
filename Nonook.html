Nonook , known as the whale killer, was a man from the Cold Desert in the Whamite Isles . Nonook represented the Cold Desert in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
