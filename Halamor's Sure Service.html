Halamor's Sure Service was a business in Kholtar , south Faerûn , circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Location

Halamor's was located on the south side of Hael Way between Thalarmol's crate-making service and the luthdren (restaurant) and gambling hall known as Sesszemur's , not far from Farrgaunlar (the southern gate out of Kholtar). Across the street was the klathlaaedin (fancy mansion) of the Horthander family named Anthormbrur .

Services

Halamor's Sure Service provided halfling runners to carry messages, supplies, or packages to and from locations within the city. They also provided pick-up service with delivery to Thalarmol's next door for crating.
