Chalara was a news hawker who worked for the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Chalara was a very pale-skinned human child with mousy-blonde hair and brown eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Chalara peddled Baldur's Mouth Gazette in front of the Blushing Mermaid tavern in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.
