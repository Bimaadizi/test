Thartryn Ilistar II was the seventh King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Thartryn became King of Westgate when he slew his cousin King Temprith in the Year of the Missing Blade, 759 DR . Thartryn then killed every descendant of Temprith's bloodline within their family, perhaps because Temprith stole the crown from Thartryn's father and banished him from Westgate. He ruled until his death in the Year of the Lizard King, 783 DR .

Relationships

The simpleton Farnath III was the father of Thartryn II and his twin brother Farnath IV . Farnath IV was suspected of murdering his brother for the throne.
