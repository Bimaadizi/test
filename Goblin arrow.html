Goblin arrows , despite their name, were not missiles used by goblins , but a type of enchanted arrow created by a black-hearted gnome Turbar Talinbar .

Powers

Even though these arrows possessed +1 enchantment, they were now known for their damaging power. The magics imbued into goblin arrows were activated once a missile hit a target. It summoned forth an incredibly agitated and hostile goblin. The monstrous humanoid remained there for the next seventeen minutes before being sent back from wherever the creature was pulled from.

History

Goblin arrows were invented sometime before the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , by an evil gnome Turbar Talinbar. Since then, these items have become known for being an invaluable method of distraction or annoyance. Most commonly, goblin arrows could be found in the hands of mischievous halflings and elves . However, several adventuring troupes have used a number of these arrows before entering challenging battles.
