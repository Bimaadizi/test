Povros Karanok was a member of House Karanok in 1372 DR .

History

In 1372 DR, Povros was the leader of one of the new Karanok holdings built outside of Luthcheq after, in 1370 DR , Entropy (secretly manipulated by Tiamat ) spawned some smaller spheres.

Personality

Raised to hate wizards, Povros used only magic items created by clerics .

Relationship

Povros was a cousin of Jaerios Karanok .
