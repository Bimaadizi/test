Malanthor Drymm was a male wizard who was one of the magelords of Athalantar and was killed in Elminster Aumar 's insurrection.

History

He was an arrogant and very self-confident magelord. He was one of the magelords forcibly summoned to the spell-battle in 240 DR in Ithboltar 's tower in Athalgard and died there.
