Fetishists were Azuposi rogues who also used a special form of animal magic called fetish magic that granted them access to specific types of priest spells .

Activities

Fetishists dealt with spirits on a regular basis. They helped the hunters of their village and protected their community from dangerous roaming wild animals.

Culture

A fetishist was a well-respected member of their Azuposi community, though they lacked the status of true priests.

Abilities

They were usually quite skilled with training, handling, and mimicking animals.

Possessions

They wielded clubs , slings , daggers , or short bows .
