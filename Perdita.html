Perdita ( pronounced : /ˈ p ɜːr d ɪ t ɑː / PUR-di-ta ) was a ghost who haunted a secret crypt below the City of the Dead during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Perdita was the ghost of a young human girl who lost her life while exploring a crypt with her older sister. Perdita looked as if she was wearing a simple tunic, breeches and a moon-pendant. She wore two braids in her black hair.

Personality

Perdita was terrified of rats . Deceased while about 8 years old, her soul was unable to pass on due to concerns over her sister's well-being.
