Gereth was an aged gray-haired magician from the City of Coin, Athkatla in Amn in the 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Gereth was a regular shopper at the Adventurer Mart in Athkatla's grand Waukeen's Promenade . He searched for salamander dust while being wary of the notorious Cowled Wizards . If anyone asked, Gereth claimed to need the substance of soup.
