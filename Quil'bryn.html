Quil'bryn was a mad drow in Szith Morcane in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa 1486 DR , Quil'bryn was a member of the Drow Resistance against the invading fire giants . However, he was infected by a kind of hedonistic madness and murdered the archmage Solom Ned'razak .

Afterward, Quil'bryn, despite the objections of Murssvayas Dhuurniv , tried to convince all drow to abandon Szith Morcane.

Personality

In his madness, Quil'bryn wanted to live again in a great drow city, with many slaves at his disposal, focusing only on enjoying life.
