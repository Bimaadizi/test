Cefrey was a spellcaster and looter who was active on the Sword Coast region during the late 15 th century DR . For a time, she worked under the employ of Gimblebock .

Description

She had long dark hair, wore a simple traveling robe , and carried a quarterstaff .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Cefrey and her fellow looters had been trying to break into a crypt for a few days. They had made some progress, and had encountered skeletons . Not wanting to go home empty-handed yet again, she was determined to get further in.
