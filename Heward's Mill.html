Heward's Mill was a gristmill found in Archenbridge in late 14 th century DR . It was powered by the strong currents of the River Arkhen that ran through the city, and under its eponymous bridge.

Owner

The mill was owned and operated by Salath Heward , a greedy and unpleasant man .
