Maxil was a member of the Adventurer's Guild of the Crossroad Keep and one of Daerred 's adventuring companions in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Maxil and his companions claimed to had trained in the art of adventuring under the illustrious Knight-Captain, also known as the Kalach-Cha . When the troupe leader, bard Daerred, was granted permission to open his guild in the Neverwintran -controlled fortress of the Crossroad Keep, the dwarf joined as one of the guild's trainers. Together, they dreamed of opening the greatest adventuring school on the Sword Coast , passing along all things they learned from the Knight-Captain to the next generation of heroes. Circa the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Maxil could be found in the Adventurer's Guildhall.
