Durilghar Szind was a drow necromancer of House Szind from Sschindylryn in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Durilghar had tied-back white hair and wore an elaborate black robe and skull mask .

Abilities

Durilghar was a powerful necromancer and, in addition to animating and controlling the undead , could cast chain lightning , enervation , finger of death , horrid wilting , necrotic ray , Niac's biting cold , and symbol of weakness .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar during the mid-1480s DR, Durilghar raised the dead in the King's Forest so that they could be used as fighters against the defenders of Cormyr . He performed much work at the razed Deep Glen village, where many fresh human corpses could be found.
