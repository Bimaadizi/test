Barmar Trull was a human magistrate of Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

Barmar had dark skin and short black hair. He wore a typical magistrate's robe.

History

Around the time of the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon , Barmar and Farrain Graymoor oversaw the processing of prisoners at the docks of Eveningstar as they were loaded onto the Wheloon Prison boat .
