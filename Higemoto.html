Higemoto was a ronin bandit in Kozakura in 1357 DR .

Activities

Higemoto was a masterless samurai who gathered 100 followers within Sogai Province , preying upon travelers from the Monodati Forest aided by the wu jen Katohara and the monk Nogori .
