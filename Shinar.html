Shinar was a cleric and elder among the centaurs of Guard in the Barony of Bloodstone in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Shinar had a warm and thoughtful personality.

Powers

Shinar was a priestess and healer, and could cast low to mid-level healing spells .

Relationships

She had a son, Vikos . She served on Guard's Council of Elders alongside Chief Kiros and War Chief Valon .

History

In 1358 DR , Shinar and her fellow members of the Council of Elders met Gareth Dragonsbane 's party.
