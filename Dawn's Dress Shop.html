Dawn's Dress Shop was a fine gown shop in the city of Westgate in 1367 DR .

Location

Dawn's Dress Shop was located on East Market Street in the south section of the city.

Activities

The shop was owned and operated by Dawn , an elven seamstress. Many of Westgate's most fashionable ladies were clients of Dawn's Dress Shop.
