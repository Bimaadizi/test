Alazub was a hamatula agent of Zariel in Avernus in the Nine Hells .

History

In 1494 DR , Zariel ordered Alazub to spy upon a group of demons led by a nalfeshnee called Trantolox that was searching for the Bleeding Citadel . However, Trantolox discovered him and ordered three chasme to torture Alazub.
