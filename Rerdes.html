Captain Rerdes was the guard captain of Lady Galvena's Festhall .

Description

Rerdes oversaw the other guards, who would patrol the lower floor of the festhall. He felt as if he was not appreciated by Lady Galvena , although he was the only guard allowed to enter her bedroom.

Rumors

He was rumored to be partial to a pint of ale whilst on shift.
