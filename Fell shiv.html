The fell shiv was a dagger found in Wheloon Prison in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The dagger had a notably wide blade for its length.

Powers

The fell shiv was especially adept at finding weak points and performing instant kills.

History

The blade was found by Cormyrean forces in the merchant's townhouse where the shadar-kai Karleth stayed during the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon .
