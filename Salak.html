Salak was a lizardfolk fellow who lived at the Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Snake had green scales with a yellowish underbelly.

History

Salak "helped" the magistrates of the prison keep the secrets of the local underground tunnels in exchange for being handsomely paid, and could be seen at the Ball and Chain Tavern meeting them for business.
