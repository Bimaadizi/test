Description

Tranjer had thick light brown hair and a beard. He wore plate mail armor that had a blue tint, and a black and green cloak. Rolsk also owned a longsword with a shining blue crossguard.

History

In 1358 DR , Rolsk wished to capture the Heroes of Ascore and turn them over to Luskan for a huge reward. He ordered his guards to search for the heroes and tried to persuade them to redirect the group to his headquarters. However, the Heroes of Ascore managed to escape Rolsk's grasp. Even if Rolsk had captured the group, the ice hunters had plans to break them out of jail.
