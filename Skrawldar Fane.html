Skrawldar Fane was a shipwright that lived in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR . They were a regular patron of the Elfsong Tavern , located in the city's Eastway district.

For some reason or another, Skrawldar had become punch drunk as of the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR . They had a noteworthy appearance, in that they had no eyebrows whatsoever.
