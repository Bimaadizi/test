Harton Tapscott was a human agent in the mercenary business during the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Harton was white-haired man with neat muttonchops, moustache, and beard. He wore a well-used suit of plate mail and carried a large greataxe .

History

Around the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Harton joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their base camp near the ruins of Myth Drannor . He organized mercenary hirelings, selling their contracts to those that went into the ruins and desired more sword-arms.
