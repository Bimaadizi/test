Shurdriira Helviiryn was the head of the College of Alteration and one of the members of the Conclave of Sshamath in the city of Sshamath in the year 1372 DR .

History

In 1379 DR , Shurdriira agreed with the proposal of Master Guldor Zauviir to elevate T'lar Mizz'rynturl , Master of Bae'qeshel Magic, to be a new member of the Conclave. After Q'arlynd Melarn 's revelation that only Lolth 's worshipers could practice Bae'qeshel, she withdrew her support of Master Guldor's proposal.
