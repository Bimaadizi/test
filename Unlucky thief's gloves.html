Unlucky thief's gloves was a pair of enchanted gloves of dubious magics.

Powers

The gloves possessed a helpful enchantment that significantly boosted the wearer's manual dexterity and sleight of hand. However, if the gloves' wearer used this magic to steal something, a small piece of coal appeared in the thief's pocket, as if the magic was judging them.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a pair of unlucky thief's gloves was displayed among splendorous enchanted rewards for those willing to partake in Akabi 's Wheel of Fortune game in the Circus of the Last Days during its stay in the Rivington neighborhood of Baldur's Gate .
