Market Street was a short street located in the Eastway district of Baldur's Gate , on the border with the Brampton neighborhood.

Location

Market Street cut through a city block, east to west. The city block was sandwitched between Nuthkhal's Way to the west and Stormshore Street to the east.

Hisotry

In the Year of the Purloined Statue, 1477 DR , a suspicious half-orc butcher named Magnus Shank was said to had been selling monster meat to the customers. This accusation came at the time of a slew of murders in the Gate, and the butchery was thought to be involved and hide a more sinister secret than monster meat. The half-orc's supplier was a tiefling Selwyn Leventide .
