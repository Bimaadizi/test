Ruathym ( pronounced : /ˈ r uː ə θ ɪ m / ROO-uh-thim ) was the largest settlement on the island of Ruathym in the Trackless Sea .

Geography

Ruathym was located to the south of Holgerstead , another settlement on the island, and close to the magical ruins of Inthar .

Defenses

The people of Ruathym were savage. The city's forces boasted not only berserkers , but shamans and witches .

History

In the Year of Maidens, 1361 DR , the Elfmaid brought Liriel Baenre and her companion Fyodor to the island, where they hoped to harness the rune magic of Yggdrasil's Child . They stayed in Ruathym for some time, investigating suspicious disappearances and deaths of local fishermen and others. Liriel defeated the drow banshee at Inthar and discovered that Dagmar , the daughter of Ulf the Shaman, was betraying her people to the Kraken Society . Liriel eventually discovered and defeated a conspiracy by Luskan and the Kraken Society.

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the leader of Ruathym, Dagh Lahrskald , had sworn fealty to the Storm Maiden after witnessing her defeat three of his best berserkers single-handedly. She tasked him with bringing the other settlements on the island to heel so that she could lead all the people of Ruathym to raid and pillage other lands.
