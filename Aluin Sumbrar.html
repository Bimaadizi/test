Aluin Sumbrar was the Hamarch , or elected mayor, of Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR .

Personality

Aluin was worn out man with a sneering sense of humor.

Description

Aluin was a short and portly fellow.

Activities

The Hamarch held little actual power behind the walls of Urmlaspyr as the prosperous merchants of the city did as they pleased.
