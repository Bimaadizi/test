Bethany Carrathal was High Queen of Callidyrr in the early-to-mid 12 th century DR . Like her predecessors, although he held the title of High Queen of the Moonshae Isles , she was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

She was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty , and was the first daughter of her predecessor, King Edwin I . Her first son and eventual successor was Edwin II .

History

Bethany assumed the throne in the Year of the Luminar Procession, 1127 DR , following the death of her father. Her reign lasted only six years before she died of illness in the Year of the Persuasive Voice, 1133 DR .
