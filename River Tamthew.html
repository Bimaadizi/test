The River Tamthew , also called the Lluirflow , was a small river found in southern Luiren in the Shining South .

Geography

The river originated near the center of the southern section of the Southern Lluirwood . It flowed almost due south for approximately 100 miles (160 kilometers) before reaching the Great Sea near Luiren's far southwest corner.
