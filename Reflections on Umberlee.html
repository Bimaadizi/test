The Reflections on Umberlee was a painting by Vin Valle of Baldur's Gate , created in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Lady Jannath hosted the art show in her Lower City estate, titled "Strange Tides: Self-Portraits from the Sword Coast." Reflections on Umberlee was one of the pieces on display with Vin Valle invited to the event. Lady Jannath asked her butler, Tarhun Mnemonis , to remind the artist of their picture being displayed upon arrival.
