Goris Ballast was a guest of Nortale's Hostel in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Goris was a dark-skinned and clean-shaven gold dwarf man with navy-blue eyes, bulbous nose, and curly black hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , and the city of Baldur's Gate being flooded with refugees, Goris Ballast was among the guests of Nortale's Hostel that offered lodgings to refugees. The dwarf's bunk was located in the hostel's second floor, however ht complained about the boisterous and loud neighbors, yet he appreciated the place's safety.
