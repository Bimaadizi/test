Taylor , was a chieftain of the Talfir settlers of the Moonshae Isles in the 2 nd century DR .

Relationships

He was the consort of the "First Mother", Gwynneth , and ruled their people after her death to establish what would come to be called the First Dynasty of the Moonshaes . He was succeeded by his and Gwynneth's son, Deric .

History

Taylor arrived in the Moonshaes with his people in the Year of the Executioner, 140 DR , fleeing their home to escape oppression under the Shadowking of Ebenfar . As consort to Gwynneth, he became chieftain after she died of consumption in the following year .

He ruled for only three years before his people came into conflict with the Llewyrr elves in the Year of the Fear and Flame, 144 DR , and he was killed in battle that year.
