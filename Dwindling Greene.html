Dwindling Greene was the daughter of Dholber Greene and one of the guests of Nortale's Hostel in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Like her father, Dwindling was a human with dark skin. She also shared his brown eyes and had straight brown hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Absolute crisis ravaged the Western Heartlands , the city of Baldur's Gate became flooded with refugees. Dholber Greene was lucky enough to secure lodgings for himself and Dwindling at Nortale's Hostel that offered beds to the refugees. Dwindling and her father lost family members, who did not make it to the Gate. At the hostel, the girl complained about lice and funny-smelling soap .
