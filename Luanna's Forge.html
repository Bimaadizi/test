Luanna's Forge was a forge and blacksmith shop located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The forge was located in south Zhentil Keep in the western section of the Foreign Quarter in between Teshwave Street and Center Street .

Activities

The forge offered common services including armor and weapon repair and making horseshoes and common tools and metal objects. The work done here was by far the best to be found within the entire city, and the prices reflected that quality.

Inhabitants

Luanna's Forge was owned by a muscular woman named Luanna in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
