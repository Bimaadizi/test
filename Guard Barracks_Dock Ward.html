These Guard Barracks housed the City Watch of Waterdeep in the city's Dock Ward .

Location

These barracks were located along the Dock Street , close to the Smokehouse and the East Torch Tower .

Structure

This building was three-stories tall. Like all barracks of the City Watch, it was built to be fireproof.
