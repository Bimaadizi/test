A Trio of Trumpets was a signature dish from the Singing Sprite inn of Secomber .

Description

The dish consisted of three hands-length horn-shaped crackers , fried to a crisp. They were stuffed with melted white cheese , chopped nuts , and fried mushrooms .

Availability

A Trio of Trumpets was a signature dish served at the Singing Sprite inn, alongside Three Crabs and the Hungry Knight .
