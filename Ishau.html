Ishau was a sunken village located at the bottom of Refuge Bay in Chult .

History

Periodically, sea hags navigated through the ruins looking for treasure or the remains of the dead.

Inhabitants

After it sunk, Ishau was home to reef and hunter sharks , along with aquatic dinosaurs called plesiosaurs .
