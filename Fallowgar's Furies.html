Fallowgar's Furies was an all-female adventuring band from Tethyr , active in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

By the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Fallowgar's Furies were believed to had been dead for a decade, perished in the Weldath forest of Tethyr . The band's name, however, was remembered thanks to stories of the women's treasures buried nearby the Hunter's Tree .
