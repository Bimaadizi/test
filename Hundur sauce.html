Hundur sauce was a sweet and savory condiment found in the city of Waterdeep on the Sword Coast North .

Description

The sauce was cooked with tamarind and clam sauce and was sour-sweet to taste. It was popular for being a medium-hot condiment, served with pan-fired quipper fish as evenfeast at the Yawning Portal .
