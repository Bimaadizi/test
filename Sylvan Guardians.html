The Sylvan Guardians was a group of centaurs living in the forests of Shadowdale . They were generally standoffish with visitors, but helpful with anyone whose goals aligned with their own.

History

Some time during the year 1369 DR the centaurs went hunting for a group of goblins that plundered their home glade. This was most likely the raiding Broken Bones clans .
