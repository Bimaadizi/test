The Herald of Zehir was a yuan-ti representative of the snake god Zehir at the Temple of the World Serpent in Chult in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

The Herald of Zehir stood taller and more muscular than other yuan-ti and had four arms, each of which had a " Fang of Zehir " that inflicted a deadly poison.

History

The Herald of Zehir was a gift from the god Zehir.
