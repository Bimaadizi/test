Tamal was the young prince of the dwarven Orothiar Clan in the Barony of Bloodstone as of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Personality

Tamal was fairly immature. He spoke constantly and had a wacky sense of humor.

History

In 1357 DR, Tamal and his tribe met Gareth Dragonsbane 's party and he may have decided to join the party.

In the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR , Tamal joined the Twilight Riders despite the concerns of his father. He turned out to be a skilled and valued member of the Riders.
