Ninrae was a bartender and cook of Swordsfall House , a shraehouse (a small restaurant/tavern), in the city of Silverymoon in the Silver Marches in the late 14 th century DR . Around 1372 DR , she was a kindly woman of middle years.

She spent time with Chesmyr Morrowynd , a regular at Swordsfall, and one night showed him how to make thaevor potatoes .
