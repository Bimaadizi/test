Lok was the leader of the Eagle Knights in Payit in 1361 DR . [ page needed ]

History

Lok was one of the most powerful men in Ulatos . He was always close to the ruler Caxal during times of war. [ page needed ]

In 1361 DR, soon after the arrival of the Golden Legion 's ships, Lok was called by Caxal to a war council with the leader of the Jaguar Knights , Gultec , to discuss how to fight the strangers. [ page needed ]
