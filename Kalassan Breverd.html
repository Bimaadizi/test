Kalassan Breverd was a Talassan Storm Knight , active some years before the late 13 th century DR .

History

Years before Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , Storm Knight Kalassan Breverd fought against the enemies of the Church of Talos in the Moonshae Isles . Kalassan wielded the unique enchanted weapon called the Storm Bow , killing numerous druids and rangers in the Moonshaes, until slain by an enemy in the struggle.
