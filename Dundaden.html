Dundaden was a Netherese fighter who owned Dundaden's Armory in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Dundaden was regarded as the finest armorer in Karsus and potentially all of Netheril . She employed Macchi and Lucio to assist her in the craft.
