Kardum , known as the Lord of the Balors and the Patron of Flames , was one of the demon lords of the Abyss . Kardum held dominion over all the balors of the Abyss, and held the concern of fire and flame.

Divine Realm

Kardum ruled over the 21 st layer of the Abyss, appropriately named The Sixth Pyre .
