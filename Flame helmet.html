Flame helmet was a cassidae shell found in the Realms.

Description

Helmet shells were sought after for making cameos. Flame helmet shells had mottled patterns of red or orange which made cameos with unusual designs. A large specimen in good condition had a base value of 40 to 50 gp, depending on demand.
