Llewellan was a human militant invoker , who adventured through the realms in the mid 14 th century DR .

Personality

A decent, but selfish person, he would make anyone underestimating him regret it.

Abilities

As a militant invoker he had less fighting ability than most fighters , so he compensated by wielding a greatsword , while slinging fireballs and lightning bolts around.

Possessions

He wielded his aforementioned greatsword and his spellbooks , and wore a set of metal armor.
