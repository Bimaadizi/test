Zook was a yellow faerie dragon in Cormanthor in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

The goddess Mielikki herself tasked Zook with a mission to protect the Cormanthor woodlands.

History

Circa 1486 DR , a family of jackalweres established themselves in the area and Zook didn't have the power to defeat them, so she used her magic powers to scare away people until an adventuring party arrived.

Personality

Zook was a little too dour for a faerie dragon. Although she enjoyed a joke at another person's expense as much as the next faerie dragon, Zook simply didn't laugh as long about it. Zook liked company, so her tactic of scaring people away was difficult for her.

Relationship

Zook was an ally of the Harpers .
