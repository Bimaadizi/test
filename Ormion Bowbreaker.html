Ormion Bowbreaker was a male Chondathan human psion seer and a soldier based at Castle Crag in Cormyr in the 1370s DR .

A young man, Ormion was one of the psionicists hired by Commander Bren Tallsword to instantly transmit secure messages via telepathy to Arabel in the event the castle was attacked.
