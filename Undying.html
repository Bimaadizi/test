The Undying referred to the trio of undead giants who were the true rulers of the necropolis of Sokkar as of 1367 DR .

Their names were Noq the Inspired , Arun the Ever-Vigilant , and Merodach the Deprived .

Activities

The rom left Sokkar to itself unless a pressing matter arose. When the city was still inhabited the Undying entertained the human rulers who made yearly pilgrimages seeking the wisdom of the undead.
