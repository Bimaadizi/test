TypeMagical dagger

A dagger of throwing was a sturdy, perfectly balanced dagger that radiated magic and carried an enchantment ranging from +1 to +4 .

Powers

It could be thrown up to 180 feet (55 meters), and on a successful hit, hurt its target twice as much as an ordinary dagger, as well as striking them with magical energy.
