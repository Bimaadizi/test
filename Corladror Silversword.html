Corladror Silversword was a nobleman from Cormyr . He was one of the heads of the Silversword house during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

He was the head of House Silversword .

History

He attended the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR . While there, he was among those who stood up suddenly at the sight of the ghost of the Regent Alusair Obarskyr .
