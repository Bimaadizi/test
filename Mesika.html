Mesika was a sacred stone monk from Mezro who sought to protect the people of Chult from the death curse around 1490 DR . As part of her secret operations, she used the alias Nerissa .

Description

Mesika was a young woman. She had dark skin, green eyes, and dark hair.

Personality

Mesika was determined and secretive. She hated necromancy in all forms.

Possessions

Mesika possessed a magical bracer engraved with the symbol of Ubtao that could grant others resistance to spells .

Relationships

Mesika was allies with the Harper agent Soggy Wren .

History

During the events of the death curse around 1490 DR , Mesika was in Port Nyanzaru . She worked to obtain the Oracle's Eye , a part of a dreamer's amulet that was being offered as a prize in a dinosaur race. She also fought against Viplo in order to investigate another piece of the amulet.
