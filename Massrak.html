Massrak was a powerful yet insane priest and prophet of Zehir , who dwelled in the Underdark during the 15 th century DR .

Abilities

Massrak could call upon Zehir to provide divine punishment , fire storm , obscuring mist , searing light , and soundburst .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Massrak operated among the yuan-ti forces from a scenic overlook near the Great Waterfall , not far from where Kressp hunted.
