A weredolphin was a type of lycanthrope that could take the form of an elf -like humanoid or a dolphin .

Description

Weredolphins enjoyed resting on the shore or on the backs of large whales .

Abilities

Like many lycanthropes, a weredolphin could only be harmed with silver or magic weapons .

Combat

These werebeasts attacked by ramming their foes. Weredolphins could summon up to ten other dolphins or even a couple of whales to aid them.

Ecology

They swore weresharks , and other evil aquatic weretypes, as their enemies.

History

Sometime in the mid-14 th century DR , a group of weredolphins in the Dragon Reach discovered an underwater tunnel in Ravens Bluff 's harbor. It had been created long ago by the Four Ravens Thieves Guild and was camouflaged by a permanent illusion of a seaweed covered reef. They found that this tunnel connected to a hidden cave system and began using the abandoned caves as a place to rest during the day.
