The Street of Lanterns was a murky, refuse-lined road in an unsavory part of the Old City section of Iriaebor in the 14 th century DR .

Location

This road intersected with the Street of Jewels .

Notable Locations

The Prince and Pauper , a gambling establishment.

Inhabitants

It was not uncommon to find painted ladies soliciting favors for coin along this street, nor rats scurrying among its trash-lined gutters.
