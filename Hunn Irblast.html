High Priest Hunn Irblast was the leader of the House of Ilmater , a temple dedicated to Ilmater , in Westgate in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Hunn was the leader of Ilmater's faithful in Westgate. His congregation within the temple consisted of 6 other priestesses and 12 followers.
