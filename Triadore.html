Triadore was a merchant of the Iron Throne who lived in Baldur's Gate during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Triadore was present in the Baldurian Iron Throne headquarters when it was thrown into chaos in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR . Zhalimar Cloudwulfe and other acolytes of Sarevok stormed the building to intercept Emissary Tar before her negotiations began with one of the Throne's leaders, Thaldorn Tenhevich . Triadore came racing out of the building just as Abdel Adrian and his companions arrived inside.
