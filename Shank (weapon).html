Shanks were archaic weapons akin to a simple knife that were sometimes wielded by thieves and ne'er-do-wells.

History

Long before the late 14 th century DR , thieves of Waterdeep were known to wield these primitive weapons. One of the alleys that were a gathering place for such criminals would later be known as the Shank Alley .

By the 14 th century DR, 2 feet (0.61 meters) shanks had become a part of a standard set of thieves' picks .
