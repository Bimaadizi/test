Velorna Jalaneth was a bard and writer of ballads who lived in the early-mid 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Adder, 1328 DR , Jalaneth published the ballad, Friends I Weep For You . The poem spoke of friends standing together from life until death, including their experiences, such as "gathering trouble as farmers reap turnips ".
