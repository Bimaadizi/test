Sorelisa Zandra , was a Zhentarim bounty hunter sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR , posing as officers of Mirabar , Sorelisa and her partner Naeremos were sent to find the notorious dwarf criminal Worvil Forkbeard by offering protection and a recruitment offer.
