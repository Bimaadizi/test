Orpher was a thriving coastal settlement found in the nation of Durpar , as of the mid–14 th century DR .

Geography

It was situated on the northwestern stretch of the Golden Water , where the River Sarren emptied into the bay.

Government

Unlike other Durpari cities, Orpher's ruling chaka was not led by a family of merchants, rather a wealthy adventuring company known as the Trollslayers .
