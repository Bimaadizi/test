History

Around 1347 DR, Agwain fell in love with Bronwyn Daggerford , sister of the Duke of Daggerford , but she refused him. So, one night, Agwain and his men kidnapped Bronwyn, believing that in time she would come to accept him as a husband.

He passed through Laughing Hollow where some adventurers who were members of the Daggerford's militia found Agwain and his followers. He proposed to settle the matter with a duel.

Personality

Despite all this, Agwain was an honorable man.
