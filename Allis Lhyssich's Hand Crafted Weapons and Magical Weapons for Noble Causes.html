Allis Lhyssich's Hand Crafted Weapons and Magical Weapons for Noble Causes was a specialist weapons store that also had standard gear for sale.

Location

The shop was located close to the south gate of Neverwinter .

Services

The master blacksmith, Allis Lhyssich , was one of the few smiths able to create magical weapons with meteorite ore. The crafting procedure took three days to make these highly enchanted meteorite weapons.
