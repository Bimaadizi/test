Scroll of protection from heat was a magical scroll found in Zakhara , the Land of Fate.

Description

Reading this scroll created a stationary area of protection centered around the caster, with a diameter of 10 yards (9.1 meters). Within the area of protection, the effects were similar to the cool strength spell and lasted for three days. Those within the protected area were not affected by the heat of the sun.
