Maethlin was an apothecary who lived in the village of Eveningstar during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

In addition to providing herbs and ointments, Maethlin secretly made poisons. He was also a spy for the Zhentarim .

Relationships

His father was Maglor , who was also a spy for the Zhentarim when he was alive.
