The Caliph's Harbor was located in Huzuz , the City of Delights, along the banks of Al-Sarif River .

Activities

This harbor housed the personal barge of the Grand Caliph as well as the admirality of Huzuz.

The Grand Caliph's barge was maintained in a state of constant readiness, since none knew when he would wish to take a sporadic trip.

Extensive repair facilities performed maintenance on at least five ships at any given time. At least a dozen launches provided access from the repair areas to the harbor.

Inhabitants

Keyfa al-Parid was the harbor master of the Caliph's Harbor in 1367 DR .
