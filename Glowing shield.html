A glowing shield was a type of magical shield that could be found within the Sword Coast circa the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The bearer of this shield heard subtle and encouraging whispers in some long-lost language, making them feel safe and well-protected.

Powers

In addition to the physical protection offered by a glowing shield , its bearer was enveloped in a loving protection : whenever they were seriously wounded in battle, the shield offered them increased vigor and vitality for a limited duration.
