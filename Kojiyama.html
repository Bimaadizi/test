Kojiyama was a mountain located in Kozakura .

Description

Kojiyama was located in Takako Province on the island of Shinkoku . The majestic peak remained snow-capped throughout the year. The Imperial Post Road ran along the base of Kojiyama, but the mountain's rugged terrain detered most from venturing beyond the road's confines.

Inhabitants

Kojiyama's unforgiving heights served as a haven for hermits, ascetics, and bandits seeking solitude or evading the world's gaze. The elusive Frozen Waterfall Shrine was located above the snow-line. The majority of Kojiyama hermits remained oblivious to the presence of this secretive sanctuary.

History

Kojiyama played a significant role as the last bastion of local resistance during the Hojo War .
