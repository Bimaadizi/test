Sargauth's Bounty was a shop in the Central Trade Lanes of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR .

Structure

Sargauth's Bounty was a two-story building in poor condition, its exterior largely concealed by a sprawling collection of salvage.

Services

Rusty swords , barrel hoops, waterlogged timber, stained garments, wagon wheels, partially assembled carts, and other assorted flotsam and jetsam could be found here at low prices, though the overall quality of these goods was correspondingly poor.

Inhabitants

Sargauth's Bounty was owned by the brothers Duram and Darum Ghaz . They retrieved discarded items from the Sargauth and arranged them in a haphazard, maze-like display around their ramshackle residence and business office.
