Kenhart was a young man of Thunderstone in Cormyr in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , he was kidnapped along with Troyappa , Joelem , and Jonren to work as a slave in the fire giant Oghalis 's smelting operation in the Hullack Forest . He and the rest were kidnapped by a satyr who was forced to kidnap them or else his own family would be killed.

Fortunately, not that long after disappearing from the town, concerned adventurers raided the operation, freeing the young men along with many enslaved centaurs and satyrs.
