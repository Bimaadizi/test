Bulta was a small mysterious island found in the Pirate Isles on the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Geography

Bulta lay on the western rim of the Bowl along the Spoon strait.

Inhabitants

Though no permanent human settlements were found on the island, a wide assortment of half-sized animals called the minimals inhabited Bulta. These creatures piqued the curiosity of the pirates who sometimes explored portions of the island. Superstitious and wary of the island's bizarre animals, the pirates were careful not to disturb anything during their visits.

Tiny winged humanoids were rumored to have been spotted flitting about the island.

Rumors & Legends

Bulta was believed to have once been much larger, and perhaps shrunken in size like the minimals with powerful magic.
