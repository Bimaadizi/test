Ihimbraskar Evendusk was an inhabitant of Myth Drannor in the 3 rd century DR .

History

In 241 DR , Ihimbraskar and his wife Duilya Evendusk consulted some business regarding Cormanthor . After Lords Malgath , Haladavar and Urddusk 's protest in the Coronal 's court, he related unto her the plan regarding the creation of a Mythal ; she noticed that the spells binding the others might well be used to bind elves.
