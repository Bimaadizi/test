The Cult of Reborn Flame was a primordial cult dedicated to Achazar, the Raging Flame , that operated out of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond . It was the most far-reaching of the primordial cults of the region, and while its headquarters were located in Imdolphyn , it also had important cells operating in Sambral and Tarmalune .

History

In 1479 DR , the Cult of Reborn Flame began to develop a ritual to try to awaken Achazar from his slumber. The ritual was completed in 1480 DR , but it was stopped, the cult destroyed and all its members killed thanks to the efforts of a band of brave adventurers , a coalition of powerful individuals from all the Windrise Ports, and even the will of Achazar himself.
