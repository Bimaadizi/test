The Wizard of Broddick was a wizard from Isle Broddick in the Whamite Isles . He was the most highly ranked leader of Broddick during this period. The Wizard represented Broddick in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
