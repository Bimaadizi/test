Infernal robes were enchanted robes found in Avernus in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The robe's magic offered the wearer resistance to fire, both mundane and magical, and granted them a magical protection similar to that of a +1 enchantment. Once per day, it also allowed the wearer to cast the fire shield spell.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , an infernal robe belonged to Mizora , a devil who served Archduke Zariel of Avernus. She was to reward the magic robe to her contractee, Wyll Ravengard , if he executed a runaway soldier of the Blood War , in reality, the tiefling slave from Baldur's Gate named Karlach Cliffgate .
