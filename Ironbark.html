Ironbark was a treant in Cormanthor .

History

In 1459 DR , Ironbark and two other treants were captured by the dracolich Dretchroyaster , who considered them a delicacy. In time, the dracolich slowly drained them of their life force.

By 1479 DR , only a half-mad Ironbark was still alive. That year, some adventurers infiltrated the lair and met the treant.

Personality

The long period of privation and fear had made him quite insane.
