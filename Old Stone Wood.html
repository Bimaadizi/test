The Old Stone Wood was a forest located on Oman's Isle within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Old Stone Wood covered much of the western region of Oman's Isle, touching the southern shore of the island along the Strait of Oman , passing west of the Grampalt Highlands along the coast, and continuing north past the western shore of the Iron Bay . The forest was approximately 100 miles (160 kilometers) in length and about 20 miles (32 kilometers) in width on average.

Notable Locations

The forest contained the island's only moonwell , located near the Cairn of Thelgaar Ironhand .
