Bane wands were enchanted wands that held magic unleashed upon command.

Description

Bane wands had 1 foot and 3 inches (0.38 meters) shafts of braided design. They were usually made using bone, ivory , or wood . The total number of charges varied from wand to wand.

Powers

Each of these items held several charges of the bane spell . The magics held within the wand could be discharged at will using a command word. Upon expenditure of a wand's last charge, it disintegrated.
