Steelmar Tolon was a noble of Cormyr of the late 14 th century DR .

History

He had only been recently ennobled for service to the crown.

In the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , Lord Steelmar Tolon fought beside King Azoun IV and was killed by a ghazneth .
