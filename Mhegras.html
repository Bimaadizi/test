Mhegras of Esmeltaran was a Zhentarim priest of Bane in the mid-14 th century DR .

Personality

He was quite bitter about owing allegiance to the Zhentarim . He was not all that brave himself.

Relationships

He bickered much with Sabran during their assignment to the capture of Shandril Shessair , and believed it would be necessary to slay him at some point. Drauthtar Inskirl and Eirhaun Sooundaeril both saw him as a rival worth slaying. Hlael Toraunt and Korthauvar Hammantle counted him as all temper and bluster.

Abilities

He had a spell he counted as a "brainbore-worm", which burrowed into eyes and gnawed at brains. He believed it to be adequate for revenge on his would-be murderer, strongly implying it was painful.

Possessions

Some of the cargo he concealed included horse barding.

History

Sabran joined the caravan from Scornubel to Waterdeep in which Shandril Shessair sought passage on late 1357 DR . He presented himself as a weaver instead of a priest, and concealed some horse armor as contraband, despite the fact he was undercover. He remained undercover through the ambush at Haelhollow , not taking part in the battle, taking note only of the fact that Aumlar Chathoun had been slain.

In the aftermath of the fight, they tried to capture Shandril while she was out of spellfire and knocked out, guarded only by Narm Tamaraith , but decided against it when she woke up too soon. However, they were then ambushed by the Harpers Beldimarr and Arauntar , who were posing as caravan guards, and got their necks broken. This left them unseen to the Zhentarim scriers Hlael Toraunt and Korthauvar Hammantle . for much of a day. Unknown to the Harpers, he survived thanks to a contingent spell.

He was slain during the caravan's next ambush; he attempted to capture Shandril but she reduced him to ashes using her spellfire .
