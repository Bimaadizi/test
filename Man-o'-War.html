The Man-o'-War , also written as the Man O' War , was a high-quality restaurant and inn on the Rock of Bral in the mid-to-late 14 th DR . It was considered by some to be among the best such establishments in all the crystal spheres . Its owners were unknown, but were rumored to be adventurers , and it was managed by Shrii Hallek .

Location

The tavern stood in the High City part of Bral , on Grand Street and at the end of High Street. It stood between House Cartan and the Royal Theatrical Company , and backed onto Lake Bral .

Description

It had an amazing view over Lake Bral.

Atmosphere

The Man O' War had such a high reputation for elegance and safety that the rulers of Bral had visiting diplomats stay here when guestrooms in the Starhaven palace were fully occupied. Hallek was very respectful to guests.

There was a strict dress code in the dining room, and both weapons and armor were proscribed for patrons.

Defenses

Security was provided by a force of elite mercenaries who were paid very well to guarantee guests' safety.

Services

A standard dinner cost as much as 8–12 gp per person, but the food was top quality and the best in Bral, while the service was exceptional.

Moreover, it had ten luxurious suites of rooms that could be rented for 10–30 gp per night. This included breakfast each morning and a bottle of fine wine .
