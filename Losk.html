Losk was a powerful hagspawn inhabiting northern Rashemen during the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Losk ruled over the Fortress of the Half-Demon in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . A savage band of brigands under his control helped enforce his influence in the region. Losk's marauders would frequently attack anyone traveling along the Long Road near the fortress.
