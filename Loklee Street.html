Loklee Street was a street in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate that began in Heapside and curved to Eastway .

History

Loklee Street got its name from from one of the earliest names of Baldur's Gate sometime before 0 DR . Circa the summer of the Year of the Purloined Statue, 1477 DR , the adventuring detective named Tav lived on Loklee Street.
