Alton Vandree was a drow cobbler in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 th century DR . He was a member of House Vandree and one of Houndaer Tuin'Tarl and Syrzan 's rebel group.

History

During the Silence of Lolth in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Alton joined Houndaer's group desiring to start a slave revolt. Vuzlyn Freth and Alton Vandree were, among others, summoned to Pharaun Mizzrym and Ryld Argith as they might be some of the males they needed to find. Alton and Tluth Melarn acted as bodyguards of the magically disguised Syrzan at one meeting of goblinoid slaves. He was presumed killed in the suppression of the revolt.
