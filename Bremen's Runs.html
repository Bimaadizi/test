Bremen's Runs was a colorfully named alcoholic drink brewed in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale .

Availability

The libation was sold as early as the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , in the city of Bremen . The drink was also served at The Salty Dog tavern in Targos where it was the cheapest intoxicant available. Sold for a single gold piece at the bar by its portly proprietor Gohar who was happy to share the town's latest rumors with paying customers.
