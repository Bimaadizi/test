The shield of morning was a magical small shield found in Cormyr in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The shield was decorated with the elaborate sunrise symbol of Lathander , also representing Amaunator . It was truly devoted to a brighter future.

Powers

The shield of morning flashed raw sunlight at enemies, burning and blinding them in combat.

History

The shield was given to adventurers fighting the forces of Archwizard Amskar in the Storm Horns at the behest of Swordcaptain Orin Arroway .
