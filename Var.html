Var , or more commonly known as Var the Golden was one of the three Shining Lands surrounding the Golden Water in southeast Faerûn and the most surrounded by water. After the Spellplague in 1385 DR , it was the only of the three to be destroyed and it was then known as Var the Drowned .

Name

Its name came from huge quantities of wheat fields found throughout the land.

Geography

This land was once known for its vast wheat fields, but in 1385 DR the Spellplague came, and Var the Golden sank beneath the waves. By 1479 DR , this area was a drowned land of silt-covered fields and ruined towns and villages. The ruins were stalked by its insane former ruler, Anwir Dupretiskava .

Locations

The capital was Pyratar . The city of Zelpir was a famed crafts spot. The "mouth" linking Var to the Great Sea was the Sunstrait through the city Myrmyr . The Isle of Ships resided to Var's northeast. The city of Vaelen beyond Var's most northwest hosted a Shou Town. The Curna Mountains isolated most of Var from the lands of Southeast Faerûn .

Other locations were: Des and Durgach .
