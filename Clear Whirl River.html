The Clear Whirl River was the major river of the kingdom of Hartsvale .

Description

The river was cold and often full of ice. On an island in the middle of the river was located Castle Hartwick , from which the monarch of Hartsvale ruled.

Geography

The river began in two separate forks in the hills between the Ice Mountains and the Ice Spires . The eastern fork started at Lake Woe . Once the forks came together, the river flowed north through Hartsvale. It continued flowing north until it emptied into the Endless Ice Sea after passing through Storming Gorge . Wyrm River was one of its tributaries.
