Skar was a goblin bandit of the Delimbiyr Vale in the late 14 th century DR . He served Hekkut and the Hark .

Personality

Skar was psychotic and spiteful, as well as stubborn, but brave.

Possessions

Like many goblins, he fought with morning stars , had a light crossbow , and wore studded leather armor , and may have had a potion of cure light wounds .

History

Late one afternoon in Eleasias of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Skar, Gvrag , and two other goblins caught the halfling merchant Blaz Merrymar on the road between Loudwater and Shining Falls . While Gvrag held Blaz in the air, Skar tormented and tortured him with a lit torch . Blaz heard him remark "This half-man has looser skin than the others. We must be getting to the bottom of the barrel." Fortunately for Blaz, a newly formed band of adventurers happened upon the scene and rescued him, slaying Skar and the others.
