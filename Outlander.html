The Outlander was the enchanted bow found in Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The Outlander appeared to be a crude wooden bow, carved with magical sigils that glowed with eerie green radiance.

Powers

When the Outlander was used to shoot, the bow's magic could summon additional arrows and send them towards the wielder's enemies. These ensorcelled arrows took aim at random targets, never sending more that one arrow per target.
