Overlapping Lines was a semiannual compilation magazine publication found in the Western Heartlands .

Contents

The magazine was made up of submitted stories and drawings from the admiring public regarding famous heroes of Baldur's Gate .

History

A copy was kept in the library of the Sharess' Caress brothel in 1492 DR .
