Chil'triss Kilsek was the leader of a group of driders .

History

Chil'triss was once a priestess of Lolth of House Kilsek before she was cursed to become a drider.

In time, she became the leader of a large group of driders living in the Underdark under the Misty Forest .

In 1375 DR , Chil'triss and her fellows were recruited into the Selvetargtlin by the judicator Dhairn .
