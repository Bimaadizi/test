Akh'gurrog the Hunter was a powerful draegloth in the Demonweb Pits in the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR , Akh'gurrog roamed the Demonweb, having been exiled from the polite society of demons and monsters that served Lolth .
