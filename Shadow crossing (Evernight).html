The shadow crossing in Neverdeath graveyard was a portal allowing for passage from Neverwinter in the Material Plane to Evernight in the Shadowfell . As of the late 15 th century DR , it was under the control of Valindra Shadowmantle and her Thayan forces.

Description

The portal made use of circle magic and was activated by sprinkling a special powder into an eternal flame that burned within the Neverdeath cemetery. A nearby teleportation circle granted easy access to the crossing for Valindra Shadowmantle and her apprentices.

Unlike the many so-called Crevices of Dusk that connected Neverwinter and Evernight, this portal was not a naturally occurring gap in reality, but rather a purposefully opened shadow crossing that had been established at a weak point in the veil between the planes .

Geography

In Neverdeath, the crossing was located at the center of the graveyard in a section known as Craftsman's Rest .

In Evernight, users of the crossing appeared between a pair of iron gates that otherwise appeared to lead nowhere. This site was located atop a small hill in a mostly abandoned neighborhood to the south. It lay within sight of the Thayan headquarters .

Defenses

On the Evernight side of the crossing, hidden Thayan guards kept watch over the portal. These included undead who were loyal to Valindra rather than to the city's ghoul rulers.
