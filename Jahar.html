Jahar was a phantom paladin of Helm in the ruin of Methegrist in the Moonwood .

History

In life, Jahar was one of the Moonwatchers , a group of paladin followers of Helm dedicated to protecting the Moonwood.

In 1384 DR , just after the death of his god, the imp Fortenesti infiltrated Methegrist, their fortress, and managed to turn the paladins against each other, the first to be manipulated being Jahar.

In 1484 DR , Jahar's phantom started appeared in the village of Quaervarr and in the area around. His goal was to attract someone able to finally defeat Fortenesti, still living inside the ruins. After the imp's defeat, Jahar finally disappeared.
