Brassfeather Lane was a street of hard-packed dirt and gravel in the North Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

In the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , this street ran from northeast to southwest, coming off of Trollkill Street and terminating at Immar Street . Along the way it acted as the western terminus of Anteos Lane .

In later years, the area that Brassfeather terminated at would be designated as part of Thunderstaff Way .

History

Brassfeather Lane was named, sometime prior to the 14 th century DR, after a famous smith who had lived along it.
