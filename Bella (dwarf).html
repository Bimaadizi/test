Bella was a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Fist Bella was a fair-skinned gold dwarf woman with light hazel eyes and hair of the same color, tied in a bushy ponytail. She kept her face clean-shaven. Bella wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist: a set of leathers , and a pair of leather gauntlets . Her chest was adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. Bella wielded a heavy crossbow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Bella was stationed in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, within the Wyrm's Crossing fortress.
