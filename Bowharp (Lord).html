Lord Bowharp was an inhabitant of Cormanthor in the 3 rd century DR .

History

In 241 DR , Lord Bowharp voiced his concerns regarding Coronal Eltargrim Irithyl 's safety during a hidden meet with other nobles, who were in favor of the coronal. Unknown to him, they were being spied by Elminster .
