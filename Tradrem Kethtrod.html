Tradrem Kethtrod was an earthsoul genasi and the Steward of the Earth of the Four Stewards of Akan没l in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Tradrem was a barrel-chested, square-jawed genasi with brown skin and golden szuldar lines.

Personality

Tradrem was an ambitious individual, but also very patriotic. He had the well-being of Akan没l as one of his priorities. Coming from a minor noble family, Tradrem believed he had much to prove, and he always was thinking on how he could get political advantage of any situation.

Relationships

As of 1479 DR , Tradrem was considering fostering a good relationship with Queen Arathane , in order to exchange favors with her in the future.

Activities

As the Steward of the Earth, Tradrem was the spymaster of Akan没l.

History

Tradrem was the first earthsoul who attained the position of Steward of the Earth, as before him that position was held exclusively by watersoul genasi . He was also the first earthsoul noble who aspired to a higher position rather than just being a high-ranking officer in the military forces.
