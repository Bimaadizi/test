Cryptknights were undead unique to the Tomb of Martek . They had an aura of fear about them.

Description

Most cryptknights were white-robed, strange figures.

Abilities

All cryptknights continuously radiated fear . Their capabilities for physical damage varied somewhat. Some of them were immune to nonmagical weapons.

History

In life, one cryptknight was killed at the same time Mobius Tower became time-trapped, while trying to assassinate the Sheik. There were three other cryptknights in the tower and there may have been teams of up to six cryptknights in the Inner Sanctum of Martek , amongst the Guardians of Nephthys.
