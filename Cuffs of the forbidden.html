The cuffs of the forbidden were a set of magical jewelry found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The wristcuffs greatly aided in the casting of necromancy spells and other forms of magic that used chilling cold, poison and negative energy . They also made the wearer more open to receiving negative energy for good or ill, making them ideal for undead and others who could benefit from such energy.

History

The bracers were found by the Cormanthor Elves in the Burial Glen of Myth Drannor, where the Red Wizards Vorinar Gall and Ezkir Taromor were despoiling the dead during Droic Orgul 's raid.
