Gilbert was a friendly dwarvish barbarian and student pf the Neverwinter Academy in Neverwinter during the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Gilbert attended the same martial combat classes as the person who would become the Hero of Neverwinter . Some rumors said that he was attracted to the future Hero and proposed to attend the feast that followed the graduation ceremony together.

He admired Aribeth de Tylmarande and considered her to be a hero and an inspiration. He also harbored a crush on the paladin and was jealous of fellow student who graduated and got an opportunity to meet her.

History

Gilbert was one of the Neverwinter Academy students in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR during the deadly outbreak of the Wailing Death . Although Gilbert was capable fighter, his life was tragically cut short in the attack on the Academy.
