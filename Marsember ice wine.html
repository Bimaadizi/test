Marsember ice wine was a type of wine from the city of Marsember in Cormyr . It was exported to the Western Heartlands , the Chionthar Valley , and the city of Baldur's Gate , as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The ice wine had a light and refreshing taste, like the mist hanging over the city's many channels. Its flavor perfectly captured the feeling of its city of origin, apart from the aroma of dead monkfish .
