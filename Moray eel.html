A moray eel was a large type of eel with a deadly bite.

Description

These eels were larger and more dangerous than typical eels. Moray eels could weigh up to 100 pounds (45 kilograms) and grow to be around 5 feet (1.5 meters) long.

Combat

Although not the most fearsome opponents an adventurer could face, one still had to be wary of these eels, as they had a deadly bite. It was even possible for a moray eel to cleanly bite an entire arm off of a body.

Ecology

Moray eels were often found in locathah lairs, where they acted as keepers and sentries.

In Serôs , the great undersea realm, moray eels, lobsters , and octopuses were considered natural enemies of one another.

History

In the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , the shalarin priestess , Tu'ala'keth , conjured a menacing underwater world with coral formations, where moray eels lurked amidst other threats in a limitless ocean.
