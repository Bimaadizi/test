The Northfurrow was a wagon trail running between Waterdeep and Goldenfields .

Description

The trail ran parallel to the west bank of the River Dessarin , passing between the Stump Bog and the Sarcrag .

Trade

The Northfurrow facilitated the heavily guarded transport of food from Goldenfields south to Waterdeep, and subsequently the return of empty wagons back the way they came. The trail got especially busy after the harvest season.

Inhabitants

Along both sides of the trail were rural cottages and farmsteads owned by the Northfurrowers, as the local humans and halflings referred to themselves.
