Booter was the dungeon master and the "official torturer" of the Shadow Thieves in Amn in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Booter was a gnomish man with an untidy, uneven beard and a single yellow tooth.

Possessions

Booter was adorned in leather armor , sometimes preferring a set of studded leathers instead. He carried an enchanted +1 flail , a small shield , and had an enchanted +2 greatsword at the ready.

History

By the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Booter was a well-respected member of the Shadow Thieves who manned the guild's torture chambers in Aran Linvail 's hideout located in the Wave District 's docks.
