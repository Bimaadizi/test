Trosken Adze was a dwarven tavernkeeper who co-operated the Ball and Chain Tavern with his wife Alda on the docks of the Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Trosken had a bald head and a long dark brown beard braided into two forks. He wore a neat green waistcoat, and stepped up on a short bench to reach the proper height while tending the bar.

History

Trosken used to be an adventurer , but retired from it and became a tavernkeeper, seeing it as less risky and more profitable. The Ball and Chain became the first and only tavern to service the docks of the then-new Wheloon Prison
