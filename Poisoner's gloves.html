Poisoner's gloves were enchanted gloves that strengthened the poison damage dealt by the wearer.

Description

These simple leather gloves appeared clean and well-cared for. However, they had a strong, cold, acrid smell.

Powers

Poisoner's gloves possessed a type of envenom enchantment that turned any poison damage dealt by the wearer, be it of magic spell , or simple weapon coating nature, into an affliction, possibly rendering the target weakened and afflicted by the poison.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a pair of poisoner's gloves was kept in Malus Thorm 's office in the House of Healing , within the deadly Shadow-cursed Lands .
