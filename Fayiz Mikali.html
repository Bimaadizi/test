Fayiz Mikali was a crewman on the pirate vessel Leap for the Throat in 1367 DR .

Activities

As a sea mage , Fayiz made good use of his powers while raiding merchant vessels sailing on Suq Bay . Fayiz was also in charge of the other wizards onboard the ship.

Possessions

Fayiz owned a special mirror of mental prowess that Almari bint-Kafira used to communicate with Prince Tannous al-Assad back in Huzuz .
