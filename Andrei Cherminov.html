Andrei Cherminov was a Raumviran farmer who lived in Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Activities

Andrei's farmstead was located on the western outskirts of Almorel just north of the Golden Way and the Golden Bridle caravanserai .
