Brawler Rot was a goblin member of the cult of the Absolute who dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Rot had orange skin and long brown hair, that went down to his feet. He wore a leather cuirass and crude bracers adorned with bones.

Possessions

His preferred weapon was a crudely-made goblin scimitar .

History

Rot was among the goblins that celebrated at the Shattered Sanctum the cult's successful raid on Waukeen's Rest , in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
