The Halls of the Unicorn was a large temple devoted to Mielikki in Elventree around 1367 DR .

Description

The entire temple was set within the hollowed-out trunks of massive fallen trees. The Halls of the Unicorn was the largest temple in Elventree at the time.

Inhabitants

The temple was run by High Priest Chandlar Hummerspoon and his five druid followers.
