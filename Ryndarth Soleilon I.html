Ryndarth Soleilon I , who was also called the Boy King , was the fourth King of the Soleilon Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Ryndarth I inherited the throne when his father, King Lemere II , died when he was an infant in −68 DR . However, his mother, Lharida , ruled in his stead as Regent until −55 DR when she died from poisoning. Ryndarth sat upon the throne at the age of 17. He ruled until −27 DR .

Relationships

Ryndarth's son and heir was Ryndarth II .
