Phantaizha was a patron of the Elfsong Tavern in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Phantaizha was a human woman with a light skin tone, short blonde hair, and brown eyes. She wore quality finery and seemed to think she was famous.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Phantaizha was a frequent customer of the Elfsong Tavern in the Eastway neighborhood of Baldur's Gate. She attended the "Laff Riot" show at the Elfong, a stand-up comedy performance hosted by Harvard Willoughby in Elfsong's side room with a stage. Phantaizha attended the show while incognito, trying to avoid being recognized.
