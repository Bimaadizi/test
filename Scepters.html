The Scepters were the city watch and army of Selgaunt in Sembia in 1358 DR .

Organization

The Scepters were 9,000 strong in 1358 DR. Their navy consisted of 36 vessels.

Activities

The Scepters maintained order within the city, harassed adventurers and visitors, and patrolled the waters of Selgaunt Bay near the city.
