Keonid was a Netherese arcanist and very prolific spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age .

History

Keonid developed nine spells during their life, most of which were focused on magic that manipulated the mind. The spells they invented in the 20 th century NY were geas in 1958 NY (−1901 DR) , confusion in 1959 NY (−1900 DR) , friends in 1969 NY (−1890 DR) , emotion in 1976 NY (−1883 DR) , fear in 1984 NY (−1875 DR) , and forget in 1992 NY (−1867 DR) .

The spells they invented in the 21 th century NY were charm person in 2004 NY (−1855 DR) , mass charm in 2009 NY (−1850 DR) , and finally, suggestion in 2010 NY (−1849 DR) .
