Corlin Cardin ( pronounced : /ˈ k ɔːr l ɪ n ˈ k ɑːr d ɪ n / CORE-lin CAR-din ) was the apprentice of Vajra Safahr , the seventh Blackstaff of Waterdeep , during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Corlin had a gentle personality. He was proud and honored to be the apprentice of Vajra, and showed a strong loyalty to her.
