Strange conduit rings were pieces of enchanted jewelry created using the arcane theories of the Oerthian wizard Tenser sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

Despite his studies being merely speculative, Tenser's writing on arcane displacement stated that the focused minds of those concentrating on active spells could unlock their hidden potential. This magic ring allowed the individual concentrating on maintain a spell to boost their offensive output, dealing additional psychic damage to their targets with each successful weapon strike.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a strange conduit ring belonged to the Inquisitor W'wargaz of Crèche Y'llek . The ring was stored in W'wargaz's quarters in the ruined Rosymorn Monastery.
