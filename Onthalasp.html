Onthalasp was a temple to Umberlee , the Sea Queen, clinging to the tiny Onthalaspan Isle in the northern part of Nelanther Isles in the Sea of Swords around the 11 th to early 13 th centuries DR .

Notable Inhabitants

It was the home of High Trident Arthaeuil Nrensam , who wrote Words on a Wave in the Year of the Roaring Tempest, 1016 DR .

History

In the Year of the Black Buck, 1226 DR , during a huge storm called Umberlee's Displeasure, both Onthalasp and its isle completely disappeared.
