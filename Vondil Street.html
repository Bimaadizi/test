Vondil Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Vondil Street was located in the easterm section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . It ran east to west, originating on the High Road at the border with the North Ward and passing west to eventually end on Diamond Street . Along the way Vondil Street was intersected by Mendever Street , Sul Street , Shield Street , and Copper Street .
