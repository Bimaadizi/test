Emser was a mysterious gnome inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He owned a small house located at the intersection of Duke's Way and the High Road across the street from the Grand Playhouse . Emser was believed to be an illusionist of unparalled skill. Most gave him a wide berth when they saw him around Daggerford.
