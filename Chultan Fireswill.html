Chultan Fireswill was a type of spirit from Chult in the Southwest Faerûn . It was exported to the Western Heartlands , the lands along the River Chionthar , and the city of Baldur's Gate , as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Chultan Fireswill had a potent and fiery aroma of heavy spices and high-proof alcohol. The drink possessed a powerful punch and was bottled in green opaque 1 pound (0.45 kilograms) bottles with wide bottoms and tall thin neck.
