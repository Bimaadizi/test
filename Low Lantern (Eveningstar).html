The Low Lantern was a tavern, dance hall, and theater located in the village of Eveningstar during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Low Lantern was located in the western section of Eveningstar along the High Road .

Food & Drink

The only food on the menu was smoked oysters and garlic-butter biscuits , (which were too salty, in the opinion of Volo ). However, it had many good wines in the cellar, including ruby-and-gold elverquisst and alurlyath from Undermountain .

Inhabitants

Maea Dulgussir owned the Low Lantern. King Azoun Obarskyr IV and Vangerdahast , under magical disguise, frequented the Low Lantern when the King wished to enjoy a relaxing evening.
