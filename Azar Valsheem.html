Azar Valsheem was a sous chef who lived in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR . Along with Chenna Fatrabbit and Klav Martilmur , they worked at the Elfsong Tavern , preparing food from midday until midnight.

Azar was known as being rather ill-tempered.
