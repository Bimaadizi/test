Velandilmur was a port town of the kingdom of Gontal .

Government

As of 1479 DR , the Docklord Lantra Orhond administered the town on behalf of Queen Imreera Hethkantan .

Background

Velandilmur was known for being a dangerous place for strangers. It was inhabited mostly by infirms, outcasts and outlaws.
