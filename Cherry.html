Cherry trees were a variety of trees in the Realms that grew a fruit of the same name.

Description

These trees grew flowers. There were several varieties of cherries, including red and black cherries.

Availability

Beyond Toril

Usages

Fruit

Wood

History

In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , three Chosen of Silvanus got together on Sapra with the leaders of the Turmishan druidic order known as the Emerald Enclave and conducted a magical ritual that ended a famine that had been plaguing that nation. This caused all manner of food to instantaneously grow throughout the land, including cherries, whose trees shook out flurries of blossoms as they were supplanted by the fruit.
