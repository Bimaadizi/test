Maraunth Torr was an evil archmage of the mid–14 th and late 15 th centuries DR .

Personality

Maraunth Torr was a young and ambitious archmage. He despised the shadovar for their haughtiness about non- Netherese . He was an expert poisoner. Elminster considered him like a young Manshoon .

History

In the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Hlael Toraunt of the Zhentarim considered Maraunth Torr together with Shaaan , Szass Tam , and Larloch to be one of the most powerful evil archmages of the time.

In the Year of the Rune Lords Triumphant, 1487 DR , he was one of the archmages who competed for possession of the Lost Spell during a spellstorm . Maraunth killed the rival Harper wizard Alastra Hathwinter but was ultimately killed by Shaaan the Serpent Queen in a fight.
