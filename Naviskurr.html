Naviskurr was a scullery boy at the Rain Bird Rooming House of Waterdeep in the late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Naviskurr was not very brave.

Activities

He was a scullery boy at the Rain Bird Rooming House . There, in addition to his normal tasks, he kept a door at the worst room of the kitchens clear of obstructions on instructions from the cook: that door had a carving of a hook-nosed wizard, and was covered in peeling blue paint. He was not very good at this.

Relationships

Phaerorn considered him his least promising scullery boy.
