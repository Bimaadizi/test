Zensil of Westgate was an enchanter pirate who sailed on the Maelstrom in the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Personality

The wizard was known as a soft spoken man.

Abilities

Zensil enjoyed bringing creatures of the sea into battles to help his shipmates, such as crocodiles , giant crabs , and on one occasion a giant sea snake . At the conclusion of each fight, Zensil would release his monstrous "allies" back into the deep.

History

Around 1359 DR , Zensil was the lieutenant and ally of Vurgrom the Mighty and the key to his success: it was Zensil who charmed small schools of vicious sea-creatures to aid Vurgrom in his raids.
