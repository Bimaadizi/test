Rings of knowing were enchanted rings that boosted the wearer knowledge.

Description

Rings of knowing were simple bands of precious metal with several impeded gemstones .

Powers

When worn, a ring of knowing magically slightly expanded the wearer's mind and imbued it with knowledge of the arcane lore.

History

A ring of knowing belonged to a trio of merchants in the Underdark under the Spine of the World , Myrvek , Wode the Guileful , and Heggr Splitsteel , in Mirtul of the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR .
