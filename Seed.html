Dawnmaster Seed was one of four devoted leaders of the Rosymorn Monastery in the Western Heartlands , who died before the late 15 th century DR and the monastery's fall to the githyanki invaders.

Description

Dawnmaster Seed's stained glass depiction showed a hooded humanoid with dark skin, a pious look, and Lathanderian garb. The depiction was shown wielding a masterfully crafted ceremonial hammer and a book adorned with Lathander's holy symbol .

History

Dawnmaster Seed was one of four high-ranking members of the clergy who served at Rosymorn Monastery sometime before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . The other three were Welkinglory , Stockhold , and Vaseid . All four were depicted on a circular stained glass mosaic in an upper central chamber of the monastery. Around the mosaic were four pedestals, upon which laid each of their ceremonial weapons – masterfully crafted enchanted +1 items that were among those great works that attracted pilgrims from across Faerûn , Lathanderian and members of other faiths alike. Dawnmaster Seed was known for conducting the reconsecration of the Rosymorn Monastery.
