Silmarien Silverleaf was a moon elf from the realm of Synnoria in the Moonshae Isles .

Description

Silmarien dressed in a blue shirt, green pants, and tall, brown leather boots. Over her shoulders was draped a purple cloak, as well as her lengthy gray hair.

Possessions

Among Silmarien's possessions were an ensorceled suit of chainmail , a magical longsword that made her more effective in combat versus giants , a pair of gauntlets of ogre power , and a girdle of many pouches .
