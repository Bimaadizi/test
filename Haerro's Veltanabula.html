Haerro's Veltanabula: Being the Triumphal Battle Spells of Vultivar Haerro, Monsterslayer was an academic magic textbook written by the Netherese arcanist Vultivar Haerro .

Contents

The book was a collection of Haerro's favorite combat spells . The spells included many early forms of more modern spells, such as a movable form of cloud of daggers , wall of force , forcecage , various mindworm spells that retributed against illithid attacks, ray-based spells designed to melt tentacles, and spells that shot cones of beads of force .

History

Haerro's Veltanabula was considered a foundational classic by the arcanists of Netheril during the Age of Discovery ( 2654 NY (−1205 DR) onwards). Any respectable arcane library would be expected to contain a copy of it, along with other works such as Sarturl's Study of Magical Fields , Skouloond's Spell Studies , Ivrynxra's Exploration of Enchantments , and Oloam's on Serpentine Matters .
