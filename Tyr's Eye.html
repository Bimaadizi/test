LocationNeverwinter , Sword Coast North

Tyr's Eye was a large and busy walled strake, or plaza, located between the Blacklake District and Castle Never .

Description

Tyr's Eye was a roughly shaped, round, and slightly elongated plaza. It was paved with stone and housed a collection of civic buildings, businesses, and other high-profile locations that benefited from the proximity to Castle Never. It also housed an open-air market of many stalls and tents. The merchants dealt in a number of goods, from school supplies, paper , parchment , quills , fruits, potions , and even expensive magical items , all of these goods were available in a mundane form and rarer exotic variety.

History

The Blacklake District survived the war with Luskan of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR almost unscathed and Tyr's Eye, which was technically a part of Blacklake, remained unaffected. Soon after, Shadow War of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR ravaged Neverwinter's economy. Soon after the war, a great fire blazed across the plaza, torching the mages ' school that was a prominent part of Tyr's Eye, and several other buildings. Eyewitnesses claimed that a red-headed sorceress was seen stoking the flames. To strengthen the Neverwinter economy, Lord Alagondar extended a personal invitation to the Church of Waukeen to establish a temple in the City of Skilled Hands and aid in its financial recovery. The Temple of Waukeen was built at the site of Neverwintran mages' academy. Along with the temple, a new Thayan enclave was established out of financial desperation. During that time in 1374 DR, access to most of the city was restricted for non-citizens. The Blacklake district was the only area open to guests, but only via a pass purchase.
