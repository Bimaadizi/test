Aldith Tresendar , also known as the Black Hawk , was a knight of great repute in the 10 th century DR .

Possessions

The knight owned Tresendar Manor , a large house to the east of Phandalin . He was also in possession of the enchanted longsword Talon .

History

When Phandalin was raided in the Year of the Empty Hourglass, 951 DR , Aldith descended into the caverns beneath his home to fight off the orcs . Unfortunately, he died in this encounter, and his longsword was lost in the cavern complex.
