Zerillo was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Zerillo was a human boy with a light skin tone, dark brown hair, and dark brown eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Zerillo attended a performance of some showy magics by three spellslingers, on the Murl's Rest plaza in front of Sorcerous Sundries . The boy was in awe and wanted to grow up to be a wizard .
