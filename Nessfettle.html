Lieutenant Nessfettle was an officer of the Neverwinter Guard that served the great city during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

She served under Captain Dunfield and had earned his trust as a reliable officer of the Guard.

History

Lieutenant Nessfettle led her soldiers into the city of Helm's Hold during the city's siege in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR . During the siege, they became overwhelmed by the Ashmadai and their devilish servitors and were forced to barricade themselves within an abandoned home. They endured ongoing attacks until a sergeant of the Guard broke through the Ashmadai forces, help Nessfettle and her men defend against a counterattack and finally head back out to the Last Redoubt camp with their lives.
