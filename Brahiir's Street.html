Brahiir's Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Brahiir's Street was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's North Ward . The road ran diagonally in between Suldown Street in the northeast just one building west of the intersection with Nindabar Street , and Gelthoon Street in the southwest. Catchthief Alley connected with the southern end of Brahiir's Street.
