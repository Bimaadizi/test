Velgor Zolond was a drow mage in Menzoberranzan in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa 1486 DR , Velgor was the second-in-command of Ryzliir Symryvvin in leading a drow battalion to intercept an expedition led by some adventurers , who'd been hired by Bruenor Battlehammer to investigate the threat of a demon lord , but she and her troops were defeated.

Relationship

Velgor was the husband and the second-in-command of Ryzliir Symryvvin .
