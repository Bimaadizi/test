Flandarr Roaryl was one of the princes of Eskorn , Laerakond , as of 1479 DR . He was a member of the Roaryl dynasty.

Background

In 1479 DR, Prince Flandarr plotted to become the next king of Eskorn, with the backing of King Ornan Roaryl 's most trusted spies and members of the Storm Rune merchant guild.

Relationships

Prince Flandarr was the second son of King Ornan Roaryl, and the brother of Imrar and Dlorna Roaryl .
