Vampiric daggers were enchanted daggers that drew strength from its victims, created sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Vampiric daggers had blades of deadly sharpness and tiny serrations. They possessed a powerful +2 enchantment that made the weapon's attacks deadlier and guided the wielder's hand toward their aim. Each successful strike with a vampiric dagger sapped the strength from the target, granting the wielder vampiric regeneration .

History

Sometime after the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , a vampiric dagger was handled by the leader of the three remnants of the Shadow Cult that followed the King of Shadows . The remnants of the cult holed up in a ruined fort in the Mere of Dead Men , trying to resurrect the King. The priests used a vampiric dagger against a band of adventurers from Crossroad Keep , sent by Khelgar Ironfist to end the cult once and for all.
