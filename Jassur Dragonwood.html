Jassur Dragonwood was a nobleman of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

According to a knowledgeable master of intrigues, Jassur was really nowhere near as smart as he thought.

History

In the lead-up to the Council of the Dragon in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Jassur was mind-controlled by the evil archmage Manshoon , just as Rorskryn Mreldrake was denounced as a traitor. The archmage looked out of his darting eyes while eavesdropping into the Council. After the Council degenerated into violence, Jassur Dragonwood was killed.
