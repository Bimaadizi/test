The Mithrest Inn was an exclusive inn for Athkatlan nobles , located in the city's Bridge District .

Interior

The inn was old but stylish and cultured. It was a well lit, single-floored inn, with many commodities. The main bar-room contained an ornate chess set and a large grand piano. The back room was a well-sized kitchen with a huge stove.

Services

Only the finest of citizens were attracted to the services of the Mithrest Inn. Pugney would serve premium beverages, at very high prices, whilst Rebecca managed the back kitchen, and served customers. Some of the drinks on Mithrest's menu were Berduskan wine , Winter Wine , Golden Sands Brews , Dragon's Breath Beer , Bitter Black ale , Undermountain Alurlyath wine , and Iriaeboran ale .
