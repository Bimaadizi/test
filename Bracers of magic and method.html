The bracers of method and magic were a set of bracers found in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The bracers were very high quality, and embellished with arcane runes around the edges.

Powers

They offered broad and subtle improvements to spellcasting and concentration when worn.

History

The bracers could have been found in Darstil's Tower in Wheloon Prison , after the mad wizard Darstil Vurusk was slain by the Riverside Rats .
