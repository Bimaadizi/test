Gallow Deely was an adventuring bard , active circa the early 11 th century DR .

Description

Gallow was a slender and lanky man who was not made for the adventuring life, but he often spent time with the heroic kind.

History

Gallow was the lucky owner of The Cittern of War , purchased from its hin maker, Relias Blackseed . The bard often used the magic instrument to write songs about adventurers and their grand deeds. He did, however, join the Company of Frozen Trees in the Year of the Hunted Whale, 1013 DR , to face a young green dragon . The Cittern of War helped the adventurers defeat the wyrm, and the tale of the adventure became the song The Fight of Green Thirteen that remained popular into the 14 th century DR .
