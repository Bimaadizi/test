Kimathi Stormhollow was a human inhabitant of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast North in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He wore a white shirt, a brown vest, a maroon cap, and black breeches , and he also had a decorated strap across his body.

History

In the late 1490s DR, Kimathi was a spectator at the revived High Sun Games held at the Neverwinter arena . He was one of the many who noticed the dark tendrils creeping down from the sky as Sofina invoked the horn of beckoning death , and remarked on it to the woman sitting beside him. He likely left with the rest of the audience, following a trail of treasure falling from a hot-air balloon .
