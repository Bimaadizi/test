Wan Tu Ma was a barbarian in Shou Lung .

History

Hailing from Ma' Yuan , Wan worked as a weapon-smith, horseman, and bodyguard for his sometime romantic interest Mei Li Mao . Around, 1357 DR , he was usually found at the Imperial Phoenix restaurant in Kuo Te' Lung .
