The stone of recall was an enchanted stone created by the Church of Tyr of Neverwinter in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

This unassuming magical stone had the power to pull its owner from danger back to the nearest temple of Tyr and back at the owner's command.

History

During the time the Wailing Death plague ravaged Neverwinter in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Lady Aribeth de Tylmarande gave the stone of recall to the Hero of Neverwinter to help them in the quest to end the outbreak. The Hero carried the stone through the plague, the Luskan -Neverwinter war, and the struggle against the ancient Queen Morag .
