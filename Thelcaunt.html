Master Thelcaunt was a skilled half-elven wizard inhabiting Saerloon in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Thelcaunt was a devout follower of Azuth , the Lord of Spells. He was a ranking member within the House of the High One , a temple dedicated to Azuth found in Saerloon.
