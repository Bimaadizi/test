Lagiluri was a small village located in Ra-Khati .

Geography

Lagiluri was located near the mighty Jumpa River in the foothills of the Katakoro Shan range in northwestern Ra-Khati.

Description

The only buildings in the village were a dozen white-washed houses and a three-story red monastery.

The monks of the village cultivated fields of green peas and golden barley . The farmland was fed by numerous streams from the river.

Inhabitants

Abbot Thongi Sam Bhota was the spiritual leader of the village.
