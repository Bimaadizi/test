Byldrath was a middle class inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Byldrath was an elfin male with pale skin, strong jaw, light brown eyes, and black hair, gathered in a severe bun. He wore clothes of quality make.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, the gates between the Lower City of Baldur's Gate and its more affluent Upper City were closed, monitored by the Flaming Fist mercenaries and allowing only the wealthy and current residents of the Upper City through. Byldrath was among those Baldurians who sought entrance into the Upper City. He felt frustrated with being forced to wait in a long queue in hot sun all day.
