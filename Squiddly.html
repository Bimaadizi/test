Squiddly was a street urchin from Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Squiddly had a slender build. He had red skin, a long tail, and the horns common of tieflings. His left eye was covered by a black eye patch.

Personality

He was impulsive, rarely thinking before acting.

Possessions

Squiddly wore an eye patch and carried a small bow and a quiver with a handful of toy arrows .

Relationships

Squiddly was part of the group of street urchins that included his friends Nat and Jenks . He communicated with the deaf Nat using a sign language Nat had invented.

History

In the late 15 th century DR, Squiddly and his friends were unwittingly entangled in the city-wide hunt for the Stone of Golorr , sometimes innocently helping or hindering the efforts of adventurers in retrieving it.
