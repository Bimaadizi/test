Gethshemeth was an ancient kraken .

Description

Gethshemeth was a massive and ancient kraken roughly 100 feet (0.03 kilometers) in length, with a dun-colored hide and large red eyes. Her presence was accompanied by the scent of a thousand rotting fish. After an encounter with the Shou monk Raidon Kane , she was left three tentacles short, having had them severed by the monk.

History

Gethshemeth was an ally to the morkoth Arcanum of Olleth . In 1396 DR , she was summoned to Olleth by Nogah and took possession of the Dreamheart .
