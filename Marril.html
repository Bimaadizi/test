Marril was an elven priest who served Corellon at the Temple of Leaves in New Sharandar during the late 15 th century DR .

History

When the settlement of New Sharandar was threatened by King Malabog circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Marril accompanied Iliyasha Guidingstar during her diplomatic mission to Neverwinter .

Marril worked with adventurers from the city to locate shards of an ancient Illefarn relic that shattered into many pieces when it was stolen from the forest by fomorian giants.
