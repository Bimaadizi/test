A masterwork lute of rest was a rare enchanted bard lute from Chult .

Description

This masterfully crafted wooden instrument had a prominent blue hue to its body.

Powers

The enchantments placed on a lute of rest accentuated the music created by the item by soothing the souls of all who heard it.
