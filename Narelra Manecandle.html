Narelra Manecandle was a late-14 th century DR Playwright of Athkatla from the Lands of Intrigue .

History

Narelra penned the Nine Lords Errant play sometime in or before the Year of the Blazing Hand, 1380 DR , and its first performance took place in Athkatla the same year. Narelra Manecandle's play's protagonist was Amlaric Amend-All, who, along with several coconspirators, plotted a murder most foul.
