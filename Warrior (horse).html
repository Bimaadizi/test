Warrior was a horse, owned by Narm Tamaraith shortly in 1357 DR .

Personality

When he was brushed, he nickered appreciatively.

History

Warrior was given to Narm Tamaraith not long before he arrived at the Rising Moon , after his marriage to Shandril Shessair .

The horse was slain not long after, when its owner was ambushed by Shargrailar .
