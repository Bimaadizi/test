Salopar's Field was a farm somewhere in Faerûn during the mid–8 th century DR .

Description

There was a sunken road between Salopar's Field and the neighboring Aerhiot's Field . The hedges outside both fields had overgrown into the ditch-level path to such a degree that they formed a gloomy tunnel-like space underneath their intertwined branches.

Flora and Fauna

Birds and squirrels darted along the path every time someone trod upon it.

History

In the Year of Laughter, 761 DR , while traversing the path between Aerhiot's Field and Salopar's Field, the farmer Nuglar fell prey to the Slayer , a mysterious, ethereal apparition, which slew him and his three oxen , leaving nothing but dust behind.
