Martens were a species of mustelid or "weasel", a family of small mammals that included ferrets and weasels .

Description

These animals were similar in appearance to other mustelids, being small and slender-bodied furry creatures. They measured around 2​ to ​4 feet (0.61​ to ​1.2 meters) in length and weighed around 2‒16 lb (0.91‒7.3 kg). Much like other mustelids, martens were skilled swimmers.

Ecology

Diet

These creatures were a carnivorous species.

Habitats

Like other mustelids, martens could be found in a wide variety of climates, though they more commonly inhabited cooler ones. They generally preferred to avoid dense settlements.

In north Faerûn , they could be found in parts of the Dalelands , particularly Daggerdale and Deepingdale . In northwest Faerûn they could be found in the Silver Marches .

Beyond Faerûn , martens could be found across much of the continent of Kara-Tur , living in temperate forests and plains as well as tropical and sub-tropical forests.

Relationships

Some wizards were known to have martens as familiars .

Usage

The hides of these creatures were quite valued, ranking among the most prized pelts of the Silver Marches. They were valued more than the hides of ferrets, stoats , and weasels, while they were typically valued less than those of ermines , minks , and sables , though they could fetch comparable prices.

The marten pelts of the Silver Marches were carried and traded all across the Sword Coast , while those of the Dalelands were traded by the Silverhand House coster to the cities of Arabel and Ordulin . In the trading posts of Everlund and Silverymoon , marten pelts could fetch anywhere from 2 to 8 gold pieces . In the further-off markets of Luskan , Neverwinter , and Waterdeep they were sold for twice those amounts. Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold marten hides at a flat rate of 8 gold pieces each.

Trivia

In the church of Baervan Wildwanderer , "Marten" was one of the titles for confirmed priests , ranking directly above " Hedgehog " and below "Red Fox".
