Mount Pojah was a mountain somewhere in Tabot near the Shou Lung border.

History

In Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) , a tortoise-shell divination at U'Chan Gompa promised the finding of a very rich gold mine east of Mount Pojah.

Notable Inhabitants

It was the home of the Forgotten One , a hermit and oracle, around 2607.
