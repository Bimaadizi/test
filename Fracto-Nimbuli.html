Fracto-Nimbuli was a cloud giant ally of Yrkhetep in Chondath in 1358 DR .

Personality

Fracto-Nimbuli was a greedy and vain individual.

Activities

Usually Fracto-Nimbuli was in his main room with three spotted lions as pets.

History

In 1358 DR, Fracto-Nimbuli agreed to help the arcanaloth Yrkhetep in exchange for gold and power. He transported a portion of Yrkhetep's troops—including the Skykillers , the Dark Watch , and others—to the Five Crown inn in Arrabar using his flying castle in order to battle the combined forces of the Sisterhood of the Oaks , the Lightblades, the Sunshafts , and the Windriders .
