King Strohm V was a king of Tethyr and a famous druid , who became an oak tree when he died.

Relationships

Strohm V was the second nephew and only heir of the previous king, Strohm IV . He had no sons but at least two daughters, the second who would become Queen Alisande .

History

Strohm V was born in the Year of the Rotting Orchard, 787 DR . He took the throne in the Year of the Patchworked Peace .

Legends say that when King Strohm V died, he changed into an oak tree. Since he had no sons, the Strohm Dynasty was ended. Tibor Mallorhen , the husband of Strohm's oldest daughter became the first ruler of the short-lived Mallorhen Dynasty . Following the death of the second Mallorhen king, Tethyr would be ruled by four of Strohm V's female descendants, a period of time known as the Queens' Era .
