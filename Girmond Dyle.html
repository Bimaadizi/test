Girmond Dyle was an elven Harper agent active in Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He had black hair and pale skin, and wore brown armor.

History

Following the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon circa 1485 DR and Oriphaun Huntsilver 's disappearance with a Nether Scroll , Girmond was sent to meet with the Cormyrean-aligned adventurers who had pieced together the scroll once more after finding its pieces across the High Road . Only one last piece of the scroll remained, lying in the hands of the Netherese forces occupying Granwell Keep within the Blooded Wood . Taking the pieces that the party had assembled for safekeeping, he sent them to recover the last pieces from the Netherese. However, he was shortly after discovered by the Netherese forces, who killed him, claimed the pieces of the Nether Scroll for themselves, and put his corpse on display for the Cormyrean forces to find at the entrance of the keep.
