Imaskari kamas were exotic weapons enchanted with the ability to cause acid damage. They may have been created by the Imaskari , given the tainted aura they showed, but rather probably were modeled after Imaskari devices.

Powers

Besides being enchanted, imaskari kamas dealt as much acid damage as they dealt slashing. Though most were modest in magical strength, a few found in the Underdark were of a might that trascended, almost twice over, some of the most powerful magical weapons available.
