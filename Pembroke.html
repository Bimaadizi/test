Pembroke was a small town located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Pembroke was located in southern Callidyrr in the county of Stirgewick . The town was situated near the center of the plains found between the Dernall Forest and Rushdown Wood east of the High King's Road .
