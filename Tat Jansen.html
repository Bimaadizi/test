Tat Jansen was a child of the numerous gnome Jansen family in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Just like her brother, Tot , Tat had fair skin and light brown hair.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Tat and Tot lived in Athkatla 's River District slums in the Jansen's family home, along with Ma Jansen , Kylie Jansen , Jan Jansen , and Uncle Gerhardt . Tat was fiercely protective of her pet cat , yelling at guests who dared to lay their eyes on the beast.
