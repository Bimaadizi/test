Behroun Marhana was a lord of the Marhana merchant family in the late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Behroun was quite charming, when he wanted to be.

Family

Behroun was half-brother to Anusha Marhana .

Business

Behroun employed both the warlock Japheth Donard and the pirate Thoster .
