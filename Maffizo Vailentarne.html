Maffizo Vailentarne was a drow wizard of Q'Xorlarrin in 1484 DR .

History

Maffizo was a minor wizard living in Q'xorlarrin.

During the War of the Silver Marches in 1485 DR , he, along his old associate Epricante , was part of a Xorlarrin reinforcement army sent to help the orc warlord Hartusk after an orc defeat near Mithril Hall . However, thanks to the plotting of Gromph Baenre and Kimmuriel Oblodra , Hartusk believed that Epricante and his army were traitors and attacked and destroyed them.
