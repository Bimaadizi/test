Sathariel was a dark angel in the service of the evil deity Asmodeus . His exact nature was unknown and, although he was described as a dark angel, he may in fact have been a devil. Alternatively, he could have been a fallen angel.

Description

Sathariel had black feathers on his wings and his body was shrouded in darkness.

Possessions

He had silver armor and wielded a silver sword .

History

Sathariel lived in Waterdeep and oversaw the operations of the Vigilent Order . In 1480 DR , he attempted to complete the ritual of the First Flensing , a spell that would have opened a gate to the Prime Material Plane for Asmodeus' use. He was slain by the deva Jinnaoth Ir'Gadohn in 1480.
