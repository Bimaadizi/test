Smee , known as the Brains, was a pirate from the Swilissaire Swipers of the pirate nation of Swil in the Whamite Isles . Smee represented Swil in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
