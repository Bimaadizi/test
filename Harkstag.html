Harkstag was a village located northeast of Serpent's Cowl during the 13 th century DR .

History

The village of Harkstag was known to be the home of the Vigilant Riders , who protected the region against armies of orcs and goblinoids circa 1265 DR . Sages believed that the destruction of the village by a dragon living in the nearby Forest of Wyrms in the Year of the Snarling Dragon, 1279 DR was due to it lacking the protective wards of Ss'thar'tiss'ssun .

During the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , the ruins of the village served as a hideout for yuan-ti of Najara .
