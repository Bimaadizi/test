Firstpeak was a mountain in the Spine of the World mountain range.

History

It was formerly known as Ironstar Mountain since it was once home to the Ironstar clan of shield dwarves . By 1370 DR , it was abandoned by the dwarves and the silver dragon Dargentum took up residence.

Geography

The svirfneblin city of Blingdenstone was located deep beneath the mountain.
