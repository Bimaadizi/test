Shelaya was a bard who performed at the Golden Goblet Inn in Mossbridges in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Shelaya was a petite human woman with a head of flaming-red hair, green eyes, and a pleasant smile.

Relationships

She was familiar with a good half-orc fighter who occasionally visited the Goblet – he retired fighter, Chester Hearth . Shelaya knew of a way to get to the orc's hut and was aware of Chester's old master – the priest named Athoomb .

History

When a new popular inn opened in Mossbridges in the late 14 th century DR, the Golden Goblet Inn became Shelaya's employer. She would often be found at the inn, plucking her mandolin and performing various songs, melodies, and tales.
