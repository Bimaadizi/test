Duric Green ( pronounced : /ˈ d ɜːr ɪ k g r i n / DUR-ick green ) was one of the owners of Green's Rest , an inn and tavern located in the city of Waterdeep during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Duric was a sturdy man sporting an impressive beard under a balding head.

Personality

His husband Zel and his business meant everything to Durin. Often jovial and smiling, he loved to hear entertaining stories from adventurers . He was willing to offer a free meal and drinks to people entertaining him and his guests with tales of their daring deeds.

Duric had a positive outlook on life, and was always ready to offer words of encouragements to guests. He was, however, terrible at keeping secrets.
