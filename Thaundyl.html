Thaundyl was a member of the Risen Cult of Bane in the mid-14 th century DR .

Abilities

Thaundyl was capable of casting the spells blindness , dispel magic , fireball , invisibility , magic missile , spider climb , wall of fire , and web .

Possessions

Like his fellow wizards at the time, Thaundyl possessed a set of robes , two daggers laced in sleep poison , and a ring of protection +4 .

History

In 1359 DR , Thaundyl was sent by the High Hands of the Risen Cult of Bane to kidnap High King Tristan Kendrick . He was accompanied by his fellow wizards , Rhammos and Zelgos , as well as twelve priests , fourteen orcs , and eight battle horrors . However, their plans were thwarted by a group of adventurers and Harpers .
