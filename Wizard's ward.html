The wizard's ward was a powerful crystal orb used by the eccentric mercenary sorceress Song in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The orb was carved from crystal that had a solid bright aquamarine hue.

Powers

The wizard's ward protected the holder from elemental threats such as heat, cold, acidic burns, and electrical shocks, and in turn could greatly boost their own casting of spells, especially ones that produced similar effects.

History

Song used the ward to enhance her magic when she fought on Greenweald Lane in an attempt to intercept a piece of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll that was being carried by the Harper Bensen Virandson .
