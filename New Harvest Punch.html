New Harvest Punch was an alcoholic drink served during the New Harvest Festival in Purfbin Doogrick 's home burrow.

History

New Harvest Punch was one of the highlights of the annual New Harvest Festival, alongside its famous Harvest Stew . The grand gnomish feast was turned into a disaster in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . Purfbin Doogrick and his Shred-o-Matic 1000 wrecked chaos on the gnomish festival attendees. Bald Dame Edza , whose prized wig was destroyed in the calamity, dived headfirst into a sizable bowl of New Harvest Punch to avoid the rampaging machinery.
