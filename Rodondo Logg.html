Rodond Logg was an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate 's Lower City in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Rodondo was a light-skinned half wood elf who kept his head shaven. He wore simple brown tunic , trousers, and a green hat.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Rodondo Logg frequented The Singing Lute alongside his significant other, a fellow half-elf man named Fuschia Vineshoot . Rodondo hated the Lute whilst his darling loved the place and was clueless of Rodond's true feelings.
