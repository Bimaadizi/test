Sapper Gald was a cultist of the Absolute who lived in the Shattered Sanctum fortress during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Gald had yellow-orange skin and wore simple clothing that included little armor.

Activities

As a "sapper", Gald was expected to strap a massive bomb to his back and directly at any structure or fortification that had to be destroyed in dramatic fashion.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Gald was among the goblins of the Shattered Sanctum who would immediately set out for the Emerald Grove , once ordered by True Soul Minthara Baenre .
