Glarleer's was a tavern in the town of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Location

It was situated in the central western part of the city.

Structure

It had two stories: an open main floor at ground level and an upper floor divided into a series of private booths.

Atmosphere

A fairly high-class establishment, it was a place people went to to be seen. There was music and dancing on the ground floor; appetizer dishes known as "lightplatters" were served; and fences, middlemen, and coinlasses of a more sophisticated sort could be encountered.
