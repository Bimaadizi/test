Darstil's gloves were the accoutrements of the Night Brigade wizard Darstil Vurusk , found in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The gloves were built to generally improve the accuracy and power of magical spellcasting.

History

They could have been found in Darstil's Tower in Wheloon Prison , after the mad Darstil was slain by the Riverside Rats .
