The Sparkle Hands were a unique set gauntletss crafted by the lightning-obsessed inventor Yrre the Sparkstruck .

Powers

Whenever the wearer of Sparkle Hands built up electrical charges within their body―a feat achieved using some of Yrre's other inventions―they gained increased physical powers. They became more swift and agile, and felt increased strength course through their body.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the gloves remained hidden away in a chest , found in the Sunlit Wetlands swamp off the River Chionthar .
