Tluth Melarn was a drow in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 th century DR . He was one of Houndaer Tuin'Tarl and Syrzan 's rebel group.

Abilities

He was highly skilled at massage and was favored by Waerva Baenre .

History

During the Silence of Lolth in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Tluth joined Houndaer's group desiring to start a slave revolt Tluth and Alton Vandree acted as bodyguards of the magical disguised Syrzan at one meeting of goblinoid slaves. He was presumed killed in the suppression of the revolt.
