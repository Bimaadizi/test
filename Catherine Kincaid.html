Catherine "Cat" Kincaid was the Captain of the Harbor Patrol of Ravens Bluff in the 14 th century DR .

Possessions

Cat Kincaid was often outfitted with her enchanted +1 chainmail and a ring of invisibility and inaudibility .

History

Catherine Kincaid was orphaned at the age of 14 and was eventually adopted by Jason Shimel , a corporal of the City Guard in Ravens Bluff who raised her to adulthood. Subsequently, Jason was assassinated by the city's black-market operatives. Catherine joined the City Guard to stop tragedies like hers ever happening again and later became the famously fierce commander of the City Guard's Harbor Patrol.

Relationships

She regularly bought information from the rogue " Otto the Mouse ".
