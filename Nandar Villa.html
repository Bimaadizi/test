Nandar Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Nandar .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's North Ward in between Whaelgond Way and Brondar's Way .

Description

The five buildings of Nandar Villa stood between two and three stories tall.
