The Leaving-House was a caravanserai located in Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Location

The caravanserai was located along the Golden Way outside of Almorel beyond the East Gate .

Activities

The Leaving-House also served as a warehouse for goods.
