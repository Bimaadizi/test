Urquirsh were a foul species of aberrations from the Far Realm that the kaorti utilized as living weapons.

Description

Urquirsh were small, stinking sacs of flesh, entrails, and tubes suspended by long, spidery legs. It was they that produced the resin that the kaorti used as armor.

Personality

Urquirsh were almost completely mindless, but were devoted to their kaorti masters.

Abilities

Urquirsh were highly nimble, as if under the freedom of movement spell at all times, and, as long as it lived, it was innately tied to the Far Realm. Urquirsh formed small rifts to their home plane periodically, and additionally used these rifts to procure their natural weapons. However, when cut off from their home plane, they were functionally harmless. Their only other defenses were a sharp stench that radiated from their bodies, able to sicken their enemies, and small claws. Possessing no eyes, they were also immune to any gaze attacks or effects that relied on sight.

Combat

Urquirsh would often conjure a spray of rubbery fluid from the Amoebic Sea, directing it toward their targets and effectively encasing them in resin, then slashing at their foes with their small claws. Alternatively, it could summon a sphere of acid within 90 feet of itself, detonating this makeshift "bomb" in the faces of its enemies. When an urquirsh died, the rift bonding it to the Far Realm would violently sever, exploding outward toward any creature near the dying aberration. Occasionally, this exploding rift would also allow another urquirsh to escape to the Material Plane .
