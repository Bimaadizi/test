Zukimoto Chikitada was a samurai in Wa .

Personality

Zukimoto was deeply loyal to his master but never admitted it to anyone. He cared a lot about Hiro's safety. He always looked as if everything displeased him. At times of laughter, he only showed a less grunting face.

Activities

Zukimoto was the right-hand man, aide, and secretary of the lord Hiro Matsu . He also acted as leader of his bodyguards.

History

Zukimoto went together with his master to Aru .
