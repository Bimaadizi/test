Zinnirit Yauntyrr was the former house wizard of House Yauntyrr of Eryndlyn .

Description

Zinnirit was aged. He normally wore spiky armor. He had red-orange eyes and his breath smelled of cinnamon and pipeweed. He was a powerful spellcaster and teleporter .

History

Zinnirit was the house wizard of House Yauntyrr in Eryndlyn until its fall circa 1362 DR . While house wizard, he gave lessons, magical and otherwise, to Danifae Yauntyrr , a noble daughter of the house.

After his house's destruction, he fled to Sschindylryn , where he obtained a gatehouse with three portals , which he protected with zombies .

In 1373 DR , Zinnirit was approached by Danifae, who had been taken as a battle captive by Halisstra Melarn of Ched Nasad . Danifae had had a binding placed on her, which she had Zinnirit remove. She then killed him and took his ring of teleportation , which had originally belonged to her mother.
