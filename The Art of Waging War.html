The Art of Waging War was a military work penned by General Bartholemew G. Blazencrow sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

Description

The book was bound in hide dyed red.

History

A copy of The Art of Waging War was kept in the library of Zerith Hold , Lord Purdun 's stronghold in the city of Duhlnarim in Erlkazar in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
