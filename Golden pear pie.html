Golden pear pies were sweet baked pastry dishes made with ripened pears .

History

During the Absolute crisis of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , golden pear pies were served at the art show titled "Strange Tides: Self-Portraits from the Sword Coast," hosted by Lady Jannath in her Lower City estate, along with wild mushroom soup , steak medallions with asparagus , and best wine procured from the Manorborn district of the Upper City .
