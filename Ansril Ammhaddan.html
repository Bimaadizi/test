High Songmaster Ansril Ammhaddan was the high priest of the House of Song , a temple dedicated to Milil , in Selgaunt in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Activities

Circa 1358 DR , Ansril was the leader of Milil's faithful in Selgaunt. His congregation within the temple consisted of 24 other priests and 49 followers. Ansril was a specialty priest of Milil called a tuneservant .

History

On Uktar 29 of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , during the Sembian Civil War , Prince Rivalen Tanthul summoned a contingent of Sharran priests to help in the upcoming Battle of Selgaunt . However, Ansril together with Aumraeya Ulmdrin and the city's high priests of Deneir , Oghma , Lliira , Lathander , Tymora , and Waukeen protested this to the hulorn Thamalon Uskevren II , but he rebuffed them with an accusation of cowardice.
