Salman bin Salman was a cleric of Hajama living in the small town of Ganam in 1367 DR .

Activities

Salman served as head priest for the entire village. He was also a source of protection for Ganam against the pranks of a deceiver genie named Ibrihim .

Possessions

Salman always carried a phylactery that was capable of warding off evil genies .
