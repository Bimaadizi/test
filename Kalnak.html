Kalnak was the leader of the Jaguar Knights of Nexal .

History

In 1361 DR , Kalnak was chosen by Naltecona to lead an ambush against the Golden Legion at Palul . Following the plan, Kalnak welcomed the Golden Legion to Palul and offering a great party, while secretly his elite army waited hidden. However, the mage Darien discovered the plan thanks to her magic and informed Cordell , who was at Kalnak's side. Quickly Cordell surprised and killed Kalnak.
