Jhoaz attained his seat on the Council when his father, the esteemed Elephstron , died in the Year of Strife, 753 DR . He was also given the title of Lord of the Keep at this time. Jhoaz gained his status from the direct support of Hamastarin , a wealthy and influential Sembian merchant, and the Council agreed to his appointment as they believed he would be easy to control.
