TypeTool, weapon

Scalpels were sharp medicinal tools designed to cut the flesh open, used by doctors and morticians of the Realms and, occasionally, wielded as weapons.

Description

Usually, scalpers were thin and extremely sharp knives made of metal. However, scalpels made of obsidian were used in some locations. In the ancient city of Cursrah of the Calim Empire , obsidian scalpels were a common tool of a healer and torturer, alongside other tools, such as forceps , needles , bone-saws , and retractors .
