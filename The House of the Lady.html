The House of the Lady was a temple of Tymora in Shadowdale . This was the oldest temple in the area, built upon the site of an earlier temple of Tyche .

Inhabitants

Preceptress Eressea Ambergyles led Tymora's faithful within the temple. She was assisted by 21 other priests and 28 followers.

History

During the Time of Troubles in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , the temple was pillaged and vandalized by devotees of the god Bane . Its clergy members were slaughtered and their bodies were littered across its inner-most sanctum, while the black hand of Bane's holy symbol was painted on the wall in their blood.
