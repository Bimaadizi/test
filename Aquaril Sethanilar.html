Aquaril Sethanilar was the Delphine Regent of the Istishian faith.

Description

Sethanilar was a young aquatic elf who lived in great secrecy in the Castle of the Dancing Dolphin . He was protected by numerous guardians of both typical and monstrous nature.

History

Some time in the mid–14 th century, the firstborn of a descendent of the Grand Oracle Seldeetha Darinaalis ascended to the position of Delphine Regent.
