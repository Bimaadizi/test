Jedren Hiller was a the Bailiff of the Wide in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

He was a wholly corrupt man who fully expected to be offered bribes, which he readily accepted. Many people in the city saw him as a blight on the marketplace, who took advantage of poor and distressed peddlers and merchants.

Activities

While he carried out his normal duties as bailiff, Hiller gave preferential market space to nobles of the Upper City , and those who offered more generous inducements.
