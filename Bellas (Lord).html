Lord Bellas was an inhabitant of Cormanthyr by the 3 rd century DR , and a member of House Bellas .

History

On the year 241 DR , Lord Tassarion was part of a covert meeting against the Coronal Eltargrim . He had concerns about the execution of the Coronal, and who'd do it. His heir had been slain in the hunt of Elminster Aumar .
