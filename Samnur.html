Samnur was a small coastal village in Chessenta during the 15 th century DR .

Geography

Samnur lay on the eastern coast of the Bay of Chessenta , between Heptios and Mordulkin .

History

Not long after the Wailing Years , early in the 1400s DR, the Chessentan war hero Ishual Karanok fought his first battle against the Abolethic Sovereignty at Samnur. He won and forced them back into the water.

Inhabitants

It was home to some 1,300 people around 1479 DR .

Notable Locations

On a hill looking over the village was a granite monument dedicated to Ishual Karanok's victory over the Abolethic Sovereignty. Samnur also had a hidden temple to Umberlee that could only be reached a secret grate in the lagoon floor.
