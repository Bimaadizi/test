Oakvale was a small forest located on the isle of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Oakvale sat along the southern shore of the Myrloch within the Myrloch Vale of central Gwynneth. The forest lay due west of the Fens of the Fallen . The forest was surrounded to the south by the Highlands mountains.
