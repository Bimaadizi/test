Tooktook was a duergar living in Mithral Hall during the reign of Haerinvureem .

Relationships

While Mithral Hall was controlled by Clan Bukbukken , Tooktook was from Clan Trilk .

History

After the death of the shadow dragon , Tooktook was killed by Bruenor Battlehammer in the outer tunnels. Later, Bruenor used Tooktook's name to deceive two gray dwarves from Clan Mc Uduck .
