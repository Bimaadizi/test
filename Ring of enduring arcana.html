A ring of enduring arcana was a magical ring that rendered a spellcaster's spells more resistant to dispelling . They were favored by mages facing rivals of similar might.

Creation

To forge a ring of enduring arcana , a spellcaster must have dispel magic available.

Description

A ring of enduring arcana was a fine latticework of gold and mithral that covers most of the length of the wearer's finger, with a joint in the middle to allow movement. It appeared to shift and flow whenever the wearer cast a spell.

Activation

A ring of enduring arcana was permanently active for as long as it was worn. However, it did require 24 hours to acclimate to the wearer, starting when it was donned.
