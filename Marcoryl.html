Marcoryl was a druid who lived in the Emerald Grove during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

He spent much of his time in the grove's archives, surrounded by tomes and stone tablets that held wealths of information.

History

In Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , archdruid Halsin offered safe haven to a community of tieflings who were exiled from their home city of Elturel . When the tiefling child Arabella attempted to steal the idol of Silvanus from the grove's altar, the druids were split as to what should be done with the girl. Marcoryl was of the opinion the young tiefling should be held captive and made to pay for her crime.
