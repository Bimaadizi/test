Volo's Guide to the Realms was one of the numerous guidebooks penned by Volothamp Geddarm , available in the late 14 th century DR .

Contents

This Volo's Guide was an illustrated book with one of the images being a woman adorned in a traveler's clothes, armed with daggers , carrying numerous trinkets, scrolls , a spellbook, and a quill.

History

Volo's Guide to the Realms was published sometime before the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR . A copy of the book could be found in the village of West Harbor , among the tomes in the local human wizard Tarmas 's library.
