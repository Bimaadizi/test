Sayings of a Wyrm was a book penned by a green dragon Naurglaur in the late 9 th century DR .

History

Sayings of a Wyrm was published in the Year of the Spitting Cat, 895 DR . The original copy of the book was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
