Ardan Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the late 8 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty . He was the newphew of High King Declan and the cousin of his immediate predecessor, High King Ciaran . His successor was his son, Byron .

History

Ardan assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Aurumvorax, 773 DR , after his predecessor was killed.

He ruled for eleven years before he died of natural causes in the Year of the Gray Mists, 796 DR .
