Al-Qirmiza Islands was a large archipelago found in the Crowded Sea within west Zakhara .

Geography

Al-Qirmiza Islands were located 210 miles (340 kilometers) due south of Gana on the mainland. The archipelago was one of several clusters of island in the area including both Al-Tarik Islands and Al-Zulm Islands .

Sixteen individual islands were part of the archipelago. The only named island was also the largest by far– Dabab Island .
