The Spoon was a small strait found within the Pirate Isles on the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Geography

The Spoon was situated in the east and northeast region of the Pirate Isles. It ran along the southern edge of Alphar Isle and the eastern shore of Ilthan and bordering the Bowl to the south.

Description

The Spoon was was a narrow strait, especially when compared with the nearby Windrace . The sharp twists needed to safely navigate its waters usually meant the Spoon was not the first course choice for sailors in the area.
