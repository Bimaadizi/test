Jarl Orel "the Grayhand" was the elderly leader of the Yargray tribe of frost giants that dwelled within Tholvarr .

History

As of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Orel's daughter, Gerti Orelsdottr , had taken over his reign.
