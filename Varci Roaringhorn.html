Relationships

While in Baldur's gate, Varci was under the care of Lord Priest Tremain Belde'ar . He was friends with Tremain's son, Casson .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Varci and Casson snuck Water Queen's House to cause some mischief for the priestesses of Umberlee . Unfortunately, they were caught and Casson was killed for the transgression. His body was being kept by the the head priestess, Jalantha Mistmyr .

Troubled over the death of his friend, Varci went searching throughout the streets of Baldur's Gate for help to have him resurrected .
