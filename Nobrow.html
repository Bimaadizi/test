Nobrow was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the end of the Age of Discovery .

History

The arcanist developed two spells during their life, both involving fire . The spells they invented were fire charm in 3124 NY (−735 DR) and affect normal fires in 3130 NY (−729 DR) .
