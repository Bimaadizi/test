Grand Admiral Karl Doenuts was a mercenary admiral from the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars . Karl's allegiances could be bought in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
