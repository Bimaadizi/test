Duke Pryden Daggerford was the head–of–state of Daggerford during the mid–14 th century DR .

Possessions

Like the other dukes of Daggerford, Pryden wielded the duchy's sword of state, Lawflame .

Relationships

Duke Pryden had three children: his eldest son Merovy , a younger son Pwyll , and a daughter named Bronwyn .

The duke had a half-brother Sir Llewellyn Longhand , who served as his Master–at–Arms. He appointed Kelson Darktreader as his Master of the Hunt.

History

Following the death of the duke's son Merovy, Pryden recalled his son Pwyll from his adventuring life. He began to prepare Pwyll for the position of rulership. At this time, Bronwyn began studying magic in preparation to serve as Daggerford's future court wizard .

The duke himself died during the First Dragonspear War and Pwyll ascended as the leader of Daggerford.
