Reland Tymin was a lord in the Chondath in 1358 DR .

Activities

Reland ruled the city of Timindar .

History

Reland in his youth was a fierce warrior. He was chosen as lord in 1340 DR after the killing of his brother, the previous lord.

In 1358 DR, he was killed by Durissa Thornion 's agents.

Personality

Reland was a somber man with a serious approach to governing. In time, he had become lame. He was a fair ruler

Relationship

Reland was a good friend of Morissa Thornion .
