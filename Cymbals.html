Cymbals were a percussion instrument played in the continent of Zakhara . They were also used by rock gnomes and in certain Khazari monasteries .

Description

Cymbals consisted of two round thin plates made of brass , which were slammed together to produce sound. A pair of cymbals typically had a diameter of 18 inches (0.46 meters), weighed 4 pounds (1.8 kilograms) and costed 20 gold pieces . A smaller version called finger cymbals was also used in Zakhara.
