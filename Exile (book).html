Exile was a religious tome dedicated to Laduguer the Exile, a duergar deity, penned by the same author who wrote Gnomefather , sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Content

Exile was a work of bleak religious fervor, celebrating the god Laduguer, a slaver deity.

History

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , copies of Exile could be found in the city of Baldur's Gate . Some were sold by Nansi Gretta in her bookstore called The Bibliophile in the Lower City . A copy was stored on the bookshelves of the non-denominational shrine – Stormshore Tabernacle , among many other books dedicated to a large number of deities.
