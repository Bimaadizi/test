Brimskarda was a fire giant and the nagging wife of Duke Zalto in Ironslag sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Brimskarda supervised the kitchen staff composed of five smoke mephits , two ogres and twenty goblins .
