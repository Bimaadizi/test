Chad Leatherman was a harness maker in the city of Ravens Bluff , living on Iron Bell Street , in the 14 th century DR .

Activities

Chad built a variety of harnesses for beasts of burden to pull carts or wagons .

Relationships

Chad was good friends with Norge Greenback , who lived a few doors down from him and used a great deal of Chad's harnesses at his store .
