Venna Alumar was a waitress and bartender who worked for Darwell Kiriag at his establishment, the Lonesome Tankard Inn and Tavern in the town of Eveningstar in the 15 th century DR .

Description

Venna was a young human woman with a pale skin tone, dark blue eyes, and long black hair gathered in braids. She wore simple barmaid's garb with sandals .

History

In the mid-1480s DR, Venna could be seen welcoming guests and adventurers at the Lonesome Tankard and plying them with food and drink, whether they were dealing with the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar or going elsewhere in the region like the Haunted Halls .
