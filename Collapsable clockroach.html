Collapsable clockroaches were portable devices that transformed into a clockroach under the control of the item's owner.

Description

Collapsable clockroaches came in the shape of a strange-looking box that, when a button was pressed, unfolded into a moving and chattering clockroach.

Powers

Collapsable clockroaches were somewhat different from the larger constructs. They were smaller and designed with an ingenuous folding pattern traceable all over their bodies. This design made them tougher than regular clockroaches but also limited the item's use. Each collapsable clockroaches could only be activated twenty-five times.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , collapsable clockroaches were used in the jungles of Samarach by Ahri "Golemkin" who worked at an illicit logging camp surrounding the Lantan-Samarach portal .
