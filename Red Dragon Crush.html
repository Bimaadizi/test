Red Dragon Crush was a fine wine produced and distributed by the Wizard of Wines winery in Barovia .

Description

The vintage was the finest non-sparkling wine produced by the winery. It had a tantalizing flavor.

History

The wine was produced by the Martikov family of wereravens and distributed for free to all villages in the valley of Barovia, together with the lesser Purple Grapemash No. 3 wine. It was sold at the Blue Water Inn in Vallaki for 1 sp per 1 pint (0.47 liters).

In the late 15 th century DR , while the Heroes of Baldur's Gate were trapped in Barovia, they were given a bottle of Red Dragon Crush by Adrian Martikov as thanks after having saved him from an ambush in the Svalich Woods .

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , A Modest Guide to Sword Coast Wine sang praises to Red Dragon Crush's throat-slamming notes.
