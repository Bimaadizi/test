The Imperious Flame was a temple in Calimport dedicated to the worship of the god Kossuth .

Location

The Imperious Flame was located in Masal Drudach , part of the Bakkal Sabban of the Faiths Ward , in Calimport.

Structure

This building stood five stories tall.
