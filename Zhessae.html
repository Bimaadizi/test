Zhessae was a member of the Zhentarim in Mulmaster in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .

Relationships

Zhessae was a follower of Fzoul Chembryl .

History

As a trusted member of Fzoul's faction, Zhessae was peripherally involved in the hunt for Shandril Shessair .
