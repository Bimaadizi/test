Snapbone was a member of The Guild and a bodyguard of its elected bursar in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Snapbone, along with Elliot , was placed to guard the Guild's elected bursar, the masked man only known as Uktar . He continued to serve the bursar and the Guild through the Absolute crisis and the conflict with the dreaded Stone Lord , a new kingpin who secretly served the Absolute .
