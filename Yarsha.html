Yarsha was a high-ranking member of the Cult of the Black Earth at the Temple of the Black Earth in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Personality

Yarsha was a murderous individual, plotting to kill and replace Miraj Vizann .

History

In 1491 DR , Yarsha and her two bodyguards controlled the guardroom of the temple.
