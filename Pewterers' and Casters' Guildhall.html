The Pewterers' and Casters' Guildhall was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild of the same name .

Location

The Pewterers' and Casters' Guildhall was located along the High Road , in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep . Its back entrance stood along the Cat's Tail alleyway.

Structure

This guildhall stood one story tall.
