Amanther was an Avowed of Candlekeep in the late mid-14 th century DR .

Description

In a group comprised of him, Larth and three others, he was the oldest and tallest.

Abilities

He was trained in the ability to perceive the nature of a spell before it was cast, through observing the verbal, somatic, and material components required of it. He also was somehow able to see through spell-guises without anyone noticing.
