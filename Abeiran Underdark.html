The Abeiran Underdark was the equivalent of Toril 's Underdark on the world of Abeir . Other than the fact that it was inhabited by drow and mysterious monsters, nothing more was known about this region.

A portion of the Abeiran Underdark was transported to Toril alongside Laerakond during the Blue Breath of Change .
