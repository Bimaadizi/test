Potato scone was a common type of potato dish served in the city of Baldur's Gate , the Western Heartlands , and West Faerûn in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Potato scones were fried foods, satisfyingly crunchy on the outside and soft on the inside. They were usually served with a ramekin of dipping sauce and a scoop of beans .

History

As of the late 15 th century DR, potato scone platters were served at the Emerald Enclave in the Chionthar Valley , west of Elturel .
