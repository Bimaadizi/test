Personality

Raithspur was a regal individual and a skilled warrior.

He was renowned for his collection of powerful protective magic items .

History

During the Sembian Civil War in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Raithspur managed to maintain control of the streets of the city under orders of the Overmistress Mirabeta Selkirk .
