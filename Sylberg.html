Sylberg was a Netherese arcanist who taught at the Institute for Sorcerous Imagery in the enclave of Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Sylberg taught alongside the other six resident teachers, made up of Driander , Dillencraft , Galanti , Coletti , Maleville , and Holstmann .
