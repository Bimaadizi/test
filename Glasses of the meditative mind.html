The glasses of the meditative mind were a set of magical glasses found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Description

These glasses had peculiar diamond-shaped lenses.

Powers

These glasses instilled a deep soothing feeling over the wearer, assisting monks channel their ki . They also made the wearer slightly incorporeal , protecting them in combat, and let them be more tricksy and sneaky in how they approached fights.

History

The glasses came to light when the Cormanthor Elves searched the Burial Glen of Myth Drannor for Earynspieir Ongluth , fighting the Red Wizard forces of Vorinar Gall and Ezkir Taromor .
