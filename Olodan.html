Olodan was a member of the Shadow Druids who infiltrated the Emerald Grove during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Olodan had white hair worn in dreadlocks, and tattoos all across her body and face. She wore a simple leather harness and loincloth.

Activities

While in the Emerald Grove, Olodan took on the form of a large, gray rat .

Relationships

While spying upon the druids of the grove, Olodan worked alongside two others of her order, Pinta and Wren .

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Olodan's cover in the Emerald Grove was still secure. She closely followed her subordinate, Kagha , during the time that she temporarily gained control over the grove, in Master Halsin 's absence.
