Gulzindar Street was a cobblestone paved avenue in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep .

Locations

The right end of Gulzindar Street terminated on Haltovar Street . Up until the mid-14 th century DR , its left end terminated on the Lions road. By 1368 DR , that section of road was re-designated as part of Jelzar's Stride .

At its midsection, Gulzindar Street was fully intersected by Darselune Street . The road also acted as the northern terminus of Caldwell Street and the southern terminus of Seawatch Street .
