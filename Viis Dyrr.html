Viis Dyrr was a member of House Agrach Dyrr of Menzoberranzan in 1373 DR .

History

Viis graduated from Sorcere at unknown time.

During the siege of Dyrr's compound for their betrayal of Menzoberranzan during the Siege of Menzoberranzan , Viis was called back to his house together with all Dyrr's other wizards to help raise magical defenses.
