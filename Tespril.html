Tespril was an apprentice of the Zhentarim wizard Gathlarue in the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

She had large, dark eyes. Her hair was long, but after her fight with Shandril, her face became disfigured and one half of her head lost most of its hair.

Personality

She was a timid person, but not very loyal.

Activities

When in camp, she entertained the guards.

Possessions

She snatched a ring of teleportation from Gathlarue before their encounter with Shandril Shessair .

Relationships

She was younger than Mairara , her fellow apprentice under Gathlarue . Both apprentices were romantically involved with their master, and they had engaged in sadomasochism with her.

History

Tespril accompanied Gathlarue and Mairara at Marsember when they joined the search for spellfire , hunting down Shandril Shessair. She formed part of her master's retinue, as they nightly scried on their quarry. Before the attack on Irondrake Rock , their scrying had her convinced that they were going to be destroyed by Shandril's spellfire as they met in battle, and she stole a ring from her mistress when the latter became stunned as Mirt , who was traveling with Shandril at the time, destroyed a gargoyle to which Gathlarue had magically linked herself. Tespril froze during the fight with Shandril.

She survived the fight by using the ring of teleportation she'd stolen from Gathlarue, though she became badly scarred. Later, she was debriefed regarding the encounter in a meeting with Fzoul Chembryl and Sarhthor of the Zhentarim . The mage slew her at the close of the meeting, on Fzoul's ambiguous direction.
