The Siren's Yearning was a powerful song that became imbued with magic if performed by a bard .

Content

When played, the song affected the performer's opponents in a similar way as a siren 's charm . Those who succumbed to the song's magics remained enthralled for ten minutes or until they suffered harm.

History

By the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , The Siren's Yearning was in the repertoire of some of the most experienced bards. It was heard being performed in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale and the village of Kuldahar .
