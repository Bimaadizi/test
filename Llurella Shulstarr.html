Llurella Shulstarr was an evil adventurer in Myth Drannor in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .

Activities

Llurella was a member of the Masked Marauders .

Personality

Llurella was an emotionless killer and an able dancer, singer and acrobat.

Relationship

Llurella traveled with her brother Irlon Shulstarr .
