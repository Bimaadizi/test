Cavil Zaltobar was a mercenary wizard in the Dessarin Valley during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

In 1491 DR , hired by Drannin Splitshield , Cavil and some minions ambushed some adventurers and Gargosh Blusterhelm (in truth a doppelganger posing as him) at the Stone Bridge . The adventurers defeated Cavil and his minions.
