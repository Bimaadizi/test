The boots of uncanny mending were a pair of boots found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The boots had space for holding small artisan's tools in their sides, and together they gave the wearer an exceptional aptitude for mending, building, and repairing.

History

The boots ended up in Myth Drannor's Temple to Labelas Enoreth , where they may have been party to some divinely-inspired time travel before being found by the Cormanthor Elves during the Elven Crusade .
