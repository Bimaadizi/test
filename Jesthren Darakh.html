Jesthren Darakh was the first Nentyarch of the Darakh Dynasty to rule Narfell .

Activities

In −399 DR , Jesthren slew the previous Nentyarch, Orlathaun of the Orgolath Dynasty , and claimed the Crown of Narfell for himself, establishing the ruling line what would reign until Narfell's destruction during the Great Conflagration in −150 DR . Jesthren ruled for many years until −322 DR .

Relationships

Jesthren was the half-fiend son of the human woman Larnaeril and the demon lord Orcus .
