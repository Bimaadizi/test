The Koalinth was a ship captained by the hobgoblin Klarz in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the years following the War of the Silver Marches , circa the Year of the Iron Dwarf's Vengeance, 1485 DR , Klarz sailed the Koalinth in search of King Hekaton and the Morkoth . He was hired by the druids of the Emerald Enclave .

Inhabitants

Klarz kept a crew of twenty hobgoblins and five bugbears aboard the Koalinth .
