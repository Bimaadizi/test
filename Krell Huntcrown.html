Lord Krell Huntcrown was the head of the noble Huntcrown family of Cormyr in the 14 th century DR .

Lord Krell was a short-tempered man, and opposed the acceptance of adventuring companies in Cormyr, and conspired to discredit them. He was a known schemer in high-society.

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Lord Krell attended King Azoun Obarskyr IV 's birthday feast, hosted by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Keep . He was accompanied there by Lady Ohrmatha Huntcrown . He used the opportunity to have a spy poke around the Manor and look for an embarrassing scandal that could be used against Lord Thistle, the Registrant General of Cormyr.
