Eldath's Patch was a vacant lot in Neverwinter as of 1370Â DR .

Description

It was the last vacant lot in Neverwinter's Silverlade Street . There were blueleaf trees growing on it, and it was bordered on its north end by the walls of the Melaraunt family gardens. There was a marker on the lot showing it was consecrated to Eldath .

History

Eldath's Patch had a refuge added upon it during the reign of Magister Maldiglas Turntower at its north end, where the blueleaf trees grew thickest. By the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , it was so popular with young lovers (and some nobility) that dogs had been banned from it, after some incidents.
