The shield of Egons was a magical shield that was associated with the Lathanderite Egon family.

Powers

The shield of Egons possessed a +3 enchantment and improved an individual's ability to turn undead creatures.

History

At some point, the shield came into the possession of man who later came to be the revenant known as Fanegonorom .
