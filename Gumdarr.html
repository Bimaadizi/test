Gumdar was a tortle that lived on the Snout of Omgar long before the era of the Second Sundering .

History

At some point, Gumdar was standing atop a rocky crag on the Snout of Omgar when he heard a ship crash on the shore. He blew a sounding horn to alert his fellow tortles. Together they all pulled out the survivors of the crash.

Later on, this moment was immortalized by the creation of the High Horn statue.
