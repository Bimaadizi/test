The Five Fine Fish was a tavern located in the town of Immersea in Cormyr during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The tavern was located on the eastern side of Immersea near the shores of the Wyvernwater . It sat on the northwest corner of Calantar's Way opposite a statue of Azoun Obarskyr III .
