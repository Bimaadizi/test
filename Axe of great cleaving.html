The axe of great cleaving was a unique enchanted battleaxe crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Tommus Tallstep in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

Silverhand designed the axe to complement Tommus' gift for strategy and his tactics for battling large groups single-handedly.

Powers

As the name suggested, the axe of great cleaving was capable of unleashing a massive cleave that allowed the diminutive Tommus to strike many foes at once.
