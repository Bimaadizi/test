Pachipol was the mayor of Amah in the Vilhon Reach in 1358 DR .

Activities

Pachipol ruled Amah, one of the Free Cities of the Vilhon Reach .

History

In 1358 DR, Pachipol participated in a meeting of leaders of both Chondath and Turmish to discuss the menace of the arcanaloth Yrkhetep . He was choosen by lottery to be spokesperson for the group. He exposed the threat, with the help of the sage Inata Eller , to an adventuring party that had ruined some of Yrkhetep's previous plots. He asked them to go to Hades and lead the Iron Legion for the last battle with Yrkhetep.

Personality

Pachipol appeared to be a passionate individual, caring for his land.
