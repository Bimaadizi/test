Waters of devotion was a potion that could be found in Zakhara , the Land of Fate.

Powers

The imbiber of this potion was placed under the control of the person who gave it to them. The imbiber's mental capacities were still intact, but they remained loyal to the giver. The potion's effects lasted for one month, but could be dispelled by magical means at any time.
