Beorg was the king of the Tribe of the Wolf .

History

Beorg was the strongest Reghedman warrior and the most powerful king alongside Heafstaag . He allied with Heafstaag and the traitor De Bernezan to unite all the tribes in an attack against the Ten Towns in 1351 DR .

However, thanks to the scouting of Drizzt Do'Urden , the men of Icewind Dale were ready for the battle. Beorg was killed in the fighting. His tribe suffered the most deaths and soon lost much of its influence, becoming a minor tribe.
