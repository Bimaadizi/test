Sylyndyrryl Moonspinner was an elf of the Misty Forest that led elven forces in the Second Dragonspear War in the Year of the Wyvern, 1363 DR .

Description

She was exquisitely beautiful and svelte, almost to the point of appearing weak.

History

Sylyndyrryl was chosen to lead two contingents during the Second Dragonspear War. She led 50 elven soldiers that hailed from the Misty Forest and another 50 archers from the Laughing Hollow . Together they fought in the final battle of the conflict, at Dragonspear Castle .
