Ulvaryl was a vampire member of Bodhi 's coven in Athkatla , active circa 1369 DR .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , assassins of the Shadow Thieves guild attacked the archmage Jon Irenicus 's dungeon underneath Athkatla. The archmage's vampire allies helped to release the assault. Ulvaryl was among the members of the coven that fought against the Shadow Thieves. She came face to face with Abdel Adrian as he was fleeing from the mage's dungeon. Ulvaryl survived the encounter but taking on her bat or gaseous form .
