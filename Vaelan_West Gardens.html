West Gardens was a tenement district near Kazrim's Plunge in Vaelan , Durpar . Inhabitants of the upscale district paid into a common fund that financed spellcasting and sword-slinging guards. Eined Datharathi lived in a tenement there up until her death on the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR .
