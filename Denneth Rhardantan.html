Denneth Rhardantan was a man from Mistledale in the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

Storm Silverhand claimed he had many fast horses.

Relationships

As of Mirtul in 1479 DR, he owed Storm Silverhand much money.

History

In Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Storm Silverhand lured out a number of highknights through a portal to Shadowdale . She convinced their leader, Eskrel Starbridge , to trade a teleport ring in his keeping, originally belonging to Queen Filfaeril Obarskyr , for the name of a man who owed her much coin and had enough fast horses for his entire troop to make it back to Cormyr . The man's name was Denneth Rhardantan; she also yielded a word, "glimmerdeep", which would make him give them away for free to whoever asked.
