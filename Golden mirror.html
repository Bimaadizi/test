Golden mirrors were magic items that adorned each major stronghold of the Brotherhood of the True Flame .

Description

These mirrors were the same size as a large circular shield. The glass was encircled in a golden frame.

Powers

Each golden mirror was linked with all of the others of its kind, allowing for instant communication between strongholds. Communication was achieved similar to a crystal ball with clairaudience properties.

Rumors & Legends

Nar-Aidiya , the Bonfire, owned a special golden mirror that allowed him to appear wreathed in flames to anyone using a regular golden mirror . The Bonfire's mirror also functioned the same as a mirror of mental prowess , allowing him to further dominate others.
