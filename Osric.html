Osric was a half-elf and member of Zhentil Keep 's wizard 's guild in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

History

He lived in Darkhold before coming to Zhentil Keep.

Rumors & Legends

Osric was actually a polymorphed osyluth who served as a spy for Lord Orgauth . He was tasked with monitoring the Red Wizards who had infiltrated the guild.
