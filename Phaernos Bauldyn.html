Phaernos Bauldyn was the innkeeper of the Ambletrees Arms inn during the 3 rd century DR .

Relationships

He was friends with Braer .

History

One evening in Flamerule of the Year of Bloodflowers, 234 DR , Phaernos Bauldyn was standing at the front door of his inn when the priestess Elmara walked into his establishment, requesting shelter and warmth. She offered to pay with spells . Later, during the night, he woke her up, and she introduced herself. He revealed that his friend Braer had told him to shelter Elmara, should she ever come by.
