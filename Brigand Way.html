Brigand Way was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Brigand Way was located in the Common Quarter in the southwest corner of Zhentil Keep. The road originated on Fire Way in the north and passed south through the quarter to end at Border Street near the North Force Gate . Along its path Brigand Way was intersected by Canter Street , Pride Street , Crypt Street , and Merchant Street .
