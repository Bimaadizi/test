Railur's Red was a type of red ale found in the eastern coastal areas of Cormyr and Sembia .

Description

Railur's Red was referred to as "red ale" but in reality it was amber in color. The libation's recipe was prepared using thrice-roasted barley , citrus fruit rinds, and a collection of spices .
