The Sylph language was the racial language of sylphs , a type of fey , that was noted for having a musical quality to it.

Speakers

Beyond sylphs themselves, Sylph was a common secondary language among some planewalkers and druids devoted to certain nature deities. These included the druids of Chauntea , druids of Eldath , druids and druid/ rangers of Mielikki , the druids of Silvanus , and druids of Shiallia . Other forms of specialty priests in the Realms for whom Sylph was a commonly known secondary language included the Eldathyn peacemen and the Shiallian woodwives .
