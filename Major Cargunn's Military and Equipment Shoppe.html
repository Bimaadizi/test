Major Cargunn's Military and Equipment Shoppe was a general store in the southern quarter of Eshpurta run by Cargunn , a retired member of the Shield Militia .

Location

The Shoppe was located two doors north of the Hamstrung Giant .

Services

The Shoppe carried all manner of adventuring gear, weapons , armor , and supplies up to a top price of 500 gp; from sturdy clothing to bronze plate mail , from knives to greatswords , bows and pole arms of all varieties, camping equipment, tack and harnesses , and even a few musical instruments .
