Helicon Avamere was an undead skeleton mage that walked in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Abilities

Helicon knew spells including ball lightning , cone of cold , hold monster , polar ray , rend the soul , and waves of fatigue .

History

Helicon lurked among the graves outside the Polyandrium of Myth Drannor during the time of the Elven Crusade in 1374 DR .
