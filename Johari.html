Johari was a half-orc resident of Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Johari was proud to call Neverwinter her home and naturally felt she and her kind should be afforded the same rights and trust as its other citizens.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Johari tried to enter the Tower District in order to help in the city's fight against the occupying Many-Arrows orcs . However, Patrol Captain Lanward of the Neverwinter Guard was preventing half-orcs passing the Fallen Tower area out of fear they would join the other side. Johari was incensed by this and the two argued. She sought out a half-orc adventurer and Guard recruit to speak to Lanward and allow their kind into the rest of the district.
