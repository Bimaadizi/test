Fenghsintzu was a large river in T'u Lung . It was called by many names including the Great Snake , Water Father , the Liquid Spirit , and Heart-road .

Geography

The source of this river was located west of Durkon . A 1,000 miles (1,600,000 meters) south of Durkon, the Rendah River flowed into it.

Flora & Fauna

Animals that were commonly found within the waters of this river included crocodiles and water buffalo . In the far southern end of this river, one could find large giant water serpents that were liable to sink barges.

History

The main temple of the Black Leopard Cult stood at the mouth of the Fenghsintzu until Kozakuran Year −757 (−831 DR) , when it was destroyed by the army of General Wo Can of Shou Lung .

In the 14 th century DR , naval officers of T'u Lung frequently sent expeditions up this river to its source.
