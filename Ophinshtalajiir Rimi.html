Ophinshtalajiir Rimi was a dragonborn member of Clan Ophinshtalajiir , and a member of the Lance Defenders in 1487 DR .

History

Rimi was one of the Lance Defenders patrolling the Catacombs of Djerad Thymar , when a maurezhi servant of the god -king Gilgeam attacked and killed the members of the Liberators of the Steel Sky , on Nightal 15 1486 DR . Rimi was attacked as well, and though she suffered grave injuries, she survived.
