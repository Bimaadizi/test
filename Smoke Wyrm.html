The Smoke Wyrm was a tavern in Ravens Bluff in the Vast run by the dwarf Tharzon Brewhammer and his son Kurzen Brewhammer in the late 15 th century DR . The tavern was best known for its excellent stout known as Old Smokey , which was shipped all over the Vast .

Structure

The tavern had a sign that depicted a sleeping dragon blowing smoke out of his nose. A storeroom had a secret entrance to a location in the sewers close to the entrance to Sarbreen .
