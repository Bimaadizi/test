Halvor II was a human fighter and king of a sizable realm in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He had great plans of seizing all lands between the eastern and western seas, and saw himself as divinely favoured in his massive ambition.

Possessions

He wore a ring of multiple wishes , sets of plate mail , and bracers of defense while upon his head there rested a helm of brilliance . He wielded either a longsword of Defending or a rod of lordly might , alongside a shield in battle. He also had in his possession a potion of superheroism .

Relationships

Duncan was his rambunctious cousin who travelled the land alongside the kingdoms champion the paladin Gabriel . Ambrose was the man Halvor had put in charge of training up his armies for conquest, and another member of Gabriel's band. Baren the wizard Halvor had put in charge of gathering and bending all possible magic that could be used for his ambitions.
