Ahtayir was a djinni in service to the Cult of the Howling Hatred at the Temple of Howling Hatred in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

In the past, Torhild Flametongue , king of the Besilmer dwarves , found a horn capable of summoning Ahtayir. Torhild used the djinni's services to build part of his royal palace.

In 1491 DR , Aerisi Kalinoth found the horn and summoned Ahtayir once again. She ordered him to rebuild all of Tyar-Besil to its former glory.
