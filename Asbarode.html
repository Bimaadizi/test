Asbarode , also known as Nith the Entertainer , was a member of the Zhentarim in Darkhold in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .

Personality

She was unquestionably loyal to Ashemmi .

History

Asbarode was the head of of the largest Zhent spy network under the alias Nith the Entertainer, and her minions wandered around posing as an itinerant circus known as the Band of Rogues .
