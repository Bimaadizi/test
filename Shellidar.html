Vanguard Shellidar was a human crusader of Torm and one of the three leaders of the Janessar , an organization that opposed slavery and protected travelers from bandits in Calimshan , in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Despite his great age, he still had a bear-like figure, being tall and looming.

History

Shellidar hailed from the town of Easting in the Western Heartlands , but came to Calimshan some time around 1330 DR and never left, which proved his commitment to the cause of the Janessar.

Activities

By 1370 DR , being too old to fight or ride, Shellidar instead helped lead the Janessar as a Speaker from the Tower of the Three in the Khanall enclave in the Marching Mountains . He also focused on training the Janessar in weaponsmithing and combat.
