Trent Vendall was a visitor to Neverwinter in the 1370s DR .

History

Trent came from Cormyr and went to Neverwinter, despite bandits attacking the caravans. He managed to arrive in Neverwinter. He offered the Kalach-Cha some alcohol, if he worked with the Watchmen.
