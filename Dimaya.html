Dimaya was a prodigal under- priestess of Lolth from Sschindylryn during the 15 th century DR .

Description

Dimaya wore an armored top and long skirt, and wore her long hair in a ponytail.

Abilities

As an aspiring priestess with great potential, Dimaya could cast chaos hammer , fear , cometfall , divine punishment , hold monster , poison , symbol of pain , and unholy blight .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Dimaya trained with a superior priestess of Lolth, praying and sacrificing at a shrine near the external portal to Sschindylryn, between the Cave of the Elements and Boneyard .
