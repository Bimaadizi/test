Mal Tarbat was a small lake found on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Mal Tarbat lay in the eastern region of Myrloch Vale in the northern foothills of the Cambro Mountains near the Cambrent Gap . Much of the lake bordered an unnamed forest to the south and west.
