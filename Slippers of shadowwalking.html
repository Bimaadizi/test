Slippers of shadowwalking was a magic item that allowed the wearer to shadow walk .

Description

The slippers were soft and black.

Powers

The wearer could use the shadow walk ability once per day.

History

Slippers of shadowwalking were first created by Marune who later shared their creation process with the Shadow Thieves of Amn .
