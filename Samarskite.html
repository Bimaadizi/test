Samarskite was a semi-precious gem used for mourning jewelry or to adorn black ceremonial dress.

Description

Samarskite was a rare-earth mineral, hard and dense, with a velvet-black metallic luster, usually cut cabochon . A typical specimen had a base value of 50 gp.

Powers

One or more samarskite gems, carried or worn on the body, was known to protect against undead by reducing the severity of their attacks by a small amount.

Trivia

The lesser deity Vhaeraun was known to occasionally send samarskites to show his favour or disfavour to his followers.
