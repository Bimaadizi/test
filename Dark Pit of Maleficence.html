The Dark Pit of Maleficence was a subterranean fortress and temple dedicated to Gargauth , the Hidden Lord.

Location

The fortress was constructed within the Landrise at the supposed location of Gargauth's first appearance on Toril within the ruins of Peleveran fifty years after the city's destruction.

Description

The Dark Pit sat along the floor of a large cavern that served as a granary for Peleveran. The building was intertwined with vast stalactites and stalagmites. The structure derived its name from the massive pit found along the floor of the cavern that was rumored to connect to Baator .

Inhabitants

The temple and grounds were maintained by a hundred clergy and over 250 followers.

Defenses

Many natives of the region fell to Gargauth's dark influence, serving as scouts for anything occurring near the Dark Pit of Maleficence.
