The Pickled Fisherman was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tavern stood two stories tall.

Location

The Pickled Fisherman was located in the west section Waterdeep's Dock Ward on the south side of Sail Street .
