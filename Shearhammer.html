The Shearhammer was a magical warhammer .

Description

The magic warhammer had a +3 enchantment.

History

In 1358 DR , a great beholder terrorized Fireshear . The Shearmaster , who was one of the three leaders of Fireshear, was in possession of the weapon. He planned to bestow the warhammer onto the heroes that killed the aberrant creature.
