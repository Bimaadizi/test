Vilhon Blanc was a cheese from Sembia . It was rarely found outside the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Description

It was made by mixing in mild wine during initial straining, which gave it a light reddish color and a delicate taste, especially as compared to Turmish brick , which was made using similar processes but a heavier wine.

Prices

As of 1368 DR , Vilhon Blanc was supplied by the Thousand-Heads Trading Coster . It was around six silver pieces for a 450 g (1 lb) "loaf".
