Basil of Lyndusfarne was an unusual verbeeg in the service of the Kingdom of Hartsvale in the 1370s DR . He was a runecaster , a rarity among his people.

Description

Basil was more than eight feet tall, with gangly arms, stooped shoulders, and thick bowed legs. He had a large belly. His eyebrows and scrawny beard were coarse and gray.

History

Basil met and befriended Tavis Burdun , just moments before the princess Brianna was kidnapped. He and the orphan Avner accompanied Tavis in the rescue mission. In time, he became a good friend of both Tavis and Brianna. Unfortunately during the final battle against the Twilight Spirit , Basil's body was permanently deformed.
