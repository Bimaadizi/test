The Trail Lords were a merchant company in Faerûn .

History

In the mid–14 th century DR , this mysterious merchant group was known to hire half-orcs , and other unsavory races, as caravan guards. The group was rumored to be composed of wealthy merchant-kings who fell under the control of Thay .
