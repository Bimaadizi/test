Karissa's goggles were the personal goggles of Karissa the Knife , Master Thief of the Night Brigade in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

Her goggles provided wide-ranging assistance to assassinating, feinting, and other combat trickery for aspiring tunnel rats and sneaky scamps.

History

The goggles were used by Karissa until she was slain by the vengeful Riverside Rats gang.
