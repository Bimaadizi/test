Siculus was a powerful Netherese arcanist who taught at the Academy of Mentalist Study in Eileanar shortly before its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Siculus was a master illusionist , and taught the subject at the Academy under the supervision of Dean Zucchi .
