Pagnola was a healer member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Pagnola was a gold dwarven woman with a fair skin, clean-shaven face, and brown eyes. She wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist: a helmet , a set of splint mail , and a tabard adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. She wielded a mace and shield in battle.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Healer Pagnola was stationed in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, where she patrolled the streets near the Blushing Mermaid .
