Ironpost Street was a lesser avenue, paved in corduroy-logs, in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This street ran from east to west, running roughly parallel to the more southerly Burnt Wagon Way , coming off of Winter Path and terminating on Zendulth Street .
