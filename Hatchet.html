Hatchets were tools sometimes used by rangers or woodsmen throughout Faerûn .

Description

A hatchet resembled a small axe with a blade on one side of the metal head and a flattened stub on the other. A typical hatchet weighed 3 pounds (1.4 kilograms).

History

Hatchets were versatile tools used for chopping and hammering wood. They could also be used as weapons.

Hatchets were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 2 gold pieces and included a leather case.
