Frostbite was a white dragon wyrmling that inhabited caves within the Wyrm's Tooth glacier atop the Spine of the World in the late 13 th century DR .

Personality

Befitting his youth and the savagery of his race, Frostbite considered both humans and demihumans to be nothing more than food and felt no remorse towards eating them. Whenever he spoke Common it came out in a very broken form.

Possessions

Frostbite held a gemstone in his possession.

History

Some time in 1281 DR , the frost giant Joril Frostbeard convinced Frostbite and the rest of his pack of white wyrms to feign death and then kill his unsuspecting father Kreg Frostbeard so that he could assume control over his tribe. In return for their cooperation, Joril regularly fed them the human and demihuman slaves that his tribe shared with the glacier's intelligent salamanders , much to the chagrin of some members of his tribe.

Relationships

Frostbite and his pack of white wyrms lived in a tense sort of peace alongside the frost giants within Wyrm's Tooth glacier.
