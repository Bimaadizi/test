Eudhis Bhamm was a human male herbalist and the proprietor of Bhamm's Herbs in Eshpurta , Amn , in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR through at least early 1370 DR, the Year of the Tankard . He had a surly disposition and was quick on the rejoinder. He was usually nervous and with good reason: he was a wizard in a land that had nearly outlawed wizardry.
