Cloves were a type of spice in the Realms noted for their aromatic nature.

Availability

Price

The price of cloves somewhat varied in Faerûn. Whilst Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold cloves for use by priests at a rate of 1 silver piece per 4 ounces (110 grams), the cloves sold for culinary use under "Aurora's Larder" were priced at 35 gp per 1 ounce (28 grams).

Over in the land of Zakhara, a 150 pounds (68,000 grams) chest of of cloves would be valued at 400 gold pieces.
