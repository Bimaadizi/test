Lady Whitburn was the high elven wife of Lord Whitburn , a member of a patriar family of Baldur's Gate in the 15 th century DR .

Description

Lady Whitburn was a pale-skinned woman with delicate elfin features, straight blond hair, and icy blue eyes. She could often be seen wearing embroidered finery, such as tailored jackets, undercoats, and trousers.

History

Lady Whitburn married into the Whitburn patriar family sometime before the late 15 th century DR.
