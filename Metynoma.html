History

In the Year of the Spur, 1348 DR , on Flamerule 15 , near Bloodstone Pass , the warlock Titus offered to the young tiefling Bysera to make a warlock pact and summoned Metynoma as her patron. Despite some reticence from Bysera, the pact was forged.

Relationships

Metynoma was a follower of the demon lord Graz'zt . She was an old acquaintance of Titus.
