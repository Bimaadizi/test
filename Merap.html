Merap "Dead-eyed" was a member of the Company of the Anvil adventuring company in 1370 DR .

Description

Merap received his nickname "Dead-eyes" from his ugly appearance. His head was bald.

Personality

Merap smiled a lot but always with a cold emotionless smile.

Possessions

Merap wore throwing daggers on his belt.

Relationships

Merap was the brother of Maera , the second in command of the Company.

History

Merap and his sister were old allies of Bramas Thorntree , like all in the Company of the Anvil.

In 1370 DR, he joined the Company, and together they recovered the Sondarr Scepter from a long-forgotten dwarven tomb in the Starspire Mountains . They had been ordered to maintain a low profile and, on their return journey on Uktar 10 , stopped at the Blue Badger Inn in the village of Toralth for drink.

Later that night, three dwarves , Bapar Ghalmrin , Odak Truesteel , and Soram Battlebellow , entered the tavern seeking the Sondarr Scepter . However, the dwarves accidentally engaged in a brawl with the Shields of the Highland adventuring company. Merap joined in the brawl. Bramas used the opportunity to escape with the scepter , but the dwarves eventually caught up with him and recovered it.
