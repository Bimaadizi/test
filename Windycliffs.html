Windycliffs was a small merchant town located north of the city of Neverwinter along the High Road .

Description

Windycliffs was a humble and safe caravan stop town, named after powerful winds that battered the town at the base of a small cliff.

Trade

Most inhabitants of Windycliffs were merchants and traders. Travelers that ventured from the town of Port Llast often used Windycliffs as a safe stop long the High Road on their way down south. The town had several inns, general, arms, and weapons stores, as well as a few jewelers.

Defenses

Due to the proximity to Neverwinter, Windycliffs was regularly patrolled by Lord Nasher Alagondar 's private guard, granting travelers and goodly adventurers their protection.

History

In the mid-14 th century DR , a usually safe town became menaced by fierce monsters. The village's Lord-Mayor suspected that Luskan 's dreaded tyrant pirate captains were responsible for the unrest. The town of Windycliffs formally requested aid in handling the monsters from Neverwinter's Lord Nasher.
