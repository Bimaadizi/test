Dragongleam was an enchanted spear that remained in possession of the Cult of the Dragon as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The spear had the phrase "Tiamat's eyes shine" inscribed upon its crossguard in the Iokharic script.

Powers

Dragongleam was empowered with ten charges of the spell daylight .

History

As of the 1480s DR , it could be found within the cult's hunting lodge in the Greypeak Mountains .
