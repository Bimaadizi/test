Mazti was a secluded Payitlan town on the shores of the Gulf of Cordell .

Description

Mazti was a minor town with population somewhere between 5,000 and 15,000 Mazticans . The town stood to the west of Ulatos , encircled by the thick Bomak Jungle . Mazti's closest western neighbor was the Pezelac town of Palenqi .
