The Dancing Court was a cul-de-sac that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Dancing Court was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . It branched northeast off of the southern end of Slop Street .

Activities

A tavern and festhall called the Jade Dancer sat at the end of the Dancing Court.

History

The Dancing Court was famous for the Moon Sphere , a magical radiance that appeared on nights of the full moon.
