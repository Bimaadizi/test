History

While living in Icewind Dale , Buster crafted Drizzt's mithral chainmail . In the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Buster worked on several pieces of armor for the Companions of the Hall , including Regis . Around 1358 DR , Buster mended the figurine of Guenhwyvar after Catti-brie cut it into two pieces with her sword, Khazid'hea .
