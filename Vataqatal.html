Vataqatal was the Zakharan war god. He wanted his followers to grow by testing their strength of body and will and preached the importance of duty. He was a local god worshiped mainly in the northern parts of the Land of Fate that bordered on foreign lands.

Vataqal's symbol was a red-bladed scimitar.

The Warrior-Slave had been described as a muscular, veiled warrior with glowing red eyes who wore robes and battle-worn lamellar armor and bore a great scimitar.

He was worshiped by warriors, especially mamluks and farisans who valued duty and dedication to one's service.

The largest temple dedicated to Vataqal was the Mosque of Blood, in the city of Qudra.
