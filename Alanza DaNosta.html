Alanza Da Nosta was a member of the Golden Legion .

Personality

Da Nosta was well known for his greed. He plotted to gain such a large amount of gold that nobody would notice the trifling amount that he diverted for himself.

Relationships

Ironspike secretly worked for him.

History

Da Nosta was appointed as commander of the garrison of Fort Cordell by the Amnian government. He spent most of his time cataloguing all the mistakes he felt had been made by Governor Cordell , especially his leniency to the Payit .

In 1362 DR , he tried to convince the adventuring party hired by Drakosa to find the Cloak of One Plume to instead give it to him after recovered it.
