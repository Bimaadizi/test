Duladora Trameldor was a half-elf in Sildëyuir in 1372 DR .

History

Duladora lived in the Yuirwood with her father Berask Trameldor . In 1372 DR, she was near the portal to the Night Realm so the nilshai Tolg'byri ordered its mercenaries to kidnap Duladora with the objective of using her star elf heritage to unlock the mysteries of Sildëyuir. The yochlol Savera instead planned to turn her in a drider under her orders.

Personality

Duladora was totally scared by Tolg'byri and only wanted to go back home.
