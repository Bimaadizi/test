Hsi Yog was a Tuigan shugenja and member of the Grand Army of the Tuigan during the mid 14 th century DR .

Activities

Hsi used his clerical powers to aid the soldiers of the army under Prince Hubadai Khahan 's command.

Abilities

The following spells were available to Hsi: deflection , cure light wounds , snake charm , hold person , levitate , snake barrier , detect lie , and possess .

Possessions

Hsi owned a pair of bracers of defense and a magical tetsubo .
