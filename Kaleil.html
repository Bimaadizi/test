Kaleil was a druid of Maiden's Glade near the Mere of Dead Men on the Sword Coast North .

History

In Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Kaleil and the animals dwelling in Maiden's Glade were corrupted and driven mad by a sinister presence in the Mere of Dead Men. He shapechanged himself into a bear , but was not able to change back. Traveling with the druid Elanee , the Kalach-Cha encountered Kaleil in Maiden's Glade and had no choice but to kill him.
