Huge spiders were a common variety of monstrous spiders in the Realms.

Description

These arachnids were typically bigger than mundane or large spiders , but smaller in size compared to giant spiders .

Behavior

Like any arachnid, huge spiders were aggressive creatures.

Abilities

Unlike other arachnids, huge spiders did not spin webs. They could leap up to 30 ft (9.1 m) into the air and delivered poison to victims through their fangs.

Combat

Some species of huge spider roved around in search of prey, such as the giant wolf spider. Other species, such as the trapdoor spider, would carefully construct camouflaged tunnels and holes to hide in until prey passed by.

History

During Halaster's Higharvestide , huge spiders were occasionally encountered in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep .

Around 1368 DR , House Phylund was experimenting with a variety of huge spider species.

Species

Ecology

Diet

These arachnids were carnivorous hunters.

Habitats

Huge spiders could potentially inhabit almost any region, except for those with cold climates.

In North Faerûn they could be found throughout the regions of Daggerdale , Shadowdale , Zhentil Keep , and the fertile areas of the Plain of Standing Stones . In the North they could be found in parts of Cormanthor 's forests and the Rat Hills .

In the Hordelands they were quite common in the Ama Basin and Hagga Shan . Elsewhere in the region of Kara-tur , they could be found in the forests of Akari island . In the land of Maztica they could be found in many hills and mountains. In the Zakhara region's Crowded Sea , they could be found on the island chain of Nada al-Hazan .

Relationships

Huge spiders were often found in the company of driders . The Tanor'Thal family of drow kept many within their residence at Skullport .

Some creatures in the Underdark used them in groups to transport goods as a caravan.

Summoning

A huge spider could be summoned with the Summon monster III spell.
