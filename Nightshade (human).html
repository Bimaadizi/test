Nightshade was a mystic in Skullport and owner of The Nightshade's Caress in the mid-14 th century DR.

Description

Nightshade presented the very image of the archetypal hag from children's tales: jagged black fingernails, warty skin, wiry chin hairs, and rotting, blackened teeth. Her eyes emitted a malevolent green glow when provoked or particularly entertained.

Personality

She was known to delight in moral corruption, often tempting individuals toward ruin through carefully offered vices.

Relationships

Nightshade was close associate of the hag Mhaug .
