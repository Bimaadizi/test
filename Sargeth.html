Sargeth was a knight of Athalantar turned to banditry in the early 3 rd century DR .

Abilities

He had very keen eyes.

Relationships

He was one of the Knights of Athalantar under Helm Stoneblade , and the leader of one particular band of outlaws.

History

Originally one of the Knights of Athalantar that turned to banditry after the coronation of Belaur Aumar and the rise of the Magelords of Athalantar , Sargeth led one band of outlaws during Hammer of the Year of the Loremasters, 228 DR , which numbered six: himself, Nind , Eladar (an alias of then-Prince Elminster Aumar ), Baerold , Arghel , and Engarl . Together, they ambushed and slew a band of a dozen armsmen of Athalantar under cover of snow; he personally slew at least one. They returned to the cave where they were hidden, Lawless Castle after; there he learned from Javal , another outlaw, that two more armsmen had been slain near Daera 's, close to Heldreth's Horn .

However, their hideout was discovered by a second patrol and attacked. Sargeth took a crossbow bolt through the eye in that fight.
