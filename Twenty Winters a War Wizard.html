Twenty Winters a War Wizard was a book by Estimyra , a War Wizard of High Horn , published in mid-14 th century DR .

History

This autobiographical tome of witty musings was published in the Year of the Dragon, 1352 DR and a copy was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
