The Winterflow River was a small river found in the northern region of Yaïmmunahar in the Hordelands .

Geography

The Winterflow River originated in between the Glittering Spires and the Swordrise Mountains , serving as the distinction point between the two mountain ranges. It flowed passed Kourmira to empty into Yal Tengri .
