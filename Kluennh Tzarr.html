Kluennh Tzarr was a wizard from Calimshan in the mid-14 th century DR .

Abilities

He could fly and hurl fireballs at the very least.

Possessions

He owned a citadel, where he was served by numerous slaves and dragons .

History

Kluennh Tzarr duelled Algarzel Halfcloak in Baldur's Gate , around the Year of the Bloodbird, 1346 DR . During their battle, one of his own fireballs missed Algarzel, and exploded on a river ferry instead, slaying everyone on board save for an 11-year old Narm Tamaraith , whose father, Hargun Tamaraith , cast into the river to avoid the explosion.

Kluennh was the victor of the duel, and was reported to have returned home to his citadel.
