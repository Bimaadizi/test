Reinhar VI was a king of the Arkaiuns of Dambrath .

Relationships

The man who would rule as Reinhar VI was the third son of King Reinhar IV . He had at least four sons.

History

Reinhar VI was born in the Year of the Spellfire, 610 DR . He took his brother Reinhar V 's throne and name upon the latter's death in 647 DR . Reinhar VI died in 691 DR , passing the throne to his fourth son .
