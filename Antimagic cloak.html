Antimagic cloaks were enchanted cloaks that could shelter the wearer from magical and physical harm.

Powers

Antimagic cloaks were created using fibers from plants grown during the Time of Troubles . The fibers were woven center to the Weave , allowing the cloth to disperse and fade magics around it safely. These cloaks gave the wearer high resistance to spells as well as gave them the ability to cast the lesser globe of invulnerability once per day.

History

As of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , one of these, antimagic cloaks could be found in the collection of a planeswalker from Westgate known only as Mintassan the Magnificent. It was available for purchase at Mintassan's Mysteries store in Westgate's Market Triangle .
