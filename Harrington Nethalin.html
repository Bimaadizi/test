Harrington Nethalin was the rather curmudgeonly man who penned the book series, Lessons for Sensible Living some time during or before the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

He believed that folks of the Realms could live their best lives by making sensible choices, such as avoiding large cities and not taking unnecessary risks.
