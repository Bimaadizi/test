Halidae's Height was a forest-covered hilltop south and a little west of the city of Myth Drannor in Cormanthor .

History

In the Year of Drifting Stars, 760 DR , Halidae's Height was warded against all types of fiends by the god Azuth , for "as long as Azuth's power endures". This ward may have collapsed with the destruction of Azuth in the Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR . [ speculation ]
