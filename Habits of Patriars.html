Habits of Patriars was a self-help book from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Content

The book cautioned its readers of the dangers of overindulgence and self-gratification so common among the patriars of Baldur's Gate.

History

By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Habits of Patriars could be found in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate and was available in the House of Grief 's garden.
