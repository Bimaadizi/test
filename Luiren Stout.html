Luiren Stout was a dark, thick ale produced by the Sholapur family of Luiren in the 14 th century DR . It was popular all throughout the Shining South region, even in the Underdark city of Underhome .

Avilability

This ale was produced in Sholapur breweries, based primarily in the city of Chethel . Luiren Stout was one of Chethel's chief exports, receiving orders of the ale from the neighboring nations of Durpar and Halruaa .

Luiren Stout could also be found in Dambrath , brought over from the Luiren trading hub Ammathluir by caravans that traveled the Great Trade Way .
