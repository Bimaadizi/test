Sinylla Zoranyian was a wizard in service to the House of the Moon during the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR .

Description

Sinylla had a family resemblance to the sisters Jannatha and Baerimel.

Abilities

Sinylla could cast blade barrier , and she was a mage in service to the Temple of Selûne , suggesting she possessed both arcane and divine magical abilities.

Relationships

She was the cousin of Jannatha Goldenshield and Baerimel Dunnath .

History

Sinylla Zoranyian helped Taegan Nightwind by casting lie-detection spells on the other mages aiding the effort to decrypt Sammaster 's notes on the Rage of Dragons in 1373 DR . Sadly, she was slain by the chasme summoned by the sunwyrm impersonating Phourkyn One-Eye .
