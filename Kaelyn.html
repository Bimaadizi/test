Kaelyn the Dove was a half-celestial cleric of Ilmater . She fought alongside the Kalach-Cha during their adventures in the Unapproachable East in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR .

If she stood on the dais in the mirror puzzle at the Academy of Shapers and Binders , the mirror form of her that appeared at the other end had the wings of an erinyes .
