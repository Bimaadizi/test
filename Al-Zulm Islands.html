Al-Zulm Islands was a large archipelago found in the Crowded Sea within west Zakhara .

Geography

The archipelago was one of several prominent island groups congesting the Crowded Sea. The northernmost island in al-Zulm Islands was located 60 miles (97 kilometers) south of the Pearl Banks , which were close to the southeastern peninsula of the Pearl Cities .
