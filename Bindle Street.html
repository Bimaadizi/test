Bindle Street was a slightly arched street that began on the edge of The Steeps , ran across Heapside , and ended in the Eastway district of Baldur's Gate .

Location

Bindle Street began at the city's Old Wall , where the famous store, Felogyr's Fireworks , stood. From there, the street ran north, slightly curving away from the harbor, and then back towards the water. Along the way, it passed the Blushing Mermaid , it crossed Stormshore Street , ran parallel to The Eastern Wet , and ended just a block east of Athcaulyr's Stand docks. Most of Loklee Street ran parallel, to the east of Bindle Street.
