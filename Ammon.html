Abilities

Among her endeavors, Ammon sought to create an alchemical solution that could reveal scripts that had been concealed by magical means.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Ammon ventured into the ancestral tombs of the Korlasz family, in order to find reagents for her experiments. She was accompanied for some time by one of the mercenaries that worked for the individual referred to as Korlasz , but she was alone in a library for her own safety.

Ammon set out on this mission as she wanted to use her concoction on a specific book that originated from Netheril , found within the great library-fortress of Candlekeep .
