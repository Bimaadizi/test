Khelben Arunsun the Younger is a human born to Zelphar Arunsun and Lhestyn in Waterdeep in 1302 DR . Although most people know him as the "Blackstaff," he actually left Faerûn to travel the planes, and his identity was taken by his grandfather, Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun the Elder.
