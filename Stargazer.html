The Stargazer was an elven heroine who lived millennia before the 14 th century DR . Her true identity is not known; surviving ballads referred to her only as "the Stargazer".

She came into the possession of the sword called One Thousand Broken Dreams , and with it she killed hundreds of drow over her lifetime. Finally, she died of grief over the death of her sister after the Battle of Nine Arrows . As for her sword, a young elf archer took it and retreated so approaching drow reinforcements could not claim it.
