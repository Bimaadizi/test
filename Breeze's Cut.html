Breeze's Cut was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Breeze's Cut was located in the northwest section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . It branched west from the Street of the Singing Dolphin to end on Westwall Street after passing several buildings. Roguerun Alley connected with the southern side of Breeze's Cut.
