The Abbey of Everard was an abbey dedicated to the god of knowledge, Oghma .

Location

Following the Dusk Road north of Iriaebor about half-a-day's ride, there was a standing stone, marking a road branching off the Dusk Road. Following the road up a low, rounded hill led to the abbey.

Description

It was a large edifice of gray stone, its ornately embellished spires rising above a walled courtyard. The entrance was a large stout oaken gate. A rope dangled near the gate that was tied to a bell.

The entrance hall, past the courtyard, was a high, narrowed room, paneled with mahogany. Faded frescoes decorated the ceiling, as well as intricate stained-glass windows. The building had a cellar and the library was filled with books.

Tyveris 's spare chamber was small, furnished only with a low bed, a chest, and a table with two chairs. The floor was of stone and it had a simple woven mat on it.

Inhabitants

The monks of the abbey wore simple brown robes. The head monk of the Abby was Abbess Melisende . One of the monks was Loremaster Ebrelias , an old man, and another was Loremaster Avros .

They took in any orphans and raised them as their own.

History

In 1363 DR , Caledan Caldorien went to the abbey to find his old friend and member of the Fellowship of the Dreaming Dragon , Tyveris , so that he could help him free his friend Ferret Talondim from an execution in Iriaebor .
