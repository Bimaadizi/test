The Star of Mo-Pelar was a star gem gifted by the wizard Martek to the Pharaoh Amun-Re of Bakar .

Description

The Star of Mo-Pelar was a fist-sized opal . It was one of the five Star Gems of Martek .

Powers

The Star of Mo-Pelar had the same powers as a gem of true seeing .

History

The wizard Martek gave the star gem to the pharaoh Amun-Re of the house of Mo-Pelar, who had it placed in his tomb . The pharaoh would later use the Star Gem as part of a death curse uttered in Osiris ' name which dried the River Athis ; this barred his soul from the afterlife. The curse stayed in place until adventurers retrieved the Star of Mo-Pelar in 1356Â DR , using it to restore Martek to life.
