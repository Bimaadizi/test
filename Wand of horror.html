Wands of horror were enchanted wands that held multiple charges of fear-causing spells unleashed upon command.

Description

Wands of horror were carved and twisted wands, 1 foot and 3 inches (0.38 meters) in length. They were usually created using red wood or ivory . The total number of charges varied per wand.

Powers

Each of these wands held several charges of the horror spell that could be discharged at will using a wand's command word . Upon expenditure of a wand's last charge, it disintegrated.
