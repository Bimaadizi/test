TypeMagic helmet

The Seahelm was a magical helmet that provided the wearer with protection.

Description

This helm had a +2 enchantment.

History

In 1358 DR , the helmet was stashed amongst treasure in Orlumbor . Specifically, it was hidden in a temple of Talos to the south of the city-state, and was protected by those of the Kraken Society , Host Tower of the Arcane , and Zhentarim . However, the Heroes of Ascore slew them, and found vital intel, as well as the helm.
