The Gate of the Simoom was one of the entrances into the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The gate was found in the southeast wall of the city, leading into the Cindersweeps near the coast of the Sea of Fire .

Description

The gate resembled a permanent fortress complete with a garrison of soldiers.
