Personality

Many believed that Tsenya had many qualities that were ill-suited to a future ruler. She was willful, impetuous, and self-centered. However, she was also goodhearted and possessed an innate sense of decency.

Her adventurous spirit left her despising the general sense of xenophobia that dominated the entire society of Ra-Khati.

Abilities

Tsenya had a degree of training in the martial arts and the use of magic. She could cast comprehend languages and magic missile .

Relationships

Princess Bhrokiti was the daughter of the Dalai Lama .

Activities

Princess Bhrokiti was forbidden from leaving the Potala by her father until she assumed control of the regency.

As a practicing wu jen , Tsenya was required to adhere to a particular taboo or lose her powers. As a result, she could never sleep in the same room with another person.

History

Tsenya cared little for the spiritual aspects that controlled much of society within Ra-Khati. Much of this arose from the fact that her mother died during her birth, leaving her to be raised by the palace staff, who often over-indulged her to the point of spoiling the young princess. The raja of Solon Ambuchar Devayam demanded Tsenya's hand in marriage else he would invade the kingdom, and after many trials and tribulations she encountered Hubadai and his 10,000 Tuigan on their way to Khazari to support his father Yamun Khahan , though they started off wary of each other they eventually fell in love. As the armies of Solon marched upon Ra-Khati, Hubadai pleaded with his father to ride to the beleaguered kingdoms aid to convince her father to accept their marriage.
