Noska Ur'gray was an enforcer of the Xanathar's Thieves' Guild in Waterdeep during the early 1490s DR .

Activities

Noska was the chief enforcer for the Xanathar . He lived inside the beholder 's lair. During the gladiator tournaments organized by his master, Noska managed the fighters.

History

Once Noska lost his left hand and forearm to a green slime 's attack. He replaced the missing part with a heavy crossbow attached to the stump.

In 1492 DR , if the Xanathar managed to obtain the Stone of Golorr , he entrusted the Stone to Grinda Garloth . She agreed to take custody of the Stone but changed her mind and decided to use it for herself, ordering her familiar to hide it. Noska led a band of the Xanathar's minions to besiege Grinda in order to steal back the Stone.

Relationship

Noska was one of the most trusted underlings of the Xanathar.
