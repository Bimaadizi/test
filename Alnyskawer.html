History

Alnyskawer personally served a wandering Elminster Aumar in the Year of the Awakening Wyrm, 767 DR , when the latter was fishing for information on Sharindala 's Scorchstone Hall in Ripplestones and wanted to tempt local seniors Baerdagh and Caladaster Daermree into talking. He put down a large feast for Elminster, who paid up front.

Alnyskawer was also personally serving on the day when a Harper giving no name and the sages Tabarast and Beldrune rode into Ripplestones. He made litte conversation, and also took notice later that Baerdagh was also known as the Lion of Elversult . He then asked him to recount at least one old war tale.
