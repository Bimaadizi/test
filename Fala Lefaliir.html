Fala Lefaliir ( pronounced : /ˈ f ɑː l ɑː ˈ l ɛ f ɑː l ɪər / FAH-lah LEF-ah-leer ) was a wood elf herbalist in Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Fala had long, braided hair.

Personality

Fala had an outgoing personality. Like Fala's patron deity Corellon Larethian , Fala was neither male nor female and requested to be referred to by name or by the pronoun "they".

Activities

Fala was an herbalist and potion seller in Trollskull Alley in the North Ward and a member of the Guild of Apothecaries and Physicians . Fala's shop was known as Corellon's Crown , a three-story building on Trollskull Alley whose third floor had been converted into a greenhouse with glass walls that allowed passers-by to see a variety of multicolored flowers inside.

Relationships

Fala was a friend of the Zhentarim agent Ziraj , who once saved Fala's life. Fala kept a room open on the second floor of the shop for Ziraj and his occasional visits.

History

Fala was an eyewitness to the detonation of the fireball on Trollskull Alley in 1492 DR . The blast broke some of the greenhouse's windows, but no one in the building was injured.
