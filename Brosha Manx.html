Brosha Manx was a half-elf apothecary and proprietor of the Loudwater Apothecary in Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He was thin and had a scarred face and shaking hands as a result of failed attempts at making potions in his younger days.

Personality

Brosha had a dour, listless, and terse manner and disliked small talk.

Activities

Publicly, Brosha did not pay protection money to the Lady of Shadows . Privately, this was because he was one of her gang already. In 1479 DR , Calla Maran hoped Brosha would be an ally in resisting the Lady of Shadows. She would be wrong.
