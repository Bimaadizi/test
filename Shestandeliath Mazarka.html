Shestandeliath Mazarka was a dragonborn member of Clan Shestandeliath in 1487 DR .

Description

Mazarka had purple scales.

History

In 1487 DR, Mazarka was a farmer in one of the Shestandeliath homesteads located near the Riders to the Sky . She was one of the survivors of the clan during the Second Sundering that was captured and enslaved by the returned Untherans .

At some point, she was killed and impersonated by the lamia Zillah .
