Hathan was an apprentice of Undarl Dragonrider in the mid-3 rd century DR and one of the Magelords of Athalantar .

Abilities

He could mindsend messages.

Relationships

He was Undarl Dragonrider 's senior apprentice, and he received calls from Ithboltar , strongly implying he numbered among the roster of the Magelords of Athalantar .

History

On 240Â DR , right as Athalgard was besieged by the forces under the leadership of Elminster Aumar , prince of that land, Hathan was outside Hornkeep as he received a message from The Old One, presumably Ithboltar . He immediately spell-mindsent to his master, Undarl Dragonrider, who responded with irritation, biding him take to his own affairs. As Undarl immediately went for his dragon, the message was almost certainly a warning that Athalgard was under attack.
