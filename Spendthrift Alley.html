Spendthrift Alley was an alley in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep that had developed into a major street by the mid 14 th century DR .

Geography

This road opened east off of the High Road , passing through Vellarr's Lane and the Street of the Tusks , then terminated at Rivon Street . Along the way, the road Mhaer's Alley jut off north from it. The length of Spendthrift was lined with streetlamps.
