Bertice Edermath was a young half-elf woman living in Phandalin in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Her father was Daran Edermath , a retired member of the Order of the Gauntlet who maintained Edermath Orchard in Phandalin.

She was in love with Grovet , a human miner who was her same age.

History

Bertice was born in the Year of the Fifth Circle, 1476 DR .

When she was twenty years old in the Year of the Duplicitous Courtier, 1496 DR , she ran away from home with Grovet without telling her father, although it was an open secret in town. The young lovers headed south to Waterdeep along the High Road , intent on starting a new life together.
