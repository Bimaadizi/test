Jack's Folly was a pair of old and weathered leather boots that once belonged to a would-be treasure seeker named Jack Corvet .

Powers

The boots' humble appearance hid their magic well. Once per day upon command, Jack's Folly unleashed the enlarge person spell.

History

Sometime before the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Jack Corvet attempted a daring robbery of a cloud giant . He claimed the giant's golden chicken , but his stealth failed the adventurer, and the giant quickly gained on Jack. The adventurer whispered a wish upon a lucky ring inherited from his grandmother. To everyone's surprise, Jack grew in size, big enough to be able to punch the giant out and flee with the precious poultry treasure. When the chicken laid a golden egg , Jack sold it and, with the money, replaced his old raggedy pair of boots.

Later, Jack Corvet attempted the robbery again, but the "magic" ring did nothing this time. Jack did not survive the burglary. Such was the story of Jack's Folly as true magic was hidden within his old boots. By 1374 DR, the boots found their way into the collection of Mintassan the Magnificent , proprietor of Mintassan's Mysteries in Westgate 's Market Triangle .
