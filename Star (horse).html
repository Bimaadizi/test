History

Circa the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Star was kept along with five other horses, Bandy , Cardinal , Ebony , Fowler , and Hopper , by the traveling company of the mages Druhallen , the married couple of Galimer and Rozt'a , while being cared for by their foster son Tiep . That year, nearly the whole group made the perilous journey together through the Greypeak Mountains , so the human members of their group could venture into the mines of Dekanter on an adventure.
