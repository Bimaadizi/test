Victoria's boots of beguiling gait was a pair of unique enchanted elven boots that once belonged to an Akh'Velahr war party leader, Victoria , who died in the Weeping War .

Description

Victoria's boots of beguiling gait were a pair of leather thigh-high boots.

Powers

These enchanted boots possessed magic that enhanced the wearer's dexterity and reflexes as well as increased her movement speed. Additionally, these boots allowed the wearer to cast the displacement spell, creating an illusory copy of herself anywhere within 2 feet (0.61 meters) of her actual location.

History

During the Weeping War, Victoria led her elven war party against the invading Army of Darkness and met her demise in the Elven Catacombs . In 1369 DR , the drow of House Freth were smuggling elven artifacts from the catacombs under the city and Victoria's gear was among the items that were collected and prepared for transport. Victoria's boots of beguiling gait never reached the Underdark as a group of adventurers from Phlan destroyed drow operations in the Elven Catacombs.
