Zazspar Bronzefire was a shield dwarf mage and a member of the Harpers in Mirabar sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

Activities

Zazspar posed as a stablehand. His duty was to protect one of teleportation circles of Krowen Valharrow 's network even from the city's authorities.
