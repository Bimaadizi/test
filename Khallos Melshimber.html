Khallos Melshimber was a Tethyrian noble inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He worked with the New Olamn bardic college.

Relationships

Kelthul was the heir and eldest son of the Melshimber noble family of Waterdeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . His mother was Hlanta Melshimber , matriarch of the family.
