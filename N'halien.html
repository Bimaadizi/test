N'halien was an early knight of Neverwinter and the seventh of the Nine knights who served Lord Halueth Never . They were the basis for the Neverwinter Nine .

History

In a battle against the armies of Illusk , N'halien saved Halueth from an arrow , but caught it in his chest and died instead. A statue of N'halien and his sword later stood guard in Halueth's tomb.
