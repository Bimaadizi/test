Mulgor of Tyr ( pronounced : /ˈ m ʊl l g ɔːr / MULL-gore ) was a diligent yet patient tax collector within the city of Waterdeep as well as a member of the City Watch . He also served in ceremonies at the temple of Tyr known as the Halls of Justice and could occasionally be found at the Elfstone Tavern .
