Ignar Turc Abolin was a mage from Kara-Tur who inhabited the Battle of Bones in 1367 DR .

Description

Ignar was gaunt and always garbed in dark robes that shielded his face.

Abilities

Ignar was rumored to be a lich and possessed considerable magical power.

Possessions

Known magical items in Ignar's possession were a wand of frost , ring of free action , and a ring of invisibility .

Activities

It was rumored that Ignar was fascinated by flora, particularly roses. It was possible that he was the creator of the mysterious Rose Garden that was located within the Battle of Bones.
