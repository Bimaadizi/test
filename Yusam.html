Yusam was a local druid of Neverwinter , affiliated with the Adventurer's Academy in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Yusam paid monthly visits to the Adventurer's Academy's menagerie to make sure Ferdinand's beasts, including a dire spider , an ettercap , a stag beetle , a hook horror , and an umber hulk , were well-treated and in good health.
