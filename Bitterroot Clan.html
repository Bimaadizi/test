Bitterroot Clan was a duergar clan of the 15 th century DR .

History

According to the report of Disciple Z'rell called Beheading the Bitterroots , in the early 1490s DR, the Bitterroot Clan held Grymforge and were ruled by King Guerdon . The members of the clan were few in number but regarded as fierce opponents in combat.

During the Absolute crisis , the clan was a threat to the plans of the Cult of the Absolute , making it difficult to use the Underdark routes connecting the Moonrise Towers and the Shattered Sanctum . It was decided to use a squad of Orin 's doppelgangers to get rid of the clan's king. Additionally, the squad should have been tasked with throwing King Guerdon's crown into the Fathomless Depths , thus preventing the clan from crowning a new king. The "beheaded" clan would fall apart, letting the cultists bring the duergar under their control.

Although the details of the actual downfall of Clan Bitterroot remain unknown, by the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Grymforge was under control of the Cult of the Absolute and Clan Flameshade was hired to help excavate the ruins.
