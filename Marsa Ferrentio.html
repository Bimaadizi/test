Marsa Ferrentio was an adventuring paladin of Herm , a member of the Band of Iron , and the ruler of Mimph , in Sespech , circa 1370 DR .

History

Before his rise to power in the Year of the Wave, 1364 DR , Baron Aldorn Thuragar was the leader of the Band of Iron, an adventuring company of which Marsa Ferrentio was a spiritual guide. Along with fellow members Gavilon and Marcius , Marsa was established as the ruler of one of the Sespechan towns. Marsa Ferrentio of Helm became the ruler of Mimph.
