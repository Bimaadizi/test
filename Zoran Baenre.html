Zoran Baenre was a member of House Baenre who was a second-year apprentice at Sorcere circa 1373 DR . Gromph considered him annoying.

History

He was one of the Baenre magic-users that Gromph Baenre gathered during the Siege of Menzoberranzan for an attack on Nimor Imphraezl through a scrying crystal.
