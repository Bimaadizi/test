Naelor's Banner was a mercenary company from Murghôm , operating from Thuldan Way on the western edge of Nimjammir in the late 15 th century DR .

Organization

Naelor's Banner, like all other mercenary companies of Murghôm, was established by the country's military veterans who were forced into hired work by the army shrinkage decreed by the Princes of Murghôm over several decades. All companies, Naelor's Banner included, were not bigger than 40 ex-military members in the full employment of the organization.

The company's insignia was a canary-yellow flaming dagger positioned diagonally pointing to the upper right corner on a royal purple background.

History

By the late 15 th century DR, Naelor's Banner was the best-reputed mercenary band in Murghôm, renowned for their military precision, superb discipline and over forty years of successful mercenary work.
