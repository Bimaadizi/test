Ghost Street was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Ghost Street was located in northeast Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter . Originating along Fortress Street , Ghost Street passed south for just 300 feet (91 meters) before ending at a gate leading into the Inner Quarter . Electrum Avenue connected with the southwest side of Ghost Street.

Notable Locations

A warehouse used exclusively by the Zhentarim was located on Ghost Street.
