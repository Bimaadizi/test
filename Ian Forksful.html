Ian Forksful was a cobbler working on the Ship Street of Waterdeep during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

In addition to his shoemaking activities, Ian also practiced thievery with some success.

History

During the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR Ian sold fake treasure map to the city's adventurers . Unbeknownst to him, those maps led to the lair of Artor Morlin and were part of a scheme organized by Bregan D'aerthe .
