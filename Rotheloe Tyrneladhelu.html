Rotheloe Tyrneladhelu was an inhabitant of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

Rotheloe Tyrneladhelu was one of the wizards staying back in the hunt for Elminster Aumar in 241 DR . Selgauth Cathdeiryn later related to the High Court Mage that though they'd prevailed, Rotheloe had completely lost his wits and could not stop crying.
