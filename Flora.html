Flora was a woman who lived in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

She had a lover and partner in a man named Daven . Together the two had one child, Daven Junior .

History

When Flora gave birth to their son, in or before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Daven was away from the city on work. Afterwards, Flora wrote to Daven, imploring him to come home on leave. Unfortunately for the couple, the letter never received Daven, as the courier bearing it was struck down by gnolls along the Risen Road .
