Alaecathra Shuinrond was the abbess of the Seekers of the Dawn in Myth Drannor in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .

Activities

Alaecathra directed the Seekers of the Dawn, a Lathanderian order devoted to helping adventurers in Myth Drannor. She also took care of the Dawnstone .

Personality

Alaecathra was a kindly, enthusiastic, yet commanding leader. She had no fear and would die for Lathander without hesitation.
