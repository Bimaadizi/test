Zook was a male rock gnome living in Gnomengarde in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Like most of Gnomengarde's inhabitants, Zook looked up to the master inventors Dabbledob Plasterpatch and Fibblestib Pinebark .

Abilities

Just like the other gnomes in Gnomengarde, Zook had some training as a wizard .
