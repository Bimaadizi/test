Semmonemily was a doppelganger working for the Kraken Society .

Activities

As a member of the Kraken Society , Semmonemily would gather the latest rumors and information from numerous Underdark sources and unusual informers.

History

Semmonemily was once the servant of an Illithid who had transformed into an alhoon .

In 1361 DR , while working for the Kraken Society, he killed and replaced a Waterdhavian courier in Yartar on behalf of Baron Khaumfros , in order to plant the seeds of a conspiracy to cause a war between Waterdeep and Ruathym .

By 1372 DR , Semmonemily was a senior agent of the Kraken Society, responsible for keeping watch over the Dessarin River valley area.
