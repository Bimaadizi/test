Zalder Faelrond was a knight Torm and member of the Order of the Gauntlet , who lived in Camp Vengeance in Chult during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Some time during or before the 1490s DR , Zalder was traveling through the jungles of Chult, when he was ambushed and kidnapped by the coven of hags known as the Sewn Sisters . He was then imprisoned within the Cradle of the Death God , in the terrifying Tomb of the Nine Gods .
