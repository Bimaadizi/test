Mint was a plant commonly used as a form of seasoning in the Realms, primarily classified as a type of herb . It was noted for its aromatic qualities, sweet flavor, and cool aftertaste.

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , the Downunda Patisserie in Ravens Bluff offered mint & green apple cookies as one of its Summer Specials. The bakery charged 5 cp for a singular cookie and 3 sp for a dozen.
