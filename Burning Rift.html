The Burning Rift was an underground volcanic rift in Chult .

Geography

The Burning Rift was located in the Firelands directly beneath the Peaks of Flame .

Description

The Burning Rift had a number of portals that linked it to the Elemental Plane of Fire . The rift was also connected to the Peaks of Flame volcanoes above it through lava tubes.

History

During the Time of Troubles , the fire god Kossuth appeared in the Burning Rift as the firenewt blackguard avatar Chassan . In this form, Kossuth led the firenewt Ak'ta tribe in war against the pterafolk of the Chultanegar . After departing the Prime Material Plane , Kossuth rewarded Chassan by allowing him to become a deathflame and return to leadership of the Ak'ta tribe.

Inhabitants

Firenewts and salamanders dwelt in the Burning Rift, having migrated there from the Elemental Plane of Fire. Azer , hell hounds , flamesnakes , and magmins also used the Burning Rift as a point of entry into the Firelands and the Peaks of Flame. Cinder swarms were also common here.
