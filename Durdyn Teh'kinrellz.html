Durdyn Teh'kinrellz was the weapon master of House Teh'Kinrellz of Ched Nasad in 1370 DR .

History

Durdyn took on the female commoner, Relirva Indagley'Belash , and she served under him. He gave her the opportunity to study creatures around Ched Nasad, such as choldrith and chitines . Her efforts led to the death of a rival houses's secondboy, and she was granted greater privileges by her house.
