Reginald was a corrupt sergeant of Hillsfar 's Red Plumes in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Reginald was well mannered and well spoken, acting with courtesy and professionalism. But his charm hid the truth, that inside he was moved only by avarice and ambition.

History

Circa 1486 DR , he decided to make money kidnapping non- human farmers around the city and selling them to duergar slavers. However, some angry farmers hired bandits to have revenge.

A bandit group including Karl attacked a caravan protected by Reginald and his men. An adventuring party caught in the middle brought Reginald and his surviving men to justice, and they would face a tribunal.
