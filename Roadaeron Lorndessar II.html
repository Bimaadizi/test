Roadaeron Lorndessar II was a king of Westgate in the late 12 th century DR , reigning over 1191 DR and 1192 DR .

A member of the Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty , he was born in the Year of the Sun Underground, 1154 DR and was the first child of King Blaervaer the Reluctant .
