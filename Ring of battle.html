Ring of battle was a variation of a ring of protection that could be found in Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

A ring of battle was a band of gold with a simple geometrical design of repeating triangle shapes. The ring had an embedded blue-green gem .

Powers

The wearer of this ring was granted magically enhanced battle instincts, effectively increasing their defenses in combat.
