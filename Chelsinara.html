Chelsinara was an influential priestess of Oghma , the Lord of Knowledge.

History

Chelsinara was one of Oghma's earliest priestesses. The symbol of Chelsinara , depicting a pair of cupped hands and adorning the vests of some of Oghma's faithful, was named in her honor.
