Brianna Tarvison was an adventurer and member of the She-Wolves mercenaries in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

In Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Brianna and Pahelhe Firecaster attended a party at Thistleflame Keep , held by Lord Partic Thistle for King Azoun IV 's birthday. They stayed the night in the Keep as guests and were interviewed as suspects after Lord Thistle appeared to be murdered that night, but proved to be completely uninvolved.
