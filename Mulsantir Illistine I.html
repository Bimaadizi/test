Mulsantir Illistine was the first King of the Illistine Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Mulsantir was a mercenary with relations to the noble Illistine family in Chondath . In the Year of the Cat's Eye, 429 DR , he attacked the city of Westgate and slew the pirate king holding power, claiming the throne for himself. He was recognized as King Mulsantir I and reigned until the Year of the Festering Heart, 440 DR .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Mulsantir II .

History

Mulsantir’s Gate , Westgate's main city gate, was named in his honor.
