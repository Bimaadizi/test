Swiresy shoes were magical boots that were likely created in tribute to the infamous diminutive vaulting thief named Lupperdiddle Swires .

Powers

The wearer of these boots became an expert vaulter, making their jumps 5 feet (1.5 meters) longer and higher. Additionally, they magically improved the wearer's skills in acrobatics.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Swiresy shoes were in possession of Grat , a merchant of Cult of the Absolute who traded at the Shattered Sanctum . The boots were among many other spoils of cultist raids on the communities and locations in the Chionthar Vallery .
