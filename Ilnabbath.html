Ilnabbath was an apprentice Red Wizard of Thay in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He was really worried about his fellow apprentices killing him.

Relationships

He was apprenticed to Azmyrandyr .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the Simbul assaulted a temple of Shar somewhere in Thay where the Red Wizard Azmyrandyr was preparing for an intrigue. This was a revenge attack after Azmyrandyr participated in an attack on Silverymoon . During her attack, Azmyrandyr and his apprentices were rehearsing the impersonation of several notables from Nimpeth .

Ilnabbath and Marlus Belraeblood arrived at the scene of the fight after Azmyrandyr and the four apprentices who were with him had been slain; the first ran off to sound an alarm, only to be eliminated with a single fireball on the spot.
