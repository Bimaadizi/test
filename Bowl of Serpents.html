The Bowl of Serpents was a drinking establishment of Scornubel in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Interior

Many mauve tapestries hung inside the Bowl of Serpents.

Services

Amongst other drinks, they offered emerald green Starlartarn wine from Tashalar . The establishment boasted many serpent-tailed dancers, only partly visible between the tapestries.

History

The Black Bowl was a relatively established watering hole as of 1369 DR. One evening, it was visited by Dove Falconhand , who patronized the place in the guise of a mule driver by the name of Tarthan .
