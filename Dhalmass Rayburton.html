Dhalmass Rayburton was a member of House Rayburton , and the only non- Tabaxi member of the barae , the seven "super-paladins" sworn to protect the holy city of Mezro in Chult .

History

Dhalmass, a famed explorer from Cormyr , traveled to Chult around 172 DR and there finally found peace.

Personality

Dhalmass was a kindly individual but liked to scoff at the notion that the Heartlands were "civilized" compared to Chult.
