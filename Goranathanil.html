Goranathanil was a fang dragon that laired in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

History

The dragon lived in a hidden cavern beneath the Dlabraddath district, the entrance of which opened into a ravine above Oacenth's Stream . Goranathanil's hoard included treasures like the band of acrobatics , the band of the holy way , and the band of mental magic . Goranathanil's child Ravcosa wasn't far away, resting at the abandoned Throne of Thought in the ruins.
