The Trollyard was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

The Trollyard was located in the west section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . It was situated between the Trolltower and the Troll Gate . The Fieldway entered the eastern side of the Trollyard.
