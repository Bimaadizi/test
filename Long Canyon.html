The Long Canyon was a ninety-mile-long (145 km) canyon in the Pasocada Basin in Maztica , through which the Pasocada River ran. It ranged between one mile (1.6 km) and ten miles (16 km) in width.

The ruins of Esh Alakar lay within the canyon, and the canyon was home to a tribe of hill giants .
