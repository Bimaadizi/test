Davon Malaeda was an official in Hillsfar in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Davon was the closest advisor of the First Lord Torin Nomerthal .

History

Circa 1486 DR , Davon was killed and replaced by Ruexohn , an incubus agent of the demon lord Graz'zt , in order to influence the First Lord's decisions.
