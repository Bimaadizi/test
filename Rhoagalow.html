History

During the Year of Mistmaidens, 764 DR , an exhausted Rhoagalow reported at least two murders to the seneschal of Galadorna, Elminster Aumar . One of those murders, the one of the merchant Anlavas Jhoavryn , he believed to be the work of Limmator . After telling Elminster of a knifing whose victim might live, he received from the seneschal a tankard of Burdym's Best , which he was told to drink in private.
