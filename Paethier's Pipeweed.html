Paethier's Pipeweed was a pipeweed shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building had two storys.

Location

Paethier's Pipeweed was located in the northern section of the Castle Ward on the north side of Trader's Way near the Market .
