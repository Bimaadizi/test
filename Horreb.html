Horreb was an ancient fallen civilization south of Barze . It was known for its potent magical artificers .

History

Horreb took part in the defeat of the primordial Maram of the Great Spear in −357 DR . Horreb artificers created a magic item known as the Horreb ritual cube , whose purpose was to keep Maram locked away in his otherworldly prison.

About a year later, Horreb was conquered and leveled by Tyranthraxus and his Twisted Ones, who also killed its rulers.
