Bonny was a piece of enchanted fabric from Waterdeep that was folded into a makeshift bandana in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Bonny was a navy-blue piece of cloth with a light-blue shape on its middle.

Powers

Bonny allowed the wearer to cast a burst of powerful healing magic onto an ally.

History

At some point in the late 15 th century DR, Calliope found herself preparing to raid a midnight ball somewhere in Waterdeep's Sea Ward . The bard knew that she would either need a mask or a good bandage and sliced a piece of Mirt the Moneylender's coat for that very purpose. Little did she know that the coat and the fabric she took from the unsuspecting Old Wolf was imbued with powerful restorative enchantments.
