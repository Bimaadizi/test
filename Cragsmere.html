House Cragsmere ( pronounced : / k r ɑː g s ˈ m ɪər / crags-MEER ) was a noble family of Waterdeep , of Illuskan and Netherese descent, who were ennobled in the year 1248 DR . They earned their considerable wealth as landowners and money-lenders.

Base of Operations

The family owned a villa in the North Ward , on the southeast corner of Copper Street and Vondil Street .
