Jurina Plem ( pronounced : /ˈ j ɜːr i n ɑː ˈ p l ɛ m / YUR-ee-na PLEM ) was an art seller in Mulmaster .

Personality

She considered Andrik Gos a genius and a true artist, not caring for the monetary value of his paintings.

History

Jurina was hired by Zor Faltyn Gos to manage the Ironheart Gallery , a new art gallery owned Faltyn and Tiega Gos . She mostly sold the paintings of Andrik Gos.
