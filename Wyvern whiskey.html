Wyvern whiskey was a rare and expensive strong distilled alcoholic drink exported from the mysterious island of Nimbral .

Description

This exquisite whiskey 's most notable feature was a wyvern 's scale floating in every bottle. Due to its high price and rarity, wyvern whiskey was often counterfeited.

History

In the 1360s DR , wyvern whiskey could be purchased at the House of Good Spirits in Waterdeep . In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a bottle of wyvern whiskey was kept in the Cloister of Sombre Embrace in the city of Baldur's Gate .
