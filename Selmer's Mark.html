Selmer's Mark was a large tavern located in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan in the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The tavern was located in the southeast section of Suldolphor.

Activities

Selmer's Mark catered to thrifty patrons, making it a rarity in the Golden City. The tavern served basic beverages including watered ale and wines . Unruly customers were usually thrown to the street via the shuttered windows, much to the entertainment of everyone present.

Inhabitants

The tavern was owned by its namesake, Selmer , in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR . All of the servers in Selmer's Mark were children who worked for room and board.
