Thradulf's Camp was located in the southwestern region of the Cold Wood and was ruled by the cruel Thradulf Longfang , chieftain of the Red Tiger Tribe .

History

Thradulf became leader of the Red Tiger tribe in 1368 DR succeeding Adalwulf Longfang , a wise leader who ruled his tribe long and well. Before this time, the Red Tigers were a solitary tribe, but under Thradulf's rule, a darker side of the tribe was born.

Inhabitants

Thradulf's camp was the largest gathering of Red Tigers. His followers hunted and gathered in the Cold Wood region during the wintertime and moved north to the Druarwood in the summer season.
