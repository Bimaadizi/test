Haeler Thommadur was a warrior during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

In 1491 DR , Haeler organized a caravan under the orders of Ascaleld Marurryn , who acted as negotiator for the Sembian Rolling Wheel . In truth, Haeler wanted to use the caravan as bait for some bandits operating in the area. With the help of some adventurers, Haeler and his men managed to clear the area.
