Guardian orbs , were wizardly orb implements that boosted the wielder's abilities, popularized in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

Those mages who touched a guardian orb had their mental prowess boosted by the item's magics and they felt their resolve and defenses steeling.

History

In the 15 th century DR, various guardian orbs could be found among bandit spellcasters of the Sword Mountains and the Crags , areas surrounding the city, markets of Neverwinter , wielded by servants of the vampire lord Ozit who laired in the ruins of Illefarn .
