Jarvy was a halfling who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

Personality

Jarvy's humor was purposefully low-brow, meant for cheap laughs. When he told his tales, he did so in a bombastic way, as he believed that was the way bards told their stories.

Activities

Jatvy was a merchant who owned an stand in the market square of the Protector's Enclave . He sold pies and vegetables. He also dreamed to be a bard, and so he made a few gold by telling tales and gossiping.

History

Jarvy survived the attack of the " Lost Heir " of Alagondar and his plaguechanged minions of 1479 DR not only once, but twice.
