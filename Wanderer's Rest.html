The Wanderer's Rest was a boarding house located in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Location

The Wanderer's Rest could be found in the Pilgrims' District of the City of Delights.

Inhabitants

The large boarding house could accommodate up to 20 guests at once. It was owned and operated by Fatima Kossi .

Activities

Guests of the Wanderer's Rest could expect to pay 1 dirham per night. However, Fatima's kindheartedness meant that she often let true pilgrim's stay for free.
