The Boyla's Court was a large outdoor courtyard found near the center of Manass in Khazari .

Uses

The courtyard served as a prime location for a variety of events such as public announcements, executions, torturing criminals, and public festivals.

Inhabitants

The personal bodyguard of the governor of Manass kept watch along the perimeter of the courtyard.
