Smokers were props in the Realms popular among bards who used the smoke they emitted to enhance their performances.

Description

A single smoker was the size of a finger and made from incense . They belched smoke when tossed onto an open flame such as a brazier .

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 3 gold pieces .
