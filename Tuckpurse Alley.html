Tuckpurse Alley was an alleyway of hard-packed dirt and gravel in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This alleyway came off the easternmost end of Spendthrift Alley , circled around a small block of buildings, then went south and west to the easternmost end of Vellarr's Lane .

Notable Inhabitants

This alleyway was quite frequented at night by thieves . Some of these thugs were known to use children to trip or snatch goods from those passing through the alley.
