Ozomar Do'Urden , formerly Ozomar Druu'giir , was the house wizard of House Do'Urden .

History

Ozomar was formerly a member of House Druu'giir . He attended Sorcere , the drow academy of magic, and was a "promising graduate". After the death of his rival (and the Do'Urden house wizard) Nalfein Do'Urden , Ozomar happily defected to House Do'Urden to serve as house wizard, presumably until its destruction in 1339 DR .
