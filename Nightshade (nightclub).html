The Nightshade was a nightclub in Scornubel in the late 1360s DR .

It was dim and usually crowded, and a home to music, passion, and shady dealings. It served as a festhall and gambling house.

It lay on top of the crypt of the Wondermen , although the club's staff denied its existence or claimed that the secret panel that granted access to the crypt moved regularly.
