Baralea Braceryn was part-owner, cook, and hostess at the Three Elves inn in Voonlar circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Description

She was rotund, but with bird-like quickness.

Personality

Baralea was flirtatious and bustled about, thoughtfully taking care of her customer's needs.

Abilities

She was a great cook, able to quickly whip up a meal. She and her sister brewed a sweet beer from berries.

Relationships

The Three Elves was a very popular inn throughout the Moonsea region and its owner/operators were highly regarded. Baralea had a sister, Ammatha .
