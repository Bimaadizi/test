The Ul-shim Guild was a thieves' guild that operated in the city of Calimport in the late 13 th century DR .

History

The Ul-shim Guild was a well-established organization in Calimshan by the Year of the Pointed Bone, 1298 DR . Possibly the most famous member of the guild was the legendary Mujibar the Foolish rogue whose luck became a cautionary tale popular among drunks of Calimport.
