Description

It was a large walled monastery.

Notable Inhabitants

Circa 1370 DR , this monastery was run by High Artificer Danactar , Most Holy Servant of Gond. As the highest-ranking mortal cleric of Gond, Danactar was the supreme voice in ecclesiastical matters, at least on Lantan and among the Lantanese, and he was also leading councillor in Lantan's ruling body, the Ayrorch .

Activities

The techsmiths of the church of Gond were based and trained at the High Holy Crafthouse.
