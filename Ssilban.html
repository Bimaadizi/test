Ssilban The Silent was a notorious thief from Theymarsh‎‎ of the Border Kingdoms , active in the 14 th century DR .

History

Ssilban of Theymarsh spent time on the Sword Coast sometime before the Year of the Crown, 1351 DR . During that time he made enemies, including Baelam , a wizard /thief of Myth Drannor .
