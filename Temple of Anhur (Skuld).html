The Temple of Anhur was a temple dedicated to the worship of Anhur , the god of war within the Mulhorandi pantheon .

Location

The temple was located in the the northwest corner of Skuld's Central Ward in the north section of the city. Strangely, the Temple of Anhur was located outside of the walled City of the Gods that housed the majority of Skuld's temples.
