The Tower of the Morning was an important temple to Lathander .

Location

The tower was located in Telpir , a free city on the Dragon Coast .

Structure

Though structurally far more simple than the Spires of the Morning , this temple was considered the second-most prominent temple to the Morninglord.

History

As of 1369 DR , High Radiance Durneth Seafarer was in command of the tower. In 1374 DR , Durneth was still in charge. In Ches 28 of this year, edicts were issued at the tower that gave merit to the Risen Sun heresy .
