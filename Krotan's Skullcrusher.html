Krotan's Skullcrusher was the name given to several enchanted bludgeons.

Description

There were several types of these weapons created. Some were simple like maces , while others such as heavy two-handed dire maces were more elaborate. All of them, however, bore permanent stains from all the blood they spilled over the years.

Powers

Krotan's Skullcrushers possessed +1 or +3 enchantments and when wielded against orcs , half-orcs , or goblinoids , these weapons caused additional harm.

History

As the late 14 th century DR , Krotan's Skullcrushers became popular along the Sword Coast , however, many of their wielders liked to hide the deeds and history of these maces.
