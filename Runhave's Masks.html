Runhave's Masks was a festhall and tavern in the Amnian city of Esmeltaran during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The festhall was found in northeastern Esmeltaran.

Services

The place provided quite excellent services at surprisingly cheap prices. The staff wore masks as they worked, and on occasion drugged, kidnapped, and sold foreign guests to drow who operated beneath the city.
