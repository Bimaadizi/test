Roq Island was a small island found in the north quarter of Suq Bay in interior Zakhara .

Geography

Roq Island sat in the middle of a narrow portion of Suq Bay just north of Ruhh Island and equidistant from the coastal towns of Aib to the west and Hayyir to the east. The island was approximately 20 miles (32 kilometers) wide and 15 miles (24 kilometers) long, making it the larger of Suq Bay's two islands. The eastern half of the island was comprised of sandy dunes while the western half was lightly forested.
