Vaelle Lurval ( pronounced : / v eɪ ˈ ɛ l ɛ l ɜːr ˈ v ɔː l / vay-ELLE lur-VAWL ) was a florist and a follower of Asmodeus in Waterdeep during the early 1490s DR .

Activities

Vaelle was a member of the cult led by Victoro Cassalanter .

History

In 1492 DR , if the Cassalanters managed to obtain the Stone of Golorr , Vaelle, her boyfriend Holiver Tornrudder and three other cultists protected the Stone inside the Cassalanter mausoleum in the City of the Dead . However two of the cultists, under the secret orders of Victoro Cassalanter, killed Holiver and the other cultist and left Vaelle for dead.

Relationship

Vaelle was the girlfriend of Holiver, who'd convinced her to join the Asmodeus cult.
