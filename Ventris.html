Ventris was an island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Ventris was found near the center of the Korinn Archipelago. It was the largest single island among the group, covering approximately 375 square miles (971 square kilometers). An unnamed mountain passed through the center of the island, and forests were found along both the eastern and western coast.

Inhabitants

Populations of dwarves , halflings , and elves lived on the island.
