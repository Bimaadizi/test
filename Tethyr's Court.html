Tethyr's Court was a rooming house located in the village of Eveningstar during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

Tethyr's Court was located along the High Road as it entered Eveningstar from the west.

Inhabitants

This rooming house was owned by Arbold Tethyr . His wife ran the establishment and employed eight maids who also lived there.
