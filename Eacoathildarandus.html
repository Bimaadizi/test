Eacoathildarandus was an ancient silver dragon who laired atop Scarsiir's Crag in the Cloud Peaks of Amn .

History

In the Year of Green Wings, 1216 DR , the megalomaniacal red dragon Balagos sought out Eacoathildarandus, seeing him as a potential threat or competitor to his wide-ranging domain. The old silver wyrm proved no equal match, and Balagos won the day.
