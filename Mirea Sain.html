Mirea Sain was a seasoned hunter operating out of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

She was a woman of few words.

Relationships

Mirea was a member of the Hunters' Guild of Chult , and took an active role in acquiring equipment—particularly leather armor —for the guild. She could often be found at Hunter's Square in Port Nyanzaru as she planned her hunts and purchased lures.

History

During the events of the death curse Mirea contacted Port Nyanzaru's Master of the Market in order to find a leatherworker who could fashion durable equipment out of manticore hide.
