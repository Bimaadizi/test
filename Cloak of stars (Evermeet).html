The cloak of stars was an enchanted cloak that originated from Evermeet .

Description

This midnight blue cloak gleamed with hundreds of small stars.

Powers

The cloak of stars imparted invisibility from all forms of normal and magical vision if worn on a starry night under the open sky. The cloak also functioned as a strong cloak of protection .
