Rare and Fine Seashell Remedies was a shop that dealt in medicinal tinctures, located in the Seaglimpse neighborhood of Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The shop was nestled by the city walls of the Harbor District , in a cluster of unnamed lanes often referred to as Gimble's Elbow . The so-called Elbow split from the wagonroad called the Front that ran along the Harbor District's shores. The The Silver Lady inn was located to the northwest of the Seashell Remedies, the Farspice Trading Company stood to its south, and further to the south, the Gates of the Watchful Raven led into the city, away from the harbors.

Services

Rare and Fine Seashell Remedies sold highly-alcoholic liquor cordials that were advertised as remedies and medicines.
