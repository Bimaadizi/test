Ollarent Hillgreen was the assistant to Old Night Shalara Swordshigh at the main library of the Heralds of Faerûn , known as Herald's Holdfast , around the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . As an assistant to one of the High Heralds , Ollarent also belonged to the Heralds Pursuivant .

He was the senior sage in charge of the Library of Man. He fiercely guarded the knowledge under his care and would only give secrets away if directed by Old Night. He required a trade of some historic work or artifact for his information—it was never for sale.
