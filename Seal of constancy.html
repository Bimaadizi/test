The seal of constancy was a unique enchanted medallion crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Halbenet Soladonel in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

The seal of constancy was nearly weightless, and was meant to serve as a reminder of how devotion to others helps to ease one's own burdens.

Powers

When Halbenet engaged in battle alongside friends, the magic of the seal rewarded their devotion by preserving and restoring their allies' minds.
