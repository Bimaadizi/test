Tuala Uwoke was a Chultan jeweler for hire from the city of Neverwinter , active in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Tuala Uwoke hailed from the jungles of Chult on the Chultan Peninsula , where she face some adversity, imprisonment, or slavery . After she was liberated, Tuala came across fascinating jewelry from the Sword Coast . She pursued the newfound passion for jewelry making way to the northern city of Neverwinter , were Tuala worked on her craft and by the late 15 th century, began offering her services to adventurers and defenders of the Jewel of the North.
