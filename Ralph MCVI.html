King Ralph MCVI was a king from the nation of Penbroc in the Whamite Isles . Along with Ralph MCVII , Ralph MCVI represented Penbroc in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
