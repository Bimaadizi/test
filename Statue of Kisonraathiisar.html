The Statue of Kisonraathiisar was a monument dedicated to the topaz dragon and the first king of Westgate , Kisonraathiisar .

Description

The Statue of Kisonraathiisar stood on the Harbor Loop of Westgate, on the street's western side, in the Loop's inner block. In the late 14 th century DR , the statue could be found to the southwest of Okuzo's Imports shop. The masterfully sculpted statue mistakingly depicted the wyrm as a red dragon , but, despite that, was impressive. Of course, it was not built to scale. Benches were placed on the sides of the dragon statue, two on each side.
