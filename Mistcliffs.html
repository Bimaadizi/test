The Mistcliffs , also known as the Mistcliff Mountains , was a mountain range that spanned the northwestern coast of Chult .

Inhabitants

As of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , the Mistcliffs were home to one of only four major colonies of aarakocra in all of Faerûn .

By the late 15 th century DR , the aarakocra population had grown several large settlements across the coastal Mistcliff . Pteranodons and quetzalcoatluses were also known to make their home within the range's jagged peaks.
