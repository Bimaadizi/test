Mithel was a small town located in the nation of Halruaa during the 14 th century DR .

Geography

Mithel was located on the coast of southern Halruaa. The town sat on the Great Sea in the Bay of Halruaa south and east of the mouth of Halagard Channel .

Activities

Mithel was a favored docking spot for Durparian merchant ships.

History

The town was occupied by the forces of Reinhar I in the 6 th century DR.
