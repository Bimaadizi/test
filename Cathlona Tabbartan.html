Cathlona Tabbartan was an Amnian socialite in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

During her first ball, she carried a peacock feather fan, and had her upswept hair dusted with diamond. Her chin was heavily rouged, and her pectoral had numerous spindle-shaped crystals hanging as ornaments. She was overly plump.

Relationships

Her cousin was Simylra Lavartil .

History

Originally from Amn , Cathlona Tabbartan was a participant in a nobles' ball in Waterdeep , her first, during 1369 DR . The ball was crashed by the drow Qilué Veladorn , who was disguised as a drow princess and assumed to be a human disguised as a drow. While put off because of the newcomer's daring costume, Cathlona had been occupied throughout the evening learning the who's who of Waterdeep from her cousin Simylra.
