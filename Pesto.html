Pesto was a herb and nut sauce, or a condiment, from Calimshan .

Usage

In Calimshan, pesto was served with rye cakes . The cakes were dipped in the sauce and topped with sturgeon caviar . The dish dated back to the ancient Calim Empire . It was served at that Palace of the Phoenix of Cursrah .
