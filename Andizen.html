King Andizen was a king of Solon during the Solonese Empire.

Personality

He was seen as cruel.

History

Under King Andizen's rule, the empire had become ever more corrupt and decadent, and the king was very unpopular. He had sent one of his nobles, Lord Yseldre , to the southern marches to combat the depredations of the blue dragon Sturykkazynarr at the most dangerous frontier of the realm. After the blue wyrm slew Lord Yseldre, the Lord's daughter, Tiphera tir-Yseldre , challenged the wyrm's forces; over ten years, she became successful and popular enough to endanger King Andizen's rule. In response, Andizen made a deal with the dragon, delivering Tiphera into an ambush under the pretense of celebrating her actions.

Legacy

After Tiphera slew Sturykazzynar, King Andizen honored her as a hero, and had her remains and helmet interred in a lavish tomb, Tiphera's mausoleum .
