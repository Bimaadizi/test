Ssintar was a small trading outpost located near the Methwood , in the heartland of Unther .

Description

Ssintar was actually a military outpost. Gilgeam had at least 1,000 soldiers stationed there, whose job was to protect Unther from invasions from either the Alamber Sea or the Methwood.

Government

As of 1357 DR , Ssintar was ruled by Lord Gudea , who was considered the most honorable and humane of the servants of Gilgeam.

Economy

Near Ssintar were mines rich with deposits of copper and iron .

History

Ssintar was destroyed alongside the rest of Unther during the Spellplague of 1385 DR .
