Endugous Than was a wizard and member of the Zhentarim during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He stood 6 feet (1.8 meters) tall.

Personality

Endugous was a cunning man whose ambitions carried him toward gathering more power.

Activities

Circa 1369 DR , Endugous was stationed in Snowmantle near the Border Forest where he served under Ragnoth .

Possessions

He carried a quarterstaff and wore a pair of bracers of defense .

Abilities

He could cast the following spells: magic missile , charm person , phantasmal force , sleep , alter self , Melf's acid arrow , fly , and hold person .
