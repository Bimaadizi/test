Bertio was a member of the Dead Rats gang in Luskan in the late 15 th century.

History

Around 1490 DR , Titus Minton introduced the Champion of the Burning Dawn to Bertio, who had been poisoned by Bregan D'aerthe and became a ravenous beast from the madness. With ingredients procured by the Champion from Aleema Meddlebrook and Miranda , Bertio was cured.
