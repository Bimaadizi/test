Lo Blank was a smuggler and an archer who worked with the infamous Guild in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Lo had a light olive skin and chestnut hair. She wore a light ruffled tunic and was armed with a simple bow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Lo Blank worked for a gruff hin smuggler named Big Huido , transporting weapons , drugs , and firewine into Baldur's Gate. The shipments were well defended by Lo and the rest of the crew and all were released into the hands of the members of the Guild only.
