The E'nukka Suindu Inn was an inn in the T'u Lung city of Balanzia around Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) .

Location

This inn was located in the western portion of Balanzia, down the street from the home of keno the moneylender and next to the wall that surrounded the castle of the E'tuing clan .

History

Around Shou Year 2403 (1153 DR) , the E'nukka Suindu Inn was one of many buildings that were destroyed in Balanzia as the city fell at the hands of the Shou Lung invasion of T'u Lung.
