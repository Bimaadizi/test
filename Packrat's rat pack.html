The packrat's rat pack was an exceptional backpack held by Karissa the Knife , Master Thief of the Night Brigade in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The backpack possessed some innate sneaking and dodging powers, and significantly lightened the load that the wearer carried.

History

The packrat's rat pack was in the possession of the greedy thief Karissa in her lair until she was slain by the vengeful Riverside Rats gang. It was desired as a pack for rats, especially those rats who were part of a pack of packrat rats who each had their own pack.
