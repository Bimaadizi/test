Alarashan Tlor was a magelord of Athalantar during the first half of the 3 rd century DR .

Personality

A careful man with notable self-control, he constantly planned on how to seize power upon the fall of his comrades. He was quick to imitate the habits of others.

Abilities

He was an extremely powerful wizard .

Activities

As a magelord, his efforts were mostly focused on keeping track of the comings and goings of merchants and adventurers in Athalantar .

Relationships

He was a member of the magelords of Athalantar until his death. One of his apprentices was Ortran , who was forced upon him by Undarl .

History

Alarashan had been a Magelord since at least some point between the Year of Much Ale, 233 DR and the Year of Bloodflowers, 234 DR .

During the siege of Hastarl by the forces under prince Elminster Aumar in Year of the Chosen, 240 DR , Alarashan retrieved Nanatha , an apprentice magelord, from the tower of fellow magelord Malanthor Drymm , who had been recently slain. Later during the siege, he heard the death scream of Ortran , a fellow apprentice, who was ambushed by swordsmen infiltrating the palace; Alarashan in turn was disabled by those same men.

He was later summoned to Ithboltar 's tower, which collapsed, killing him.
