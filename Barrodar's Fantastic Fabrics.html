Barrodar's Fantastic Fabrics was a fabric shop located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR following the city's destruction in Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .

Location

The shop was located in south Zhentil Keep in the western section of the Foreign Quarter just off of Zhent Avenue .

Description

Apartments were located about the shop where the proprietor lived.

Inhabitants

The shop was owned by its namesake Barrodar in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
