Juggling daggers (or juggling knives ) were a type of jugglable , small knives or daggers designed specifically for the purpose of juggling. They could also be used as weapons or for other more inventive purposes. Their design made it easier to juggle them in comparison to other daggers.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold juggling knives under its "Bards' Emporium" section in sets of three for 7 cp . Their knives were advertised as having dull edges, thus ensuring that even novice jugglers would not have to worry about accidentally slicing their fingers off.

History

In the late 15 th century DR , it is possible that at least one adventurer among those that defeated Felbris the beholder , Deathsleep the green dragon , and Orn the fire giant possessed five daggers for juggling.
