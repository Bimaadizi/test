The Temple of Thoth was a temple dedicated to the worship of Thoth , the scribe of the Mulhorandi pantheon .

Location

The temple was located in the the northeast section of Skuld's Central Ward in the center of the city.

Description

Thoth's temple was easily the largest structure in the Central Ward.
