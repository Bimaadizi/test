The Floating Gardens of Aerdrie Faenya were a beauteous set of gardens found in the elven city of Evereska . They were situated near the center of the city, just north of Bellcrest Hill .

Description

The gardens were known as a place of peace, serenity and breathtaking beauty. They were named after Aerdrie Faenya , the elven goddess of the long-lost winged elves known as the avariel .
