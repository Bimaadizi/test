Geography

The town lay on the northwest shore of the Akanamere in western Chessenta, just south of the Akanapeaks .

Inhabitants

Some 5,000 people lived in Rodanar in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , and 1,000 of these belonged to the garrison. Rodanar was led by a warrior named Hyurkes .

History

The war god Tchazzar founded Rodanar to serve as a fortress to thwart orc incursions from the Akanapeaks. Rodanar used to be a large city, but a war with Akanax during the 12 th century DR left it in ruins. The new Rodanar that sprung up since was much smaller.
