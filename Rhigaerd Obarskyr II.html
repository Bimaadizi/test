Rhigaerd Obarskyr II was a king of Cormyr and son of Azoun III .

Relatives

Rhigaerd II was the father of Azoun IV and had a daughter named Sulesta . He was married to Tanalusta Truesilver .

History

Rhigaerd was renowned for his spies. He gave them all boots of stealth and sent them off to map all castles and towns. Eventually the order of spies was betrayed from within and the boots were dispersed into the realms so that nobody could amass such a band again.

Background

When Azoun III died, prince Rhigaerd II was only six years old, so Salember Obarskyr took the throne of Cormyr as regent. Salember plotted to kill Rhigaerd and take the throne, leading Cormyr into civil war.
