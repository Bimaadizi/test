Belthar Garshin was a paladin of Torm that lived in Impiltur during the 8 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Twisted Horn, 729 DR , the Battle of Horned Heads broke out in Impiltur between the nation's forces and the dark army of Soneillon . During the battle, Belthar slew Argosh the Scaled , the half-fiend spawn of the demon lord Eltab and whose consort was Soneillon.
