Lac Sielya was a small freshwater lake located in northern Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Lac Sielya was located on Gnarhelm's eastern arm halfway between Leechhome in the northeast and Salmon Bay in the southwest. Lac Sielya was one of Gnarhelm's smaller lakes measuring only 7 miles (11 kilometers) in length.
