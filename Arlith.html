Arlith was a halfling adventurer living in Ravens Bluff in the late 15 th century DR . She was a member of an adventuring group known as the Blue Wyverns .

Abilities

In a fight, she preferred to use ranged weapons rather than melee attacks and was proficient in the use of the crossbow .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Arlith participated in the company's foray into the ancient dwarven city of Sarbreen to recover the Sarkonagael , a tome of shadow magic .

Description

Arlith had reddish hair.
