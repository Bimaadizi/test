The Far Quey were a martial tribe of Nars found in Narfell who were renowned for their toughness. There were 2,300 Far Quey in 1357 DR .

Activities

Given their rugged lifestyle and fierce attitude toward outsiders, the Far Quey were one of the more dangerous tribes to encounter in Narfell. However, their habitat was not an area that received many travelers.

Base of Operations

The warriors of the Far Quey inhabited the frigid lands between the northern shores of Icelace Lake and the Great Glacier .
