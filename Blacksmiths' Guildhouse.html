The Blacksmiths' Guildhouse was the guildhall for the blacksmiths in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The guildhall was located along the Trade Way where Water Street connected in the southern section of Daggerford near the River Gate .
