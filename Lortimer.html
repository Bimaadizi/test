Lortimer was an old drunkard that lived in Baldur's Gate .

Description

Lortimer wore a blue long-sleeved shirt and a dark-gray mantle. He had a thin gray mustache and dark-gray hair.

Personality

The old man was often found to be asleep at his table in the bottom right corner of the Elfsong Tavern .

History

Circa 1374 DR , Lortimer was a regular patron of the Elfsong Tavern.
