Halric Bonesnapper was the ruler of Griffon's Nest sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

Relationship

Halric was the grandson of the great Kralgar Bonesnapper

Activities

Halric lived in comfort in his longhouse where he met other chieftains. He sometimes launched bold raids in fear that other chieftains believed his tribe had lost the favor of Uthgar .

Personality

Halric once dreamed of wiping out all the cities of the North but his tribe was too small. He lived in fear that he had truly lost Uthgar's favor.
