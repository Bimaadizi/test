Sharp-eye Raagg was a goblin member of the cult of the Absolute during the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

Raagg carried a crude, goblin -made bow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , cultists of the Absolute raided the inn of Waukeen's Rest . They killed many innocents and took several captives. After their victorious raid, many goblin cultists returned to the Shattered Sanctum temple to celebrate. They had a grand feast, that included roasted dwarf , and copious amounts of ale . Raagg and several of her allies got thoroughly drunk and passed out in the upper reaches of the temple's courtyard.
