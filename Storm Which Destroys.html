The Storm Which Destroys was a holy slayer organization in Zakhara comprised mostly of fanatical moralists .

The symbol of the Storm Which Destroys was an obsidian blade.

Activities

Some considered the Storm to be a covert force of the Pantheon , but rumors abounded that the Storm was too extreme for the likes of the Pantheon.

Organization

Rimaq al-Nimar of I'tiraf was rumored to give orders to the Storm.

Base of Operations

The Storm used Al-Sayaj Mountains within the Pantheon Lands as their base.

Relationships

The Prince of Kadarasto , Nari ibn Aman , allied himself with the Storm, allowing them to form a small base within the catacombs of his city.
