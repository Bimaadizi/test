Devout Mrak was a cleric of the Absolute who dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Mrak had yellow-brown skin and light brown hair he kept sheared on the sides.

Possessions

He wore a crude leather cuirass, bracers , and a simple loin cloth.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the cult of the Absolute launched a terrible raid on the Waukeen's Rest inn. After the assault, Mrak hoped to receive the mark of the Absolute from High Priestess Gut .
