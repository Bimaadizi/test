Bolts of piercing were specialty crossbow bolts , crafted to penetrate and become lodged deep within the target's flesh.

Powers

Unlike regular bolts, this type of ammunition dealt additional piercing damage to the targets.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , bolts of piercing were commonplace and more expensive than the mundane crossbow bolt variation. Once could find these bolts crafted and sold throughout Faerûn , in such places as Neverwinter , the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale , and the City of Splendors, Waterdeep .
