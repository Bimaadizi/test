Traagor's Guide to Inns and Taverns was a guidebook to the city of Ravens Bluff , penned by Rooth and Eldwin Traagor sometime in the mid- 4 th century DR .

Contents

Unlike many other guides to the cities of Faer√ªn , Traagor's Guide to Inns and Taverns was designed to be useful for those who could read, as well as for those who could not. The book featured illustrations of the establishments' location, their signage, and ratings from one to four stars.

History

The guide was the creation of Rooth Traagor and her sage son, Eldwin. The duo ran Traagor's Tours and Souvenir Shop in Ravens Bluff, where the guidebook was sold for a single gold coin in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR.
