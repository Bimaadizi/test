Xeva Hunzrin was the House Wizard of House Hunzrin , one of the ruling drow Houses of Menzoberranzan in the Northdark , in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Xeva was a drow woman with obsidian -black skin and hair dyed a loud red color to match her red eyes.

History

Xeva Hunzrin claimed the title of the House Wizard sometime during or before the rule of Matron Mother Shakti Hunzrin , which began in Year of the Dauntless Dwarves, 1412 DR . Xeva also ran a tavern and a gambling hall in Menzoberranzan, simply named Xeva's Den . It counted Aumon Baenre among its big-spender clients, despite Houses Hunzrin and Baenre being bitter rivals.
