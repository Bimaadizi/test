Garvlin was a barbed devil in service to the pit fiend Kurgaloth .

History

In the late 15 th century DR , Garvlin was one of many lesser devils brought to the Lightfinger Estate in Waterdeep to enforce a lapsed contract between Kurgaloth and the Lightfinger family . Garvlin was assigned to guard the approach to the mansion.

When adventurers arrived to investigate the chaos that the devils ' arrival had caused, Garvlin was the first devil they encountered. Garvlin was defeated in the ensuing battle but managed to escape, thus avoiding being banished back to Avernus .
