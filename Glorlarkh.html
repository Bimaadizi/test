Chief Glorlarkh was the former ruler of the Tornskulls orcs , that dwelled within Dead Orc Pass during the 14 th century DR .

Description

He was known as a ferocious chieftain and war-leader.

Relationships

The chief was survived by his wife and mate Baerzel , who was known for her exceptional wisdom.

History

Glorlarkh died sometime around the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , after he was caught within a cavern when it collapsed.
