Raelaryn Xorlarrin was an elder of the Council of Spiders . He sought to gain the support of a high-ranking wizard in Menzoberranzan and believed that Archmage of Menzoberranzan Gromph Baenre would soon back the Council.

History

Raelaryn invited his cousin, Hoshtar Xorlarrin , to meet with the Council in the compound of the fallen House Srune'Lett in Narbondellyn . During the meeting, he and the other elder present were assassinated by a member of the Jaezred Chaulssin to start a civil war.
