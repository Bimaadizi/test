Haddrum was a drug . It was an elixir made out of dragon 's blood and other worse components.

History

In 1373 DR , it was smuggled in and out of Huorm , per the elves of Al Qahara .

It was imported from Raurin into Cormyr by the Spice Lords of Marsember as of 1479 DR . That same year, it was banned from Ragheb by its leader, Alzubra , because of the violent fits it could cause.
