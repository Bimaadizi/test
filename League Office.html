The League Office was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the League of Basketmakers & Wickerworkers .

Location

This building was located along the Wall Way , in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep . The league's warehouse was situated behind it.

Structure

This guildhall was one-story tall.
