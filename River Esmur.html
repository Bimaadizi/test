The River Esmur flowed from the west out of mountains of Gontal and into the Dragon Sea , across the lands of the Dusk Ports .

History

The River Esmur was known for its rich banks of fishes, eels, and the coveted rock crabs . The lands surrounding the river, known as the Esmur Flats , were a burgeoning fishing center because of that.
