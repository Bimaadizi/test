Thunsroon "Thunderhand" Frostbryn was a human wizard of Elversult on the Dragon Coast .

Description

Thunsroon was a powerful and brash man.

Personality

Thunderhand often participated in magical duels and enjoyed publicly humiliating anyone who threatened or tricked him. He was also a known planewalker traveling to other worlds and planes .
