Sarsantyr's Tapestries & Draperies was a type of textiles shop in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

This store was located along Vondil Street , in the North Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This building stood two stories tall.

Inhabitants

Sarsantyr's was operated by the couple Embre Sarsantyr and Raustril Sarsantyr .
