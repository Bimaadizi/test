The Iron Skeleton was a powerful unique enchanted set of plate armor .

Description

This heavy armor of silver and black looked eerily unsettling to most. The armor slithered over the body and reshaped itself into a unique-looking new set of plates for each wearer. It protected the wearer from darkness and helped them to meld into shadows.

Powers

This set of heavy armor carried a powerful +6 enchantment, granted the wearer the boon of darkvision and made them immune to all effect of illusion magic. Additionally, the Iron Skeleton increased the wearer's dexterity and nimbleness.

History

In was unknown who was the mad smith who created this set of armor after being possessed by some darkness. By the late 14 th century DR , the Iron Skeleton cropped up several times throughout history, including in the dreaded depths of the Underdark and in the city of Mulmaster during the Order of the Crippled Fang crisis of 1373 DR .
