Haslet Quarren was one of the leaders of the Kardryn in Yeshpek along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Haslet was a tall, soft-spoken individual.

Activities

His skills in strategy and anticipating events made him a valuable asset to the Kardryn.
