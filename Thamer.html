Thamer was a Netherese arcanist and a co-owner of Transgate Industries , who lived in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

After the death of his parents Ocelot and Ytartia , Thamer operated Transgate Industries alongside his siblings Acciajuoli and Shander . The trio managed to do extremely well in running the company, and found great success in the following years.
