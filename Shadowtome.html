The Shadowtome was an arcane spellbook penned by the mysterious wizard known only as Shadowhands . It contained 9 spells ranging from common to unique or rare.

Description

A pair of wooden covers protected the beaten electrum pages within the book. Stretched black and silver crocodile skin covered the wood. The book had a single clasp that looked like a black human hand that swiveled to grab a bestial hooked tail.

History

The tome was initially found in Calimport by Azkhazan . It changed hands several times after that, even passing into Elminster 's care for a brief period.
