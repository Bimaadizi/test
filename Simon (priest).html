Simon was a high priest of Stronmaus for the kingdom of Hartsvale .

Description

Simon was portly and bald and wore lots of gold jewelry. He carried a silver staff shaped like forked lightning.

Personality

Simon of Stronmaus came across as arrogant and domineering.

History

Simon served as the personal priest to both King Camden and his daughter Queen Brianna. When the queen became pregnant and the firbolg seer Galgadayle prophesied that she would give birth to twins, one ugly and one handsome, Simon disagreed. He had used divination magic to view within her womb and claimed that only one child was there. Ultimately, she gave birth to a single child, Kaedlaw , as Simon had said she would.
