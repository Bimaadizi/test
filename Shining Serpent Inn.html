The Shining Serpent Inn was one of the most popular inns in Neverwinter in the late years of the 14 th century DR .

Location

The Shining Serpent Inn was located in the Beggar's Nest district.

Description

The inn was the largest of Neverwinter, four stories tall, with several flights of wooden stairs running down the back.

Services

Albeit expensive, the inn was considered good. The suites were pleasant, but also bare. The inn also provided, fluffy robes for guests to wander about in, embroidered with the silver serpent logo to discourage theft.

History

The Shining Serpent Inn was destroyed during the Ruining of 1451 DR .
