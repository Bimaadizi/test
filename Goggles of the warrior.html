The goggles of the warrior were a set of magical goggles found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

These goggles best fitted skilled warriors and fighters , helping them hit more accurately and see past enemy tricks, as well as helping them land their own combat tactics better.

History

The goggles of the warrior came to light after the Elves of Cormanthor found and destroyed a lost Temple to Moander hidden in the city.
