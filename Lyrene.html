Lyrene ( pronounced : / l aɪ ˈ r i n / lie-REEN ) was a half-elf in Mulmaster .

Personality

The half-elf dreamed of joining the Zhentarim like her ancestors.

History

Lyrene and her family lived near Zhentil Keep , where her father worked for the Zhentarim. She trained as a warrior. After that city's destruction, they transferred to Mulmaster, where her older sister began working as a maid and her father as a mason.

In 1491 DR , she was offered a chance to join the Zhents by Equarz if she could find a lost treasure at the bottom of the Lost Drop lake. An adverting party hired by her sister Dylohra saved her from some strange monsters inside the lake.
