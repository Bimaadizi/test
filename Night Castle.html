The Night Castle , also known as Blacknight Manor , was a ruined fortification used by the Cult of the Dragon during the mid−14 th century DR .

Location

It was situated southeast of the city of Elversult , some ways north of the nameless swamp .

Structure

The Night Castle was once a many-towered fortress before falling to ruin.

History

During the mid–14 th century DR , the Maces of Elversult repeatedly chased Dragon Cult assassins back to the Night Castle, only to have it become shrouded in complete darkness.

Inhabitants

The ruined castle was the home of Indith Shalla , a Sharran leader within the local Dragon cult, as well as a haven of the cell's leader, the black Dracolich Hethcypressarvil .
