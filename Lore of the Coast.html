Lore of the Coast was a book penned by Irigoth Mmar , High Sage of Baldur's Gate in the early 13 th century DR .

History

Lore of the Coast was published in the Year of the Trembling Tree, 1223 DR . A copy of the book was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
