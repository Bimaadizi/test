Dock Street was a major road in the asteroid city of Bral .

Description

Dock Street was the major road that overlooked the Docks of the Rock of Bral on its leading edge. The street ran from the starboard edge of the city about 1,400 feet (430 meters) to the port edge, overlooking the Yards . At its starboard end, it merge into Lamp Street , while it merged into Anchor Street at its other end.

The road was intersected by Low Street , Hammer Street , Grand Street , and Dragon Street and formed the leading-edge border for Giff-town and Shou-town .
