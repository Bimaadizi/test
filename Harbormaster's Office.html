The Harbormaster's Office was a dome-like civic building that was the center of the Westgate 's harbor bureaucracy, official offices, and a museum of the city's naval history. It was easy to recognize by its height, banners, and a bas-relief above the main entrance in the late 14 th century DR .

Location

Harbormaster's Office shop was located on the Harbor Loop of Westgate, northwest of Wizard's Cut , the street that split Harbor Loop in two. It stood on the Sea of Fallen Stars 's harbor just west of the Ssemm family's docks.

Interior

The offices' interior was filled with rows of books, contracts, forms, and documents overseen by the Harbormaster and a group of clerks. The entryway was often filled with crates, boxes, and barrels of goods that passed through the city's harbor. Some of the items on display included models of ships, dioramas, and plaques commemorating significant naval events of the past. The Rout of the Pirate King of the Year of the Tomb, 1182 DR was one of such displays. A model of Dragon's Flame , the famed flagship of Westgate's navy in the 14 th century DR, was another item proudly shown to the visitors.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Harbormaster Grenn took over the position after Harbormaster Lanley fallen ill.
