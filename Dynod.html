Dynod was a member of the Evereskan Tomb Guard that served his city as of the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

He served alongside his fellow elves , Nimieye and Ehamond among others, under their unit commander Galaeron Nihmedu .

History

In the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , Dynod and his squad came upon a band of Vaasan tomb raiders in the crypts of the reviled Vyshaan elves. They captured the human intruders, along with their leader Vala Thorsdotter , but were convinced by the sorcerer Melegaunt Tanthul to demonstrate mercy.

Dynod kept guard over the prisoners until his commander Galaeron led their group back to Evereska, so the humans could face the city's Hill Elders for judgement.
