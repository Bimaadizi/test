Melf was a male elf archmage and spell -inventor. Both his work and spells were known by many inhabitants of the Realms.

Description

Melf developed two spells that were common in the Realms: Melf's acid arrow and Melf's minute meteors .

As of the 15 th century DR , Melf was over 200 years old.

Personality

Though he was opinionated and vain, Melf dedicated much of his life to fighting the forces of evil .

Possessions

Melf fought with and owned a magical spear and shield , a cloak of illusions , elven chainmail , a spellbook , and an exploding meteor. During his time leading the Knights of Luna , Melf owned a staff called Melf's first staff .

History

In 1364 DR , Robillard of the Sea Sprite and Harkle Harpell of Longsaddle engaged in a spellcasting competition. When Harkle took out the material components for Melf's acid arrow , Robillard recognized the spell instantly, and said that he knew Melf. Harkle was surprised and asked his age, and Robillard clarified that he didn't know him personally, but was familiar with his work. Robillard then cast Melf's minute meteors and Harkle commented "Better Melf".
