Tammert Landral was an apprentice wizard inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Tammert was a student of Blackstaff Academy within Blackstaff Tower in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . He was recognized by his teachers as having tremendous potential and a brilliant mind for magic .
