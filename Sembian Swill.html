Sembian Swill was a type of a distilled alcoholic drink found in the city of Westgate and the nation of Sembia .

Description

This concoction was distilled from furbid plant's root. It was dark in color and bitter to taste. Tavernkeepers often claimed that Sembian Swill was created with a single purpose—to topple a horse with a single gulp of the drink.

History

By the late 14 th century DR , Sembian Swill was served in many of Westgate's inns and bard, including The Track , the underground racing rink, and gambling establishment on the Market Triangle .
