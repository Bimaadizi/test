Inglore was a dwarven nobleman and brother of Lord Onglore who perished in the Weeping War of Myth Drannor . He eventually was transformed into a ghast who survived into 1369 DR .

Possessions

Inglore died wearing his trusty enchanted platinum +2 amulet of strength .

History

Inglore died alongside his brother defending Myth Drannor during the last months of the Weeping War. Their bodies were displayed in the Main Halls of the Dwarven Dungeons for people to pay respects. When the surviving citizens of Myth Drannor fled the fallen city, the Dwarven Dungeons, along with many other locations, were sealed using the elven words of power and glyphs to prevent grave-robbing and desecration. The bodies of the dwarven lord and his brother were not buried or entombed and were abandoned.

In 1369 DR, a group of adventurers from Phlan , while on a quest to stop the Cult of the Dragon , discovered the two dwarven brothers in a state of undeath and frenzied hunger. Now ghasts , both brothers were destroyed by the adventurers, granting them the peace of death.
