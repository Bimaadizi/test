Olafstaad was a small town located in the kingdom of Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Olafstaad was located on the Sea of Moonshae on the northern end of Prince's Sound on the western coast of Gnarhelm. It was the westernmost settlement in the kingdom.
