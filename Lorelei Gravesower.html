Lorelei Gravesower was a female half-elf cleric of Kelemvor who tended the Kelemvor's Garden cemetery in the town of Thunderstone in Cormyr in the late 1360s DR .

History

Lorelei became caretaker of the graveyard in the late 1360s DR, and renamed it from the "Thunderstone Cemetery".

Activities

Living out of a small house on the grounds of the cemetery, Lorelei's task was to provide counselling and advice to the grieving and conduct maintenance on the cemetery.

Rumors

A scurrilous rumor told that a woman dwelling in a hut beside the cemetery (perhaps Lorelei herself) would sell her favors to a man for a night, but this was a lie.
