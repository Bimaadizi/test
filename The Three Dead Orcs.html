The Three Dead Orcs was an inn in the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the 13 th century DR .

History

The Three Dead Orcs burned down and, perhaps due to its name, some very much alive orcs were thought to have encouraged the flames. Afterward, Riothar Orlsyr built a new inn called The Wizard's Hand to the north, some time around 1277 DR .
