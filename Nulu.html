Nulu was a shadovar warrior in Anauroch in 1479 DR .

History

In 1479 DR, Nulu was a veteran shadovar warrior who had served under captain Durbela many times.

She was chosen by her captain to be part of the mission to gain control of the Spiral Gate , a crucial portal nexus .

Personality

Nulu was vengeful, fearless, and sadistic. Only Durbela's words could control her.
