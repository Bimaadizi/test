Frulam Mondath was a member of the reformed Cult of the Dragon of Severin Silrajin , holding the rank of Wearer of the Purple.

Relationships

Mondath considered Langdedrosa Cyanwrath her right-hand, and often kept the half-dragon at her side.

History

She was the leader of the Cult army in the assault on Greenest , and Langdedrosa Cyanwrath usually followed her directives. At the Cult's camp near the city, Frulam deferred to the half-dragon Rezmir , who was her superior.

When adventurers raided the camp, Frulam fought them but was easily defeated and captured. She hesitantly told the adventurers what she knew about the Cult's plan to summon Tiamat , which was minimal.

Later, Frulam was held captive by the Harpers for trial.
