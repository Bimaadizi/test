Tracker Dullwill was a cultist of the Absolute who served his goddess during the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

He carried a crudely-crafted bow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , cutlists from the Shattered Sanctum launched a vicious raid on the Waukeen's Rest inn, taking several captives and killing many more. The goblinoid cultists returned tot he Sanctum to celebrate their victory with a great feast and many kegs of ale . Dullwill was among the goblins who got exceptionally drunk, and passed out across the exterior of the temple's courtyard.
