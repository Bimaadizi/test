Chaumyk Vorn was a male human Red Wizard and khazark of the Thayan enclave in Hillsfar .

Abilities

As a high-ranking Red Wizard and skilled evoker , Chaumyk was talented in the use of evocation spells that could be highly destructive in battle.

Activities

Chaumyk Vorn held the position of khazark in the Thayan enclave of Hillsfar .

History

In 1371 DR , land for a new Thayan enclave were purchased in Hillsfar with permission from Maalthiir . By 1372 DR , Chaumyk was the khazark of this new enclave but he was soon replaced in 1373 DR by Tola Vrass .
