Anammelech was a Talontar blightlord operating in the Rawlinswood as of 1373 DR .

Description

He had empty eye sockets that built up slime , with a sparkle at the back of them. His armor was composed of flowing slime plates, never reaching above his neckline; he kept his head bare and filthy. However, Anammelech was actually amorphous, the armor being just a form he preferred from time to time.

Personality

Annamelech was vindictive, , covetous, overconfident, and a bit of a jokester.

Abilities

Anammelech could use ooze mephits as extensions of his will. He could also transport himself through the ooze left behind by his mephits. Fire had no power over him, and he was capable of moving without shifting his weight, leaving behind a trail of slime. Finally, he could engulf his foes with his amorphous body.

History

In 1373 DR , Anammelech was sought out by Gloomgate , which teleported towards him once Gameliel was disintegrate d. He learned of Marrec and Ususi Manaallin 's party, as well as Araluen . He activated one of his agents, Fallon , bading him kidnap Araluen and fetch him the Keystone . After receiving the Keystone from Fallon, he set up an ambush for the party coming behind him. Though he managed to split Ususi and Marrec from Gunggari Ulmarra and Elowen , almost slaying the first two, Marrec barely managed to defeat him by employing his petrifying gaze against the blightlord just as the latter was about to personally engulf him.

Relationships

Anammelech coveted Gameliel 's Gloomgate , and he planned on taking revenge on Damanda for past slights. He cultivated the patronage of the Nentyar hunter Fallon , eventually turning him into betraying the Nentyarch .
