Locations

The shop was found in the south central part of the city.

Defenses

A pair of stone golems shaped like gynosphinxs guarded the establishment.

Services

Though the prices were certainly high, the shop did indeed provide magic items for anyone with enough coin to purchase them. Other available items included spell components , gems , and artwork. The shopkeeper was known for mercilessly haggling with adventurers to get their prices down until they agreed to his terms.

Inhabitants

As the name would imply, the shop was owned by a gruff man named Laris .
