The Gentle Rest Stables was a stable located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The stables stood two stories tall.

Location

The Gentle Rest Stables was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on both Deloun Alley and Quill Alley .

Inhabitants

The stables were ownedby Torst Urlivan in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .
