The Seven Traders were traders in Marsember as of the late 14 th century DR .

History

After the spring of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , when trying to gain access to lady Joysil Ambrur , the bard and Harper Roldro Tattershar threatened Elward Daunthideir , the steward of Haelithtorntowers , her residence, with revealing the steward's involvement in the dispute between the seven traders and the tax takers of the Marsember port a season prior.
