Ryckaert was a Netherese arcanist who worked at Ioulaum Industries in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Ryckaert worked under Ghazzalee , managing the research and development of the company. He wrangled the eccentric and addled geniuses that worked on mythallars , and through great effort kept them focused on being creative and productive.
