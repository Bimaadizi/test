An amulet of terror was a handglyph used by the Geomancers of Kadar .

Powers

This amulet cloaked the wielder in a purple aura of awful power. Anyone within a 30 feet (9.1 meters) radius of the wielder would be frozen in terror and unable to perform any other actions for several minutes.

Most amulets of terror had between 20—50 charges.

Description

These items could be found in a variety of shapes and sizes.
