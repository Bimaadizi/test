History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Yonas, Meygan , and Lassandra embarked on a mission through the Shadow-cursed Lands , having embarked from the Last Light Inn . During their expedition, Yonas strayed too far from the torchlight provided by his allies and was abducted by a shadow . In short order, he was transformed into a malformed husk that resembled something akin to a zombie .
