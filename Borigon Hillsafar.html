Borigon Hillsafar was the king of Clan Hillsafar in Vaasa in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In Year of the Second Circle, 1470 DR , the Warlock Knights of Vaasa invaded Hillsafar Hall . Borigon assumed command, taking on the mantle of king, and repelled the invaders. Afterward, he welcomed inside the Hall a handful of neighboring clans displaced during the war, and with great reluctance, ordered the gates to be closed and sealed. As of Year of the Second Circle, 1470 DR , the gates were still closed.

Relationship

Borigon was the third son of Garumbelly Hillsafar .
