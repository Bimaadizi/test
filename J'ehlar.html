J'ehlar was a member of the Flaming Fist who served Counselor Florrick and Duke Ulder Ravengard in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

J'ehlar had shoulder-length black hair and she wore a set of splint armor with a tabard over it decorated with the Flaming Fist cast.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist J'ehlar was a member of a Flaming Fist troop that accompanied Counselor Florrick and Duke Ravengard on a mission to Elturel . They stopped at the Waukeen's Rest inn, where the entourage fell victim to the Cult of the Absolute 's ambush in which Ulder Ravengard was kidnapped. Florrick and her Fists marched towards the Moonrise Towers to rescue the Duke but were waylaid by the dangers of the Shadow-cursed lands . Fist J'ehlar and the rest found shelter in the Last Light Inn , where they met High Harper Jaheira and her Harpers , who sought to end the life of General Ketheric Thorm , the leader of the Absolute's army.
