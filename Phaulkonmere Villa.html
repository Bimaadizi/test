Phaulkonmere Villa was the home of the Phaulkon noble family located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The villa was composed of four buildings, each standing two or three stories tall.

Location

Phaulkonmere Villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter . The villa sat in the south section of the Adventurers' Quarter on Snake Alley facing Kerrigan's Court .
