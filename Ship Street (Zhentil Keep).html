Ship Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Ship Street was located in Zhentil Keep's Harbor District in the south section of the city. The road ran west to east for a single block in between Canter Street and the gate to the Common Quarter and the docks.

Notable Locations

A pair of warehouses used by the Zhentarim flanked both sides of Ship Street near the docks.
