Sshansalue Wonderharp was a half-elf minstrel and adventurer of the Vast in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

She was beautiful and tall, and commonly clad in black leather armor .

Reputation

She was one of the more well-known itinerant minstrels wandering the Vast, and was hailed as an old friend in the village of Maskyr's Eye .
