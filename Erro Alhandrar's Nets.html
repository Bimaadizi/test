Erro Alhandrar's Nets was a small shop that sold fishing nets imported from Sheirtalar and the Tashalar , located on Saerdoun Street in the North Ward of Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Location

Erro Alhandrar's shop shared a three-story-and-an-attic building with a number of other businesses. The building stood on the southern side of Saerdoun Street, three doors west from the intersection with Thunderstaff Way . Other tenants of the office building were Tantra Jaressra's Fine Gowns , Demra Samdro's Best Beds , Laskalan's Lamps and Lanterns , and Vressa's Sweep Begone , as well as Tiger's Eye at one point.

History

Erro Alhandrar's Nets was established sometime before the building changed management in the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , now in the hands of the gnome named Hardyn Glorjarn , who took over the building's attic.
