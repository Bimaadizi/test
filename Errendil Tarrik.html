Duke Errendil Tarrik was the a noble of Impiltur during the 8 th century DR .

Relationships

Errendil was secretly a worshiper of fiends . Once tryst in particular led to the birth of his half-fiend son Argosh who was nicknamed "the Scaled" because of his infernal heritage.
