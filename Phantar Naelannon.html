Phantar Naelannon was an official in Scornubel in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

As one of the Lord High Advisors, Phantar aided Lady Rhessajan Ambermantle with governing the city with regards to profit and trade.

History

Phantar was a trader and caravan master before turning toward politics.
