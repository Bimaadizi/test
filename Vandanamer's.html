Vandanamer's was a popular luthdren (restaurant) located in Khôltar , south Faerûn , circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Location

This establishment was located near the western end of Hael Way on the south side of the street and on the southwest corner of the intersection with Galaglavur Street . Across Galaglavur Street was Krostur's Forge . Next door, to the west, was the Maerador House of Welcome and beyond that was Dubrinlar, the western gate into Khôltar. Across Hael Way was Manycoins Hall .

Description

This luthdren had a dining area that was one very large room with a high ceiling. The stone architecture made it a noisy, echoing place when full of customers.

Services

A very popular luthdren, Khôltans mainly went here for an evening meal of roasted meat and soup. Rothé and ox carcasses were turned on spits over open flames in the dining hall and the meat was trimmed off in full view of the customers. While the meat was served in thick slabs, the soups tended to be thin, like broth .
