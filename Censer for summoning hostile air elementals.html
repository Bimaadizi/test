A censer for summoning hostile air elementals was a cursed magic item that summoned an onslaught of angry air elementals .

Powers

This item summoned four enraged air elementals that attacked anything in sight. The elementals arrived one at a time over the course of several minutes. Once lit, this item could not be extinguished until either the user or air elementals were dead.

Notable Owners

Quelzur Naismen , a Red Wizard of Thay , had a censer for summoning air elementals that he used to power his pirate ship. Thankfully, his censer summoned air elementals that could be controlled.
