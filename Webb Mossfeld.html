Webb Mossfeld was a human of the village of West Harbor on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a brother of Wyl and Ward Mossfeld .

Activities

Webb, like his brothers, was a member of the West Harbor militia.

History

Webb was the bully of West Harbor, who would tease the Kalach-Cha , Bevil Starling and Amie Fern . They were the champions of the Harvest Fair for several years.

During the Harvest Fair in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Webb and his brothers competed in the Harvest Brawl. The Kalach-Cha, Amie, and Bevil, after defeating the Lannon siblings , would've faced Webb, Ward, and Wyl. If the Kalach-Cha, Amie, and Bevil were defeated, they could've asked Merring for a rematch or acknowledged that the Mossfelds had won.

During the attack of bladelings and duergar , the Kalach-Cha could've encountered Webb, who would fight the attackers. Once finished, he could've been asked to go to the field to defend the town.

In the subsequent attack on West Harbor by bladelings and duergar , the Mossfields aided in the defense. The Kalach-Cha, Bevil, and Amie may have encountered Webb fighting the attackers. Once finished, he could've been asked to go to the field to defend the town.

When the Kalach-Cha and companions later returned to the village to reassemble the Sword of Gith , they encountered Ward and his brothers. However, it was an ambush, as they had been slain and transformed into shadows .
