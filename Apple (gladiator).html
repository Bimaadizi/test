Apple was a druid and slave of Baeloth Barrityl .

Description

Apple fought with a spear and wore studded leather armor , including a helmet . She could cast some minor spells , such as cure light wounds and charm person or mammal .

History

Apple and her party were taken from a "shabby saloon" to the Black Pits . She was killed by the Champion of Baeloth's Pits and their party in the Black Pits arena.
