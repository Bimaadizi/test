Gondal was a human wizard living in Baldur's Gate on the Sword Coast in the mid–14 th century DR .

He was one of the most notable mages in the city and was known to be studious.

However, sometime before 1357 DR , he was killed by an ogre mage named Gornak who took his place and use his appearance and residence.
