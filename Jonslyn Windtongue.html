Jonslyn Windtongue was a halfling thief of some infamy, who operated in Amn , Tethyr , the Nelanther Isles , and sometimes even on the coastal lands of the Shining Sea in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the late 14 th century DR , Jonslyn Windtongue managed to steal one of Lantanna spellwebs . These weapons fed off magic , enchantments , and spells , unleashed and still held within a caster's mind. As such, spellwebs were rumored to be weapons used in defense of Lantan from pirates and other invading forces. The Ayrorch , the Council of Twelve dispatched the Hands of Gond to find the halfling thief and return what was stolen by all means.

Jonslyn went on a run and was rumored to hide somewhere in the lands of the Sea of Fallen Stars .
