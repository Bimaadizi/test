Sledge Pettifeet was a member of the Guild from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Sledge appeared to be a middle-aged halfling woman with light skin, brown eyes, and dirty blonde hair. She wore an archer 's leathers dyed in the Guild's green colors and carried a bow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Sledge Pettifeet was tasked with standing guard at the secret entrance into Baldur's Gate's Undercellar , where the Guild's Guildhall was hidden. Whilst a half-orcish enforcer Tusgront manned the main entrance, Sledge was ready to dispatch any unwanted guest from a distance.
