Air genesi armor was a type of intricate enchanted plate mail created by, and for, air genasi .

Description

These sets of armor were decorated with things of swirling designs that covered the entirety of their surface.

Powers

Air genesi armor granted the wearer protection against wind and air magic and increased their reflexes. When worn, the plate mail glowed with gentle blue radiance.

History

Air genesi armor were crafted for the use of air genasi, who, like the other genasi, were known to make great warriors. One of these magic armors fell into the hands of drow merchant of exotic goods – Nym sometime circa the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , as he camped in Icewind Dale 's Wandering Village .
