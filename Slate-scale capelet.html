The slate-scale capelet was a magical capelet found in the ruins of Tethyamar during the 1480s DR .

Description

The item was made from many tiny interlocking scale-like pieces of slate stone joined together.

Powers

The capelet greatly improved the wearer's strength and physical fortitude, protecting them from all kinds of threats and sources of harm.

History

The capelet was found in Ironvale Deep as the dwarves of the Tethyamar Mining Outpost and the drow of House Argith fought for the jewels of ardor .
