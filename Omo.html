Omo was a titan thane of the Jotunbrud Tribes of Merrouroboros during the Days of Thunder ( −35,000 DR to −30,000 DR ).

Omo killed the batrachi High One Zhoukoudien in battle around −31,500 DR , bringing an abrupt end to their golden age.
