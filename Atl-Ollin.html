Atl-Ollin was a high priest of Calor in Nexal .

History

Around 1351 DR , after a series of portents, Naltecona gathered all the high priests to ask them what the gods wished. Afterward, Naltecona questioned Atl-Ollin directly, who answered honestly that he did not understand the portents' meaning.
