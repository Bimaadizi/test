Olavi was one of the inhabitants of Daggerford who died and was buried long before 1372 DR .

History

Olavi was buried in the Daggerford Graveyard in the Eastern Farmlands outside the city walls. Her grave was a chest tomb to the east from the mortuary mausoleum at the graveyards center. Even though her grave's engravings hoped for her to one day rise, she still remained undisturbed in her death as of 1372 DR.
