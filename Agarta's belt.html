Agarta's belt was the personal belt of the brutal Night Brigade officer Agarta the Breaker in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The belt inherited Agarta's physical might, aiding its next wearer.

History

The accessory was worn by Agarta the Breaker in Wheloon Prison until she was slain by the forces of the Riverside Rats .
