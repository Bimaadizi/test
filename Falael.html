Falael was a laborer, packer, and a delivery woman of the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Falael was a young half wood elven woman with a light skin tone, short curly light brown hair, and brown eyes. She often wore a simple laborer's jacket over a tunic .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Falael was employed by Baldur's Mouth and could be found outside the broadsheet publisher's office counting papers packed for delivery. Counting in several hundreds was a challenge, and miscounting meant she had to start over.
