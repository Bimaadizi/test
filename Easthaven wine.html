Easthaven wine was an unnamed type of wine that was fermented locally in the town of Easthaven in Icewind Dale as early as the late 13 th century DR .

Description

This wine was made by the inhabitants of Easthaven, not professional wine-makers. Its ingredients were a mystery and the wine itself had a slight fishy scent.

History

Circa the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , Easthaven wine was available for purchase from Pomab Ak'azmhir , the proprietor of Pomab's Emporium in Easthaven. The local drunk named Old Jed was an avid consumer of the concoction.
