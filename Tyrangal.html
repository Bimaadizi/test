Tyrangal , or Gaulauntyr , was a copper dragon who lived in a large, heavily warded mansion in an abandoned section of Ormpetarr , in the guise of a human female.

Description

In her copper dragon form, Tyrangal had broad bat -like wings, teeth as long as a man's forearm, and shiny copper-colored scales as smooth as polished glass . While in human form she was taller than average for the race and had copper-colored skin, round golden eyes, and long metallic auburn hair that ran down to her knees.

Abilities

Tyrangal knew a wide variety of psionic powers, which she used as though she was an egoist . Her known psionic powers included the following:

The most notable of these powers was glorytongue , the namesake of her more familiar nickname, which she used alongside remote viewing and unseen servant to steal gems and magic items from others.

Possessions

While in her human form Tyrangal typically wore a rust-colored, silk robe that was embroidered with runes.

Relationships

Tyrangal was a friend and mentor to the rogue Duvan .

History

Tyrangal was known as Gaulauntyr prior to the Spellplague . She was affected by the Rage and went into self-imposed exile. At some point after the Spellplague she moved to Ormpetarr and established herself as one of the powers in the city. In 1479 DR she became entangled in a scheme by the Order of Blue Flame to enlarge the area of the Plaguewrought Land and was seriously injured in a fight to stop the order from carrying out their plan.
