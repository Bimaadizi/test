Haeromos Dothwintyl was the ruler of Port Llast .

Description

A retired stonemason, Haeromos carried himself in a proud manner and wore the finest clothing, armor, and weaponry money could buy. The source of this wealth came from the taxes of the people of Port Llast.

Possessions

The First Captain wielded a longsword +3 enspelled so it could send a message to the wizard , Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun when Haeromos was in danger or the village was attacked.

History

Haeromos would be slain during the Deepwater War part of the Twelfth Serôs War with the Sahuagin in Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR by a force of crabmen that was raiding Port Llast and preventing the fishing boats from leaving harbour. Sargent Kendrack of the Port Llast militia would be promoted to First Captain after helping 'Hylas of Elturel', an arrogant knight of Torm’s fury sent by the Lords' Alliance, to free the crabmen from the sorcerous stranglehold of the Sahuagin.
