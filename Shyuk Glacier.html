The Shyuk Glacier was a large glacier nestled within the steep peaks of the Katakoro Shan mountain range in southern Ra-Khati .

Geography

The glacier measured roughly 60 mi (97 km) in length and 30 mi (48 km) wide at its farthest points. As of 1367 DR it had begun to creep out of the mountains into the foothills bordering the northern boundary of the Wild Yak Meadows and Katakoro Plateau .
