Mangobarl Lorren was the baker of Red Larch during the Elemental Evil crisis. He ran his shop in that village.

Relationship

He was a staunch and discreet ally of the Zhentarim , thanks to some of his distant relatives who were in that organization. He told them all about what he heard in the village.

Personality

He loved to gossip and knew all kinds of wild tales, few of them true.

History

In 1491 DR , he heard about Pell Mhandyvver 's scare at the Haunted Tomb and went to look for himself, discovering a goblin .
