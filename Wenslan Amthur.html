Wenslan Amthur was a priest of Deneir at the temple of Deneir in the city of Procampur in the Vast .

History

By circa 1361 DR , Amthur was Prelate or Hierarch of the temple of Deneir in Procampur, where he had the title of High Scrivener .

Amthur permitted a historian writing a history of the Tuigan Horde to examine the temple's complete copy of Vilhiard 's A Discovery of the World .

The High Scrivener praised the lama Koja of Khazari , who was a guest at his temple in the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR , for his wisdom and knowledge. The priest Foxe promised to have the High Scrivener distribute Koja's new work, A History of the Tuigan , to every other temple of Deneir.
