Swordsman's belt were enchanted belts created by Vangerdahast Aeiulvana for the distinguished Purple Dragons of Cormyr .

Powers

These were two types of these belts. Regular swordsman's belts and much more powerful greater swordsman's belts . Both types were enchanted to shield their wearers from slashing damage; however, the greater versions of swordsman's belts' enchantments were significantly more potent.

History

Sometime before King Azoun Obarskyr IV perished in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , he commissioned these belts from his Royal Magician Vangerdahast Aeiulvana to honor the most dedicated and venerated Purple Dragons of his realm. By 1372 DR , his reigning daughter Alusair Obarskyr continued the tradition, awarding swordsman's belts and sometimes even offering them to capable adventurers as an incentive to serve the Dragon Throne.

By the late 14 th century DR , there were quite a few of these enchanted belts scattered around the Sword Coast , found in Neverwinter 's various shops , and temples of Hillsfar .
