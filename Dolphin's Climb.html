The Dolphin's Climb was a river located in Dambrath .

Description

The Dolphin's Climb flowed from the southwestern range of the Gnollwatch Mountains into the Bay of Dancing Dolphins .

Locations

The city of Herath was located along the banks of the Dolphin's Climb.
