Dorhun was a stone giant living in Gracklstugh in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Dorhun was one of the apprentices of Stonespeaker Hgraam , leader of Clan Cairngorm . Circa 1486 DR , his fellow apprentice Rihuud went mad and went on a rampage in the streets. Dorhun was tasked to take him back alive to their quarters.
