RaceBlack dragon

Zarkursk was the evil old black dragon who was an enemy of the dwarves of Clan Stonehand sometime before 1372 DR .

History

Zarkursk laired within a swamp in close vicinity to Clan Stonehand's mine. Given their close proximity, the dragon and the dwarves developed a bitter rivalry that culminated in Clan Stonehand's young warriors planning and executing a fateful attack on Zarkursk. They successfully slew the beast and inherited the sizable hoard that consisted mostly out of precious gems .
