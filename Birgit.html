Birgit of the Iron House was a respected dwarven sage, old even for a dwarf in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

She had a surprisingly deep voice.

Abilities

She was able to cast the spells command , detect evil , detect lie , detect magic , dispel magic , find traps , glyph of warding , hold person , know alignment , light , meld into stone , tongues , and withdraw .

Possessions

She walked with a walking stick and wore a cloak of displacement and a ring of blinking . She also possessed a wand of earth and stone .

Activities

She sought to find and reclaim the old wonders made by dwarves during the time of Delzoun .

Relationships

She was training up her niece Tamara to become a sage, and was very protective of the younger dwarf.

History

In the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , she had gotten her hands upon an ancient map showing the location of old Hammerkeep and something called the ship of night and was seeking adventurers to find out what it was and whether it could be reclaimed or reconstructed.
