Morghal was a duergar slaver and a cleric of Laduguer who took up residence in Grymforge during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

She had dark-gray skin and wore leather armor .

Relationships

She hated True Soul Nere , one of the lesser lieutenants in the Cult of the Absolute .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Morghal and her fellow slavers from the Clan Flameshade aligned themselves with the Cult of the Absolute, as the cultists began to excavate the fortress of Grymforge , with the hope of uncovering the Admantine Forge . She and her cultists raided myconid and deep gnome communities for slave labor, but were not promptly paid for their efforts by Nere.
