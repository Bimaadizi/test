Rocky was one of the favorite and most loyal hounds of the Durinbold patriar family of Baldur's Gate , who died sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

Rocky's sarcophagus contained his body, a potion of glorious vaulting , and three harpy feathers.

History

By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Chief Houndmaster Pol was long dead and interred within the Durinbold Mausoleum of the Lower City Graveyard , surrounded by the interred bodies of his favorite beasts, Gordis , Rocky, and Zina . The trainer and his three dogs ended up as ghosts that would attack anyone who dared to invade and disturb the Durinbold family tombs. Rocky's sarcophagus was inscribed with: "Brave and loyal Rocky, Who saved his master from an ambush."
