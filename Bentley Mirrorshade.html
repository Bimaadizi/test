Bentley Mirrorshade was an industrious gnome illusionist who ran the Friendly Arm Inn .

Description

Bentley had curly hair and a very large nose.

Personality

Always clever and alert, Bentley had a habit of humming when in deep thought and scratching his very large nose when concerned.

Possessions

Bentley was never short of money and it was rumored he had a portal nearby that led to an unknown location. Bentley was concerned about the iron shortage around the Sword Coast .

History

In 1346 DR , Bentley abandoned his life as an adventurer to run the Friendly Arm Inn after he and his comrades cleared it out.
