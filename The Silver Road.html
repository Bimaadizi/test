The Silver Road linked Silverymoon to Sundabar and was 165 miles long.

Starting at Silverymoon the road passed by Khelb before entering Silverymoon Pass . The road exited Silverymoon Pass at Auvandell , and continued on to terminate at Sundabar. Between Khelb and Auvandell inside Silverymoon Pass near the top of the pass was the watchtower Hawk's Nest . The road was patrolled by the Knights in Silver , from Silverymoon, and by troops from Sundabar to stop monsters and bandits preying on the road's traffic.
