Winter Path was a lesser avenue, paved in corduroy-logs, street in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This street ran from northeast to southwest, running off of Coffinmarch and terminating at the High Road alongside Burnt Wagon Way . Close to where it terminated, the road Ironpost Street jutted eastward off of it.
