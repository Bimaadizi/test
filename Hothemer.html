The Hothemer ( pronounced : /ˈ h oʊ t ɛ m ɛ r / HOT-em-er or : /ˈ h oʊ θ ɛ m ɛ r / HOTH-em-er ) family was part of the nobility of Waterdeep , gaining nobility status in Year of the Cockatrice, 1248 DR .

Organization

As of the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , the matriarch of the Hothemer family was Malas Hothemer , with Citta Hothemer as consort. Chynna Hothemer was his eldest daughter and heir. During this period, there were seventeen living members of the Hothemer noble family.

Activities

House Hothemer gained their fortune trading, and owned a great fleet of caravans and wagons.

History

The family first received their nobility ranking in Year of the Cockatrice, 1248 DR .

Having been turned into a troll while under the influence of fell magic, eight-year-old Chynna accidentally killed her two elder brothers, Hathin and Robin, circa 1367 DR . Her affliction was cured by Lord Maskar Wands . Consequently, the family went into deep mourning for their two sons and late heirs.

Base of Operations

The Hothemer family owned a noble villa located in the North Ward of Waterdeep .
