Duergar-Forged Axes were huge perfectly-balanced enchanted battle axes of dull gray steel , forged and ensorcelled by the duergar dwarves underneath the Spine of the World .

Powers

The sharpness of these dangerous axes was evident that the duergar smiths used magic in their creation. Duergar-forged axes possessed +1 enchantments to the weapons' aim and damage, while their keen blades were ready to cleave the opponents.

History

In Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , one such ax belonged to Barud Barzam , a duergar leader who was in charge of an underground outpost in the River Caves , where the Black Raven River spills from under the Spine of the World . Barud offered his duergar-forged axe to anyone willing to descend into the lower caverns underneath the outpost to exterminate hook horrors and mustard jellies who infested the area.
