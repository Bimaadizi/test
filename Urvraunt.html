Urvraunt was an enclave of Netheril that was destroyed during Karsus's Folly , notable for its distance from Netheril.

Geography

When the enclave crashed, it came to a rest near the Yellow Sea at the eastern end of the Mountains of Amara , north of the Talmaruk Islands .

History

After crashing during Karsus's Folly in −339 DR , the enclave was ruined but remained somewhat intact. Due it its incredibly remote location, it stayed completely unknown to the wider world through the 15 th century DR .
