Faheed was a veteran adventurer who lived in Amkethran circa the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .

History

After a long time adventuring, Faheed and his companion Majira moved to Amkethran to deal with the powerful monk Balthazar . They bought a key to Balthazar's monastery from local smugglers and aimed to kill the Bhaalspawn for all the trouble he had brought to the village.
