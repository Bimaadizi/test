Ebenz Farbough was a farmer in the Moonsea in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Ebenz and his wife Bea Farbough managed a farmhouse near Hillsfar . When the city's First Lord Torin Nomerthal proclaimed again the Great Law of Humanity , expelling all non- humans , Ebenz and Bea decided to house the ten abandoned children living in Madame Delilah's Home for Orphaned Children .

A year later, Bea gave birth to six sons, so things became even more chaotic.

Trouble arrived the year after, circa 1486 DR , when the tiefling boy Pynroth disappeared.
