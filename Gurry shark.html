Gurry sharks were a variety of shark found in the Sea of Moving Ice .

Description

These sharks were quite large.

Ecology

Diet

Gurry sharks were primarily scavengers.

Usages

The meat of these sharks was poisonous if not properly prepared, causing a sort of drunken-stupor when eaten. Preparation of their meat commonly involved fermenting and drying over a period of many months
