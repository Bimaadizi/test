Calantar's Way ( pronounced : /ˈ k ɑː l ɑː n t ɛər / CAH-lan-tare ) was a long road running north to south in the nation of Cormyr . The northernmost point of Calantar's Way was the city of Arabel . The southernmost point of Calantar's Way was the city of Suzail .

Geography

Calantar's Way crossed the Starwater River at Calantar's Bridge , just south of the town of Hilp .

Description

Calantar's Way had been well-cared-for over the years by those who used it.

History

Calantar's Way was surveyed and built by the Calantar noble family in the late 900s DR as a cart road.
