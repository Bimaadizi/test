Skullcap was a type of mushroom found in the Underdark .

Usage

This fungus was lethal and used by drow poison makers. It was potentially dangerous on contact even raw.

History

The drow of Zesraena cultivated skullcaps for their poison-making, letting them grow throughout their settlement. The scholastic Una Soverson collected interesting toxins while visiting the Whispering Caverns , and sought skullcap mushrooms for her samples.
