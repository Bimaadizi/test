Essimuth's Equipment was a shop that sold tools and adventuring gear located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Location

Essimuth's Equipment was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter where Weeping Maiden's Run joined Slop Street next door to the Temple of Good Cheer .

Inhabitants

The shop was owned and run by Essimuth in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
