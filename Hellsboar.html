Hellsboars were a species of semi-intelligent fiends that resembled ordinary boars of the Prime Material plane .

Description

These boar-like fiends moved on four hoofed legs. Their stout and powerful bodies were covered in red leather with obsidian-black bony protrusions on their joints, along the spines, covering their heads, with large jagged bone spikes growing out of their shoulder blades. Hellsboars' black bony heads resembled skulls with small eyes and maws glowing with hellfire from the inside. Two forward-facing horns pointed forward from their snouts.

Lesser hellsboars were medium-sized creatures that weighted 143.3 pounds (65 kilograms) on average.

Abilities

In battle, hellsboars relied on brute strength, charging at their prey horns first. The creatures possessed darkvision .

Ecology

Hellsboars were known to serve stronger fiends in the battles of the Blood War , alongside Zariel 's cambions and imps .

A golden variant of these creatures could be conjured forth via the summon gilded hellsboar spell .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Zariel's hellish forces attacked a plane-hopping nautiloid that served the Cult of the Absolute . Aboard, cambions, imps, and hellsboars, under the command of Commander Zhalk faced illithid forces and surviving group of True Soul adventurers who broke out after being captured on Toril .
