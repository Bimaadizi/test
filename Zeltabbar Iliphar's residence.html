Zeltabbar Iliphar's residence was a row house and the home of Zeltabbar Iliphar located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The home stood three stories tall.

Location

Zeltabbar Iliphar's residence was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on northwest side of the Court of the White Bull .
