Opoboquo Valley was a valley located in the Keryjek Ridge of the Great Glacier .

Geography

Opoboquo Valley was a narrow valley.

Flora & Fauna

The valley was the only known home of the rare Opoboquo remorhaz . An Opoboquo remorhaz was only about 10 feet (3 meters) long, light green in color, and not nearly as hot as its cousins. Angulutiuns hunted the Opoboquo remorhaz to use its shell to make jakerek , a fast caribou sled.
