Traveler's Cloak Inn was an inn in Mulmaster on the Moonsea .

Structure

The inn was a welcoming establishment where each room had a fireplace.

Staff

The Traveler's Cloak was run by Dela and Vesvur Alder .

Activities

Vesvur was a Harper agent who reported to Chesslyn Onaubra .
