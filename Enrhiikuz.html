Enrhiikuz was a mind flayer mercenary and resident of Sschindylryn during the mid-1480s DR .

Abilities

Enrhiikuz possessed the powers of ego whip , mind blast , mind thrust , and frost lance .

History

The mind flayer waited near House Szind for work during their invasion of Eveningstar .
