Zabardan Barpar was a shipwright inhabiting Waterdeep , the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Zabardan was the public spokesperson for the Order of Master Shipwrights circa 1357 DR . He worked out of the Shipwrights' House , which was their headquarters on the corner of Asteril's Way and Dock Street in the Dock Ward .
