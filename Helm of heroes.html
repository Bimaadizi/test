Helms of heroes were enchanted helmets that offered limited protection to the wearer, popularized in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

Helms of heroes slightly improved the wearer's protection against mundane and magical dangers, as well as spell effects. The enchantments also made the helmets bright and shiny, never needing a polish.

History

In the 15 th century DR, various helms of heroes could be found among bandits and heroes of the Sword Coast , areas surrounding the city of Neverwinter .
