Ulvryn Hamdarakh was the Sage of Saelmur , active circa 1284 DR .

History

In the Year of the Dying Stars, 1284 DR , Ulvryn Hamdarakh penned and published a collection of ruminations on the topic of mortality, aptly titled Musings on Mortality . In the book, Ulvryn conceded his thought to a simple point: whatever the purpose of life was, it was of great amusement to the gods.
