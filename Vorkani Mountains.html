The Vorkani Mountains were a mountain range that arced across the land of Koryo in northeastern Kara-Tur , lying at the end of the great Koryo Peninsula . They were also known as the Sillan Mountains .

Geography

The headwaters of the Malu River lay high in the Sillan Mountains.

Locations

The Vorkani Mountains defined much of Koryo as a rough mountainous country. The towns of Manchar and Xi lay in or close beside the Vorkani Mountains, and the village of Mu Tong stood near the source of the Malu River. The mountains separated the settlements of T'o na'Chee and Xi from the rest of Koryo. One could view the mountains from Xai Chung .

Inhabitants

Circa 1357 DR , the Panthers of Xi ninja controlled the northern mountains between Xi and the Malu River in the east.

Somewhere, high in the mountains, likely near the town of Pan in the west, the yakuza wu jen No Ziar claimed a keep, in which he stored his hidden wealth.

A handsome breed of "birdmen" ruled the tallest peaks of the mountains. They were enigmatic to the folk of Koryo. They were later theorized to be crane hengeyokai , forming a larger-than-normal flock.

Fauna

The monstrous Krestung lurked in the depths of the Vorkani Mountains. Packs of these creatures raided even the best-guarded caravans, snatching away horses and humans alike.

There were also giant spiders to be found in the mountains.

Rumors and legends

It was told that the ancient Han people had hidden a great hoard of jade somewhere in the Sillan Mountains.
