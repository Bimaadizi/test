Al-Haul River was a small river found in the Free Cities of North Zakhara .

Geography

The river flowed down from the Furrowed Mountains on a northwestern course before reaching the Great Sea .

Description

Qadib , the City of Wands, was founded along Al-Haul's eastern bank. As with all rivers found along the coast of North Zakhara, the lands surrounding the banks of Al-Haul as it neared the coast were highly fertile, allowing the city and its inhabitants to flourish.
