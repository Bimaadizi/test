Caer Westphal was a fortified castle that overlooked the city of the same name on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Caer Westphal was located on the southern coast on Snowdown Ride along the Sea of Swords overlooking the cove that served as a harbor for the city.

Structure

The castle was a stone fortress similar in layout to Caer Callidyrr on a smaller scale, with high walls and many towers. A sealed dungeon was located beneath the castle.

History

Caer Westphal was the only true castle on Snowdown, and was the seat of the monarchs of the Kingdom of Snowdown . Inhabitants

Following the annexation of Snowdown by Amn in the early 15 th century DR, the remaining members of the Kincaid royal family were kept imprisoned within the castle.

The castle was ruled by Lady Erliza Daressin , a vampire from Amn , as of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .
