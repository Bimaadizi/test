Blurg was an orog member of the Society of Brilliance in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Details of Blurg's early life were unknown, though he eventually joined the Society of Brilliance alongside Omeluum , Grazilaxx , Sloopidoop , Y , and Skriss . Blurg and all the other members of the Society went on a pilgrimage to Gravenhollow some years before the demonic crisis that struck the Underdark circa the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR .

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Blurg was studying alongside Omeluum at the Ebonlake Grotto .

Subsequently, Blurg and Omeluum departed the Ebonlake Grotto and returned to the Lodge of the Society of Brilliance in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate . There he and other members of the Society may have been murdered by Ptaris , a githyanki hatched from an egg acquired by Lady Esther , whose development had been magically accelerated as part of an experiment run by the Society.
