Activities

He led his brethren into battle with a fury that unnerved allies and enemies both. He had never left the Deep Realm but longed for the day when he would led his kin outside to drive back their foes.

Personality

He was a proud follower of the Father of Battle, Clangeddin Silverbeard , and looked forward to venturing forth and slaying all enemies of dwarvenkind.

Possessions

He strode to battle clad in enchanted banded mail , and wielding an enchanted battleaxe . He carried on him a silver horn of Valhalla , and a heward's handy haversack sized for dwarfs.
