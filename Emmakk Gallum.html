Emmakk Gallum was the younger brother of Jordy Gallum , Counut of Greenshores and the Constable of Mosstone circa 1370 DR .

History

Sometime before 1370 DR, Emmakk Gallum succeeded his older brother, Jordy Gallum, as the Constable of Mosstone , a lush green town on the edge of the Forest of Tethir along the Trade Way . In his new position, he was one of the biggest authorities in Mosstone, apart from the local druids , however little actual authority there was in the town. Emmakk led a small guard and defenders troop of twenty-five and occasionally acted as Mosstone's representative.
