Udzgul Thairharrow was a farmer in the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the late 13 th or early 14 th century DR .

Personality

He was considered to be half-witted.

History

When the folk of Maskyr's Vale came to investigate the collapse of old Maskyr's Tower , Udzgul claimed to have spotted a silvery doorway appearing for an instant in the air high over the ruins at sunset, but no-one believed him because they thought him a half-wit. Some others reported the same thing later, but they too were dismissed. Despite this, Khonduil Ammargath , who'd been in the tower when it fell, felt Udzgul was likely to have been right in what he saw.

Udzgul was deceased by the late 1350s DR.
