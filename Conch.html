Conch shell was a generic name for a number of different varieties of sea snail shell. A large specimen in good condition had a base value of 10 gp .

Usages

In Maztica 's Payit region, conch-shells used to craft battle horns and horns to signal sacrificial processions. In a similar manner, the Waterdhavian Watch used conch horns to sound the alarms in the city harbors.

Some priests of Umberlee , namely Magat of Lathtarl's Lantern , wore helmets carved out of large conch shells, worth 15 gp undamaged.

Aquatic species, like morkoth , used large conch shells affixed to belts, as wearable pouches or containers.
