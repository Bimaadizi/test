Juetta Baelcrest was a brewmistress and the proprietor of the Old Dirty Dwarf inn and tavern in the city of Helm's Hold during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Juetta directed the Old Dirty Dwarf to cater to newcomers to Helm's Hold. While she welcomed spellscarred individuals into her establishment, some of her staff went against her wishes and discouraged their patronage.

In addition to running the inn and tavern, Juetta regularly tended the taproom bar herself.

History

Ever the intrepid entrepreneur, Juetta managed to keep the inn open and running during the Siege of Helm's Hold in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .

She stepped down as innkeeper of the Old Dirty Dwarf some time during or before the Year of the Iron Dwarf's Vengeance, 1485 DR , when it was taken over by Kharissa Anuvien and renamed the Venturer's Rest.
