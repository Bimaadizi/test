Tymora's Melody was a bardic song, imbued with blessings of the Lady Luck.

Content

When performed by a bard, gifted with the use of the Art , the song magically gave the singer's allies luck. The luck improved their chances of resisting malevolent spells , avoiding danger, and significantly improved rogueish skills.

History

By the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , Tymora's Melody was performed by some skilled bards. It was heard being performed in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale and the village of Kuldahar .
