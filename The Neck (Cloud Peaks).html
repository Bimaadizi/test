The Neck was a mountain pass in the Cloud Peaks of Amn .

Geography

The Neck was far east of Fang Pass , the route that the Trade Way took through the Fangs . It followed the valley of the Amnurflow from Lake Weng into the mountains, passing near the fortress of Citadel Amnur and beneath Scarsiir's Crag . It emerged on the northern side of the Cloud Peaks, distantly east of Nashkel and the Uldoon Trail .
