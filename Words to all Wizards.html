Words to all Wizards was a book written by Melliyechorl Varast , Master Teacher of Archmages of the Inner Fire , also known as the Defenders of Halruaa , and published in the Year of the Bright Blade, 1347 DR .

Contents

It was a collection of the mage's advice.

History

The original was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep around 1372 DR .
