Uilken was a pathetic dwarf rogue inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Uilken did not let his lack of basic dexterity prevent him from attempting to earn a living as a thief . His home was located along Tymora Alley in north central Daggerford behind the Harvest House .
