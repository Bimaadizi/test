The Wallward was a loosely defined poor neighborhood located in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan in the late 14 th century DR .

Location

Wallward bordering the Golden City's interior outer wall. The buildings found here along the wall were much lower quality when compared to those located inward.

Activities

Wallward was usually crammed with visiting travelers, adventurers , and anyone else wishing to save some money within the expensive city.
