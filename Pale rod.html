The pale rod was a magical club brought to Cormyr during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar .

Description

The device was a short wooden rod, capped with metal at each end.

Powers

The rod greatly empowered the spellcaster's ability to cast necrotic and poison-related spells, while protecting them against radiant light damage.

History

The pale rod was brought from Sschindylryn by House Szind necromancers when they raided the Deep Glen Graveyard .
