The Hill giant language , referred to in Jotun as Jotunhaug , was a language derived from Jotun spoken by both hill giants and mountain giants . It was closely related to Jotunise, the language of frost giants .

Speakers

In addition to the hill giants spread across the breadth of Faerûn , the hill giant language was used among many of the merchants who did business within the Shining Lands of the Shining South . It was also a common secondary language among oreads .
