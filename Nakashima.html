Nakashima was an island located in Kozakura .

Geography

Nakashima was located roughly at the midpoint between Tenmei and Shinkoku . The island was infamous for the dangerous reefs that surrounded the coast.

Inhabitants

Nakashima was inhabited by fishermen who safely sailed the waters around the island in fishing boats with shallow drafts. They made a profitable living from fishing for abalone , eel and shellfish. They also harvested pearls from oysters . Wako were occasionally seen in the shallow waters around the island.
