Lake Eredruie was an exceptionally beautiful pool of water that was technically a large pond rather than a lake. It was the source the Glaemril river in the area of Deepingdale . It was sacred to the followers of Labelas Enoreth and guarded by both the Dalesmen and moon elves of Bristar for its supernatural powers.

Description

The pond was renowned for the healing property of its waters. One vial of gathered water acted as a potion of healing , as potent as the spell cure light wounds , when consumed by an elf or half-elf .
