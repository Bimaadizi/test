Lupperdiddle Swires was an infamous small and high-vaulting gnomish rascal who was active sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

History

Lupperdiddle was known for his numerous daring raids and burglaries of henhouses. By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , and enchanted pair of boots called Swiresy shoes was in existence, possibly created and named in tribute to the diminutive cavorting thief . Another item from Lupperdiddle's adventuring career was called Swires' Sledboard , a flat shield that he once used to slide down a slope, away from his enemies. This shield would end up in stock at the Rivington General shop of Baldur's Gate .
