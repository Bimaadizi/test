Myrkiel's Rest was an inn of Narthil in the 3 rd century DR .

Location

Myrkiel's rest was in Narthil .

Description

There was a sign with the name of the inn written over the door.

Interior

The inn had windows, and plastered walls.

Services

Food was served at the inn, including butter-bread, rabbit stew, hot cider, and sausage.

History

When the Mystran priestess Elmara, who was secretly Elminster Aumar , prince of Athalantar , entered Narthil and challenged the occupying forces from Athalantar, the magelings sought her there and attacked her. She repelled them successfully, and spent the night in the inn.

Staff

The innkeeper was Asmartha .
