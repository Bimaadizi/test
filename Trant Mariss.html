Trant Mariss was one of the inhabitants of Daggerford who died and was buried long before 1372 DR .

History

In life, Trant was known fo this speed. After his death, he was buried in the Daggerford Graveyard just outside the walls of the city in the Eastern Farmlands . His headstone was located several feet away from the main structure of the graveyard, the mortuary mausoleum. Circa 1372 DR, his headstone was in good shape with its writings still quite legible.
