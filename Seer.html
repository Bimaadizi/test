The Seer was a leader of drow rebels and a priestess of Eilistraee at the rebel drow encampment of Lith My'athar in 1372 DR .

Activities

She gathered several people to stand against the Valsharess , including Nathyrra and Valen Shadowbreath . She enlisted help to fight the army of the Valsharess.
