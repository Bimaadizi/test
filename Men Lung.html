Men Lung Chen-Shan-Tien-Kung-Te , or simply Men Lung , was a great lung dragon of the Celestial Empire .

Relationships

Men Lung had at least seven sons; the seventh was Mei Lung Chen-Shan-Tien-Kung-Te , who would be appointed Grand Historian of the Celestial Bureaucracy. Men Lung was the great-grandson of Chih Shih Chen-Shan-Tien-Kung-Te , one of the Nine Immortals and the god of history. Men Lung told the story of the creation of the Shao Mountains by the Nine Immortals and the Celestial Emperor to Mei Lung, and had heard it from his father, and so on back to Chih Shih himself.
