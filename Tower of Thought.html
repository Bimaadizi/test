The Tower of Thought was a temple dedicated to Oghma , the Lord of Knowledge.

Location

The temple was located in Selgaunt in Sembia .

Inhabitants

Most High Learned Priest Urdiyvan Eraen led the faithful of Oghma in the Tower of Thought.
