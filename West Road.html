The West Road was a trade route along the River Arkhen that stretched from Archenbridge in the south to White Ford in the north of Archendale .

Geography

The road was located to the east of River Arkhen and to the west of Arch Wood , providing a safer passage for traders traveling through the Dale.

Travel

The road stretched for about 45 miles (72 kilometers) between Archenbridge and White Ford.

The West Road was overlooked by the old fortress called Swordpoint towering above the southern end of the road and the Arkhen Ford .
