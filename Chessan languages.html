The Chessan language group was a part of the Faerûnian language family and included the language of Chessenta .

Subgroups

The Chessan group contained two diverse subgroups, the Chessentic group—which included languages spoken around the Sea of Fallen Stars near Chessenta—and Akalaic —including languages spoken in the Shaar , the Shining South , and the Chultan Peninsula .
