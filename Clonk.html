Clonk was a kenku mechanic who was a member of the Knucklebones Gang in Avernus in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Clonk was a large, strong kenku.

Activities

Circa 1492 DR , Clonk sometimes drove Mad Maggie 's infernal war machine .

Possessions

Clonk lost its weapon, before replacing it with a hellfire warhammer .

Personality

Clonk was fairly silent.

Relationships

Clonk and Chukka , known together as "the Magpies", were members of the Knucklebones Gang and were loyal to Mad Maggie.
