The blooded greataxe was a minor enchanted axe from the city of Elturel in the Western Heartlands .

Description

The weapon was covered in brown caked blood from its countless victims, emitting a potent stench of iron .

Powers

The blooded greataxe was enchanted to become more deadly, the closer its wielder was to death.

History

This grim-looking axe was once wielded by a notorious and deranged killer whose deeds were so grim that their name was magically removed from everyone's memories and records. By the late 15 th century DR , the weapon. found its way into the hands of blacksmith Dammon of Elturel.
