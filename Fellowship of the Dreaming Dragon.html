The Fellowship of the Dreaming Dragon was an adventuring company of the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . It consisted of Caledan Caldorien , Kera , the mage Morhion Gen'dahar , Ferret Talondim the thief , Tyveris , and the halfling Estah .
