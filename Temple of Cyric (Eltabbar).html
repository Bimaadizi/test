The Temple of Cyric was a temple dedicated to Cyric , the Prince of Lies, in the city of Eltabbar in Thay during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The temple was located in the northwest section of the city along the banks of the River Eltar where it entered Eltabbar. The Temple of Loviatar stood to its south along the river.
