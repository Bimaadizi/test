Waldo Walnut was a patron of the Elfsong Tavern from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Waldo was a well-dressed male rock gnome with a light skin tone, dark brown eyes, and short curly brown hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Waldo Walnut was a frequent customer of the Elfsong Tavern in the Eastway neighborhood of Baldur's Gate. He attended the "Laff Riot" show at the Elfong, a stand-up comedy performance hosted by Harvard Willoughby in Elfsong's side room with a stage. Waldo hoped the show would entertain him, as he heard that Harvard was a peculiar man and had a "smashing monocle ."
