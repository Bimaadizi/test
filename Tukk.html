Tukk was a small village in the Alpuk region within the Great Glacier .

Geography

Tukk was located on the eastern shore of the Nakalpgotak Sea , near Novularond .

Inhabitants

Tukk was an impovershed village. As a result, the villagers were desperate to trade meat for clothing and weapons.
