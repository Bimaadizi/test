The Simbul was, in ancient times, the title given to a Yuir power whom some described as the goddess of "the edge and the moment of choice."

History

The Simbul, like all the Yuir powers, was worshiped first by primitive humans who dwelled in the Yuirwood , then by the Yuir elves that came to live there, and was later forgotten.

However, around midnight on Eleasis 25 in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , the queen of Aglarond who had become her better-known namesake, the Simbul , had a conversation with her even as a battle raged in the Sunglade .
