The Mistkeep was a temple devoted to Leira , the Lady of the Mists, located on Prespur in the Sea of Fallen Stars during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The temple was notable for the "Pillars of Smoke," which were also called "the Pipepillars."
