Vance was a man that lived in the Realms during the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

He was a handsome young man, with wild black hair.

Relationships

Vance was childhood friends with the future mercenary hero and god of the dead, Kelemvor Lyonsbane .

History

In the Year of the Spur, 1348 DR , Vance was killed by a band of assassins . Vance's friend Kelemvor was powerless to save his freinds life, due to the curse of lycanthropy that transformed him into a vicious beast any time he performed a good deed beyond self interest.
