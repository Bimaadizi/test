Holloway was a Netherese town and archaeological site within the Hollow during the time of ancient Netheril .

Geography

The town was located inside the Hollow on the edge of Netheril, next to the Bodensee River .

History

Holloway was established in 2714 NY (−1145 DR) when Netherese archwizards took note of the ancient ruins on the surface and within the cave systems around the valley. They decided to examine the ancient human habitations of the Hollow, which they believed to be the earliest humans on Toril , and hoped to find powerful artifacts on par with the Nether Scrolls . As a result, Holloway was quickly built and fortified, and adventurers were hired to explore the deeper cave systems within the Hollow. Disappointingly for them, no powerful relics were ever recovered, only prehistoric drawings, pottery , and stone spearheads from their ancestors.

The lack of materially significant results caused the archwizard benefactors to lose interest over time, and in 3222 NY (−637 DR) they withdrew their financial support. Most of their hired explorers and adventurers left as the money dried up, but a small population remained. Though impoverished, the town was sheltered from the unnatural storms and destructive battles between the Netherese, phaerimm , and sharn forces, and it was only threatened by occasional monsters from the depths that were disturbed by ongoing underground expeditions. Holloway was ultimately abandoned in 3589 NY (−270 DR) , well after Karsus's Folly , and the inhabitants joined with other Netherese populations to the south such as Indoria .
