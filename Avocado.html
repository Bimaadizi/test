Avocado was the name given to a tree and its fruit cultivated in the Realms.

Description

Avocado fruit was shaped like a pear with dark green skin. Its flesh was of light green color and soft like goo, notably rich and buttery in flavor.

Availability

Avocados were grown around Imnescar in southwestern Amn .

These soft fruits were available on the markets of Westgate where they sometimes were consumed, smeared on bread .

In Cursrah , Calimshan , avocados were used to create edible dip, accompanied by beer .

The continent of Maztica had widely cultivated avocado trees.
