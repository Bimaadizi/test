Thundaerl was an archmage and chef of Tethyr in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Abilities

He developed spells. His latest spell as of around 1367 DR was Thundaerl's universal taster .

Possessions

He was rich.

Relationships

He was acquainted with Elminster Aumar .

History

According to Elminster, he'd been an attendee of Magefairs at some point, his visits coinciding with Mordenkainen of Oerth , and he had been the first archmage to labor as a chef in some king's court.

Some time around 1367 DR , Elminster Aumar came across Thundaerl in the week before meeting with Mordenkainen and learned of his latest spell. Mordenkainen mentioned the Circle of Eight had paid him a visit once whilst in magical disguise; he described the pastries the man made as "excellent". Elminster mentioned Thundaerl had grown quite rich over the last dozen years in Selgaunt and devised a spell that revealed poison, Thundaerl's universal taster .

In the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Thundaerl returned to Tethyr, trying to take the post as the palace chef, but came into conflict with the current chef, Gisbertus . Thundaerl eventually became chef of the Great Hall, where he was in charge of feasts rather than day-to-day matters.
