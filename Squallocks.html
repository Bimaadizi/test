Squallocks was a vrock advisor of the demon lord Graz'zt in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa 1486 DR , Squallocks was at the Undying Temple in Maerimydra assisting Graz'zt and the queen of Maerimydra Hledh Hellspawn in the ritual to permit Graz'zt to resist Vizeran De Vir 's spell .

Possession

Squallocks possessed a robe of stars .
