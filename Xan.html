Xan was an elven enchanter and member of the Greycloaks of Evereska during the mid–14 th century DR .

Personality

He was a consumate pessimist and always believed himself to be eternally doomed for failure.

Abilities

Due to his preferred school of magic , Xan's enchantment spells were more potent. As a result however, he was incapable of casting spells from the school of evocation .

Relationships

Xan's cousin Erevain was actually quite fond of him.

Possessions

His most treasured possession was his moonblade , a magical sword that was forged in the fabled city of Myth Drannor .

History

Sometime during or before the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Xan was tasked by the Greycloaks to investigate the iron crisis that befell the region of the Sword Coast . He was eventually caught by the cleric Mulahey and imprisoned within the mines beneath Nashkel .
