High Mistmistress Halautha Immerstar was the leader of the Hidden House , a secret temple dedicated to Leira , in Westgate in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Halautha was the leader of Leira's faithful in Westgate. Her congregation within the temple consisted of six other priestesses and four followers.
