Overlooked Alley was a small alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Overlooked Alley was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward just west of Castle Waterdeep . The alley originated on Watchmen's Way , branching east then turning north to end on Soldier's Street .
