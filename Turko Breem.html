Turko Breem was the cook and waiter of the Old Skull Inn in Shadowdale in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He believed that cooking was art and as such, it was worth the wait. He was confident in his skills and had a high opinion of his cooking. Perfectionist in the kitchen, Turko always took too long to get the orders out to the customers, unless Jhaele Silvermane , the proprietress of the Skull, was there to expedite the cook with her strong will and strong arm. Despite the timing, Turko's dishes were delicious.
