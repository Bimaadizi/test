Samuel Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid–7 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the uncle of his predecessor, High King Rowland the Boy King . His elder brother, Alec I , had been Rowland's predecessor and his father, Embro II , had been High King before that. Samuel's successor was his first son, Warren II .

History

Samuel assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Sifting Sands, 642 DR , following the death of his young predecessor.

He ruled for nine years before he died of heartstop in the Year of the Waning Sun, 651 DR .
