Yusendre was one of the Nimbral Lords and one of the leading Elders.

Personality

She was an impish fun-loving figure.

History

Yusendre was forced to help her fellow Elder Skouloun to sell many rival wizards to the Shadovar .

Yusendre escaped from the Spellplague in the Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR , using a gate to another plane . There she entered into stasis using the Phezult's sleep of ages spell.

Yusendre awoke around 1487 DR and competed with Skouloun and other archmages for possession of the Lost Spell during a spellstorm . However, she was killed by Tabra for her dealings with the Shadovar in Tarsakh of the Year of Dwarvenkind Reborn, 1488 DR .
