Saurglyce was a female white dragon dracolich and a partner of Pox in Yartar in 1369 DR .

Personality

Saurglyce had a very close bond with her humanoid partner.

History

Saurglyce was transformed into dracolich by Pox and served the lich as her mount, partner, and confidante. One day, Pox arrived in Yartar riding Saurglyce. They then started to gather followers to establish a Cult cell in the city.

In the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , after the Rage of Dragons , the drow-dragon Nurvureem aided Cult rebels to drive Pox and Saurglyce out of Yartar.
