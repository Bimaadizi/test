Tulmaster's Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Tulmaster's Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . The road branched south from the Rising Ride near the High Road . Tulmaster's Street then passed southeast for several blocks before ending on Caravan Street just north of Tilman's Lane . The road also connected with Sethma's Court and Tezambril's Street .

Notable Locations

Coach & Wagon Hall and Saddlers' & Harness-Makers' Hall sat at opposite sides of the intersection of Tulmaster's Street with the Rising Ride. Jastra's Flame of Hope was also located on Tulmaster's Street.
