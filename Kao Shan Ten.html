Kao Shan Ten was the Imperial Wu Jen of Shou Lung in 1358 DR .

History

Kao Shan Ten had assisted and protected Emperor Kai Tsao Shou Chin since he was very young. He was a master of White Chung Tao .

In 1358, only Kao Shan Ten knew that the Emperor had sent an adventuring party to find General Goyat Nagumo under the suggestion of a dream.

Personality

He was a simple and loyal man, with a deep intellect.
