Samia al-Giss was an inhabitant of the Furrowed Mountains in 1367 DR .

Activities

Samia was a member of the Dervish faction of holy slayers. She served as an advanced scout for the Everlasting , warning them of potential threats entering the Haunted Lands .

Possessions

Samia owned the Amulet of Dreams and some dust of truth .
