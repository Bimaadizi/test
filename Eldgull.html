Eldgull was a sailor aboard the Wicked Wench fishing and cargo vessel that sailed Maer Dualdon and the Shaengarne River in the early 14 th century DR .

Description

Eldgull was a human albino man and a hard worker who felt at home aboard a ship, more than he ever did on land.

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , the Wicked Wench was among several ships that carried reinforcements of mercenaries and adventurers from the city of Luskan to Targos , to aid the town's defense against the Legion of the Chimera . Eldgull was working aboard the ship at the time and befriended the group of adventurers who would later become the heroes of the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale .
