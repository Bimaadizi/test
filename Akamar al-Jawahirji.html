Akamar al-Jawahirji was an accomplished merchant living in Huzuz , the City of Delights, around 1367 DR .

Description

Akamar preferred to wear a simple smock and long striped caftan . His sleeves were cut away for comfort. Akamar was a short and stocky man who made himself appear taller by wrapping his turban around a tall bulky fez .

Activities

Akamar sold pearls imported from Gana .
