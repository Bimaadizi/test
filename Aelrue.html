Aelrue was a moon elf neighbor of Elminster Aumar in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

Aelrue was youthful and slim, with long legs and long waves of silvery-gray hair reaching as far down as her tiny waist. Her eyes were blue-gray, flecked with gold.
