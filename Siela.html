Siela was a female elf who resided in the Malar's Throat district of Port Nyanzaru , Chult in the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the events of the death curse in the late 15 th century DR, Siela was one of several citizens of Malar's Throat to be captured by a group of pterafolk who attacked the district. She realized that she was to be sacrificed to the jungle, and was placed as an offering in a cluster of thorny brambles alongside a tabaxi named Ashen Rock . Despite her frantic calls for help, she was dragged screaming into the brambles and killed by twig and needle blights .
