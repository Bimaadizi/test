Lances of burning blackfire were +1 corrosive heavy flaming lances and the preferred weapons of the Griffon Cavalry of Waterdeep . They were made from the wood of a zalantar tree and inscribed with runes of the deity Mystra .

Fashioning one of these weapons required the crafter to utilize one of these spells: acid fog , acid storm , Melf's acid arrow , or storm of vengeance ; and one of the following: flame blade , flame strike , or fireball . The cost of materials was typically 9,410 gp , but they sold for almost double the cost.
