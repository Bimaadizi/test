Sydon Gundwynd was an apprentice wizard inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was of Illuskan descent.

Activities

Sydon was a student of Blackstaff Academy within Blackstaff Tower in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
