Bream was a common species of panfish that inhabited several regions on both salt and freshwater bodies of water.

Description

Brown bream was a cold climate freshwater species of bream. They were small and flat bony brown animals with delicious flesh.

Ecology

They were edible and were a part of a winter's hero's feasts served during the annual Simril celebration in Twilight Tor . The feast also consisted of cooked brown bream , blackjaw , and candlefish .

Habitats

Known habitats of the bream included the Lake Glorfindar in the Starmetal Hills , wherein they remained active through the winter months under the lake's ice, and Doff's Lake in the Veilstone Peaks .
