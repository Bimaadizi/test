Arundoon sauce was a condiment which paired well with people .

Use

Arundoon sauce was to be applied prior to cooking people, preferably alive.

History

The lich Thalon had a preserved decanter of arundoon sauce in his lair as of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR . He poured it on Laeral Rythkyn , a Harper wizard , before cooking her. Laeral managed to destroy the lich with a fiery explosion before Thalon was able to devour her; Laeral later awakened, and found her way back home, where the Harper cleaned herself of the arundoon sauce. Laeral found the sauce to be "gluelike."
