Description

He was a short and vigorous Ffolk man.

Activities

Lord Koart sat on the council of King Bryon Kendrick of Corwell , alongside Lords Dynnatt and Nowll .

History

During the Darkwalker War of the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR , Lord Koart participated in the Battle of Caer Corwell . As Corwell Town was evacuated, his troops fled. This forced the remaining defenders to fall back and caused Lord Dynnatt to be mascaraed.
