Vainrence was the second-in-command of the War Wizards of Cormyr in 1479 DR .

History

In 1479 DR, Vainrence, together with the Royal Mage Ganrahast Aeiulvana , was imprisoned by one of Manshoon 's servants in magical stasis in a crypt beneath the Royal Palace in Suzail . Elminster and Vangerdahast 's shade rescued Vainrence and Ganrahast.

Later, he and Glathra Barcantle explored the crypt.
