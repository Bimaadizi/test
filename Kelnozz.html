Kelnozz was a commoner drow warrior of House Kenafin of Menzoberranzan in the early 14 th century DR .

History

Kelnozz was classmates with Drizzt Do'Urden at Melee-Magthere , the fighters' academy. Their class, which began circa 1317 DR was unusually large; it began with 25 students and ended with 22. Kelnozz was Drizzt's first "friend". They partnered during the first Grand Melee , but Kelnozz turned on Drizzt, leaving him in eighth place and revealing to him the wicked nature of the drow.

At the next Grand Melee, Drizzt gained his revenge on Kelnozz by eliminating him early, reducing him to last in their class.
