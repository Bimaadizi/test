Letto was a buccaneer of some renown, active sometime before the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR .

History

Letto was known for sailing in the great lakes and the Sea of Moving Ice in Icewind Dale and fighting the Reghedmen barbarians of the north. As of 1312 DR, a painting depicting Letto fighting barbarians on the deck of his ship was displayed on the second floor in the Weeping Widow Inn of Targos .
