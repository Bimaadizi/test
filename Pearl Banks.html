The Pearl Banks , named for their abundance of treasured pearls , were a large coral reef system found off the coast of the south and southeast region of the Pearl Cities of west Zakhara .

Geography

The reef stretched for nearly 400 miles (640 kilometers) around the coast of the Pearl Cities, beginning in the west near Ajayib and continuing all the way to Jumlat in the northeast.
