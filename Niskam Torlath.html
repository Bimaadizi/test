King Niskam Torlath "the Doomed" was an unfortunate Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty.

Relationships

Niskam was the great-grandson of King Imnel IV .

History

Niskam took the throne in the Year of the Crested Thrush, 1094 DR , following the assassination of King Imnel IV by the Skeletal Finger thieves' guild . Niskam failed to last the year, and was himself slain by agents of the Braethar merchant house who then installed Rhor Torlath as a puppet.
