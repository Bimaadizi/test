Tristan was a soldier of the Flaming Fist that served Baldur's Gate and its people during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He had hair skin and sandy brown hair. He wore the Flaming Fist leather armor uniform, emblazoned with the symbol of military group.

History

Tristan was present at the Waukeen's Rest inn when it was raided and burned down by goblinoid cultists of the Absolute in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . Trisan became a bit panicked during the disastrous event, but had enough sense to try and rescue those trapped within rather than attempt to douse the out-of-control blaze.
