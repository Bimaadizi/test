Arrows of detonation were arrows enchanted with volatile and dangerous magics.

Powers

When fired, arrows of detonation flew towards their targets and upon collision, exploded in a debilitating burst of fire and shrapnel. The exploding effect was similar to that of the fireball spell .

History

In the late 14 th century DR , arrows of detonation could be found on the Sword Coast , notably, in the cities of Athkatla in Amn and sold at the Sorcerous Sundries of Baldur's Gate . Some such ammunition could be recovered from Durlag's Tower on the Sword Coast. Arrows of detonation were also among enchanted ammunition sold at the gladiatorial arena in Thay , ran by Dennaton , a Red Wizard .
