Kestin was the best tailor in Skullport .

History

Around 1370 DR , Kestin ran Buttons, Boots, and Rags , a shop repairing and selling used clothing. Usually Kestin gained the dresses from the Iron Ring slavers, and if a customer caused trouble they could be sent to his slaver friends.
