Astrolabes were scientific items in the Realms often used by wizards during astrological experiments to track the movements of celestial bodies.

Description

Sembia crafted solid bronze astrolabes.

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 8 gold pieces .
