Jaris was a wealthy man who came into ownership of one of the buildings within the Crossroad Keep in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Jaris was a pale-skinned human man with bright blonde slicked-back hair. His demeanor, clothes, and appearance all loudly advertised his wealth and complete lack of any hardships in his life.

History

Shortly after the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Jaris inherited a home in the Crossroad Keep on the Sword Coast North . The building was an alchemical laboratory that once belonged to Jaris's uncle. Shortly after the Crossroad Keep welcomed the Mercantile House led by Sa'Sani of Samarach , Jaris arrived at the keep but found the laboratory a mess. His uncle hoarded dangerous and volatile substances and chemicals that could easily damage the property if handled by someone unlearned. He attempted to hire a group of adventurers to handle the situation, but it was unrecorded how much damage, if any, they dealt to the alchemist's home in the process.
