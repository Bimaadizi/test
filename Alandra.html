Alandra was a member of the Cult of the Dragon in 1489 DR .

History

Alandra was a Cult member who joined the reformed faction under Severin Silrajin . She was the main cultist delegate to the great dragon Claugiyliamatar of Kryptgarden Forest .

She managed to establish an alliance with the green dragon 's primary agent, Garbul , also acquiring the Green Dragon's Mask . However, in the end, both Garbul and Alandra were killed by an adventuring party hired to defeat Old Gnawbones.
