Melshimber Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Melshimber .

Location

The villa was located in the north section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward on the northwest corner of the intersection of the Street of Whispers and Grimwald's Way .

Description

The elongated walled estate contained three separate buildings and a garden. Each building was four or five stories tall.
