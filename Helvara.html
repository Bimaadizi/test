Helvara was a city found in the Lost Kingdom of Anauria .

Geography

It was situated on the southern bank of Lake Miir near the Desertsmouth Mountains .

Trade

Helvara served as the kingdom's primary producer of agricultural goods and personal supplies.

History

The city was founded some time after the fall of Netheril in −339 DR , as the enclave of Anauria expanded into a great kingdom of multiple cities.

In the Year of Fallen Guards, 111 DR , a horde of orcs and goblinoid forces led by the shaman Andegril the Wise caused the collapse of Anauria and all of the cities within.
