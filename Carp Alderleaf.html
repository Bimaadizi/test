Carp Alderleaf was a young halfling boy that lived in Phandalin .

Description

The lad of ten years aspired to become an adventurer. He had high spirits and enjoyed playing in the nearby woods.

Relationships

He was friends with Pip Stonehill , and shared secrets with him.

History

Circa 1491 DR , Carp discovered a secret tunnel by chance. He saw Redbrands come out of it, and rendezvous with others, leading him to believe the bandits had a base under the Tresendar Manor . Carp managed to sneak off without being caught.
