Orlathaun Orgolath was a Nentyarch of the Orgolath Dynasty of Narfell .

Activities

Orlathaun's rule began and ended in −399 DR . He was killed during is first year as Nentyarch by the Ayarch Jesthren , thus ending the Orgolath line of rulers.

Relationships

Orlathaun was the only son of Nardrad Orgolath .
