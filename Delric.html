Delric was one of the four bartenders who also worked as bouncers of the Copper Coronet in the River District of Athkatla , in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Delric was a balding mountain of a man. His ears were decorated with earrings and he worked wearing his breastplate , greaves , and armored codpiece . Judging by his posturing and physique, Delric was an experienced fighter . When at the Coronet, Delric showed only cold stoic face, rarely acting over-friendly with customers..

History

By the late 14 th century DR, Delric was an established member of the Copper Coronet's staff. He, along with Vaermaer , Bordovan , and Othlo , was supervised by Old Sarvo Droostan , the Coronet's tavernmaster. Delric worked in full armor and was proficient in stopping trouble, fighting off rowdy drunkards, spotting drunk spell-casting, and faked intoxication. The bouncer and the rest of the mountainous men kept large rocks behind the bar. These rocks were used as projectiles, thrown around with immaculate precision at drunken troublemakers. Delric's attitude changed to glacial when a city official or an obviously rich visitor entered the Coronet. He and his fellow bartenders did not tolerate patrons harassing the Copper Coronet's female employees.
