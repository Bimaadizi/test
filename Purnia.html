Purnia was a Netherese arcanist remembered for the creation of one spell.

History

Purnia lived during Netheril 's Golden Age . She or he invented the spell fool's gold , then consistently called Purnia's fools' gold , in 2165 NY (−1694 DR) .

All other facts about Purnia's life (including her or his gender) were lost to history.
