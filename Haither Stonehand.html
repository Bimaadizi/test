Haither Stonehand was the general of Daggerford 's militia during the mid–14 th century DR .

Relationships

Haither considered all her soldiers to be more friends and family than subordinates, gaining their absolute loyalty. She was honest but not well versed in diplomacy.

Haither's mother died during the Second Dragonspear War , in the Year of the Wyvern, 1363 DR .

History

The masons of Clan Stonehand lived in the town of Daggerford for generations before Haither.

General Stonehand led the Daggerford troops during the War of the Shining Crusade in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .
