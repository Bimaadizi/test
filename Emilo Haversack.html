Emilo Haversack was a kender from the world of Krynn .

History

In 1368 DR , Emilo met Joel the Rebel Bard and Jasmine of Westgate , a spacefaring adventurer formerly from Krynn, after being gated to Sigil .

Physical description

He had pointed ears and his hair was drawn up into a topknot. His brown hair was streaked with gray.
