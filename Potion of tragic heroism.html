Tragic heroism was a type of potion uniquely sold at Holly's Mill in Ravens Bluff .

Powers

The primary effects of tragic heroism were similar to the potion of super heroism . In addition, upon quaffing the potion any creature within 20 feet (6.1 meters) of the drinker would stop what they were doing and fight them until either were killed.
