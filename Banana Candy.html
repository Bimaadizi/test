Banana Candy was a well-trained hadrosaurus who competed in the dinosaur races of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 th century DR .

History

As of the start of the death curse , Banana Candy was a favorite at the races, with bookies giving it 3:1 odds of winning. Banana Candy was present at the inaugural Na N'buso's Haka , and may have competed in the first leg of that event in which racers drove hadrosaur-pulled chariots .
