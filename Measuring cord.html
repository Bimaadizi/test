TypeMeasurement tool

Measuring cords were ropes retrofitted for the purpose of measuring the width, length, and height of objects.

Description

These ropes were knotted at regular intervals to indicate lengths. On average they spanned 12​ to ​20 feet (3.7​ to ​6.1 meters) in length.

History

In the mid–14 th century DR , measuring cords were a common variety of tool in the city of Waterdeep .
