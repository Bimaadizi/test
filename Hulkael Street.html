Hulkael Street was a short street in the Heapside district of Baldur's Gate , near the city's eastern walls.

Location

Hulkael Street was a much larger street that cut across Heapside, Eastway , and Brampton towards the Fox Bottom moot; however, the street became dramatically shortened by the 15 th century DR . Hulkael Street began at the strake called Lathdell , near the Shrine of the Suffering , and moved to the Murl's Rest strake where Sorcerous Sundries stood.
