Manger was a small isolated coastal town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Manger was located along the coast of the Strait of Oman in northern Gwynneth. The settlment was found along the eastern edge of the Codsbay Mire north of the Black Mountains .

History

During the height of the Northlander kingdom of Oman in the 14 th century DR , Manger fell within Oman's territory on Gwynneth.

By the late 15 th century DR, following the rise of Sarifal , Manger lay in ruins.
