Thurstwell Vanthampur ( pronounced : /ˈ θ ɜːr s t w ɛ l ˈ v ɑː n θ ɑː m p ɛ r / THURST-well VAN-tham-per ) was the eldest son of Thalamra Vanthampur of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Thurstwell was born to Thalamra Vanthampur and her first husband in the 1450s DR.

By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Thurstwell commanded several imps , which he used to spy upon his brothers. Thurstwell was also a customer of the Sword Coast Couriers and relied on them to receive valuable deliveries, such as special and confidential parcel from the Zhentarim that contained an iron flask with a spectator inside it. However, the parcel never reached Baldur's Gate, the courier who carried it was slaughtered by a gang of gnolls serving The Absolute .
