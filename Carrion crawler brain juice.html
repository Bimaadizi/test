Carrion crawler brain juice , also referred to as carrion crawler mucus , was a paralytic contact poison extracted from, sometimes by simply bashing, a carrion crawler 's brain, or scraping it off from its carapace.

Description

This substance was a sticky and membranous liquid.

Effects

Carrion crawler brain juice was not deadly but very quick-acting upon contact with flesh. When touched by unprotected skin, the poison could paralyze for approximately one minute.

Creation

One of the recipes for the refined version of the poison called for salts of carrion crawler tentacles and a special type of alchemical vitriol.
