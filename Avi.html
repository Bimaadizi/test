Avi was a water genasi armorsmith in Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Avi managed the Steam and Steel forge together with his husband Embric on Trollskull Alley in the North Ward . He was a member of the Most Careful Order of Skilled Smiths and Metalforgers and also of the Splendid Order of Armorers, Locksmiths, and Finesmiths .
