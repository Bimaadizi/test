The Ministry of Riches was found near the stone wharves in Dihliz in 1367 DR .

Activities

The Ministry of Riches was located within the open bazaar in Dihliz where smuggling was prevalent. Their main responsibility was levying a small tax on all reported treasure recovered from local ruins. A 1% tax based on the item's value was imposed on mundane items, and the Ministry always offered to purchase magic items at full market value.

Members

The title of Minister of Riches, and head of the Ministry, belonged to Jel al-Galiz as of 1367 DR.
