Bearclaw was an unsavory barbarian gnome mercenary from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Bearclaw was a light-skinned gnomish woman with brown eyes and short bright-red hair. She wore barbarian hides and hid her face behind a crimson mask. The gnome used a massive axe in battle.
