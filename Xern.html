Xern was a monk at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose .

Activities

Xern was a member of the Order of the Chisel of the Disciples of St. Sollars the Twice-Martyred . His duty was to act as wine master and cook for the monastery and occasionally also for the Guest House. Secretly, he was also a member of the Cult of the Howling Hatred .

History

Xern was one of the cultists who infiltrated the monastery in 1491 DR . He brewed the cordial that cultists used to keep Mother Savahn unconscious.

Relationships

Xern was one of the few to know fellow monk Nym personally. He also was a good friend of Ovon .
