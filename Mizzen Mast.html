The Mizzen Mast was a business in the Lower Trade Lanes of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR .

Structure

The Mizzen Mast was a three-story building in good condition.

Description

The Mizzen Mast specialized in the construction of a wide range of watercraft, guided by the motto, “No boat too large or small.” Owned and operated by Zulthir "Spyglass" Hestern , a seasoned craftsman with decades of experience, the Mizzen Mast constructed everything from simple rafts to grand galleons . Known for delivering quality results at reasonable prices, Zulthir's work was particularly favored by smugglers, who valued his expertise in crafting hidden compartments within vessels, ideal for concealing illicit cargo.
