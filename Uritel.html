Uritel of the Garden was a half-elf druid from the world of Garden in Realmspace in the midâ€“14 th century, who founded a new religion worshiping world-plants like Yggdrasil's Child that held Garden together in its root system.

Personality

A devout person, Uritel was enamored by nature, and especially by the massive planet-binding plant that was Yggdrasil's Child. After he began worshiping the plant, he left his home to spread his new faith to others.

Possessions

In his missionary travels, he wore simple leather armor and wielded only an ordained scimitar and sling .

History

Born and raised in a pirate enclave located on the world of Garden, he spend his youth amongst the great roots of Yggdrasil's Child. It was here he ended up establishing a faith of worshiping not just Yggdrasil's Child, but all such world plants.
