The Happy Horse Ranch was a family-owned business that operated in Triboar in the Dessarin Valley during the late 15 th century DR . It was owned and operated by Janele Karnveller , who was aided by her three sons.

Inhabitants

Together, the Karnveller family bred, reared, and housed horses that were later sold.

Structure

The ranch comprised two-story log-built house with an outhouse that served as the family home; a stone storehouse used to hold saddles , bridles, bits, and reins ; a stone stables leading to a fenced riding yard; and a fenced paddock that was used for grazing. It lay in the southwest of Triboar, south of Gwaeron's Slumber .
