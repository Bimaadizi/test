Telstang Brook was a long stream located in the western region of the Ride on the Moonsea North .

Geography

The stream flowed northwest from the West Galena Mountains , west through Vercy Wood , before joining with Razack's Tears and emptying into Lake Longreach .

Telstang Brook crossed both the Ilinvur Trail , and the Way of the Powrie just outside the settlement of Ilinvur .
