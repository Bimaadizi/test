Vincent was an ox belonging to Elmar Barthen in Phandalin in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Elmar used Vincent to pull the wagons that transported supplies from his store, Barthen's Provisions , to locations such as Tibor Wester's loggers' camp .

Personality

Vincent was very reliable. He could pull a laden two-wheeled cart 24 miles (39 kilometers) in a single day, even when travelling through Neverwinter Wood .
