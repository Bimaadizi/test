Narrak was a derro savant cultist of Demogorgon in Gracklstugh in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Narrak's area of expertise was demonology.

Activities

Narrak was a junior member of the Council of Savants and a long-time cultist of Demogorgon.

History

Circa 1486 DR , he gathered a small retinue of followers and started a plot to deliver the city to the demon lord . The first step in his plan was to curse the stone giants of Clan Clairngorm with Demogorgon's madness. He was waiting for a delivery from Droki when an adventuring party escaping from drow slavers of Velkynvelve ruined his plan and defeated him.
