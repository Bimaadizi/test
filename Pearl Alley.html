Pearl Alley was a small backalley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Pearl Alley was located in the northeast section of Waterdeep's Dock Ward . It originated on Zastrow Street , branching west then northwest before terminating at a dead end near the Three Pearls Nightclub .
