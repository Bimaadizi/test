The crown of evening's rise was a magical crown found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Embodying the cool and quiet calm that came with the fall of night, the crown gave some protection against magical threats, helped the wearer physically dodge, and helped them concentrate on intellectual pursuits.

History

The crown may have been found when the Elves of Cormanthor fought Red Wizards of Thay in the Irithlium , defeating Thramont the Watchful in the process.
