Ylyndar Starscatter was an inhabitant of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

Rumors & Legends

Per Ivran Selorn , Ylyndar had snuck into Castle Dlardrageth for trysts across six summers up to 241 DR . This led Ivran to judge that the castle must not have been haunted.
