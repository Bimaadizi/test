Zzar was a fortified sherry wine popular in Waterdeep .

Description

Zzar had a fiery orange color and an almond scent and flavor. Many elves disdained it, considering it vulgar. It was made by fortifying sluth (a dry, sparkling white wine) with an almond liqueur.

Availability

Zzar was produced at wineries such as the House of Good Spirits in Waterdeep. It was the feature drink served at the Sword and Sash festhall in Tegal's Mark .

It was also available from the Yawning Portal , where it was commonly ordered by Esvele Rosznar .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , A Modest Guide to Sword Coast Wine stated that zzar was as suitable for consumption as " bugbear taint."
