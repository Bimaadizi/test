Darkhold Vale was a small valley found within the Far Hills of the Western Heartlands .

Geography

The vale sat within the Far Hills between two arms of the Sunset Mountains northwest of the Tun Marshes and due east of the Dusk Road .

Description

Darkhold Vale was relatively small, measuring approximately 3,500 feet (1,100 meters) in length by 2,000 feet (610 meters) wide in a roughly rectangular shape. An unnamed stream flowed through the center of the vale and turned south at the vale's mouth. The southeast section of the vale held a quarry. A fortified perimeter wall was constructed at the vale's mouth.

Notable Locations

Darkhold Vale's most imposing feature was the large castle of Darkhold that sat near the rear of the vale.
