Mistress of Dread Shalarlane Druistrar was a high priestess of Beshaba inhabiting Suldolphor in the far eastern reaches of Calimshan in south Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Shalarlane was the ranking priestess within the Deep House of Doom —a temple devoted to the Maid of Misfortune located in the south section of the city. She lead her "Cloaked Ones", which was a nickname for her clergy, one nocturnal excursions through the Golden City wearing antlered caps that symbolized Beshaba's influence.
