The Last Place was the lone surviving ale -house in Yûlash around 1367 DR .

Activities

Business thrived at the Last Place because of a lack of competition and a surplus of soldiers quartered in the area. Despite the melting pot of customers, violence was kept to a minimum in the Last Place.

Inhabitants

The Last Place catered to Red Plumes from Hillsfar , local citizens, angry faction members, and even the occasional Zhentarim spy.
