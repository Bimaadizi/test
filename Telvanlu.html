Telvanlu ( pronounced : / t ɛ l ˈ v ɑː n l uː / tel-VAN-loo ) was the moderately sized capital city of the aquatic elf kingdom of Naramyr .

Description

This city consisted of clamshell -like buildings, low to the ground and dug into the silt in order to avoid issues with trade ships that frequently passed overhead.

Geography

Telvanlu was located in the western reaches of the Dragonmere , 200 feet (61 meters) beneath the surface. It was situated 40 miles (64 kilometers) southwest of Suzail and 30 miles (48 kilometers) north of Ilipur .

Government

Circa 1370 DR , the latest ruler in the city's history was Coronal Aravae Daudil . She had a standing policy of having armor and weapons stripped from all who entered the city.

History

This city was founded in the Year of the Prying Gods, 768 DR .

Reputation

Telvanlu had a reputation for being a city of artisans.
