The Cult of the Sacred Skull was a minor Bedine religious cult operating in the 14 th century DR.

Organization

The Cult of the Sacred Skull consisted largely of human warriors and rogues. Their high priest was a mind flayer whom the phaerimm controlled.

Base of Operations

The cult operated mainly out of the eastern part of the Sword of the Anauroch desert .

Members

The cultists were described as fanatics.
