Bol was a tiefling warrior and a member of the Bounty Hunters of Cavus Dun in the mid–15 th century DR .

History

Bol was one of the members of the Cavus Dun squad tasked by Effron with finding Dahlia Sin'felle and Drizzt Do'Urden .

However, in the first skirmish against the pair of elves, Bol and most of his fellows were killed by Dahlia and Drizzt.
