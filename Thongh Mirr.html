Thongh Mirr was a Red Wizard of Thay of the mid–14 th century DR .

Thongh Mirr was killed by the mercenary Gayrlana in single combat in the streets of Teziir , for which she became famous. Her explanation of her victory, "the blade is faster than the Art ", became a popular saying in Faerûn by 1356 DR .
