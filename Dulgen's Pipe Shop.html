Dulgen's Pipe Shop was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The rear of the shop faced a small unnamed alley.

Location

Dulgen's Pipe Shop was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the north side of Simple's Street in between Virgin's Square to the west and the Way of the Dragon to the east.
