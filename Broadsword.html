A broadsword , or a broad sword , was a medium-sized sword used for slashing.

Description

The definition of what a broadsword was often differed depending on the culture they were used in. However, broadswords were specifically used to slash, unlike longswords , which were used to both slash and pierce. Most of these swords had extra protection around their hilts.

Broadswords were roughly the same length as standard swords, but most had basket hilts or shell guards. More specifically, broadswords were heavy, had double-edged blades, and were around 3.5 feet (1.1 meters) long. The metal hilts of such swords could be used similarly to metal gauntlets . Thus, they were favored by those who were skilled in hand-to-hand combat, as well as those who utilized parrying.
