Seviria Malderos was a transmuter in Skullport in 1379 DR .

Activities

Seviria and her brother Tantrus Malderos lived in a tower inside the lair of the slaver Lanador . They acted as gatekeepers, interrogating all the people that entered the lair, supported by some minotaur guards.

History

In 1379 DR, Tantrus and Seviria were confronted by an adventuring party supported by the Xanathar who'd attacked the lair.
