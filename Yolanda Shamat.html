Yolanda Shamat was a cleric of Loviatar in Waterdeep in 1372 DR .

Description

Yolanda always stood rigidly erect and had an icy stare.

History

Yolanda was a slave until she was bought and freed by Lady Ellandra Tolbert , who was the primary source of funds for the church of Loviatar in Waterdeep. Yolanda then served as her chamberlain.
