Flamingos were aquatic birds known to exist in Maztica .

Combat

When attacking, flamingos used their beaks.

Ecology

Flamingos, along with other waterfowl such as ducks , geese , storks , and ibises , could be found swimming in the large pool on the grounds of the Palace of Kukul in Tulom-Itzi .
