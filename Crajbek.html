Crajbek was the Chieftain of the kobold tribe that laired in the infamous Haunted Halls near the village of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the 15 th century DR .

Activities

In the 15 th century DR, Crajbek's gang included High Priest Pimulpekk of Kurtulmak , who led a group of priests. When a group of adventurers who fell through a rift to Toril from the world of Eberron investigated the haunted depths of the dungeon, they faced High Priest Pimulpekk and Crajbek, possibly slaying both and claiming the kobold tribe's looted treasures.
