History

In 1370 DR , Sabrae Tellendon could be found in the Yawning Portal healing adventurers brave enough to delve into the dangerous mega dungeon of Undermountain. She offered her clerical aid to the lone hero tasked with investigation into kobold activities by Khelben Arunsun one behalf of the Lords of Waterdeep .

During that time, she served as an informer for the lone hero, keeping them up to date on the drow activities within the Undermountain, and their recent return to the deep cavern within the mega dungeon where the ancient Garden of the Drow could be found.
