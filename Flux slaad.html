Flux slaads were an uncommon variety of slaadi that emerged in the Elemental Chaos following the Spellplague . They were generally smaller and weaker than other varieties of slaadi, garnering them little respect among their peers.

Personality

A flux slaad completely lacked any sense of self-awareness or self-preservation. Their actions were blindly motivated by a desire to cause chaos and carnage, often beset by unpredictable and violent rages.

Ability

Unlike other slaadi, a flux slaad lacked the ability to reproduce. They could teleport short distances and could see in areas of low light . Their monikor of "flux" derived from their resistances and vulnerabilites towards various elements and elemental energies always being in a state of flux, such as cold, fire , lighting, psionic energy , necrotic energy , and sound. Whenever they were injured by one of these, they would become resistant to it and something else would in-turn becoming its vulnerability.

Combat

These slaadi solely fought with the use of their claws.

Society

A flux slaad occasionally slipped by accident through portals , commonly ending up near gatherings of bullywugs . They would often make themselves revered rulers over the bullywugs and showed great restraint in harming their subjects.

Languages

Flux slaads spoke both Common and Primordial .
