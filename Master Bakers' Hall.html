The Master Bakers’ Hall was the guildhall of a Waterdhavian guild known as the Bakers' Guild .

Location

The Master Bakers’ Hall was located along the Street of the Sword , in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This guildhall stood two-stories tall.
