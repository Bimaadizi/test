Ecology

These insects traveled in groups called swarms, described by many as "clouds". They were often seen in the company of mosquitos and other biting insects.

They were attracted to scents of death and decay, such as overripe fruit.

Habitats

Known locations of these pestering bugs included the jungles of the Chultan Peninsula , Cold Run , in Cormyr and its Farsea Marshes , in the Fens of the Fallen on Gwynneth , in the land of Thay and the Yuirwood of neighboring Aglarond , in the Vast at a marshy bog within the vicinity of Ravens Bluff , in the Zehoarastria region of Amn , and in the Underdark .

Beyond the Prime Material , gnats could be encountered in Smaragd , the jungle-like 74 th layer of the Abyss .

Predators

These creatures were preyed on by animals that typically ate insects, such as frogs , jumping fish like the sunsilver , and spiders .
