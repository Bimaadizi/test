Amaratha Ruendarr was an innkeeper in Waterdeep around 1367 DR .

Activities

Amaratha managed the Jade Jug inn in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep , one of the most luxurious inns of the city.

History

Amaratha was an adventurer until a lich destroyed her right arm.

Personality

Amaratha ran her inn like a place where she liked to stay. She had a good memory and learned the names and details of all her guests in order to better serve them. This ability was very useful to the City Watch too.
