The Selvetargtlin , meaning the "Warriors of Selvetarm" , was the primary religious organization dedicated to the worship and service of the god Selvetarm , Lolth 's champion.

Organization

Their ranks were largely made up of males,

Possessions

The Selvetargtlin were known for wearing blood-red robes, often over a coat of chain mail .

History

In the Year of Risen Elfkin, 1375 DR , the Selvetargtlin launched an attack against the Promenade of Eilistraee . They were led by a judicator , Dhairn . Although the followers of Selvetarm failed to take the temple, they did considerable damage to the followers of Eilistraee.
