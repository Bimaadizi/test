The Alzho languages was a sub-grouping of the Untheric languages group of Rauric languages .

Languages

The only surviving language in this category was Calishite Alzhedo .

History

The Alzho languages branched off from the other Untheric languages by mixing with the tongue of the Elemental Plane of Air , because of the influence of djinn on the earliest inhabitants of Calimshan .
