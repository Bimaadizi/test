Fildaern was a wealthy merchant inhabiting Saerloon in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Fildaern was so rich that he owned his own fleet of ships. He decided to use his impressive wealth to buy one of the Dales and set himself up as a Lord, but had trouble deciding which one to purchase.
