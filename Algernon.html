Algernon was a wealthy paunchy man who became a target for Pheirkas , a dwarven ne'er-do-well in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Algernon was staying in one of the most expensive rooms of Feldepost's Inn in the town of Beregost . The man traveled with his enchanted cloak , the item that caught Pheirkas's eye. She sought a competent thief to acquit the magic item without shedding Algernon's blood, offering gold and payment.
