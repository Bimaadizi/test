Han Chao was a town in Tabot .

Geography

It was situated at the southeastern end of the Haraki Ridge , amongst the southern foothills. It lay on a road between Bidnop So and Motra .

History

When soldiers of Shou Lung invaded Tabot in Shou Year 646 (−604 DR) , the priests and the barbarians of the land were forced to ally against them. Baron Ohn of Han Chao led his warriors and joined with the great sohei Ramara and they repelled the Shou in the Battle of Tsagang . Afterward, Ohn and Ramara negotiated for ten days and established the Peace Land Treaty between the tribes and the monks.
