Ayaeqlarune was the beloved of Iymbryl Alastrarra .

Legacy

Upon his death, Iymbryl Alastrarra was grateful to go to her at last. Elminster overheard this, as the dying elf attempted to introduce the two of them. Afterwards, Elminster related this unto his family.
