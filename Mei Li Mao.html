Mei Li Mao was a yakuza in Shou Lung .

Activities

Around 1357 DR , Mei Li Mao owned the Imperial Phoenix restaurant in Kuo Te' Lung , a very popular meeting point for secret societies. She also sometimes acted as a fence.
