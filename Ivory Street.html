Ivory Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Ivory Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . It originated on Diamond Street where it branched west, crossing the Street of the Singing Dolphin where it turned northwest to end on the western edge of the Street of Glances . Ivory Street was intersected by Sighing Maiden's Walk , Flint Street , the Skulkway , Bliss Street , Ignorance Alley , the Street of Whispers , and Torch Lane .
