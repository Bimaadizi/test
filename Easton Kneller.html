Easton Kneller was a human resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Easton had light skin and short red-brown hair. He wore a long tunic and carried a quarterstaff .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR , Easton tried to get heroic individuals such as adventurers to rescue Eveningstar residents from being stranded in the King's Forest , which was a battlefield with the drow of Sschindylryn .
