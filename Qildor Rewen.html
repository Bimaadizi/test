Qildor Rewen was an inhabitant of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

Appearance

He was young and handsome, with a very eloquent stare.

Possessions

During a revel, he was seen with a silver-hilted sword.

History

He was a guest together with Aurae Shaeremae at a revel in 241 DR , where Elminster Aumar substituted Eltargrim . There, he was rather blunt with the human Armathor, but appeared to come out of it with as good an impression as possible.
