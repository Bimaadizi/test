Ehlemm Zoar was a guildmaster inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, in the mid-to-late 13 th century DR . He was the guildmaster of the Gemcutters .

Activities

In the Year of the Black Wind, 1262 DR , Ehlemm Zoar was one of two guildmasters to survive the brutal Guildwars that occurred in Waterdeep several years after Ahghairon 's death. Ehlemm and his fellow surviving guildmaster, Lhorar Gildeggh , declared themselves the "Two Lords Magister" of the city.
