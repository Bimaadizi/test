Ord Artemel was a noble of the Waterdhavian noble house Artemel during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He lived within Artemel Villa located at the corner of Sul Street and Vondil Street in the northeast section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward .

Relationships

Ord was the eldest son and heir of his father, Bresnoss Artemel , and his mother, Lydda , who was his father's consort.
