Hebopbe was a myconid and leader of the Circle of Growers in Neverlight Grove in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Hebopbe had no interest in the feud between sovereigns Basidia and Phylo . It did not like Phylo's innovations but thought that Basidia had exaggerated the danger. It also the myconid community's mass meldings.

History

When, circa 1486 DR , Zuggtmoy 's will twisted most of the Circle, Hebopbe was infected by the spores but had resisted her evil influence.
