Irisroth was a half-elf in the Doomvault dungeon in 1486 DR .

History

Irisroth was one of the many Chosen captured by the Red Wizards of Thay and transported to the Doomvault dungeon for experiments. More specifically, she was the chosen of Loviatar . Irisroth was freed from her chains by the heroes of Daggerford .
