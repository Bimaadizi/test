Gloves of recovery were enchanted gloves made out of hide of an unknown animal, used in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

Gloves of recovery could be only used by thieves and rogues and their magics enhanced the ability to overcome afflictions, both mundane and of arcane nature. Additionally, some pairs enhanced the wearer's thievery skills.

History

In the 15 th century DR, gloves of recovery received some use by bandits and heroes of the Sword Mountains , were sold in the city of Neverwinter and its markets, and could be recovered from the ancient ruins of Illefarn .
