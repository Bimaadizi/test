Eluraunt Malabrak was a Shadovar arcanist of Thultanthar in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Eluraunt was one of the few Shadovar to survive the fall of Thultanthar in the Year of the Rune Lords Triumphant, 1487 DR .

He came to serve the Three . In Tarsakh of the Year of Dwarvenkind Reborn, 1488 DR , he was observed from afar to be in the Oldspires mansion in Cormyr when Tabra , on her crusade against all surviving Shadovar arcanists, found him and killed Eluraunt.
