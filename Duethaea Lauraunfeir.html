Duethaea Lauraunfeir ( pronounced : / d uː ɛ θ ˈ eɪ ɑː l ɔːr ˈ ɔː n f ɪər / doo-eth-AY-ah lore-AWN-feer ) was a Chosen of Mystra of the Delimbiyr Vale in the 9 th century DR .

Description

She was tall and very skinny, more hard-bitten than beautiful, and often dressed in a rigid leather greatcoat that reached her ankles.

Personality

She was spirited and difficult, but gregarious enough to mingle. She also liked very strong drink.

Abilities

Duethaea was strong in the Art .

Possessions

She wore two enchanted bracers that launched a number of spells .

Relationships

Her family saw her as difficult. She had been trained by Elminster Aumar .

Activities

She liked to mingle in taverns all around the Savage North.

History

Duethaea met her match in the Battle of Blades Afire , where she attacked heedlessly and was ultimately mortally wounded by the demonic foes. She survived until the end, but later died of her injuries in Elminster's arms, spilling silver fire on the battlefield.
