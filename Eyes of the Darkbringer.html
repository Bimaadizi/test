The Eyes of the Darkbringer , who referred to themselves as the Darkeyes , was an organization of rogues who worshiped Moander , the Rotting God.

Base of Operations

Agents of the Eyes of the Darkbringer were secretly placed within most major cities spread across Faerûn .

Activites

The Darkeyes carried out assassinations and served as spies for Moander's cult, warning the larger organization of potential threats in advance.
