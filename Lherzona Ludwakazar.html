Lherzona Ludwakazar ( pronounced : /ˈ l ɜːr z oʊ n ɑː l ʌ d ˈ w ɑː k ɑː z ɑː / LUR-zohn-ah lud-WAK-ah-zah ) was a dwarf of the Ludwakazar clan in the Earthspur Mountains in 1491 DR .

History

Lherzona lived with her father King Iorn Ludwakazar and the rest of her clan in the Earthspur Mountains , acting as custodians of the mystical Chamber of Falling Stones .

However, after mining excavations, a medusa and her gargoyles minions took control of the Chamber. In a battle, King Iorn was mortally wounded. Lherzona and the others remained to await the death of Iorn, guarding against intruders, until an adventuring party hired to clear the mine arrived. After the adventurers solved the matter, Iorn finally died and Lherzona was crowned queen of her people.
