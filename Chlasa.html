Chlasa was a woman in Hastarl during the mid-3 rd century DR .

History

During the attack on Athalgard led by Elminster Aumar on the Year of the Chosen, 240 DR , Chlasa was rutting with local nobleman Jansibal Otharr at the moment when the alarm was rung by the Magelords of Athalantar . The dandy left her somewhat late, worrying that he may be looked on in disfavor for disregarding the alarm at first; she stayed in bed, though he warned her to go easy on the wine.
