Chultan tree slugs were a species of slug that inhabited the jungles of Chult .

Description

These slugs had slimy and soft bodies. They were notable for growing to lengths of 8 feet (2.4 meters) and beyond.

Trivia

The mist dragon Thalagyrt considered these slugs to be a delicacy and one of his favorite types of slugs to feast upon.
