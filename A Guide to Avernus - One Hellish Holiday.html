A Guide to Avernus - One Hellish Holiday was a guidebook dedicated to Avernus , the first layer of the Nine Hells , penned for planeswalkers sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

Content

The overview of Avernus included information on the plane's geography and a primer on its inhabitants, and their involvement in the endless Blood War . The politics section of the book was a collection of asterisks, addendums, and corrections on the fluid and chaotic chain of command under the Archduke of Avernus, Zariel .

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , copies of this book could be found available for sale in the The Bibliophile – a book shop in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate .
