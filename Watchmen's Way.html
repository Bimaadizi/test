Watchmen's Way , which was also seen as Watchman's Way , was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Watchmen's Way was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward at the base of Castle Waterdeep . The road originated on Soothsayer's Way where it branched northeast to terminate just east of Hillock Court . Both Soldier's Street and Crossbow Lane merged with Watchmen's Way north of the Bell Tower .
