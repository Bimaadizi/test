Tallstones was a manor in Athkatla , and the residence of the heads of the Phaerdinzer clan during the late mid-14 th century DR .

Interior

As of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Tallstones was the residence of Othlo Phaerdinzer and Jasurra Phaerdinzer . They liked to be naked, and float everywhere with their magic inside the house; their windows and doors were boarded up, with the inside of the house full of tinkling, ethereal music and magically created lights.
