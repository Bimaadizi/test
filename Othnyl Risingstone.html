Othnyl Risingstone was a highly skilled stone sculptor that lived in Daerlun , Sembia , circa the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Description

Othnyl was a female dwarf , which made her a rarity a town that was ninety-six percent human , with the rest being mostly halflings and half-elves , in the days after the Time of Troubles .

Personality

She was rough around the edges and had a hot temper when riled, but she respected others and expected it in return. If anyone gave her grief, she told them exactly what they could do with it.

Abilities

Risingstone was famous in the lands around the Dragon Reach for her skill in stone sculpture and masonry. She could also make and repair most personal weapons.
