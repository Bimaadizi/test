Twoedge was a shopkeeper in Skullport .

History

Around 1370 DR , Twoedge was the owner of the weapon shop Twoedge's , were he repaired and sharpened blades. Sometimes, he sold blades of unpaid customers and a couple of times in past people found enchanted weapons for sale.
