Vayloss Talanthil II , who was also called the Elder , was the fifth Sorcerer - Arkhan of the Talanthil Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Vayloss II inherited the throne when his father, Nayloss Talanthil , died in −408 DR . His rule was short and ended with his death in −404 DR .

Relationships

Vayloss II had two sons. The younger, Vayloss III , would inherit his throne.
