Kromnlor Sernar was a sage and midwife inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Kromnlor rented a room at an inn called Warm Beds located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward where Presper Street met Ship Street . She could typically be found there early in the morning or late at night.
