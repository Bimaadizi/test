Asana Haraad was the daughter of the mayor of Amkethran , Omar Haraad . She wished to help its people after the invasion by Balthazar .

History

She would often save the lives of the starving citizens. In the late 1360s DR , she stole money from Balthazar's mercenaries, and they threatened to publicly execute her. She begged to be spared, but ultimately regretted nothing. Asana may have been saved by Gorion's Ward , with Montolio's cloak being his reward.
