The Merchant Square of Neverwinter was the financial hub of that city prior to the eruption of Mount Hotenow .

Geography

The Merchant Square was located in Neverwinter's Tower District .

History

In the years prior to the eruption of Mount Hotenow in the Year of Knowledge Unearthed, 1451 DR , the Merchant Square was a marketplace where merchants from all across the Sword Coast came to do business. The amount of money exchanged daily nearly rivaled the markets of Waterdeep .

After the volcanic eruption, the area was abandoned and came to be infested with elemental weevils . The vast wealth of the merchants was swiftly pillaged by bandits .
