Chasstil was a vegetable found in southern and southeastern Faerûn .

Description

A hardy plant, the chasstil had stalks that looked like twisted or coiled green spears that were edible and tasted like asparagus . Diced up, it was a main ingredient in mlael , a possibly spicy brown gravy favored by the people of Delzimmer circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
