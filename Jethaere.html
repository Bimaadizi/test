Jethaere was a Netherese enclave.

History

To its inhabitants, or at least Evaereol Rathrane , Jethaere was a refuge for those who truly loved magic, rather than seek it for power. However, it had suffered great losses fighting the Phaerimm ; some of its mages fled rather than perish.

Jethaere wasn't amongst the cities whose remains were identified after the Fall of High Netheril.
