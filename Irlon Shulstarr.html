Irlon Shulstarr was an evil adventurer in Myth Drannor in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .

Activities

Irlon was a member of the Masked Marauders .

Personality

Irlon was a talented salesman and an able calligrapher, forger, and maker of inks and cosmetics. He was cocky and cruel.

Relationship

Irlon traveled with his sister Llurella Shulstarr .
