The Darni House was a caravanserai located in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Description

A small mosque dedicated to Jisan decorated the center of the Darni House. An impressively stocked library could also be found here.

Activities

Prices at the Darni House were steep, but a combination of good food, quick services, skilled craftsmen, and fine lodgings kept most patrons coming back on a regular basis.

Inhabitants

Janik Darni was the proprietor of the Darni House. He employed two full platoons of the Diligent to maintain security.

Due to its upper-class caterings, the Darni House attracted gem and silk merchants, as well as others who carried valuable cargo.

Darni employed a rawun to entertain guests at night, and a barber to keep them well-groomed.
