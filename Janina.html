Janina was an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate who died some years before the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Janina was a human woman with fair skin tone and blonde hair.

History

Janina was a mother who loved his son but she was deeply unhappy in life. She felt remorse over her feelings and self-destructive behavior. Janina planned to run away from her family and found a handsome man who offered her a trip away from the city. The woman collected coin to pay for the passage into the life she thought to be worth living. However, that was not to be. Janina died sometime in or shortly before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR as was evident by her body's lack of decay. She was buried in the Lower City Graveyard , not far from its mortuary. Janina grave may had been accidentally desecrated, and her body risen as a zombie by Nina Dortmell , a talented child with a gift , who was trying to resurrect her recently deceased brother.
