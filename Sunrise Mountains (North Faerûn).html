The Sunrise Mountains , also known as the Taurax Mountains , were a mountain range in the far Moonsea North .

Geography

The Sunrise Mountains lay north of the Frozen Forest and the Great Mount of Ghaethluntar , situated between the High Ice of the Anauroch to the west and the Great Glacier to the east. It was the furthest north mountain range in the region.

History

In the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the Sunrise Mountains served as the northern border of the Tyrantfog that spread out from Zhentil Keep .
