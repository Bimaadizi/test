Nonabune was an inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Nonabune was a hin man with a light skin tone, dark brown eyes, and long dark brown hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Nonabune could be found in the docks of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, where commoners could buy fresh fish . The docks were attacked by a band of sahuagin when the area was visited by the Absolute Adversaries , if was unknown if Nonabune survived the attack. If he managed to survive, he was eager to write a story about the attack, calling it "When Fish Fight Back."
