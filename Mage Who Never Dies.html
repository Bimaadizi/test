The Mage Who Never Dies was the mysterious master of the Flooded Forest in the Vast and the Moonsea regions of north Faer√ªn in the late 14 th century DR . The title was given by folk around Ylraphon , but whoever or whatever this being was remained unknown.

The Mage Who Never Dies was said to have claimed the southern part of the Flooded Forest as his domain circa 1370 DR . This included the duskwood forests at its southern edge and the Mage's Tower , with the village of Tavilar lying at the southern border.

Some folk in Ylraphon rumored that certain evil groups and orcs , who regularly stocked up on supplies in Ylraphon before venturing into the Flooded Forest, served the Mage Who Never Dies.
