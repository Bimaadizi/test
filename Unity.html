Unity was a Netherese city during the time of ancient Netheril .

History

Unity was founded in the early 600s NY (around −3260 DR ) and served to defend against goblins.

Unity was the site for the largest of all temples constructed for the glory of Amaunator —the Forested Enclave of the Face of the Sun . This towering structure soared 17 stories into the sky and was visible for miles around.

In 3348 NY (−511 DR) , Unity was successfully attacked by the bandit lord Olostin , starting his 33 year reign of terror.

In 3499 NY (−360 DR) , the city was besieged by extra-planar fiends, who waged a three-year war for the city before being banished or killed by Karsus .

In the spring of 3514 NY (−345 DR) , magically mutated creatures began coming out of the Far Horns Forest near Unity, believed to be caused by Karsus's use of heavy magic during the war against the fiends.
