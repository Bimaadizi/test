Surgath Ilder was a prospector of the Western Heartlands in the early mid-3 rd century DR .

Description

He was a bearded and weatherbeaten man. On the night of his death, he was wearing tattered clothing, boots, and wielding a stout, worn sword.

Personality

Surgath was greedy and somewhat boisterous.

Possessions

Amongst other possessions, he had a wardstone with fire-based defenses, and a wyrmtongue scepter . He later sold the scepter to Elminster .

Activities

He claimed to have ransacked a number of elven tombs.

Relationships

He appeared to have a rivalry with Karlmuth Hauntokh .

History

Two summers prior to 241 DR , Surgath had traded two rubies for his wardstone . On that year, he discussed at length his activities one night while in the Herald's Horn inn; there, he mentioned having acquired a scepter roughly a tenday prior to the date. He sold that scepter on the same night to a young Elminster for a hundred rubies , but died later when attacked by Heldebran .
