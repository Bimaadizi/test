Ogre zombies were powerful zombies created from the corpses of ogres .

Combat

Like other zombies these undead fought with their fists, though they were also known to be armed with greatclubs or morningstars .

Usage

The casting of summon undead III could conjure ogre zombies to fight for the caster.
