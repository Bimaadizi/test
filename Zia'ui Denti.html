Zia'ui Denti was a merchant in the nation of T'u Lung around Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) .

History

Denti lived in the great city of Balanzia , a century prior to its destruction. There they lived in the southern reaches of the city, just down the road from the Cotnui Park and Zoo .
