Bralzaer's Bold Basilisks were a mercenary company in Impiltur in the mid–14 th century DR .

Relationships

The organization received no money from Sabrast Windriver .

Base of Operations

They were based in Impiltur .

History

Per Sabrast Windriver , his youngest son, Bralzaer Windriver had founded the company recently as of 1356 DR .
