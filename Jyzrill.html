Jyzrill was a member of the College of Conjuration in the city of Sshamath .

History

In 1377 DR , alongside Gilkriz , Jyzrill was one of the conjurers sent to help teleport the combined force of wizards of Sshamath and priests of Eilistraee to the main temple of Kiaransalee , which they planned to attack.
