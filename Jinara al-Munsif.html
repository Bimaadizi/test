Jinara al-Munsif was a princess of Hudid in the Pantheist League of interior Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Personality

She despised the strict nature of the League of the Pantheon. Unlike the rest of her family, she had no problem lying to get what she wanted.

History

Jinara was born in Huzuz . Upon reaching maturity she declared her independence and left her family to find her own way.

She lived in Tajar within the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara in 1367Â DR , where she managed a trading firm. She did not use her family name in order to remain anonymous, instead choosing to go by the name "Jina".

Relationships

Jinara was the daughter of Caliph Sajah al-Munsif . She had a single sister named Inara .
