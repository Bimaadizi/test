History

Circa 1372 DR , Shermata was exploring an ancient temple and tomb complex deep within the northern Moonwood alongside a group of adventurers . Within the temple she and two members of the adventuring group were ambushed and killed by gnolls living within it. Those within her community mourned her passing for a tenday period before her position in the temple was replaced by Aratha Sul .
