Princess Ola-al Farhan was the eldest daughter of Caliph Abir al-Farhan in Hilm in the Pantheist League of interior Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Description

Ola possessed striking beauty.

Activities

Ola was trained in the ways of the moralist and, with the church's blessing, would marry the next caliph in order to ensure her father's bloodline. She spent most of her time secluded in prayer. On the rare occasions when she ventured out into Hilm, the people showed their deep love for her by showering her feet with flowers.
