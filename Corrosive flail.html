Corrosive flails were rare enchanted weapons that exuded corrosive magic.

Powers

Corrosive flails possessed a simple +1 enchantment that guided the wielder's hand towards the target and boosted its damage. These flail constantly produced corrosive magic that managed to damage the target even when the weapon missed.
