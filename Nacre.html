Nacre was a precious substance that made up pearl and mother of pearl , as well as some shells .

Description

As a precious material, nacre was distinguished from mother of pearl by its "fire luster". Like other shells, ivory , and precious metals, nacre was used for decorative inlays on artistic objects.
