Norys was a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Norys was a human woman with fair skin and gray eyes. She wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist: a helmet , a set of chainmail , and a tabard adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. She wielded a spear .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Norys was stationed in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, where she patrolled the streets near the Blushing Mermaid , accopanied by a steel watcher .
