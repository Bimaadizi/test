Giant's bane were enchanted sling bullets created for defense against giants .

Powers

Giant's bane bullets were enchanted to deal additional damage when used against giants.

History

Giant's bane ammunition was created by halflings , defending their homes from giant raids. Over the years, these bullets were the result of many giants' demise.
