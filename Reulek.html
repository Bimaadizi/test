Reulek was a duergar ghost at the Fane of the Eye under Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Relationship

In life, Reulek had a partnership of convenience with an oni called Obratu .

History

Reulek descended into the Fane with an intent to recover the treasures of Besilmer . After he stole a helm from the king's tomb, he was ambushed and killed by four drow ghosts and rose again as a ghost himself.

In 1491 DR , some adventurers infiltrated the Fane and were confronted by Reulek's ghost. During the encounter, Reulek was ultimately destroyed.
