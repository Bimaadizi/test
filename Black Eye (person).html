The Black Eye was an Eye of Gruumsh orc shaman who lived in the Many-Arrows -occupied Tower District of Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

The Black Eye acted as the guardian of a shrine of Gruumsh that had been erected by the Many-Arrows orcs in the district's Merchant Square.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the Black Eye fought a battle with Gord Roundshield and wounded the dwarf in the gut. To get his revenge, Gord Roundshield enlisted a dwarven adventurer to kill the Black Eye and burn his shrine to the ground to draw the shaman out.
