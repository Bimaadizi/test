Pieron Ramdon ( pronounced : /ˈ p ɪər ʌ n ˈ r ɑː m d ʌ n / PEER-un RAM-dun ) was a Lords' Alliance 's agent in Mulmaster .

History

Pieron was a soldier in Cormyr until he decided to go to the Moonsea in search of a better fortune.

In 1491 DR , he was possibly involved in the case of the burning of Elmwood .
