Kylia was a rock gnome illusionist who served as a messenger and informant for Laeral Silverhand in Skullport , in the 14 th century DR .

Description

She was dressed in an all-red, hooded robe and wore a golden necklace.

Personality

She had a very pleasant personality, to the point that many of her foes underestimated her. Kylia also had a flair for the dramatic and was especially fond of appearing and disappearing in a puff of smoke.

Possessions

Kylia owned several magical items, among them a robe of eyes , a staff of thunder and lightning , a pair of bracers of defense , and a ring of gargoyles .

Activities

She was an active member of the Moonstars , Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsuns secretive organization.

Relationships

Gnossos was in a romantic relationship with Gnossos , a gnome bookmaker, circa 1368 DR.

History

When she was in skullport on assignment she stayed at The Deepfires inn, in the guise of Liak a half-elven apprentice to Irusyl .
