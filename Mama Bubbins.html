Mama Bubbins was the mother of hin twins, Flymber and Homer Bubbins , from the town of Beregost in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Flymber and Homer Bubbins were born some years before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR in the town of Beregost. At some point, Homer Bubbins set out to pursue a career as a member of the clergy of Ilmater in Baldur's Gate to the delight and pride of Mama Bubbins. However, despite what he said in his letters, Homer did not become the "right-hand temple manager" under Father Lorgan , but joined the criminal organization known as the Guild . Eventually, in 1492 DR , Mama Bubbins's second son, Flymber, followed in his twin's footsteps and left home to join the Open Hand Temple. Mama Bubbins happily announced that in her birthday letter to Homer.
