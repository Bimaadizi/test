Lhemako Taraskh was a caravan master of the Zhentarim during the mid-14 th century DR , who served under the Head of Caravans, Meer .

History

At some point, Lhemako recovered the lost scimitar Shazzellim and had it identified by the sage of Hillsfar , Helavaster . He then proceeded to bestow it upon his superior, Meer.

Lhemako was believed to have died in 1368 DR , during the destruction of Zhentil Keep by the Cyricist army comprised of giants and white dragons .
