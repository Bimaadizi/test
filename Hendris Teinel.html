Hendris Teinel was the landlord of the Scarlet Shield inn in the town of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Around 1372 DR , Hendris promised free meals nightly at the Scarlet Shield to any adventurer who could bring him back a piece of the Fools Finger obelisk. These rosy-red magical stones he carved into small shields and sold to merchants leaving for Waterdeep . Apparently they were quite popular there among adventurers and nobles, who used them to make their drinks cold and glow. Despite the quality of the Scarlet Shield's food, many adventurers took him up on this for the sake of a ready source of food, and almost as many made it back with one of the stones. However, Hendris required them to be at least as big as his own, sizeable, big toe.
