Brandilar was the guard captain of the Merchant's League headquarters in Baldur's Gate .

History

During the iron crisis , Brandilar was aware of abnormalities in his organization's leaders. When he met Gorion's Ward , he expressed his concerns and helped the Ward in the investigation.
