Vees Talendar was an advisor of the Hulorn of Selgaunt in Sembia and a secret priest of Shar .

Relationships

Vees was a scion of the Talendar family, being a nephew of Marance Talendar . He was a servant and ally of the Prince of Shade Rivalen Tanthul .

History

In his youth, Vees was sent to Waterdeep to avoid the dark influence of his family. He went back to Selgaunt as a new man and priest of Siamorphe and soon became a key advisor of Thamalon Uskevren II during the start of his reign as hulorn. In the meantime, he ordered the construction of a new temple dedicated to Siamorphe, but in truth a temple to Shar . In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , after the start of the Sembian Civil War , Vees suggested Tamlin ask the Shadovar for assistance and an alliance against Mirabeta Selkirk , as Rivalen had ordered Vees to do.

After the alliance between the Shadovar and Selgaunt was secure, Rivalen decided he no longer needed Vees, who was an unnecessary witness of his plots. So Rivalen revealed part of the truth to Tamlin, who accused Vees of being a traitor to Selgaunt. As a final step for his conversion to Shar, Tamlin killed Vees in cold blood. Publicly, it was announced that Vees had died during the Battle of Selgaunt .
