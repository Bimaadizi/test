Gelthoon Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Gelthoon Street was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's North Ward . Gelthoon Street branched south from Suldown Street and eventually ended on Golden Serpent Street . Other roads intersecting Gelthoon Street included Aveen Street and Brahiir's Street .
