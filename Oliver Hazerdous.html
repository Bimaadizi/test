Admiral of the Puce Oliver Hazerdous was a mercenary admiral from the Whamite Isles . Oliver's allegiances could be bought in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
