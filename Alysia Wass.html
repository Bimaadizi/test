Alysia Wass was a half-elven fighter , mage and adventurer , wandering the Realms in search of her long-lost mother in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

She was a half-elf with platinum blonde hair.

Personality

Alysia idolized elven society and was fascinated by everything elven . She would ask as many questions as she could get away with from those elves she encountered on her travels.

Abilities

Her great dexterity was her greatest advantage, as it allowed her to forego armor .

Possessions

She wore traveling clothes and wielded a sling and longsword in combat.

History

Alysia Wass grew up in Waterdeep with her human father, being abandoned by her elven mother who went adventuring. As an adult she herself became an adventurer, hoping to one day locate her mother.
