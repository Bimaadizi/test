The Winding Way was a tavern located in the center of Soubar in the Western Heartlands .

History

The tavern was also the home of Mag , a mysterious woman who owned the bar around 1367 DR .

Geography

The Winding Way was located by the ruins of the old temple or abbey of Bane that was the heart of the village.
