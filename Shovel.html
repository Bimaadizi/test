A shovel was a digging tool that had a curved blade on its end. A very similar tool was the spade .

Description

The ideal shovel had a four-foot-long wooden handle and a curved metal head with a blunt edge, allowing it to carry soil and not be damaged from cutting into heavy earth. These digging and shoveling tools were used by workers and miners across Faerun , Kara-Tur , and Zakhara to excavate dirt, gravel, sand , clay , and other soft or loose materials from the ground. Instead of just being used as a tool, a shovel could also be used as a weapon . Hoes served a similar purpose in breaking up soil, though they were used quite differently.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold generic and specialized shovels. Basic coal shovels cost 2 gp , gardening and snow shovels cost 3 gp, whilst postholers cost 4 gp each. Small hand spades could be had for only 2 sp .

Magic

There were several magical shovels known around the multiverse .

History

In the Year of the Crystal Casket, 249 DR , the Magister Galeerie Mulurkrath was killed by a shovel to the head.

The symbol of Fireshear was that of a crossed pickaxe , blade , and shovel.

In Zakhara , the Pick and Shovel Corps was a group of ex- slaves that won their freedom. The "shovels" indicated the dwarf members of the corps.
