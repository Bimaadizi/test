The Hallwinters were a human family of knights who were renowned up and down the Sword Coast . Their coat of arms depicted a crown with six blunt points.

History

Members of the Hallwinters served as knights in cities like Neverwinter and Waterdeep . They had been doing so honorably since before the destruction of Neverwinter , and were still famous as of the late 15 th century DR .
