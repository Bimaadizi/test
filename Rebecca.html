Rebecca was the cook and a server at the Mithrest Inn in the City of Coin, Athkatla , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Rebecca was a human woman with sun-kissed bronze skin and blonde hair. She was a diligent worker and polite with the inn's clientele.
