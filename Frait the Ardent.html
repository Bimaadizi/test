Frait the Ardent was an elf favored soul in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Frait had gray skin, gray eyes, and short dark gray hair. She wore a sleeveless dress of sorts, which was entirely black and gray.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR during the Elven Crusade , Frait joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp outside the ruins of Myth Drannor . She provided training to prospective favored souls at the camp.
