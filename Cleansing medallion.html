Cleansing medallions were a type of small amulets imbued with healing magics but not associated with any faith in particular.

Description

These medallions were small round silver disks that radiated an aura of well-being and comfort.

Powers

Cleansing medallions possessed magical healing capabilities. When wielded by clerics and paladins that worship powers of good, the amulet drew upon their faith and focused it into divine magical healing, on par with the cure light wounds spell once per day.

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , cleansing medallions were sold by the Temple Pavilion in the city of Targos , during the Legion of the Chimera crisis.
