The Temple of Torm was a temple devoted to Torm , the God of Duty, located in the city of Orvyltar in Ulgarth during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Given the high social standing of knights in Ulgarth, the temple received many of the sworn warriors who paid homage to Torm. The king of Ulgarth even came to the temple to worship.
