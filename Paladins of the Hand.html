The Paladins of the Hand , also known as the Twelve Champions of Torm , was an order of paladins and other holy servants of Torm that were active in the Shining Plains during the 12 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Blood Tusk Charge, 1158 DR , the Paladins of the Hand fought in the defense of Lothtia in the Shining Plains against the orc army of Thoktor Ironfangs .
