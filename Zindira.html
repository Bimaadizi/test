Zindira was a priestess of Eilistraee .

Activities

Zindira was one of the Protectors of the Song in the Promenade of the Dark Maiden .

History

In 1377 DR , she was one of the six protectors chosen by Cavatina Xarann to lead an assault on the main temple of Kiaransalee .

In 1379 DR , Zindira was on guard at one the entrances of the Promenade when the-then immaterial Cavatina tried to call her attention.
