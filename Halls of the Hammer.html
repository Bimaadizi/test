Geography

The dwarfhold was located a bit west of Mount Hlim beneath the lands near Highstar Lake .

Located above ground, near to the Halls of the Hammer, was a wooden fort called Hammer Hall . The fort's namesake organization used it as their base to lead exploratory journeys into the dwarven halls.

Features

The subterranean domain was believed to comprise a large central chamber that was affected by illusions of terrifying violence.

Inhabitants

As of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the halls were inhabited by a number of watchghosts and helmed horrors .
