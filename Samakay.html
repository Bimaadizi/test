Samakay the Wry was the king of Golconda in Turmish in 1358 DR .

Activities

Samakay ruled the small kingdom of Golconda.

History

In 1355 DR , the self-styled king of Muktar , Hetep , started a war against Golconda.

In 1358 DR, during Samakay's war council, the spymaster Boubakar reported that one of his men had discovered that Hetep's battle plans were hidden in the small keep of Fort Blood . Convinced by Boubakar's arguments and despite Count Belim 's objections, Samakay hired an adventuring party well known to him to resolve the matter.

Soon after, however, Samakay died of old age without an heir.

Relationship

Samakay trusted only Lord Daud until the latter's death in 1357 DR .

Personality

Samakay was a just and kind ruler but knew nothing of military matters.
