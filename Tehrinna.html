Tehrinna was the proprietor of The Lake Spirits , a bathhouse and inn located in the Amnian city of Amnwater during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Tehrinna had flame red hair and a sweet nature. Despite being only 4 ft 6 in (1.4 m) tall, she was muscular enough to expel most troublemakers from her inn.
