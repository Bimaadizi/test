Tanisell , who was also nicknamed the Cloaked , was the seventh High Mage of Silverymoon .

Activities

Tanisell was elected to the position of High Mage by the citizens of Silverymoon in the Year of the Giant's Oath, 883 DR , and served until the Year of Great Riches, 920 DR , when he died of a fever.

History

He was originally from Ascalhorn .
