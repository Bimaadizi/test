Great Gark was a king among the goblins of Nachtur in the Feywild in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .

Activities

Gark attempted to expand his kingdom into the Moonshae Isles by sending goblins, worgs and other creatures to the Dernall Forest in Callidyrr . This was initially a small nuisance for the scattered villages found within the forest, but Gark's minions later infested the entire wood.
