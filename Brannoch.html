Brannoch was a small town located on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Brannoch was located in eastern Snowdown along the coast of the Sea of Swords . Brannoch was unique among Snowdown's settlments in that it was the only one not connected to Snowdown Ride . This was largely because the marsh of Maloren Sods separated the town from the rest of the island, making constructing a road to Brannoch nearly impossible.

History

During the height of the Kingdom of Snowdown , Pengram was one of the larger and more influential cantrevs in regards to influencing the monarch's policies.
