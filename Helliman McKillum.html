Helliman Mc Killum was a human priest of Tempus in the 14 th century DR .

History

Helliman Mc Killum was a simple warrior worshiper of the Lord of Battles when he used his expertise in sword-fighting to rescue a village. He was inducted into the Church of Tempus after that heroic deed and quickly claimed the rankings within the organization.

Later, he set out on a search for a legendary sacred chalice .
