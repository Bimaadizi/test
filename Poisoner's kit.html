Poisoner's kit were collections of tools and supplies used in the creation and portioning of various toxins, venoms, and poisons .

Description

A typical poisoner's kit was a pouch filled with poisonous substances, plants, dust, and extracts, all to be used in the mixing of potent poisons. The kits also included measuring tools, such as spoons and vials. Some kits included recipes and manuals for toxins as well intrusions for mixing a basic antitoxin.

Notable Users

Poisoner's kit were commonly used by various rogues and assassins of the Realms .
