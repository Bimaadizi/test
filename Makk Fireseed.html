Makk Fireseed was an elven priest of Rillifane Rallathil .

Activities

He convinced his followers that the deity's favorite creature was the Duathamper canary , a type of canary that nested in the butternut trees on the River Duathamper . The canary's yellow feathers were symbolic of the sun, and its green throat represented the leaves of trees.
