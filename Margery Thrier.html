Margery Thrier , also known as Mags , was a businesswoman and slaver in Hillsfar in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Mags managed the finances of the Hillsfar Arena .

History

Circa 1486 DR , she bought many non- human prisoners from sorcerer Breex Vandermast .

Relationship

Mags always walked with her lieutenant Horac and a goliath slave-bodyguard.
