Potions of infravision were a type of potion that allowed the imbiber see in the dark.

Description

These potions had a brackish taste.

Powers

This potion temporarily gave the imbiber the ability to see in infravision . A human who took this potion would be capable of seeing in infravision with a 60 feet (18 meters) range. If the potion was taken by a creature who had natural infravision abilities, their sight range increased by 60 feet (18 meters).

Creation

An elixir of darkvision could be created by combining the alchemically -extracted essence of crystalline lenses and certain types of salts .
