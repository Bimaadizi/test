Prince Mehtmid was a political figure from the nation of Zagrus in the Whamite Isles . Mehtmid represented Zagrus in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
