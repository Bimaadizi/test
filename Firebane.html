Firebane was an enchanted jambiya stored within the Gallery of Antiquities in the Jade Palace of the Necromancers in Ysawis .

Description

The hilt and scabbard of this weapon were made from pure jade .

Powers

This jambiya made it substantially easier for the wielder to strike and harm their opponents. As its name implied, the powers of Firebane were doubled against fire-dwelling creatures or any creature using flame magic.
