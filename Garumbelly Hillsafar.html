Garumbelly Hillsafar was the leader of Clan Hillsafar in Vaasa in 1359 DR .

Activities

Garumbelly gained his nickname, "Grumble", for his habit of murmuring to himself. As leader of a clan of 2,000 dwarves, he was a powerful man in Vaasa.

Personality

Garumbelly was a generous and compassionate leader despite his gruff appearance. He always said what he thought. Garumbelly was always concerned about the good for his clan and what he could do to help.
