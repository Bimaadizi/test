Onthaer was a priest of Ilmater from the city of Athkatla in Amn .

History

Described as "saintly", Onthaer became a hermit on the lonely, windswept isle of Falconsrise in the Trackless Sea . There he wrestled and killed the last rock cat on the island with his bare hands, some time before 1267 DR .
