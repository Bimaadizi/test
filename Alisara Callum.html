Alisara Callum was the Chief Speaker of Helm's Hold circa 1479 DR .

Description

She wore a purple tabard emblazoned with a white dragon sigil.

Personality

Alisara was well liked within Helm's Hold, helping inspire loyalty among its residents and granting a degree of legitimacy to its local government.

History

She was once a soldier in the Purple Dragons of Cormyr .

Activities

Alisara was accepting of spellscarred refugees.
