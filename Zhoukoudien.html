Zhoukoudien , known as the High One, was a ruler of the batrachi civilization of Merrouroboros during the Days of Thunder ( −35,000 DR to −30,000 DR ).

The batrachi grew to the height of their power under Zhoukoudien's wise leadership. However, their golden age ended abruptly when the High One was killed in battle by Omo , a titan thane, around −31,500 DR . The batrachi lord Bazim-Gorag later called Zhoukoudien a fool who unwisely made war against the Jotunbrud and lost the empire.

The High One shared the same name as the batrachi realm of Zhoukoudien .
