Hask Bloodaxe was the sword-thane of the Black Lion tribe of Uthgardt barbarians that lived in Beorunna's Well during the late-14 th century DR . As sword-thane, Hask served as the personal bodyguard to chief Andar Heartwood .

In addition to guarding the chieftain, Hask was the primary law-keeper for the Black Lions of Beorunna's Well.
