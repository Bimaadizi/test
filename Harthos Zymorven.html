Harthos Zymorven was the commander of Zymorven Hall in the Rauvin Vale during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Harthos was the lord of the Zymorven family of Silverymoon . His duty was to maintain the keep that watched over the Rauvin Road , which was patrolled by the keep's soldiers.

Relationships

Lord Zymorven had one son, Harthal , with whom he was estranged.

He was friends with the innkeeper Urgala Meltimer of Triboar .

Possessions

He wielded a giant slayer greatsword until it was stolen by his son.
