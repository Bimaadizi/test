Shenlazeen was an ancient lich -like undead servitor found in Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

Abilities

Shenlazeen possessed a mantle of invulnerability that made him immune to most lesser spells . It could also call forth and control other lesser undead, including zombies , wights , and skeletons .

History

The fellow was stuck inside Tyrintar's Hall , along with others like Gothror the Ravager and Bonehoof . The addled arcanist was familiar with the Icon of the Dwarves from before the Weeping War but had trouble finding it, which was eventually sought after by Uhlsdrin Nirveth in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR .
