Fel'rekt Lafeen ( pronounced : /ˈ f ɛ l r ɛ k t l ɑː ˈ f i n / FEL-rekt lah-FEEN ) was one of the top agents of the Bregan D'aerthe in Waterdeep during the early 1490s DR .

Activities

Fel'rekt, together with Soluun Xibrindas and Krebbyg Masq'il'yr , was tasked by Jarlaxle with doing dirty deeds in the city. Fel'rekt and Krebbyg preferred to act during the night. Fel'rekt resided at the Scarlet Marpenoth ship, where he cleaned his firearm.

History

Fel'rekt was born female but at some point changed to being male. However, disgusted with the treatment of males in drow society, he joined Bregan D'aerthe . Almost immediately, Jarlaxle was impressed with Fel'rekt, who volunteered for all the most dangerous missions.

In 1492 DR , if Jarlaxle managed to obtain the Stone of Golorr , then Fel'rekt and Krebbyg confronted some adventurers first in Mistshore , where they had the support of the Lantanese mechanical dragon turtle Big Belchy , and again in the Dock Ward .

Personality

Fel'rekt lacked the typical drow cruelty and killed opponents only if he had no other options. He was the most loyal lieutenant of Jarlaxle.

Relationship

Fel'rekt and Krebbyg were a working team.

Possessions

Like his fellow Soluun Xibrindas and Krebbyg Masq'il'yr, Fel'rekt was equipped with Lantanese firearms .
