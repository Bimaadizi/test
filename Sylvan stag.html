Sylvan stags were a unique breed of deer found inhabiting the depths of Neverwinter Wood in the Northwest Faerûn .

Description

These impressive stags were considered to be some of the most majestic deer. They had large, powerful bodies and a crown of awe-inspiring antlers on their heads.

Ecology

Sylvan stags were possible to tame and train and were sometimes used as mounts. They granted small amounts of magical healing to their masters.

History

Some tamed sylvan stags were sold by the beast masters of Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR .
