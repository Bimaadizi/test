Candoria was a gang leader in Nexal .

History

Candoria was one of the renegades living in the human quarter of Nexal in 1362 DR . He led gang of thieves .

That year, he was paid to discourage people from investigating the recently captured Tabaxi . For this, he and his men may have attacked Golotl and an adventuring party hired by Drakosa to find the Cloak of One Plume .
