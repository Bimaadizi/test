Swipe was the nickname of the rat-faced head of the Rogues' Guild in Hillsfar in 1367 DR . He and his guild acted as personal spies in the city for Lord Maalthiir but working too close with the Red Plumes prevented Swipe and his men from uncovering the true menace.
