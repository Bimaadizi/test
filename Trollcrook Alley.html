Trollcrook Alley , sometimes misnamed Trollcrush Alley , was a minor avenue of hard-packed earth and gravel in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This curving road stretched from southeast to northwest, linking the streets of Zastrow and Snail .

History

Prior to the 14 th century DR this alley was frequented a night for some time by a vicious gang of trolls that had been dwelling in the city's sewers . They managed to slay many citizens before they were finally all hunted down.

By the 14 th century DR, this alley was relatively safe and frequently used by carters.
