Blind Ippsley was a merchant and vendor that lived in Helm's Hold during the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the Siege of Helm's Hold in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Ippsley set up shop in the Old Dirty Dwarf inn. He offered a variety of useful items that benefited the adventuring types frequenting the inn while trying to help Helm's Hold with his horrific devil situation.
