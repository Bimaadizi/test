TypeJo stick

Kane's jo stick was a pure white jo stick , carried by Grandmaster Kane . It was formed from a branch of the White Tree that grew in the courtyard of Gareth Dragonsbane 's Castle in Bloodstone Village on the shore of Lake Midai .

It was said that in the hands of Kane, this white stick was as hard as adamantine and could shatter stone. Also, it never got dirty or dusty from use as a walking stick.
