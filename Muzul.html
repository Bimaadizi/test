Devout Muzul was a novice priestess of the Absolute that resided with the goddess' other cultists at the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

She was blessed with the mark of the Absolute , given to her by Priestess Gut .

History

Muzul was present at the Shattered Sanctum in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , when the leaders of the Shattered Sanctum went searching for individuals present aboard a mind flayer nautiloid that came crashing near their fortress along the shore of the River Chionthar .
