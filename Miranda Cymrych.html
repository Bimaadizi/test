Miranda "the Fair" Cymrych was High Queen of the Moonshae Isles at the turn of the 7 th century DR .

Relationships

She was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the daughter of her predecessor, High King Gordon . Her successor was her first son, Embro Cymrych I .

History

Miranda assumed the throne of the High Queen in the Year of Tumbled Bones, 569 DR , after the death of her father.

She ruled for forty years before dying of natural causes in the Year of Old Bones, 609 DR .
