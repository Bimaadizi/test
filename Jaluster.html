Jaluster was a mage and prolific author of spellbooks who enjoyed traveling .

History

Jaluster was carrying his latest work, Jaluster's Orizon , when he visited Ascalhorn . He attempted to save the city from demons but was torn apart in the process, leaving his final work incomplete. Before he died Jaluster was said to have personally killed three liches and five demons.
