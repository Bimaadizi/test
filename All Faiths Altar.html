The All Faiths Altar was a shrine in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . It was open to worshipers of all non-evil faiths (with Bane , Cyric , Malar , and such excluded).

Description

It was a large shrine.

Services

Poor travelers sometimes slept in the shrine's forechancel, usually when the weather was bad. However, pilgrims and wandering priests were permitted to stay no more than a tenday .
