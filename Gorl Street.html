Gorl Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Gorl Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . It originated on the the Street of Glances where it branched southwest to end on the Street of the Singing Dolphin just one building north of Ivory Street . Gorl Street also connected with Sniff Alley and Staghunter's Way .
