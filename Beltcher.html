Beltcher was a Netherese bard who lived in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

The bard was an avid historian, and was fond of visiting the Library of Netheril , where he, Degliangeli , and Ebrard would pour over the texts within in search of historical insights and lore.
