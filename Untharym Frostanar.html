Untharym Frostanar was the high priest of Darkwave Hall , a temple dedicated to Umberlee , in Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR .

Activities

Untharym was the leader of Umberlee's faithful in Urmlaspyr. His congregation within the temple consisted of seven specialty priests called waveservants and four followers.
