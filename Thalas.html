Thalas was an elvish lute -playing bard from Evereska who found himself in the Ten-Towns in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Thalas was a slender and spindly elf with red hair and long thin fingers.

Personality

Thalas had a fear of public speaking, something that made his bardic aspirations problematic. He was a nervous man and even conversing with a stranger was hard for him, however, he only felt comfortable when singing.
