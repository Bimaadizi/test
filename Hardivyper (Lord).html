Lord Hardivyper was a nobleman from Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

History

He attended the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , where he was unfortunately among those that died after steel was drawn on King Foril Obarskyr I .
