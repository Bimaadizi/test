Clan Flameshade was a duergar clan of the 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Clan Flameshade members were hired by Sergeant Thrinn to help the True Soul of the Absolute Nere enslave more people for the further excavations of Grymforge .
