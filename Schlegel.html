Schlegel was an aspiring wizard and would-be adventurer inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Schlegel was eager to test her skills by joining an adventuring group but had difficulty finding anyone who would accept her. She owned a house in west Daggerford located along Wall Street near the Caravan Gate .
