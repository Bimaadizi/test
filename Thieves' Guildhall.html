The Thieves' Guildhall was the headquarters of the naug-orls in the city of Zhentil Keep during the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Location

The guildhall was located in Zhentil Keep's Inner Quarter in the southwest on the Street of Bones

Description

The Thieves' Guildhall was a fortified structure and stood four storys tall. The guildhall's basement connected directly to Zhentil Keep's sewer system, which was used extensively for illicit operations.

Activities

The naug-orls used this location as a base of operations, a training area, and living area.

Inhabitants

Most of the thieves who lived here had not yet accumulated enough wealth to purchase their own homes elsewhere in Zhentil Keep.
