Simon Dessino was a Lord of Arrabar , notable for his short reign circa 1370 DR .

History

Simon Dessino claimed the position of the Lord of Arrabar sometime before the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR . During his rule, Lord Dessino focused on two cities in particularâ€” Lachom and Nimpeth . Simon was actively trying to force Lachom to ask for Chondath's aid and increase Arrabar's power. To achieve this goal, Lord Dessino hired a gang of fire giants from the Deepwing Mountains led by a witch doctor named Kinondra . The giants raided and burned down several farms outside of Lachom and were ready to mount several assaults to deal humiliating defeat to the city's defenders and force Lachom to seek aid from their old master nation.

Lord Dessino's relationship with Nimpeth's Lord Woren was kept peaceful. Woren fed intel on other cities of Chondath and independent city-states to Lord Dessin, appeasing Arrabar. Of course, Lord Woren bent the facts to make himself and his city shine in the eyes of the Arrabarran ruler.

Simon Dessino's lordship over Arrabar ended sometime between 1370 and 1372 DR .
