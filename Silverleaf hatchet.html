The Silverleaf hatchet was an enchanted dwarven axe created in the glory days of the City of Song, Myth Drannor .

Description

The Silverleaf hatchet was created with a distinctly dwarven shape and design. The axe's head was silver -plated.

Powers

The weapon carried a simple +2 enchantment, however, when wielded against orcs and orogs , it became functionally an enchanted +3 axe. Additionally, when battling orcs and orogs, the hatchet magically improved the wielder's defenses, similarly to a +1 enchantment.

History

The axe was created by the dwarven smiths and enchanters of Myth Drannor, before the city's destruction in the Weeping War in 714 DR . By the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the Silverleaf hatchet , along with its sister weapon, the Silverleaf rod , fell into the scaly hands of lizardfolk , led by a mysterious sorcerer named Malgi Hi who laired in tunnels of the Dwarven Dungeons .
