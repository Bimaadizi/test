House Gheldieg was an Amnian noble house originating from the Gildeggh family of Lord-Magister Lhorar Gildeggh of Waterdeep . The family was exiled and came to Amn, marrying into its families and gradually transforming into the Gheldieg family. Its power was eventually controlled by House Tanislove .
