History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , a horde of goblins took up residence with a cave near the home of the old mage Adoy . After they fell under the sway of a hobgoblin named Rogdok , the goblins killed Rilsa, leaving Magreb saddened and a bit paranoid.

Following Rilsa's death, Magreb wanted to recover her remains and take vengeance on her killers.
