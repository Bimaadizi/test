Kumed Hahpret replaced the murdered Aznar Thrul by 1385 DR as zulkir of Evocation .

Importance

Kumed supported the council of zulkirs in their war against the lich Szass Tam , who proclaimed himself regent of all Thay.

He was supposedly killed by Nevron in 1385 DR before all remaining zulkirs left Bezantur in flight of Szass Tam's undead forces.
