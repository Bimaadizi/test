Simron was a Zhentilar warrior in the mid-14 th century DR , part of the Eastern Stonelands Company.

Personality

He was open about not being in a rush to get slain.

Possessions

He wore black armor, and carried a soot-darkened sword during the night of their raid on Shandril Shessair.

Activities

He formed part of the Eastern Stonelands company of the Zhentilar .

Relationships

When trying to seize Shandril Shessair , he was part of Karkul Memrimmon 's squad.

History

Simron was part of the Eastern Stonelands Zhentilar company that Karkul Memrimmon led in the capture of Shandril Shessair in late 1357 DR . However, he ran away at some point during the battle.

Back at Zhentil Keep , he was personally tortured by Manshoon as part of his debriefing, then slain.
