Kothont Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Kothont .

Location

The villa was located in the west section of Waterdeep 's North Ward at the intersection of Delzorin Street and Shield Street at the border with the Sea Ward . The east side of the villa also sat along Copper Street .

Description

Four separate buildings composed the walled villa compound. Gardens and courtyards lay between each building. The buildings were one or two stories tall.
