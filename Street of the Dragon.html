The Street of the Dragon was a main thoroughfare in Daerlun , Sembia , circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . This street ran east-west from the Sunset Gate (the western gate out of the city to the Way of the Manticore ), through the Westmarket area, and on to the Place of Banners , a large open area in the center of the city that contained the seat of local government, Bergun Hall . On the north side of the street, not far from the Sunset gate was the luxurious Black Banner Inn .
