Helm Dwarf-Friend was the ruler of Sundabar and a member of the Council of 12 Peers , and its successor, the nine-member Council of the Marches .

Personality

Helm was known as a wise and fair leader.

History

Helm first gained notoriety when he was a the war-captain of the Bloodaxe Mercenary Company , his valiant campaigns earned him popularity in Sundabar. In 1355 DR , Helm became the sixth Ruling Master of the city of Sundabar.

Relationships

Helm Dwarf-Friend had a son Frosthelm , that become the king of Sundabar after him. Frosthelm had a son that became the next king of the city, Firehelm .
