Lhaaril was a shadovar of Sakkors and seneschal to Prince Brennus Tanthul in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Awakened Sleepers, 1484 DR , Lhaaril announced to Brennus that his father Telamont Tanthul had just arrived in Sakkors to speak with him.

Later, he introduced to his master a scout, Ovith , with important news to tell.

Some time after, Brennus ordered him to muster a force of loyal soldiers to go to the Abbey of the Rose to capture Vasen Cale .
