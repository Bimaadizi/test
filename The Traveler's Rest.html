The Traveler's Rest was an inn located in the city of Scornubel in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Though quite large, the inn was an older establishment and showing signs of its age as of Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

The accommodations within the Traveler's Rest were good and the prices were cheap.
