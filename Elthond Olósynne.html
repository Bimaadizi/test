Elthond Olósynne was the third King of the Olósynne Dynasty who ruled the nation of Aglarond .

Activities

Elthond's short reign lasted from the Year of the Lynx, 1189 DR , to the Year of the Bloody Wave, 1194 DR . He inherited the throne upon the death of his father, King Althon .

Relationships

His younger brother was Philaspur .
