Miirphys Irithyl was fourth Coronal of Cormanthyr , having led the city for hundreds of years from −1338 DR to −791 DR . He was a revered priest of Sehanine Moonbow and was remarkable as the only moon elf to rule the empire as Coronal.

Activities

He did lots of work sending ships into Realmspace to prevent the incursions of Netherese spelljammers from expanding into his domain.

Possessions

Unlike other coronals, Miirphys only raised the ' Rulers' Blade once, during the blade-ritual, and never again.

History

Coronal Miirphys was known for for raising the Crescent Court , a temple of his patron deity Sehanine Moonbow , in the −1293 DR .

He was also known for having imprisoned the Trio Nefarious , a band of nycaloths . These fiends returned to the realm centuries later, causing the downfall of his empire in the 8 th century DR .

Lastly, Miirphys was involved in the binding the demon-consorting elves of House Dlardrageth to their villa in Myth Drannor . The elves mostly destroyed themselves, but later returned in the 14 th century DR to attempt to take the city. He had his moon elf mother's unique blue hair and silver eyes.

Rumors & Legends

It was rumored that Coronal Miirphys returned to the Crescent Court after his death, in the form of either a ghost , baelnorn , or even as one of the elven Reverend Ones .
