Koyoss was an Iulutiun village known for the quality of its sled dogs.

Geography

Koyoss was located in the Alpuk region of the Great Glacier , alongside a stream flowing between the Uppuk River and the Lugalpgotak Sea .

Trade

The inhabitants of Koyoss raised sled dogs of every breed native to the Great Glacier, which included the heteff , mukteff , and okteff breeds.
