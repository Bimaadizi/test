Shrunken heads were a type of powerful spell fetish used by heroes of the Malatran Plateau in Kara-Tur .

Description

The fetish was a small carved head trinket.

Powers

The owner of a shrunken head fetish received limited protection from foul necromantic death magics of the headshrinkers of the Living Jungle and necromancers from beyond the Plateau.
