Sunstone bullets were a variety of enchanted bullets that were crafted from sunstone gems . They were were used as ammunition for certain ranged weapons such as slings .

Powers

When these +1 bullets they struck a person, creature or object they detonated in a small burst of flames and scorched their target. They had a functional range of approximately 100' (30.5 m).
