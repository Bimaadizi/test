Mulhorandan lion-wine was a type of wine that was exported from the country of Mulhorand . Select vintages were considered to be of exceptional taste by wine connoisseurs.

Trivia

Lord Chess of Zhentil Keep was known to appreciate the taste of lion-wine around the Year of the Blazing Brand, 1334 DR .
