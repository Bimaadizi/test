Harandil Durlaven I was the first King of the Durlarven Dynasty to rule over Impiltur .

Activities

Harandil defeated a massive horde of orcs from Vastar in the Year of the Wyvernfall, 512 DR , at the Battle of Bloody Reeds , earning him a favorable reputation that would eventually lead to his rise to the title of king by acclamation. He reigned until his death in the Year of the Basilisk, 537 DR .

Relationships

He married the daughter of King Sharaun Mirandor , Princess Tayissa . Harandil's son and heir was Harandil II .
