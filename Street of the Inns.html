The Street of the Inns was located in Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Description

The Street of the Inns branched off of the Pilgrims' Route to head directly north into the vast courtyard that housed the Golden Mosque and the Palace of the Enlightened Throne . The Street of the Inns led foot traffic to the various mosques, while the Palace Lane branched off toward the palace.
