The Var were a strong dangerous tribe of Nars found in western Narfell and Damara . They measured 2,400 strong in 1357 DR .

Activities

The Var expressed interest in mining gems from the Giantspire Mountains . They were tolerant and even welcome of outlanders to their territory.

Base of Operations

The Var ranged along the base of the Giantspire Mountains in western Narfell.
