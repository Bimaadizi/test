Sleda was a Netherese cleric and weaponsmith who worked at Dundaden's Armory in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Sleda worked alongside Cheron to meet the demand for Rualadeen 's blades. Though they were proficient smiths in their own right, he still put work into their projects to ensure the highest quality.
