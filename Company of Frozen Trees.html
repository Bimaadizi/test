The Company of Frozen Trees was an adventuring band active in the early 11 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Hunted Whale, 1013 DR , the Company of Frozen Trees was joined by a bard named Gallow Deely . He played an enchanted instrument made by a hin master, Relias Blackseed , called The Cittern of War . It aided the adventurers with its inspiring magic in battling a young green dragon . This deed was commemorated in a ballad called The Fight of Green Thirteen , penned by Gallow. The song remained popular in the taverns of west Faerûn into the 14 th century DR .
