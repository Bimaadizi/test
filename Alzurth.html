Alzurth , also called the Dozenking , was the Mage Royal of Westgate who overthrew the Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty and ruled as king for over four decades.

Activities

Alzurth was the Mage Royal to King Roadaeron before usurping his throne in the Year of the Twelverule, 1117 DR . He would rule until the Year of the Prancing Centaur, 1162 DR , when the exiled Roadaeron returned, and he was slain by Zeladazar the Ghost Sword .
