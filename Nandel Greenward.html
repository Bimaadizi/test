Nandel Greenward was an Illuskan wizard inhabiting Waterdeep and Undermountain during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Nandel was a student of arcane studies at Halaster's Heirs , a school of magic established Trobriand and Muiral who were apt past pupils of Halaster Blackcloak . She was actually the first student to join the school.
