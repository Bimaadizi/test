Description

She was heavily muscled and had blonde hair worn in two long braids. Many dwarves thought she would be the perfect woman if she was just a few feet shorter.

Activities

She owned Luanna's Forge which was located in the Foreign Quarter in between Center Street and Teshwave Street .

Luanna had a reputation for crafting the best weapons and armor in the city.

Relationships

She did not get along well with her neighbor Balrud Thal . The pair tried to make the other's life as miserable as possible.
