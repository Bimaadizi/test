The Plaguechanged Thing was a plaguechanged creature that lived in the caverns below Westgate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The Plaguechanged Thing was a blob of protoplasm about 1 mile (1.6 kilometers) long.

Abilities

The Plaguechanged Thing had incredible psionic abilities thanks to the mutations caused in its brain by the Spellplague , but its powers were limited to the confines of its lair.

History

The Plaguechanged Thing was born as a consequence of the Spellplague of the Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR . It was originally a dwarf excavator, but the extreme alterations of its body left it trapped in the caves below Westgate. The Plaguechanged Thing was still alive as of 1479 DR .
