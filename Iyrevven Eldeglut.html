Iyrevven Eldeglut was the wife of Halonder Eldeglut in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . She and her husband were targetted by drow infiltrators.

Personality

She quite obviously disapproved of his husband participating or considering carnal carousing with his friends, and didn't like being upstaged in terms of luxury wear.

Activities

To the drow trying to impersonate them, the Eldegluts had a broad selection of business interests, and were influential people.

Relationships

She was married to Halonder Eldeglut .

History

Iyrevven Eldeglut and her husband were targetted by spell-disguised drow infiltrators who intended to replace them with some of their own numbers in 1369 DR . The drow made their move on them during a party at the Eldegluts' home, successfully drugging them. However, there was a complication, as the drow also discovered one of their own agents had been replaced by a spell-disguised Qilué Veladorn .
