Thyaast's staff was a unique and powerful enchanted staff that belonged to the mad spectral lich of Myth Drannor , Thyaast Ammath .

Description

Thyaast's staff was an ancient-looking simple quarterstaff of the elven carving.

Powers

The mad lich's staff held a powerful +5 enchantment. Additionally, it raised the wielder's constitution at the coast of their strength. The wielder of the staff also had an increased chance of survive negative effects of spells and mundane challenges. The staff held three charges of the resurrection spell.

History

This enchanted staff belonged to Thyaast Ammath, an elven wizard who venerated the art of necromancy . She survived the fall of Myth Drannor in the Weeping War and continued her work in developing magics that could raise the dead. Eventually, she reached the state of lichdom and over the centuries that followed, suffered from deteriorating mental state. She kept Thyaast's staff close at hand as she haunted her ancient laboratory at the very bottom of the Elven Catacombs , by the chasm that led into the Underdark . In 1369 DR , the staff could be found within the lich's lair, guarded by its mistress and her undead horrors.
