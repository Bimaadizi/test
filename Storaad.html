Storaad was a small isolated coastal town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Storaad was found along the coast of the Strait of the Leviathan in western Gwynneth. It sat among some plains in the shadow of the Black Mountains which were located to the east.
