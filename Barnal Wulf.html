Barnal Wulf was a human resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

Barnal had shoulder-length light brown hair, a noticeable scar on his face, and wore a long gray and brown robe.

History

While living in Eveningstar during the drow attacks , Barnal noted that the animals of the King's Forest were greatly disturbed and it had become incredibly dangerous to go into the once-safe forest.
