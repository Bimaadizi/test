Author(s)Laeral Silverhand
Date1335 DR

Thoughts On Life and Wizardry was a book written by Laeral Silverhand , writing as "Laeral of the Nine". It was written in the Year of the Snow Winds, 1335 DR , while she was a member of the adventuring company known as the Nine . It was a collection of her observations on the world and on magic .

Availability

Around 1372 DR , a copy was in the holdings at Candlekeep .
