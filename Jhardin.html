Jhardin was a street thief of Hastarl in Athalantar in the early 3 rd century DR .

Personality

Jhardin was direct and skeptical of plans.

Description

He had a deep voice, and was a large man.

Abilities

The thief had exceptional strength.

Relationships

He was a founding member of the Velvet Hands thieves' gang.

History

Jhardin was part of the meeting of thieves where the Velvet Hands gang was founded and local thief Farl was selected as its head. Shortly after, the Moonclaws gang decided to slay the new gang. At dawn after the first fight in the turf war, he was found dead by his fellow Velvet Hands in the small room where he lived.
