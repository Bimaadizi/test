Wyrms' Way was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Wyrms' Way was located in central Zhentil Keep within the Inner Quarter . The street branched south from the eastern end of Elephstron Way and ended at the wall of the Harbor District .

Notable Locations

The residence of Lady Alicia and the Wizards' Workshop were both located along Wyrms' Way.
