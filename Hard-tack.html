Hard-tack , sometimes called hardtack , was a dry unseasoned water-and-flour biscuit. It was available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .

Description

The biscuits were dry and bland. However, due to its extraordinary resilience, hard-tack was a popular choice for long sea voyages. It was also a common component of travelers' rations .
