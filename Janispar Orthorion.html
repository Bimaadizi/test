Janispar Orthorion was a noble elf of Evereska and a member of the city's fighting force known as the Swords .

History

In the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , Janispar fought alongside Lord Dureth and the other Swords against the phaerimm at the old outpost of Rocnest . They banded together with the human archmage Khelben Arunsun , managing to survive the conflict and return to Evereska alive.
