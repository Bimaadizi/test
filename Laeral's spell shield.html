Laeral's spell shield was a magical steel shield named after the creator of the first of its type, Laeral Silverhand .

Powers

It was a +1 large steel shield that gave its bearer protection against force -based spells, and reflected non-magical projectiles such as arrows .

Creation

The spells used to craft this shield were shield , Spell resistance and wall of force .
