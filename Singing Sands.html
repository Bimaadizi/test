Singing Sands was a reach across the River Umber from the Tannath Gap in Aglarond . It had been the site of the Battle of Singing Sands in 1194 DR .

Description

The stretch of the river was bordered by wind-blown dunes, with only dry grass and small trees.

Government

Aglarond controlled the area, and Thesk was not interested in more than that as of 1368 DR

Trade

Thesk found the area to be of minimal interest and did not contest it from Aglarond.

Defenses

Forces from Emmech regularly patrolled the area.

History

The zone had been the site of the Battle of Singing Sands in 1194 DR , where king Elthond Olósynne was felled by Thayan forces.

Rumors & Legends

The name of the zone had been given in the aftermath of the Battle of the Singing Sands, after the funeral chants of Aglarondan women.
