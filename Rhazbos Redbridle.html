Rhazbos Redbridle ( pronounced : /ˈ r ɑː ˈ h ɑː z z b oʊ ɛ z z ˈ r ɛ d b r aɪ d ʊl / Ra-HAZZ-boe-zz RED-bry-dul ) was a stout and jolly ex-adventurer who operated a stable within Waterdeep . He was also a former mercenary and caravan leader.

Personality

He was a noted member of the Stablemasters' & Farrier's Guild within the city. Though he was quite busy, Rhazbos took time off to tutor the occasional fighter who can afford his services.
