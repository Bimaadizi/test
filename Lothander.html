Lothander was an individual who lived in the city of Baldur's Gate during the midâˆ’14 th century DR . He had a career as an unwilling assassin for the Iron Throne mercantile organization.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Lothander was placed under a geas spell by Marek , an agent of the Iron Throne. He was forced by Marek to participate in an assassination attempt of the adventurer Abdel Adrian . The two would use a special poison that would kill Abdel within a tenday if an antidote was not consumed.

Rumors & Legends

It was believed by the Baldurian Haspur the fortune teller that a geas removal scroll owned by Jalantha Mistmyr could alleviate Lothander of his misfortune.
