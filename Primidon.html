Primidon was a Netherese arcanist and inventor of fire -based spells who lived during the Golden Age of Netheril .

History

The fire mage developed five spells during their life. The spells they invented were flaming sphere in 1892 NY (−1967 DR) , flame arrow in 1900 NY (−1959 DR) , incendiary cloud in 1912 NY (−1947 DR) , pyrotechnics in 1918 NY (−1941 DR) , and lastly, burning hands in 1950 NY (−1909 DR) .
