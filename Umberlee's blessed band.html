Umberlee's blessed band was an enchanted Umberlant ring forged sometime before 1372 DR .

Description

This ring was in a sea-side forge and called in the salty waters of Umberlee 's domain. The metal surface of the band was rippled with a pattern reminiscent waves in a sea.

Powers

When the wearer of Umberlee's blessed band touched the ring to their forehead and uttered the name of the Bitch Queen, a water elemental was summoned under the wearer's command. The effects of the ring were similar the summon water elemental spell and could be activated once per day.

History

In 1372 DR, Umberlee's blessed band was discovered in the stomach of a dead fish, washed ashore the Crab's Claw Coast region of the Sword Coast . How the band ended up inside a fish remained a mystery.
