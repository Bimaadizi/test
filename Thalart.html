Thalart was a malaugrym in the mid–14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , Thalart was plotting to usurp the position of the Shadowmaster Supreme Dhalgrave . Caught out, he volunteered to kill Elminster in Shadowdale early in the Time of Troubles , but was instead destroyed by Elminster.
