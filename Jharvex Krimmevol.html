Lord Jharvex Krimmevol was the head of the Krimmevol mercantile family in Eshpurta , and held a seat on the Shield Council during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Jharvex was the uncle of the family's patriarch, Lord Aernos Krimmevol of Crimmor . Jharvex had a younger brother, Cahorl , but framed him for the murder of a young daughter of the Zoar family. While Cahorl fled to Tethyr , Jharvex seized his lands and took them for his own.

History

By 1370 DR , Jharvex had sat on the Shield Council for more than 30 years.
