The Island of Firethorns , also called the Isle of Fear , was the largest and most prominent island of the Genie's Turban archiepelago found in the Corsair Domains of northwest Zakhara around 1367 DR .

Geography

The island measured 150 miles (240 kilometers) in length and 100 miles (160 kilometers) in width at its widest point. The northern and southern land rises were separated by a thin neck in the middle of the island formed by Chaos Bay in the east and an unnamed bay in the west where the village of Safe Cove was found. The "capital" of the Corsair Domains, Hawa , was sheltered by the dynamic reefs of Chaos Bay.

Description

Much of the Island of Firethorns was forested, but the island took its name from the dangerous firethorn plants that were abundant on the eastern coast of the island.
