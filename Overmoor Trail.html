The Overmoor Trail was a road found on the Dragon Coast , that ran from Elversult northwest to Proskur .

Geography

The trail connected the High Road of Cormyr , with the far-reaching Trader's Road of the Dragon Coast, at two points far beyond the town of Easting . While it bypassed the majority of the Lightning Steppes region, the Overmoor Trail passed through the Gritstone Moorland .
