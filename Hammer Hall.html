Hammer Hall was a log house and adjoining stables, surrounded by a wooden palisade.

Built by the adventuring group known as the Men of Hammer Hall to explore the nearby Halls of the Hammer , it became a viable place of refuge in the rugged wilderness of the North .

Geography

Hammer Hall was located near Highstar Lake , just west of Mount Hlim , in the Delimbiyr Vale .
