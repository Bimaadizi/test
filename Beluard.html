Personality

He was reticent to wade through zombies .

Activities

He was apprenticed to Manshoon and performed a number of important duties and tasks for him. Manshoon expected him to be up to date on his conversations with Sarhthor , implying Beluard was in both their confidences.

History

Beluard accompanied Manshoon late in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , to an encampment when the latter got ready to capture Shandril Shessair . Before Manshoon set off to fight her, he ordered his apprentice to arrange shortages of pork and sugar in Sembia .
