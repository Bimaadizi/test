Lord Evan Ghastkill was the head of the prominent Ghastkill family of Nashkel in Amn in the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Evan was the elder brother of Mayor Berrun Ghastkill . He was wed with Cara , and their only son was Evan the Younger .

History

Lord Evan had been very ill for a long period and, although he had stubbornly clung to life, he was not expected to live through 1370 DR . His son was eager to take over, but Cara and Berrun both believed he was an unsuitable heir.
