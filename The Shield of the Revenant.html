The Shield of the Revenant was a unique enchanted shield wielded by paladins who dedicated themselves to The Triad – Torm , Tyr , and Ilmater .

Description

This shield was decorated with red and white enamel.

Powers

This item was blessed by the deities of the Triad to be wielded against the undead but those who stood in the front lines. The shield's holy magic fortified the area before its owners entered battle with the forces of evil.

History

The Shield of the Revenant was used in central and northern Faerûn long before the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR . Its most known wielder was Kardod of Tyr, who died battling an overwhelming horde of wights . After Kardod's death, The Shield of the Revenant was sold by the necromancer Shanin to a priest of Myrkul . Subsequently, in 1281 , The Shield of the Revenant ended up in the hands of Edion Caradoc , an aging necromancer who resided on the Burial Isle of Icewind Dale , among numerous other enchanted items, which were flush with magic.
