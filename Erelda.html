Erelda was the second-in-command of battlemistress Rylla of the Promenade of the Dark Maiden .

History

In 1379 DR , during an attack by Ghaunadaur 's followers on the Promenade, Erelda remained to control the seals that trapped the god's avatar. However, a large group of oozes attacked Erelda and her Protectors and managed to break the seal, awakening the avatar of Ghaunadaur.
