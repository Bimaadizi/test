The oasis was located on the eastern side of the region known as the Sword , south of El Ma'ra and the Bowl of Loneliness .

History

In the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR , the oasis was taken over by the Zhentarim spellcaster Yhekal , and a contingent of the Zhentilar army led the warrior known as Zarud . Their presence troubled some tribesmen of the Bedine people, and even the great place spirit of Rahalat .
