Karselridrith , also known as Brightstrike , was a blue wyrm whose lair was near Wreathe in Netheril .

History

During the Shadowed Age and around −1859 DR , Karselridrith was slain in her lair by more than fifteen adventurers. In −544 DR , her three ancient blue dragon children descended upon Wreathe and savagely destroyed the city in less than two weeks of breath weapon bombardments.
