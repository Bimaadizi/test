Rorrina Stoneshaft was a shield dwarf cleric of Abbathor that Artor Morlin , the vampire lord of Waterdeep , turned into one of his spawn . Circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , she dwelled in the abandoned dwarven settlement of Melairrin in the depths of Mount Sar in the Sword Mountains just north of Waterdeep.

Relationships

Rorrina was the daul (daughter) of Tuvala of Clan Stoneshaft .

Rumors & Legends

Rorrina was the source of many reports of a vampire lurking in the depths of the Sword Mountains.
