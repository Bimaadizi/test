Gond Sobor was a temple dedicated to Gond , the god of invention, located in the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The temple was located along the Golden Way in eastern Almorel. It sat near the outer city wall in the shadow of the Gospoda to the west.
