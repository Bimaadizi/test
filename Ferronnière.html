A ferronnière was a type of decorative jewelry worn primarily by upper-class women on their foreheads.

Description

A ferronnière was a thin, delicate chain of precious metal worn on a person's head, displaying one or more gemstones on their foreheads. Ferronnière were considered to be a tasteful sign of wealth and elegance.
