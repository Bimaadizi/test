Saraclarr was one of the Palace families who served the Crown of Cormyr in the 15 th century DR .

History

By the late 15 th century DR, Saraclarrs were career officiers for multiple generations. Like other officers and chamberlains, the Saraclarr family resided at Cormyr's Royal Court south of Castle Obarskyr .
