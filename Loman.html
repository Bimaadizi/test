Loman was an employee of the Gallaway Trade Depot in Targos under the supervision of Deirdre Gallaway , or "Deird," as he called her, in the early 14 th century DR .

Description

Loman was a brown-haired timid human man.

Possessions

Loman worse simple leather armor , and carried a dagger as well as a wooden club .

History

Loman worked at the Gallaway Trading Coaster's shop on top of the cliff overlooking the docks in Targos, moving crates and organizing the depot's inventory. He remained in Deirdre Gallaway's employ through the Legion of the Chimera crisis of the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR .
