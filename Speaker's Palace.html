The Speaker's Palace was the personal residence of the Speaker of Bryn Shander , the individual who represented the settlement to the rest of the Ten Towns .

Geography

This residence was located a bit south of the center of town.

Structure

The "palace" was made of dwarven design, constructed with stone walls and a slate roof. Its facade featured a notable colonnade that contributed to its out-of-place appearance among the other structures of Bryn Shander.

History

In the Year of the Crown, 1351 DR , a great battle took place in Bryn Shander between a barbarian horde and the folks of the Town Towns accompanied by the Companions of the Hall . Following the conflict, the Speaker's Palace was gifted to the adventurer Regis .
