Obo'laka ( pronounced : / oʊ b ɑː ˈ l ɑː k ɑː / oh-bah-LAH-kah ) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult . She was a primal spirit that took the form of a zorbo .

Personality

Obo'laka was obsessive, anxious, and risk-averse. She was a creature of nervous habit, and insisted on her routines.
