Pelauvir's Counter was a general store and the site of a former warehouse in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

This store was located along the major intersection Waymoot , in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This building was rather large, standing five stories tall.

Services

This general store sold almost everything except food, including such goods as carts , lotions, pots , and marbles . It was quite crowded, being stocked at all times, and its goods were moderately priced.
