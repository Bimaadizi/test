King Belhinn Wavegold II was the sixth and last Amnian monarch of the Wavegold dynasty.

Relationships

Belhinn was the grandson of Belhinn I , and the younger brother of Gelhinn .

History

He married his daughter Princess Peldara to the only good man in the royal court he could find, Malek Parhek , who came to found the ruling Parhek dynasty. He was slain by the Black Horde when it attacked Amn in 1237 DR , putting an end to the Wavegold dynasty as Malek became King.
