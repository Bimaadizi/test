The Ramparts of Night was a massive abyss in the Earthroot domain of the Underdark .

Description

The abyss did not reach the surface, but it plummeted an immense distance before connecting to the Shadowfell . That is, it was effectively bottomless; falling into the abyss would lead to a great canyon on the Shadowfell.

Geography

The abyss was located underneath the Sunrise Mountains , the mountain range forming the eastern border of Thay .

Inhabitants

The walls of the Ramparts of Night were inhabited by cloakers and other creatures of shadow, including shadar-kai from Netheril . A large enclave of gulgars also made their home in the walls, and tribes of gulgars living on the surface in the Sunrise Mountains were descended from there.

Notable Locations

The shadar-kai settlement of Ikemmu was located on the southern edge of the Ramparts.
