A heartstone was a specific type of gemstone associated with the malevolent night hags . They held within them several different magical properties.

Description

They each appeared as a glossy black gem.

Night hags were each required to craft their own heartstone , a process that took 30 days.

Powers

When a night hag had her heartstone in her possession, she could become ethereal at will. Additionally, mere contact with a heartstone would cure an individual of any disease with which they were afflicted.
