Fig was an orphaned child adopted by the High Harper of Baldur's Gate , Jaheira in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

In the late 15 th century DR, Fig was a young human child with light skin tone, phone hair gathered in two buns, and large hazel eyes. Fig was a rambunctious tomboy who liked to practice her sword fighting with a wooden sword on a dummy outside and played a guard letting people into the family home.

Relationships

She eagerly took on the idea of the militaristic aspect of the Harpers and considered her adoptive sister Rion to be "the commander." Fig and Jaheira's old comrade Minsc shared a friendly bond.
