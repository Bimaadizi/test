Sendai Argrim was the heiress of the Amnian noble merchant house of the Argrims .

History

She was hunting in the Fire Leaf Forest with her companions Alexander and Delgod when they came upon Gorion's Ward .

Gameplay

If Gorion's Ward feels offended by Sendai, then Gorion's Ward might kill her and her companions or dismiss them.
