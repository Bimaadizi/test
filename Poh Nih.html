Poh Nih was a sage in T'u Lung .

History

Poh Nih was a sage and a member of the Black Leopard Cult .

At one point, he fought drow in Shaddath Gap together with followers of Selûne .

In −830 DR , he was slain when Shou Lung forces under General Wo Can took H'sin-to . He was buried according to the Black Leopard Cult's ceremonies and his tomb was guarded by a stone spirit .
