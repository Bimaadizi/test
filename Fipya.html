Fipya was a member of the barae , the seven "super-paladins" sworn to protect the holy city of Mezro in Chult .

Personality

Fipya was a shy girl, and her responsibilities left her feeling overwhelmed.

Relationships

Fipya followed the instructions of the other barae , especially those of Kwalu , whom she considered an older-brother figure.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , Fipya was the newest bara , having replaced the bara T'fima , who had been stripped of his powers in 1362 DR . After the Batiri attack on the city of Mezro that same year, in which they burned the Agricultural Quarter, Fipya used her new powers to restore the area.
