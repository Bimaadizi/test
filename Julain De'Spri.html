Julain De'Spri was a ghost noble in Undermountain in 1479 DR .

History

In his past, Julain was a magistrate in Waterdeep . He was buried in Undermountain after his death with his wife Amori De'Spri .

In 1479 DR, the vampire Dayan resurrected Julain as a ghost during an experiment. Julain wandered alone inside his tomb. Meanwhile, Amori had been resurrected as a living being by Dayan and moved to another location.

He was concerned about Amori but urged her to continue to live without him protecting their family.
