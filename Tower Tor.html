Tower Tor was a hill in the mid–8 th century DR .

Geography

Until 761 DR the Walking Tower of Warglend was in Tower Tor. There was at least one lake close by.

History

In the Year of Laughter, 761 DR , the sage Tabarast of Moonshorn Tower reported that, amongst other signs from the gods , the Walking Tower of Warglend had moved for the first time in a thousand years, approximately since −250 DR , and left its place in Tower Tor for the middle of a nearby lake.
