Orsar "Greencloak" was a half-elf wizard of Elturel in the Western Heartlands in the midâ€“14 th century DR . Circa 1358 DR , he was one of the notable mages of the city.

Personality

He was tight-lipped and reclusive.

Activities

Orsar busied himself in creating and perfecting spells , magic items , and potions involving the ability to fly. He funded himself by selling his potions, but at incredible prices.

Secretly, Orsar was a friend to Lord Dhelt , the ruler of Elturel, and served him as a spy inside the city. He often went invisible and in a wraith form to sneak around the city, a habit unknown to the majority of Elturians.
