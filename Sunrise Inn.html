The Sunrise Inn was a small and quiet inn that stood in the south-eastern edge of the city of Phlan in the Moonsea in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

As of the Year of the Morningstar, 1350 DR , the inn did business in rebuilt Phlan after the defeat of Tyranthraxus a decade earlier. A single sign that hung over the inn's door proclaimed the establishment's name welcoming its guests.. The innkeep of Sunrise Inn offered free lodgings to respected individuals, such as the Heroes of Phlan.
