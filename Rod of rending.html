A rod of rending was an enchanted rod favored by servants of the Savage God Ragarra in Zakhara .

Description

These rods were typically made from ebony and had a crocodile 's claw attached to one end.

Powers

As with the rending spell used by cultists of Ragarra, this rod was used to destroy personal property, including both livestock and slaves. Up to 8 cubic feet of matter could be destroyed at a time with this item. The magic had no effect if used on a free citizen or a wild creature.
