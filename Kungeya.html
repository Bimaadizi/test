Kungeya was a shadovar knight in Anauroch in 1479 DR .

History

In 1479 DR, Kungeya was chosen to by Captain Durbela to be part of the mission to gain control of the Spiral Gate , a crucial portal nexus .

Personality

Kungeya was very ambitious, trying to undermine Nulu . He consistently sought out the weakest opponents in combat.
