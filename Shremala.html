The Shremala were one of the Bedine tribes that roamed across the desert of Anauroch during the mid–14 th century DR .

Organization

As with the case with other tribes of the Bedine, the Shremala were lead by a single male sheikh. As of the early 1360s the sheikh of the Shremala was the wise elder Yatagan .

History

The Shremala were one of the Bedine tribes that join forces to fight back against Zhentarim influence in the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR . They fought bravely at the siege of Orofin , the final conflict that broke the Zhent forces led by the dark spellcaster Yhekal .
