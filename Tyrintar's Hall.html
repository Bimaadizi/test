Tyrintar's Hall , previously known as Gargoyles' Grim' , was a grand festhall found in the city of Myth Drannor .

Location

The building was located just north of Sixstar, the junction of six major city roads in the Kerradunath ward of the city.

Structure

The great hall was three stories tall, constructed with a triangular layout.

Atmosphere

Tyrintar's Hall was enchanted in such a manner that pleasing dance music continuously played throughout, which could be adjusted depending on the mood .

History

The hall was renamed after the Opening, in the Year of Soaring Stars, 261 DR . It honored Tyrintar , the paladin that served as one of the city's great heroes, who had a particular affinity for dancing.
