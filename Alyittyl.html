Alyittyl was a sahuagin captain of the City of Caverns in the late 1360s DR .

Combat

Captain Alyittyl fought with a standard spear and a light crossbow . He also carried a scroll of fireshield (blue) .

History

The captain supported King Ixilthetocal during the civil war that divided the sahuagin city. He was on watch to see if the forces of the rebel, Prince Villynaty , attacked.
