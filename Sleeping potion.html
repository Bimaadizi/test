Sleeping potions were magical potions that could be found in Zakhara , the Land of Fate. They were only carried or used by holy slayers .

Description

Sleeping potions had a tart taste.

Powers

After imbibing this potion, the imbiber fell into a peaceful sleep for a few hours. Sleep from this potion had the safe positive effects of a full night's rest; thus, this potion was often used by spellcasters to rapidly increase the rate at which they could re-memorize spells . Being woken during this sleep nullified any of these positive effects.
