Agyrtclughwaur , or the Forest Wyrm , was a legendary green dragon who resided in the Forest of Wyrms . Many of the green dragons in the region were descended from her.

History

Agyrtclughwaur was known to be dead as of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR .

Rumors & Legends

Her hoard was sometimes sought after by dragon hunters, but none had ever found it.
