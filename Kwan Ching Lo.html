Kwan Ching Lo was the Minister of Public Works of Shou Lung in 1358 DR .

History

Kwan Ching Lo was appointed Minister of Public Works around 1328 DR . For many years, he was embarrassed by the success of Governor Kawabi of Chu' Yuan province, who demonstrated a great ability in managing public works in his province.

At last, in 1358 DR, he understood the true potential of the Gathering of the Pearls proclamation and started sending adventuring party to all uncharted areas of the Empire and supported rebuilding projects. His dream became to restore the cursed city of Kuo Meilan , an idea most of the Mandarinate were adverse to.
