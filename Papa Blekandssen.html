Papa Blekandssen was a businessman inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He owned the lavish Chateau Elite inn located in central Daggerford along Hill Road . Papa Blekandssen catered to every whim of his patrons, and charged appropriately for his services.
