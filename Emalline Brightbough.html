Emalline Brightbough was an officer of the Elves of Cormanthor in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Emalline had pale skin and long blonde hair. She wore an ornate green robe.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Emalline was part of the Elves of Cormanthor to reclaim Myth Drannor , and was the security officer of the forward camp outside the city. She was kept busy addressing the myriad threats to the camp, and had to handle the furious Irrwyss Fret'tar , who demanded assistance in finding her friend Kelgoth Darksong .
