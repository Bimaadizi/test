Alchemist's supplies were an array of supplies that were used by a prospective alchemist .

Description

A typical set of alchemist's supplies included two glass beakers , a metal frame to hold a beaker in place over an open flame, a glass stirring rod, a small mortar and pestle , and a pouch of common alchemical ingredients such as salt , powdered iron , and similar materials.
