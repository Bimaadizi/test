The Dusty Gnome Tavern was an inn and a tavern located in the town of Leilon on the Sword Coast North .

Location

The tavern stood just off the town's sleepy central square.

Atmosphere

The Dusty Gnome catered to local miners who boisterously partook in ale -fueled revelries after their long hard shifts. Any visitor was greeted with laughter, loud voices, and clanking of ale flagons.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , the establishment was ran by a middle-aged male human innkeeper who offered libations and cheap guest accommodations.
