Matilda's Boarding House was one of few businesses that offered lodgings in the town of Mossbridges in the late 14 th century DR .

Servies

Matilda's Boarding House offered apartments priced at two silver pieces per night per person; morningfeast included in the price.

History

In the late 14 th century DR, the Boarding House was run by the woman named Matilda .
