Sultlue Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Sultlue .

Location

The villa was located in the north section of Waterdeep 's North Ward in between Shield Street and Copper Street where they met Vondil Street at the border with the Sea Ward .

Description

Each of the four buildings of Sultlue Villa were arranged in one corner of the compound. All four buildings stood four stories tall.
