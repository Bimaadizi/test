Busain was a pahari inhabiting the Elemental Plane of Water in 1367 DR .

Personality

She was quite intelligent and crafty.

Activities

Through the use of a clever genie contract , Busain was able to "borrow" the voice of a powerful and annoying marid named Ibnul , effectively making him powerless.
