Ergoth Eversworn was a dwarf cleric in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Ergoth had short white hair and goatee. He wore a suit of scale mail under a white and red surcoat , a shield , and carried a mace with exaggerated flanges.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR during the Elven Crusade , Ergoth joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp on the outskirts of Myth Drannor . He served as a guide for priests there.
