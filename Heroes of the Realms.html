The Heroes of the Realms was a line of clay model figures depicting various renowned adventurers . They were made in the 14 th century DR , each figure being custom made, by the Toysmiths toy business in Ravens Bluff .

History

Sometime in the late 14 th century DR , a group of adventurer friends of Dawn of the Desert Moon and Opal Starlight donated Heroes of the Realms figures to their shelter for orphaned youth, the Desert Moon Sanctuary And Starlight Cavalcade . These figures included personages of the druid Morra Starson and her cousins, Warhawk and Zaque Dral , Opal and Dawn themselves, Sir Alexander the Noble , Lady Fawn , Sara , Tara Sojourn , Vedima Stareye , the City Guardsman Voltroy Starson , and Wyvernn Silverwane .
