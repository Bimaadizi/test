Ivellin Farwanderer was an elf ranger and arcane archer of Mielikki operating in Cormanthor in the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Ivellin was the second-in-command of the warrior priestess Vishara in her campaign against Nashyim of the Desolated Wood , a volodni blightlord of Talona and servant of the Rotting Man who had invaded the forests of Cormanthor. Ivellin was in charge of scouting missions, which kept track of the movements and plans of Nashyim and his blightspawned creatures .
