Brother Calwern was an old cleric of Deneir in Silverymoon .

History

Calwern was one of the clerics that took care of the Vault of the Sages archive in Silverymoon. In 1374 DR , Araevin Teshurr went to the Vault asking for lore about Arcorar magic and spoke with Calwern.
