The Midani languages composed a sub-group of the Untheric languages group of Rauric languages .

Languages

This grouping included the dead languages of Noga and Kadari , as well as Midani , the language of Zakhara , and Maran .

History

The Midani languages branched off from the other Untheric languages by mixing with the Jannti languages of genies .
