Baastat was a zulkir of Abjuration of Thay in 1366 DR .

Personality

Baastat was rather complacent in his role as zulkir.

Abilities

The amethyst dragon Nyadnar suggested that Zulkir Baastat was likely more powerful than the fiend -in-disguise Armenides .

History

Some time before the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR , Zaranda Star was hired by the Zazesspuran sorceress Nyadnar to steal a magical brazen head from the Red Wizards , Nyadnar's longtime enemies. Zaranda succeeded in stealing the talking head from Baastat, who had been unable to acquire any useful information from it beforehand.
