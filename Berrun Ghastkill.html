Berrun Ghastkill was the mayor of Nashkel around 1370 DR .

Abilities

Berrun was very skilled with the bow and spear , and specialized in fighting ogres .

Relationships

Berrun was the younger brother of Lord Evan Ghastkill , head of the local Ghastkill family.

History

Berrun served as mayor of Nashkel and was an active adventurer in his time, and earned a great local reputation for saving many residents from a tribe of Cloud Peaks goblins .

In the year 1368 DR during the iron crisis , Berrun called on the Harpers for aid, ultimately leading to his past acquaintance Jaheira and her husband Khalid being dispatched. He later met the Bhaalspawn Abdel Adrian , and directed the young hero onwards to the Nashkel mines , the source of the issue.
