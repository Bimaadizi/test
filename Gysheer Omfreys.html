Gysheer Omfreys was a worshiper of Asmodeus in Waterdeep in the early 1490s DR .

History

In 1492 DR , Gysheer instigated some spined devils to terrorize people in the Field Ward of Waterdeep . This attracted the attention of Savra Belabranta , who, under orders of the Order of the Gauntlet , hired some adventurers to eliminate the devils, eventually discovering and defeating Gysheer.

Personality

Gysheer was a member of the devil cult led by Victoro Cassalanter , but only magical compulsion could force her to implicate Victorio.
