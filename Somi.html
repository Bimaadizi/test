Somi was one of the two bodyguards who worked for the proprietor of Danthelon's Dancing Axe in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Somi was a gruff and tough half-orc with leathery tan skin and long mop of brown hair. He had brown eyes, yellow tusks, and a single nose ring decorated with a tiny skull. Somi wore a breastplate and carrie da massive greataxe .
