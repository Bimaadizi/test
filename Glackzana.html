Glackzana was a marilith who masqueraded as an avatar of Garagos in Chondath in 1368 DR .

Activities

Glackzana began forming a cult in Garagos' name, using her powers to change her appearance to appear as the Reaver's avatar. She had her followers construct a temple near the village of Gosra in the Fields of Nun in central Chondath . When Garagos learned of Glackzana's activities he appeared and destroyed the imposter, demanding that the followers rebuild the temple to a grander scale fitting for his tastes.
