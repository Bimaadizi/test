The kavalrachni was the duergar cavalry that protected the underground city of Gracklstugh .

Activities

The kavalrachni rode atop great female steeders , giant-sized tarantulas native to the Underdark .

Possessions

They carried with them heavy crossbows , which they wielded with masterful skill.

Members

The majority of the kavalrachni hailed from Clan Xundom .
