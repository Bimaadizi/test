The Children of the Ebon Hand , who were called the Kenswa-Tokashow in the tongue of the Eshowe , were a militant order who served Eshowdow , the Shadow Giant within the Chultan pantheon .

Base of Operations

The Children of the Ebon Hand operated out of the Valley of Lost Honor within the Chultan jungles.

Organization

The group was composed of crusaders , warriors, and rogues. They served as Eshowdow's military arm in Chult. They organized themselves into strike groups of three called Triads.

Activities

The Children assaulted Tabaxi settlements and planned for the ultimate destruction of Mezro .
