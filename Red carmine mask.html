Red carmine masks were leather helmets that bore a minor enchantment , created sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Description

These leather helmets were dyed in red color, reminiscent of spilled gladiatorial blood.

Powers

When worn, a red carmine mask slightly boosted the wearer's reaction to danger.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , one such helmet could be found in the Szarr Palace in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate .
