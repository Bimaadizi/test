Hammermaker , who was nicknamed the Small , was a dwarf blacksmith who lived in Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Activities

Hammermaker's smithy and minehead were located just south of the Golden Way near Almorel's western gate.
