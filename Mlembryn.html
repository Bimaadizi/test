The Mlembryn Lands were a territory of the Western Heartlands in the 3 rd century DR .

Description

Settlements in the region were sparse, with steadings and walled villages most common.

Geography

The Mlembryn stretched from the western end of Siluvanede to the southeast of Yarlith . At its south end lay Ladyhouse Falls . It was in the area around the River Dessarin .

Government

The only settlement of any size in the region was Baerlith , on its western end.

History

The area came to be settled around the turn of the 3 rd century DR , by settlers from the frontiers of Athalantar , Elembar , Uthtower , and Yarlith moving to the area around the River Dessarin. Some settlers from the area would then help re-settle Illusk in the Year of the Greengrass, 205 DR .

Elmara , a priestess of Mystra (secretly the prince Elminster Aumar ) spent some time there around 231 DR .

The sorceress Chassabra ruled some of those lands at some point during the 3 rd century DR .

The Kingdom of Grimmantle in Mlembryn was destroyed in the Year of the Fanged Horde, 306 DR .
