The Black Horse was an inn/ tavern located in Archenbridge during late 14 th century DR . It was mostly frequented by adventurers and travelers looking to save some money on more modest accommodations.

Prices

A room at the Black Horse cost 8 sp a night. The tavern offered Archenwood stout , a locally brewed lager, for 5 cp a draft.
