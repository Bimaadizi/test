Cassie Wheynstown was an inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Cassie Wheynstown was an aged human woman with a shaved head and light skin.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Cassie was slaughtered alongside Courageous Little Kimmabeth by Orin the Red , a Bhaalspawn member of the Murder Tribunal . In her depraved commitment to Bhaal , the deity of murder, Orin turned Kimmabeth's, as well as several other victims' bodies, into a gruesome effigy glorifying the Lord of Murder.
