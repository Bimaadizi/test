Lady Chastity was a beautiful noblewoman from Daggerford circa 1372 DR .

Description

Lady Chastity was a beautiful red-headed young woman with seductive eyes that were reminiscent those of a spider luring its prey into the webs. She was dressed in ruffled finery.

Personality

Lady Chastity had a very seductive and flirty personality.

History

In 1372 DR , Lady Chastity could be found within the Chateau Elite , the most expensive and pompous inn in the city. Her room was next door to the suite occupied by Astriel Shalyn , a spy on a mission from the Lords' Alliance . During the attack on her by the assassins of Zhentarim , Lady Chastity was cowering in her room in fear.
