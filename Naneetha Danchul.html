The Hand of the Lady Naneetha Danchul was a cleric of Tymora and the high priestess of the Gate of Good Fortune temple in Mulmaster in the Moonsea in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

At the temple, she led nine priests and eleven lay followers.
