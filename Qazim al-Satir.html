Qazim al-Satir was skilled wind mage inhabiting Qudra in north Zakhara in 1367Â DR .

Activities

Qazim was a trusted advisor the the court of Qudra, though he was not technically associated with them. He spent most of his time within his manor, but always answered a summons from the court.

History

Qazim played a pivotal role during the Battle of Hawa by saving most of Qudra's fleet from destruction at the hands of the corsairs .
