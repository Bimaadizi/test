The Book of the Coast was a book penned by Mespert of Baldur's Gate in the late 13 th century DR .

History

This book of musings included Mespert's thoughts on adventurers whose bark was worse than their bite. The Book of the Coast was published in the Year of the Talking Skull, 1293 DR , and a copy could be found in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
