A pole of angling was a magical 10-foot pole .

Powers

When a special command word was spoken, this 10‑foot (3‑meter) long pole would transform into a fully functional fishing rod . The transformation included a hook, line, and a reel. When the command word was spoken again, the rod transformed back into a pole.
