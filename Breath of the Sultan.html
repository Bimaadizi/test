The Breath of the Sultan was a special fountain found within the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Description

As a symbol of the Sultan's rule, the great fountain was huge. It spewed purple flames high into the air from where it sat.

Geography

This fountain was located in the Furnace ward of the city, where it sat in front of the Charcoal Palace .

History

The fountain's fire would die out or dim in the event of the Sultan's death or deposition. The new Sultan would then relight the fires of the fountain in a thunderous explosion easily heard by every occupant of the City of Brass.
