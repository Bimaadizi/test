Dur-baagal was one of the primordials who fought against the gods before the Sundering of Abeir-Toril .

History

At the dawn of time , Dur-baagal opposed the gods and was killed by Auril .

Legacy

The throne room of Auril's Winter's Hall, the Lyceum, was crafted from the still beating heart of Dur-baagal.
