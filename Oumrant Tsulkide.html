Primate Oumrant Tsulkide was the head of the temple fortress known as the Tower of Swift Death .

Activities

As a Primate, Oumrant's role was to oversee the ongoings of fortress under his command, the Tower of Swift Death in Thay . Within, the Primate's murderers and priests were trained in the tower, and often sent on missions to aid the Red Wizards of Thay .

History

In Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the temple under Oumrant Tsulkide's care converted from the worship of Bhaal, to that of Cyric.
