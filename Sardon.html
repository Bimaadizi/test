Sardon was a sorcerer dying on or before the mid–8 th century DR .

History

At some point before 767 DR , Sardon had a romantic relationship with Saeraede Lyonora , an apparently unique Netherese ghost -like entity. He fed her his own lifeforce for a time, lasting about as long as Brandagaeris lasted, but she'd drained him completely by the end.
