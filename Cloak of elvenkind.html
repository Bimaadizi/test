A cloak of elvenkind was a magical item that granted increased powers of stealth.

The jellaba of concealment functioned in the same manner as a cloak of elvenkind . This magical robe originated in Zakhara .

Description

A cloak of elvenkind was an ordinary-looking cloak, and gray in color.

Powers

When the hood was drawn over the wearer's head, it increased the wearer's ability to hide.

Creation

The creator of a cloak of elvenkind had to be an elf and had to know the spell invisibility .
