Cyriana was a queen of Tethyr in the Rhindaun Dynasty in the 15 th century DR .

Relationships

She was married to King Errilam .

History

At some point during her reign, Cyriana and her husband's life were saved from assassins by Vajra Safahr , Vajra's father, and Mynda Gyrfalcon-Thann .
