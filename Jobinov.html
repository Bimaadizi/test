Jobinov was a spacefaring halfling bounty hunter .

Description

A somewhat rotund man, Jobinov had slicked-back, black hair, and wore purple and black clothing.

Possessions

Jobinov owned and carried a lasso .

History

After running away from his childhood home at the age of thirty-two, Jobinov was kidnapped by neogi slavers. He eventually escaped, but never thought of returning to a "groundling" existence again. On a visit to the world of Toril , Jobinov was taught to master the use of a lasso by a group of Tuigan warriors.
