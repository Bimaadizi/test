The Ulyoon were a tribe of Nars inhabiting the town of Peltarch in western Narfell .

Organization

The tribe numbered more than a 1000 members circa 1479Â DR .

Activities

The Ulyoon were avid fishermen and traders and welcomed caravans all throughout the year, receiving several each winter when the Icelace Lake froze over and the easiest route to Damara went over the ice instead of through the Giantspire Gap .

Base of Operations

The Ulyoon lived primarily in Peltarch, and fished the bountiful waters of the Icelace lake.

Relationships

The trade and contact with foreigners made them both tolerant and even welcome of outlanders, and quite wealthy compared to other Nar tribes. The other tribes in turn viewed the Ulyoon as outlanders instead of fellow Nars, and blamed encounters with very aggressive wildlife, especially cave and dire bears , and dark spirits on the mingling of ancient and modern traditions in the town.
