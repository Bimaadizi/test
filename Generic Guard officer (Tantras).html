A high-ranking member of the Guard of the city of Tantras in the Vast was an officer .

Possessions

Officers of the Guard were outfitted in blue oiled plate mail armor emblazoned with the coat of arms of Tantras: three silver stars over a silver breaking wave on a field of royal blue, called the "blue and silver". They wielded maces, morningstars , or short swords.

Activities

Officers commanded multiple units and sections of the city, such as the harbor.
