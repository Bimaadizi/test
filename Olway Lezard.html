Olway Lezard was a mercenary leader in Chondath in 1358 DR .

Activities

Olway led the Gray Doom mercenary company.

History

They survived many skirmishes on the Sword Coast .

In 1358 DR, Olway led his men to the city of Timindar in Chondath, having been hired by Lord Reland Tymin . Olway and his men were waiting the arrival of the Sailors of the Crimson Sea in order to attack the enemy but in the meantime they all drunk a lot at the Black Eye inn. Olway provoked Araband Tymin .

Later he became involved in the Battle of Thurgabanteth .
