A brooch of shielding was a magical protective device. A full-capacity brooch had a base value of 1,500 gp .

Appearance

A brooch of shielding was a small piece of jewelry used to fasten a cloak or cape . They were often made of silver or gold . Most commonly, brooches of shielding were adorned with Mystra 's or Azuth 's holy symbols .

Powers

A brooch of shielding could harmlessly absorb the force darts from a magic missile spell directed at the wearer. A single brooch could only absorb a set amount of energy before melting.
