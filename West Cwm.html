The West Cwm was a large high valley in the Shaeradim , the hills surrounding Evereska .

In the south, there was a large deep lake of very cold water, and to the east lay the Vine Vale , through the Sunset Gate .

It was the site of the Battle of the Cwm between the daemonfey army and the defending elves of Evereska and Evermeet , on Ches 27 of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR .
