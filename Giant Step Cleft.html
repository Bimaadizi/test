Giant Step Cleft was a narrow gorge along Northknife Pass .

Description

Closer to the summit than Breakheart Cleft , the walls of the gorge were more than 300 feet (91 meters) high, and there was a small stream along its left edge. The gorge was about 0.25 miles (400 meters) long, roughly the same length as its sister cleft.

The place was favored for ambushes.
