High Crown Harangor Urlingscar was a dragonborn member of Clan Urlingscar and the king of Relmaur as of 1479 DR .

Background

Harangor was considered a stern man by most, but he also was a benevolent ruler. During his mandate, he allowed all the prominent Relmorn clans to participate in the government of Relmaur by establishing the Crown Council .
