Sharala was a priestess of Eilistraee during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Sharala was known to participate in certain rites to Eilistraee within Ardeep Forest .

Relationships

Sharala served Eilistraee under the guidance of Qilué Veladorn .

History

During one night of 1369 DR , Sharala was one of the priestesses dancing under Qilué Veladorn's guidance in an Eilistraeean rite which was interrupted by the arrival of the Harper Dove Falconhand , who sought Qilué for a task in Scornubel . After the priestess Ierembree tried to kill Dove, she was affected by one of the Harper's kisses, which involved some level of mind-melding. Sharala was quick to suggest, noticing her peer's placidity, that her wits may no longer be her own. Ierembree rebuffed the idea.
