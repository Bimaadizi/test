Schuyler was a food critic in Phlan during the Tyranny of Dragons .

History

In 1489 DR , Schuyler went to the Madame Freona's Tea Kettle in order to test their food. She was briefly interrogated by adventurers hired by Buhrell Caah about a missing dangerous magical item.
