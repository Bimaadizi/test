Personality

He was known to slip into bouts of depression when faced with seemingly overwhelming circumstances. He was receptive to words of self-determination and an earnest call to action.

Possessions

Jospil was in the possession of an enchanted quarterstaff that had several owners over the years, and was stored for at least some time in Candlekeep .

Activities

For at least some time Jospil was stationed at the High Hall in the Upper City .

History

Jospil became increasingly distressed when the War of the Shining Crusade broke out on the Sword Coast in Uktar of the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .
