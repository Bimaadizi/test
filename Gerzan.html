Gerzan was a legendary Master Smith of the ancient dwarven nation of Delzoun .

History

By the late 14 th century, weapons forged by Gerzan were considered some of the best examples of dwarvish craftsmanship as well as valued for Gerzan's skills as a weapon enchanter . In the 1370 DR , an adventurer and explorer of the Undermountain , Tolem Grandelwood , owned one of the legendary master's swords . He cherished his thrice-enchanted blade and refused to trade it for any treasure from the great dungeon underneath Waterdeep .
