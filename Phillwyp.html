Phillwyp the Wary was a twisted gnome who staked a claim to the ruins of the castle at Caer Corwell in the late 15 th century DR . From there, he aspired to establish his own kingdom of unseelie fey to rival Sarifal .

Relationships

His followers included boggles and other small, capricious fey who had overrun Caer Corwell.
