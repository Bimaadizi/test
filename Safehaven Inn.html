The Safehaven Inn was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The two buildings of the inn stood three stories tall. The interior of the inn featured private booths in addition to the common room, a bar, and a large fireplace that connected to the kitchen. The kitchen also had its own service entrance.

Location

The Safehaven Inn was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter . The buildings sat on the eaastern side of the Adventurers' Quarter, with parts of the two buildings touching Slop Street , Snake Alley , and Grocer's Lane
