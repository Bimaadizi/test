The Palazzo of Stamping Hooves was a mansion located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The mansion was located neart the center of the city within the Plume district. It sat along the northwest border of the Furnace .

Inhabitants

The Palazzo of Stamping Hooves was reserved for the Sultan's vizier.
