The Statue of Verovan Lorndessar was a monument dedicated to Verovan Lorndessar and the last king of Westgate .

Description

The Statue of Kisonraathiisar stood on the Harbor Loop of Westgate, on the street's eastern side, in the Loop's inner block. In the late 14 th century DR , the statue was ravaged by time and vandalism: beheaded, and chiseled with obscene graffiti. The king's beheaded body stood proud, wearing a cape and holding a mace .
