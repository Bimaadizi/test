Malek Stones was a reporter employed by Baldur's Mouth in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Malek was a beardless male shield dwarf with pudgy features, brown eyes, and short curly brown hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Malek Stones worked on investigative scoop articles for the Baldur's Mouth Gazette . During the Absolute crisis , the irate dwarf could be found working on his pieces within Baldur's Mouth's offices in Heapside of the Lower City .
