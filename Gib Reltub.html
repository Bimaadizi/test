Gib Reltub was a powerful elven thief and champion, active in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Reltub was an average man in appearance, who wielded a sword and wore a set of chainmail armor.

Abilities

Beyond his impressive roguish skills, Reltub was a powerful arcane spellcaster and an earthwalker, who could freely traverse different lands and worlds. He had access to such powerful magics as the wish spell , which could not be countered or turned by his opponents.
