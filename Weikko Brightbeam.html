Weikko Brightbeam was an elven guide in the ruins of Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Weikko had short blond hair and blue eyes eyes. He wore a suit of fine plate mail and carried an ornate spear .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Weikko served as a gatekeeper and guide to the western ruins of Myth Drannor, operating from the Cormanthor Elves base camp nearby. He wasn't allowed to let foolish newcomers wander through his gate into the dangers endemic to the western ruins, instead directing them to take boats over Glyrryl's Pool to safer parts of the city. When a seasoned ruins explorer came to him, he was happy to escort them through the city without worrying too much about keeping them alive.
