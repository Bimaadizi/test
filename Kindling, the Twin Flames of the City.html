Kindling, the Twin Flames of the City were a pair of matching magical elven scimitars found in the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The two adamantine scimitars were infused with great and powerful fiery magic that burned away everything they struck. When matched together, the blades grew even more deadly.

History

The blades were created during the Weeping War , first used in the defense of the city right before the situation became hopeless. They eventually reappeared in a Cult of the Dragon base hidden in the ruins, since taken over by a Red Wizards strike force that enslaved the dracolich Karliath . The blades may have then been taken by the Cormanthor Elves when they removed the threat.
