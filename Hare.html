Hares were a species of small, gentle herbivorous mammals that lived in burrows. They were distantly related to the similarly looking rabbit , though were larger in size.

Behavior

Hares had a tendency to be skittish, though those that were familiars were far braver.

Abilities

Hares had a keen sense of both hearing and smelling. They were also known to be swift runners and jumpers.

Combat

When confronted with danger a hare would often try to dash away or hide.

History

The existence of hares on Toril dated back to the days of Netheril . In that era of history, the citizens of Ythryn were known to keep them as pets, often splicing them with wings.

At some point following the fall of Netheril, a sorceress known as the Weeping Witch used grimoires from that civilization to transform hares and foxes into monstrous beasts under her command.

Species

Ecology

Diet

Hares were known to eat a wide variety of vegetables.

Habitats

Hares could be found in much of northwest Faerûn , especially in the Icewind Dale region. In west Faerûn , they could be found throughout both the Eastern and Western Heartlands .

In the Hordelands , hares could be found in the region's arctic plains and in the upper reaches of the Katakoro Plateau .

Relationships

Some arctic rangers were known to have snowshoe hares as animal companions .
