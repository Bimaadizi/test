The Unicorn's Horn was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The inn stood an impressive six stories tall.

Location

The Unicorn's Horn was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the northeast corner where Waterdeep Way merged with the bend in the High Road .
