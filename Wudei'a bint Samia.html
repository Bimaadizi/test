Wudei'a bint Samia lived within the Tower of Adnan al-Raqi in Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Activities

Wudei'a was one of Adnan al-Raqi instructors. She also spent time studying under Adnan to learn how to create genie prisons and deal with genies .
