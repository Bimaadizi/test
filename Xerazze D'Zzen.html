Xerazze D'Zzen was a drow wizard adventuring mercenary from the city of Neverwinter , active in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Xerazze grew up in a family of several bickering sisters in the Underdark . Seeking freedom from hostilities, Xerazze left the depths and relocated to Neverwinter. She offered her virtuoso magic skills to those seeking to hire adventurers to collect or retrieve certain items and materials.
