Lanley was a Harbormaster of the city of Westgate in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Harbormaster Grenn took the title and responsibilities from Harbormaster Lanley, who had recently fallen ill. Grenn diligently did his job, despite being overwhelmed by the amount of work. He was amazed by how Lanley managed to do this job effectively.
