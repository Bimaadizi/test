Personality

He was an exceptionally vindictive and spiteful individual.

Possessions

Yago wore a knave's robe and kept a personal spellbook that was filled the specifications of various curses .

Relationships

He was married to the "witch" Brielbara .

History

Circa the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Yago's wife Brielbara had an affair with the adventurer named Coran . The two had a half-elven child called Namara . Yago cursed the baby and holed himself away at the Low Lantern gambling den in the Eastway district of Baldur's Gate.
