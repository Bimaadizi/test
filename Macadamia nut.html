Macadamia nuts were edible nuts from the jungles of the Malatran Plateau in Kara-Tur .

Availability

Among the tribes of the Malatran Plateau, Koshiva date wine and pouches of macadamia nuts were a trade commodity sold together.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , a hedgehog katanga named Abu the Lakey ran his trade business from his hut in the Tribe of Big Chief Bagoomba . Macadamia nuts were in Abu's stock and available for sale.

During his many visits to the home of Ed Greenwood on Earth , Elminster Aumar came to love macadamia nuts and garlic butter roasted almonds served by Ed.
