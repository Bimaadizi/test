The North Market was a large open-air market and landmark found in Saerloon in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Location

This roughly circular area was located in the north-central section of the city near the docks.

Description

The North Market was several times larger than the Dolphin Market which was also found within Saerloon. A large statue of Saer , the merchant whom Saerloon was named after, stood within the North Market.
