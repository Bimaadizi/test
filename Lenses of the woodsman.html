The lenses of the woodsman were magical goggles found in Cormyr during the 15 th century DR .

Description

The goggles had bright green lenses that glowed in the dark.

Powers

The lenses of the woodsman let the wearer see in any light conditions and even provided true seeing . They also helped the wearer land very precise and lethal strikes one after another.

History

These goggles were given to the woodsmen who served the Crown of Cormyr in the King's Forest . Arcton Greenwort provided these during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar .
