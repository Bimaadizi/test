Kaulvaeras Greymantle was a half-elven stablemaster inhabiting the town of Ashabenford in Mistledale during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activties

He owned Kaulvaeras Stables located on the west side of town. His stables were known for being one of the best in all of the Dalelands and he had a fine selection of riding horses and the occasional warhorse .
