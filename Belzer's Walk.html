Belzer's Walk was an alleyway in Waterdeep that stood on the boundary line of the city's Southern Ward and Trades Ward . Some referred to the road as simply Belzer's , much to the chagrin of Southern Ward locals, who were quick to correct those who called it such.

Location

Paved in dirt and gravel, this alley stretched northeast out of Caravan Court and terminated on Wall Street .
