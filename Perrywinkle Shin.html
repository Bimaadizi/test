Perrywinkle Shin was the highest-ranking active monk of the Monastery of the Yellow Rose in the Bloodstone Lands in 1484 DR .

Personality

Perrywinkle was at his core a man who was at peace with himself and he retained a sense of humor. He greatly disliked subterfuge.

History

Perrywinkle agreed to guide Jarlaxle and the two copper dragon sisters Tazmikella and Ilnezhara to the presence of the immortal grandmaster Kane .

In 1491 DR , he had become Grandmaster of Flowers but the monastery was infiltrated by cultists of the Cult of the Howling Hatred . With the help of some adventurers, he cleared the monastery of their presence.
