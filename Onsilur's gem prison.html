Onsilur's Gem Prison was a kiira of a forgotten elven house that was used to imprison Onsilur Maerdrathom at some point between 321 DR and 326 DR .

History

The kiira belonged to a house unknown as of the late 14 th century DR . Ansel Burwyth employed it to trap Onsilur Maerdrathom inside at some point before 326 DR , and hid the gem. It was still unfound by the late 14 th century DR, by which point Onsilur went insane after being trapped inside for so long.

Powers

Onsilur's sentience faintly babbled out from inside the gem in the form of speech or descriptions of memories stored inside the kiira .
