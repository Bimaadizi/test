Narthor was a hell hound living in northwest Faerûn in the 15 th century DR , being one of two that were owned by the fire giant Duke Zalto .

Activities

Narthor lived with Zalto, alongside the hell hound Zerebor , inside the ancient dungeon complex of Ironslag .

At times the two hell hounds chased around and blasted fire at a hollow iron ball Zalto that would throw around, much to their owner's amusement. This ball measured 4 feet (1.2 meters) across, was riddled with holes, and held inside of it the roasted corpse of a rock gnome .
