Tor Millows was a male human on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR . He was a priest of Ilmater .

History

In 1374 DR , Tor went to Fort Locke to heal people who were robbed by bandits. However, he was robbed and his symbol of Ilmater was taken away. He managed to get to the fort, but requested the Kalach-Cha to find and bring the symbol back. The symbol was located inside the bandit's camp . If retrieved, Tor would comment on the Kalach-Cha's approach on dealing with the bandit leader . Rgardless, Torn was able to heal the Kalach-Cha and their companions.
