The Helm's Shieldhall was a temple to Helm , God of Guardians, situated in the city of Elturel in the Western Heartlands in the midâ€“14 th century DR . Thanks to Lord Dhelt 's patronage, the Shieldhall was both the most influential temple in the city and one of the strongest temples of Helm in the North .

Circa 1358 DR and through 1367 DR , it was run with an iron hand by High Watcher Berelduin Shondar , with twenty-one priests and eighty-five followers.

It was large fortress of a temple. It provided aid and shelter to the faithful.
