The Courageous , or Abd al-Jari , was a group of farisan who fought for the Preachers in 1367 DR .

Base of Operations

The Courageous were based in the fortress of Sarahin located deep within the Haunted Lands .

Activities

This group served the holy slayers of the Preachers as a source of muscle when needed.

Members

All of the members of the Courageous were dwarves . Their commander was Mamoud ibn Asra .
