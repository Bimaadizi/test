Garauder was a pit fiend in the late 14 th century DR slain by the Simbul .

Personality

He had hatreds and schemes in place at the time of his death.

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Garauder was one of the pit fiends sent to strike at the Simbul when she assaulted Avernus in search of Elminster Aumar . He was the last survivor after the creatures summoned by the Simbul's blood ring spell struck out to defend her; however, when he attempted to make a suicide attack on her, he was eaten by a dragon summoned by the blood ring that he had missed before.
