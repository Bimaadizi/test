Emperel Ruousk ( pronounced : / ɛ m p ɛ r ˈ ɛ l / emp-er-EL ) was a member of the Purple Dragons and the guardian of the Lords Who Sleep .

Activities

Emperel lived in Ashabenford but often was out in the Stonelands fighting alone against goblinoids and trolls .

History

He was killed by the ghazneth Xanthon Cormaeril while investigating a series of murders in 1370 DR .

Personality

Emperel was a polite man with a noble heart and calm reasoning. He was worried that goblinoid find on accident the tomb of the Lords Who Sleep.

Possessions

Emperel was equipped with a weathercloak , a magical dagger , a magical ring that emitted light, and magical gauntlets that shot king's bolts . Unfortunately, none of these items were of any use against the ghazneth.
