Aida al-Irrahman was a the true leader of the Winged Bulls in 1367 DR .

Personality

Aida's cunning knew no bounds. She was a master of disguise and often paraded around as a man. She was also a superb orator. Aida took a practical view on romance, bedding powerful men if she believed she could manipulate them to her advantage and further the goals of the Everlasting .

Decription

Aida had pale skin and dark, curly hair. She had dark brown eyes. Her fingers and limbs were long and slender.

Activities

The Shadow Caliph recognized Aida's potential to be his successor upon his eventual death. Because of this he had her operating far away from the intrigue and danger of Sarahin .

Aid worked in the city of Qudra , building the power of the Everlasting there and in other great cities across Zakhara through spreading propaganda.

Possessions

Aida owned a pair of bracers of defense , chime of opening , dagger of life stealing , jellaba of protection , and a ring of human influence .
