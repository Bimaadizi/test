The Dark Sun was a temple to Cyric in the town of Llorkh in the 1360s DR . It was led by the high priest Mythkar Leng .

It was constructed by Zhentilar soldiers to reinforce Zhentarim power over the town, which had been conquered by them before 1357 DR . It was built and thriving by 1367 DR , and was still operating through 1370 DR .
