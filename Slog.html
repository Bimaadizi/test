Slog was a customer of the Blushing Mermaid in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Slog was a large half-orcish man with a muddy-green hide and dark brown hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the time Captain Grisly was replaced by the green hag Auntie Ethel as the owner of Blushing Mermaid, Slog was often seen drinking on the tavern's second floor.
