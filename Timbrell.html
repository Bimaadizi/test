Timbrell was an older citizen of Womford in the Dessarin Valley during the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the 1490s DR , the Pale Claw bandit gang took over the town of Womford and began stealing the wealth of all of its citizens. Old Timbrell believed that the terrible beast the bandits commanded was a terrible wyvern . Unbeknownst to Timbrell and other folks of Womforn, the Pale Claw was actually led by the terrible white dracolich Glyrgoth .
