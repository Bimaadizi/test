Mathira Melarn was an Eilistraeean sword dancer from Ched Nasad who wielded the Crescent Blade .

History

Mathira Melarn was a member of the Ched Nasadan House Melarn . There was a broken bust of Mathira in House Melarn's great hall. At some point, she joined the worshipers of Eilistraee and became a sword dancer . She wielded the Crescent Blade , a sword said to be created by Eilistraee for the purpose of destroying Lolth .

Mathira was last seen in Harrowdale , making her way to Black Feather Bridge . She was burned to death on the Cold Field by acid from a black dragon . She then became a ghost , who appeared to her descendant, Halisstra Melarn , in 1373 DR as she sought the Crescent Blade.
