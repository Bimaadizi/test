Lady Beth was a wealthy noblewoman from the City of Coin, Athkatla , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Beth wore green finery with blur trim. She had fair skin and brown hair.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Lady Beth was one of the eyewitnesses of a fierce magic battle between Jon Irenicus , the Shadow Thieves , and the Cowled Wizards that collapsed a part of the Waukeen's Promenade in Athkatla's Center District . She later recalled the visitors of the Promenade ran scared from the spell-battle and a young spell-caster named Imoen being taken into custody by the Cowled Wizards along with the cruel archmage . Lady Beth also was not surprised by the fighting as she kept an ear close to the ground regarding the workings of Athkatla through gossip from her stableboy and other servants.
