The kiira N'Vaelahr were 24 gems were used by the undercover agents of the N'Vaelahr before the fall of Myth Drannor . They stored memories and magic like a regular kiira , but also facilitated mental communication.

History

In 1374 DR , Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun attached a stone like this to the forehead of Tsarra Chaadren to avoid her mind being overloaded by his thoughts after the pair became linked during a magical explosion.
