Reavers' Run was the main street in Luskan 's South Bank .

Description

Reavers' Run was a wide boulevard that served as a divider between the neighborhood known as the Reach to the northeast—where the bulk of Luskan's homes and shops were located —and the slums to the southwest.

Geography

The road ran from the South Gate northwest to the city market before terminating at the Short Span , a bridge leading to Blood Island and from there across the Long Span over the River Mirar to the North Bank of the city. It was also the quickest route to get from the South Gate to the bridge leading to Closeguard Island .
