Al-Sabiya River , also called the River of Maidens , flowed through the central region of the Pearl Cities of Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Description

Al-Sabiya flowed south from Al-Yabki Mountains for 120 miles (190 kilometers) before emptying into Gana Bay on the Crowded Sea . The large trade city of Gana sat at its mouth.

Geography

Al-Sabiya originated high in Al-Yabki Mountains near the center of the Pearl Cities region.
