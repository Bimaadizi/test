Repo La , or Broken Wall Pass , was a mountain pass located in eastern Khazari .

Geography

Repo La was the only accessible route passing through eastern Khazari and the end of the Katakoro Shan range. The pass ran between the peaks of Ahs Trango Shan and Nas Trango Shan .
