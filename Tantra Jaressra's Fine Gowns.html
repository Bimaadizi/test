Tantra Jaressra's Fine Gowns was a small shop that dealt in sewing, relating, cleaning, and importing fashionable gowns and revel wear that was located on Saerdoun Street in the North Ward of Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Location

Tantra Jaressra's Fine Gowns shared a three-story-and-an-attic building with a number of other businesses. The building stood on the southern side of Saerdoun Street, three doors west from the intersection with Thunderstaff Way . Other tenants of the office building were Erro Alhandrar's Nets , Demra Samdro's Best Beds , Laskalan's Lamps and Lanterns , and Vressa's Sweep Begone , as well as Tiger's Eye at one point.

History

Tantra Jaressra's shop was established sometime before the building changed management in the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , when it came into the hands of a gnome named Hardyn Glorjarn , who took over the building's attic.
