The frostbite blade was a scimitar found in the Storm Horns of Cormyr in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The sword appeared to be made from a large sliver of magical ice, with leather and bone attached to make it into a sword.

Powers

The frostbite blade possessed a deep chilling cold, slowing and numbing those it hit, even freezing them to death.

History

The sword was recovered by Cormyrean forces while exploring Rungnir's glacier in the Storm Horns.
