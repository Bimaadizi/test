Vaeren Dragonskorn was a sky pirate.

Activities

Vaeren had managed to repair an old Halruaan skyship that he used for his business.

History

In 1480 DR , Simbul went to Dragonskorn's sky ship asking for a blueflame item . He instead ordered his crew to kill her but in the end the Simbul killed Dragonskorn.

Personality

Vaeren considered himself not a pirate but a "sky's adventurer".
