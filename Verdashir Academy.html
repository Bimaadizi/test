The Verdashir Academy , also known as the Dervish Academy , was a martial academy located in Baldur's Gate during the 15 th century DR . It could be found in the Outer City district of Little Calimshan .

The academy offered training for Calishite warriors, in the tradition of their homeland . Outsiders were only admitted to the academy if they proved to already be exceptionally skilled in combat.
