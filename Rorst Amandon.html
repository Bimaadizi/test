Battlelord Rorst Amandon was a member of the Council of Lords in the city of Zhentil Keep during the midâ€“14 th century DR .

History

Rorst proved to be an influential member of the Council. His title of Battlelord gave him supreme command of the military forces in Zhentil Keep. He openly denounced both Manshoon and the Zhentarim and ultimately died a slow and agonizing death by poison in the Year of the Blazing Brand, 1334 DR . Manshoon was widely suspected of being behind his death.

Rorst lived with his family in their estate in the southeast corner of Zhentil Keep's Inner Quarter .

Relationships

Rorst's only son was Syal Amandon who inherited his seat on the Council upon his death.
