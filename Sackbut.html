Sackbuts were musical instruments in the Realms popular among bards .

Description

A sackbut was a horn with a large bell at its end that produced a wide variety of sounds via a sliding mechanism.

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 45 gold pieces .
