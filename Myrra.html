Myrra was a Red Wizard of Thay in the Doomvault dungeon in 1486 DR .

Personality

Myrra was extremely cruel and liked to toy with her opponents even when she was losing.

History

Myrra was a Red Wizard assigned to the secret complex of Doomvault located under Thaymount . She was one of the most loyal and fanatical followers of Szass Tam and was tasked with the custody of the Chosen of Ghaunadaur Therzt in the deepest levels of the dungeon. Heroes from Daggerford fought and defeated her in 1486 DR.
