The Order of the Calming Wave was a monastic order in service of Valkur in the 14th century DR .

Activities

Members of the order traveled the world by land and sea, spreading the teachings of Valkur.

History

The order was formed by a group of pirates who ran into a fierce storm. After the crew had survived the storm, the captain decreed the storm was the doing of Umberlee , and that it had been Valkur that saved them. This lead to the crew abandoning their pirating ways in favor of Valkur's service.
