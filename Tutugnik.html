Tutugnik was a bugbear king living in Gauntlgrym in the mid–14 th century DR .

History

Tutugnik was the king of a tribe of bugbears, orcs , and goblins living in Gauntlgrym when an army of House Xorlarrin arrived in the Year of the Reborn Hero, 1463 DR . After brief fighting, Tutugnik and most of the goblinoids were captured by the drow .

Ravel Xorlarrin offered Tutugnik the chance to remain leader of his tribe under the drow's authority, but the bugbear understood that they would all only be slaves and refused. Ravel quickly killed Tutugnik and decided to purge all of Tutugnik's loyalists while the others would live as slaves.
