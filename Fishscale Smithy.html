The Fishscale Smithy was a blacksmith shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The smithy stood two stories tall. A fighting arena called the Monster Pit was located beneath the smithy and adjoining Thirsty Sailor tavern.

Location

The Fishscale Smithy was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Dock Ward where Adder Lane met Gut Alley .
