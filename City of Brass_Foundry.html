The Foundry was a district within the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire . Due to its name, this quarter was sometimes mistakenly visited by outsiders seeking metalsmiths.

Description

This area of the City of Brass had many plazas, wide streets, and large opulent homes or mansions. There were also beautiful memorials and tributes to past notable personages.

Inhabitants

Some of the wealthiest and most powerful efreet lived within the Foundry. Non-efreet were also permitted to live within this quarter.

Notable Locations

Reputation

The Foundry was considered to be the third most exclusive neighborhood within the City of Brass.
