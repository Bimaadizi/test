Azirrhat , meaning the Slashed Rocks in Common , was a small cluster of peaks located in the Sword region of Anauroch . More recently the spelling Azrrhat has become accepted.

Description

The spires of Azirrhat were arranged in a form that resembled the foot of a chicken .

Geography

They were located in the northeast region of The Sword, north of the Colored Waters oasis.

History

Some time around the mid–14 th century DR , agents of the Zhentarim made contact with the asabi of Azirrhat in order to garner allies for their takeover of overland trade across Anauroch.

Until shortly after 1372 DR , when the Shadovar returned and destroyed it, armands maintained a trading post and monastery on Azzirhat.

Rumors & Legends

Some native Bedine people spoke of finding deposits of gold within the clefts found between the peaks.

Inhabitants

The caverns and chasms beneath Azirrhat were home to a large population of asabis, nocturnal lizard-folk hunters that threatened any creatures who dared pass close by the peaks at night.

Sazeztlorund , a young male pink dragon , made his lair in the caves beneath Azirrhat.
