Madame Garah's Boarding House was a row house used as a boarding house located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Location

Madame Garah's Boarding House was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter on the south side of Slop Street .

Inhabitants

As its name implied, the boarding house was owned and run by Madame Garah in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
