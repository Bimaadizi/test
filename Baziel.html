Baziel was a powerful pit fiend in the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Baziel was often summoned by Brennus Tanthul . By 1374 DR, he had entered into service to Mephistopheles .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Brennus summoned Baziel to have more information about Kesson Rel . However, because of Baziel's recent change in loyalties, it was was Mephistophles himself that answered the summons.
