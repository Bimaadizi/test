A sentinel's cudgel was a special type of enchanted club or mace crafted by the druids of the Moonshae Isles .

Description

There were several sentinel's cudgels in existence. They were stout, oak clubs that each had a unique shape and features as determined by the manner in which the individual tree from which it was crafted had died.

Creation

To create a sentinel's cudgel , a druid of the Earthmother harvested the wood from a dead oak that had lived for at least a century. The wood was then shaped using a blessed blade and immersed in the waters of a moonwell for at least a full day. The method of crafting a sentinel's cudgel was very similar to the method for crafting a druid's cudgel .

Powers

These cudgels were capable of landing powerful blows, but were particularly dangerous in the hands of someone who fought alongside beasts or summoned creatures . Once each day, it had the power to galvanize allied beasts and summons to attack a foe struck by the cudgel, as well as to strike quickly against foes those allies attacked first.

History

The sentinel's cudgels were crafted prior to the coming of Kazgaroth in the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR , but following this event, any attempts to create new ones produced weaker weapons, likely due to the Earthmother withdrawing much of her power from the moonwells.

However, as of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , some claimed that Antola Pilark —the last Great Druid of Alaron —had at last succeeded in crafting a true sentinel's cudgel .
