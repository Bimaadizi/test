Farnath Ilistar III was a Prince of the Ilistar Dynasty who never sat on the throne of Westgate .

Activities

Though he was the sole heir of King Farnath II , Farnath III was a simpleton who was eventually banished from the city of Westgate by his uncle.

Relationships

Farnath III's uncle, Temprith would become the next King of Westgate by seizing the throne from him when his father died.
