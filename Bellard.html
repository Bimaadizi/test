Bellard was one of the bandits under Helm Stoneblade during the early-to-mid 3 rd century DR .

Description

He had a deep voice.

Personality

Bellard was sarcastic, skeptical, and a bit greedy.

Activities

He was a bandit in Athalantar .

Relationships

Bellard served Helm Stoneblade .

History

After Heldon was torched by Undarl and his dragon, the bandit Helm Stoneblade came at night to investigate with at least two companions, one of which was Bellard. The latter bandit was skeptical that the town and its people had been set ablaze by a dragon, and he was eager to loot the dead.
