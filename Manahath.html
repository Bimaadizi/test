Manahath was a caravan guard from the Moonshae Isles in the mid–14 th century DR . He sold spices to the drow of Menzoberranzan .

History

He could be encountered by adventurers in the underground tunnels of the plains of Icewind Dale , which lead to the Underdark . Manahath and his barbarian fellows were cheated by House Do'Urden via illusion magic .
