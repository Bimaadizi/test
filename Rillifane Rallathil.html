Rillifane Rallathil ( pronounced : /ˈ r ɪ l ɪ f eɪ n ɛ ˈ r ɑː l ə θ ɪ l / RILL-ih-fayne RALL-uh-thihl ) was a deity of the Seldarine who oversaw woodlands, wild elves , druids , and nature.

The Yuir elves worshiped a giant treant called Relkath of the Infinite Branches , which was purported to be an aspect of Rillifane Rallathil.

Relationships

The Leaflord was friendly with the fellow deities of Seldarine, and many of sylvan and faerie deities, in particular Eldath , Mielikki , Silvanus , Oberon , and Titania , as well as Baervan Wildwanderer , Cyrrollalee , Eilistraee , Emmantiensien , Sheela Peryroyl , Skerrit , Osiris , Verenestra and various Animal Lords . He was opposed to Malar , Talos and the Dark Seldarine .

Khalreshaar , an interloper goddess in the elven pantheon, aspect of Mielikki serves Rillifane Rallathil under this aspect.

Worshipers

Temples

Though not a temple, Rillifane's Grove in Leuthilspar on the island of Evermeet served as a sacred place for wood and wild elves to conduct marriages, funerals, or other ceremonies, as well as a place used to contact the Seldarine by moon and sun elf priests.

Notable Followers

History

When the Sy'Tel'Quessir settled in the Yuirwood , the Seldarine merged with the ancient gods of the Yuir, taking them on as aspects. Magnar the Bear and Relkath of the Infinite Branches became a part of the Leaflord. As a result of these changes, over the centuries since, the Wild One once more become more primordial in spirit.
