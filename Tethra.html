Tethra was an extra-planar fomorian who came to be in the ruins of Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

History

The giant originally came from some other world, but ultimately ended up in Myth Drannor after passing through a portal caused by its unstable mythal . It then resided in the north-west part of the city, near the Burial Glen .
