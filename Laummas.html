Laummas the Liegelord was a Hlontar lich allied to the Army of Darkness .

History

Laummas allied with Malimshaer and fought in the Weeping War . He fought in the decisive battle in Hammer 25 – 29 of 712 DR against the Harpers at Twilight where most of the Harpers died. However, Laummas was destroyed by the Harper leader Dathlue Mistwinter .

Laummas knew about the Gatekeeper's Crystal and possessed the mantle stone of Vhyridaan .

Relationship

Laummas was the master of the traitor Nezras .
