Morlen Askalan was a highknight of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Morlen Askalan was a highknight .

History

During the fugitive Elminster Aumar 's attempt to sneak into Castle Obarskyr of Suzail in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , he was one of the highknights set upon him. When the ghost of Princess Alusair Obarskyr came to his aid, Morlen Askalan did not accept her commands, so she walked through him, subjecting him to her ghostly influence. The man lost consciousness on the spot.
