Chesslyn Onaubra was an agent of the Harpers who operated within the city of Mulmaster in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Chesslyn Onaubra was a tall reserved woman with long brown hair. She appeared stern and intimidating but her eyes and voice betrayed tenderness and compassionate personality.

Abilities

As per Elminster , she was an accomplished swordswoman and renowned for being an expert longsword -hurler.

Possessions

Chesslyn protected her identity with an amulet of non-detection that shielded her mind from probing by the Cloaks of Mulmaster. Chesslyn's house was used as a Harper warehouse available only to those who revealed their Harper affiliation. Her home was located in the southern part of Mulmaster, and was a humble brick and wooden structure with a spade bedroom for her fellow Harpers.

Activities

Though she kept a low profile within the city, there were a few nobles who were aware of her true identity. They kept this intel secret, hoping to one day use Chesslyn to overthrow Mulmaster's High Blade .

History

In the late 14 th century DR, Chesslyn worked as a temple guard at the temple of Tymora , the Gate of Good Fortune on the East Ride . From there, she was keeping an eye for the Harpers on the activities of the Cloaks of Mulmaster. In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Chesslyn Onaubra aided famed Volothamp Geddarm who arrived to Mulmaster to aid an old acquaintance. During the Order of the Crippled Fang crisis in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Chesslyn Onaubra advised the Hero of Daggerford to visit the notorious Zhent Quarter of Mulmaster to find information of the Archmage Maganus 's youth in the city of Zhentil Keep .
