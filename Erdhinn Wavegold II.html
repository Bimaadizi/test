King Erdhinn Wavegold II was the fourth Amnian monarch of the Wavegold dynasty.

Relationships

Erdhinn was the second son of Belhinn I , and the younger brother of Rhinn .

History

King Erdhinn II died of plague in the Year of the Private Tears, 1204 DR , putting his nephew Gelhinn on the throne.
