Helve Moonstar was the patriarch of House Moonstar in Waterdeep in 1372 DR .

Personality

Helve was rebellious in his past, but matured over time. Helve secretly feared that his heir, Rober Moonstar , didn't have the qualities needed to lead the house, so he tested all his nieces and nephews hoping to find a better candidate.

History

Helve was an officer in the City Guard's cavalry until an accident ended his career. To the delight of his father, he devoted himself to his family's businesses. The business prospered thanks to his wife's guidance.
