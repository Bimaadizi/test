Nalmissra was a succubus who had a connection with Watcher's Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Abilities

Nalmissra possessed some inherent resistances that were typically found in demonic beings, such as natural regeneration .

Her kiss could charm others and even drain them of their life force .

Activities

Nalmissra and her companions served as one of the three groups connected to the fifth level of Watcher's Keep, which held the final seal that kept away the being known as the Imprisoned One . The group was summoned to the keep when the heart key was used to unlock the seal.

Relationships

Her allies included her fellow demons Xei Win Toh , Y'tossi , and the Huntress , along with the drow Ameralis Zauviir and an unnamed hive mother .

History

Nalmissra was killed in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , when Abdel Adrian and his companions bypassed the final seal of Watcher's Keep and ventured down to reach the Imprisoned One.
