The Hughloch was a small freshwater lake located in southwest Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Hughloch was located south of Hardangheight close to the coast of the Sea of Moonshae and on the border of Callidyrr . The lake was part of the Ironflow as it swept down from the mountains into the sea. The Hughloch was one of the smaller lakes found in Gnarhelm, measuring only 10 miles (16 kilometers) in length.
