Raskival Rhendever was a merchant of Suzail in the mid-15 th century DR .

Personality

The man was "crabbed".

Description

To Arclath Delcastle 's recollection, the man was hunched, and walked with two canes.

History

In Arclath Delcastle's youth, Raskival Rhendever had owned a house later used by Larak Dardulkyn .
