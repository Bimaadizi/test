Delsart's Drive was the name given to a cobbled lane in Scornubel in the 14 th century DR .

History

Per Dove Falconhand in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Delsart's drive was named after a wagonmaker who liked to get drunk and race his creations up and down the street, which had eventually been the death of him. His descendants owned a coach yard on that same street.

She went through that street when looking for the trader Sarltan during her investigation of a drow infiltration on that year.
