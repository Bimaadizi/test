Screed was a sailor aboard the Wicked Wench fishing and cargo vessel that sailed Maer Dualdon and the Shaengarne River in the early 14 th century DR .

Description

Screed was a friendly man with pale skin and dark hair. He dined on knucklehead and often flamed his flatulence on the fish .

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , the Wicked Wench was among several ships that carried reinforcements and hired adventurers from the city of Luskan to Targos , to aid the city's defenses against the Legion of the Chimera . Screed was woking aboard the ship at the time and befriended the group of adventurers who would later become the heroes of the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale .
