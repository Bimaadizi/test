Vargor was an ogre in the late 15 th century DR who was one of the Burntbone Horde 's many leaders. He helped lead its forces in occupying Sharpstone Keep , from where he oversaw banditry operations on the road to Waterdeep .

Possessions

Vargor wielded a large, two-headed battleaxe .

History

He was slain by adventurers on the orders of Open Lord Laeral Silverhand of Waterdeep after they drove his goblin and kobold bandit underlings back to the Keep.
