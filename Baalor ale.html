Baalor ale was a popular type of ale that originated from the Blushing Mermaid tavern in Baldur's Gate during the 14 th century DR .

Description

This brew was the creation of the Baldurian brewer known as De'Tranion . It was brewed with pimentos for an "extra little kick". It was particularly favored by Captain Tollar Kieres . The beverage was also well-liked throughout the Sword Coast and was available as far away as Amn .
