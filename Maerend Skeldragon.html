Maerend Skeldragon was a half-elf adventurer and chef who traveled across the lands of southeast Faer没n in the 15 th century DR .

Description

Maerend had blond hair, a very slender frame, and an agile, attractive body.

Personality

His personality could be described as mischievous.

Activities

He occasionally worked as a caravan guard, protecting valuable cargo. Over time he was part of two separate adventuring companies, the defunct Valade's Shields of Morvar and Telmark's Stalwarts in Suldolphor , the latter of which fluctuated in its activity.

History

Over the course of his life as an explorer, Maerend visited many of the nations of southeast Faer没n, including Durpar , Estagund , Luiren , Thommar , Ulgarth , and Var the Golden . Beyond southeast Faer没n he had explored the Tashalar , a little of the nation of Chondath , and the highlands and ranges of the Yehimal Mountains east of Ulgarth. As he journeyed across these varied lands, Maerend cooked dishes using the local herbs , flowers, and wildlife. Of all the dishes he ever concocted, his favorite evenfeast was a roasted hraztam in Ulgarth. For this he stuffed it with roasted chestnuts , dulthunn (a local variety of spinach ), shallots , and his own combination of seasonings.
