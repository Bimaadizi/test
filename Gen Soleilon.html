Gen Soleilon , who was also called the Radiant King , was the first King of the Soleilon Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Gen was a powerful paladin of Lathander , the Morninglord. He claimed the throne after killing King Orlak , the Night King, ending his long-standing reign of terror over the city. Gen reigned from −137 DR until −91 DR .

Relationships

Gen's son and heir was Lemere I .
