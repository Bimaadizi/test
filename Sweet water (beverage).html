Sweet water was a naturally occurring, sweet-tasting carbonated water.

History

As of the late 15 th century DR , sweet water was available for sale by the flask at the Apothecary in Triboar . The shop's owner, Tarmock Felaskur , harvested the drink from a small spring hidden in a shack behind his home.

The primary buyer and reseller of sweet water in Triboar was The Pleasing Platter , an upscale restaurant that catered mostly to visiting Waterdhavian nobility .
