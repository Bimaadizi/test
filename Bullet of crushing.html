Bullets of crushing were a type of enchanted sling ammunition.

Powers

Bullets of crushing appeared to be ordinary sling bullets at firs glance. However their enchantments become evident the moment one of these bullets hit their target. Upon collision, bullets of crushing instantly expanded to a size of a boulder, crushing them.

History

By late 14 th century DR , bullets of crushing could be found in the city of Hillsfar of the Moonsea region.
