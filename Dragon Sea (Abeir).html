The Dragon Sea was the largest inland body of water in Laerakond .

Geography

The sea stretched from the coastline of Relmaur all the way south to the Great Sea . Its major areas included the bays of Relmaur to the north, Melabrauth and the Dusk Ports to the west, and Skelkor , Eskorn , the Sword Lands , and the Windrise Ports to the east.
