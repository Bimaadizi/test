Abu bin Sujah was a renowned Zakharan desert rider who sought the fabled city of Suj around 1367 DR .

Description

Sujah wore voluminous, white desert robes, crowned with a white turban . He wore leather riding boots , and carried a curved longsword in a scabbard attached to his waist. He had tan skin and a short, black goatee.

Abilities

He was considered to be the foremost horseman of his people.

Possessions

Sujah's sword was magically enchanted, and was especially powerful when wielded against genies . His horse, named "Hisan", was outfitted with a bridle of speed .

Activites

Owing to his great skill at mounted combat, Sujah was entrusted to lead his people in raids against other tribes. He became interested in the seals of the princes , said to have been secreted away in the mythical city of Suj.
