Torriage Wood was a small forest located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Torriage Wood was located on the eastern coast of central Alaron where the Swanmay River met the Sea of Swords . The forest was located on the river's north bank. The western edge of the forest bordered the Central Plains . It covered roughly 100 square miles (260 square kilometers) of land.

Notable Locations

The forest sheltered a single Moonwell .
