Aurelia was a vampire spawn and one of the favorite "children" of the vampire lord Cazador Szarr , who resided in the Szarr Palace of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Aurelia was a female tiefling with crimson skin and black straight horns. Her eyes glowed with the typical sinister red color of a vampire's gaze. Like her "siblings," Aurelia's back was carved with infernal runes.

History

Like her "siblings," the purpose of Aurelia's existence was a powerful ritual to be performed by Cazador Szarr in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . As a result, the vampire spawns were to be destroyed, and their power would transform Cazador into a vampire ascendant . Aurelia believed that that ritual would instead let all of Cazador's "children" cheat death.
