Tigermist was a landscape painting created in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Description

This serene landscape depicted a mist filling a valley in between improbable peaks. This fog was known as "tigermist" in Faerûnian folklore for its uncanny padding quality.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , copies of the Tigermist painting were found in certain homes and establishments of the city of Baldur's Gate , the Western Heartlands , and within the ruined apothecary shop in the blighted village of Moonhaven .
