Sarissa Duncastle was a female human wererat from the Moonwood and was a rogue for the tribe of the People of the Black Blood living there.

Description

Sarissa was the best scout of her tribe. She loved efficiency and she was cold and calculating, a woman who knew no fear and felt a secret ambition to supersede the Bloodmaster. Sarissa had been a lycanthrope since her birth and a descendant of a long line of wererats.

Possessions

Among the possessions of Sarissa there were a mithral chain shirt, a +1 dagger , a ring of jumping , and a potion of invisibility .
