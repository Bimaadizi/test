High Whipmistress Spyrytara Xalass was the high priestess of Painbliss Hall , a temple dedicated to Loviatar , in Westgate in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Spyrytara was the leader of Loviatar's faithful in Westgate. Her congregation within the temple consisted of 27 other priestesses and 34 followers.
