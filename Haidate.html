A haidate was a type of armor used in Kara-Tur .

Description

A haidate was an armored apron made from laced armor worn around the waist. It was made of large metal plates that hung loose below the waist. Its purpose was to protect the thighs and knees of an individual. It was designed to work with a do-maru or a hara-ate .
