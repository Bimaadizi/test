Sarsor yi Tashluta was an enforcer in the service of the Rundeen in 1370 DR .

Activities

Sarsor was a yuan-ti pureblood of the Se'Sehen tribe in Tashalar . With the support of the Grand Yrshelem , who knew his true heritage, he infiltrated the Rundeen under the alias of a contortionist from Tashluta . He used his racial abilities to twist his body in order to grapple the people he was ordered to punish, which gave him the nickname the Rope .
