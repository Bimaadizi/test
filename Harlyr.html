Harlyr ( pronounced : / h ɑːr ˈ l ɪər / har-LEER ) was the cheapest and the most common perfume found on the streets of Waterdeep in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Harlyr was a clear, slightly oily red liquid that had no smell unless applied to bare skin. On most humanoids 's skin, harlyr was absorbed and exuded a pleasant aroma of freshly bloomed roses for about three hours. When it was applied to the skin of half-orcs , orcs , goblins , and hobgoblins , harlyr turned black and released stench of charred goldflow .

Preparation

The recipe for harlyr was commonly and correctly known as using nut oil, alcohol , and the sap of three types of common wildflowers .
