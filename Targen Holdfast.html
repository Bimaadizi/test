Targen Holdfast was a ranger and retired adventurer inhabiting the town of Ashabenford in Mistledale during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Targen became a member of the Council of Six in Ashabenford several years after retiring. He was the voice of loggers and farriers in the dale.
