Nottin was a hedge wizard who lived on the Sword Coast North during the late 15 th century DR .

History

While Helm's Hold was under siege , circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Nottin set himself up at a small camp located just outside of city on the outskirts of Watcher's Forest , joininh the likes of the ranger Dassar Truebow , the merchant named Kella the Fair-Dealing , and Meryth Heartwood . He aided the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge with the sabotage of material components used by the Ashmadai conjurers to complete their devil -summoning rituals .
