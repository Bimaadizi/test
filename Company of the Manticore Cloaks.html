The Company of the Manticore Cloaks was a renowned and chartered adventuring company of Cormyr , active in the early 14 th century DR .

History

By the Year of the Spur, 1348 DR , the Company of the Manticore Cloaks was a famous group of heroes in service of the Crown of Cormyr. Even mention of their name caused whispers and excited murmurs. The company was mentioned by King Azoun Obarskyr IV when then-unknown adventurers – Swords of Eveningstar , were granted Cormyr's official adventuring charter. King Azoun held a grand ceremony in the town of Espar and competed the young adventurers to still famed Company of the Manticore Cloaks, predicting the Swords' bright future.
