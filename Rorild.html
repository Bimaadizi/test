Rorild was a steward in the High Palace of Silverymoon in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He was the steward.

Relationships

As the steward, he worked for Alustriel .

History

Rorild was the one who discovered the body of Garthin Muirtree on 1369 DR . He was also one of the people Alustriel called upon to solve the mystery, as he gathered all of the pages serving at the palace.
