Ko Ho San was a powerful wu jen in 1358 DR .

History

Ko Ho San was a member of the mercantile Ko clan and a powerful wu jen. He was said to have once buried an entire town in a mudslide for an offense by the mayor's son to a Ko grandniece.

In 1358 DR, after one of his brothers, Ko Lei Kho , governor of Akari island, told him about the menace of the Opawang defeated the year before, he started to investigate the Black Leopard Cult , together with his grandson Ko La Ko . Thanks to a suggestion from La Ko, Ho San found a link to H'sin-to , at the mouth of the Fenghsintzu River in T'u Lung . So he hired some adventurers to accompany Ko La Ko to there in order to investigate.

Personality

Ko Ho San was kindly and polite to a fault, unless people crossed his path.
