Azaki was an assassins ' guild of Kara-Tur that existed in the late 12 th century DR .

History

Sometime circa the 1172Â DR , the Azaki guild was plagued by the evil entity only known as The Calling . The mysterious creature elevated a minor attendant and his family within the guild's ranks in exchange for performing dark deeds and one day using the attendant's body to house itself on the Prime Material plane . When The Calling took over the attendant's body, it sowed chaos within Azaki, creating dozens of enchanted shurikens called Many Talons in the process. Eventually, the attendant was slain by an unknown sohei .
