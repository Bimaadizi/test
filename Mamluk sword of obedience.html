The Mamluk swords of obedience were magical swords that could be found in Zakhara , the Land of Fate .

Description

These swords were available in a wide variety of sword types.

Powers

These weapons enhanced a wielder's fighting skill, but their main power was over all human, demihuman , and humanoid creatures. When struck by the sword, these creatures were charmed unless they were able to resist. Charmed creatures immediately surrendered to the wielder and behaved as if under a charm person or charm monster spell.

The wielder did not have to be a mamluk to use the sword's power.
