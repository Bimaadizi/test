The band of mental magic was a magical copper ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The ring gave a broad boon to magical power for spellcasters, with particular benefit to illusions , necromancy , and enchantment magic.

History

The ring was held in the hoard of the fang dragon Goranathanil , who laired in the ruins of Myth Drannor when the Elves of Cormanthor and Red Wizards of Droic Orgul fought each other.
