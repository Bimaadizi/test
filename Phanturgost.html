Phanturgost Amalree was a human wizard of the early 11 th century DR .

Personality

He was adventuresome.

Possessions

He owned Thrauntspire .

Relationships

He married Nathra , and fathered Ieiridauna Amalree .

History

In his time, Phanturgost made his name devising warding spells. Still in his youth, he fathered Ieiridauna Amalree with the elf Nathra.

He died in early autumn of 1036 DR , after inviting eight sorcerers from lands south of Calimshan with whom he and his wife had been exploring a Netherese ruin. The sorcerers slew the two of them swiftly, but encountered difficulty slaying their daughter.
