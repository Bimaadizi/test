The Ilistar Dynasty ruled over the city-state of Westgate from the Year of the Ensorceled Kings, 616 DR , until the Year of the Red Rain, 927 DR . The Ilistar Dynasty began when the androsphinx King Lyonarth and the lamia noble Nessmara killed each other, and Farnath Ilistar was chosen to be the next king of Westgate.
