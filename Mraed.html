Mraed was a type of a nut -based dessert prepared in the lands of Calimshan , Tharsult , and the Tashalar .

Description

Mraed was one of many types of desserts found throughout the Realms , similar to the Earth 's halva . It was a very sweet and nutritious treat of shaped and pressed raisins , almonds , pecans , dates , coconut milk , and durr (what was known as semolina on Earth). High fat content ensured the dessert's long self life.
