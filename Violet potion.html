The violet potion was a peculiar yet powerful that offered for sale by a traveling carnival-merchant during the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

Close examination of this potion revealed quite the odd solution.

Powers

Whomever drank this potion underwent a variety of physical changes, both beneficial and harmful in effect. They were inflicted greatly reduced physical resilience and were seemingly sapped of their agility and deftness of movement. However they were granted heroic strength, roughly equivalent to that of the mighty titans .

Both of the potion's effects lasted for a full 24 hours.

History

A single violet potion was offered for sale by a merchant at the Nashkel carnival in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .
