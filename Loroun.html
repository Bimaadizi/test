Loroun was a noble family of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Lord Relgadrar Loroun was one of two nobles targeted by Manshoon for mental subversion. Relgadrar had participated in the crafting of plans to capture certain bridges and keeps within Cormyr after the War Wizards had been slain.
