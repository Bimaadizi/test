The Immer Trail was an overland path beginning in the town of Immersea in Cormyr and ending at an intersection with the East Way near Masoner's Bridge . The trail roughly followed the northwestern coast of the Wyvernwater and then along the Immerflow . On its journey, the trail crossed the Sword River at the hamlet of Yeoman Bridge . The haunted hamlet of Sunset Hill was west of the trail.
