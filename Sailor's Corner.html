The Sailor's Corner was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The inn stood two stories tall.

Location

The Sailor's Corner was located in the western section of Waterdeep's Dock Ward in a small cluster of buildings that sat in the middle of Dock Street just south of Fishgut Court .
