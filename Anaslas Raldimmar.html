Anaslas Raldimmar was the Magister from 404 DR to 407 DR .

Appearance

Anaslas was sharp-featured and handsome.

Personality

He despised "brutish warriors", which meant any man of arms seeking to rule by force.

Activities

Anaslas loved to take the form of giant raptors ( hawks or owls ) and hunt warriors. This eventually became an obsession for him.

History

After tricking his teacher, Arghorst Talandor , into spending most of his spells, Anaslas challenged the Magister to a duel, gaining the mantle of the Magister in the process. He spent most of his tenure obsessed with hunting warriors in the shape of a giant bird, until he was ambushed and slain by Eleedra Nathchant in 407 DR .
