Meremoth was an undead lamia who lived in the royal keep of Nornglast in Eskorn , Laerakond , and a high-ranking member of the Eminence of Araunt in the late years of the 15th century DR .

History

Meromoth started as a high-ranking member of the Eminence, the only other member equal to Lod in power and influence within the organization.

As the only other high-ranking member of the Eminence, some believed that she became the de facto leader after Lod's destruction in 1479 DR .
