Gorin was an inhabitant of the Fortress of the Old Man , and one of the Blades of the Old Man . He was, along with Valmir , one of the few survivors of the night of Tarsakh 25 who met up with Talieth , Lewan and Ulaan after the three of them emerged from the Tower of the Sun . Though he was aware that Sauk was still inside, he preferred not to go check up on him.
