Wyl Mossfeld was a human of the village of West Harbor on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a brother of Ward and Webb Mossfeld .

Activities

Wyl, like his brothers, was a member of the West Harbor militia.

History

Wyl was the bully of West Harbor, who would tease the Kalach-Cha , Bevil Starling and Amie Fern . They were the champions of the Harvest Fair for several years.

During the Harvest Fair in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Wyl and his brothers competed in the Harvest Brawl. The Kalach-Cha, Amie, and Bevil, after defeating the Lannon siblings , would've faced Wyl, Ward, and Webb. If the Kalach-Cha, Amie, and Bevil were defeated, they could've asked Merring for a rematch or acknowledged that the Mossfelds had won.

In the subsequent attack on West Harbor by bladelings and duergar , the Mossfields aided in the defense, but Wyl was injured. The Kalach-Cha, Bevil, and Amie may have encountered Wyl, who would ask for help, and either healed him (through magic or getting Merring's herbs ) to get him back into the fight or simply slew him due to "his wounds being too thick to be healed".

When the Kalach-Cha and companions later returned to the village to reassemble the Sword of Gith , they encountered Wyl and his brothers. However, it was an ambush, as they had been slain and transformed into shadows .
