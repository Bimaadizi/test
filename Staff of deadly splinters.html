The staff of deadly splinters was an enchanted ancient quarterstaff found in the Elven Catacombs of Myth Drannor .

Description

The staff of deadly splinters appeared to be a simple slab of thorny wood.

Powers

The weapon allowed the wielder to cast a powerful version of the harm spell at will. The staff of deadly splinters contained five charges of the spell.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , a staff of deadly splinters could be found in the Elven Catacombs of Myth Drannor, among the treasures claimed by the Cult of the Dragon usurpers, allied with the drow of House Freth .
