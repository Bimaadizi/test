Dultann was a small village on the island of Gwynneth .

Description

Dultann consisted of a pond owned collectively by the residents, a woodcarver's shop, and a tavern that were clustered among a few sheep farms.

Geography

This village was located on the eastern edge of the Myrloch Vale .

History

In 1359 DR , the inhabitants of this village were wracked with fear over tales of the Risen Cult of Bane and their leader the "Dark Druid" . Around that time the cult's priests Anphrantul and Oishannan were living in the village, along with other informants of the cult.
