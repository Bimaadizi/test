Last Charge of the Hellriders was a book about the Hellriders of Elturel . It had no real value.

In the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , Duke Thalamra Vanthampur of Baldur's Gate had a copy in her library at Vanthampur Villa , but kept a key to her dungeons concealed within a hollow in its pages.
