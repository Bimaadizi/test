Mi'Shan , also written as Mishan , was one of the River Cities of Shou Lung that was part of the Ch'ing Tung Province or Kao Shan Province .

Geography

It stood on the Silver Road beside the Upper Hungtse River .

Description

A small city, it was well known for its waterfalls.

Trade

Caravans in the Hordelands were known to go to and from Mi'Shan circa Shou Year 2609 (1359 DR) .

History

In late Shou Year 2608 (1358 DR) or early Shou Year 2609 (1359 DR) , the chanshi caravan guard Zarwu Koi slew some bandits in the hills of Ching Tung Province then journeyed to Mishan in order to travel down the Hungtse River and compete in the Komite in Suijeng .

Notable Locations

It had an impressive shrine to Kwan Ying , one of the Nine Immortals and goddess of compassion, that was administered by priests of the Way .
