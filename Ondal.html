Ondal was a wizard who lived in the Heartlands in the 14 th century DR . He was best known as a vintner who made very good wine .

History

When the Spellplague struck in 1385 DR , Ondal's manor was destroyed and the mage disappeared and was thought to have perished. Several wagonloads of Ondal's wine survived destruction and were recovered. These bottles contained wild magic that was released when the bottles were opened.

Personality

Most accounts depicted Ondal as a kindly wizard .
