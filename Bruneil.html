Bruneil was a human soldier of Fort Locke near the Mere of Dead Men on the Sword Coast North .

History

Bruneil was one of Fort Locke 's newest soldiers (who did not want to become a merchant) that went with Tann and his men to patrol. However, they were captured by the shadow priest .
