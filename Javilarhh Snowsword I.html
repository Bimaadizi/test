King Javilarhh "the Dark" Snowsword I was a human of Elembar in the early 6 th century DR who became the first king of Delimbiyran and a founding monarch of Phalorm .

Relationships

Javilarhh's descendant was King Javilarhh Snowsword II .

History

In the Year of the Fortress Scoured, 511 DR , the human realm of Elembar was overrun by an orcish horde that ultimately foundered on the House of Stone . Although the capital of Delimbiyran and the surrounding land survived, northern and middle Elembar was ruined and the realm was reformed as Delimbiyran with Javilarhh as the first king. In the Year of Trials Arcane, 523 DR , King Javilarhh joined King Ruardh Lightshiver of Ardeep and King Torghatar of Dardath as one of the three kings of Phalorm at the Council of Axe and Arrow , joining together to mutually benefit their people.

During the invasion of the Howltusk Horde in the Year of the Fortress Scoured, 511 DR , the orcs were defeated at the House of Stone, though King Javilarhh died in the battle.
