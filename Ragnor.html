Ragnor was an ogrillon fighter and one of the three leaders of the military contingent of Castle Trinity in the mid–14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Maidens, 1361 DR , Ragnor, with the support of the wizard Dorigen Kel Lamond , guided the Trinity's invasion of Shilmista , causing great damage to the elves there. The tide of the battle changed when Cadderly Bonaduce and his friends arrived, especially the dwarven brothers Ivan and Pikel Bouldershoulder . Ragnor's army was soon defeated and Ragnor was killed by the elf Elbereth .
