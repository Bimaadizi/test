Shadow's embrace was a magical belt found in the ruins of Tethyamar during the 1480s DR .

Description

The belt had an intricate buckle, and when fastened the whole belt seemed to became more subtle as the belt, the wearer, and the shadows become one together.

Powers

Thanks to its innate ties with shadows, the belt let the wearer more easily trick, stab, and avoid their foes.

History

The belt was found in Ironvale Deep when the expedition of Hal Stoutheart arrived to seek treasure and the jewels of ardor .
