Lufala was a tasked harim servant genie working within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Description

Lufala was described as being matronly.

Activities

She ran the kitchen within the Royal Harim . She prepared many exotic dishes for the Grand Caliph 's wives and concubines.
