Felina Tatch was a priestess on a quest to release tortured spirits of the undead from their cursed existence sometime before 1372 DR .

History

Felina used her enchanted flail called Last Rest in her righteous pursuit. Eventually, the priestess met her doom at the hands of the vampire Milikaia , who slew Felina and turned her into a fellow blood-sucking undead. Sometime later, Felina was seen wandering the graveyard of her hometown, still destroying the undead. Milikaia's fate remain unknown.
