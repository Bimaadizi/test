The Watchfire Legions , also called the Orphans , were part of the standing army of the efreet in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire .

Organization

The 40,000 efreet soldiers of the Watchfire Legions were irregulars who received military training.

Activities

Members of the Watchfire Legions usually did not serve on the field of battle. The Sultan used them as secret police.

Base of Operations

The City of Brass served as the base of the Watchfire Legions.
