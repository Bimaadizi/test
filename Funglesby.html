Funglesby was a beast master thug who opposed the The Guild crime gang of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Funglesby was a gold dwarven woman with short curly brown hair and a clean-shaven face. She wore a simple leather armor jerkin and carried a small crossbow .

Abilities

Funglesby had access to a ranger's spells , including poison spray , hail of thorns , and protection from poison . She was accompanied by her loyal animal companion â€“ a large armored wolf .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Farlin led a gang of Guild members in dealing with the Stone Lord and his men. The new kingpin secretly served the Absolute and was trying to remove the Guild's influence from across the Gate. Farlin's group faced a band of the Stone Lord's men led by the tiefling Cairos , including Funglesby. They clashed under the Wyrm's Crossing bridge in Rivington over a shipment of supplies. However, the meeting devolved into a battle and either of squads's fate remained uncertain.
