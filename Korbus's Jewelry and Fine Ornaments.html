Korbus's Jewelry and Fine Ornaments was a rather small jewelry shop located in the town of Daggerford . It was owned and operated by the duke's court jeweler, Korbus Brightjewel .

Location

It was located on the southern side of Daggerford's Money Quarter at the junction of the Trade Way , High Road , and Water Street .

Structure

While the shop itself was only one story, there were gnomish living quarters within its basement.

Services

Korbus crafted all manner of ornamental jewelry, from the usual earrings and bracelets to even bejeweled belts and bracers . While it was believed beauty of these pieces were enhanced by means of his magical abilities, his trinkets could not be detected by magic .

His custom pieces were in high demand among the wealthy Waterdhavian merchants, and even those individuals that worked for the city's masked lords .

Additionally, Korbus offered services of identification and appraisal for the small fee of 10 gp per piece examined.
