The Laughing Lass was a cozy tavern in Suzail , Cormyr that transformed into a festhall on warm evenings. The establishment was a popular spot through the 14 th century and well into the late 15 th century DR .

Location

The tavern was located in the commoners' part of the city, its southeast section. The establishment catered to locals and travelers alike.

Interior

The upper floors of the festhall were illuminated by perfumed oil lamps . The wait staff staffed the upstairs private rooms, offering ales and foods.

Inhabitants

The Lass was frequented by the High Wizard Vangerdahast , although he was careful to disguise his identity. Emthrara Undril , a Harper operative, worked in the tavern as a stripper. Apart from cooks and servers, the festhall employed a number of well-muscled bouncers. The patronage consisted mostly of sailors, sellswords, and adventurers , while War Wizards and Purple Dragon knights were not welcomed here.
