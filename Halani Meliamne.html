Halani Meliamne was a slave of a yak folk village near Ironslag sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

Halani was a princess of the Moonwood elves until, in the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, one night several yak folk possessing some moon elf hunters killed her brothers and took her as a slave. From then on, she lived as a slave of Chief Kartha-Kaya , learning all about his yak folk tribe.
