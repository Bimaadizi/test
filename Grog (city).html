Grog was a small, agricultural city-state in the ancient Netherese Empire .

Geography

The settlement sat in the fork of the Zweihaus River and Westview River , upriver of Fluvion and Heip Lake .

History

The city was founded as an agricultural town in 753 NY (−3106 DR) , early in Netherese history. Little of import occurred there, with its main claim to notoriety being the creation of its local specialty beer , "grog." Though by all accounts a terrible drink, it remained inexplicably popular in Grog, then spread to became popular among Netheril 's sailors who used it as a rust remover and a vile rite of passage for their guild . The recipe eventually spread across Netheril, and the drink became common in all ports throughout the land.

Grog became the enemy of the militant Jannathan holy order, Jannath's Defenders , who they called "murderers who wantonly massacred entrepreneurs." The rangers operated from the Glorifier and developed a fearsome and brutal reputation, though not an entirely deserved one.

Grog continued its quiet pastoral existence until the farmlands around it began to turn to desert after 3400 NY (−459 DR) , and by 3503 NY (−356 DR) it had been abandoned. The only residents were rumored to be the ghosts of those killed while under the influence of the vile grog drink.

By the late 14 th century DR there existed a book detailing the city's history. This book notably alleged a connection between the city-state and the similarly named variety of alcoholic beverages, grog .
