The Company of the Swan was a band of former adventurers who fought together in their prime years and retired together at the Nellie Thursday Home for Experienced Adventurers retirement home once old age claimed them.

History

After surviving the trials and tribulations of the adventuring life into old age, the group settled down in a retirement home to while away their remaining days. Like most of the home's residents, they had spent most of their riches during their careers and were relatively poor; however, even this was enough to be provided a comfortable life with small luxuries.

However, resentment from one of their members towards the rest forced the group to venture forth once more long after they thought their adventuring days were behind them.
