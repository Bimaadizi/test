Kurgin Stonehand was the leader of dwarven Clan Stonehand sometime before 1372 DR .

History

Under Kurgin Stonehand's leadership, Clan Stonehand owned a mine not far from a dreaded black dragon Zarkursk 's swamp lair. The clan's warriors led an attack and slew the beast, inheriting its horde of gemstones . This sudden wealth attracted unwanted attention from the dwarven bachelorettes of the Clan Licehair . To fight that, Kurgin Stonehand commissioned the creation of dwarven belts of charming , enchanted items that lowered appeal and charisma of the young dwarven bachelors of Clan Stonehand.
