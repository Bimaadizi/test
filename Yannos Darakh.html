Yannos Darakh , who was also called the Slayer , was the third Nentyarch of the Darakh Dynasty to rule Narfell .

Activities

Yannos assumed the title of Nentyarch upon the death of the previous ruler, Heldakar Darakh , in −299 DR . He ruled until −270 DR .

Relationships

Yannos was the half-fiend offspring of a human and the demon lord Orcus .
