Leda Widris was a mercenary just before the Tyranny of Dragons .

Personality

Leda was an honest and courageous woman. She wanted to experience what a real cold wind felt like.

History

Leda worked for many years as a mercenary in south Faerun .

At the start of the Tyranny of Dragons in 1489 DR in exchange for passage to north Faerun , Leda acted as a guard for a great caravan, which unknown to her was full of Cult of the Dragon agents transporting treasures for the Hoard of the Dragon Queen .
