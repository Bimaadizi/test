Haedyll was a halfling cleric of Eldath from the Thornwood .

Description

Haedyll had bright, blue eyes, brown hair, and a pug nose. He dressed in white clothes, and wore a golden necklace with red gemstones.

Personality

He was known as a calm and harmonious person.

Possessions

Haedyll owned a pair of boots of levitation .

Relationships

He had at least one brother.

History

While traversing the Evermoors en route to Silverymoon , Haedyll and his brother came under attack. His brother was struck by an arrow and died, and Haedyll tried desperately to scrounge up enough gold to pay for both his resurrection , and their return home.
