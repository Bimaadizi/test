The aptly named Fishing Tackle was a tackle shop that catered to the fishermen, anglers and sailors of Ravens Bluff during the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The store was located just a bit east of the city's docks, on Aldiger's Cut within the Silverscales neighborhood of the Harbor District .

Services

This shop offered the usual selection of nets , hooks, gaffs , fishing rods and lines as would be expected from a dockside fisherman's shop. However, the Tackle also provided its own fare of nourishing comfort food including fish pie and spiced grog to the sailors and fishers of the Bluff.
