Old Goatbeard was the skilled yak-folk governor of the Red Goat province in the World Pillar Mountains in 1367 DR .

Activities

Old Goatbeard was a member of the Seven Sages and the appointed governor of the Red Goat province within the Yikarian Empire . He was well known for his collection of courtesans, all of whom lived within his small but splendid palace on the shores of the Lake of the Emerald Eye .

Skills

In addition to being a powerful wizard and priest of the Faceless God , Old Goatbeard was a master shapeshifter who used this ability to develop rogue-like skills.

Rumors & Legends

Discrete circles of yikarians were aware that Old Goatbeard smuggled goods through the Blackwash Fens near Lipo .
