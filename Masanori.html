Masanori was a shogun of Wa who ruled around 1717 ( 1299 DR ).

History

In 1717 (1299 DR), two ninjas infiltrated Aru disguised as pilgrims and tried to assassinate Daimyo Koji Kubahachi . They were killed in the attack, Koji couldn't find who hired them, and in revenge and as a message to his unknown enemy, he executed every one of the pilgrims in the train. At this brutal excess, Masanori removed Koji both from office and from the mortal world.
