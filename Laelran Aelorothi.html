Laelran Aelorothi was a fey'ri conjurer of House Aelorothi of Siluvanede and a member of the daemonfey in the late 1360s DR .

Powers

As a fey'ri, Laelran had the unique powers to cast dimension door and suggestion once a day each, and was resistant to fires and electricity.

Relationships

Laelran had a quasit familiar, Noxit , who helped him in his deception.

Activities

Circa 1370 DR , Laelran posed as an aspiring male human wizard called Sorndyrn Scarletsash and joined the Arcane Brotherhood of Luskan in order to keep an eye inside the organization.
