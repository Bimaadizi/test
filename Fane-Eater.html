Fane-Eater was a magical battleaxe .

Powers

This battleaxe inflicted terrible damage to a target. When struck, a targeted creature suffered from necrotic damage. At the same time, the bearer of the weapon absorbed some of the life-force of the targeted creature.

History

As of the late 15th century DR , Fane-Eater was in the possession of the dragonborn paladin Arkhan the Cruel .
