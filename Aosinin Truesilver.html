Aosinin Truesilver was a member of the Truesilver noble house of Cormyr and the cousin of King Galaghard III .

Background

Aosinin fought in the Battle with the Witch Lords in 900 DR alongside King Galaghard III and Thanderahast . He survived the battle.

Possessions

In battle, Aosinin wore full plate mail armor with an open helm and rode a brown gelding .
