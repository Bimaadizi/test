Braxow was a stone giant thane sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

Braxow was one of the giant lords at the time of the shattering of the ordning .

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Braxow was one of the giant lords who had arrived in Maelstrom in search of news about Princess Serissa 's plans for the future.

Personality

Braxow feared not following the giant pantheon 's ways.
