Korlessa was a dragonborn bard that lived in Baldur's Gate some time during or after the late 14 th century DR .

Abilities

Korlessa had the powers of a spellsinger , unique spellcasters that accessed their magic by means of song or dance. Her music attracted small, spectral dragons wherever she played.
