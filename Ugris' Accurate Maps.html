Ugris' Accurate Maps was a shop located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The shop was located on the Throat outside of the Throat Gate of the Foreign Quarter .

Activities

Maps of the ruins of Zhentil Keep following the city's destruction in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , could be purchased here for 5 gp , though the shop's proprietor did not claim any responsibility for loss of life, limb, or property as a result of following one of his maps.

Inhabitants

The shop was owned by a man named Ugris .
