Hunting traps were items sometimes used by rangers or woodsmen throughout the wilds of Faerûn .

Description

Common hunting traps were shaped like small cages and could capture a variety of animals live. Lethal hunting traps were baited and consisted of opposing blades that sprung closed to decapitate the target for a swift death.

History

Hunting traps were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue , where the price was based upon the trap's size. Traps for rabbits sold for 4 gold pieces , traps for boar for 7 gp, and traps for bears for 12 gp. The lethal variety of a hunting trap, which was effective against any animal, sold for 10 gp.
