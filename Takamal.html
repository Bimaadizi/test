Takamal was the ruler of Kultaka in 1361 DR .

History

Takamal originally was a great warrior that save Kultaka from the Nexalan armies led by Chalco , the Nexalan ruler predecessor of Axalt , the father of Naltecona , around 1295 .

When Takamal heard of Cordell 's victories over the Payits , he pondered alone whether he should meet the Golden Legion in peace or in war, but at last he decided for war.

Takamal developed a good strategy, using the characteristics of the terrain to the advantage of the Kultakans . During the furious battle, he directed his troops together with his advisor Naloc and quickly understood that his opponents were normal humans . However, in order to turn the tide of the conflict, the mage Darien levitated to them and killed first Naloc and afterward Takamal.
