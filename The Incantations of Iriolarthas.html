The Incantations of Iriolarthas was the personal spellbook of Iriolarthas , a demilich of ancient Netheril who haunted the halls within the necropolis of Ythryn .

Description

The black leather cover of the tome was inset with worms that surrounded the golden mage sigil used by Iriolarthas.

Contents

The Incantations of Iriolarthas contained the specifics for nearly 50 spells, including several powerful spells such as demiplane , blade of disaster , and power word, kill .

History

As of the Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR , the spellbook could be found within Iriolarthas' personal study, in the lost Netherese enclave of Ythryn.
