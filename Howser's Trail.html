Howser's Trail was a small mountain trail found in the Duchy of Soravia within the eastern region of the Bloodstone Lands near the border with Narfell .

Description

The trail connected the small town of Cutrock with Howser's Mine , passing west to east through the peaks of the Giantspire Mountains for roughly 75 miles (120 kilometers).
