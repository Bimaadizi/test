A dulcimer was a stringed instrument known in the Realms. The Zakharan version was known as a santur .

Description

The dulcimer had strings stretched over a sounding board, which was typically trapezoidal in shape. The strings would be plucked or struck with a hammer . The santur was similar in appearance to the qanun .

They were also popular instruments in Damara , where they provided hearthside entertainment.

A dulcimer typically cost 25 gp and weighed 10 lb (4,500 g). Dulcimers produced by Damaran craftsmen were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .
