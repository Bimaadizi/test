Old Riyas lived atop the highest peaks on the island of Kaff in the Djinni's Claws in 1367 DR .

Description

Old Riyas was an aging great roc .

Activities

In his youth Old Riyas could pluck whales from the sea, but as of 1367 DR spent most of his time sleeping.
