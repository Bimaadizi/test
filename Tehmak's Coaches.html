Tehmak's Coaches was a shop located in the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The shop stood on the northeast corner of the intersection of the High Road and Coachlamp Lane in Waterdeep's Southern Ward .

Description

The building stood three stories tall.

Activities

Tehmak's Coaches provided coach and transportation services with the southern regions of Waterdeep.
