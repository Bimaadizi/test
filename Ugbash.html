Ugbash the Unwashed was a short-lived King of an unknown region in the 12 th century DR .

History

He was born in the Year of the Maelstrom, 1101 DR to King Leopold the Gap Toothed . While he was the favorite son of the king, this did not stop him from taking the crown from his father in the coup of '54 . This rule only lasted a month, after which he was deposed by his own son, Reggie the Clean .

For unknown reasons, there was a statue in memory of him on the second level of Undermountain as of 1372 DR , which related the above information about him.
