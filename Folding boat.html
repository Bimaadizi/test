A folding boat was a type of boat coming out of a small wooden box and became a fully fledged sailing boat.

Description

The boats always came in wooden boxes about 1 ft (30 cm) long by 0.5 ft (15 cm) wide and 0.5 ft (15 cm) deep, that acted just like normal boxes, but radiated magic.

Powers

Upon receiving their command word the box would unfold into a boat, 10 ft (3 m) long by 4 ft (1.2 m) wide and 2 ft (0.61 m) deep, that acted boat with one pair of oars, an anchor, a mast, and lanteen sail. This little boat could carry three to four people. Upon another use of the command word and the boat would fold itself back into its box. Some versions of these boxes, would upon receiving the command word a second time instead fold out to an even bigger boat, 24 ft (7.3 m) long by 8 ft (2.4 m) wide and 6 ft (1.8 m) deep, that was decked, had five sets of oars, a steering oar, an anchor, a deck cabin, a mast, and a square sail. This bigger boat could carry about 15 people. With a third use of the command word, this bigger boat would return into a wooden box.

History

An unknown adventurer , lost and dead in the mines of the Dungeon of Death , had carried one of these items, and there it still lay in 1370 DR as the situation in those lost dwarven mines culminated.
