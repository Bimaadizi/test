A Farrberjik was an item that could be used to line the inside of boots .

Description

When boots were lined with Farrberjiks , they effectively became magical . These boots allowed the wearer to jump much further than usual, and potentially move faster. Thus, Farrberjiks could be used to transform ordinary boots into boots of striding and leaping .

History

In the 14 th century DR , Farrberjik boots could be purchased from Farr Windward .
