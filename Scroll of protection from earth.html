Scroll of protection from earth was a magical scroll found in Zakhara , the Land of Fate.

Description

Reading this scroll created an area of protection centered around the caster, with a diameter of 10 feet (3 meters). It protected the caster from all earth -based attacks, as well as natural effects. It offered protection against avalanches, earthquakes, quicksand, sand slides, etc . It also allowed those within the area of protection to walk safely across unstable ground. While the scroll did not protect against physical attacks from dao or earth elementals , it did protect against their earth-based effects.
