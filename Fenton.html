Fenton was a small coastal town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Fenton was located in southern Gwynneth in the far western reaches of the kingdom of Corwell . Fenton sat long the western edge of the Meirig Peatlands on the coast of Corwell Firth .
