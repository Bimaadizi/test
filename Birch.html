Birch was a type of deciduous tree .

Habitat

Birch trees grew throughout Faerun , though were considered relatively rare.

Varieties

Water birch grew in the Vast Swamp of Cormyr .

Silver birch trees decorated with silver filigree grew in a grove in Verenestra 's bower in the Seelie Court .

Uses

Wands of flame strike were carved out of scorched birch wood.
