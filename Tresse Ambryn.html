Tresse Ambryn was a skilled jeweler for hire from the city of Neverwinter , active in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Unlike many of her fellow jewelers, Tresse showed no passion in her craft. Her work was motivated only by profit. Despite lack of passion, Tresse was a very skilled jeweler with her services in high demand. In the late 15 th century DR, Tresse was offering her skills and services to the adventurers and defenders of Neverwinter, the Jewel of the North.
