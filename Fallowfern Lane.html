Fallowfern Lane was an angled southbound street in the walled town of Mosstone that stood along the Trade Way in Tethyr .

Geography

Sairnor Street merged with the southern end of the Fallowfern Lane at a steep angle, forming the distinct address where Taluth's Shrewd Flight , an archery store, could be found. Another location of some note that stood on the crossing of the two streets was a forgettable hot-bun shop.
