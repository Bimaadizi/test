Panana was a rock gnome baker living in Gnomengarde in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Along with Uppendown , Panana was responsible for prepping bread dough made from Gnomengarde's distinctive green mushrooms. Her role was to mix and knead the dough using a rolling contraption, which required her to stand atop her workbench in order to operate.

Abilities

Like all the other gnomes in Gnomengarde, Panana had some training as a wizard in addition to her innate abilities. In combat, she preferred to attack with spells like ray of frost and magic missile .
