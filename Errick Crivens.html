History

Errick Crivens perished sometime before the fall of Reithwin and it being consumed by the Shadow-cursed Lands before the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR . He was buried in Reithwin's graveyard behind the House of Healing . His tombstone read: "Here rests Errick Crivens, sole inheritor of the Crivens family. May the gods forgive him." Errick's grave marker remained undamaged as of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
