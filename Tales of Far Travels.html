Tales of Far Travels was a book written by Seroun of Calimport and published in the Year of the Rock, 1286 DR .

History

The book collected the author's accounts of ventures to the nations outside Calimshan and shared lessons learned from the experiences. The book was published in the Year of the Rock, 1286 DR . A copy of the book was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
