Fish Street was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Fish Street was located in Zhentil Keep's Common Quarter in the southwestern corner of the city. Fish Street connected Nool Street in the east with Beggars' Way in the west.

Notable Locations

A Zhentarim warehouse was located along Fish Street.
