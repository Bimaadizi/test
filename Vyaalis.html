Vyaalis was from the Coral Kingdom , a sea elven kingdom in the Trackless Sea near Evermeet .

History

For a time, he lived in Memnon , learning the art of longsword fighting from Shieldlord Hastor el Khafar .

Something of a wanderer, Vyaalis's curiosity led him to explore the surface world in Calimshan and Tethyr . His interest in the longsword stemmed from his discovery of one among the wreckage of the Pasha's Palace , a sunken Calishite vessel.
