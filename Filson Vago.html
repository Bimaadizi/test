Filson Vago was a visitor of the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Filson was a human man with sharp features, pale skin, and shaved head.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Filson Vago and his hin friend, Drana Hoyte , were visiting the city of Baldur's Gate during the looming threat of the Cult of the Absolute . Drana was convinced she knew how to navigate the Lower City , and was unwilling to accept that they were lost. Filson, on the other hand, was very aware of their situation but still needed help finding a way in the busy warrens of the Lower City.
