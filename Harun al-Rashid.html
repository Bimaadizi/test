Harun al-Rashid was a holy slayer who lived in Liham in 1367 DR .

Activities

Harun and his brothers were members of a merchant house in Liham. They used this as a front to mark a caravanserai for robbery.

Relationships

Harun was brother to Ramas al-Rashid and Beyab al-Rashid .
