Elvish seedcakes or simply seedcakes , were sweet bread -like treats that came in loaves and in bite-sized handfood shapes.

Description

Elvish seedcakes were delectable breads, baked with crushed aniseed , caraway , and coriander seeds. Some versions of these treats possessed magical capabilities to cure moderate wounds on the imbiber.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , small-sized handfood seedcakes were available in the City of Splendors, Waterdeep . In 1372 DR , Alicine Bricket and Muriel Bricket , served neatly individually wrapped elvish seedcakes during a party in their Western Heartlands summer home, outside Daggerford , in celebration of their brother Elveronne Bricket 's demise.
