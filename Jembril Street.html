Jembril Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Jembril Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward . The road originated on Shoor Street where it branched north into a neighborhood, intersecting Lathin's Cut before coming to a dead end.

Notable Locations

A city building called the Plinth sat on the northwest corner of Lathin's Cut and Jembril Street.
