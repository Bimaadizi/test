Nine Stars Around a Silver Moon was a ballad penned by a famed adventuring bard Mintiper Moonsilver , also known as The Lonely Harpist, in the early 14 th century DR .

History

Nine Stars Around a Silver Moon was published in the Year of the Highmantle, 1336 DR and a written copy of the ballad was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
