Senior Fury Barasta Cleeith was a wealthy noblewoman of House Lurraxol who lived in Athkatla during the mid–14 th century DR She a high-ranking Waukeenar priestess at Goldenspires as well as a member of the Knights of the Shield as of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .

Activities

She invested much effort in trading with Maztica , via her estate in New Amn . Her business successes over the years proved her devotion to the Golden Lady.

Her other concern was undermining the nomination of her rival Daerea Ethgil , a fellow priestess at Goldspires, for membership in the Knights.

Relationships

Barasta was the daughter of the Amnian noble Lord Lurraxol and the wife of fellow noble Lord Cleeith .

While she served at Goldpsires, Barasta became the oldest and most trusted confidant of Holycoin Tharundar Olhem , the High Priest of the entire Faerûnian church of Waukeen.

History

Barasta became a senior priestess of Waukeen at the abbey of Goldspires outside Athkatla .

Following Maztica's disastrous war with sahuagin , Lady Barasta redoubled her efforts investing in the region.
