Bug Bludgeon was a gloom stalker ranger member of The Guild in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Bug was a gold dwarf man with a pale skin tone, curly light brown hair, and hairless face. He wore a splint mail with its metal dyed in emerald -green â€“ the Guild's telltale colors. He was armed with a bow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Bug Bludgeon worked under Farlin , who led a gang of Guild members, dealing with the Stone Lord and his men. The new kingpin secretly served the Absolute and was trying to remove the Guild's influence from across the Gate. Farlin's group faced a band of Stone Lord's men led by tiefling Cairos , under the Wyrm's Crossing bridge in Rivington . However, the meeting devolved into a battle. Bug Bludgeon's and his squad's fate remained uncertain: were they killed by the gangsters or survived and returned safely to the Guild's safehouse.
