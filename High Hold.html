High Hold was one of the many small towns and villages found in the Rauvin Vale , within the Silver Marches . It was named for the foreland it was set upon, that looked over the Evermoors from the opposite side of the River Rauvin .

Government

The leader of the community was known as the Holdmaster. The town fell under the authority and governance of Silverymoon .
