Graygruun was an island within the kingdom of Norheim in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Graygruun was found toward the center of the Norheim Isles , just east of the large island of Giantspine . It sat at the end of Northwind Strait where the Sea of Moonshae met the Trackless Sea . Graygruun was only 20 miles (32 kilometers) long and about 5 miles (8 kilometers) wide.
