Hassantyr's Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Hassantyr's Street was located in the west section of Waterdeep's North Ward and the eastern side of the Sea Ward . Hassantyr's Street originated in the west on Sul Street in the Sea ward where it branched due east, crossing into the North Ward and eventually ending on Saerdoun Street near the outer city wall. Hassantyr's Street also connected with Shield Street , Copper Street , the High Road , Ilzantil Street , Vhezoar Street , Brondar's Way , Whaelgond Way , Ussilbran Street , and Nindabar Street .
