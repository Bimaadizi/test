Eaengul Skullcrown was a tavern owner inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was bald and towered over most men. He was rumored to be able to naturally see the auras of magical enchantments.

Activities

Eaengul owned the Friendly Flounder , a tavern located on Book Street in Waterdeep's Dock Ward .
