Patient Fingers Finework was a woodcraft shop in the city of Waterdeep that was owned by Dannath Lisosar in the 14 th century DR .

Location

This shop was located along Sleepers' Walk , in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

Patient Fingers Finework was a two-story-tall building.

Services

This business sold both wood carvings and wooden toys.
