Althon Olósynne , who was also called the Old , was the second King of the Olósynne Dynasty who ruled the nation of Aglarond .

Activities

Althon inherited the throne when his father, Brindor , died in Year of the Peryton, 1128 DR . He ruled until Year of the Lynx, 1189 DR .

Relationships

Althon was a half-elf and the sole heir of King Brindor. He had two sons, Elthond and Philaspur .
