Nariako was a human paladin wandering her native Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Possessions

Wearing an enchanted leather armor +2 , she fought with a shortbow +3 , light lance +1 and handaxe .

Activities

The Tuigan seldom begat female paladins, yet Nariako traveled the Taan doing what good she could.

History

Nariako was raised by an old wise man who trained her to become a paladin. In her travels in the Endless Wastes, she came upon Terasaka Tadafusa and saved his life. From that day, the two became near inseparable.
