A wand of flame extinguishing was a magic item used to put out magical and nonmagical fires.

Powers

This item could extinguish smaller nonmagical fires without using a charge. One charge was expended to extinguish a larger normal fire or a smaller magical fire, and two charges were needed for larger magical fires. Additionally, these wands could be used to cause a great deal of harm to fire-based creatures such as fire elementals .
