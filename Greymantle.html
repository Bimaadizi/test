Greymantle was a Chondathan town built near the Nagawater to supply Chondathan armies with horses from the Golden Plains .

History

Greymantle was established during the period of time known as the Calmwaters , a century of relative peace and prosperity in Chondath that lacked any significant historic events. Greymantle's founding year was sometime between the Year of the Jealous Hag, 154 DR , the assassination of Florentine Dempster of Turmish , and the Year of the Raised Banner, 227 DR , the year dwarves of Alaoreaum revealed themselves to the humans of the Vilhon Reach .
