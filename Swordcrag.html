Swordcrag was an orcish fort in the Tarkhaldale , the Lost Dale.

Description

The tall grey rocky crag, which had given the name to the village, stood as a marker, visible for miles.

Geography

Swordcrag stood tucked away in the south-western part of the Desertsmouth Mountains ' foothills.

Society

By 1367 DR , among Swordcrag's residents were only the members of the remaining orc tribe and ogrish guards of the local chieftain .

History

Swordcrag was once a small mining village which was built before the Year of Hostile Hails, −88 DR .

The village was conquered during an invasion of the orcs of the Dragonspine Mountains , which had overrun the whole Tarkhaldale by Year of Dashed Dreams, −87 DR . Since then the orcs controlled the mines below the imposing crag.

By 1367 DR the remaining orcs and ogres living in Swordcrag had fought in several conflicts against the saurials from Tarkhaldan . The orc chieftain of Swordcrag, Durang Head-taker with help of his shaman, Yegg Blackteeth , spent years forging alliances among neighboring tribes to challenge the saurials and was successful at it.
