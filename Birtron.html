Birtron the Magnificent was a ruler of the Southern Reaches .

History

He was born in the Year of Much Ale, 233 DR . At some point he became known as the Magnificent and as the Unholy Avenger of the Nine Hells . He enjoyed a long reign of terror in the Southern Reaches, wherever they were.

For unknown reasons, there was a statue in memory of him on the second level of Undermountain as of 1372 DR , which related the above information about him.
