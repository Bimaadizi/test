Jhyndara Thiolstar was a High Priestess, or High Luck Priestess, of Tymora in Yhaunn in 1358 DR .

Activities

Jhyndara was the ranking priestess in the Ladysluck Tower . She oversaw 34 other priests and priestesses of Tymora. A congregation of 71 followers filled the temple to worship.
