Plate of Silver was a dish served at the Stargath House inn in the Amnian town of Purskul .

Description

The dish contained grilled until soft silverfin fish dabbed with a sauce mix of raspberry juice and vinegar . The fish was served on a mountain of cooked crab , onions , and fried sliced potatoes .

This platter went well together with grilled calf's liver braised in zzar , mintjuice mash , and lemon , or mussels in spicy vegetable broth .
