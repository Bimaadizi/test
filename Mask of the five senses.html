Masks of the five senses were a type of enchanted masks of unknown origins that were in use in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

These masks sharpened senses of the wearer when worn, hence granting them enhanced perception.

History

In the 15 th century DR, masks of the five senses could be found among bandits and heroes of the Sword Mountains , Neverwinter environs, raiders from the Mere of Dead Men , and among the ruins of Illefarn .
