Angelica was a plant harvested in Faerûn that was used for food, medicine, and certain magical purposes.

Availability

Aurora's Emporium sold angelica seasoning for 5 cp per 1 ounce (28 grams), and candied angelica for 1 gp per 1 ounce (28 grams).
