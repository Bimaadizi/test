The Brownie language was the racial language of brownies , a type of fey .

Speakers

Beyond brownies themselves, Brownie was a common secondary language in Faer√ªn among forest gnomes , lightfoot halflings , rock gnomes , and tallfellow halflings . Beyond demihumans , Brownie was also commonly spoken by other fey creatures, such as dobies , grigs , killmoulis , quicklings , and stwingers . And elven cats who attached themselves to brownie families typically spoke Brownie to a rudimentary degree.

Brownie was also common as a secondary language among druids devoted to certain nature deities. Such as the druids of Chauntea , druids of Eldath , druids and druid/ rangers of Mielikki , the druids of Silvanus , and the druids of Sheela Peryroyl . Other forms of specialty priests in the Realms for whom Brownie was a commonly known secondary language were the Eldathyn peacemen .
