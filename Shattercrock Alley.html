Shattrcrock Alley was a small alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Shattercrock Alley was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's North Ward . The alley branched south off of Stabbed Sailor Alley , reconnecting with the alley after encircling Margaster Villa .

History

The alley was named appropriately when a wizard , one Phantamos of the Purple Cloak , was learning to fly and crashed wildly through a cart of crockery.
