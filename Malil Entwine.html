Malil Entwine was a business owner and a mayor of a Chondathan fishing town of Samra circa 1370 DR .

Description

The mayor was a quiet and timid man whose bark was worse than his bite. Malil's biting words were rarely backed up by actions. Mayor Entwin made sure Samra's taxes made their way to Arrabar in a timely and secure manner according to schedule.

History

Malil Entwine was an elected official in Samra, who joined the city's ruling council of elders sometimes in or before the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR . Malil's political career sprouted from owning a local fishery.
