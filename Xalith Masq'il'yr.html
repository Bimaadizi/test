Xalith Masq'il'yr was a drow trader living in Gracklstugh in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Xalith was a trader in Gracklstugh and an ally of House Mizzrym of Menzoberranzan .

History

Circa 1486 DR , Xalith was contacted by Ilvara Mizzrym and ordered to search for an adventuring party that had escaped Velkynvelve .
