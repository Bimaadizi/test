Kruurk was an orc bloodrager of the Many-Arrows Kingdom , active in the 15 th century DR .

History

During the tumultuous century that followed the Spellplague , orcs of the Many-Arrows Kingdom expanded their influence into the Sword Coast North . Kruurk was put in charge of the Many-Arrows orcs who held the the Thunderhoof Pass in the Sword Mountains and the entrance to the Old Owl Well 's valley. Kruurk and his followers were defeated by adventurers financed by Lord Dagult Neverember of Neverwinter , tasked with reclaiming the Old Owl Well.
