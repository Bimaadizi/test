Yegor was the leader of a cell of the Cult of the Black Earth in the Moonsea .

History

Yegor led an earth cultist cell in the Moonsea; they acted as minor pirates until 1491 DR . That year, they were tasked with taking the elemental codex from Furgis Boltsmelter . Yegor's minions tried many times to take it but were always defeated. At last, Yegor himself led a last group of cultists against Furgis and/or his adventuring party bodyguards, who were talking with the drow wizard Murssvayas Dhuurniv , but they were defeated and Yegor killed.
