A gargoyle cloak was an enchanted cloak capable of transforming the wearer into a gargoyle .

Powers

The wearer could use the cloak's power up to three times per day. The transformation occurred as soon as the cloak was donned. There was a cumulative risk that the wearer would actually believe they were a gargoyle the longer they remained in that form. If this occurred, the wearer could only be returned to their normal form via a wish .

Notable Owners

Sememmon , the ruler of Darkhold , owned a gargoyle cloak .
