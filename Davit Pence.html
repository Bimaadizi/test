Davit Pence was a Cormyrean Purple Dragon who served in the town of Eveningstar in the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Davit was recovering in the Eveningstar War Hospital after his company was attacked in the King's Forest . Along with others of his company, Davit was infected with lycanthropy . One night, the head priest of the hospital Hurim Jaskyr was brutally murdered and torn to pieces, to the shock and horror of the patients and staff. Healer Delethir Mistveil accepted the aid of adventurers and bade them investigate. After finding the halls empty, the adventurers discovered Davit in the hospital courtyard as night fell, when he involuntarily transformed into a werewolf and attacked during questioning, revealing the true nature of the problem in the hospital.
