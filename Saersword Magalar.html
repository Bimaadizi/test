Saersword Magalar was a notable thief and swindler inhabiting Yeshpek along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Saersword had a silver tongue and usually able to manipulate others with ease.

Description

A childhood disease left half of Saersword's face pockmarked, otherwise he would be considered a handsome youth. He had long hair.

Activities

Saersword employed children to be his spies and could call on a gang of toughs to back him up if necessary.
