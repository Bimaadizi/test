Zara Dalkor was a knight of Helm in Helm's Hold sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

Zara was a member of the Order of the Gilded Eye . In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR Zara and her squire Thora Tamlarrin went to South in order to defeat a group of frost giants that attacked caravans.
