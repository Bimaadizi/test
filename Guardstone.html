Guardstone was a gargoyle in Myth Drannor , who protected Ildrannath's Tower until at least the late 14 th century DR .

History

The stony beast roamed the ruins of Ildrannath's Tower. It was eventually encountered by the Elves of Cormanthor , who sought the Key to the city's mythal stored there.
