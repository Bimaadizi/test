Location

The Port District was situated in the northwest corner of Skuld. It was bordered on the west by the Alamber Sea .

Aside from the docks, access to the Port District was achieved by a single gate along the southern wall that connected to the Merchant District .

Activities

The Port District handled all incoming and outgoing commerical vessels trading wares in Skuld.
