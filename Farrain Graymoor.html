Farrain Graymoor was a human magistrate of Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

Farrain was a large, heavily-built man with white hair.

History

Around the time of the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon , Farrain and Barmar Trull oversaw the processing of prisoners at the docks of Eveningstar as they were loaded onto the Wheloon Prison boat . He had strong misgivings about the operation given what he had heard about the prison.
