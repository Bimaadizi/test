Imbrus II , son of Imbrus I , was the 41st Obarskyr monarch and one of the three "boy kings" of Cormyr while Rathdar Orlenthar ruled as regent. He died in the Purplethroat Plague as did the other boy kings and was succeeded by the granddaughter of Gorauna , "Mad Meurthe" .
