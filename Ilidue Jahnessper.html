Ilidue Jahnessper was a priest of the church of Oghma , called a Namer, based in Silverymoon in the Silver Marches in the late 15 th century DR . By around 1480 DR , with her sister Narlylle , Ilidue had gained great prosperity from work in an arts or entertainment industry.
