Pilea Skysage was an elf sage who specialized in mythals in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Pilea had shoulder-length brown hair and gray eyes. She wore a bright blue and white robe and pants outfit, and fingerless gloves .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Pilea was a senior member of the Elves of Cormanthor at their forward camp outside the ruins of Myth Drannor . She provided expertise on the subject of the malfunctioning mythal , which could be seen flashing in the sky overhead and was responsible for many of the dangers within the city.
