Chief Claw was the leader of the Black Lion tribe of Uthgardt people in the North , for a time during the mid–to–late 14 th century DR .

Description

The chief had stark black hair and spoke with a accent that came out during words outside of his regular use.

History

When the phaerimm emerged as a great threat in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Chief Claw led his tribe to battle alongside dwarves , elvess , and the people of the free cities of the North into a unified army . During their time fighting together, Claw drew the ire of the elven commander Yoraeida of Evermeet .
