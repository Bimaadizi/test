First Blood was a bastard sword found in Wheloon Prison in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The blade was heavily chipped, while the hilt was well ornamented.

Powers

First Blood was enhanced to quickly inflict terrible and lethal blows.

History

The sword was found by Cormyrean forces after defeating the shadar-kai Karleth in the merchant's townhouse during the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon .
