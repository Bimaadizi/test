Musalim was one of the D'tarig people that lived in the desert of Anauroch during the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

Like other D'tarig, Musalim was rather diminutive in size, standing about 4 ft (1.2 m) tall with a slender build.

Personality

He was rather subservient to others, readily deferring to others and calling them "Lord", despite their protest.

Relationships

Musalim served as the assistant to Bhadla .

History

In the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR , Musalim and Bhadla served as a guide for the Harper agent Lander , while at the same time working for Yhekal of the Zhentarim . After a sandstorm separated Lander from the two D'tarig, Musalim went missing, presumably having ridden into an asabi ambush and been slain by one of the reptilian beasts.
