Vial of revenge was a potion invented by the rashemi wizard Shevarith Kendia in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The potion was ingested prior to battle. If the imbiber were ever slain, it would trigger an arcane curse that inflicted the imbiber's full magical strength against all of their enemies at once.

History

Shevarith had travelled to Waterdeep to earn the respect of the Blackstaff , Vajra Safahr , and was driven to pursue magical excellence at all costs. She devised the vial of revenge to make sure that no one would ever best her in combat without paying a price.
