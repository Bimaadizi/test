The Lisloch was a freshwater lake located in eastern Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Lisloch abutted the Lisbunt Mountains on its southern and western shores. The lake was fed by an unnamed river flowing down from the mountain and emptied into the Sea of Swords on the far eastern coast of Gnarhelm near the island of Amarune . The Lisloch was one of the larger lakes found in Gnarhelm, covering approximately 150 mi² (390 km²).
