Harijo Ten Rasid Nahdatul was the military commander of the kingdom of Bawa circa Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) .

History

In his youth, Harijo was a valiant adventurer, defeating many monsters. After retiring, he joined the Bavanese army and rose through the ranks until only the king could command him.

Harijo was plotting an anti-pirate operation: he wanted to invade Panjardu to defeat the local pirates, led by his old enemy Wibisono .
