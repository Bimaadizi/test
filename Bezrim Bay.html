Bezrim Bay was a small inlet found within the Lake of Steam of south Faerûn .

Geography

The bay was located in the southwest corner of the Lake of Steam. It was wedged between the Narrows Trail to the west with the island of Felmer's Keep and a small peninsula from the mainland in the east.

Notable Locations

The city of Yeshpek was founded along Bezrim Bay.
