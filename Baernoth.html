Description

Baernoth was a very strong man with a gigantic build and a beard.

Personality

Despite his build, he was softly spoken, as well as careful and reasonable, yet he seemed to fear nothing in the whole world.

Activities

By around 1357 DR , Baernoth had been elected Elder of the village's governing Council. Those of the vale had great respect for Baernoth; as such, he was expected to hold the position of Elder for as long as he wished.
