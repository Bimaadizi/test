Gloves of the Growling Underdog were enchanted gauntlets that gave the wearer an upper hand in combat.

Powers

When in battle, facing unfavorable odds, the gauntlets' underdog's bravery magic gave the wearer an advantage in landing successful attacks but only when they were surrounded by two or more opponents. Additionally, the gloves increased the wearer's physical strength.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a pair of gloves of the Growling Underdog was stored in the treasure room within the ruined Shattered Sanctum in the Chionthar Valley . Presumably, the gauntlets were among treasures collected in the Cult of the Absolute 's raids on nearby communities.
