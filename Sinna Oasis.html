The Sinna Oasis was an important oasis found in west Zakhara .

Geography

The oasis was located 120 mi (190 km) west of Tajar after al-Adib River terminated, making it an important watering hole for caravans travelling between Tajar on the Crowded Sea in the east and Akota on the Great Sea in the west, as well as al-Badia roaming the desert found between al-Yabki Mountains and al-Suqut Mountains . The Sinna Oasis sat in the southern shadow of the Range of the Marching Camels .
