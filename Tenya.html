Abilities

Despite her young age Tenya possessed tremendous power, which she drew from her patron deity Umberlee.

Activities

She and her mother lived in a house near the Wyrm's Crossing bridge of Baldur's Gate , on the opposite side of the River Chionthar .

Tanya served as a priestess at the Water Queen's House , located in the Lower City docks.

Possessions

The young girl wore pair of bracers of defense .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Tenya's mother was robbed by numerous fisherman, including Jebodah , Sonner , and Telman . She spent weeks searching for the thieves, trying to recover her mother's lost bowl of water elemental summoning .

During her hunt for the malicious fisherman Tenya repeatedly harried them, drowning two of them and sinking a ship with a dozen more.
