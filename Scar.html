The Scar was the massive, eponymous gorge that spanned the width of Scardale within the southern Dalelands . The deep cliffs of the canyon enveloped the River Ashaba on the tail end of its course from the Feather Falls waterfall in the west until it emptied into the Sea of Fallen Stars in the east.
