The Old Monster Shop was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building was four stories tall.

Location

The Old Monster Shop was located in the eastern section of the Southern Ward on a small alley called the Jar just south of Tilman's Lane .

Activities

The shop specialized in poisons and other extraordinary items.
