A red rose ring was an honorific distinction, bestowed upon knights of the Order of the Dove , in the city of Ravens Bluff during the late 14 th century DR . This award was reserved for Doves who had defeated a powerful monster without the use of lethal weapons or harmful magic .

If a Knight of the Dove earned this award, they were eligible to serve as one of the city's "Dove Emissaries", who acted as guides and protectors for Ravenian and foreign diplomats.
