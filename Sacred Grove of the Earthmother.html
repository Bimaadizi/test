The Sacred Grove of the Earthmother , which was also simply called the Sacred Grove , was a small forest located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Sacred Grove was located within the southeast region of Myrloch Vale nestled between the Myrloch and western slopes of the Cambro Mountains .

Notable Locations

The forest contained a single Moonwell .

Inhabitants

As of the late 15 th century DR , the Grove was home to Branwyn Moonsinger , a former Great Druid who had spent her life watching and preparing for the return of the Darkwalker .
