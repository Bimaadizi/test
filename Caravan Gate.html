The Caravan Gate was the main entry point into the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Caravan Gate was located in western Daggerford at the intersection of Duke's Way and Wall Street .

Defenses

The gate was only accessible after crossing a moat and bridge. The road leading through the gate passed beneath a section of Daggerford's outer wall that was guarded by a pair of large towers.
