The Brazen Buck was an inn and tavern on the Arnrock in the Lake of Steam as of the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

It was located within Sleeping Hollow , the island's closest approximation of a town.

Services

The Brazen Buck was both the only inn and the only tavern on the island, and its proprietor, Belathra Kyndle was the only non- halfling on the island. Her inn was the only place in which other non-halflings could find lodgings and drink.

History

Along with the rest of the settlements on the island, the inn was lost after the Arnrock erupted in the Year of the Halflings' Lament, 1386 DR .
