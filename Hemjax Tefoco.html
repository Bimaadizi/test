Hemjax Tefoco was the youngest child of Oliver Tefoco , the proprietor of the Beehive Goods and Wares general store in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Hemjax was a child of Oliver and Kroyce Tefoco , and had a number of siblings. The oldest of them was Bubbles Tefoco , who lived in the Stonyeyes district of the Gate with her family and the newborn Dorimara . Another older sibling was Dariel Tefoco who served the Flaming Fist . Thegglis and Crowell were traveling caravan guards, while the youngest of the bunch, Maggie and others, still lived with Oliver and Kroyce as of the late 15 th century DR.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute threatened Baldur's Gate's safety, Kroyce Tefoco took Hemjax, Maggie, and other youngest kids to the town of Beregost , to live with Uncle Sternwell , away from the threat of war. Oliver left behind to run the shop, to Kroyce's dismay. Hemjax and the family were given Uncle Sternwell's shearing shed to live in.
