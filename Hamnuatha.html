Hamnuatha was a leviathan inhabiting the harbor of Leuthilspar in Evermeet in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Hamnuatha patrolled the waters of the harbor and would attack any unauthorized vessels attempting to approach the city as of the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .
