Miraj Vizann was the second-in-command of the Cult of the Black Earth at the Temple of the Black Earth in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Personality

Miraj was a very rational and deliberate individual. He joined the earth cult only to deepen his understanding of elemental magic . He believed that enemies could be bribed or persuaded, such as to attack instead fire cultists. For his practice of both earth and water magic, Miraj called himself a "mud sorcerer". He was a perfectionist. He was so focused on his magical research that he had no interest in challenging Marlos Urnrayle for leadership.

History

Miraj hailed from Calimshan .

In 1491 DR , Miraj oversaw the Black Earth temple in Tyar-Besil.
