The Circle of Steel was a famous all-female adventuring company in the early 14 th century DR .

History

In their youth, they dared to explore the ruins of Myth Drannor and, most impressively, lived to tell the tale. And indeed they did, for one member, Szuszalan, wrote of their exploits in her biography, Walking on a Swordblade , published in the Year of the Dragon, 1352 DR .
