Turmish black was a cheese made in Turmish in the Vilhon Reach in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Sword, 1365 DR , Sir Rivaldo was traveling in a coach along the Trade Way from the Way Inn to Waterdeep with an assortment of fine cheeses, including Turmish black.
