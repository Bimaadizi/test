Equarz ( pronounced : /ˈ ɛ k w ɑːr z / EH-kwarz ) was an elf and a member of the Zhentarim in Mulmaster .

History

Around 1491 DR , Equarz was based just outside of Mulmaster. He heard stories about a lost treasure at the bottom of the Lost Drop . When Lyrene began pestering him, wanting to join the Zhentarim, he said if she could recover the treasure he would help her. At last, an adverting party hired by Lyrene's sister Dylohra saved her from some strange monsters inside the lake.
