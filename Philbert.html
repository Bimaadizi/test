Philbert was a commoner human from the City of Coin, Athkatla in Amn who lived in the 14 th century DR .

History

Philbert met his demise in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR . He, along with several other men, was captured by a drow druid Pai'Na who built a massive spider nest in ancient tombs underneath Athkatla's Grave District . Philbert and other peasants were consumed from the inside by Pai'Na's spiders.
