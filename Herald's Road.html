The Herald's Road was a major trade route that traversed Impiltur , connecting the Royal Road to the Merchant's Run in the town of Ilmwatch .

Geography

This road spanned the three major regions of Impiltur, from the Easting Coast in the south, through the Uplands and north to Farwater . It had a major crossing in the strait that connected the Old Water and the Bluefang Water .
