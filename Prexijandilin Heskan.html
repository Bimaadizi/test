Prexijandilin Heskan was a dragonborn warrior member of Clan Prexijandilin , and a member of the Lance Defenders in 1487 DR .

History

Heskan was the field commander of the detachment of Lance Defenders from Djerad Kethendi that survived the siege of their city and were taken prisoners by Gilgeam during the First Tymanther-Unther War .

He and his companions were tortured and interrogated by Gilgeam about Djerad Thymar . When Heskan refused to yield, he was killed alongside all his companions by the God -King.
