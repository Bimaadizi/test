Talisovanar "Tally" Fellbranch ( pronounced : / t ɑː l ɪ s ˈ ɔː l v ɑː n ɑːr / tal-iss-AWL-van-ar ) was a half-elf carpenter and woodcarver in Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Tally managed the Bent Nail shop on Trollskull Alley in the North Ward .

Relationships

Melannor

Tally was brother to Melannor Fellbranch .
