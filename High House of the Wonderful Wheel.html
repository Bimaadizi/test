The High House of the Wonderful Wheel was a temple dedicated to Gond located in the city of Ordulin in 1358 DR . It was destroyed alongside Ordulin in 1374 DR , until the city was rebuilt in 1499 DR .

Inhabitants

The temple was run by High Priest Gondsman Chansibral Thjestnal . Chansibral was assisted by 22 other priests and 38 followers. With Nightal 1 Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the temple and its clergy were destroyed with the city in the planar rift to the Plane of Shadow opened by Sharran priestess Elyril Hraven .
