Ullu Lake was a lake in the Angalpuk region of the Great Glacier .

Description

Ullu Lake was a freshwater lake with an average depth of 5​ to ​20 feet (1.5​ to ​6.1 meters).

Geography

Ullu Lake was located in eastern Angalpuk next to the Angsaas Chain .

Flora & Fauna

The lake was a dangerous place because of the high number of polar bears , who frequently hunted for fish in the lake's waters.
