Darthen Nhalass II was the seventh Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Darthen II reigned from −700 DR to −691 DR , inheriting the throne upon the death of his father Shendar Nhalass .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Umathar V .
