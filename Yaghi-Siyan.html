Emir Yaghi-Siyan was the ruler of Delbyl during the mid 14th century DR .

Activities

The position of Emir of Delbyl was typically given to incompetent individuals, or as punishment from the bey. Yaghi-Siyan controlled all activity in the small backwater town of Delbyl. He was assisted by a civil watch made up of twelve maliks , or chiefs, and the men under their command.

Relationships

Yaghi-Siyan was the cousin of the wife of the Bey of Murghôm .
