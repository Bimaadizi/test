Flandro "Tarrasque-Biter" Grassley was an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate who died some years before the late 15 th century DR .

History

Flandro was a hunter who died sometime before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . He was buried in the Lower City Graveyard , next to the Hhune Mausoleum . The gravestone was etched with a single inscription: "Flandro 'Tarrasque-Biter' Grassley - The hunter hunted, the biter bit." The hunter's grave may had been accidentally desecrated, and the body risen as a zombie by Nina Dortmell , a talented child with a gift , who was trying to resurrect her recently deceased brother.
