The Gentle Moon was an inn within the walls of Westgate on the Dragon Coast as of 1358 DR .

Structure

The inn was in good condition and offered average prices.

Location

The Gentle Moon was found in the southeast section of the city.

Inhabitants

The owner of the inn was killed by members of a cult of Shar in the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR . After the Time of Troubles the owner's ghost began haunting the inn with increasing violence and driving away most staff and customers.
