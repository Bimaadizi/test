The Teshpost Trail was a small roadway in the Moonsea region that connected Teshwave with the Citadel of the Raven .

Description

The Teshpost Trail exited Teshwave on the city's north side, entering the Dragonspine Mountains near Mount Tesh where it joined the Zhentilar Trail just south of the Citadel of the Raven.
