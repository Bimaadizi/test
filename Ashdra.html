Ashdra was a drow in the Doomvault dungeon in 1486 DR .

History

Ashdra was one of the many Chosen captured by the Red Wizards of Thay and transported to the Doomvault dungeon for experiments. More specifically, she was the chosen of Ibrandul , a god long believed dead. Ashdra was freed from her chains by the heroes of Daggerford .
