Jhor the Bleeder was an enchanted bastard sword that caused profuse bleeding when it struck a person or creature.

Powers

The sword possessed a +2 enchantment . The wounds it inflicted would not heal properly as the sword itself prevented blood from clotting.

History

Jhor was wielded by Ho Mang , a skilled torturer who served various royal courts across Faerûn . At some point it was stolen by its previous owner and eventually taken to the city of Athkatla .

As of the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Jhor was available for sale within the Adventurer Mart .
