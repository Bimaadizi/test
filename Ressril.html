Ressril was a drow wizard and a participant in the infiltration of Scornubel until the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Abilities

He could cast spells.

Relationships

He worked for Sarltan .

History

He was one of the men standing with Sarltan when he was approached by the drow trader Iylinvyx, actually a disguised Dove Falconhand , in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR . After Sarltan decided to attack her, he called upon Ressril; however, Dove effectively neutralized him early in the fight, and later finished him off by landing both her knees on his jaw, almost certainly killing him.
