Torgga was a duergar looter who was active on the Sword Coast region during the late 15 th century DR . For a time, she operated under the command of Gimblebock .

Description

She had dark skin and white hair that she wore back in a braid.

Possessions

Torgga wore a suit of padded armor and carried with her a shortbow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Torgga and the rest of Gimblebock's gang ventured into an ancient crypt near the banks of the River Chionthar . She was among the more battle-hardened group that delved deepter into the crypt, while Gimblebock and a few of his lackeys laid claim to an alien vessel that crashed nearby.
