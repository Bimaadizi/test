Ralzala was a dao in Avernus , first layer of the Nine Hells , in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Ralzala made a pact with the archdevil Zariel in order to destroy a rival but found herself in servitude to the devil.

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Ralzala was assigned to watch over a demon zapper that imprisoned the unicorn Mooncolor . However, she was tired of servitude and was searching for someone that could help her to break the pact, possibly the former archdevil Bel .
