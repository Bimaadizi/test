Professor Skant was a professor orb , a sentient magical orb imbued with specialized academic knowledge.

Description

Professor Skant was a typical professor orb : a smooth sphere of smoky gray quartz weighing 5 pounds (2,300 grams) and approximately the size of a grapefruit . Inside, pinpricks of silver light gleamed faintly, betraying the arcane presence inside.

Personality

The orb exhibited the personality of a scholar—learned, talkative, and prone to condescension. It regarded all humanoids as intellectually inferior. Professor Skant's personality was lawful good .

Powers

As a professor orb , it was capable of speech and could see and hear out to a distance of 60 feet (18 meters). Professor Skant communicated in Common , Draconic , Elvish , and Loross .

Professor Skant was an expert in four specialized academic fields: Netherese history; vampires and their traits; the tarrasque ; and the rituals involving in producing, bottling, and consuming Elverquisst , a rare elven liquor.

Additionally, it could cast the mage hand cantrip as it willed, though it used this ability solely to maneuver itself.

History

It eventually came into the possession of the Harpells as a family heirloom and was used by Vellynne Harpell, a necromancer of the Arcane Brotherhood . During her visit to Icewind Dale in the Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR , Professor Skant was stolen by Nass Lantomir, a rival member of the Brotherhood, who then perished on the isle of Solstice . The orb was later recovered by adventurers and may have been returned to Vellynne. During subsequent events, Professor Skant served as a vital source of lore, particularly about the ancient city of Ythryn and Netherese civilization.
