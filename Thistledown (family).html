The Thistledown family was a human farming family who lived in the Silver Marches in the early 14 th century DR .

Organization

The family did honest work, living off the land. The three generations of the family lived in a secluded farm in the western outskirts of Maldobar .

History

In the Year of the Lion, 1340 DR , the renegade drow Drizzt Do'Urden had begun exploring the surface world. The human Thistledown family were the first good people he encountered since he escaped from the Underdark . He saw them as a peaceful and loving society, unlike his own family in Menzoberranzan . However, the family were under threat from the local gnolls , who'd tried to befriend Drizzt. But he saw their evil intentions and slaughtered them.

The gnolls had been sent by the barghest Ulgulu and when Ulgulu discovered his ploy was foiled by a drow , he hatched a plan to frame Drizzt for a massacre. He had his subordinate quickling Tephanis steal one of the drow's scimitars , shapeshifted in a form approximate to a drow and slayed as least two Thistledown humans, then left the scimitar under a bed, which led to the successful, albeit temporary, framing of Drizzt to the authorities. Drizzt's pursuers would come to realize his innocence and declared the Thistledowns avenged by the drow.
