Rak Alumeater was a gnome apothecary inhabiting Zhentil Keep 's Foreign Quarter during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Rak owned Rak's Apothecary on Zhent Avenue . He sold all kinds of potions , medicines, and spell components . He also sold poisons, but only after feeling out potential buyers first.

Though he was quite an accomplished alchemist , Rak was nearly insane from the constant exposure to dangerous chemicals. His antics and experiments drove all of his neighbors away.
