Laurel was a paladin in service of the Most Noble Order of the Radiant Heart in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Laurel was a human woman with blonde hair. She was adorned with a plate mail and carried a bastard sword with a shield .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , as the iron crisis threatened the Sword Coast, Laurel traveled coastal areas to the west of the town of Nashkel . There, Laurel stood against a gibberling infestation, seeking to end their threat. She spent two weeks nightly slaughtering waves of the attaching creatures. Her devotion to the code of law and goodly morals led Laurel to attack any evildoer or criminal that came across her path.
