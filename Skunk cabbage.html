Skunk cabbage was a common plant, useful as a part of certain magic spells .

Availability

Skunk cabbage was among items available through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue 's Wizard 's Laboratory section. Each cabbage head was sold for mere 3 copper pieces .

Usages

Skunk cabbage's main use was as a material component for the stinking cloud spell.
