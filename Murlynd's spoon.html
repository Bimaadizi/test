Murlynd's spoon was a type of enchanted spoon capable of creating a nourishing gruel .

Description

These utensils were usually fashioned from horns.

Powers

When placed within a smaller empty container, Murlynd's spoon would create enough gruel to fill the container. The gruel was warm and relatively flavorless, but quite nourishing. The spoon could create enough gruel to feed up to four human -sized individuals for the entire day.

History

Specialty priests of Waukeen called goldeyes had a chance of receiving a Murlynd's spoon as one of the magic items they were given upon their admission to the clergy.
