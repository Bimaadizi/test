The Street of the Craftsmen was a prominent avenue located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The street was located within the Pyraculum near the center of the city.

Description

The Street of the Craftsmen branched southwest from the Edible Bazaar , terminating eventually on the Street of Stelae one block northeast of the Munhani Canal . It crossed the Albrazi Canal near its midpoint.
