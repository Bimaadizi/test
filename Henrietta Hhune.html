Henrietta Hhune was the daughter of Hurlbut Hhune of the Hhune patriar family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Henrietta Hhune became romantically involved with handsome Manip Leythim of the Flaming Fist . Despite their tryst, Henrietta was already betrothed to Derque Rillyn of the Rillyn patriar family, a man of a notoriously poor attitude. Henrietta's father, Hurlbut, organized Leythim to be beaten up by a group of thugs to scare away the manip's affections and secure Henrietta's highborn marriage. Leythim accepted the physical threat and the marriage, being afraid of any blowback against Henrietta.
