Haleera Shundyl was a sorceress who was active several centuries before the mid–14 th century DR .

Haleera fought the infamous "spellslinger" Durshult the Mage in a sorcerous duel in Baldur's Gate . Victorious, she polymorphed Durshult into the humiliating form of a wingless wonder . For some decades after, it became a fad among mages to trap their defeated foes in wonder form.
