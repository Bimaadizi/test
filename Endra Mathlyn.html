Endra "Watchever" Mathlyn was a human woman of the city of Procampur in the Vast .

She was a priestess of Helm and was High Guardian of the Tower of the Eye temple by 1358 DR , a position which she still held by 1367 DR . She had 18 subordinate priests and 24 lay-followers.
