Hothemer Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Hothemer .

Location

The Hothemer Villa was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep , on side of Aveen Street and Suldown Street . It stood down the road from the Wands Villa .

Structure

Like many Waterdhavian villas, this was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden. Inside the walls of the estate were two high-quality buildings that both stood three stories tall, one being the mansion proper and the other a guesthouse.
