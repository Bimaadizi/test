Iyleth Lloodrun was a Zhentarim mageling in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was a thin man, known to dress in brown silks. He spoke nasally and sounded supercilious.

Personality

Storm Silverhand judged him to be insecure.

Abilities

He knew at least one spell to mind ream .

Activities

He worked for Calivar Murpeth .

History

Iyleth Lloordun was at Warmfires House in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , during the revel in which Maervidal Iloster was to be killed. After the scrivener escaped instead of being eliminated, Calivar Murpeth's men captured a woman, the escort Talantha , for questioning. Iyleth was called on to mind ream her, but he found her to be warded. Calivar bade him continue and push forward at all costs. But Talantha was actually the Chosen of Mystra Storm Silverhand in spell-guise; trying to pry into her again caused deadly feedback on him.
