History

Sarlor was guarding Orthil's caravan in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , when it was joined by Shandril Shessair and Narm Tamaraith . He was assigned briefly to keep watch on Shandril; when she demanded to see Narm after he was injured during a bandit raid, he protested that they didn't follow her orders. The situation would've likely ended in a violent confrontation if it weren't for Arauntar 's timely intervention.
