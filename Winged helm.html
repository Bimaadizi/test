Winged helms were enchanted helmets that made its wearer fleet of foot in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

These helms boosted the wearer's speed and subtly improved their intelligence.

History

In the 15 th century DR, winged helms could be found among bandits and heroes of the Sword Mountains , in the city of Neverwinter , the cultists of a beholder eye of flame who laired in the Crags , and in the hands of the vampire lord Ozit from the ruins of Illefarn .
