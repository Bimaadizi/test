The Calling was a mysterious evil that plagued Kara-Tur in the late 12 th century DR .

History

Sometime circa the Year of the Hoary Host, 1172 DR , the being only known as The Calling targeted Azaki guild in Kara-Tur. The Calling enthralled a minor attendant, part of the Azaki assassins ' guild, and in exchange for uplifting the attendant's family, it used the human's body to house its own soul. Now infiltrating the guild, The Calling sowed chaos, generating numerous copies of enchanted shurikens called Many Talons , seemingly at will. Eventually, The Calling's attendant body was slain by an unnamed sohei .
