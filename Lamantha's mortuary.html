Lamantha's mortuary was a business owned by the necromancer Lamantha that provided mortician and taxidermy services to the undead inhabitants of Evernight in the Shadowfell .

Location

It was located north of the river of lava that flowed through Evernight.

Services

As a mortician and taxidermist, much of Lamantha's work involved preparing bodies (or parts of bodies) as decorations, sporting equipment, tools, or trophies for her undead clientele. She was also involved in preparing living humanoids to be killed and eaten by the undead of Evernight in ritualistic " funerals " held in the Graveyard . This included sedating the victim with a powerful paralytic (derived from the humors of ghouls ), then cleaning the victim, dressing them in fine clothes, and delivering them to the Temple of Filth .

Lamantha also provided "healing" to injured undead as well as cosmetic services to make them appear to be alive.
