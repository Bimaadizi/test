Yalanilue was a maid of house Alastrarra of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

In 241 DR , she was dancing off on a corner of the house. According to the selu'kiira of Iymbryl Alastrarra , he'd seen her dance like that once, except unclad.
