Himeji was a samurai captain of the Korimori clan in Maeshi Province in Kozakura circa Kozakuran Year 1433 (1359 DR) .

Relationships

Himeji was a lieutenant of Ogaki , the second-in-command of Lord Korimori .

Himeji and rival lieutenant Sakigahara disliked each other and both wanted Ogaki's position if he was promoted after their lord's victory.

History

In 1433 SY, Ogaki, Himeji, and Sakigahara led a bushi army to a village formally of the Michimori clan . There, however, an adventuring party hired by Michimori Uemon to retrieve the Seven Swords may have fought and defeated them and their troops.
