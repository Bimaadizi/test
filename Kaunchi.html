Kaunchi was an Oigur guide living in the Hordelands during the mid 14 th century DR .

Personality

Kaunchi was a thoroughly wicked individual who was dangerous and deceitful. He was adept at masking his true nature from others. Easily insulted, Kaunchi viewed any form of reprimand as a direct challenge. He looked down upon anyone who was not a nomad of the steppes and felt no bond of honor or duty to them.

Activities

He earned a living as a guide through the Hordelands, though this was usually a ruse to lure caravans into traps set by his fellow Oigur.
