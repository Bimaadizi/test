A phylactery of long years was a magic item that greatly slowed the natural aging process of the wearer.

Powers

This item would slow the natural aging process of the wearer by one quarter so long as it was worn. Thus if worn for a full year the wearer would only age nine months. A phylactery of long years would also slow magical aging.
