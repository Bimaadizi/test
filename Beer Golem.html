The Beer Golem was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood two stories tall. The interior of the tavern contained a fireplace on the far wall and a kitchen with its own service entrance.

Location

The Beer Golem was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter . The front of the tavern sat along Kerrigan's Court while the west side of the building touched Snake Alley .
