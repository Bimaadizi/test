Radiance of Tyr were enchanted helmets of the Tyrran faith in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Radiance of Tyr helmets were used as ceremonial pieces of armor. Nayeli Goldflower's helm was covered in dents and scrapes that she repaired by hand, evident of her dedication to her god and battle experience.

Powers

These enchanted helmets gave their wearer certain protection on the battlefield and made them stand out as a beacon of hope as well as a warning to the enemies. The prominence of these helmets often caused the wearers to be targeted first on the battlefield.
