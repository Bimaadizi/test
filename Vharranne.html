Vharranne was a ranger and thug from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Vharranne was a human woman with a light skin tone, light brown eyes and long blonde hair. She wore a simple set of leathers and armed herself with a dagger and a longbow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Vharranne was a member of a gang assembled by Churg Elvek , secretly a cultist of the Absolute , who sought to strengthen its grasp of Baldur's Gate. When the famed writer Volothamp Geddarm returned to the Gate after misadventures in the Emerald Grove and coming face-to-face with the Absolute's army, Volo became a target for Churg and his gang. The master traveler was captured by Vharranne and the rest of the thugs and tied to a stack of smokepowder barrels, ready to be "brought to justice" for being an alleged agent of the Absolute at the hands of the gang and street gawkers. When a band known as the Absolute Adversaries interrupted the street execution, Vharranne, Churg Elvek, and the rest of the gang attacked the adventurers but were defeated.
