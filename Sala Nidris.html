Sala Nidris was a Calishite merchant in Neverwinter .

History

Sala migrated from Calimshan to live in Neverwinter with her son Zan Nidris . In 1479 DR , she became one of the targets of the kidnappers that plagued the city, but she was saved by an adventuring party. However, soon after, she discovered that the kidnappers had captured her son Zan and hired the adventurers to find him

After defeating an Ashmadai cell, the adventurers freed Zan and returned him to his mother
