Dzaan was a former Red Wizard of Thay and later member of the Arcane Brotherhood of Luskan in the late 15 th century DR .

History

As a Red Wizard of Thay, he studied the arcane art of illusion but later moved west to the Sword Coast North , where he joined the Arcane Brotherhood. They accepted him with open arms, being eager to gain a Red Wizard among their ranks. Dzaan could have been a great asset to the Brotherhood. However, he went north to Icewind Dale where he was seemingly burned to death by the people of Easthaven after slaughtering an adventuring party. Despite his apparent death, Dzaan had cast the mighty simulacrum spell, and had kept a copy of himself at a lost Netherese spire within the Dale. This construct stayed with Dzaan's previous bodyguard, Krintaas, a wight that has accompanied him from Thay.

Adventurers would eventually find this Simulacrum and potentially assist it come to life and become real with a lost piece of Netherese technology.

Personality

Abilities

Dzaan was capable of casting arcane spells .

Possessions

He had a scroll of fireball in a copper tube and a gray bag of tricks .

Relationships

Although their relationship was minor, Dzaan trusted his Wight bodyguard Krintaas to protect his vital Simulacrum. Having brought him from Thay, the Wight was his only real ally in the Dale, and seemingly possessed a significant amount of loyalty to his master beyond his enslavement as a Wight, as he was determined to do whatever it took to ensure the Simulacrum of Dzaan obtained a life-spark upon learning of his master's demise.
