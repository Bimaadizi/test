Cloaks of protection versus evil were magic cloaks that proved increased defense against evil-aligned creatures.

Powers

Cloaks of protection versus evil and their stronger version, greater cloaks of protection versus evil both gave the wearer enchanted protection of +2 and +5 enchantment respectively.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , the most common source of the cloaks of protection versus evil came from Waterdeep . Khelben Arunsun spent months training one of his student in creation of these cloaks to supply the City Watch with defensive and fashionable magic armor. Eventually that apprentice opened a shop where he started experimenting with designs and stiles of the cloaks of protection versus evil .
