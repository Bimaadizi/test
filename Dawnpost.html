The Dawnpost was a prominent roadway found in Sembia that connected Daerlun in the west with Ordulin in the east.

Description

Originating in Daerlun, the Dawnpost branched off northeast of the Way of the Manticore , passing through the cities and towns of Kulta , Saerb , and Archenbridge before reaching Ordulin. The Dawnpost crossed the River Arkhen near its midway point.
