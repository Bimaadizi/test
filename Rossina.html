Rossina was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Rossina was a forest gnome with a pale skin tone, icy blue eyes, and long straight brown hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Rossina and several other citizens of Baldur's Gate were randomly attacked by a gang sahuagin who emerged from the Gray Harbor as Rossina was strolling along the shore. Serendipitously, a band of adventurers known as the Absolute Adversaries were nearby as the attack commenced. Were she to survive, Rossina would note that being attacked by sahuagin was not the best date she's ever had, but not the worst either.
