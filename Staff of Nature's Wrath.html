The Staff of Nature's Wrath was an enchanted staff created by a group of militant Silvanites .

Powers

This enchanted druidic staff possessed a +1 enchantment and allowed the wilder to cast the flame strike spell as well as entangle with 12 feet (3.7 meters) radius. The wielder was only restricted by their visa. range to cast the staff's spells.

History

The Staff of Nature's Wrath was crafted by a militant group of druids of Silvanus. The enchanted weapon was used to clobber the enemies of the forest, dispense nature's punishment, and protect the dignity of druidic orders. The main targets of the Staff of Nature's Wrath , however, were the clerics of Malar and his fellow Gods of Fury .

By 1281 DR , the Staff of Nature's Wrath was in the hands of Arundel , the archdruid of Kuldahar on the Spine of the World . Later that year, Arundel was slain by Belhifet , and his enchanted staff was picked up by the heroes of Icewind Dale .
