Farene was a merchant in Westgate in the mid-late 14 th century DR . She was specialized in selling elegant cosmetics from far-away countries.

Description

Farene was notorious in the city for her excessive flirting and gambling. She was known for arming herself with poison -laced needles, hidden within her lavish clothes.
