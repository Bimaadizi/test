The Highlands were a mountain range located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Highlands were a prominent feature in central Gwynneth, stretching for over 75 miles (120 kilometers) and forming most of the southern border of Myrloch Vale . The Highlands were separated from Aspenheight in the west by the Aspenheight Gap , but the Highlands ran into the Cambro Mountains in the east at Synnoria .
