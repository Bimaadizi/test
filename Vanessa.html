Vanessa was a human templar stationed at Camp Vengeance in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

She had slightly tanned skin with freckles, and brownish red hair. Her eyes were green.

History

Vanessa had a relationship with the tabaxi bard Birdsong . They had to meet in secrecy, as Vanessa's commanders would not allow such trysts by soldiers posted in the camp. During a Grand Revel in the late 15 th century DR, Vanessa asked a group of adventurers to find out what had happened to her lover. The adventurers found that Birdsong had been captured by Nanny Pu'pu in Mbala , and managed to free the tabaxi.
