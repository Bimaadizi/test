Hasoctl was a human fighter in Maztica .

History

Hasoctl gained everything in life thanks to his connection with his great-uncle, the Revered Counselor Caxal . He entered into the service of Lord Drakosa but secretly worked for Caxal.

In 1362 DR , he was tasked with accompanying an adventuring party hired by Drakosa to find the Cloak of One Plume . In truth, he would deliver it instead to his great-uncle.

Personality

Hasoctl was always worried about something: his health, his wealth, the respect of others, the place of the gods in the universe, etc. At heart, he was a coward, always escaping from danger. However, he considered himself a practical philosopher, particularly in dealing with others.
