Aluminum was an elemental material found in the Realms .

Properties

Aluminum was a silvery-white metal, appearing very similar to platinum . It was extremely light for a metal, and in its pure form weighed 170 pounds (77 kilograms) per 1 cubic foot (28 liters).

Availability

In 3520 NY (−339 DR) , the Elemental Store of Karsus Enclave sold pure elemental aluminum for 3 gp per 1 ounce (28 grams).

Occurrences

Spinel gems were made up of crystalline manganese oxide and aluminum.
