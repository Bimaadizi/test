Hulvaarn Farm was a turnip and potato farm in Nightstone .

Description

This 1,600 ft² (150 m²) turnip and potato farm was owned by the Hulvaarns , a human farming family. The square farm was 40 ft (12 m) on both sides. Attached to the farm's northern fence was the Hulvaarn family's homestead.

Geography

Hulvaarn Farm was located to the east of Nightstone, directly east of the trading post and south east of the main square.

History

In the late 15 th century DR , cloud giant invaders bombarded Nightstone. During the raid, a rock plummeted into the farm and instantly killed Nestor Hulvaarn .
