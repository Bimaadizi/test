Glimmergad's Selgaunt fiddle was a unique fiddle that once belonged to Fiddlesticks Glimmergad of Selgaunt in Sembia sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

History

The instrument was created sometime before the late 15 th century DR, when it was used by an infamous gnome bard Fiddlesticks Glimmergad. According to the stories, Fiddlesticks used his fiddle to play such tear-jerking ballads, bank guards never realized that the gnome's associates were emptying the vaults.

By Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , this unique but mundane instrument was sold at the Chromatic Scale musical instrument shop located on the second floor of The Glitter Gala in Baldur's Gate . It was among several unique instruments in Thomas C. Quirkilious 's inventory.
