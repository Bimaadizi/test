Description

The main road leading up to the House of Many Tomes was surrounding by serene grasslands and well-manicured trees. The temple itself featured a number of columns, massive front doors and a massive stained-glass window that resembled Oghma's holy symbol .

Just outside the entrance, beyond the central walkway, was a single-tiered amphitheater.
