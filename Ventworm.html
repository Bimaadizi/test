Ventworms were a species of aquatic deepwater worms that inhabited the proximity of the thermal vents in the Sea of Moving Ice in Icewind Dale .

Description

They were small red worms that clustered around the thermal vents, thriving in the noxious gases released from the underground. Ventworms were among a very small number of animals that could survive such an inhospitable environment.
