Activities

They acted as spies and assassins for anyone with the coin to hire them. They were not involved in politics and were not supporters of Prince Nuyen Phi , ruler of Sayan. They cared only that they could move freely across the land to ply their dark trade. They were the most active such group in the realm.

Base of Operations

The Sung operated all across Laothan, but remained based in the seaport city of Sayan . They were also known to found in the floating city of Tran , a place where killers could be hired cheaply.

They would even conduct their espionage in the neighboring Kuong Kingdom , where they often posed as merchants.

History

Of Shou heritage, they originated in T'u Lung , but established themselves in Sayan in Laothan.
