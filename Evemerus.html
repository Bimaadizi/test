Evemerus was a Netherese arcanist who worked at the Netheril Mint in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Alongside Elgiva , Evemerus led the Mint's team of wizards in producing the magical anti-counterfeiting prismatic effects on the coinage.
