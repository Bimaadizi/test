Falwan Underbough was a community leader in Cormanthor in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Falwan was a famous baker in Hillsfar , known among nobility for his "half cakes" until the reintroduction of the First Great Law of Humanity by Torin Nomerthal forced Falwan and his family to escape the city. He took refuge in Cormanthor with many other non-human refugees. In time, they formed a small community and Falwan somehow became their leader and spokesman.

Circa 1486 DR , he was afraid of numerous drow arriving on the surface in the area.

Personality

Falwan was very proud of his work as a baker and suffered for the loss of his business. He was a friendly individual but experience made him cautious toward armed humans .

Relationship

Falwan had good dealings with Elanil Elassidil and the leaders of Elventree .
