The Masked Marquis was a legendary pirate who terrorized the Dragon Reach long before the late 14 th century DR .

History

By the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the Masked Marquis was believed to had left a treasure hidden somewhere on the rocky islet of Two Rocks in the harbor of Harrowdale . Despite the stories, not a single coin from the pirate booty had ever been found.
