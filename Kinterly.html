Kinterly ran a magic shop in Zhentil Keep in 1357 DR .

Activities

Kinterly ran Kinterly's Magics , one of the few recognized magic shops in the Realms . Usually he tried to buy genuine magic items while claiming they were mundane, and sold almost-empty and non-functional magic items as if they were full-powered.

Relationship

Kinterly had two fire giant assistants and a very good relationship with the Zhentil Keep guard.
