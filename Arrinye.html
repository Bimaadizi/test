Fist Arrinye was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Arrinye had a fair complexion with long light chestnut hair. She wore a set of leather armor as a part of her Flaming Fist uniform and was armed with a deadly heavy crossbow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Arrinye was stationed within the barracks of the Heapside district of Baldur's Gate, where she, a steel watcher , and several other Flaming Fists guarded a small dungeon prison.
