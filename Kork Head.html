Kork Head was a headland on the east coast of the island of Moray in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

It sat above Kork Bay at the narrowest point of the Strait of the Leviathan . This made it an ideal location for the use of signal fires to communicate between Moray and the island of Gwynneth across the water.
