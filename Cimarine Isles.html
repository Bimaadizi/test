The Cimarine Isles were an archipelago located in the Sea of Swords off the Sword Coast west of Candlekeep .

Inhabitants

Circa 1479 DR , they were populated by watersoul genasi and trade settlements from Amn . The city of Branestria on the western island, inhabited by watersoul genasi and partially submerged, traded with Baldur's Gate and some smaller settlements.
