Iku-In was a structure located in the heart of the city of Semmishi in Wa .

History

Iku-In was originally constructed as a memorial shrine, dedicated to lives lost in a great fire that occurred in Wa Year 1674 (1256 DR) .

Over time, as Semmishi became a city of vice, Iku-In fell into disrepair. By Wa Year 1775 (1357 DR) , the structure was being used to host wrestling matches.
