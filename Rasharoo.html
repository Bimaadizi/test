Rasharoo was a myconid leader of a Circle in Neverlight Grove in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Rasharoo was the leader of the small Circle of Explorers in Neverlight Grove, exploring the Underdark more often than its fellow. When, with the Rage of Demons, Zuggtmoy 's will twisted most of the Circle, Rasharoo secretly started making plans to escape. Thanks to the cooperation of a party of adventurers escaping from drow slavers of Velkynvelve , Basidia led Rasharoo, Loobamub , and their followers out of the Grove before was too late

Personality

Always a loyal follower of Basidia, Rasharoo's knowledge of the outside world permitted it to more clearly notice the signs of madness in Phylo and its followers. Because of this, it only trusted Basidia .
