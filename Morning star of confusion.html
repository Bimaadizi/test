Morning stars of confusion were enchanted bludgeons of unassuming appearance.

Description

The weapon consisted of a wooden shaft and a spiked golden head. It was around 4 feet (1.2 meters) long. The golden head was magically hardened to avoid the metal's malleability.

Powers

These morning stars were enhanced with a +1 enchantment that aided the wielder's hand, guiding it towards the target as well as improving the punishment dealt. Additionally, they had a chance of rendering the target creature confused and disoriented for a short period of time.

History

One morning star of confusion could be found in the top levels of the Dragon's Eye caverns on the Spine of the World circa the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR .
