The House of Spires was a temple devoted to Loviatar , the Maiden of Pain, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

The House of Spires rose to prominence rapidly during the mid–14 th century DR, thanks mostly to the efforts of the temple's high priestess.

Inhabitants

Queen of Torment Chalathra Nyndra served as high priestess of the House of Spires during this time.
