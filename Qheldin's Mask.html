Qheldin's Mask was a small river hamlet located half-way between Scornubel and Berdusk on the River Chionthar .

Geography

It stood on the eastern bank of the Chionthar .

Locations

Qheldin was once the home of Six Spanglestars a fine inn that burned down and was never rebuilt.
