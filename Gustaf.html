Gustaf was a human who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Gustaf was a rival of his nearly identical twin brother, Rodrik . Gustaf was considered to be the "evil" brother.

Activities

As of 1479 DR , Gustaf was a regular of the Beached Leviathan . He was a purveyor of stolen goods and rare magic , taking advantage on the tavern's popularity to make contacts for his illicit deals. Gustaf usually disguised himself as his brother to thwart Rodrik's plans to make him return to "the good path". He never raised his sword against his brother, however.

History

In 1479 DR, Gustaf located an entrance to Gauntlgrym . He sold that information to the Ashmadai .
