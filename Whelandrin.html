Whelandrin was a servant in Suzail , Cormyr , in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Whelandrin was an older man.

Personality

He gave off an impassible appearance, and took care to stay expressionless.

Activities

Whelandrin was a trusty under the nobleman Marlin Stormserpent 's roof in Suzail. He was not above accepting money from elsewhere.

History

In Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , after the blueflame ghosts that Marlin Stormserpent summoned completed their first assignment for him successfully, Marlin called on one of his trusties, Whelandrin, to find him a wench for enjoyment. Whelandrin returned with the coinlass Amarune Whitewave , but he also allowed inside Arclath Delcastle , another nobleman, who bribed the trusty with at least one gold piece to be able to talk to Marlin.
