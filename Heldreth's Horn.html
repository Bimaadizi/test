Heldreth's Horn was a fortress in Western Athalantar during the 3 rd century DR .

Location

The Horn was on the west of Athalantar. It was the outermost fortress in the realm.

Activities

Forces garrisoned at Heldreth's Horn protected loggers and farmers near the Horn Hills , where banditry was rampant.

History

This led to a number of ambushes around the Year of the Loremasters, 228 DR , where brigands targeted groups of soldiers coming back from a nearby festhall.

After a patrol came across an outlaw fortress, the Lawless Castle , a number of outlaws, including Eladar the Dark (actually an incognito prince Elminster Aumar ) tried to pursue the ones that rode back to Heldreth's Horn to prevent them from spreading word of the location.
