Dualdon's Wake was a small but important cargo ship from the town of Targos in Icewind Dale , active in the early 14 th century DR .

Description

Dualdon's Wake was a long two-masted cargo ship with its name proudly inscribed on the hull.

History

Circa the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , the Dualdon's Wake sailed the waters of Maer Dualdon and the Shaengarne River . When Targos became besieged by the goblin and orc forces of the Legion of the Chimera in Mirtul of the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , Dualdon's Wake was used to transport lumber from Lonelywood to Targos for the construction of defensive palisades. The ship was targeted by goblin arsonists trying to cripple Targos by destroying its docks, but the invaders were slaughtered by a group of adventurers from Luskan , and the ship was defended by a member of the Targos Guard named Crandall .
