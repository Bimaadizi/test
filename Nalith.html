Nalith was a merchant of Hastarl in Athalantar in the early 3 rd century DR .

Personality

He was not patient with people who bandied words, even when he should really know better.

Possessions

He was known to own a warehouse.

History

Nalith was a guest at Havilyn 's party in the Year of the Black Flame, 229 DR , where the Magelord Neldryn Hawklyn , displeased with Nalith's impatience, made a display of casting an illusion on his dinner showing him the head of the last man to try and steal from his warehouse. Neldryn used the Crystal Chain of Binding shortly afterwards to try and capture Magister Dorgon Heamiiolothtar , which pretty much brought the party down.
