Brabras was a bullyblade for Lord Traevyn Illance in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Brabras looked impassible.

Relationships

Another bullyblade he worked with was Rorn .

Activities

The man was a bullyblade for Traevyn Illance .

History

Brabras was one of the bullyblades protecting Traevyn Illance when the latter met with Morligul Downdagger , who had just captured Rauligus Helderstone (actually Mirt the Moneylender) for him. Unknown for him, they were being pursued by the fugitive wizard Elminster Aumar ; Brabras was quickly disabled after the mage conjured a veil of darkness around him, then enspelled him into a deep sleep.

After his master found him sleeping, Elminster, then impersonating Morligul Downdagger, told him that Brabras had been laid low by a wench, and that Rorn had gone off to chase her.
