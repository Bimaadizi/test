The cragwardens were defenders of the ancient dwarven stronghold of Highhome on Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles . These dwarves were accordingly known by the title of Cragwarden .

History

Following the return of the fey realm of Sarifal to Gwynneth in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , evil fey would subsequently attack the local dwarves, forcing them to all but abandon the island and flee through the Dwarvenhome Tunnels . By the late 15 th century DR , only a small retinue of cragwardens remained in Highhome as the last guardians of their ancient fastness.
