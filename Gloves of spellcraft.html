Gloves of spellcraft were enchanted gloves that fortified the wearer's ability to weave spells .

Powers

These magic gloves allowed the wearer to be able to weave and recognize complex spells during magic battles. They were designed to be used by wizards , sorcerers , druids , and clerics . Two stronger variants were also created: greater gloves of spellcraft and epic gloves of spellcraft .

History

Gloves of spellcraft were developed by the Red Wizards of Thay as a tool to stave off the expansion of the Mulhorandi theocracy. These gloves were a personal project of several Red Wizards who remained unnamed. They assisted the magocracy of Unther by providing them with gloves of spellcraft . Despite the gloves being useful, Mulhorand still dominated the struggle because of its dramatically higher numbers.
