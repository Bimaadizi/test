Street of the Tusks was a street of hard-packed earth and gravel in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This road began at Burnt Wagon Way and ran south to the High Road , crossing Spendthrift Alley and Vellarr's Lane along the way.

Notable Locations

Links to Sewers

An entrance to the sewers of Waterdeep was located at the mouth of a dead-end alley opening that was just east off this street.
