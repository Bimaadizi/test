Windpennant Pillars was a townhouse in the North Ward of Waterdeep in the 14 th century DR .

Location

The townhouse was in the middle of a row of business shops. There was a large, grand mansion behind it.

Structure

The building sprawled over the other businesses. Inside, there were numerous offices, and a stable that connected to a manor behind it through a large iron door.

Activities

The place was for rent.

Defenses

The knocker on the door was trapped, making a planter fall on top of anyone who used it.
