Dhag Greybeard was the Herald of both High Horn keep and the town of Waymoot in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

While residing at High Horn, Dhag served under Lord Commander Thursk Dembarron . At Waymoot, he served under Lord Filfar Woodband .

Circa 1367 DR , Dhag was actively training a bard named Alatha Korduis to succeed him as herald of Waymoot, since he held two positions concurrently at that time. She assisted him in his role at that town.
