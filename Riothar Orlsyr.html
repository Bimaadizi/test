Riothar Orlsyr was an innkeeper of The Wizard's Hand in the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the late 13 th century DR .

History

Following the burning down of a prior inn to the south, The Three Dead Orcs , Riothar Orlsyr built The Wizard's Hand some time around 1277 DR .

Relations

His son was Lhullbannen Orlsyr , who was running The Wizard's Hand by 1357 DR .
