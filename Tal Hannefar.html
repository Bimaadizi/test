Tal Hannefar was a town guard who lived in the settlement of Idyllglen .

History

Tal was present in Idyllglen when Yeenoghu assaulted the settlement with his demonic horde. [ as of when? ] During the battle, Tal and the other guards bravely defended their fellow citizens, but were covered in demon ichor . This caused them to go insane and crave the flesh of humanoids .
