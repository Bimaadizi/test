Fasim Kaldur was the leader of the Riders of Blood in 1367 DR .

Activities

As the leader of the Riders of Blood, Fasim coordinated attacks upon caravans traveling between Halwa and Huzuz .

Skills

Fasim was quite adept at throwing his magical jambiya .

Possessions

Fasim owned a magical jambiya he named the Fate maker , a set of ring mail of the desert evening , a magical shield, a ring of regeneration , and a ring of genie summoning .

His personal mount was named the Whirlwind .
