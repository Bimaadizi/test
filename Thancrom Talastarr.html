Thancrom Talastarr was the high priest of Stormhaven House , a temple dedicated to Talos , in Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR .

Activities

Thancrom was the leader of Talos's faithful in Urmlaspyr. His congregation within the temple consisted of four specialty priests called stormlords and three followers.
