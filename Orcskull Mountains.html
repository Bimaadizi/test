The Orcskull Mountains were a mountain range located on the island of Moray within the Moonshae Isles .

Description

The Orcskulls were a curved line of jagged peaks and glaciers with dry upland meadows ideal for pastureland. These meadows were full of wildflowers —including buttercups , columbines , and lavender —while the slopes were covered in gorse and juniper . Most of the vales were barren and hosted dens and holdfasts of orcs and giants .

Geography

The mountains covered the southern tip of Moray. They were bordered on the east by the Strait of the Leviathan , and by the Trackless Sea to the south and west. The Shannyth River flowed north from the center of the Orcskull Mountains into central Moray.

Inhabitants

While at one time the upland meadows were home to Northlander farmers and ranchers famed for raising long-haired cattle , by the late 15 th century DR , the mountains were ruled by various tribes of orcs, ogres , giants, and other monstrous humanoids .
