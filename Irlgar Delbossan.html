Delbossan was a stable master in Espar in 1357 DR .

History

Delbossan was a valiant adventurer and thief active along the trade route linking the Inner Sea lands with the Sword Coast . In time, he stole so many horses and acted as horse-tender for so many caravans that he actually learned how to train horses.

He decided to settle in Espar and become the assistant of the old stable-master, Heuran . After Heuran's death, Delbossan succeeded him as the new stable-master.

Activities

Delbossan was the stable-master of Lord Hezom .

Personality

Delbossan was a taciturn man with a quiet competence in the training and care of horses.
