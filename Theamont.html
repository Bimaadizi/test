Theamont was a satyr who dwelled within the Chondalwood forest during the late 15 th century DR . He was one among the group living in the Chondalwood referred to as the "forest children".

Description

He was rather tall for a satyr, with a narrow but rugged-looking face.

History

In Nightal of the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Theamont encountered three humans traveling through his forest, one of whom was the chosen of the goddess Sune . At the behest of the Forest Queen Mielikki , Theamont escorted them through the forest along the Forest Way .
