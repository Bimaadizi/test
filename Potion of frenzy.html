Potions of frenzy were magic concoctions that allowed the imbiber to enter a state of rage, similar to that of a berserker barbarian .

Description

These potions were vials of swirling red liquid.

Powers

Consumption of a potion of frenzy was similar in effect to the blood frenzy spell. The imbiber entered a state of battle berserker rage. Their strength and stamina increased, as well as their mental defenses, however the state of frenzy made them easier targets for successful strikes.
