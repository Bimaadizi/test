A hammock was a type of suspended bedding.

Description

A hammock was a sort of blanket woven through with sturdy cords such that it could be hung above the ground between two vertical supports, most commonly two trees.

Hammocks were typically made of hemp or linen and weighed about 2 pounds (0.91 kilograms).

By the late 14 th century DR , the cost for someone in Faerûn to purchase an imported hammock was a silver piece .

History

The hammock was invented on the continent of Maztica .

Notable Users

Hammocks were widely popular among sailors and rangers .
