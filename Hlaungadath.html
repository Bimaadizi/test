Hlaungadath was a ruined former flying city of Netheril lying in the desert of northwestern Anauroch . The city landed near the High Ice and was eventually abandoned, but the ruins remained largely intact.

Geography

It was some 40 to 50 miles from the Nether Mountains , east of Ascore in the Frozen Sea south of the High Ice.

Hlaungadath was the destination of a hidden portal in the Dangling Tower in Ched Nasad .

A two-way portal in Hlaungadath designed for travel to the dwarven city Ascore had additional keys to a destination in the now ruins of Anauria , to a site in the High Moor that was once within the elven kingdom of Miyeritar , to a tree in northern Myth Drannor , and to the Astral Plane .

Description

Hlaungadath's architecture was clearly Netherese , with square column bases and pointed arched windows. The city was filled with sand-covered ruined domes, colonnades, ramparts, and towers.

Inhabitants

The ruins of the city were inhabited by lamias with their thralls: manticores , asabi , and gargoyles .

Legends & Rumors

The Bedine believed some kind of evil lived in the place, and over the years multiple tribes occupied then abandoned it due to the evil they found in their dreams but could not describe.
