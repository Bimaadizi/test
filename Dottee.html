Dottee was a teamster's wife in Hillsfar in the Moonsea in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa 1486 DR , Dottee announced to Wittel that she was pregnant and they decided to go for a trip in the Cormanthor forest. There, a family of jackalweres ambushed them: Wittel was left unconscious and Dottee taken. An adventuring party however saved her.
