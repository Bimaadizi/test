Cats were a variety of feline animals of various sizes.

Varieties

Ecology

Halruaan mages believed that cats were attracted to spellcasting. Folk in the region considered that fact to be a well-known and obvious truth.

Thomas C. Quirkilious of the Chromatic Scale instrument shop in Baldur's Gate experimented with using catgut to make lute strings. The experiment was a failure, despite being better than sheepgut . The main problem was sourcing the cat innards as such inquiries resulted in death threats.

Felines

Felines were animals that were related to cats.

Catlike creatures

There were a number of non-feline creatures that resembled cats in various ways.

Reputation

Many deities favored cats. The Mulhorandi god Anhur and the god Malar were associated with hunting cats. The elven god Solonor Thelandira also favored the great cats. Both Azuth and Savras favored gray-colored cats in particular, while Bane and Vhaeraun favored black ones. Mask favored either of those colors. Brown cats were sacred to the halfling goddess Cyrrollalee , and calico cats to Deneir , Milil , and Savras . Striped tabbies were considered holy to Eilistraee 's worshipers, but white cats were special to followers of Mystra . Rare gold-furred cats were considered a blessing from the dwarven god Vergadain . Sharess was considered a cat deity, and she had a particular association with tressym. Hellcats often served the god Set .

The city of Nathlekh was also known as "City of Cats" for its large population of felines.
