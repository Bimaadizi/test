Abu Jafar was a bandit and member of the Desert Wind during the mid–14th century DR .

Activities

Abu was a priest of Cyric . He used his clerical powers to aid the Desert Wind on raids to southern Murghôm . He was in service to Sheik Ar-Rabi of the Grinning Skull oasis.
