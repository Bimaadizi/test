Tankardstall Hall was a small manor in the town of Purskul in Amn .

Location

Tankardstall Hall stood conveniently on the Orc Road that stretched north from Purskul , making it a main stop along the route.

History

Circa the Year of the Tooth, 1270 DR , the Tankardstall Hall belonged to the Hamlar family of Amn. By the late 14 th century DR , the manor was purchased by the Talltankard hin family of wine merchants. The master of the manor was Cumber Talltankard circa the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR . Under his control, the manor hosted annual lavish celebrations of the first batches of the Talltankard seasonal wines to be sold north.
