Gnolls' tongues was a peppered delicacy dish from the Red Wizards ' nation of Thay .

Description

Gnolls ' tongues were cooked, peppered, and served encased in molded gelatinous aspic. History

As of the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Aznar Thrul , the Zulkir of Evocation and the Tharchion of Priador considered peppered gnolls' tongues to be a favorite but not worth eating once warm and shapeless.
