Darlow was the name of a black bear that lived within the Harvesthome Abbey of Goldenfields during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Some time after the War of the Silver Marches , Darlow and its companion wandered into the southern farms of Goldenfields. After they were offered food by the Chauntean farmhands, the bears lingered in the complex for a matter of months.
