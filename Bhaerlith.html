Bhaerlith was the name of a secret, manufactured oasis located in the Anauroch desert.

Description

The oasis was rich with a variety of fruiting trees including palms . Within the camp was a single underground stone structure used by the Zhentilar soldiers.

Geography

Bhaerlith was located in the northern region of the Sword , just north of Elah'zad in the Hills of Scent .

Defenses

Like other Zhent oases, Bhaerlith was protected by a ring of spring-sword traps that would harm any would bring great harm to any would-be trespassers or wandering wildlife. The remains of local Bedine tribespeople that did make it into the camp were displayed on wooden posts outside the perimeter as a warning.

History

During the mid-14 th century DR it served as an outpost for the Zhentilar army. It was used as a major way point for Zhent caravans crossing the desert to trade goods to and from Zhentil Keep .

Rumors & Legends

Rumors stated that the oasis was haunted by a single guardian daemon .

Inhabitants

The camp supported its contingent of Zhentilar soldiers, who were in turn given support by a cadre of Zhentarim spellcasters .
