Zastreva was an Untaken of Umberlee , a member of the Bitch Queen's clergy under Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queen's House in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Zastreva was a fair-skinned human woman with blond hair and blue eyes. Her skin was freckled and hair was gathered in two ponytails. Zastreva's garb was a simple jerkin and a tunic, as well as a cook's apron and a toque blanche .

History

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Untaken Zastreva was serving in the Water Queen's House's kitchens. After the death of Holli Dylford , a member of the church, Untaken Padulla and Untaken Zastreva worked on a funeral feast to the served in the drowned priestess ' honor. Despite her affections for the deceased, Zastreva was appoyed by the service and its incessant singing.
