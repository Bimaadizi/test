Siksuv was a small village in the Nakvaligach region within the Great Glacier .

Geography

Siksuv was located in eastern Nakvaligach.

Inhabitants

In 1359 DR the inhabitants of Siksuv lacked leadership. This was a result of the villages urit and yaaurit becoming fatally ill after consuming spoiled meat.
