Lamb talthaek was a type of local pie-like dish from the city of Arrabar in the Vilhon Reach .

Description

Talthaek was a lamb meat stuffed pie, popular for its rich, creamy, brown gravy sauce that poured out of its pastry crust when cut into.

History

Circa the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , lamb talthaek was one of the specialties of the Dark She Looks Upon Me eatery in the Mercantile District of Arrabar. That dish, along with roast beef were favorite dishes of Vambran Matrell of the noble House Matrell and member of the Order of the Sapphire Crescent .
