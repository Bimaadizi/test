Ricks Garven was a human commoner in the late 15 th century DR who lived in Baldur's Gate 's Rivington district.

Description

Ricks was a human man with a light skin tone, blonde hair and beard, and dark blue eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate , displacing countless refugees along the way in the process. Some of the Baldurians did not take kindly to the refugees flooding the outskirts of the city, while others took to protesting. Ricks Garven was among the protestors in Rivington, who thought that the refugee tent city hid treacherous agents of the Absolute in their midst.
