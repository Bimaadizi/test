Windcrag House was the expansive country estate of the Barelder family in Cormyr .

Location

Windcrag House was on the Tun Shores , southwest of the Storm Horns .

Inhabitants

The Barelder family usually preferred to spend their time at Heldartowers in Suzail rather than at Windcrag House. The country estate and farm was tended by about a dozen servants, who raised quality riding horses , cantankerous goats , and hundreds of sheep .
