Budaera was an evening-lass of Hastarl in the early 3 rd century DR .

Description

She was known to use rose-scented perfume.

Personality

Budaera had wit.

Activities

Budaera was known to have sought company in the Kissing Wench tavern.

Relationships

She was believed to have fancied Farl , and was often seen in the company of Ashanda .

History

In the early summer of the Year of the Black Flame, 229 DR , Budaera was present at the Kissing Wench tavern when fellow thieves Farl and Eladar the Dark came in. Farl attempted some sleight-of-hand after his initial offering price was rejected, but they were interrupted by the bodyguards of Jansibal Otharr , a local dandy and high-born bully.

Rumors & Legends

After noticing she was offering him a reduced rate, Farl said the prostitute must've been making money hand over fist. Eladar seemed skeptical of where that logic was leading Farl.
