Whichcoot was a Netherese fighter who worked at the Mythomian Warehouse in Eileanar in Netheril until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Whichcoot worked for Adrian Freeman at the warehouse, serving as a security guard for the premises alongside Beaufort .
