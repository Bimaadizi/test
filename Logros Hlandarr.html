Logros Hlandarr was a human wizard and service mage for the Aurora's Realms Shop, South High Road Catalogue Counter in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep in the 1360s DR .

Personality

He was arrogant and acted as if he was an important and mysterious mage, despite his occupation. Secretly, he really wanted to be involved in some actual intrigue, albeit with little actual threat to him.

Description

He was dark and handsome.
