Amaeraszantha was an amethyst dragon in 1359 DR .

Activities

Amaeraszantha spent her time swimming and basking in a lake in the ruined city of Tempus's Tears . If someone came across her while she was in the water she tried to play dead. On other occasions, Amaeraszantha polymorphed into a human girl posing as a sacrifice to the dragon. To eat, she waited until dark and went to eat fish on the Sword Coast or in winter on the Shining Sea .
