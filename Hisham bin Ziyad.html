Hisham bin Ziyad was a moralist and member of the League Conclave in I'tiraf in 1367 DR .

Activities

Originally from Mahabba , Hisham was elected to lead the group of delegates from that city to represent them within the League Conclave. Hisham and his fellow delegates ensured Mahabba's best interests were heard at court.
