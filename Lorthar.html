Lorthar of the Waves was a storm giant adventurer in the −2 nd century DR .

Possessions

In the Year of Tapestries, −107 DR , during the Seventh Serôs War , Lorthar was noted as possessing the triton artifact Kayas the Krakenscourge .

History

Lorthar died alongside a legion of elite morkoth warriors in the Year of the Valorous Kobold, −106 DR , during one of the last battles of the Seventh Serôs War.
