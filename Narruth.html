Narruth was a small island located within the Land of the Lions region of South Faerûn .

Location

Narruth lay in the northwest corner of the Lake of Steam west of Mintar and north of the island of Olodel .

Inhabitants

The island served as the base and home for a group of warrior-merchants called the Baldovans . The Baldovans operated their own trading coster from the island. The group did not welcome uninvited visitors.
