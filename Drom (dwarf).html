Drom was a dwarf fighter worshiper of Helm and a member of Order of the Gauntlet in 1489 DR .

Personality

Drom believed that you could fix problems with a big hammer.

History

He was the Gauntlet's delegate to help a hired adventuring party to defeat the great dragon Claugiyliamatar in Kryptgarden Forest . The objective of Drom's order was to destroy the Cult of the Dragon 's contingent without warning the green dragon and to destroy all undead raised by Thayan necromancers .
