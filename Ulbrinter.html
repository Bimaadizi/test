Ulbrinter ( pronounced : /ˌ ʊl ˈ b r ɪ n t ɛ r / UL-BRIN-ter ) was a noble house of Waterdeep . Their trade and interests included shipping and shipwrights.

Base of Operations

Their estate within the city of Waterdeep , Ulbrinter Villa , housed 24 guards and six servants and was located on Delzorin Street in the North Ward between Vhezoar Street and Brondar's Way .
