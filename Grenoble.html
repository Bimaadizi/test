Grenoble was a Netherese arcanist who taught at the Academy of Mentalist Study in Eileanar shortly before its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Grenoble was extremely skilled at natural divination techniques, and taught them at the Academy under Dean Zucchi .
