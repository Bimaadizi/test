Tharyann "the Elder" born in the Year of the Resounding Call, 157 DR , was the 15th Obarskyr monarch and a king of Cormyr .

Relatives

Tharyann was the tenth child and third son of King Irbruin .

His second son, Boldovar was his lovechild.

History

Tharyann was known for putting down the first rebellion of Arabel . He outlived most of his own children, therefore giving Boldovar the opportunity to take the crown.
