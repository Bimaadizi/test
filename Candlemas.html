Candlemas was the high steward for Lady Polaris of Delia in the Netherese Empire during the Shadowed Age .

Description

The bearded wizard was short, fat, and balding.

Possessions

Candlemas preferred to dress in a plain brown sackcloth smock , which was tied with a length of rope.

Relationships

The mentalist looked up to Lady Polaris, but butted heads with her chamberlain , Sysquemalyn .

History

Circa −700 DR , Candlemas and Sysquemalyn gambled on whether or not Sunbright Steelshanks would survive various dangerous tasks.

Around −339 DR , the archwizard known as Karsus stole Candlemas from the past and brought him to the future to aid him in crafting his most powerful spells .

After returning from the future, Candlemas was murdered by Sysquemalyn in the Year of Embattled Ground, −694 DR .
