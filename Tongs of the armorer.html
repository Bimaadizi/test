The tongs of the armorer were a pair of enchanted tongs , used by the best craftsmen of the ancient Myth Drannor .

Description

The tongs of the armorer appeared masterfully crafted and radiated dim shine.

Powers

These magic tongs were enchanted to dramatically enhance the wielder crafting and armorsmithing skills when working with metal.

History

The tongs of the armorer , along with the hammer of the weaponsmith , were placed on display within the Artisan's Hall within the Crypt of Rothilion . The magic tools could be found in the Smith's Alcove to the left from the entrance to the tomb. The Crypt of Rothilion was sealed before the advent of hte Weeping War and the fall of Myth Drannor. The tomb remained undisturbed until it was unsealed in 1369 DR buring the Cult of the Dragon 's attempts to take over the pool of radiance within the ruined City of Song.
