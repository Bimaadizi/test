Drongo Bixworth , husband of Florean Bixworth , was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Drongo was a young human man with a dark brown skin tone, black eyes, and black hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Drongo Bixworth and his husband, Florean, found themselves unjustly detained by Fist Elthira Gossen during what was meant to be a promenade through the Lower City of the Gate. The couple, taking a rest due to Florean's lame leg, appeared suspicious to the overzealous Fist patrolwoman. Despite their innocence and only wanting to return home, Florean and Drongo were subjected to unwarranted accusations and harassment for simply loitering.
