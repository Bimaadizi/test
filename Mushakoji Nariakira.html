Mushakoji Nariakira was a samurai retainer of the Niwa clan and the jito of Miki in Kozakura in 1357 DR .

Activities

In Miki, a large fishing village that had recently grown significantly, he led a company of ten samurai and thirty bushi . Thanks to the growth of the village and the necessity of also managing the adjacent central Niwa shoen , he had to recruit fifteen more bushi but was sure to need more.
