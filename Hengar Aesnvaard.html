Hengar Aesnvaard ( pronounced : /ˈ h ɛ n g ɑːr ˈ ɑː k ɛ ɛ n v ɑːr d / HEN-gar ACE-en-vard ) was a young barbarian in Icewind Dale circa the Year of the Iron Dwarf's Vengeance, 1485 DR .

History

Hengar was born into the Tribe of the Elk . He always idolized Wulfgar of the Companions of the Hall .

When the Chosen of Auril , Hedrun Arnsfirth , began menacing the Elk Tribe, Hengar thought he needed to do something. He secretly departed for Ten Towns to ask for help. After arriving in Bryn Shander , Hengar hoped to meet with the authorities but at the same time the Auril cultists identified him as a good sacrifice. The Auril cultist Brinna Alcott accused him of robbery and Hengar was arrested and sentenced to death by exposure to the cold.
