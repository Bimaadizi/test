The goatskin boots were a pair of fine boots found in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The boots were sensible footwear made from the hide of a goat .

Powers

The unfortunate goat who became these boots had only one thought before being caught: escape. In its later form, the boots let the wearer run much faster, and increased the speed of their strikes.

History

The boots were taken from the treasury of the Shadovar Archwizard Amskar 's flying glacier enclave by Cormyrean forces as they attacked it. The battle destroyed the enclave and the army upon it, but the goatskin boots were among the treasures recovered intact.
