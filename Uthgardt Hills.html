The Uthgardt Hills , or Wild Hills , were a stretch of wildlands in the Savage Frontier inhabited by the Uthgardt . It was generally known of as "where the barbarians hunt."

Description

The Hills were made up of rolling wilderness dotted with frequent scrub forests, bogs, ponds, streams, and rills.

Geography

The Uthgardt Hills lay between the Crags , Starmetal Hills , Lurkwood , and Surbrin Hills . They made up much of the ground that the Long Road traversed between Longsaddle and Mirabar .
