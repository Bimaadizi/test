Falten was an employee of Baldur's Gate 's Elfsong Tavern in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Falten was a human man with olive skin tone, brown eyes, and blond hair and beard. When at the inn, he wore a simple workman garb and an apron .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Falten spent his days begrudgingly mopping floors, collecting dirty dishes, and serving the guests of the Elfsong Tavern while complaining that the job was beneath his education level. He was under the employ of Alan Alyth .
