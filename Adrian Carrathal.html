Adrian "the Twinblade" Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr from the mid–12 th through the turn of the 13 th century DR . Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles , he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty . He was the sole heir of King Edwin II and Queen Eleanor , and his eventual successor was his grandson, Cedric .

History

Adrian was only ten years old when his father died in the Year of the Petulant Dragon, 1135 DR , and so his mother named Adom Kimball , King of Moray , to serve as regent over Callidyrr. Adrian assumed the throne in the Year of Eyes, 1141 DR , when he was sixteen.

In the seventh year of his reign , agents of the Kraken Society began operating throughout the North and the Moonshaes .

Adrian ruled for sixty-six years before he died of natural causes in the Year of the Bloated Baron, 1207 DR .
