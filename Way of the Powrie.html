The Way of the Powrie was a trail that ran though the Moonsea North .

Geography

The road started at the village of Ilinvur (where it connected to the Ilinvur Trail ), and stretched north over 100 miles (160 kilometers), skirting the eastern edge of the Ride before it crossed into the Tortured Lands . The trail ended at the gnoll community of Frozen Flindyke where a bridge crossed the River Pelauvir , but the way continued northwards along the Remorhaz Ride .
