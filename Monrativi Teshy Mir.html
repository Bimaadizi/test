Monrativi Teshy Mir was Alzhedo for the " First Kingdom of Mir ". It was the term used by the Calishites to refer to a piece of land in Monteshi that was contested between Calimshan and the kingdom of Tethyr . Shoonach , the capital of the Shoon Imperium was located there.
