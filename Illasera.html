Illasera was an elven Bhaalspawn who emerged as a member of the group known as the Five during the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Personality

She was supremely confident in her abilities and prowess in battle.

Possessions

Illasera wore a suit of elven chainmail .

Relationships

She was seen as one of the weaker members of the Five, and was held in low regard by the Bhaalist priestess Amelyssan .

History

Illasera was tasked by the Five with murder of their fellow sibling Abdel Adrian . To accomplish this feat, she and her allies set up an ambush for him and his companions near the Grove of the Ancients in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Abdel and his allies emerged victorious from the battle, and Illasera was slain.

The Echo

In the late 15 th century DR, a collection of Bhaalspawn: Amelyssan , Abazigal , Illasera, and Sendai , were brought back as bloodied echoes of their former selves by Sarevok Anchev , placing them on the Murder Tribunal , hidden deep within Baldur's Gate 's Undercellar , in the ruins of the Temple of Bhaal .
