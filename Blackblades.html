House Blackblades was a mercantile house that operated within the city of Ravens Bluff during the 14 th century DR . Their business involved the shipping of goods in and out of the city, specializing in quick runs across the Reach to several ports in Sembia . They were known for buying stakes in shipping vessels that regularly sailed the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Despite the common misconception, the family had no involvement with any of the activities that centered around the Pirate Isles , despite their numerous nearby holdings.

Members

As of the year 1370 DR , the family was led by Eldivvyr Blackblades , who held a seat on the city's Merchant Council .
