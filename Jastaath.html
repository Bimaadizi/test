Jastaath was a petty kingdom located in ancient Narfell that predated the Empire of Narfell . Ancient texts claimed that it was the first Nar kingdom to practice demonbinding .

Location

The kingdom was centered on Jastaath Castle , located on Mount Nar .

History

Jastaath was ruled by priest-kings who were said to be the first of the Nars to parley with demons . They were said to have been powerful enough to control the weather.

The kingdom disappeared and their capital was abandoned for unknown reasons, but the reputation of Jastaath's ancient power persisted. As of the late 15 th century DR , the ruins of their kingdom were occupied by Branimern Rythil , who sought their ancient demonbinding secrets.
