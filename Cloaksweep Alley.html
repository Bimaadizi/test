Cloaksweep Alley was a small back alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Cloaksweep Alley was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . It passed through a small neighborhood bordered by Zarimtar Street to the south, Mendever Street to the west, Sul Street to the east, and the Rough Road to the north. Cloaksweep Alley connected to all four larger roads.
