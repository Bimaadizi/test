Millston was a mercenary.

History

Millston served under Cordell of the Golden Legion in the war against the pirates of Akbet-Khrul .

In 1361 DR , he was a member of Don Vaez 's expedition. However, following the imprisonment of Cordell and all the Legion's officers, Millston agreed to conspire with Rodolfo and others to free Cordell.
