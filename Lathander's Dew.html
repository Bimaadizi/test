Lathander's Dew was a soft drink that came in several flavors, including the original, Code Reth , Moonshae Blue , and Icewind Freeze.

Description

Lathander's Dew was advertised as dew from the Sword Mountains . In reality, it was not dew, but it was effervescent water sourced from the Sword Mountains, dyed a greenish color, and flavored.

Code Reth was a red concoction flavored with cherry , likely referencing deadly gladiatorial battles in Reth of the Vilhon Reach . The red dye used in the drink was extracted from tiny scalebugs .

Moonshae Blue was flavored with a citrus fruit native to the Moonshae Isles but dyed blue to evoke the color of the Sea of Moonshae .
