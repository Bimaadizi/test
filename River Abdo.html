The River Abdo was a winding waterway found in the Ruined Kingdoms region of east Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Geography

The river originated at the southern tip of Al-Sayaj Mountains where it flowed south before turning east to wind its way through the Grey Jungle before joining with the mighty Nogaro River .
