Meggin ( pronounced : /ˈ m eɪ g ɪ n / MAY-ginn ) was an innkeeper inhabiting Waterdeep during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Meggin was a woman in her mid-twenties sporting fiery red hair. She ran the Ship's Prow in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

History

Meggin won the inn during a high-stakes game of Three-Dragon Ante . She ran it for a couple years until the drow Xzand're Hsyl'ran killed her circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR and proceed to impersonate her using a hat of disguise .
