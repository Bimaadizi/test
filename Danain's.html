Danain's hardware shop was a store located on the Promenade in Suzail , the capital of Cormyr in the 14 th century DR .

Location

Danain's store was nestled between the posh Gilded Feather club to the west and the Witch-Duke's Bride , formerly known as the High Tankard tavern. The hardware shop stood on the southern side of the Promenade, three city blocks away from Cormyr's Royal Court .

History

Danain's hardware shop could be found on the maps of Suzail as early as the Year of the Sword, 1365 DR .
