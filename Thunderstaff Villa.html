Thunderstaff Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Thunderstaff .

Location

The villa was located in the north section of Waterdeep 's North Ward where Thunderstaff Way met Immar Street .

Description

The villa contained four separate buildings. The largest of the four sat in the northwest corner while the smaller three buildings were found in the southeast corner. The buildings stood two or four stories tall.
