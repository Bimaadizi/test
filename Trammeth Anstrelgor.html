Trammeth Anstrelgor was a famous cartographer that lived during the 12 th century DR . He authored what was considered the most famous single collection of maps, A Manyrealms Gallimaufry .

History

Trammeth Anstrelgor died around the Year of the Last Enclave, 1114 DR . He was buried with the original copy of his magnum opus.
