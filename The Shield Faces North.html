Location

The tavern was located near the center of Zhentil Keep's Harbor District along Harbor Street near the waterfront.

Activities

The Shield Faces North was a popular tavern for those wishing to enjoy a quiet evening. Among the usual crowd were halflings , dwarves , and older merchants. The serene atmosphere within the tavern existed because the strict access to the Harbor District prevented most of Zhentil Keep's scum from entering.
