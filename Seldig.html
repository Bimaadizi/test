Seldig was a deep gnome fighter / illusionist from Blingdenstone .

History

Seldig was the third svirfneblin who Drizzt Do'Urden would know by name. Drizz met him some decade after leaving Menzoberranzan , and to Seldig and his fellow youths, he shared the tale of how he battled and slayed a basilisk .
