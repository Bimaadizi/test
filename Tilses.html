Tilses was a tiefling member of the Hellriders , protectors of the holy city of Elturel , during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Tilses had light red skin and curly black hair she kept short.

Possessions

She wore a suit of padded armor and carried with her a longsword .

History

Tilses served with the Hellriders until the disaster known as the Descent befell Elturel in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . After the city was pulled briefly down to the plane of Avernus in the Nine Hells , all of the city's tieflings – including those in the Hellriders – were exiled from the city. Tilses joined Commander Zevlor as he led a group of his people west along the Risen Road towards Baldur's Gate , and aided him as the group took refuge at the Emerald Grove druidic enclave.
