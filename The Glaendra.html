The Glaendra was a lamia noble who led a community of lamia in the Lion's Eye Oasis .

Description

The Glaendra was extremely beautiful and very tall, reaching nine feet.

Possessions

It was said that she had a large treasure of both precious gems and magic items recovered from Netherese ruins.
