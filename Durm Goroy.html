Durm Goroy was a convicted criminal from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Durm Goroy was accused of an assassination attempt on Lord Enver Gortash during The Absolute Crisis. Whether this was truth or a political setup, Durm was arrested, tried, and condemned to public execution. As the Absolutist army marched on Baldur's Gate and unbeknownst to all, Lord Gortash being one of the Absolute's leaders, Durm, alongside a fellow prisoner named Vince Torell , was to be transferred from the Stonyeyes Precinct of the Flaming Fist to the Basilisk Gate prison on the orders from Manip Farthingale . Basilisk Gate had a handy plaza with a hanging stage set up for public executions at the time.
