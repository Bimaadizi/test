Aslyferund elven chain were sets of enchanted elven chainmail crafted by Aslyferund of Myth Drannor .

Description

These chain mails, suits of finest mesh, were soft and light, weighting and feeling like regular clothes rather than heavy metal links.

Powers

These masterfully-crafted suits of chainmail were enhanced with +3 enchantment and allowed for the armor to soak part of the damage, further protecting the wearer. Due to the creation process of the Aslyferund elven chains , they could only we worn by creatures of elven blood.

History

These enchanted items were crafted by Aslyferund , one of the most famous armorers of Myth Drannor, before the city's fall in the Weeping War of 714 DR . Many sages entertained rumors that the more powerful sets of Aslyferund elven chain were created with the aid of a gold dragon .

By late 14 th century DR , some of these sets of armor could be found in the Sword Coast North region, as well as in the town of Elventree of the Moonsea . By that same time, the imp Cespenar that once served the god Bhaal had learned Aslyferund's secret and could replicate it using a suit of bladesinger chain and a spell scroll of protection from normal weapons .
