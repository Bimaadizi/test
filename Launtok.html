Launtok was a town in Athalantar during the 3 rd century DR .

Description

Launtok was a large town.

History

In Eleasias of the Year of the Chosen, 240 DR , the prince Elminster Aumar and the sorceress Myrjala Talithyn decided to begin their plan to besiege Athalgard and overthrow King Belaur while at a tavern in Launtok.
