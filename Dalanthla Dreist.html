Dalanthla Dreist was one of the leaders of the Kardryn in Yeshpek along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

She was known for her fiery temper.

Activities

Dalanthla earned her fame as an adventurer where her skill at throwing daggers , or whatever else was within easy reach, preceded her.
