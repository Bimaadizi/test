Shoshara was a priestess of Eilistraee in Shilmista .

History

In 1377 DR , she was arguing with Glorst , a former priest of Vhaeraun , about which of their gods had absorbed the other in their duel in 1375 DR , when they were approached by Halisstra Melarn . Glorst tried to kill her but was defeated. Later Halisstra interrogated Shoshara about the current situation and the location of Cavatina Xarann . However, Shoshara distrusted her and refused her aid, despite Halisstra's suffering. So, at last feeling betrayed by Eilistraee, Halisstra killed Shoshara.
