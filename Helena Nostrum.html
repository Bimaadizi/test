Helena "Hook" Nostrum was a giantslayer who roamed around the Savage Frontier in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Helena had a strong will and a good sense of leadership, capable of keeping people under her command sharp and focused.

History

Some time after the shattering of the Ordning in 1485 DR , when the Savage Frontier became a hotbed of squabbling giant activity, Helena witnessed the destructive power of giants firsthand. From that day forward she became a giantslayer, dedicating her life to always being one step ahead of the towering humanoids .
