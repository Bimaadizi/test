Beulla Sandfoot was a criminal who worked against the infamous Guild in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

She was a hin woman with a fair skin tone, blue eyes, and blonde hair. She wore simple garb consisting of a navy blue jacket, brown trousers, and a light blouse.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Beulla could be found with her co-conspirator, the half-orc Fyrd Nuugh in the Lower City near one of the docks. Their meeting place was a seaside gazebo, where they discussed the Guild and Nine-Fingers Keene while pretending to simply discuss the weather if unwelcome listeners were near.
