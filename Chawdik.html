Chawdik was a poor island that was part of the Korinn Archipelago in the Sea of Swords and home to the largest gnomish community among all the archipelago islands as of the 14 th century DR .

Description

Chawdik was a small and humble island located to the west of its much larger neighbor, the isle of Ventris . The island's large gnomish population resided on the island's southern coast.
