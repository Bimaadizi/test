Reptar's wall was a large, heavy magical iron shield of unknown origin. It earned its name for its use by the ranger known as Reptar .

Powers

This +1 shield was impervious to rust and corrosion and did not conduct heat or cold, thus protecting its wielder. It was also magically silenced and gave no sound when struck, dropped, or otherwise handled.
