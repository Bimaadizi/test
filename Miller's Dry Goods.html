Miller's Dry Goods was a shop in Daggerford that specialized in the sale of dry goods and household items. It was owned and run by Bess Miller a local businesswoman who owned three nearby grainmills .

Location

It was located in the town's Caravan Quarter .

Services

The shop sold all manner of grains and ground flour in addition to a variety of that could be used around the homestead.
