Boots of the Mountain King was a variety of enchanted boots around in Northwest Faerûn in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Boots of the Mountain King were sturdily crafted and looked imposing on the wearer.

Powers

These boots possessed powerful enchantment that granted the wearer freedom of movement and the ability to turn their skin rock-hard once per day.

History

A pair of boots of the Mountain King belonged to King Karagg of a small fire giant community in the Mount Hotenow 's lava caverns shortly after the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR .
