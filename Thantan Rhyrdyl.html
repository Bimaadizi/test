Thantan Rhyrdyl was one of the leaders (a Boar's Head ) of the Order of the Blue Boar , a mercenary recruitment company in Amn .

Description

Thantan was charismatic to the point of seeming arrogant.

Activities

He was the battlefield leader of the Order. He was a very cabable cavalry officer.

History

Hailing from a wealthy Amnian merchant family, Thantan in his youth formed his well-armed and armored private mercenary cavalry company, and left the family business. While disappointed in this, his family did not disown him.
