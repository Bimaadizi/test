Tressardon was a short-lived kingdom in Faerûn that was raised by the bandit Tresset .

Geography

The kingdom was based around the town of Tresset's Ringyl .

Government

The bandit Tresset crowned himself king after declaring his kingdom; after his death, the kingdom disbanded within days.

History

At some point prior to the Year of Laughter, 761 DR , the bandit Tresset established the town of Tresset's Ringyl, based around a castle called the Ringyl . He hoped to build himself a kingdom, which he named Tressardon, but the kingdom barely outlived him.
