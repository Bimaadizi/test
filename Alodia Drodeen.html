Alodia Drodeen was a drow cleric and eldest daughter of house Drodeen.

Description

Growing up Alodia were naught but a slave to her aunts, but with her mothers rise to matron mother of the noble house, she too saw her position rise above the rest of the family. As eldest daughter of house Drodeen she had quite the ambition of seeing her house rise up in status and power in the city.

Possessions

She wore an enchanted drow chainmail , a helm of telepathy , and an amulet of the planes , while wielding an enchanted Scimitar , 3 hidden daggers , and a staff of withering .

Relationships

She was the eldest living daughter of Grazia Drodeen , and thus the heir apparent of their house.
