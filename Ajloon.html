Ajloon was a shade who served the Night Guard of the Prince of Shade Yder Tanthul , for a brief period as his second-in-command, during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He had a square jaw, and a complexion that was rather dark.

Personality

Ajloon could not be easily intimidated, even by those the more powerful of shades of Thultanthar . While he lacked some insight, he would readily learn from his experiences and those who offered him advice.

Relationships

While Yder could often be short with Ajloon, he acted as a mentor of sorts with regards to acting on behalf of Shar and Netheril .

History

Ajloon was among the shades that followed Yder in pursuit of the Eye of Gruumsh during the month of Uktar in the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR . After Most High Telamont Tanthul reached out to his son Yder, Ajloon returned to Thultanthar to protect the Hall of Shadows .
