Palenqi was the northernmost town of the Maztican nation of Pezelac .

Description

Palenqi was a minor town with a population somewhere between 5,000 and 15,000 humans . It was nestled between the Bomak Jungle to the east and a small jungle at the foot of Mount Plutoq to the west. The town was built on a savannah, bordering arable land to the southwest. The closest large body of water was the Gulf of Cordell , miles to the north of Palenqi.
