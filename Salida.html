Salida ( pronounced : / s ɑː ˈ l i d ɑː / sah-LEE-dah ) was a yuan-ti pureblood posing as a human guide in Port Nyanzaru during the late 15 th century DR . She was in fact a spy for Ras Nsi who relayed information about expeditions that ventured south into the jungles of Chult .

Appearance

She was 5 ft 4 in (1.6 m) tall and weighed 120 pounds (54 kilograms). Salida appeared physically fit and maintained the appearance of an experienced traveler.

Due to her yuan-ti heritage, areas of her skin appeared as scales.

Personality

Salida was adept as creating vivid and fantastic lies to obscure her identity as a spy. She was particularly witty and adroit.

Possessions

Salida possessed a sending stone that she used to communicate with Ras Nsi.
