Ramathanhur was a son of the god Anhur who warred against Red Wizards in the 10 th century DR . After his troop of soldiers were defeated, he became sealed in the Darkcrypt .

Description

Ramathanhur appeared as a nearly 7 feet (2.1 meters) tall Mulan human male. He had a hooked nose and shining blue eyes. His skin was gold in color, and appeared to glow softly. He had no hair anywhere on his body.

Personality

Ramathanhur was charismatic and clearly regal. He was particularly knowledgable about religious matters. He preferred to talk rather than fight.

Abilities

Ramathanhur had access to a variety of powerful abilities. He could not be harmed by acid , cold, disease , or electricity . He could replicate the effects of the spell light any time he chose, and Three times per day he could replicate the effects of the spells holy aura and protection from evil . He could also replicate the effects of the spells aid , bless , cure serious wounds , detect evil , hallow , holy smite , holy word , neutralize poison , remove disease , and symbol . He could channel Anhur's power to increase his power in battle. He could also smite those who did not follow Anhur and heal those by touching their body.

He was extremely skilled in fighting with a khopesh .

Possessions

Ramathanhur used a pair of gloves of lightning , as well as a holy keen khopesh , a lion's shield and enchanted scale mail .

Activities

In the Darkcrypt, Ramathanhur regularly spent time honing his fighting ability.

History

In the Year of the Spouting Fish, 922 DR , Ramathanhur was a commander in the armies of Mulhorand . He was stationed on the plateau of Priador when the Red Wizards rebelled against Mulhorand. His forces lost the battle and were pushed back to Thazalhar . In order to atone for his military failure, Ramathanhur elected to remain at the Darkcrypt , a disused memorial to Re . He made an oath to protect the Darkcrypt for the rest of his life, or until Mulhorand reclaimed its Thazalhar.

To this end, a high priest of Horus-Re sealed him in with a forbiddance spell, and collapsed a portion of cliff onto the shrine. Along with Ramathanhur was a collection of valuables taken during battles with Red Wizards. The high priest was slain shortly afterwards by a force of Red Wizards.

Some time after the Time of Troubles of the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , a spectral mage named Kethoth Uuthrakt discovered the location of the Darkcrypt using spell scroll of locate object . Kethoth had been on the side of the Red Wizards in 922 DR , and some of the treasures buried with Ramathanhur were his. Seeking to reclaim his posessions, he animated skeletons and sent them to tunnel into Darkcrypt. Ramathanhur slew many of the skeletons himself.

Rumors & Legends

All who knew that Ramathanhur guarded the Darkcrypt were slain shortly after he took up his oath. Because of this, none knew what had happened to him or that he remained buried beneath the earth.
