Cecie was a member of the Dead Rats gang in Luskan in the late 15 th century.

Relationships

Cecie was the daughter of Gylan .

History

Around 1490 DR , Cecie had been poisoned by Bregan D'aerthe and driven mad to the extent that Titus Minton asked the Champion of the Burning Dawn to put them out of their misery.
