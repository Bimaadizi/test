Golden girdles of Urnst , or simply golden girdles , were enchanted belts imbued with defensive magics.

Description

Golden girdles were weaved out of red silk and strewn with flakes of gold .

Powers

The wearer of a golden girdle gained increased protection against slashing damage.

History

The first golden girdles were created ages before the late 14 th century DR by a powerful wizard who sought to protect themselves from a group of evil swordsmen.

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , a band of the Knights of Solamnia from the world of Krynn became trapped in a massive Planar Sphere that crash-landed in the city of Athkatla in Amn . One of the Knights was in possession of a golden girdle .
