Eduardo Jansen was a member of the gnomish Jansen , active in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Eduardo was known for his intentions. One of his inventions was proudly displayed in Athkatla 's Center District , at the center of Waukeen's Promenade . Eduardo's flying machine was a small device with four wings, big enough to accommodate one person.
