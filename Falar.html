Falar was a agent of the Knights of the Shield who operated throughout the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Falar was an underling of Kaddrus , a cambion who served lady Lutecia Hhune of Baldur's Gate .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Falar and two other agents, Nulra Blacksaddle and Zaroud al'Ryshal , were tasked with aiding Kaddrus on a specific mission. While Kaddrus disguised himself as a human , the group were to seek out and bring back the missing Shield of the Hidden Lord .
