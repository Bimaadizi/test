The cloak of sunlight was a resplendent cloak found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Description

The cloak was made of white and tan cloth, with elaborate sun-based designs decorating it.

Powers

The cloak gave great protection against diseases and physical blows, and aided in healing magic. It also distorted light about it, causing attackers to miss.

History

The cloak was made and worn in ancient pre-Fall Myth Drannor. It was eventually found in the ruins of Ildrannath's Tower .
