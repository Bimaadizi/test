Sashes were a type of accessory garment that could be found all across the Realms.

Description

Sashes came in a variety of colors and were typically made of either linen, silk, or wool. Occasionally they were woven from more rare or exotic materials, such as samite or shimmerweave .

They were often used to fasten an article of clothing in place, much like a belt. Such clothing included abas , caftans , dishdashahs , gowns, kimonos, and robes . Some were known to conceal weaponry in their sashes, such as chains, kyoketsuogi , and shurikens .

Availability

History

In the year 1352 DR , the samurai Guiji Yui , then- shoya of the village Osata in the Fochu Peninsula , executed a farmer in front of a large crowd for having been caught wearing a silk obi. The man had been in violation of the Keian no Furegaki (a set of laws for farmers) , which mandated that farmers were forbidden from wearing any form of silk clothing.
