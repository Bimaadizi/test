Silaha Mnunzi was a weapon buyer in service to the merchant prince Ekene-Afa of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

She was brusque in her business dealings.

Activities

Silaha acquired weapons on behalf of her merchant prince, who by law controlled all trade in weapons within Port Nyanzaru. This meant that foreign weapons dealers or blacksmiths were advised to seek her out and impress her if they wished to sell their wares in the city.

Silaha imported a great deal of weapons from lands north of Chult . She also worked alongside the Master of the Market to seek out skilled or notable smiths who visited Port Nyanzaru.

History

During the events of the death curse , Silaha was seeking a smith to produce axes , lances , and swords of titansteel .
