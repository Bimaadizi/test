The helm of the sun's wrath was a magical helmet found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The helm bore the blessings and curses of the relentless summer sun. It filled the wearer with power and vitality that let them tear through enemies, as well as aiding their healing magic.

History

The helm of the sun's wrath may have been found by the Elves of Cormanthor when they raided Tyrintar's Hall while in search of Uhlsdrin Nirveth .
