Shilau M'wenye was a priest that oversaw the temple of Helm in Fort Beluarian during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He was half Chultan on his father's side, and Shou on his mother's side.

Personality

Shilau always did his best to not judge others and find the best in their actions.

Relationships

His father was also a priest, while his mother was a mercenary.
