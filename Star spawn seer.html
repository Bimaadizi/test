Star spawn seers were a type of star spawn , creatures created by Elder Evils to act as their agents on the Prime Material plane .

Description

Star spawn seers manifested in the physical world first as a disembodied entity. When a warlock or other spellcaster attempted to commune with it, the star spawn seer would fuse with them in form and spirit. Who they once were became obscured under layers of strange, tumorous skin. They wielded a staff made from flesh and bone in their flipper-like hands.

Behavior

A star spawn seer's ultimate goal was to perform workings of magic to bring the Elder Evil that created them out of their prison in the ethereal plane and into the Prime Material plane . They did this by organizing cults around their Elder Evil.

Star spawn seers were usually accompanied by star spawn hulks , which acted as muscle for it.

Abilities

Star spawn seers are capable of bending space, forcefully teleporting its opponents around or swapping itself with a different star spawn to avoid an attack. It was also capable of phasing through solid objects, inflicting psychic damage on any living being it phased through.
