Edals Creek was the southeastern tributary of the Neverwinter River , located in the heart of the Neverwinter Wood .

Description

Edals Creek was a small two-prong waterway that began in Neverwinter Wood and trickled northwest to merge into the Neverwinter River. Like other tributaries, Edals Creek's waters were warm and teemed with fish , turtles , and other aquatic life, but no creature that inhabited it could be called a "river monster."
