To'Ming was a trade city in Shou Lung that was part of the Ch'ing Tung Province .

Geography

It stood on the Silver Road beside the Upper Hungtse River , southwest of Shangtou via a trail.

Notable Locations

It was the site of a Summer Palace of the Emperor of Shou Lung.
