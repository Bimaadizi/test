Raven Sommersrace was a priestess who represented the Church of Sune in Crimmor during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Raven was a charismatic woman, known for her amber hair and her orations on the theme of beauty. She tended to the faithful of Sune in the Theater of Joy , which was dedicated to the deity from dawn until highsun.
