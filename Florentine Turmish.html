Florentine Dempster was the mayor of Alaghôn and ruler of Turmish in the Vilhon Reach in the mid–2 nd century DR , reigning soon the nation's founding.

History

The wife of Dempster Turmish , Florentine succeeded him after his death in the Year of the Lost Library, 150 DR . She quickly ended her husband's wars with Hlondeth and sued for peace. Thereafter, she focused on commerce.

However, she was assassinated in the Year of the Jealous Hag, 154 DR . Thereafter, power changed hands between Turmish's various merchant houses, beginning the hundred-year-long Calmwaters period of Vilhon Reach history.
