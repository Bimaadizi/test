Samir was a talented blacksmith from the city of Neverwinter , active some time before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Samir befriended a famous armor crafter from the planar City of Brass , Gisnervi, after the brassworker was banished from the Elemental Plane of Fire in disgrace. Sometime before the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Samir managed to break a curse placed on a set of magic armor created by Gisnervi, causing his exile. With the storm armor restored, so was Gisnervi's reputation.
