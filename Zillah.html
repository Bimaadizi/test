Zillah was the lamia queen of the ruins of Unthalass in Unther in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Zillah was a devoted follower of Graz'zt .

Activities

Zillah ruled over the lamia inhabiting the ruins of Unther's former capital.

History

In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Gilgeam and his army arrived in the city from the lands of Shyr in the world of Abeir . In the Year of the Rune Lords Triumphant, 1487 DR , Gilgeam, thanks to his pact with Graz'zt, reclaimed rulership of the city from the lamias.

Later Gilgeam sent Utu and Zillah to steal Nanna-Sin 's divine spark. However, Utu died and Zillah was captured and put in jail.
