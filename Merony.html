Merony was a rogue guard who worked at the Szarr Palace of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Merony was a gołd dwarven woman with an olive skin tone and a dark chestnut eyes. She kept her face clean-shaven and wore a cowl atop of her armor . Merony was armed with a light crossbow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Merony was one of three enthralled sentries who stood guard of the entrance to the massive Szarr Palace – home of vampire lord Cazador Szarr located in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. On the day of the accession ritual, the palace's chamberlain Antwun Dufay‎ ordered no visitor nor delivery to be allowed an entrance, promising a "reward" from Cazador upon the ritual's completion.
