The Fool's Altar was a small shrine dedicated to Finder Wyvernspur , the Nameless Bard, located in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the 15 th century DR .

Location

The Fool's Altar stood just south of Ascloakan's Fine Inn , along Hammaskarl's Ride , a street that began at the city's easternmost gate—the Basilisk Gate —and curved south then ran north until it reached Loklee Street .

History

The shrine was tended by a finhead saurial cleric named Aszloakh the Hooded in the late 15 th century DR , who was known for his patience, calm, and cynical good humor.
