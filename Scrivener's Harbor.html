Scrivener's Harbor was a temple dedicated to Oghma beneath the Sea of Fallen Stars , in the undersea city of Myth Nantar .

Location

This temple was located on Five Temples Square , in the Trade Quarter of Myth Nantar.

History

Scrivener's Harbor, much like the four other temples of Five Temples Square, was founded in Myth Nantar by immigrant races.

Around the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , six priests of this temple were allowed by the Dukars to work and study in Myth Nantar's Great Library in return for having their collections of writing merged.
