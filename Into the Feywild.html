Into the Feywild was an unpublished memoir by Gyldro Angleiron from sometime in or before the 15 th century DR .

History

A copy of this unpublished memoir could be found in Gyldro Angleiron's home, adjacent to his smithy and shop, the The Rivington General in the Rivington district of Baldur's Gate in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
