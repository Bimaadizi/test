The maranthae was a flower, most common in the area once occupied by Eaerlann .

Description

The small flower grew in woodlands. It had petals that were gold in color, and rich blue at the hub, shading progressively between the two colors within a third of their length. The whole flower gave off a very faint glow, which it absorbed from available light sources.

History

Eaerlann used the maranthae as its symbol during its height.
