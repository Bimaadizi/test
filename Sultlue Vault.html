Sultlue Vault was a tomb located within the City of the Dead in Waterdeep .

Location

Sultlue Vault was located in the western section of the City of the Dead.

Defenses

The tomb was heavily warded with magic .

Inhabitants

Sylsinssath Sultlue , a yuan-ti pureblood and prior member of House Eselemas , slumbered within the tomb which he used as a refuge. He was accompanied by a group of tainted ones and members of the Sultlue family visited on a regular basis to attend to his needs.
