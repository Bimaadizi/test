Laris was a merchant and the proprietor of Laris's Curios within the city of Hillsfar during the mid–14 th century DR . He dealt in art, gems , and all sorts of curios.

Description

He was known for being a particularly rude and all-around unpleasant man.

History

Shortly after the Thayan enclave was established in Hillsfar in 1371 DR , Laris reportedly greatly offended either the Red Plumes or Maalthiir himself in some manner, and was thrown into the city's arena . The behir he faced proved to be quite the deadly opponent.

Possessions

Laris used his ring of golem control to command a pair of stone golems that guarded his shop.
