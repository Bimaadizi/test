The Gate of the Mamluks was an entrance for the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

This gate served as the main entrance and exit to and from the Palace for all of the mamluks and other guards. Security here was just as tight as any of the other gates providing access to the Palace.
