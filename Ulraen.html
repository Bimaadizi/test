Ulraen of Eltabbar was a Red Wizard of Thay during the early 14 th century DR .

History

Ulraen slew Veldribold of Calimport in 1322 DR , shortly after the latter finally managed to bring down Phelandern Ghemelt , the Magister . He was mid-gloat when he was savaged by two other challengers' summoned monsters.
