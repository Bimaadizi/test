Rendilar Bluthlock was a member of the criminal underworld of Scornubel in the mid-14 th century DR .

Possessions

He wore boots.

History

In late 1357 DR , Tornar the Eye carried a message to Rendilar Bluthlock that he could spend freely, backed by Belgon Bradaskor , to send as many people to their deaths as he wished in hunting down Shandril Shessair . He led them in the hunt for spellfire. He was slain before the caravan arrived at Triel .

Rumors

The men under his command were under the impression that one of the caravan's people was a mage from the Cult of the Dragon who was also a double agent for the Arcane Brotherhood .
