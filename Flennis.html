Flennis was a high-ranking member of a Dead Three cult in Baldur's Gate in Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR .

Activities

During the late 15 th century DR , Flennis was the highest-ranking follower of Myrkul inside the cult in Baldur's Gate. She was busy creating new zombie minions for the cult. Usually she used the remains of the Dead Three's victims.
