Dwyn the Still was an elf monk in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Dwyn had green eyes and a close-shaven scalp. She wore a leafy green outfit.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR during the Elven Crusade , Dwyn joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp outside the ruins of Myth Drannor . She provided training to monks at the camp.
