The Raor Dynasty was the first of the four dynasties of the Third Age of Calimshan .

Organization

Like the dynasties of the previous age, the Raors ruled as bakkal priests over Calimshan.

Base of Operations

The seat of power of the Raor bakkals was located in the city of Calimport .

Activities

The Raor Dynasty saw the empire of Calimshan expand to include Calimshan proper, Tethyr , Iltkazar , and Mir , stretching as far north as the Snowflake Mountains . The lands outside of Calimshan were collectively known as the Calishar Emirates .

History

Little is remembered about the Raor Dynasty, beyond the fact that it ushered in Calimshan's Third Age and the greatest extent of its empire. The dynasty was founded in −1900 DR .

During the Raor dynasty, the Caltazar Hills became a place of constant conflict with the Alimir Hive of beholders , which would escalate for many centuries.

The dynasty's short time was brought to a swift end in −1838 DR by the red wyrm Ylveraasahlisar the Rose Dragon. Calishite adventurers had killed one of her offspring in a raid of her horde. In revenge, she attacked the city of Calimport, destroying nearly two-thirds of the city, including the Caleph's Court . The army was decimated before her wrath, and the bakkal was slain. Ylveraasahlisar took the throne of the empire for herself and reigned for over a century.
