Shander was a Netherese arcanist and co-owner of Transgate Industries , who lived in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

After the death of his parents Ocelot and Ytartia , Shander operated Transgate Industries alongside his siblings Acciajuoli and Thamer . They proceeded to do extremely well in managing it, and greatly increased the success of the company in the following years.
