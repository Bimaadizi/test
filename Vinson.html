The Vinson family was an influential Amnian mercantile family during the 14 th century DR .

Activities

In Trailstone , they operated Nastral's Provisioners , the biggest general supply store in the town. They tried to avoid notice beyond this, and it was widely rumored that they had some unpleasant business with the Council of Six .

History

In the Year of the Striking Falcon, 1333 DR , Lord Kirsoph managed to become a founder of the Council of Six and became Dahaunarch . He attempted to have Pommarch Wulver Xornnag assassinated in the Year of the Behir, 1342 DR , but he was slain by his own bribed guards in return.

Losing their patriarch in Athkatla , the Vinsons retreated to Trailstone under the leadership of Nastral Vinson and quietly managed their countryside estates and vineyards. They remained relatively powerful and wealthy, but consciously chose to live in obscurity and refused to engage in the high-stakes plots and intrigue of Amn.
