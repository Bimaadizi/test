The Chamber of the Court was a part of the Coronal's palace in Cormanthor .

Description

The floor was glassy-smooth. Tapestries hung somewhere on the room. There were pillars in the room, which was large enough to hold a green dragon , and the ceiling, while closed, was the only source of magical light other then the Throne. The roof was vaulted. At least one person in 261 DR had brought a footstool. At least one of the tapestries had a window behind it.

Across from the doors was the Pool of Remembrance , above which floated the Throne of the Coronal . Above the roof, if broken, there was a statue of an elven hero with a sword pointed at the throne.

Defenses

There were wards, that would, amongst other things, prevent a dragon from being summoned.

Courtiers usually remained at both sides of the room, while armathors stood watch by the door.

History

In 241 DR , the Chamber of the Court was visited by Lords Haladavar , Urddusk , and Malgath , who after threatening violence, were informed of the plans to raise a Mythal .

The chamber was the site of a coup attempt by Llombaerth Starym in 261 DR , which was followed immediately by the raising of the Mythal .
