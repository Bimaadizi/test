The Masked One was a female human sorceress and rogue adventurer, and an apprentice of the Simbul in 1374 DR .

By 1368 DR , she had retired from adventuring to live in Ingdal's Arm in Aglarond , where she lived in a house guarded by a tribe of sprites . It was rumored that when an individual attempted to remove her hood, The Masked One turned that person into a teapot.
