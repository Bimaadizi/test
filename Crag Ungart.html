Crag Ungart was a shield dwarf giantslayer who roamed around the Savage Frontier in the late 15 th century DR , some time after the shattering of the Ordning in 1485 DR .

Personality

Crag had a very keen and strategic mind, making him quite an efficient leader. However, he was rather detached from those he worked with.

Relationships

Crag was a member of the giantslayer organization Hook, Line, and Sinker .
