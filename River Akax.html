The River Akax was a river located in the country of Chessenta .

Geography

The river flowed from the Akanamere in the southwest to empty into the western coast of the Bay of Chessenta . The river split the city of Soorenar into eastern and western halves.

Activity

The entire length of the River Akax was navigable by small and medium-sized vessels.
