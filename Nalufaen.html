Nalufaen was one of three cities in Naramyr , a small sea elf kingdom beneath the lake of Dragonmere .

Geography

In relation to the surface, Nalufaen was located 25 miles (40,000 meters) south of the port city Marsember and 210 feet (64 meters) below in the waters of the Dragonmere.

History

In 459 TS (389 DR) , a Cormyrian cog known as the Valashar's Bane sank close to Nalufaen, coming to rest 39 miles (63,000 meters) east of the city.

Centuries later, the city was notably one of the many undersea locations studied and documented by Ondam Moray in his work Notes on Serôs .

Reputation

Nalufaen had a reputation for being a city of artisans.
