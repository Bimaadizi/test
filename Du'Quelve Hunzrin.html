Du'Quelve Hunzrin was a priestess of House Hunzrin in Menzoberranzan in the early 14 th century DR .

Activities

Du'Quelve was often ordered by Matron Arolina Hunzrin to lead trade expeditions outside Menzoberranzan .

History

In the Year of the Shattered Oak, 1313 DR , during one of these missions, the Hunzrin met two priestesses, Iccara and Bolfae , who said they'd come from the previously unknown Arach Enclave . Matron Arolina ordered Du'Quelve to secretly transport the two priestesses to her. However, a mission headed by Beniago and Zaknafein Do'Urden was following them. Iccara promised to Du'Quelve a load of arandur and the service of a deep gnome slave, Symvyn Rivenstone . Some time later, Du'Quelve went with Iccara to the meeting with the deep gnome and forced him to dispel the wards of the gnome's outpost. However, Zaknafein, Jarlaxle , and Kimmuriel Oblodra suddenly arrived and in the fighting Iccara was revealed to be a yochlol . In the end, Zaknafein destroyed Iccara, injured Du'Quelve and freed Symvyn.

In later years, Du'Quelve's career stagnated despite her claim to having been in league with two yochlols, as all knew she had only been tricked by the handmaidens.
