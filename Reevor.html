Reevor was a guard in Candlekeep who watched over the store-houses during the mid-14 th century DR . He was aided in his duties by a small army of ever-vigilant cats.

History

Reevor occasionally enlisted Gorion's Ward to assist with clearing the storage rooms of rats, especially when the feline soldiers of Candlekeep were not up to the duty.
