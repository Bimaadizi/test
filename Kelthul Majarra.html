Lord Kelthul Majarra was a Tethyrian bard inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He served as president of the New Olamn bardic college.

Relationships

Kelthul was the patriarch of the Majarra noble family of Waterdeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . His consort was Lady Regan . His two eldest sons were Axor and Kehlann .
