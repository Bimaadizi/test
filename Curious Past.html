The Curious Past was the antiquities store of Bronwyn Caradoon in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

This store fronted the Street of Silks from the east, in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep.

Services

The shop dealt in exotic items of fairly dubious legality while also being a front for Harper activity.
