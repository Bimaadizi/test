Josinn Kell was a commoner from the village of West Harbor in the Mere of Dead Men circa 1374 DR .

Description

Josinn was brash young man with blond hair and neatly short-trimmed facial hair. He had an intense stare as if constantly looking for weaknesses and vulnerabilities.

History

Circa the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the recently rebuilt mire village of West Harbor was menaced by a duo of black dragons . In fear of his home's destruction, Josinn Kell, along with many other villages, picked up arms and began training in case West Harbor needed defending. Josinn's weapon of choice was a single club and he defended himself with a small shield .
