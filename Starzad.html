Starzad was a vorpal scimitar .

Powers

As a vorpal weapon, Starzad was especially suited for beheading opponents.

History

Starzad was forged by a genie in the Zakharan city of Qudra . The scimitar was acquired by the sha'ir Tejan ibn-Dhabar , who carried it with him on his travels through the elemental planes . Tejan eventually transformed himself into a water elemental on the Elemental Plane of Water . Just before doing so, he sent Starzad to a former colleague. Said colleague eventually became a member of the Doomguard in Sigil , and Starzad came to be stored at the Doomguard headquarters.

The scimitar was eventually stolen on behalf of the marilith Kaliva , who sought powerful swords to help her defeat her old mentor.
