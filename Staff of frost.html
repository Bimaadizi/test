A Staff of Frost was magical staff that could cast a number of spells related to ice and cold.

Powers

The staff granted upon its wielder protection from cold -related spells and spell-like abilities. It held ten charges—a number of which would be restored every day at dawn—and could cast the spells cone of cold , fog cloud , ice storm and wall of ice .
