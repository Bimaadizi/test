Flarm was a chapbook author in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Flarm had been responsible for penning a description of the Open Feast of the Year of the Dark Circle, 1478 DR in Castle Obarskyr , Suzail , in one of his chapbooks.

One of his readers had been Amarune Whitewave , who borrowed his chapbook on that open feast from her employer, Tress .
