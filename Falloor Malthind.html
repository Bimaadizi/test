Falloor Malthind was a cobbler and retired warrior inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Falloor was the guildmaster for the Order of Cobblers & Corvisers circa the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR . He held the title of "Senior Merchant" within the guild and worked out of the Cobblers' & Corvisers' House , which was their headquarters located along the Soothsayer's Way in the Trades Ward .
