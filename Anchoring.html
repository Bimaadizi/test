Anchoring was a magical enchantment applied to armor and shields that allowed the wearer, or bearer, to stand their ground more firmly. They would be incredibly difficult to move their position during battle.

Creation

To apply the anchoring effect to a shield or suit of armor, the magical craftsperson used the spell bull's strength .
