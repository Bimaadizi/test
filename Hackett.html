Hackett was a Calishite human journeyman laborer who worked on the Sword Coast during the late 14 th century DR .

History

On Uktar 2 of the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , Hackett passed through the sleepy village of Moonhaven . His activities were observed by the exiled Red Wizard Ilyn Toth .
