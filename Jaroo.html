Jaroo was a gnome wizard and a the Master of the Arcane at the Adventurer's Academy of Neverwinter in the late 14 th century DR .

History

By the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Jaroo was known for crafting numerous rods of frost and giving them out to the best students of the Adventurer's Academy. By the the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , many of Jaroo's rods were found in the hands of mages and adventurers of the Sword Coast North , despite the mage's timely demise when the Adventurer's Academy was attacked in 1372 DR.
