Thiam was a town in Laothan in Malatra .

Geography

It was situated on Laothan's northern coast, on the shore of the Eastern Sea south of the Hanchi Shoals . A trail ran east along the coast to Sayan .

Notable Locations

Outside Thiam stood a monastery to the Ku Nien school of the Path of Enlightenment . It was believed to be a holy site, among the oldest known to the Seng , where legend held that a monk known as Tulang shifted a hill and made a spring.
