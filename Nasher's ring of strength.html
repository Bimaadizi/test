Nasher's ring of strength was an enchanted ring that once belonged to Lord Nasher Alagondar of Neverwinter .

Powers

Nasher's ring of strength possessed a straightforward magic that improved the wearer's strength significantly.

History

Lord Nasher Alagondar wore his magic ring during his adventuring days, and it saved his life on several occasions, earning Nasher's fondness for the trinket. In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Nasher gifted the ring to the Hero of Neverwinter , after the conclusion of the Luskan –Neverwinter war and the city being cured of the Wailing Death . It was believed that Nasher's ring of strength was part of a four-item set that was used by Lord Nasher in the past and that if the items were used together, they unlocked each other's additional magical benefits.
