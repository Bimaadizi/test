The Grainhouse was an ancient landmark and the guildhall of the Granary Workers' Guild of Purskul and Outlying Villages in Amn .

Structure

The Grainhouse was one of the oldest structures in the town of Purskul . It was a large stone tower, a gristmill , and a granary.

History

By the late 14 th century DR , the Grainhouse was claimed by the Granary Workers' Guild of Purskul and Outlying Villages and used as its guildhall. The landmark mill produced cornflour made using local corn , worked by the half-tusk population of Purskul.
