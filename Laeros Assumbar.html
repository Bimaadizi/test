Lord Laeros Assumbar was the patriarch of the Assumbar noble family of Waterdeep during the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Laeros funded the majority of the Chapel and Chalice of the Divine Right 's expenses. The Chapel, which was located within the Assumbar Villa , was dedicated to Siamorphe . Laeros was pleased with the positive attention the Chapel and Siamorphe's worship brought to his family.

Relationships

Laeros married Lady Kerri of the Gulderhorn family. Their children were Harlaa and Myklos . Laeros' sister was Circe Assumbar .
