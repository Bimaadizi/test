Antara o' Bones was a Shaaran human cleric of Kelemvor in Lapaliiya in the 14 th century DR . As of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , she was the ruler of Sheirlantar , the Lapaliiyan city dedicated to Kelemvor, holding the title of Keeper of Crypts, having been appointed by the Overking of Lapaliiya .
