Crypt Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Crypt Street was located in the southwest corner of Zhentil Keep within the Common Quarter . The road ran west to east roughly parallel to the outer southern wall of the city, originating on Beggars' Way and ending at the wall of the Harbor District . Crypt Street was intersected by Dixon Street and Brigand Way .
