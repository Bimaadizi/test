Jondalar was a mage living in Candlekeep during the mid–14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , the sage Gorion asked Jondalar to train his foster son Abdel Adrian the finer skills of combat. He and his friend Erik taught Abdel how to fight one–on–one while being shot at with arrows .
