Ganilon was a doomguide who lived in the city of Neverwinter during the late 15 th century DR .

History

After being assigned to Neverdeath Graveyard , circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Ganilon and his fellow doomguides were lured into an area referred to as the Rift and assaulted by Red Wizards and their undead minions. While some of the Kelemvorites were killed, Ganilon, Severin , and Winlof were all being in crypt's in the Pauper's Field cemetery of Neverdeath under the orders of Valindra Shadowmantle , so they could be used for dark Thayan ritual s.

Ganilon was rescued by an adventurer who was hired by the senior doomguide, Volahk . Ganilon was the last of the group to be rescued.
