Harheldur ( pronounced : / h ɑːr ˈ h ɛ l d ɜːr / har-HELL-dur ) was a small, little-known kingdom of dwarves in east Faerûn . It was home to the Harheld clan of dwarves.

Geography

The realm lay beneath the easternmost Sunrise Mountains , near Thay and Murghôm .

Trade

As of the 15 th century DR , Harheldur traded with Murghôm but not with Thay, producing and exporting salt , bowls, small tools, and hinges.
