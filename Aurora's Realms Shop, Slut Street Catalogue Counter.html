Aurora's Realms Shop, Slut Street Catalogue Counter was an outlet of the Aurora's Emporium retail chain in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep in the midâ€“14 th century DR . It was one of six such outlets in the city.

Location

It was located on Zastrow Street (formerly Slut Street), next door to the Purple Palace festhall (with a narrow side alley separating them) and two doors up from the Old Xoblob Shop .

Description

It was located in a three-story-high tenement building of unremarkable appearance.

Services

This shop sold the various items found in Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue . Opening hours were dawn to dusk.

The nearby Old Xoblob Shop was not a competitor for the catalogue counter, as the curio shop sold everything Aurora's did not, could not, or would not.

In the late 1400s DR, the store was a good place to get rumors, especially on local shipping at the docks.

Staff

In the mid-1360s DR , Aglatha Shrey worked here as a counter clerk and Beradyx Halfwinter was a transportation mage who delivered goods to the store by teleporting them. In addition, there were two brawny guards who worked during opening hours.

History

In the late 1480s DR , crew of the Sea Queen's Fury bought trade goods in bulk at this Aurora's Emporium.
