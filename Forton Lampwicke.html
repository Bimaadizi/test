Forton Lampwicke was a human resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

Forton was an elderly man with a stooped posture and short white hair.

History

While living in Eveningstar during the drow raids , Forton pessimistically predicted the Battle for Eveningstar .
