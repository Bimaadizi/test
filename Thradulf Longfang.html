Thradulf Longfang was a chieftain of the Red Tiger tribe of Uthgardt barbarians living in the Cold Wood in the midâ€“14 th century DR . He led the tribe with a heartless demeanor and his wrath struck fear into his own people.

Although he had the authority as chieftain of the Red Tigers, he was in reality a puppet of the tribe's shamans .

Personality

He was known among his own tribe as a particularly cruel individual.

History

Thradulf was born the nephew to Adalwulf Longfang , a wise and long-ruling leader of the Cold Wood barbarians. His cousin Shinoras , Adalwulf's son, was in line to succeed his father as ruler but was killed while out on a hunting expedition. Thradulf, who had become a strong voice among the tribesmen, became Adalwulf's heir and ascended as chieftain in 1368 DR .
