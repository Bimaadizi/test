Tapdragon was a variety of strong molasses and sugarcane -distilled clear alcoholic beverage from Faerûn .

Usages

Tapdragon could be used in the mixing of the Yeti's breath manyslake found in Suzail in the mid-14 th century DR . This ice-cold cocktail was made with crushed ice, tapdragon, darsargar (powdered ginger root), and menthol , which was made from wild peppermint plant's oil.
