"One-Arm" Mc Ginty was a former thief , and the proprietor of The Red Dragon Tavern in Freedale during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was a one-armed man.

Personality

One-Arm Mc Ginty was a sneaky sort who listened in on things habitually.

Activities

Before losing his arm, he was a thief who visited a number of dungeons. Afterwards, he became a tavernkeeper.

Relationships

He was friends with Slinker , and often chatted about the latest news with him.

History

One-Arm Mc Ginty lost an arm while delving in a dungeon, and opened the tavern after that.
