Valin Geldencross was a diviner from Bryn Shander .

Activities

Valin was hired by Elytharra Dinnesmore of Targos to locate a goblin headquarters when the Legion of the Chimera attacked in 1312 DR . He fell ill when he hit a scrying barrier made by Isair and his orc shamans.

Valin was revived of his visions when given a dose of Braegh . Though his visions, he was able to warn adventurers of the danger near the Western Pass .
