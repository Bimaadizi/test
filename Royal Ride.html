The Royal Ride was a wide street in the city of Suzail , Cormyr , that began at the easternmost part of the Promenade and was the location of the city's most wealthy noble villas and estates.

Description

The Royal Ride circled Castle Obarskyr 's Royal Gardens to the north and ended reaching the front of the Marliir Mansion and the street that ran perpendicular to The Royal Ride and merged with the madly curving Court Close further south. The entire length of the Ride was dotted with the mansions of Suzail's most established and oldest families. The eastern end of the the street was the place of Suzail's most prominent temples, separated from the manors by the bridge over the Jaws of Lake Azoun .
