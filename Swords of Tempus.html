The Swords of Tempus was a mercenary company, active in the Western Heartlands , Northwest Faerûn , and the Sword Coast as of the late 14 th century DR .

History

One of the most well-known Swords of Tempus was Astar Graysteel , an ex- Blade of Mulmaster . Astar spent several years with the company after leaving home in the Moonsea sometime in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR, before the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR .
