Jaddat Jamila was the human ruler of Madinat al-Mumin in the Steaming Isles in 1367 DR .

Appearance

Jaddat Jamila was an aged woman. She was very lean and had gray hair. Jaddat never wore jewelry of any kind, believing it to be a sign of vanity.

Activities

She spent most of her time discussing town matters with citizens since Madinat al-Mumin did not have a formal court.

Possessions

Jaddat owned a mahogany cane that she used as a walking aid.

Relationships

Jaddat was a direct descendant of Jamila bint-Susan the Virtuous. She was married to Jadd Ala'i .
