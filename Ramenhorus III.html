Ramenhorus III was the twelfth pharaoh of Mulhorand .

History

Ramenhorus III was born in 941 DR . He ascended to the throne of pharaoh of Mulhorand in 955 DR .

Ramenhorus III's reign lasted until 1098 DR , when he died fighting the Red Wizards of Thay as they made their first attempt to invade Mulhorand.
