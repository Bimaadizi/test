History

Jhalikoe participated in the Open Feast held at Castle Obarskyr on Mirtul 26 of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , where they briefly entertained the envoy from Milvarune , discussing subjects such as the Silent Shadow , Skult , and Vandarl .
