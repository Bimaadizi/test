Mac Sheehan was a small town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Mac Sheehan was located in the kingdom of Corwell just north of the Corwell Road in southern Gwynneth. The settlement sat in the foothills of the hidden kingdom of Synnoria along the southern end of the Cambro Mountains .
