A chisel was a simple and useful tool found in the Realms . One particular variation of it was called a graver .

Description

A chisel resembled a metal rod with a knife -like wedge at one end, able to be driven into material with a hammer -blow.

Usage

Chisels could be used in almost every trade that required material to be cut, etched, or chipped away, including metalworking, masonry, and woodcarving. They were also useful as part of thieves' tools for dealing with locks and climbing up walls.

A variation called tatami nomi was used by ninjas to chisel away the wood around locks .
