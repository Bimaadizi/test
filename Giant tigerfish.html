Description

The name "tigerfish" was not a reference to the fish's appearance, but rather its behavior: it was said to have the ferocity of a tiger .

Ecology

Giant tigerfish were one of the deadliest predators in the River Soshenstar . They hunted kapenta fish, which were common near the mouth of Chult's rivers near Port Nyanzaru . As such, giant tigerfish were known to attack Chultan fishers as they angled for kapenta.

History

During the Amnian occupation of Port Nyanzaru in the 15 th century DR , foreign sport fishers were known to travel to Chult seeking to land a giant tigerfish.
