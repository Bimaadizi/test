Aqua regia , otherwise known as royal water , was a type of highly corrosive acid in the Realms that was prized by alchemists and chemists.

Properties

This substance was orange in color. Aqua regia was so potent an acid that it could melt through even gold and platinum , one of the few, if not only, known acids in the Realms capable of it. The acid was extremely dangerous to touch or consume, and tasted terrible.

Crafting

Aqua regia could be created by distilling aqua fortis and sal ammoniac with nitre .
