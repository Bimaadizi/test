Ardic was the son of Count Santele and Countess Mirnielle . He had a good friend in Mateo , a soldier in the Tethyrian army.

History

During the siege of Saradush, in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Ardic was abducted by the traitorous thief Kiser Jhaeri . Kiser used Ardic's incarceration to blackmail Countess Santele to frame Mateo for the traitorous crime for which Kiser was responsible.
