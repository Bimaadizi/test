Jallian Horgontivar was a cleric of Lathander living in the small village of Feather Falls in the late 14 th century DR . Jallian had achieved the rank of Morninglord and headed the temple known as the House of Morning , the only temple of significance in Featherdale at the time. Under his order were 16 other priests and acolytes who also served Lathander.
