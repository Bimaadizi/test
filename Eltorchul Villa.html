Eltorchul Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Eltorchul and the home of the Eltorchul Academy .

Location

The Eltorchul Villa was located in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep , situated on the southwest corner of Diamond Street and Ivory Street , and enclosed from behind by Pharra's Alley and Torch Lane . Along Ivory Street, it stood across from the Gauntyl Villa .

Structure

This building complex consisted of two high-quality buildings, one that was three stories tall and another that stood a single story tall, one being the mansion proper and the other a guesthouse. Like many Waterdhavian villas, it was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden.

Interior

One of the buildings of this estate housed a quite impressive library with a great deal of magical information, rivaled only by those of Maskar Wands and Khelben Arunsun .

History

In the early 14 th century DR , inheriting the fortune his family had garnered over the years through imports and spices , Lord Thesp Eltorchul began to run a school for magic out of this family villa entitled Eltorchul Academy.
