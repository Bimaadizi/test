Lady Polaris was the archwizard leader of Delia in the Netherese Empire during the Shadowed Age .

Description

The Netherese female was attractive with golden skin and snow white hair. However as Shadowed Age reached its cresendo her once thin and defined frame mutated to obesity.

Relationships

The mentalist enjoyed bossing around her steward , Candlemas , and her chamberlain , Sysquemalyn .

History

Circa −700 DR , Lady Polaris was concerned with a blight known as rust appearing on the wheat crops of her people.

Around −339 DR , Lady Polaris succumbed to fear and perished in her castle during the End of the End .
