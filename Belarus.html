Belarus was a loyal warrior who worshiped the Lord of Battles, Tempus .

History

In ancient times, Belarus was stationed at a fortress where they fought in honor of Tempus. Much time after the devout warrior passed, the Abbey of the Sword was founded upon this hold after the Time of Troubles .
