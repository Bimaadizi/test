Dherk was the leader of the Razorhides in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Dherk was a tall man in his twenties, with many tattoos and scars. He wore spiked leather armor and kept his arms bared.

Personality

A hardened criminal, Dherk was bloodthirsty, but not without fear and he could be cowed into compliance. His loyalty to those stronger than him only lasted until someone stronger came by, however.

Abilities

He could throw knives very quickly and precisely, finding his target's throat before they even saw the knife.

History

The warlock Japheth Donard strong-armed Dherk into tributing him thirty percent of the daily profits of the Razorhides at some point in 1396Â DR , leaving behind a quickly made construct . The arrangement lasted a few tendays, until Raidon Kane 's party came looking for the warlock; Dherk managed to strike Seren Juramot in the throat with a poisoned knife before the battle began. After a short fight where she still managed to bring down Japheth's construct, Dherk told them where to find their target.

Rumors & Legends

Dherk was rumored to have killed a whole family by blocking the exits to their home and then setting fire to it.
