A wand of illumination was a magic item capable of producing light.

Powers

These wands mimicked the effects of several wizard spells including dancing lights (1 charge), light (1 charge), continual light (2 charges), and sunburst (3 charges).

A wand of illumination could be recharged.

Notable Owners

Akim al-Kalaas , a sha'ir of the Unclean in Zakhara , carried a wand of illumination .
