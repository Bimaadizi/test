Siren Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Siren Street was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward . It branched south off of Ship Street one building south of the intersection with Odd Street , passing through a block of buildings south and west to end on Dock Street on the waterfront.
