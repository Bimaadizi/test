Description

Oatcakes were a common type of food that dated back to the age of ancient Netheril . There, oatcakes were sprinkled with salt and served with maple syrup , dried herring , game meat such as bear , deer , or bison , and accompanied with water or ale .

Oatcakes and fruit was a morningfeast served in the town of Sundabar in the Silver Marches , fried to order on a griddle. These breads were also available in Arabel 's The Hungry Man eatery, and in establishments in Calimshan .

Barleycakes were also considered to be staples of dwarven cuisine.
