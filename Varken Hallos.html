Varken Hallos was a mage agent of the Zhentarim who resided in the city of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Varken Hallos rented a small apartment in Ravens Bluff sometime before the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR . He kept his affiliation with the hated Zhentarim secret and worked covertly to further the organization's goals as a hireling and a middleman. On the 16 th of Flamerule , Varken was tasked by infuriated Zhent Threll Whitebane to find a band of adventurers or mercenaries to recover a lockbox with a Book of Vile Darkness from the House of Mystery of Ravens Bluff. Servants of Mystra , in turn, stole the Baneinte Book of Vile Darkness earlier the same day from the Black Network. Varken Hallos instructed mercenaries with written instructions: "Recover the tome at any cost. You must get it to Keldar's Shrine ." One of the mercenaries was Ulijaz Thenumbrin , who was offered hundreds of golden pieces upfront and thousands later upon the book's delivery. None of Varken's mercenaries knew of the mage's name and only knew of his apartment address and description. He used the whispering wind wind spell to communicate with his men. However, Varken's attempts were unsuccessful and a band of goodly adventurers succeeded in delivering the Book of Vile Darkness to an extra-dimensional vault in the village of Dragon Falls .
