The River Mirar , known as the Tanlur to the ancient dwarves , was a river in the North .

Geography

The river traveled down from the Spine of the World and through the Valley of Khedrun , until finally ending in Luskan where its waters caused the city to be separated into two parts.

Further upstream, Mirabar was situated on the river's north bank.

History

Mirabar was known to hold a 20 miles (32,000 meters) long white water khyek race on this river during their annual Midsummer festival.
