Knight Vision Goggles were enchanted goggles that likely fell into the possession of several adventurers in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

This magical item took the form of goggles used for eye protection while working in workshops.

Powers

Using this item allowed the owner to look like a knight and nullify one attack. However, this ability had varying recovery periods before it could be used again.

History

In the late 15 th century DR , such goggles could be found in Neverwinter , Gauntlgrym , Mount Hotenow , and Neverwinter Wood . They were also available for purchase at shops in those locations. Such items were likely found by the adventurers that stopped Deathsleep , the green dragon .
