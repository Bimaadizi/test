Al-Anf , also called The Nose , was a small easterly island found in the Genie's Turban archipelago in the Corsair Domains of northwest Zakhara around 1367 DR .

Al-Anf and Al-Oyun were collectively referred to as the "Beauty Marks" by local corsairs . Ever distrustful of strangers, the corsairs would use the term as a form of password to test outsiders.

Geography

The island was the farthest southeast point of the Genie's Turban. It was a small forested island measuring roughly 15 miles (24 kilometers) in diameter.
