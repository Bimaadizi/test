Lady Kessemer was a noblewoman in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Lady Kessemer was known to be hatching some plot in the early 1370s DR . In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Vangerdahast surprised Queen Filfaeril Obarskyr of Cormyr by mentioning he knew of Lady Kessemer's plot, while discussing another issue.
