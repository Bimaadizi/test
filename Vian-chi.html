The Vian-chi was a secret rebel army commanded by Baron Mukow Jian of Pim Tung in Tabot by Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) .

Mukow's wife, Lady Holang disguised herself as a man and joined the Vian-chi, and trained to fight as a ninja .
