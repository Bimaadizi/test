The magistrate's scepter was an ornate heavy mace found in the Storm Horns of Cormyr in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The mace resembled a large, heavily-built and decorated rod with a bulbous head.

Powers

The scepter was powerfully aligned with law , allowing it strike down chaotic foes.

History

The mace was given to Cormyrean adventurers after fighting Shadovar forces at Yarrow Glade in the Storm Horns.
