Kolbaz was an old and cantankerous mage and a member of the Harpers in Yartar sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

Activities

Kolbaz lived in a villa owned by the Harpers in Yartar. His duty was to protect one of teleportation circles of Krowen Valharrow 's network. He always cast a hallucinatory terrain spell in order to frighten away strangers.
