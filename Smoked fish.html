Smoked fish was a common way of cooking and preserving fish used across Faerûn as preserved provisions, or consumed as a simple meal with bread , cheese , and stew .

Description

Smoked fish was a savory succulent food, delicious and salty .

Availability

Regular preserved smoked fish was common in Altumbel , other nations on the shores of the Sea of Fallen Stars , the Moonsea , the Sword Coast , and even as far as the Hordelands and the Spice Road . In Waterdeep , smoked fish was among customary gifts exchanged during the annual Last Sheaf feast on Uktar 20 .

The town of Good Mead in Icewind Dale produced smoked knucklehead trout , fished out of the region frigid lakes. The town of Aurilssbarg on the Ice Peak Island was no different, smoking fish caught in the Sea of Swords .

In West Faerûn , smoked fish can be commonly found served at the Arnise Hold near Athkatla , where it was fried up with bacon and served as hack . And in the village of Kalathtyr in Amn , smoked fish tarts were one of the mainstay dishes. Smoked fish and crab was used as a squid stuffing in the town of Tulmene in Tethyr . Smoked fish was consumed as far south as the village of Eichul in the mage nation of Halruaa .

Sarkul was a regional variety of smoked fish beloved in Tantras . It was made with the use of honey and seasonings.

Smoked salmon was one of the most well-known types of smoked fish, found from Waterdeep to Cormyr. And smoked barbfin was a favorite smoked fish of Elminster .
