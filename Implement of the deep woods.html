The implement of the deep woods was a magical staff used by the druids of the King's Forest in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The staff was a traditional druidic implement, and greatly empowered the spells of the wielder that worked with fire, lightning, and healing. It also protected the wielder from electrical, fiery, freezing, and corrosive threats, and could cast restoration once per day.

History

The staff was held by the local druids during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar . Arcton Greenwort may have given it to a worthy sort who proved their value during the conflict.
