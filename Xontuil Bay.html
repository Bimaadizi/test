Xontuil Bay was a bay in the Golden Water , lying between Durpar and Ulgarth in southeast Faerûn . It was fed by the river Xon . It was also known as Xonaluir Bay and the Narrow Seas .

Politics

The heavily defended and fortified border between Durpar and Ulgarth lay in Xontuil Bay and along the river Xon.

Locations

The settlements of Suormpar and Ravvan lay on the Ulgarthan side of the bay.
