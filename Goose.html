The goose ( pl : geese ) was a domesticated species of fowl, widely bred across the Realms and had many uses - from food to pillow stuffing.

Description

A well-fattened goose before slaughter could fetch a price between 8 silver coins and 1 gold .

Species of Geese

Ecology

Geese were domesticated and used as a food source not only by humanoids , but also verbeegs .

Habitats

Geese were widely domesticated across the Realms, including Shadowdale , Phlan in the Moonsea region, Selgaunt in Sembia , Turmish , Moonshae Isles , Delimbiyr Vale , Hartsvale of the Ice Spires , Innarlith in the Lake of Steam region, the Silver Marches , Rashemen , Thar , Halruaa , and the ancient city of Cursrah in the Calim Empire , as well as Calimshan .
