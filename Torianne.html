Torianne was a barmaid who worked in the Cracked Tankard tavern in the Crow's End district of Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late-14 th century DR .

History

They kept an eye on the suspicious customers and were useful to a group of the Living City adventurers, informing them of suspicious Zhentarim -type mercenaries who had recently been seen at the Tankard. Torianne also stated that the Crow's End district had been in decline of late, with many businesses closing due to the lack of city guard patrols.
