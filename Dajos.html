Dajos was a Captain of the Elite Guard from the city of Samargol in Samarach , circa 1374 DR .

Description

Captain Dajos was a tall human male with slicked-back brown hair. He was a strict man with a commanding presence, and he took his job seriously. Dajos wore polished brass armor that indicated his rank.

Activities

Dajos owned books published by Volothamp Geddarm . Not surprisingly, he did not hold the author in high esteem.

History

Circa the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Captain Dajos supervised Samargol Elite Guard Patrols within the city and outside its walls. His job included routing out all traces of yuan-ti corruption with extreme prejudice at the orders of the nation's High Phantasmage . In 1374 DR, one of Dajos's patrols brought back survivors of the Vigilant shipwreck on the Omgar's Teeth . The outsiders were to be brought in and interrogated. However, the famed author Volothamp Geddarm was among the survivors, and shortly after their arrival, a respected merchant Sa'Sani vouched for the new arrivals, sparing them from Samarach's law. Captain Dajos's reluctance was swayed by Sa'Sani's threat of angering the High Phantasmage.

Sometime later, when one of Sa'Sani's aides was revealed to be a yuan-ti infiltrator, Captain Dajos was dispatched to inform her that the Council revoked her authority and commanded the merchant woman to leave Samargol my the following day's sunset. The laws that dealt with the much-feared yuan-ti threat were an immediate danger, forcing Sa'Sani to flee for the Sword Coast .
