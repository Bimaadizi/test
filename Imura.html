Imura was a rakshasa who traveled the deserts of Anauroch during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Imura ventured across the desert with their traveling companion Sikkat , disguised as nomadic Bedine . They attempted to lure travelers to their doom, by offering them guidance to a "magical oasis".
