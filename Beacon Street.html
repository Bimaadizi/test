Beacon Street was a hard-packed dirt and gravel paved alleyway in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This alley ran in a straight line from north to south, situated along blocks of buildings between Blackmul Street and the Way of the Dragon . It stood directly south of Shoor Street , being connected to its western end by a short unnamed alley.

Notable Locations

Links to Sewers

An entrance to the sewers of Waterdeep could be found at the midpoint of this alleyway.

Notable Inhabitants

Quite a number of retired adventurers lived in the block of buildings between Beacon Street and Blackmul Street.
