The Firebird was an elemental of air and fire that was part of a cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame that operated out of Sambral , one of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond , in 1479 DR .

Description

The Firebird was completely made out of fire.

History

In 1479 DR, the Firebird was kept as Pyrultimus ' pet in a house of the cell of the cult in Sambral, under the care of a cultist named Bazamarl . To feed it, Bazamarl kidnapped citizens from Sambral on a regular basis.

The Firebird was killed by a band of adventurers that were sent by Halagothra the Healer to Sambral to destroy the cell of the cult operating there, while trying to rescue the doppleganger Iphimedra .
