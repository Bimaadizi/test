Ragged Dain was the Captain of the Guard in Citadel Felbarr in 1479 DR .

History

He was a friend of his colleague, Reginald Roundshield Sr. , so Ragged became a mentor his son, the young Reginald Roundshield (in truth, Bruenor Battlehammer reincarnated). The younger Reginald served under Ragged as a caravan escort.

In 1484 DR , at the start of the war with orcs and drow , he was one of the military leaders convicted by king Emerus Warcrown
