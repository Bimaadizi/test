Murtil Dragonsbane was the King of Bloodstone in the mid-to-late 15 th century DR , and the last of the Dragonsbane dynasty.

History

He likely ruled from the assassination of Brianne Dragonsbane in the Year of the Forged Sigil, 1459 DR until the Year of the Elven Swords Returned, 1465 DR , when he died as a childless bachelor. Many believed that Yarin Frostmantle murdered him in order to become king, and indeed Murtil had been killed by Yarin's favorite hitman, Rafer Ingot .

Murtil's reign saw the beginning of the end of the unified Kingdom of Bloodstone when the Warlock Knights of Vaasa rebelled in the Year of the Malachite Shadows, 1460 DR .
