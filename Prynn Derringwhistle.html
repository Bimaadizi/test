Prynn Derringwhistle was a barnacle scraper who worked in the docks of Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR . He was distinctive in that he wore spectacles and enjoyed singing old sea shanties.

Prynn was a patron of the Low Lantern tavern in the district of Brampton .
