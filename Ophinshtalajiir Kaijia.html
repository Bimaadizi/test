Ophinshtalajiir Kaijia was a dragonborn of Tymanther and the matriarch of Clan Ophinshtalajiir in 1486 DR .

Description

Kaijia had patina-green scales.

History

In 1486 DR, Kaijia sought out Verthisathurgiesh Anala and Shestandeliath Geshthax in order to investigate a series of murders in the Catacombs of Djerad Thymar .
