Strelsi was a small town located along the shores of the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Geography

Strelsi was found near the center of the southern shore of the Lake of Mists in between Antoumek to the east and Budennov to the west. It was located approximately 30 miles (48 kilometers) north of the Golden Way .
