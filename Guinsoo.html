Guinsoo was a magical dagger that could be thrown at enemies.

Description

This unique weapon was found in the upper Waterdeep sewers. The Heroes of Waterdeep , having entered the first floor of the sewers, may have come across a collection of magical scrolls on a shelf. The enchanted dagger, Guinsoo , appeared by placing an ordinary dagger on to the shelf. The magical weapon had a +4 enchantment.
