Hamarhraft was an enchanted maul of dwarven origin that had been imbued with the power of thunder.

Description

The great hammer had an inscription, written in dwarvish , that alluded to the importance of making a strong first impression.

Powers

Whenever the wielded of Hamarhraft leapt or jumped onto the group, their landing was followed by a thunderous wave that extended out 9.84 ft (3 m).

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Hamarhraft could be found within a locked chest in one of the upper floors of the Waukeen's Rest inn. It was not entirely clear who remained in possession of the hammer.
