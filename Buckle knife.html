Buckle knives were a variety of enchanted concealed knives with a unique hilt.

Description

These knives were so named because their hilt resembled an ornament typically found on belt buckles. Because of this, buckles were also typically used like a sheath to conceal them.

Powers

Buckle knives inflicted far more damage than a mundane knife, with enchantment bonuses ranging from +1 to +4 .
