A glow ring was a magical ring that gave off a soft glow, useful in nighttime thieving and other illicit activities.

Description

It was a silver ring set with an opal . A command word was typically inscribed within the band.

Powers

Activated and deactivated by the whispered command word, the opal radiated a cool, dim glow, similar to moonlight, to about 3 inches (0.076 meters). It was enough to read by or perform detailed work, and could not be seen from far away. It did not work in magical darkness .

Usage

The glow ring was made specifically for rogues to open locks and conduct spying.

Notable owners

Several glow rings were known to exist in Faerûn , typically in the possession of leaders of thieves' guilds. The master thief known as Silverfox had a glow ring .

History

The Halruaan mage Daltim Flamefist wrote details of the glow ring in his spellbook Daltim's Tome of Fire , which he lost in Tethyr in the 1360s DR .
