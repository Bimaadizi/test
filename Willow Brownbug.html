Willow Brownbug was a druid who worked as an apothecary in the city of Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR . Willow was a regular at the Elfsong Tavern as of the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR .

Willow was distinguishable by both a haughty disposition and a vividly colored cape.
