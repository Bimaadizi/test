Gingrid was a strange follower of Myrkul who lived in the Underchasm in 1486 DR .

History

Gingrid resided in a castle in Underchasm, just above the earth primordial Grumbar 's temple, waiting for a sign from her presumed-lost god. In 1486 DR, she met a group of Chosen : Arietta Seasilver , follower of Siamorphe ; Malik el Sami , follower of Cyric ; Joelle Emmeline , follower of Sune ; and Kleef Kenric , paladin follower of the dead god Helm . Gingrid helped them, telling them how to get down to the temple.
