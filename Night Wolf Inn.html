The Night Wolf Inn was an inn located in the city of Arabel in Cormyr . It was considered an inn of lesser quality and was notable for being the place where Cyric once stayed during the Godswar .

Location

The inn was located outside the city walls north of the High Road just before it passed through High Horn Gate into the city. It was across a path from Mother Lahamma's House , a boarding house.
