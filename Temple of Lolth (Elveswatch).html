The Temple of Lolth was a temple devoted to Lolth , the Spider Queen, located in the small city of Elveswatch in northern Dambrath during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The Temple to Lolth was the largest temple found in Elveswatch, perhaps because attacks from elves from the Forest of Amtar brought a large influx of drow to the city.
