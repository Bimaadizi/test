Quon Tok was a bushi in Shou Lung .

History

In 1358 DR , at the komite in Suijeng , Quon Tok picked fights for a living. He was helped by his companion Jo Chuik , who organized bets about Quon's fighting. Obviously, Quon signaled to Jo Chuik if he thought he would win or lose so his companion could make the right bets.
