Oren Yogilvy was a halfling musician in the Goldenfields in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Oren resided permanently at the Northfurrow's End inn. After becoming drunk, he wandered the fields in search of inspiration.

History

Oren was at the inn when giants attacked the city after 1485 DR .

Personality

Oren truly lived for and by his music. Sometimes he got into trouble but his music had always saved him from harm.
