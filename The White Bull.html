The White Bull was a tavern found in the town of Aithelar in Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles in Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR .

Location

The tavern was located near the center of town at the intersection of Callidyrr Ride and Aerynn's Street .

Activities

The White Bull provided a fine dining experience to its patrons.
