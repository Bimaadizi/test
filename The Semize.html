The Semize was a back alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

The Semize was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . It branched south off of Gawenknife Street , passing through a neighborhood that ran along the wall of the North Ward .
