The Hand of Azuth was a temple dedicated to the god of mages found along the Lake of Steam in the coastal city of Yhep .

Inhabitants

Lydeia Harauntan served as the high priestess of the Hand of Azuth during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . The temple was also the chosen house of worship for Yhep's ruling family.
