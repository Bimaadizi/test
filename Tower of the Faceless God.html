The Tower of the Faceless God was a large structure within the Yikarian Empire dedicated to the Faceless God in 1367 DR .

Location

The Tower was found in the capital city of Nathong , specifically within the Emperor's Quarters .

Description

Easily the tallest structure within Nathong, the Tower soared high above the rest of the city. A massive bronze bell decorated the top of the Tower. The bell was believed to weigh at least two tons.
