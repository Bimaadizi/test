Sherbet was a type of common and cheap drink that originated from the land of Zakhara .

Description

Sherbet was a sweet drink made out of fruits and served chilled. It was a staple in all major cities in Zakhara, sold in shared reused tankards by street vendors out of big cooled tanks off their backs. Men of wealth, including those in the city of Liham , were served chilled sherbet-filled ewers or as an appetizer by the caliphs of the Palace of Sly Ravens . Sherbet was also sometimes served as ice.

Availability

Sherbet was exported into Sembia and its capital of Selgaunt .
