Olturret was a wayhamlet along the Rattlebones Ride in Sembia . It did not appear on most maps as of the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Geography

It lay about halfway between Mulhessen to the south and Saerb to the north, and was very near to the intersection of the Rattlebones Ride and Thelnwalk .
