Choris Steelsar was an unfortunate child of the exiled Amnian Parhek dynasty.

Relationships

Choris was the child of Princess Rasa Parhek Dornar Steelsar , and was the eldest sibling of Rosa , Rohav , and Rhothar .

History

Choris was born in Silverymoon , and was only 10 years old in the Year of the Singing Skull, 1297 DR when the family was attacked by assassins from Amn. Dornar and Choris fought to the death to allow the others to escape, with only Rasa and Rhothar fleeing while Rohav was kidnapped and Rosa was killed.
