Mannus was a house guard for the noble Seasilver family of Marsember during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He was a rather tall and gangly fellow.

History

When the city of Marsember was invaded by the Shadovar , on Uktar 2 of the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Mannus failed to heed the warning of the servant Odelia , leaving Lady Arietta alone with two shades. He and his fellow guard Suther were under strict orders not to yet the young noblewoman leave her family's estate in the city. They both failed in their duties.
