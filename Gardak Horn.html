Gardak Horn was a shield dwarf mage who served as the Mage Defender of the Wyrm's Rock fortress in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , the old commander of Wyrm's Rock, Skorpin Crane , had passed away in his sleep. With Ulder Ravengard in Elturel and unable to appoint a replacement, Gardak assumed responsibility, using his personal homunculus to spy upon the garrison, for which his troops hated him.
