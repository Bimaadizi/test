Oparil was a bard and innkeep in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR before that city's desttruction.

Activities

Oparil owned an inn called Oparil's Tower which was located in the Harbor District along Barge Street . She hired the best singers and performers in Zhentil Keep to provide live entertainment within her inn.
