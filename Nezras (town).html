Nezras was a village located near the eastern border of the country of Mulhorand during the mid–14 th century DR .

Geography

Nezras lay along the eastern foothills of the Sword Mountains on the fringes of both the Plains of Purple Dust and Fuirgar . The Lonely Lake was located due north of the village.

Inhabitants

Nezras was inhabited by a group of wizards descended from the mighty Nezram in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .
