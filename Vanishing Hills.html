The Vanishing Hills were a grouping of hills located along the northern coast of the Moonsea region of North Faerûn .

Geography

They were located in the area of the Moonsea referred to some as the Moonsea North .

Geographical Features

The hills were the source of the Trank River , which ran south and opened up into the Twilight Marsh and the actual Moonsea .
