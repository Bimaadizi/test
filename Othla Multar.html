Othla Multar was a sage from the city of Spandeliyon in Altumbel in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Othla Multar studied the legends of Adhe Wood in the Vast and theorized that the many mutant spiders there were bred by drow , who released them to guard their passage to the local Underdark , from which they occasionally emerged and crossed into the Tsurlar Forest to trade with pirate vessels that anchored in hidden coves.
