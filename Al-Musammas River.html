Al-Musammas River was found in the far southeast corner of the Cities of the Pantheon in interior Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Geography

Al-Musammas River originated in the center of the southern fork of Al-Sayaj Mountains . The river flowed south and emptied into the Crowded Sea . The great city of Mahabba was found along its western bank.
