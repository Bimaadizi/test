The Maelstrom's Notch was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The inn stood two stories tall.

Location

The Maelstrom's Notch was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the east side of Mhaer's Alley just north of Spendthrift Alley .
