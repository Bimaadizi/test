Deelin was a dwarf wizard who lived in Neverwinter during the late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Like almost all the other students of the Neverwinter Academy , Deelin was fond of Lady Aribeth and was grateful that she didn't blame all the wizards during the spreading of the Wailing Death and other events in Neverwinter.

Relationships

During the graduation ceremony in 1372 DR, Deelin was chatting with other students, Kiki and Fonlim .

History

Deelin was a student of the Neverwinter Academy and finished her studies in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . Deelin was one of the eight students, among whom was the future Hero of Neverwinter , present at the graduation ceremony of that year. During the ceremony led by Lady Aribeth, the Academy was attacked and she fought against the mysterious mages in the Graduation Chamber. It wass not known if Deelin survived the attack.
