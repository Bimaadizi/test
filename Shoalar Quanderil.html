Shoalar Quanderil was a high-ranking member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Personality

Shoalar always appeared jovial, but with a biting sense of humor and tending to laugh at people rather than with them. He saw Olhydra 's power as a means to become rich.

History

Shoalar was once a pirate captain on the Sword Coast and a Crushing Wave cultist. In 1491 DR , he transferred to the Dessarin Valley , where he was involved in an assault on the Mirabar diplomatic delegation. However, at Ironford bridge, he was confronted and defeated by some adventurers hired to find the delegation.
