Hathfell was an ettin inhabiting Oman's Isle within the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Hathfell was granted rulership over the ruins of the Iron Keep after other giants on the isle slew the northlanders who previously lived there. Hathfell, along with his orc and ogre followers, were tasked with preventing the Iron Keep from falling into enemy hands. They trapped Iron Bay to prevent vessels from sailing toward the keep for an attack by sea.
