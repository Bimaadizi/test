Kuto's Gate was the westernmost city gate in Phlan on the Moonsea North in the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

This double gate was named after a landmark of Kuto's Well , found at the heart of reconstructed city of Phlan. The Troll Toss Tavern stood to the south, and a fortuneteller's shop was to the northeast of Kuto's Gate.

History

In the Year of the Morningstar, 1350 DR , Councilwoman Sasha of the Council of Ten , rendezvoused with the Heroes of Phan outside Kuto's Gate before her journey to the lands of Thar , towards Taydome's Keep .
