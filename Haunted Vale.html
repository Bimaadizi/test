The Haunted Vale was a vale in Athalantar during the 3 rd century DR .

Geography

The Haunted Vale was south and west of Heldon, and west of Darrigo Trumpettower 's farm, very close to it.

Flora & Fauna

History

In the Year of Bloodflowers, 234 DR , the Mystran priestess Elmara (actually Prince Elminster Aumar of Athalantar ) rescued the outlaw sorceress Myrjala Talithyn , who was being attacked at Darrigo Trumpettower's farm east of the Haunted Vale; she took her west into it first.

She was later trained by Myrjala, who assigned her as one task restoring nature on a ravine between the western end of the Haunted Vale and the Darthtil , where the land showed the scars of a long-gone fire. She succeeded at this task.
