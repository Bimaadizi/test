Arconi was a Netherese arcanist who was the Dean of Teachers at the Inventor's Conservatory in the enclave of Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Arconi oversaw and taught alongside the other seven resident teachers, made up of Dolci , Agassaz , Belair , Koler , Clayton , Ballarini , and Staufehaus .
