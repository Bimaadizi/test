Klaraunth was an alcoholic beverage made in Chessenta , Tethyr , and the Vilhon Reach in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Similar to vodka , klaraunth was distilled from barley .

It was a key ingredient in the manyslake known as flaming gullet .
