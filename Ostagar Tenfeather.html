Ostagar Tenfeather was the leader of the Uthgardt Black Raven tribe during the late 14 th century DR .

History

Tenfeather was nearing the end of his life as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . During this, many young barbarian warriors performed dangerous feats to prove themselves worthy of being the next chieftain.
