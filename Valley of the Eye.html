The Valley of the Eye was a dangerous valley that laid in the heart of the Deepwing Mountains , Vilhon Reach .

Description

Waters of the Vilhon Reach ran into the hidden Valley of the Eye. It was rumored to be a home to a colony of beholders , however, eyewitness account was scant. The valley was mentioned in a guide to the Vilhon Reach, written by Adamar Session, who chronicled his experiences and adventures over the course of twelve years in the region, before the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .
