The Green Goddess Falls were a series of waterfalls in Amn .

Description

The Falls were part of the River Rimril , as its waters flowed out of the Troll Mountains . The largest of the falls was 400 feet (120 meters) in height and poured over the cliffs on the west side of Eldath's Mount and into Duskwood Dell . Afterward, the waters continued cascading in a number of smaller falls and pools called the Steps .
