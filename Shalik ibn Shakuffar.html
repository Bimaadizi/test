Shalik ibn Shakuffar was a skilled sha'ir who created the Urn of the Blue Vishap for use as a genie prison .

History

Shalik used the urn to trap a marid called Merifa of the Pearl Lagoons. The urn eventually traveled outside of Zakhara , where the trapped marid was able to trick a blue dragon in changing places with her.
