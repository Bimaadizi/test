Shalane , also known as Shalana , was a small town in the Shalanar Barony of Erlkazar , a small kingdom east of Tethyr .

Description

Shalane was a small fishing town renowned for the best shipbuilders of all Erlkazar.

Geography

Shalane was a town on the Tethir Road , south of where it connected with the Pikemen's Folly . It stood on the northeastern end of the Deepwash lake known as the Shalane Lake .
