The Red Wizard's sight was a set of magical goggles found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Imbued with a certain vision for the future, these goggles aided a spellcaster with their magic, with particular focus on blasting foes with force or bludgeoning them with blunter methods.

History

The goggles were found after the baelnorn Phathroi had the Elves of Cormanthor raid a once-lost Cult of the Dragon base , where the Red Wizards of Kathul Sep performed necromantic rites upon the dracolich Karliath .
