Rippers were ancient enchanted longbows that were once wielded by half-elven scouts in Anauroch .

Powers

These bows possessed +3 enchantments and had the capability of delivering devastatingly mighty shots. Additionally, Rippers made their wielders immune to any mind-affecting abilities and spells .

History

The name of the master who crafted these longbows was long forgotten. Many Rippers were lost, stolen, and destroyed. By the late 14 th century DR , only a handful of Rippers were known to exist and a few could be found on the Sword Coast , in the city of Neverwinter , and in Elventree in the Moonsea region.
