Ruldegost Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Ruldegost .

Location

The villa was located in the western section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward on the southwest corner of where Grimwald's Way met the Street of Whispers . The west side of the villa also sat upon Seawatch Street .

Description

There were four buildings within the walled estate with gardens and a small pond in between. The two larger buildings sat in the northeast and northwest corners of the estate. The buildings were three or four stories tall.
