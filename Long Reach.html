The Long Reach , also called the Centaur Trail , was a trail that ran through the Vast , connecting the village of Orlimmin and the town of King's Reach .

From west to east, it began at Orlimmin, passed around the edge of the Highbank Forest , ran through Bambryn and across the plains to Sendrin , and ended at King's Reach at the foot of the Trollcrag Mountains . On the way, it linked with the Tantras Trail , the Hlintar Ride , Blaern's Trail , and the North Road .
