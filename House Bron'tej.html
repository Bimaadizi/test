House Bron'tej was a deceased drow house of Menzoberranzan .

History

In the Year of the Wall, 1227 DR , House Bron'tej was the 27 th house of Menzoberranzan. Later that year, House Barrison Del'Armgo gathered their mages and took down House Bron'tej. All that remained of the house was a pool of sludge, that was suspiciously similar to the house insignia of House Barrison Del'Armgo.
