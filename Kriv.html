Kriv was one of the few dragonborn wizards of Tymanther .

Description

Kriv had yellow eyes and bronze scales flecked with black.

History

In 1479 DR , Kriv was one of the wizards Vanquisher Tarhun sought to study the dragonspawn -controlling orbs created by Nala . Under Kriv's leadership, the thymari mages were able to devise a counterspell to disable the orbs, giving an edge to Tymanther military forces.
