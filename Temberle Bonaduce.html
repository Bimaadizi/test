Temberle Bonaduce was the son of Cadderly Bonaduce and Danica Maupoissant and twin brother of Hanaleisa in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR , on a trip to Carradoon , the twins encountered hordes of undead sweeping the streets, which were summoned by a servant of the Ghost King .
