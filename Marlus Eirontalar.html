Marlus Eirontalar was a noble inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Marlus was the patriarch of the Eirontalar noble family of Waterdeep. He lived within Eirontalar Villa on Diamond Street in Waterdeep's Sea Ward .

Relationships

His consort was Chalan Eirontalar and his eldest son and heir was Silas .
