Follebu was a small village located on the island of Norland within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Follebu sat along the northern fringes of the South Jotunhammer Mountains within the region of Norland called Grondsgrunn which was controlled by giants . The village was found at the end of a long unnamed bay on the island's western coast on the Trackless Sea .
