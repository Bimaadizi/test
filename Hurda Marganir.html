Hurda Marganir was a human businesswoman in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Hurda was a heavyset woman with shoulder-length gray hair. She wore a white and gray dress and a choker .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Hurda accompanied the Elves of Cormanthor to their forward camp near the ruins of Myth Drannor . She set up a fancy tent with numerous secure chests, and offered her storage services to those who passed through the camp.
