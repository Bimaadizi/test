Luthkant was a city in Calimshan during the 3 rd century DR .

History

In the 240 DR , Taraj Hurlymm , a Magelord of Athalantar , had a Luthkantan peddler seized and released near Dalniir to hunt him for sport. That man was a Calishite from far away, strongly implying the city of Luthkant was somewhere in Calimshan .
