Swamp striders were a species of swarming, predatory insects that could walk across bodies of water thanks to their physiology.

Description

These insects were about the size of mundane beetles , measuring 7 inches (0.18 meters) in length and weighing 0.5 pounds (0.23 kilograms). They had dangerously long, spiked suckers.

Abilities

Swamp striders could see in areas of total darkness , as well as sense creatures within 30 feet (9.1 meters) of them walking in the same body of water.

These insects were capable of walking across water as if effected by water walk and would even float to the surface if submerged.

Combat

When in a swarm, swamp striders would surround and attack living warm-blooded prey larger than them. They pierced them with their suckers to cause bleeding.

Sub-Species

Ecology

Diet

These creatures subsisted upon blood.

Habitats

Swamp striders were found within warm marshes of the Realms. They were particularly known to inhabit the Adder Swamp of Chessenta and the Vast Swamp of Cormyr , often terrorizing the inhabitants of the latter.

Usages

Some humanoid races hunted swamp strider swarms on occasion, considering them to be a fine delicacy when roasted.

History

Around 1372 DR , swamp striders were counted among the many creatures that one could only rarely encounter while traversing the sewers of Waterdeep .
