Chophon was a town in Tabot .

Geography

It stood amongst the northern foothills of the Haraki Ridge in central Tabot, at the end of a road from Ji and Tsetu .

Government

Circa Shou Year 2605 (1355 DR) , the dzong-pon (sheriff or district official) of Chophon was Pag Hou Ling , who had held the role for two years. He had good knowledge of his district and no tolerance of crime, and kept his forces disciplined and alert for troubles both domestic and foreign. Pag was highly respected for his shrewd leadership and his skills as a sheriff while Chophon was known as a safe and well-patrolled place.
