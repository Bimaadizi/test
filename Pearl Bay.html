Pearl Bay was found in the extreme northern reaches of the continent of Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Geography

Pearl Bay was located in the Sun's Eye province of the Land of the Yak-Men . Both the city of Lipo and the Blackwash Fens were found along its edge.

Description

A small ring of mountainous terrain formed the boundary of the bay.
