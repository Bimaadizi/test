The Trader's Rest was a caravanserai located in the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The caravanserai was found near the center of Almorel, just down the street from the Hanging Place next to the Fief of the Lesser Lord .
