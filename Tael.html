The tael was the second-highest unit of currency used in Kara-Tur .

The tael took the form of a silver coin with a hole through the center, similar to the fen . One tael was worth 100 fen, 20 yuan , or 1 ch'ao , while 10 tael equaled 1 ch'ien .
