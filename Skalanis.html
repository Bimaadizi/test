Skalanis was the wild elf champion on Evermeet in 1367 DR .

Activities

Skalanis wandered the isle's forests acting as an advocate and spokesman for the various wild elf bands.

Relationship

Skalanis respected and obeyed Queen Amlaruil Moonflower but did not acknowledge her as the ruler of all elvenkind.
