Private Quickfoot was a halfling soldier of the Neverwinter Guard during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Quickfoot served under Captain Janros , working alongside other specialized guardsmen such as Scout Gaelen and Private Mather .

History

Following the Siege of Neverwinter , in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Quickfoot and his fellow guards were stationed in the Tower District of the city, near the Cloak Tower . Janros sent an adventurer working with the Guard to meet with Quickfoot so Quickfoot could show them the way to Scout Gaelen .
