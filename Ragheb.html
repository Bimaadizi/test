Ragheb was an oasis town located in the southern region of Raurin desert. In the late 15 th century DR the town was ruled by the tiefling Alzubra .

Description

The town surrounded a small but deep lake known as Lake Ragheb . The central area of Ragheb was protected from sandstorms and invaders by a high stone wall. Outside the wall residents lived in semi-permanent tents.
