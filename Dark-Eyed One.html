The Dark-Eyed One was an adventurer and mage in the ruin of Spellgard .

History

The Dark-Eyed One was an evil adventurer member of the Free Company of the Crescent . In 1479 DR , he went with his fellows to Spellgard hoping to find Lady Saharel 's treasures.

Personality

Nobody apart from Devlin knew the motives of the Dark-Eyed One or even his name. He followed Devlin's orders without question. He did not speak much with others but often he murmured to himself.
