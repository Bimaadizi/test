Pleasant Valley Gateway Oasis was located at the southern end of the Glittering Spires mountains in the Hordelands .

Description

This green valley was fed by glacial run-off that formed a small lake that slowly drained the excess water deep underground.

Inhabitants

A group of elves from Sossal settled the valley after fleeing from their cold homeland to begin their own society. The elves desired harmony with nature as well as their neighbors.
