Imphras V was a boy king of Impiltur who never ruled.

Relationships

Imphras was the oldest nephew of King Soarimbrar . His mother was named Ilmara, and he had a younger brother named Imbrar .

History

King Imphras V was next in line to be king when his uncle was murdered just before his coronation. Since Imphras was only four years old at the time, Queen-Regent Sambryl continued to reign in his stead.

In 1363 DR , however, Imphras died of a wasting disease before he had even come of age. His younger brother, Imbrar became the next king.
