Thond Glass and Glazing was a glasswares shop in the city of Waterdeep , owned by Jhalassan Thond in the 14 th century DR .

Location

This shop was located along Sleepers' Walk , in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This shop stood two stories tall.

Services

This shop sold various glassware goods.
