Ty Willowbrook was a reporter employed by Baldur's Mouth and a frequent customer of the Elfsong Tavern in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Ty Willowbrook was a human man with a dark skin tone, green eyes, and short, curly, dark brown hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Ty Willowbrook worked at Baldur's Mouth's offices in Heapside of the Lower City . He regularly struggled with finding words and inspiration, especially for catchy headlines like Open Hand Loses its Grip! , Savage Butchery of Revered Priest! , Templed Turned Slaughterhouse in Shock Horror Priest Murder! and Stabbed and Stabbed Again!
