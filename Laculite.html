Laculite was a precious aquamarine-colored crystal found in the waters of the Underdark .

Description

Laculite crystals formed in subterranean lakes and water pools of Underdark. Even though they were considered to be cheaper than rubies , svirfneblin often mined laculites.

Usages

A suspension could be extracted from laculites through alchemy . This suspension of laculite could be used to brew greater elixirs of arcane cultivation .
