The Hallowed Temple of Selûne was the temple of Selûne in the city of Neverwinter .

Location

The Hallowed Temple was located in the Blacklake District , near the entrance to the River District .

Activities

As of 1479 DR , the Hallowed Temple was the main center of the Selûnite faith in Neverwinter.
