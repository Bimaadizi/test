Rollo was a very young dwarf of Mithril Hall in the late 15th century DR .

History

Rollo was born circa 1465 DR .

During the late stages of the War of the Silver Marches in 1485 DR , Rollo and his old cousin Winko Battleblade were among the dwarves that marched from Mithril Hall into battle against orcs and drow .
