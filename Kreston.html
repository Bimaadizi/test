Kreston was a black dragon born in the Forest Country . He was the spawn of Casarial and Miranatol , grandchild of Hesior , and the blood of Thauglorimorgorus the Black Doom .

Impetuous like his mother, he made the mistake of interrupting his great-grandsire Thauglor's meal in −400 DR .
