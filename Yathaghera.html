Yathaghera , the Winged Queen, was a pegasus demigoddess bound to Toril and the Prime Material Plane . She dwelled on the island of Evermeet .

Appearance

Yathaghera appeared as a magnificent pegasus with a silver mane and rainbow-colored wings.

Relationships

Yathaghera was the daughter of the winged unicorn god Eachthighern , and the younger sister of Lurue . Another story claimed that Yathaghera was simply the name and appearance under which Lurue was worshiped among the elves .

Worshipers

Yathaghera was queen of the pegasi and unicorns of Evermeet, but her subjects actually worshiped her father, Eachthighern, rather than her. The chaotic unicorns rarely even paid attention to her.
