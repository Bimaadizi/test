A ring of swimming was a magic item that enhanced its wearer in the water.

Powers

These rings provided several useful water-related benefits to the wearer. They enhanced the wearer's swimming speed and enabled them to swim for hours without tiring, allowed them to dive safely from up to 50 feet (15 meters) above water, kept the wearer afloat under all but the most severe weather conditions, and allowed the wearer to hold their breath for four minutes underwater.

Notable Owners

Setara bint Farid , a famous corsair from Zakhara , wore a ring of swimming .
