Amberfire was a strong whiskey brewed in the town of Three Swords , located near the Landrise on the Trader's Way between Eartheart and Channathgate , circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Description

Amberfire had a fiery taste and was a popular import to Khôltar where the local brewers added their own proprietary ingredients that they claimed made for a higher quality, more palatable, beverage.
