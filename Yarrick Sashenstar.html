Yarrick Sashenstar was a Ser patron of the arts from the Sashenstar patriar family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the Absolute crisis of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Ser Yarrick Sashenstar was invited to attend the art show titled "Strange Tides: Self-Portraits from the Sword Coast," hosted by Lady Jannath in her Lower City estate. Yarrick RSVP'd to attend but insisted on bringing his very young triplets along, despite Lady Jannath politely reminding him that her manse didn't offer attendant nursemaids for the guests.
