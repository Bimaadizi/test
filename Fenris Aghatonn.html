Fenris Agathonn was the Lady of Hawk's Nest sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

Relationship

Fenris was the wife of Arthus Cavilos . She was also the grandniece of Lord Taern Hornblade of Silverymoon .

Activities

Fenris took care of her spirited daughters Lavencia Cavilos , who wanted to become a wizard like her mother, and Eryl Cavilos , who wanted to become a knight of Tyr like her father.
