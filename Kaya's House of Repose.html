Kaya's House of Repose was a prominent inn in Port Nyanzaru , Chult . Visiting adventurers were often referred there if they wanted a quiet and restful night.

Location

The inn was located in Port Nyanzaru's Market Ward just south of the Red Bazaar .

Services

Kaya's House of Repose was known to be relatively opulent and quiet compared to the average inn in Port Nyanzaru, and as a result, charged a full gold piece for a night's stay. It also served drinks, and was one of the main places in Port Nyanzaru to solicit the services of wilderness guides, in addition to the Thundering Lizard and the Harbormaster's Office .

History

During the events of the death curse in the late 15 th century DR , Kaya's House of Repose was a favorite drinking and meeting spot for the Harper spymaster known as Soggy Wren .
