The Raumkreml (also spelled as "Raumkremyl"), also called Chern-kreml or the Black Citadel , was a tower located in the city of Almorel within the Hordelands .

Location

The Raumkreml was located in the center of Almorel.

Structure

This tower was built as a fortress during the height of the Raumathar Empire. The entire ziggurat was built of wood that had been painted black on the outside, spawning the nickname of the Black Tower.

Inhabitants

Almorel used the Raumkreml as their seat of government.
