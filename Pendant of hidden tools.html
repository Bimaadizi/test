The pendant of hidden tools was a pendant found in the ruins of Tethyamar in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The pendant was a small and innocuous round thing tied with a short cord. The pendant could be opened to reveal a secret compartment that contained lock picks and other thieves' tools .

Powers

The pendant broadly aided the wearer's roguish abilities in finding and disabling traps and locks.

History

The pendant was found after the Tethyamar Mining Outpost dwarves attacked the House Argith drow stronghold in the ruins of Tethyamar.
