Sabriz Rinzyn Kho was a priest of the Spinner of Shadows in the world of Eberron .

Description

Sabriz had gray skin and white hair tied back. She wore an elaborate mask and a cultist outfit that exposed most of her body, and wielded a spiked mace .

Abilities

Sabriz could call on a number of powerful spells, including mass inflict critical wounds , firestorm , cometfall , and unholy blight .
