Kramp was a warrior who fought for the Absolute during the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

Kramp wore a crudely-made suit of metal armor that vaguely resembled goblin plate mail . He preferred to wield a goblin-sized greataxe in battle.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Kramp was among the Absolute's fervent warriors who remained poised to raid the Emerald Grove , once directed by True Soul Minthara Baenre .
