Tears of Ubtao was a type of sap extracted from the bronzewood trees believed to had been blessed by the deity Ubtao of Chult .

Description

Tears of Ubtao were gathered from bronzewood that lived and thrived in the depths of the deadly tomb of the foul lich Acererak . The locals believed that the trees survived thanks to the jungle god's blessings.

Tears of Ubtao were a valuable substance widely used in masterwork crafting. It could be mixed with lakh varnish to create magical living varnish . In its pure form, Tears of Ubtao could be used to treat bronzewood lumber.

History

In the late 15 th century DR , Tears of Ubtao were harvested in Chult by and for the masterwork artisans of Port Nyanzaru by the adventurers of Neverwinter , during the death curse crisis.
