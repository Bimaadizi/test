The Ascension Hall , or the Golden Road , was located within the complex of the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Description

Ivory and alabaster statues flanked the main hallway which was lined with solid gold . The statues honored various heroes and legends known to the Enlightened Throne.

Activities

This hall was reserved for special ceremonies including the annual honoring of the Grand Caliph on Ascension Day .

Rumors and Legends

Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir had plans to honor the White Agate with a statue in the Ascension Hall.
