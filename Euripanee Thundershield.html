Euripanee Thundershield was a female dwarf mercenary in Phlan during the Tyranny of Dragons .

History

Euripanee left Citadel Adbar for a life as a mercenary.

In 1489 DR , during the Cult of the Dragon 's coup in the city of Phlan, she and others were captured by the cultists led by Farnvik . Euripanee was freed by some adventurers.
