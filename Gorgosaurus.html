Gorgosaurus , also known as Albertosaurus , was a type of dinosaur .

Description

Gorgosaurs looked like other carnosaurs like the tyrannosaurus , but were somewhat smaller. They had a long jaw that was filled with rear-facing teeth.

Behavior

Gorgosaurs were known for being aggressive.

Ecology

Gorgosaurs were carnivorous and solitary creatures. In the Realms, gorgosauruses could be found inhabiting the land of Chult and the Malatran Plateau .

Rumors & Legends

It was speculated by sages that gorgosauruses were the ancestors of tyrannosaurs.
