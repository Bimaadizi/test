Diloule "Lanternhand" was a human thief and a member of the adventuring company known as The Four . He was hanged as an assassin in Tethyr on the day before Higharvestide in 1369 DR following the siege of Myratma , along with fellow members Telorn and Fiirfar Nulomn .
