Saghirah was a vizier's turban living in Simbaya in north Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Description

When not in the form of a turban, Saghirah appeared to be a snake-like creature.

Relationships

Saghirah formed a symbiotic relationship with the ruler of Simbaya, Hayfa al-Simbaya .

Activities

She spent most of her time in utter boredom since Hayfa rarely wore her. Saghirah ultimately decided to leave Hayfa and seek a new host.
