Stagford was a county located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The county of Stagford was found in western Callidyrr. Most of the county was filled by the dark expanse of the Dernall Forest . The Swanmay River formed Stagford's eastern border with Talffolk .
