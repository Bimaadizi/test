Finn's Alley was a small street found in the coastal town of Aithelar in Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles in Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR .

Location

Finn's Alley was found in central Aithelar between Callidyrr Ride and Bottle Lane . It intersected Aithe High Street near the center of town.
