TypeArtifact

The Circled Serpent was an artifact which could be used to open two portals . Both were located on the Chultan Peninsula . One portal lead to Smaragd , Sseth 's layer in the Abyss , the other to the lair of Dendar the Night Serpent on the Fugue Plane .

Arvin used the Circled Serpent to open the portal to Smaragd and destroyed the artifact after he had rescued Karrell and returned with her from that plane.
