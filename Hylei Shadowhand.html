Hylei Shadowhand , and her sister Kylei Shadowhand , were members of the Sisters of the Blood Moon in the early 14 th century DR .

Abilities

Hylei was an adventuring illusionist who was as proficient in arcana as she was in stealth.

Equipment

The gnome was armed with a quarterstaff and a sling .

History

Circa the Year of Storms, 1310 DR , the rock gnome twin sisters Hylei and Kylei befriended a half-elven ranger Nevae Quar . Together, the women adventured and and tried to stay out of trouble despite Kylei Shadowhand's love of pickpocketing. One fateful night, they came across a site of a goblin ambush in a forest outside Silverymoon . Three fellow adventurers S'feria Ranul , Oagla'ta , and Nyzeil Nighthammer were fighting for their lives, and the trio of women rushed to help. The group dispatched bandits with natural ease, battling in synch, S'feria became enamored with the new allies' battle prowess and convinced them to join her adventuring band - the Sisters of the Blood Moon .

In 1310 DR, S'feria Ranul received a letter – a plea for help from the town of Targos in Icewind Dale . Despite the promise of gold and fame, S'feria led her Sisters to the northern city, answering its pleas for help.
