Mara Trollsblood was a half-orc guard in Zhent -occupied Daggerdale in the 1360s DR .

History

In 1367 DR , Mara was hired by Red Morgan to guard his caravan from Dagger Falls to Zhentil Keep . After a battle with a band of Freedom Riders and another with a goblin pack, Mara was left the only guard with a horse.

Personality

Mara loved taking prisoners, tying them loosely, and killing them when they tried to escape.
