Gilkriz was a senior member of the College of Conjuration in the city of Sshamath .

History

In 1377 DR , Gilkriz was chosen by Master Urlryn Khalazza to help teleport the combined force of Sshamath wizards and priests of Eilistraee to the main temple of Kiaransalee , which they planned to attack.
