Maerklos Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Maerklos .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward at the corner of Sulmor Street and Vhezoar Street , and part of the estate also lay along Brondar's Way .

Description

The buildings that composed the villa included one, two and four stories.
