Peasant Blades were enchanted kama created to be used as weapons rather than tools of the trade.

Powers

Peasant Blade was enhanced with a simple +3 enchantment that made its slashes harmful even to creatures unaffected by mundane weapons and guided the wielder's hand towards the target. Additionally, the weapon significantly boosted the wielder's constitution.

History

Unsurprisingly, Peasant Blades were once tools used by peasants in the fields, later adapted to be used in battle because they were forbidden from wielding swords . By the late 14 th century DR, some of these enchanted kama or copies made from the original, were found in the city of Neverwinter and nearby lands.
