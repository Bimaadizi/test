The Lake of Drifting Stars was a freshwater lake that laid to the north of the town of Gildenglade in Turmish .

Description

The Lake of Drifting Stars was a popular rendezvous spot for lovers who sought a romantic encounter during the Feast of the Moon , or the Festival of Lovers , a grand revelry for the inhabitants of Turmish. Its shores were grass-covered hills that overlooked the calm lake. The shores of the lake became commonly patrolled after the Time of Troubles and the increased activities of the dreaded Cult of Malar .
