Sandan's Sawmill was a lumber mill found in Archenbridge in late 14 th century DR . The mill was used to strip and saw timber felled from the nearby Arch Wood . It was powered by the strong currents of the River Arkhen that ran through the city, and under its eponymous bridge.

Owner

The mill was owned and operated by a former captain of the Archenriders named Sandan , who was known to work with great care.
