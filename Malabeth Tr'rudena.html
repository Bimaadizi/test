Malabeth Tr'rudena was a drow priestess and a member of the She-Spider Cult .

Description

Malabeth wore a metallic bikini and a white loincloth, and accessorized with various chains and white feathers. She had pure black skin and white hair.

Possessions

She owned a pair of bracers of defense , as well as a necklace of prayer beads and twin daggers of venom .

Activites

Although she was a member of the She-Spider Cult, an organization forsaken by both Lolth and Shar , Malabeth still retained her divine powers. As a result, it was suspected that she was a spy for Lolth.
