Ogre Magi was the racial language of ogre mages .

Speakers

Beyond ogre mages themselves, Ogre Magi was the primary language spoken by ogres throughout Kara-Tur and in the Northern Wastes .

Related Languages

The Wu-haltai language , the native tongue of the Wu-haltai , had many points in common with Ogre Magi. These similarities likely arose from the proximity of ogre tribes within the Ama Basin region of the Northern Wastes.
