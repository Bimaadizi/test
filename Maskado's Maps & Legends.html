Maskado's Maps & Legends was a bookshop in Neverwinter in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . Its owner was the mysterious Maskado .

Location

It was one of a number of other booksellers, bookbinders, and scribes on a winding street running southeast of the House of Knowledge , in the City Core district.

Services

Among these, Maskado's dealt specially in maps, records, and information concerned with exploration of the North , such as trails, uncommon and secret routes, and treasure tales.

Simple maps or scraps of writing with information sold at Maskado's had a cost of 100 gp , while more detailed and accurate maps and treasure maps could cost twice as much. However, Maskado purchased such things from adventures for far less.
