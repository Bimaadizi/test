Festina was the mother of Ch'kk'ch and an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Ma Festina was a human woman with a light skin tone, light brown eyes, and long, wavy blond hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Festina brought her daughter, Ch'kk'ch, to a performance of some showy magics by three spellslingers, on the Murl's Rest plaza in front of Sorcerous Sundries . The mother was delighted by her daughter's joy seeing the magical show.
