Ferla Treerunner (also seen as Ferla Treeclimber ) was a wild elf who lived in the Forest of Amtar in northern Dambrath .

Activities

Ferla guided the Trunadar tribe as their shaman for over 300 years, through at least the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR .

The Trunadar people were in constant conflict with the Crinti of Dambrath, caused by the ingrained hatred between the elves and the drow .
