The Deepglen was a small valley located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Deepglen was found nestled along the western end of the Black Mountains in the northern Myrloch Vale . The Deepglen Stream flowing down from the mountains passed through the center of the valley. The Deepglen was approximately 10 miles (16 kilometers) long.

Notable Locations

A trio of Moonwells were found in the forests surrounding the Deepglen.
