The Sea Demon was a fearsome creature from the waters surrounding the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars . While a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of raged on the Whamite Isles, the Sea Demon would appear suddenly and attack sea vessels. This was at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
