White Crane Temple was a temple located in the city of Kirin , Hungtse Province , in Shou Lung . It was the base of the Order of the White Crane , a sect devoted to the Path of Enlightenment and to Shu Chia , one of the Nine Immortals and the God of Artificers and the Arts,

Services

The sect freely allowed travelers to stay at their temple, but could share only a simple sleeping mat and vegetarian food, and anyone staying for more than a week was expected to work and earn their keep. Visitors were also permitted to attend their training.

Inhabitants

Circa Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) , the temple was led by the abbot Ch'ang Tui , "Long Legs", who upheld its traditions.
