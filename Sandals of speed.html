Sandals of speed were sandals enchanted with the hishna spell create talisman .

Description

This set of footwear was made from thick leather.

Powers

Similar to boots of speed , this item greatly enhanced the wearer's movement speed. However, unlike the boots of speed , these sandals did not require a period of rest, allowing the wearer to jog for up to sixteen hours in a day.
