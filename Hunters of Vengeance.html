The Hunters of Vengeance was an informal order of vigilantes and bounty hunters devoted to Hoar , the Doombringer.

Base of Operations

The group did not have a specific base of operations, preferring instead to roam the Heartlands and north Faerûn to exact their justice.

Activities

Though few in number, the deeds of the Hunters of Vengeance were spoken of with great admiration, and a bit of dread, among the faithful of Hoar.
