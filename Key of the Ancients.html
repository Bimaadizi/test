An Key of the Ancients was a magical circlet that originated from the Emerald Grove .

Description

The circlet was created from partly-fossilised branches taken from trees grown in the Emerald Grove. Several small rubies were embedded in its from, which were uniquely connected to the stones that formed the foundation of the enclave.

Powers

The wearer of Key of the Ancients garnered a greater understanding of the natural world.
