Snuggleguts was a kitten gifted to Imraea by the thief Torm in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Description

The kitten was silver and black, with glistening black eyes.

History

In early Flamerule of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Torm gifted Snuggleguts, then a kitten, to a young kid named Imraea, alongside nine gold pieces and instructions to find it a good name, for Snuggleguts was only its kitten name.
