The Lorious Inn , most often called just the Lorious , was an upscale inn in Veltalar as of the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Lorious Inn was in Veltalar .

Structure

The front door was wide.

Interior

The Lorious' entrance hall was packed with tables draped with red fabric. The saloon past the entrance hall had a bit of haze due to the burning incenses and pipes within the room. Within the Lorious, there were only magical lights.

The hallways leading to the rooms had golden lamps, and tapestries. There were plaques announcing the latter were looted from the ruins of Mulhorand .

The lead-lined vaults had marble interiors.

Services

The Lorious was counted among the city's finer establishments; a day's stay cost five gold pieces at its rooms with adjoining lead-lined vaults, more than twice what other upscale suites cost.

Defenses

For those in dire need of privacy, and due to the volume of money that changed hands inside, the Lorious offered suites with lead-lined vaults.
