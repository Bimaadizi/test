Asala was a priestess of Kiaransalee in Szith Morcane in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Asala was a young priestess of Kiaransalee under Velasta T'sarran .

History

Circa 1486 DR , after the fire giants occupied the outpost, Asala's lover was killed by a group of hell hounds and azers while they tried to escape. Asala wanted vengeance.
