Grelinda was a young woman that lived in Phlan and worked at Madame Freona's Tea Kettle during the late 15 th century DR . She was one of Madame Freona 's five daughters.

Description

She had short-cropped hair that was the greenish color that copper turned to over an extended period of time.

Relationships

Grelinda's other sisters included Blaizette , Briez , Reece , and Whittlee .
