Thartryn Ilistar III was the eleventh King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate . He was also the last Ilistar King of Westgate.

Activities

Thartryn III began his rule when his father, King Halcoranth , died in the Year of the Fell Firebreak, 886 DR . He only reigned for a few years before succumbing to plague, along with the remainder of the Ilistar heirs, in the Year of the Burning Tree, 890 DR .
