Othlor Fydra Night-Tree was a Rashemaar Wychlaran inhabiting Immilmar in Rashemen in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

Activities

As othlor, Fydra was the leading representative of the Wychlaran in Immilmar. She directly supervised the three hathran who advised Iron Lord Yvarrg Bearkiller . Fydra lived within the Witches' Hall south of the Huhrong's Citadel .
