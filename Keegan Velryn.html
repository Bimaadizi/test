Keegan Velryn was a scrimshander who lived in Targos during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He was described as being rather handsome.

Relationships

Keegan was married to the rugged outdoorsman Garret Velryn .

History

In the Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR , Garret Velryn went missing while leading an expedition across Kelvin's Cairn . Over the next few days, Keegan became increasingly worried, and eventually hired some adventurers to uncover his whereabouts.
