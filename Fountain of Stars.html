The Fountain of Stars was a white fountain in Leuthilspar on the island of Evermeet .

Description

The white fountain depicted birds, flowers, trees and other icons of nature.

History

The fountain was said to have been given to Zaor Moonflower as a gift by Sehanine Moonbow .

Rumors and legends

It was rumored the fountain offered a glimpse of the future to those who looked into it.
