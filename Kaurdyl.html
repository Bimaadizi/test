Kaurdyl was a mildly toxic spice in the Realms.

Use

Kaurdyl was a spice. However, per Anastra Syluné Silverhand , it was deadly to mix huge doses of it with specific oils.

History

In 1369 DR , Alaithe , a servant, served her master Blandras Nuin (actually a transformed Auvrarn Labraster ) a plate of morning broth with kaurdyl in it. Smelling it, he leapt to the conclusion she tried to kill him, and murdered her instead.
