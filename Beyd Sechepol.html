Beyd Sechepol was a merchant during the Tyranny of Dragons .

Personality

Beyd was a very diplomatic individual, always evading arguments that divided public opinion, with the exception of topics regarding horses and gear.

History

Beyd boarded a great caravan in order to sell his ale and beer at the start of the Tyranny of Dragons in 1489 DR . Unknown to him, it was full of agents from the Cult of the Dragon who were transporting treasures for the Hoard of the Dragon Queen .
