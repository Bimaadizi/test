Sharkroar, Horth Shalark's Broadsheets was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood one story tall.

Location

Sharkroar, Horth Shalark's Broadsheets was located in the north section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the south side of Trader's Way just north of the Market .

Inhabitants

The shop was owned by its namesake, Horth Shalark in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
