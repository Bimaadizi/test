Mu'awiya was the eldest son of Sheikh Kaldhun bin Hanif of the House of Hanif tribe in the Haunted Lands of interior Zakhara around 1367 DR .

Description

Mu'awiya was tall and gaunt.

Personality

He was a calm man.

Activities

Mu'awiya believed his father and grandfather's support of the Grand Caliph , by serving as his guards in the desert, was the proper path for the tribe.

Relationships

Mu'awiya's had a half-brother named Yazid . Mu'awiya was conceived later on the same day as his brother and born before his brother on the same day.
