Alathann Ruil was a fence of stolen goods inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Alathann operated out of a tavern called the Bloody Fist located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward on Presper Street . His point of contact within the Bloody Fist was a man named One-Eyed Jukk . Alathann kept his stolen merchandise, consisting primarily of arms and armor , at the Red Sails Warehouse on Cod Lane in the Dock Ward.
