Rivven Melshimber was the husband of Devana Melshimber and the member of the noble Melshimber family of Waterdeep in the mid-14 th century DR.

History

Rivven and Devana Melshimber were married in the early 1370 DR . The happy event was saddened by the mysterious disappearance of the bride's sister. Lady Tryola Perrentine who was kidnapped by orcs on her way from Tilverton to Waterdeep. Presumed dead, the unfortunate young woman was rescued from the orcs of Undermountain several months after her "death".
