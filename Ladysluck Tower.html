The Ladysluck Tower was a house of worship for Tymora in Yhaunn in the 14 th century DR .

Inhabitants

High Luck Priestess Jhyndara Thiolstar and her 34 fellow priests and priestesses of Tymora ran the temple in 1358 DR . Together they attended a congregation of 71 followers.

By 1373 DR , the High Luck was Colle Shoondeep . The temple had benefited from several very favorable leases granted by Lady Monstaed 's deceased husband, which Variance Amatick transferred to Moonshadow Hall in the guise of Lady Monstaed.
