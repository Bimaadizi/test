Draevin Flarwood was a trader from Silverymoon in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . He was a suspect in the murder of Garthin Muirtree.

Description

He was a young trader.

Relationships

He represented the Braeder Trading Collective .

History

Draevin Flarwood was one of the people who met with the tradelord Garthin Muirtree one day in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , on the day he was murdered in the Red Griffon Room of Silverymoon 's palace.

He was interrogated by Alustriel Silverhand over Garthin's murder that same day. Their conversation instead turned to Alustriel's plans over the running of her city rather than the murder, but she was nonetheless satisfied he was not their killer somehow. He asked if she couldn't just use magic to reveal the events, to which she answered she could not. He also asked if Garthin would recover, impolying he was not aware yet that the attack was a murder.
