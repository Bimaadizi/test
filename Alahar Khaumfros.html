Alahar Khaumfros was the Waterbaron of Yartar during the mid–14 th century DR . During this time, he was also the leader of the Kraken Society .

Relationships

Khaufros employed the doppelganger Semmonemily to do his dirty work and had an elderly steward named Cladence .

History

Around the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR , four illithids walked into the Waterbaron's Hall and murdered Alahar. He was killed for his fraudulent dealings of the Kraken Society's money.
