Quimby was a mage of the city of Procampur in the Vast , who lived some time prior to the late 9 th century DR .

Activities

Quimby was a devoted gourmand, a lover of good food and lots of it, but an impatient cook. For aid in meal preparation, especially whilst studying or traveling alone, Quimby modified the unseen servant spell to create Quimby's enchanting gourmet . This spell would carry out the work of cooking for him and provide him with fine meals. Later users would nickname the unseen servant created by this spell "Quimby" after him.

Description

Needless to say, he was fat.
