Rorry was a farmer from Liam's Hold circa 1372 DR .

Description

Farmer Rorry was a black-haired gnome who was not used to big cities and often looked at city-folk and adventurers with admiration.

History

In 1372 DR, farmer Rorry traveled to Daggerford along with his fellows Gaslo and Hevesar Dimpet to seek help with the mysterious disappearances of the cattle from Wild Pastures . The farmers o F Liam's Hold were unable o find helm among the militia, nor the temple of Lathander . Eventually, they came across a group of adventurers who offered their help. Rorry, alongside his fellow farmers left Daggerford for Liam's Hold as soon as the temporary city lockdown was lifted and the gates were open.
