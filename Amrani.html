Amrani was a general laborer inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Amrani earned a living laundering clothing. Many of her clients were Waterdhavian nobles, and their sometimes frequent use of her services on multiple times per day had her suspicious about their discretions.
