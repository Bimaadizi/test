Angath was an Executor with the Warlock Knights of Vaasa in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

As a Knight-Executor, Angath was the equivalent of a landless nobleman. He was understood to serve Warlock Knights above himself in their hierarchy.

Personality

His enemies considered him to be a fool.

Relationships

Angath had an adversarial relationship with Knight-Fellthane Vorlanth , and was known or suspected to meddle in his affairs. As of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Vorlanth had warned Angath to stay away from his business dealings.
