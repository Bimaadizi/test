The ancient gemstone was a magical item similar to an Eberronnian warforged docent , recovered from the Temple of Mystra in the King's Forest in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The "gemstone" was actually a sphere studded with metal and gemstones. Seemingly intended for use as a power source for a magical device, it could also be slotted into a warforged.

Powers

The ancient gemstone gave the bearer improved resilience and fortitude, and even seemed to twist luck in their favor.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Cormyrean forces from Eveningstar searched the Temple of Mystra for the lost Ana Brabener , who had been captured by the fell Eldrin Szind , Odezzt Avithoul and Phargred Avithoul . In the process they also found this peculiar item.
