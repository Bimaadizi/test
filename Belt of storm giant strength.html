A belt of storm giant strength , also referred to as a girdle of storm giant strength , was one of six varieties of belts of giant strength . It conferred its wearer with the strength of a storm giant .

Description

The great girdles were typically decorated with a silver buckle set with crossed lightning bolts. .

Powers

These belts enacted their magic when being tightened notch by notch. Some described the power given by these belts as being equivalent to drinking a potion of storm giant strength .
