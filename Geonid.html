Geonids were creatures originating from the Elemental Plane of Earth .

Behavior

Geonids were known to enjoy collecting all sorts of curios.

Society

Homelands

Geonids dwelt primarily in natural caves and enjoyed collecting curios. Some geonid lairs were known to contain a shrine, constructed of large and oddly shaped boulders, to some unknown deity.

A significant number of Geonids dwelt on the Snout of Omgar .
