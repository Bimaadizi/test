Saphardin Olen was an apprentice wizard of Athalantar in the early 3 rd century DR .

Personality

Saphardin was ambitious.

Relationships

He was apprenticed to Harskur .

Possessions

He carried two enchanted wands of some sort.

History

Saphardin Olen was using an eavesdropping spell on the Knights of Athalantar at Lawless Castle from a nearby hillside; after the meeting, he aimed both wands at the entrance, intending to attack before they left, whether to slay or to blockage the entrance. Before he couse loose spells, however, a rock as big as his head struck him from behind. A sorceress fitting the description of Myrjala Talithyn materialized afterwards, presumably responsible for his slaying.
