The Gate of the Fallen was a large monument located in the City of Brass in 1367 DR .

The Gate of the Fallen was found within the ward of Castings .

Description

Despite its name, the Gate of the Fallen was not a gate or a memorial to the efreeti dead. It was in fact a large decorated archway depicting scenes of the efreet dominating all other races found on the Elemental Plane of Fire .
