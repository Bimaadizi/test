Calamastyr Lane was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Calamastyr Lane was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . The road originated on Julthoon Street where it branched southeast toward the Market where it terminated on Bazaar Street . Several other roads connected with Calamastyr Lane along its length including Swords Street , Gothal Street , Elvarren's Lane , Tchozal's Race , and Melshar's Street .
