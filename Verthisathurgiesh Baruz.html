Verthisathurgiesh Baruz was a dragonborn member of Clan Verthisathurgiesh in 1486 DR .

Relationships

Baruz was the third son of Verthisathurgiesh Anala .

History

Baruz was one of the few known members of the Liberators of the Steel Sky .

On Nightal 15 of 1486 DR, Baruz was killed, alongside his fellow companions of the Liberators, by a maurezhi granted to the God -King Gilgeam .
