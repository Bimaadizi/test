Wyrmsmoke Keep was a cloud palace occupied by the Wyrmsmoke cell of the Cult of the Dragon during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Keep floated high over the Galena Mountains , halfway between Hulburg and Glister .

Defenses

The fortress was garrisoned by three dragonkin tribes; a tribe of draconic orcs , which raised fire drakes as guardians; and several commanders, mostly orcish or ogrish variants.

Notable Inhabitants

The Wyrmsmoke cell operated out of the citadel.
