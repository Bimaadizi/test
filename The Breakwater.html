The Breakwater was a multi-story inn favored by adventurers in Melvaunt around 1367 DR .

Location

The Breakwater was located near the center of the city.

Services

The inn was renowned for its fine wine and exceptional seafood. Guests enjoyed spacious comfortable rooms and a plethora of services including laundering, livery, and weapon and armor repair.
