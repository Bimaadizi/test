Joltshooter was a unique bow that crafted by the eccentric inventor Yrre the Sparkstruck . It was said to have been their favorite weapon, primarily because of the large spikes that extended out from its shaft.

Powers

Whenever the wielder of Joltshooter shot true at their target, a course of electricity shot through their body. This charge was not harmful, bur rather allowed them to move with greater speed and strength.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the bow remained in the possession of Counsellor Florrick .
