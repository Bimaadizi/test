Froya Harmoot was a Reghedmen human and the mistress of King Jarund Elkhardt from the Tribe of the Elk in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa Year of the Iron Dwarf's Vengeance, 1485 DR , hushed whispers among the tribe suggested that Froya had mothered a child for King Jarund Elkhardt, potentially weakening his wife Wynneth 's standing. These rumors turned out to be nonsense as none had seen the supposed child even in Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR .
