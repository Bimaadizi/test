Secomber sour vinegar was a popular condiment that originated from the peaceful town of Secomber .

Description

The condiment was made in halfling vineyards northwest of Secomber in the far northern Western Heartlands . They used unripe sour dry white grapes for the recipe. It was served with pan-fried quipper fish as evenfeast at the Yawning Portal in the City of Splendors, Waterdeep .
