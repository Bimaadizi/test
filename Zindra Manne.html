Zindra Manne was an elderly human commoner, displaced by The Absolute , and as a refugee, sought shelter in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Zindra had a fair skin, wrinkled and weathered by time. The cranky old woman had mousy-gray hair and was dressed in a simple blue dress.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate , displacing countless refugees in the process along the way. Zindra found her way to the Rivington district outside the city walls, but, like all other refugees, she was refused entrance. The woman's tent was located near a warehouse that was run by the Flaming Fist , used for collection and storage of rations and other supplies for the refugees.
