Suzale , also known as Purple Dragon ale , was a fine-quality nutty ale .

Availability

Apart from Suzail , Purple Dragon ale was a staple at Hultail 's the High Helm festhall.

The Jovial Juggler of Beregost in the Western Heartlands offered Suzale to its patron for a meager single gold coin per serving.

In the Dalelands , suzale cold be found in the Silver Taproom of Essembra for six copper pieces.

The Golden Goblet tavern in the Trades District of Calimport saved imported Purple Dragon Ale.

Prices

History

The ale was first brewed for King Azoun IV of Cormyr but over time it became sold across Faerûn . The name was a pun on the city of Suzail, the seat of power in Cormyr.

Suzale was served at a reception for the Merchants' Guild held in the Sheaf of Wheat inn in Ghars , Cormyr, on Eleint 19 in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR . Jasper suspected Mayor Tobald had had several mugs of either Suzale or Elminster's Choice .

Cultural Significance

The Purple Dragons' Goblin Joke Scroll was a collection of anecdotes about goblins . One such joke on the popular scroll included a goblin who imbibed suzale.
