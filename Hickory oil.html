Hickory oil was a type of edible vegetable oil in the Realms, produced from the seeds of hickory trees.

Availability

Hickory oil was commonly available in most markets, and used by bakers and alchemists alike.

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold hickory oil at a standard price of one gold piece per 1 gallon (3.8 liters) in its "Priest's Alcove" section, intended for use with spells and rituals .

Usages

Hickory oil found some use as a material component by priests and druids . It could be used for the reflecting pool spell, with walnut oil as an alternative.
