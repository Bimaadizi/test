Thael leaf was a dried plant substance used as an additive to flavor pipeweed and enhance the scent of temple incense .

Description

Thael leaf were dried leaves that added pleasant taste and scent when burned along with incenses or pipeweed. The fragrant smoke resembled that of almonds , roasted nuts, and herbs , notably lacking sweetness.
