OccupationButcher

Whaendel was a butcher in the mid–8 th century DR .

History

Whaendel brought a bag of money to purchase South Spire and the other landholdings of Thessamel Arunder about two tendays into the Chaos of the Art in the 767 DR , together with Hulder Phelbellow and others.
