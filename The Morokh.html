Classwarlock

The mysterious individual only known as The Morokh was a warlock active in Almraiven during the 15 th century DR .

Abilities

Although little was known about The Morokh , they were said to be a formidable spellcaster who got help from a flying snake and relied on an impressive arsenal of battle-oriented magic items .

History

Sometime between the Year of the Godly Invitation, 1449 DR and the Year of the Emerald Sun, 1454 DR , The Morokh suffered an attack from the Sword of Savras and consequently lost access to their pact magic. They however retained the ability to wield their magic items.

Rumors & Legends

According to rumors, The Morokh never slept.
