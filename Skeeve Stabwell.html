Skeeve Stabwell was a member of The Guild from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Skeeve was a human with with golden hair and bronze skin. He had gray eyes and face, concealed by a mask. Like other members of the Guild, Skeeve wore leathers dyed with the organization's emerald colors.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Skeeve Stabwell was tasked with standing guard of the secret entrance into Baldur's Gate's Undercellar where The Guild's Guildhall was hidden. Whilst a half-orcish enforcer Tusgront manned the main entrance, Skeeve was ready to dispatch of any unwanted guest from a distance.
