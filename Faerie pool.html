The Faerie pools were small pools of water tainted with an odd mineral in the rocks, located across the Sword Lands , Laerakond .

Description

The pools produced gases that cause an "air frost" (snowflakes) that, if inhaled, caused brief but vivid hallucinations . Sages believed those gases were product of a mineral infected with the Spellplague . People with strong will could control those hallucinations and a few ones knew how to use that gas to divine the future.

Common people believed the Faerie pools were portals to the Feywild , and that fickle fey produced the hallucinations, hence the name of the pools.

History

The Faerie pools began to appear in Laerakond after the Spellplague.
