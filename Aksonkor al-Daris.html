Aksonkor al-Daris was a moralist and member of the League Conclave in I'tiraf in 1367 DR .

Activities

Originally from Talab , Aksonkor was elected to lead the group of delegates from that city to represent them within the League Conclave. Aksonkor and his fellow delegates ensured Talab's best interests were heard at court.
