Stingray poison was a type of venom produced by and extracted from a species of fish called sting rays .

Effects

Despite not necessarily being deadly, stingray venom was still potent and dangerous. Those affected by the poison were recommended to immediately seek a healer's aid. If the substance was introduced into a creature's bloodstream, it became paralyzed for 30–120 seconds. Additionally, the poison affected the target's constitution and caused bad nausea. Individuals of good health and resilience were expected to fully recover from the potion's effects within 24-48 hours.

If by some unlucky occurrence, a creature consumed stingray poison, the substance had a high chance of affecting the imbiber.

History

Vials of stingray poison were in common use around the late-14 th century DR . Notably, Captain Allendry of the Midnight Rose outfitted his crew and offered the deadly substance for sale.
