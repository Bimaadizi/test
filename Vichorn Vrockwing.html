Vichorn Vrockwing was a tiefling who frequented the Driftwood Tavern in Neverwinter around 1484 DR .

Activities

Vichorn wanted to recruit a tiefling adventurer, claiming there was a bargain between his master and the adventurer's ancestors, to sabotage the Ashmadai .
