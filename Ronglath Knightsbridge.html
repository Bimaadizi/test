Ronglath Knightsbridge was a human warrior of the Zhentarim and a worshiper of Bane in the mid–14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , just before the Time of Troubles , he infiltrated the guards of Arabel to conspire against the city but was unveiled by Kelemvor and Cyric .

Ronglath later became a commander in the Zhentilar army sent by Bane against Shadowdale . He fought with great courage in the Zhent assault until he was again confronted by Kelemvor, who finally killed him.
