The Oasis of Akhir was a body of water in the Raurin in southeast Faerûn .

Overview

In the Year of the Worm, 1356 DR , the efreeti Pasha Khalitharius burned everything around it to the ground shortly after being released. Some tracks around the oasis led straight towards the Oasis of the White Palm .
