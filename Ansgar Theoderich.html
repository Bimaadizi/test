Hatemaster Ansgar Theoderich was a priest of Iyachtu Xvim that carried on the Godson's faith in the city of Phlan during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Haemaster Ansgar was a priest at the Lyceum of the Black Lord in Phlan, the temple whose clergy venerated Iyachtu Xvim in what was seen as dissident by the greater church of Bane . History

At some point Ansgar was abducted to the Lyceum of the Black Hand and was tortured by devotees of Bane to divulge these perceived heretical beliefs.
