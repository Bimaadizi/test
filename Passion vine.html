Passion vine was a fragrant blossoming vine that grew in the tropical climate of the Vilhon Reach .

Habitats

Noble gardens of Arrabar in the Vilhon Reach were filled with overpowering scents of flowering plants, including bougainvilleas , passion vines, orchids , and wandering hearts . Notably, the flowers could be found in the gardens surrounding mansions of House Matrell and House Pharaboldi .
