A staff of the owlbear was an enchanted shaman staff found in the jungles of Chult in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

These weapons were intricately carved and decorated with owlbear feathers and leather thongs. Staves of the owlbear possessed a +2 enchantment and slightly improved the powers of the wielder and their allies alike.
