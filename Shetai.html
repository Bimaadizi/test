Shetai , the Vulgar Inquisitor , was a high-ranking paeliryon that was a fiend patron for warlocks in the 14 th and 15 th centuries DR .

History

In the Year of the Dragon, 1352 DR , Shetai, still an erinyes in the service of Malagard , was summoned by Bryseis Kakistos and forged a warlock pact with her.

Shetai was responsible for the fall of the Hag Countess and afterward gained much power. By 1353 DR , Shetai had become a paeliryon and explained to Bryseis that Asmodeus himself was interested in renewing her pact.

In the 14 th century, it was collecting warlocks and double-crossed Lorcan . In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Shetai had some trouble and so asked Lorcan for help, using his sister Neferis as a messenger.

Relationship

Shetai had some dealings with Lorcan. The Vulgar Inquisitor was the patron of a warlock heir of the Toril thirteen called Lachs the Yellow . Shetai commanded some of its fellow erinyes.
