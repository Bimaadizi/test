Oliver Tango was a novice priest of Chauntea who served the House of Bountiful Harvest temple dedicated to the The Golden Goddess in Ravens Bluff 's Temple District of Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Oliver was one among five novices who served the House of Bountiful Harvest under the leadership of High Harvestmistress Arabella Ieruin of Chauntea by the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR . Other novices were Vail Rosehill , Puck , Xena Phobia , and Nicolas Biden .
