Meditations on a Clear Pond was a book of poetry by Chi Chan of T'u Lung . The work was banned in some parts of Kara-Tur .

In Shou Year 2608 (1358 DR) , the scholar Ko La Ko of Shou Lung might have discussed the work with his adventurer companions, and bored them to tears.
