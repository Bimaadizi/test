Jierta was a female frost giant warrior in the late 15th century DR .

History

Jierta was one of the warriors in Jarl Greigor Kundknoddick 's tribe, who allied with drow and orcs during the Darkening .

In 1485 DR , during the late stages of the War of the Silver Marches , Jierta reported to Greigor that an army from Silverymoon had defeated their orc allies. Soon after, Gregor was approached by Jarlaxle , who suggested he withdraw his troops in the wake of the imminent defeat of Many-Arrows .
