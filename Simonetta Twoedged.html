Personality

Simonetta was a skilled tactician and had an exceptionally keen mind towards strategy.

She was a proponent of learning as much as she could about both her foes and those she fought alongside.

History

When Simonetta was just 18 years old, she joined Waterdeep's City Guard. She served her city for over three decades, eventually rising to the rank of Warden of Waterdeep. For her service, she was awarded the Waterdhavian medal of valor.

By the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Simonetta was retired for a number of years. When war broke out that year and the Shining Crusade threatened the security of the Sword Coast , she decided to join up with the Coalition as a military advisor.
