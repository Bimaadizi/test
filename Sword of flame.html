Swords of flame were swords that were lit aflame with enchantments , especially useful a gainst trolls and similar creatures venerable to fire.

Powers

Swords of flame were made out of enchanted silver and possessed a +1 magics that made the blade deadlier and guided the wielder's hand towards the target. The words "Meta Infernum" were engraved into their hilt and their blades were burning with magical flames.
