Hurlbut Hhune was a member of the Hhune patriar family of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Hurlbut's daughter, Henrietta Hhune , became entangled with lowborn Manip Leythim despite being betrothed to Derque Rillyn of the Rillyn patriar family. When Hurlbut became aware of the relationship between the two, he hired muscle to beat the Manip up in an attempt to secure his daughter's noble marriage.
