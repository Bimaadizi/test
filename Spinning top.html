A spinning top , or simply a top , was a type of toy in the Realms that had a squat body and sharp pointed bottom, designed to be spun on its vertical axis and balance on its tip while doing so.

Description

Tops could be made from a variety of different metals, including brass , bronze , and copper . Among the Iulutiuns of the Great Glacier , they were commonly made of either ice or wood.

Usage

The wu-jen version of the spell Whirlwind required a spinning top as a material component .
