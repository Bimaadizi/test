Adrian Martikov was a vintner who lived and worked at his family's vineyard, the Wizard of Wines , in the domain of Barovia .

Personality

He was a gracious and generous man who did all he could to honor the Martikov family name. Unlike so many others in Barovia, he kept an optimistic and positive outlook on things, even during dire circumstances.

Activities

Adrian worked hard to help his family's business, making deliveries by cart despite the obscuring fog and dangers found along the road.

Relationships

Adrian was a part of the Martikov family, that also included his father Davian .
