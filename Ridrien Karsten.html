Ridrien Karsten was a merchant and owner of Karsten's Imports in Luskan during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Some time around 1490 DR , Ridrien contracted with Captain Falcrow of the Sancarla , and was expecting a visit from the Captain to his shop, when instead he encountered the Champion of the Burning Dawn .
