Newfort was a small hamlet built atop a hill found in the Sundabar Vale , within the Old Delzoun region of the Silver Marches .

Description

The small hamlet comprised about two score wood cottages built around a small stream, built atop a small hill encircled by a wooden palisade.

Geography

Newfort was located approximately 15 mi (24 km) east of Sundabar , 3 mi (4.8 km) south of the Fork Road , in the foothills of the the Rauvin Mountains .

History

Newfort was founded in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , by two retired members of the Zhentarim named Trevis Uhl and Gyreth Ilgarn . Trevis took it upon himself to serve as the town's first mayor while Gyreth served as its constable.

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Newfort and the settlements of Jalanthar and Quaevarr were all terrorized by the barlgura Scauuont , all within the span of a single week.

By the time of the War of the Silver Marches in the late 15 th century DR , Newfort had become a way station welcoming any travelers to stay the night in safety.

Notable Locations

Inhabitants

Because Newfort's founders both had a strong affiliation with the Zhent armies of the Moonsea , it attracted many people that fled the region following the rise to power of the Zhentarim .
