Tezzarq was a drow spellspinner in the Underdark in 1379 DR .

Activities

Tezzarq was a member of House Masq'il'yr .

History

In 1379 DR, as a birthday gift for his matron mother, Tezzarq decided to buy ten elf slaves. So he went to Skullport and hired the slaver Lanador to fulfill the task. However, Lanador's minions accidentally captured a member of the Agents of the Eye so when Tezzarq went back to Skullport to pay Lanador and recover the slaves he was confronted by an adventuring party supported by the Xanathar .
