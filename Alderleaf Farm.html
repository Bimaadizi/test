Alderleaf Farm was a farm in Phandalin .

Geography

The farm was located to the southeast of Phandalin.

History

Circa 1491 DR , the farm was owned and ran by Qelline Alderleaf . She was willing to let adventurers sleep in the farm's hayloft.
