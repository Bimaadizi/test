Entar Silvershield was a nobleman from the wealthy Silvershield family living in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century.

Personality

Relationships

Entar was the younger son of Torlin Silvershield and Evelyn Silvershield .

Entar's son Parendarkh left the family after a quarrel, but he still lived in Baldur's Gate in the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR .

History

Entar was born in the Silvershield family and lived in the Silvershield estate in Baldur's Gate. In the Year of the Narthex Murders, 1482 DR , Entar was six and he was living in the estate's nursery with his sister Alana .

He had children , but some of them left Baldur's Gate in the 1470s DR and there was no information about where they had gone or what had become of them.
