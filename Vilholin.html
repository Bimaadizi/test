Vilholin was a black dragon wyrm whose lair was in the ruins of Orogoth in High Moor of the Western Heartlands .

Relationships

She was the mate of the black wyrm Wastirek and the mother of the black dragons Woklef , Cheleen , Aswidorg , and Kasidikal .
