Hammer Veneficus was a unique exquisitely crafted combat hammer from the ruined city of Myth Drannor .

Powers

This weapon was imbued with a powerful +5 enchantment that granted it fearsome power in battle. Hammer Veneficus also possessed defensive magics that improved the wielder's protections as well as reactions. Lastly, the hammer had eight charges of the dispel magic spell .

History

By the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Hammer Veneficus was stashed within the lair of a lich who dwelt in the passages underneath Castle Cormanthor , along with another unique enchanted item, Sylmorrir , Shield of Power , as well as the lich's phylactery . The treasures were guarded by eight mohrgs subservient to their undead master.
