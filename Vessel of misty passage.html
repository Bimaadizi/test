A vessel of misty passage was a very rare enchanted receptacle that carried transportation magics.

Effects

When activated, a vessel of misty passage produced a cloud of mist that allowed a creature to step through and be transported to a new location. The artifact's magic transported a creature undetected. The cloud of mist and its gate -like magic remained active for a single minute.
