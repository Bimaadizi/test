Angus was an old moorhound owned by Tristan Kendrick , Prince of Corwell in the Moonshae Isles in the early 14 th century DR .

Description

Deep in his years, Angus had a worn brown face and a shaggy neck.

History

In 1345 DR , Daryth became the kennel master of Angus and Tristan's other hunting dogs.

Angus was previously the top dog of Tristan's pack, but he was quickly dominated by the new dog Canthus . Shortly afterwards, Angus was killed protecting Tristan and Robyn from a wild boar during a hunt.
