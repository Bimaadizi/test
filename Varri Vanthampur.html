Varri Vanthampur was an unwanted member of the Vanthampur family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Varri Vanthampur perished in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate. Varri's tombstone was commissioned to be carved by Candulhallow's Tombstones in the Lower City . The freshly-carved grave marker was stored in Candulhallow's shop's main room, just left of the main entrance. It read: "Varri Vanthampur. Unwanted in life, welcomed in death."
