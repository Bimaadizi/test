A wyrmtooth dagger was a type of enchanted knife carved from a dragon 's tooth.

Powers

Wyrmtooth daggers were deadly weapons that could be enchanted to varying levels of power. Those struck with a solid blow found themselves stripped of any elemental resistances. Additionally, sorcerers could channel their magics through a wyrmtooth dagger to bypass these same resistances.

Reputation

These daggers could be found from the Sword Coast to Tymanther . They were used by the frost giants of Kultaakarr and by members of the Cult of the Dragon .
