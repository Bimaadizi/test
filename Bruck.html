Bruck was a Netherese shipwright who worked at Gallery Steamers in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Bruck served Stuckley as a work crew manager for the construction of flying ships used in Netheril . She worked alongside fellow managers Fourcrow , Apulee , and Gau .
