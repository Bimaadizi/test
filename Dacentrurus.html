Dacentrurus ( pronounced : / d eɪ ˈ s ɛ n t r uː r ʌ s / day-SEN-troo-rus ) was a species of dinosaur .

Description

Dacentrurus looked like its cousin stegosaurus . Instead of large bony plates running along its back, dacentrurus had long, sharp spikes. It's tail ended in a cluster of spikes.

Behaviour

Dacentrurus were known for being aggressive.

Combat

When attacked, dacentrurus always positioned its body so their spikes were facing their opponent. It was capable of delivering significant damage with its spiked tail.

Biology

Dacentrurus were herbivores and lived in herds.
