Fist Repossessions was a collection agency from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Pammella Nortale of Nortale's Hostel fell behind on paying for deliveries from Durinbold & Oathoon . Martnev Oathoon , Valued Customer Disciplinarian, mailed Pammella a notice that unless she made a minimum payment for the previous month's shipment, she would be turned over to Fist Repossessions, despite the hostel being a non-profit.
