The temple of Waukeen in Bezantur was a major center of worship of Waukeen in Thay .

Location

This temple was located in Bezantur, the so-called "City of a Thousand Temples".

Activities

The temple was one of two major sites of Waukeen worship in Thay, the other being in Tyraturos .
