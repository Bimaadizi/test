Staves of vision were enchanted staves that gave the wielder access to four vision-related spells.

Powers

A staff of vision contained the darkvision , remove blindness and see invisibility spells, and two copies of the true seeing spell. Each time one of these was used, there was a chance that the caster would become mentally fatigued. It was worth around 43,000 gold pieces.

Creation

The creation process of a staff of vision was possible for someone skilled in staff-crafting. The spells needed for the creation included darkvision , see invisibility , remove blindness , and true seeing .
