Reverend Brother Harvestmaster Derim Whiteshield was a priest of Chauntea inhabiting Mistledale in the Dalelands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Derim was a soft-spoken old man as of the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

Activities

He was the head of the Abbey of the Golden Sheaf near Ashabenford .
