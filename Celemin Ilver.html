Celemin Ilver was an elf of the King's Forest in Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Celemin had long brown hair in a braid, pale skin, and pale eyes.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Celemin, Ravanys Woodsoul , and Arcton Greenwort represented the interests of the druids and dryads of the King's Forest at the village. Celemin implored the forces of Eveningstar to defend the forest, as the drow of Sschindylryn razed and corrupted the woods as they went.
