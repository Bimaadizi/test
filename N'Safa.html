N'Safa was the leader of the yuan-ti Se'Sehen tribe around 1374 DR .

History

Around 1374 DR, N'Safa led her tribe in betraying Sseth , instead pleading their allegiance to the new god Zehir . So the Se'Sehen destroyed the orthodox Sauringar tribe and took control of the Temple of World Serpent .
