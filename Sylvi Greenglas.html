Sylvi Greenglas was a dwarven bartender who worked with Hal Stoutheart 's Tethyamar expedition during the 1480s DR .

Description

Sylvi had light skin, dark eyes, and a bob of dark brown hair.

History

Sylvi joined the Tethyamar Mining Outpost not as a miner, but as a bartender. She worked at a makeshift tavern in the outpost, supplying the miners and guards with food and drink as they toiled away trying to find the jewels of ardor .
