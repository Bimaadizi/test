Thunn , who was also called the Usurper , was the second King of the Mulan Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Thunn betrayed his uncle, King Ashtukzu I , and claimed the throne for himself in −8 DR . He ruled until 4 DR when his cousin, Ashtukzu II , reclaimed his birthright.
