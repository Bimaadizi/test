The solution of stillness was a thick, oily substance that when poured in the ocean, could calm the waves and torrents in a space within a storm.

Effects

One bottle of this liquid calmed the waters in an area 50-feet in radius and last for 2-24 hours. It could also be used to negate any magical waves, such as those created by the spell tidal wave , or have the same effect of an antipathy spell when used on creatures from the plane of water .

Creation

This solution was created with the spells antipathy and control weather .
