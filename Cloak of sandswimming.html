Cloaks of sandswimming were a variety of magical cloaks that aided their wearer in traversing sand .

Description

These slick cloaks were made of satin and were rust-red in coloration.

Powers

Those who wore a cloak of sandswimming could effortlessly swim through sand as if it were water and could even breathe in sand at no detriment to themselves.

History

In 1374 DR , a group of priests of Tiamat living within the Altar of Scales (a secret temple in the undercity of Unthalass ) kept one of these cloaks in their treasury.
