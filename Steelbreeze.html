Steelbreeze was a manor owned by the Dauntrael clan's heads in the late mid-14 th century DR .

Location

The manor laid southwest of Beregost , nestled between coastal hills.

Structure

The manor laid in a walled garden state, and devoted one of its towers to an orrery belonging to Thurlad Dauntrael .

Defenses

The defenses of the Dauntrael clan usually sufficed.

Inhabitants

Besides them, there were twenty more Dauntrael family members, twelve mages and eight veteran warriors .
