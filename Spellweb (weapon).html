Spellweb were magic weapons , that were rumored to be used as tools to defend the island nation of Lantan from pirates and invaders.

Powers

These weapons fed off magic, enchantments , and spells , unleashed and even those spells that were still held within a mage 's mind. Any spellcaster that entered the area of a spellweb was rendered feebleminded and lost all magical abilities for a period of time, or even forever.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , the hin thief named Jonslyn Windtongue managed to steal one of the spellwebs . The Ayrorch , the Council of Twelve, dispatched the Hands of Gond agents to find the halfling thief and return what was stolen by all means.
