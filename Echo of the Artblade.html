The Echo of the Artblade was a powerful longsword derived from the legendary Artblade , found in the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The Echo was a deadly and fine weapon in its own right, though only a shadow of the true Artblade . It made the wielder a more powerful and precise spellcaster, granted them true sight and immunity to blindness, and even afflicted attackers with temporary nullmagic in response to magical assaults.

History

The weapon originated when the Artblade's own nature turned against itself, shedding a small part of its own power as an echo that formed as a lesser blade, also creating the armor of the Warblade . This Echo was potentially found during the reclamation of Myth Drannor, and was willing to be wielded by non-elves. If the Red Wizards of Droic Orgul managed to infuse it with the necromantic energies of the dracolich Karliath , the blade's strength of will would overcome the corruption and remain a tool of righteousness.
