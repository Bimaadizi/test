T'Raam , known by the Dukar as Peaceslayer , was the founder of the sahuagin kingdom of Aleaxtis .

History

In the year −740 TS (−810 DR) , T'Raam founded the kingdom of Aleaxtis. His reign would go on for 297 years, lasting until −443 TS (−513 DR) . Over the course of his reign T'raam would come to slay over a hundred Dukars , leading them to dub him Peaceslayer.
