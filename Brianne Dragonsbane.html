Brianne Dragonsbane was queen of the Kingdom of Bloodstone in the mid–15 th century DR .

History

Brianne Dragonsbane was assassinated in the Year of the Forged Sigil, 1459 DR , and the civil unrest caused by this event distracted the Kingdom of Bloodstone from the mustering army of the Warlock Knights in Vaasa . Spysong believed that Yarin Frostmantle was involved in her death.

Shortly before her death, she had an illegitimate son named Kaggen Entreri . The pregnancy was kept a secret from all but her most trusted advisors.
