Charri Hunzrin was a drow of Menzoberranzan and the heir of House Hunzrin in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Charri discussed with her mother, Matron Mother Shakti Hunzrin , about forging an alliance with House Melarn in order to attack the reformed House Do'Urden . She agreed to support the plot as a way to damage the surface businesses of Bregan D'aerthe that menaced the Hunzrins's own businesses.

Some months later, Charri went alongside the young Shak'kral Hunzrin to a meeting with a clan of spriggan followers of Malcanthet in Damara arranged by the Hunzrin trader Denderida .
