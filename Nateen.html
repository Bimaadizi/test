Fist Nateen was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Nateen had a human man with tanned skin tone, red hair, and beard. He wore a set of chainmail armor as part of the Flaming Fist uniform, decorated with the organization's emblem on a tabard . He was armed with a halberd and was ready to use it to punctuate his stern and cruel attitude.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Nateen was tasked with patrolling the Lower City, near the Basilisk Gate . He and his fellow patrolmen had no qualms with shutting protesters up with force.
