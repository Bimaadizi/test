Vol was a member of the Zhentarim that operating within secluded caves hidden beneath the Risen Road in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

She had a no-nonsense attitude much like her superior Zarys .

Relationships

Vol was often accompanied by the massive wolf Indra .

History

When Zarys ordered the Zhentarim hideout to be demolished in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Vol was among those members that helped set out explosives and gather all their loot.
