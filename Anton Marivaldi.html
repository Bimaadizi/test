Anton Marivaldi was a pirate from Turmish in the Sea of Fallen Stars in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Anton originated from Sapra in Turmish but after some crime he went into exile and became a pirate captain.

Wanting the reward of a bounty, Anton tried to capture the young Chosen of Lathander Stedd Whitehorn in the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , during the Second Sundering but in the end he chose to help him in his mission.
