Cheron was a Netherese fighter and weaponsmith who worked at Dundaden's Armory in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Cheron worked alongside Sleda to meet the demand for Rualadeen 's blades. Though the pair were proficient smiths in their own right, Rualadeen still contributed to their projects in order to ensure the highest quality.
