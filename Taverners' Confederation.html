The Taverners' Confederation was the guildhall for tavern owner's in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The guildhall was located at the junction of Guild Alley , Rogar Alley , and the Hill Road in the southern section of Daggerford north of the drill field.
