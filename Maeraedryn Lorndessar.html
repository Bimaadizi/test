Maeraedryn Lorndessar was the seventh King of the Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Maeraedryn came into the throne after his brother, King Roadaeron II's brief reign, in the Year of the Guide, 1192 DR . He ruled until the Year of Prideful Tales, 1219 DR .

Relationships

His grandson Ilphael would later become King.
