The Lifebringer was a unique circlet created by Yrre the Sparkstruck .

Powers

Whenever the wearer of Sparkle Hands built up electrical charges within their body―a feat achieved using some of Yrre's other inventions―their life force was bolstered. That increased vitality stayed with them until the charge left their body.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the circlet was in the possession of Blurg , a member of the Society of Brilliance who worked near Ebonlake Grotto .
