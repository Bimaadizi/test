Mamun al-Garib was the chief vizier for the sheikh of the House of Dhi'b in the High Desert of west Zakhara around 1367 DR .

Personality

Though he commanded genies and jann , Mamun would never dare question a ruling from Sheikh Anwat al-Makkar , whom he admired and feared.

Activities

In addition to advising his sheikh, Mamun was a skilled sha'ir in his own right.

Relationships

Mamun was actually the grandson of the sheikh's first vizier.
