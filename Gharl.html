Gharl was a human man and the War Lord of Nimoar's Hold (which would later become Waterdeep ).

Personality

Gharl was a standoffish man, but was determined and loyal to his people, viewing it as his duty to protect the people of Nimoar's Hold at any cost.

Possessions

He was known to possess a javelin of lightning .

History

Gharl spent much of his life engaged in warfare. He obtained the position in 936 DR when War Lord Nimoar the Reaver died. Previously, Gharl had been Nimoar's best general.

In the Year of the Cold Claws, 940 DR , Gharl ordered his men to light the fields outside of the Trollwall on fire to halt the advance of trolls . Meanwhile, he used his javelin of lightning to hold a number of trolls at bay.
