Dragon's Grasp was a minor enchanted handaxe from the Emerald Grove in the Western Heartlands .

Description

Dragon's Grasp was an ornate weapon, always warm to the touch.

Powers

This handaxe possessed an enchantment called burned alive that dealt additional damage to targets lit aflame.
