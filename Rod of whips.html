A rod of whips was a magic rod that could be commanded to transform into a +1 dancing whip by sprouting a tendril of force.

It functioned three times per day, with each transformation lasting for an hour. This whip acted as a spiritual weapon against incorporeal creatures.

The rod was worth around 21,600 gold pieces . It could be created with animate objects and spiritual weapon spells.
