Kroegel's Arms & Armor was a weapon and armor shop in the city of Neverwinter in the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

The shop was located not far from the Neverwinter Square , one of the oldest locations in the Jewel of North. America hanging sign above the entrance proclaimed the business's name to passersby.

History

Kroegel's was established sometime before the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR . The proprietor had a humble selection of mundane weapons and armor available for a reasonable price.
