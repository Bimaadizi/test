Knesha was a bard in Mulhorand in 1357 DR .

Activities

Knesha was the greatest non-divine expert in lost relics and artifacts in south Mulhorand. She spent all her free time studying about places where ancient magic items disappeared.

History

Once Knesha was a scribe in Skuld until a mistake in her calculations caused a loss of taxes. She was imprisoned but managed to escape and settled in Outer Sultim , where nobody respected Skuld's laws. There she found her true vocation as a bard specializing in satirical poems.

Personality

Knesha had grown bored of her work as a bard and dreamed of going on adventures.
