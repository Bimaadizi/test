Ulbaereg Bloodhand was a warrior who lived during the 5 th century DR .

Activities

In the Year of Full Cellars, 472 DR , Ulbaereg Bloodhand conquered the various tribes that settled and farmed the land that would one day become the foundation for Waterdeep . He organized the tribes into the Bloodhand tribe .
