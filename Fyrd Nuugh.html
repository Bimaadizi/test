Fyrd Nuugh was a criminal who worked against the infamous Guild in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Fyrd was a large half-orc with brownish tanned hide, aquamarine eyes, and and a good head of straight well-groomed strawberry blonde hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fyrd Nuugh could be found with his co-conspirator, the hin woman named Beulla Sandfoot in the Lower City near one of the docks. Their meeting place was a seaside gazebo, where the duo discussed the Guild and Nine-Fingers Keene while pretending to simply chat about the weather if unwelcome listeners were near.
