Sylgar was the name of the prized pet goldfish of the Xanathar , Zushaxx . As of 1492 DR , unbeknownst to its owner, the fish had died and been replaced by the members of the Xanathar's Thieves' Guild several times.

Possessions

Sylgar had two fishbowls : a larger one that weighed 6,000 pounds (2,700 kilograms) and was over 17 feet (5.2 meters) in diameter, and a smaller one used primarily for feeding that weighed 60 pounds (27 kilograms).

Relationships

Sylgar's main caretaker was Ott Steeltoes .
