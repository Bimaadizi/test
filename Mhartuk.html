Mhartuk was a human sorcerer and the Overking of Lapaliiya in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

His son was the proud and willful Prince Royal Shaliim Wyrmslayer .

History

In his younger days, Mhartuk wielded mighty magics and was considered one of the best battle-mages in Faerûn . He was described as a lion of a man.

However, he not only survived, he got old, and senile. By 1357 DR , the aged monarch had gained a reputation as a gentle scholar who passed his days playing games.

Finally, in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , he died of heartstop and Shaliim succeeded him as Overking of Lapaliiya.

Activities

Mhartuk enjoyed playing board games.
