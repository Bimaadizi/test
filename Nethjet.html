History

Nethjet fell into ruin during the events of the Spellplague in the Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR , when the upheaval of the Thayan Plateau and eruption of the Thaymount caused the entire region to become broken and transformed into the Bonedark Hills .

In the late 15 th century DR , Thayan forces began rebuilding and reinforcing Nethjet into a ringed fortress known as a Dread Ring , and patrols of Thayan troops (including undead increased around the city.
