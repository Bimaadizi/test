Ritual daggers of the Loviatan faith were enchanted blades created to inflict pain and suffering on the willing and unwilling.

Powers

Loviatan ritual daggers were decorated, sharp blades with their leather handles smothered by much use. These daggers possessed the Pain Maiden's blessing enchantments that boosted the wielder's ability to hit the target and increased their ability to withstand punishment after each successful slash against the target.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a ritual dagger was wielded by the torturer cleric of Loviatar named Abdirak , who allied himself with the Cult of the Absolute , interrigating the cult's prisoners in the Shattered Sanctum , a ruined temple to Selûne in the Western Heartlands .
