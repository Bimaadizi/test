Relationship

Dermin tutored many young Harpers, including the druid Jaheira , who he had originally recruited into the organization.

He was a member of the Athkatlan Harpers, who operated out of a luxurious estate near the city's docks. His associates included Galvarey , Berinvar , Rylock , among others.

History

As of the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Dermin, Galvarey and other Athkatlan Harpers were conducting themselves in a manner quite out of line with the greater organization. They lived a lavish life at Galvarey's estate, and resorted to murdering those they perceived as their enemies.
