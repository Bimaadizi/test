Rokkek Ingerr's residence was a row house and the home of Rokkek Ingerr located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The home stood two stories tall.

Location

Rokkek Ingerr's residence was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter on the west side of Weeping Maiden's Run .
