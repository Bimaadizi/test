Ramenhorus I was the second pharaoh of Mulhorand .

History

Ramenhorus I was born in -969 DR . He ascended to the throne of pharaoh of Mulhorand in -926 DR .

His reign lasted until -623 DR , when he died in battle against Narfell as they attempted to invade both Mulhorand and Unther by sea.
