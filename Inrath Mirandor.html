Inrath Mirandor was the founder and first king of the kingdom of Impiltur . He started the Mirandor Dynasty of Old Impiltur .

Born the son of Ornrath in −106 DR , he crowned himself king in the Year of Splendor, −74 DR , and founded the kingdom of Impiltur. His reign ended in −47 DR and he was succeeded by his eldest son, Iranth II .
