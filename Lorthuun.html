Lorthuun was a beholder member of the Zhentarim in Mantol-Derith in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Lorthuun joined the Zhentarim and was appointed to the crucial garrison of Mantol-Derith. At one point, it battled a behir , which left it greatly injured and missing four of its eyestalks.

Circa 1486 DR , it assisted Ghazrim Du Loc in keeping the situation in the trading post under control. It was targeted by the madness-possessed drow assassin Kinyel Druu'giir .

Relationships

Lorthuun had with Ghazrim Du Loc, the main delegate of the garrison, a relationship like that of a married couple, with them arguing and insulting each other often, but knowing both were loyal to the Black Network.
