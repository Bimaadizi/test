Northwind Strait was a channel that connected the Trackless Sea with the Sea of Moonshae in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The strait was located on the northern side of the Sea of Moonshae in Norheim Isles between the large island of Giantspine and the smaller islands of the archipelego to the east. Northwind Strait was approximately 50 miles (80 kilometers) long and 18 miles (29 kilometers) wide.
