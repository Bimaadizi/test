Corlarth Talanthil was the second Sorcerer - Arkhan of the Talanthil Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Corlarth inherited the throne when his father, Vayloss I , died in −559 DR . He ruled until his death in −499 DR .

Relationships

He had three sons. The throne passed to his third son, Horlarth .
