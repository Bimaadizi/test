Doraven Mirandor was one of earliest kings of Impiltur and the 16 th monarch of the Mirandor Dynasty . His reign lasted a mere three years during the 2 nd century DR . He was known throughout history as Doraven the Bloody .

Relationships

He was the third son of Inrath Mirandor III and the stepbrother of Inrath IV .

History

Doraven Mirandor staged a coup against his older stepbrother Inrath the IV and seized the throne of Impiltur for himself in the Year of the Lost Profit, 159 DR . He ruled over the Kingdom of Wand and Sword for a mere three years, before being slain by another stepbrother, Pendarn Mirandor .
