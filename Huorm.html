Huorm was a city along the Golden Water , east of Sezilinta . It was the home of the chakas Turamir and Salicur .

Description

The city was close to Adama's Tooth , a mountain 25 miles (40 kilometers) west; its shadow caused early sundowns all year round. The Datharathi chaka based their mining operations on the monolith from there.

The city had a port and a north gate. Sometimes, elves from Al Qahara traded the desert relics they uncovered in the city, looking for grain, cloth, or spices. They were aware of haddrum contraband smuggled in and out of the city to oasis towns around the desert.

History

The city had fallen into the Golden Water by the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .
