Sentinel was an enchanted medium shield that originated from the Sword Coast .

Description

This shield was forged from star metal . As such it was exceptionally lightweight, weighing only 3 pounds (1.4 kilograms).

Powers

Sentinel possessed a powerful +4 enchantment .

History

One day during the mid–13 th century DR , a comet from Realmspace landed on Toril on the coast of west Faerûn . A few days later the blacksmith Huffum Fuiruim encountered the crash site and gathered the pieces of metal he found. He used them to forge the magical shield called Sentinel .

At some point during or before the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the shield came into the possession of a drow merchant that operated within the city of Ust Natha .
