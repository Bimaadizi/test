Thrakkus was a red dragonborn butcher in Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Thrakkus came off as stern and serious, not taking kindly to those whom he saw as "fools" or those that wasted his time.

Abilities

Thrakkus, being a red dragonborn, could breath a cone of fire up to 15 feet (4.6 meters) and was resistant to fire.

Activities

Thrakkus worked as a butcher in Waterdeep's Field Ward , having converted a burned-down windmill into his butcher shop. However, his true activities involved butchering humanoid corpses delivered to him by the Zhentarim for disposal, and selling the meat.

Relationships

The dragonborn allowed squatters to reside in his home and business, provided they helped in other tasks or are not disruptive.
