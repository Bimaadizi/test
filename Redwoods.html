The Redwoods was a forest of redwood trees in the foothills of the Storm Horns in Cormyr .

History

When Mystra was slain by Helm in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , her death caused widespread destruction in the area surrounding Castle Kilgrave and razed the Redwoods, creating the Helmlands . Over the following century the land slowly recovered and by the late 15 th century DR the Redwoods had largely regenerated.
