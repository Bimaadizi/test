The Creel were one of the smallest tribes of Nars found in Narfell . There were 750 Creel in 1357 DR .

Activities

Because of their humiliating defeat at the hands of the undead forces under the command of Zhengyi , the Creel were notoriously violent toward anyone found on their land, including other tribes of Nar.

Base of Operations

The Creel inhabited the lands in northwestern Narfell along the shores of Icelace Lake .
