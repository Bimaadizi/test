Warrior Plagg was a devoted cultist of the Absolute who dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

He wore a suit of goblin -made plate mail armor and bore a greataxe in battle.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Plagg and other members of the Absolute's cult raided the nearby inn known as Waukeen's Rest . There they killed many Flaming Fist soldiers, and made off with some captives they marched off towards Moonrise Towers .

After the raid, Plagg, Nik-Nuk , and Crub played a favored game among the Absolute's goblin followers: fantasize how they would torture and kill someone.
