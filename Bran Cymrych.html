Bran Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid-to-late 4 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty , and was the second son of his predecessor, High King Cameron the Wise . His successor was his first son, Conall Cymrych .

History

Bran assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of Sleeping Dangers, 370 DR , after the death of his father. In that first year of his reign, the so-called " Great Colony Fleet " of Calimshan sailed for the Moonshaes, but it was blown off course and never arrived.

Bran ruled for eight years before dying of illness in the Year of Autumn Drums, 378 DR .
