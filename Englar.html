Englar was a Red Wizard of Thay in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the aftermath of the early spring 1373 DR War Wizard raid on the meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy in Marsember , the Red Wizard Narvo mentioned Englar during a debriefing to a superior through a speaking stone . He claimed Englar was not reachable through a mindlink spell, very strongly implying he had perished.
