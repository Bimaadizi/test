Nayloss Talanthil , who was also called Fireyes , was the fourth Sorcerer - Arkhan of the Talanthil Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Nayloss inherited the throne when his father, Horlarth Talanthil , died in −460 DR . He ruled until −408 DR .

Relationships

Nayloss' son and heir was Vayloss II .
