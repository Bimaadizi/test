Chenna Fatrabbit was the head chef of the Elfsong Tavern during the late 15 th century DR . Under her lead, the kitchen of the Elfsong produced tasty dishes such as syrup-covered loaf pudding, cheese-and-potato soup and crab cakes.

Chenna was known for being particularly cheerful. They supervised two other chefs, Azar Valsheem and Klav Martilmur .
