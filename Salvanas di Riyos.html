Salvanas di Riyos was an elven sailor who could be found philandering in the Copper Coronet of Athkatla in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Salvanas was a pale-skinned elf who wore green-colored clothing.

Personality

Salvanas accosted attractive women who frequented the tavern with flattery and attempts at seduction, however, in reality, his behavior hid insecurity and fear of actual intimacy.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Salvanas di Riyos frequented the Copper Coronet in Athkatla's River District slums. During that time he have had at least one encounter showing unwanted attention to the aspiring halfling paladin Mazzy Fentan , referring to her as his "little chestnut ." Another frequent visitor of the Coronet, Nalia de'Arnise , was well aware of Salvanas and eagerly turned him down, not letting him shower her with unwanted attention.
