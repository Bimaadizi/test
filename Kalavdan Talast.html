Master Kalavdan Talast was a worshiper of Gond inhabiting Tulmon along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was a thin man with a pronounced stoop to his posture.

Activities

Kalavdan was the high priest of the House of Cogs and Hammers which was one of Tulmon's newer temples at that time. Kalavdan excelled at envisioning new creations.
