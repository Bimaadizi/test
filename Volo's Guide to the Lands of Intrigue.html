Volo's Guide to the Lands of Intrigue was a book written by Volothamp Geddarm . It was a travelogue about the realms of Amn and Tethyr , two realms in the Lands of Intrigue . It was written over 1360 to 1370 DR .

Around 1372 DR , a copy was in the holdings at Candlekeep .

Elminster shared excerpts and an incomplete draft of Volo's Guide to the Lands of Intrigue with chroniclers on Earth , who published it as Volo's Guide to Baldur's Gate II .
