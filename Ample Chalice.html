The Ample Chalice was a rowdy tavern found in the fabled city of Myth Drannor .

Location

It was located at the intersection of Moonshadows Street and Scatterhorn Street , in the Sheshyrinnam district of the city.

Services

The Ample Chalice was one of the favorite haunts of the Akh'Velahr .

History

The boisterous Arnarra Gildenguard won ownership of the tavern in a game of chance. It quickly garnered a lot of business, becoming the tavern of choice for Ilphas Miritar , son of the city's Lord-Speaker.
