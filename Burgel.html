Burgel was an apprentice Red Wizard of Thay in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

He was apprenticed to Azmyrandyr .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the Simbul assaulted a temple of Shar somewhere in Thay , where the Red Wizard Azmyrandyr was preparing for an intrigue. This was a revenge attack after Azmyrandyr participated in an attack on Silverymoon . During her attack, Azmyrandyr and his apprentices were rehearsing the impersonation of several notables from Nimpeth ; Burgel himself was practicing his impersonation of Noster . He was slain by her opening salvo of magic missiles .
