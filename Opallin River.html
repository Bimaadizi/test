The Opallin River was a prominent river found within the Pasocada Basin of northern Maztica .

Geography

The Opallin River branched from the larger Pasocada River where the Azuposi pueblo of Opallinoc was founded. The river flowed south then southeast for approximately 15 miles (24 kilometers). The river branched at Kinopal with the western branch terminating in the land of the Dog People while the eastern branch terminated within the lands of the Rockjaw clan .

Notable Locations

Settlements found along the shores of the Opallin River included Opallinoc, Kinopal, Keshtin Pueblo , Mitzlato , and Kin Elbhaz .
