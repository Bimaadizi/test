The Iron Hand was a smithy shop located in the village of Eveningstar during the later 14 th century DR .

Location

The Iron Hand was located just west of the Market Square near the center of Eveningstar.

Inhabitants

Master Armorer Dhurthal Ironhand owned this establishment.
