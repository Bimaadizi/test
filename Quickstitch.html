Quickstitch was a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Fist Quickstitch was an olive-skinned human male with blue eyes. He wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist: a helmet , a set of chainmail , and a tabard adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. Quickstitch wielded a halberd .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Quickstitch was stationed in the Outer City of Baldur's Gate, within the Wyrm's Crossing fortress.
