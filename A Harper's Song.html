A Harper's Song was book penned by Alustriel Silverhand , the High Lady of Silverymoon , in the late 13 th century DR .

History

A Harper's Song was published in the Year of the Dying Stars, 1284 DR . One notable part of the publication talked about the powerful high magic and how it was still operating with the Mother of Magic Mystra's permission. A copy of the book was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
