"Lanric" was the name given for a Harper agent active on the Sword Coast North in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Lanric worked to stop the bandits and pirates that plagued the Sword Coast and the High Road north of Neverwinter in the late 1470s DR. He was based out of the Broken Crown inn.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , during Neverwinter's Protector's Jubilee, Elminster visited the city and bade adventurers who sought him out to carry an important coded message to Lanric. It contained new orders and a message from Elminster that Lanric was to relay to other Harpers between Neverwinter and Luskan . Lanric figured it was safer for the adventurers not to know the details.
