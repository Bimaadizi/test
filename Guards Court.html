Guards Court was a hard-packed dirt and gravel paved cul-de-sac in the southern section of the Castle Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This cul-de-sac originally stretched south off of Niles Way , ending in a dead-end block of buildings that were bounded by Crossbow Lane , Niles Way, and Snail Street . By the 15 th century DR , Guards Court had expanded and it came to instead run off of High Hillock Lane .
