The Red-eyed Owl was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tavern stood two stories tall.

Location

The Red-eyed Owl was located in the far southeast corner section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the souteast corner of the intersection of Belnimbra's Street and Rainrun Street .
