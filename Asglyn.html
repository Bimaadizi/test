Asglyn was an adventurer and cleric of Tempus during the early-to-mid 3 rd century DR . He was a member of the Brave Blades .

Description

He was hard-eyed.

Abilities

Asglyn was capable of recognizing some esoteric spells.

Possessions

He carried a warhammer .

Activities

As one of the Brave Blades, he was an adventurer.

Relationships

He was one of the Brave Blades .

History

Asglyn was a member of the Brave Blades, who was a part of the party in their expedition to Ondil's Tower in the Year of Much Ale, 233 DR .

After reaching Ondil's Tomb, they found that all other pathways out of the tower led back to Ondil's tomb; after Dlartarnan died trying to open the door by hand, Asglyn attempted a battleshatter spell that was responded with a deadly flame, killing him quickly.
