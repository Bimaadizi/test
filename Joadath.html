Joadath was Lord of Shadowdale between 1273 DR and his death in 1300 DR . He was an agnostic who believed that the deities held no power. This was a belief that led, in 1297 DR , to the massacre of Tyrists on Watcher's Knoll .

Joadath died in 1300 DR when an unknown individual brought a beast from the nether planes to Shadowdale.
