Roads' End was a tomb located within the City of the Dead in Waterdeep .

Location

Roads' End was located in the eastern section of the City of the Dead near the outer wall of the city.

History

The tomb was used as a communal burial site for travelers who perished while visiting the City of Splendors. Any unclaimed possessions of the deceased were interred with their corpses, making Roads' End an appetizing target for thieves .
