Zelphar Arunsun was a powerful human wizard, originally from Neverwinter . He was the son of Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun and married Lhestyn . His children were Khelben Arunsun the Younger , who was often confused with the Blackstaff, and Cassandra Thann , matriarch of the winemaking House Thann . Zelphar was killed by magical means, although his killer was never discovered.

Background

In 1311 DR , Zelphar was killed by Bigby's crushing hand , a spell cast by a lich of the Twisted Rune , as a favor to the Shadow Thieves .
