Vince Torell was a convicted criminal from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Vince Torell was accused of being a spy for the Absolute . He was arrested, tried, and condemned to public execution of treason. As the Absolutist army marched on Baldur's Gate and unbeknownst to all, the new Grand Duke Lord Gortash being one of the Absolute's leaders, Vince Torell, alongside a fellow prisoner Durm Goroy , was to be transferred from the Stonyeyes Precinct of the Flaming Fist to the Basilisk Gate prison on the orders from Manip Farthingale . Basilisk Gate had a handy plaza with a hanging stage set up for public executions at the time.
