Saichik was a small village in the Alpuk region within the Great Glacier .

Geography

Saichik was located in central Alpuk.

Inhabitants

The residents of the village has vowed to take revenge on a particular white dragon . The white dragon was identified by a birthmark on its forehead in the shape of a star. The grudge was due to the fact that this white dragon killed the son of a kiam .
