The Lands Against the Wall was a group of foothills located on the southern slopes of the eastern stretch of the Spine of the World .

Geographical Features

This region was completely free from any man -made landmarks such as roads or settlements. It was full of pine trees and a number of small lakes.

History

During the late 14 th century DR , the orcs of Many-Arrows maintained regular patrols across the region, turning away travelers and adventurers.

Inhabitants

Several herds of particularly large deer roamed across the regions. Orcs, hobgoblins , and Uthgardt barbarians claimed it under their respective hunting territories.
