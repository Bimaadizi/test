Roberts was Teziir 's harbormaster in 1357 DR .

History

In 1357 DR Giogioni Wyvernspur , on a mission for Azoun Obarskyr IV , took a boat from Suzail to Westgate with a stop in Teziir. Roberts was present on board, negotiating a ten gold pieces increase in the docking fee. He later supervised a disembarking of the passengers.
