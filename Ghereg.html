Ghereg was a ogre living in Kuldahar Pass during the 11 th century DR .

Description

The large ogre wore silver jerkins and fought with a morning star .

Personality

Ghereg was not a mean ogre, but did suffer from horrendous headaches.

History

In Eleint of the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , Ghereg was drawn to Kuldahar Pass by some unknown calling and met a group of mercenaries. In pain and anguish, the ogre told the adventurers of his headaches.

Rumors & Legends

One rumor was the company told Ghereg how to cure his ailment, possibly with the help of Arundel . The ogre was to pick red and purple frostberries , mash them, and combine them with boiled tundra grass .
