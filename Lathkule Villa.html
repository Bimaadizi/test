Lathkule Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Lathkule .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's North Ward on the southwest side of the intersection of Whaelgond Way with Hassantyr's Street . The south side of the villa lay along the Street of the Manticore .

Description

Four buildings faced each other surrounding a garden within the walled compound. The buildings were three storys tall.
