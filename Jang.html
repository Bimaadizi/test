Jang was a Shou member of the Marsember Watch in Cormyr during the late 15 th century DR . He was Kleef Kenric's first blade.

Personality

He was a loyal and dedicated watchman, who lived an honorable life. He had notable skill as a swordsman.

Possessions

Unique among his comrades in the Watch, Jang wielded a sleek katana .

Relationships

Jang had a good relationship with his superior officer, Topsword Kleef Kenric , and could regularly be relied upon to carry out important tasks.

History

The Second Sundering since the Year of the Narthex Murders, 1482 DR flooded the Xiousing district of Marsember , forcing many Shou like Jiang to move.

On Uktar 2 of the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Jang was part of a contingent of Marsember Watch and House Seasilver guards who accompanied Kleef Kenric; Arietta Seasilver ; and two Chosen, Malik el Sami , follower of Cyric , and Joelle Emmeline , follower of Sune , out of Marsember in order to fulfill their mission to rescue Elira Seasilver .

During their sea voyage, he was the only watchman to stay behind when all the other watchmen tried to deceive Prince Yder Tanthul and his men into believing that the Eye of Gruumsh was with them. With the Shadovar busy, Jang, Kleef, and the others assaulted the Wave Wyvern , a vessel the Shadovar had commandeered. Afterward, Jang and the other watchmen were tasked with taking Arietta's mother , who had been the Shadovar's prisoner, to safety to Westgate .
