Yul Summerstar was a moon elven leatherworker and furrier for hire from the city of Neverwinter , active in the late 15 th century DR .

History

During her armorer for hire days in the late 15 th century DR, Yul Summerstar's work was guided by one simple rule: the resulting clothing or armor should always be at least as beautiful as the animal who died for its fur and hide.
