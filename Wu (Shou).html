Wu , who was also called Mad Wu , was a Shou who lived in Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Activities

Mad Wu was an innkeep who owned the Hospice of Wu the Mad located just outside of Almorel's East Gate along the Golden Way .
