Shan Karsha was the dominant peak of the Teyla Shan mountain range.

Geography

Shan Karsha stood on the eastern rim of the range near the midpoint of its north to south expanse approximately 150 mi (240 km) southwest of Nanhu Oasis .

Description

This peak rose to an elevation of 14,331 ft (4,368.1 m).
