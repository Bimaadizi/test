Adarbron was a swordmaster captain of the guard in Hastarl during the early to mid-14 th century DR .

Description

He kept his graying beard neatly trimmed.

Relationships

Off-duty armsmen were quick to line up behind him, strongly implying he was a figure commanding some respect among the guard.

History

As swordmaster in the Year of the Black Flame, 229 DR , while off-duty, he stopped a fight from breaking out at The Kissing Wench , a tavern of Hastarl in Athalantar , where Jansibal Otharr responded poorly to Thelorn Selemban . He threatened to report to the magelords any death or bloodletting.
