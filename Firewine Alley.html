Firewine Alley was a back alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

Firewine Alley was located in the west section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . It ran between the Fieldway to the south and Nanze Street to the north, connecting with both larger roads at several locations.
