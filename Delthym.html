Delthym of Chauntea was a cleric of Chauntea and one of the leaders of the Fellowship of the Purple Staff in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Delthym was a huge man who was losing what white hair he had left.

Personality

He was known to be a kind man.

Possessions

He had some land west of Alaghôn in Turmish on which he grew crops.

Activities

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , he and other clergy of Chauntea, Helm , and Selûne from around the region got together and started an organization called the Fellowship of the Purple Staff whose aim was to bring about a new country they called the Holy Realm . They planned to occupy and settle in the portion of the Gulthmere Forest east of Cedarsproke . Over time, they acquired members from the churches of Lathander and Sune , and convinced the followers of Nobanion that they would care for the forest and its creatures.
