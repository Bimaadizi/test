Scrivenscry was a conceited arcanaloth who traveled the planes in order to locate the Books of Keeping .

Description

Scrivenscry referred to itself in third-person, and enjoyed licorice . The arcanaloth was ferocious and heinous, especially to those in service to it.

Relationships

It had in its servitude a nervous, albino, blind, awakened giant penguin called Kingsport .

Possessions

The arcanaloth owned a chime of opening , and three spell scrolls .

History

In the 15 th century DR , the fiend made its way to the Prime Material Plane . It sought out four tomes containing the true names of every yugoloth in existence.
