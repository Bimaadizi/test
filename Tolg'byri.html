Tolg'byri was a nilshai sorcerer in Sildëyuir in 1372 DR .

History

In 1372 DR , Tolg'byri discovered the Night Realm and the portal that connected Sildëyuir with the Yuirwood in Toril . It used this opportunity to gather an orc and hobgoblin mercenary army in order to conquer all Sildëyuir.

However, Tolg'byri was taken by surprise by the yochlol Savera , who infiltrated it army by posing as a half-orc . The nilshai underestimated the enemy, seeing a weak half-orc, and Savera managed to kill Tolg'byri.

Afterward, Savera hid Tolg'byri's corpse in a flooded laboratory. However, there the deathlock Kyjal Stardancer discovered the body and turned it into a zombie .
