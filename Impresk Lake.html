Impresk Lake was a lake in Erlkazar .

Geography

The water of Impresk Lake was ice cold. It was fed by a large number of rivers and streams, some flowing off from the Snowflake Mountains and others from the Shining Plains . Impresk Lake drained into the Deepwash by means of the short Shalane River . The lake formed part of the eastern border of the barony of Impresk .

Notable Location

The town of Carradoon , the birthplace of Cadderly Bonaduce , was located on the southern shore of the lake on a small peninsula.
