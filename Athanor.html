TypeAlchemical device

An athanor was an alchemical device that was used in the process of transmutation .

History

Over the course of many years, the alhoon known as the Beast Lord used athanors of varying sizes to create twisted creatures from the goblin population of Dekanter . Eventually, it create a man -sized, egg-shaped athanor made of brass that was powered by one of the lost Nether Scrolls from the ancient empire of Netheril .
