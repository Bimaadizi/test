Avalathar trees were scrub trees native to the Shaar .

Description

These trees were hardy enough to grow in the water-poor Shaar. They had characteristically curved, bow -like trunks.

Uses

The strong trunks were used as ceiling supports in dugout-style homes favored by Shaaran communities like Rethmar .
