The Red Larch Rambler was a tavern in the town of Red Larch .

Interior

The tavern was a large, well-known, and well-lit tavern. The establishment was decorated with hanging plants. Families and respectable folk of Red Larch came to the Rambler to drink and converse. The Red Larch Rambler was a public gathering place.
