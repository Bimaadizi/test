Sticky Dondo was a thief and fence member of Fetcher's Brats and later the Guild in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Dondo was a young human boy with a tan skin tone, light brown eyes, and strawberry blonde hair. He wore a garb dyed with the Guild's green color and had a direct and straight-to-business demeanor.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Sticky Dondo was a part of a street urchin gang led by Osgur "Fetcher" Hallorn . When the man disappeared following a guild war breaking out between Nine-Fingers Keene and the Stone Lord , Fetcher's Brats were taken in by the Guild and given shelter in the Guildhall. Sticky Dondo took on the role of leader among the kids, fencing stolen and looted goods to the members of the Guild, and Li'l Fezmor kept herself busy, sending clients to Sticky, and masterminding heists.
