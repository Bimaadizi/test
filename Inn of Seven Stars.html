The Inn of Seven Stars was an establishment in the small city of Ormath .

Services

This inn offered hot meals and strong alcohol. For those staying they offered hot baths, and clean sheets. Some of the food served as this establishment included roast mutton, stewed potatoes , candied yams , and sweetbread .

Inhabitants

Those who ran this establishment catered to both locals and travelers.
