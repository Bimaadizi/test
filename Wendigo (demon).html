A wendigo was a type of demon found within the Abyss . Wendigos were brought to creation when a creature engaged in cannibalism. The act would open a gateway allowing the wendigo to come from the Abyss. Those that were turned into wendigos through willing cannibalism sometimes enjoyed their newfound form and power, while those who committed the offense out of starvation would suffer as a result.

Abilities

Wendigos could sense fear, and were able to turn invisible.

Combat

Wendigos fought with their bites and claws, relying on stealth as they hunted their prey.
