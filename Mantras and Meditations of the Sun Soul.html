Mantras and Meditations of the Sun Soul was a religious work of the Order of the Sun Soul , published sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a copy of Mantras and Meditations of the Sun Soul belonged to Old Jol and was found in his humble home in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate .
