The River Eltar was a small body of water located in the nation of Thay in the Unapproachable East .

Geography

The River Eltar originated high on the Thaymount in central Thay. It flowed east down the mountain for a short distance before connecting with the southwest corner of Lake Thaylambar .

Notable Locations

Eltabbar was located on the south side of the river near Lake Thaylambar.
