Ellyka was a tiefling woman who lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

She had light pink skin, white hair, and two black curved horns that extended upwards.

Personality

She described herself as just "another fool" just trying to survive in the world.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Ellyka encountered a group of githyanki led by Kith'rak Voss and his red dragon steed, facing off against a contingent of Flaming Fist soldiers at a canyon crossing. She made sure to stay clear away from the group and not draw any unwanted attention to herself.
