Sorgar Illance was the head of the Illance noble family of Cormyr in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

He was an ex-adventurer who was both bald and bitter. He was a thief and cruelly enjoyed brawling, especially to the death. He was put to death by Gaspar Cormaeril in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
