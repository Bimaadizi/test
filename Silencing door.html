A silencing door was a Dweomered door that, when shut, would cast silence upon itself, affecting anyone nearby. These doors were always made to automatically shut when unattended and often had an accompanying pass phrase or key that would temporarily negate it's magical effects.

Creation

These doors were crafted using the spell silence .
