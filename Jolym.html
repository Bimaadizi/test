Jolym was a cooper and crate maker at Jolym's Barrels & Packing in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 th to late 15 th centuries DR .

Activities

From his store circa 1366 DR , Jolym sold barrels , hand kegs , and standard-sized crates. Jolym also manufactured packing crates of custom size or purpose.

History

In the 1490s DR, the thieves Edgin Darvis , Holga Kilgore , Forge Fitzwilliam , Kira , and Simon Aumar pulled a robbery in Loudwater. The sorcerer Simon tried to cast a spider climb spell but couldn't make it work until Jolym set his dog on him, and then he had no trouble at all.
