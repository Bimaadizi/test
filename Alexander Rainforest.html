Alexander Rainforest was an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Alexander Rainforest perished in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate. Rainforest's tombstone was commissioned to be carved by Candulhallow's Tombstones in the Lower City . The grave marker was stored in Candulhallow's workshop. It read: "Alexander Rainforest. Meticulous in life, mellifluous in death."
