Organization

The Carthclarrs had very angular features and were considered good looking. They carried themselves well and put on airs, denigrating those around them with snide comments and observations. They were well educated, and some members practiced the Art . Their plots against family rivals were executed over long periods of time, putting their skill as subtle manipulators to good use. For quicker results, they were not above the use of poisons .

Activities

Many of their business ventures were in the cities on the coast of the Shining Sea , in countries such as Tashalar , Lapaliiya , and Calimshan .
