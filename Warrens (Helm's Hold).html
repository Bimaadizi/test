The Warrens , also known as the Underscar , was an series of underground tunnels that snaked beneath an area of Helm's Hold .

Geography

The Warrens' other nickname originated from the fact they were located beneath Scar Alley .

History

As Helm's Hold grew in population, local engineers were forced to expand city infrastructure. They began excavation beneath Scar Alley in order to accommodate new tunnels for drainage. Unfortunately they inadvertently broke through into the subterranean Crypts of the Vigilant Eye , and were overrun by monstrous creatures. By the time the Spellplague wreaked destruction upon the region, in the Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR , The entire undertaking was left unfinished and the tunnels were abandoned by city officials.

During the Siege of Helm's Hold , circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the Ashmadai took hold within the Warrens, led by a devil known as the Shepherd of the Damned . The monstrous fiend was slain by the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge before the siege finally came to an end.

Rumors & Legends

It was rumored that some tunnels deep within the Warrens connected to the underground Sanatorium , located beneath the Cathedral of Helm .

Inhabitants

Since the Warrens' abandonment, its passageways became a haven for small, monstrous humanoids such as kobolds and goblins , that had been afflicted by spellscars .
