Jensena was a strong female soldier who drank at the Laughing Goblin Inn .

Description

Jensena was tall, attractive, and had powerful muscles.

Personality

Bawdy and tough, Jensena was straight forward and could tangle with any man.

Possessions

Like her friends, Salen and Gwen , Jensena fought with a sword , a few daggers , a throwing axe , and wore chain mail .

History

On the 19th of Flamerule , 1340 DR , Jensena challenged Ren o' the Blade to a contest of strength, but loss to Ren's sly actions. Nine days later, Jensena lost her companions in a battle with a raging troll outside the city walls and was saved by Ren.
