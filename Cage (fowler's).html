Cages , also called fowler's cages , were tools sometimes used by rangers or woodsmen throughout Faerûn .

Description

Cages were typically constructed from brass or bamboo imported from Hungtse province in Shou Lung . They collapsed to a flat panel for easy storage when not in use. A bamboo cage weighed 2 pounds (0.91 kilograms), while brass cages weighed around 5 pounds (2.3 kilograms).

History

These items were used to train birds and small animals .

Cages were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 8–12 gold pieces depending on the material used for their construction.
