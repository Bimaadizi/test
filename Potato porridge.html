Potato porridge was a common simple dish found in the Western Heartlands in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

These stewed starchy potato bowls were served in eateries of Baldur's Gate , settlements along the Chionthar Vallery , and Waukeen's Rest inn along the Risen Road . The porridge was sprinkled with herbs and diced yams .
