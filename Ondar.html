Ondar was a farmer in the village of Toralth in Tethyr in 1370 DR .

History

In 1370 DR, he lost six head of cattle to some gnolls .

Soon after, on Uktar 10 , Ondar was invited by his best friend Tadaz to the Blue Badger Inn in order to honor Tadaz's son Tural heading off to join Tethyr's new army at Riatavin . He was there when three dwarves , Bapar Ghalmrin , Odak Truesteel , and Soram Battlebellow , arrived, seeking the Sondarr Scepter .
