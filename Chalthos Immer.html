Chalthos Immer was a man living in the city of Wheloon in Cormyr . He was the proprietor of the Immerhand Inn .

Chalthos Immer was once a mercenary warrior. During his career, he relied on huge storm lanterns . He also managed to loot a number of paintings from all around Faerûn .

He later retired to Wheloon to run the Immerhand Inn by 1358 DR , and was still in business circa 1369 DR . He furnished each room of his inn with his favored storm lanterns, and with one of his looted paintings.
