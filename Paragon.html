Paragons were specialty priests of the Church of Torm who behaved more like paladins and maintained a close relationship with the church mainly around Tantras in the Vast .

Activities

These individuals performed specific quests in Torm 's honor, hoping along the way to attract more followers for their god through their virtuous example. They were required to complete difficult heroic quests in order to advance their station.

Culture

Paragons were completely loyal to Torm and took any vows with extreme devotion. All paragons were human .

Abilities

Paragons shared all of the abilities of a paladin save for their spell abilities. They also shared all of a paladin's weaknesses.

Possessions

Paragons wore white plate mail polished to a glistening shine. Large white helmets and sky-blue cloaks completed their ensemble. They usually did not carry a shield since most wielded either a two-handed sword or a bastard sword with two hands.
