Badr Al-Mosak was an evil ruler who stole the Star of Shah-Pelar and had it buried with him upon his death.

History

Badr Al-Mosak stole the Star of Shah-Pelar from its rightful owner, a pharaoh of the desert whose name was lost to antiquity. The gem became his favourite treasure, and he had it placed in a trapped room within his crypt upon his death.
