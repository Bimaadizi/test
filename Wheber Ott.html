Wheber Ott was a necromancer that hailed from the Sword Coast region of Faerûn for a time during the mid–14 th century DR .

Possesions

He wore a robe of cold resistnace , and was armed with a +1 enchanted dagger .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Wheber joined the Zhentarim mage Niemain , along with two other spellcasters, Willaim Garst and Oulam , to cast an especiallyparticularly difficult spell on the second flor of the Sorcerous Sundries in Baldur's Gate .
