Small Klaw was a worg kept by the goblin members of the cult of the Absolute .

Description

She was significantly smaller than Klaw , another of the cult's worgs.

History

For a time in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Small Klaw was forced to guard an outpost just beyond Shattered Sanctum .
