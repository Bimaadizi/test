Rhys Ravenwind was a renowned bard and member of the Harpers .

Personality

Despite being a bard, Rhys was quite shy.

Relationships

Rhys was friends with both Danilo Thann and Arilyn Moonblade .

History

Rhys was killed in Waterdeep by the Harper Assassin in 1361 DR .
