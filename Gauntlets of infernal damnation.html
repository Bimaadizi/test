The gauntlets of infernal damnation were a type of cursed set of gloves.

Description

These gloves were made of a hard, black leather.

Powers

Being a cursed item, these gloves became affixed to whoever put them on and they could only be removed by the spell remove curse . In addition, upon donning these gloves the spell shroud of flame would be cast upon the wearer.

History

Some time prior to the late-13 th century DR , a sinister priestess of Beshaba invented this type of cursed item. Wealthy adventurers would occasionally buy these to place in their treasure hordes as traps for potential thieves .

In 1281 DR , a pair of gauntlets of infernal damnation were found within the Wyrm's Tooth Glacier in the possession of a tribe of frost giants led by Joril Frostbeard .
