The Lion Tower was one of the towers in Castle Obarskyr .

History

Per Vangerdahast 's recollection in Alturiak of 1365 DR , the Lion Tower had been built in 1306 DR and much furniture was moved about. One of the rugs in the tower, bought in bulk from Wheloon , wound up in a room last used by Amedahast .
