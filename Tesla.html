Tesla was an individual in the town of Heldon in the early 3 rd century DR .

History

In the Year of the Flaming Forests, 224 DR , during the razing of Heldon by Undarl Dragonrider and his steed Anglathammaroth , Prince Elminster Aumar , while escaping, took notice of an escaping fat woman, whom he took to be one of Tesla's seamstresses; this strongly implied Tesla owned some shop.
