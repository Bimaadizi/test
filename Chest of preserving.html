A chest of preserving was a magical chest that prevented its contents from rotting or decaying.

Description

A typical chest of preserving had a half-barrel lid and was 2.5 feet (76 centimeters) long, 1.5 feet (46 centimeters) wide, and 1 foot (30 centimeters) tall. Its interior was divided into compartments for storage. It could be closed with a non-magical lock, which, if destroyed, rendered the entire chest non-magical.

Powers

Food, drink, and any perishable items placed inside a chest of preserving did not age or decay.

Notable Owners

The wizard Muiral owned a chest of preserving , which he kept in the Muiral's Gauntlet level of Undermountain .
