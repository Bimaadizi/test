Wackernagel was a Netherese arcanist who worked at the Miners' League in Karsus Enclave .

Activities

Wackernagel and Ochsenbein were hired by Gneissenau to manage the various arcanists and diviners who were responsible for locating and identifying new deposits of rich ore.
