Dagnan was an outlaw member of Broadshield's Beasts in Cormyr .

History

In 1480 DR , Dagnan was with Broadshield when they tried to to capture Arclath Delcastle and Amarune Whitewave in order to deliver them to Alorglauvenemaus .
