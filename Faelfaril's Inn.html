Faelfaril's Inn was one of the oldest and least comfortable inns in Bryn Shander .

Description

Many small holes in the walls let the chill air in, cutting the wind chill factor but otherwise leaving the inside barely warmer than the outside.

History

The inn was originally built by newcomers who hadn't spent a winter in Icewind Dale . As of the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , the proprietor was Faelfaril .
