The Splurging Sturgeon was an inn in the city of Baldur's Gate .

Location & Structure

It was a small two-story building located in the northeast region of the city.

Description

Rooms were spartan and the sheets threadbare, and the inn had a rowdy atmosphere, but for all that it was decent and cheap.

As of 1482 DR , the Splurging Sturgeon served as a popular location for seafood dishes from around Faerun.

History

The Splurging Sturgeon was in operation by 1358 DR , was still running by 1368 DR , and later struggled for decades as a "threadbare" tavern until taken over by Hennut Griot , from Ormpur , who revitalized it and as of 1482 DR it served as an establishment specializing in seafood, both exotic and local.
