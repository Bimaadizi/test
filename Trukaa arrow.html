A trukaa was a type of arrow used by Angulutiun and Iulutiun hunters and by other followers of Ulutiu .

Description

A trukaa was a bone arrow with a blunt tip, specifically designed for the purpose of dispatching small game without causing harm to their fur.

Availability

Though relatively rare outside of the Great Glacier , twelve trukaa arrows could be purchased for 3 sp .
