The ceremonial garments of the Black Orchid were enchanted robes that belonged to the aarakocra people of Kir Sabal in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The garments of the Black Orchid were magical vestments that increased the wearer's defense, as well as aided them in channeling the powers of the Wind Dukes of Aaqa during rituals of the Dance of the Seven Winds . The ritual bestowed temporary flight upon its subjects.
