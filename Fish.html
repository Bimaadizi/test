A fish was a type of limbless aquatic animal that was often used as a source of food by many creatures.

Description

Most types of fish were generally not a threat, but giant fish could be deadly foes, sometimes swallowing foes whole.

Ecology

Fish of the same type often gathered in "schools". They often did not attack others unless magically commanded to do so. They were herded by merfolk , and sometimes guarded by nixies .

Religion

Blibdoolpoolp , the goddess of the kuo-toa (who themselves were fish-like humanoids), often took the form of a woman with a fish's head.

Some societies believed that krakens were deities—such creatures may have bestowed plentiful harvests of fish upon their worshipers, should the krakens be pleased with their deeds.

Usages

Several creatures ate fish as either part of or all of their diet. Some of these creatures included bronze and black dragons . Tantran finefish was a type of spiced and pickled fish produced in Tantras.

Fish could be summoned by a variety of spells, from monster summoning I (which could summon a single lamprey ) to monster summoning VIII (which could summon a giant shark ). They were also sometimes used as familiars . Rancid fish could be used as a material component for the spell lich touch .

The infamous beholder Xanathar kept a goldfish named Sylgar as a pet.
