Shields Italazar was an armorsmithing business in Calimport , Calimshan around the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .

Location

The shop was centrally located in the Taorahl drudach (neighborhood), part of the Erar√© sabban (district) of Calimport's Hook Ward . Shields Italazar was considered to be part of the Eye Tyrant's Khanduq , even if it was located outside the khanduq 's main building.

Structure

Shields Italazar was a simple, one-story building.

Services

Famous for the craftmanship of its proprietor, Kahim el Tunril , Shields Italazar mostly dealt in bulk armor orders for the guards of various pashas . The business also made copies of individual armor pieces, particularly unique pieces that intrigued Kahim.

Inhabitants

The shop was owned and operated by Kahim el Tunril, who oversaw a small staff.
