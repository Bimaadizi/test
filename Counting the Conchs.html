Counting the Conchs was book of devotions for the Umberlants , the devotees of the Bitch Queen Umberlee , published sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Content

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , one of the conch -themed religious books could be found in the Water Queen's House , the temple of Umberlee in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate .
