Yeti skin cloak were enchanted cloaks that carried magical properties of yeti ' pelt they were made of.

Powers

These cloaks granted the wearers resistance to mundane and magical cold.

History

Circa Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , Oswald Fiddlebender crafted various magic items and clothing using pelts of yetis that plagued Icewind Dale Such yeti fur items were a great protection against harsh weather of the Frozenfar . Such clocks appeared as early as the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR
