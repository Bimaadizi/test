Strukkat Stephammer was an experienced dwarvish artificer -for-hire from the city of Neverwinter , active in the late 15 th century DR .

History

By the late 15 th century DR, Strukkat Stephammer had years worth of experience in artificery. However, he began experiencing failing eyesight, which diminished his capabilities to a degree. Despite that, he was still a skilled and hard worker. He made a living by selling his armorer's services to the adventurers and defenders of Neverwinter .
