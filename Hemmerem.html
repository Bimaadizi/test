Hemmerem was a laborer inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He discovered that slaves were being secretly transported with a caravan while he was working a job for the Piiradost clan. For his discretion regarding the matter, the Piiradost bribed Hemmerem and was provided work with the family's horse -breeding business.
