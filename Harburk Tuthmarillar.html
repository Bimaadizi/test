Harburk Tuthmarillar was the constable of Red Larch during the Elemental Evil crisis on 1491 DR .

Activities

Harburk directed his duties from a shop in the village, where he lived together with the town's butcher Jalessa Ornra . He also worked in Jalessa's shop as a butcher.
