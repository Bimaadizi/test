Olin Mirynne was an elf fighter in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Olin had blue eyes and light skin. He wore a full suit of silvery chainmail with leafy-styled plates, and carried a fine shield and axe .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR during the Elven Crusade , Olin joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their large camp on the outskirts of Myth Drannor . He served as a trainer for fighters there.
