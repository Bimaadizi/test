Heroes' Height was a broad mountaintop in southern Sossal in the Cold Lands of northeast Faerûn .

Geography

Heroes' Height stood in southern Sossal, 200 miles (320 kilometers) west of the Icerim Mountains , and 100 miles (160 kilometers) south of the settlement of Dharvil .

History

It was on this mountaintop that the last dwarves of Dareth and white dragons of Hoarfaern destroyed each other in a climactic battle in Year of Crying Wolves, −318 DR .
