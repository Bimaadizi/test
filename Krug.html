Krug was a goblin devotee of the Absolute who lived in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He wore the attire commonly worn by goblin booyaghs and warlocks .

History

Krug was among the cultists of the Absolute who raided Waukeen's Rest in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . During the assault, he fell and cut his neck on a dagger . He subsequently died from his wounds.
