Jandar Stargazer was a Mystran adventurer from the Living City , Ravens Bluff , active in the late 14 th century DR .

History

As reported in the Tarsakh , the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , issue of Ravens Bluff Trumpeter , Jandar Stargazer of Mystra was one of many adventuring comrades of Briar Oldhill who attended the party and the grand opening of Briar's Patch , a huge residence, boarding house, chapel, and colocated stables on the northeast side of Ravens Bluff. Jandar Stargazer signed the event's guest book and partook in the complimentary punch and cookies .
