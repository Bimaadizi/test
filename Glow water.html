Glow water was an alchemical substance made by hedge wizards .

Powers

Its effects were unspecified, but it may have possibly glowed. They possibly lasted for up to four hours, or perhaps a full day.

History

The substance was very challenging to synthesize, requiring a combination of very advanced alchemical and herbal skills; hedge wizards often traded in it. It sold for about 200 gold pieces .
