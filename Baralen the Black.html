Baralen the Black was an infamous pirate who once kidnapped the daughter of Lord Tygarian .

History

Sometime before 1372 DR , Baralen managed to capture and hold hostage Lord Tygarian's daughter. The pirate's ransom was paid with a set of enchanted light armor , Fencer's Grace . However, Baralen refused to release his prisoner anyway. When Lord Tygarian's daughter found out, she flew into blind rage and killed her kidnapper with a single strike. She took the ransom armor and used it to become a pirate herself.
