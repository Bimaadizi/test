Merkul was a priest of Bane from the Dark God Reformed , tempe of the Dark Lord in Voonlar circa 1373 DR .

History

In the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Merkul descended into the catacombs under Voonlar, home of banelich named Shrazagul , imprisoned there by the Church of Bane mani years prior. With Bane's death and return, wards that restarted the banelich faded and the temple of the Dark God Reformed attempted to restore the ancient prion. Merkul was slain by Shrazagul's undead, leaving behind his enchanted +1 plate mail , adorned with Bane's holy symbols and regalia.
