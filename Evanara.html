House Evanara was an insular sun elf house. As of 1368 DR , it was led by the fighter - wizard Nylaathria Evanara . It was notable for producing war wizards . The house's estate was a large grassy compound filled with moon horses and several small villas.

The house colors were blue and white and its symbol featured a prancing white horse .
