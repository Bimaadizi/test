Manthuran Glaezel was the head of the Glaezel family of Baldur's Gate in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Manthuran came to lead the prominent and long-standing Glaezel family some years before the late 14 th century DR. Under his control, the family came to own Glaezel's Dock in the Seatower district of the Lower City .
