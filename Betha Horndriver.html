Betha Horndriver was a skilled young woman who performed music in Yhaunn in Sembia in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In Eleasis of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Betha appeared at a high-society party hosted by Ammanas Aumleagarr at his estate in Yhaunn, where she played the harp for the attendees. Among others, she briefly met Moonmistress-Designate Feena Archwood of Moonshadow Hall .
