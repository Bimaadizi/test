Tomboldt the Pallid was a member of the Cult of the Absolute who serviced "special" clients of Felogyr's Fireworks shop in Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Tomboldt was an ashy-skinned Lolthite drow with snow-white hair and a stern facial features. He wore black and dark red robes .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute planted its roots in Baldur's Gate and ravaged the Western Heartlands , Felogyr's Fireworks shop fell under the cult's sway. Its proprietor, Avery Sonshal , was made into a True Soul and used the shop as a front for the cult's terrorist activities. During that time, shop's second floor, which was only accessible to customers who knew a code phrase, and warned anyone from proceeding to the shop's third floor, which was off-limits to all but the Banite members of the Cult of the Absolute, which included Tomboldt the Pallid.
