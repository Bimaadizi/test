Rosymorn firewine was a variety firewine distilled by the Rosymorn Monastery of Lathander before the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Rosymorn firewine had a pleasant color but had a harsh strong smell of alcohol and a weak soft flavor.

History

Rosymorn firewine was produced by the Rosymorn Monastery before its fall to a force of githyanki sometime in or shortly before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . Bulbous green glass bottles of Rosymorn firewine could be found in the city of Baldur's Gate and in the locales of the Chionthar Valley in the late 15 th century DR.
