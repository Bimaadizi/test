The skullduggery kit was a magical set of thieves' tools found in Wheloon Prison during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The toolkit was worn about the waist as a belt.

Powers

The skullduggery kit broadly improved the mental acuity and manual dexterity of the user as they worked, benefiting almost any roguish project.

History

The tools were recovered by Cormyrean forces from the interior of Wheloon Prison after fighting with Archwizard Tyvrus during the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon .
