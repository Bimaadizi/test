Teremon was the king of the pixies of Panjuis until his death in the early 14th century DR .

Personality

Teremon was a disciplined ruler who maintained order within his special fortress.

Activities

Panjuis fell into a state of disrepair after Teremon's death since his leadership was not present to keep the pixies in line.
