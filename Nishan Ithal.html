Nishan Ithal was the third king of Tethyr and the Ithal Dynasty .

Relationships

Nishan was the second son of King Silvam Ithal . He had a younger brother named Tyrom . He was the father of twins, Garynor and Rhynda .

History

Nishan was born in the Year of the Huntress, −179 DR . He succeeded his father King Silvam upon his father's death in −118 DR .

Nishan had a short reign of about 20 years. He died in the Year of Hearts Pledged, −98 DR , and was succeeded by his son Garynor.
