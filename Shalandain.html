Shalandain was a human paladin crusader member of the do-gooder group called the Company of the Chalice based on the Rock of Bral in the mid–14 th century DR .

Possessions

Shalandain wore a powerful magical +3 field plate , a +5 holy avenger long sword and a mundane shield . Other enchanted items of hers included boots of starstriding and potions of flying .

History

Shalandain belonged to a band of heroes who reverted to good and hunted down evil – the Company of the Chalice. Shalandain was the second-in-command of the group's Rock of Bral base that focused on crusades against the neogi .
