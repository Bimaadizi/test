Reynald was a duck -breeder from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Reynald was a beloved family man, a father and a husband, who perished in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate. Reynald's tombstone was commissioned to be carved by Candulhallow's Tombstones in the Lower City . The freshly-carved grave marker was stored in Candulhallow's workshop. It read: "Beloved Reynald. Father, husband, duck-breeder. Gone too soon."
