Athyr Worrel was a Cormyrean armor trader in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Athyr had dark skin, long black hair in braids, and wore a purple dress.

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar during the mid-1480s DR , Athyr had a stall in the Eveningstar Market Square , where she sold second-hand armor. There was no word on what happened to the former wearers.
