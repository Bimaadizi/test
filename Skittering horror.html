A skittering horror was a giant, worm-like aberration that originated from the Far Realm .

Description

They had many curved claws running down the sides of their bodies.

Personality

While they did not regularly possess knowledge of known languages, at least one skittering horror was capable of intelligent speech. It had the tendency to offer valuable information to others for its own benefit.

Combat

Skittering horrors could crawl along the sides of surfaces or along ceilings with ease. They attacked others with their numerous claws or giant mandibles.

These aberrations were so horrifying that their mere presence could cause onlookers to become paralyzed with fear.
