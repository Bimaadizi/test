Master Mirren was a powerful wizard inhabiting Saerloon in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Mirren was a devout follower of Azuth , the Lord of Spells. She was active with the House of the High One , a temple dedicated to Azuth found in Saerloon.
