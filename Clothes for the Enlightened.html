Clothes for the Enlightened was a shop located in the Grand Bazaar of Huzuz in 1367 DR , owned and operated by Hakim Sayad .

Description

This shop was actually a pair of large red-striped pavilion tents .

Services

Hakim carried all of the items needed to complete an entire wardrobe.

With silk purchased directly from the House of Al-Kamari , Hakim earned a reputation for offering fabulous clothing items. Several members of the Royal Court of Huzuz purchase their garments from Hakim.
