Bolok was a domesticated bear , formerly belonging to a group of gypsies , that lived in the Kingdom of Mertwig around the 14 th century DR .

History

When King Mertwig was on the verge of death at age 65, various fortune hunters and individuals claiming to be his long-lost heirs scoured his kingdom in search of wealth and treasure. During their journey to claim the throne, the would-be heirs of King Mertwig met Bolok and purchased him from his owners for 15 gold pieces to join them on their adventure.
