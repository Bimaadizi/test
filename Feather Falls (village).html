Feather Falls was the formerly largest settlement in Featherdale , though by the late 14 th century DR it was second to Blackfeather Bridge . It was located at the base of the waterfall after which it was named. The village served as the primary dock for boats that navigated down the River Ashaba .

Notable Locations

Featherdale was also home to the ruins of a temple dedicated to Leira .
