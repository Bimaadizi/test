The College of the Herald was a bardic college from Herald's Holdfast that instructed the bard members of the Heralds of Faer√ªn .

Organization

The main focus of the Heralds was to collect and preserve knowledge of ancient history, legends, heraldry, and folklore, and to make it available for those of good and peaceful intentions. For that reason, most Herald bards studied the College of Lore and practiced its methods. There was little focus on musical performance, but the library had a vast collection of songs.

Base of Operations

The college's main site was Herald's Holdfast, northwest of Silverymoon .

Relationships

The Heralds were allies of the Harpers , but chose to remain neutral in most conflicts in order to keep preserving knowledge as their top priority.

History

The college was founded at the same time the Heralds themselves were formed in 922 DR by the Harper Aliost Oskrunnar .
