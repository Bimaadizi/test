Snakes or serpents were reptilian animals that had no legs. Any snake that managed to live for a particularly long time was referred to as an elder serpent .

Behavior

While the majority of snakes possessed a basic animal-level of intellect, many believed that they grew wiser and more cunning with age, with those referred to as "elder serpents" believed to possess a semi- or low-intellect. Elder serpents were said to fearless in the face of fire, hoarders of gems , and having grown intelligent enough to speak languages with a lisp, such as Midani in the case of Zakharan serpents.

Significance

Hordes of snakes were seen as a sign of favor from Gargauth , the Lost Lord of the Pit.
