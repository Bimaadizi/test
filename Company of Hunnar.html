The Company of Hunnar were a mercenary company based in Riatavin in Amn around 1358 DR , specialized in siege warfare, usually in concert with other mercenary units.

Organization

Membership

The Company was divided into three units: 100 men-of-arms expert in siege weapons like catapults, ballistae , rams, and bores ; 50 fighters and thieves expert in destroying wall with ladders, siege towers, and other means; and 50 sappers who dug underneath.
