Description

Pai'Na dwelled within a massive spider nest that she had built with the tomb complex beneath the city's Grave District . She was protected by a number of giant spiders , including a wraith spider and a sword spider .

Within her lair, Pai'Na kept a number of human bodies that were filled with spiders. At her command the spiders would burst forth and swarm at her direction.
