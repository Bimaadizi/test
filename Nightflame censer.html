Nightflame censer was an enchanted censer created by the Black Flame Order dedicated to Kossuth , the Lord of Flames.

Powers

When used in battle, the nightflame censer channeled the deadly power of the forbidden black fire . The black flames spewed from the artifact, burning the enemies surrounding the wielder. Those unfortunates who were scorched by the black fire, were lit aflame, physically weakened, and made vulnerable to all incoming forms of harm, for the duration of them burning.

History

Black Flame Order's nightflame censer was created sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .
