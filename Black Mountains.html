The Black Mountains were a mountain range located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Black Mountains were found in northwest Gwynneth, forming much of the northern border of Myrloch Vale . They touched Aspenheight in the west and were separated from the Cambro Mountains in the east by the Codsrun Creek . The Deepglen Stream flowed south from the Black Mountains' eastern slopes. The Black Mountains were approximately 60 miles (97 kilometers) long.
