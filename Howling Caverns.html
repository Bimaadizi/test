The Howling Caverns were a network of tunnels beneath the Orsraun Mountains ; by the late 10 th century DR , they were mostly large enough even for the largest dragons to move in. By the late 14 th century DR , they had collapsed.

Description

The labyrinth joined various meltwater caverns; wind constantly moaned through them, giving them their name.

History

In the late 10 th century DR , Meldryn Jalensifer lured the Magister , a black dragon named Constulgrael , to the location with a rumor and ambushed him, slaying him and claiming the mantle of the Magister for himself.

By the late 14 th century DR , the caverns had collapsed.

Rumors & Legends

To lure Constulgrael, Meldryn had spread the rumor of an ancient draconic crypt, belonging to a sorcerous dragon and full of spells unique to dragons. Some sources implied that the caverns may have in fact been dug by dragons.
