Declan Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid–8 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the son of his predecessor, High King Pendar . His successor was his son, Ciaran Cymrych . Ciaran's successor would be Declan's nephew, Ardan Cymrych .

History

Declan assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Glass Eye, 749 DR , following the death of his father.

He ruled for thirteen years before being poisoned by his son Ciaran and dying in the Year of the Snow Sword, 762 DR .
