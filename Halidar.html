Halidar was a Knight of Athalantar who turned to banditry between the early and mid-3 rd century DR .

Abilities

He was uniquely fast.

Relationships

He was one of the Knights of Athalantar , and secretly, became at some point a spy for the Magelords of Athalantar .

History

In Year of the Chosen, 240 DR , Halidar was a bandit with the Knights of Athalantar under Helm Stoneblade . On that year, a scrying crystal was discovered by their elven allies, and Ruvaen , one of them, brought the crystal to Helm's attention, informing him that one of his men had it hidden when he wasn't using it. Helm showed the crystal afterwards to Halidar, who immediately moved as if to flee; before he was able to flee, a fellow Knight, Anauviir , struck him down.
