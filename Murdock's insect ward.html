Murdock's insect ward was a type of magical , aromatic oil with an insect repellent effect that was invented by Murdock .

Powers

Any mundane or monstrous insectoid creature that tried to come within 5 feet (1.5 meters) of the wearer would be repelled by the fragrance. Though insectoid creatures with a level of intelligence that was Semi- or greater were capable of potentially resisting the effect.

The magical effect of an application of this aroma remained potent for around two to four hours, after which time it would evaporate from the wearer.
