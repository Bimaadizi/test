Everard's morning star was a weapon that was once owned by the famous priest that lived in Easthaven during the late 13 th century DR .

Powers

Everard's morning star possessed a +2 enchantment .

When it struck an arcane spellcaster there was a chance they would forget one of their previously memorized spells .

History

Some time after Everard saved the Ten Towns in the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , his namesake morning star came into the possession of the merchant Joluv .
