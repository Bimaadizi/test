The Dragonviper's Anchor was an inn located in the city of Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR .

Description

A large anchor that was once equipped on the Dragonviper ship dominated the front foyer. The inn appeared old and worn out.

Inhabitants

Olem Surtrar , who was once the captain of the Dragonviper , owned and operated the inn.

Activities

The Dragonviper's Anchor was quite comfortable despite its aged appearance.
