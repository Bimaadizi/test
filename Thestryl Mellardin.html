Thestryl Mellardin was a pale girl mage and a member of the Harpers in Waterdeep sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

Activities

Thestryl lived inside the City of the Dead cemetery. Her duty was to protect one of teleportation circles of Krowen Valharrow 's network using a shield guardian .
