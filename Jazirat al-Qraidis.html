Jazirat al-Qraidis , also known as Shrimp Island , was an island in Jazayir al-Sartan .

Geography

The island was dominated by a massive volcano formed at its center.

Activity

Sams Bandar , a small settlement, was located on the southern coast of Jazirat al-Qraidis in the shadow of the looming volcano.
