Windspell Street was a major street in the Temples District district of Baldur's Gate and the location of the High House of Wonders and the Ducal High Hall .

Location

Windspell Street was a mostly straight street that stretched from the Citadel Gate and The Run in the northwest, passing the Lionsmoot and Sornbanner Street , and the High Hall right after as well as crossing Belltoll Street , separating the High House of Wonders and the Hall of Wonders and reaching the Old Wall in the southeast. There was at least one rooming house located on Windspell Street, west of the Wide .
