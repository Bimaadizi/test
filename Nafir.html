The nafir ( pronounced : / n ɑː ˈ f ɪər / nah-FEER ) was a Zakharan wind instrument similar to a trumpet.

Description

Nafirs resembled long, thin trumpets and were made from metal.

History

Though less common in south Zakhara, the nafir was used widely in both Qudra and the Free Cities as a signalling device and to send military orders into the field.
