Sommersdal was a small village located on the island of Norland within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The village sat along the western edge of South Jotunhammer Mountains within the region of Norland called Grondsgrunn which was controlled by giants . The village was found on Norland's eastern coast with the Sea of Moonshae .
