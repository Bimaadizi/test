Jasim Island was an island within the Golden Gulf and Crowded Sea of interior Zakhara .

Geography

Jasim Island was located west of the southwest corner of the Cities of the Pantheon region of Zakhara. It was the northernmost island in Al-Faraq Islands . The island sat roughly 15 miles (24 kilometers) west of the mainland near Fanus . The interior of the island was elevated above the perimeter, and the entire western coast was covered with coral reefs. Jasim Island was approximately 45 miles (72 kilometers) long and 15 miles (24 kilometers) wide. The island was uninhabited as of 1367 DR .
