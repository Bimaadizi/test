Karvin's and Thurnan's Oven was a walk-by, takeaway style of food shop in Tantras in the late 14 th to 15 th century DR .

Services

Rather than being a proper restaurant or tavern, Karvin's and Thurnan's Oven served handfoods to people on the street from out of a serving shutter. One of the handfoods they were known to offer was the Tantrasan classic baeranth . Like many shops of its type, the business was open from highsun to dusk .
