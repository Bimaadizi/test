Trollwall Way was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

Trollwall Way was located in the western and central section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . It ran along the Trollwall on the western side of the Field Ward in between the High Road and the Trollyard .
