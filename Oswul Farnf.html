Oswul Farnf was a retired mercenary and owned the Diamond in the Dung inn in Hillsfar .

Activities

Oswul retired from mercenary life at a relatively young age. He owned and operated the Diamond in the Dung and though his prices were high, he would allow guests to trade equipment to pay for their visits.
