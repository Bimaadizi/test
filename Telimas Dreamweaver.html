Telimas Dreamweaver was the elderly founder, and patriarch, of the gnomish town of Stormpemhauder in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Possessions

Telimas dressed in multicolored robes and carried with him an ornate pipe, a +1 staff , and a +2 ring of protection .
