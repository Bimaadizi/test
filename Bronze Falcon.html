The Bronze Falcon was a province of the Yikarian Empire in 1367 DR .

Geography

The Bronze Falcon was the southern most province of the empire, covering the southern tip of the World Pillar Mountains where they bordered the Jungle of Monsters in northeast Zakhara .

Inhabitants

The Mountain Prince ruled this province, usually at the head of his army.

Activities

Given its location, the Bronze Falcon was well situated to combat any threat to the Empire from the great jungles or any lands from the east.
