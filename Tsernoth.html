Tsernoth was a necromancer that inhabited Iriaebor .

History

In the 14 th century DR , the necromancer made a deal with the banshee , Agatha . As part of the deal, Tsernoth received Bowgentle's Book , a spellbook of the renowned mage , Bowgentle . Over 100 years later, the Harpers attempted to discern the location from the banshee, to no avail.
