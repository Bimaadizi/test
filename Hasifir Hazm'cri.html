Hasifir was the half-drow Queen of Dambrath in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR .

Activities

Hasifir assumed the throne of Dambrath in the Year of the Worm, 1356 DR , when she transformed her mother, Queen Yenandra into a spectral guardian.
