Stellok Kolraavi was the dreadful great chief of the Black Lion tribe during the late 15 th century DR . As a leader he did not believe in acting diplomatically and had no ties with nearby "civilized" cultures.

Relationships

Stellok's younger sister, Tysis served as the shaman of the Black Lion tribe. She was the only advisor he could truly rely upon.

Possessions

Great Chief Stellok wore hide armor made from the skin of an orc .
