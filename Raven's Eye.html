The Raven's Eye , formerly known as the Red Sail Inn or Red Sail Tavern , was a tavern and inn in the hamlet of Crow's Foot in the 14 th century DR .

Location

The Raven's Eye was situated close to Ravens Bluff 's protective outer stone wall. The back of the building faced an alley, where trash was strategically piled to ensure it was little trafficked.

Structure

The tavern was housed in a stout wooden building that stood two stories tall and had a basement. The stairs to the second floor, which boasted sixteen rooms, were along the western wall of the building. In addition to the front entrance, the building had an exit in its office and kitchen, the latter of which was aligned with the stairs to the basement.

Atmosphere

The business primarily catered to sailors and rogues . It was considered rather standard as far as taverns went around Ravens bluff, but was always full of customers. Patrons could often be found playing card games or darts .

Activities

Under its third owner the Raven's Eye was used as the center for a fencing and smuggling operation, a forgery racket, as well as a safehouse for wanted criminals. The criminals were mostly confined to the inn's basement in order to avoid detection, whilst its wanted "guests". Those wishing to conduct illicit business with the owner usually entered from the back entrance.

Inhabitants

The tavern was first owned and operated by Gov Landrum , who originally bought it with the aim of helping others with their problems. By 1360 DR , the tavern was sold to an old fisherman and later Verity Overinn .

Verity employed a number of tavern wenches, all attractive and buxom, as well as a watchmen who stood guard on the second floor, watching out from a boarded-up window.

History

On a foggy night around the spring or fall season of the Year of the Harp, 1355 DR , a group of human hooligans posing as members of the Night Watch were trying to press a protection racket on Landrum, demanding he pay them 40 silver pieces . Luckily for Landrum, actual members of the Night Watch arrived on the scene alongside vampire hunter Jack Harker and chased off the imposters.

In the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR , a group of fire worshipers emerged in Crow's Foot called the Cult of Red Ruin and established a temple close to the tavern. Its members frequented the Red Sail Inn quite often, giving Landrum the impression that their cult was an excuse to party.

Sometime later that year the cult found and trapped within an iron flask a pyre elemental . Over the next two weeks the cultists caused several fires in Ravens Bluff, until one day they caused a significant wave of fires in Crow's Foot, Old Town , and the Warehouse District . A group of volunteer members of the city's firefighting service, the Red Ravens , later stopped by the Red Sail Inn on the night of the fires just as four members of the Cult of Red Ruin were stopping by to pick up eight bottles of wine for their evening services. The cultists would leave the establishment at around 11 and a half bells .

In 1360 DR, Gov Landrum suddenly had to leave Crow's Foot in a hurry and sold the Red Sail Inn to an old fisherman who renamed it "The Bloated Crab". Business floundered under the fisherman and it was not long before Verity Overinn convinced him to sell the building to her. She would go on to change the name to "The Raven's Eye" and start using it as a base for various criminal operations.
