The Delimbiyr Road , also known as the Delimbiyr Route and the Shining Trail , was a trade road in the Delimbiyr Vale that ran from Loudwater in the east to the Trade Way in the west.

Geography

It roughly followed the route of the River Delimbiyr from its northern banks, taking in the settlements of Loudwater, Zelbross (at about the halfway point), and Secomber . In the west, it joined the Trade Way near Julkoun , and a short trail led south to Daggerford . East of Loudwater, it joined the Dawn Pass Trail . The road was roughly 20 feet (6.1 meters) wide and was formed of hard-packed earth with deep wagon ruts. Tall grasses and wildflowers covered the sides of the road, but trees were prevented from growing right up to the edge of the road by outriders from passing caravans.

Trade

Traders doing business in the Delimbiyr Vale preferred to follow the Delimbiyr Route, rather than go through sleepy Uluvin .
