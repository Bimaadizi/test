The ' Umberwood' was a forest nestled at the eastern base of the Glittering Spires in the northern Hordelands .

Geography

The Umberwood lay protected from the frozen winds blowing off the Yal Tengri . It was watered by the snowmelt that ran eastwards from the mountains and was a dry forest, unlike the swampy Ama Basin . The rivers quickly faded into the steppe, making the woods a narrow band between mountain and steppe.

Geographical Features

The Umberwood covered the lower slopes of the mountainside, occupying deep-sided valleys. The rivers rushed down, cutting sharp narrow valleys into the land, separating the rivers themselves with knifelike ridges.

Flora & Fauna

Birch , elm , and larch were abundant, and their trunks were often covered in fungi and mosses . Brush, willows , and berry bushes stood in thick patches wherever an opening was made in the dense woods.

Monstrous centipedes , huge spiders , giant vultures , and ki-rin were to be found roaming the forest.

Small tribes of bakemono and hobgoblins lived in the Umberwood, fighting amongst themselves for food and land while raiding out into the steppes.

Yu lungs could be found in the rivers and lakes of the forest, while chiang lungs were a rarer inhabitant of the Umberwood.

During winter season, earth kala and yuki-on-na were to be found in the forest, disappearing as spring came to the land.

Inhabitants

Delmyr , a remote and little-known kingdom of hin , lay in the Umberwood and on the eastern slopes of the Glittering Spires.
