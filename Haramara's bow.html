Haramara's bow was a non-magical, masterfully crafted bow , originally belonging to Haramara Alenuath .

It was said that Haramara slew the red dragon Skarra with a single arrow from the bow. It was an item of great significance to House Alenuath and by 1368 DR it was in the possession of the house's leader, Thalanil Alenuath .
