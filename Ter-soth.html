Ter-soth was a balor commander in the service of Orcus in the Abyss in 1357 DR .

Activities

Ter-soth led a regiment of 100 glabrezus guarding the conduit to the palace of Orcus. If a powerful enemy attacked, he abandoned his men and fled to the palace asking for reinforcements.
