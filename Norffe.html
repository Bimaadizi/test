Chymist Norffe was an alchemist and cultist of Bane who served the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Norffe was a human man who wore dark hooded Baneite robes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Cult of Bane joined forces with the Cult of the Absolute , led by the Chosen of Bane, Lord Enver Gortash . Chymist Norffe was a part of the Cult's operations that targeted the countless refugees that flooded Baldur's Gate. His branch of the Cult assembled donations and toys rigged with smokepowder explosives in the Felogyr's Fireworks shop's third floor. Presumably, Norffe perished during or shortly before the Cult attacked Baldur's Gate.
