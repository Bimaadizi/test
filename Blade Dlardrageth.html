The Blade Dlardrageth was an enchanted broadsword wielded by Xhalph Dlardrageth in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The Blade Dlardrageth possessed a powerful +2 enchantment that made its blade more deadly and guided the weapons towards its target. It was also a giant slayer â€“ a weapons particularly destructive to true giants and giant-kin such as trolls , ogres , ogre magi , titans , ettins , and the giants of Zakhara . The same enchantment also could knock a giant down on a successful slash. Lately, the Blade Dlardrageth greatly boosted the wielder's physical strength.

History

In the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the Blade Dlardrageth was still in Xhalph Dlardrageth's hands within the dreaded Hellgate Keep , where the sword was used to gleefully dice unwitting adventurers.
