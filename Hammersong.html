Hammersong was a warhammer that was wielded by the avatar of Gaerdal Ironhand .

Powers

This powerful warhammer had a +4 enchantment. Whenever Hammersong hit a solid surface, it emitted a deafening clang . Everyone who heard this ear-shattering ringing would be rendered deaf for a time, but Gaerdal himself was immune.
