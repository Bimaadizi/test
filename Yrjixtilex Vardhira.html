Yrjixtilex Vardhira was a dragonborn of Tymanther and the matriarch of Clan Yrjixtilex in 1486 DR . She was a direct descendant of Esham-Ana .

Description

Vardhira had coppery-tinged scales with a silvery-gray patina.

History

In 1486 DR, Vardhira got involved in an investigation into a series of murders in Djerad Thymar , when a city guard member of Clan Yrjixtilex was killed by a maurezhi servant of the God -King Gilgeam .

In the wake of the first Tymanther-Unther War , Vardhira allied with other clan elders, such as Verthisathurgiesh Anala and Fenkenkabradon Ishkhanak , to put forward Yrjixtilex Kallan as a candidate for the Vanquisher title, in the hopes of raising her clan's status, and to outmaneuver Clan Kepeshkmolik .
