Traveler's Yarn was a type of magical spirits available in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The enchanted beverage was on the menu at the Yawning Portal tavern of Waterdeep, with its bottles stashed away in Durnan 's cabinet. One serving of Traveler's Yarn was priced at ten gold coins .
