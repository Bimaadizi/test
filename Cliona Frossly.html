Cliona Frossly was a client of the Sharran House of Grief and an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Cliona sought the services of the House of Grief aid. The Mapping of the Heart revealed Cliona's "violent heart." The ritual was paid for in full and Cliona was referred for Unburdening .
