Oil of stillness was a magical substance that originated from the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Powers

When poured into violent seas oil of stillness would have a calming effect on the water around the ship similar to the calm water spell. This effect last for one hour but worked for both normal and magical waves.

Description

Oil of stillness typically came in a 1 pint (0.00047 cubic meters) flask.
