Codicillary Rules of Engagements for the Flaming Fist was one of the book of rules to be followed by the members of the Flaming Fist in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Codicillary Rules of Engagements for the Flaming Fist were found across the Flaming Fist barracks and detention centers of Baldur's Gate. One of these books, found in the Basilisk Gate barracks, was hollowed out to hide a common-size flask.
