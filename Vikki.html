Vikki was a Cashguard Basher in the employ of the Counting House of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Vikki was a human woman with olive skin and long straight dark hair. As a Cashguard Basher, she wore a set of uniform plate mail armor and a helmet . Vikki was armed with a two-handed war hammer .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Cashguard Basher Vikki was tasked with guarding and patrolling the private docks that belonged to the Counting House in the Lower City , alongside a number of fellow Cashguards and the city's steel watchers .
