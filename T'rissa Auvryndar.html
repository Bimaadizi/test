T'rissa Auvryndar was a drow lieutenant of House Auvryndar in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR .

Activities

T'rissa lead the Auvryndar advance army that settled inside the Sargauth Level . There she began experimenting with how use humanoids as incubators for giant spider eggs. She was busy with her experiments inside the main room of the temple.

History

T'rissa was a priestess of Lolth and a daughter of Matron Mother Vlonwelv Auvryndar .

In 1492 DR , T'rissa lead a Auryndar contingent inside the Sargauth Level and managed to occupy a ruined temple to Dumathoin , which she decided to use as a hatchery for giant spiders.

Personality

T'rissa truly hated non-drow, even refusing to speak with them. She was as malevolent as her goddess. She believed that her experiments would gave birth to a new larger and more formidable breed of giant spiders.

Relationship

Once T'rissa was in love with Krenrak until she grew bored and put him in prison. She had a pet chimera .
