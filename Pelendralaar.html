Pelendralaar was a dracolich in league with the Followers of The Scaly Way , a sect within the Cult of the Dragon , that sought to gain control of all of Faerûn by corrupting the Mythal of Myth Drannor .

Description

In life, Pelendralaar was a red dragon .

Relationships

His partner was Kya Mordrayn , a powerful sorceress who operated a cell of the Cult of the Dragon.

History

In 1369 DR , the dracolich aided by the Cult of the Dragon tried to spread corruption to the Mythals of Myth Drannor and gain control of the cities of Faerûn.
