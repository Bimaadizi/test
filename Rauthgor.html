Rauthgor , which was also called the Rainbow Falls was a prominent waterfall located in in the country of Mulhorand .

Geography

The Rainbow Falls were located in north Mulhorand where the River Rauthenflow cascaded into the Alamber Sea near the Cliff of Leaping Horses .

Activity

A small fortified settlement sprung up around the waterfall as of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .
