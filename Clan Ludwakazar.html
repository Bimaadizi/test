Clan Ludwakazar ( pronounced : /ˈ l ʌ d ˈ w ɑː k ɑː z ɑː / Lud-WAK-ah-zah ) was a dwarven clan that dwelled deep in the Earthspur Mountains in Impiltur .

Some believed that the Ludwakazar dwarves had an elemental earth bloodline from some unknown source. The dwarves were silent on the issue.
