Cage Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Cage Street was located in Waterdeep's Castle Ward . It originated on the southern end of the Street of Silver , branching west through a neighborhood of buildings to end where it merged with Shadow Alley . Mulgomir's Way intersected Cage Street close to where it branched from the Street of Silver.
