Vrindula Mardassus was a wealthy inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Vrindula was a human woman with a light skin tone green eyes. She had her long light brown hair tightly braided.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Vrindula Mardassus was a frequent customer of the Counting House in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. She could be seen happily counting coin , leaving the bank, in front of beggars, desperate for coopers and food.
