Spider hounds were predatory creatures found in the wild-magic zones of the Helmlands .

Description

A spider hound appeared as a yellow three-headed dog with eight spider -like legs.

Ecology

Spider hounds preyed on the white-skinned creatures that shared their habitat.

History

Spider hounds started appearing in northeastern Cormyr during the Time of Troubles .

In Kythorn of the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , a large pack of more than twenty spider hounds attacked Adon , Caitlan Moonsong , Cyric , Kelemvor Lyonsbane and Midnight as they were traveling through a wild-magic zone en route from Arabel to Castle Kilgrave . The spider hounds ended up being driven off by Adon, who had given his horse a potion of speed and stormed the pack wielding his warhammer to great effect.
