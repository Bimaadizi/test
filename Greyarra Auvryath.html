Greyarra Auvryath was an Eilistraean drow of Eilistraee's Wanderers in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Greyarra had backswept white hair, red eyes, and a small beauty mark. She wore a white shirt with pants and an ornate leather girdle , and carried a walking stick with a backpack .

Personality

Greyarra was a very relaxed and understanding individual, happy to support her friends.

Relationships

Greyarra was a dear friend of Zelliira the bard .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Greyarra and the other drow followed Kelgoth Darksong to Myth Drannor in order to restore the mythal and impress the Elves of Cormanthor . The group stayed at their fortified camp , but the surface elves were uncooperative and refused to join in on their quest. Frustrated by this, Kelgoth went into the ruins on his own, followed by Zelliira and Uhlsdrin Nirveth as they searched for the Key to the Mythal . Greyarra was a touch worried about her friend heading to the House of Song , and so sent a team of adventurers to find her. Fortunately for them, Zelliira was safe and well, and the Icon of the Humans was found.
