Even's Gulf was a gulf in the Easting Reach that separated Impiltur and the Great Dale .

Geography

The Gulf of Lath was the northernmost edge of the Easting Reach, which, in turn, was part of the much larger Sea of Fallen Stars . The Buzzard Beak Harbor was located immediately to the gulf's south. Even's Gulf was fed by two major rivers: the Easting River what continued to separate Impiltur and the Great Dale, and the Forest Blood River on the Great Dale's side.
