Vrushnom was an unusual illithid cleric of Shar .

Vrushnom lived in the secret Shar-worshiping enclave of Loobli Shar in the Middle Darklands of the Underdark . However, also Vrushnom acted spied on the city for the illithids of Oryndoll .
