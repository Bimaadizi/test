Mannock was the Captain of the Watch of the local militia in the village of Deadsnows , as well as a member of the town's council, during the late 1300s DR . He served Lady Arletha Icespear as she oversaw justice for lawbreakers, whether their crimes were petty or more severe.

Possessions

Captain Mannock was outfitted with a masterwork breastplate and equipped with a +1 magical longsword .
