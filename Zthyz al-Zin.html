Zthyz al-Zin was a zin living among the waters of the River of Gold on the Island of Gardens in 1367 DR .

Personality

Zthyz enjoyed playing tricks and pranks on any visitors to the River of Gold.

Activities

Zthyz would confront anyone attempting to take water from the river, claiming to be a servant of the great noble marids .
