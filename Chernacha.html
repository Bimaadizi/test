The Chernacha , which was also referred to as the Little Black Citadel , was an official building located in Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Location

The building was located in northeastern Almorel behind the Gospoda .

Activities

In addition to being a barracks for Almorel's soldiers, the Chernacha was also the city's main armory.
