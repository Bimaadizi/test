Esbresh was a settlement near the Utter East and Ulgarth in southeast Faerûn , lying on the shore of the Great Sea .

There was a certain slaving ring based out of Luskan in far northwest Faerûn , fronted by one Inther Blackfeather , that reached all the way to Esbresh and Kelazzan .
