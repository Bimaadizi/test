Roaringcrest was a ruined keep once used by pirates on the isle of Raerest in Sea of Swords , just off the shores of Candlekeep .

Location

Roaringcrest built on a natural harbor on Raerest's southeastern side, on the southern shores of the island's freshwater lake chain.

Structure

By the late 14 th century DR , Roaringcrest was a ruin reclaimed by vegetation.

History

Long time before the late 14 th century DR, Roaringcrest was a pirate stronghold, until its destruction at the hands of the Twilight Three , a trio of liches from the isle of Thelve . The liches pelted the keep with spells that turned it into an area of wild magic . After the pirate occupants, the ruins were home to crimson deaths , darktentacles and automatons . Even by the late 14 th century DR, pirates of the Sea of Swords told stories of ancient buccaneer treasures hidden within Roaringcrest.
