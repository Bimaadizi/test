The Tower of Al-Kalb , or Tower of the Dog , was located within the great fortress of Sarahin in the Haunted Lands .

Description

Grey stucco covered the outside of the tower.

Inhabitants

This tower was given to Akim al-Kalaas when he joined the Everlasting . His servants, genies and apprentices lived in the tower with him.
