Shipwrights' House was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Order of Master Shipwrights .

Location

This building was located at the corner of Dock Street and Asteril's Way , in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This building stood two-stories tall.

Reputation

Like other guildhalls in the Dock Ward, Shipwrights' House was considered by locals to be a landmark.

Trivia

The Shipwrights' Ball was annually held here on the last tenday of Ches , as part of the Fleetswake festival.
