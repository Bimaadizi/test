Zeltabbar Iliphar was a human sage inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Zeltabbar's areas of expertise as a sage included human languages , legends, and folklore; general history; and, to a lesser degree, the planes of existence . He understood a great number of human languages, as well as Dwarvish and Elvish .

Abilities

Zeltabbar was highly intelligent, but completely deaf . Nevertheless, he could read lips without error and understood many human languages, as well as Dwarvish and Elvish . He was able to speak, but regularly wrote his replies on slate boards, and he had many to hand in his office.

Residence

He lived in a row house on Nephranter's Street on the northwest side of the Court of the White Bull , located in the southern section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward . His office lay on the ground floor.

Relationships

He had two daughters, described as lovely, who helped him manage his library.
