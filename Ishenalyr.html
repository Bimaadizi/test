Ishenalyr was an ancient green dragon who was one of the agents of Sammaster during the Rage of Dragons of 1373 DR .

Abilities

Ishenalyr was a type of draconic mystic called hidecarved dragon .

History

In 1373 DR, he was part of the chromatic dragon force at the siege of the Monastery of the Yellow Rose . However, thanks to a deception by the copper dragon Chatulio , Ishenalyr provoked the red dragon Malazan and they soon fought a duel to the death, which saw Malazan the victor and Ishenalyr dead.
