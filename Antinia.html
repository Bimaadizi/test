Antinia the Goon was an unsavory element that resided in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Antinia was a human woman with a light skin tone, black eyes, and long straight black hair gathered in a ponytail. She was a proficient fighter who wore a simple breastplate and armed herself with two axes .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , was part of the same gang with Gal Vharranne , Churg Elvek , Dart Dirtwood , Imprudence , Nobster , and Ormo who could be found in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate near the docks where the Steel Watch Foundry and Water Queen's House stood. Unbeknownst to most, her gang's leader, Churg, was a True Soul , an individual implanted with an illithid tadpole that indoctrinated him into serving the Absolute .
