Bright shields were enchanted heavy shield created to be used by dungeon-delving adventurers some time before the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Bright shields possessed a simple yet effective +1 enchantment that increased the wielder's protection and illuminated 15 feet (4.6 meters) around the shield with a yellow light.

History

Bright shields were birthed from the eternal dilemma among adventurers: carrying a torch for light or wielding a shield for protection. Over the decades, many magic items were created to satisfy both needs, however, bright shields were among the more practical items. In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , bright shields could be purchased from armor vendors of the Market Triangle in the city of Westgate .
