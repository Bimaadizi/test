Fea Eltorchul was a young wizard and noblewoman inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Fea worked within the Eltorchul Academy in Waterdeep's Sea Ward where she helped run the school.

Relationships

She was a member of the Eltorchul noble family. Her father was Thesp Eltorchul and her older brother was Oth Eltorchul .
