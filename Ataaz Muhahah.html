Ataaz Muhaha , meaning Laughing Gorge in the Chultan language, was a canyon on the River Olung in Chult .

Description

The canyon was a deep chasm over 100 feet (30.5 meters) in depth. At one point, it was crossed by the Monkey Bridge .

Inhabitants

The gorge was inhabited by troops of monkeys that hooted, howled and shrieked incessantly. The echoes of these sounds resonated along its depth forming sounds that sounded a lot like malevolent laughter.
