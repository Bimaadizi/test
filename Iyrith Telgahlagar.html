Iyrith Telgahlagar was a Zulkir of Enchantment sometime circa late 1300 DR and was the closest thing to the true leader of Thay at the time.

Description

Iyrith was a dark-featured and bearded handsome Thayan human man. He was courteous, polite, and soft-spoken.

Abilities

The zulkir was ruthless when it came to disposing of his foes. Iyrith dispensed magical punishment through flying bats .

History

Iyrith Telgahlagar claimed the title of the Zullkir of Abjuration sometime before the late Year of the Starfall, 1300 DR . He was listed among several zulkir names that became widely known after being identified and described in the book called Twelve Winters Under the Lash , penned by an anonymous slave and smuggled out of Thay sometime in late 1300 DR. Iyrith Telgahlagar earned his status through expert strategy and diplomacy. His statecraft gained him numerous alliances among fellow zulkirs and tharchions .
