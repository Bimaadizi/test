Hawkwing's Brook was a tributary of the Sulduskoon River in Tethyr .

Geography

The brook began in the Starspire Mountains and flowed down through County Uluran to join the great Sulduskoon.

Notable Locations

History

The brook used to be called Banshee's Brook or Tethir Brook until a brave, young elven warrior woman called Hawkwing lost her life here in 1364 DR defending the Forest of Tethir against those corrupting it.
