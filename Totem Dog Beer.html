Totem Dog Beer was a type of magical alcohol available in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The enchanted beer was on the menu at the Yawning Portal tavern of Waterdeep, stashed away in Durnan 's cabinet. One serving of Totem Dog Beer was priced at ten gold coins .
