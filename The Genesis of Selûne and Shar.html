The Genesis of Selûne and Shar was a religious book that described one of Toril 's creation-myths, as it pertained to the sister-goddesses Selûne and Shar .

Content

The book talked about the event that led to the great conflict between Selûne and Shar. When there was but only darkness in existence, Selûne ignited flame from nothingness, creating the sun in the center of what would become Realmspace . This act created the light and warmth necessary for life to form in the world.

It also brought about the ages-old conflict between the two sisters, pitting them in a struggle against each other that continued throughout all of Toril's existence.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a copy of The Genesis of Selûne and Shar could be found in a secluded shrine found just outside the village of Moonhaven .
