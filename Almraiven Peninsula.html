The Almraiven Peninsula was a rocky peninsula in west Faerûn 's Lands of Intrigue region, named after the mountain chain that was part of it - the Alimir Mountains .

Geography

This peninsula was within the borders of both eastern Calimshan and eastern Tethyr . It included the Qartan Hills and eastern expanse of the Darthiir Wood that stretched alongside it. It was framed by the Shining Sea to the south and the Lake of Steam to the north.

Notable Locations

History

At the height of Shanatar 's power in -8100 DR , the Underdark depths of the Almraiven Peninsula were within its borders.
