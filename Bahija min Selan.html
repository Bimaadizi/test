Bahija min Selan was a pragmatist and a member of the royal court of Rog'osto 's khedive, Samia al-Sa'id , in 1367 DR .

Personality

Bahija was known to be very hedonistic and vain.

Activities

Bahija acted in khedive Samia's court as the representative of all priests in the city of Rog'osto.
