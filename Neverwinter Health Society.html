Neverwinter Health Society was a social club, a spa, and a collection of gloveball courts in the city of Neverwinter in the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

The Health Society was located not far from the Neverwinter Square , one of the oldest locations in the Jewel of North, with one of its entrances facing the wharves and the waterfront. A painted sign was affixed above the door.

History

Neverwinter Health Society was established sometime before the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR . By that year, the spa building was abandoned and its pool was completely dried out. The abandoned Health Society was infiltrated by kobold and goblin bandits who had to be driven out by a band of adventurers .
