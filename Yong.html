Yong was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age .

History

Yong developed three spells in their lifetime. The spells they invented were imprisonment in 2185 NY (−1674 DR) , binding in 2190 NY (−1669 DR) , and finally, vanish in 2192 NY (−1667 DR) .
