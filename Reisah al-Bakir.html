Reisah al-Bakir was a moralist and member of the League Conclave in I'tiraf in 1367 DR .

Activities

Originally from Fahhas , Reisah was elected to lead the group of delegates from that city to represent them within the League Conclave. Reisah and her fellow delegates ensured Fahhas' best interests were heard at court.
