The sickle of shorn bonds was a unique enchanted sickle crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Farideh in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

Silverhand gifted this weapon to Farideh in recognition of the latter's recognition of the importance of forming bonds with others.

Powers

The sickle of shorn bonds forced enemies to fight alone, and then punished them for it. Receiving an injury from the sickle had a chance of placing a maddening hex on the recipient that prevented their allies from healing them. It further had a chance of hexing not only the target but all of that target's nearby allies.
