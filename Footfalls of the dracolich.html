The footfalls of the dracolich were a pair of powerful magical boots found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

These boots were tied to the dracolich Karliath , granting them unusual powers. They left a cold and lingering echo with every footstep, and gave the wearer permanent freedom of movement while protecting them from magical and physical threats.

History

The boots were found by the Cormanthor Elves when Phathroi had them destroy Karliath at the Cult of the Dragon base in the ruins.
