Spellstrike was a weapon quality that could be applied to all kinds of weapons.

Power

The wielder of a weapon imbued with the spellstrike quality could use the stored magic to protect themselves against harmful magic or similar effects directed at them.

Creation

A cleric used the resistance spell to add the spellstrike quality to a weapon.
