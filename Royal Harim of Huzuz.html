The Royal Harim of Huzuz , or simply the Royal Harim , was part of the complex of the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Description

The Royal Harim was one of the largest sections of the palace. It contained dormitories, beautiful gardens, and opulent suites.
