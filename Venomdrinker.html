The Venomdrinker was an enchanted scimitar that once belonged to a yuan-ti Zehirite cultist who worked under Sheydann Zinn in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Like other weapons of the Forktongue Cultists, the Venomdrinker was enchanted with a blessing of the dark god Zehir . The wicked-looking weapon oozed deadly poison as well as darkness and negative energy . When swung in battle, the Venomdrinker acted like a +1 enchanted blade and with each successful strike it damaged the opponent with deadly negative energy and poisoned them.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , yuan-ti who followed an interloper deity Zehir revealed their sinister intents for the Sword Coast . The yuan-ti House Se'Sehen began attacking the operations of House Sauringar and their Mercantile House trading organization led by Sa'Sani . In secrecy, these Forktongue Cultists spread, routing in the Mere of Dead Men among other places. The swamp cabal was led by a well-disguised pureblood named Sheydann Zinn. The unique blade called the Venomdrinker was in the hands of Zinn's cultists.
