Lady Namyriitha Alastrarra was the Speaker of House Alastrarra from the 3 rd to the 7 th century DR .

Description

She was smooth and beautiful, making it difficult for any but a trained eye to determine she had not been young for centuries. Her rage was only ever betrayed by her eyes; her voice was commanding.

Possessions

On the evening of the Year of the Hippogriff's Folly, 241 DR , when Riluaneth was slain, she wore a plum, revealing yet courtly gown that flowed constantly, covering some parts of her in rainbow droplets and laying others bare. The gown later flowed into a sculpture of interlaced spines, from which her hair flowed normally. The lower half of her gown could be removed independently.

Personality

She was used to being obeyed. Even in difficult situations, she watched for her family's honor, warning her younger members of the house not to gawk at humans too lustily.

Abilities

She could enspell a sword to reveal who'd used it to kill.

Relationships

One of his fellow guardians was named Noldorn .

History

In 241 DR, Namyriitha Alastrarra tracked down Elminster Aumar , who had shapeshifted into Iymbryl Alastrarra and wore his kiira , after he slew Riluaneth . She then called for Ornthalas Alastrarra as Elminster requested, wanting answers. She ultimately did not kill Elminster.
