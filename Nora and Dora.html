Nora and Dora were the cook twins of Thistleflame Manor and servants of Lord Partic Thistle circa 1369 DR .

Personality

Nora and Dora were dutiful enough in their work, but they did have a tendency to gossip too much.

Activities

The twins were excellent cooks, and handled most of the Manor's meals. They worked alongside Malda , who they wisely prevented from getting involved in anything except boiling.

Relationships

Like Malda, they found Dom 's histrionic baking style rather tiresome to work around.
