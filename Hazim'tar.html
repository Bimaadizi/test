The Hazim'tar were a clan of yuan-ti who were a part of the greater Eselemas tribe in the 14 th century DR . They dominated the city of Lushpool in Lapaliiya .

Activities

By 1373 DR , nine of the sixteen satraps who governed Lushpool were secretly Hazim'tar purebloods . The city had thus been effectively under yuan-ti control for over two centuries. This was an open secret among Lapaliiya's rulers and top advisors for generations but, too aware of yuan-ti power and the prosperity it brought, they continued to purposefully overlook it, despite the murderous hatred of serpentfolk endemic to the common people. In theory, the Overking of Lapaliiya ruled over Lushpool via their heir, the Prince Royal and nominal ruler of the city. However, in practice, to enact a new policy, an Overking typically had to negotiate with the Lushpan council of satraps. Lushpool was the third most politically powerful city in Lapaliiya, giving the Hazim'tar great influence in the realm.

Throughout their history, the Lushpan satraps were perforce reclusive, remaining cloistered within their luxurious, fortified compounds, issuing decrees in writing via servants ( human but often tainted ones ). They were only seen masked, and when they wished to address the citizens or give an audience, they used magic to speak through the mouths of chained female slaves.
