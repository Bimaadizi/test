Dalekeva , also known as Oberon's Hold , was a Low City near Tinnainen in Netheril circa −700 DR .

History

During the Shadowed Age , members of the Beneficent Traders' Guild of Dalekeva requested an audience with Lady Polaris's High Steward Candlemas to beg the Netherese to protect their city from the orc and human army of the One King , which had destroyed the neighboring city of Tinnainen.
