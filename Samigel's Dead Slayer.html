Samigel's Dead Slayer was a magic bludgeon effective against the undead .

Description

Samigel's Dead Slayer was a club made of wood and bound in silver .

Powers

The Dead Slayer had a +4 enchantment placed upon it that also significantly improved the wielder's reflexes. When used against undead creatures, this bludgeon effectively became a +7 enchanted weapon. Additionally, this club allowed the user to cast the protection from evil spell on themselves.

History

In 1369 DR , Samigel's Dead Slayer was one of the artifacts scavenged from the Elven Catacombs by the drow of House Freth . This enchanted club was deposited in one of the chests in the makeshift prison area used by the drow and their Cult of the Dragon allies.
