Buntaro Ostato was a samurai in the town of Aru in Wa .

Personality

Buntaro had the personality (and the look) of a dyspeptic bear: he rarely smiled and always seemed arrogant. Buntaro was deeply loyal to his daimyo , one of the few people who liked Buntaro. He was a man of honor and duty, hating people of unstable loyalty. Buntaro was in love with his servant, Sono .

Relations

Buntaro was the son-in-law of the daimyo Benju Matsutomo , though his wife died in childbirth.
