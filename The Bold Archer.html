The Bold Archer was a club on the Promenade in Suzail , the capital city of Cormyr in the 15 th century DR .

Location

The entrance to the Bold Archer was marked with a carved hanging sign depicting a calm woman on a forest rock wielding a bow against a pack of wolves . The club's two sets of heavy doors were made out of copper -sheathed duskwood .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the Bold Archer became a site of a murder. Two ghostly warriors clad in blue flames murdered Lord Seszgar Huntcrown and started a panic among attending nobles. It was later discovered by Storm Silverhand , the Marchioness of Immerdusk , that these blueflame ghosts were under the control of Lord Marlin Stormserpent .
