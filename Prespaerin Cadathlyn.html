Loremaster Most Exalted Prespaerin Cadathlyn was a high priest of Oghma within the House of Many Tomes in Impiltur in 1368 DR .

Activities

Prespaerin led the Church of Oghma in Impiltur. He claimed to have reached a new closeness with the Binder and claimed the title of Binder of Faerûn for himself.
