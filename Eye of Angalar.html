The Eye of Angalar was a rare magical amulet.

Description

The Eye was a small gilt steel pin for fastening a cloak.

Powers

The user of the Eye of Angalar could call upon it to keep them awake and alert, at the only cost of their lifeforce; the effect lasted for 48 hours. It could also cast infravision , wizard eye , and remove curse twice a day each.

History

Talatha Vaerovree wore it as of 1370 DR , but had abandoned it as of 1372 DR .
