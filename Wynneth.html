Wynneth was a Reghedmen human and the wife of King Jarund Elkhardt from the Tribe of the Elk in the late 15 th century DR .

History

After the death of her son, Jarund Twice-Born , almost a decade before Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR , she and King Jarund Elkhardt have not succeeded in conceiving a new child resulting in deliberations about the tribe's future leadership.
