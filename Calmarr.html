Calmarr or ornsoelaun was a variety of cheese , staple on the tables of Turmish in the Vilhon Reach .

Description

Calmarr was a soft and crumbly variety of cheese, veined with chives and other types of herbs . These cheeses were locally made across Turmish and served as a part of daily meal routine as cheese platters alongside brown nutbreads , onion and chive soups , meats, and spiced greens.
