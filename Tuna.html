Tuna , also known as bluefin , were a common species of fish in the realms.

Species

Ecology

Habitats

In interior Faerûn , tuna inhabited the Sea of Fallen Stars and the Dragon Reach . In west Faerun , they could be found in the Amnian river of Imnestream . In northwest Faerun , they could be found in many of Luruar 's rivers. In southwest Faerûn , they could be found in the Shining Sea . In the Unapproachable East , they could be found in the waters around Aglarond .

Beyond Faerûn , tuna could be found in the Celestial Sea and the Sea of Patience around Wa .
