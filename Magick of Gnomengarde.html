Magick of Gnomengarde was a spellbook that was studied and shared by all gnomes of Gnomengarde as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The leather -bound spellbook displayed the title "Magick of Gnomengarde" in both Gnomish and Common . It was stored on a wooden pedestal atop a ledge overlooking the workshop in which Gnomengarde's inventors crafted magic items.
