Manasowar was a large lake found in Ra-Khati .

Geopgraphy

Located deep within the Katakoro Shan range, Manasowar sat just north of the capital city of Saikhoi . It was fed by a smaller tributary of the Gogrus River to the west, and flowed into the Akundi River to the east.
