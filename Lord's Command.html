Lord's Command was a set of enchanted heavy armor that belonged to Lord Francis Tenraw sometime before 1372 DR .

Powers

Lord's Command possessed the +1 enchantment to the wearer's defenses and offered armor resistant to piercing weapons. Additionally, the armor's magic improved the owner's strength and provided some resistance to magic.

History

Lord's Command was a set of armor commissioned and custom crafted for Lord Francis Tenraw when he was expecting a war that ended up never happening. With no battles to protect the nobleman in, Lord's Command became Francis' ceremonial garb and served him in this fashion until his death of old age. The lord's daughter inherited this piece of armor but eventually solid it to pay for a new library building for her subjects.

By 1372 DR , Lord's Command was in stock at the shop of Derval Ironeater in Daggerford .
