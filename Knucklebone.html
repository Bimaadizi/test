Knucklebone was a female rock gnome wizard , a self proclaimed mistress of the magical arts, and an adventurer in the 14 th century DR .

Description

The red-headed gnomish woman had bad skin and was quite eccentric, often prone to fits of cackling.

History

In the 14 th century DR , several adventurers were summoned to aid the Open Lord Piergeiron the Paladinson in dealing with the recent increase in crime and monster activity pouring out of Undermountain .

Knucklebone camped out at the Golden Hammer Inn that had direct access to the city sewers , teeming with kobolds and the undead of the Albrik Army . She offered to join a small group of heroes personally assembled by Piergeiron to stop the sinister alliance of enemies of Waterdeep.
