The Vault of the Word of the Loregiver was part of the complex of the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

Contained within this vault was the oldest known recorded copy of the Word of the Loregiver , created by the first Grand Caliph . These precious scrolls were displayed for all to see, but kept safely behind walls of force .

Defenses

In addition to the walls of force protecting the vault, many magical protective measures were used to guard the ancient scrolls.

A tasked slayer genie lay hidden within the base of the stand holding the scrolls, ready to spring out and destroy any intruder or thief with unbridled fury.
