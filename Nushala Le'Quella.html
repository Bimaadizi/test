Nushala Le'Quella was an elf jeweler who lived in Leuthilspar on the island of Evermeet .

She created jewellery and works of art, and once spent 18 months on a single piece for Queen Amlaruil Moonflower . As of 1368 DR , she was over 600 years old and was held in much respect in the elven community.
