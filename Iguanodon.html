Iguanodons ( pronounced : / ɪ ˈ g w ɑː n ɑː d ɑː n / i-GWAN-a-dan ) were a species of dinosaur .

Description

Iguanodons were large quadrupeds that looked somewhat like a small sauropod. When eating they rose up on their hind legs.

Combat

When confronted by a predator, iguanodons used their powerful legs to flee. If pressed, they defended themselves with a thumb-like spike on their hands and their strong tail.

Biology

Iguanodons were herbivores and lived together in herds.
