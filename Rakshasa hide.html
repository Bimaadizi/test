Rakshasa hide was a kind of hide armor found in Eberron .

Description

The armor closely resembled a tigerskin outfit, being made from the tanned hide of a rakshasa .

Powers

The hide gave the bearer many of the same innate abilities that rakshasas had. It improved their awareness, aim, and reaction speed, and produced a roar that frightened attackers.

History

A suit of rakshasa armor was taken from (or made from) the rakshasa Tahmael , who was at the Lords of Dust safehouse in Stormreach when the Church of the Silver Flame attacked, during a plot to release the Overlord Spinner of Shadows .
