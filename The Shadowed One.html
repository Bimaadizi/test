The Shadowed One was a human psionicist in Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Possessions

He would wield a scimitar of speed during fights, and wear a pair of bracers of defense , being otherwise hidden inside his great cloak .

Activities

He would appear during fights, parties, and once during a parade, and act unpredictably for his own bizarre reasons.

History

He had been stalking the streets of Waterdeep for at least two years.
