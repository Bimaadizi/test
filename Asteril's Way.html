Asteril's Way was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Asteril's Way was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward . It originated on Sternpost Street near the intersection with Ship Street , branching south then southwest to end on Dock Street on the waterfront. Several unnamed alleys branched off of the southeast side of Asteril's Way
