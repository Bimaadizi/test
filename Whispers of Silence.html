The Whispers of Silence was a unique cloak of non-detection that veiled its wearer from magical ways of detection.

Powers

The Whispers of Silence was enchanted to render its wearer completely undetectable by magical means, such as true seeing and invisible to creatures that possessed true sight , when hidden in shadows or under the effects of the spell of invisibility . The non-detection was not achieved via illusion and was active as long as the cloak was worn.

History

This magic cloak was believed to be commissioned by the lineage of the most accomplished and infamous burglars in Faerûn . The cloak's powers are quite evident considering that the names of its previous owners remained unknown.

One such cloak, the original or a replica, was worn by Purvis , the assassin who was hired by the speaker of Lonelywood in the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR .

The clock came into possession of Gurke , an old dwarf who traveled to the Jovial Juggler of Beregost in 1368 DR . On his trek through Cloak Wood he was ambushed and robbed by a band of tasloi . The creatures stole the Whispers of Silence letting the dwarf escape with his life. Serendipitously, Gurke encountered Abdel Adrian and his companions and requested help retrieving his enchanted cloak from the scaly monsters.

Sorcerous Amon owned the Whispers of Silence later the same year. However it was unknown whether the cloak he was selling in the Den of the Seven Vales of Athkatla was the genuine item, or a replica.
