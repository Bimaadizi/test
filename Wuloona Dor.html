Wuloona Dor was a gold dwarf commoner from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Wuloona was a clean shaven dwarven woman with dark skin and pale green eyes. Her graying hair was gathered in a braid wrapped around her head.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Wuloona Dor was among those citizens of Baldur's Gate who did not trust the new Grand Duke Lord Gortash .
