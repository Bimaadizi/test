Gilded armor were elaborately decorated enchanted breastplates , worn by loyal ranking servants or the council of Samarach .

Powers

Gilded armor possessed +1 enchantment and enhanced the wearers' ability to assert dominance and intimidate creatures.

History

By the late 14 th century DR , gilded armor was commonly worn by Captains of the Patrol in the nation of Samarach. In 1374 DR , one of these Captain, along with a band of patrolmen, invaded the hamlet of Nimbre , after hearing of the possible yuan-ti corruption. A group of adventurers hired by a merchant Sa'Sani walked in on the harassment of peaceful villagers. Even though the outcome of the encounter remained unclear, some said that the gilded armor might've changed hands that day.
