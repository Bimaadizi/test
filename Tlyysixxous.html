Tlyysixxous was a sahuagin priestess , who lived in the City of Caverns .

Description

The priestess, like many of her kind, did not like other types of humanoids , thinking them nothing more than spurious beasts.

Combat

The priestess did not use melee weapons, and instead had a selection of spells , the most powerful of which were blade barrier and slay living .

History

Around 1369 DR , she try to persuade King Ixilthetocal to kill Gorion's Ward and his party. After a heated argument with Senityili , the king ordered her to be killed via holy sacrifice.
