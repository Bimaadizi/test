Trebonius was a Netherese arcanist who worked at the Mythomian Warehouse in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Trebonius worked for Adrian Freeman at the warehouse by keeping the accounting books in order, though only with heavy supervision from Adrian.
