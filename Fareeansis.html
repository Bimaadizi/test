Fareeansis was a balor who taught Jesanta Beril about demonbinding in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

While disguised as an old man, Fareeansis kept a sharp, red glint to his eyes.

History

At some point, Fareeansis was summoned and bound in the Prime Material plane . In the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , Fareeansis met Jesanta Beril while disguised as a sage named Benedict . He taught her enough of Nar demonbinder lore, but not enough for her to bind him.
