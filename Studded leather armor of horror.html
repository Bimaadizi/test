Studded leather armor of horror were types of enchanted light armor .

Powers

These sets of armor proved regular protection on par with mundane studded leather . Its surface was affixed with numerous metal rivets placed close together and forming a flexible layer of metal atop leather . Additionally, the wearer was immune to the effects of the horror spell .

History

One such set of armor could be found within the Dragon's Eye in Icewind Dale circa the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR . The magic leather was locked inside a trapped chest looted by a gang of histachii .
