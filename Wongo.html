Wongo ( pronounced : /ˈ w ɒn g g oʊ / WONG-go ) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult . He was a primal spirit that took the form of a su-monster .

Personality

Wongo was deranged, violent, and egocentric. He had no concern for the well-being of others.
