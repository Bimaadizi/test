Huulfor Manor was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood three stories tall.

Location

Huulfor Manor was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward near the City of the Dead where Quill Alley met Wall Way .
