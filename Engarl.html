History

By Hammer of the Year of the Black Flame, 229 DR , Engarl had looted a lance after slaying a soldier of the Magelords of Athalantar with a sling. On that date, he participated in an ambush that slew a half-dozen of the Magelords' men. In the aftermath, he'd looted six daggers, and brought horse meat back to Lawless Castle , where the bandits were fortified.
