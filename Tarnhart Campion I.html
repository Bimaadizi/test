Tarnhart Campion I was the third King of the Campion Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Tarnhart ruled from the Year of Perilous Halls, 954 DR , until his death in the Year of the Slain Mountain, 993 DR . His reign would be the longest of the Campion Dynasty. Tarnhart I inherited the throne upon the death of his uncle Jhentyr I .

Relationships

He had two sons. His second son, Jhentyr II would succeed him on the throne.
