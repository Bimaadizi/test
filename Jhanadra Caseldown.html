Jhanadra Caseldown , known as Joybringer Caseldown after joining the Church of Lliira , was a song dragon and the high priestesses of the Cry of Joy temple in Hill's Edge in the 14 th century DR .

Personality

Caseldown was known to be quite enthusiastic.

Abilities

Caseldown was renowned for her skills in acrobatic dancing and her high, clear singing voice. Rumors also circulated around Hills Edge that she also had strange magical powers, resulting from her being a song dragon.

Activities

When not leading her clergy, Caseldown worked closely with the Harpers in secret to ensure the cultural growth of Hill's Edge, making sure it continued to be a pleasant place to live. But she also worked with them to ensure the city's security, keeping it from falling under the sway of the Church of Cyric and the Zhentarim .

Relationships

Jhanadra was largely loved by the inhabitants of Hill's Edge, who affectionately referred to her as the Rose-Red Lady.
