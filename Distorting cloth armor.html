Distorting cloth armor was a type of enchanted cloth armor , used in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

These sets could have protective enchantments of a +1 or +2 strength. The armor's cloth flowed and shifted when worn, making the wearer harder to hit. This type of magic was especially effective in defending against ranged attacks.

History

In the 15 th century DR, distorting cloth armor was used across the Sword Coast North area, namely the Sword Mountains , the Old Owl Well , the city of Neverwinter , and by the ne'er-do-wells that laired within the ancient ruins of Illefarn .
