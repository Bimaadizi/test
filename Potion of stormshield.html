Potions of stormshield were a sort of damage resistance potion that existed in the period between the Spellplague and the Second Sundering .

Description

The liquid within these potions had a slate gray hue and a smell evocative of ozone.

Powers

Ingestion of a potion of stormshield temporarily gave the imbiber's skin a metallic sheen and made them resistant to the next instance of lightning and thunder damage that afflicted them.

History

In the late 15 th century DR , potions of stormshield could be found in use by many members of the Zhentarim .
