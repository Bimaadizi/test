The leathers of the woodsman were light leather armor used in Cormyr during the 15 th century DR .

Description

The armor gave full body coverage in dark leathers.

Powers

The leathers of the woodsman helped make the wearer sneakier and more agile, and better able to launch surprise attacks from the shadows.

History

These suits of armor were created for the servants of Cormyr, and layered with protections by the War Wizards . They were given to the rangers and other woodsmen who served the Crown and aided druids in the King's Forest , such as when provided by Arcton Greenwort during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar .
