Description

The stew was a thick mixture into which game from the forests of the Vast were stirred, including cooked chunks of squirrel , boar , porcupine , and black-masked bear . This was flavored with wildsage , imparting a taste of buttered leek . It was often eaten in accompaniment with hardbread slathered in bloodlick , and washed down with beer .

A similar dish was wildsage vegetable stew , which was served at The Wizard's Hand inn.
