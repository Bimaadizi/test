Ramas al-Rashid was a holy slayer who lived in Liham in 1367 DR .

Activities

Ramas and his brothers were members of a merchant house in Liham. They used this as a front to mark a caravanserai for robbery.

Relationships

Ramas was brother to Harun al-Rashid and Beyab al-Rashid .
