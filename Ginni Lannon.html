Ginni Lannon was a human inhabitant of the village of West Harbor on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR .

Description

Ginni had blonde hair affixed in a tight ponytail. She had a face that showed determination and her bright eyes were clear and focused.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , soon after, the Mere of Dead Men was briefly consumed by a short but deadly conflict known as the Second War of the Shadow . As the village was being rebuild and recovered, the town's mage , Tarmas , disappeared. Ginni and Pitney Lannon volunteered to follow the wizard's tracks and attempt the rescue. The Lannons discovered Tarmas captured by lizardfolk within the Dragon Caves south of West Harbor. They quickly returned the man back to the village. However, unbeknownst to them, Tarmas they retrieved was a magically disguised black dragon . Then the deception was revealed, Pitney joined the soldiers in defending the village against Voaraghamanthar and Waervaerendor , who wanted to take over the village and its Harvest Mead brewery. It was up to adventurers to aid the soldiers or the dragons.
