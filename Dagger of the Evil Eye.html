Daggers of the Evil Eye were magical weapons that could be found in Zakhara , the Land of Fate.

Description

These weapons could be found in any dagger form including jambiyas , dirks , stilettos , etc.

Many other weapons in Zakhara could be cursed with the same effect, but daggers were by far the most common.

Powers

Using this weapon during combat cursed the wielder with the evil eye . This curse could be removed by a rawun or through the avert evil eye spell but would strike again if the wielder used the dagger again in combat.
