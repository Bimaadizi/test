The Watchlord's Tower was a small keep in Thentia and the sea of power for the Watchlord , named for the black tower that rose from it.

Location

The keep was located in the center of Thentia, halfway between the main gates and docks.

Defenses

The Tower was protected by a force of 10 veteran Warders .
