Harette Oberon was a Lady patron of the arts from the Oberon patriar family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the Absolute crisis of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Lady Harette Oberon was invited to attend the art show titled "Strange Tides: Self-Portraits from the Sword Coast," hosted by Lady Jannath in her Lower City estate. Harette RSVP'd to attend with a plus one guest.
