Fowler's snares were tools sometimes used by rangers or woodsmen throughout Faerûn .

Description

A fowler's snare consisted of a wooden structure surrounded by netting with an closable opening.

History

As its name implied, a fowler's snare was used to catch birds of various sizes.

Fowler's snares were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 1 gold piece for a small snare and 3 gold pieces for a larger snare.
