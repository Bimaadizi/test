The Quiet Deaths of the Brotherhood of Assassins was a recipe book that collected recipes and formulae for the creation of a number of animal-born toxins and poisons , published sometime before 1374 DR .

Content

The book offered guidance and instructions for alchemists on how to extract poison from giant centipedes ', giant bees ' and giant scorpions ' venom glands. Each of the animals could be sourced to produce three types of poison of various strengths that could be used to kill via ingestion or to coat weapons.
