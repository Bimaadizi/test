Prince Ugreth was an orc barbarian of the Silver Marches in the mid–14 th century DR . He was a son of Obould .

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Ugreth was sent to scout out the area around Deadsnows and hoped to earn some plundered gold from the prospectors whilst he was there.
