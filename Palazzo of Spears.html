The Palazzo of Spears was a mansion located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The mansion was located in the eastern section of the city within the Plume district. It lay southeast of Krak al-Nayyiran .

Inhabitants

The Palazzo of Spears was reserved for the commander of the City of Brass's armed forces.
