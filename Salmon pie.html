Salmon pie was a dish of baked salmon covered with a fluffy dough crust that puffed out delectable-smelling steam once broken.

Availability

Full-sized salmon pies were rectangular in shape, with various designs cut into the crust. The pies were commonly baked and sold in the city of Baldur's Gate as well inns and settlement along the Chionthar Valley between the Gate and the city of Elturel .
