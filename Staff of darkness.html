The staff of darkness was an enchanted staff from the ruined Elven Catacombs of Myth Drannor .

Description

The staff of darkness was an ebony wood-carved crooked stick.

Powers

This dark staff allowed the wielder to cast the darkness spell at will. The weapon held six charges.

History

In 1369 DR , a staff of darkness was looted from the Elven Catacombs by a group of drow elves of the House Freth who operated in alliance with the Cult of the Dragon at the time. The staff was briefly possessed by a drow cleric .
