Grunnarch the Red was a Northman fighter and the king of Norland in the Moonshae Isles in the midâ€“14 th century DR . His insignia was a scarlet sword .

Description

He had a red beard and flowing red hair. While he was only average height, he carried himself with such authority that he often seemed much taller.

Personality

Grunnarch was a fierce and proud man. He reveled in raiding, capturing slaves , and fighting glorious battles. During winter months when the seas were not safe for his ships, he would stress and rage in impatience for the Spring to arrive. However, he was also a gracious host and was eager to entertain his guests.

Relationships

The dozen or so lesser kings of Norland all owed their fealty (and troops) to him.

The elite Bloodriders served as his personal guard, led by Laric .

History

Grunnarch the Red ruled Norland from his royal lodge at Rogarsheim as of the Year of the Snow Winds, 1335 DR , when Elminster toured the Moonshae Isles.

In the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR , Grunnarch the Red and Raag Hammerstaad brought their hosts of longships to Thelgaar Ironhand 's Iron Keep on Oman's Isle to prepare for raiding and pillaging the cities of Caer Corwell and Caer Callidyrr in the lands of the Ffolk . He was in charge of attacking Gwynneth from the eastern shore.

In the Year of the Bright Blade, 1347 DR , Grunnarch was party to the signing of the Treaty of Oman , which halted the centuries-long conflict between the Northmen and the Ffolk .
