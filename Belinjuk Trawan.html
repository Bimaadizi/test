Belinjuk Trawan , also known as Old Juk , was an inhabitant of the Dalelands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was a portly, balding man.

Activities

Once a farmer, he later simply raised chickens . At that time, he also became a regular at the Old Skull Inn .

Relationships

He was married.
