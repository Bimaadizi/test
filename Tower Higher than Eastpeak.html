The Tower Higher than Eastpeak was a landmark structure found in the secluded elven settlement of Evereska . It held the distinction as the tallest building in the city, one that reached into the skies above the mountain of Eastpeak in the hills east of the city.

Location

The tower stood along the eastern edge of Evereska, north of Moondark Hill .
