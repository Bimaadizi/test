Questele was a moon elf mage and member of the Moonstars in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Questele's duty was to protect the young Chosen of Lathander , Stedd Whitehorn . However, she was killed by the reaver captain Anton Marivaldi , who tried to collect the bounty on Stedd.
