Topaz was an adult yellow dragon in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

She lived in a sandstone cave inside a great cliff looking out over a vast desert. She had chosen the location mostly because the enjoyed the view, and had become somewhat famous ever since the discovery of their race.

Abilities

Besides her breath weapon , she was also able to cast wizard spells of the first two circles.
