The Blackstar Inn was an inn located in the city of Waterdeep during the 14 th century DR .

Description

The exterior windows were all barred for security, giving the inn the look of a fortress from the outside.

Location

The inn was located on the east side of Gut Alley were it was intersected by Blackstar Lane in Waterdeep's Dock Ward .

Inhabitants

The Blackstar Inn was owned by Asiyra Boldwinter during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . Armed guards protected the entryway and the stables.
