A monster slayer glaive was an enchanted weapon used by professional monster hunters to keep the ugly parts of the job at the other end of the polearm .

Powers

This glaive possessed a simple +1 enchantment that guided the hands of the wielder towards the target and boosted the weapon's damage. Its monster slayer enchantment made the weapons effective against monstrosities , always landing more severe wounds than to any other type of creature. Additionally, a monster slayer glaive improved the wielder's jumping strength and distance, extending it by 5 feet (1.5 meters).

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , one such glaive belonged to Roah Moonglow , a hin agent of the Zhentarim who did business with the recently emerged Cult of the Absolute in the Western Heartlands of Faerûn .
