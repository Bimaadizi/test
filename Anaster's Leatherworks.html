Anaster's Leatherworks was a leathergoods business in the city of Ordulin in the 14 th century DR .

Services

Anaster's sold a variety of different goods made of leather, including leather balls that came in 2‑inch (0.051‑meter), 4‑inch (0.1‑meter), and 8‑inch (0.2‑meter) sizes.

History

Sometime in the mid–14 th century DR, having took notice that Anaster's leather balls were more durable than any others made in Faerûn at the time, Aurora struck up an exclusive licensing deal with Anaster's to have her catalogue be the only business that could export their balls beyond the borders of Sembia .
