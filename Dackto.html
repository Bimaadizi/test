Dackto was a Maztican drider . He was one of the Ancient Ones .

History

In 1361 DR , after the Night of Wailing , Dackto was one of the many drow who survived and were transformed into driders by Lolth .

He died at the hands of the Itza during the advance of Darien 's army of giant ants .
