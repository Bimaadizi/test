Caemon was a priest of Amaunator in Hillsfar .

History

During a pilgrimage, he came across the Cult of the Dragon 's hunting lodge in the Greypeak Mountains and was captured by Talis Kasterel . However, he received good treatment, not like Craggnor and Miresella . Caemon suspected that this was because she wanted to convert him or to use him as a sacrifice to Tiamat .
