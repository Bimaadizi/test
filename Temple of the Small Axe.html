The Temple of the Small Axe was a mosque dedicated to Kor in Liham , in 1367 DR .

Description

The temple had a golden dome that dominated the city-scape of Liham. The main altar for the temple lay in the center of a beautiful garden.

Location

The Temple of the Small Axe was situated across the street from the Palace of Sly Ravens .

Inhabitants

The priests of Kor inhabiting the temple were all pragmatists .
