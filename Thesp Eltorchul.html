Lord Thesp Eltorchul was a noble inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Thesp was the patriarch of the Eltorchul noble family. Upon inheriting his family's wealth from the spice trade, Thesp invested a large sum of money into establishing the Eltorchul Academy in Waterdeep's Sea Ward . In addition to educating young wizards , the Academy procured rare spell components . Thesp was forced to be far less discriminating on who was admitted to Eltorchul Academy or risk complete bankruptcy following a magical accident involving his son and heir, Oth Eltorchul .

Relationships

Thesp's younger daughter, Fea Eltorchul , became his heir apparent following Oth's accident.
