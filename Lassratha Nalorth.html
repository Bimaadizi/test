Lassratha Nalorth was the High Rider, or commander, of the Yevven Riders in Yeshpek along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Lassratha's bloodthirsty nature was matched by her menacing 7 ft (2.1 m) frame. She had raven-black hair and was rumored to sleep in her armor .

Activities

She dreamt of leading Yeshpek's forces in victory over Suldolphor and even Calimshan , making her city truly free. She realized this was likely an unobtainable fantasy, but kept her troops in top fighting shape regardless.
