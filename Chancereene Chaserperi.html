Chancereene Chaserperi was the wife of Faereldan Chaserperi , and the matriarch of the Chaserperi clan of mages , in the late mid-14 th century DR .

Possessions

She lived with her husband in a small manor on Skellin Street , Athkatla . Their home had a walled garden on the back.

Activities

As of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , she had taken over her husband's responsibilities as clan patriarch and was looking for a successor amongst the clan's younger generation.

Relationships

She used to spar verbally with her husband; as his memory began to fail, she became increasingly supportive, writing down names and sketching faces on his hand before meetings.
