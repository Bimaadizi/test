Squammulsh was a mimic in Undermountain in 1375 DR .

Activities

Squammulsh was a mimic placed by Halaster Blackcloak in a prank trap in the Hall of Mirrors in the dungeon.

Personality

Squammulsh was more interested in eating than killing adventurers and preferred to eat normal food rather than human meat; in particular, it liked wine. If in danger, it begged for its life.
