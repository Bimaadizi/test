Morthak , known as Morthak the Everhungry , was a demon who ruled over a legion of insane human followers in the northern hills of Impiltur as of the late 15 th century DR .

History

Morthak was one of many dreadful demons unleashed upon Impiltur when the Weave failed during the events of the Spellplague in the late 14 th century DR.

By the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Morthak had amassed a large and dangerous following of traitorous and insane Impilturians.
