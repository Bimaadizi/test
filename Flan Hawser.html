Flan Hawser was a Seawind of Umberlee , a member of the Bitch Queen's clergy under Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queen's House in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Flan Hawser was a human woman with red hair and a reddish-dark skin tone. She had watery blue eyes and bore an aquamarine tattoo depicting a flock of birds on her left cheekbone.

Posessions

Flan's Seawind uniform was a set of skin-tight ceremonial chainmail dress with light pauldrons and decorative bracers . Her weapon of choice was heavy crossbow .

History

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Seawind Flan Hawser was among the church's members who performed the ceremonies and sang hymns in praise of the Bitch Queen. She was alongside Flood Tide Allandra Grey and other priestesses during Holli Dylford 's last rites. The recently perished priestess perished while swimming in Baldur's Gate's Gray Harbor , the death Seawind Hawser thought to be beautiful.
