The Crypts was a jail and constabulary located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the midâ€“14 th century DR . Their name derived from the fact that all of the cells were underground.

Location

The jail was located in south Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the Foreign Quarter along Bridge Street and Zhent Avenue .

Inhabitants

A wizard named Orisk acted as a judge here for petty offenses. Lord Orgauth himself passed judgment on serious offenses.

Defenses

A staff of at least 24 Zhentilar occupied the Crypts at all times.

History

The structure was originally used as a military detention center, but became the center of Zhentil Keep's city security after the city's destruction in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR
