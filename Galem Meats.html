Galem Meats was a smuggler who worked with the infamous Guild in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Meats was a mountainous grey-skinned half-orc with long wavy lush hair. He was dressed in a simple breastplate atop bright green cloth, and carried a massive two-handed axe .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Jezzy worked as a muscle for a gruff hin smuggler named Big Huido , transporting weapons , drugs , and firewine into Baldur's Gate. The shipments were well defended by Galem and the rest of the crew – all were released into the hands of the members of the Guild only. One such shipment during the Absolute crisis was a chest containing essence of ether , dream mist , silkroot , and terazul – all illegal substances in the city.
