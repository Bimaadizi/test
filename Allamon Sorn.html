History

He was an acquaintance of Pasha Abon Duum and sat with him in the stands at the Arena of Blood in Manshaka , Calimshan , in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR . Sorn remarked that the recaptured famous gladiator Vajra Valmeyjar might perish in her first match against a troll , but Duum, her captor, spoke in her favor. Sorn fled as the illusion of a manticore appeared to assault Duum.

Description

Allamon was thin, with a pointed black goatee and dark skin, and wore blue robes and turban .
