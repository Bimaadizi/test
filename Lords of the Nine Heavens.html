The Lords of the Nine Heavens were members of the Lotus Court , the ruling body of government for the Yikarian Empire in 1367 DR .

Activities

In reality, only two members of the Lords of the Nine Heavens amounted to anything more than lackeys of the Emperor of the Lotus Throne . These were the Emperor's consort and his most skilled general.
