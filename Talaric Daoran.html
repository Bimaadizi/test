Lord Protector Talaric Daoran was a member of the royal House Daoran of Phlan and the ruler of the city-state during the mid–15 th century DR .

Relationships

He was the son of Phlan's first monarch Cvaal and the father of Anivar , the last member of the Daoran dynasty.

History

In the Year of the Mithral Hammer, 1456 DR , Talaric broke the treaty his father had formed with the dark fey of the Quivering Forest , by directing loggers to start cutting down its trees. He vanished from Valjevo Castle the very next day and was never seen again.
