Jardeth Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Jardeth .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's North Ward at the corner of Delzorin Street and Whaelgond Way .

Description

The estate was composed of three separate buildings, each of which was two stories tall.
