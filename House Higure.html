House Higure was one of the ruling drow Houses of Menzoberranzan in the Northdark in the late 14 th and 15 th centuries DR .

History

House Higure rose to its Ruling Council status sometime after the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR and before the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , when it was the seventeenth House of Menzoberranzan. By the Year of Deep Water Drifting, 1480 DR , House Higure climbed up to claim its place as the thirteenth House of Menzoberranzan.
