Hesione was a popular tailor who lived in Sambral , one of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond , as of 1479 DR .

Description

Hesione was a lovely human female. When she converted to the worship of Sune , she dyed her hair red to honor the goddess .

History

At some point before 1479 DR, Hesione converted to the worship of Sune.
