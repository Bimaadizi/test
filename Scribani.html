Scribani was a Netherese arcanist who taught magic at the Variator's Intelligentsia in Eileanar before its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Scribani was a master at transmutation . She taught the topic at the college under Dean Arrias , particularly focusing on its applications with mythallars .
