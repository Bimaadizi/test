Naerlus was a shadovar spy inside Candlekeep .

History

Naerlus went to infiltrate Candlekeep. He killed and replaced the monk Andannas Dalkur .

In 1487 DR , after receiving a pass-phrase, he killed a person but was uncovered by Elminster Aumar , also disguised as Dalkur. Elminster interrogated him but with little success. Soon after, Naerlus managed to free himself and tried to kill the Old Mage but instead he died.
