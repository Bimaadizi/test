The docent of moonlight was a magical docent -like item, found in Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The docent filled a warforged with the mystical energy of the moon , making them tougher in battle, more resistant to magic, and aiding the power of their spells.

History

The docent was found after agents of the Cormanthor Elves helped Kaylie and Jurin'ildren defend the Burning Library in the ruins.
