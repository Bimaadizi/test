Sarraver was a human cavalier mercenary and later lord in Baldur's Gate in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Sarraver had a daughter, Coril , who ran away some time before 1357 DR , and he had no idea as to her location.

History

Known as a cavalier in the late 1350s DR, Sarraver was a rich mercenary lord in Baldur's Gate by the early 1370s DR.

Circa 1372 DR , Sarraver had recently found his wayward daughter in Waterdeep . Coril was unwilling to leave the city to avoid his attentions, and so had to stay and reject his agents' growing numbers of attempts to take her back to be married off.
