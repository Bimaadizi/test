Lord Whitburn was the husband of Lady Whitburn , and a member of a patriar family of Baldur's Gate in the 15 th century DR .

Description

Lord Whitburn was a fair-skinned bespectacled human man with light chestnut hair and brown eyes. He wore quality firebird-embroidered suits of teal finery.

History

The elfin woman known as Lady Whitburn married into the Whitburn patriar family sometime before the late 15 th century DR.
