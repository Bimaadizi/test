Sparkers were simple, non-magical items in the Realms often used by wizards during scientific experiments to induce a fiery spark into a beaker of vapor.

Description

A sparker was a small hand-held device consisting of flint and steel set on a wire loop hinge .

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 5 silver pieces .
