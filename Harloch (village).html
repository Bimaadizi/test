Harloch was a small village located on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The village was located near the center of the island on the eastern shore of Harloch for which it was named. Snowdown's one road, Snowdown Ride , passed through Harloch.

History

During the height of the Kingdom of Snowdown , Harloch was one of the larger and more influential cantrevs in regards to influencing the monarch's policies.

Inhabitants

Heinrich Mucklepratt was a low-ranking employee of the mayor in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .
