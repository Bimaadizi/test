Abigail was a follower of Gond who worked at the High House of Wonders in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Abigail was absolutely enamored with the inventions dreamt up and created by her fellow Gondar devotees.

Relationships

Both of Abigail's parents were alive for much of her life. She had at least one sibling, a brother named Benji who was a member of the Emerald Enclave .

History

At some point, Abigail wrote a letter to Benji imploring him to come visit her at the High House in Baldur's Gate.
