The Dragonriders Club was a private club in Suzail owned by Tress . It was the favorite nighttime hangout of many nobles and wealthy merchants.

Location

The club was located in northwest Suzail.

Description

The club's primary attraction was its beautiful Mask Dancers , who danced on stages wearing nothing but a mask. During the daytime hours, the club offered meals and services such as haircuts and baths to its members. Amarune Whitewave danced at the club and was one of the most popular dancers.

In 1479 DR the club was heavily damaged during a brawl between nobles.
