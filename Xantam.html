Xantam was the guild master of Xantam's Thieves Guild , an emergent criminal organization in Baldur's Gate , with the secret backing of Eldrith .

His primary agent was Karne , in truth a Zhentarim agent.

His guild was destroyed and Xantam was killed by the heroes Vahn , Adrianna , and Kromlech Bruenghor .
