Dreb was a barbed devil in Avernus , first layer of the Nine Hells , in the late 15 th century DR .

History

When the city of Elturel was transported to Avernus in the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , Dreb led a cast of giant crabs through the High Hall cathedral. Though otherwise normal, these crabs had spiny shells and stank of brimstone.
