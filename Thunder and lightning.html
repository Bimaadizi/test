Thunder and lightning were a pair of magical handwraps in the mid-1480s DR .

Powers

The handwraps were excellent at dealing suddenly lethal blows, and also imparted a blast of thunder and lightning with each strike.

History

The handwraps were found after a group including Oriphaun Huntsilver destroyed Archwizard Amskar 's flying enclave over the Storm Horns mountains, winning victory for Cormyr from the Shadovar .
