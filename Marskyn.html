Marskyn was an inhabitant of Westgate in the mid-8 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Awakening Wyrm, 767 DR , Marskyn had doubled the pay offered to mercenaries for stopping Saeraede Lyonora , on behalf of the Duke of Westgate . He still couldn't find any more people in the city willing to take the job, however.
