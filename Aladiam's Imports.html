Aladiam's Imports was a shop in Tegal's Mark that sold spices and silks imported from Sembia during the mid-14 th century DR . The establishment was run by a retired Calishite adventurer named Aladiam . He was a known smuggler and fence that used his shop to look out for and recruit adventurers to move his contraband.

The shop was defended by some sort of invisible guardian. On one occasion it ripped through three Sembians who confronted Aladiam about some stolen goods.
