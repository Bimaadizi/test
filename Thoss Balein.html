Thoss Balein was Master of Waterdeep 's Solemn Order of Recognized Furriers and Woolmen in the mid–14 th century DR .

Personality

He was an extraordinarily conservative, uncompromising, and ruthless guildmaster.

Activities

As Master of his guild, Thoss implemented a series of policy changes. These included refusing to accept new members for the entirety of his tenure, and encouraging wealthy guild members to buy out their less-moneyed peers from the organization.

History

Thoss was born in the Year of the Singing Skull, 1297 DR , and was elected Master of the Solemn Order in the Year of the Wanderer, 1338 DR when his predecessor died. Under his leadership, the Order's membership decreased by 25% while revenues grew by 40%.
