Thousand Year Old Egg was a dish of preserved eggs considered to be a delicacy on the Malatran Plateau of Kara-Tur .

Description

Thousand Year Old Egg were duck eggs, stored in lime , ash, and salt . The preservation process lasted for one hundred days and resulted in eggs colored blue and green. Thousand Year Old Egg had an acquired taste and tasted rich and slightly fishy .
