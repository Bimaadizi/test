Lump's war horn was a unique war horn created and carried by the brilliant ogre mercenary, Lump the magnificent .

Description

The mouthpiece of the horn was encrusted with bits of food.

Powers

When sounded, Lump's war horn would alert Lump and his allies Chock and Fank . The mercenaries would respond to the call, aiding whomever sounded it during their next battle. In return, the trio of ogres expected to feast upon any creatures they slew during the fight.

The ogres would typically try to avoid harming whomever sounded the horn, but that was not a complete certainty.

History

The horn still remained in the possession of its creator as of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
