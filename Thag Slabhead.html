Thag Slabhead was a ogre of the Sword Coast North in the late 15 th century DR .

History

He served Vansi Bloodscar of the Many-Arrows orc kingdom in their occupation of the Tower District of Neverwinter . In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Hulgen Proudfist asked a half-elf adventurer to kill Thag to prove their strength when considering whether the Proudfist tribe should switch sides.
