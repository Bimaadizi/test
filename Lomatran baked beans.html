Lomatran baked beans was a morningfeast and dinner dish unique to the Blade Kingdoms and the city-state of Lomatra in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Lomatran beans were cooked mixed with local sausages inside decorated and covered ceramic pots . Once the dish was baked thoroughly, it was served in the same individual pot they were cooked in. To eat, one just needed to remove the lid.

Cooks baked numerous pots simultaneously and transported them to the establishments and individuals who ordered the meals. One such bean bakery shop stood on the High Street in Lomatra.
