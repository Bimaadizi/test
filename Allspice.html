Allspice , also called pimento or everry , was an expensive spice produced by a berry-bearing bush.

Availability

In the Cormyrean city of Gladehap , Wyrmkindler Sausage Works used one thruppinch of allspice berries per 4 pounds (1.8 kilograms) of stag meat in preparation for their ever-popular stag sausages .

Elves of the ancient city of Myth Drannor in the forest of Cormanthor were quite fond of allspice-seasoned pumpkin soup . A single whiff of the soup evoked memories of a legend about Aerdrie Faenya , which told of her transforming the heads of slain orcs into pumpkins . The story symbolized elves using orcs' heads more effectively than they ever did themselves.

Bakers of the wizardly nation of Halruaa used sugar , cardamom , mace , and allspice mixture to dust freshly fried sweet buns and hangman's nooses .

Dwarves of the Northkingdom used allspice to prepare sweet, savory, and spicy Tide-Me-Overs .

Allspice, or pimento, was sold across the Realms via Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue , priced at 40 gold pieces per 1 ounce (28 grams).

On the Malatran Plateau in Kara-Tur , allspice was used as a pickling spice, together with mustard seeds, black peppercorns , red chiles , cloves , coriander , ginger , and mace .

Usages

Allspice berries could be used to create enchanted oil that could improve one's strength. A vial of such oil could cost up to 750 gp .

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Gravia's hot apple cider , prepared by Gravia Maxillian of Daggerford 's farmlands, called for two allspice berries per pot. Secretly a werewolf , Gravia drugged her cider with Zakharan knockout drops to capture unwitting travelers to be hunted by her bloodthirsty family.
