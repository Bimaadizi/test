Valtreth was one of many hamlets sprinkled along the Firedrake Road of the Firedrake Shores region of Tethyr .

Description

Valtreth was built along the minor trade route of the Firedrake Road, that hugged the Firedrake Shores , passed through the Dragon's Neck Peninsula , and reached the Dragon's Head Peninsula . Valtreth was a small collection of cottages notable for its fishing net weavers.
