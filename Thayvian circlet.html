Thayvian circlets were enchanted headbands created by the Red Wizards of Thay sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The circlets were designed to boost the spellcasting abilities of the Red Wizards by improving their concentration on magical energies being worked and enhancing their intelligence. However, these bands also boosted the wearer's hardiness, reflexes, and willpower.

History

These Thayvian circlets were tools to aid the Red Wizard in fending off competition from other wizardly organizations, such as the Arcane Brotherhood of Luskan and the Zhentarim . One such item belonged to Magda , the proprietress of the Veil Theater in the Rashemi city of Mulsantir . She offered the circlet for sale to the theater guests.
