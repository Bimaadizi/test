Theryis was an eladrin Harper agent who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

Personality

Theryis was a serene and understanding person, in contrast to her brother's personality.

Relationships

Theryis was the half-sister of Toram .

Abilities

Theryis was an arcane archer .

Activities

Besides being a Harper , Theryis was also the owner and administrator of the House of a Thousand Faces .

History

The House of a Thousand Faces was originally a clothing boutique. After the Ruining of 1451 DR , Theryis closed her business and left the city to explore the world. She returned a few years later alongside her brother, both of them now Harper agents. Theryis re-purposed her old business as a tavern, to serve as an undercover base for the Harpers operating in the city.

As of 1479 DR , Theryis was one of the only two official Harper agents operating in Neverwinter. The other one was her brother.

In 1479 DR, Theryis may have aided of group of adventurers who were searching for information about the " Lost Heir " of Alagondar .
