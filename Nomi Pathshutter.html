Nomi Pathshutter was the second-in-command of the Stoneheart Enclave in Blingdenstone in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Nomi concentrated her efforts on eradicating Ogremoch's Bane .

She also managed the normal activities of the enclave while her master and leader of the Enclave Yantha Coaxrock was in Mantol-Derith .
