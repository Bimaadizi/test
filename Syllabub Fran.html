Syllabub Fran , better known as Shimmer , was a dancer and performer from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Syllabub was a human man with dark bronze skin and brown eyes. His long hair was of striking blue color, matches by the blues of his outfit.

Activities

When he was not performing, Shimmer could be found intoxicated and passed out in or near the Blushing Mermaid tavern.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Shimmer was a regular performer who entertained the customers of the shady Blushing Mermaid tavern in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. Shimmer was a popular performer, known for his loud make up, glittering outfits, and impressive dancing.
