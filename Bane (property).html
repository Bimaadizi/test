Bane was a quality that could be added to magical weapons. They excelled at striking and doing greater injury to foes of a particular racial type.

Creation

In order to forge a bane weapon, the creator must have summon monster I available.

This quality could be temporarily applied to weapons by means of the transmutation spell , bane weapon .
