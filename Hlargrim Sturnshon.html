Hlargrim Sturnshon was the proprietor of the Dryad's Dreams , the only festhall in Mosstone , Tethyr in the mid-to-late-14 th century DR .

Description

Hlargrim was a hardy man with an open-mind.

History

Hlargrim Sturnshon was a forester by trade and after a long career, the retired and became the owner of the Dryad's Dreams festhall in Mosstone that he ran along with his lovely daughters, Braetha , Kalaeria , Charethra , Hestroone , and five others.
