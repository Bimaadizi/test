A skin of pouring was a waterskin enchanted with the pluma spell create talisman .

Description

The exterior of these waterskins was feathered.

Powers

The enchantment placed upon a skin of pouring ensured that it was always full of clean drinking water. Water could be poured from the skin at a rate of 1 gallon (3,800 cubic centimeters) per minute.
