The Singed Bolt was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tavern stood two stories tall.

Location

The Singed Bolt was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the northeast corner of where River Street joined the High Road .
