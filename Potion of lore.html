Potions of lore were magic concoctions , the arcane knowledge in a bottle, always in high demand among scholars and adventurers delving deep into magical mysteries-filled dungeons.

Description

Potions of lore were often red or pink in color.

Powers

Drinking a potion of lore was similar in effect to the identify spell .

History

Potions of lore became popular and wide-spread in the areas surrounding Neverwinter , Daggerford , Nelanther Islands , Cormyr , and many other places circa 1372 DR .
