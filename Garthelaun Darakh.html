Garthelaun Darakh , who was also called the Goreclaw , was the fourth Nentyarch of the Darakh Dynasty to rule Narfell .

Activities

Garthelaun's reign began when the previous Nentyarch, Yannos the Slayer , died in −270 DR . The Gorevlaw ruled until −235 DR .

Relationships

Garthelaun was the half-fiend offspring of a human woman and the demon lord Orcus .
