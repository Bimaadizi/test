Ascot Stumblefoot was a halfling shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Ascot was polite and well-spoken.

Activities

He owned the Spice of Life , a well-stocked spice and herb shop located in the Foreign Quarter near the River Trail Gate . He imported spices from all across Toril including Kozakura , the Moonshae Isles , Maztica , and Zakhara .

He initially decided to settle in the area because of its close proximity to his favorite vintage of wine and tobacco . Ascot sacrificed much during the destruction of Zhentil Keep in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , by cutting down to just three meals a day.
