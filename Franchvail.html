Franchvail was a Netherese cleric of Amaunator who worked at Benante's Butchery in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Franchvail used her priestly abilities to bless the carcasses of slaughtered animals. She removed any parasites or disease present before they were carved up for customers.
