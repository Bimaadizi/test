Khass'insara was a fortress located beneath the waters of the Thingulph on the Chultan Peninsula .

Location

The fortress was located in the western Thingulph 10 miles (16 kilometers) offshore from the capital of Thindar .

Description

Khass'insara was made entirely from coral and blended in so well with its surroundings that it was practically unrecognizable as a fortress.

Inhabitants

Yuan-ti from House Sseradess claimed the fortress as their own.
