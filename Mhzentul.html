Mhzentul ( pronounced : / m ɑː ˈ z ɛ n t ʊl / mah-ZEN-tul ) was a powerful and well-respected mage who wandered the Realms in latter half of the 13 th century and early 14 th century DR . Although he spent much of his time traveling, he was known to have kept a hidden abode in some unnamed mountain range.

Activities

During his years traveling Faerûn , Mhzentul spent much of his time studying arcane spells that involved the conjuring of flames, the creation of magical rings.

He was famed for creating the Seven Lost Rings of Mhzentul , each of which contained extremely powerful magic.

History

Mhzentul died at the Battle of River Rising , after he transformed himself into a pillar of living flame. His legacy lived on his famed book , titled Mhzentul's Runes .
