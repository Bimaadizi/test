TypeMagical stones

The Seeing Stones were a magical set of stones used for communication.

Abilities

The Seeing Stones allowed communication between two individuals regardless of the distance.

History

The Seeing Stones were considered to be a state treasure of the kingdom of Eskorn , in Laerakond .

As of 1479 DR , the stones were used by King Ornan Roaryl to communicate with his seneschal, Mreskan Haundle , whenever the king was traveling across his realm.
