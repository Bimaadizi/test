Atharstone , or athar-stone , was a heavy type of rock found in asteroids and in a few mountain ranges of select crystal spheres .

Properties

Atharstone was noted to be both heavier and more durable than lead .

Applications

The durability of atharstone made it suitable for constructing dead boxes .
