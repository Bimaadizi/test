Galandro Luna was a performer who lived in the Sword Coast North during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Galandro's greatest joy in life was seeing joy in the audiences for whom he performed. While he was a skilled musician, Galandro was also quite adept at dishing out insults. He had a good sense of humor and did not mind them returned in kind.

Possessions

His most beloved item was his fiddle , which was given to him as a gift by a dear friend.

Activities

Galandro's aim in life was to become famous and earn great acclaim as a musician. He would do just about anything to achieve this goal.
