Father Jamad was the head priest of St. Faelar's Cloisters in 1370 DR . Under Jamad's leadeship, the monks and rangers of the Cloisters trained many escaped slaves in survival skills in the foothills of the Marching Mountains .

History

In Kythorn of 1370, the Cloister suffered an attack of arson, and Father Jamad was one of the survivors and witnesses. He claimed that he saw a "heavily cloaked wizard " cast and control a flaming sphere while laughing. Some suggested this wizard was Tara el Viilam , but Jamad was unable to identify her conclusively.
