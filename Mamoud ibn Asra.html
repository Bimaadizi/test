Mamoud ibn Asra was a dwarven faris who lived within the fortress of Sarahin in 1367 DR .

Activities

Mamoud was a a confidant of Batina al-Shaat , the leader of the Preachers . He served as her prime enforcer for both the human and dwarven warriors.

Mamoud also served as the commander of both the Courageous and the Eagles .

Possessions

Mamoud owned a magical shield and lamellar armor of the desert evening .
