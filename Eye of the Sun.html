Eyes of the Sun were enchanted kama created by monks of the Order of the Sun Soul sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Eyes of the Sun were always warm to the touch and were enhanced with a simple +3 enchantment that made its slashes harmful even to creatures unaffected by mundane weapons and guided the wielder's hand towards the target. Additionally, the weapon burned the targets with acid and fire with each slash.

History

It was unknown when the first Eye of the Sun was created, but the kama bore markings of the Sun Souls. By the late 14 th century DR, some of these enchanted kama or copies made from the original, were found in the city of Neverwinter and nearby lands.
