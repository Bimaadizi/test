Mauj was a reef giant living on the various lands of the Great Sea in 1367 DR .

Description

Mauj was short for his race standing 15 ft (4.6 m) tall. He had the look of a terrifying monster which contradicted his intelligent and wise nature.

Activities

Mauj worked as an enforcer for the moneylenders of Gana . He preferred to solve problems without bloodshed.
