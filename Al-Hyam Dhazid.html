Al-Hyam Dhazid was a magic-user in Phlan in 1340 DR .

Activities

Al-Hyam acted as an advisor for Tyranthraxus , trying to learn his secrets. However, Tyranthraxus tricked Al-Hyam and he believed he served a true dragon .
