Daggerroot , also known as the executioner's garnish was a species of herb found in blood-soaked areas of the Western Heartlands .

Description

Daggerroot was known to flourish in spots where blood was spilled onto the ground. Executioner's garnish was often sold in bundles, each worth fifteen gold coins .

Usages

When dried and burned, daggerroot plant ash was combined with special types of salt to create oil of accuracy .
