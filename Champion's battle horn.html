Champion's battle horn wan a fabled enchanted horn , blown by defenders of the city during the Siege of Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

When blown on a field of battle, champion's battle horn produced the sound that lifted the spirits of those who heard it, boosting their vitality, morale, and speed.

History

This horn was used by many heroes over the years. Most notably, it was used in the late 15 th century DR, during the Cult of the Dragon 's Siege of Neverwinter.
