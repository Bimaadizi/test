The Chamber of the Brazen Fool was a room inside Castle Obarskyr in 1357 DR .

Location

It was distant from the Hall of Scrolls and Ledgers .

History

On Marpenoth 17 1357 DR , Vangerdahast commanded the Purple Dragon Knights close to him in the Hall of Scrolls and Ledgers to seal off the wing of the palace and then maintain a perimeter as far away as the Chamber of the Brazen Fool, after discovering a hand merged with the floor of the Hall of Scrolls and Ledgers.
