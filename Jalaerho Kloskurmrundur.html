Jalaerho Kloskurmrundur was the Guildmaster Emeritus of the Stationers' Guild of Waterdeep in the late-14 th century DR .

History

By the Year of the Cauldron, 1378 DR , Jalaerho had been the Guildmaster Emeritus for over ten years. He wrote down his musings and experiences in a book published in 1378 DR. His work was dramatically named A Seat That Draws Blood: Ten Years a Guildmaster and Jalaerhoe proclaimed his unfavorable attitude towards change and rebellious youth while stressing the importance of business and coin it brought.
