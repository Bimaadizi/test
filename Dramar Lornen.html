Dramar Lornen was a collector from the "Royal Keep" of Ironhar in Eskorn , Laerakond .

Background

Dramar was obsessed with the Dawn Titan's Spire , and, as of 1479 DR , she had hired many adventurers and agents in all of Laerakond to investigate any clues that would allow Dramar to gain entry to the Spire.
