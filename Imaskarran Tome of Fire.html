The Imaskarran Tome of Fire was a heavy and tattered tome with a single rune on the cover, translating simply to "Fire". The Imaskarran Tome of Fire detailed the process of creating a wand of fireballs from a quartz crystal and the belly of a fire beetle , through a spell of fireball .

Eltoora Sarptyl sought, maybe with the help of the Hero of Neverwinter , this tome around Port Llast in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . Though allegedly of Imaskarran origin, they were readable to the Hero of Neverwinter . It was in the possession of the undead Kharlat Jhareg of the Charwood .
