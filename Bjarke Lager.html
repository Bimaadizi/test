Bjarke Lager was a dwarf commander in Gauntlgrym in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In 1488 DR , during the demon attack on the underground city, Bjarke welcomed Catti-brie and Penelope Harpell who had just been teleported inside by Kipper Harpell .
