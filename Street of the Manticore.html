The Street of the Manticore was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Street of the Manticore was located in the central section of Waterdeep's North Ward . The road ran roughly east to west in between Vhezoar Street in the west and Whaelgond Way in the east. Several smaller roads intersected the Street of the Manticore including Little Street and Sprangalar's Swordcut .
