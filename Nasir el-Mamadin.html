Nasir el-Mamadin was the mamluk leader of the Diligent Mamluk Society in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

The mamluk took orders directly from Grand Caliph Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir or Preani Qin .

History

Nasir was originally from a distant land called Anauroch .
