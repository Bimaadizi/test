Based onChipmunk

A berrygobbler was a small, scurrying, chipmunk -like rodent common in the Heartlands and other areas of Faerûn .

In warmer climates of Faerûn, such as Calimshan or Tashalar , there were black-furred and speedy berrygobblers called " slinks ".
