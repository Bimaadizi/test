The Winking Eye was a popular tavern that stood on the grand Promenade in the city of Suzail , Cormyr .

Location

The tavern stood on the northwestern corner of the crossing of the Promenade and the looping Court Close . The establishment was located on the second block to the west from Castle Obarskyr .

History

The Winking Eye was opened sometime before the Year of the Worm, 1356 DR .
