Eel Street was a major road in the asteroid city of Bral .

Description

Eel Street began with an intersection with Lamp Street on the starboard side of the asteroid and ran to its port side at Anchor Street , covering most of the width of the Low City . Along the way, it crossed Hammer Street , Grand Street , and Dragon Street , for a total distance of roughly 1,300 feet (400 meters). It formed the trailing-edge borders of Giff-town and Shou-town .
