Elderstars was a grand manor located in the Temple District of Ravens Bluff during the 14 th century DR . It was a massive, 5-story manse, decorated with gabled roofs and gargoyle heads, located on the corner of Manycoins Way and Hawk Passage within the district's Holyhouses neighborhood.

Around the year 1370 , it was the home of Sir Uldren Stonefist Deepaxe , chair of the city's Knights Council .
