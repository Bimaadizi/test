Wangle's Livery was a stable in the town of Thunderstone in Cormyr in the late 1360s DR . It was managed by Wangle Towerstruck .

The livery traded in all kinds of livestock, especially horses , which could be of top-quality. Mounts could also be stabled for 2 gold pieces a night. Patrons could expect their mounts to receive good feed, grooming, and a cleaning, as well as veterinary care and re-shoeing as needed.
