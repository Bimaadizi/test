Punkins was a child from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Punkins was a human boy with pale skin skin and blue eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Punkins spent his days playing with fellow children of the Gate, Cinnamon and Slow-Butt . The kids played steel watchers and the spies of the Absolute . Punkins was assigned the steel watcher's role who was to find the spies and find smuggled candy in the children's pockets. In his role, Punkins loved snitches.
