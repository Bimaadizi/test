Therndle Daglefodd was a svirfneblin trader who lived in a small village in the Underdark .

Description

Therndle was a merchant and a concerned father. His son, Bedlen had gone missing for a few days. Until he was found, he would not trade a single gem . His normal wares consisted of armor , weapons , ammunition, potions and spells .
