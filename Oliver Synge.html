Oliver Synge was a merchant who operated on the Sword Coast during the late 14 th century DR . Synge was of the Calishite ethnicity.

History

On Eleasis 26 of the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , Synge spent one day in the small village of Moonhaven . The merchant returned later on Nightal 14 , but merely passed through the town. Synge's activities were observed by the paranoid, exiled Red Wizard Ilyn Toth .
