Nafay Mizz'rynturl was a drow traitor priestess of Guallidurth in 1378 DR .

Activities

Nafay was a noble of House Mizz'rynturl of the city of Guallidurth but secretly also a traitor priestess of Vhaeraun .

History

She arranged a meeting with a mute wild elf who knew about a very ancient temple to Lolth on the surface. There, however, she was attacked and killed by her old childhood friend T'lar Mizz'rynturl , under orders of the Velkyn Velve , the assassin branch of the new Temple of the Black Mother .
