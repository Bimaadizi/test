Tep yen was a variety of spice found in the Realms.

Description

Though its composition was not public knowledge, Berun , a druid of the Shalhoond , took it to be the crushed seeds of some plant. He kept it in containers not made from leather, such as glass, clay, or bone. It was extremely spicy, or "hot", and very painful when it got into the eyes.

Availability

It was sold in Almorel in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .
