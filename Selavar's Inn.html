Selavar's Inn was a large inn of note in Suzail , the capital city of Cormyr .

Location

The establishment stood one block south of the grand Promenade , along the veering Court Close . It was located on the same eastern side of the Court Close as Zhaelun's Stockyard .

History

Selavar's Inn was opened sometime before the Year of the Worm, 1356 DR .
