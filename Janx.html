Janx was a caravan guard. He was a youth with sharp features. On Flamerule 12 of the Year of Moonfall, 1344 DR , he was helping guide a caravan to Yulash from Hillsfar when it was attacked by a hungry red dragon . Janx suggested they scatter, but he was talked out of it: he was soon slain when it attacked. The only survivor was a 9-year old Dorn Graybrook .
