Yolen Nieldot was an artificer for hire from the city of Neverwinter , active in the late 15 th century DR .

History

By the late 15 th century DR, Yolen Nieldot had spent two years apprenticing under a belligerent master artificer of Neverwinter. After quitting, Yolen made a living by offering her artificer services and doing smaller odd jobs until she found her own place in the world and home where Yolen could practice her trade.
