Haerlit Thomm was a fence of stolen goods inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Haerlit worked out of a tavern called Felzoun's Folly located in Waterdeep's Trades Ward on Salabar Street . His point of contact there was the tavern's owner, Felzoun Thar . Haerlit specialized in furniture and other distinctive furnishings, keeping most of his goods within Thomm Warehouse on Sambril Lane in the Dock Ward .
