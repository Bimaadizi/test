Lorold or Torold was the porter of Delcastle Manor in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

He kept the door at Delcastle Manor .

Relationships

He worked for the Delcastle family Marantine Delcastle called him "Torold", while Arclath called him "Lorold".

History

Lorold opened the door on two occassions to Amarune Whitewave , coinlass and lover of Arclath Delcastle . On the first time, when she was accompanied by Lord Arclath Delcastle , and the second time, when she came to tell him about three young lords' search for an enchanted axe , only to be stopped by Lady Marantine Delcastle.
