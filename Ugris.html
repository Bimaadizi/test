Ugris was a thief inhabiting Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Ugris sold maps of the ruins of Zhentil Keep following the city's destruction in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR . The maps sold for 5 gp a piece a Ugris did not claim any responsibility for loss of life, limb, or property as a result of following one of his maps.

His shop was located on the Throat outside of the Throat Gate of the Foreign Quarter .
