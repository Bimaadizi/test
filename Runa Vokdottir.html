Runa Vokdottir was a member of the Stone-Cold Reavers , a group of reckless adventurers who made their living sometimes as sellswords and sometimes as brigands.

Description

Runa was a muscular woman. She had black hair and cold blue eyes.

Personality

Runa knew nothing but violence to solve her problems and considered cruelty to be normal. She was a taciturn person but when she talked, her bitter sarcasm became apparent.

History

Runa grew up in the harsh north of Icewind Dale . Later, she traveled south where she joined the Stone-Cold Reavers. The group tried to steal the presumed treasure hoard of the young white dragon Cryovain from its lair, Icespire Hold . However, Cryovain hadn't amassed any treasure yet.
