Munzrim Marlpar was a lizardman bartender inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He stood a staggering 9 feet (2.7 meters) tall. Despite his beastly appearance, Munzrim was quite intelligent.

Activities

Munzrim worked in a tavern located in the North Ward called the Misty Beard . When he wasn't serving drinks he spent his time talking with a spectator called Thoim Zalamm who was a close friend.
