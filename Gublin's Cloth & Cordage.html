Gublin's Cloth & Cordage was a shop in Daggerford that specialized in selling cloths and ropes . It was run by the successful businessman Gilly Gublin .

Location

The shop was located in Daggerford's Farmers' Quarter .

Interior

Within the top floor of the shop was some specialized machinery that wound strands together to make rope. The device originated from the High House of Wonders in the southern city of Baldur's Gate .

Services

Gilly's sold high-quality textiles and cordage, particularly those varieties that were imported from elsewhere. Despite the high caliber of his goods, Gilly's prices were so low that few others could compete.
