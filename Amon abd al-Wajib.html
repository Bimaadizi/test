Amon abd al-Wajib was a mamluk of the Dutiful Mamluk Society in Huzuz , the City of Delights, around 1367 DR . He was originally from Qudra .

Description

The markings of the Dutiful were tattooed on Amon's face.

Activities

Amon was permanently assigned as a guard for Alara bint Kira 's family. He was very protective of his ward, especially from would-be suitors.

Possessions

Amon wore padded leather armor and a blue-dyed kilt.
