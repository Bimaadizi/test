Revi was an inhabitant of Vaelan in Durpar in the late 14 th century DR . He was one of Zeltaebar Datharathi 's men.

Description

He dressed in all gray, sported greasy hair, and wielded a prying bar.

Hitory

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , he was searching through Eined Datharathi 's room in the West Garden district of Vaelan when Warian Datharathi stumbled upon him. Warian nearly removed his leg during the fight; all the combatants broke off when Zeltaebar arrived at the department, thankfully.
