A hoe was a common digging tool and farming implement found in Faerûn .

Description

A hoe was essentially a shovel-like metal blade fixed perpendicular to the end of a long pole .

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue offered hoes with 6‑foot (1.8‑meter) hardwood handles and iron heads for 3 gp , and steel -headed ones for clay -heavy soil for 5 gp.
