AlignmentLawful evil

The Scepter of the Tyrant's Eye was a minor artifact of Bane created by Teldorn Darkhope and Fzoul Chembryl .

Description

The scepter was equivalent to a +3 heavy mace and was sentient. Non-Banites who touched the scepter were hit by a ray of enfeeblement until their strength was depleted, at which time their soul would be trapped within.
