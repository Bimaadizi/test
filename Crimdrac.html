Crimdrac was a dracolich ally of Dracandros in Battledale in 1357 DR .

Personality

Crimdrac was basically a compulsive liar: he tried to claim as his own all kinds of exploit made by a dragon in the Dragon Reach . He planned to betray Dracandros after the Red Wizard had taught him magic.

History

Crimdrac was a red dragon dracolich created by the Cult of the Dragon . However, he allied with the Red Wizard of Thay Dracandros for the chance to learn spells . Thanks to him, Dracandros ruled the village of Hap .

Crimdrac was killed by the adventuring party possessing the Azure Bonds .
