Tespergates , which was also called Tesper Villa , was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Tesper .

Location

The villa was located at the corner of Mendever Street and Delzorin Street in the central section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward .

Description

Tespergates was composed of four structures of two and three stories in height. The walled estate's largest building sat in the center and was surrounded by a garden and the three smaller structures.

Activities

The Tespers were known to host parties at Tespergates that even commoners were allowed to attend. Live music and wrestling contests were common events at such parties.

Rumors & Legends

Most Waterdhavians believed Tespergates to be haunted.
