Keltarn Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Keltarn Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . It ran east to west just south of Siren Lane , connecting the much larger Street of Silver to the east with Swords Street to the west. Keltarn Street also intersected the Street of Silks near its midpoint.
