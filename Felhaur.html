Felhaur was a fishmonger inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Felhuar owned a fishmongery called Felhaur's Fine Fish located on Spices Street in Waterdeep's Dock Ward .

Relationships

He had six children who all helped run the store.
