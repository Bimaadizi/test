Isarr Kronenstrom , also known as the Wolf King , was a Malarite chieftain who led a clan of Tribe of the Wolf barbarians in Icewind Dale during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Chief Kronenstrom was a vicious and brutal man who inspired fear in all the folks across the dale.

Abilities

He had an exceptional sense of hearing and smell. Whenever he attacked a foe who had spilt blood, Kronenstrom went into a complete frenzy.

Fitting to his name, the chieftain would let out howls to communicate with his allied tribesmen.

Possessions

He wielded a sickle and a heavy crossbow in battle.

Relationships

While the Wolf King only garnered support from a small number of Wolf Tribe barbarians, his followers were particularly loyal and ferocious. Other chieftains believed themselves too weak to challenge his rule.

History

Around the Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR , Chieftain Kronenstrom led the assault on a camp of his fellow Reghedmen barbarians, also of the Tribe of the Wolf. The only survivor was the son of the camp's chieftain, Aerix Vokototh .
