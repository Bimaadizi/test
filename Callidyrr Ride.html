Callidyrr Ride was a street found in the coastal town of Aithelar in Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles in Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR .

Location

Callidyrr Ride was found in southwest Aithelar in between Finn's Alley and Blackdorn's Way . Aerynn's Street branched from its midpoint to the northeast.
