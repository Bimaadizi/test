Jurash was a priest of Ishistu and cultist of the Shorn who inhabited Moradask in the Burning Lands in 1367 DR .

Activities

Jurash captured a desert centaur named Tebessa al-Khawati as she was bathing in an oasis near the Sea of Salt . He attempted to shave all of the hair from Tebessa's body to serve as a sacrifice, but succeeded only in removing her proud warrior's braid before she was rescued.
