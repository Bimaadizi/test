Nytharri was the wife of the Iron Lord, or Huhrong , of Immilmar in Rashemen during the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Nytharri was married to Thydrim Yvarrg . They had several children together, the eldest of whom was Fyldrin . Nytharri was widowed in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , when her husband was killed by assassins from Thay .
