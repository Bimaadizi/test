Deidre was a merchant and employee of Aurora's Emporium who sold her unique wares at the Adventurer Mart in Waukeen's Promenade in Athkatla , Amn , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

As a member of the Aurora's magic emporium, Deidre was able to sell from Aurora 's collection of magical treasures. In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , these included a variety of unique items from Sigil and the companions of the immortal Nameless One and mementos of the explorer Balduran :

She rented her store space from Ribald Barterman .
