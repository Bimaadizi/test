The Burning Pool of Natifa was a small wonderous body of water located in west Zakhara , the Land of Fate.

Geography

The Burning Pool of Natifa was located near a cluster of crescent-shaped barchan dunes west of the Mountains of Forgotten Dreams .

Description

The pool measured 40 ft (12 m) wide and was 20 ft (6.1 m). The water of the pool had a sweet smell of lemons and roses. Spindly plants called arafaj covered the entire surface of the pool, burning continuously and illuminating the entire pool with a magical glow that could be seen 10 mi (16 km) away. These plants burned whenever they were in contact with the pool's surface; even rain did not extinguish them.

Periodically, a fountain would shoot water 20 ft (6.1 m)) into the air.

A stone oven could be found near the southern end of the pool, complete with pots and other accessories.

Inhabitants

The pool took its name from Natifa , a ghost who inhabited the pool.
