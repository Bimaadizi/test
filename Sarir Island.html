Sarir Island was an island within the Crowded Sea of interior Zakhara .

Geography

Sarir Island was located southwest of the southwest corner of the Cities of the Pantheon region of Zakhara. It was part of a chain of islands that formed Al-Faraq Islands . The island was situated 60 miles (97 kilometers) southwest of Fanus on the mainland. Sarir Island had an elevated interior. Coral reefs surrounded the eastern shore of the island while a series of dangerous submerged rocks covered the western shore. Sarir Island was approximately 30 miles (48 kilometers) long and 10 miles (16 kilometers) wide. The island was uninhabited as of 1367 DR .
