Wruz Olg'lmar was a drow in Gauntlgrym in the Underdark in 1484 DR .

History

Wruz was a drow who was captured by a mind flayer sect led by Thlorrog . He was used as a slave and guinea-pig for the illithid's experiments until an adventurer sent by Soren Ravaris broke into the laboratory and freed him. Wruz had already become an illithid host , but before he succumbed to death he directed the adventurer to destroy most of the illithid's resources.
