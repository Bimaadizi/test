Slarkas was a young black dragon that dwelled within Arkhan's Tower on the plane of Avernus in the Nine Hells , circa the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR .

Slarkas resided within the tower along with three other young chromatic dragons , Flash , Vermilius , and Vistalancer , and the adult white dragon Obatala . Collectively they protected their master, the devoted servant of Tiamat , Arkhan the Cruel .
