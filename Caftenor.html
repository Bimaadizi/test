Caftenor was an island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Caftenor was the southernmost island within the Korinn Archipelago, sitting just 20 miles (32 kilometers) off the coast of northern Gnarhelm near the Lake of the Leech . Several small unnamed islands were found just off the island's coast. The village of Caftenor was located near center of the island on the eastern coast.

Inhabitants

A large population of halflings made their home on Caftenor.
