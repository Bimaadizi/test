Embryn Crownsilver was a member of the Crownsilver noble house of Cormyr .

History

In 1369 DR , Embryn pledged allegiance to Vangerdahast as the Regent of Cormyr.

Description

Embryn wore a jeweled tunic with a pair of cloth of gold breeches , and a half- cloak .

Possessions

Embryn possessed a jeweled blade.
