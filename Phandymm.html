Phandymm was a member of Zhentil Keep 's Council of Lords in 1368 DR and secretly a cleric of Bane .

History

Phandymm was a cleric of Bane loyal to the High Imperceptor of Bane until he met Fzoul Chembryl ; Fzoul gained Phandymm's loyalty and used him as spy in the Orthodox faction of the Church of Bane and also thank to him in 1263 DR Fzoul gained control over all the Zhentil Banites. For recompense Fzoul appointed him in the Council but keep his cleric's role secret ( Manshoon however had suspects about). Phandymm always supported Fzoul in Council.

Phandymm's estate was located in the north section of Zhentil Keep's Inner Quarter on the Street of the Gods .
