The Temple of Entropy was a house of worship devoted to Entropy , a strange primordial striving to destroy all magic .

Location

The Temple of Entropy was located in the city of Luthcheq in Chessenta in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR . The temple had its own walled section of the city along the eastern outer wall just south of the Palace of the Karanoks .

Inhabitants

The High Priest within the Temple of Entropy during this time was Maelos Karanok .
