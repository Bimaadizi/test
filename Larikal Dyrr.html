Larikal Dyrr was a member of House Agrach Dyrr of Menzoberranzan in 1373 DR .

History

Larikal was the third daughter of matron mother Yasraena Dyrr .

During the siege at Dyrr's compound for their betrayal of Menzoberranzan during the Siege of Menzoberranzan , Larikal happened across the archmage Gromph Baenre , who had infiltrated the compound in order to destroy the Lichdrow Dyrr 's phylactery .
