Breagar Coppervein was a dwarf merchant in Cormyr during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Breagar had a bald head and a twin-forked braided black beard.

History

Breagar set up a simple shop of sorts in the ruins of Beckside Castle on the High Road around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar . He offered useful supplies to choice-starved adventurers , such as weapons , armor , potions of healing and restoration , oil of repair , and torches .
