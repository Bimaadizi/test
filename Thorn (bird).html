RaceRaven

Thorn was a raven working for Rhespen Ash during the Dawn Age circa −25,090 DR .

Personality

For a raven, the creature was quite snarky.

Relationships

Thorn was one of the woodland animals that the elven wizard and commander Rhespen Ash communed with for information.

History

Around −25,090 DR , when Rhespen Ash was trying to find the hidden stronghold of the Duskmere elves , he called upon a number of woodland birds to help him. Thorn was the bird who found them; he reported their location to Rhespen Ash.
