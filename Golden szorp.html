Golden szorp was a species of tiny fish that inhabited the Lake Glorfindar in the Starmetal Hills , as well as in the Sea of Moonshae , and remained active through the winter months under the lake's ice.

Description

Golden szorp was a tiny creature not used in cooking. They were a rare baitfish beloved by other bigger species of fish consumed in the north.

Ecology

Golden szorp fishes were sought after during the annual Simril celebration in Twilight Tor . They were used to fish for bigger edible species such as brown bream , blackjaw , candlefish , and durgrace .
