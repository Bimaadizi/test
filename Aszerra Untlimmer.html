Aszerra Untlimmer was a priestess of Shaundakul , the Rider of the Winds, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

She was overweight and had a motherly air about her.

Activities

Aszerra lived in Ordulin in northern Sembia . She was one of only a handful of known priests of Shaundakul following the end of the Time of Troubles in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .
