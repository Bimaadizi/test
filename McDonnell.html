Mc Donnell was a Ffolk inhabitant of Blackstone in Callidyrr in the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR .

History

Mc Donnell served as Blackstone's mayor. He was quite active with the militia, marching with them on a daily basis. Mc Donnell enjoyed spending his downtime in one of Blackstone's many taverns.
