Guiding Light was a magic ring that belonged to the cleric of Mystra L. De Hurst , the resident mistress of the Arcane Tower .

Powers

The ring had the power to reflect light even if there was no available source.

It was used by its original owner as a key to access their secret study in the basement of the Arcane Tower.

History

De Hurst wore Guiding Light during her travels into the Underdark during the 1480s DR .
