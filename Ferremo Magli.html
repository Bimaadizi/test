Ferremo Magli was an assassin and the chief servant of the green dragon , Andareunarthex .

Description

The blonde-haired Ferremo liked to wear nice clothes because he found it made him less noticeable than clothing favored by most assassins.

History

In 1479 DR , Magli helped kidnap Nazra Mrays 's young son so Andarenarthex could extort the Dragonstaff from Nazra.

During his rescue of Clytemorrenestrix , Tennora Hedare killed Magli in Andareunarthex's hideout.
