Benson Delmo was the mayor of Maldobar in 1340 DR .

Description

Delmo was "obese" and cheerful.

History

In 1340 DR , Mayor Benson Delmo and several other residents of Maldobar arrived at the Thistledown house, where Bartholomew Thistledown's children told them of their encounter with the drow Drizzt Do'Urden . The local hunter, Roddy Mc Gristle , cast skepticism over Connor Thistledown 's claim and insisted on leading a search party to verify it. Despite calls to summon a ranger , Delmo put off the request, believing it to be too premature. Sure enough, Mc Gristle encountered the drow and was scarred in the attempt, giving Delmo no other option but to send a missive to Sundabar.

A few days later, the Thistledown family of Maldobar was found murdered. It was believed that Drizzt had done the deed (when in fact Ulgulu had done it). Though Mc Gristle desired to go after Drizzt immediately, Delmo was concerned that the drow's presence was the sign of a larger war band, and negotiated a reward of 2,000 gold pieces for the head of the drow. He also convinced Mc Gristle to wait for Dove Falconhand 's party to arrive from Sundabar before initiating pursuit.
