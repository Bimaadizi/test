Therus ( pronounced : /ˈ ð ɛ r ʌ s / DHER-us ) was a leader of the Cult of the Crushing Wave in Mulmaster in 1491 DR .

History

Therus established a small chapter of the cult inside a former Zhent watchtower near Mulmaster. There, in 1491 DR, he met and recruited the Cloak Drayson Fivestars .

At last, an adventuring party hired by Salvar Brix , a fellow Cloak of Drayson, defeated him and his minions.
