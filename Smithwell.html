Smithwell was a human child who grew up in the rebuilt town of Leilon in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Smithwell was the son of one of Leilon's fishers. His best friend was Burnice .

History

Circa 1491 DR , Leilon was attacked by the Cult of Talos and the townsfolk were driven out. Smithwell and Burnice were left behind in town and their parents were still out on the water. Luckily, a group of adventurers arrived just in time to reclaim the town. Both children were left unharmed.
