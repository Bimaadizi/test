The Bonaku Boarding House was a boarding house in the T'u Lung city of Balanzia around the Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) .

Location

This boarding house was located in the eastern reaches of Balanzia, down the street from the city's market and the observatory of Minkra the Sage .

History

Around Shou Year 2403 (1153 DR) , the boarding house was one of many buildings that were damaged in Balanzia as the city fell at the hands of the Shou Lung invasion of T'u Lung.
