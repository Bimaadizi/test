Anhur's lance was a unique melee weapon wielded by the avatar of Anhur , the General of the Gods within the Mulhorandi pantheon .

Description

The weapon was a lance that Anhur wielded in his right arms.

Powers

This weapon carried a powerful +5 enchantment . In addition to making it easier for Anhur to strike and harm opponents during combat, his lance imparted all of the benefits of a ring of fire resistance , a ring of protection , an amulet of the planes , and a talisman of pure good . The weapon could also change shape to resemble a large combination of a scimitar and khopesh .
