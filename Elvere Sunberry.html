Elvere Sunberry was a reluctant client of the Sharran House of Grief and an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Elvere sought services of the House of Grief aid. The Mapping of the Heart revealed Elvere's "craven heart" and the Unburdening ritual was to take place, paid for it in full. However, Elvere backed out, loosing the nerve. The House of Grief only provided partial refund.
