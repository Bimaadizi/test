Rornagar Breakblade was a member of the Bloodshields Band adventuring party in the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

He had keen eyes.

Activities

As an adventurer with the Bloodshields Band , he mostly kept watch at the rearguard.

Relationships

Rornagar Breakblade was a member of the Bloodshields Band; his leader was Gaerond .

History

While looking for Elminster Aumar in Shadowdale during the early spring of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the leader of the Bloodshields Band, Gaerond, called on Rornagar's talent at spotting to determine where their guide, Thal , had disappeared to after showing them the way to Elminster's cave.

The whole band was slain by doppelgangers , led by a fake Elminster, after they conversed with him briefly.
