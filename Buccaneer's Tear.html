The Buccaneer's Tear was a skyship owned and operated by Company Yargo during the late 15 th century DR . It was captained by the debonair Shil Yargo .

Description

The ship resembled a large galleon that took flight in the sky rather than across the waves of the Realms.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the Shil and her crew navigated the Buccaneer's Tear from the city of Neverwinter up to Pirates' Skyhold in order to investigate rumors about lost skyships found on the soaring earthmote .
