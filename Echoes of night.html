Echoes of night was a powerful rune arm -like armament in the possession of Karissa the Knife , Master Thief of the Night Brigade in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The echoes of night produced blasts of weaponized sonic energy, and was especially deadly to fire elementals and similar creatures as it disrupted their forms. The sound of it echoed in the night, haunting those that heard it.

History

The armament was held by the thief Karissa in her lair until she was slain by the Riverside Rats gang.
