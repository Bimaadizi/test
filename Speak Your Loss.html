Speak Your Loss was a self-help book published sometime in or before the 15 th century DR .

Content

Speak Your Loss taught its readers that true happiness could be carved through cleanliness. Simple things like tidy home and well-polished floorboards were the first steps towards happiness.

History

The book could be found in the city of Baldur's Gate in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
