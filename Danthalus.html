Danthalus was a Highknight of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Danthalus was a highknight .

Relationships

During the raid in 1479 DR on Tethgard , Danthalus was part of the team including Ilvellund Nordroun , Hondryn , and Merlar .

History

Danthalus was part of the raid on Tethgard led by Kelgantor , looking for the fugitive wizard Elminster Aumar , who had recently stolen the Royal Gorget of Battle from the Royal Palace in Cormyr . The raid happened in Mirtul 5 ; Danthalus did not number among the survivors.
