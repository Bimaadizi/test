Larion Keenblade was a member of Severin 's reformed Cult of the Dragon .

History

He was forcibly conscripted into the Cult and not very happy about his situation. He was part of the Cult's army in the assault on Greenest . Later, he was at the Cult's camp near the city.

Gameplay

Adventurers could potentially befriend him and convince him to betray the Cult, providing them with very useful information.
