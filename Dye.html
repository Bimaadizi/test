Dyes were a kind of substance used to impart colors to fabrics , leather , other materials, and even food and drink. It was also used in inks , paints , pastels , and enamels to give them specific colors.

Availability

Dyes were a valuable trade good, and were worth the effort for adventurers to steal.

Aurora's Emporium offered a wide variety of dyed colors for the clothing and fabrics in its catalogue, with white, black, gray, beige, and brown available by default, and brighter colors such as red, blue, yellow, and green available for an extra fee. Raw dyes were also available for sale in 8 ounces (230 grams) packs. These included Calimshan blue (33 sp ), copper blue 9 sp, ultramarine blue (1 gp ), mollusk green (9 sp), indigo (3 gp), ochre (12 sp), purple (33 sp), cochineal red (4 sp), iron red (5 sp), safflower (4 sp), sulfur yellow (3 sp), and vermilion (44 sp).

Crafting

The Most Excellent Order of Weavers and Dyers was responsible for regulating the dyeing industry in Waterdeep , and their livery was rainbow-hued cloaks and gowns. Bolts of cloth could be dyed for 5 sp to 10 gp, depending on the complexity involved.

In Cormyr , the Guild of Naturalists was the authority regarding all manner of substances, including the origination of dyes. The Guild of Weavers and Coopers made up the realm's fabric dyers, while the Tanners and Leatherers Guild represented the leather dyers.
