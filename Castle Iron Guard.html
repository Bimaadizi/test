Castle Iron Guard was the largest castle in the city of Ravens Bluff and one of its two primary troop garrisons in the 14 th century DR . During the Three Women War , the fortress acted as a base of operations for the City Defense Forces .

Location

This castle was located on the northeastern hills of the city, overlooking the Fire River and across from the Central Cemetery .

Description

Castle Iron Guard was built atop an ancient dwarven keep, which was repurposed as a vast underground storage space. The fortress also had one of the largest stables in Ravens Bluff, including one pen that was meant to act as a backup stable for the Griffon Guard 's griffons .

Inhabitants

Castle Iron Guard garrisoned more than one third of Ravens Bluff's military, as well as a considerable number of priests and wizards . This included the High Sorcerer, commander of the Guardians of the Celestial Mysteries , and the army chaplain core's High Patriarch. The fortress also acted as the secret headquarters of a clandestine organization known only as the Brotherhood .

Beyond combatants, the fortress housed a large number of workmen, tradesmen, and livestock to keep it operational.

History

Around the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , in the early days of the Three Women War, the Ravens Bluff Army put out a call to mercenaries in the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter , telling them to report to Castle Iron Guard for a contract and assignment.

Late into the war, the Ravens Bluff Army caught win of the enemy planning an attack on Mossbridges and routed their forces there for battle. However, this was merely a ruse orchestrated by the turncoat Commander Nioga and his Company of the Bloody Hatchets , meant to draw the army away so that Myrkyssa Jelan 's forces could invade the city without interference. A group of six adventurers in the army learned of Nioga's deceit and were sent home by General Obespieri to warn General Koran Therogeon , commander of the City Defense Forces.

They rode hard through the night to reach Ravens Bluff in time. When they arrived around dawn, the City Guardsmen informed them that Nioga had rode through earlier asking where he could find Therogeon. Sources differ on whether the adventurers managed to arrive to Castle Iron Guard in time to confront Nioga, but most agree that by the time they arrived they found Therogeon murdered at his desk. Lady Caroline Skyhawk , Therogeon's second-in-command, would arrive shortly thereafter. Shocked at the gruesome scene, she demanded to be debriefed on the situation.

Quickly ascertaining the truthfulness of the adventurers' words, Skyhawk snapped to action and wasted no time in yelling out the door at office boy Tremai to "Quickly, rouse the commanders, the runners and the entire staff! We are under attack! Sound the warning bells, prepare the gates! And Tremai, get the High Sorcerer in here, in his small clothes if necessary!" Before long cries of "Attack" and "Awaken" rang throughout the fortress. She then turned to the adventurers, told them to get rest and report to her by next dawn, and began to intently study a map that had been spread across Therogeon's desk.
