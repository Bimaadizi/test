Nyu Lake was a lake in the Alpuk region of the Great Glacier .

Description

Nyu Lake was a salt water lake with an average depth of between 5​ to ​20 feet (1.5​ to ​6.1 meters).

Geography

Nyu Lake was located in eastern Alpuk just north of the Novularond .

Flora & Fauna

Ring moss grew around the lake. It was the only place other than the Lugsaas Chain where the moss grew.
