The robe of moonlight was a magical robe in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Description

The robe was a rich blue, and decorated with moon and star designs.

Powers

The power of moonbeams let the robe improve the accuracy and effectiveness of the wearer's spells, and sheltered them from incoming magical attacks.

History

The robe was used by the forces of the Cormanthor Elves during the Elven Crusade around 1374 DR , after they fought Thramont the Watchful in the Irithlium .
