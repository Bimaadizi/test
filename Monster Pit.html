The Monster Pit was an arena located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Monster Pit was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward beneath the Fishscale Smithy and the Thirsty Sailor .

History

Once owned by Elaith Craulnober , the Monster Pit was abandoned as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , though local gangs sometimes met there to settle their differences.
