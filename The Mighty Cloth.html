The Mighty Cloth was a type of enchanted monk 's garb from Port Nyanzaru in Chult .

Powers

The Mighty Cloth was enchanted to constantly bestow the bull's strength onto its wearer, and it possessed the unwavering bull magic that made the wearer impossible to push or knock off their feet. This massive strength boost also made it much harder for the wearer to be restrained. They also could perform a bull rush, fiercely and powerfully tacking their opponents down.

History

Sometime before the late 15 th century DR , The Mighty Cloth was worn by Kimigha Poji , one of the monks of the port city of Nyanzaru , who fought against the Headless Serpent, Secret Venom gang. The cloth later found its way into the hands of Talli , a Harper quartermaster, who followed a fellow Harper hero Jaheira to the Last Light Inn in the Shadow-cursed Lands in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
