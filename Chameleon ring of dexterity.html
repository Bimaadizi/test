Chameleon rings of dexterity were minor enchanted rings beneficial to rogues and thieves .

Powers

These bands of precious metal were enchanted to slightly boost the wearer's dexterity and improve their ability to hide in the shadows.

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR a chameleon ring of dexterity was among the possessions of Elytharra Dinnesmore , an enchantress and wife of Speaker Ulbrec Dinnesmore of Targos in Icewind Dale .
