The River Tath was one of the three great rivers of Chult .

Geography

The river was in the western region of the jungle and flowed from the Aldani Basin into the Trackless Sea between the Kobold Mountains and the Mistcliffs at Jahaka Bay , ending in a series of misty waterfalls. As it meandered to the west, it carved out the gorge of Ataaz Kahakla .

Inhabitants

The aldani lived in this river, as in the other large rivers of Chult.

Rumors & Legends

It was said that if one were to follow the river up past Kahakla gorge, he or she would find a ruined city guarded by a wise and giving naga , but few made it out alive.
