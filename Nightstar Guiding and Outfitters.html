Nightstar Guiding and Outfitters was a general shop found in Waymoot , Cormyr , that catered to travelers and foresters.

Activities

In addition to supplying quality trail gear such as ropes , maps , boots , and other goods, the store was a popular hangout for travelers and adventurers who enjoyed each other's company and swapped tales over brandy or hot tea .

Inhabitants

The store was owned and operated by Liriel Nightstar , who was also rumored to be a member of the Harpers .
