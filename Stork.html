Storks were a variety of wading birds .

Ecology

Storks were preyed upon by eagles .

Habitats

Storks were found in the Sumber Hills , and would nest in such places as Gladestone .

In ancient Netheril , white storks were a symbol of the floating enclave of Eileanar . They were also sacred to Mystryl , and were referred to as the "guardians of the heights" and the "wings of Mystryl" among her faithful.

Beyond Faerûn , storks nested among the trees of the Katakoro Plateau . They were also found across Zakhara , where they were commonly for sale at its bazaars, typically sold for about 7 silver pieces . Storks were especially common in Huzuz .

Across the oceans, storks also lived in Maztica , and were a feature of the pool at the Palace of Kukul at Tulom-Itzi .

Notable Storks

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Arcturia was served by a stork she turned into a stork-headed humanoid.
