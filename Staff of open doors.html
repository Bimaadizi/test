A staff of open doors was a magic staff containing spells of opening, known around the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

It was a wooden staff covered in carvings of doors, some with tiny decorative keyholes and some with iron bindings.

The staff contained the knock , open/close , password , and shatter spells.

It was worth around 42,200 gold pieces .
