History

In the late 15 th century DR , Regis , the halfling hero of Lonelywood , was known to partake in chocolate brownies.

In the late 15 th century DR, one of the Right Pashas of Little Calimshan of Baldur's Gate was known to bake brownies using the secret recipe inherited from his grandmother. These Calishite brownies were once used to raise coin for a child's birthday party in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
