Lord Braelbane was a nobleman of Cormyr , and a head of House Braelbane in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Per Arclath Delcastle , he was "a tiresome windhorn".

Description

He was notably old, "year-in and year-out".

Relationships

He was the head of House Braelbane .

History

Lord Braelbane was one of the guests of the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .
