Okuma was a, stark raving mad, human fighter in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was once a samurai , praised and honored, but was disgraced once he started to go insane. In his insanity he would go into fits of rage, going on killing sprees, attacking everyone around him.

Possessions

He fought with his enchanted naginata , while wearing a robe , a pair of bracers of defense and an amulet of the planes . He also possessed a cube of force .

Relationships

In Okumas insane mind he blamed his own brother Toraga for his disgrace and hunted him down to gain the vengeance he felt owed.
