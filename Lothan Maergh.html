Lothan Maergh was a cleric of Chauntea with the rank of Watchful that resided in the Bounty of the Goddess temple in Voonlar .

Abilities

Watchful Brother Lothan was a horticulturist that specialized in the production and storage of bulbs and seeds as well as grafting techniques.
