Jazzt Dyrr was a member of House Agrach Dyrr of Menzoberranzan in 1373 DR .

Description

Jazzt was small and scarred.

History

Jazzt was the second cousin of Matron Mother Yasraena Dyrr . During the Silence of Lolth , when the duergar and tanarukk armies marched against Menzoberranzan , he was ordered by his matron mother to escort the disguised Nimor Imphraezl to Rhazzt's Dilemma .
