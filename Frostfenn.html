The Frostfenn was a large marshland located on the island of Norland within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Frostfenn was found in central and northern Norland, wedged between the North Jotunhammer Mountains to the west and open grassland to the east. It was a dominating feature of inner Norland, covering approximately 1,000 mi² (2,600 km²) of the island. Norfen Lake sat along the northeast edge of the Frostfenn.
