Belmonder's Meats was a butcher shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The shop stood a single story tall.

Location

Belmonder's Meats was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the northeast side of the High Road where it was joined by the Way of the Dragon .

Inhabitants

The shop was owned by Morathin Belmonder in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .
