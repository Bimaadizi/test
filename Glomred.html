Glomred was an orc shaman of the Spleen Eaters tribe that lived in Big Bone Deep during the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

He wore a simple hauberk of chainmail .

Relationships

Glomred acted as a spiritual guide and advisor to his tribe's chieftain, Hadarog , acting as negotiator when the time arose.

History

After the Eye of Gruumsh was stolen by a pair of humans, in the latter half of the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Glomred and the other Spleen Eater orcs set out in pursuit of the thieves, guided by the One-Eyed God .
