Rukn ud-Din Mahmud was a caravan guard commander living in the Hordelands during the mid 14 th century DR .

Activities

Rukn was a devout believer of the Muhjari teachings, though he maintained a more practical outlook on situations. He forbade the consumption of alcohol among his soldiers, but allowed other vices he deemed necessary.

Possessions

Rukn wielded an enchanted mace , wore enchanted plate mail , and carried an elixir of health and a rod of flailing .
