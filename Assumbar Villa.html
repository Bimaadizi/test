Assumbar Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Assumbar . Later through marriage, it also became home to members of House Gulderhorn , who would go on to construct the Chapel and Chalice of the Divine Right on the property.

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward on the northeast corner of where Diamond Street and the Street of the Singing Dolphin joined.

Description

The walled compound contained four separate buildings. The larger buildings stood on the south side of the estate. Each building stood between one and three stories tall.

Rumors & Legends

In the 14 th century DR , rumors circulated that the goddess Siamorphe had manifested in the villa during the Time of Troubles and protected both the North and Sea Wards from suffering large-scale damages at the hands of Myrkul 's undead legions. Whilst initially shrugging it off, Lord Laeros Assumbar came to take pride in the notoriety that the rumor brought House Assumbar.
