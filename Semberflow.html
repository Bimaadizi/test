The Semberflow was a river in the Cormanthor forest .

Geography

The stream flowed from Lake Sember to the River Verire .

Inhabitants

The waters of the Semberflow ran clear and was home to large numbers of bass and catfish . Around 650 DR , aquatic elven warriors guarded the waters of the creek. Originating in the Storm Peaks , flocks of red sheep grazed along the river's banks.
