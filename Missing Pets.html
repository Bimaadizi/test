Missing Pets was a potion concocted by the green hag Auntie Ethel from within her disturbing laboratory beneath the Gnarled Teahouse of the Putrid Bog .

Description

The bottle of the potion appeared as if it contained the skittering legs of thousands of little creatures within.

Powers

Whomever drank from Missing Pets suddenly became covered in hundreds upon hundreds of tiny illusory spiders . While they had no corporeal form, these imaginary arachnids prevented them from fully engaging in combat or dedicating focus to a single task.

History

Only one bottle of Missing Pets was known to exist as of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
