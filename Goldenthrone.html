Goldenthrone was a palace in Port Nyanzaru that served as the meeting place for the city's merchant princes as well as its seat of government. It was the prime location for those who sought an audience with the merchant princes.

Location

The palace was located in the city's Merchants' Ward atop Throne Hill .

Structure

The palace was one of the most luxurious buildings in all of Port Nyanzaru. It was adorned with decorative stonework.

Defense

The merchant princes were protected by eight members of the Chultan honor guard.
