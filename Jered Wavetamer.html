Jered Wavetamer manned the Finger of the Gods lighthouse in Melvaunt around 1367 DR .

Description

Jered may have been old, but he was a well-respected former sea captain.

Activities

Jered enjoyed company of any kind day or night. He was an excellent source of good stories and rumors.
