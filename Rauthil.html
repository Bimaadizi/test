Geography

Rauthil was located along the southern bank of the River Rauthenflow due north of Maerlar on the Great South Road in north Mulhorand.

Inhabitants

The precept of Rauthil in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , was a priest of Horus-Re named Ethnestus .

Activities

Rauthil was an important location in that it was the only place along the River Rauthenflow where a bridge was constructed allowing for easy crossing.
