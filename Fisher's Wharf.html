Fisher's Wharf was a pier used by fishing vessels and an occasional live-in boat tied to the docks, located in the city of Baldur's Gate .

Location

Fisher's Wharf stood on the coastal border of the Eastway section of the Lower City in Baldur's Gate. Sailor's Crossing was located just a street away to the south, and Jopalin's tea and kaeth cafe was built on the outer side of the block that faced Fisher's Wharf.

First thing in the morning, all fishing vessels departed the pier, leaving behind docked houseboats of those who lived on the water.
