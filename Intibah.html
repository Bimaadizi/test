Intibah was a small town found in the western reaches of the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara .

Geography

Intibah was located near the source of Al-Malja River upstream approximately 50 miles (80 kilometers) from Ajayib in the shadows of Al-Suqut Mountains . Though somewhat isolated from other civilization, an occupied qal'at nearby provided military protection.
