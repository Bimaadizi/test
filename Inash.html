Inash was an assassin in Mulmaster in 1373 DR .

Activities

Inash and his two associates were the Golden Skulls , a small-but-very-efficent assassins guild in Mulmaster.

History

In 1373 DR, Inash was hired by the Red Wizard of Thay Ahryn Firefinger to kill some escaped slaves.
