Colin Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the first half of the 6 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the son of his predecessor, High King Carrig III , and of High Queen Abigail . His successor was his son, Gordon Cymrych .

History

Colin's father was assassinated in the Year of the Flame Tongue, 500 DR , when Colin was only eight years old. His mother thus appointed Edmund Kincaid of Snowdown to rule as regent in his stead. Colin would eventually assume the throne of the High King in the Year of the Rotting Pox, 507 DR .

Colin became famous for clearing werewolves from Torriage Wood in Callidyrr . However, he himself had contracted lycanthropy by the Year of the Unstoppable Ogre, 549 DR , and so he was slain by his son, Gordon, who assumed the throne that same year.
