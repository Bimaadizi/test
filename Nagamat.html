Nagamat was a dragon general of Tiamat during the Time of Dragons .

History

In −30,000 DR , Nagamat was in charge of the siege of the aearee kingdom of Shara , destroying its hatcheries and claiming the realm as his own.

He was soon drawn into religious conflicts between the factions of the draconic gods, and in −29,500 DR , he was killed at the hands of followers of Xymor . His death sparked the Dragonfall War , a conflict between the followers of Bahamut and Tiamat, which persisted for thousands of years.
