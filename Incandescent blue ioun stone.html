An incandescent blue ioun stone was a type of spherical ioun stone .

Powers

These blue ioun stones increased the wisdom of the wearer.

History

In 1311 DR , a specific ioun stone of this variety lost to its owner, the illithid called Oinchalk'olp . The mind flayer ordered a group of adventurers to fetch the githzerai -crafted stone.
