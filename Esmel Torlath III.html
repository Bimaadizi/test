King Esmel Torlath III , originally named Dharik Torlath , was an early ruler of Amn of the Torlath dynasty in the 7 th century DR .

Relationships

Esmel was the second son of King Esmel Torlath II , and Marcos Vymmar was his maternal grandfather. Corl II was his only son.

History

Dharik's mother and elder brother disappeared in the Year of Fire and Frost, 600 DR when the realm of Minsorran was destroyed. His father died of grief the following year, putting him on the throne at age 16 with the regnal name Esmel III. He was guided through a long regency by his mother's father, Marcos Vymmar, which ended in the Year of the Shattered Scepter, 614 DR . From then on, Esmel reigned until his death in the Year of Peaceful Seas, 656 DR .
