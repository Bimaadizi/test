Helve Urtrace ( pronounced : /ˈ h ɛ l v ɛ ˈ ɜːr t r eɪ s / HEL-ve UR-tray-ss ) was a City Watch officer in Waterdeep in 1372 DR .

Personality

Helve was a old, bald, and quiet man.

Activities

Helve's duty as Armsmaster was to manage all weapons used by City Watch officers. If a weapon disappeared, he investigated. He was an expert in weapons and street fighting. Usually, Helve led the second wave of reinforcements if there was danger, usually accompanied by his daughter Lassree Urtrace , a watch-wizard .
