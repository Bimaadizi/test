Luko was a young man who worked for the Taverners, Innkeepers, and Provisioners guild of Zazesspur in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Luko had dirty blond hair that was curly. He had skinny arms.

Luko wore a dirty apron over his other clothes. Around his bicep, he wore a green and brown rag, as a symbol of his membership with the Taverners, Innkeepers, and Provisioners.

Activities

Luko delivered butter in pails to various inns and distributed it with butter paddles.

History

In the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR , Luko was on his way to make a delivery to the buttery of Bustamante's Excellent Hostelry , when he claimed that the girl Scab somehow soured his butter with her witching power. He was part of a small mob of workers who had dragged out Scab from an alley, at which point Zaranda Star intervened.
