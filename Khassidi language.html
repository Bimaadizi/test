Khassidi was a language in the Imaskari languages group, spoken in the western Hordelands .

Description

Khassidi, like other languages in the same region, had round and broad vowel sounds with rolled R sounds.

Speakers

This tongue was spoken by the nomadic Khassidi tribe.

Script

Khassidi did not have a written form.

History

It was considered to be one of the Northern Imaskari languages and was strongly influenced by the empire of Raumathar .
