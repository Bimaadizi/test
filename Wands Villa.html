Wands Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Wands .

Location

The Wands Villa was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep , on the corner of Magecourt and Shando Street . It stood down the road from the Hothemer Villa .

Structure

Like many Waterdhavian villas, this was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden, with the entrance to the estate being a massive oaken gate. The building complex consisted of two high-quality buildings, one that was five stories tall and another that stood a three stories tall, one being the mansion proper and the other a guesthouse. Notably, the mansion was adorned with twin towers over a large central chamber.

On the exterior walls of the estate's buildings were hung purple banners bearing the Wands family's insignia.

Interiors

The interior of the Wands mansion featured a long marble entry hall, leading to a sweeping staircase. The second floor featured a gallery. Inside the cellars of the estate, there stood a vault of artifacts that had permanent web spells draped across their ceiling.

Inhabitants

In addition to the Wands family, the house was inhabited by iron golem servitors and a watchspider that watched over their artifact vault. If ever there was more than two intruders, the watchspider was trained to drink from a basin above the webs that held a unique variant of potion of heroism .

History

In the late 14 th century DR , the Wands family put a great many magical wards in place over the villa to protect the household's members from increasing attacks by creatures from the Far Realm .
