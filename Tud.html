Tud was a goblin warlock who became a cultist of the Absolute in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

She believed herself to be on the verge of being made a True Soul by her commander Dror Ragzlin .

History

After Dror Ragzlin's raiders destroyed Waukeen's Rest in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Tud and her fellow goblinoid cultists celebrated at their reclaimed temple fortress .
