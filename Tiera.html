Tiera was a human ranger and bard ; a hero of the Finnish pantheon ; and the proxy of the goddess Mielikki , the Mistress of the Forest in the 14 th century DR .

Relations

Tiera was the shield man of Lemminkainen , who was a fellow proxy of Mielikki, and always tried to aid him as best he could in his adventures.

Possessions

Tiera wielded a +3 spear that glowed whenever a falsehood was told nearby.

Home

He served Mielikki from the Grove of the Unicorns , on the layer of Krigala in the plane of the Beastlands .
