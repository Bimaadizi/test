Vadalma Tlabbar was the drow matron mother of House Faen Tlabbar , the fourth house of Menzoberranzan , in the 14 th and 15 th centuries DR .

Personality

She was fanatically devoted to Lolth .

History

Vadalma ascended to the position of matron mother in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . In that year, Nimor Imphraezl of the Jaezred Chaulssin killed both matron mother Ghenni Tlabbar and her favorite daughter, Sil'zet Tlabbar . Vadalma was the fifth daughter of Ghenni'tiroth Tlabbar and thus ascended to matron mother position. She was still in charge as of 1484 DR .
