Terrinton was a village in the Veilstone Peaks .

Description

Owing to its comparatively fertile farmlands, which yielded reliable crops of apples , grapes , and other fruits, Terrinton was the most affluent village in the region, boasting the most diverse selection of goods available for sale in the 1300s DR .
