Kura was a painter who lived in the city of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Kura was married to the Ekene-Afa , one of the merchant princes of Port Nyanzaru . The couple had two twin sons named Soshen and Tiryk , both of whom were named after Chultan rivers.
