Esalor Harumaar was the field agent of the Moonstars for Cormanthyr in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .

History

Esalor was a half-elf ranger of wild elf descent from Semberholme . In 1370 DR , Esalor was chosen to be field agent of the Moonstars for Cormanthyr.
