Queltar Thaeloon was the presumed Archmage Arcane of the Arcane Brotherhood in Luskan in the late 1360s. He might have been the one known as the Wizard of Green Fire , a top lieutenant of another Archmage Arcane, Arklem Greeth .

History

By 1366 DR and through to 1370 DR , Queltar Thaeloon was thought to hold the title of Archmage Arcane of the Hosttower. He resided on the upper levels of the central spire of the Hosttower of the Arcane in Luskan .

Eventually, however, two senior mages named Eldeluc and Valkebar assassinated the Wizard of Green Fire, and on to ambush the Archmage Arcane Arklem Greeth on Eleint 14 in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR .
