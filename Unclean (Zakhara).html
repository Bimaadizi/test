The Unclean was a faction within the Everlasting in 1367 DR .

Base of Operations

The Unclean were based in the fortress of Sarahin located deep within the Haunted Lands .

Activities

The sha'irs of the Unclean provided the Shadow Caliph with a unique source of power and information given their relationship with genies .

Members

The Unclean were led by a sha'ir named Akim al-Kalaas . The Unclean was the smallest faction of the Everlasting with only 15 members as of 1367 DR.
