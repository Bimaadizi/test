Marek was a human thief and a high-ranking member of the thieves' guild of Zhentil Keep in the mid–14 th century DR .

Relationships

Marek was a mentor to the young thief Cyric . However, Cyric later escaped from Zhentil Keep and tried to live a more honest life.

History

During the Time of Troubles in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , Marek traveled to Arabel and then Shadowdale to persuade Cyric to rejoin the guild and abandon the people of the Dalelands to Bane . Cyric refused firmly and to fully embrace his freedom he killed Marek.
