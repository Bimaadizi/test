Lethchauntos the Black was a powerful and evil mage active in the Sword Coast as of the 10 th century DR .

History

At some point prior to the Year of the Sky Riders, 936 DR , Lethchauntos travelled to live among the orcs of Uruth Ukrypt , led by the shaman Wund . While there, he crafted the Ettin Axe of Uruth as a gift to the orcish realm's first king, Uruth , and at some point he parlayed with the sage Orfidel . He was believed to be one of the first known owners of The Nathlum , a tome of poisons and evil magic , although he lost possession of the book during the Orcfastings War of 936 DR.
