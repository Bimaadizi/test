Nool Street was a large road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

Nool Street was located in west Zhentil Keep within the Common Quarter . It originated in the southwest corner of Zhentil Keep at Merchant Street and passed north and northwest through the city before terminating at a large intersection in front of the Trail Gate with Fortress Street , Wyvern Road , Cobble Road , and Border Street . Smaller roads including Winter Walk , Pride Street , Plague Street , and Fish Street branched from Nool Street along its path.

Notable Locations

A Zhentilar guardpost was located on Nool Street near the Trail Gate and a Zhentilar barracks was located at the southern end of the street.
