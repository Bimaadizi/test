The Shield of the Maimed God was a divinely enchanted tower shield that belonged to Charissa Maernos in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

The Shield of the Maimed God was a sturdy and well-balanced heavy shield. Its edges were engraved with Tyr's holy runes. These mysterious symbols radiated divine protection.

Powers

This tower shield possessed a +1 enchantment to its already significant defensive properties.

History

In 1374 DR , Charissa Maernos wielded the Shield of the Maimed God during her pursuit of vengeance agains the Night Masks of Westgate who were responsible for her family's downfall.
