Yazka was a kobold in northwest Faerûn .

Yazka and Kipper were the lieutenants of Urko during their attack on Hilltop in 1372 DR , on a mission from Tymofarrar .

Yazka preferred peaceful solutions to battle, and was therefore taunted by Kipper as a coward and "girl kobold."
