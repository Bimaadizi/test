Stint was a strong gnomish pale ale from the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast .

Description

Stint was a variety of aggressively smelling ale made with mushrooms and spices . It was sometimes served with two highsunfeasts, Brick or Rosto .

History

Stint was a staple of a gnomish out-of-the way café in Ravens Bluff called Le Po Teete Café in the late 14 th century DR . It served only gnomish pale ales, with Greentart and Rockgrit being the only alternatives.
