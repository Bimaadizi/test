Carrig Cymrych I was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid-to-late 3 rd century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the second son of the dynasty’s legendary namesake, Cymrych Hugh . He was the brother of both of his immediate predecessors: High King Warren I followed by High Queen Tamara . Carrig I's successor was his first son, Gwylloch Cymrych , while his second son, Gorham , would become High King after Gwylloch.

History

Carrig I assumed the throne of the High King in Callidyrr during the Year of the Worn Pages, 263 DR , after the poisoning of his sister, High Queen Tamara. At the time, Cedric Riker ruled in the family's ancestral seat of Corwell as its regent, and would do so until the ninth year of his reign .

Carrig I ruled for twenty-four years before dying in battle against Northmen invaders from Tuern and Gundarlun in the Year of the Warped Narthex, 287 DR .
