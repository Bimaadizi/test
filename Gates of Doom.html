The Gates of Doom was the name given to two pillars of rock in the Dalelands that had a path between them. They marked the entrance to Oversember Vale in the Thunder Peaks .

Inhabitants

When the Cult of the Dragon occupied the area, they guarded the Gates of Doom. Any who passed through the gates without leave was attacked without question or hesitation.
