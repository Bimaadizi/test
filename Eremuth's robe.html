Eremuth's robe was the enchanted robe that belonged to Eremuth Silverwind , the bastard of the Hawkwinter noble family of Waterdeep , and later, the malaugrym agent of the Unseen who assumed Silverwind's identity.

Description

Eremuth's robe was masterfully crafted clothing of the finest quality.

Powers

This robe possessed a minor enchantment that enhanced the wearer's ability to dodge out of the harm's way as well as granted them small resistance to fire, mundane and magical.

History

Eremuth's robe was created to be at the hight of Waterdhavian fashion circa 1323 DR . Strangely outdated design, however, did not bother Eremuth Silverwind when he, secretly a malaugrym, joined the Hero of Daggerford 's adventuring party. The hero wondered how Eremuth hasn't been killed for audacity to wear this outfit in the Moonsea region.
