The Wings of Diabolicus was a deadly unique magic warhammer that could kill a living creature with one blow.

Description

The Wings of Diabolicus was a heavy two-handed warhammer decorated with bat -like wings.

Powers

Apart from possessing a +2 enchantment, the Wings of Diabolicus could cast the slay living spell of considerable power. This heavy two-handed maul could be used by clerics .

History

In 1369 DR , this grim weapon could be found in the Halls of Stone of the Dwarven Dungeons of Myth Drannor in one of the chests far, in the most secluded are of the ruins. Upon opening the chest, its guardians became apparent, a master shadow , leading four weaker shadows.
