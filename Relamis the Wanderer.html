Relamis the Wanderer was a druid in Cormyr during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Relamis had tanned skin, brown eyes, and tied-back blond hair. He wore a brown robe and carried a sickle .

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar circa 1485 DR , Relamis arrived in the village with his wolf companion and offered aid in training prospective druids.
