Lannort was a hamlet in the North founded in the 15 th century DR .

Geography

Lannort lay a short distance downstream of Loudwater on the Delimbiyr River , in the upper Delimbiyr Vale .

Trade

Lannort was home to the noxious Falthryn and Daughters Tannery , and Galdegut's Boneyard , operated by Morluth Galdegut . Both businesses were linked by merchants' map to Samril's Fine Blades in Secomber , to whom they supplied leather and bone .
