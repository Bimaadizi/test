Garum was a type of fish sauce from Malatra .

Description

The sauce was made from a base of fermented fish and other flavors. It was extremely salty and widely used in a variety of dishes throughout the jungles of Malatra.

Garum was available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .
