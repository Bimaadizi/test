Akonhorus II was a the fifteenth pharaoh of Mulhorand during the mid–14 th century DR .

Activities

Akonhorus II assumed the title of pharaoh in the Year of the Watching Cold, 1320 DR , reigning until his assassination by the Cult of Set in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR . He was succeeded by the boy-king Horustep III .
