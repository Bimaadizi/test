Drizzt's mithral chainmail was the suit of enchanted armor belonging to Drizzt Do'Urden in the 14 th century DR .

Description

The armor was fashioned from mithral and had a blue tint.

Powers

The armor was a +4 mithral chainmail . It was extremely lightweight without compromising its protection or hindering the wearer's skills and spellcasting .

History

The armor was crafted for Drizzt by Buster Bracer while living in Icewind Dale or by his companion Bruenor Battlehammer .
