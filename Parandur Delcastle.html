Parandur Delcastle was a head of House Delcastle in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Parandur Delcastle drank himself into a stupor daily as of 1479 DR .

Relationships

By 1479 DR , Parandur had grown distant from his wife Marantine Delcastle and son Arclath Delcastle , having been driven to drink.

History

Parandur's house, the Delcastle nobility, was represented by Mirt in the royal banquet of 1479 DR .
