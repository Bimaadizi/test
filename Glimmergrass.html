TypeGrass

Glimmergrass was a type of grass with bioluminescent properties. It could be used to make a luminous unguent with a green glow.

Dovo of Ghars , Cormyr , painted himself and his armor with this unguent in order to impersonate the ghost of Fastred and scare townsfolk in 1367 DR .
