Krote Word-Maker was a gnoll cleric of the Burnt Fur tribe .

Description

Compared to other gnolls, Krote was very thin, with a sunken snout and eyes. He had mustard-colored skin and tattered ears.

Possessions

Krote usually wore a dirty scarf around his head and a ring of teleportation . The latter was gifted to him from Martine and used to be owned by Jazrac . He fought with spear and longbow .

Relationships

Krote became a lifelong friend of the Harper Martine, and he accompanied her on her travels.

History

Krote was a shaman of the god Gorellik in the Burnt Fur gnoll tribe living in the Samek Valley . He was banished from the tribe in the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR the ice devil Vreesar for refusing to submit to its rule.

He joined Martine and, with help of the hermit Vilheim and the gnome outcast Jouka , helped her fight against Vreesar. Krote was decisive in the killing of the devil. Afterward, Krote decided to travel with Martine.
