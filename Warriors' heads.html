Description

Warriors' heads was a dish served in a bowl in which baked garlic heads were drenched in olive oil . Around it was a wall of twelve types of pickles . Some were sweet and hot, others were salty , and some were mixed with pitted dates . Warriors' heads were to be picked and tasted a lot and often.

History

By the late 14 th century DR , travelers from outside of Amn were advised to learn the name of the dish to avoid any confusion. One of the places that served warriors' heads was The Battlescarred Bard inn on the Trade Way in Amn.
