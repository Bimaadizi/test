Proctiv was a Netherese arcanist , prolific spell inventor, and master of variation .

Description

Proctiv was an extraordinary mage who developed the second most powerful spell in the Empire of Netheril , Proctiv's breach crystal sphere (which was tied with Mavin's worldweave in terms of power).

History

Proctiv developed eight spells during his life. Spectacularly, in 1746 NY (−2113 DR) , he developed his first spell: the epic move mountain , which became crucial for creating the base of a flying enclave . After this, he invented stone shape in 1749 NY (−2110 DR) , move earth in 1764 NY (−2095 DR) , glassteel in 1769 NY (−2090 DR) , transmute rock to mud in 1774 NY (−2085 DR) , transmute water to dust in 1796 NY (−2063 DR) , and dig in 1799 NY (−2060 DR) . In 1808 NY (−2051 DR) , Proctiv had an enormous breakthrough and developed the epic Proctiv's breach crystal sphere (and its reverse version).
