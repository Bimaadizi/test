Whitebone armor was a variety of enchanted heavy armor created on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Whitebone armor was a set of plate mail , bone-white in color, that possessed a +2 defensive enchantment.

A set of whitebone armor could be created from an enchanted suit of armor and a skull of a gargoyle .

History

Whitebone armor was forged by Master Blacksmith Marrok of Shining Knight Arms and Armor shop of Neverwinter to help the heroes who sought the cure from the dreaded Wailing Death of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
