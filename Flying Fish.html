Location

The Flying Fish was located along the bend in Water Street in southern Daggerford.

Services

All types of seafood were available for sale within the Flying Fish. In addition, it was the locale where merchants would bring their fish for inspection and packaging, before it was sent along the Trade Way to settlements throughout the North .
