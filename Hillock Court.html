Hillock Court was a courtyard that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Hillock Court was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward at the base of Castle Waterdeep . The court sat south of Waterdeep Way and north and west of Watchmen's Way . Niles Way connected with the northern part of the courtyard.
