The Oakengrove Abbey was a human temple stronghold dedicated to the worship of the deity Silvanus .

History

It was founded in 591 DR . It stood about 70 miles (110 kilometers) west of the Standing Stone in Cormanthor .

Inhabitants

The abbey was lead by Oakfather Gannon Durei in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR . He supervised eight other priests .
