Athis Al-Din was the fiance of Sithal Al-Husn . After Habrauk Al-Nirin kidnapped the princess, he led his troops in numerous attacks against the Al-Alisk clan; he continued to lead the charge after his death.

History

The Sultan of Pazar married Sithal Al-Husn to Athis Al-Din, but the princess was stolen on her wedding night by Habrauk Al-Nirin . Athis Al-Din's family entered into a blood feud with the Al-Alisk clan, to which Habrauk belonged. Even after the extinction of all their bloodlines, Athis Al-Din fought at the head of the Lancers of Death every midnight.
