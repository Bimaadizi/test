Phaerl Hawksong was a senior priestess of Sehanine Moonbow at the Tower of the Moon's Magic in Evereska and a member of the Moonstars in the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

She was the half-sister of Khelben Arunsun by the same father, Arun Maerdrym .

Personality

She shared Khelben's dream of bringing elves and humans together. She was happy to join the Moonstars, seeing it as an opportunity to repay her debt to him.

History

Some time in the mid-to-late 9 th century DR, Phaerl almost lost her life in Anauroch but Khelben risked his own life to save her. They found they had similar ideals about being together elves and humans.

In the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Khelben asked her to join his Moonstars and Phaerl gladly accepted in order to repay her debt to him.

She was among the many powerful mages Arunsun brought together in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , to cleanse part of the High Moor and restore Faer'tel'miir , a lost city of Miyeritar .
