Halavin Symryvvin was a drow of Menzoberranzan and a member of House Symryvvin in the late 14 th century DR . She was a novice priestess of Lolth at Arach-Tinilith .

Description

She was known for being fond of wearing jewelry.

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , she joined Drisinil Barrison Del'Armgo 's cabal against headmistess Quenthel Baenre and was killed with all the other conspirators.
