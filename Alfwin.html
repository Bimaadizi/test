Alfwin was a Shadovar sorcerer serving under the Netherese emissary Herzgo Alegni in the mid–14 th century DR .

History

He was part of Herzgo's retinue when, in the Year of the Reborn Hero, 1463 DR , the Shadovar ambushed Dahlia Sin'felle and Drizzt Do'Urden in Gauntlgrym .
