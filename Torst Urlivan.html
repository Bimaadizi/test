Torst Urlivan was a fence of stolen goods inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Torst operated out of an inn he owned called the Gentle Rest located in Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the High Road . Specifically, he sold stolen horses and harnesses out of the Gentle Rest Stables found several buildings from the inn.
