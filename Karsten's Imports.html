Karsten's Imports was a business that operated in the city of Luskan in the late 15 th century DR .

Location

Karsten's Imports was located in the Market District of Luskan, right next to the northern gate.

Structure

Karsten's Imports was a sparsely decorated single room with a table and two chairs.

History

Around the Year of the Rune Lords Triumphant, 1487 DR , the Champion of the Burning Dawn encountered Ridrien Karsten here.
