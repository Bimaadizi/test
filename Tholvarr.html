Tholvarr , meaning "our place" in an ancient dialect of Jotun , was a region of the Spine of the World mountains that was home to several communities of frost giants .

Inhabitants

Among the frost giants that dwelled within the mountain peaks and valleys that encompassed Tholvarr were those from the Arthlarr , Borumn , Joront , Klevvyn , Turtorst and Yargray tribes. While they lived amongst one another, these groups regularly came into quarrels and clashes.
