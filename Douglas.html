Douglas was an apprentice to Booter , Shadow Thieves ' "official torturer" in Athkatla in the late 14 th century DR .

History

By the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Douglas was still a human youth who began his apprenticeship under Booter in the guild's torture chambers of Aran Linvail 's hideout located in the Wave District 's docks. The child was tasked with cleaning up expired "guests" and dismembering eager corpses for his master. Despite still working for Booter, Douglas began having reservations about his choice of future vocation.
