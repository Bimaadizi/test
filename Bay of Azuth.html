The Bay of Azuth was a bay located in southeast Halruaa along the Great Sea .

Description

The Bay of Azuth offered a natural barrier to towns a cities located in southeast Halruaa, protecting them from pirates and harsh weather rolling in from the Great Sea.

The bay was more enclosed than the Bay of Taertal , allowing for calm waters that could be easily fished by locals.
