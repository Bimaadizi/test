Mina Neyman was an acolyte of Kelemvor who lived in Neverwinter during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The priest had blond hair and a scar on the right side of her face.

Possessions

When protecting the Neverdeath Graveyard , Mina carried a longsword and dressed in chainmail .

Relationships

She served in a contingent under Volahk with the mission of clearing the Neverdeath Graveyard of undead .

History

As of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Acolyte Neyman was stationed at Doomguide's Watch in Neverdeath. After a contingent of doomguides went missing in the Rift , Neyman and Volahk worked with an adventurer to uncover their whereabouts.

Some time later she accompanied that adventurer on a mission to cleanse the graveyard surrounding the Tree of Souls , an area that had been corrupted by Thayan Red Wizards .
