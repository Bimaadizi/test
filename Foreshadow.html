Lord Foreshadow was a foppish noble from the City of Splendors, Waterdeep , active in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Lord Foreshadow was traveling along the Trade Way , north of the town of Nashkel . When coming across adventurers, Foreshadow complemented their garb that added to the local color, and planned on imitating the adventurers' costumes for Lord Ribald 's masquerade in the city of Neverwinter the following year. Lord Foreshadow visited Neverwinter once before and claimed to have been the talk of the town. He also bragged about visiting Athkatla , the City of Coin, several times in the past.
