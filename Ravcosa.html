Ravcosa was a fang dragon that laired in Myth Drannor in Cormanthor during the late 14 th century DR .

History

Ravcosa, child of Goranathanil , rested at the old Throne of Thought in the ruins. It may have fallen afoul of time traveling Elves of Cormanthor who meddled in the long-past life of Earynspieier Ongluth there.
