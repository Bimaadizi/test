Lann Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles at the turn of the 5 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty , and was the first son of his predecessor, High King Conall . His successor was his own first son, Carrig Cymrych II .

History

Lann assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Wooded Altar, 389 DR , after the death of his father.

He ruled for thirty-one years until he drowned when his ship sank off the coast of Snowdown in the Year of the Last Breath, 420 DR .
