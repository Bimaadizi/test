Cadella Thylight was a priestess of Mystra in the city of Waterdeep in the 14 th century DR .

History

At some point Volothamp Geddarm interviewed Cadella. During this interview they discussed triptych magic , as Volo had previously heard many accounts of the magic of Klauth from various adventurers and mages .
