The Gundar Forest was found in Estagund in the Shining Lands .

Geography

The forest lay in the southeast corner of Estagund parallel with the shore of the Great Sea . The River Gundar blocked the forest's encroachment in the east and an unnamed road hemmed the forest to the north and west.
