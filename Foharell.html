Foharell was a sea captain and a worshiper of Umberlee who prayed at the Water Queen's House in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Foharell was a dwarvish man with a charcoal gray skin tone, extremely uncommon among gold dwarves like he. The captain had cold gray eyes, bushy white beard and kept his head clean-shaven. Foharell wore a simple leather armor .

History

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Captain Foharell frequented the Water Queen's House, uttering prayers to her goddess. He was at the temple shortly after one of its priestesses , Holli Dylford , drowned whilst swimming in the Gray Harbor . Foharell thought it to be a good luck to appease the goddess after a drowning.
