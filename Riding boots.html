Riding boots were a variety of boots designed to be worn while riding a mount . They were commonly seen in both the lands of Faerûn and Zakhara .

Description

A riding boot was typically constructed from stiffened leather. Some were designed with a break away strap system, allowing one to sheathe a short sword against their calf, while others had a sheath within their cuffs for concealing daggers or stilettos .

Availability

History

In the 14 th century DR , leather riding boots that reached up to the thighs were fashionable footwear among men in Waterdeep .
