The Palace of Poison Tears was the divine realm of Talona .

Location

In the Great Wheel cosmology , the Palace of Poison Tears was located in Carceri in the layer of Cathrys .

In the World Tree cosmology , the place was located in the Barrens of Doom and Despair .

Description

The palace was a pyramid-shaped thing that looked like the symbol of Talona. It was in the middle of a hot and humid marshland that was full of parasites, mosquitoes, biting midge swarms, infectious agents, and so on. This was unique, as it was impossible for life of any form to live on the Barrens of Doom and Despair.

Inhabitants

Talona ruled the realm. She was served by rats , dire rats , otyughs , quasits , and monstrous spiders .
