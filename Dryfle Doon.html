Dryfle Doon was a gold dwarf commoner, displaced by The Absolute , and as a refugee, sought shelter in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Dryfle was a gold dwarf with a rosy complexion, light brown hair, and brown eyes. She wore a simple leather armor and a leather circlet.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate , displacing countless refugees in the process along the way. Dryfle Doon and brothers Giftu and Hasmet Hudd fled from the Absolutists for over a tenday , and eventually found their way to the city walls and were allowed to enter the Lower City , unlike many other refugees who were refused entrance due to lack of coin or paperwork. Dryfle was impressed with a number of inns in the Lower City, happy to have options and coin to afford lodgings.
