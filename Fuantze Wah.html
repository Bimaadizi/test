Fuantze Wah was a yakuza participant at the Komite in Shou Lung .

Relationships

Fuantze Wah was a member of a small yakuza gang.

History

In 1358 DR , he was initiated into a ninja clan. From then on, he started to criticize his yakuza elders. However, they were also secretly the leaders of the ninja clan. The elders ordered Fuantze to participate at the Komite. They knew that participation would teach a lesson of humility to Fuantze.
