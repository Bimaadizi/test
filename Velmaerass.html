Velmaerass was a Red Wizard of Thay in the late 14 th century DR .

History

When captured and questioned by Elminster in the wake of the raid of the War Wizards on the meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy in Marsember , Cormyr , during the early spring of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Thauvas Zlorn revealed that he had been praised by Velmaerass for his as-yet-unexecuted plan to use the Company of Crazed Venturers to retrieve magic for him. Elminster jested that Velmaerass might even reward Thauvas with "a tharch or two", if he lived.
