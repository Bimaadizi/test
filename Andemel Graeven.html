Andemel Graeven was a Cormyrean noble in the mid-14 th century DR .

Personality

He was quite given to joking about his fellow nobles.

Possessions

His home had a garden, and he owned at least one shop.

Relationships

He was a friend of Sabrast Windriver . He had not stopped shuddering for a tenday after the first time he met Elminster Aumar , at some point prior to 1356 DR . As of that year, he'd recently bought a Godsfrown Shield .

History

During a revel in 1356 DR , Andemel talked to Sabrast Windriver and Raurild Sarpath about his shop. He also considered propositioning a lady in a particularly revealing dress; Sabrast warned him the lady was actually Elminster. He also became a witness to Immult Murauvyn trying to collect back taxes on Sabrast Windriver.
