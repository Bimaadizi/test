Scottie Torilson was the leader of the Sons of Light , a knighthood devoted to Torm in the early 8 th century DR . He fought with them during the latter days of the Weeping War . He died in the Year of the Firedrake, 713 DR , while defending a strategically unimportant elven village, because his duty demanded it. He was the possessor of the one true enchanted longsword Daystar .
