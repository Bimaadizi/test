The Everlight Theatre was a theater in Elturel .

Services

The playhouse put on performances from bardic troupes such as the Sharptongue Travelling Troupe . The Everlight Theatre hosted Cagon Fegg 's great play, The Folly and the Fall , which was shown for one season only.

Rumors & Legends

The theatre was said to have erupted into chaos after The Folly and the Fall was performed.
