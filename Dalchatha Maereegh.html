Dalchatha Maereegh was a high priest of Moander , the Rotting God, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

She attained this title upon the death of the previous holder, Mogion , in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Dalchatha controlled a vast network of spies and special task forces. She kept Moander fed with an endless supply of victims. In exchange, Moander blessed her with perpetual youth.
