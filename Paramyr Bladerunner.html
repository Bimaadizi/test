Paramyr Bladerunner was a male human fighter and an agent of Zhentil Keep based in the town of Thunderstone in Cormyr around 1369 DR .

Description

In his early 30s, he was handsome with a square jaw.

Personality

Paramyr was arrogant but disciplined and utterly dedicated to the service of Zhentil Keep.

Activities

Paramyr led a contingent of six Zhentish soldiers quartered in Thunderstone at the time, though he tried to keep his secret mission a secret. They were well-equipped for all manner of fighting.

Possessions

Paramyr wielded a +2 longsword and wore +2 chainmail . He also possessed a ring of spell turning and an ivory goat figurine , and rode a light warhorse .
