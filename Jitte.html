A jitte ( pronounced : / dʒ ɪ t t ɛ / jit-teh ) was a weapon unique to the continent of Kara-Tur .

Description

The jitte was a defensive weapon and lacked a sharp edge. It was a tapered iron bar between 1​ to ​1.5 feet (30​ to ​46 centimeters) in length that incorporated a short hook near its handle. The jitte's main purpose revolved around blocking incoming attacks and seizing weapons. Additionally, it could be utilized for delivering impactful strikes.
