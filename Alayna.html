Alayna was a hakima and the only daughter of Al'ia bint Hazir and Sultan Yusef al-Wadi of Gana in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara .

Activities

Alayna was uncomfortable with her parental situation and worried about what the future would bring. She devoted her time to a quiet holy life aiding others.

Description

She had a mole under her right eye in the shape of a butterfly.

History

As with all of her siblings, Alayna's parentage was kept secret from the population of Gana. She was raised in a monastery high in Al-Yabki Mountains .
