Geography

Originating in an underground spring within the Sanrach Mountains on the border with Samarach , the River Salban flowed briefly eastward before reaching the Thingulph and the Great Sea . The capital of Thindar was located along its banks where it met the Thingulph.

Description

The river emerged from the mountains on a cliff face high above the grasslands below, plunging downward in a massive waterfall known as Salban Falls . Once past the waterfall, the river broadened and the flow slowed down considerably.

Rumors & Legends

A side passage from the river was rumored to provide access to the Mines of Selgol and the Firelands beneath the jungles of Chult . Many brave explorers and adventurers sought this secret trail but none returned to speak of what they discovered.
