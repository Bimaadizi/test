Haroun ibn Abbak served as Grand Admiral of Huzuz 's navy in 1367 DR .

Personality

Haroun was flamboyant and extremely well-liked by his crews.

Activities

Grand Admiral Haroun commanded 40 vessels that patrolled the Golden Gulf and Suq Bay .
