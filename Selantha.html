Selantha was a shopkeeper in Skullport in 1370 DR .

History

Selantha managed Emerald Fires , a stone-and-wood shop alone from 1364 DR , when her husband disappeared on a trading mission. She specialized in intricate decorative filigree work on armor. In 1368 DR , she befriend the mercenary Sergeant Belan Kargis , who courted her. Selantha, however, wanted to know the true fate of her husband before marrying another man.
