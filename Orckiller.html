Orckiller was an enchanted jeweled dwarven dagger from the ruined city of Myth Drannor .

Powers

The dagger's blade was enchanted to be especially deadly to orcs and orogs , doubling all damage done to these creatures.

History

Orckiller was locked away in a chest along one of the smaller subterranean structures on the Avenue of Smiths of the Dwarven Dungeons sometime before the complex was sealed by the fleeing survivors of the Weeping War . The dagger was still hidden on the Avenue of Smiths as of the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
