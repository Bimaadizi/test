Krenadir (also seen as Krenalir ) was a coastal city in the halfling realm of Luiren .

Geography

Krenadir was located on the coast of Luirenstrand in the Great Sea . It was located some miles east of Mortick Swamp . A road around the swamp connected the city to Beluir and the rest of Luiren.

History

Krenadir was founded in 218 DR . In 1105 DR , the city received widespread damage from a storm over the Great Sea.

Armed Forces

During the height of the harvest season 100 warders protected the city and surrounding land. Many of these Warder's were laid-off during the winter season and spent their time patrolling the Long Forest for Fasruil and Crimel .
