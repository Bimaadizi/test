Auziiyra Twelvestars was a human mage of Tethyr .

Alongside Brondeth of the Broken Staff and several other mages, Auziiyra was guarding a human mining community when they were attacked by drow . The mages used the wonderform spell to hide in the form of wingless wonders and escape being killed, but became lost in the Underdark . They were thought to still be wandering the Night Below by the mid–14 th century DR .
