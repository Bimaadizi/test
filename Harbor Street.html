Harbor Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Harbor Street was located in the south section of Zhentil Keep in the Harbor District along the docks. The road was located in the central section of the Harbor District, running north to south between the wall of the Inner Quarter and the docks.

Notable Locations

The Shield Faces North , one of the oldest taverns in all of Zhentil Keep, was located along Harbor Street.
