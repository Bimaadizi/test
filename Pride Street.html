Pride Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Pride Street was located in the southwest corner of Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter . The road ran west to east, beginning along Nool Street in the west and passing east to end at Brigand Way . Pride Street was intersected by Beggars' Way , Dixon Street and Silver Street .

Notable Locations

The Tower of Pain Exalted , a notorious temple to Loviatar , and a Zhentarim warehouse were both located along Pride Street.
