Okuzo's Imports was a shop in Westgate that dealt in a wide variety of exotic goods imported from the eastern nations of Kara-Tur , located in the city of Westgate in the late 14 th century DR .

Location

Okuzo's shop was located on the Harbor Loop of Westgate, near the Statue of Kisonraathiisar , three doors away from where the Loop crossed with the Silverpiece Way .

Services

Okuzo's shop sold imported goods from Kara-Tur, from art, curios, perfumes , and spices to exotic magical and mundane weapons and armor of the Orient.

History

Okuzo's Imports opened her shop sometime before her business faced the strife of violent competition in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR . The new business that imported perfumes and spices threatened the Urdo clan's market dominance. The family representative Dallia Urdo and her hired thugs threatened her and battered her, and filled the store with giant spiders to try and force Okuzo's Imports out of business.
