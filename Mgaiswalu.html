Mgaiswalu was a small coastal village located within Jazayir al-Sartan archipelago on the Crowded Sea of southwest Zakhara .

Geography

The village was located on one of the northernmost islands of the southern branch of Jazayir al-Alfar on the lagoon of Batihat al-Saji .
