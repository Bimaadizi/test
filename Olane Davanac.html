Olane Davanac was a human cleric of Tymora and the Luckmaster of the Towers of Good Fortune in Suzail , Cormyr , in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Davanac was an older human woman.

Personality

Luckmaster Olane Davanac herself was willing to cast cleric spells for petitioners and adventurers in return for donations to the temple left in the poor box on their way out of the Towers of Good Fortune.

History

Olane began her service to Tymora in Arabel . After that, in her youth, she traveled all around the Heartlands and served in many temples.

By 1479 DR , Olane Davanac was the Luckmaster of the Towers of Good Fortune. She oversaw more than a dozen priests, called luckbringers, and a several hundred lay worshipers.

Rumors & Legends

Rumors had it that in her traveling youth she fell in love with a bard from Berdusk . Some people said that he broke her heart, others say that he was killed by a rival in a fit of jealousy. Luckmaster Davanac herself never spoke of her past, but it was known that she'd never been married.
