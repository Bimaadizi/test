Coneth was an early knight of Neverwinter and the sixth of the Nine knights who served Lord Halueth Never . They were the basis for the Neverwinter Nine .

History

Coneth's blood was "impure" in some way, but he was trusted and true. A statue of Coneth and his sword stood guard in Halueth's tomb.
