Alders were a type of tree that could be found across the Realms with red and white alders being common. However, some, like Volothamp Geddarm , claimed that alder trees did not exist on Toril .

Habitats

Usages

Red alder bark was used in various medicines , potions , and incense . In the days of the ancient Netheril , alder wood was used as a weapon crafting material.

Alchemical smoke powder that produced dense gray smoke and was used as a thrown weapon used alder and mangrove wood in preparation of the substance.

It was believed that a tonic made out of alder was a curative substance that possessed anti-inflammatory properties.
