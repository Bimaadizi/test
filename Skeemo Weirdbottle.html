Skeemo Weirdbottle ( pronounced : /ˈ s k i m oʊ / SKEE-moh ) was a rock gnome agent of the Zhentarim , operating within the city of Waterdeep during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Skeemo had no loyalty to his friends.

Relationship

Skeemo was a member of the Doom Raiders adventuring company. He and his former companions composed the leadership of the Zhentarim of Waterdeep. Skeemo secretly spied on his former companions under the payroll of the House Gralhund and Manshoon's Zhentarim.

History

Skeemo was once an adventurer but together with his adventuring companions he decided to join the Zhentarim. In 1489 DR , they were sent to establish a Zhent foothold in Waterdeep.

In 1492 DR , as a cover, Skeemo managed a shop in the Trades Ward , Weirdbottle's Concoctions , where he sold non-magical potions. One of his customers was Esvele Rosznar
