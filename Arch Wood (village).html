Arch Wood was a tiny village hidden inside the forest of Arch Wood in the Dalelands and largely unknown to the outside world.

Description

The village consisted of a small cluster of cottages, a blacksmith, and a watermill.

Inhabitants

The Selûnite priestess and werewolf Feena Archwood lived in Arch Wood in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , both fighting a protracted war against Malarite lycanthropes and serving the community as a healer. She was preceded by her mother, Maleva , also a priest of Selûne who fought against Malarites.
