Baldurian seafood stew was a signature dish from the harbors of Baldur's Gate .

Description

A hearty and versatile stew , these dishes exuded fragrance of garlic , fried tomato paste, and usually consisted of a wide variety of day's catch seafood. Common versions were filled with clams , prawns , fish , and peas .

Availability

Created by the busy bustling inhabitants of Baldur's Gate's harbors, this recipe became a staple on the city by the late-14 th century DR , and even served in the Western Heartlands .
