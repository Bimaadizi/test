Sergor Marsk was a warmage from Damara , and an infiltrator from the Cult of the Dragon . He led the ritual to capture the soul of Gareth Dragonsbane .

Description

Sergor habitually had a dyspeptic look, a squint, tense shoulders, and a tightly set jaw.

Abilities

He led the ritual to capture Gareth's soul. After he was discovered, he only managed to cast one fireball .

Possessions

Sergor possessed, at the time of his death, black thread and a miniature doll representing king Dragonsbane. Presumably it was a focus for the ritual he employed.

History

On Kythorn 10 , Celedon Kierney had given Sergor and his co-conspirers a place close to the king because of their unusual success at gathering intelligence from the Bandit Army of Vaasa .

Sergor Marsk was part of the forces led by king Gareth Dragonsbane in Mirtul 3 of 1373 DR to fight off a dragonflight roused by the Rage of Dragons . He used the cover of battle to lead a rite which trapped the king's soul; only the timely intervention by a young soldier, Igan , prevented his treachery from being completely hidden. Igan slew Sergor immediately.
