The House of Revels was a temple dedicated to Sune located in the city of Ordulin in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Inhabitants

The temple was run by High Priestess Aummerglau Tistrin . Aummerglau was assisted by 29 other priestesses and 56 followers.
