Alakinyon Ken'viir was a drow arcanist of Sschindylryn during the 1480s DR .

Description

Alakinyon had his long white hair worn loosely.

Abilities

Alakinyon was a powerful spellcaster and knew ball lightning , chain lightning , delayed blast fireball , force missiles , Niac's biting cold , Otiluke's freezing sphere , and polar ray .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Alakinyon lived in the city. He and Tras'ekhan guarded the great portal that was set up to connect to the Eveningstar cavern .
