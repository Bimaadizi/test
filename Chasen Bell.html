Chasen Bell was a human resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

Chasen had mid-length light brown hair and pale skin.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Chasen found the hidden underground movements of the drow to be just as concerning as those they could see.
