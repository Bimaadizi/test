Buhrell Caah was an agent of the Emerald Enclave in Phlan during the Tyranny of Dragons .

History

Buhrell was an agent of the Emerald Enclave sent to Phlan. He went to Madame Freona's Tea Kettle searching for a powerful and dangerous magical item. With the help of some adventurers he managed to locate and recover the item.
