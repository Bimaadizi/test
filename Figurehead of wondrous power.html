A figurehead of wondrous power was a magical wood statue that granted beneficial powers to a spelljamming ship .

Description

Each type of figurehead was carved in the shape of a creature, typically humanoid , using a particular type of wood. Each figure was inlaid with one or multiple jewels , depending on the boon it provided.

Varieties

Each type of figurehead provided different boons, and were constructed from different materials.
