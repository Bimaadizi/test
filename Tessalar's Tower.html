Tessalar's Tower was a wizard 's tower located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The tower was located at the southwest corner of Sul Street and Chasso's Trot in the northeast section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward .

Description

Tessalar's Tower stood four stories tall.

Inhabitants

The tower was home to a wizard named Tessalar Hulicorm .
