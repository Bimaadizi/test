Dragon Head was a rocky peninsula located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Dragon Head jutted from the southern shore of Gwynneth into the Sea of Swords for approximately 40 miles (64 kilometers). The vast majority of the peninsula was composed of rocky mountainous terrain. The region of the peninsula closest to the mainland was covered by part of the Llyrath Forest .
