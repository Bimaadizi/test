Sylvara was a wood elf scout who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Sylvara was the twin sister of Ayala .

Equipment

Sylvara had a magic sword .

Activities

Alongside her twin sister, Sylvara led a band of elves that operated out in the Blacklake District . As of 1479 DR , the sisters' goal was to become stronger so they could join a band of Bregan D'aerthe that was operating near Castle Never .
