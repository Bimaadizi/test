Majira was a veteran adventurer who lived in Amkethran circa the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Description

She was one to speak up much to those she did not know.

History

After a long time adventuring, Majira and Faheed moved to Amkethran to deal with the individual known as Balthazar . They bought a key to the monk's lair from local smugglers as part of a plan to kill the Bhaalspawn as he had caused too much chaos in Amkethran for their liking.
