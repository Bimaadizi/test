Bottle Lane was a street found in the coastal town of Aithelar in Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles in Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR .

Location

Bottle Lane ran along the southwest perimeter of the town. It intersected Aithelar's main street, Aithe High Street , as well as Whitefish Alley and Rope End Lane .
