Yturn was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Age of Discovery .

Description

This arcanist focused on spells which manipulated how long one could stay in the air. Over a millennium before Yturn developed his levitate spell, Ioulaum used a form of what would become levitate in order to construct his enclave .

History

Yturn developed three spells during their life, each one year after the other. The spells they developed were levitate in 2699 NY (−1160 DR) , jump in 2700 NY (−1159 DR) , and finally, feather fall in 2701 NY (−1158 DR) .
