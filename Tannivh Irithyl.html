Tannivh Irithyl was fifth Coronal of Cormanthyr , ruling from −791 DR to −223 DR . He is known for having successfully routed the drow from the Twisted Tower in −331 DR , passing control of the tower to followers of Eilistraee . In gratitude to the reformed and surface-acclimatized drow who helped in the downfall of the tower, he granted them rights to settle remote woods east of Cormanthor . He died in −223 DR , passing the rulership of the realm to his son, Eltargrim Irithyl .
