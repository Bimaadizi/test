High Artificer Ashuntira Elhorn was the high priestess of the House of the Wheel , a temple dedicated to Gond , in Westgate in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Ashuntira was the leader of Gond's faithful in Westgate. Her congregation within the temple consisted of 11 other priestesses and 26 followers.
