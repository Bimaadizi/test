Wyvernlorn's Wayhouse was an inn located in the city of Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR .

Location

The inn was located near Urmlaspyr's western gates.

Description

Though a bit derelict, Wyvernlorn's Wayhouse's accommodations were fair and the prices were cheap. This popular spot was always crowded.
