Ehjax was a greater vampiric ixitxachitl and ruler of the Xedran Reefs from the Year of the Thistle, 885 DR to the Year of Bright Nights, 985 DR .

History

Crowned in 885 DR, Ehjax founded a new ixitxachitl society of the Xedran Reefs and the tradition of a vampiric ixitxachitl ruling for no more than one hundred years. If all their enemies were not defeated in one century under one vitanar, then that ruler was deemed unworthy in the eyes of Ilxendren and had to abdicate, biting the chosen successor to create a new vampiric vitanar.
