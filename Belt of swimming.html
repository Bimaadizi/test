The Belt of swimming was a unique magic belt that allowed the wearer to swim very fast and keep warm.

Powers

The wearer could swim as fast as a merfolk on the surface, and as a triton underneath, while keeping warm as per a ring of warmth , such that one could swim in even frigid waters. Once the wearer exited the waters the belt would instantly dry them.
