Wenta was a daughter of a brewer who was abducted and enslaved by the Cult of the Dragon in 1369 DR .

Description

Wenta was a friendly large young woman.

History

In 1369 DR, Wenta was one of the unfortunate townies captured by the Cult of the Dragon's slavers. Alongside Tren , Jorsh , and Ristim , the villagers were taken to the Cult's slave pens in the ruined Darnarest's tower . Most of the abducted folk were transported to the ruins of Myth Drannor to work in the Cult's excavations or to be turned into undead servants. Even though Wenta's fate remained unknown, the Cult's nefarious plan was stopped by a group of adventurers sent to search for the missing townies as well as the group known as the Veiled Ones .
