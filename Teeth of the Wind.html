The Teeth of the Wind was an armed militia from the djinn freeholds and the Citadel of Ice and Steel .

Organization

The exact numbers of the Teeth of the Wind was unknown, though it was believed to be in the tens of thousands. It was made-up of djinni and their elemental allies.

Activities

This combined force worked as a gigantic cloud of flying combatants, raining missiles down from above with archers and skirmishers.

Base of Operations

The Citadel of Ice and Steel within the Elemental Plane of Air served as base of all djinni military organizations.
