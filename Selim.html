Selim was a human fighter and tribal leader in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Besides being leader of his tribe, he was also quite the talented warrior and many a beast and foe had fallen to his blade. The tribe was always on the move and as such he had also become a competent trader.

Possessions

He possessed a pair of bracers of brachiation along with a large dagger .
