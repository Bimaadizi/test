The Company of the Cleaver was a moderately successful adventuring troupe active in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

The Year of the Spur, 1348 DR saw the formation of the adventuring company that gained renown in Cormyr â€“ the Knights of Myth Drannor , but the same year birthed several less known organizations, including the Company of the Cleaver. The adventure concluded with the members of the group riding a giant silvery snail along the streets of Waterdeep's Castle Ward .
