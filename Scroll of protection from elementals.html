A scroll of protection from elementals was an enchanted scroll that offered protection from some or all types of elementals.

Powers

After reading this scroll a 10 ft (3 m) sphere of protection was centered on the reader. The sphere protected anyone inside from one or all forms of elementals. Elementals in this case included aerial servants , djinn , invisible stalkers , and wind walkers from the air element, xorn from earth, efreeti , and salamanders from fire, and tritons , and water weirds from water. The sphere lasted between 1 and 4 minutes.

History

A scroll of protection from elementals lay hidden in the Crypt of Badr Al-Mosak , in the Year of the Worm, 1356 DR .
