Cappa Delourese was a woman of means and a client of Sorcerous Sundries in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Alice was a wood elven woman with woody-green skin and blur eyes. She head a head of bright red hair and was dressed in jerkin of fine quality.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Cappa Delourese was trying to collect supples to get her through the tumult of the Cult of the Absolute and its march on the city of Baldur's Gate. She was looking to purchase some mage's supplies at Sorcerous Sundries even though many of the city's store were running low on goods.
