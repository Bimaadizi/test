Alastrah was a cloud giant who resided within Lyn Armaal during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Alastrah was 6 ft 6 in (200 cm) tall and weighed 250 lb (110 kg), equivalent in size to a muscular human male.

Relationships

Alastrah was the daughter of the cloud giant Countess Sansuri .

She had a twin brother named Kaaltar . During their youth they were seldom given attention by their mother, but periodically visited by their uncle Thullen .
