Tomato soup was a soup made from tomatoes known in Faerûn in the mid–14 th century DR . Butter could also be added, giving it a pale red color. It had some sweetness to it.

History

Butter-tomato soup was served at the Sheaf of Wheat inn in Ghars , Cormyr , around 1367 DR .
