Divine Simfray was a matron mother in the early 11 th century DR .

History

Divine ruled House Simfray in Menzoberranzan , a very small house. However, she acquired the services of a very talented weapon master, Zaknafein .

In 1018 DR , House Simfray was attacked by House Tr'arach . Meanwhile, Arathis Hune and Jarlaxle were hired by Matron Mother Zeerith Q'Xorlarrin to deliver the weapon master Zaknafein to Matron Malice of House Do'Urden . In the last moments of the battle, Arathis infiltrated Divine's private room, killing the guards, and confronted the matron mother. He offered to Divine a chance to join Bregan D'aerthe but she refused and the assassin quickly hit her with his poisoned sword, killing her.
