Olaim's Cut was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Olaim's Cut was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . It ran east to west, connecting Caravan Street in the west with Wall Street in the east near the outer city wall. An unnamed alley branched off the southern side of Olaim's Cut.

Notable Locations

Thauntle's Good Heels and Soles sat on the north side of Olaim's Cut.
