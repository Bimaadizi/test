The Viper Fang was the enchanted dagger that dripped with poison akin to an actual fang of a viper .

Description

The Viper Fang was unassuming in appearance apart from the always wet edge of the dagger's blade that constantly exuded extremely potent venom or poison.

Powers

This dagger possessed a 3 enchantment. Additionally, it was coated in strong magically generating poison. When this poison was introduced into a target's bloodstream, it started weakening them, withering the creatures' muscles. Just several shallow cuts could easily bring down even the lightest warriors.

History

In 1373 DR , the Viper Fang could be found in the Moonsea region, the vicinity of Elventree .
