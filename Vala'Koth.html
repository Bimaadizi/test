Vala'Koth was a githyanki city in the Astral Plane .

Geography

Vala'Koth was 100 miles (160 kilometers) away from the Elserryn Cluster , on the petrified body of a dead god.

Forces

Vala'Koth had an astral fleet of three planar raiders , five astral brigs , and twenty astral skiffs . Despite these forces, they often found themselves attacked by small armies, usually in retaliation for something.

History

As of 1372 DR , the forces of Vala'Koth had yet to successfully extract concessions out of Crosswinds Keep , or even destroy the location.
