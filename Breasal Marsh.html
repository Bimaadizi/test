The Breasal Marsh , also called the Breasal Swamp , was a marshland located on the isle of Moray within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Breasal Marsh was located toward the center of the island. It covered approximately 200 mi² (520 km²), making it one of the prominent geographical features of Moray. The marsh was fed by the Shannyth River flowing in from the south, which also exited the marsh's northern edge at Lac Dynnegall .

Notable Locations

Inhabitants

Tribes of lizardfolk lived within the marsh. Some of the tribes wished to reawaken Kazgaroth , while others were more peaceful and followed Silvanus .
