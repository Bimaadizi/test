Ornagar's Smoked Eels & More was a cannery in the city of Ravens Bluff circa 1370 DR .

Location

This store was situated in the Silverscales , a neighborhood in the Harbor District of Ravens Bluff.

Services

Ornagar's Smoked Eels & More processed and canned a variety of seafood including fish , mussels , oysters , squid , and of course, eels . Despite being a seafood cannery, it was known locally for the pleasant smell of burning cherry wood . The cannery slowly smoked their eels before packaging in tightly sealed jars . This ensured their product remained edible as it was delivered throughout the Vast and as far north as the Moonsea .
