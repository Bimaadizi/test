Aedelthrang the Black was a cleric of Uthgar in the Red Tiger tribe of Uthgardt barbarians that lived in the Silver Marches during the mid-14 th century DR . He was an important authority figure within the tribe, serving as their "spirit talker".

Although the tribe was officially led by Chieftain Thradulf Longfang , following the death of his uncle Adalwulf , Adelthrang acted as their true leader.
