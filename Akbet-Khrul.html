Akbet-Khrul was a pirate lord of the Pirate Isles .

History

In 1361 DR , Akbet-Khrul's fleet plagued the seas near Amn until the Council of six hired the Golden Legion to defeat the pirates.

Cordell and his men fought Akbet-Khrul's pirates in a great battle and were victorious.

Halloran found the body of Akbet-Khrul himself with multiple arrows in him after the battle.
