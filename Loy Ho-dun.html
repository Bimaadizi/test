Loy Ho-dun was a kensai adventurer in Shou Lung .

History

Loy Ho-dun had a great natural talent for swordsmanship. He studied more than twelve different styles and mastered two before developing his own personal style, the Dragon Swarm .

Afterward, Ho-dun opened his school in the hills north of Shangtou in the Ch'ing Tung province to teach Dragon Swarm style, and students begged to enroll. However, he was often away, duel against other kensai in order to learn more.

In 1358 DR , Ho-dun decided to attend the Komite .
