Umbergoth was the tallest mountain in Aglarond . As of 1368 DR , it had never been scaled.

Geography

It lay north of the Watchwall and south of Shyvar Pass . The mountain was so tall that its peak was always covered in snow. The sheer slopes of the mountain were largely considered unscalable by humanoids.

Flora & Fauna

There had been sightings of griffons and hippogriffs around the peak. By 1372 DR , Nartheling had scared off most of them, alongside the giant eagles , out of the mountain peak. There were asperii on the mountain, in fact.

History

In 1360 DR , Nartheling , an ancient fang dragon , moved into the mountain.

As of 1372 DR , the Cult of the Dragon regularly scaled the mountain to seduce Nartheling with the tranformation into a dracolich . The copper dragon Filauxerimos preferred to instead avoid the location. Nartheling himself was known to raid nearby caravans.

In 1373 DR , Nartherling, while succumbing to the rage, got into a number of skirmishes with the beholders, which spilled into the surrounding area.

Rumors & Legends

Asperii were a rumor in 1368 DR . They were correct, and several such creatures had aeries close to the peak.

Notable Locations

The dragon Nartheling had made his lair in a labyrinthine complex of caves inside the peak, surrounded by the nests of griffins and asperii. As of 1374 DR , he had discovered a network of hidden portals in the tunnels.

Inhabitants

Galeb duhr were known to inhabit the mountain. The mountain's fauna included wyverns , and in fact, beholders lived in hidden tunnels.

Nartheling inhabited the mountain from 1360 DR to at least 1374 DR .
