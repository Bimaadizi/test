Grimstad was a village located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The village was located along the northern tip of the island at the northern mouth of the Strait of Alaron on the Sea of Moonshae . The town was surrounded by the trees of the Winterglen .

History

During the height of the Northlander kingdom of Oman in the 14 th century DR , Grimstad fell within Oman's territory on Gwynneth.

By the late 15 th century DR, following the rise of Sarifal , Grimstad lay in ruins.
