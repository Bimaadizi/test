Cera Whucknolls was a human follower of Oghma and a librarian serving at the Font of Knowledge temple in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , working alongside her sister Aria . She was knowledgeable in matters of religion and human deities .
