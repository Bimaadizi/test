Crests, Flags, and Banners of the Moonsea was a book on the topic of heraldry of the Moonsea region, penned by Her Ladyship Salanida Lenafae of Ordulin sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

History

A copy of Crests, Flags, and Banners of the Moonsea belonged to Manshoon XII , a clone of Manshoon , in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR . The book was stored in the mage 's library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep .
