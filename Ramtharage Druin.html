History

On Kythorn 19 in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , just after the start of the Time of Troubles , Ramtharage imprisoned a trio of rangers of Mielikki , accusing them of desecrating the shrine to Eldath, Eldath's Water , which had been destroyed the night before. However, Elminster arrived to intercede for the rangers. Ramtharage did not listen to their reasons, so Elminster apparently called the avatars of both Eldath and Mielikki to his side. They declared they were sisters and friends. Ramthar was utterly shocked by meeting the two goddesses and released the rangers.
