The waistwrap of wild wisdom was a fine belt found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The belt was designed to aid those who wandered the forests, and improved the wearer's judgment and mental resilience. Further enchantments gave a general improvement to their attributes and their ability to cast healing magic.

History

The accessory ended up in Tyrintar's Hall in the ruins of Myth Drannor, guarded by hordes of the undead like Gothror the Ravager and Aerich the Steward . It may have been found by the Elves of Cormanthor during the Elven Crusade , when they searched for Uhlsdrin Nirveth in the Hall.
