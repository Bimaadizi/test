Bolo's Tentside Inn was an boarding house set up in the makeshift settlement that formed around Boareskyr Bridge during the latter half of the 15 th century DR . It was run by a rather talkative halfling woman named Bolo .

Location

The inn was set up in the central pavilion of Boareskyr Bridge's tent-structure settlement.

History

Some time in the 1480s DR , a senior member of the Cult of the Dragon named Varram killed a yuan-ti spy in the inn, by stabbing it with his dragontooth dagger .

Rumors & Legends

It had a reputation as a rather rowdy establishment.
