Mindi was a child fan of the dancer known as Shimmer from the Blushing Mermaid in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Mindi was a young girl with curly blonde hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Shimmer was a regular performer who entertained the customers of the shady Blushing Mermaid tavern in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. Shimmer was a popular performer known for his loud makeup , glittering outfits, and impressive dancing.
