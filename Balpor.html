Balpor was a Shadovar soldier and scout in a reconnaissance unit that served Prince Escanor Tanthul of Thultanthar during the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Balpor's unit led was led by the young Shadovar officer Parth Gal . He served alongside Carlig , Elar , and eight others.

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Balpor and Parth Gal's other scouts ventured into the Irithlium of Myth Drannor to gain information about phaerimm activity within the ruins. Balpor was among the first of the Shadovar to die. The only remains left after his death was his head and a single arm.
