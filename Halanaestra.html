Halanaestra was an elf of mixed wild elf and moon elf heritage (and possibly aquatic elf , too).

History

Around 1368 DR , she was the proprietor of the Green Sword tavern in Leuthilspar , Evermeet . Prior to this, she was a famous dancer.
