Gheta Xax was a merchant of Tabot around Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) .

Trading in the nation of T'u Lung , Gheta kept yards within the great city of Balanzia , a century prior to its destruction. These lay against the northern wall, neighboring the Bahn clan's own yards and storehouse.
