Sumbar was a small island in the Pirate Isles on the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Geography

Sumbar was found near the center of the Pirate Isles along the northwest edge of the Bowl .

Description

A series of treacherous reefs surrounded the island. To make matters worse, the reefs appeared to shift on a daily basis so they were never in the same position.

Inhabitants

The island was inhabited by the mysterious Oracle of Nasmen , which would answer a single question after an appropriate offering was made. The Oracle took the form of a well atop a ridge.
