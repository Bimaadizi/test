In later years Alais joined the Harpers at Twilight and fought well in the Weeping War . Alais was one of the commander in a decisive battle against the Army of Darkness in Ches 4 -5 of 712 DR when thank to the Chosen of Mystra Elminster and Khelben Arunsun the Army suffered a defeat at Elven Wood

Alais was one of the few Harpers to survive alongside Elminster , Khelben Arunsun and Meil Araeln . Alais was at the Gathering of the Gods in the 720 DR together with her fellow Harpers.
