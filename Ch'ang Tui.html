Ch'ang Tui , nicknamed Long Legs , was the abbot of the Order of the White Crane , a sect devoted to Shu Chia and Path of Enlightenment , in Shou Lung circa Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) . They were based at the White Crane Temple in the city of Kirin , Hungtse Province . He focused on upholding the sect conservative traditions.
