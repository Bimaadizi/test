Valon was the military leader of the centaurs of Guard in Bloodstone Barony in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Valon was known for this raw but precise intelligent with a cold and logical mind, making him perfect for the military.

History

As War Chief, Valon served on Guard's Council of Elders alongside Chief Kiros and Elder Shinar .

In 1358 DR , Valon and his fellows met Gareth Dragonsbane 's party. As of 1359 DR , Valon was a key member of the Alliance of Belt Watchers with local halflings and dwarves . He was their most able commander.
