Nicos Corynian was an influential Chessentan noble in 1479 DR . He was also a secret servant of the green dragon Skuthosiin .

Description

Nicos was a middled-aged man with graying brown hair. He was a family man and had a minor talent for magic.

History

In 1479 DR Nicos hired the Brotherhood of the Griffon to serve as a police force due to unrest in Luthcheq . When Aoth Fezim won Shala Karanok 's favor and was tasked to protect Soolabax , Nicos asked him (on behalf of Skuthosiin) to loot for Tchazzar .
