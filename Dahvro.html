Dahvro was a thief in Westgate until 1355 DR .

History

Born in Sembia , Dahvro relocated to Westgate.

Around 1355 DR, he decided to join an adventuring party with the objective of exploring Myth Drannor . There however, during the exploration of the Guild of Naturalists ' tower, the party was slaughtered and Dahvro also died.
