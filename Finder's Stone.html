The Finder's Stone was a powerful artifact created by Finder Wyvernspur in an attempt to achieve immortality through his songs.

Abilities

It was made by inserting para-elemental ice into an existing minor artifact, also named a finder's stone . It contained magical recordings of Finder performing his songs, as well as teleportation powers and the finder's stone's original detection powers.

History

The stone was destroyed so the para-elemental ice could be used to destroy Moander .
