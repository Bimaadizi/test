Karmandon's Finery was a clothing and jewelry shop in Eileanar up till Karsus's Folly in −339 DR .

Services

Karmandon sold fine clothing and jewelry that made up complete fashion ensembles. His business started to suffer due to the superior practices of Chanragoupta's Exotic , and he responded by spreading scurrilous rumors to sink Chanragoupta 's reputation.
