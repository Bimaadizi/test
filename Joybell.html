Joybell was a rock gnome cook living in Gnomengarde in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Joybell operated the iron stove in Gnomengarde's kitchen, keeping it hot by stoking it with a fire poker . Along with Uppendown and Panana , she was responsible for making bread using Gnomengarde's distinctive green mushrooms, a principle food of the gnomish settlement.

Abilities

Like all the other gnomes in Gnomengarde, Joybell had some training as a wizard in addition to her innate abilities. In combat, she preferred to attack with spells like ray of frost and magic missile .
