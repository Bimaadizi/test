Congee was a simple dish of savory and thick rice gruel found on the islands of the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Description

Inhabitants of the Pirate Isles consumed sticky congee, dipping fried bread into the gruel.

History

Kwan Shang-Li , a half-elven Shou monk , partook in fish -flavored congee and fried bread during his nautical adventures on the Sea of Fallen Stars in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .
