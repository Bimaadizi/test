Brokengulf Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Brokengulf .

Location

The villa was located in the north section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward . The villa sat along Ivory Street , Mendever Street , and Wrightstone Street .

Description

A trio of separate buildings were found within the walled estate. Each building was either three or four stories tall and arranged around a central garden.
