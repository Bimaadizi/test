The Two-Form Glacier was a large glacier locked within the peaks of an unknown mountain range near the monastery of Fatula Chupa in what was once the Kingdom of Guge .

Geography

The glacier covered roughly 900 mi² (2,300 km²) within the mountain range. Several small tributaries fed by glacial runoff flowed onto the plains from the north and northeastern portions of the glacier.
