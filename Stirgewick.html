Stirgewick was a county located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Stirgewick was by far the largest county in Callidyrr, stretching for over 100 miles (160 kilometers) from the Dernall Forest and Swanmay River in the north, all the way to the tip of the South Great Head peninsula on the southern tip of Alaron. The southern half of Stirgewick was covered by the rolling plains of Bregonshire .
