The Eye Killers were a unit of orog soldiers in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR that fought for Lord Vorik Aris of Cormyr .

Description

The Eye Killers were a well-organized formation of 60 orog soldiers equipped with horses , splint mail , scimitars , and shortbows with flight arrows . Their three officers had plate mail and carried extra sheaf arrows , while their chief Gribbergrash bore some magical equipment.

History

The Eye Killers were hired by Lord Aris in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , joining his service alongside the Twilight Brigade , other mercenaries, and various amoral adventurers . They were based in Wyvernwatch Castle and performed scouting and raiding throughout the Stonelands , and Gribbergrash became one of Lord Aris's inner circle of advisors.

When Lord Aris marched on Darkenshield to begin his military campaign against Cormyr, the Eye Killers rode with him. Unfortunately for the orogs, the first battle they fought was against the combined spirits of Darkenshield's residents and Starburst Swords , and they were destroyed.
