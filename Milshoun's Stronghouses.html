Milshoun's Stronghouses was a collection of three stone warehouses in Beliard around 1368 DR .

Inhabitants

The warehouses were owned by Ahbhaer Mhilshoun , who provided storage facilities per day, tenday, or month. Milshoun's Stronghouses were protected by a dozen guards, who hunted game in the hills to the north during their spare time.
