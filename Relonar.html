Relonar was a drow mage who lived in Ust Natha .

History

Around 1369 DR , he was part of a sect of Ghaunadaur worshipers who were crushed by Taso Kala . They subsequently fled to the south-east of Ust Natha. Gorion's Ward and his party may have been put into service to destroy the sect completely.
