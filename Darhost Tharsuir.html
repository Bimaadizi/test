Darhost "the Mongrel" Tharsuir was a Tashalan human fighter and, circa 1373 DR , the hereditary ruling Shoonsar of Ithmong , a city holy to Bhaelros , in Lapaliiya .

Ithmong was the second-most-powerful city of the Lapal League . In practice, the Overking of Lapaliiya needed to negotiate with Shoonsar Darhost to have decrees passed by the Grand Council.
