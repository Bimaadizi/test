A potion of spell enhancement was a potion that increased the power of the caster's spells.

Powers

When a mage consumed such a potion , the spells they cast became much more powerful. This potion, however, only lasted for a short duration.

History

In 1358 DR , a chamber to the southwest of Leilon was highly guarded by undead such as spectres and wights . The undead guardians appeared to be protecting a potent potion of spell enhancement .
