Vexilanthus was a young , male copper dragon that lived in the Savage Frontier during the late 15 th century DR .

History

One day while flying lazily in circles over his domain, a group of adventurers caught the attention of Vexilanthus. While conversing with him the adventurers mentioned that they were on the lookout for giants . Vexilanthus informed the group that recently some hill giant had been prowling around a decrepit old tower, somewhere in the hills near Dessarin Valley . After pointing the adventurers in the direction of the tower, Vexilanthus offered to escort them to the nearest settlement for a fee of 500 or more gold pieces .
