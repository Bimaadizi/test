Argen was one of the guards who served the noble family of Arrabar - House Matrell , circa 1373 DR .

History

Argen was one of the guards of House Matrell circa the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR and could often be found in the family's barracks. He forged a friendship with the family's youngest scion - Emriana Matrell , a rebellious and talented rogue . He taught her the use of daggers in combat.
