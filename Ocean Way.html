Ocean Way was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Ocean Way was located in the Harbor District of south Zhentil Keep. The road was located on the eastern side of the Harbor District. It originated on Border Street and passed northeast to terminate along the waterfront of the docks. Sullen Street connected with Ocean Way.
