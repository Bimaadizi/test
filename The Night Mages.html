The Night Mages , also known as Felodar's Sendings , were the sorcerers sent by Felodar Aumar to seize his native realm of Athalantar in the early-to-mid 3 rd century DR .

Tactics

The Night Mages worked most often at night.

Relationships

The Night Mages worked for Felodar Aumar, and were opposed by the Magelords of Athalantar .

History

Once settled in Calimshan and established as a successful merchant, Felodar Aumar sent wizards to Athalantar to try and seize control of the realm on his behalf. These wizards were a threat to his brother, King Belaur Aumar for most of his reign, which lasted between 218 DR and 240 DR . These mages hid by day, combating the Magelords mostly during the hours of darkness. However, at least two of them, Maulygh and Nathgarl , joined the Magelords before 229 DR .

Though Felodar died in the Year of the Wandering Leucrotta, 239 DR , the Magelords were still combating one of his Sendings, Orthalar , until his assumed death in the Year of the Chosen, 240 DR .
