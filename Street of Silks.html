The Street of Silks was a major road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Street of Silks spanned much of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . It ran north to south, running parallel with the Swords Streets to the west and Street of Silver to the east. The street originated in the north on Bazaar Street where it branched south to end on Waterdeep Way at Ahghairon's Tower and the Palace of Waterdeep . Other roads connecting with the Street of Silks along its path included Keltarn Street , Selduth Street , Shadow Alley , Tharleon Street , and Sevenlamps Cut .
