TypeHoly symbol, weapon

The tentacus was the holy symbol of Garagos , the Reaver.

Description

The tentacus was a flattened disc with five sharp protrusions extending from its perimeter.

History

The clergy of Garagos carried the tentacus to honor their lord, but they were quite adept at using it in combat as well. Garagos' faithful could wield the tentacus effectively in hand to hand combat as well as when thrown.
