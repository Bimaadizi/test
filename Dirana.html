Dirana was a member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave at the Fane of the Eye under Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

In 1491 DR , Dirana lived at the Temple of the Crushing Wave in Tyar-Besil . However, she was captured in a skirmish by the Cult of the Eternal Flame and put in a jail in the Fane's Eternal Flame section until some adventurers infiltrated the fane and defeated the earth cultists and found her.
