Bryseis Kakistos , a.k.a. Bysera , was a tiefling warlock who lived near Vaasa in the 14 th century DR .

History

Bysera born in 1331 DR .

In 1339 DR , Bysera was living in the village of Darmshall in Vaasa with her twin sister Alyona .

In the Year of the Spur, 1348 DR , on Flamerule 15 , near Bloodstone Pass , the warlock Titus offered to the young Bysera to make a warlock pact and summoned the succubus Metynoma as her patron. Despite some reticence from Bysera, the pact was forged.

Legacy

Very little was known about Bryseis, other than that she was the leader of the Toril thirteen , the original thirteen tiefling warlocks who made pacts with Asmodeus . She was said by the cambion Lorcan to have been a thoroughly wicked person who was partially responsible for Asmodeus's ascension to godhood .

One of her tiefling descendants named Farideh became a warlock in 1477 DR when she made a pact with the cambion Lorcan . She also has descendants in Lachs the Yellow, a warlock pacted to Shetai the Paelyrion; Nasmos, pacted to a bone devil named Incus ; and Adastreia Tyrianicus, pacted to a Logokron from the 8th layer named Kulaga .
