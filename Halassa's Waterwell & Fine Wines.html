Halassa's Waterwell & Fine Wines was a spirits shop found in the city of Yartar . It was owned by the acerbic proprietor Halassa , an elderly woman who seemed to know every single person in the North .

Location

The shop was located just south of the Fishyard marketplace.

Structure

Located within Halassa's was its namesake well. The well was encircled by the establishment's well-stocked cellars that went deep underground.

Services

Halassa sold a great variety of spirits, of human , dwarven , halfling , and gnomish origin, at very sensible prices. Additionally, she offered a complimentary bucket of cooled well water to any Yartarran that requested one.
