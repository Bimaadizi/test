Messanth was a bronze dragon turned shadow dragon in Szith Morcane in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The Archmage of Szith Morcane, Solom Ned'razak , at one point captured the bronze dragon Messanth and imprisoned him inside a pocket of Shadowfell . The shadow energies changed Messanth into a shadow dragon that attacked anything he saw.

In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , the Order of the Gauntlet asked adventurers affiliated with them to find Messanth.

Relationships

Messanth had good dealings with the Order of the Gauntlet.
