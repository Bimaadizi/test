The North Ride , alternate spelling Northride , was a road that ran north from Tilverton through the Shadow Gap to the Dalelands . It crossed the River Ashaba at the town of Shadowdale , passing through to Voonlar and the Moonsea region.

History

Guards from Shadowdale would regularly patrol their stretch of this road in 1369 DR . Also around this time, an adventuring party called the Initiates of the Sacred Order could regularly be found here.
