Bromdurr Tathen was a mercenary recruiter in Amn .

Activities

Bromdurr was a leading member ( Boar's Head ) of the Order of the Blue Boar . He was an expert of naval operations. He had a ship and, if needed, happily sailed for battle.

History

Bromdurr saved his hometown of Memnon in Calimshan from a pirate attack, gaining some fame. Later, he helped built the navy of Calimsham.
