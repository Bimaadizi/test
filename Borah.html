Borah was a small town located in the nation of Halruaa during the 14 th century DR .

Geography

Borah was located in central Haluraa along the edge of the Akhlaur Swamp . The town lay along the northern fringes of the swamp just south of the River Halar and northwest of Lake Halruaa .
