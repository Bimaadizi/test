The Sky Lizard Mountains , also known as the Luo Peaks , was a branch of the Peaks of Flame that jutted toward Thindol on the Chultan Peninsula .

Description

The towering peaks showed signs of wind erosion.

Geography

These mountains were found in southeastern Chult near the border of Thindol and Samarach . Along with the Sanrach Mountains to the south, they formed the mountainous border of the Chultengar and the Valley of Dread , and also separated both from the Tabaxi Lands to the north. The western end of the range was anchored by an active volcano known as The Crown .
