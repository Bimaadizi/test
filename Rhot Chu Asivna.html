Rhot Chu Asivna was a wealthy merchant in the nation of T'u Lung around Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) .

History

Asivna lived in the great city of Balanzia , a century prior to its destruction. There they lived within a grand mansion in the far northern reaches of the city, just a bit west of the Gate of Shuza and north of the towers of Angu Riz .
