The Twilight Harp was an inn and tavern found in Yhaunn in the 14 th century DR .

Services

The Twilight Harp was one of the newer establishments in Yhaunn as of 1358 DR . The adventurers and caravan masters who favored the Twilight Harp could expect excellent accommodations and moderate prices.
