Galvan Longarms was a firbolg proxy of the Giantish power known as Hiatea .

Description

Like all proxies of Hiatea, this firbolg was unusually large and strong for his race.

Personality

Galvan had a deep hatred for evil giants and their deities.

Possessions

Galvan dressed in a set of scale armor , fashioned from the scales of a hydra , and carried two huge flaming spears +3 . These functioned similarly to sun blades .

Activities

Though Galvan would never betray Hiatea, he would take some indirect action to aid those seeking to combat giants.
