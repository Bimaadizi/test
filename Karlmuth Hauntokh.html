Karlmuth Hauntokh was a prospector of the Western Heartlands in the mid-3 rd century DR .

Description

He was fatter and hairier than Surgath Ilder, and dressed in tattered clothes.

Personality

He was more arrogant than Surgath Ilder .

Possessions

As of 240 DR , he had a stout, worn sword, a smattering of utilitarian devices, and had looted a golden sphere set in with gems from an elven tomb which he claimed to be from Elanvae . He also wore a ring with a scratched device of a serpent ready to strike, which was shared by some bandits of the Skuldaskar , including Drace and Algan .

History

In 240 DR , he was present at the Herald's Horn tavern the night that Elminster Aumar purchased a wyrmtongue scepter from Surgath Ilder . Karlmuth Hauntokh showed some of his own loot, and later inspected one of the rubies which Elminster had paid Surgath with. The prince noticed that Karlmuth wore a ring with the same motif as some bandits that had attacked him earlier.
