The Golem Slayer was an enchanted adamantine warhammer created by Mintassan the Magnificent, a planeswalker from the city of Westgate , active in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

When the Golem Slayer was wielded against constructs , it effectively became a +1 enchanted weapon. Additionally, the hammer dealt a small amount of sonic damage to its targets.

History

This enchanted warhammer was a minor curiosity created by Mintassan the Magnificent to keep "his children" in line. The Golem Slayer name was given to the weapon by its planeswalking creator. In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the weapon was available for purchase from Mintassan's Mysteries in Westgate's Market Triangle .
