Anabis was the imp familiar of the mercenary Lychor , who he would accompany everywhere in the form of a raven .

History

In 1358 DR , when Lychor was contracted by the Zhentarim with helping Amelior Amanitas reconstruct the legendary stone golem known as Awtawmatawn and then steal it for themselves, Anabis traveled with him to the village of Volkumburgh . There he acted as the guard of Lychor's rented room in the village's inn, Crimson Wyrm .
