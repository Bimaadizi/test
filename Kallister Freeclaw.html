Kallister Freeclaw was a brawler from Reithwin , once a Selûnite town in the Western Heartlands , sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Kallister Freeclaw died sometime before the fall of Reithwin and its consumption by the Shadow-cursed Lands , before the 1374 DR . Kallister was said to have been a legendary brawler who died in his sleep. He was buried in Reithwin's graveyard behind the House of Healing , at its very edge. His tombstone read: "Here rests Kallister Freeclaw, legendary brawler. Died in his sleep, to the surprise of many." The grave marker remained undamaged as of the 1492 DR .
