A scroll of protection from possession was a scroll capable of protecting the reader from any attempts at possession.

Powers

Reading this scroll generated a 10 ft (3 m) sphere that moved around the reader, protecting anyone inside the sphere from magical forms of possession or mental control such as a magic jar spell . These effects lasted for ten minutes to one hour, but a small percentage of these scrolls lasted for several hours but conjured a stationary sphere of protection.

Notable Owners

Hasan ibn al-Din , a corsair travelling the Great Sea near Zakhara , carried a scroll of protection from possession .
