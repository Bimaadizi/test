Myntharan , called the Magus , was a Shoon wizard-king of Westgate .

Activities

Myntharan was a skilled wizard who hailed from the Shoon Imperium . He challenged and killed King Mulsantir II in the Year of the Rolling Heads, 452 DR , and seized the throne of Westgate for himself. He would rule until the Year of the Winter Sphinx, 480 DR , when he was slain by the androsphinx Lyonarth .
