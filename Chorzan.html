Chorzan was a hezrou demon and aspiring demagogue of the Demonweb Pits , who invaded the Prime Material plane .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Chorzan emerged from the Demonweb Pits into the Underdark near the ruins of Riz Malag , not far from Sschindylryn .
