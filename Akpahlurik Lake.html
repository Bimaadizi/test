Akpahlurik Lake was a lake in the Alpuk region of the Great Glacier .

Description

Akpahlurik Lake was a salt water lake with an average depth of between 5​ to ​20 feet (1.5​ to ​6.1 meters). Along the southern shore, oil desposits oozed from the lake bed. The oil was skimmed and collected from the water.

Geography

Akpahlurik Lake was located in eastern Alpuk, between the Lugalpgotak Sea and the Keryjek Ridge .
