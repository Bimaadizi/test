Sladis was a lizard man who lived in the Great Swamp of Rethild in western Dambrath .

Activities

Sladis served in the Servants of the Royal Egg as their field commander. He would dispatch soldiers to fight in distant wars. As of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , they were active in Tethyr and the jungles of Chult .
