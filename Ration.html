Rations were packs of various dry foods that were convenient for extended travels.

Description

Typical rations packed an assortment of jerky , dried fruits, hardtack , and nuts, although several other combinations were possible. Each day's worth of meal was packed in waxed parchment and sackcloth and tied with strings to increase durability, which was at least two weeks.

Ration packs containing hard rolls, dried cheese and fruit, jerky, and tuber vegetables such as potatoes and turnips were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .

Reputation

Ration packs produced by the inhabitants of Arvandor were famous for their sweetness.
