Antola Pilark was a halfling Great Druid and an ally of House Kendrick in the Moonshae Isles in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .

Activities

Antola led troops from House Kendrick against the spreading evil of the goblin king Gark 's minions within the Dernall Forest near Caer Callidyrr .

She was said to be able to craft weapons that rivaled the sentinel's cudgels made by druids of yore.
