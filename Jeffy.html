Jeffy was a young boy living in Easthaven during the 11th century DR .

Description

The blond haired boy liked to wear dark tunics.

Personality

Jeffy was an rowdy young man who liked to mess with the animals of Icewind Dale , like squirrels .

Activities

Like many kids in the small community, Jeffy was interested in all the strangers coming in from the Frozenfar .

Relationships

Jeffy was friends with a few other local children, like Chizelo , Dillsandra , and Ahmadora .

History

In Eleint , 1281 DR , Jeffy and a few other children met a group of mercenaries who decided to head with Hrothgar on his expedition to Kuldahar .
