History

After emerging from the tomb of an old Netherese wizard in the Year of the Missing Blade, 759 DR , the Chosen of Mystra , Elminster Aumar , wandered the land using the alias of "Wanlorn". He met the horse breeder Nantlin at some point after that.

At some point prior to the Year of Laughter, 761 DR , Elminster liberated Buckralam's Starn from the bandits of the Iron Fox . After that, he told a local woman, Immeira , to look for the horse breeder Nantlin at Saern Hill . He also told her to ask if his harp sounded as sweet as ever, strongly implying the man was a Harper.
