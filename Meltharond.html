Meltharond was a once-powerful wizard -ruler and former consort and companion of the white dragon Arveiaturace .

Relationships

At some point in their relationship, Meltharond was considered to be Arveiaturace's master. The white dragon served as his aerial steed, and he instructed her in the arcane arts . They became close friends, lovers, and in fact, Meltharond was the only being that Arveiaturace ever truly confided to.

History

Meltharond was once the ruler of Ice Peak , a frigid island south of the Sea of Moving Ice .

He died at some point during or before the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , but his body stayed with Arveiaturace for over a century afterwards. The white dragon refused to accept Meltharond's death, and his frigid remains were bound to his saddle strapped on her back.
