Lady Aelieyeeva was an inhabitant of Cormanthor in the 3 rd century DR .

History

In 261 DR , her business with Coronal Eltargrim Irithyl at court was prematurely concluded due to the intrusion of Llombaerth Starym , who brought Elminster Aumar to the coronal seeking justice for a murder.
