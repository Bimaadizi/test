Zarod was a fallen angel in the Labyrinth in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Zarod's petrified state showed the angel with its face rent and ripped open by its own claws.

History

The gods condemned Zarod and seven other fallen angels to remain as stone statues in the depths of the Labyrinth for unknown crimes.

Powers

Zarod could use divination powers within seven days for people in the vicinity who talked with it.
