Emveolstone Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Emveolstone .

Location

The villa was located in the north section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward on the west side of the Street of the Singing Dolphin where it was intersected by Ivory Street . The west side of the villa also lay on the Street of Whispers .

Description

Four separate structures were found within the walled compound arranged around a central garden that included an elongated pond. The two larger buildings lay at the northern and southern ends of the property. All of the buildings stood two or three stories tall.
