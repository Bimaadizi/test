Noose was a businessman and owner of Noose's Knots in the Lower Trade Lanes of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR ..

History

Once a sailor of the Sword Coast , Noose started a business dealing in rope , nets and knots.

Around 1370 DR , he was famous for demonstrating the good quality of his products by hanging himself.
