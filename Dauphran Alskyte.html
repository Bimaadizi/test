Dauphran Alskyte was a tradelord from Luskan in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . He was a suspect in the murder of Garthin Muirtree .

Personality

The man acted constantly with scorn and superiority. In her short time mentally contacting the man, Alustriel Silverhand determined he was exactly that unpleasant and even worse, being quite violent with women.

Possessions

He carried an amulet capable of blocking truth-telling spells.

Activities

Tradelords arranged trade agreements on behalf of other authorities.

Relationships

He was a sworn foe to many people.

History

Dauphran Alskyte was interrogated by Alustriel Silverhand over Garthin's murder that same day. She determined that he was using an amulet that blocked truth-telling spells, but found his surface thoughts not to be so guarded. She determined he'd stolen figurines from a certain Talanther , but that he was innocent of murder. She soon dismissed and banished the diplomat from her court.
