The Bloodstone Mines were a mine located within the Valley of Bloodstone .

Description

The mine was a large mining complex. It's tunnels were usually 10 feet (3 meters) in height and 15 feet (4.6 meters) tall. The tunnels of the upper parts of the mine were worn with age and often covered in a layer of dust. The tunnels found in the lower parts of the mine better maintained. Meandering irregular circular tunnels with smooth walls were found here.

Geography

The mine was located in the eastern part of Bloodstone Pass . It's three entrances were found within a high canyon.
