N'Jast was a village in Narfell that sat at the crossroads of the Long Road and the Cold Road .

Geography

N'Jast lay in central Narfell. It was east of Jiyyd and south of the Teardrops .

Trade

The village was predominantly a trade outpost. Merchants on the Long Road came from Damara in the west and Rashemen in the east, while merchants on the Cold Road came from the settlements and dwarfholds of the Icerim Mountains to the north and Thesk to the south.

It was an important stopover for both merchants and Nars heading to the Bildoobaris trademeet.

History

As of the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , N'Jast was little more than a waymeet , but it had become a permanent settlement by the late 15 th century DR.
