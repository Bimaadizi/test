Murenstur's Imported Vintages was a wine shop in Suzail , Cormyr , in the late 15 th century DR .

Services

Almost certainly, the store sold vintages.

History

The owner, Belgryn Murenstur , was kept from going back home one evening before the Council of the Dragon , where he was briefly pressed into service by the blueflame ghosts Treth Halonter and Relve Langral while on their murderous rampage.
