Shal was a succubus posing as the female duergar consort of Deepking Horgar Steelshadow V in Gracklstugh in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Shal was loyal to the demon lord Graz'zt , whom she served.

History

Her only mission was to hide the madness that had infected the Deepking circa 1486 DR . She may have been exposed by an adventuring party that had escaped Velkynvelve .

Description

She wore a gown made entirely of gold coins that was valued at 750 gp and weighed 75 pounds (34 kilograms).
