Noose's Knots was a business in the Lower Trade Lanes of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR .

Structure

Noose's Knots was a one-story building in fair condition.

Description

Noose's Knots was owned by veteran sailor known simply as Noose . He had turned his extensive seafaring knowledge of ropes , nets , and knotwork into a thriving business. Known for providing ropes of superior quality, Noose and his small team of skilled hands were regularly found braiding fibers such as hemp into durable rope, charging above-average prices that reflected their craftsmanship.
