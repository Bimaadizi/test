Kartra Boulderstern was a dwarf laborer in Elturel in the late 15 th century DR .

History

After Elturel was transported to Avernus , first layer of the Nine Hells , in the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , many quakes struck the city and three dwarves—Kartra Boulderstern, Strovin Ironfist , and Velkora Ashenwell —became trapped in the rubble of a collapsed building. If they were lucky, they may have been rescued by passing adventurers.
