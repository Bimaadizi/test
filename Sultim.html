Geography

Sultim was located in northern Mulhorand within a region of high plains called the Taranoth . It sat along the coast of the Alamber Sea due west from the Ganathwood .

Inhabitants

The precept of Sultim in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , was a warrior of Horus-Re named Koramon .

Activities

Sultim was a major center of worship for Anhur .

History

The armies of Thay were defeated at Sultim in their first invasion attempt in the Year of the Rose, 1098 DR . Thay's second invasion in the Year of the Manticore, 1280 DR , resulted in their beseiging Sultim and nearly taking the city, but reinforcements arrived in time to save the city.
