The Taeya Bog was a marshland located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The bog was located in northeast Gwynneth along the coast of the Sea of Moonshae and the Strait of Alaron . The Taeya Bog sat at the edge of the Winterglen . The Tunnels of Dwarvenhome ran beneath the bog to connect to the island of Alaron . Roughly 120 square miles (310 square kilometers) were covered by the Taeya Bog.
