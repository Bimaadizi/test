Mharane Featherbone was an elven member of the Griffon Cavalry in the late mid-to-late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Mharane worked as both an animal trainer and breeder for the Griffon Calvary.

History

In 1379 DR , Mharane was one of many Waterdhavians kidnapped and enslaved by Lanador .
