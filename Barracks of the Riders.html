The Barracks of the Riders was the base of operations for the Riders of Mistledale during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The compound was located on the north side of Ashabenford in Mistledale.

Activities

In addition to housing both the Riders of Mistledale and the local militia, the Barracks of the Riders also served as the town jail for Ashabenford.
