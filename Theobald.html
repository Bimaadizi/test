Theobald was a Netherese arcanist who worked at Ioulaum Industries in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Theobald worked under Ghazzalee , and was responsible for the final transportation of mythallar components to the customers at their chosen locations. The goods were extremely valuable, and so he was always on the lookout for experienced and trustworthy fighters to guard the shipments.
