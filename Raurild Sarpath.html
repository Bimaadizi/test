Raurild Sarpath was a merchant working out of Cormyr in the mid-14 th century DR .

Personality

He liked to joke about his wife being very controlling.

Appearance

His beard and moustache were wispy, with small golden rings in them.

Relationships

He was in on Andemel Graeven 's scheme, and had been married for sixteen summers as of 1356 DR .

History

During a revel in 1356 DR , Raurild Sarpath joined them late, after the music had ended, and commented about Andemel Graeven's shop. He also became a witness to Immult Murauvyn trying to collect back taxes on Sabrast Windriver .
