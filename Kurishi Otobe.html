Kurishi Otobe , known as the Dragon Lady , was a human wizard and a member of the Council of Captains of the city of Bral in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Kurishi was cloaked in mystery. Even during most meetings with business partners, she tended to remain hidden in the shadows.

Activities

Kurishi was a speculator and moneylender. She also purchased exotic goods and sold them later for profit. Dozens of loyal followers worked for her, most of them rogues .

Relationships

The Dragon Lady was a rival to the Underbaron Ozamata Ku Murawa , another of the members of the Council of Captains. Lord Chan Fu Wi , the Shou emissary to Bral, chose to ignore her.
