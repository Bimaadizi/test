Zamanyeh bint Salman al-Kahn was a mamluk who lived in Liham in 1367 DR .

Activities

Captain Zamanyeh was in charge of guarding caravanserai from Liham. Those guards under her command were all female mamluks.

Possessions

Zamanyeh owned a khopesh , short sword , and short bow . She wore lamellar armor.
