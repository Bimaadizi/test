The Queen's Fury was the pirate vessel of Catharyn of Dambrath .

Description

The flag of Queen's Fury was a dolphin jumping over a ship. The sight of this flag struck terror into the hearts of any ship on the Great Sea .

Activities

The Queen's Fury was sailed from Dambrath across the Great Sea to raid as far west as Nimbral on the Trackless Sea .
