Tharnadar Edge was a village in Turmish during the late mid-14 th century DR .

History

Thabrant Swordsilver lived in Tharnadar Edge until he returned to Cormyr in the late mid-14 th century DR , after his daughter Caladnei rode there to tell him about the death of Maela Rynduvyn , her mother and his wife.
