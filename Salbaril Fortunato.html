Salbaril Fortunato was the priest of the Lodge of Luck , the shrine of Tymora in Halfhap circa 1369 DR .

Description

He was a handsome young man who seemed to have a perpetual smirking expression. He was ridiculously lucky in all things.

Relationships

He was greatly amused by the hatred the local priest of Tempus Boneblaster had for him.
