Katherine Starlight was the innkeeper in the Evening Star Inn of Freedale in the Dalelands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

She felt her husband spent too long staring at the stars.

Activities

Together with her husband, she ran the Evening Star Inn of Freedale; most of what she did was cooking and cleaning.

Relationships

She was married to Toobar Starlight .
