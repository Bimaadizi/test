Klim Jhasso was a minor noble of the Jhasso family in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , the Jhasso family was on the verge of bankruptcy as their trading coster was struggling and they had very little money. To add more bad luck, Klim was abducted while walking in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate by Dead Three cultists. While imprisoned within the cult's lair in the city, he was tortured by Ygnath while Kazzira watched.
