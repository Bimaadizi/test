Haresk Malorn was the High Councilor and Mistran of the council that ruled over Mistledale , the Dalelands , between 1367 and 1372 DR . He also commanded the Riders of Mistledale , Mistledale's mounted defenders. Despite this, Haresk was known as more of a wise and compassionate merchant rather than a true warrior. Despite this, Haresk was well liked by the people of Mistledale.

The high councilor would carry a black " rod of office ", and there was speculation that Haresk's rod of office was in actuality a rod of rulership . He would very appear at the head of his troops, but if he was required to do so, he would use as much magical armor and protective magic as possible.

The House of the High Councilor was Haresk Malorn's residence and it was the largest building in Ashabenford . It was also used by the Council of Six as a nearly official seat of government.

History

As there were more attacks from the drow as of 1372 DR, Haresk felt that he might have to step down for someone with more military experience.
