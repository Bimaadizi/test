Ayana was a child of Fiona who lived in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Ayana died in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , and her body was buried in the Lower City Graveyard . Fiona, the child's mother, could not thank the mortician Valmorba enough for going above and beyond for the funeral in a letter and sent the Lower City Mortuary gold for the coffin and for Valmorba to bury the girl's beloved teddy bear with her.
