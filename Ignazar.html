History

Tens of thousands of years before the Present Age , General Ignazar fought for Maegera during the Dawn War .

In the late 15 th century DR , Ignazar aligned himself with the mad fire giant Gommoth , the self-styled King of Mount Hotenow and leader of the burgeoning cult of Maegera . Ignazar was granted use of the ancient dwarven stronghold of Fireguard Fortress to use as his own personal keep, to defend Gommoth and his followers as they took action to reawaken the slumbering primordial in the Prime Material plane .
