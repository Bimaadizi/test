The Talons of Timindar were an adventuring company associated with Timindar in Chondath in the early 13 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Bright Star, 1231 DR , the Talons of Timindar explored the overgrown ruins of the city of Ilimar , and encountered nagas , lamia , and giant spiders . Then they unexpectedly discovered Yornar's Trail Companion , a lost holy Mielikkian spellbook , which was hovering in the air in the middle of a tower-top room. Curiously, the whole room was scorched by flame, with broken windows and broken bodies all around, as if blasted by an explosion, possibly a fireball spell.
