The Hillsfar Docks was a small hamlet located outside the walls of Hillsfar on the shore of the Moonsea .

Activities

The hamlet was home to around 200 people around 1367 DR . Several inns and taverns were constructed around the main harbor area which served as the staging area for Hillsfar's water traffic.

Defenses

A force of 100 Red Plumes guarded the docks at any given time. They were stationed in an easily defended stone tower that also doubled as a lighthouse for the harbor.

Rumors & Legends

Spies for the rogues' guild were rumored to lurk in the area.
