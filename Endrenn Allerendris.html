Endrenn Allerendris was an outcast half-elven rogue of Tantras in the Vast in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Born from a drow mother and a high elven father, she always had a penchant for evil and crime, which allowed her to survive in the dark parts of human cities. She ended up haunting the dark streets of Tantras.

Possessions

She wielded a dagger of venom and owned a pouch of accessibility alongside her thieves' tools .
