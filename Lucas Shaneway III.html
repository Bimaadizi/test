Lucas Shaneway III was a knight in the service of the Lords' Alliance in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa 1486 DR , Lucas was sent to Mantol-Derith to help Drizzt and the Heroes of Sleeping Dragon liberate Gauntlgrym . However, he was unprepared to face the Cult of the Eternal Flame and was wounded.
