Nimlith was a small fishing community on the island of Evermeet , consisting of around 200 non-magical wooden structures and inhabited mainly by moon elves (silver elves).

Fishing

The fisherfolk of the town caught hake , salmon , and whitefish from the waters west of the island.

Governance

As of 1368 DR , the town was led by Lady Ialantha Rumithamal , a cousin of Queen Amlaruil Moonflower .

However, as of Alturiak , 1374 DR , the town was governed by Lady Ammisyll Veldann , a member of the Council of Evermeet .

Notable features

The town was home to the Holy Ground tavern, and also the mage Kavrala Nightsong .
