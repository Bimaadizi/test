Alpharra Anabravvur was a famed luxury shipwright from the opulent merchant city of Selgaunt in Sembia , in the 1490s DR .

Description

In her mid-thirties, Alpharra was a good-looking albino woman who was blessed with sharp intellect, a cold calculating personality, wealth, and status.

History

By the late 15 th century DR , Alpharra was the third-generation businesswoman who ran Anabravvur's shipbuilding workshop in Selgaunt. Her vessels were some of the largest and most luxurious ships on the Sea of Fallen Stars .
