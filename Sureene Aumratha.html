Sureene Aumratha was the high priestess of Selûne in Thentia circa 1367 DR and in 1373 DR . She was the head of Thentia's House of the Moon .

Description

As of 1373 DR , Sureene Aumratha was a middle-aged woman, tall, handsome, with hair dyed moon blond.

Activities

Sureene was a shrewd businesswoman, who wisely invested the offerings in the temple in shipbuilding and caravans. She was said to be the wealthiest woman in the city. The Hero of Daggerford may have contracted for temple services from her.

Relationships

She had two deputies, Jannatha Goldenshield and Baerimel Dunnath , both trained by Firefingers .

History

Sureene aided Taegan Nightwind by casting lie-detection spells on the other mages aiding the effort to decrypt Sammaster 's notes on the Rage of Dragons in 1373 DR. She also kept Rilitar Shadow-water comfortable and cared for in his final moments.
