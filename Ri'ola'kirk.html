Ri'ola'kirk was the protege of the eadar of As'arem .

History

His father was killed during the Twelfth Serôs War in a battle to protect the Ys'ivis hatcheries.

As the only heir of the eadar Ri'ola'con following the war, Ri'ola'kirk had to abandon his life as a ranger in order to be instructed by the old shalarin. However, he was often distracted by his affection for the young shalarin dukar Ka'ioa'Tara and his hunts in new waters with tritons and dukars.

Relationships

Ri'ola'kirk was in his younger years in Os'dahr a friend of the new young queen of Es'roch Ri'ena'wyn .
