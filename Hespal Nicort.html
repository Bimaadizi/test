Hespal Nicort was a half-orc commander of the city guard of the city of Suormpar in Ulgarth around 1367 DR , leading 500 footmen and 200 archers. Although half orc, Hespal vehemently hated orcs .

By 1367 DR, warnings circulated that the gray orcs of the Dustwall Mountains were gathering in preparation for a major assault or raids into Ulgarth. Suormpar was directly threatened, and Hespal Nicort would be leading the defense.
