Krak al-Uruj was a qal'at found in the Cities of the Ancients region of east Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Geography

The fortress was located just north of where Al-Sayaj Mountains ended. The fortress occupied a brief expanse of land in far southern reaches of the desert of the Haunted Lands that was sandwiched between the mountains and the jungles of the Cities of the Ancients. It was approximately 45 mi (72 km) west of the Nogaro River .

Activities

Historically, the fortress was a strategic location to guard against any attackers skirting Al-Sayaj Mountains into the Cities of the Ancients. It was unoccupied as of 1367 DR.
