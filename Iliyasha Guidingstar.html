History

When the fomorian king Malabog threatened the elves of New Sharandar circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Iliyasha was one of the envoys who traveled to the Neverwinter to recruit allies from among its residents. She worked with some adventurers to fight back against the trolls and dark fey creatures that continued to plunder the ruins of Old Sharandar, before they ventured into her home in the Feywild .
