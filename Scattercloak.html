Scattercloak was a mage in Thentia in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

As of 1373 DR , Scattercloak wore always a gray mantle with the hood up.

History

Scattercloak was one of the mages in Thentia assisting Karasendrieth 's party in decoding Sammaster 's notes about the Rage of Dragons in 1373 DR , and one of the people who helped slay Phourkyn One-Eye 's impersonator. When in that fight, he cast spellstrike to reverse the wyrm's escape.
