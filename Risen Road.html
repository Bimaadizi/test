The Risen Road was a long road that connected the metropolis of Baldur's Gate with the city of Elturel .

Geography

The road was located north of the River Chionthar , running parallel with its route.

Notable Locations

History

As of the late 15 th century DR , many travelers preferred the road over traveling the Chionthar by river barge.

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a number of githyanki and their red dragon steed blocked passageway across the road as they searched for the Astral Prism , an artifact lost to their people.
