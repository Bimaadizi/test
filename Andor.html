Andor was a member of the criminal underworld of Scornubel . He was replaced by a shapeshifter in 1357 DR .

Relationships

Either Andor or a shapeshifter taking his identity were people Tornar wanted to see dead in late 1357 DR .

History

Andor's body was found tangled up in the nets of Ornrim in mid-to-late 1357 DR , a fact known to few other than Belgon Bradasker . The latter related unto Tornar that Andor had been replaced by a shapeshifter.
