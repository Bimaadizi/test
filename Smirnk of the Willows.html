Smirnk of the Willows was a hermit and wiseman who lived on Hokla Mountain in Tabot .

Smirnk was one of only five wisemen recognized by the monks of Tabot around Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) as being a true oracle, being capable of predicting the future and raising heroes from the dead for good causes.
