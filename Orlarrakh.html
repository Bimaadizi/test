Orlarrakh , also known as The Green Duchess , was the ruler of the Dusk Ports in Laerakond as of 1479 DR . She was a vassal of Melauthaur , the ruler of Melabrauth .

Personality

Orlarrakh was known for not forgetting any slight against her. After visiting the cities of the Dusk Ports, it was normal that its inhabitants had to elect a new ruler. She was also cruel and enjoyed eating the inhabitants of her lands.
