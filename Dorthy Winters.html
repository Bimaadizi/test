Dorthy Winters was the daughter of Beartrid Winters and Kalas Winters , who worked at the Wolf's Pelt Inn in the Icewind Dale community of Targos circa 1369 DR .

Description

Dorthy was a beautiful and charismatic 17-year-old young woman. She was quite short compared to most humans of the region, being 5 feet and 5 inches (1.7 meters) tall and weighing 110 pounds (50 kilograms). She had long black hair and big brown eyes.

Personality

Dorthy was quite strong-willed and always achieved her goals and needs. Her parents put her to work in the kitchen to be able to keep an eye on her. That did not stop Dorthy from sneaking out at every opportunity she got.

Possessions

She carried her trusty kitchen knife with her.

History

Around Tarsakh , 1369 DR , Dorthy lived with her family in the Ten Towns and constantly tried to slip out of the kitchen to avoid work.
