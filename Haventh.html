Lord Haventh was a wine merchant and one of the members of the wealthy high society of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

At some point, Lord Haventh set his eyes and amorous ambitions on Lady Firelia Jannath , one of the city's patriars and a notable patroness of the arts.
