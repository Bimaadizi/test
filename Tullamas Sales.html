Tullamas Sales was a general goods store owned by Orloro Tullamas located in Tulmon along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The shop was located on Shandlas Street in Tulmon.

Activities

The shop carried a wide variety of dry goods including anything from " gloves to grain and candles to carts " according to the shop's owner.
