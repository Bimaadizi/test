The Shipgrave Isles were a small island chain off the coast of Halruaa in the Great Sea .

Geography

The Shipgrave Isles were about 100 to 200 miles (160 to 320 kilometers) south of Halruaa in the Great Sea, depending on the map being used.

Defenses

As of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , these islands were surrounded by the Mlossae nation of sea elves . They were very militant in their outlook, known to attack passing ships.
