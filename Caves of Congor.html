The Caves of Congor were an infamous network of caves on the side of a limestone cliff in the Kingdom of Mertwig .

History

These caves had a long history of being used as a hideout by brigands, thieves , and various villainous types.

When King Mertwig laid on his deathbed, his would-be heirs were forced to traverse through this dangerous locale along their journey, encountering both unexpected dangers and treasures.
