Lyrna Aster was a halfling rogue in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Lyrna had tanned skin, brown eyes, and dark brown hair tied back in a bun. She wore a suit of patchwork leather armor and had a pair of fancy daggers .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Lyrna joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their base camp at the ruins of Myth Drannor . She served as a trainer for prospective sneaks and thieves at the camp.
