Borlyth was once a fishing hamlet on Cloak Bay of the Sword Coast North that turned into a monster-infested ruin.

Description

Borlyth stood on a sheltered anchorage immediately west of the Cloakwood , on the land bordering the forest and Borlyth Bay .

History

By the late 14 th century DR , Borlyth was abandoned by its former inhabitants who fled from pirate raids and Cloakwood monster attacks. The ruined village was infested with monsters and its shipyard was left in ruins.
