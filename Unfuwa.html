Unfuwa was a gnome sea mage who sailed upon the Ebony Queen in 1367 DR .

Activities

She was in the employ of Captain Soot .

Abilties

Spells known by Unfuwa included charm person , sea sight , wall of fog , ESP , true bearing , and stone hull . She was also knowledgeable about genie culture.
