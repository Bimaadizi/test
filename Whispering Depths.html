The Whispering Depths was a treacherous cave system found on the Sword Coast . It was home to a great many giant spiders , and a single arachnid that claimed to be their queen.

Geography

The caves were found immediately beneath the village of Moonhaven . They were in face accessible by well found in the village's center square.

The furthers reaches of the depths housed an especially deep chasm that led into the Underdark below.

Geographical Features

The Whispering Depths featured rocky ledges and pathways that extended over the cavern floor. Some rock faces were jagged enough that they could be climbed upon to reach greater heights. Massive webs covered large swaths of the caves.

Flora & Fauna

The spiders of the Whispering Depths captured many humanoids and bound them in body-sized web sacs.

History

At some point, a single individual came to take up residence within the Whispering Depths. They were a worshipper of Lolth and were utterly facsinated with arachnids and all types of spider-kind. After they came across the body of a drow arachnomancer , they realized their single meaningful purpose in life: to transform into a spider themself.

Rumors & Legends

Hidden within the Whispering Depths was the large, dark amethyst that could be used to unlock The Necromancy of Thay .
