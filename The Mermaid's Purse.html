The Mermaid's Purse was the best inn in Corth , Aglarond , around 1368 DR , operated by the ex-fisherman Droze .

Structure

It was constructed in the typical Aglarondan architectural style, made from weathered wood with a steep roof and dormers. It was built with three stories. The rooms offered were small but comfortable.

Services

The inn served local fish and sometimes beef or vegetables.
