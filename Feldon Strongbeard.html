Feldon Strongbeard was a dwarf who lived in the Protector's Enclave within Neverwinter in 1484 DR .

History

He was a merchant member of the Harpers during the Tyranny of Dragons and helped equipped the adventurers sent to fight the Cult of the Dragon .

At the climax of the resurrection of Tiamat , Feldon went to the Well of Dragons to offer support.
