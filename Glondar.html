Glondar was a Zhentilar of the mid-14 th century DR .

Relationships

He was part of a squad led by Fimril .

History

In late 1357 DR , Glondar was part of the squad led by Fimril that briefly captured Shandril Shessair at Hullack Forest . He was slain by Mirt , who came to her rescue.
