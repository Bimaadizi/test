Penruddock was a Netherese priest who was a librarian at the Library of Netheril in the enclave of Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Penruddock worked alongside Vincent and Linden to catalogue the texts of the library, and help visitors find them on demand.
