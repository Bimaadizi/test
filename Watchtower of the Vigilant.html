The Watchtower of the Vigilant was a temple dedicated to Helm and located in Harkstag during the 13 th century DR .

History

The Watchtower was a small temple located in a small village near Serpent's Cowl . It came to prominence during the Year of Flowers, 1265 DR , for hosting the Council of Helm that resulted in the creation of the Vigilant Riders .

The temple was destroyed alongside the rest of the village during a green dragon attack in the Year of the Snarling Dragon, 1279 DR . During the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , its ruins served as a hideout for yuan-ti of Najara .
