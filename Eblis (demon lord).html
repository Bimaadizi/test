Eblis of the Unbended Knee was a demon lord associated with the refusal to surrender, who lived on the layer of Pazunia in the Abyss ].

Realm

Eblis was known to have an iron fortress on Pazunia in which the demon lord left their physical body while astrally traveling. It was guarded by ten mariliths .
