Bran was a grain-producing crop found in Faerûn .

Availability

Bran was cultivated in ancient Netheril alongside wheat , spelt , timothy , rye , and oats .

Ecology

Bran was affected by the wheat rust blight, which destroyed the plants and their grains during the time of Netheril. The spreading disease was cured in 3165 NY (−694 DR) by the arcanist Candlemas , who used a complex combination of magics to produce a cure and save the crops.
