OccupationAuthor, scholar

Bracetar was an author and scholar writing in or before the 14 th century DR .

History

By the 1370s DR, Bracetar's work Notes On Preservation of Foodstuffs and Oils was amongst the works in Candlekeep .

Rumors & Legends

In the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Noumea Cardellith , pretending to be a merchant from Lantan , claimed to be interested in Bracetar's Notes On Preservation of Foodstuffs and Oils when seeking admittance to Candlekeep.
