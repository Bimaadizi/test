The Hall of the Enlightened One was a shrine and part of the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 th century DR .

Description

The Hall was located on the ground level of the temple on the right side of the Grand Stairs . The Hall itself was roughly 40 feet (12 meters) long while the chamber at the end of the Hall measured 25 feet (7.6 meters) wide by 20 feet (6.1 meters) long.

A trio of statues of the Enlightened One were found within the chamber, though the heads of each statue had been shattered by the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR . Frescoes adorned the walls of the chamber, but these had been defaced. A single small altar to Li Pei sat upon the chamber's central dais.
