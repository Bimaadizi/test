C'Saxah ( pronounced : /ˈ k ɪ s ɑː ks ə / KISS-ax-uh ) was one of the nine towns of the sahuagin kingdom of Kurrimal and its predecessor Aleaxtis .

Geography

This town was located within the Alamber Sea , in the Pythan Trench .

Government

Like all nine of the towns of Kurrimal, this town was governed by a prince of the same name, C'Saxah , who was the son of the last king of Aleaxtis.

History

C'Saxah was found in the Year of the Unforgotten Fire, 319 DR , though under a different name. In the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , when the kingdom of Kurrimal was founded as the successor of Aleaxtis, this town's name was changed to that of the prince put in charge of governing it.
