Kutson was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age and Age of Discovery .

History

This arcanist developed three spells during their life. The spells they invented were programmed illusion in 2618 NY (−1241 DR) , antipathy/sympathy in 2645 NY (−1214 DR) , and finally, mind blank in 2685 NY (−1174 DR) .
