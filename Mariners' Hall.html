The Mariners' Hall was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Master Mariners' Guild .

Location

This building was located along Cedar Street , just off the Way of the Dragon , in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

Mariners' Hall stood three-stories tall. The building was in a wattle-and-daub style and its roof featured a minaret.

Interior

The main floor of the Mariners' Hall featured full tavern facilities, a private bar for visiting members of the Master Mariners' Guild. Elsewhere in the building were accommodations, also for visiting members of the guild.

Reputation

The Mariners' Hall was among some of the more notable and recognizable buildings in the Dock Ward, considered by locals to be a landmark.

Trivia

Behind a loose stone in the minaret atop this roof was a cavity where the wizard Coril hid their spellbooks .
