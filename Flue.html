Flue was a Netherese shipwright who helped oversee Gallery Steamers in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Flue served Stuckley as a high-ranking supervisor on the construction of flying ships used in Netheril . She worked alongside Rousselet , and had Apulee , Fourcrow , Gau , and Bruck directly managing the work crews.
