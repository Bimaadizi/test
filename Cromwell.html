Cromwell was one of the best blacksmiths of Athkatla in Amn . His forge was located to the south-east of the Docks District.

Description

Cromwell was a master blacksmith, proficient in crafting many types of magical armor, as well as a multitude of weapons. Among . He did not have an apprentice to help him at his forge, so would often ask the customer themselves to provide assistance. If the customer wished to have a unique magical piece of equipment made from parts, they would often have to stay a few days, manning Cromwell's forge.

He could make plate mail out of animal carapaces, such as ankheg plate mail .
