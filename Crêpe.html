Crêpes were a type of dessert dish found in the Cormyrean town of Immersea .

Availability

Crêpes were a common dish, by standards of nobility, served in the Redstone Castle of Immersea in the 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , a noble scion of House Wyvernspur , Giogioni Wyvernspur , and his then-new acquaintance Cat , were served a meal of cheese fondue , venison broth with noodles , fish poached in wine , and crêpes with boysenberry jam. Giogi was unimpressed with the meal but his guest, unaccustomed to such feasts, was delighted by each of the courses served by Thomas , young Wyvernspur's manservant.
