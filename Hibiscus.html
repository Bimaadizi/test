Hibiscus were hardy flowers from which tea could be made.

Ecology

These flowering plants could be found in Halruaa , the mountains of Chessenta , the Utter East , and Zakhara .

Uses

Hibiscus were sometimes used as decorative flowers. They were cultivated in the Public Gardens of the City of Delights in Zakhara alongside roses and similar blooms.

A spicy hibiscus tea was said to be widely popular in Chessenta as of the late 15 th century DR .
