The Net of Pearls was a curios store in Marsember , Cormyr .

Atmosphere

It was owned and operated by Tannuth Ormbyr . It sold all kinds of antiques and bric-a-brac, such as statues, glassware, Purple Dragon arms and armor, Chessentan items and all the materials necessary for fishing.
