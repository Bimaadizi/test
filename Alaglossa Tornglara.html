Lady Akaglossa Tornglara was an inhabitant of Cormanthor in the 3 rd century DR . She was involved in the raising of the Mythal .

History

In 241 DR , during a party at Satyrdance Pool , she and a number of other ladies shared concerns about the future of Cormanthor; in particular, she believed it might be destroyed within her lifetime. They then agreed to try and nudge their husbands towards stabilizing the city. After the hunt for armathor Elminster Aumar , she was discussing their successes in the matter, when they were stopped by Elandorr Waelvor ; he implied strongly that he would harm them if they got in his way.

In 261 DR , during the laying of the Mythal , Alaglossa Tornglara was briefly frightened by the negative reaction of her servant, Nlaea , who appeared to come to harm by the Mythal' s power. However, she ultimately was a contributor to the energy that the Mythal required.

Rumors

Alaglossa Tornglara was said to scry distant cities to discover new fashions.
