Len-jes was the harbormaster of the Docks District of Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

Description

Len-jes was known for her scars.

Personality

Len-jes appeared to be calm and collected, but actually the fury of the Elemental Chaos burned in her.

Activities

Len-jes was Neverwinter's habormaster, chief exchequer and accountant. As the master of trade of Neverwinter, she was vital to keep commerce flowing into the City.

Abilities

Len-jes was a "hydromancer", and so her abilities were stronger that those of the standard watersoul genasi . She had a reputation of being a powerful combatant.

History

Years before 1479 DR , Len-jes was a corsair in the Sea of Fallen Stars . However, her enemies forced her to flee and hide in Waterdeep . In the City of Splendors, Len-jes was contacted by Dagult Neverember , who hired her to be the harbormaster of Neverwinter.

In 1479 DR, she became a semi-permanent resident of the Beached Leviathan .

During the civil unrest caused by the supposed " Lost Heir of Alagondar ", Len-jes took sides with Neverember's loyalists.

Rumors

People were unsure of Len-jes true allegiances. Many believed she was working undercover for the Thayans , the Ashmadai , or for pirates from Laerakond , while others believed she was a Harper agent instead. For other people, however, she was just a power-hungry lackey of Neverember.
