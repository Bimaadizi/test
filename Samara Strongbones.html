Samara Strongbones was a Zhentarim spy that supported Manshoon 's bid to take the organization over. In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , she was held prisoner in Xanathar's lair , where she was pitted against the minotaur Umpok in the Pit of Blood and Fortune .
