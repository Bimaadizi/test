Hatit Abd al-Wajib was the emir of Qudra and commander of the Dutiful Mamluk Society in 1367 DR .

Description

Hatit was clean-shaven. His face was tattooed with the marks of the Dutiful.

Activities

Hatit's duties as emir required that he did not take an active role in the Mamluk Council .
