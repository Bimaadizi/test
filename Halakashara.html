Halakashara was a magical , +2 broadsword that originated from the elven city of Myth Drannor .

Description

Halakashara was a finely crafted blade with the image of the sun on its pommel.

Powers

The blade of this sword was particularly damaging to the undead , becoming a temporary +3 broadsword , and could outright destroy them upon upon contact.

History

The broadsword once belonged to a long-forgotten warrior in Myth Drannor. It was eventually found by Prince Lamruil Moonflower during one of his excursions into the ruins.
