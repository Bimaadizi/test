Dounalis Guff was the High Priest of Tempus at the Vault of Swords in Hillsfar . He also served as the chaplain for the city's military force, the Red Plumes .

A gruff and loud man in his mid-forties by 1374 DR , Dounalis believed that salvation from cowardice could only be found through death on the battlefield.
