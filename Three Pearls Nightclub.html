The Three Pearls Nightclub was a festhall and nightclub located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building was a single story tall.

Location

The Three Pearls Nightclub was located in the eastern section of the Dock Ward on Pearl Alley just east of Snail Street .
