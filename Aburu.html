Aburu was a camel living in Marigold's Menagerie in the mid-14 th century DR .

Behavior

Aburu was rather arrogant, had a foul temper, and would spit at whoever passed her by.

History

Before being moved to Marigold's Menagerie, Aburu worked as an animal performer at the Mooney & Sons Circus . Being such a hassle to take care of, Marigold Mufflefoot was in search of a trustworthy individual to take the camel back to the desert land she had originated from.
