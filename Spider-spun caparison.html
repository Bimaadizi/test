The spider-spun caparison was an extraordinary spidersilk outfit found in the Demonweb Pits in the 15 th century DR .

Description

The caparison was all-black except for a dark red waistwrap. It was durable, flexible, and almost weightless.

Powers

The spider-spun caparison was best suited to monks , who benefited in their mastery of unarmed combat and use of ki while wearing it.

History

This outfit was found during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar —when Ymris the yugoloth worked with Cormyrean forces to invade the Realm of Ruin and kill Lolth 's servant Kishnaurac . Along the way he may have summoned a group of arcanaloth reinforcements—only to have their services be out-bid by Kishnaurac and turned against him. The spider-spun caparison may have been taken from these overly-opportunistic fiends .
