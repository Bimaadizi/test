Uramyl Widemeadow was an elf cook in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Uramyl had short white hair and brown eyes.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Uramyl joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their forward camp outside the ruins of Myth Drannor . She worked there at a field kitchen, feeding and watering the expedition.
