Filidar was a prince of Turmish who lived long before the 14 th century DR .

Known as a glutton, Filidar had such unflattering nicknames as Filidar the Fat, Filidar the Fool, and Filidar the Fop. The name survived in Turmic and Common as filidar , meaning a dainty food item, picnic, or feast.
