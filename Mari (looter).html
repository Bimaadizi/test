Mari was a looter who was under the employ of Gimblebock .

Description

The woman wore a cuirass . She fought with a yew longbow and Stygian arrows , and kept her thieves' tools close.

History

In the 1490s DR , Mari and her fellow looter, Barton , had been trying to break into a crypt for many days.
