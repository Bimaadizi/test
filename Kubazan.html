Kubazan ( pronounced : /ˈ k uː b ɑː z ɑː n / KOO-bah-zahn ) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult . He was a primal spirit that took the form of a froghemoth .

Personality

Kubazan was wild, free-spirited, and adventurous. He was unafraid to take great risks.
