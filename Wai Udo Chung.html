Wai Udo Chung was a member of the Wai Clan of T'u Lung around the Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) , as well as a cousin to the nation's then-emperor, Shin Gisen .

History

Chung lived in a mansion in the great T'u Lung city of Balanzia , a century prior to its destruction. This mansion was located just a bit north of the castle of Balanzia's ruling family, the E'tuing clan .
