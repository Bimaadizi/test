Imre was a lesser-known Imaskari artificer wizard .

Description

Imre was a brown-haired man of unremarkable looks, often wearing ankle-length robes .

Personality

Imre delighted in spell innovation and crafting, putting little stock in political importance, and was a teacher to many.

Abilities

Imre was one of the earliest specialists in the school of transmutation in the record.

Possessions

Imre bore a satchel that hovered around shoulder-level. In the satchel, he carried tea -making equipment, tools, a dereth (the Imaskari word for a clipboard), scrolls , inks , and quills .

History

Between −7979 DR and −7975 DR , Imre was involved in the raising of Inupras .
