Flar Oldbottle's Nets, Ropes, Cables, Hawsers, and Cords was a warehouse and a shop in the 14 th century DR .

Location

Flar Oldbottle's Nets and a shop attached to its northern wall, a small shack called Jumble's Fresh Fish , was located on the eastern side of the canal that connected the Basin with the Dragonmere lake on the southern side of Suzail. It stood to the northwest of Silverscales Street . Exterior

Flar Oldbottle's warehouse was a thick, long, two-storied building with a hammerbeam wooden loft and slate roof perched atop.

History

Flar Oldbottle's Nets, Ropes, Cables, Hawsers, and Cords once belonged to a wealthy hin investor from Suzail named Imprur Jusk , sometime before the late 14 th century DR. The halfling was the person who discovered Jusk's Stroll gate hidden behind his warehouse.
