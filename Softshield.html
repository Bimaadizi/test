Softshield was an ointment that helped prevent contracting a disease.

Description

Softshield was thick paste with a strong odor made by the sorceress and alchemist Angrath Dree from herbs and saps found in northwest Faerûn . It could be purchased from her at Old Angrath's Smokehouse in Red Larch , or possibly from secondary vendors elsewhere. The usual price for a jar of ointment was 10 gp .

Powers

If applied to the skin within ten minutes, softshield slightly improved the chance of resisting disease. After ten minutes, there was no benefit.
