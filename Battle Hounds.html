The Battle Hounds was a mercenary company led by Rathgar Malynd , that operated out of Tarmalune , one of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond as of 1479 DR .

Background

The Battle Hounds provided guards for shops, homes, and warehouses as well as personal security to all paying customers.
