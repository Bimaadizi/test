Kerim Najar was a powerful dwarven sha'ir who lived in the Court District of Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

Kerim was famous in the Court District for building his tower in a single night, an event that sparked many bets on exactly how many genies were used to construct the tower so quickly.
