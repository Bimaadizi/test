Whim Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Whim Street was located in the southeast section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . The road passed roughly east to west, branching from Sul Street in the east to eventually merge with the Sutherlane after turning southwest. Mendever Street also connected with Whim Street.
