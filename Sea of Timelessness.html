The Sea of Timelessness was a divine realm within the Elemental Plane of Water belonging to Istishia , the power of elemental water .

Cosmography

This realm existed within the Great Wheel cosmological model.

Rumors & Legends

In the Waveriding ceremony performed by Istishia's clergy, water elementals were said to bring the gifts bestowed upon them back to this realm.
