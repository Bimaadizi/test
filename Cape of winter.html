Capes of winter were enchanted cloaks that were commonly used by worshipers of Auril and residents of the Frozenfar region.

Description

Capes of winter were snow-while and trimmed with white fur of arctic beasts.

Powers

These capes were enchanted to give the wearer resistance to cold weather and cold magic, allowing them to survive even the harshest storms of the north. Additionally, these capes gave the wearer improved resistances to negative effects of magical and mundane nature.

History

In late 14 th century DR the capes of winter were commonly found among the possession of residents of far northern regions, frost giants , and other humanoid worshipers of Auril.
