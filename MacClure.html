Mac Clure was a gnome thief inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Mac Clure excelled at comprehending languages and his sense of hearing was finely tuned. He wished to sign on with a local adventuring company but not venture too far from Daggerford. His home was located on the Farmer's Road and Fields Lane near the rear of the Grand Playhouse .
