Noble Artificer Yanneth Kerigg was the high priest of the House of Wonders , a temple dedicated to Gond , in Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR .

Activities

Yanneth was the leader of Gond's faithful in Urmlaspyr. His congregation within the temple consisted of 16 other priests and 6 followers. Yanneth was personally known for his exquisite crafstmanship for weapons.
