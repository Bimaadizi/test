Kerr Halfhand owned a warehouse in the Lower Port of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Around 1365 DR , Kerr won the warehouse during a gambling game, but he never cared much about it. Around 1370 DR , only desperate individuals used his warehouse.
