Invadiah was the most powerful erinyes in the Nine Hells and was the leader of Glasya 's elite erinyes guard, the Pradixikai .

Relations

She had fifty-eight erinyes daughters one cambion daughter and a cambion son named Lorcan , whom she regarded as worthless.

History

In the Year of the Dark Circle, 1478 DR , Invadiah sought to carry out Glasya's plot to trick Asmodeus into fighting the Abolethic Sovereignty . Unfortunately for her, the plot unraveled, primarily due to betrayal by the succubus Rohini .
