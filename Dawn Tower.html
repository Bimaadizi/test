The Dawn Tower was a temple dedicated to Lathander located in the city of Ordulin in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Inhabitants

The temple was run by High Morninglord Ombital Duskroon . Ombital was assisted by 19 other priests and 17 followers.
