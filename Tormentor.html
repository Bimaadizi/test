A Tormentor was a four-wheeled infernal war machine that could rapidly transport small teams and carry a moderate amount of cargo.

Description

A Tormentor had four bladed iron wheels. It was designed for raiding and scouting.

Abilities

The blades of a Tormentor's wheels could damage other vehicles or standing creatures when driving it close to them. It could carry up to 500 pounds (230 kilograms) in cargo.
