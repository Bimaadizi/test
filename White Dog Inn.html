The White Dog Inn was an inn in the T'u Lung city of Balanzia around Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) .

Location

This inn was located in the southern portion of Balanzia, down the road from the homes and warehouse that were owned by a man named Xu .

History

Around Shou Year 2403 (1153 DR) , the White Dog was one of many buildings that were left intact in Balanzia as the city fell at the hands of the Shou Lung invasion of T'u Lung. Centuries later, around Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) , the building that once housed the White Dog Inn still stood within the abandoned cityand was recorded as such on a map made of the city in that year.
