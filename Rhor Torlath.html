King Rhor Torlath "the Lackey" was an unfortunate Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty.

Relationships

Rhor was the bastard son of King Imnel IV .

History

Rhor was installed on the throne in the Year of the Dawndance, 1095 DR , following the assassinations of King Imnel IV and King Niskam . Rhor was intended to be a puppet used by the Braethar merchant house, but their plans fell apart when he was in turn assassinated by their rivals, the Villax merchant house. They installed the distantly-related Dornak Torlath as a puppet.
