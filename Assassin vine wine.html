TypeWine

Assassin vine wine was a wine brewed from the berries of the murderous assassin vine .

Description

The berries were tough and bitter, yet hearty. They made for a heady wine.

History

Five casks of assassin vine wine were kept by the Sharran cultists at the Lost Refuge in the Vast Swamp in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR . It was rather cheap.
