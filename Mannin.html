Mannin the Stout was a mercenary officer in Westgate .

Activities

Mannin was one of the field officers of the Mercenaries' Guild of Westgate . He was responsible for discipline and training, together with Losifan Urdo . Mannin was a pragmatical and harsh commander.

possessionss

He wore an enchanted chain mail +3 , and fought with an enchanted greataxe +4 .

History

In his youth, Mannin was a duelist, fighting in various arenas in the Vilhon Reach . His years as a gladiator meant that his fighting style, were somewhat flamboyant, but he would still train extremly hard. He joined the Guild as a recruit but impressed Palla so much that she hired him permanently.
