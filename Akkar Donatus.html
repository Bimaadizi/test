Father Akkar Donatus was a priest of Ilmater residing in the city of Zazesspur in 1370 DR .

Activities

Daily, Father Akkar led the roughly 60 clerics and 200 laypersons in sacred rituals at the House of Tears , a temple to Ilmater and local chapter house for the Order of Ilmater's Brethren .
