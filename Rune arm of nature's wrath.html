The rune arm of nature's wrath was a rune arm -like implement found in Spiderhaunt Wood in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The rune arm strengthened artificers bold enough to use it, improving the force and physical power of their abilities. It also produced swarms of magical bees that harried and stung targets.

History

The branches that made up the artifact were from the Tangled Trees and witnessed the fall of the green dragons Venomindhar and Venominhandar , and carried a murderous and vengeful spite from before the beginning of Dale Reckoning . They ended up in the hoard of the fang dragon Nylerian , who laired in the Spiderhaunt Wood with ettercaps around the time the Elves of Cormanthor and Elven Crusade reclaimed Myth Drannor .
