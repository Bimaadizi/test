Teshaurus was a skull lord who was a member of the Dominion of Bone in 1479 DR .

History

Teshaurus acted as the "ambassador" of Prince Nathur Hethkantan in Glaur , dealing with the genasi of the Dominion of the Burned , or with adventurers or treasure hunters who were exploring the fortress. Teshaurus invited those explorers who he deemed particularly interesting to meet Prince Nathur in the Dominion of Bone.
