The Khoroshen was a tribe of lizardfolk who inhabited the low marshes of the Evermoors .

History

In 1372 DR , First Speaker Tessarin Alaurin of the city of Nesmé recruited the tribe to help defend the city against troll and giant attacks.

Organization

The tribe was led by Uhndek , and most only spoke Draconic .

The Viperkin were a group of Khoroshen who carried shields adorned with a snake-like design, from which they derived their name.
