Marcus was a middle-aged fisherman from the Chionthar Valley , whose life was cut short in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Marcus was an aging human man with a balding head of red hair. At the time of his death, Marcus wore a blue tunic and simple short pants.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a spelljammer squid ship crashed into the shores of the River Chionthar . Marcus along with several other poor souls, witnessed the crash and rushed towards the site, looking to help the survivors. He saw a beautiful black-haired elven lass run from the crash, one of the survivors – the woman named Shadowheart . Unfortunately, the elf was not the only one to survive. While Marcus was distracted by a beautiful woman, he was jumped and murdered by intellect devourers who were onboard the magical vessel. The fisherman's body was left on the beach by the burning spelljammer .
