Alian was a dwarf innkeep inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Alian was the owner of the Dragonback Inn . The inn catered mostly to Daggerford's farmers, who spent months at a time tending their crops. The Dragonback Inn was located in the northwest corner of Daggerford along Wall Street , Fields Lane , and Horse Way .

History

Alian enjoyed the life of an adventurer before retiring and buying the Dragonback Inn.

Relationships

Alian lived on the second floor of the Dragonback Inn with his girlfriend and a pet dire wolf hybrid named Sheeba .
