The Mermaid's Bosom was a large vile inn, even by orc standards, found in the dock section of Hillsfar around 1367 DR . Maalthiir 's hoped visitors to the city would stop here first and kill each other, saving the Red Plumes the effort.

Description

Blood, ale , and saliva made the floor slick in the Mermaid's Bosom.

Location

The inn was situated on the coast of the Moonsea near the main docks.

Services

A room could be had for 3 gp per night. Murder and robbery were common occurrences in the Mermaid's Bosom.
