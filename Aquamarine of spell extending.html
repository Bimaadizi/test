An aquamarine of spell extending was an enchanted aquamarine gem mounted within an hourglass made of gold and silver .

Powers

Once per day, the wielder of the aquamarine could extend the duration of any of the spells they cast, of 6 th level or lower. Those who did not prepare their spells the day before they were cast had to take extra time casting for this to take effect.
