Galduck was a wealthy merchant inhabiting the Shining South during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Galduck lived in the capital city of Pyratar in Var the Golden within the Shining Lands . He was a member of Chaka Semanra and a member of the Shining Lands' ruling Council of Merchants .
