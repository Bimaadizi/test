Wine boxes of holding were a variety of bags of holding created and enchanted to be used for wine bottles exclusively.

Description

Wine boxes of holding were beautifully decorated oaken containers. Their wooden surfaces were a work of art, being lovingly carved with images of plant life, leaves, and grapevines .

Powers

When opened, a wine box of holding appeared to only have space to hold a single bottle of wine; however, upon closer examination, it was obvious that its enchantments allowed for six bottles. Only one bottle was visible in the box, and once it was removed, the next stored bottle took its place.
