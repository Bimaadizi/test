The Shrouded Lands was a stretch of land located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Shrouded Lands were wedged between the Highlands and Meirig Peatlands in western Gwynneth. This region of hills and plains stretched for nearly 50 miles (80 kilometers) between the Toadstool Thicket in the coast of Corwell Firth to the southeast.
