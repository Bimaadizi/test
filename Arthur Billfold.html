Arthur Billfold was an art connoisseur who once lived in the city of Baldur's Gate , sometime in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Arthur died some time between the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR and the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . He was buried in a small wealthy graveyard located by the Lower City Mortuary in the Lower City on the inner side of the city walls, northwest from the Basilisk Gate . His well-kept headstone read: "Here lies Arthur Billfold, dedicated patron of unconventional arts."
