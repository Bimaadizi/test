Oakarm was an enormous and generally belligerent wood woad that wandered the northern King's Forest during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Oakarm was seen in the northern eaves of the King's Forest near the High Road in the mid-1480s DR, around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar .
