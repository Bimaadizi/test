The sahuagin language was the racial language of the sahuagin .

Speakers

Beyond sahuagin themselves, this was a common secondary language was among sea elves of the Inner Sea and Outer Sea, even though the two races were typically mortal enemies.

Some of the individual specialty priests of aquatic deities were known to learn various other aquatic languages, such as Sahuagin. These included the sea druids of Deep Sashelas , the waveservants of Umberlee , and the waterwalkers of Istishia . It was also a common secondary language among jungle druids , specialty priests in the Church of Ubtao , who sought to learn the exotic languages of the creatures that lived around the Chultan Peninsula .

Phonology

The language of the sahuagins was difficult for most surface dwellers to understand without some magical aid, as it involved a combination of body language and sounds made through the gills.
