Spirali was a Maztican drow . He was one of the Ancient Ones .

History

In 1361 DR , a prophecy indicated that a young girl, Erixitl , would be the doom of the Ancient Ones. So Spirali was sent to find and kill her, while leading a pack of hell hounds .

At last, he found her in Payit but before Spirali could kill her, the drow and his hell hounds were killed by Halloran of the Golden Legion and the Eagle Knight Poshtli .
