Trollfort was a fortification in what later became known as the Field Ward of Waterdeep .

Location

The structure stood on Trollyard courtyard, directly across from the Trolltower .

Structure

This structure stood four stories tall and was directly connected to the city walls that surrounded the Field Ward.
