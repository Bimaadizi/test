TypeChoker
OriginDomains of Dread

The apocalypse choker was a unique neckwear worn by a long-forgotten legendary sorceress in the Domains of Dread , long before the 15 th century DR .

Powers

When worn, the apocalypse choker greatly boosted the wearer's vitality and combat prowess, both offensive and defensive. When the choker was used in tandem with two other items in the set, apocalypse bindings and apocalypse dagger , the damage dealt to one's enemies with the dagger produced painful bleeding cuts that slowly sapped their vitality.

History

The apocalypse magic items set's history was lost to antiquity, and its last wearer was a legendary sorceress. Despite the history and even the sorceress' name being long-forgotten, the tales of the apocalypse choker were widespread. The stories claimed that the choker had the ability to summon forth the bringers of the apocalypse. But others said "it probably just called your mom , or maybe not."
