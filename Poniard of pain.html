The poniard of pain was a magical poniard dagger held by Karissa the Knife , Master Thief of the Night Brigade in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The blade left particularly deep wounds and chilled the victim with a numbing cold, but its true value was in its adaptive nature. It innately targeted the vulnerabilities of those it struck, hitting angels as if evil , demons as if good , fey as if cold iron , werewolves as if silver , and so on. The wounds inflicted by the dagger refused to heal normally.

History

The poniard was in the possession of the greedy thief Karissa in her lair until she was slain by the Riverside Rats gang.
