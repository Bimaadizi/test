Snowdown Ride was a well-maintained road found on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles .

Description

Snowdown Ride cut through the heart of Snowdown for nearly 70 miles (110 kilometers). It began in the north at the town of Llandrain and continued south the southwest through the island to end at Pengram . Along the way were Caer Westphal and Harloch , as well as all three of Snowdown's lakes..

History

Snowdown Ride was built during the height of the Kingdom of Snowdown with the help of the realm's high taxes.
