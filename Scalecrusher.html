Lizard King Scalecrusher was a lizardfolk monarch of the Muckmarsh tribe in Twilight Marsh near Phlan in he late 15 th century DR .

Activities

During his reign, the Muckmarsh lizardfolk achieved great advancements in warfare by crafting weapons to conquer the Rootwater and Dusk-Dusk tribes.

Possessions

Over the years, King Scalecrusher amassed a small fortune of six platinum pieces , 196 gold pieces , a single agate , and one jar of Keoghtom's ointment .

History

In the Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR , the black dragon Throstulgrael arrived to the Twilight Marsh and laid waste to many lizardfolk communities. Scalecrusher remained defiant, making a last stand at King's Island in the Ringcurrent archipelago.

The king's mighty efforts were made in vain however, as Throstulgrael killed Scalecrusher and leveled the island fortress with his acidic breath. The dragon enslaved the Muckmarsh tribe along with the other two nearby tribes.

Relationships

The king was survived by one known son, Bogclaw .
