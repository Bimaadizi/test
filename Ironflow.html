The Ironflow was a small river located in western Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Ironflow originated in the southern Hardangheight , flowing south from the mountain range's southwestern slopes. The river widened to form the Hughloch near the coast of the Sea of Moonshae . The Ironflow was approximately 25 miles (40 kilometers) long.
