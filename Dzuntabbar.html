Dzuntabbar of Thay was an individual of the mid-14 th century DR who briefly required the attention of Elminster Aumar .

History

In late 1357 DR , Elminster Aumar revealed to Storm Silverhand that he needed to deal with Dzuntabbar of Thay before going off to help Shandril Shessair , shortly before her fight with Manshoon .
