Relationships

Narlen operated under the leadership of none other than Alatos "Ravenscar" Thuibuld .

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Narlen had planned a heist to break into a noble estate in the Upper City of Baldur's Gate along with another thief named Rededge . The pair were in need of a trustworthy lookout.

Some time later he also heard rumors about a rogue stone being transported from Waterdeep to the home of a man named Gandolandan , and had the intent of stealing it.
