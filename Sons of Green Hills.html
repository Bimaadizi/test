The Sons of Green Hills was a once famed gnomish adventuring band led by Harl Jamison in the 9 th century DR .

History

Led by Harl in defense of their homeland, the Sons of Green Hills slew many ogres and goblins who threatened the local communities. Even though Harl Jamison died of old age in the Year of the Ruby Pendant, 845 DR , the Sons of Green Hills were immortalized in gnomish folk ballads and tales, still remembered as of the late 13 th century DR. Harl's final resting place, however, was robbed by human grave robbers circa the Year of the Burning Tree, 890 DR .
