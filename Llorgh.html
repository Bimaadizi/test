Llorgh was a large island within the kingdom of Norheim in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Llorgh was located in the southeast region of the Norheim Isles approximately 20 miles (32 kilometers) off the coast of Gnarhelm . It was second in size only to Giantspine among the islands of Norheim. Northwind Strait passed between Llorgh and Giantspine. Llorgh was 25 miles (40 kilometers) long and about 10 miles (16 kilometers) wide on average.
