Owaren Kendrick was a prince of the Kendrick Dynasty of the Moonshae Isles active during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Owaren was born in the Year of the Plagued Lords, 1471 DR , and was the youngest son of High King Derid Kendrick of Callidyrr .

As of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the boy had been kidnapped by Lady Erliza Daressin and taken to the island of Snowdown where he was held as a hostage to ensure that High King Derid did not try to wrest control of Snowdown from her and her Amnian allies. He was held in a tower within Caer Westphal .
