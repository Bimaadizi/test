Plenerond Silverbell was a knight of the Knights in Silver during the late 15th century DR .

History

In 1484 DR , during an assault by orc and drow on the Silver Marches , Plenerond and Doughty were part of a contingent of Knights under Aleina Brightlance and helped in the defense of Sundabar .
