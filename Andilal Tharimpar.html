Andilal Tharimpar , also called the Stout , was the Nessarch of Yhaunn in Sembia in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He enjoyed spending his time feasting, gaming, and joking, preferably in the company of comely human women.

History

Andilal was a retired adventurer and merchant who by 1358 DR was elected to the position of Nessarch by the Fives Council .

On Uktar 20 of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , after the devastation of the city's docks by the kraken enslaved by the Shadovar during the Sembian Civil War , Elyril Hraven , main advisor of Overmistress Mirabeta Selkirk , went to Yhaunn to speak with Andilal and went on a tour of the dock.
