The Marnu Cliffs were a series of cliffs in the Po Yul Dzayul Range in Tabot .

Geography

From the foot of the Marnu Cliffs emerged the Nam River , which rushed westward to fill Nam Tso . Hoshki Gompa stood nearby.

Notable Inhabitants

Tzu Wan , a hermit and oracle called the Entrusted, lived above the Marnu Cliffs around Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) .
