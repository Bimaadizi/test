Universal solvent , also known as ultimate solution , was a magical liquid that was similar to a potion or magical substance.

Powers

This item appeared to have the properties of both oil of slipperiness and a potion of delusion if examined with magic. What a dose of universal solvent would actually do was to dissolve any adhesive or sticky material that it was applied to. This included cement and magical substances such as sovereign glue , as well as the goo from a tanglefoot bag and the adhesive made by kuo-toa .

A single ounce of universal solvent would work on 1 cubic foot (0.028 cubic meters) of adhesive material. A typical container held 27 fl oz (0.8 L) of fluid. If distilled to one-third of its normal potency, one ounce would cause 1 cubic foot (0.028 cubic meters) of organic and inorganic material to disintegrate .

Creation

Creating it required skill at crafting magical items and the spell disintegrate .
