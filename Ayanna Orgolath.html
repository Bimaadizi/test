Ayanna Orgolath was a human lover of the demon lord Graz'zt whose affections would eventually birth the Orgolath Dynasty of Nentyarch rulers in Narfell .

Relationships

In −666 DR , Ayanna gave birth to twin boys who were the half-fiend offspring of Graz'zt. The brothers, named Ulthas and Ulthar , were nicknamed "the Darkwing" and "the Darkhorn" respectively because of their demonic heritage. The twins would slay Nentyarch Belevan the Unliving of the Crell Dynasty in −633 DR , thus beginning the Orgolath Dynasty which would last for nearly 500 years.
