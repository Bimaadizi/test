Saisho was a wu jen / ninja adventurer in Miyama Province in Kozakura in 1357 DR .

History

Saisho was born in Miyama and as a child met and befriend Etsu-no-kami Makoto . Saisho's family died when he was very young and he was taken in by his uncle, a talented wu jen living in a ninja village. Saisho was taught magical skills by his uncle and ninja skills by the myusho . When a message arrived of the banishment of the Etsu family, Saisho escaped from the village to find his friend.

In 1357 DR, Saisho had just met Makoto when they decided to return to Miyama. Meanwhile, Saisho's uncle sent letters saying he had forgiven him for his escape.
