Bi'anza Dossouin was a drow wizard of Bregan D'aerthe in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Bi'anza hailed from the long-destroyed House Dossouin and had joined Bregan D'aerthe.

In 1488 DR , Bi'anza was the spellcaster of the patrol led by Braelin Janquay sent to monitor the demon army of Zhindia Melarn . However, Bi'anza reported that thanks to his spell he had identified a very large drow army approaching, composed of all major houses except House Melarn and their allies House Hunzrin . So Braelin decided to gather more information about this new development.
