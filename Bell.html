A bell was a hollow metal instrument that made a ringing noise when struck.

Description

As well as being used as ringing devices, bells were material components for a number of spells . Bells were used in spells such as alarm , vocalize , Leomund's secure shelter (should it be augmented with other spells), watchware , Detho's delirium , and elemental guide .

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold many types of bells, such as golden ones for 5 gp each. Dinner bells were also sold here, and were cast from either brass or silver . Standard silver bells were 1 gp each, and brass bells were 1 ep each. Finally, the catalogue stocked ornate bell pulls, which were embroidered with fine Kozakuran silk.
