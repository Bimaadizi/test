The Shimmering Gate was one of the entrances into the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The Shimmering Gate was found in the northwest corner of the city within City of Brass/Ashlarks .

Description

The gate resembled a permanent fortress complete with a garrison of soldiers.
