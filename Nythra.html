Nythra of the Seven Rivers was a Rashemaar Hathran inhabiting Urling in Rashemen in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR .

Activities

Since the hathrans of Urling ran their affairs smoothly without the need for much intervention, Nythra served as the spokeswoman for Lady Yhelbruna and others. Nythra was quite friendly, even going so far as to speak openly with outsiders.
