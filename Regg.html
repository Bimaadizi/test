Regg was an abbot of Lathander in Sembia in the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Regg was a good friend of Abelar Corrinthal , like his father Torar was a good friend of Endren Corrinthal .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , like many inside the church of Lathander , Regg joined the Risen Sun heresy despite the objections of his friend Abelar. During the Sembian Civil War , Regg provided refuge for Abelar and his men for a time. Afterwards, Regg rode with Abelar in battle in order to save Saerb .
