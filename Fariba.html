Fariba was a devoted worshiper of Ilmater who collected donations to aid the struggling poor folk of Neverwinter in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Fariba was a human woman with blonde hair and brown eyes. She wore a simple dress while collecting money for her cause.

History

After the wars of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , and the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the Sword Coast North suffered greatly. The city of Neverwinter's commerce was struggling to recover while its poor inhabitants in the Docks District were hit the hardest by the strife. Fariba spent her days in the Crossroad Keep , accosting everyone she came across, asking for donations aimed at helping Neveren citizens. Despite her best attempts, the donation purse only held scant coppers .
