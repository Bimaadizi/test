Boffo Arglebargle Cosgrove Bullwubble II was a male gnome fighter and owner of the tavern called the Furniture's Fate in the town of Thunderstone in Cormyr in the late 1360s DR .

Activities

Boffo, being an eccentric prankster, was usually at the center of brawls in the aptly named Furniture's Fate. When he wasn't, he usually had his bouncer Stig lift him up and hurl the gnome into the fray.
