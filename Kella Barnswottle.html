Kella Barnswottle was a halfling resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

Kella had light brown hair and wore a brown and white dress.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Kella put all her efforts into building barricades for defending the village.
