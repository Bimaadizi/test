Adarbrent Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Adarbrent .

Location

The Adarbrent Villa was located on the corner of the Manycats Alley and Shield Street , in the North Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This building complex consisted of two high quality buildings, one that was four stories tall and another that stood three stories tall. Like many Waterdhavian villas, it was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden.
