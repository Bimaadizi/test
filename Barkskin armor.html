Barkskin Armor was a suit of enchanted hide armor imbued with protective forest aegis magic.

Description

The hide of this armor was decorated with bark motifs reminiscent of the oak tree.

Powers

The forest aegis that gave the armor its protective quality was similar to that of a permanent barkskin spell . It also improved the wearer's constitution, allowing them to resist any magical or mundane effect that was aimed at the wearer's physique.

History

One of these sets of hide armor belonged to the Harper quartermaster named Talli , who followed the High Harper Jaheira in the Shadow-cursed Lands in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
