The Staff of the White Well was a magical staff for use by druids . It was carved of white ash by the Great Druid Brianna Moonsinger .

In the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR , it was inherited by her daughter, Robyn Moonsinger , allowing her to cast powerful lightning to scatter enemy units.

Robyn lost it the following year during the battle to defend the Moonwell when she was abducted by a flying creature.
