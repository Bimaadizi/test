The Veiled Tears was an infamous enchanted greatsword of elven make.

History

The Veiled Tears was created sometime before 1372 DR by an elven master smith named Quilin . Subsequently, the powerful enchanted sword gained a negative reputation. With time, a lesser cousin of the Veiled Tears was created by one of Quilin's apprentices for mistress Lissial‎ . That sword was originally named the Gentle Rain‎ .
