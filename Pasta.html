Pasta was a type of shaped food made of dough and sometimes served with cheese .

Notable Pasta Dishes

There were pasta dishes prepared using Luiren spring cheese . These rare pasta recipes made the cheese edible, usually unpalatable for most humans .

A Feast for a Tenday was a dish that required several days of cooking and consisted of roasted ham, tomato sauce, and served with hard-boiled eggs , pasta, and salty cheese .

Availability

In the Vilhon Reach and the Blade Kingdoms , spirelli was a known type of pasta.
