Fearghus was an animal trainer and entertainer from the City of Coin, Athkatla , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Fearghus had light skin and dark brown hair. He was a timid man who spoke with stutter.

Abilities

Fearghus trained exotic beasts displayed by the circus to Athkathlans. Some of the creatures he was in charge were: a moose , a squirrel , a leopard , and a dangerous-looking huge snake .

History

Fearghus's circus could be found at the center of Athkatla's Center District , in Waukeen's Promenade in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR . That year, the circus' tent fell victim to a mad gnome illusionist Kalah , who transformed the tent's interior into a fanciful tower filled with guests and performers, ensorcelled to appear as monsters. When the illusion was broken and the gnome defeated, Fearghus rushed back to the tent to check up on his friends and co-workers.
