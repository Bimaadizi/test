The Blackriver Bandits were a division of the Neverwinter thieves' guild with power and influence in the city's Docks District in the late 14 th century DR .

Organization

The gang was led by four bosses, and was a part of the Neverwinter Thieves Guild.

Base of Operations

The Blackriver Bandits had four hideouts scattered around the Docks District.

Possessions

The Bandits wore custom dark-grey armor . They also used distinct and custom-made daggers , maces , bastard swords , and axes .

Relationships

The Blackriver Bandits were enemies of the Bloodsailors .

History

Circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , the Bandits were in a gang war with the Bloodsailors. Some believed that they were destroyed by the adventurer known as Eater-of-Garm during this conflict.
