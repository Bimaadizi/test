The Street of the Vine was a prominent avenue located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The street ran through the southwest section of the city.

Description

The Street of the Vine originated at the Gate of the Eternal Sun on the western wall of the city. It passed southeast through the Rookery where it first crossed the Sahhar Canal and then Albrazi Canal at the border of Iskalat . The Street of the Vine eventually ended at the Street of Stelae in the north section of Iskalat.

History

The street was named for the many wine shops located along its length.
