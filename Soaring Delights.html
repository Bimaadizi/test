Soaring Delights was a restaurant located in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

The restaurant was built inside the hollow Hawk Man , one of the Walking Statues of Waterdeep , and was located on the first floor.

Structure

Atmosphere

The dining area of the restaurant was elegantly decorated, including details such as candles lighting the tables.

Services

The Soaring Delights served fine dishes made of fish , cheese and vegetables paired with wines . The dining room was managed by Shani and Vonda , two Turmishan sisters. In the kitchen Adeyo , Shani's husband, elaborated elegant culinary creations.

History

Originally catering to the building's well off residents, the restaurant was opened to the general public during the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
