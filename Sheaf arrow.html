Sheaf arrows , also called war arrows , were arrows that were designed to cause the most physical harm.

Description

Far shorter than flight arrows , sheaf arrows measured between 20 inches (51 centimeters) and 27 inches (69 centimeters) in length. Sheaf arrows were heavy arrows terminating with sharpened steel tips. These arrows could only be effectively fired with a longbow . Six sheaf arrows could be purchased for 3 silver pieces .

Uses

Sheaf arrows were primarily used in warfare.
