Alatelo De Bonito was a spell -waving guard and the financial advisor of the Den of the Seven Vales tavern and an inn in the City of Coin Athkatla in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Despite his official position and job at the Den, Alatelo was an aspiring singer-songwriter. The man's talents were so lackluster the inn's proprietress, Patricia , regularly stopped his "performances" and scolded Alatelo. She supported Alatelo's work of magics and financial graphs but could not tolerate his wailing, threatening Alatelo with being sacked.
