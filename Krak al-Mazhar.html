Krak al-Mazhar was the secretive base of operation for the Brotherhood of the True Flame in 1367 DR .

Description

Krak al-Mazhar was found within an ancient volcano deep within the Great Anvil of the Haunted Lands in Zakhara .

Inhabitants

Nar-Aidiya , the Bonfire, executed all of his plots and master schemes for the Brotherhood from this fortress.
