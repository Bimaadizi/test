The Swords' Rest was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tavern stood one story tall.

Location

The Swords' Rest was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward on the east side of the High Road where it was intersected by Telshambra's Street .

Activities

The tavern was a good place to hire mercenaries .

Inhabitants

Elgorel was the bartender at the Swords' Rest.
