Hammer Street was a major road in the asteroid city of Bral .

Description

Hammer Street began near the docks at the intersection of Low Street and Dock Street and extended uphill through the Low City and into the Middle City , where it curved around the starboard edge of the Temple District . On its journey, it covered about ~2,900 feet (880 meters) and crossed Privateer's Way , Eel Street , Galleon Street , Squid Street , Lamp Street , Man O' War Street , Mainmast Street , and Temple Street . It formed the port border of Giff-town and passed through a good portion of the Dwarven District .
