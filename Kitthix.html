Kitthix was a semi-intelligent extra-planar spider that could be summoned to the Prime Material Plane by means of a figurine of wondrous power .

It was unknown exactly which plane of existence from which Kitthix originated.

Abilities

Once every eight hours, Kitthix could be summoned by the unique black spider figurine .

Like most spiders, Kitthix had a toxic bite and could entangle foes within a form of magical webbing. This webbing paralyzed any creature caught within, but could be readily dispelled .

It also had a teleportation ability that resembled those found amongst phase spiders .

History

As of the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Kitthix's figurine was in the possession of Pai'Na , the half-drow druid that dwelled within the catacombs beneath Grave District of Athkatla .
