Spinach was a variety of a green leafy vegetable found in some areas of Faerûn and primarily used as food.

Availability

Spinach was a staple crop in most family-run farms in Sembia , often being grown in the same farm plot as other vegetables.

Usages

Spinach was used as a type of pie filling in Cormyr and in the city of Neverwinter , it was used for spinach quiches .

Runsun was a variety of common trail food—a pie made with meat cooked in spinach-and- mint or spinach-and-hot- pepper simmer sauce.

Notable Users

History

In the late 14 th century DR , Dradeel's Vegetable Surprise , made with spinach, was a recipe developed by Dradeel , a mad elvish mage and a survivor of Balduran 's final voyage to the isles of Anchorome .

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Emmeline Hallowleaf , mother to Shadowheart , one of the Absolute Adversaries , knew a recipe for Feast Day Cheese Bake made with various vegetables, including spinach, which she may have had shared with her daughter once the war with The Absolute was over.
