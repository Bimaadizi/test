Fist Bella was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Bella was a human woman with dark skin and dark brown eyes. She wore a Fist uniform helmet and a set of chainmail armor decorated with a tabard embroidered with the Flaming Fist insignia. Bella wielded a halberd when on duty.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Bella was tasked with patrolling Bloomridge Park in the Lower City.
