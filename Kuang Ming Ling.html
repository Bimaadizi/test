Kuang Ming Ling was a shukenja -in-training from Linshung , Hungtse Province , in Shou Lung circa Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) .

History

Kuang Ming decided to train as a shukenja and so, around 2607, she went on pilgrimage to a far-away monastery, and remained firm in her choice. Her father discreetly dispatched two korobokuru bushi retainers to protect her.

Personality

Kuang Ming was a romantic but stubborn-minded woman.

Relationships

Kuang Ming was the daughter of Kao Ling , a wealthy merchant of Linshung, who hoped her dream of being a shukenja would just be a passing interest. She traveled with two korobokuru retainers of her father, Hsiao Nan Tzu Wen and Ling Yu Pao , to travel with her and protect her. While Hsiao encouraged Kuang to visit inns, wine shops, and the like, Yu Pao advised caution and returning home, which aggravated the headstrong young woman.
