Bonecruncher was a young and ill-tempered allosaurus who competed in the dinosaur races of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 th century DR .

History

As of the start of the death curse , Bonecruncher was a favorite in the races, and bookies were giving it 2:1 odds of winning. Bonecruncher was present at the races on the day of the inaugural Na N'buso's Haka .
