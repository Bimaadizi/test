Menziphur was the court alchemist in Cormyr as of the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

The man could be extremely sneaky; hopefully, he could do alchemy as well.

History

Glathra Barcantle sought out Menziphur in the aftermath of the Council of the Dragon briefly, before she was sidetracked by the presence of Arclath Delcastle and Amarune Whitewave .
