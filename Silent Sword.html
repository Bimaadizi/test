The Silent Sword was an inn found in the Port District of the city of Procampur in the Vast in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

It was frequented by adventurers visiting the city. It was a much quieter place than the Happy Hippocampus . It had good quality service and moderate prices. A number of small "bed-and-beer" houses huddled around it.
