Marg the Trembler was a brigand from the town of Targos in Icewind Dale in the late 13 th century DR .

History

Marg the Trembler was the second owner of the Lucky Knucky , a well-known, by the 14 th century DR, lucky charm from the town of Targos. Sometime after the Year of the Wandering Waves, 1292 DR , Marg played cards against Young Ned , he first owner of the Lucky Knucky . To none's surprise, Marg cheated and won the game, claiming the young fisher's lucky charm. Soon after the game, the brigand changed the Lucky Knucky for supplies and fled Targos.
