A pick of piercing was a magical heavy pick that originated from the Great Rift region of southern Faerûn .

Powers

These +1 picks were capable of destroying magical constructs made of force . These included, but were not limited to the effects created by spells like Bigby's hand or wall of force

Creation

Picks of piercing were crafted with the casting of a disintegrate spell.
