R'zz Fr'zz was an enchanted imp that resided in the ruins of Myth Drannor around the late 14 th century DR .

History

The tiny devil lurked in the depths of the Irithlium , behind intact wards that kept it stuck in a small section of it. It may have been found by the Elves of Cormanthor , who were there to fight the Red Wizards of Thay under Droic Orgul .
