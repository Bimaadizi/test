Parson Glaive was a dwarf priest of Moradin of Citadel Felbarr in the mid-to-late 15 th century DR .

History

Parson was appointed as Steward-in-Waiting of Citadel Felbarr in the Year of the Reborn Hero, 1463 DR .

He was a decisive person during the War of the Silver Marches . In the Year of the Awakened Sleepers, 1484 DR , Parson confirmed to King Emerus Warcrown that young Reginald Roundshield was in fact the legendary Bruenor Battlehammer reborn.

He was made King Parson Glaive of Citadel Felbarr in the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR when King Emerus Warcrown abdicated his throne and title to join Bruenor Battlehammer on the march to reclaim Gauntlgrym .
