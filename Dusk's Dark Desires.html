A Tour of Tempest was an Umberlant book that discussed the divine tempest domain , written by and published sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Content

Dusk's Dark Desires was a series of intrigue and lewd romance chapbooks. One of the later novels in the series was noticeably more smutty than its prequels with the blood and sex being, arguably the books' best parts. Some also praised the book's fine intrigue and character work of Bedford, a beautifully-written werecow , as well as Dusk, the novels' protagonist.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Book Brigade , a book club in the city of Baldur's Gate , met to discuss several books in the series.
