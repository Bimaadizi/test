Location

Weaponers of Cormyr neighbored a rowdy tavern called The Curse , and stood not far from the Windlord's Rest inn.

History

In the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Weaponers of Cormyr was in business dealing in simple weapons. The shopkeep proudly announced that the store carried the finest steel from Cormyr: axes , swords , bardiches , bec de corbins , guisarmes , fauchards , voulges , lucerne hammers , bo sticks , jo sticks , javelins , and others.
