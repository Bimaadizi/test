Carrig Cymrych II was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the early-to-mid 5 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty , and was named in honor of Carrig I , son of the dynasty's legendary founder, Cymrych Hugh . Carrig II was the first son of his predecessor, High King Lann . His successor was his own first son, Kemble Cymrych .

History

Carrig II assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Last Breath, 420 DR , after the death of his father.

He ruled for eleven years until he died of plague in the Year of the Hearth, 431 DR .
