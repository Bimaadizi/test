Glathra Barcantle was a War Wizard of Cormyr and the de facto third-in-command of the organization.

Personality

Glathra could best be described as rude, imperious, and arrogant and was liked by no one.

Relationships

Because of her personality, Glathra was disliked by most people she met. She hated Elminster , whom she was convinced was an enemy of Cormyr.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , King Foril's called the Dragon Council when Royal Mage Ganrahast and his second-in-command Vainrence were imprisoned by Manshoon in the palace crypts. At that time, Glathra assumed command of the War Wizards and helped to prevent an uprising by disgruntled nobles.
