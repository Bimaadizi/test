Enchantment spells affect the minds of others, influencing or controlling their behavior. Such spells can make enemies see the caster as a friend, force creatures to take a course of action, or even control another creature like a puppet. This school includes the spells charm person and mind fog . The enchantment school had two subschools: charm and compulsion . A wizard who specializes in enchantment is known as an enchanter or enchantress .
