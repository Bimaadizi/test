Orem Arvaeyn was a kanqat of the Vale Guard that protected the city of Evereska and the Evereska Vale during the late 14 th century DR .

Description

He had a severe look about him and spoke in the serious manner befitting a guardsman or soldier.

Personality

Orem possessed some wisdom during his years and made a habit of offering thoughtful insight to his friends.

Relationships

He was on good terms with Galaeron Nihmedu , a patrol leader of the Evereskan Tomb Guard . The pair had graduated together at Evereska's Academy of Arms .
