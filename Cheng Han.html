Cheng Han was a noble officer in the army of Shou Lung .

History

Cheng Han was a noble and an officer in the personal armies of the nobles, the Twenty-Five Armies . In 1359 DR , the Twenty-Five Armies led by Hsuang Yu Po were assigned to support General Batu Min Ho in his campaign against the Tuigan . Cheng Han tried many times to undermine Batu in front of Hsuang but was unsuccessful. After the Tuigan totally destroyed the Twenty-Five Armies, nobody knew what happened to Cheng.
