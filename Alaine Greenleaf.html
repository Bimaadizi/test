Alaine Greenleaf was an elven hero who lived some years before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Alaine was believed to be the last wielder of the longsword called The Equalizer , sometime before the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR . She and the sword were lost during an expedition to the Underdark , where the The Equalizer was broken in three and remained lost until 1369 DR.
