The Temple of Shar was a temple dedicated to the goddess Shar in the city of Waterdeep , during the reign of Ahghairon , around the 11 th century DR .

Location

This temple was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This temple was constructed of black stone and its domed roof was dotted with sharp steeples.

Interior

The interior of the temple was dimly lit by torchlight . Its main chamber of this measured 40 feet (12 meters) by 30 feet (9.1 meters) wide, while its domed ceiling was 30 ft (9.1 m). The main chamber also had stairs leading down to the temple's basement.

Activities

The temple's adherents were known to produce pamphlets for the public that lauded their goddess.

History

Around 1035 DR , the Sharran clergy fell under the sway of a vampire who hid themselves within the temple's basement. Also around this time, the normally irreligious Hultmarks became frequent visitors to the temple, falling under the sway of its vampire.

Eventually, the vampire lord Artor Morlin noticed that vampire activity around the city had increased, to such a degree that their rise in population could endanger Waterdeep. Promising his ruler Ahghairon that he would try to keep the city free of other vampires, Artor hired a group of adventurers to seek out and eradicate vampire nests in the city. This mission eventually lead them to the Hultmark Manor , where Joy Hultmark informed them she suspects the Temple of Shar as being a vampire den. When the adventurers made their way to the temple they found the Hultmarks inside, debating whether or not to remain loyal to their master.
