Karokrath was a large githzerai outpost on Toril as of the late 15 th century DR .

Location

The stronghold's location was a secret, but it was located somewhere within the badlands of Xian in Faerûn .

Activities

From Karokrath, the githzerai deployed scouts and spies to monitor both the mind flayer city of Oryndoll —located in the Underdark beneath the outpost—and the githyanki citadel of Gatchorof —located beneath the Snowflake Mountains to the south.

History

As of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Karokrath's agents were in the midst of a plan to spark open hostilities between the mind flayers of Oryndoll and the githyanki of Gatchorof, with the ultimate expectation being that the githzerai could then capitalize on the conflict.

Inhabitants

The githzerai stationed at Karokrath were part of a larger effort to keep watch on the mind flayers who inhabited Old Shanatar . The outpost was led by githzerai elders.
