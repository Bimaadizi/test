The Shields of Hope was a sisterhood of swanmay rangers devoted to Mielikki , the Forest Queen.

Activities

Though dedicated to Mielikki, the sisters of the Shields of Hope spent most of their time protecting the priestesses of Shiallia , who were called the Sisters of Life and Mercy , within the High Forest , safely escorting them in groups of three through the more dangerous sections of the forest.
