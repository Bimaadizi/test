Asref was a saint of the Church of Ilmater established in the 14 th century DR , though by 1370 DR they had yet to be officially sanctioned by the Church. They were considered by the Nadhari — an organization dedicated to freeing slaves in Calimshan — to be their patron saint.

History

Around 1370 DR, a personal shrine and mission dedicated to Asref were established in the Hook Ward of Calimport by the Tethyrian priest Brother Dolmas .
