Mamblat , nicknamed "Happy" , was a minstrel from the city of Hillsfar by the Moonsea .

Personality

Mamblat was a friendly and good-humored figure.

History

Around 1370 DR , Happy Mamblat was one of the more well-known itinerant minstrels wandering the Vast . They were loved by many for their personality.
