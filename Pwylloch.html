King Pwylloch was ruler of Snowdown in the Moonshae Isles during the early-to-mid 14 th century DR . He was the last king of his line.

Activities

He ruled from his castle of Caer Westphal , where he maintained a fortune worth more than 250,000 gold pieces .

History

In the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR , King Pwylloch was assassinated by Razfallow on the orders of Cyndre , along with any of his heirs.
