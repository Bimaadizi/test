Raml al-Nau , also known as the Sandstorm , was an elite group of jann soldiers in Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Organization

Raml al-Nau served as an army under the direct command of the Grand Caliph .

History

Though they were known for numerous acts of valor, the Sandstorm was most famous for their actions defending the shrine of Hajama in the High Desert .

Base of Operations

Huzuz served as the technical center of power of the Sandstorm, but the group was active throughout Zakhara based upon the needs of the Grand Caliph.
