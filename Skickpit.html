Skickpit was a deep gnome slave who was taken to Grymforge along with the other gnomes of Clan Ironhand in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

He was not one to be pushed around without pushing back. He responded to his captors' demeaning nicknames with some of his own.

Relationships

He was friends with Thulla , who became separated from the rest of her kin during the raid.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the gnomes of Clan Ironhand were attacked by duergar allied with the cult of the Absolute . Many of their clan were killed, while others were taken captive as slaves to excavate the ruins of Grymforge. Skickpit was among the latter group. He was forced to endure the ridicule and degradation doled out by Ward Magmar .
