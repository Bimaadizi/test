Tazixor was a secluded port city on the island of Ixinos , off the coast of Vilhon Reach in the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Description

Tazixor was the only known settlement on Ixinos. The settlement and the island were surrounded in mystery and governed by Queen Elissa Tarixon in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . The island's population was a community of amazons and were predominately female. A small population of males, reportedly, were used as slaves. Males were considered to be valuable commodities. Tazixor's seclusion permitted no outsiders. No visitors nor diplomatic envoys were allowed to dock in the ports of Tazixor.
