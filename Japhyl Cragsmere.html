Japhyl Cragsmere , who was nicknamed the Hawk , was a noble inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Japhyl was the patriarch of the Cragsmere noble family of Waterdeep. He lived within Cragsmere Villa on Vondil Street in Waterdeep's North Ward .
