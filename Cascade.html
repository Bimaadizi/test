The Cascade was the palace and the seat of power in the Chondathan city of Iljak .

Structure

The Cascade was a large white marble building. The grand structure was not built as a palace but at some point before 1370 DR was repurposed to serve as the Governor's seat of government.

History

Circa the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the Cascade was occupied by Governor Anton Yinoran whose rule was criticized as being puppeteered by Arrabar .
