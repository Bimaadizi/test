The Purple Wyrm Inn and Tavern was a very famous inn and tavern in Baldur's Gate , located next door to the north from the Watchful Shield shrine.

Inhabitants

Compared to the Elfsong Tavern , the tavern was frequented more by merchants, employers and adventurers. The inn was used by the Harper Randalla Brasshorn as her base of operations.
