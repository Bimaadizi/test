Runic shield was a magically enchanted shield usable by sorcerers .

Powers

Runic shields were inscribed with magical sigils that allowed sorcerers to use them with almost no chance of spell failure. The shield itself provided a +2 defense enchantment when held.

History

In 1369 DR, one of runic shields could be found in Dwarven Dungeons of Myth Drannor in a chest along with gemstones and an eidolon , a mysterious stone head, guarded by two wraiths .
