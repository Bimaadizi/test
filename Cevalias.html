Cevalias was a mythic and historic figure and the Champion of Cimbar , who was instrumental in making the city the capital of Chessenta longe before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Cevalias was prominently featured in the history of Chessenta as a mythic hero who helped elevate the city of Cimbar to the nation's capital. Armed with a sparkling enchanted hammer called Storm , he became the Champion of Cimbar after falling in love with the ruler's daughter, Saphia , who quickly learned to manipulate her paramour. With her father's guidance, Saphia made Cevalias into a deadly weapon of destruction that took lives of over a thousand warriors of other Chessentan cities.
