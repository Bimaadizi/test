Location

Bridge Street was found in the western section of Skuld in the Merchant District near the coast of the Alamber Sea . It ran north to south through the city, originating in the south at the Bridge Gate near the naval yards and the River of Shadows , and passing north to end at another gate located on an inner city wall.

Notable Locations

A moneychanger and market were located along Bridge Street.
