The Yellow Magpie Inn was an inn in the T'u Lung city of Balanzia around Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) .

Location

This inn was located in the eastern portion of Balanzia, a few blocks away from the Gate of Hang'to.

History

Around Shou Year 2403 (1153 DR) , the Yellow Magpie was one of many buildings that were destroyed in Balanzia as the city fell at the hands of the Shou Lung invasion of T'u Lung.
