The Glory Mines were located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire .

Description

The Glory Mines were located within the very bedrock upon which the City of Brass was located. A salamander guide was needed to navigate the warren without becoming hopelessly lost.

Inhabitants

Salamanders infested this warren of the City of Brass.
