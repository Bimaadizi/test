The barge yard was a site in Leilon where barges could be housed or rented in the late 15 th century DR . The gruff dwarven matron Rorsta Anvilhand served as the barge-master.

Location

The barge yard was located south of Quayside .

Services

Barges were rented out for ten days at the barge yard for 10 gp each.
