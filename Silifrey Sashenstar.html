Lady Silifrey Sashenstar was a member of the Parliament of Peers from the noble patriar Sashenstar family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Lady Silifrey Sashenstar was already a member of the Parliament of Peers. When the Absolute crisis arrived in the Gate, Lady Sashenstar was noted by the Cult of Bane as one of the eight top patriars of the Parliament, vulnerable to bribery, blackmail, or direct threats.

She was among nobles who attended Lord Enver Gortash 's correction in the Wyrm's Rock of Baldur's Gate in 1492 DR.
