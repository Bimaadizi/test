Gorzil was the leader of the troglodytes in the service of House Auvryndar in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR .

Activities

He lead his fellow twenty troglodytes that were trained and equipped by the drow .

Gorzil secretly plotted to kill Vlonwelv Auvryndar and become the only ruler of Muiral's Gauntlet .
