The Coast Road ran along the west coast of the Vast , connecting the cities of Ravens Bluff and Tantras .

From south to north, it started at Ravens Bluff, passed over the Fire River via the great multi-span bridge at Mossbridges , and ended at Tantras. On the way, it linked with the High Trail , Feldar's Trail , the Tantras Trail , and the Cross Road .
