Jungle Princess was a deinonychus who competed in the dinosaur races of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 th century DR .

History

As of the start of the death curse , bookies were giving Jungle Princess only 1:5 odds of winning at the races, perhaps because of her easily frightened jockey. Jungle Princess was present at the races on the day of the inaugural Na N'buso's Haka .
