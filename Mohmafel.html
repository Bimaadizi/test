Mohmafel was an elf of unknown origin, desert-dwelling per Iahn Qoyllor 's judgement. He was accompanying Deamiel during the latter's pursuit of Ususi Manaallin in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , while both were under Pandorym 's influence. He was slain by a poisoned bolt fired by Iahn Qoyllor ; at the time of death, he was wearing a long white gown and wielding a bow .
