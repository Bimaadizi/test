Engarth was a Triadic knight and one of leaders of the Knights of Imphras II in during the late 14 th century DR . As of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR Engarth was one of the youngest members of the Lords of Imphras II alongside his fellow lords, Delimbrar and Silaunbrar .
