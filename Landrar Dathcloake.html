Lord Landrar Dathcloake was a noble of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Landrar Dathcloake proposed a council of nobles that would have governance over the War Wizards of Cormyr , the armies, and that would choose successors for the royal line. He got cheered for that.

Rumors & Legends

Later, after the Council degenerated into violence and was assaulted by a blueflame ghost , and the nobility left the chamber, the evil vampire archmage Manshoon believed he spotted Dathcloake blustering at the Purple Dragons guarding the entrance, trying without success to gain access.
