Bottom Feeder was a riverboat run by a crew of dwarves .

Description

Bottom Feeder was as 20-foot-long (6.1 meters) square-bottomed riverboat built by dwarves. The crew propelled the ship using long poles to push it along. A locker on board stored a number of crossbows , which were used by the ship's crew to defend the ship.

Activities

Bottom Feeder plied the waters of the River Chionthar from Baldur's Gate to Scornubel , occasionally even as far as Iriaebor .
