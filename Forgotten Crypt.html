The Forgotten Crypt was a subterranean home of Velsharoon during his mortal life.

Description

Located deep within Skull Gorge , the Forgotten Crypt was a large mausoleum constructed on top of the ruins of an ancient Netherese city.

Inhabitants

The crypt was inhabited by Velsharoon's clergy as of the 1370's DR .
