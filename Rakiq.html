Rakiq was tangy beverage made in Fabada in the High Desert of west Zakhara .

Description

This flavorful drink was made from sheep 's milk and garlic .

History

The Numtanajd made this drink locally and often gifted it to the enlightened tribes of the High Desert. They would also trade rakiq for coffee , honey , or salt.
