Yathlanae was a sorceress serving the Coronal of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

In 241 DR , she joined a meeting with the Srinshee and other sorceresses in the Vault of Ages , discussing that they needed to be watchful for the houses of the city and their response to a human made armathor . After the hunt of Elminster , she was set to cast spells to monitor whether anyone spoke of Mythanthor .

She survived the fight with Llombaerth Starym and his forces when they struck against Coronal Eltargrim in 261 DR , recovering a number of wands from the dead. She may have made use of them during the laying of the Mythal .
