Uligar was the chieftain of the Bleeding Eye orc tribe that raided the road of Kuldahar Pass in the 11 th century DR .

Possessions

Uligar carried a wand of trap detection , an axe , a scroll of blur , and several gold coins .

History

In Eleint of the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , , the Bleeding Eye orcs and a tribe of goblins allied with them raided the traders passing through Kuldahar Pass, between Easthaven and a small town of Kuldahar . During the avalanche in Kuldahar Pass, Uligar and his raiders killed Jermsy's family that owned a mill outside of Kuldahar and used it as a base.
