Hartouchen Rethnor was a High Captain of Luskan in the 15 th century DR .

Activities

Hartouchen led a violent group that did not engage in many meaningful activities.

History

Some time in or before 1463 DR , Hartouchen took over from his dead father, Borlann the Crow , and became the new High Captain of Ship Rethnor . He wished to hunt the one that killed his father. As of 1489 DR , Hartouchen Rethnor was still in charge of the Fifth Ship of Luskan .
