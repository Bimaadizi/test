Wastirek was a black dragon wyrm whose lair was in the ruins of Orogoth in the High Moor of the Western Heartlands in the late 14 th century DR .

He was the grandson of the black wyrm dracolich Calathanorgoth the Old One, the mate of the black wyrm Vilholin , and the father of the black dragons Woklef , Cheleen , Aswidorg , and Kasidikal .
