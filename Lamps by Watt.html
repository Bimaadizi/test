They sold all manner of lighting fixtures and paraphernalia, including wall lamps , chandeliers , torches , sconces , and lanterns . They specialized in items and fixtures made of brass .

Highlighted by a bright yellow awning over the front entrance, this small and well-lit shop was quite prosperous among Ravenian home-and-land-owners. It had prime real estate, located on the corner of O'Kane Court and Hawk Passage , within the Swordspoint neighborhood.
