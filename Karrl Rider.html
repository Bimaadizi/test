Karrl Rider was a caravan worker employee of the Gallaway Trade Depot in the Ten Towns under the supervision of Rawleigh Gallaway , in the late 13 th century DR .

Description

Karrl was a human man with light skin and blonde hair. He wore a simple leather armor and carried a shortsword .

History

In the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , Karrl Rider and Jemeliah accompanied Rawleigh Gallaway's caravan to the town of Lonelywood . The business venture was cut short by a barbarian army that besieged the town, stopping the Gallaway Trading Coaster wagons from leaving. Karrl, Jemeliah, and Rawleigh Gallaway all were stuck until the barbarian threat was over.
