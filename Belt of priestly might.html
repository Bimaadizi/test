Belts of priestly might were defensive magical items, created by servants of a given deity , that boosted the wearer's strength and natural defenses. They were normally named for their faith of origin e.g. Mystran belt of priestly might .

Appearance

Belts of priestly might were made of broad leather, and featured a stamp of a deity 's holy symbol on the front.

Creation

To manufacture belts of priestly might , an arcane spellcaster or divine spellcaster had to know both barkskin and bull's strength .

They were created by many different faiths, and could only be worn safely by persons who were either of that deity's alignment , or had that deity as a patron.

History

The first belts of priestly might appeared in Halruaa at the end of the Godswar ; the Halruaans took this as a sign that Mystra had returned. By the late 14 th century DR , Sharran belts of priestly might were worn by the followers of the Lady of Loss.
