Ulls was a small lake in the Highlands of the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles .

Description

The waters of the Ulls were calm and dark, appearing black in the evening light. At low tide, a narrow strip of sand allowed for passage along the shore to Crane Point , a small peninsula that was the site of a temple to the moon during the reign of the Kendricks and of a hunting lodge during the reign of the le Shay .

Geography

The lake lay below Ulls Peak . It was very near to another small lake, Innes .
