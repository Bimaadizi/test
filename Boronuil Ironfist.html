Boronuil Ironfist was a dwarven craftsman living in Galdel in 1367 DR .

Activities

Boronuil was renowned for being one of the best electrum craftsmen in all of Halruaa .

With his supreme skill he was able to craft splendid items that were ready for enchanting. Because of his reputation, he was able to charge quadruple the normal rates. The citizens of Halruaa were more than willing to pay as he had a waiting list exceeding two years.
