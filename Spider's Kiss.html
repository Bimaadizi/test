The Spider's Kiss was an enchanted scimitar that once belonged to the King of Spiders of Westgate .

Powers

The blade of this scimitar was constantly coated with potent dark oozing spider poison . Additionally, the Spider's Kiss possessed a +2 enchantment that increased the damage and guided the wielder's hand towards their target.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the so-called King of Spiders – Sirachna , gained entrance to a home on Westgate's Market Triangle from a cavern underneath. His arachnid pets ended up murdering a family who lived inside, leaving them haunting the home as restless ghosts . Some said the King of Spiders was killed by an adventurer, accompanied by Charissa Maernos , Mantides , and Rinara , who claimed the Spider's Kiss and several other magic items.
