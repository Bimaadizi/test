The Rosebud was a tavern found in the city of Westgate in 1367 DR .

Location

The Rosebud was found along the Harbor Loop within sight of the River Thunn in the eastern section of the city.

Activities

This tavern was actually an outdoor ale garden. It was frequented primarily by merchants and young nobles.
