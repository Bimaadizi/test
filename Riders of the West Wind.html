The Riders of the West Wind was a military order devoted to Shaundakul , the Rider of the Winds.

Organization

The Riders of the West Wind were composed of windwalkers and a few rangers . They hired themselves out as mercenaries to guard caravans passing through uncharted territories. They even planned to venture to fabled Anchorome .
