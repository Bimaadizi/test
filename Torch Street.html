Torch Street was a curved street in the city of Suzail that ran parallel to Blade Lane , not far from the city's eastern edge.

Description

Torch Street began on the crossing of Stonebow Street and a Y-shaped Swordstars Lane to the north. From there, the street moved south, past the Street of Staves , and came to an end in front of The Lockup , Suzail's prison.
