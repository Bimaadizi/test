Sherm was a dealer in herbal remedies from Luskan during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Sherm had dark brown skin and dark hair gathered in a small bun on his head. He wore a green vest over his brown clothes.

History

Some time around 1490 DR , Sherm had a small shop called Sherm's Herbs in the Luskan market district, where he encountered the Champion of the Burning Dawn .
