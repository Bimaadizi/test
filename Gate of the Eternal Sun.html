The Gate of the Eternal Sun was one of the entrances into the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The Gate of the Eternal Sun lay along the Street of the Vine on the western wall of the city within the Rookery .

Description

The gate resembled a permanent fortress complete with a garrison of soldiers.
