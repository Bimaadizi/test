Ingerr & Ingerr Warehouses was a warehouse compound located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Each of the three buildings stood two stories tall.

Location

Ingerr & Ingerr Warehouses was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter . The buildings sat in the southeast corner of the Adventurers' Quarter on Slop Street and Kerrigan's Court in between the Safehaven Inn and the Beer Golem .
