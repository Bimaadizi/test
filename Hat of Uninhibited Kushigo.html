The Hat of Uninhibited Kushigo was a unique monk hood that once belonged to the famous monk Kushigo sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The wearer of the hood received a boost to their defense against magic attacks after landing a successful unarmed strike on their target. This magical defense lasted for six seconds.

History

Once, long before the late 15 th century DR, the Hat of Uninhibited Kushigo , along with the Garb of the Land and Sky , was worn by Kushigo, a warrior who gave up his heavy shell of armor for freedom of movement after suffering a great loss. In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the hood could be found in Harper quartermaster Talli 's inventory during the mission into the Shadow-cursed Lands under the command of the High Harper Jaheira .
