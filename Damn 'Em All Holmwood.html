Damn 'Em All Holmwood was a duergar from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Holmwood wore a simple set of chainmail armor .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Damn 'Em All Holmwood, along with other duergar, giant spiders , and drow , was slaughtered in the basement cave under the home of Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica . Holmwood's body was left charred and blackened by flames near the mutilated body of Incontinentia Figgin , who was turned into a grim effigy by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal .
