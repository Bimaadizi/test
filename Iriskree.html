Iriskree was an old, reclusive half-elf sorceress of considerable power from the Starmantle area as of the mid-late 13 th century DR . She was the first teacher for the Magister Ohland Grethgar .

History

She died at some point prior to the Year of the Manticore, 1280 DR , of a winter fever ; before that point, she'd written to her last kinswoman that the young Ohland had a spark for the art that was brighter than most.
