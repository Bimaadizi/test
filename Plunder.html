Plunder was a mimic that lurked in the ruins of Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

History

The mimic lay in ambush in the northwestern part of the city, near the Temple to Labelas Enoreth . As usual, it disguised itself as a treasure-filled chest to lure in prey.
