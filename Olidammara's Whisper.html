Olidammara's Whisper was a part of the Fading Rings set. This unique enchanted ring bore the name of Olidammara , the Laughing Rogue, a bardic deity from the world of Oerth .

Powers

Olidammara's Whisper possessed an enchantment that enhanced the wearer's dexterity and allowed them to cast the invisibility spell at will. The ring was created exclusively for rogues .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Olidammara's Whisper came to be in possession of the Great Stone Head , a mysterious magical monument in Myth Drannor . Eidolons , small totemic statues depicting various gods, were scattered throughout the ruins of the City of Song and could be deposited into the Great Stone Head . In exchange for each eidolon , the statue spat one of the Fading Rings out. Olidammara's Whisper was one of the rings dispensed for the Veiled Ones on their quest to stop the Cult of the Dragon .
