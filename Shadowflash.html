Shadowflash , also called Silverflash , was the +1 silver short sword owned by Vhaeraun , the drow deity of thievery.

Description

Shadowflash was a short sword of gleaming silver.

Powers

As the name suggested, Shadowflash could flash. The intensity of this light was about that of a continual light spell. Everyone but Vhaeraun within 70 ft. (21.34 m) who saw this light went blind for two to five minutes

History

How Vhaeraun came to own Shadowflash was unknown. The short sword was his favored weapon. His clerics preferred the usage of a short sword too. They often mastered a combat style called sargh'elgg with a short sword.

The sword was also used in his assassination attempt against Eilistraee .
