Akongo was one of the nyama-nummo , the offspring of the deity Olurobo in ancient Katashaka .

Akongo was known by the ancient Tabaxi tribe to be a particularly perfidious entity dwelling in the southern highlands. When the couatl Ecatzin came to them to preach of Ubtao some time before −2809 DR , they were initially resistant, fearing the feathered serpent to be a minion of Akongo.
