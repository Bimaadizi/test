Slingshot Way was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Located in southern Zhentil Keep within the Foreign Quarter on the southern bank of the River Tesh , Slingshot Way was a small road in the western section of the Foreign Quarter that passed perpendicular to the River Trail Gate . Twilight Street connected to the southern end of Slingshot Way.
