Toram Gyvvalstorm was a High Watcher of Helm , and the leader of the Tethyrian Helmite order of knights known as the Champions Vigilant during the late 14 th century DR . During the Tethyrian Interregnum , Toram and his fellow knights defended many of the nation's poor from bandits and corrupt barons.
