Nylaersyn Floshin was a member of House Dlardrageth that infiltrated the Unseen in Waterdeep by the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

History

Nylaersyn was a fey'ri member of House Dlardrageth. Some time before 1372 DR she posed as a doppelganger named Synyl and managed to infiltrate the Unseen, a spy network in Waterdeep.
