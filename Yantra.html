Yantra was an inhabitant of the Dalelands in the mid-14 th century DR .

Personality

She took comments on cooking personally.

Relationships

She thought highly of her husband, Pardus .

History

Yantra and her husband were patrons at the Highmoon Inn in 1357 DR while Shandril Shessair was away, after Mirtul but before Flamerule . She had a brief argument with other patrons and ordered roasted boar for her man, though he wanted roasted deer.
