Flanagus Gnarlybone was a gnome fighter and the tavernmaster of the Troll in Flames tavern in the settlement of Mornbryn's Shield around 1370 DR . He was gruff and rotund. He made sure to stock up on the favored drinks and brews of his bar's regulars.
