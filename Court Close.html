The Court Close was hook-shaped street in Suzail that connected the Promenade with one of the city's handouts, ending almost reaching the Bolliver's Wharf .

Description

The street began on the doors of the Winking Eye that stood on the northern side of the Promenade, one block southeast of Castle Obarskyr . The Court Close then moved north, hooked around the block, crossed the Promenade, and darted south, slightly veering to the west.
