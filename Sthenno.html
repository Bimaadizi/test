Sthenno was a medusa .

Relationships

Sthenno was Marrec 's aunt. Unlike her sisters, she sometimes missed his hiding spot while playing hide and seek. Neither she nor her sister, Euryale , liked when Marrec's mother brought up the memory of his father, insisting that he was too vulnerable, and not of their kin.

History

Auntie Sthenno was playing hide and seek with Marrec on the night he went missing.
