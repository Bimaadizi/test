Blackgorgons was the tower home of Baskor Tranth , a surly hard-drinking retired adventurer of significant magical repute, who settled in Suzail , the capital of Cormyr as of the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

The grim-named wizardly tower stood on the southern side of Stonebow Street , rising above Courtyard of Zathchos to the south from Blackgorgons, located in the middle os the city block. Stonebow Street's crossing with Torch Street was located one door east of the tower.

History

Blackgorgons was Baskor's home as early as the Year of the Worm, 1356 DR . The tower contained it's masters impressive collection of spell scrolls that he was willing to trade.
