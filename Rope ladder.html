Rope ladders were a type of ladder made largely of rope , with wooden slats between.

Description

The average rope ladder was made from hemp rope, with wooden slats measuring 2 inches (5.1 centimeters) wide.

Notable Users

History

Rope ladders were available for sale through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue , where they were sold in increments of 2 feet (0.61 meters) for 1 silver piece per 4 feet (1.2 meters).
