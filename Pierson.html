Pierson was a male human of the village of West Harbor on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR .

History

Pierson was one of the members of West Harbor militia. He was a reluctant friend of thieves, since he kept goods he collected over the years. During the battle against duergar and githyanki , Pierson could've been asked to join the attack. Regardless, he was killed in the battle.
