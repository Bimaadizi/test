Fatik al-Amin was the falconer of the Dark Wind Stables in Sarahin in 1367 DR .

Personality

Fatik was a quiet man who loved his work.

Activities

Fatik was focused solely on training an impressive array of birds including a golden eagle, four peregrine falcons, a pair of saker falcons from Dirs , and the great roc , Sar .

He was also a member of the Loyalists .

Possessions

Fatik owned a ring of avian control , allowing him to easily tame and train his birds.
