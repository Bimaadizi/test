The Nymph language was the racial language of nymphs , grain nymphs , and unseelie nymphs . It was noted for having a musical quality to it.

Speakers

Beyond nymphs themselves, Nymph was a common secondary language among unicorns .

It was also fairly common as a secondary language among druids devoted to certain nature deities. These included the druids of Chauntea , druids of Eldath , and the druids of Silvanus . Other forms of specialty priests in the Realms for whom Nymph was a commonly known secondary language were the Eldathyn peacemen .
