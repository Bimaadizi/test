The Star of Aga-Pelar was a star sapphire which the wizard Martek employed to bind the efreet Pasha Khalitarius . It was one of the five Star Gems of Martek .

Description

The Star of Aga-Pelar was a huge midnight black star sapphire with an inner asterism of pure white. While set in the prison of Khalitharius in Pazar, the gem would radiate pulses of white light.

History

After the wizard Martek used the star to bind Khalitharius , he gave it to the Lord of Pazar in exchange for keeping the efreet prisoner. By the mid-14 th century DR, it was the only treasure of note still left in Pazar.

Notable Owners

Trivia

Of the Star Gems of Martek, only the Star of Aga-Pelar was actually a star gem: it was a star sapphire.
