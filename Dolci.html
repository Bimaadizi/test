Dolci was a Netherese arcanist who taught at the Inventor's Conservatory in Karsus 's enclave of Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Dolci taught alongside the other seven resident teachers, made up of Arconi , Agassaz , Belair , Koler , Clayton , Ballarini , and Staufehaus .
