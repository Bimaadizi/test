A poisoner's robe were a variety of magical robes that were associated with poisons .

Description

The garment smelled particularly acrid and actually stinged to the touch.

Powers

Whenever the wearer of the poisoner's robes cast spells utilizing poison, those spells became significantly more potent. Unfortunately, the appeared to also poison the wearer themself a bit.

History

At some point, during or before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , someone wearing these robes was consumed by the phase spider matriarch of the Whispering Caverns .
