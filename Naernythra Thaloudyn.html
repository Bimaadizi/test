Base

She made her home in Moonrise Towers , a fortified compound. It lay in the Western Heartlands , beside the River Chionthar west of Elturel .

Activities

As Crescentcoat, Naernythra was primarily responsible for training the local Heralds and was also given the difficult task of presenting all sides of issues brought up for debate among the High Heralds. Crescentcoat traditionally played the role of "devil's advocate" to ensure the council came to their conclusions honestly after considering the ramifications of each aspect. Hence, they had to be a thorough researcher and debater.

Possessions

In her position as Crescentcoat, Naernythra wore a beautiful cloth-of-silver tabard woven with a pattern of interlocking crescent moons, for which the office was named. In addition to a regal appearance, the garment gave the wearer the power of a ring of shooting stars . The rod of office was made of ivory enchanted to be harder than adamantine and with the same function as a rod of smiting .

History

She held her office by 1368 DR .
