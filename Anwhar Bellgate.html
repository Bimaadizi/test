Anwhar Bellgate was a spellthief in the late 14 th century DR .

Abilities

Besides some skill at stealth, Anwhar Bellgate was a spellthief of some power. He could also convert stolen spells into other spells, belonging to one of Bhaal's domains.

Activities

He was a merchant who mostly plied the route between Scornubel and Waterdeep , but also a highly successful assassin.

History

During the Time of Troubles in 1358 DR , Anwhar Bellgate came into contact with the godsblood shed between Bhaal and Cyric , eventually developing his spellthieving abilities. He continued to ply the route, but became an assassin by 1372 DR .
