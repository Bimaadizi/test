Raisa el Catahras was wife of Atiq el Catahras , who was the owner of The Roving Rune , a popular inn in Teshburl , in 1370 DR .

Relationships

Raisa and Atiq had a daughter named Oma .

Activities

Mother and daughter were both mages —a rarity in Teshburl—and with Atiq's blessing they practiced their skills in a magically hidden basement level, which the two of them had discovered. There, Raisa taught Oma magic.

History

Raisa had been trained in secret in the magical arts by her own mother.
