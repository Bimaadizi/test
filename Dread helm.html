A dread helm was a magical helm that made its wearer's eyes glow red.

Description

Dread helms were typically made of steel and had a fearsome appearance.

Powers

The dread helm caused its wearer's eyes to glow red, and in some cases shrouded their visage in darkness. This power only worked while the user wore it.
