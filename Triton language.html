The Triton language was the racial language of the tritons .

Speakers

Beyond tritons themselves, Triton was commonly a secondary language among asrai , water sprites of the Upper Planes .

Some of the individual specialty priests of aquatic deities were known to learn various other aquatic languages, such as Triton. These included the sea druids of Deep Sashelas , the waveservants of Umberlee , and the waterwalkers of Istishia .

Grammar

To modify a word to be a collective term, such as in order to refer to the members of a family or the various colonies surrounding the capital of one of their protectorates, tritons would add a "(a)th" to the interceding vowel where needed. For example, the Pumana Protectorate and Vuuvax Protectorate would be rendered as Pumanath and Vuuvaxath respectively. When used with a name, such as Numyn, depending upon the context it could refer to either possessions of the individual or their children.

Naming Conventions

On average, a name in the Triton language had two to three syllables. Feminine names always ended in the letter "n", whilst masculine names always ended in the letter "s". Around the 9 th century DR , the customs of masculine names changed, with such names now taking on a vowel at their end.
