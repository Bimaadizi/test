Ilkin Ermbroar was a cleric of Mielikki circa the mid– to late–14 th century DR. He usually shepherded the preparations for the annual Rite of the Stag Lass in Amphail .

Description

Ilkin lived in the forested areas ( Westwood and Kryptgarden Forest ) not far from Amphail and his face showed the years of exposure to the elements. His hands were like knotted wood and he was thin and wiry. His hair tended to fly free and, as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , it was more gray than not. He favored plain robes and boots. He had green eyes that glowed like a lamp when his ire was roused.

Possessions

He carried a staff made from a tree branch that always sprouted green shoots and leaves.

Activities

Ilkin was one of the senior members of the church of Mielikki in the vicinity of Amphail and was often involved in preparations for the Rite of the Stag Lass celebrated every year in that village.
