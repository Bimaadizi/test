RegionThunder Peaks , Dalelands

Oversember Vale was a small valley located in the Thunder Peaks in the Dalelands .

Description

Small pools of water lay at the northern end of the vale. A line of stunted, scraggly trees hugged the steep rock-face on the northwest side of the valley.

Inhabitants

The Cult of the Dragon inhabited the vale. The cult had at least one hidden cavern in the vale.
