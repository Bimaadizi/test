The Imaskarran Tome of Ice was a heavy and tattered tome with a single rune on the cover, translating simply to "Ice". The Imaskarran Tome of Ice detailed the process of creating an amulet of natural armor from a quartz crystal and the knuckle of a skeleton , using the spell mage armor .

Eltoora Sarptyl sought, maybe with the help of the Hero of Neverwinter , this tome around Port Llast . Though allegedly of Imaskarran origin, they were readable to the Hero of Neverwinter . It was in the possession of the wizard Wanev, close to Port Llast.
