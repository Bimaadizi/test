Murghir's Lane was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Murghir's Lane was located in the southwest section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward . The road branched northeast off of Snail Street at the border with the Castle Ward . It continued northeast and ended within a neighborhood just west of the northern reaches of Book Street .
