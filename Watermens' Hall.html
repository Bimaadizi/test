Watermens' Hall was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Guild of Watermen .

Location

The Watermens' Hall was located along Dock Street , in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This guildhall stood three-stories tall.

Services

This building doubled as a warehouse for the Guild of Watermen, acting as a place where they could store their ferries and skiffs . As well as a place to store any and all things found while trying to keep Deepwater Harbor clean.

Reputation

The Watermens' Hall was among some of the more notable and recognizable guildhalls in the Dock Ward. Like other guildhalls in the Dock Ward, the Watermen's Hall was considered by locals to be a landmark.
