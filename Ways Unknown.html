Ways Unknown was a wilderness guide business that operated out of the Uptown District of Ravens Bluff during the late 14 th century DR . It was run by the excitable, but experienced, half-elven outdoorsman Eryn Lashir .

The business was headquartered in a short, thatched-roof building located on Manycoins Way in the city's Torchtown neighborhood.
