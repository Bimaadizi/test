Khalreshaar was an interloper goddess in the elven pantheon, aspect of Mielikki . Believed by half-elves to be the daughter of Silvanus and Hanali Celanil , and as such, the first truly half-elven deity. Khalreshaar was Mielikki's name on Evermeet and served Rillifane Rallathil .

History

She was originally a human druid before being magically transferred to Arvandor by the Seldarine . She was friend with the elves and even helped them to fight against a local human warlord. His soldiers slew her.
