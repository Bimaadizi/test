Samak was the sole settlement found on Maribar Island in the Cities of the Pearl region of west Zakhara .

Geography

Samak sat along the southwestern shore of the island facing the city of Sikak roughly 60 mi (97 km) to the southwest. The Bountiful Shoals coral reef filled the waters of the Golden Gulf directly offshore from the town.
