Dissection instruments were scientific items in the Realms often used by wizards to harvest material components from dead organisms or to study their anatomy.

Description

A set of dissection instruments included knives , pins, blotters, sponges , lenses, etc.

History

These items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 10 gold pieces .
