Sydney was a human wizard of Luskan in the mid–14 th century DR and a member of the Arcane Brotherhood as an apprentice of Dendybar .

History

Sydney became Dendybar's apprentice some time before 1356 DR . Cunning and ambitious, she continually sought her master's favor against all whom she considered rivals, including the guard Jierdan . She had many sources of information, both inside and outside of Luskan. Among these was Harkle Harpell , who had an unrequited attraction to her.

In the Year of the Worm, 1356 DR , Sydney was ordered by her master to kill the Companions of the Hall with the help of the flesh golem Bok . They also partnered with assassin Artemis Entreri for a time, as he sought Regis and agreed to Dendybar's proposal of an alliance.

She twice fought the companions in Mithral Hall but was eventually slain by Catti-brie .

Possessions

Sydney owned a mirror of mental prowess that was gifted to her by Dendybar. She kept this mirror within her small room at the Host Tower of the Arcane .
