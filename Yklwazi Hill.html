Yklwazi Hill ( pronounced : / j ɪ k ʊl ˈ w ɑː z i / yick-ul-WAH-zee ), meaning "hill of blades", was one of the four hills in Chult atop which Port Nyanzaru was built. It was located on the western end of the Market Ward .

Description

The top of Yklwazi Hill was dominated by the city's Grand Coliseum . Its slopes were characterized by terraces and steep, stepped streets. A wide stone bridge connected the top of Yklwazi Hill to the top of neighboring Mount Sibasa to the east.
