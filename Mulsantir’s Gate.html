Mulsantir's Gate was the main gate in the city of Westgate .

Location

Mulsantir's Gate was located on the western side of Westgate. The Trader's Road passed through the gate into the city where its name was changed to the King's March .

History

The gate was named in honor of Mulsantir Illistine I , a mercenary from Chondath who overthrew the last pirate king of Westgate and claimed the throne for himself in the Year of the Cat's Eye, 429 DR .
