Lydda Artemel was a noblewoman of the Waterdhavian noble house Artemel during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

She lived within Artemel Villa located at the corner of Sul Street and Vondil Street in the northeast section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward .

Relationships

Lydda was the consort of Artemel's patriach, Bresnoss Artemel . Ord was her eldest son.
