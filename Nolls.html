Nolls was an evil-hearted enchanter who infamously created his master work – Fey Duster , a powerful enchanted short sword made from tortured pixie 's dust .

History

Sometime before the late 14 th century DR , Nolls's twisted mind decided to capture and crush pixies to collect their dust to enchant the Fey Duster. The sword ended up being enchanted to possess the ability to make the enemies fall asleep, however, druids claimed that it was the death screams of tortured pixies that sounded in the opponents' heads was what rendered them unconscious.
