Dilgar Sneese was a worshiper of Umberlee who prayed at the Water Queen's House in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Dilgar was a light-skinned rock gnome woman with light brown hair and dark brown eyes. She wore a simple cloth jerkin and a blue tunic.

History

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Dilgar Sneese frequented the Water Queen's House, uttering prayers to her goddess. She was fearful of messing up the prayers and sought to come in for a single devotion and promptly leave.
