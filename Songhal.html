Songhal was a town located in the foothills of the Earthspur Mountains , within the Uplands of Impiltur . The enlightened town served as a center of learning for the kingdom, which contrasted with the surrounding farmsteads and agrarian holdings of the Impilturan thaedar.

History

In the Year of the Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , the Silver Stag of the High Heralds , a loremaster by the name of Ilvarthaele Everstone , established her office in the Stormspire , located within the central district of Songhal. This was the first occasion such a post had been centered within a city located near the Sea of Fallen Stars since the founding of the High Heralds in the 10 th century DR .
