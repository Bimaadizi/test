The Welcoming Hand Inn was an inn located in the village of Eveningstar during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The inn was located in along the High Road on the western edge of the village.

Inhabitants

The Welcoming Hand Inn was a burned-out ruin in the late 1300s DR.
