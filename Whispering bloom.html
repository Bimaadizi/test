Whispering bloom was a species of flowering plant known for its pleasant scent.

History

In the late 15 th century DR , whispering bloom flowers were available in the city of Baldur's Gate in the Western Heartlands . Some used widow's blossom and whispering bloom to scent wax used in flower preservations, however, adding scents affected the solution's integrity.
