Ihanvas was a spirit naga in the Doomvault dungeon in 1486 DR .

History

Ihanvas was tasked by the Red Wizards of Thay with managing captured dragon turtles . She fulfilled her duty until 1486 DR when heroes from Daggerford fought and defeated her.
