Drogthu Elfeater was an orc warband leader of the kingdom of Many-Arrows in the late 15 th century DR . He served under commander Vansi Bloodscar in their occupation of the River District of Neverwinter in the late 1470s DR.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Drogthu and fellow warband leaders Kragmar Deepscar and Lurg Bloodspear were in charge of coordinating attacks against the Neverwinter Guard . Finally, Scout Gaelen of the Guard sent an adventurer to infiltrate their barracks through a secret passage and eliminate all three of them.
