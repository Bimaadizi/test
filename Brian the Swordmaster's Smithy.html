Brian the Swordmaster's Smithy was a blacksmith shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The smithy stood two stories tall.

Location

Brian the Swordmaster's Smithy was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward on the south side of Brian's Street where it was joined by Fishwife Alley .

Inhabitants

The smithy was owned by Brian the Swordmaster , a Lord of Waterdeep .
