Klavaeron was a priest of Mielikki living in Cedarsproke in the Gulthmere Forest on the Dragon Coast in the late 12 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Shrike, 1196 DR , the ranger Rhighaermon O'Antlers gave the holy spellbook Yornar's Trail Companion to Klavaeron.
