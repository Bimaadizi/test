Melarvyn was a doorguard for Haelithtorntowers , the home of Lady Joysil Ambrur in Marsember , Cormyr , in the late 14 th centur DR .

Personality

He was quite long-winded with words, and had an active, suspicious mind.

Possessions

One of his bracers could be used to ring a gong.

Activities

He watched the door for Lady Joysil Ambrur, under the supervision of the steward Elward Daunthideir .

History

Melarvyn refused entry to Roldro Tattershar in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR ; he summoned the steward, Elward, when Roldro refused to leave. After Roldro threatened the steward with revealing his secrets, he was granted permission to enter; Melarvyn wondered if the steward would let him live after what he'd witnessed.
