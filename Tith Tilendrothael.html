Tith Tilendrothael was one of the flying enclaves of Netheril .

Along with Lhaoda , it was brought down from the sky and destroyed by the magic of the phaerimm in the Year of Breaking Storms, −427 DR . The other enclaves established wards to guard against suffering the same fate.
