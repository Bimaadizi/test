A potion of giant strength was a type of concoction that modified the imbiber's raw physical power to that of a certain kind of giant .

Description

The liquid of these types of potions was transparent and contained a fingernail of the specific giant . They varied in rarity from uncommon to legendary, which scaled with the power of the giant. Potions of giant strength were sold across Faer√ªn .

Potions of giant strength had a sliver of hill giant 's toe nail floating in the vials. The liquid itself had a fetid smell, making it hard to swallow.

Powers

The imbiber had an increased chance to hit opponents, as well as do more damage with thrown or melee weapons . The types of potions available modified the user's strength from their own to that of a hill giant, frost giant / stone giant , fire giant , cloud giant , a storm giant , or even a reef giant .
