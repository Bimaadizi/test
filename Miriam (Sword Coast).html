Miriam was a woman from the Sword Coast who fell in love with the man named Edmund who became a warlock .

History

At some point in her life, Miriam became indebted to Maggie Two-Fingers . Somehow, Maggie learned of Miriam's name and began harassing her for the money. Miriam's lover Edmund took decisive action. He agreed to an infernal pact with a cambion to gain warlock powers and joined a mercenary group to earn money, and pay back Miriam's debt.
