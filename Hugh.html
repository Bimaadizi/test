Hugh was a young man living in the village of Tweyar in Tethyr in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR who joined the Tweyar Self-Defense Force .

Abilities

Hugh was a relatively stealthy and skilled hunter.

History

Because of his skills at hunting, Hugh was chosen to accompany Vander Stillhawk in the Tweyar Self-Defense Force's second battle, in which they attacked a bandit camp. Before dawn, each shot a sentry, successfully allowing the entire group to raid the encampment without an alarm being raised.
