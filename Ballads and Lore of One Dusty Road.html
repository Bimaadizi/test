Ballads and Lore of One Dusty Road was a work of two volumes penned by Sharanralee Crownstar , a renowned adventuress and secretly a Harper agent in the mid-14 th century DR .

Contents

Lost and Away was a traditional elfin fireside ballad sang by the Fair Folk recorded in Ballads and Lore of One Dusty Road .

History

Ballads and Lore of One Dusty Road contained stories and musical works collected by Sharanralee Crownstar during her long adventuring career. The books also collected her musings, notable about the true value of friendships.
