Giant otters were a monstrous variety of the mundane aquatic mammal of the same name .

Description

Giant otters were both fast runners and fast swimmers. Their bodies measured 10​ to ​15 feet (3​ to ​4.6 meters) in length.

Behavior

Much like the mundane otter, these creatures were relatively peaceful and playful creatures. Sliding around and playing tag were some of their favorite past times.

Giant otters would not attack others unless threatened, cornered, or their young were threatened. In which case, they fought fiercely. Even their young would fight to defend themselves.

Ecology

Diet

Giant crayfish were one of many creatures they were known to prey upon.

Habitats

Much like their smaller kin, giant otters could be found inhabiting a variety of lakes and rivers. They were particularly known to inhabit the Lake of Steam in South Faerûn , the Moonsea lake , freshwater bodies in the Moonshae Isles , as well as the rivers and coasts of Phlan .

Beyond the Prime Material plane , they could be found in the River Oceanus .

Relationships

Giant otters tended to cause some collateral damage as they played. Breaking carts, overturning boats or wagons, and panicking horses .

Some nereids were known to keep giant otters as pets. And some aquatic rangers were known to have them as animal companions .

Summoning

Those who specialized in sea-based magic could summon between 1-3 giant otters with the monster summoning IV spell . A giant otter could also be summoned by means of the conjure animals spell.

Usages

Much like the mundane otter, creatures were known to hunt this giant variety for their pelts. Their pelts typically sold for around 1,000 to 4,000 gold pieces .
