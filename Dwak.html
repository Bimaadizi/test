Dwak was a hulking ogre bouncer at the Bubbling Bulwark in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . He was responsible for guarding the main public door to the establishment, while Louella Bonecracker guarded the private inner door.

Relationships

Dwak rather liked Annalise , one of the barmaids. He disliked the adventurer Garit Strongarm for his repeated rowdy behaviour and harassment of Annalise.
