The Haddenbils brothers of Deepingdale were known for being the first to use dams to solve the local irrigation problems and provide water for their fields of oat .

Description

The Haddenbils made dams from giant granite blocks and sealed them with clay and giant spiders ' webs. The brothers gained the use of a spade of colossal excavation which they borrowed from a warrior whose identity is not known.
