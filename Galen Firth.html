Galen Firth was the captain of the Riders of Nesmé , the armed forces of the city of Nesme in the Savage Frontier in the late 14 th century DR .

When Nesmé was attacked by trolls in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , he came to Mithral Hall to ask King Bruenor Battlehammer for military support.
