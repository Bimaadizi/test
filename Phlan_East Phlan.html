East Phlan was the newer region of the Phlan that formed along the eastern bank of the Stojanow River .

Description

Cobbled streets wound through the area between houses and buildings that leaned against one another.

History

The growing community of east Phlan developed over the course of the 15 th century DR , following the reconstruction of the city over decades prior. It formed grew eastward from the city's Nobles' Quarter .

During the occupation of Phlan by a cult of the Dragon cell and its green dragon patron Vorgansharax , during the early 1480'S DR , East Phlan was largely destroyed and overrun by the cult's hobgoblin and orc minions. Arcane energy spilled over from Valjevo Castle corrupting some of its native plant life. A Gulthias tree formed from the earth, spawning forth a number of blights . A trio of Emerald Enclave druids attempted to restore order in that part of the city
