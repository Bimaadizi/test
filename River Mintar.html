The River Mintar was a small waterway found near the Lake of Steam .

Geography

The river originated within the western region of the Thornwood . It flowed south and west for approximately 200 miles (320 kilometers) before emptying into the Lake of Steam.

Notable Locations

The city of Mintar was founded along the river's western bank where it met the sea.
