Ranaghax Auvryndar was a drow member of House Auvryndar in the drow settlement in the Sargauth Level in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR .

History

Ranaghax was a warrior and a son of Matron Mother Vlonwelv Auvryndar .

In 1492 DR , Ranaghax was off-duty with six of his soldiers and he was reenacting a one-on-one battle he'd fought against a hobgoblin captain.

Activities

Ranaghax led a contingent of twelve drow warriors under the orders of his older sister T'rissa Auvryndar .

Personality

Ranaghax knew very well his subordinate position in drow hierarchy and waited for the right time to fight.
