The Animal Handlers' Affiliation was the guildhall for ranchers, drovers and veterinary physicians in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The guildhall was located in the northwest section of Daggerford on Fields Lane near the Farmer's Gate .
