The Coin Spinners were a Luskan street gang that was based in the old Clearlight temple of Tymora . The Coin Spinners were active at least as early as 1475 DR . The gang took its name from the coin that symbolized the goddess and its symbol was a coin that incorporated both Tymora's and Beshaba 's symbols.

The gang used charitable activities such as as feeding the poor as a front for criminal acts, which included protection rackets.
