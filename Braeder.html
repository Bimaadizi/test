Braeder was a gnome and a local hero of the village of Ieirithymbul in the Sword Mountains .

Braeder was the namesake of Braeder's Pit , a sinkhole that swallowed up the Felrenden stream in the western edge of the Sword Mountains near Ieirithymbul, by the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .
