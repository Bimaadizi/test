Wydda was the monk sacristan of the Monastery of the Yellow Rose .

History

Wydda was a monk of vaguely elven ancestry from Aglarond who went to the Monastery of the Yellow Rose, where she became the high cleric.

Personality

Wydda was patient, wise, and caring. However, she spent more time praying than among her flock. She was impressed by people showing compassion, endurance, or theological knowledge.

Activities

Wydda was the sacristan of the Temple of Ilmater inside the Monastery of the Yellow Rose. Her duty was praying and offering guidance to her fellow monks.
