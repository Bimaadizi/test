Torisk Haut was a member of the Red Wizards during the 14 th century DR . He served as the "spymaster" and second-in-command of the Thayan enclave in the city of Proskur , under khazark Chathin Zurn .

Spymaster Haunt oversaw the security of the enclave in Proskur, closely monitoring any residents that he believed were suspicious or otherwise unreliable. He accomplished this by means of a vast network of informants and agents that fed him intel from within the enclave, and throughout the rest of the mercantile city.
