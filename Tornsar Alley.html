Tornsar Alley was a small side alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Tornsar Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . The alley ran east to west, connecting Buckle Street in the west with the Rising Ride in the east just south of Caravan Court .

Notable Locations

A portion of the House of Good Spirits was located along Tornsar Alley.
