Kossuth ruby was a rare magical gem imbued with a spark of Kossuth 's elemental fire .

Description

Kossuth ruby was a red gemstone with a tiny piece of elemental flame dancing inside it, emitting a warm pulsing glow.

History

In 1370 DR , Ibbalar Thrul , an apprentice of Halaster Blackcloak , used the Kossuth ruby's elemental energies as a power source of a magical device that gave the mad wizard a small army of spectators under his control. He used them to pester Waterdeep citizens and cause chaos within the City of Splendors. Khelben Arunsun sent his agent into the depths of Undermountain to find the lair of Ibbalar Thrul and stop the invasion. Subsequently, the Khelbenite found a way into the laboratory past the wizard's spectators and deactivated the portal by stealing the Kossuth ruby . The magic gem was left with Khelben for safekeeping afterwards.
