Rain Dancer was a minor enchanted staff from the Emerald Grove in the Western Heartlands .

Description

Rain Dancer was a pale wooden staff crowned with a tear-shaped topaz gem . The weapon was always cool to the touch.

Powers

The staff's wielder could cast the create water spell at will.
