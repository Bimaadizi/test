Brusha was a bandit who lived in the Hordelands near Khazari during the mid–14th century DR .

Personality

He considered himself to be sly and romantic.

Activities

Brusha was the leader of a secret bandit base located somewhere within the valleys southwest of Ahs Trango Shan beneath Katakoro Shan near Khazari. He commanded between 50 – 100 other bandits.

Rumors and Legends

Rumored to be of noble birth, Brusha styled himself a rebel against the lord of Khazari.
