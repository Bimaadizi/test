Alonso de Bartuella was a sea captain in 1357 DR .

Personality

His biggest flaw was being very vulgar at dinner.

Activities

His ship sailed to Kozakura sometimes. He always demanded the most exorbitant price for his services, but was a very competent captain and navigator with a loyal crew.
