Sarya yr Kadila yr Pesarkhal el Thamal was the granddaughter of Syl-Pasha Ralan el Pesarkhal of Calimshan through his daughter Kadila el Thamal. She was born in 1370 DR . When el Pesarkhal heard the news of the birth of Queen Zaranda Star and King Haedrak Rhindaun III 's triplets two months prior, el Pesarkhal proposed a marriage between his new granddaughter and their son, Prince Coram Rhindaun :

The results of this proposal are not recorded in history.
