Ernest Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles at the turn of the 8 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High King Tobert . His successor was his own first son, Carthus Cymrych . His second son, Alec II , then ruled as High King after Carthus abdicated.

History

Ernest assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Scarlet Sash, 679 DR , following the death of his father.

He ruled for fifty-two years before being dying of natural causes in the Year of the Visions, 731 DR .
