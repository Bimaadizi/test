Jalimin Frindos was a human fighter and the Lord Protector of the town of Triboar in the Dessarin Valley in the late 14 th century DR .

History

A one-time adventurer , Jalimin retired and settled in Triboar, where he served as an aide to Lord Protector Faurael Blackhammer for many years.

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Faurael died fighting the tanar'ri hordes at Hellgate Keep and Jalimin was elected by the people as his successor.

Activities

As Lord Protector, his duties were to command the town's militia; to settle disputes; and to make, amend, or remove the Lord's Decrees at will.
