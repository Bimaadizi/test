The Simbulmyn , also known as the Simbul's Guard , was the personal guard of the Simbul .

Possessions

Human , elven , and half-elven members of the Simbulmyn were given powerful enchanted bracers that granted them iron will and unnatural toughness.

History

In 1374 DR , the Simbulmyn was commanded by High Captain Hovor Seawind .
