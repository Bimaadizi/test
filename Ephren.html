Ephren was a soldier of the Flaming Fist that fought in the army of Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

She was particularly close with her fellow soldier Jacek , whom she convinced to join the Flaming Fist.

History

Ephren was among the contingent of Flaming Fist soldiers present at Waukeen's Rest when the inn was raided by goblin and drow cultists of the Absolute in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . Even though the inn was set aflame and Duke Ravengard was abducted, Ephren could not bring herself to take action, stricken by grief at the death of Jacek.
