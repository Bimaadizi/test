Honest Jon was a member of the Targos Guard , active in the early 14 th century DR .

Description

Honest Jon was dressed in the Targos Guard's leather armor , a horned helmet , and was armed with a spear and dagger .

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , the town of Targos in Icewind Dale was besieged by the Legion of the Chimera . One of the Legion's surprise attacks hit the Targos docks, where Jon, Brohn , and Reig Redwaters stood guard. Jon survived unscathed, but Brohn was killed and Reig was badly wounded. Jon did his best to bandage his partner's wound, but he needed a healing potion or risk bleeding to death. Shortly after, the Wicked Wench arrived with a group of adventuring mercenaries from Luskan , who proceeded to aid Reig and the town at large.
