Flamaerdown was a noble family from Athkatla in Amn .

History

By the late 14 th century DR , Flamaerdowns were presumed extinct. The family's huge estate stood in the Temple District on Aundle's Walk . Subsequently, the estate was repurposed into the Temple Bell , a shop that dealt in religious paraphernalia, holy symbols , and religious texts from numerous denominations.
