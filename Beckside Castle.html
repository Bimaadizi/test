Beckside Castle was a fortification located along the High Road in the kingdom of Cormyr , to the east of Tyrluk .

History

The castle had lain in ruins since the end of the Goblin Wars in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR .

Circa 1485 DR , adventurers used the castle ruins as shelter when traveling down the High Road.
