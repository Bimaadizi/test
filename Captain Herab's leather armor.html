Captain Herab's leather armor was a unique enchanted light armor worn by Captain Herab who sailed the Sea of Swords in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

This lest of leather armor was masterfully crafted and reinforced with lightweight metal plates and ensorcelled to provide extra defense without a loss of flexibility.

Powers

The armor possessed a +3 enchantment.

History

Captain Herab wore this set of magic armor during her tenure working for the Black Network in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , transporting smuggled weapons and supplies of an orcish army that menaced the city of Daggerford . She was adorned in the armor when faced with the Hero of Daggerford and his allies in the pirate hideout of the Crab's Claw Coast . With her life likely being forfeit, the fate of Captain Herab's leather armor was unknown after that point.
