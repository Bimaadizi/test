Valantha Moonbreeze ( pronounced : / v ɑː ˈ l ɑː n θ ə / vah-LAHN-thuh ) was a merchant in Elventree during the late 1480s DR.

History

Valantha sold finger foods at a celebration for Mielikki at the Hall of the Unicorn in Elventree .

Personality

Valantha had a terrible sense of humor. However, she often only asked for a joke or a funny story as payment.
