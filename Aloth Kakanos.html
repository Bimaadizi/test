Aloth Kakanos was a mage who worked within the Thayan enclave in the city of Proskur during the 14 th century DR . Despite his choice of working arrangements, he was not an official member of the Red Wizards . Aloth was rarely found within the city, as he often undertook missions for the khazark of the enclave, Chathin Zurn .
