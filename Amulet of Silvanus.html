An amulet of Silvanus was a magicall amulet associated with the Silvanite faith.

Description

The amulet had a depiction of a waterfall. It offered the wearer a sense of peace and serenity.

Powers

The wearer of the amulet could cast the spell lesser restoration .

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , one of these amulets could be found beneath a large rock, outside of the Emerald Grove .
