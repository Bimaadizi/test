Amethyst Trueblood was a gnome priestess active in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Amethyst Trueblood disliked and mistrusted all elves after an elven thief once lifted her coin purse, armor, weapons, and even her holy symbol , leaving her defenseless in face of danger. Nevertheless Amethyst continued praying for the trickster elves' salvation.

Possessions

Amethyst never went anywhere without her war pony named Stardancer . She usually was armed with throwing axes , a magical +2 shortsword , a magical +3 shield , and of course, her holy symbol .
