Mierngras was a flowering plant from the Vilhon Reach that blossomed in early Mirtul , punctuating the season change in the region.

Description

Mierngras were low flowering shrubs with beautiful blossoms of violet and gold. These plants flowered once per spring and called "nature's waterclocks" by the inhabitants of Chondalwood .
