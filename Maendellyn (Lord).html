Lord Maendellyn was an inhabitant of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

Lord Maendellyn hosted a covert meeting with the people of other houses at Castle Maendellyn , conspiring against Coronal Eltargrim , on 241 DR after the hunting of Elminster Aumar . He'd lost a son in that hunt.
