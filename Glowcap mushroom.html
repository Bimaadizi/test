Glowcap mushrooms were edible luminescent fungi found in the depths of the Underdark .

Description

Glowcap mushrooms were popular among people traveling through the unwelcoming deep cavers of Underdark, providing them with much needed illumination and pleasant-tasting food. Glowcaps' emitted cold flickering radiance.

Availability

Even though glowcaps grew only in the Underdark, they were a trade commodity. Merchants from the Underdark traded wines and glowcap mushrooms for Calishite silks , pitted dates , and metal-works of good quality. This trade was especially lucrative among surface expatriates from Underdark.

Glowcaps were one of the ingredient in preparing duergar fragrant fungus stew that possessed a bright flavor and was popular in the Western Heartlands .
