Euphorbia , also known as spurge , blasting root , or springwort , was a flowering desert plant that was valued by spellcasters.

Usages

Eating roots of the euphorbia plant was believed to improve a thief 's manual dexterity, improving their chances of picking a lock.

Some used euphorbia plant in powder form as an aphrodisiac, increasing the imbiber's interest in the opposite sex.

Euphorbia bean oil and spring water were used to mix an elixir that was the material component required to cast the run spell .
