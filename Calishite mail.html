Calishite mail was a general term for magic chainmail from Calimshan with the additional quality of keeping the wearer cool in that country's hot desert climates.

Description

This armor weighed about 40 pounds (18 kilograms) and produced an abjuration aura. It could be crafted by a mage trained in the enchantment of magical weapons and armor who could also cast the endure elements spell.

Powers

Calishite mail provided no protection from fire ; it simply regulated one's body temperature, keeping it at normal levels.

Reputation

Calishite mail was sought after particularly in the region of the Calim Desert and elsewhere along the Shining Sea . A suit typically sold for 3,840 gold pieces .
