Marcabruk was the leader of the hybsil Autnak Tribe in Chondath in 1358 DR .

History

Marcabruk and his tribe in 1358 joined the Company of the Singing Dawn , the Llandrydd's Steel group and Enhanen 's men in fighting the undead army led by the mad cleric of Tempus Theodoric .
