Wands of evil protection were enchanted wands that held multiple charges of protective spells unleashed upon command.

Description

Wands of evil protection were 1 foot and 3 inches (0.38 meters) shafts with a round flat head. The total number of charges varied from wand to wand.

Powers

Each of these items held several charges of the protection from evil spell. The magics held within the wand could be discharged at will using a command word . Upon expenditure of a wand's last charge, was destroyed and consumed by magic.
