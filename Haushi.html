Sheikh Haushi was the head of the Ruwaldi tribe of Bedine in the years leading up to the early 1360s DR .

History

Haushi represented the Ruwaldi at the great meeting of Bedine tribes at Elah'zad in the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR . He was quite skeptical of the display the sheikhs witnessed, that of the exiled spellcaster Ruha channeling the voice of the Mother of Waters .

All the warriors of the Ruwaldi tribe were murdered in their sleep by Zhentarim agents utilizing magic to make themselves invisible. Although there were no direst witnesses, it was presumed that Sheikh Haushi was among them.
