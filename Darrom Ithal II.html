Darrom Ithal II was the first crown prince of the kingdom of Tethyr .

Relationships

Darrom was the son of King Darrom Ithal and Queen Saraala . He was the older brother of Silvam Ithal and had a younger sister as well.

History

Prince Darrom was born shortly after the marriage of his parents in −211 DR . Sadly, the kingdom's first prince and heir to the throne of the Ithal Dynasty died of a fever in −206 DR , while not even six years old.
