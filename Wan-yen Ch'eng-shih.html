Wan-yen Ch'eng-shih was the Shou magistrate of Kwachow in 1359 DR .

Personality

Wan-yen was a lazy ruler who was disinterested in his post.

Activities

Wan-yen cared little for his subjects, preferring to tax them heavily to fund his extravagant luxuries and desires. He allowed the guards to bully the population of Kwachow.
