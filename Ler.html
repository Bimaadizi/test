Ler was a commoner from the City of Coin, Athkatla , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Ler was a helpful man of average built with brown hair and fair skin.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Ler was one of the eyewitnesses of a fierce magic battle between Jon Irenicus , the Shadow Thieves , and the Cowled Wizards that collapsed a part of the Waukeen's Promenade in Athkatla's Center District . He later recalled that a young spell-caster named Imoen was taken into custody by the Cowled Wizards along with the cruel archmage .
