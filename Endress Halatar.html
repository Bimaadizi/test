Joybringer Endress Halatar was the high priestess of the High Hallowed Festival Hall , a temple dedicated to Lliira , in Yhaunn in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Endress was a playful and lively person, keeping a light heart her whole life.

Activities

Endress was the leader of Lliira's faithful in Yhaunn. Her congregation within the temple consisted of 22 other priestesses and 49 followers. Endress was a specialty priest of Lliira called a joydancer .
