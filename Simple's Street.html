Simple's Street was a hard-packed dirt and gravel paved street in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This street opened south off of the courtyard Virgin's Square , then ran from east to west. At its western end the street terminated on Snail Street , while its eastern end terminated on the Way of the Dragon .
