Rilira was a former drow noble in Szith Morcane in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Rilira was the head of a noble house in Szith Morcane.

Circa 1486 DR , after the fire giants occupied the outpost, Rilira hid her identity when the nobles were slaughtered.

As a slave, Rilira tried to organize her fellow drow.
