Dalniir was an outlying settlement of Athalantar in the 3 rd century DR .

Geography

Dalniir was near the edge of Athalantar, in a position that would be northwest-by-north of the future Secomber .

Flora & Fauna

The forests around Dalniir provided for good hunting opportunities.

Government

The Magelord Taraj Hurlymm took over governance of the territory personally until his death.

History

In the Year of Old Danger, 219 DR , the prince Othglas Aumar joined the local Church of Malar , abandoning Hastarl . In the Year of the Sword Violets, 220 DR , he was killed by the Magelords of Athalantar , who transformed him into a boar and had his own underpriests hunt him down.

Later, Magelord Taraj Hurlymm took personal governance of the territory, until his death in the Year of the Chosen, 240 DR , when he was ambushed and slain by Prince Elminster Aumar and the sorceress Myrjala Talithyn .
