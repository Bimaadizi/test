Ignatia was a high-ranking member of the Cult of the Eternal Flame at the Temple of the Eternal Flame in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

In 1491 DR , Ignatia led the warriors at the temple and commanded the hobgoblins of the south barracks. However, some adventurers infiltrated the temple and confronted and defeated her.
