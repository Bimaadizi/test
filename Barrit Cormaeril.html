Lord Barrit Cormaeril was a member of the noble Cormaeril family of Cormyr in the 14 th century DR .

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Lord Barrit attended King Azoun Obarskyr IV 's birthday feast, hosted by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Keep . He was accompanied there by Lady Alise Cormaeril .
