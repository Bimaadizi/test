Gloves of lightning were pairs of enchanted , ornate gloves unique to the Old Empires region.

Description

These gloves were made of a flexible leather and covered with small copper rivets.

Powers

When wearing both gloves, a wielder was able to fire powerful bursts of electricity from their fingers three times per day. This electricity shot out to a range of 30 feet (9.1 meters) This was especially dangerous against those who wore metal armor.

To activate this effect, the gloves could not be worn alongside gauntlets .
