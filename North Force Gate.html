Location

The North Force Gate sat on the northern side of the Force Bridge that spanned the River Tesh where it met the Moonsea .

Defenses

The gate was flanked by a pair of guard towers. Furthermore, a pair Zhentilar guardposts were located just inside the gate. Ten to fifteen Zhentilar were stationed at each guardpost.

Activities

The North Force Gate provided access to both the Common Quarter and Harbor District in southwestern Zhentil Keep from the Foreign Quarter via the Force Bridge.
