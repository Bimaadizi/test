Haliator Mendara was a seasoned Red Plume of Hillsfar and the leader of Hillsfar's troops stationed in Y没lash around 1367 DR .

Description

Commander Mendara was a hardened and cruel man, middle-aged by 1373 DR . He had a reputation of turning a bland eye on extortion and looting done by the Red Plumes of Hillsfar.

Activities

Haliator commanded roughly 240 Red Plumes arranged into 24 detachments. He condoned looting so long as his troops were the ones doing the looting.

History

In the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , the Zhentarim became targeted by a mysterious Order of the Crippled Fang .
