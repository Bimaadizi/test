Diamond Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Diamond Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . It originated on Ivory Street , branching south then southwest to cross the Street of the Singing Dolphin and eventually end at Westwall Street . Diamond Street was intersected by Delzorin Street , Vondil Street , Chasso's Trot , the Rough Road , Satchel Alley , Seawatch Street , the Street of Whispers , and Pharra's Alley .
