Tharren Nhalass was the thirteenth Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Tharren inherited the throne when his older brother, Rhendar II , was slain by demons sent by Narfell . Tharren met the same fate and was killed during his first year as Arkhan in −605 DR .

Relationships

Tharren was the fourth son of Darthen Nhalass III . His eldest brother died with their father while invading Narfell. His next oldest brother was Rhendar II, and his younger brother was Sharath .
