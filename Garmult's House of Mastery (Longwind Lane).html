Garmult's House of Mastery was an academy founded by retired sea captain and fleet owner Darbrand Garmult on Longwind Lane in Baldur's Gate active in the 14 th century DR . The center of learning officially taught law and commercial business . In fact, Garmult used his establishment to direct his pupils to the financing of piracy and other illegal endeavors, as he was himself a smuggler .
