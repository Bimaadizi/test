Thoroar the Mediogre was a merchant who sold his wares on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

Thoroar sold a variety of goods at his merchant's stall, including a variety of potions , gold trade bars , healing kits that allowed people to treat their own injuries, and even small portable altars that allowed them to pay homage to their deity while far off from a temple.

History

During the Siege of Helm's Hold circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Thoroar set up shop at the Last Redoubt war camp just outside the city of Helm's Hold .
