Sniden Elkslodge was an old apprentice inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Though he was fifteen years his elder, Sniden was apprenticed to Derf and worked in Derf's Skin Art learning the trade of decorating a person's body with tattoos and piercings. He gained notoriety for tattooing map on a dwarf 's back once.
