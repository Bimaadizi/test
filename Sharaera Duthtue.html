Sharaera Duthtue was a moon elf wizard and a member of the Company of the Cloak in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

She was enamored with the party's leader, as well as her magical teacher, Mhair Gulzrabban . Although Sharaera knew he was holding her back, Mhair reminded the elf of her deceased uncle Phondelarrus as they were both supreme actors and manipulators.

Although she would not betray Mhair, Sharaera would leave him the moment he seriously hurt her or any other elves . .
