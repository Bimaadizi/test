Ayken's Stump was a small village in the High Duchy of Langalos during the 8 th century DR .

Trade

The people of the village were loggers.

History

The entire village was annihilated by the Slayer in the Year of the Awakening Wyrm, 767 DR . Within the next tenday, reports of their disappearance were related to the High Duke by his seneschal, Bresmer .
