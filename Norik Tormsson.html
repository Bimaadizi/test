Norik Tormsson was a merchant that sold his wares on the Sword Coast North during the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

He offered a variety of wares for sale, including several types of magical potions, gold bars , and preconstructed kits that provided an advantage to adventurers or other outdoorspeople to utilize their skills while out in the wild, exploring dungeons, breaking into secure locations, or dealing with matters of an arcane or religious nature.

History

As of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Norrik had set up shop at the entrance to the Cragmire Crypts alongside the Harpers agent Garl Gnarlclaw .
