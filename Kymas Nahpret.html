Kymas Nahpret was a vampire Red Wizard of Thay and a necromancer in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , together with Umara Ankhlab , Kymas was tasked by Szass Tam with the mission of finding the young Chosen of Lathander , Stedd Whitehorn . When Umara and the pirate captain Anton Marivaldi finally decided to instead help Stedd in his mission for Lathander, Kymas tried to stop them but was instead killed.
