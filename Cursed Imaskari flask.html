the cursed Imaskari flask was an Imaskari relic unearthed from the ruins of Solon by the slaves of Ambuchar Devayam .

Description

This was a copper flask.

Powers

The flask could provide up to 10 gallons of water per day. The water out of the flask would be green and foul-smelling.
