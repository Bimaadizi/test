Jhiarl , also called the Emerald Tapal and Elfkiller was a magical sentient tapal , a traditional weapon of the tritons of Serôs .

Personality

It was claimed that Jhiarl chose its own wielder. When their need was great, a rogue water current would coil up and along their arm and finally solidify into a tapal, becoming Jhiarl . However, it often possessed the wielder with its own evil intelligence.

History

It was much used in the fall of Aryselmalyr , earning it its sobriquet.
