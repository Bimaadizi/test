Nicomantis was a cleric with strange powers in Daggerford around 1370 DR .

Activities

Nicomantis had a strange power over all predacious six-legged insects. He also all-consuming interest in plants of all kinds, alive or dead.

Rumors said that Nicomantis spoke with plants and/or the plants advised him about evil nearby, or that he could make them come to life.

His home was located in the northwest corner of Daggerford along Horse Way .
