An ersatz eye was a type of enchanted eye prosthesis .

Powers

When embedded within one's eye socket, they could see through this orb as though it were a real eye. In addition, only the one wearing an ersatz eye could remove it from their eye socket.

History

In the late 15 th century DR , the archeologist Marville Du Coot discovered the burial site of an ancient orc tribe in a cave system south of Bryn Shander. Within that burial site, he found an orcish-style ersatz eye , which uniquely gave its wearer the additional ability of being able to read and understand Orcish .
