Clan Ophinshtalajiir was one of the dragonborn clans of the nation of Tymanther .

Description

Members of Clan Ophinshtalajiir used two light jade rings on the right side of neck as their distinctive clan piercings.

History

Clan Ophinshtalajiir was one of the oldest Thymari clans, dating back to the foundation of Tymanchebar .
