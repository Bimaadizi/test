Aban al-Hadhar was a gnome barber who lived in Hawa in 1367 DR .

Description

Aban possessed a loud voice and could usually be heard at any time in the cities' Free Market . He favored speaking quickly and it was said his razors were almost as fast as his speech.

Activities

Aban was a wealth of information and always seemed to be aware of important gossip and news. He was an excellent source of tips for travelers willing to provide an appropriate tip in exchange.
