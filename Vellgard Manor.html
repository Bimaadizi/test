The Vellgard Manor was a villa located in the Blacklake District of Neverwinter .

Description

The manor was flanked by metal gates and an inner wall that could stop a small army.

History

Mordai Vell inherited the manor after the eruption of Mount Hotenow , in 1451 DR , when all his human relatives died in the cataclysm. He then repurposed the manor to use it as the headquarters of his Ashmadai cell.
