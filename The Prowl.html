The Prowl was a small backalley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Prowl was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . It ran in between Alnether Street and the western end of Cymbril's Walk , branching west off the southern end of Alnether Street to loop through a small neighborhood before connecting with Cymbril's Walk.

History

The alley was named for a nobleman who enjoyed loosing his pet panthers in the alley to terrorize the neighborhood. The Prowl was also truly haunted. A pair of skeletal hands lurked in the alley, strangling unsuspecting victims from behind four times per year.
