Floshwa was a female loxo wizard and a member of the Mindulgulph Mercenary Company , based in Mindulgulph Castle just south of Priapurl on the Dragon Coast .

In the 1360s DR, Floshwa went with the company to the Eastern Shaar , where they took an extended contract with the gold dwarves of the Great Rift . Their purpose was to study the chances of war in the local Underdark . They were still here at the end of 1367 DR . During this time, Floshwa joined her commander, Gayrlana , and a wemic named Karrlon on a scouting expedition in the Shaar.
