Reece was a halfling girl that lived in Phlan that worked at Madame Freona's Tea Kettle during the late 15 th century DR . She was one of five daughter's the restaurant's owner, Madame Freona .

Description

Reece had ginger curly hair. She was not quite yet considered an adult as of the 1480s DR .

Relationships

Reece's other sisters included Blaizette , Briez , Grelinda , and Whittlee .
