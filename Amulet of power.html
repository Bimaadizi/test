An amulet of power was a powerful magical amulet that periodically boosted the wearer's latent magical energy.

Powers

Once acquired, amulets of power took 30 days to meld with their new wearers and become fully functional. Once functional they would, one day a week, boost the effective amount of spells that the wearers could cast, alongside the power, duration, and range by 50%, rounding down. As the wearer's spell capabilities went up, so did their ability to overcome their target's resistances against their spells.
