Fzoul Chembryl ( pronounced : / f z ˈ uː l ˈ tʃ ɛ m b r ɪ l / fz-OOL CHEM-bril ) was the Faerûnian god of service to evil as well as an exarch of Bane . Formerly the mortal Chosen tyrant of his dark overlord, Fzoul was killed while fighting the Shadovar and was granted the status of an exarch as his reward for dying in Bane's service.

History

Fzoul's tower was located in the western section of Zhentil Keep's Inner Quarter .
