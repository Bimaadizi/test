The Wheel of Elements was an artifact that was believed to had been created by the Elemental Evil cultists, however, because the artifact contained all four elements and the cult's inalbility to cooperate, the item's true origins remained quite mysterious.

Powers

When the Wheel of Elements was harnessed, it allowed the wielder to call forth the elements and bestow beneficial boons onto themselves or others. Each of the elements could create a symbol of elemental power in an area of effect, allowing all friendly creatures who were in the area or passed through it to be blessed with these elemental boosts. The power of the fire granted them the ability to deal flame damage, wind gave them the benefits of the freedom of movement spell and made them light on their feet, earth boosted their defences and vitality, and lastly, water healed them and bestowed the gift of regeneration . Additionally, the Wheel of Elements increased the wielder's vitality, physical power, and vitality.

History

Despite the Wheel of Elements' mysterious origins, it was recovered from the cultists of the Elemental Evil by the heroes of Neverwinter in the Year of the Scarlet Witch, 1491 DR .
