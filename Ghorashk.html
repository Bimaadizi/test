Ghorashk was one of the commanders of the fiendish horde that assaulted the Sword Coast during the Second Dragonspear War of the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Description

The leader was in charge of the Storm , a division of a hundred bugbears that fought alongside ogres and the black dragon , Maelestor Rex .

Personality

He was a devious and savage individual, willing to do whatever it took to win, whether on the battlefield or in his life.

History

Ghorashk gained his level of power within the Dragonspear horde after murdering his previous superiors.

In the Year of the Wyvern, 1363 DR , Ghorashk and his forces fought in the final battle of the war at the site of Dragonspear Castle .
