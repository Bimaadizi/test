Phelos Marmwitz was a librarian of the town of Ghars in Cormyr in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Activities

Managing the small Ghars library , Marmwitz lent out books , took returns, insisted patrons be quiet, and obtained new books of no less than forty or so years old and that in his view were "serious literature". He was disappointed his library was not frequented more often.

Marmwitz had a special interest in history and shared his own collection of history books with the library. He searched for certain tomes and was pleased to find them.

History

On Eleint 17 of the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , Jasper went to the library to return The Internal Structure of the Brachiopod on behalf of Benelaius and questioned Marmwitz about recent research into the Vast Swamp and the legend of the bandit chieftain Fastred , as part of his investigation into Dovo 's murder. Marmwitz was irritated at Jasper for disturbing Grodoveth in his research. He returned the next day for follow-up questions.

Later, on the night of Eleint 20 , Marmwitz was collected by the local Purple Dragons for a midnight meeting at Benelaius's cottage regarding the murders of Dovo and Grodoveth; Marmwitz rode in a carriage driven by Khlerat . There, Marmwitz merely confirmed Grodoveth's research into local legends of Fastred and the Vast Swamp.

Personality

He was rather fussy and a stickler for library rules.

Description

He was lean and withered with great age.
