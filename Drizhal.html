Drizhal was a young soldier and lieutenant in the allied army of the Dalelands that lived and died during the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

He was a slender man with gangly limbs and long, vivid blonde hair.

History

In the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , while less than 20 years old, Drizhal fought alongside his countrymen to defend their home from the Zhentilar in the second battle of Shadowdale . For a time during the battle, he served under the authoritative Kelemvor Lyonsbane , facing the soldiers of Ronglath Knightsbridge .

Facing insurmountable odds and unable to execute a tactical retreat, Drizhal stood firm against the Zhent soldiers but was ultimately killed soon after the fighting broke out.
