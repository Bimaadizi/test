The Heltharn Dynasty was the fourth of the ruling dynasties of Impiltur .

Overview

House Heltharn was the family of paladin war-captain Imphras Heltharn the Great , who united what were four independent cities against a hobgoblin horde in 1095 DR . After his victory the second realm of Impiltur was formed.

The dynasty included many paladins as kings and its queens were sorceresses or wizardesses . It included six kings, one queen, two regents, and five others who never ruled but were marked for succession.

Its last king was Imbrar II , who died without an heir in the Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR .
