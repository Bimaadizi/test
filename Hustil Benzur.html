Hustil Benzur was a Zhentarim agent in Daggerford around 1485 DR .

Activities

Hustil usually resided at the River Shining Tavern , posing as an agent working for Amnian investors. In truth, he searched for opportunities for business or blackmail that could advance the Zhent agenda.
