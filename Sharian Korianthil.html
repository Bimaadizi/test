Sharian Korianthil was a windrider of House Korianthil in Evermeet in 1367 DR .

Activities

Sharian was the head of the isle's eagle -riders. He was known for his near-suicidal tactics, acrobatics maneuvers, and use of exotic weapons. Sharian was also known to indulge in sylvan celebrations, drinking contests, passionate affairs, and numerous brawls, especially against sun elves .

Personality

Despite his reputation, Sharian was a powerful warrior loyal to Queen Amlaruil Moonflower .
