Marlgate was the warehouse district within the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire .

Geography

Marlgate was situated north of the Iskalat district and south of the Ashlarks district.

History

This warehouse district of the City of Brass was largely abandoned in 1367 DR. Some time following the Spellplague , it began to see far more use as a place to store goods brought from other planes .

Inhabitants

Alongside the vermin found throughout Marlgate, private armies of dao merchants could be found here.
