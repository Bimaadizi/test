Quaerthara Gudelmer was the youngest member of the Gudelmer merchant family and a daughter of Albaerlus Gudelmer who resided in Athkatla in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Like her sister, Quaerthara Gudelmer was a smart, astute, and charming woman.

History

By the late 14 th century DR, Quaerthara Gudelmer had three sisters. Iylandra and Raeveshara Gudelmer were the oldest siblings, while Nilunele was Quaerthara's older twin. The sisters lived with their father, Albaerlus, in the family mansion, the famously haunted and beautiful Dancing Dolphins House in Athkatla's resplendent Gem District . All four sisters were professional escorts and guides to Athkatla, offering their services to visitors in need of companionship. The Gudelmer sisters were well-known in Athkatla's upper classes through their social status and professional life. Those visitors who requested Quaerthara, or any other specific sister, had to wait for up to three days on average for the first availability.
