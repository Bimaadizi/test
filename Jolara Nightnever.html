High Moonmistress Jolara Nightnever was a Selûnite priest and the leader of Moonshadow Hall in Yhaunn during the 8 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Lost Wayfarers, 757 DR , during her time at Moonshadow Hall, Jolara was informed of the charges laid against the New Moon Pact by High Moonmistress Brima Chintamn of Bright Lady's Tower in nearby Chancelgaunt . Believing that the Pact had indeed fallen to the heresy of believing Shar and Selûne the same being, she supported the charges and condoned the extermination of the Pact.

Unknown to Jolara, the priestess Asha the Silent who arrived later that year was in fact the wolf Halftail, transformed into a human by Selûne in order to protect the legacy of the Pact, and who eventually succeeded Jolara as High Moonmistress.
