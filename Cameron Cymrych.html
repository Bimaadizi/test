Cameron "the Wise" Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid–4 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty : he was the grandson of the dynasty's founder, High King Warren I , and the great-grandson of the dynasty's legendary namesake, Cymrych Hugh . Cameron was the cousin of his predecessor, High King Markus , and his successor was his second son, Bran Cymrych .

History

Cameron assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Drawstring, 333 DR , after the death of his predecessor.

He ruled for thirty-seven years before dying of natural causes in the Year of Sleeping Dangers, 370 DR . In that final year of his reign, the so-called " Great Colony Fleet " of Calimshan set sail to colonize the Moonshaes, however it was blown off course and never arrived.
