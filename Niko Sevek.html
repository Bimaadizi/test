Niko Sevek was a member of the Cult of the Howling Hatred in Mulmaster in 1491 DR .

History

Niko was the steward of Zor Aleksi Drejkov . Secretly, they were part of a cell of the Cult of the Howling Hatred in the city.

In 1491 DR, however, the high masters of the cult believed that Dreikov had failed and send a cadre of assassins led by Little Wing to eliminate him. Little Wing first killed Sevek.
