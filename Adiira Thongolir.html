Lady Adiira Thongolir was a member of the Thongolir noble family of Waterdeep in the early 1370s DR .

She was also an active member of the Confluence , a secret society of steel dragons and dragon-blooded humans and their patrons, who battled the influence of the Unseen and other shapechangers in Waterdeep. She was an important figure within the organization around 1374 DR .
