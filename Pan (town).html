Pan was a settlement in the kingdom of Koryo in Kara-Tur , lying on the shore of the Yellow Sea at the western end of the Vorkani Mountains .

Organizations

Based in Pan was a yakuza family. Their symbol was a blue feather, which was tattooed on the heel of the left hand of their agents. They were allied with the notorious wu jen No Ziar the Mole.

History

In 1357 DR , a band of adventurers headed by Glei Yuindo returned to Pan and it was rumored they had discovered the famous lost fortress of Toranu Ganppo . However, before they could tell their story, they died of a mysterious sickness.
