Osber was a thief in service to the Zhentarim living in Cormyr in the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

He looked like a warrior.

Possessions

He carried at least one poisoned knife, which held sleep poison .

Relationships

He was known to Baergasra , a local undercover Harper , to have a number of thugs in his service; three of them were not with him in the evening he attempted to capture Shandril.

History

Late in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Osber was at the Wanton Wyvern inn near the Hullack Forest when Shandril Shessair , Mirt , Narm Tamaraith , and Delg Ironstar entered. When Shandril Shessair attracted some attention by falling asleep and then waking up, Osber approached them and recognized her as the waif wanted by the Zhentarim . He hurled accusations and then attacked the group together with six of his underlings. Before he was slain by Delg alongside his team, he managed to poison Narm.

Baergasra later revealed his name was Osber, with some details about him.
