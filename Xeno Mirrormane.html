Xeno Mirrormane was a cleric of Cyric .

Appearance

Xeno's face was covered with wrinkles and he had curly silver-white hair. He wore long purple robes and a death's-head ring on one finger.

History

Xeno murdered his brother Maskul Mirrormane .

Xeno turned 60 in 1368 DR .

Possessions

Xeno kept a dirk in the sleeve of his robe.
