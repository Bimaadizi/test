High Radiance Durneth Seafarer was the leader of the temple known the Tower of the Morning .

Activities

Durneth was involved in recruiting leading clerics along with Daelegoth Orndeir .

History

Seafarer led the clerics of the Tower of the Morning from at least 1369 to 1374 DR . In Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Durneth proclaimed edicts that gave credit to the Risen Sun heresy .
