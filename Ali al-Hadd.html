Ali al-Hadd was the Sheikh of Tajar in the Pearl Cities of Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Relationships

Ali maintained a longstanding alliance with Amir Bouladin al-Mutajalli of the House of Sihr . His father was the previous sultan, Kori al-Zafiri . Ali's magical advisor, Sheera bint Tanar was actually his daughter. He did not recognize her publicly.

History

Ali was tasked by his father with gaining an audience with the Grand Caliph in Huzuz to bring light onto the dire situation occurring in Tajar under the oppressive rule of the Sultan who preceded Kori.
