Rilyanna Saerfynn was a young noblewoman of the late 15 th century DR .

History

Rilyanna's parents died when she was young and she lived at the family manor with her brother. The dark angel Sathariel visited her when she was a child and she agreed to serve Asmodeus .

In the Year of Deep Water Drifting, 1480 DR , Rilyanna attempted to finish the ritual of the First Flensing to bring Asmodeus to Waterdeep but was killed by Quessahn Uthraebor .

Relationships

Together with her brother Callak , she was the last of her family.

Rilyanna was rumored to have had incestuous relations with her brother.
