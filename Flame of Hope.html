The Flame of Hope was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Activities

Run by the candle artisan Jastra Blynthard, this shop offers all her creations as well as complementary items to shed light.

Location

The Flame of Hope was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward on Tulmaster's Street at the mouth of Sethma's Court .
