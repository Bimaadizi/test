Durinbold Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Durinbold .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's North Ward . It sat on the southwest corner of the intersection of Vhezoar Street and Sulmor Street . The west side of the villa sat on Ilzantil Street .

Description

The walled compound contained three buildings and a large courtyard. The buildings were three or four stories tall.
