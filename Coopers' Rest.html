Coopers' Rest was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Coopers' Guild .

Location

This building was located along Pressbow Lane , in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep . It was located across the street from the Sleeping Wench tavern and was next to both the Shippers' Hall and their warehouse .

Structure

This guildhall was two-stories tall.
