Gundar Brontoskin was the King of Grunwald and the Thunderbeast Tribe .

Description

The king was a good looking man who ruled Grunwald with a light touch.

Possessions

In battle, Gundar used a broadsword , a handaxe , and carried hidden daggers on his person.
