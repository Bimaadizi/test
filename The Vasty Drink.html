The Vasty Drink was a large freshwater lake located in northern Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The lake was found in southcentral Gnarhelm along the foot of the Fairheight Range . The lake was approximately 20 miles (32 kilometers) long from north to south. Along with Salmon Bay , the Vasty Drink separated Gnarhelm into eastern and western halves. The lake flowed northward to empty into Salmon Bay via a small unnamed river. The southeastern corner of Hardangheight bordered the Vasty Drink on its northwest shore.

Notable Locations

A moonwell was located in a small forest along the Vasty Drink's southwest shore.
