The Mountains of Forgotten Dreams were located southeast of the Genies' Anvil within the High Desert of Zakhara .

Description

This mountain range boasted peaks as high as 5,000 ft (1,500 m), many of which were former volcanoes. Ash and chalky rock could be found along the foothills surrounding the mountains. Travel through the mountain range was relatively easy because of the many wide passes that could be found every few miles.

Inhabitants

The Mountains of Forgotten Dreams were the home of the rare white saluqi .
