Ma'mur Island was part of Al-Tarik Islands in the Golden Gulf and Crowded Sea within west Zakhara .

Geography

Located just northeast of the much larger Tarikat Island , Ma'Mur Island was roughly circular in shape with a diameter of 30 miles (48 kilometers). The island was surrounded by coral reefs. This otherwise insignificant island was the location of mysterious ruins that, as of 1367 DR , remained unexplored.
