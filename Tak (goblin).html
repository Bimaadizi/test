Tracker Tak was raider for a goblin camp that made their home within the Shattered Sanctum on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

She wore a minimalist set of leather armor decorated with what appeared to be claws taken from some manner of ferocious beast.

History

Tak was among the goblins that pillaged Waukeen's Rest in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . Her raiding party stole what goods they could before setting the inn on fire, leaving many dead.
