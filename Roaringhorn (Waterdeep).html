Roaringhorn ( pronounced : /ˈ r ɔːr ɪ ŋ h ɔːr n / ROR-ing-horn ) was a noble house of Waterdeep . Both the Waterdhavian and Cormyrean branches of the family had a historical reputation for being bold, impetuous, and living with a zest for life. Their detractors characterized them as reckless, boorish, and lustful.

Activities

As of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , they bred horses in Amphail , where they owned an estate and had personal guards. The head of the family had six nieces, all of whom were wizards . These nieces used their magic to keep watch for thieves and other ill-doers.

Base of Operations

The Roaringhorn Villa, known as the " High House of Roaringhorn ", was located in the North Ward , on the west side of Belzound Street between Tarsar's Street and Suldown Street .
