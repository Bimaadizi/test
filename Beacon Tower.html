The Beacon Tower was a city building located in Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tower stood seven stories tall.

Location

The Beacon Tower was found in the north section of Waterdeep's City of the Dead along the perimeter wall.
