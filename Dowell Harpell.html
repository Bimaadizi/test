Dowell Harpell was a member of the Harpell family and their spokesman in the late 15th century DR . He also served as Longsaddle 's representative on the council of the Lords' Alliance .

Abilities

Dowell was an adept enchanter , but was a notably unskilled evoker .

Relationships

Dowell took the Harpell name following his marriage to Penelope Harpell . The two of them loved each other dearly, and had an open relationship.

He helped Penelope in teaching magic to the reborn Catti-brie .

History

Sometime prior to the 1480s DR, Dowell met and fell in love with Penelope during a battle with a hill giant king in which Dowell was nearly eaten alive, but was saved by his future wife.

As of 1489 DR , he was recognized as the spokesman for all of Longsaddle at the Lords' Alliance's council.
