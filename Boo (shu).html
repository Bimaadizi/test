Boo was a female shu of the Jengi tribe who lived on the Malatran Plateau on the shores of the Lake of Giant Flies in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Like other shu of the Jengi village, Boo was shy and wary of visitors from other tribes. Even those outsiders who proved themselves were not accepted by Boo and her tribe. However, shu showed no fear of visitors.

History

In the Year of Gathering Darkness, Malatran calendar, sinister earth tremors shook the Living Jungle. The shu Jengi tribe was hit hard by the disaster. The tribe's witch doctor , Kumon Ubok , was badly hurt, and a collection of his spell fetishes unleashed waves of wild magic . A tree sprung out of nowhere in the village and dragged unwitting shu 100 feet (30 meters) feet into the air as it grew. Boo was among six terrified shu who ended up dangling off the massive tree. Boo's fate was unrecorded. However, a group of heroes of the Living Jungle came to the Jengi tribe's aid.
