Maldiglas Turntower was the Magister from 1318 DR to 1322 DR . He created a number of refuges.

Appearance

He was six and a half feet tall, with sharp brown eyes and a firm stride.

Personality

Maldiglas was polite, and used to be humble and straight about the limits of his powers. Over time, he developed a bit of a superior attitude as he became a more accomplished mage.

Possessions

He was never seen without a staff half again as tall as himself.

Abilities

Maldiglas soon found he was susceptible to just about all diseases he encountered.

History

Originally from Sembia , his family moved to Amn and then to Chessenta over trouble with an inheritance. As a youth, he decided to become a mage when a mage named Kheldivur died in front of him, spellbook in hand; Maldiglas took the book and resolved to become a wizard. From the start, he decided he wanted to be independant from rulers, and to travel broadly, hoping to see as much of Faerûn as possible. During his travels, he was attacked by Qilder Yarven , whom he defeated, gaining in the process the mantle of the Magister .

After a prayer to Mystra which she answered, he embroiled himself in politics, calling on rulers uninvited, often cowing them into removing oppressive laws that were an obstacle to sorcerous learning, or cajoling them into peace agreements on terms that best suited his ends. He also publicly reproached mages who employed their magic in petty bullyings, and strongly encouraged the plunder of old tombs and older magic throughout Faerûn. His most important project, however, was the creation of a number of refuges , which eventually drained all of his lifeforce.

Rumors

Maldiglas was said to have been greatly rewarded by Mystra for giving up all of his lifeforce to the task of creating the Refuges. However, his ultimate fate after Mystra's Choice remained unknown as of the late 14 th century DR .
