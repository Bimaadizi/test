Reptilla Half-elven was a half-elf wizard who dwelt in the Dragonspine Mountains in the early 1360s DR .

Skills

She was a highly dexterous mage , and skilled in making poisons. She modified her magical curdled death oil to incapacitate rather than kill, and to evaporate into a breathable substance when exposed to high temperatures.

Activities

Reptilla and her small dragon familiar , Thsst , dwelt in a cave on the sunnier side of the Dragonspine Mountains. From here, they ambushed animals and lone travelers and used them for Reptilla's experiments with poisons . She daubed her curdled death on rock faces along mountain paths around her lair. As the stone warmed under the daylight sun, the curdled death activated and knocked out passers-by, so she could take her victims alive.

Possessions

Reptilla owned a staff of the serpent , bracers of defense , and a cloak of displacement , as well as a vial of curdled death .

History

She lost her staff of the serpent in the lair of a bronze dragon .
