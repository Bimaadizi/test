Jhasso was a merchant from the city of Baldur's Gate on the Sword Coast in the mid–14 th century DR .

History

He originally owned Jhasso's Wagons , a fast-haul transport company. However, Jhasso partnered with six other merchants— Alvund of Ormpetarr , Chond of Calaunt , Dzunn of Sheirtalar , Nammna of Milvarune , Pomphur of Almraiven , and Shield of Everlund —to form the Seven Suns Trading Coster . The "Seven Suns" referred to the seven merchants themselves. Jhasso's own small merchant company was converted into a regional base for the coster .

Jhasso and the Seven Suns were in business by 1358 DR , and still going by 1374 DR .

He found the Jhasso family, part of the Nobility of Baldur's Gate by 1482 DR .

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a member of the Jhasso family was taken by the vampire spawns of Cazador Szarr , a vampire lord of the Lower City , prompting investigations into the disappearances of several other patriars.

Activities

Jhasso was based in Baldur's Gate, where he managed the Seven Suns' regional base there.
