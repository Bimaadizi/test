Narur al-Khawati was a waterfinder for the desert centaurs of the Khawati Clan in the Burning Lands near Moradask in 1367 DR .

Activities

The Khawati Clan's leader, and Narur's sister, deferred to her judgement in important decisions.

History

She rescued her sister from the clutches of the Shorn , a devious cult active in the region. Her auguries warned of danger to the clan if they pursued a vendetta against the Shorn, but the clan's leader ignored the threats in pursuit of vengeance.

Possessions

Narur carried a jambiya .

Relationships

Narur was the older sister of the clan's leader Tebessa al-Khawati .
