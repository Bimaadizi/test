Ansel was an human cleric student of the Neverwinter Academy in Neverwinter in the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Ansel attended the same school as the person who would become the Hero of Neverwinter . He studied divine magic and healing under Elynwyd , the instructor in charge of the school's clerics.

History

When the Wailing Death struck the city of Neverwinter in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Ansel resided and trained in the Neverwinter Academy, honing his magical and healing skills. However, his graduation was not to be—Ansel's life was cut short in the attack on the Academy that left most of its staff and students dead and the school itself put to the torch.
