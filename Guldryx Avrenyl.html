Guldryx Avrenyl was a male drow arch- necromancer of House Szind from Sschindylryn during the 1480s DR .

Description

Guldryx had a tall white mohawk of hair and wore an armored robe .

Abilities

Guldryx was a powerful spellcaster, and could cast ball lightning , cause fear , frost lance , hold monster , negative energy burst , ray of exhaustion , and waves of fatigue . In addition to raising the dead as undead , he could open a portal back to the Underdark in order to draw in a constant stream of reinforcements.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Guldryx patrolled the King's Forest on the surface as the drow forces pushed ever onwards, raising the undead and summoning living reinforcements.
