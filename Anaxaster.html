Anaxaster was a blue dragon who lived in the ruined city of Ascore during the 15 th century DR .

Anaxaster made its lair within one of the wrecked, dwarven stone ships that dotted the landscape.

Relationships

Anaxaster was born to the ancient blue dragon Iymrith .

Anaxaster had a playful relationship with its sibling Chezzaran . They spent their time playfully fighting in the skies above their mother's domain.
