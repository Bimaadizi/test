The Fallow Field Tavern was a tavern located in the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands .

Location

The Fallow Field Tavern was located in the southwest section of the city in the western shadow of the Moneylender's Guild .

Inhabitants

The tavern was owned by a Raumviran named D. Iaroslav .
