Brackleberry was the term Cormyreans used to describe any jam made with mixed berries from the wilderness.

Description

The base for these jams was typically a mixture of gooseberries , strawberries , raspberries , or currants . These berries were leavened with overripe peaches or quinces and packed in sugar.

Brackleberry jams were imported into Cormyr from warmer countries such as Chessenta .
