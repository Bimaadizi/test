Jonah the Unlucky was the King of the Urgh in the 9 th century DR .

History

He was born in the Year of the Heavy Heart, 815 DR and became ruler of something called the Urgh. Known for his cruelty, he almost conquered the North and nearly became the champion of the West Marches .

However, he ultimately died of shock when he won the state lottery in the Year of the Roiling Cauldron, 874 DR .

For unknown reasons, there was a statue in memory of him on the second level of Undermountain as of 1372 DR , which related the above information about him.
