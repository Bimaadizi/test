Rinyon Merrymar was a strongheart halfling shopkeeper in the village of Shining Falls in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Relations

She was the wife of Blaz Merrymar . Together, they had two daughters and four sons, as well a number of nephews.

Activities

Rinyon handled the day-to-day running of Merrymar's Merchandise , the only general store in Shining Falls.

Personality

She was known to be confident and strong-willed, and felt her husband's get-rich-quick schemes, such as discovering the dwarven complex behind the Shining Falls waterfall, to be foolish.

History

In the summer of Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , goods meant for Merrymar's Merchandise were raided on the road by bandits, thought to be orcs of the High Forest . Tragically, Blaz and Rinyon lost not only merchandise but also all four of their sons and three of their nephews, leaving the extended family decimated and the store understaffed. Rinyon and her two daughters were left to run it alone while Blaz went on the road as a merchant.
