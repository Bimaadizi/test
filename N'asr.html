N'asr was the Bedine god of the dead. For a time he was an aspect of the deity Cyric , but some time following the Time of Troubles he became an aspect of Kelemvor .

Relationships

According to the legends of the Bedine, N'asr was an adulterous lover of the sun goddess At'ar the Merciless .
