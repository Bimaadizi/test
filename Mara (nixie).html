Mara was a nixie living in Zhent -occupied Daggerdale in 1367 DR .

Activities

Mara lived in a cavern inside the Crypt of the Mage-lord .

History

In 1367 DR , Colderan Morn filled the Crypt with his nightshades . Mara managed to expel them from her home and tried to stop them from wandering in Dagger Falls , but she needed spells and couldn't stop night-roving wood woses.
