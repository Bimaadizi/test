Rowans were a type of tree in the Realms.

Description

On average rowans could grow to be 10‒20 ft (3‒6.1 m) tall. Their branches grew in comb-like formations, upon which flowers grew on their tips. They also produced red berries that would induce a state of vomiting in most humans and demihumans that ate them.
