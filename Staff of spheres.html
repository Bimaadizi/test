The staff of spheres was a magical staff that created transparent floating bubbles.

Powers

The magical bubbles created by this staff measured 6 feet (1.8 meters) in diameter and could be directed to move by the bearer. Each individual sphere could contain up to 140 pounds (64 kilograms) of matter. Exceeding this weight limit caused the sphere to cease to exist, along with anything contained within, including living matter. Piercing a sphere with any point would also destroy it.

Any living creature within a sphere enjoyed the benefits of a feather fall spell . The spheres did not serve as cages and any creature within a sphere could leave at any time.

History

The staff of spheres was originally created by Elminster in Myth Drannor .
