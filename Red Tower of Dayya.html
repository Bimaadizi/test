The Red Tower of Dayya was located in the Lost City of Ubar in 1367 DR .

Description

The Red Tower rose from the center of the city. Much of Ubar's buildings were in drastic need of repair, but curiously, the Red Tower appeared to be in pristine condition.

History

The Red Tower served as the palace of the Amir of Ubar.
