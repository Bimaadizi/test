Nathoudi stallions were a special breed of horse found in the city of Nathoud where the northern reaches of the Hordelands bordered Rashemen .

History

Nathoudi stallions were breed and prized for their endurance and stamina. They were particularly hardy in cold weather and sure-footed in snow.
