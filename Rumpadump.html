Rumpadump was a myconid sprout of Neverlight Grove in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationship

Rumpadump was a friend of Stool .

Personality

Rumpadump was quite introverted, in contrast to its companion Stool's outgoing nature. Rumpadump would rather stay back from social encounters and would not release its rapport spores until it was vital.

History

Rumpadump lived in Neverlight Grove in the "troupe" of Voosbur . Directed by Zuggtmoy 's will, Voosbur led its troupe to Gracklstugh to collect bigwig and pygmywort mushrooms. Rumpadump was the only one that remained free from Zuggtmoy's corruption, and was concerned that Voosbur's "strange spores" had affected the rest of myconid community.
