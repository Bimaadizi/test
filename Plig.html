Warrior Plig was a guard in the cult of the Absolute who protected the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Plig wore a simple leather jerkin decorated with animal bones or teeth and a metal helmet.

Possessions

She wielded a large battleaxe in battle.

Activities

Plig spent her time guarding the worg pens in the bowels of the Shattered Sanctum.
