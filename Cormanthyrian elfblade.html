A Cormanthyrian elfblade was a magical sword produced in the old elf realm of Cormanthyr , where the keen enchantment was first developed.

Description

They carried the symbols of the old elven houses of Cormanthor .

Powers

An elfblade was a +1 longsword with the keen quality.

Creation

The keen edge spell was required for their creation.

Cost

They were worth around 8000 gold pieces .
