Vargaun Orgolath was the first tiefling Nentyarch of the Orgolath Dynasty of Narfell .

Activities

Vargaun reigned from −578 DR until −535 DR . He was the third Nentyarch during the Orgolath Dynasty.

Relationships

His father, Ulthar the Darkhorn , was Nentyarch before him. Vargaun was Ulthar's firstborn son.
