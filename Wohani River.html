The Wohani River was a river in the Po Yul Dzayul Range in Phutan and Tabot .

Geography

Joined by a tributary, the Wohani River had its headwaters in the Po Yul Dzayul Range, near where the range joined the Haraki Ridge . It flowed south through the foothills, crossing the border between the nations of Phutan and Tabot, before emptying into Nam Tso . The lake was also fed by the Nam River , which emerged from the range further to the south. The Wohani River was known for its misty trails and it was overlooked by Hoshki Gompa .
