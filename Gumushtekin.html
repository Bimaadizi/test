Gumushtekin was a noble efreeti who occasionally visited the island of Al-Zira in the Djinni's Claws in 1367 DR .

Description

He favored dark black and silver clothing.

Personality

Like most noble efreeti, he was arrogant and had a fiery temper.

Activities

He occasionally visited Atabeg al-Mazdaghani on the island of Al-Zira
