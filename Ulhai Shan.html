The Ulhai Shan , also called the Ulhai Mountains , was a large mountain range found in the Hordelands and Kara-tur .

Geography

The Ulhai Shan served as a natural barrier between the vast Ama Basin and the frozen wastes of the Land of the Snow Spirits . The mountains formed a wall along the western and northwestern swamps and forests of the Ama Basin. Many unnamed tributaries flowed south from Ulhai Shan to feed the mighty Ama River . The Ulhai Shan terminated on its southern end at the Mountain of Iron .
