The maul of focused destruction was a unique enchanted maul crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Raika in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

Silverhand crafted the maul to acknowledge and reward Raika's ability to preserve her rage until the perfect opportunity for a devastating strike.

Powers

As this mighty maul was wielded in combat, it granted its owner the ability to forcefully counterattack their enemies.
