Winter's Mask was a unique enchanted face-covering crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Shevarith Kendia in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

Silverhand crafted this mask according to Shevarith's own design. It was intended to evoke the land of Rashemen .

Powers

The Mask allowed Shevarith to create a powerful wind blast to push her enemies around the battlefield.
