Inrath Mirandor III was one of the first kings of Impiltur , the 14 th ruler of the kingdom's Mirandor Dynasty . He ruled for over thirty years in the early-to-mid–2 nd century DR .

Relationships

Inrath was born the first son to Baranth I . He had two sons― Inrath IV and Pendarn ―with his first wife, who eventually succumbed to the plague. Inrath had one other son, Doraven , born to another woman.

History

Inrath III ruled until the Year of the Resounding Call, 157 DR , when he was succeeded by his eldest son Inrath IV.
