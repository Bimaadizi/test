Thurlad Dauntrael was the patriarch of the Dauntrael family in the late mid-14 th century DR .

Activities

His greatest passion was geography. However, he was completely uninterested in history, except where it pertained to magic; he had studied extensively the geography of Toril, completely disregarding political divisions during his study.

Possessions

His mansion of Steelbreeze , southwest of Beregost in some rolling, coastal hills, had a tower with an orrery of Realmspace .
