Kindori were enormous, whale -like mammals native to wildspace .

Description

Adult kindori could grow to a length of up to 80 ft (24 m). They had a row of small eyes on either sides of their snouts, that emit radiant beams of light.

They had no notable mouths and did not require food or even air for sustenance. Rather, they nourished themselves from starlight.

Abilities

These celestial beings were so massive they had their own gravitational pull, and formed their own air envelopes .

Behavior

To communicate with their kind across the vast reaches of wildspace, kindori flashed the light beams from their eyes.

Combat

If forced to fight, kindori used the light emitted from their eyes to blind foes. They would could also whip their tails at enemies to devastating effect.

Ecology

While kindori were not prone to hostility, they were preyed upon by various carnivores native to wildspace, including lunar and solar dragons .

Various species of mold and moss, along with some parasites, grew along the bodies of kindori. Scavenging creatures subsequently fed off these lifeforms. Interestingly enough, kindori carcasses did not completely deteriorate.

Kindori skeletons were sometimes used as frames for spelljamming vessels .
