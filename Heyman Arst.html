Heyman Arst was an inhabitant of the Rivington neighborhood of the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Heyman Arst attempted to report that carts of children's toy donations to be distributed to the refugees of the Absolute crisis had been sabotaged by some bad-faith actors. Despite the claim being true—the toys were rigged to explode using Felogyr's fireworks by the agents of the Absolute in the Cult of Bane —the reports made to Liara Portyr 's garrison were dismissed as Heyman Arst failed to provide evidence of his claims.
