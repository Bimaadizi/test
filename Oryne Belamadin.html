Oryne Belamadin was an Amaunatori priestess in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

Oryne had light skin and golden hair in twin braids.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion , Oryne aided the defenders of Eveningstar . She used spies and divination to learn about the masters of the invasion, House Szind , and discovered that they were on the verge of a necromantic revolution—powerful, intelligent undead that lost none of their living faculties and were entirely loyal to their creators. She quickly organized a strike team to eliminate this threat in particular and weaken Szind and the drow more generally, eschewing all subtlety in favor of simply destroying everything while using drow-seeming illusory disguises to implicate rival houses. She instructed them to use captured flashstones to destroy the special necromantic cauldrons while eliminating the house's necromancers and extant undead.

The Cormyrean team managed to fulfil the objectives, wrecking the estate and eliminating the lead necromancer Relig , and possibly slaying Matron Zhivsha Szind . In the end it may have merely been Relig's overly-grandiose pronouncements that drew Oryne's notice in the first place, with the ultimate undead being nothing more than a phantom menace.
