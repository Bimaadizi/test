Carina Tchazzam was a member of the nobility of Waterdeep and the heiress to the Tchazzam family in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . She died before assuming control of the family.

Possessions

She had a beloved pet cat .

Relationships

Carina was the sister of Ulboth Tchazzam , the patriarch of the Tchazzam family in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR. She also had a younger brother named Tarlon Tchazzam .

History

Carina became heir to the Tchazzam family after her nephew, Ulboth's only son and heir Corahk Tchazzam , disappeared in the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR , and was presumed dead.

She was killed in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , when her pet cat was turned into a beast of Xvim and attacked her. This left Tarlon as the new heir.
