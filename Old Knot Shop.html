The Old Knot Shop was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Location

The Old Knot Shop was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the east side of the Street of the Sword north of Buckle Alley .

Services

The shop specialized in gear for adventurers .
