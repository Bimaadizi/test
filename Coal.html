Coal was a form of fossilized carbon -rich mineral used as a fuel, similar to coke and charcoal in function, and related to petroleum in origin.

Properties

The weight of coal varied on the type, usually between 87 pounds (39 kilograms) to 112 pounds (50.8 kilograms) per 1 cubic foot (28 liters).
