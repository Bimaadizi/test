Lady Narnathra Barelder was a Cormyrean noblewoman and the head of the Barelder family in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Narnathra wore a short haircut suitable for wearing a helmet , and only wore gowns at weddings.

Activities

Lady Narnathra practised with two swords daily, and used clever and shrewd investments in the rebuilding of Sembia to enrich the family.

Relationships

Narnathra was the eldest of Lord Montror and Lady Asmrae 's three children. She married Varkander Heldruth , a lowborn seacaptain who she loved dearly, and had two children, Maereenra and Braeryl .

Two of her closest friends were Lady Laeyalane Tantorn and Lady Cathone "Cat" Ravenhill , who were also her lovers.

History

Narnathra was raised by her mother to be a sensible and wise ruler, and became head of House Barelder in 1479 DR when her father Montror Barelder was slain.
