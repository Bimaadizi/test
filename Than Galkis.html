Than Galkis was a fighter in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

Than had dark skin and wore a full suit of plate armor , carrying a longsword .

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Than helped train fighters in the village.
