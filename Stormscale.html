Stormscale was a unique set of enchanted chainmail designed in honor of the sahuagin Umberlants sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Stormscale was a set of chains made of a dark metal. It granted the wearer limited immunity to lightning and electricity and discharged a damaging spark of electricity on anyone landing a successful strike on the wearer, similar to the electric jolt spell .

History

The first wearer of Stormscale was the infamous Dread Pirate Admiral Bluenose who served Umberlee , the Bitch Queen. Admiral Bluenose parted with Stormscale sometime before the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR when it was stored amongst Governor Blackeyes ' buried treasures near Spindrift Town on the Nelanther Isles off the Sword Coast .
