Belintholme was a small island located off of the southeastern coast of Evermeet on the Trackless Sea .

Geography

The island sat roughly 100 miles (160 kilometers) southeast off of the southern tip of Evermeet.

Description

Belintholme was a forested island that was roughly circular in shape. It was the largest of the islands found off the nearby coast of Evermeet. Several smaller unnamed islands sat off of Belintholme's northeast coast.
