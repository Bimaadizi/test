Mulser was a male warrior and a member of the Zhentilar in High Dale .

History

In 1358 DR , Mulser was part of a secret Zhent occupation army in High Dale. However, his patrol was attacked and defeated by Elminster Aumar and Sharantyr . Before he died, Elminster pretended to be a Zhentarim lord and interrogated him to discover all the details of the Zhent plot against the dale.
