Imaerphil Clovenshield was a female human who led Maalthiir 's guard in Hillsfar .

Abilities

As an experienced fighter , Imaerphil could hold her own in most battles. She was also versed in the skills of a rogue and could operate quietly behind the scenes, if need be.

Activities

Imaerphil Clovenshield led Maalthiir 's 32 elite guards and helped protect the First Lord against any threats.

History

By 1372 DR , Imaerphil had risen to the position of leader of Maalthiir's guard. This duty meant Imaerphil was personally responsible for protecting the First Lord of Hillsfar.
