A tentacle rod was a magic rod crafted by the drow .

Description

Tentacle rods had three rubbery tentacles at one end, which could extend to a length of 15 feet (4.6 meters).

Powers

The wielder of a tentacle rod could attempt to restrain the movement of a creature by wrapping the tentacles around the target. If all three tentacles were successful in the attack, they imposed severe limitations on the victim's movement and ability to act.

Notable Owners

The drow priestess and slaver Ilvara Mizzrym wielded a tentacle rod .
