Belnak Vikom was a dragonborn noble and a member of Clan Belnak . He was Clan Belnak's representative in Ruinspoke in 1480 DR .

Activities

In 1479 DR , Vikom was under the tutelage of Lord Jalt Tuanek .

In 1480 DR, when servants of the lamia Queen Sisay tried to kill Lord Tuanek, Lord Vikom hired some adventurers to dealt with the monsters.
