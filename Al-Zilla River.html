Al-Zilla River was the smallest of the mighty rivers that traversed the Cities of the Ancients in east Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Geography

Al-Zilla originated in the northeast region from a pair of nameless tributaries in the deep jungles near the base of the mountains forming the border with the Haunted Lands .

Description

Though much smaller than the other rivers that dominated this area of Zakhara , Al-Zilla adopted the same criss-crossing pattern as it made its way to the Foreigners' Sea .
