" Rhauron " was mysterious and notorious individual who lived on Tarsith , in the Dusk Ports of Laerakond , in the 1480s DR .

Background

Nobody knew anything concrete about this individual. Elminster Aumar knew that this man was in fact a Laerakondan from Tarsith, however not even he was sure who this individual was. Many urban legends surrounded this mysterious man, and in the Dusk Ports it was believed that he was either a powerful archmage , a thrallherd psion , a guildmaster , or an investor of some sort. Nobody knew even his true name, and people gave him various aliases or titles, with "Rhauron" being the most common and preferred by Elminster.

It was believed that "Rhauron" was the one who created, and later controlled, the mysterious artifacts known as the Winking Eyes of Rhauron , and then procured those gems to Faerûn in order to kill specific people in that continent, including some Waterdavian nobles and merchants.

History

In the decade of the 1480s, the story of "Rhauron" became popular in Faerûn, and many people credited the bard Lothlyn Arngoblet as the creator of this "tale". Many believed Lothlyn had made up the name to demonized Asgandur Durhauron , the innkeeper of the Dragonhaven Hearth in Proskur , who didn't pay the bard for his services on one occasion for a feast.
