Itzujima was an island located in Kozakura .

Description

Itzujima was located in Omitsu Bay directly north of the island of Hinomoto . The island was a rocky prominence enveloped by bamboo and surrounded by reefs.

Inhabitants

The island was uninhabited and served as a sanctuary for a thriving population of sea turtles and sea birds.
