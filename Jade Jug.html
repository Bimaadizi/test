The Jade Jug was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The inn stood four stories tall.

Location

The Jade Jug was located in the far southeast section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the northwest corner of the intersection of Waterdeep Way and the High Road . The rear of the inn sat on the Cat's Tail .
