Lathalshal was a legendary enchanted spear created on the island of Evermeet .

Description

This spear measured more than 8 feet (2.4 meters) in length. Its head was blue steel and the shaft was shaped from white wood.

Powers

Lathalshal functioned as a +5 weapon against enemies. However, the spear's magic was especially deadly against baatezu , yugoloths , and tanar'ri .

Rumors & Legends

Lafarallinn slew the great pit fiend Aikkaraekh with Lathalshal .
