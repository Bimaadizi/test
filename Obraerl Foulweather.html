Obraerl Foulweather was a noble of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR . He was the head of House Foulweather .

Description

He was an aging man.

Relationships

He was the head of House Foulweather .

History

During the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Obraerl Foulweather was one of the proponents of a noble council that would wield power in behalf of, and instead of, the throne.

Later, as the council was assaulted by a blueflame ghost , he was amongst those hewn down. His death was later confirmed.

Reputation

Per the War Wizard of Cormyr Welwyn Tracegar , his death wasn't much loss.
