Aradoness Angharad Odaeyns was an elven priestess and healer that lived in Evermeet during the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , Angharad was dispatched by the Island Council to Blackstaff Tower in Waterdeep via its elfgate , in order to tend the injured Gervas Imesfor . The High Lord of Evereska sought aid from his friend Khelben Arunsun in the city, after finding himself unable to planewalk back to his home city.

After the safety of the High Lord was ensured by Khelben and his allies, the priestess gated back to Evermeet to report what she learned aboutt he goingson at the elven Last Home.
