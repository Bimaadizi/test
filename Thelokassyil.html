Thelokassyil was a sahuagin baron, who advised King Ixilthetocal .

Description

The baron was very xenophobic, hating outsiders. He believed that non-sahuagin humanoids were no more worthy than crab meat, and were only useful for consumption or sacrifices.

Combat

The baron fought with three main weapons: a quarterstaff , a spear and a crossbow . The crossbow was loaded with poisonous bolts of biting .

History

Old sahuagin tales told of outsiders who would decide the fate of the City of Caverns . Gorion's Ward may have arrived in the city with his adventuring party, and the baron advised his monarch to kill them.
