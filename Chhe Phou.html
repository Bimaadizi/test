Chhe Phou was a human warrior from the continent of Kara-Tur in the late 14 th century DR .

Chhe Phou had, alongside his dagger and leather armor , a scimitar of speed and a helm of telepathy .

Relationships

Both his parents died when he was still young and he sought no relationships with others apart from the short companionship of other adventurers.
