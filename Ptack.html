Ptack was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Shadowed Age .

History

Ptack developed five spells during their life, all within a two year period. The spells they invented were crystalbrittle and knock in 3356 NY (−503 DR) , and secret page , nondetection , and wizard lock (which was used to upgrade the quality of a lock) in 3357 NY (−502 DR) .
