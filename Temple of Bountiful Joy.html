The Temple of Bountiful Joy was a temple dedicated to Isis , the Mulhorandi goddess of agriculture and fertility.

Location

This temple was located in Skuld , the capital city of Mulhorand , within the city's Central Ward .

History

As of the 14 th century DR , it was considered to be among the most prominent temples of Isis, along with the Mystic Cornucopia .
