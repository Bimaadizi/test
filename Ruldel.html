Ruldel was a Cormyte soldier in the mid-14 th century DR , part of the Eastern Stonelands Company.

Appearance

He had a hard face and a gray moustache.

Personality

He was eager to fight the Zhents.

History

Ruldel was part of the guard on Thundarlun in late 1357 DR when Shandril Shessair 's party arrived at the town. While she was being interrogated as a traveler, the guard was attacked by Zhentilar forces in pursuit of the girl; Ruldel was quickly slain.
