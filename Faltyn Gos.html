Zor Faltyn Gos ( pronounced : /ˈ z ɔːr f ɔː l ˈ t i n ˈ g ɑː s / ZOR fawll-TEEN GAHSS ) was a noble in Mulmaster .

Personality

Faltyn indulged in self-appreciation, always reminding everyone of his contributions to the city. He became very upset if not called by his correct title of "Zor Gos".

History

In 1491 DR , Gos competed with other Mulmaster nobles over arts. Thus, for the birthday of his wife Tiega Gos , Faltyn commissioned his nephew Andrik Gos to make a painting of her and hired Marim Tem as a musician. However, during the party, his wife was the target of a mysterious attack, so he decided to hire an adventuring party to uncover the truth.
