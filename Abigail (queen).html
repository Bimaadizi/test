Abigail was wife of High King Carrig III of the Moonshae Isles during the late 5 th century DR . Their son was Colin Cymrych .

History

Abigail became High Queen of Moonshae when Carrig III inherited the throne in the Year of the Wager, 499 DR , following the assassination of his father . Carrig III was also assassinated just a year later , at which time Colin was only eight years old. Thus, Abigail named a regent, King Edmund Kincaid of Snowdown , to rule Callidyrr until Colin was older. Colin would assume the throne in the Year of the Rotting Pox, 507 DR .
