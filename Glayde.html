History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Glayde and his associates were the target of a scam by the bard Silke Rosena . She arranged to meet with them at the Red Sheaf inn under the pretense of purchasing some gems . Silke then tried to have Glayde and his fellows attacked by some locals, pretending as if they were local thugs hired to collect from a poor defenseless bard.
