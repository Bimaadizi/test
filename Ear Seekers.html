The Ear Seekers were a tribe of orcs that lived in the North during the late 15 th century DR . They earned their name from the necklaces they wore, which were made out of the ears of elves .

Relations

The Ear Seekers were at war with the wood elves of Ardeep Forest .

History

After a battle between the Ear Seekers and the Ardeep elves, Gurrash was left with a mortal wound. The 20-some-strong war band ventured south to the settlement of Nightstone and tried to force their way beyond its walls.
