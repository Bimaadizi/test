Rings of Crimson were common minor magic items found across the Sword Coast in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

These rings were imbued with simple enchantments that made them glow with soft red light, illuminating the area of 15 feet (4.6 meters) around them.

History

By 1372 DR , these common pieces of enchanted jewelry were found across many pockets, fingers, and merchant stalls across the Sword Coast, and the Moonsea , and in cites such as Neverwinter and Daggerford . Especially popular among adventurers, freeing their hand for weapons and tools, instead of holding lanterns and torches . One of these rings was found in an ancient crypt in the Brush of Barbs of Delimbiyr Vale .
