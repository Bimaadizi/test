The Blackthorn was a unique piece of headwear that originated from the Feywild .

Description

The crown-like headpiece was an intimidating sight to behold.

Powers

The wearer of the Blackthorn was granted protection against physical harm and given enhanced vitality.

Any individual who harmed the wearer was similarly damaged, and would fumble any other attacks they made.

History

As of the late 15 th century DR , the Blackthorn could be found in the depths of Malabog's Castle deep within his realm in the Feywild.
