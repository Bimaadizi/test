Chond was a merchant from the city of Calaunt in the Vast in the 14th century DR .

Chond partnered with six other merchants— Alvund of Ormpetarr , Dzunn of Sheirtalar , Jhasso of Baldur's Gate , Nammna of Milvarune , Pomphur of Almraiven , and Shield of Everlund —to form the Seven Suns Trading Coster . The "Seven Suns" referred to the seven merchants themselves. Chond's own small merchant company was converted into a regional base for the coster .

Chond and the Seven Suns were in business by 1358 DR , and still going by 1374 DR .
