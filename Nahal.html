Nahal was a wild mage and spell inventor.

Description

Nahal the Incorrigible was the student of Hornung , the "king of the wild mages". However, her teacher accidentally ended up on Oerth , and Nahal set out on a search for him, even though she didn't know his location. Nahal's spells were used around the Realms ; these spells were Nahal's reckless dweomer and Nahal's nonsensical nullifier .

Personality

Always curious, Nahal experimented with how wild magic affected the Realms. The curious Nahal wanted to use her untamed powers for a good cause, and so used her knowledge of wild magic to help others control it.

Possessions

The skilled wild mage owned a pair of bracers of defense , a wand of wonder , and a warp marble .

Rumors & Legends

Chants around Sigil claimed that Nahal (along with her teacher, Hornung) taught Quake , a chaotic wild mage of the Xaositects .
