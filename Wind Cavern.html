Wind Cavern was a cavern in the Horn Hills during the 3 rd century DR .

Description

The cave was accessible from atop a series of high ravines. It had a narrow opening, and uneven floor throughout; winds made a strange moaning sound passing through the cave.

Geography

Wind Cavern lay within the Horn Hills .

Flora & Fauna

The inside of the cavern had glowing fungi.

Defenses

The entrance was barely accessible. Beyond the cavern was an old dwarven redoubt also known as Lawless Castle by the Knights of Athalantar .

History

During the early 3 rd century DR , the outlawe Knights of Athalantar used Wind Cavern and Lawless Castle as a base from which they conducted small raids on the forces of Athalantar during the reign of Belaur Aumar .
