Dalanyrr was a young silver dragon that dwelled in the North during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

She respected the sacred spirit mounds of the Uthgardt barbarian tribes, and would not plunder them if requested.

Activities

Dalanyrr would offer her services to help adventurers , if they provided her some type of payment.
