Satama was a trader and later the founder of the cult of the Adama .

History

At some point around −256 DR , the Durpari merchant , Satama had an epiphany and developed the philosophical and spiritual concept that everything in the Toril was interconnected, including the deities , making everything part of one singular, all-encompassing entity, simply known as "The One" or "Adama" in the language of the durpari .

After developing his teachings, Satama began preaching his philosophy, quickly establishing a fast-growing cult in Durpar.

In −252 DR , the influence of the Adama became so great in Durpar that Satama was named Maharajah of the nation, becoming the new head of state.
