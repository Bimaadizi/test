"Honest Jack" Bomhoff was a reputable merchant in Sigil that dealt in exotic novelties.

History

In the month of Hammer , in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , a group of adventurers from Ravens Bluff found themselves transported to the plane of Carceri after defending the githzerai fortress of Tah'Darr from a tanar'ri incursion. There they met the fallen paladin Elendil , who knew a good deal about trade in Sigil and was willing to tell the adventurers the names of many reputable merchants, such as "Honest Jack" Bomhoff.
