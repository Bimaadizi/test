Sumbrylstairs was an elegantly designed manor located in the Temple District of Ravens Bluff . The sophisticated home was located where Crothmar's Lane intersected with Moorland Ride and Rhabie Promenade within the Swordspoint neighborhood.

The front of the build was decorated with graceful pillars that led up to magnificent rooftop spires. The manse earned its name from a set of gilded staircases that were built as an external link to a tower that was never completed.

History

As of 1370 DR , the manor served as the home to the elven adventurer, Talila Dawnsong .
