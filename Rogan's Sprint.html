Rogan's Sprint was a long road that ran through the Ride in the Moonsea North .

Geography

The road originated in the mining town of Whitehorn in the White Peaks , and extended east across the Ride to Verdigris , another mining town in the Dragonspine Mountains .

A stretch of the road ran along the northern banks of Lake Maltorus .
