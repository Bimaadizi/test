Zedoary was a plant used as a spice and alchemical ingredient.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold raw zedoary for 3 sp per 1 ounce (28 grams) for alchemy, and processed zedoary cooking spices for 20 gp per ounce.
