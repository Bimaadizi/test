Naloc was the high priest of Zaltec at Kultaka and one of the closest advisors of the Kultakan's ruler Takamal .

History

In 1361 DR , he was with Takamal during the battle against the Golden Legion , when, in order to turn the destiny of the conflict, the mage Darien levitated to them and killed first Naloc and then Takamal.
