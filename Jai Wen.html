Jai Wen was a town guard that lived in the settlement of Idyllglen .

History

Jai was present in Idyllglen when Yeenoghu assaulted the town with his demonic horde. [ as of when? ] Jai and the other guards bravely defended their fellow citizens during the battle but were driven temporarily mad. In the conflict, they became doused in demon ichor and were compelled to consume the flesh of humanoids for a brief period.
