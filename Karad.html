Karad was a member of the Zhentarim that operated out of their hideout in some caves on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

He appeared to have a particularly strong dislike of the Flaming Fist , especially those that accepted Zhentarim bribes but still threatened their business.

Possessions

Karad carried with him a shortbow that he wielded with some skill.

History

When Zarys ordered the demolition of their hideout in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Karad was among those gathering their goods and setting up explosives for demolition of the caves.
