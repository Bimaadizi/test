Mor Kulenov was a wizard working for King Gareth Dragonsbane of Damara .

Description

Master Kulenov was a pudgy, little man with a billy-goat beard.

Abilities

While in the Plane of Shadow , Mor Kulenov cast Nybor's wrathful castigation on a shadow dragon , and with a different spell, he made his staff and beard shimmer; the latter shimmered longer, suggesting he was casting silverbeard .

History

Master Kulenov was the one who allowed Sergor Marsk and his traitors into the King's retinue on Mirtul 3 in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR . He was present there too, and attempted to rouse the King after he was cursed, but failed. He was later part of the incursion into the Plane of Shadow to recover the King's soul on Kythorn 26 .
