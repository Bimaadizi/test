Niamh was a musically-inclined gynosphinx guardian in Myth Drannor , who remained there at least until the late 14 th century DR in the service of Oghma .

History

The sphinx protected the inner sanctum of the House of Song when Zelliira and her allies came looking for the Icon of the Humans in 1374 DR .
