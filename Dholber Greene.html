Dholber Greene was a guest of Nortale's Hostel in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Dholber was a dark-skinned human man with hazelnut eyes and trimmed gray beard. As a refugee, he was dressed in simple commoner's garb.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Absolute crisis ravaged the Western Heartlands , the city of Baldur's Gate became flooded with refugees. Dholber Greene was lucky enough to secure lodgings for himself and his daughter, Dwindling Greene , at Nortale's Hostel that offered beds to the refugees. Dholber was tired and devastated by the loss of other members of the family, who did not make it to the Gate.
