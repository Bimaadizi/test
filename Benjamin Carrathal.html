Benjamin Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr in the late 13 th century DR . Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles , he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty and was the first son of his predecessor, King Rieger . His eventual successor was his own first son, Alec Carrathal IV .

History

Benjamin assumed the throne in the Year of the Wagon, 1273 DR , after the mysterious disappearance of his father.

In the eighteenth year of his reign , an enormous and unexplained explosion rocked the island of Flamsterd , resulting in the devastation of the island and the disappearance of the wizard for whom it was named .

Benjamin ruled for a total of twenty-three years before he was assassinated by nobles from Ogden in the Year of the Black Hound, 1296 DR .
