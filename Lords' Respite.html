The Lords' Respite was a tomb located within the City of the Dead in Waterdeep .

Location

The Lords' Respite was located in the southern section of the City of the Dead.

Description

The small tomb was made from black marble . Statues of famous Open Lords of Waterdeep faced all sides of the tomb with the exception of the western face. A door made from a mysterious material prevented anyone from seeing or passing through the opening.

History

The Lords' Respite was reserved for any Lords of Waterdeep who wished to be buried there, though only a single Lord had been buried here within the past several decades as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . The identities of all Lords buried within the tomb remained a secret.
