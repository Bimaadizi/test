Lasishal Ama was the greatest spirit in the Northern Wastes of Kara-Tur .

She was the most powerful river spirit , famous for assisting many legendary heroes, and punishing those who had sought to despoil the land. She was presumed to live inside the Ama River . It was likely that the many spirit folk in the area had descended from her.
