A whorlspell was a phenomenon of Avernus in the Nine Hells .

Effects

Per the archdevil Nergal , whorlspells were natural phenomena in Avernus that snatched objects from other planes and deposited them in that one. He mentioned magic , treasure, food, and water; other whorlspells were known to deposit sheep , fish , and sometimes even creatures capable of slaying fiends .
