Mikey was a member of the Order of the Burning Dawn in the late 15 th century.

History

Mikey worked as a bookkeeper in the guildhall of the Burning Dawn in Luskan , where he would man the front counter and negotiate guild contracts.

Around 1490 DR , Mikey was murdered in the guildhall offices by the Order of the Gilded Eye , along with the rest of his guildmembers.
