Orstrar Galannavruk was a merchant from the city of Zazesspur in Tethyr who worked alongside Andred Kuroone in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Orstrar was a long-haired human man with a bushy beard who generally left negotiation and haggling to his more charismatic partner.

History

In the late 15 th century DR, Orstrar and Andred made a living by trading in thael leaf (a type of pipeweed flavoring), herbs , and spices , as well as dried foods and fruits, such as apricots . Orstrar was the coin behind the enterprise. Some of their customers were found in Triboar and Scornubel and included Lurandural Veltree of Scornubel's merchants' guild, the Halanthor .
