Ba'akreth was a githyanki youth from Crèche Y'llek , on Toril during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Ba'akreth had a yellow spotted leathery skin typical for her people and blonde hair.

History

Youth Ba'akreth, along with other gith of what would become Crèche Y'llek, was brought to the recently claimed Rosymorn Monastery of Lathander in the Western Heartlands sometime before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . Soon after, the function of the crèche was changed as the githyanki war against the mind flayers in Faerûn shifted in balance, while Ba'akreth was still a teenager and not fully trained. She continued her military education along with other youths of the Crèche under instructor Sa'varsh Kethk .
