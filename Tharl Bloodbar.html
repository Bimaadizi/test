Tharl Bloodbar was a Knight of Athalantar , who turned to banditry for a time in the early-to-mid 3 rd century DR .

Description

By 240 DR , he had a wild beard.

Possessions

By 240 DR, his armor was piecemeal.

Activities

He had been a bandit for a while, until 240 DR.

Relationships

Tharl Bloodbar was a Knight of Athalantar under Helm Stoneblade .

History

Tharl Bloodbar was a Knight of Athalantar, but became a bandit at some point on or after 218 DR , when King Belaur took over and placed the kingdom under the supervision of his magelords. As a bandit, he participated in the siege of Hastarl in 240 DR, where he sworded at least two warriors, Riol and Sauvar .
