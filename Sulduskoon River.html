The Sulduskoon River was a major river in the kingdom of Tethyr . This spring-fed river flowed west from the highlands all the way into the ocean at the Bay of Bormul in Zazesspur .

Description

The river was wide and slow-moving.

Etymology

The river received its name from the tribe of Suldusk elves living within the Forest of Tethir .

History

Circa −2600 DR , the last survivors of High Shanatar were slain by Calishites on the shore of the Sulduskoon.
