Umber hulk hide breastplate was a type of medium armor crafted using umber hulk chitin and imbued with the creature's natural magic.

Powers

These breastplates were created through stacking together thick umber hulk plates and hide. They offered sturdy physical protection, similar to a +2 enchantment and gave the wielder complete immunity to any mind-affecting abilities and spells.

History

As of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , umber hulk hide breastplates were sold by a drow merchant who did business from the Undergate , Westgate 's unsavory underbelly.
