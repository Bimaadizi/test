Maelstrom's Mesh was a set of enchanted light armor crafted by a sorcerer named Maelstrom .

Powers

Maelstrom's Mesh possessed a simple +1 defensive enchantment. The armor was embedded with countless paralyzing spider poison glands that discharged the effects of a hold person spell when the wearer of the armor suffered damage.

History

Maelstrom's Mesh was among the items ensorcelled with nasty surprises by Maelstrom sometime before the late 14 th century DR . The light armor was created for the sorcerer's employer at the time. Since then, Maelstrom's Mesh changed many hands. As of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Maelstrom's Mesh was worn by Mordecai , the foul-tempered cheating proprietor of the Track in Westgate .
