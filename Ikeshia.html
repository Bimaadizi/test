Ikeshia was an elven noblewoman and philanthropist from the ancient Myth Drannor .

History

Ikeshia, who was known by her family name only, died long before the advent of the Weeping War of 711 - 714 DR which destroyed the great City of Song. She was interred in the Ikeshia's Crypt , at the heart of Polyandrium , one of the burial sights of Myth Drannor on the outskirts of the city. Her final resting place was desecrated by the Cult of the Dragon in 1369 DR .
