Hurgoth Malachite was an undead skeleton dwarf who lingered in Myth Drannor up to the late 14 th century DR .

History

The dwarf once served as the warden of a sealed vault in Onaglym , the House of Gems, and remained as a relentless undead guardian till later ages. He was potentially later found and destroyed by the Cormanthor Elves when Sharora Leonala searched for the phylactery of Droic Orgul in 1374 DR .
