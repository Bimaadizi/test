Ardabad was a gnome and a local hero of the village of Ieirithymbul in the Sword Mountains .

Ardabad was the namesake of Mount Ardabad , a mountain located in the western edge of the Sword Mountains near Ieirithymbul, by the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .
