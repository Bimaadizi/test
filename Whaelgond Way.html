Whaelgond Way was a large road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Whaelgond Way was located in the north and central sections of Waterdeep's North Ward . It stretched through much of the North Ward, orginating in the north on Immar Street and passing southeast then due south to eventually end on Suldown Street . Along its path Whaelgond Way was intersected by Sashtar Street , Tower March , Horn Street , Saerdoun Street , Delzorin Street , Sulmor Street , Hassantyr's Street , the Street of the Manticore , and Tarsar's Street .
