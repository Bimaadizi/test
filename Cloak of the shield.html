A cloak of the shield was a type of magical cloak that offered significant protection to its wearer.

Powers

Each cloak of the shield possessed a number of charges, one of which was used when the cloak's powers were activated. When a charge was expelled, the wearer was surrounded by a sphere of protective energy that turned away physical projectiles and slowed attacks made with melee weapons .
