The bracers of the unseen assassin were a set of magical bracers found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The bracers were specially made to aid assassins in landing sudden, lethal blows. They also aided them in lying, fast-talking, appearing innocent, and generally deceiving their foes in every situation.

History

The bracers were made for elite elven assassins . They may have been recovered by the forces of the Elves of Cormanthor during the Elven Crusade around 1374 DR , after they fought the drow raider Niziira Xiltun .
