Bhaal's Fire was an enchanted longsword associated with Bhaal , the once-dead god of murder.

Description

Little was known about the lore and history of Bhaal's Fire , save for its name and the powers it held.

Powers

Bhaal's Fire possessed a powerful +3 enchantment . Every time it struck true, the sword seared its victims with flames and on occasion rendered them unable to take action for a few moments.

History

As of the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , Bhaal's Fire could be found in the possession of Maiden Ilmadia , a rogue elf that schemed to annihilate an entire city of drow .
