Melon pie was a rarely-liked type of pie pastry stuffed with melon , predominately found in Amn and the city of Westgate .

History

As of the late 14 th century DR , melon pie was a rare sight indeed. It was served at the Harfin Draether eatery and tavern of Athkatla 's Temple District , where the "treat" could be seen sitting rotting away, plastered with flies . Despite its "appetizing" appearance, this pie was often purchased not for its taste but rather because it was served alongside a plateful of universally beloved sugar-cookies .
