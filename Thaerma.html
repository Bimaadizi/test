Thaerma was an individual working with or for the War Wizards at court in Cormyr during the late 14 th century DR . Their skills almost certainly lay at healing.

Abilities

Thaerma was recommended by Huldyl Rauthur when he believed there may be lingering effects from magic attack, strongly implying she was either a healer or some sort of expert in lingering curses.

Relationships

Per Pheldemar , the two of them had history, and he gave the implication of a grudge, or bad blood, between them.
