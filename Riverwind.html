Gate Captain Riverwind was an elven soldier who lived in New Sharandar during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Riverwind was tasked with guarding the three gates of New Sharandar, portals that connected they fey city to the three regions of the Feywild that had been corrupted by the dark fey . He was quite knowledgeable about these areas, the Blighted Grove , dark fey enclave and the realm of Malabog , offering insights about the dangers that could be found within.
