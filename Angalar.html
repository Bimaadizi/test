Angalar was a mage of the Vilhon Reach that was active in the late 9 th century DR .

History

He made a fortune between 860 DR and 880 DR DR , selling minor magic he crafted. Amongst the objects he crafted was the Eye of Angalar , one of his rarest and most powerful.
