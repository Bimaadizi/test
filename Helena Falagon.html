Helena Falagon was an Evenor villager in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Helena had tanned skin and long brown hair, wearing a dark red dress.

History

Early in the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Helena took care of the poor servant Maisby , who had just escaped from the nearby Midler farmstead . The priestess of Lolth Vicala Szind had taken over the farm and captured the Midler family, interrogating them for information about the Thread of the Weave . Helena had Adamor Thellik send two Purple Dragons to investigate, only for them to disappear, and next wrangled some adventurers to assist. The ensuing battle led to the destruction of the drow forces and the rescue of the Midler family.
