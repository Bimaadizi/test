Insanity's Kiss was a unique potion formulated by the hag named Auntie Ethel , originating from her laboratory beneath the Gnarled Teahouse of the Putrid Bog .

Description

The potion was kept in an earthenware jug, plugged by a stopper adorned with a single feather. When holding the jug, it appeared as if there was some enraged creature trapped within.

Powers

Whoever drank from Insanity's Kiss was thrown into a murderous frenzy and became immediately hostile towards anyone around them.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , there was only one known bottle of Insanity's Kiss in existence.
