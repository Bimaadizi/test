TypeMagical powder

Powder of coagulation was a type of magical , yellow powder that helped to heal wounds.

Description

This powder typically came in either packets or pouches that contained around four to sixteen pinches.

Powers

When an open wound was applied powder of coagulation all bleeding ceased and the recipient's body healed to a small degree. If one used a whole packet or pouch, they could apply the powder to all creatures in an area by means of a blowpipe .

History

In the 14 th century DR , three pinches of the powder of coagulation were one of the many rewards that Calvin Longbottle gave to a group of adventurers after they had brought back artifacts from the legendary ship Strongwind .

Following the Time of Troubles , this powder was one of many magical items that a newly admitted goldeye could obtain, curtesy of the Church of Waukeen .
