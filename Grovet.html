Grovet was a young human miner living in Phandalin in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

He was in love with Bertice Edermath , a half-elf woman who was his same age.

History

Grovet was born in the Year of the Fifth Circle, 1476 DR .

When he was twenty years old in the Year of the Duplicitous Courtier, 1496 DR , he ran away from Phandalin with Bertice without telling her father, Daran Edermath . Even if Daran had no idea, the fact that the two had disappeared together was something of an open secret in town. The young lovers headed south to Waterdeep along the High Road , intent on starting a new life together.
