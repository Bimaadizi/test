Abas for All was a shop located in the Clothiers' District of Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

Abas for All sold a wide variety of hand-crafted abas . They specialized in selling abas depicting dragons and wyverns since no other clothier in Huzuz could accurately depict the beasts on cloth.

Inhabitants

Gundar Silvershine owned and operated this establishment.
