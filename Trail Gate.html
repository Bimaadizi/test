The Trail Gate was one of two entry points into the portion of Zhentil Keep located on the northern banks of the River Tesh during the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

The Trail Gate was located in northwest Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter . The Zhentilar Trail outside of the city merged with Wyvern Road at the Trail Gate.

Defenses

The gate was flanked by a pair of guard towers. Furthermore, a Zhentilar guardpost was located just inside the gate.

Activities

The Trail Gate provided access to northwestern Zhentil Keep via the Zhentilar Trail.
