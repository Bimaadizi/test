Throleatha's Public House was a restaurant in Leuthilspar on the island of Evermeet . Its proprietor was Throleatha , an gold elven sorceress .

Atmosphere

It was a quiet and reserved place. Most of its clientele were gold elves. Nobles often met here to discuss their affairs as the establishment offered a number of private rooms and booths. Silver elves and green elves were accepted but visited infrequently, often finding it too quiet.
