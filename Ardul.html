Ardul was a guardsman who served in the Marsember Watch during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He began serving in the Watch at quite a young age.

Relationships

Ardul's moral compass was regularly tested by his superior in the Watch, Topsword Kleef Kenric .

History

On Uktar 2 of the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , when the city of Marsember was being evacuated in response to the invasion of the Shadovar , Ardul and his fellow watchmen were tasked with clearing out Wilhastle Square .

Later on, when Grand Duchess Elira Seasilver was abducted by Yder Tanthul aboard the Wave Wyvern , Ardul was among those brave Cormyrians to board the Lonely Roamer with Topsword Kenric to ensure her safety.
