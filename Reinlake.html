Lord Reinlake was a nobleman of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He wore a monocle.

Relationships

He was acquainted with Lord Parespur Bloodbright .

History

Lord Reinlake was a guest to the Open Feast in Mirtul 26 of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR held at Castle Obarksyr , where he briefly conversed with Lord Parespur Bloodbright and praised the beauty of the lord's escort, the mask dancer and coinlass Amarune Whitewave .
