Vine Street was a road in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the 14 th century DR .

Location

It lay in the western part of Loudwater. In the east, Vine Street joined Pool's Walk where that road descended to the lower docks, though the two roads remained close. By its name, in the west, it ended at the Vine Gate near the Vine Tower .

Description

It was a major thoroughfare, taking over from Pool's Way where it turned toward the docks and receiving much traffic of pedestrians, horses , and wagons. It was dense with the shops that attracted them.
