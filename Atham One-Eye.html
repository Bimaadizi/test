Atham One-Eye was a blacksmith from Suzail , the capital of Cormyr , active in the early 14 th century DR .

History

Atham One-Eye was the proprietor of his smith shop, located to the south of Suzail's Market , on the bank of the Dragonmere . By the Year of the Worm, 1356 DR , Atham was out of business and his smithy was taken over by the Purple Scar festhall.
