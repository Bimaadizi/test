Mirrormul Tszul was a human sage in the city of Waterdeep around 1368 DR . His expertise was centered around the biology and languages of both humans and giants , though he also had a minor specialty in demihuman languages.

Personality

Mirrormul was a very energetic, excitable yet nervous man. He was known to speak at a breakneck pace, even when calm. His excitable energy was quite visible whenever someone inquired with a task or question, as his emotions always got swept up in the chance to tackle an intellectual challenge.

Activities

Mirrormul was a devout worshiper of Silvanus . As such, he never hesitated to accept requests for aid from the god's priests .
