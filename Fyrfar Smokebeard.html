Fyrfar Smokebeard was a dwarf and a Master Smith of the dwarves who would found the kingdom of Roldilar in the Vast in north Faerûn in the 7th century DR .

In the early 7th century, Fyrfar forged over a thousand weapons from orcslayer metal over several years at the order of Deep King Tuir Stonebeard . Among these was the short sword Sarghathuld in the Year of the Many Serpents, 605 DR . The weapons were enchanted by High Old One Turbaern , the human mage Beldossan the Short and the elven sorceress Aleratha Ilnatar .
