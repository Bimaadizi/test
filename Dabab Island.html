Dabab Island was part of Al-Qirmiza Islands in the Crowded Sea within west Zakhara .

Geography

Dabab Island was by far the largest of the sixteen islands comprising the archipelago. It measured 60 miles (97 kilometers) wide by 60 miles (97 kilometers) long. Dabab Island was located in the northwest corner of al-Qirmiza Islands, roughly 180 miles (290 kilometers) due south of Gana Bay .

Description

The perimeter of Dabab Island was a mix of rocky and sandy beaches. A lightly forested landrise was found in the central area of the island.
