Harandil Durlarven II was the second king of the Durlarven Dynasty to rule over Impiltur .

Activities

Harandil II inherited the throne from his father Harandil Durlarven I in the Year of the Basilisk, 537 DR . He reigned until his death in the Year of the Waking Feyr, 562 DR

Relationships

He was the first son and heir of Harandil I and Tayissa Mirandor . Harandil II's only son and heir was Thorodil "the Fierce" .
