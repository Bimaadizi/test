Thargaun Crell was the first Nentyarch of the Crell Dynasty to rule over Tharos which would later become Narfell .

Activities

Thargaun assumed leadership of his tribe with the death of his father Tharos in −987 DR . He immediately established the kingdom of Tharos in his father's honor and was the first to hold the title of Nentyarch.

In −970 DR , Thargaun built the capital of Dun-Tharos . Later that year he forged the Crown of Narfell and subverted the surrounding Nar kingdoms. However, it was not until −946 DR when he was able to unite all of the surrounding lands into what would be named Narfell. Thargaun ruled Tharos and Narfell from -987 DR up to his death in −912 DR .

Thargaun was also a devout follower of the demon lord Orcus .

Relationships

Thargaun's firstborn son, Derevan , would succeed him as Nentyarch.
