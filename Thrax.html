Thrax was a young dark one who was a member of the Glumguts family of dark creepers in the Shadowfell city of Evernight as of the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Thrax's cousin was Blackclaw , the terrifying and merciless leader of the Glumguts, and Thrax was one of only three Glumguts who dared to cross him.

Along with another cousin, Vynnia , and Blackclaw's son, Blackcut , Thrax made merry by playing pranks and engaging in sneaky ventures in Evernight. Their antics mostly focused on visitors to the city, but on occasion they did things that might embarrass and thus bring down the wrath of the Glumguts' employers, Netheril , if they were discovered. This in turn meant that the three youngsters ran the risk of invoking Blackclaw's wrath, although they believed they would be fine because Blackcut was his son.
