Flender Albottle was an inhabitant of Wright's Ferry in Featherdale in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

Activities

Flender owned and operated the Albottle Ferry with his brother Dregon . They charged customers a fee to haul them across the River Ashaba with a team of horses and rope.
