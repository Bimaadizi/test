A bottomless pouch was an enchanted pouch that could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The bottomless pouch contained an extra-dimensional space similar to a bag of holding , but it was much more valuable in combat as it allowed the owner to use it to flexibly change plans and launch counterattacks.
