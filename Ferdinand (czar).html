Czar Ferdinand was the czar of Bolgor in the Whamite Isles . Ferdinand represented Bolgor in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
