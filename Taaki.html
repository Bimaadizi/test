Taaki was Sauk 's arumwon , or animal companion.

Description

Taaki had ochre lines painted into her flanks and the top of the head.

History

On Tarsakh 14 of 1374 DR , Taaki had been attacking livestock around the Shalhoond . After killing one shepherd and wounding another, Berun and Lewan were hired by the locals to stop her. They found lettering scratched into a boot track close to its own, spelling out "KHEIL". Berun continued alone, until he came across the tigress menacing a man. Taaki was distracted by Perch , as Berun attempted escape in more than one occassion.

Taaki was slain on the night of Tarsakh 25 . On finally swallowing Perch whole, the lizard slew her from inside.
