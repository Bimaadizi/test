Karzon Kul was a ruffian dwarf from Loudwater in 1479 DR .

Description

Karzon's head was shaved, and he had tattoos across his body.

Personality

Karzon Kul was a local ruffian who usually hanged out at the Fisher's Friend Pub in Loudwater, and had a reputation for starting fights with other patrons.

Relationships

Karzon was an ally of the Lady of Shadows , leader of the crime gang in Loudwater in the late 15th century.
