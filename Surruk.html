Surruk was an wild elven woodsman in Phlan during the Tyranny of Dragons .

Possessions

When adventuring, Surruk wore dirty leather armor and fought with a purple-hued Morcant Burl longbow . To protect herself from harm, Surruk usually kept a healing potion in her backpack .

Personality

The elf was not forthcoming and preferred the wilds of the Moonsea to speaking with others.

History

In 1489 DR , Surruk patrolled the Quivering Forest hunting fey creatures. Later, she went to the Madame Freona's Tea Kettle and was briefly interrogated by adventurers hired by Buhrell Caah about a missing dangerous magic item.
