Wavesilver Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Wavesilver .

Location

The villa was located at the corner of Ivory Street and Flint Street in the central section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward .

Description

The villa compound was walled and composed of several two and four story structures. The estate included a large garden and pond.
