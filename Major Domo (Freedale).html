Major Domo was the name by which the manservant of the wizard Netheril in Freedale was known during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . He was either a majordomo, or his name was in fact Major Domo.

Possessions

He was armored in leather armor and wielded a long sword .

Activities

His job was being a manservant.

Relationships

He worked for Netheril , a local wizard.

History

During the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , the individual referred to as Major Domo was a manservant at around the same time that Taran Goldenstar was kidnapped. Netheril, the wizard, may have called upon him to remove adventurers he felt weren't showing him proper respect.
