Evermead was a liquor crafted by the elves of Evermeet . Outside of the elven homeland, it was only sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .

Description

Evermead was a sweet drink, flavored with cherry juice, vanilla beans, and rose petals. Some compared the drink's taste to honeysuckle breeze, while others - to the taste of upper planes.

Evermead was the highest prized liquor consumed by the elves. The process by which Evermead was made remained unknown, but it was rumored to be aged for centuries before consumption. A single sip of this fine drink was said to be like tasting the higher planes themselves.

Availability

Evermead was sold by Feldepost's Inn of Beregost in the Western Heartlands and the Friendly Arm inn for twenty one gold coin per serving.

Evermead was known to be imported into the city of Westgate for enjoyment of the wealth and nobility.
