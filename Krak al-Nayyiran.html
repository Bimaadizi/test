Krak al-Nayyiran , or the Castle of Sun and Moon , was a castle located in the City of Brass in 1367 DR .

This keep was found in the Plume district of the city.

Activities

The finest of the Sultan's military units were trained within the castle of Krak al-Nayyiran. Additionally, the most elite of troops from those units were awarded quarters here.
