The Prism Gate was one of the entrances into the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The Prism Gate was found in the northeast wall of the city, leading into the Plume district east of Krak al-Nayyiran .

Description

The gate resembled a permanent fortress complete with a garrison of soldiers.
