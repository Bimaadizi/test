The Caliph's Orphanage was a large center for housing orphans located in the city of Dhaztanar in Semphar during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Caliph's Orphanage was located within the Furdah Rustauk District in southwest Dhaztanar. The orphanage was located just down the street from the southern gate leading into the Madinat .
