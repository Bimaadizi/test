Umbero Zastro ( pronounced : / ʌ m ˈ b ɛər oʊ ˈ z æs t r oʊ / um-BAIR-oh ZASS-troh ) was a half-elf magister in Waterdeep during the early 1490s DR .

Activities

Umbero presided over courts in the Dock Ward 's courthouse. He was known for his unconventional punishments and his use of poetic justice.

History

In 1492 DR , if the Cassalanters managed to obtain the Stone of Golorr , then the Cassalanters' doppelganger valet, posing as a City Watch captain, ordered the arrests of some adventurers that had recovered the Stone. Their case was discussed in front of magister Umbero.
