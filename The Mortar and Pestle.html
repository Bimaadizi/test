The Mortar and Pestle was an apothecary and magical supply shop in the Central Port of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR .

Structure

The Mortar and Pestle was a one-story building in fair condition.

Services

The Mortar and Pestle was recognized as one of the most dependable sources for spell components and potion reagents within Skullport. The shop also offered related magical services and engages in limited trade of arcane goods.

The shop maintained a broad inventory of material components for spellcasting and created both magical and non-magical potions and elixirs. Additional services included the scribing of protective scrolls , with a minimum turnaround time of three days, and the purchase of rare or unusual items potentially useful to spellcasters. A posting at the entrance listed specific items currently sought by the shop, along with the bounties offered for their delivery. All transactions were subject to negotiation; the staff frequently engaged in barter, exchanging potions for potions, scrolls for scrolls, or items for services.

The most frequently requested service was the identification of magical items recovered from Undermountain . The shop provided no guarantees regarding the accuracy of identification and offered no refunds under any circumstances. If an item was discovered to be cursed and the curse subsequently binded to the identifying wizard, the client was responsible for arranging and funding the necessary removal by a qualified cleric or wizard .

Inhabitants

The Mortar and Pestle was owned by Ruuth .
