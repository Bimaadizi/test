Drado Weny was a magic-user in Hades in 1358 DR .

History

In 1357 DR , Drado went into Hades offering his service as an ally to the arcanaloth Yrkhetep . However, for his pride, Yrkhetep enslaved him and cursed him to remain in the conduits that permitted access to the Infinity Train until Drado had killed a thousand enemies of Yrkhetep.
