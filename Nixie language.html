The Nixie language was the racial language of nixies , a type of fey .

Speakers

Beyond nixies themselves, Nixie was a common secondary language among asrai , water sprites of the Upper Planes . Nixie was also common among some druids , particularly druids devoted to the nature god Eldath . Other forms of specialty priests in the Realms for whom it was a commonly known secondary language were Eldathyn peacemen and the waterwalkers of Istishia .
