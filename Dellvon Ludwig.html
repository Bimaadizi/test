Delvon Ludwig was a priest of Torm who lived in the Sword Coast North during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

While in the Ten Towns , Delvon befriended the fisherman-turned-acolyte Sirac of Suzail .

History

Delvon came from the city of Neverwinter and moved to Bryn Shander some time around the Year of the Iron Dwarf's Vengeance, 1485 DR . He took up residence within the House of the Triad .
