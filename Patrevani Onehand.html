Patrevani Onehand was the shaman of the Black Lion tribe of the Moonlands during the mid and late-14 th century DR .

Outlook

Shaman Onehand followed the old traditions of the Uthgardt people. He disagreed with the tribe's chieftain, Andar Heartwood , about abandoning their nomadic lifestyle and settling in Beorunna's Well .

While group's spiritual leader during this transitional time, he was the only cleric who maintained veneration of the Black Lion totem or the deity Uthgar . The other Black Lion clerics took up worship of Helm , Tyr , Torm or Ilmater as was common in the North .
