Altarl Campion was the King of the Campion Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate . He was famous for instigating the Templeban Edict after becoming the King, the edict lasting until 1018 DR .

Activities

Altarl was a wealthy merchant inhabiting Westgate. His peers selected him to rule the city after the death of Gostaraj "the Reaver King" in the Year of the Red Rain, 927 DR . Due to acknowledging Westage's numerous troubles because of religious zealotry, he implemented the Templeban Edict that forbade the public appearance of religious temples within Westgate. The edict lead to the creation of the Seven Lost Gods' hill . Altarl reigned until his death in the Year of the Sky Riders, 936 DR .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Jhentyr I .
