Hulrivior was a Red Wizard of the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

His voice was dry.

Relationships

He had a number of underlings working with him in Triel . One of them had hired Marlel at some point, per Marlel himself.

Hulrivior was also aware of numerous players in the Realms, including Thoadrin , Rendilar Bluthlock , Tornar the Eye, Drauthtar Inskirl , Eirhaun Sooundaeril , Hesperdan , and Aumlar Chaunthoun . He was also familiar with Pheldred , and a few others, though he was not familiar with Sharantyr .

Possessions

Whether by spell or something else, the table Hulrivior used for his final meeting had a blue flame free-floating above head level for those seated at it.

History

Hulrivior was in Triel as of 1357 DR .

When Orthil Voldovan 's caravan, of which the spellfire wielder Shandril Shessair was a passenger stopped at Triel , Hulrivior held a briefing regarding what was known of the caravan. During the briefing, he was introduced to Marlel and, after offering to capture spellfire for them, ambushed them by hurling an enspelled gem into the flame at the middle of their table. That caused an explosion killing most of the mages; to Marlel's understanding, wherever Hulrivior got to, he would be "highest" no more.

Rumors & Legends

Hulrivior's name was whispered as far as Scornubel .
