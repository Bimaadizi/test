Grains of Sand were enchanted Kara-Turan shurikens what once belonged to Miri Shokiro , a warrior , whose life was long forgotten by the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Each of the Grains of Sand was a tiny thrown weapon that dealt minimal damage to its targets; however, each successful hit with a Grain of Sand had a coin-toss of a chance to afflict the target with sleep .

History

Grains of Sand originated from Miri Shokiro's armory. As a warrior, her deeds and life were long forgotten, leaving behind only records of the philosophy that defined Miri's life.
