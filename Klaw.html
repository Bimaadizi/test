Klaw was a worg kept by the goblin members of the cult of the Absolute .

Description

She was a massive worg of the Nordiland worgata family. Like others of her kind, she had an absolutely voracious appetite.

Relationships

Klaw was disciplined and kept in line by Sentinel Olak . She had several different pups, including Small Klaw among others.
