Jamis Tombelthen was a general and a representative of the king and queen of Tethyr in the late 1360s DR .

Possessions

Jamis wielded a strongly enchanted unique longsword named The Answerer . He donned an enchanted set of plate mail and carried a potion of fire giant strength .

History

General Tombelthen was a trusted general of Tethyr, and an old friend to Queen-Monarch Zaranda Rhindaun .

In 1368 DR , the Bhaalspawn Gorion's Ward and party made their way to Tethyr. As soon as they arrived, they were confronted by Jamis and his contingent. He accused the Ward of committing crimes against his nation and humanity itself. He then informed the Ward that they were guilty of destroying Saradush , would have no trial, and were due to execution.
