Tracker Rindle was a member of the Cult of the Absolute who served as a lookout at a fortress near the Shattered Sanctum .

Description

Rindle had sickly yellow-green skin. She wore mismatcher leather rags accented with a feathered hood.

Possessions

She carried with her a crude bow crafted from wood and bone.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Cult of the Absolute launched a successful raid agains the Waukeen's Rest inn, situated along the Risen Road . Unfortunately for Rindle and a handful of other goblins , they were forced to carry out guard duty at a nearby outpost, rather than join in the full celebrations.
