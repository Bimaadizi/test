A kiseru was a type of weapon unique to Kara-Tur .

Description

A kiseru was a large iron tobacco pipe, 2 feet (61 centimeters) in length. It was a popular weapon in Kara-tur, as it was cheap, deadly, and could be used as an actual pipe to smoke from. It was a common weapon among peasants, as well as geisha-ninjas , monks , shugenja , and wu jen .
