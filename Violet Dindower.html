Violet Dindower was a halfling cook and smuggler who worked aboard a spelljammer .

Description

Violet had light skin and reddish-brown hair. She dressed in a low-cut brown tunic, and wore several pieces of jewelry.

Personality

She truly enjoyed her line of work. In addition to granting her a decent income, it provided her with enough risks to "keep life spicy."

Possessions

Violet carried five packets of dust of disappearance , which she used to hide herself on occasions when pirates or neogi boarded the ship. Since the dust made her invisible even to spells like detect invisibility , she was able to kill intruders without ever being seen. Although the dust was enhanced by permanency , its effect lasted only a limited time.

She also owned a short sword +1 , and a suit of leather armor +2 .

History

Violet was dissatisfied with trying to eke out a living in Waterdeep . When a spelljammer stopped for repairs in the city, Violet jumped at the chance to sign on as a cook.

Her stealthy eliminations of unwanted guests endeared Violet to the ship's captain on many occasions.
