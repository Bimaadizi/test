The Incanabula of Velsharoon was a holy text of the Church of Velsharoon that dealt with some magical subjects.

Description

The book appeared older than it was.

Subjects

Amongst other subjects, the book explained the steps of the Pact of the Everlasting .

History

A copy was present at the Chantry of St. Sammaster in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR . Notably, Velsharoon had ascended to divinity in the previous year.
