Murial's decapitator was a unique magic weapon crafted by Halaster Blackcloak for Muiral when he served as the Mad Mage's personal bodyguard.

Description

Murial's decapitator was a massive greatsword made from adamantine that weighed 16 pounds (7.3 kilograms).

Powers

The weapon possessed the vorpal weapon quality , making it much easier for the wielder to decapitate and dismember foes. A touch from Murial's decapitator also made magic items glow for several minutes.

History

Following his transformation into a giant scorpion , Murial had the weapon reforged by a renegade of Clan Melairkyn to better suit his new body. Murial retained possession of the greatsword as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
