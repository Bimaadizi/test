Purung was a chieftain in Bawa in 1357 DR .

History

Purung was the chieftain of the Kabuaya tribe, one of the most civilized Bawani tribes. Under his leadership, the Kabuaya progressed much and forged durable relations with the kingdom of Bawa. Purung hoped that one day he would become the governor of a modern Bavanese province.
