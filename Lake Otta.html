Lake Otta was a small freshwater lake located in western Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Lake Otta was found just east of Hardangheight near the shore of the Sea of Moonshae .

Notable Locations

The village of Otta was found along Lake Otta's northern shore.
