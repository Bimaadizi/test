The Iron Edge was an adventuring company active within the Delimbiyr Vale in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Active members of the Iron Edge met every year in the city of Daggerford to celebrate the birthday of Bronwyn Daggerford who joined them for several years to hone her magical skills and earn her fortune.

Many of the original members of the group later retired to Waterdeep , Secomber , and Ruathym .
