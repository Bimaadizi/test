The Tower of Riches was a temple dedicated to Waukeen located in the city of Ordulin in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Inhabitants

The temple was run by High Priest Irtlban Norleth . After the disappearance of their goddess, 26 priests fled the church, leaving only seven behind to assist Irtlban and the church's 42 followers.
