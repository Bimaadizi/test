Mulharahold was a town located in the country of Mulhorand during the mid–14 th century DR .

Geography

Mulharahold was situat due east of Maerlar and the Great South Road near the border of Murghôm south of Murghyr . The town was located along an unnamed road that ran north of the Road to the Dawn .
