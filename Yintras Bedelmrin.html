Yintras Bedelmrin was a mage during the 8 th century DR .

Description

He wore patchy robes.

Personality

Yintras was quite devout.

Abilities

The places where he dwelt had wards that could sear off limbs with a touch.

Relationships

He dwelt alone.

History

In the Year of Laughter, 761 DR , Yintras Bedelmrin was alone at his tower , praying to Azuth for guidance on who to pass his Art on to. He received a sign from the god, who magically armored him and forewarned him about a danger coming to him. At that point, the Slayer , an enigmatic entity, flowed into his tower room and towards him. With Azuth's boon, Yintras was able to repel it, and the entity fled his tower.
