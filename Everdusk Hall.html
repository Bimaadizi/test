Everdusk Hall , before being burnt to the ground, was one of the oldest and most revered temples in Silverymoon . It was a member of the Conclave of Silverymoon .

Interior

The lower levels of this temple were decorated with many marble statues of the elven gods .

In the upper levels of the diamond-shaped hall was a library that contained many shelves filled with tomes and scrolls, all written in the Elven tongue .

History

Long a place of worship for the elves of Silverymoon, Everdusk Hall was sadly burnt down by a mysterious fire in the Year of the Bright Blade, 1347 DR . Despite attempts by nearby Spellguard to quell the flames, the building had to be rebuilt from scratch.

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Everdusk Hall was still being run by the venerated elven priest, Loremaster Elaith Waterstill .

Sometime before 1372 DR , the new Loremaster in charge was Vadalathra Rivermantle .
