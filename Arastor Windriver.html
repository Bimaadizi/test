Arastor Windriver was the middle son of Sabrast Windriver in the mid-14 th century DR .

Activities

Per his father, Sabrast Windriver , Arastor was turning out to be the most important man in stonemasonry within the city of Westgate as of 1356 DR .

Relationships

In a revel on 1356 DR , Sabrast Windriver explained he wasn't sending his middle son any money.
