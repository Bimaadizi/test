The Fair Winds was a rental villa in the city of Waterdeep , owned by the noble family Thann . It was the only rental villa in the Cliffride that was regularly vacant in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Location

This house was located along the Cliffride, on the side of the road facing the sea, in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This building stood two stories tall. It had enough room within it to potentially house up to forty people.

Services

Like other rental villas that stood along the Cliffride, the Thann family rented out the Fair Winds for a price of 25 gold pieces per month.
