The Chapel of the Valiant was a room in Castle Obarskyr .

History

In the aftermath of the massacre during the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the bodies of several lords, including Jassur Dragonwood , Lord Ambrival , Obraerl Foulweather , Montror Barelder , Lyrannus Tantorn , Lord Hardivyper , Lord Ravenhill , and Lord Briarbroke , were taken to the Chapel of the Valiant and identified. Per Glathra Barcantle 's orders, there were three War Wizards she wanted to have them studied by before any priests or kin were allowed access.
