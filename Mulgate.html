The Mulgate was the easternmost of the two gates leading into the Southbank of Silverymoon , (the other being Blacklar Gate .) Within the city, the Moonway began at the Mulgate on its journey toward the Moonbridge . The gate was constructed sometime after the expansion of Southbank that occurred after Alustriel announced the formation of the Conclave of Silverymoon .
