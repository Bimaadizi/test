Jhansczil Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Jhansczil .

Location

The villa was located in the northeast section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward on the northeast corner where Copper Street met Thunderstaff Way .

Description

The walled compound contained four separate buildings. Each building stood one or three stories tall.
