Ashni was the student of the Neverwinter Academy of Magic .

Biography

Ashni was a student of Neverwinter Academy. When Qara caused trouble at the Academy, Praven went after her. Once Qara and the Kalach-Cha came out of the Sunken Flagon and defeated Praven , Ashni would confront them and attack. After the battle, the headmaster, Jochris , appears and scolds Qara, then leaves off to prepare a plan for revenge for her.
