Zilar Fenner was an Evenor local and customer of the Lonesome Tankard Inn and Tavern in the 15 th century DR .

Description

Zilar had pale skin and dark brown hair in twin braids.

History

In the 1480s DR, around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar and Shadovar invasion of Wheloon , Zilar spent time at the Tankard with a tressym , Olmer Twiggins , and Alyn Farrago , who told stories of the Haunted Halls of Eveningstar to his friends.
