Sune's Laurel of Favor was a divinely enchanted wreath from the Seldarine's Hand citadel on the Spine of the World .

Description

This never-wilting wreath was made out of woven together laurel leaves and roses .

Powers

The wearer of this garland was blessed with beauty and charisma, as well as magical protection from harm, similar to that of a +1 enchantment.

History

Sune's Laurel of Favor was a gift of peace and comradely from the human Church of Sune to the clerics of the Church of Hanali Celanil in the citadel of the Seldarine's Hand. The gift helped cementing kinship between the churches and the elven priestesses of Hanali wore Sune's Laurel of Favor every time they were visited by humans. When the citadel fell, orc invaders tossed the magic wreath into piles of garbage. That is where Sune's Laurel of Favor remained as of the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR .
