Peppercorns or black pepper , was a spice , a dried berry, harvested from a peppercorn plant vine, a staple in cooking across Faerûn .

Description

Peppercorns grew in small berry clusters and there existed a variety of colors.

Availability

Black pepper was a common table spice in the Dalelands . The dish called stuffed turkey pie at The Watchful Eye in Essembra , Battledale , used black pepper as a seasoning; Old Tower Fowl at The Rising Moon in Highmoon , Deepingdale called for a handful of black pepper; and so did High Dale Lamb served at The Swordsmith's House in Highcastle , the High Dale .

Peppercorns were available in the Blade Kingdoms of the Vilhon Reach , where they were used in establishing of "peppercorn democracy," a legal way of giving voice to all citizens of the Kingdoms, and not just the Blade Captains . The political movement adopted banners emblazoned with peppercorns.

Usages

In Waterdeep , black pepper was used to season Dragon Soup prepared by Feldyn Goadolfyn of The Old Monster Shop . While in Baldur's Gate , black pepper and nutmeg were commonly used to season mashed potatoes . And Neverwintran prime rib steak was made with a sauce of peppercorns and wine .

The Old Wheel club in the City of Caravans, Crimmor , of Amn , served smoked Athkatlan harpfish , encrusted in sweet sauce and cracked black peppercorns. Further south, in the land of Tethyr , pepper cheese was made with peppercorns. This cheese was sold across the realms via Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .

The simple eatery of The Last Jack in the city of Ravens Bluff , served subpar selection of dishes, including bread with peppercorn butter .

Peppercorns were used by the inhabitants of the Malatran Plateau of Kara-Tur and were considered a valuable trade commodity there as part of pickling spices.
