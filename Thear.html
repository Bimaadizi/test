Thear was a dwarven cleric of Tempus who resided in Shadowdale .

Description

Thear's face was dominated by a sizable nose, framed by a mane of shoulder-length white-and-gray hair with a matching beard. He wore dark green robes underneath his armor.

Personality

He had a reputation for flying into fits of rage, typical for clerics of his religion.

Possessions

In addition to his enchanted suit of plate mail , Thear owned an extremely potent magical warhammer .

Activities

Owing to his explosive temperament, the residents of Shadowdale tended to avoid Thear. He was very interested in purchasing a warhorse, but had trouble finding a suitable mount: he found horses too large, and ponies too scrawny.
