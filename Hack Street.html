Hack Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Hack Street was located in Zhentil Keep's Harbor District in the south section of the city. The road was located in the northwest section of the Harbor District, running between the wall of the Common Quarter and the docks.

Notable Locations

A warehouse used by the Zhentarim was located on Hack Street.
