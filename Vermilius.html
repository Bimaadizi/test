Vermilius was a young green dragon that dwelled within Arkhan's Tower in the plane of Avernus in the Nine Hells around 1494 DR .

Vermilius resided there with three other young chromatic dragons , Flash , Slarkas , and Vistalancer , and the adult white dragon Obatala . Collectively they protected the master of the tower, and servant of Tiamat , Arkhan the Cruel .
