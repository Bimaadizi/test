Description

Mac Intyre Path began at the North Road in the Sixstar neighborhood of the Uptown Distict . It extended south through Tentowers , into the Swordspoint and Altarside neighborhoods in the Temple District , and ended at Unicorn Lane , within Anvil in the city's Harbor District .

There was a break in the road between Dragonscale Street and Griffon Trail .
