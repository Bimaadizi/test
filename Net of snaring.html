A net of snaring was a magic item that could entangle targets. It was generally only usable underwater.

Powers

This net could be targeted at any single creature within 10‒120 ft (3‒37 m), entangling them to the point where it was difficult for them to move and perform any actions. Ensnared targets would be held for ten minutes unless released by the wielder. Dragons and creatures of similar size could break free of the net within one minute.

Creation

The hair of korreds could be used to manufacture these nets.

Notable Owners

Minak al-Zafyi , a shopkeeper in the Grand Bazaar of Huzuz , owned a net of snaring .
