Al-Bakbuk , or the Babbler , was a gibbering mouther living in the Pit of a Thousand Voices within Sarahin in 1367 DR .

Description

The Babbler was formed from the carcasses of holy slayers and saluqi greyhounds, giving it a distinct voice that was a combination of both.

Activities

The Babbler fed on holy slayers who were lowered into the Pit of a Thousand Voices as punishment for their crimes against the Everlasting .
