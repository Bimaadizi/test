The Laughing Lass was a brothel in the city of Waterdeep in the 15 th century DR .

Location

This brothel was located along Ship Street , in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Inhabitants

The Laughing Lass was operated by Alanthra Ghellowsar .
