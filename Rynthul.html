Rynthul was a Ffolk armorer inhabiting the town of Aithelar in Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles in Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR .

Activities

Rynthul owned a shop located toward the center of town on Blackdorn's Way where he made and repaired armor .
