Lake Woe was a large lake in the North .

Description

The lake was full of ice and covered in a thick mist that drifted down from the massive mountain at its center .

Geography

The lake was located south of the Ice Spires South and north of the Ice Mountains . The lake fed the Clear Whirl River .
