Olmaer Naxrin was a traveling merchant that traded in Sembia . He occasionally traveled to Voonlar to oversee investments for the Zhentarim .

Description

Olmaer moved with catlike grace and usually spoke quietly and diplomatically.

Personality

He was merciless when the need arose. He killed quickly and efficiently, often using poisoned weapons.

Abilities

Olmaer was both nimble and strong, and a specialist with the longsword .

Possessions

He wore bracers of defense , a ring of protection and wielded a longsword. He also had a hand crossbow and a poisoned dart gun.

Activities

Olmaer was an agent of the Zhentarim that traveled to Voonlar as part of his itinerary. He gathered intelligence and kept an eye on Bron Buorstag Hlammythyl and his deputies, occasionally making suggestions that were carried out like orders.
