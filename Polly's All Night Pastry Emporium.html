Polly's All Night Pastry Emporium , or simply Polly's , was a bakery, café, and pastry catering business that operated in Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

Polly's All Night Pastry Emporium was a several-story-tall building, with the shop taking the first floor and rental apartments above it. It was located two streets west and half a mile north from the Jack Jaw rowdy drinking establishment. At night, the street on which Polly's stood was illuminated by a pleasant red radiance of street lights.

Interior

The shop's interior felt a bit out of place among the establishments that surrounded it. The dining room of the café was plush with velvet fabrics and massive woven rugs and throws. The room was made out of dark hardwood. The dark wood floors were covered by comfy rugs and hardwood furniture was upholstered. A warm fireplace illuminated the room with a large birdcage hung above.

Services

The cage was the office, of sorts, where the Emporium's original proprietor– Polly , could be found. The large green parrot with an eyepatch was the establishment's mascot who greeted guests in human speech, periodically squawking like an ordinary parrot would. The parrot did her best to run the Emporium out of her cage despite having difficulties with writing and reading. She sold the bakery's pastries to the guests and fulfilled catering orders that were prepared by two teams of employees who worked day and night shifts.

History

Polly's All Night Pastry Emporium was in operation for several decades by the late 14 th century DR. Its owner, Polly, once made a deal with the powerful cleric of Tyr , Seejil . She sold him a lion 's share of the Emporium for the price of most of the cleric 's savings. Part of the deal was that in case Polly was ever killed, she would be brought back to life and to health by Seejil. Sometime later, Polly died in a horse carriage accident, but Seejil was unable to resurrect his business partner because of a misadventure that made Tyr turn away from the man, stripping Seejil of all of his divine magic. Thankfully, Seejil convinced a druid friend to use the reincarnation spell on Polly, technically fulfilling the agreed-upon obligation. She was reincarnated as a big green parrot.

Several years after a group of adventurers helped to redeem Seejil's name, Sorduel Meytauc of the Ill Eagle Inn , bought out Polly's pastries to pass them as his own in a competition at the Mossbridges 's First Annual Year-End Festival in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR . "Coincidentally," Polly's own invitation to partake in the competition disappeared at the same time. Sorduel Meytauc entered Polly's dark chocolate cherry cake but did not win the competition due to being found out and embarrassed by a group of adventurers of the Living City.
