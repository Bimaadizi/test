The dwarf Adhamh ( pronounced : /ˈ ɑː d ɛ m / AH-dem ) was a gravedigger in Waterdeep during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Adhamh was a sturdy dwarf with a long gray beard and a receding hairline. He relied on a flock of sheeps to help him tend the lawns of the City of the Dead .

Personality

Adhamh was a simple man with simple means, who loved his job and who usually kept to himself while observing the guests visiting the cemetery. While he felt more at ease among the dead than the living, he still loved the occasional opportunity to gossip.
