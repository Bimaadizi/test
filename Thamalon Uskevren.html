Thamalon Uskevren was the patriarch of the Sembian noble family, House Uskevren of Selgaunt , during the 14 th century DR .

Activities

Thamalon enjoyed literature of all kinds and possessed a vast collection of chapbooks and other small tomes that were gathered from all across Faerûn .

He had a considerable interest in the Elven Court and the glory that was ancient Myth Drannor .

In addition to his other pastimes, Thamalon was an avid vintner and over the years had developed a number of successful wines including Usk's Fine Old , Thamalon's Own and Storm Ruby .

Relationships

He was married to Shamur with whom he had three children: Thamalon II , Thazienne , and Talbot .
