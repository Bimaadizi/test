Ignorance Alley was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Ignorance Alley was located in the western section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward just south of Bliss Street . It branched east from Westwall Street and ended on the western curve of Ivory Street after passing several buildings. Seawatch Street branched south from Ignorance Alley's midpoint.
