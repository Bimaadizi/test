A cloak of reflection was an magically protective cloak worn by the rangers and agents in service of ruler of Silverymoon , Lady Alustriel Silverhand . Although the source of their power was unknown, these garments would reflect back upon the caster certain magical spells and spell-like abilities that were directed upon its wearer.

Powers

The cloak would reflect back the following spells: color spray • feeblemind • entangle • forget • hold animal / monster / person • irritation • magic missile • polymorph other • ray of enfeeblement • shocking grasp • Tasha's uncontrollable hideous laughter

The caster of any of these spells could only escape the re-directed effect if they were able to quickly move into another plane of existence or extra-dimensional space.

Origin

Originally designed by the mage , Irentalar , the secrets of their enchantment was lost to time.
