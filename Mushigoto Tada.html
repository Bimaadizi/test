Mushigoto Tada was a samurai of Fochu Peninsula in Wa .

Activities

Mushigoto Tada was the shoya of the village of Sokijan in Fochu Peninsula.

History

In 1358 DR , Tada was worried about a bajang living near the village. So when Yusuke T'ing went back to the village with Osari Minhiro and a foreign adventuring party, he asked the adventurers to kill the bajang.
