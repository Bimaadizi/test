Shrii Hallek was a hadozee restaurateur and innkeeper of the Man-o'-War in the city of Bral on the Rock of Bral in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . Shrii managed the restaurant and inn on behalf of its unknown owners, who were rumored to be adventurers . Shrii was very respectful to guests.
