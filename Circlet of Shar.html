The circlet of Shar was a Sharran circlet found in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The circlet offered broad benefits to divine spellcasting, and offered complete protection against effects that would block the wearer's connection to the divine, such as the intercession of quells .

History

The accessory was recovered from leader of the Night Brigade , priestess Storna Harnwood , when she was slain by the Riverside Rats .
