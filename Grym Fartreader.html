Grym Fartreader was an adventurer that lived during the late 14 th century DR . Grym's lasting legacy was a journal entry left within the depths of the Underdark , that remained nearly a century after their untimely death.

History

Grym's party ventured into a group of dungeons beneath Rothé Valley in the Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR . After encountering duergar that accused them of wielding dangerous magic, the group was beset by a cave-in on the fifth day of their expedition. While searching for a way past a set of locked doors, Grym became no more after being engulfed by a gelatinous cube .
