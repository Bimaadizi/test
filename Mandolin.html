Mandolins were small pear-shaped string instruments.

Description

When played as accompanying instruments, mandolins provided a delicate fluttering sound. They were also performed as solo instruments.

Mandolins produced in Calimshan were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .
