The Room of the Red Banner was a room in Castle Obarskyr , Suzail .

Location

The room was in Castle Obarskyr , Suzail .

History

The nobleman Nethglas Stormserpent had pranked the nobility by taking the Wyverntongue Chalice from the Room of the Red Banner to a room near the Dragonskull Chamber at some point on or prior to Year of the Silent Flute, 1437 DR .
