The Council of Cities was the ruling body of government in Thindol since the War of Unmasking in the Year of Many Serpents, 605 DR .

Activities

The Council vigilantly guarded Thindol's borders from yuan-ti interlopers, unaware that agents of House Sseradess were hiding beneath their noses in the waters of the Thingulph at the very heart of Thindol.
