The crown of morning's glory was a magical crown found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Representing the power and safety of dawn after a long night, the crown gave broad protection against magical threats, with extra protection against burning light and fire.

History

The crown may have been found when the Elves of Cormanthor found Zelliira in the House of Song while they searched for the Icon of Humans .
