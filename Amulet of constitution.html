Amulets of constitution were self-descriptive commonly encountered enchanted necklaces.

Description

Some of the amulets of constitution appeared to be simple heavy necklaces of a chain.

Powers

These pieces of magic jewelry enhanced the wearer's constitution, even though not by much.

History

In 1369 DR , one of these heavy magic necklaces could be found in the remnants of one of the towers of Stillwater Ruins district of Myth Drannor .
