Keresta Delvingstone was the leading cleric of Shar in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR .

History

Once Keresta was an outlaw on the streets of Waterdeep . In search of money, she decided to became an adventurer and went into Undermontain but there Keresta was made a vampire spawn by a vampire lord . Her salvation came when Vanrak Moonstar destroyed her vampire master and reclaimed the lair for Shar. Keresta quickly converted to the worship of the Goddess of the Night and became a lieutenant under Vanrak. When at last Vanrak redeemed himself, she went on to be the main leader of the Dark Army of the Night .

In 1492 DR , Keresta was plotting an attack on the Spires of the Morning or the House of the Moon .

Activities

Keresta was planning a great attack against a major temple to Selûne in the city in order to avenge Vanrak. She also plotted to assassinate notable clerics and paladins of Selûne and Lathander . If injured, she turned to fog and retreated inside her amethyst coffin. Keresta was also busy testing her human cultists in order to decide whether to turn them into vampire spawn or engulfing them in a swarm of rats .

Personality

Keresta was a true zealot of Shar and really wanted to avenge the loss of Vanrak.

Relationship

Under her guidance, most of the cultists were vampire spawns under her command. She had enthralled the adventurer Portia Dzuth . Usually Keresta had at her side her yeti bodyguard Thagor . Artor Morlin greatly desired her death.
