Booyagh Gnat was a goblin spellcaster and a member of the Cult of the Absolute during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

He was notably rude to people outside the Cult of the Absolute.

History

Gnat was present when the True Soul Dror Ragzlin attempted to commune with a dead mind flayer brought into the Shattered Sanctum in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
