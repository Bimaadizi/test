Activities

Tralia served as Duchess of the North for Dambrath. Though she was sixth in the line of succession, Tralia still had designs on the throne, including assassination attempts on the other duchesses. The most recent of these attempts as of 1367 DR was on Duchess Merial Luenath , where Tralia arranged for a group of young males to kill Merial in her bedchambers during one of her marathon "pleasure and pain" exercises. The attempt failed, but Tralia remained blameless.
