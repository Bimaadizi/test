Mara al-Gurab Sakir was a member of the Unclean in 1367 DR .

Personality

Mara was demure and loyally obeyed every command of her master. She was more confident than her sister Safia .

Decription

Mara had shiny black hair and dark eyes.

Activities

Mara served as one of Akim al-Kalaas 's apprentices, learning the mystical ways of the sha'ir .
