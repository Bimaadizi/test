Description

The Courtyard of Zathchos was at the center of a block between Stonebow Street to the north, the Street of Staves to the south, the Promenade to the west, and Torch Street to the east. The entrance to the plaza was a wide opening by Blackgorgons , the tower home of wizard named Baskor on the Stonebow Street.
