The Dragonslayer House was a large manse located in the Tentowers neighborhood in the Uptown District of Ravens Bluff . Both before and after the Ravenian war of 1370 DR , it served as the home of the since-weakened Paerindon noble family.

Description

The grounds of the Dragonslayer House featured a fore tower with noteworthy bay windows.
