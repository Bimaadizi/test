Venominhandar , known as simply Venom , was a green dragon who dwelt in the southern woods of Cormanthor in the millennium before Dalereckoning . He always acted in unison with his mate Venomindhar , also called Venom, and nobody realized they were in fact two dragons.

History

Settling in the Emerald Vale in −982 DR , they corrupted the area until it became known as Tangled Vale .

They also killed many centaurs during this age.

The female Venom, Venomindhar, was slain in battle in Year of Silver Wings, −249 DR .

The male Venom was eventually confronted and killed by the moon elf warrior Jassin Aunglor in the Year of Elfsorrows, −206 DR .

At one point they sired an heir, Vendemniharan , who later joined the Cult of the Dragon .
