Lhoril was a wizard who inhabited the city of Waterdeep .

History

Lhoril perished in a back alley in Waterdeep when one of her apprentices summoned a tanar'ri beyond their control. Lhoril fought with the demon but perished in the attempt. The alley was later named Lhoril's Alley in her honor. In reality Lhoril's apprentice, Maaril , summoned the demon to slay her so he could take possession of what would later become known as the Dragon Tower .
