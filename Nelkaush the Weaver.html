Nelkaush the Weaver was a textiles shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood one story tall.

Location

Nelkaush the Weaver was located in the southeastern section of the Southern Ward on the north side of Coach Street west of Carter's Way .

Inhabitants

The shop was owned by its namesake, Nelkaush , in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
