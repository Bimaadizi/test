Nasher's nimble boots was a pair of enchanted boots that once belonged to Lord Nasher Alagondar of Neverwinter .

Powers

These boots significantly improved the wearer's dexterity through magical enchantments.

History

Even though many doubted their authenticity, Nasher's nimble boots had a capital letter "N" carved into the heel. The letter indicated the boots' owner as none other than Lord Nasher. It was believed that Lord Nasher lost this pair at some point before the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , but the story remained unknown to anyone but the ruler's closest friends. Many believed that there existed a total of five items, including these boots, that belonged to Lord Nasher, and if worn together, they could grant great powers to their owner. However, that was never proven to be true.

In 1372 DR, Nasher's nimble boots found their way to a ruined Highcliff Castle , which was taken over by the King of Shadows 's shadowpriests.
