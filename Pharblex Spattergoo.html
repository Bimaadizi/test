Pharblex Spattergoo was the chief and shaman of the bullywug tribe in the Mere of Dead Men during the time of the Tyranny of Dragons in 1489 .

Personality

Pharblex greatly enjoyed tormenting the lizardfolk , thanks to Dralmorrer Borngray putting them under his control, and he killed the lizardfolk chief Suncaller .

Possessions

In battle, Pharblex wore studded leather and fought with a spear and shield .

Relationships

Pharblex's bullywugs acted as servants and henchmen for the Cult of the Dragon 's local leader Rezmir .

History

He was probably killed by the lizardfolk chief Snapjaw , helped by a band of adventurers who were enemies of the Cult.
