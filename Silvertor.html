Silvertor was among the oldest noble houses of Waterdeep in the early 14 th century DR, but as of 1368 DR it was no longer considered nobility.

History

In the Year of the Great Harvests, 1325 DR , the young Lord Corin Silvertor had recently assumed the title after his father's passing, when he was lost on a hunting trip into Undermountain below Waterdeep. The half-orc thief Artek Ar'talen , called 'the Knife', was hired by Lord Darien Thal to rescue Lord Silvertor from Undermountain, and with the help of the wizard Beckla Shadesar , they located the young nobleman, and the three of them battled their way through Undermountain.
