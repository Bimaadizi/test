Toucans were a colorful species of tropical bird that could be found on the Malatran Plateau , the world of Eberron , and possibly Chult .

Sub-Species

History

Sometime in the late 14 th century DR , the lacerial shaman Starr crafted a technicolored feather cloak , primarily sourcing its feathers from parrots and toucans.
