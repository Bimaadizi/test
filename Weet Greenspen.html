Weet Greenspen was an alchemist in the Moonsea in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Circa 1486 DR , the doppelganger Tak , posing as Doctor Jubal of the wandering Doctor Jubal's Carnival of Curiosities , kidnapped Weet and forced her to brew alchemical items. Weet was imprisoned inside the mirror maze together with Berl Cuttler , Aelia , the true Doctor Jubal , and others.
