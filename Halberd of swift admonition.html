The halberd of swift admonition was a unique enchanted halberd crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Nayeli Goldflower in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

Silverhand crafted the halberd to honor Nayeli's "gift of perspective" given her ability to recognize all actors who fell within the purview of her revenge mission, including those who supported or benefitted from her enemies' actions.

Powers

The halberd of swift admonition could release a powerful smite that could strike not only the enemy in front of Nayeli, but also the enemies behind them.
