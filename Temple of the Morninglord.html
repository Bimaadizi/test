The Temple of the Morninglord was a temple to Lathander , the Morninglord, situated in the city of Elturel in the Western Heartlands in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Relics

The temple housed the amulet of the Dawnbreaker , a magical relic made from a fragment of the skull of the Dawnbreaker , a prophet of Lathander, and wrapped in metals.

History

In the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR , the master thief "Pinch" stole the amulet of the Dawnbreaker from the Temple of the Morninglord. The young priestess Lissa was sent to track down Pinch and his band, following them to Ankhapur , but he convinced her he'd taken it from other thieves, deserved a reward, and even should hang on to it for safekeeping.
