Lord Jevahn Jhalvar was a noble drow that served High Priestess Syndiyth at the Temple of the Spider in the Northdark .

Abilities

Lord Jhalvar was a skilled melee combatant, preferring a fighting style that utilized a longsword and dagger .

Relationships

He commanded a group of warriors and blademasters that would defend them to their deaths.

History

The good lord was slain in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , by the Heroes of the Sleeping Dragon Bridge as they ventured through the Underdark towards Priestess Syndrith's temple.
