Level Up Your Life was a self-help book that promised to teach its readers charisma, published sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

History

A copy of Level Up Your Life belonged to Gyldro Angleiron , the grumpy master smith and owner of The Rivington General in the Rivington district of Baldur's Gate in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . The book was kept in the man's home, adjacent to the Rivington General's smithy.
