Bridge Street was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Located in southern Zhentil Keep within the Foreign Quarter , Bridge Street was a small road in the eastern Foreign Quarter that connected the Throat with the Tesh Bridge . Zhent Avenue connected with Bridge Street from the west.
