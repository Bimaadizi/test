The Board Laid Bare was a small restaurant located in Neverwinter in the 14 th century DR .

Location

The Board Laid Bare was located inside the city's northeast gate, in the River District .

Description

The Board Laid Bare served no beer , wine , or spirits. Instead, it offered a selection of fresh fishes and meat cooked in a variety of sauces, as well as fried vegetables and potatoes . The prices for such dishes were surprisingly low.

History

The Board Laid Bare may have been destroyed during the Ruining of 1451 DR .
