Ranger Leokas was a mercenary who worked for Company Yargo aboard the Buccaneer's Tear during the late 15 th century DR .

History

When the Buccaneer's Tear soared up to Pirates' Skyhold above the Sea of Swords in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Leokas was among the first mercenaries to establish a beachhead on the earthmote . He worked with a hired adventurer to eradicate some of the local lizardfolk that threatened the expedition from the nearby Vilebog swamp the swamp.
