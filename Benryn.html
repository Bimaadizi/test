Benryn was a man that lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

He was married to a woman named Mirileth , whom he loved very much.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Benryn and Mirileth spent some time at the Waukeen's Rest inn, which was found on the Risen Road west of Elturel . The two were carrying with them a dowry for Mirileth's sister's wedding: a simple ring of gold .

Unfortunately, the couple's stay was interrupted, when goblin raiders from the Cult of the Absolute assailed the inn and set it aflame. Benryn remained trapped in the inn's upper story, while Mirileth, who remained outside, was not so fortunate.
