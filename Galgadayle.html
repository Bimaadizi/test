Galgadayle oin Meadowhome was a firbolg seer of Hartsvale , who prophesied about the birth of Queen Brianna's child.

Description

Galgadayle was lanky for a firbolg, looking more like a verbeeg . He had pale white eyes, and a thick white beard.

His name meant "one who dreams for us."
