Ammatha Braceryn was part-owner, cook, and hostess at the Three Elves inn in Voonlar circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Description

She was pleasantly plump, but light on her feet.

Personality

Ammatha was flirtatious and circulated among the customers making sure their needs were met.

Abilities

She was a great cook, able to quickly whip up a meal. She and her sister brewed a sweet beer from berries.

Relationships

The Three Elves was a very popular inn throughout the Moonsea region and its owner/operators were highly regarded. Ammatha had a sister, Baralea .
