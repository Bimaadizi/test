Agada Vane was a shopkeeper in Waterdeep in 1479 DR .

Personality

Agada was a fierce competitor and fair employer but also had a softer side, which she showed at tea-parties.

Activities

Agada was a regular customer at the Yawning Portal inn. She had a successful shop that sold metalwork for the home and dry goods. Agada organized weekly tea-parties in order to gossip. Secretly, she was also a personal spy of the Open Lord Dagult Neverember in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep .

Relationship

Agada was respected by the lords, merchant community, and by the guilds.

History

In 1479 DR, Lord Neverember ordered Agada to keep an eye on Sanfin Lotte , whom the lord distrusted. Agada grew convinced that Sanfin's secret was his parakeet Chloe and started investigating it.
