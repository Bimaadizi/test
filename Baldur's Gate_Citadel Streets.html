Citadel Streets was a small, and rather segregated, district located on the north side of the Upper City of Baldur's Gate .

Notable Locations

The predominant feature of this district was the grand Watch Citadel , the fortress that served as a barracks and training grounds for the Upper City police force known as the Watch . The citadel itself was partitioned by a city wall that extended in from northern border of the city proper, with traffic being directed through the Citadel Gate .

A couple of patriar families kept their manses within this district, most notably the Provoss and the Whitburns .
