Redflame armor was a magical breastplate that made the wearer resistant to fire .

Description

Redflame Armor was a set of +2 magic armor. Wearing the breastplate made one take far less damage from fire-based attacks.

History

In 1358 DR , the enchanted breastplate was found in the horde of Golozz the Greedy, a red dragon who lurked in a smuggler's dock close to Waterdeep .
