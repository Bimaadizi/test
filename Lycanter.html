Lycanter was a Netherese arcanist who taught at the Academy of Mentalist Study in Eileanar shortly before its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Lycanter was an expert in telepathic spells, and applied this by teaching under the supervision of Dean Zucchi at the Academy.
