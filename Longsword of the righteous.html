Longsword of the righteous was a longsword enchanted to be especially effective against undead creatures.

Powers

Longsword of the righteous was an imposing and severe-looking weapon, blessed with divine holy magics. The sword turned undead creatures struck by it to dust. Additionally, this blade enhanced a paladin 's divine spellcasting abilities.

Notable Owners

Dragonbait : a saurial paladin and a one-time companion of Artus Cimber during their foray into the jungles of Chult in the late 15 th century DR .
