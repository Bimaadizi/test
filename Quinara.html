Quinara was a part of a roaming adventuring troupe that traveled the Sword Coast North during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Quinara was a crimson-skinned tiefling with Asmodeus 's blood. She had long twisted horns and burning orange eyes. Quinara's hair was purple and her right eye was crossed by a scar. She had a direct and no-nonsense personality.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Quinara was a member of an adventuring band that included the gnome Yonks , the elf Sarafin , and Zog the half-orc . The group traveled the Sword Coast in search of steady adventuring work. They traveled from Waterdeep to Baldur's Gate during the Absolute crisis and, unable to find work, planned to move out to Neverwinter as the city was searching for adventures. Before they could move on, Quinara sought to sharpen all of her blades and pack her bags.
