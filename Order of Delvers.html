Race(s)Dwarves

The Order of Delvers was a dwarven guild of delvers, those dwarves dedicated to exploration and discovery. It had chapters in many dwarven settlements.

Organization

The leader of each chapter was known as a Delvemaster. Although most members were dwarves, the Eartheart chapter had a human member, Torrin Ironstar , around 1480 DR .

Possessions

Each member of the Delvers had a magical backpack that only they could open.

Delver's armor was a type of enchanted heavy armor used by the members of the guild during their dangerous subterranean missions.
