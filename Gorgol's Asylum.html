Gorgol's Asylum was one of the unique Fading Rings that possessed defensive magic only usable by sorcerers .

Description

Gorgol's Asylum was a cold metallic latticework ring.

Powers

Gorgol's ring possessed a +3 enchantment similar to a powerful ring of protection . Additionally, the ring gave the wearer the ability to cast globe of invulnerability .

History

In 1369 DR , Gorgol's Asylum was in the possession of a mysterious monument, the Great Stone Head , located in the Nightingale Court in the ruins of Myth Drannor . In exchange for one of the eidolons , small totem statues depicting various gods and demons, the monument dispensed powerful magic rings, one being the Gorgol's Asylum .
