Kergaard Twinblade was the seneschal of the Mulan Dynasty before becoming the King of Westgate .

Activities

Kergaard served as a loyal seneschal for King Ashtukzu II . Following the death of Astukzu II in the Year of Dark Venom, 37 DR , and months of civil strife since no heir was apparent, Kergaard was elevated to the position of King by Westgate's nobility. He ruled until the Year of Clinging Death, 75 DR , when he died of plague.
