A werecougar was an exceptionally rare lycanthrope that dwelt in Maztica .

Description

These shapechangers were similar to weretigers , but could transform into cougars instead. They survived in the harsh land of the Pasocada Basin , which was far to the north of the mainland of Maztica, where other humans were scarce.
