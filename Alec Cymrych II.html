Alec II of the Cymrych Dynasty was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the early-to-mid 8 th century DR .

Relationships

He was the second son of High King Ernest Cymrych and the younger brother of his predecessor, High King Carthus . His successor was his first son, Pendar Cymrych .

History

Alec II assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Visions, 731 DR , following the death of his father and the abdication of his brother.

He ruled for seven years before being dying of plague in the Year of the Gliding Man, 738 DR .
