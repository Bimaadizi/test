Lord Gante Demelin was a half-elf fighter and commander of the fortress of Emmech , known as Undumor after its conquest by Thayan forces. As of 1372 DR , he kept his fortress prepared against any eventuality, being uneasy with the peace that there was with the Red Wizards . Demelin was a marshal of Aglarond in 1372 DR .

He still ruled the fortress in 1375 DR .
