The Thirsty Throat was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tavern stood two storys tall.

Location

The Thirsty Throat was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward where Candle Lane and Zastrow Street met the Way of the Dragon .

Inhabitants

A fence named Thunna worked out of the tavern. The Thirsty Throat was also a good place to hire mercenaries .
