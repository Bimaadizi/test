Slaker was the name of a dark beer brewed in Amphail , a village in northwest Faerûn on the Long Road , circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , where it was served at the Stag-Horned Flagon . At three copper pieces for a tankard, this heavy, barley -based beer was popular with the locals and visitors alike.
