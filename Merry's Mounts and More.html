Merry's Mounts and More was a stable in Halfhap . It was operated by the elf Merialeth Loriamanthor .

Services

The stables housed and sold horses, ponies and livestock, as well as riding gear and other supplies. On rare occasions, Merialeth could obtain a young hippogriff and would only sell it to a trustworthy potential master.
