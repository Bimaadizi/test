The Bomak Jungle was a wild tropical forest on the shores of the Gulf of Cordell .

Description

The jungle encircled the Payitlan town of Mazti , built on the arable land west of the city of Ulatos . Bomak Jungle's western edge was its largest, bordering the lands of the Maztican Pezelac tribes.
