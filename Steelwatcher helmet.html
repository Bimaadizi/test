Steelwatcher helmets were enchanted morion helmets forged out of infernal iron by the master smith of Avernus , Carixim , in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

These ornate helmets possessed artificial sight that allowed the wearer, or the steel watcher it was attached to, to see in the dark within 40 feet (12 meters), could never be blinded , and were equipped with gyronetic and targeting modules. Additionally, steelwatcher helmets boosted protection of the wearer's constitution.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , steelwatcher helmets , as well as the other parts of the steel watchers of Baldur's Gate , were created by Carixim in Avernus. The items went on to being mass-produced in the Gate by the Gondians , enslaved by Lord Gortash , who paved his way to power and tyranny through the construct soldiers.
