Ganather was a wizard active in Secomber during the 15 th century DR .

History

Circa the Year of the Emerald Sun, 1454 DR , Ganather volunteered to act as a lure to help Elminster capture the Sword of Savras . The capture succeeded, but Ganather unfortunately lost their life in the process.
