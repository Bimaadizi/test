Embric was a fire genasi weaponsmith in Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Together with his husband Avi , Embric managed the Steam and Steel , a forge on Trollskull Alley in the North Ward . He was a member of the Most Careful Order of Skilled Smiths and Metalforgers and claimed to descend from Calimsham 's efreet .
