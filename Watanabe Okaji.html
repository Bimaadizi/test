Watanabe Okaji was one of the best actors in Kozakura in 1357 DR .

Activities

Watanabe was the star of the Imperial Noh Troupe of Fukama , the best noh troupe based in Fukama . Secretly, he was also a spy in service to the emperors, with ties to Konishi Ieyasu .
