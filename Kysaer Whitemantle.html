Kysaer Whitemantle , also known as the Rider in White , was the most famous assassin in the Sea of Fallen Stars and secretly a member of the Night Masks in 1370 DR .

Possessions

Kysaer was known to use Elquillar , the famed "Sword of Many Faces".

History

As an independent agent, Kysaer was hired by most of the governments of the Inner Sea , usually to kill evil people. This he kept his association with the Night Masks a very closely guarded secret. Despite this, he managed to develop a network of local contacts. Kysaer had an technical approach to killing, instead of a brutal one.
