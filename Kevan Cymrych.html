Kevan Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the early-to-mid 9 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High King Byron I . His successor was his second son, Dolan Cymrych .

History

In the Year of Broken Locks, 818 DR , High King Byron I fell into a magic slumber, and Kevan stepped in to rule while his father was incapacitated. This short regency lasted until his father awoke the following year .

Kevan became the true High King in the Year of Wolfpacks, 826 DR , after the real death of his father. He ruled for twenty-four years before he died of natural causes in the Year of the Empty Throne, 850 DR .
