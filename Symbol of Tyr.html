A symbol of Tyr was an enchanted defensive bracelet of the Tyrran faith in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

A symbol of Tyr was a metallic piece of jewelry, decorated with red gems and a disk, decorated with Tyr's holy symbol , proudly announcing the wearer's devotion to the God of Justice.

Powers

The enchantments placed on these bracelets magically reflected partial damage dealt to the wearer back onto the attacker.
