Keldor Hannith was a human man of the city of Procampur in the Vast , who lived circa 1358 DR .

He was a wizard who sold scrolls and potions , and performed spellcasting services for a fee. Hannith was described as a quiet and careful man, but was one of the more notable Procampan mages of his day.
