Kalam Bloodsword was a veteran Zhentilar warrior in High Dale .

History

In 1358 DR , Kalam was one of the Zhentilar lieutenants in the secret Zhent occupation army in High Dale. When an insurrection started thanks to Elminster Aumar the Rangers Three 's actions, Kalam led a company of lancemen and tried to order the rioters to go home. However, the Dalesfolk lead by Irreph Mulmar attacked the Zhents and Itharr killed Kalam.
