Erve was a priest of Jergal in the Moonsea in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Erve went to an abandoned temple of Waukeen to take an inventory of the dead buried in the church's graveyard and crypt. However, some of the builders of the temple were secretly cultists of Graz'zt and their leader, by now a shadow demon , possessed Erve. Under demonic possession, Erve re-opened the temple as a sanctuary to Graz'zt. At last an adventuring party found and defeated the demon.

Around 1492 DR Erve joined the army that tried to free Phlan from the Vorgansharax .

Relationship

Erve had dealings with the Order of the Gauntlet .
