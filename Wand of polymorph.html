A wand of polymorph , also known as a wand of polymorphing , was a magical wand that mimicked the effects of the spell polymorph .

Powers

This type of wand held seven charges. At any time, its wielder could expend one of these charges to emit a thin green ray that shot out towards a target person or creature. Unless the target resisted the effect, this beam of energy polymorphed them into the form of a another being.
