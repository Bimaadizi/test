Joru was a shaman of the Huroola tribe from the jungles of the Malatran Plateau , active in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Joru traveled and battled unarmed. Uncharacteristically for a Huroola, the shaman was was respectful towards warriors of other tribes, as well as his enemies and opponents.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , the Tribe of the Wise Ones became aware of a looming threat of the evil spider katanga . The Mother of a Thousand Young was swollen with a thousand children ready to overflow the jungles with ravenous spider creatures. In preparation for the birthing, she initiated a ritual that sent deadly poisons into the rivers cascading off the Fire Mountain . Joru was assigned a group of experienced heroes of the Living Jungle despite him being significantly less powerful than other group members.
