The Pool of Yeven was a small lake found within the Yevenwood in Battledale .

Geography

The Pool of Yeven was located due east of Lake Sember and south of Cormanthor near the center of the Yevenwood. The lake was fed by the mighty River Ashaba as well as the Semberflow and Glaemril .
