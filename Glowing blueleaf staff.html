A glowing blueleaf staff was an enchanted druidic staff found in the jungles of Chult in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

These staves were imbued with elemental magics that, when mixed with blueleaf tree sap, resulted in a wondrous glowing effect. the staves possessed a powerful +4 enchantments and strengthened abilities of the wielder druid as well as the druid's allies.

Notable Owners

Qawasha : a druid of the Emerald Enclave from Chult in the late 15 th century DR.
