Castle Mintarn was a fort in Mintarn and the home to its ruler.

Structure

The beautiful castle had large spires of smooth stone, that looked over the sea.

History

The dragon Hoondarrh , although given proper tribute, landed atop the castle and toppled two of its many towers.
