History

In the Year of Holy Thunder, 1450 DR , Derreg welcomed Varra and called the priest Erdan to help her to give birth to Vasen Cale . Soon after, Drasek Riven also arrived at the abbey; he explained to Derreg that the child had a crucial role in the future and asked Derreg to protect Vasen from evil until that day.

In later years, Derreg acted as substitute father for Vasen and trained him as paladin of Amaunator.

Relationships

Derreg was the son of Regg and he was a good friend of Erdan.
