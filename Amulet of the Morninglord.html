Amulets of the Morninglord were enchanted amulets , often reward as boons to individuals who performed special services for the Church of Lathander .

Description

There amulets were made out of pure gold .

Powers

Amulets of the Morninglord were imbued with various beneficial magical enchantments. One amulet of note glowed with orange radiance at 15 feet (4.6 meters) and allowed the wearer to cast the aid and bless spells once per day each.

History

One such amulet was gifted to the Hero of Daggerford in 1372 DR by Dawnbringer Liam Sunmist for stoping a vile necromancer Daxiong who raised victims of an epidemic in Gillian's Hill to perform dark experiments on them.
