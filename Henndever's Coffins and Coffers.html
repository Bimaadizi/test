Henndever's Coffins and Coffers was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Location

Henndever's Coffins and Coffers was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward near the City of the Dead where Spindle Street joined the Coffinmarch .
