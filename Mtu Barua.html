Mtu Barua was a seneschal in the service of Ifan Talro'a , a merchant prince of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

He often made trips to Port Nyanzaru's Bazaar to run errands for his master.

History

During the events of the death curse , he contacted the Bazaar's Master of the Market to find an armorer who could supply titansteel armor .
