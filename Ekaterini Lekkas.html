Ekaterini Lekkas was a noble and member of a cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame that operated out of Sambral , one of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond , in 1479 DR .

Description

Ekaterini was a young human female with a strong chin and sunken eyes.

History

In 1479 DR, Ekaterini was a member of Bazamarl 's cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame. She joined as an act of rebellion against her family.

When a band of adventurers that were sent by Halagothra the Healer to Sambral to destroy the cell of the cult operating there, killed both Bazalmarl and the Firebird , Ekaterini fled in panic, avoiding being killed by the adventurers.
