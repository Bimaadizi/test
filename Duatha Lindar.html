High Priestess Duatha Lindar was the ranking priestess of the Lady's Hall , a temple dedicated to Tymora , in Ordulin in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Duatha was the leader of Tymora's faithful in Ordulin. Her congregation within the temple consisted of 16 other priests and 24 followers.
