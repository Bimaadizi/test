A wand of ever-searing flames was a magical wand .

Description

Nouméa Fairbright 's wand was made of wood.

Powers

When triggered, Noumea's wand launched a bright white beam. Its target was enveloped in flames, which also scorched the window she soon fell from.

History

Nouméa Fairbright carried a wand of ever-searing flames to her encounter with Elminster on Eleasis 4 in 1364 DR .
