Prug was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age .

History

Prug developed six spells during their life. The spells they invented were domination in 1985 NY (−1874 DR) , hold monster in 1995 NY (−1864 DR) , repulsion in 2044 NY (−1815 DR) , hold person in 2054 NY (−1805 DR) , charm plant in 2114 NY (−1745 DR) , and hold undead in 2188 NY (−1671 DR) .
