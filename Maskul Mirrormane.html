Maskul Mirrormane was the High Priest of the Circle of Darkness , a temple devoted to the worship of Cyric in Zhentil Keep in 1358 DR .

Activities

Maskul oversaw a staff of 11 other priests of Cyric. He had only attracted a handful of worshipers of the Dark Sun as of 1358.
