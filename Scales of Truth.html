Scales of Truth were a variety of enchanted heavy armor created on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Scales of Truth possessed a simple +1 or a stronger +3 defensive enchantment. Additionally, they offered the wearer a slight boost to their fortitude and allowed them to see invisibility once or twice per day.

A set of Scales of Truth could be created from an enchanted suit of armor and a special type of holy water .

History

Scales of Truth with a simpler +1 enchantments were forged by Master Blacksmith Marrok of Shining Knight Arms and Armor shop of Neverwinter to help the heroes who sought the cure from the dreaded Wailing Death of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . Later the same year, more powerful versions were created by Master Blacksmith Barun of Beorunna's Well to help the forces of Neverwinter stand against Lady Aribeth the Betrayer , who led a marching army of Luskan in the deadly but short-lived Neverwinter-Luskan war.
