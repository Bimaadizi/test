Clan Arkeld was a dragonheir clan of the Sword Lands of Laerakond .

History

Clan Arkeld was one of the oldest clans of Laerakond. Its members were descendants of the first human roostmasters of the primordials , who lived millennia before the Spellplague .

In the late years of the 15th century DR clan Arkeld was a diminished clan, in decline and with only a few surviving individuals, scattered throughout the Sword Lands.
