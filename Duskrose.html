Duskrose was a prominent non-noble family who served the Crown of Cormyr up to the 15 th century DR .

History

The Duskroses were long-time servants of the Crown in the Royal Palace and Royal Court , alongside the Bracetels , Lionwinters , Ormantrels , Saraclarrs , and Windstones . Additionally, several members of the family were knighted in the 12 th and 13 th centuries DR.
