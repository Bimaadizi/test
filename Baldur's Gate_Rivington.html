Rivington was the southern-most district of Baldur's Gate . It was the only region of the Outer City located on the southern shore of the River Chionthar , on the far side of Wyrm's Crossing . The Trade Way led south from Rivington to the Lands of Intrigue and Calimshan .

Inhabitants

The Rivington Rats was a local guild that plagued the neighborhood during the late 15 th century DR . Smugglers also frequented the district, running goods by river to Brampton by way of the river.
