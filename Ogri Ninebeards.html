Ogri Ninebeards was a member of the Swords of Leilon , a group of adventurers based in Leilon , in the 14 th century DR . After his death, Ogri's ghost remained bound to Leilon until the late 15 th century DR.

Description

Ogri was a dwarf with a red beard.

Activities

As a member of the loosely organized Swords of Leilon, Ogri protected Leilon until its fall in 1385 DR .

History

In the 14 th century DR, Ogri Ninebeards was an adventurer in the region around Leilon. Together with Aubrey Silverspun and Augusta Krul , the other two members of the Swords of Leilon, Ogri protected Leilon before its fall. The Spellplague of 1385 DR broke the magical wards of the High Tower of Thalivar and released all of the monsters within, who then and rampaged through the village. The Swords battled to cover the townsfolk's escape. They died, becoming bound to Leilon's ruins as ghosts.

By the early 1490s DR, great strides had been made to rebuild both Leilon and the High Tower of Thalivar. Ogri and his companions kept a close eye on the town's reconstruction, hoping that the repaired tower would not cause another calamity. The Swords of Leilon helped defend Leilon during the Battle of Leilon , a three-way battle in which Leilon was besieged by both the Cult of Talos and the Cult of Myrkul . Together with a group of adventurers, they fought against the Ebondeath who had possessed the ancient green dragon Claugiyliamatar .
