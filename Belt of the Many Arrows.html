A belt of the Many Arrows was a distinctive enchanted belt awarded to the fiercest warriors among the Many-Arrows orcs .

Description

The belts were decorated with the traditional totems of the Many-Arrows tribe, and served as markers of power and status. Some belts were customized by their wearer.

Powers

These belts enhanced their wearers' durability in battle.

History

During the mid-to-late 15 th century DR , orcs who had earned the honor to wear these belts were known to lead warbands in the ruined Tower District of Neverwinter . This included the Many-Arrows' vicious taskmasters, who whipped lower ranking orcs into frenzies before sending them at the Neverwinter Guard .

Notable Owners

Kragmar Deepscar wore a unique Belt of the Many Arrows while he led an orc warband in Neverwinter.
