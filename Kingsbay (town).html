Kingsbay was a small coastal town located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Kingsbay was found at the end of Corwell Road in the kingdom of Corwell in southeast Gwynneth. The town sat along the coast of the Strait of Alaron on the Kingsbay for which it was named.

History

Kingsbay fell into ruin following the conquest of Gwynneth and expulsion of the humans by the fey of Sarifal . By the late 15 th century DR , it was occupied by escaped Ffolk slaves , and was one of only two such free human ports on the island (the other being nearby Borth ).
