Lahib Island was found on the Great Sea off the coast of north Zakhara .

Geography

The island measured roughly 30 miles (48 kilometers) in length and 20 miles (32 kilometers) in width. It sat off the coast within eyesight across from the small town of Mina . The center of the island was covered in dense forest with surrounding brushland.
