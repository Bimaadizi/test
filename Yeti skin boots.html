Yeti skin boots were types of enchanted footwear that carried magical properties of a yeti ' pelt they were made of.

Powers

These boots granted the wearers resistance to mundane and magical cold.

History

Circa Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , Oswald Fiddlebender crafted various magic items and clothing using pelts of yetis that plagued Icewind Dale . Such yeti fur items were a great protection against harsh weather of the Frozenfar .
