A harsh songblade was an enchanted sword meant to be wielded by a bard .

Description

Any heavy or light blade could be enchanted to become a harsh songblade .

Powers

Like other songblades , harsh songblades were magically sharp and strong weapons that could be used as implements for bardic spellcasting .

A bard could use the blade to channel thundering magic , causing it to unleash a wail that could daze the bard's foes, leaving anyone within 10 feet (3 meters) senseless.
