Kumakawa was a type of enchanted light hide armor crafted out of a displacer beast 's hide.

Powers

Kumakawa armor possessed a +3 enchantment to the wearer's defense. Additionally, the item's magics imbued the wearer with reflexes of a predator, increasing their evasion and dexterity.

History

These sets of potent enchanted armors were named after their inventor, a famed artificer Kumakawa , renowned for working with the most exotic skins and hides.
