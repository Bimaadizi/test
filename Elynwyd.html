Elynwyd was an elven clergy instructor of the Neverwinter Academy in Neverwinter in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Elynwyd was an elf with a pale skin tone and slender frame. He had chestnut-colored hair and he was clad in the purple garments of a devotee of Tyr .

Relationships

Elynwyd taught at the same school as the person who would become the Hero of Neverwinter was attending as a student. He oversaw all divine magic and healing students at the academy.

History

When the Wailing Death stricken the city of Neverwinter in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Elynwyd resided in and trained the students of the Neverwinter Academy, helping his student hone their magical and healing skills. However, Elynwyd's life was cut short in the attack on the Academy, that left most of its staff and students dead, and the school itself put to the torch.
