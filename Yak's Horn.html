The Yak's Horn was a southwestern province of the Yikarian Empire in 1367 DR .

Geography

This province was split into equal parts of rocky terrain from the World Pillar Mountains on the northeastern side, and a portion of the desert wasteland known as the Haunted Lands in the southwest. The Yak's Horn was easily the driest province of the Yikarian Empire.

Inhabitants

Mother Millstone served as governor of the Yak's Horn.
