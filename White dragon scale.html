White dragon scale was a variety of armor made from the scales of a white dragon .

Powers

This armor offered the wearer protection from ice and cold energy, such as was emitted by white dragon's breath weapon . They were also granted the power to emit a blast of frost and ice , up to three times per day.

History

The blacksmith Conlan of Kuldahar knew how to craft this type of armor, as did the imp Cespenar from the Throne of Blood .
