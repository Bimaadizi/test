Prison Break was a crude dwarven waraxe found in Wheloon Prison in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The axe was made from various salvaged materials, including a handle made from a large femur bone.

Powers

In addition to simply chopping, Prison Break innately weakened and staggered those it hit, making them more vulnerable to other threats.

History

The axe was found after Cormyrean forces battled with the Sharran priest Stanwicke Gall in the Wheloon Prison during the Shadovar invasion of Wheloon .
