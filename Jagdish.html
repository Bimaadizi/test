Jagdish was a priestess of Velsharoon in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

She cut a dark figure, 70 inches (1.8 meters) tall.

Personality

She was fanatical, almost martyr-like.

Abilities

Besides her unique necrophant abilities, Jagdish was notably agile.

Possessions

She was armed with two vials enchanted with Deathmaster's vial .

Activities

Jagdish was an acolyte.

Relationships

She served under Yerian .

History

In 1370 DR , when the Chantry of St. Sammaster was attacked by Zhentarim , she received orders to hole up in the laboratory and ambush anyone who walked in.
