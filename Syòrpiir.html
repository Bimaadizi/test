Syòrpiir was an ancient kingdom of moon elves and sun elves created during the First Flowering in −23,100 DR .

History

Syòrpiir was drawn into the War of Three Leaves against Thearnytaar and Eiellûr by the actions of assassins and spies from Ilythiir in −17,100 DR .

In −11,600 DR , the kingdom was destroyed by the dark elf kingdom of Ilythiir during the second war of the Crown Wars . Over the years, the ruins of the elven civilization were taken over by the Chondalwood and the Ankhwood .
