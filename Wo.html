Wo was the owner of the House of Wo in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

Wo oversaw all food preparation for his caravanserai . When not spending time in the kitchen, he enjoyed conversing with his guests, even though his command of midani was limited.

Wo also tutored Fayaniz al-Nokar in the ways of the wu jen .

History

Wo was initially from a land in the far east called Shou Lung . No one knew why he left his home or why he decided to settle in Zakhara , but his culinary skills were greatly appreciated in Huzuz.
