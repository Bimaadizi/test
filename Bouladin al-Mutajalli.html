Bouladin al-Mutajalli was the Amir of the jann of the House of Sihr within the High Desert of Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Personality

Bouladin was an honest and noble janni, but did not hesitate to strike against those who opposed him or threatened the House of Sihr.

Relationships

A longtime ally of Tajar and Sheikh Ali al-Hadd , Bouladin aided the great city during times of need. Bouladin also made sure a janni representative from the House of Sihr appeared before the Grand Caliph to pledge their support to him as well.

Bouladin's oldest daughter was named Jamala .

Activities

Bouladin had to spend much of his time and effort keeping the ghul-kin and great ghuls of the Pit of the Ghuls in check.
