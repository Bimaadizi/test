King Rhinn Wavegold "the Pious" was the third Amnian monarch of the Wavegold dynasty, who ruled in the 12 th century DR .

Relationships

Rhinn was the first son of King Belhinn I , and the brother of Erdhinn II .

History

King Rhinn sponsored public religious works in Amn and had great personal interest in matters of faith, going so far as to mandate daily prayers to the gods for his subjects. His influence led to the creation of the many temples and shrines in Esmeltaran , though most of them fell out of use once his reign ended. He was eventually assassinated by Banite agents in the Year of the Peltast, 1193 DR , putting his brother Erdhinn on the throne.
