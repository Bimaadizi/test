Tehrinna the Towering was the proprietor of the Tymoran Trails inn of Crimmor during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Tehrinna was a red-haired woman of forceful character and striking good looks. She was a former adventurer known for her wealth and for her sense of humor. She owed her nickname to her great stature, being about 7 ft (2.1 m) tall.

Relationships

Tehrinna was the lover of Mikaal Krimmevol , head of one of the grand families of Crimmor.
