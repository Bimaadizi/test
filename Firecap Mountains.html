The Firecap Mountains were a small mountain range in northwest Faerûn , part of the larger Ice Spires South .

Geography

This mountain range was largely dominated by a trio of semi-active volcanoes, known as the Three Sisters , whose foothils were adjacent to a small dale that they kept warm. The mountain range also overlooked Lake Fief .
