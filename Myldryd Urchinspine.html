Myldryd Urchinspine was a sea hag that dwelled within a wrecked ship on the shore of the Snout of Omgar during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

The wretched hag made her lair within the ruined hull of the Dirty Dastard , that ran ashore on the Chultan isle.

Possessions

Hidden in the sand beneath the wrecked ship was a treasure chest that held all of Myldryd's possessions. Among these were several simple trinkets, a comb made of whale bone, a stuffed parrot that housed a single carnelian , and hidden within a secret compartment, a medallion of thoughts .

Relationships

She commanded a pair of giant crab minions.
