Scroll of protection from divination was a magical scroll found in Zakhara , the Land of Fate.

Description

Reading this scroll created an area of protection centered around the caster, with a diameter of 10 feet (3 meters). It protected the caster from all divination spells . Within the area of protection, divination spells were unable to be cast. The scroll offered no protection from abilities and spells that revealed magical properties.
