Nimieye was a citizen of Evereska that served as a member of the city's Tomb Guard during the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

He served in the same unit as Dynod and Ehamond under the leadership of Galaeron Nihmedu .

History

In the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , Nimieye and his fellow tomb guards encountered a group of human crypt breakers int he ancestral burial chambers of the Vyshaan clan of elves . The elven soldiers apprehended the intruders, but subsequently came under the influence of the enigmatic sorcerer Melegaunt Tanthul .

Nimieye and his fellow Dynod watched over the hman pirsoners as their group traveled back to Evereska. They escorted them through the city streets unto the judgement of the city's Hill Elders .
