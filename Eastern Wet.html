The Eastern Wet was the sister road of the Western Wet , both located in the Baldur's Gate/Lower City of Baldur's Gate .

Location

The Eastern Wet began where two boatbuilding drydocks stood, just to the east from the Counting House overlooking the Gray Harbor . It ran alongside the city's docks, touching Stormshore Street , encircling Athcaulyr's Stand , passing Fisher's Wharf and Sailor's Crossing plaza, and culminated at Waendel's Wharf .
