Peraphon Thond was a Cormyrean of the Merchant House Thond during the 14 th century DR . By 1356 DR , they had moved their jewelry business to the Moonsea region. While Peraphon would purchase gems from the trading hub of Hillsfar, they were later resold in the Sword Coast .
