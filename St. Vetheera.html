Vetheera was a saint of the churches of Jergal and Oghma from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a posthumous autobiography dedicated to Saint Vetheera was completed, as per the announcement by the joint priesthoods or Jergal and Oghma. The announcement was published on the pages of the Baldur's Mouth Gazette .
