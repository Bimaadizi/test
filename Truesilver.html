House Truesilver was a noble house of Cormyr , whose estate was situated beyond the Royal Gardens in Suzail .

Activities

The family was known for its militarism and it pushed to expand the Imperial Navy's influence in the late 15 th century DR. The Truesilvers and Dauntinghorns shared similar views on Cormyr's military might.
