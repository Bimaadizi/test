Hsiao Nan Tzu Wen was a korobokuru bushi in Linshung , Hungtse Province , in Shou Lung circa Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) .

Activities

Hsiao Nan worked as a retainer for a wealthy merchant, Kao Ling of Linshung. When Kuang Ming Ling , the daughter of his employer, decided to go on pilgrimage to a far-away monastery, Kao Ling discreetly sent Hsiao Nan and another korobokuru retainer, Ling Yu Pao , to protect the girl.

Personality

Hsiao was usually friendly and always encouraged Kuang Ming to go to inns, wine shops, and all other kinds of food or drink shops. He liked tackling "over-stuffed humans with clumsy swords".

Abilities

Hsiao was quite skilled with the sectional staff .
