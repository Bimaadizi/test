Harmond Hawklin was a noble of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

He almost certainly belonged in the Hawklin family.

Rumors & Legends

One trick by Marlin Stormserpent had someone accuse Harmond Hawklin, alongside Rothglar Illance and Seszgar Huntcrown , of plotting to unleash a ball of spellplague from a chest somehow during the Council of the Dragon . The rumor reached Ganrahast Aeiulvana , Royal Magician of Cormyr ; he did not know quite what to do with it.
