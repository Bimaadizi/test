The bear tribe was part of the Azuposi nation of the Pasocada Basin of northern Maztica in the 14 th century DR .

Base of Operations

In addition to inhabiting the Pasocada Basin, the bear tribe maintained a center near the heart of Michaca , the City of Gold.

History

The bear tribe was one of six separate tribes that composed the Azuposi people. They represented the southwest direction on the compass and their sacred animal was the bear .
