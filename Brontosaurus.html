A brontosaurus ( pronounced : / b r ɑː n t oʊ ˈ s ɑːr ʌ s / bran-to-SAR-us listen ), also known as Thunderbeast , was a massive quadruped dinosaur .

Description

Brontosauruses were so large that most predators did not dare attack them.

Behavior

These animals were not aggressive. They calmly lumbered through the jungles ignoring smaller creatures.

Combat

Brontosauruses only fought in self-defense. If necessary, they could stomp enemies and hit them with their enormous tails, which were powerful enough to even kill smaller predators.

Ecology

Brontosauruses were common to the jungles of Chult , near swamp areas and rivers. They were also known to have once roamed the lands of the North and some shamans of the Thunderbeast tribe claimed that they could still be found living within the High Forest .
