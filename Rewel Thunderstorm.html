High Morninglord Rewel Thunderstorm was the High Priest of Lathander in Morningdawn Hall in the village of Shadowdale in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Rewel was the leader of Lathander's faithful in Shadowdale. He was assisted by seven other priests and eleven followers.
