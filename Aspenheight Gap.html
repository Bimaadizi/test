The Aspenheight Gap was a mountain pass located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The pass separated the Aspenheight in the north from the Highlands in the south. The Aspenheight Gap connected the western region of Myrloch Vale with the Shrouded Lands . The pass was approximately 20 miles (32 kilometers) long.
