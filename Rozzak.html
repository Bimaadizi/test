Boss Rozzak was a lieutenant of True Soul Minthara Baenre , who served the Absolute within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

He equipped himself with a goblin -made warboard and a crude scimitar .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Minthara dispatched Rozzak and his scouts to recover a mysterious artifact , which had fallen from a mind flayer nautiloid near the River Chionthar . After Minthara and her fellow True Souls came to believe the artifact was taken to the Emerald Grove , they along with Rozzak began to rally an assault force to take it back from the druids and tieflings within.
