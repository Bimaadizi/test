Manysteps Alley was a minor avenue of hard-packed earth and gravel in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This road ran from north to south, standing between and running parallel to both Zastrow and the Way of the Dragon .

Inhabitants

This alleyway was home to a variety of soothsayers, shady moneylenders, message runners, and thieves . It was generally avoided by those who displayed their wealth.
