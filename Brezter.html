Brezter was an individual in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

During a revel in Warmfires House , Shadowdale , in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the Zhentarim leader Calivar Murpeth called on his henchman, Aldluck Dreen , to bring Brezter and the others to him. Aldluck Dreen later rejoined his boss with several men in apparent distress; presumably, Brezter numbered among them.
