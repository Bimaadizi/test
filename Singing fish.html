Singing fish was a species of fish found in the Akanamere of the Akanal .

Ecology

Usages

These fish were consumed in the coastal city-states of the Blade Kingdoms , such as Sumbria , Kirenzia , Zutria , Lomatra , and Lotoli . The fish was considered a fanciful treat and was served in the Palace of Manniccis during the Festival of Blades , alongside whole-roasted ostriches , sweetened orange rinds, hamsters in sauce, and copious amounts of local wines .
