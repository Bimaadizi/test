Valken Naspeer was the half-elf bodyguard of the Black Network recruiter Urlam Stockspool some time after the War of the Silver Marches of 1485 DR . The two operated out of the Triboar Travelers caravan company located in the town after which it was named .

Valken was a charming and sophisticated individual.
