RaceWhite dragon

Rinxabalax was one of the white dragons working under Zethrindor in the invasion of Sossal for Iyraclea on behalf of Sammaster during the Rage of Dragons of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR . In the aftermath of the siege of Ironspring Grove of Eleint 2 , the dracolich sent him, alongside Ssalangan , after the transformed druids of the Council.
