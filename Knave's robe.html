Knave's robes were common enchanted robes worn by many wizards across Faerûn in the 14 th century DR .

Powers

Knave's robes were defensive robes often used by shady wizards and thieves who dabbled in the Art. The robes' enchantments shielded the wearer from poisons , blades , and death spells.
