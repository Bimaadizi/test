The Neddic Legion was a military group from Al-Ubaid in the Whamite Isles , in the Sea of Fallen Stars . They represented Al-Ubaid in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
