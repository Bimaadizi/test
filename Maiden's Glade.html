Maiden's Glade , also known as Eridis in the language of Illefarn , was a glade near the Mere of Dead Men along the Sword Coast North .

Geography

Located between Fort Locke and Highcliff , Maiden's Glade was a clearing and a retreat for druids , rangers , animals , or any traveler.

Inhabitants

The inhabitants were druids , rangers , and animals, who protected this place.

History

Around 1374 DR , the glade was where druids and rangers would protect the land from intruders. However, the King of Shadows ' influence had an impact on the land, causing the local druid Kaleil to be trapped and enraged in the form of a bear , allowing the animals to attack travelers and intruders. The Kalach-Cha , paired with Elanee , could attack Kaleil and learn of the shadow presences in the Mere of Dead Men before Kaleil died.
