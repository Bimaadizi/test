Saekolath , or the Place of Shade , was the mysterious Lorendrow jungle city located within Chult .

Description

This city was located in a dense forest that was unknown to outsiders. Its buildings were surrounded by tall trees and flowing rivers.

History

When the majority of drow followed Lolth 's ways and descended to the Underdark , a small group instead travelled southward. Here they established the city of Saekolath.
