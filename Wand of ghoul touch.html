Wands of ghoul touch were ghastly magic wands that allowed the wielder to cast the ghoul touch spell.

Powers

Wands of ghoul touch held up to twenty five charges of the spell and could discharge it at will.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , wands of ghoul touch were used by the necromancers of the Cult of the Dragon during their occupation of the ruins of Myth Drannor . The wands and their wielders were stationed in the Tower of Bones of Polyandrium with several wizards holding groun in Castle Cormanthor .
