A Merchant's Tale was a book written by Jarn Tiir of Lantan in the late 13 th century DR .

History

A Merchant's Tale was a collection of life lessons and musings published in the Year of the Smoky Moon, 1287 DR . The work shared some insights into the nature of those who lived to travel and the lesson that a human was more dangerous than any monster waiting in the wild. A copy of the book was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
