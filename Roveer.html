Roveer was a cook from Baldur's Gate 's Elfsong Tavern , who, apparently, cooked ducks daily, in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Roveer sought exterminators to kill rat vermin in the cellars of the Elfsong Tavern. Despite him carrying a massive axe , and owning two cats , Tambourine and Zambomba , the inhabitants and Alan Alyth 's employees could not or were unwilling to resolve the egregious food safety issue.
