Cholk min Kado was a shopkeeper inhabiting Kadarasto in 1367 DR .

Activities

Cholk owned The Shop of Faded Carpets where he sold antique rugs and tapestries. Unbeknownst to most, he also ran an illegal spy network and smuggling operation out of his quaint shop.

Relationships

Cholk was married to Mauza Dalidasis who owned The Broken Wheel .
