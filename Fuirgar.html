Fuirgar , also called the Land of the Stone Giants , was the name given to a small mountain range and surrounding lowland found in the Hordelands and east Faerûn .

Geography

Fuirgar was located on the northern side of Rolling Stone Gap across from the Giant's Belt Mountains . It was bordered to the east by the Raurin desert, and the the west and north by the Dragonsword Mountains . Some of the mountainous heights of Fuirgar were host to passive air shafts, carved out by gold dwarves to draw more air down into the depths of the Deep Lands .

Inhabitants

This mountain range's epithet was derived from the fact that hundreds of stone giants inhabited the area.
