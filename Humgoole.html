Humgoole was a Cashguard Basher in the employ of the Counting House of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Humgoole was a human man with dark skin, trimmed black beard, and short hair. As a Cashguard Basher, he wore a set of uniform plate mail armor and a helmet . Humgoole was armed with a two-handed war hammer .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Cashguard Basher Humgoole was tasked with guarding and patrolling the private docks that belonged to the Counting House in the Lower City , alongside a number of fellow Cashguards and the city's steel watchers .
