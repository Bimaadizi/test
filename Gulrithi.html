Gulrithi was a very old dark naga necromancer who sought to extinguish the Shrine of the Silver Fire in Myth Drannor in 1369 DR .

Abilities

Gulrithi was a powerful spellcaster and a necromancer who was served by risen wights and mohrgs .

Possessions

Gulrithi carried an amulet of perfect health and enchanted dagger called Delvendoor's Chisel .

History

At some point before the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Gulrithi created a lair within the Elven Catacombs underneath Castle Cormanthor . The dark naga hated Mystra , the goddess of magic, and attempted to defile the Shrine of the Silver Fire nearby. The shrine was maintained by two guardian naga priestesses of the Lady of Mystery— Ethe and Gloran . Gulrithi lured the clerics into a magical trap. The two nagas remained in stasis until a group of adventurers known as the Veiled Ones slew Gulrithi and his mohrgs and master wights. The heroes broke the head pole that maintained the stasis trap, releasing the Sisters of the Silver Fire .
