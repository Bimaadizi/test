TypeMagical trap
OriginAbyss

A mirror of madness was a phenomenon that acted as a viewing portal into the Abyss , functioning as a window; those who gazed upon it risked madness . The mirror was always positioned on a wall.

The mirror of madness was created when Abyssal cults corrupted a location for several years. The mirror recognized the cultists and their allies as allies and any other creatures as enemies, and acted as a trap for the latter. Those attacked could experience effects from immobilization to domination of their will. If the mirror was physically attacked, it would lash out at the surrounding creatures.

Creatures with rogueish , arcane , or divine inclinations had the greatest chances of resisting the mirror's effects and destroying it simply by standing in its proximity. Once destroyed, the mirror's effects ceased.
