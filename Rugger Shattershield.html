Lord Rugger Shattershield was a senior member of the noble patriar Shattershield family of Baldur's Gate and a member of the Parliament of Peers in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Rugger was a gold dwarf with luscious head of pampered brown hair, clean-shaven face, double chin, and hazel eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldur's Gate, Lord Rugger Shattershield was a Parliamentarian of the Gate who voted in support of crowning lowborn Lord Enver Gortash as the city's Archduke. Rugger Shattershield was present for Gortash's coronation that took place in the audience hall of Wyrm's Rock . Following the coronation, he proudly supported the Archduke's steen constructs and the safety they brought in face of a looming war.
