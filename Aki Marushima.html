Aki Marushima was a restaurateur who ran the well-known Aki's restaurant in the town of Aru in Wa in the 1770s on the Wa Calendar .

Relations

His brother Tojo Marushima ran a competing restaurant, and the two were fierce rivals.

History

In Wa Year 1775 (1357 DR) , Aki declared his restaurant to be the best in Aru, but so did Tojo. To settle the question, they each might've offered a group of adventurers a free meal, one night after the other, so they could serve as impartial judges. However, Aki would pay a person to slip a mild purgative into their food when they dined at Tojo's , which would cause them gastrointestinal problems during their adventures.
