Josef the Ferret was a duergar from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Josef wore a simple set of chainmail armor .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Josef the Ferret, along with other duergar, giant spiders , and drow , was slaughtered in the basement cave under the home of Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica . Josef's decapitated body was left broken near the mutilated body of Incontinentia Figgin , who was turned into a grim effigy by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal .
