Dragoneyes Street was a stone-paved road running through the center of Myth Drannor , in the City Heights districts, and perpendicular to the Street of the Evening Star .

Geography

Dragoneyes Street was one of the central streets of Myth Drannor. It ran along Castle Cormanthor 's northern wall and ended just past Serpentspires , merging into a street perpendicular to it. The street stretched all the way to the city's western border, passing te Sixstar intersection, where it crossed with Shandol Street and the Street of Song , among others, in front of the Windsong Tower .

At one point, Dragoneyes Street crossed over the Great Skywalk , notable for its snowshield in the shape of a flying dragon .
