Fist Elthira Gossen was a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Elthira was a zealous human woman with dark freckled skin and brown eyes. She wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist â€“ a helmet , a set of chainmail , and a tabard adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. She wielded a massive halberd in battle.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Elthira Gossen was tasked with patrolling the Lower City of Baldur's Gate , near the Basilisk Gate . Her zealous attempts to protect the city from the agents of the encroaching army of Absolutists led to harassment common folk who happened to strike suspicion in the Fist. Some of the people harassed by Elthira were Drongo Bixworth and his husband, Florean Bixworth , who were detained and interrogated for the crime of loitering.
