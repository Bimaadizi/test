Blindelsyn Olósynne was the appointed leader of Relkath's Foot in Aglarond from at most 1372 DR to at least 1375 DR . She spent most of her time overseeing the functioning of the town from the Royal Hall . She was the sister of Prince Branelwyr ; both of them were direct, yet distant descendants of Brindor Olóssyne .
