Vestments of Faith were enchanted cloaks gifted by Ilmater to his favorite followers.

Description

These vestments were designed to be worn over normal clothing or armor.

Powers

Vestments of Faith could protect its wearer absorbing some of the punishment dealt. Ilmater's followers who were given the cape used it to ease physical suffering of their faithful souls.

History

Vestments of Faith appeared and went into circulation around the Sword Coast and the areas surrounding Neverwinter in the late 14 th century DR.
