Belabranta Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Belabranta .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward on the northeast corner of where the Rough Road intersected Mendever Street .

Description

The walled compound contained four separate buildings arranged around a central garden. Each building stood between three and five stories tall.
