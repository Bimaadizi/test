A ring of vigor was an enchanted ring that had restorative powers.

Description

The ring was a simple golden band with a single rectangular aquamarine gemstone .

Powers

Although it did not always function, a ring of vigor bestowed the wearer with a "cleansing touch" that allowed them to magically heal and cleanse themselves of negative ailments.
