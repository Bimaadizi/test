Groth was a firbolg chieftain on the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles as of the mid–14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR , Groth was in charge of capturing the unicorn Kamerynn and strengthening the Darkwell in Myrloch Vale by the Beast Kazgoroth , both of which tasks he failed. Groth joined the Northlander chieftain Grunnarch the Red King in the siege of Caer Corwell , and was killed in the fighting.
