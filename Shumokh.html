The Shumokh family was an influential mercantile house of Eshpurta in Amn in the 14 th century DR .

Activities

The Shumokhs had traditionally dealt in blacksmithing and armorcrafting, but Lord Bhaem pursued his own interest in lute -making and built a small fortune doing so.
