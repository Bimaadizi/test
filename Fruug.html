Fruug , was a servant of Zariel who served her alongside Florenta and her flame, the tiefling named Karlach , during her time in the Nine Hells in the late 15 th century DR .

History

When Karlach Cliffgate escaped the Hells in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR abroad a spelljammer ship, Florenta sent sulphur -reeking notes to Toril . One such note that found Karlach talked about Fruug getting stuck in a butter barrel and, when his little imp tried to help its master, both plummeted into a volcano to Florenta's amusement. Flo laughed so hard that Zariel sent her out of the room.
