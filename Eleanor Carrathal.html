Eleanor Carrathal was a Queen of Callidyrr in the early-to-mid 12 th century DR . She was the wife of King Edwin II .

History

Eleanor and Edwin II had one son, Adrian , who was born in the Year of the Lupine Torque, 1125 DR .

She became Queen of Callidyrr when her husband inherited the throne in the Year of the Persuasive Voice, 1133 DR . However, Edwin II died soon after in the Year of the Petulant Dragon, 1135 DR , and because Adrian was only ten years old, Eleanor named King Adom Kimball of Moray as regent of Callidyrr.

Adrian assumed the throne six years later, in the Year of Eyes, 1141 DR .
