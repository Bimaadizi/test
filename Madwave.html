Captain Madwave was the human captain and owner of the Gontese merchant ship Argus Treader .

Background

Captain Madwave was one of the few gontese ship owners who didn't worked for the Twelvefold . She had the reputation of never having lost a cargo, a member of her crew, or a bet.
