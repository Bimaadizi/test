Torrence was one of the names used by a jackalwere that lived in or around the city of Tilverton in Cormyr during the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

In his disguised form, Torrence appeared as a silver-haired man .

History

In the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , Torrence drew out the solitary Kelemvor Lyonsbane , believing him to be a fellow antherion that would enjoy feasting on the people of Tilverton. He confronted Kelemvor at the city's temple of Gond , but soon realized the other man was actually cursed with a unique form of lycanthropy when he made his transformation into a great black panther .

The two were-beasts fought in the city for a brief while, until eventually Kelemvor delivered to Torrence a fatal blow, and the silver jackal lay dying in the street.
