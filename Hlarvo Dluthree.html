Hlarvo Dluthree was the owner of the Flying Stag , an inn that catered to well-to-do travelers in Voonlar , as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . Hlarvo was formerly a skilled carpenter with dreams of opening his own inn. His skeptical wife Valladonra goaded him into it by snidely observing that he would follow his dream when "stags learned to fly".
