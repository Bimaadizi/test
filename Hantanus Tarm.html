Hantanus Tarm was the castellan of Goldenfields sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR , like all castellans of Goldenfields, Tarm was appointed by the Lords of Waterdeep to lead the defense of the place, despite having no military experience. His position was a political and ceremonial one and he had no influence outside the gatehouse.
