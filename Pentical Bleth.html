Pentical Bleth was a high-ranking member of the Bleth noble house in Westgate and member of the ruling Council of Merchants around 1374 DR .

History

In 1374 DR, in order to increase her power, Pentical allied with an adventurer who was fighting the Night Masks . At last, however, Pentical was captured by the Night Masks and killed by the vampire cabal that ruled the guild.
