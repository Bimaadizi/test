Roekandal of Reth was a mage of the late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He was seen as ruthless, cold, and a master of intrigues.

History

Roekandal had long since left the city where his tower stood, to build his power by seizing the magic of mages he thought he could best and slay. He sought the Crown of the Undying Flame in the early 1370s DR .
