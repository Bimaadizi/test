Nalambra was a maid in Neverwinter in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The young maid had a noticeably muscled back.

Personality

The woman was notably idiotic.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , she and fellow maid Karlae was hired by Auvrarn Labraster , who was spell-disguised as Blandras Nuin , after he slew his maid Alaithe .

On the last day that they worked for him, he arrived wet and soiled from a late evening of drinking, to find them holding garden shears and fire-tongs, which they had no reason to be using. They fled at his screams. He took a bath immediately, and when he called for them to bring him hot rocks, they screamed and made a racket. He'd later find them undressed in his bed, with their hair combed, after being confronted by the ghost of Anastra Sylun√© Silverhand , who was pretending to be the ghost of the maid Alaithe.

He leapt between them, waking and startling them. They ran out the door, tripping on each other, and then down the hallway, where the ghost ensorcelled them into a deep sleep, and then made their bodies hover, open-eyed yet knocked out, in a stiff position, outlining their shapes with dancing lights to better haunt him. That spooked him alright.

Afterwards, as the Simbul arrived, she made a point of making the house explode. However, she spared Karlae and Nalambra from the explosion, leaving them unharmed at the end of the ruckus.
