Sabran was a Zhentarim priest of Bane in the mid-14 th century DR .

Personality

Sabran could find humor in Zhentarim hierarchy and its workings. He got quite bitter at nosy people.

Relationships

He bickered much with Mhegras during their assignment to the capture of Shandril Shessair . Drauthtar Inskirl and Eirhaun Sooundaeril both saw him as a rival worth slaying. Hlael Toraunt and Korthauvar Hammantle counted him as the most formidable Zhent in the caravan.

Abilities

One of his spells allowed him to cheat death in exchange for a live sacrifice. He was likely the most formidable Zhentarim in the caravan.

Possessions

Some of the cargo he concealed included horse barding.

History

Sabran joined the caravan from Scornubel to Waterdeep in which Shandril Shessair sought passage on late 1357 DR . He presented himself as a weaver instead of a priest, and concealed some horse armor as contraband, despite the fact he was undercover. He remained undercover through the ambush at Haelhollow , not taking part in the battle.

In the aftermath of the fight, they tried to capture Shandril while she was out of spellfire and knocked out, guarded only by Narm Tamaraith , but decided against it when she woke up too soon. However, they were then ambushed by the Harpers Beldimarr and Arauntar , who were posing as caravan guards, and got their necks broken. This left them unseen to the Zhentarim scriers Hlael Toraunt and Korthauvar Hammantle . for much of a day. Unknown to the Harpers, he survived thanks to a contingent spell.

He was slain during the caravan's next ambush, by Marlel .
