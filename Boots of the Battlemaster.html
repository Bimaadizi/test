The boots of the Battlemaster were the boots of the Night Brigade officer Agarta the Breaker in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The boots made the wearer extra fast and agile, and better able to avoid danger.

History

The boots were worn by Agarta in Wheloon Prison until she was slain by the forces of the Riverside Rats .
