The Stalwart Scales was an enchanted suit of splint mail armor , crafted by the dwarven armorsmith Hedda Perigot .

Description

The armor comprised shimmering bands of steel that had been affixed to a leather breastplate. It was noticeably lightweight compared to other suits of armor, and extremely flexible for the wearer.

Powers

The +2 splint mail offered significant protection against blades and other slashing weapons .

History

At some point after the armor's creation, it came into the possession of the dwarven smith Jegg Hillcarver , of Bridgefort .
