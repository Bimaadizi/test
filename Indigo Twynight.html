Indigo Twynight was a noble elven wizard who was a member of the Elven Court .

Description

Fair-skinned and brown-haired, Indigo wore a hooded and voluminous blue robe.

Personality

She was a skilled conciliator and was often tasked with settling court disputes.

Possessions

Indigo owned a crystal ball , as well as a ring of spell turning .

History

Despite her young age, Indigo was admitted to the Elven Court on account of her noble heritage. Indigo hoped to become the primary court wizard one day, and was generally considered to have a bright future ahead of her.
