The Cairn Road was a battered wagon trail located in the Dessarin Valley , within the greater Savage Frontier .

The road connected the village of Red Larch , as it intersected with the Long Road , to the trading town of Bargewright Inn . A number of farms and ranches were found on either side of the road's length.
