Canthraxis was a blue dragon dracolich living in the Moonshae Isles around 1370 DR .

Relationships

The dracolich worked with the Cult of the Dragon cell operating on the Isles.

Rumors and Legends

The blue dragon's lair was rumored to be on the isle of Snowdown .
