Halaster's teleportation rings were magic items that allowed the wearer to teleport without error .

Powers

Once relatively common, these rare rings could teleport the wearer from their current location to a predetermined location on the same plane upon speaking a command word or through sheer force of will. However, Halaster cursed these rings to transport the wearer to a location of his choosing. To make matters worse, all of the equipment carried by the wearer, including the teleportation ring , would be stripped from them during the teleportation and scattered throughout Undermountain .

The command word for these rings was "Athlas", which meant "lost" in an ancient northern dialect.

History

These rings were placed throughout the ruins of Undermountain.
