Sergeant Hethat Deegrell was a strongheart halfling warrior of the militia in the village of Shining Falls in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Alongside Nemmet Viccstray and Jren Prencertal , he served Sheriff Vendal Viccstray .
