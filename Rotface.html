Rotface was a green hag of the Bloodroot Coven in Twilight Marsh near Phlan in 1489 DR .

Personality

Rotface was petty and had a bit of mean streak. She was rude and offensive, with no concern for social rules. In the absence of Deathwallow , she tried to act as the voice of reason but with few results.
