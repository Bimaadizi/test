Copper Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Copper Street was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward and formed part of the western border of the North Ward . The road branched south from Skulls Street and continued due south to eventually end on Trader's Way at the border with the Castle Ward .
