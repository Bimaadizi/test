Baylox , the Master Mage of Shussel , was a skilled wizard and sage and frequent visitor of the small town of Azun in Okoth in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Activities

Baylox was the most learned individual regarding Okothian magics and was often consulted regarding his expertise. Reports of increasing reptilian activity around the Lake of Salt had him convinced that the ancient Sarrukh civilization of Okoth had returned to the region.
