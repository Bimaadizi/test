Edik Brey (pronounced ED-ick BRAY ) was a foreman in Mulmaster .

History

Edik was a foreman retainer of House Peytir . He worked as manager at Peytir Timber until, in 1491 DR , a strange quake destroyed it and his employers disappeared.

Personality

Edik was fiercely loyal to the Peytir family and was a natural leader. He truly cared about his job and the men and women who worked for him. He had a need for action and was anxious about the disappearance of his workers.
