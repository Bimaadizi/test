The sword of Icewind Dale was a magical sword .

Powers

This enchanted sword had a +1 enchantment. It dealt double damage to fire -based monsters.

History

In 1358 DR , a group of pirates wished to present the enchanted weapon to High Captain Kurth in his tower . However, they were intercepted by the Heroes of Ascore whilst waiting for Kurth. After a difficult battle, the heroes claimed the sword as their own.
