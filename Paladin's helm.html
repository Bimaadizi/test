Paladin's helms were, as the name suggested, enchanted helmets favored by paladins . They could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

A paladin's helm had a protective enchantment that rewarded wearers who put themselves in danger to save others. If a paladin drew the attention of their foes away from their allies, the helm gave them the benefits of a shield of faith spell.
