Krostal was a thief of the Cult of the Dragon in the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

Krostal had a ginger beard.

Personality

Krostal was dismissive of matters he hadn't personally experienced. He disliked alanthe wine from Sheirtalar , and yhaummarind , too.

Abilities

Krostal was an expert thief.

Possessions

Krostal wielded a number of throwing knives.

Activities

Krostal was a genuine dealer in Lantanna clockwork when he wasn't undertaking thefts.

Relationships

Malivur and Krostal were working together to capture Shandril Shessair late in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , though they didn't get along too well. For some reason, they both sought to kill Aumlar Chauntoun .

History

According to Korthauvar Hammantle, Krostal had been the one to seize the Tiara of the Eyes from Lady Thaulindra of Sheirtalar a few years before.

Malivur and Krostal were assigned to attempt the capture of Shandril Shessair while she was in a caravan from Scornubel to Waterdeep in late 1357 DR. However, before attempting to capture Shandril, they sought to kill Aumlar Chaunthoun when the opportunity presented itself. They were slain by Shandril, almost by accident. They had both been scried on and identified by the Zhentarim scriers Hlael Toraunt and Korthauvar Hammantle , as well as Red Wizards scrying under the command of Hulrivior in Triel .

Rumors & Legends

Krostal told Malivur he recognized another, especially powerful, fellow mage of the Cult of the Dragon in the caravan. The people scrying on them weren't able to identify that mage; it is possible he was lying.
