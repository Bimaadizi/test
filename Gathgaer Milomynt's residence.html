Gathgaer Milomynt's residence was a row house and the home of Gathgaer Milomynt located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The home stood two stories tall. The house was quite run-down.

Location

Gathgaer Milomynt's residence was located in the north section of Waterdeep's Dock Ward on the east side of Drawn Sword Alley .
