Abbey Fargo was a soldier in the Neverwinter Guard who served during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

She had a sister named Zoey , who was also a member of Neverwinter 's guard force.

History

Following the Siege of Neverwinter in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Abbey was stationed with her sister in the city's Blacklake district .

Abbey was part of a force that stormed the Tower of Solace in order to rescue Dorothea Linkletter and stop the evil wizard Rhazzad . She fought alongside Zoey and their fellow guardsmen: Sergeant Knox , Dell Mc Court , Hawthidon Faalwun , and Dorothea's husband Josef .
