TypePotion
Value50 gp

Potions of animal speaking were magical concoctions that allowed their imbibers to verbally speak with animals for a period of time.

Description

Potions of animal speaking smelled of hay, musk, and manure. The effects of this potion lasted for one day.

Creation

A potion of animal speaking could me created by mixing an essence of acorn truffles and certain types of alchemical salts .
