Lhupo was a goblin of the Cragmaw tribe of goblins, bugbears , and hobgoblins at Cragmaw Castle in Neverwinter Wood in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Though he was a priest, Lhupo had no divine power, but claimed the god Maglubiyet spoke to him. He commanded two acolytes. Lhupo kept a pet grick in Cragmaw Castle to protect him.
