King Esmel Torlath II was an early Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty who had a tragic reign.

Relationships

Esmel was the first son of King Corl Torlath I , and Marcos Vymmar was his wife's father. Esmel III was his second son. His brother Prince Ereskas bore the family blade Namarra north to found Mirabar .

History

Esmel's wife and their eldest son disappeared in the Year of Fire and Frost, 600 DR when the realm of Minsorran was destroyed by Icehauptannarthanyx . He died of grief the following year, putting his younger son Esmel III on the throne at a young age under the regency of his father-in-law Marcos Vymmar.
