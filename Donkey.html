A donkey or ass was an equine species, often domesticated and used as a working animal.

Species

Ecology

These creatures were often herded among other grazing beasts in order to protect them from wild predators, such as coyotes or wild dogs .

Diet

Donkeys were well accustomed to eating grass , requiring no separate fodder.

Rumors and legends

The appearance of donkeys could indicate the presence of Ilmater , the deity of suffering and martyrdom.
