Orgaal was a member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave who served the Elemental Prince Olhydra during the late 15 th century DR .

History

At some point during or before the Year of the Scarlet Witch, 1491 DR , Orgaal was sent on a mission to spy upon the Temple of Eternal Flame for the Crushing Wave. Orgaal was caught in the act by the cultists of the Flame , imprisoned within their temple complex, and interrogated by Flamewrath Ignatia .
