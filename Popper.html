Popper was a nihilistic kobold merchant member of the Circus of the Last Days troupe in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Popper was a short red-scaled kobold with a cheerful attitude and a snazzy hat.

Possessions

The kobold ran a stall at the Circus of the Last Days where he sold various "edible" treats, some potions , like elixirs of guileful movement , potions of flying , elixirs of necrotic resistance , as well as smokepowder arrows and a single pair of dark displacement gloves .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Popper accompanied the Circus of the Last Days to Toril and the city of Baldur's Gate . There, the kobold was known to steal various items and "treats" off of corpses, including a severed hand from the corpse of Dribbles the clown, on which he promptly gnawed.
