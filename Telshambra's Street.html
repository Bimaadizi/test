Telshambra's Street was a road paved in hard-packed dirt and gravel , that stood on the boundary line of the city of Waterdeep 's Southern Ward and Trades Ward .

Geography

This road ran from east to west, spanning between the High Road and the Way of the Dragon . Along the way it acted as the southern terminus of Blackmul Street and the terminus for both ends of the curving Ilisar's Alley .
