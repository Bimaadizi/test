Waelmar Zemphrin was talented fighter and thief inhabiting Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR .

Description

Waelmar was a stout man known for his fabulous mustache and gruff demeanor. He was quick to draw weapons when confronted.

Activities

Waelmar loved a good fight, especially with pirates. He was often hired as a cargo guard by merchants departing Urmlaspyr for Westgate .
