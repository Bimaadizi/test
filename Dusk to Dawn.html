Dusk to Dawn was a traveling tavern in the late-15 th century DR . The tavern's name derived from the fact that it always set up shop around dusk, then once dawn hit the proprietor would pack everything up.

Description

This tavern largely consisted of a tent , casks , and kegs .

Geography

Dusk to Dawn was always situated in the Mistshore neighborhood of Waterdeep 's Dock Ward . It set up shop wherever there was a large open area.
