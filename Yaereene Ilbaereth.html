Yaereene Ilbaereth was an elf of House Ilbaereth and the proprietor of the Elfstone Tavern , a tavern aimed at elves and half-elves in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep . She usually had a blue-green faerie dragon familiar named Pyrith on her shoulder.

Appearance

Yaereene was tall with silvery eyes, and pale copper skin.

Relatives

Malruthiia Chaadren was Yaereene's illegitimate half-sister. After Malruthiia's death, Yaereene sorely missed her, despite her family's general view on the matter.
