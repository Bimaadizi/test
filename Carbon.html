Carbon was an elemental material found in the Realms .

Availability

Carbon was commonly found in the near-pure forms of soot , charcoal , coke , and graphite . It was much more rarely encountered as diamonds , a pure and naturally forming kind of crystalline carbon.

Aurora's Emporium sold amorphous carbon for 1 gp per 6 oz (170 g) and graphitic carbon for 3 gp per 6 oz (170 g), which were intended for the arcane uses of wizards .

The Elemental Store of Karsus Enclave sold pure elemental carbon for 2 gp per 1 ounce (28 grams).
