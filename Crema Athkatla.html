Crema Athkatla was a sweet custard , and one of the few desserts in Chultan cuisine. It was a remnant of the Amnian occupation of parts of Chult.

Description

Crema Athkatla was caramel in color, and was served in bowls. One bowl was worth 5 silver pieces in Port Nyanzaru near the end of the 15 th century DR .
