Dragon cult cubes was a set of two enchanted hellish dice .

Description

Dragon cult cubes were six-sided dice said to have been carved out of Abyssal stone and tempered by the flames of Tiamat herself. Each side of the dice was inscribed with glowing Iokharic script.

Powers

Believed to hold a portion of Tiamat's divine power, dragon cult cubes , when activated, imbued the user with dragon's ire , granting them draconic strength.
