Whitlock Brandish was a member of the Ridesmen of Archendale in the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Whitlock had a single sibling, his sister Melann Brandish .

History

Circa Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Whitlock left the army and went on a quest with his sister to try and lift a deadly curse that was cast on their family by Chare'en .
