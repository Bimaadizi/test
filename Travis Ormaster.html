Travis Ormaster was the steward of Lord Partic Thistle , and served him at Thistleflame Keep around 1369 DR .

Personality

He was quick-witted and clever, with a brusque manner and sharp wits.

Possessions

As the steward of his lord, Travis was entrusted with Lord Thistle's signet ring.

Abilities

Travis had a variety of simple arcane spells which he used to perform his administrative work for Lord Thistle.

Activities

As steward, Travis was responsible for a wide variety of issues at Thistleflame. He managed Lord Thistle's schedule, handled the staff, administered the finances and ensured the manor's stores were kept stocked. Because of his position, he was well aware of the gossip and activities of his staff.

Relationships

Travis was friends with Lord Thistle's messenger Kaleb Fleetwing , but jealous rivals with the gardener Berke Glorata .
