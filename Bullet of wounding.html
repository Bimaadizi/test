Bullets of wounding were a type of enchanted sling bullet that left behind grievous bleeding wounds.

Powers

Bullets of wounding were enchanted with magical currents that made these stones more deadly and balanced than their mundane alternatives. In addition to that, the bullets dealt bludgeoning damage upon impact and left behind bleeding wounds.

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , these bullets were used by the Legion of the Chimera to bring destruction on the Ten Towns and Kuldahar of Icewind Dale , as well as the mercenary heroes, who set out to defend the region.
