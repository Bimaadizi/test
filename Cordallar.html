Cordallar was a noble house of Cormyr .

History

In Alturiak of 1365 DR , Baerune Cordallar and other members of the Cordallar family were discovered scheming against the Cormyrean royal family. Baerune planned to marry Princess Alusair then assassinate Crown Princess Tanalasta so he would take the throne.
