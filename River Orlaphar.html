The River Orlaphar flowed from the north out of the Glaur Lake and into the Trackless Sea , across the lands of the kingdom of Gontal .

Features

The valley surrounding River Orlaphar was wild and trackless, full of strange monsters that defied description such as the anaxims . Because of that, people preferred to navigate the river than to risk to use the overland routes when they needed to travel across Gontal.
