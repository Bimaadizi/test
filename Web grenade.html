Web grenades were a an alchemical thrown weapon that exploded in sticky webs , entangling an enemy.

Description

Web grenades appeared to be round tight tangled orbs of webs pulsing with magic, ready to discharge all of its sticky contents with a simple throw.

Creation

These weapons could be created through alchemy by combining a suspension of spider silk extracted from giant spiders ' or ettercaps ' silk glands with certain types of alchemical vitriol.
