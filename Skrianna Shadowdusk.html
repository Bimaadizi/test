Skrianna Shadowdusk was a human wizard of the Shadowdusk family who lived in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR .

Activities

Skrianna was a student of the magical school at the Dweomercore level of Undermountain.

History

Skrianna enrolled in the school sometime before 1492 DR . At one point a fellow student wizard rival Yarek summoned a bone devil in order to kill her but the devil first killed his summoner.

Personality

Skrianna was always in search of a challenge and for this she enrolled in the academy. She was a rude, privileged individual, disliking common people and with a sadistic side in controlling other people with her magic. Skrianna never trusted newcomers.

Relationships

Skrianna had always at her side a shield guardian bodyguard and a grell valet. She had an imaginary lover called Acamar that in her dreams invited her to visit him in the Far Realm .
