Esclarotta was a cloud giant and the deceased wife of Blagothkus .

History

Esclarotta was the wife of Blagothkus before she died. Somehow, he managed to resurrect her spirit and used her to manage the flying Skyreach Castle .

If Blagothkus died for any reason, then he would replace Esclarotta as the spirit powering the castle. Presumably she would then go to the afterlife .

Personality

Esclarotta greatly disliked all those cultists, dragons , and kobolds inside her castle, but loved her husband too much to act.
