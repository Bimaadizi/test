The embrace of the Spider Queen was a suit of adamantine plate armor found in the Demonweb Pits of Lolth in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The armor was made of dark, near-black adamantine and covered in spider engravings, giving it a disturbing appearance.

Powers

The embrace gave the wearer unparalleled resilience in the face of physical danger, deflecting and absorbing most blows while fortifying their health. It also gave them functional immunity to natural venoms and poisons , and strong resistance to the most lethal of unnatural and magical poisons too.

History

This armor may have been recovered by the forces of Cormyr when Valana sent them into the Demonweb to close the Rift Between Worlds , defeating Lolth's champion Khaszlokhar in the process.
