Nukqup Lake was a lake in the Nakvaligach region of the Great Glacier .

Description

Nukqup Lake was a fresh water lake with an average depth of between 5​ to ​20 feet (1.5​ to ​6.1 meters). Due to a high content of dissolved minerals, the lake's water was black and bitter to the taste. Despite the bitternesss, the water was potable.

Geography

Nukqup Lake was located in eastern Nakvaligach near the Keryjek Ridge .

Flora & Fauna

No fish lived within Nukqup Lake.
