Arbrace was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 th century DR .

Rumors & Legends

In Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , while grumbling about how difficult it was to hunt down Elminster Aumar , Arbrace was named by the War Wizard Eskrel Starbridge alongside Belandroon and Hawksar as one of the few War Wizards who might well be as competent as they thought themselves to be. All mages present grinned at that.
