Ikaron was a tiefling from Elturel that was forced out of the city along with others of his kind, following the Descent of the city into the Nine Hells in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .

Possessions

He wore a suit of battered metal armor and carried a greataxe .

Activities

Ikaron served as a protector of his people and was readily willing to put anyone down that dared harm them.

History

After Elturel was pulled down into Avernus and subsequently returned to the Heartlands all of the tieflings in the city were exiled and forced to fend for themselves. Ikaron was among those that followed the former Hellrider Zevlor that eventually settled at the Emerald Grove for a time.
