Skitter was the giant spider companion of the ranger Kuldail Ukien during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Skitter served its master Kuldail as he defended Druid's Deep for the insane druid Halsaime . Kuldail and the other druids and rangers of the grove had been subverted by the strange magical powers of Halsaime's staff , which turned them into single-minded soldiers against the world.
