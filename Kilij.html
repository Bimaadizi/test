Kilij was a male halfling holy slayer who lived on Nada al-Hazan in the Crowded Sea .

Activities

Kilij was a member of the Gilded Palm . He worked for the Ben Ayyub merchant house.

Relationships

Kilij and Maneira formed a matched team of assassins .
