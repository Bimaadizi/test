Stone cursed were humanoids that had been turned to stone and reanimated through alchemical rituals.

Description

Stone cursed were the statue-like remains of those who had been petrified and had a dim echo of life restored via a foul alchemical ritual.

During this ritual, a fist-sized obsidian skull was formed within the stone cursed's torso. It was not visible from the outside, but survived if the stone cursed was destroyed. This skull held a dim echo of the original being's memories and could be extracted by a skilled magic-wielder.

Personality

Stone cursed were imbued with a strong, malevolent drive to slay the living. However, they were beholden to their creator and would follow their commands as best they can.

Abilities

An alchemical sludge was produced from the claw-like fingers of a stone cursed, which petrified anything scratched by them.

Creation

Stone cursed were created through an alchemical ritual that involved the blood of a basilisk and the ashes of a cockatrice 's feathers.

History

Several groups of stone cursed were encountered inside Father Llymic 's prison in the Thunder Peaks , many being the very guards maintaining his prison that had succumbed to the foul curses of the elder evil .
