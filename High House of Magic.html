The High House of Magic was the guildhall of the Wizards Guild of Ravens Bluff in the Vast .

It was a large, needle-spired castle-like structure with small bolts of crackling purple lightning washing over the walls. It stood on Mac Intyre Path, The building hosted a large library and had enchanted busts of former archmages in the entryway.
