Kabina was a Bedine warrior of the Bait Mahwa tribe that fought against the Zhentarim invasion of Anauroch during the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Description

Kabina was a brawny man.

Personality

He was a superstitious man that believed spending time around spellcasters would displease the gods.

Relationships

Kabina was considered to a favored warrior of Sheikh Sa'ar .

Although he fought alongside them during the conflicts against the Zhentarim, Kabina was extremely weary of the sorceress Ruha and the foreign member of the Harpers called Lander .

History

Kabina was present at the important meetings between the sheikhs of the Bedine tribes leading up to the Battle of the Fissures , which took place in the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR .
