Makred the Foul was an orc shaman of Gruumsh of the Many-Arrows kingdom and one of the lieutenants of Vansi Bloodscar in the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

Makred fought in a manner that was common among the Eye of Gruumsh shamans that served the One-Eyed God.

Possessions

He wore a pair of magical bracers and wielded a spear in battle.

History

During the Many-Arrows orcs' occupation of the River District of Neverwinter , Makred took up residence within the Cloak Tower , along with Vansi Bloodscar and her other lieutenants. When adventurers in service to Captain Janros of the Neverwinter Guard finally penetrated the tower in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , they confronted and slew Makred the Foul in the Alchemy Chamber.
