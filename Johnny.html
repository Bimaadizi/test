Johnny was the brother of Footy and one of victims, murdered by a mad cleric Basillus .

History

In 1368 DR , Johnny and his mother were among victims of a crazed Cyricist Basillus. He raised the body of the slain innocents as skeletons and zombies , including Johnny. Footy witnessed Basillus madly ranting to his undead, thinking they were his dead family. The cild recognized Johnny's knickers on one of the zombie but did not realize that the shambling dead was his brother.
