Sabbagh was a human cleric from Raven's Bluff who enjoyed giving small hammers to children.

Description

Clad from top to toe in chainmail , adorned with a hooded white tabard with blue trim, he had light skin and blonde hair.

Possessions

Aside from his bag of hammers and chainmail, Sabbagh carried a large hammer and a magical shield .

Activities

Sabbagh originally hailed from Ravens Bluff , but took to the road after becoming frustrated with the Lord Mayor's intervention in the matters of his church. He was fond of children, and handed out small replicas of his own hammer to any he met.
