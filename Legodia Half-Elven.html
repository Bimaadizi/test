Legodia Half-Elven was a half-elf mage inhabitant of Bremen , one of the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Legodia was a half-elven woman with a light skin tone and long brown hair.

Possessions

As a wizard, Legodia wore a blue-colored robe of cold resistance , a pair of boots of expeditious retreat , a ring of clear thought , and was armed with a wand of missiles .

History

In the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , a large pack of yetis attacked Bremen. During the invasion, Legodia tried to defend the town by slinging spells , but that was not enough to stop the monstrosities. Dorbulgruf Shalescar , a local shopkeeper tried to help Legodia but both of them ended up being cornered, attacked by a massive abominable yeti . Dorbulgruf witnessed the woman whom he held in high esteem, despite her elvish blood, being ripped apart and clawed to death. Dorbulgruf survived with the Hero of Neverwinter 's timely intervention, too late to save Legodia.
