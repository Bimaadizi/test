Cachiko was a waitress in the town of Aru in Wa .

Relationships

Cachiko was the granddaughter of Fusae and her translator, being very good in sign language.

History

Cachiko helped Fusae in managing the House of Heavenly Sand . In 1358 DR , she helped Fusae tell an adventuring party hired by the daimyo Benju Matsutomo the truth about the dead body found inside her House.
