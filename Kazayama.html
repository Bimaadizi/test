Kazayama was a mountain located in Kozakura .

Description

Kazayama was situated on the border between Iwari and Fukudo Provinces on the island of Shinkoku . Once a lofty and snow-capped summit, Kazayama now exhibited a dished-out shape, a peculiar aftermath of a magical confrontation.

The slopes of Kazayama bore the remnants of curious glasslike stone and obsidian , a result of the mountain's magical destruction. The locals utilized these glassy rocks to craft knives and decorative items, and it was rumored that such artifacts possessed an affinity for enchantment, boasting arcane magical properties.

History

Kazayama was also known as Broken-Dish Mountain due to a duel between the wu jens Ch'u Tei Zao and Goboro the Lame in Kozakuran Year 1215 (1141 DR) , which resulted in the mountain's peak being ravaged by their magical clash.
