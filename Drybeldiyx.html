Drybeldiyx was a dragonborn inhabitant of the Lower City on Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Drybeldiyx had green scales and flaming red eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Drybeldiyx was worried over the failing sources of drinking water across the Lower City. Like other citizens, the dragonborn thought the Upper City cared naught for the common folk. Drybeldiyx mused over switching to drinking ale if the drinking water becomes unavailable during the crisis.
