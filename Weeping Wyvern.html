The Weeping Wyvern was an inn in the city of Tantras in the Vast .

Location

The Weeping Wyvern lay close by the eastern wall of Tantras, at the end of Stumble Street.

Description

It was a large, good-quality establishment, with high prices but often crowded.

History

The Weeping Wyvern was still fairly new in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR . During the Time of Troubles of that year, it avoided being commandeered by the Guard of Tantras for use as an army barracks.
