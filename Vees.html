Vees was a firesoul genasi guard in Neverwinter .

History

Vees was always in the employ of Sala Nidris and followed her and her son Zan Nidris to Neverwinter.

In 1479 DR , however, a gang of kidnappers attacked the Nidrises' house and kidnapped Zan, despite Vees' resistance. Vees survived with only a few injuries and told about the attack to the adventurers Sala hired to to find her son.
