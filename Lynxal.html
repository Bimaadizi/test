Lynxal was the thief for the Company of the Bright Spear in the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

He was thin with short blond hair and a ratlike face.

Possessions

He had a thin knife hidden behind his neck. He rode a horse .

History

In Mirtul 6 of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , he was slain by Ghondarrath after trying to pickpocket him at the Rising Moon .
