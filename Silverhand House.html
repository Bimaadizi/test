Silverhand House was a trading coster in Highmoon in the late 14 th century DR . It was founded and owned by the retired adventurer Gaelin Silverhand just at the turn of the century. This company specialized in exporting rare woods, amber, resin and small-mammal furs along the East Way to the kingdoms of Cormyr , via the metropolis Arabel , and Sembia via its then-capital city of Ordulin .
