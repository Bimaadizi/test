Garm was the champion of a troglodyte tribe living on a small isle off the Sword Coast .

History

Around 1370 DR , an adventurer onboard the Midnight Rose ship was captured by the tribe and forced to fight Garm. If the adventurer won, Garm would follow them until the adventurer decided to kill and eat Garm.
