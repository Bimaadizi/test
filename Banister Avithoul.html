Banister Avithoul was a drow House Avithoul slavemaster of Sschindylryn during the 1480s DR .

Description

Banister had white tattoos and his hair tied back. He wore a chain shirt .

Abilities

Banister could cast disintegrate , Otiluke's freezing sphere , Otto's irresistible dance , power word stun , power word blind , and symbol of weakness .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Banister worked his craft at the defenses outside Sschindylryn, overseeing operations near the Boneyard . He kept a large contingent of guards and slaves on hand.
