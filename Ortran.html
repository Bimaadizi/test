Ortran was an apprentice of Alarashan Tlor in the mid-3 rd century DR .

Abilities

The man was inept at sorcery.

Relationships

He was an apprentice to Alarashan Tlor, one of the Magelords of Athalantar .

History

Ortran had been forced as an apprentice on Alarashan Tlor at some point prior to the Year of the Chosen, 240 DR . On that year, Ortran was slain in the jakes of Athalgard during the siege of the castle by the forces coordinated under Prince Elminster Aumar .

Rumors & Legends

Alarashan believed him to be a spy for Undarl Dragonrider .
