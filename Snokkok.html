Snokkok was a small village in the Alpuk region, in the Novularond , within the Great Glacier .

Geography

Snokkok was located in the northern half of the Novularond.

Inhabitants

The residents of Snokkok relished combat and hunting, making them an oddity among Innugaakalikurit . They were often hired on as mercenaries.
