The Smiling Company was a band of adventurers that operated out of Yartar during the late 15 th century DR . They were fairly successful, having journeyed and quested together for nearly a decade.

Organization

The company was part of a larger group of warriors, though other members had since ceased their adventuring ways.

Activities

Every year the Smiling Company made notable contributions to the Tymoran temple in Yartar, the Happy Hall of Fortuitous Happenstance .

History

The Smiling Company formed sometime around the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR . Unlike many Yartarran adventuring groups that were formed every summer, they remained active and prosperous for many years.
