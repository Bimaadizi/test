Ukolo was an Azuposi settlement in the Pasocada Basin in Maztica .

Activities

Despite its small size (its population was about 300), it was nonetheless important, for it was the center of Azuposi turquoise mining. Raw turquoise was exported from Ukolo to other, larger settlements were it was carved into jewelry and such.
