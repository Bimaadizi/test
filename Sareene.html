Sareene was a Harper during the late 14 th century DR .

History

Sareene participated in a meeting alongside Brammagar and others in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , where they discussed their options regarding Vangerdahast 's coming scheme to trap and bind dragons to service. Sareene was skeptical of Brammagar's proposal; unknown to them, they were eavesdropped on by Narnra Shalace .
