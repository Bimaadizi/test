Firemane's Fury was a powerful enchanted heavy crossbow created in honor of Nobanion , Lord Firemane sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

It was believed that this crossbow sent forts flaming bolts directly from Nobanion's flaming mane. The weapon itself possessed a potent +3 enchantment that made the bolts trike true and increased their damage upon collision. Additionally, the crossbow had an unlimited supply of magical bolts of fire that it fired into the targets.

History

In the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Firemane's Fury belonged to Petyr of Nobanion, a traveling cleric , who received a vision from his deity and followed it to Icewind Dale and the strife the regions experienced during that time.
