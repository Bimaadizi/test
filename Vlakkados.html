Vlakkados was a cleric and one of the seven High Hands in the Risen Cult of Bane in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

Vlakkados was a tall and thin man with a soft voice.

Personality

He was known to have a gentle demeanor.

Abilities

Vlakkados was skilled in the art of torturing and interrogating individuals.

Activities

Vlakkados often passed himself off to others as destitute beggar or a pilgrim in service to another deity , typically Ilmater , whenever he was instructed by the cult to go somewhere that worshipers of Bane were not welcomed..
