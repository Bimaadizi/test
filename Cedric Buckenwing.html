History

Cedric was a buddy of Jasper and other young men in Ghars in the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR . In Eleint , they got drunk on cheap beer and Cedric challenged Jasper to steal something from the retired War Wizard Benelaius . He agreed and they all rode out together to his cottage. Jasper was caught and later reflected that real friends wouldn't've let him be so stupid.
