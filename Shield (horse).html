Shield was a white horse ridden by Shandril Shessair on her journey away from Shadowdale in 1357 DR .

Description

Shield had a white coat.

Personality

It snorted and shook its head while Florin Falconhand talked to Shandril Shessair .

Relationships

Florin was aware of its name before Shandril rode it.

History

In the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , when Shandril Shessair parted ways with the Knights of Myth Drannor for the last time, right before heading to the Rising Moon , she was riding a horse whose name was known to Florin Falconhand.

The horse accompanied her until her encounter with Shargrailar , where it was slain by the dracolich 's breath weapon, on Flamerule 9 .
