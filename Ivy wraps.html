Ivy wraps were a pair of magical handwraps found in the Druid's Deep of Thorngap in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The handwraps were made of strips of heavy leather and tied with knotted thorny vines. The viciously spiked vines seemed to brighten and gain vitality when they tasted blood.

Powers

The thorns on the ivy wraps were savage and caused deep hemorrhaging wounds, as well as draining blood and healing the user in a vampiric manner. The thorns oozed virulent poisons and toxins, even causing acidic secondary reactions that further burned the recipient.

History

The handwraps were found in the Druid's Deep of Thorngap when adventurers pursued the insane druid Halsaime . They were in the possession of the bog hag Aghilde , who lurked nearby.
