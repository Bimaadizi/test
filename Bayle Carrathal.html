Bayle Carrathal was a High King of Callidyrr in the early-to-mid–11 th century DR . Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles , he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty of Callidyrr and was the sole heir of his father and predecessor, Alec Carrathal III .

History

Bayle assumed the throne in the Year of Warlords, 1030 DR , after his father died in a fire.

In the Year of the Frozen Kingdoms, 1052 DR , Bayle sent a small fleet to annex the islands of Jannath's Tears into his kingdom. However, Baldur's Gate came to the islands' defense and sank his ships. Due to this humiliation, Bayle was deposed and killed that same year. He was succeeded by his father's grandniece, Yolanda Carrathal .
