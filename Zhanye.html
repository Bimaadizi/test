The Zhanye family of Athkatla was a wealthy and an influential Amnian house in the 14 th century DR .

History

Zhanye was renowned for its gem merchant business, and took on apprentices from outside of the family. One of them was Jared Blevyn , who traveled and purchased diamonds on behalf of House Zhanye before parting ways with the family. By the late 14 th century DR , Jared had built his own map-making business Blevynsoar House using the knowledge he'd acquired from his busy travels for the Zhanyes.
