Rivrel was an apprentice of Maerjanthra Shalace in the mid–14 th century DR .

Personality

Rivrel almost never spoke.

Relationships

In Narnra Shalace 's recollection, her mother, Maerjanthra Shalace , had five apprentices: Goraun , Rorgel , Jonczer , Tantheld , and Rivrel. She learned that at least one of them believed her mother to be a literal dragon . Other than Rivrel, she could not confirm that any of them were dead, knowing only they disappeared after her mother's death.
