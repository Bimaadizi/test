Jamila al-Muhib was the Chief Vizier and mentor of Caliph Amel al-Yuhami in Fahhas in the Pantheist League of interior Zakhara .

History

Jamila was responsible for bringing Amel from the clergy into the ranks of political power. Jamila died of a fever in 1362 DR . Her death had a deep impact on the caliph who cherished her friendship and advice.

Abilities

In addition to being the caliph's vizier, Jamila was a skilled sea mage .
