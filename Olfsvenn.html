Olfsvenn was a Northlander prince of Norland in the Moonshae Isles in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR .

Relationships

Olfsvenn's grandfather was Jarl Rault the Wise . He was the son of Olfgaut , the Jarl's son.
