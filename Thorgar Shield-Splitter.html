Thorgar Shield-Splitter was a dwarven noble of Tethyamar before its destruction, who returned as a restless decaying skeleton ].

Description

Even as a skeleton, Thorgar had a thick reddish-brown beard. He was equipped with a suit of plate mail , a shield , and a dwarven war axe .

History

Thorgar's remains wandered Ironvale Deep , until the dwarves of the Tethyamar mining outpost and the drow of House Argith came searching for the Tethyamar Master Manifest in the 15 th century DR . He was likely destroyed by these forces as they converged on the library.
