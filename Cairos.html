Funglesby was a fighter -thug and an Envoy of Stone Lord , who opposed the The Guild crime gang of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Funglesby had a crimson skin tone of a tiefling with the taint of Asmodeus running through his veins. He had shortly-cropped brown hair and black horns. Funglesby worse a set of heavy armor and was armed with an enchanted hammer â€“ Corpsegrinder .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Farlin led a gang of Guild members in dealing with the Stone Lord and his men. The new kingpin secretly served the Absolute and was trying to remove the Guild's influence from across the Gate. Farlin's group faced a band of the Stone Lord's men led by Cairos, who served as Stone Lord's envoy. In his foul attitude and arrogance, Funglesby would not believe that Minsc of Rashemen was Stone Lord even if he confronted the tiefling directly. The groups clashed under the Wyrm's Crossing bridge in Rivington over a shipment of supplies. However, the meeting devolved into a battle and either of squads's fate remained uncertain.
