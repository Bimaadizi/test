OccupationWizard
Died1246 DR or before, slain by the Simbul

Ammarask was a mage who challenged the Simbul prior to 1246 DR . She won, and Ammarask perished.

Legends & Rumors

In 1262 DR , Elminster Aumar judged that the Simbul 's slaying of mages, whether from Thay or not, had become excessive, and the feats of her power were tilting the balance of power around Aglarond . Amongst other mages slain, he counted the spellhunter Ammarask.
