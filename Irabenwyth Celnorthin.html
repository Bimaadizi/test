Irabenwyth Celnorthin was an elf wizard in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Irabenwyth had messy white hair and wore an elaborate white and tan robe .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR during the Elven Crusade , Irabenwyth Celnorthin joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp on the outskirts of Myth Drannor . He served as a trainer for aspiring wizards who passed through the camp.
