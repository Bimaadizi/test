The Flass were an old noble family from Thay .

Activities

As members of the nobility, the Flass family were among the rulers of Thay, and counted many prominent Red Wizards and at least one Tharchion among their name. As Thayvian nobles, even members without positions of formal power were referred to with the honorifics of "Daeron"/"Daeronness" (literally, "noble-born"), or with "Sir"/"Mistress", "Your Excellency", or "Your Honor".

Members of the family shaved their heads and wore tattoos or body paint, as per Thayvian custom.

History

The Flass were an ancient Mulan noble family from Mulhorand . They were among the most influential Thayvian families as of the mid–14 th century DR , however they were clearly in decline by that time. A dispute between the family and the young Dmitra Flass in the mid-to-early 15 th century DR had led the noblewoman to distance herself from the family, and although the argument itself was nearly forgotten by the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , she still preferred to use titles or pseudonyms rather than her family name.
