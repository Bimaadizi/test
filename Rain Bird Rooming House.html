Rain Bird Rooming House was an inn in Waterdeep during the late 14 th century DR .

Interior

One of the doors in the kitchens had a peeling figure of a wizard, painted blue. This door was actually a portal of sorts.

History

Phaerorn then explained to his scullery boy, Naviskurr , that the man was actually Elminster and that was why nothing should block that door; Naviskurr fainted after Elminster left.
