Malyanna was a noble eladrin and a follower of the Abolethic Sovereignty . She wished to open a portal to the Far Realm in order to unleash god-like elder evils upon Toril .

Description

Malyanna had slender limbs and a graceful pose. Her white skin glowed like moonlight and her eyes were black. Her hair was dark blue-black and she had pointed ears.

Abilities

Malyanna could employ influence of Acamar , or a stronger yet similar spell.
