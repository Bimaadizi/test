Lord Dheolur was the head of the Dheolur noble house of Cormyr in the 5 th century DR .

Relationships

He married Threena Cormaeril . His sister was Pella Dheolur .

Description

He wore a suit of black chased and fluted plate armor with a helm, from Chondath , and bore a long slightly curved blade.

History

In the Year of the Sea Princes, 432 DR , Dheolur was killed by King Duar Obarskyr after his house had defected against Cormyr to side with Magrath the Minotaur . Duar severed Dheolur's head from his body with the sword Orbyn .
