Langreth Ironhorn was a steward of King Foril Obarskyr I of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

He was gruff.

Relationships

The sight of the ghost of Princess Alusair Obarskyr only made him chuckle, strongly implying he had seen it before.

Abilities

He knew first aid, most likely.

Appearance

He was a very old, tall and girthy man, who carried himself around in two sticks.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Langreth was part of a meeting called by King Foril Obarskyr I about the toxic smoke in the Hall of Justice , where the Council of the Dragon was supposed to have been held that day; at least one War Wizard had to have been a traitor, to teleport inside the materials to create the poisonous vapors. During the converse, Queen Alusair Obarskyr entered, causing a fellow in their converse, the Lady of Graces Jalessa Windstone , to faint; King Foril called on Langreth to revive her.
