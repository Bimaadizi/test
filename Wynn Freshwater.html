Wynn Freshwater was a street vendor from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Wynn was a high elven woman with fair sun-kissed skin, blue eyes, and straight red hair. While working, Wynn wore a simple white blouse and a brown leather corset .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Wynn Freshwater could often be found manning her produce stall in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, on a moot near The Glitter Gala jewelry shop. Unlike many fellow citizens of the Gate, Wynn thought articles published by the Baldur's Mouth to be nothing but fluff pieces penned to uplift the new Lord Gortash . She did not approve of the new army of steel watchers , thinking them a menace.
