Doomwarding was a weapon quality that could be applied to magic weapons that warded off fate.

Powers

As new, it usually had seven charges, one of which could be used by the wielder to give a very brief burst of speed in which to act or to improve their luck in one action.

Creation

Applying the doomwarding quality required the limited wish spell.

History

Doomwarding weapons were occasionally gifted to adventurers who won the favor of the church of Tymora .
