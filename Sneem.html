Sneem was a goblin sharp-eye that pledged his allegiance to the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Sneem had hay-colored hair he gathered in a ponytail, and his face bore smudges of war paint. The goblin wore roughly-stitched leathers and was armed with a goblin shortbow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Sneem became indoctrinated into the worship of the Absolute , and he joined the cultist army led by the drow paladin named Minthara Baenre . Along with other humanoids of the army, Sneem stayed in the ruined temple of Selûne in the Western Heartlands and later participated in an attack on the nearby Emerald Grove that sheltered a group of tiefling refugees from Elturel . The outcome of the conflict and Sneem's fate remained unrecorded.
