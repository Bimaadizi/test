Garrathmaw , also known as Incisor and Insyzor , was a fang dragon dracolich in the Greypeak Mountains and the River Loagrann .

Activities

The Incisor's hoard was very wealthy with many dwarven treasures from Ammarindar and countless Netherese magic items.

History

Around 1370 DR , Incisor preyed over the Fallen Lands and the Greyvale .
