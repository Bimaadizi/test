Aerel Hassammar was the owner and operator of the Swords Meet inn and tavern located in Voonlar , circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Description

Aerel was a burly man, often described as a " wolf of a man with an eagle eye."

Personality

Aerel was a fair man, but relentlessly drove his staff to be fast, efficient, silent, and unseen. On the other hand, he also encouraged them to be "as friendly as guests want them to be". He did not have much of a sense of humor.

History

In the winter of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Voonlar fell undead a sudden fiendish attack by the Order of the Crippled Fang . As the lower plane denizens started during into the city, Aerel Hassammar barred the doors and windows of his establishment to keep everyone safe. Nevertheless, a vrock broke through and would've slaughtered everyone inside if it was not for a knight named Astar Graysteel .
