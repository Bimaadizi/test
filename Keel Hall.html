The Keel Hall was a festhall located in the middle level of Skullport during the 14 th century DR .

History

The hall was closed down after the Spellplague struck Toril in the Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR , and never reopened its doors. In the following years, the abandoned building was used as a hideout by members of the Zhentarim .
