The Mage's Friend was a magic ring that once belonged to L. De Hurst of the Arcane Tower in the Underdark .

Description

After years of being stored away and disused, the ring became covered in dust and cobwebs.

Powers

Befitting its original owner, a cleric of Mystra , the Mage's Friend granted its wearer insights into arcane knowledge and religious lore.

History

De Hurst left her ring in a chest in her tower when she left her underground abode for Baldur's Gate in the Year of the Grinning Halfling, 1481 DR .
