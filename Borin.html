Borin was a trapper from the town of Mossbridges in the Vast , who ran a modest trapping business and traded with the Merchant House alongside his brothers in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Like his siblings, Borin had short-cropped brown hair and brown eyes.

Relationships

Borin had two brothers, Golin , and the youngest among the three, Chad . The brothers were acquainted with the retired half-orc fighter named Chester Hearth , who frequented the Golden Goblet Inn in Mossbridges. They considered the fighter to be a talented trapper and thought him to be a fine fellow despite being a half-orc. At one point, Borin and his siblings' dog was denied breeding with Chester Hearth's powerful moorhound .

History

When the Golden Goblet Inn was opened in the late 14 th century DR, after the war in the Vast, Borin and his brothers became frequent patrons of Georgio 's fine establishment. The family even celebrated Chad's 20 th birthday at the Goblet.
