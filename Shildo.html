Shildo was a thug of Hastarl in the early 3 rd century DR .

Description

The burly man was bald.

Activities

Shildo was a street thug.

Possessions

He wore leather armor and was armed with a sap.

Relationships

He carried out the orders of Undarl Dragonrider .

History

In Year of the Loremasters, 228 DR after Prince Elminster Aumar made for Hastarl , trying to get closer to the Magelords of Athalantar he meant to slay, he came at one point into a blind alley, which Shildo's men followed him into. He was saved from harm by the intervention of fellow thief Farl , who told Shildo Antaerl had directed Farl to warn Shildo that he appeared to be about to snatch a man already involved in Neldryn Hawklyn 's experiments; Farl also reminded him that Undarl Dragonrider promised him consequences if he snatched the wrong people. Shildo backed off, all but confirming he served under Undarl Dragonrider.
