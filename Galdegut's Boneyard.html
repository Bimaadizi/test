Galdegut's Boneyard was a glue factory, workshop and shop in Lannort in the 15 th century DR .

Services

The Boneyard sourced a wide variety of bones thanks to Morluth Galdegut 's network of shady suppliers. These bones were worked into various alchemical ingredients, melted into glue , or sold wholesale for other purposes. It was linked by merchants' map to Samril's Fine Blades in Secomber , who used bone (particularly the more durable bones of perytons , owlbears , dire wolves , and fire lizards ) to carve pommels for weapons such longswords .
