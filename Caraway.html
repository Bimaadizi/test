Caraway was a flowering plant that produced a common spice seasoning, caraway seed.

Availability

Caraway seeds were sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for two silver pieces per ounce.

Caraway seeds were used for cooking in Cursrah of the Calim Empire .

Usage

Caraway-distilled alcohol was a potent drink sometimes carried by travelers the Great Glacier .
