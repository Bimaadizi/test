Igan was a squire who fought for Damara in Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR .

Description

Igan was a gangly youth, with a face scarred by pox.

Personality

Igan idolized King Gareth Dragonsbane . He was also very curious and eager to fight for the King.

Possessions

His horse was a spotted, male destrier called Rain.

History

He was in the King's company fighting near Ostrav on Mirtul 3 . While there, he discovered the treachery of Sergor Marsk , but later was put out of the fight by a telekinesis spell from a fang dragon whil trying to protect the King. He was part of the incursion into the Plane of Shadow of Kythorn 26 to rescue Gareth Dragonsbane 's soul, where he fought shadow dragons .

Igan was slain by an arrow while rescuing Queen Christine Dragonsbane in the fight against the Vaasan horde of Flamerule 11 .
