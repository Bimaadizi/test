Nightstalker , or Night Stalker , was a magical longsword .

Description

Nightstalker was a longsword with a golden guard and pommel, as well as a bright red hilt. The sword could be found in a secret compartment among the drow outcasts, a couple of floors above Xanathar 's outer sanctum. It had a +3 enchantment.
