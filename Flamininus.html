Flamininus was a Netherese priest who served as an Underchief at Constabulary Branch One in Eileanar before its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Flamininus aided Chief Constable Zacharia at the constabulary and organized the constables of the branch alongside Chantonay and Chanteclaire .
