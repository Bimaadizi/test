Dragonsyr House was a mansion located in the Temple District of Ravens Bluff during the 14 th century DR . It could be found on the corner of Waelstar Way and Griffon Trail within the neighborhood of Swordspoint .

The Dragonsyr was a magnificent mansion of polished green marble. Its face featured columns that were carved to resemble rampant dragons of traditional heraldry.

History

Originally commissioned by the pirate, Murardo the Black , the Dragonsyr Houses originally served as a residence for adventurers that were fortunate enough to live into retirement. By the year 1370 DR , It was owned by the swashbuckling Marisalea Cat .
