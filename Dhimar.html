Dhimar was a worshiper and former high priest of Myrkul in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Abilities

While he had once been powerful, Dhimar received no spells from his dead god.

Activities

Dhimar oversaw the Temple of Myrkul in the Thayan city of Bezantur , and continued to maintain it long after Myrkul's death during the Time of Troubles of 1358 DR . He refused to convert to the worship of Cyric or Kelemvor , and instead clung to the god for whom he had once served as a high priest.
