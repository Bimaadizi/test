Shaele Flamehair was the mercenary commander of Shaele's Shields that operated out of Vurlakh in Murghôm in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Shaele was the most notorious mercenary of Murghôm, known for her beauty, calm yet reckless and bold attitude, and bright orange mane.

History

By the late 15 th century DR, Shaele, like many of her fellow mercenary commanders, left the armed forces of Murghôm as the army was reduced by decrees from Princes of Murghôm . Once a military commander, later a leader of the most expensive, prosperous, and busiest mercenary bands of her country.
