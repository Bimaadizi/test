Shatterstone was the divine realm of Vaprak , a patron of ogres and trolls in the Giant pantheon , prior to the Spellplague .

Cosmography

In the Great Wheel cosmology , this realm was located in Abyss , in the 524 th layer . While in the World Tree cosmology , Shatterstone was situated in Jotunheim alongside the divine realms of the other members of the Giant pantheon.

Description

In the World Tree cosmology, Shatterstone was said to be a pitiful cave at the base of a great cliff.

Inhabitants

This divine realm was primarily inhabited by Vaprak's petitioners . They were greatly despised by all the other giant inhabitants of Jotunheim and hid from them at all cost.
