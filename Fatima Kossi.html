Fatima Kossi was a halfling who lived in the Pilgrims' District of Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

Fatima owned the Wanderer's Rest , catering to pilgrims visiting Huzuz. She often let true pilgrims stay for free if they could not afford her rates.

Fatima was a devout follower of Kor . She felt like she wasted much of her life in pursuit of money when she could have spent that time productively as a priestess.

History

Though long retired, Fatima used to be an adventurer.
