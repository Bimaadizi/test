Fi Lendicol was a silver dragon in the Stonelands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Fi Lendicol opposed the schemes of the red dragon Grinnsira in the Stonelands , who was trying to raise an army out of the local goblinoids. Thus, she actively thwarted raids along the border of Cormyr . She often took human disguise, pretending to be a wizard.

Relationships

She had as constant companions the human ranger Dreik Lorne and the dwarven warrior Thurn .

History

As of the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , the mage Maris Khorna Terrabin had spoken to Fi Lendicol at length in the Stonelands; she mentioned that in a report for Elminster.
