Tinderstrike was a unique flint dagger forged by the drow wizard Vizeran De Vir , and wielded by Vanifer , the Prophet of Fire and leader of the Cult of the Eternal Flame , in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Tinderstrike was a flint dagger with an exceptionally sharp blade. Whenever it struck a hard surface, sparks flew from its edge, and after hurting someone with it, the blade smouldered for a few minutes. The handle felt warm at all times.

Powers

Whoever used Tinderstrike became impatient and reckless.

History

Vizeran De Vir , a drow worshiper of the Elder Elemental Eye , forged the dagger Tinderstrike using part of the essence of Imix , Prince of Evil Fire, in a temple known as the Fane of the Eye . He left the dagger to await its prophesied bearer.

Sometime in the Year of the Scarlet Witch, 1491 DR , Vanifer found the dagger.
