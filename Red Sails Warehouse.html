The Red Sails Warehouse was a public storage warehouse in Waterdeep .

Location

This warehouse was located along Cod Lane , in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This warehouse was two stories tall.

Services

This warehouse offered rental space, no questions asked, within longboxes that were roughly the size of a coffin. They also offered storage in the form of empty cubicles, which were up to eight longboxes tall. They only required that stored material wasn't flammable or alive.

The cost of a longbox was a single copper piece per day. If cooling was needed in a cubicle, clients could pay an extra two copper pieces per day per longbox for the space to have ice and be watched.

Reputation

The Red Sails was a commonly used rental space in the Dock Ward.
