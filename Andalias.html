Andalias was a man who ran a currency -conversion business in Zhentil Keep around 1369 DR .

Personality

Andalias was noted as being mathematically gifted, methodical, and precise. He often thought to think steps ahead of his opponents.

Possessions

Andalias consistently had around 75,000 gp worth of coins inside his store. He also possessed a magic ring , gifted to him by an archmage friend, that controlled two clay golems named Bar and Ingot. He utilized these two as security for his store.

Activities

Andalias ran the store The Dancing Coin in Zhentil Keep.
