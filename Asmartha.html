Asmartha was the innkeeper at Myrkiel's Rest in Narthil during the mid-3 rd century DR .

Description

She was a stout woman.

Activities

Asmartha was the innkeeper at Myrkiel's Rest , an inn in Narthil .

History

At some point between Marpenoth of the Year of Much Ale, 233 DR and Flamerule of the Year of Bloodflowers, 234 DR , Asmartha had the priestess Elmara as a guest. While she was eating at her establishment, she was accosted by agents of the Magelords of Athalantar , who died in combat with her.

Asmartha then witnessed Elmara regenerating from her injuries, which she attributed to a divine miracle.
