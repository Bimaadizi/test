Viperfang was a unique magic weapon located in Waterdeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Description

The weapon had a black curved blade that was serrated. The dagger weighed 4 pounds (1.8 kilograms).

Powers

Viperfang functioned as a dagger of venom but also carried the corrosive weapon quality . Its enchantment also made it easier for the wielder to strike and harm foes during combat.

History

Viperfang was created by Hadrhune specifically for Alauneth the "Black Viper" Orrane who wielded it as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
