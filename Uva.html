Uva was a Black Gauntlet of Bane , who served the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Uva was a pale-skinned human woman who wore her decorated Banite Black Gauntlet armor that included a breastplate , pauldrons decorated with a hellish face, and a large and intimidating helmet , decorated with a pair of metal tusks and a big image of Bane's black hand. Uva was armed with an infernal mace .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Cult of Bane joined forces with the Cult of the Absolute , led by the Chosen of Bane, Lord Enver Gortash . Black Gauntlet Uva was a part of the Cult's operations that targeted the countless refugees that flooded Baldur's Gate. Her branch of the Cult assembled donations and toys rigged with blackpowder explosives in the Felogyr's Fireworks shop's third floor. Presumably, Black Gauntlet Uva perished during or shortly before the Cult attacked Baldur's Gate.
