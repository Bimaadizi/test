History

Hugo Hobbs perished sometime before the fall of Reithwin and it being consumed by the Shadow-cursed Lands before the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR . He was buried in Reithwin's graveyard behind the House of Healing . His tombstone read: "Dedicated to Hugo Hobbs, cobbler. Hobbs the Cobbs to his friends." Hugo's grave marker remained undamaged as of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
