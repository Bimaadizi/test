The Way of the Manticore was a long road that connected the nations of Cormyr and Sembia .

The westernmost point of the Way of the Manticore was located at the town of Hilp , where it met Calantar's Way . The easternmost part of the Way of the Manticore was the city of Selgaunt in Sembia.
