Farid was a wealthy and influential Zakharan merchant inhabiting Hiyal around 1367 DR .

Activities

Farid encountered an Al-Badia named Jamal al-Karim who he believed insulted him. Farid ended up killing Jamal and incurring the wrath of Jamal's tribe, the House of Al-Karim , in the form of a blood feud.

Relationships

Farid adoptive dwarven son was Umar bin Farid .
