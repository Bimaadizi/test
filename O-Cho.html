O-Cho was the handmaiden of Sumi-ko in Nakamaru around 1357 DR .

History

O-Cho started working for the Funada clan in 1347 DR .

O-Cho always supported her master, even during her illicit romance with Nagoya Kenko . However, she noticed that Kenko did not love Sumi-ko, so O-Cho told Funada Genzo about the matter and asking for help.

Personality

O-Cho considered Sumi-ko to be like a little sister in need of protection and guidance. During family difficulties, she often secretly helped Sumi-ko from behind the scenes. Her devotion was noticed and she was considered a good and loyal servant.
