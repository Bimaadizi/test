Thilzoun was a small town located in the nation of Halruaa during the 14 th century DR .

Geography

Thilzoun was located on the plains of western Halruaa. The town sat on the east banks of a northern tributary of the River Halar in the foothills of the Nathaghal Mountains south of Mount Talath .
