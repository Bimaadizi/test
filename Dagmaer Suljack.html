Dagmaer Suljack was a High Captain of Luskan in the 15 th century DR .

Activities

As the Third High Captain of Luskan , Dagmaer was responsible for a large number of pirate raids.

History

Dagmaer lived in Luskan, and by 1489 DR , was the captain of Ship Suljack .
