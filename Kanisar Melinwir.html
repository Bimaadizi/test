Kanisar Melinwir was a Captain who served in the region of the Vilhon Reach sometime in or before the 14 th century DR .

Activities

Kanisar made his opinion of the nation of Sespech known in a quote recorded by Adamar Session , Bard of Starmantle . Kanisar stated that there are only two things of importance to remember about Sespech, how close one's enemies are, and one's loyalty to Baron Thuragar Foesmasher .
