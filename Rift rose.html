OriginNeverwinter Chasm

Rift rose was a type of flowering plant, a variety of rose , found growing only in the Spellplagued Chasm of Neverwinter . Its flowers had a strangely beautiful appearance.

History

In the course of their duties defending Neverwinter from the Chasm, Private Olliver of Scar Company noticed that Private Mara Taliren was still despondent over becoming heavily spellscarred . Seeking to cheer her up a bit, Olliver had someone collect the local rift roses and present them "from an admirer." Shocked and confused by the unexpected gift, Mara was still pleased by the gesture.
