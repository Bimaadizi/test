Baron Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Baron Street was located in the Zhentil Keep's Harbor District in the south section of the city. The road was located on the eastern side of the Harbor District. It originated at the wall to the Common Quarter then passed west before ending at the docks.

Notable Locations

A Zhentarim warehouse and Tern's Shipwright Service were located along Baron Street.
