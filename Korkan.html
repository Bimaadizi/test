Korkan was a hobgoblin slave in Szith Morcane in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Korkan was the head slave in the noble house led by Rilira .

Circa 1486 DR , after the fire giants occupied the outpost, Korkan and all the goblinoids were seen as the lowest-ranked slaves. This greatly enraged Korkan.
