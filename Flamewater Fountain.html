The Flamewater Fountain was a popular landmark fountain in Athkatla 's Gem District .

Location

The Flamewater Fountain stood in the chaotic and resplendent westerly part of the Gem District, to the northwest of Jalasra Fourwynds 's manse in the 14 th century DR and to the north of the Shadowgates House .

History

By the late 14 th century DR, the Flamewater Fountain had become a beloved meeting place of the Athkatla's wealthy inhabitants. The plaza surrounding the fountain was often swarmed by perfume peddlers and makeup "touchover lasses."
