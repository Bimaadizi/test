Mashed potatoes was a simple dish of boiled potatoes found in the west and Northwest Faerûn .

Description

A typical mash was a playful of creamy paste flavored with black pepper and nutmeg and sprinkled with chopped chives or parsley , or drowned in brown gravy .

Availability

Mashed potatoes were known to be prepared and served in eateries of Baldur's Gate and roadside inns in the Western Heartlands , such as Waukeen's Rest . In the city of Neverwinter , mashed potatoes topped with gravy was served in lavish noble feats. In Hillsfar in the Moonsea region, potato mash was served in feasts with roast meat and sauerkraut . The dish's simplicity made it a staple in the messhalls of Ravens Deep , Ravens Bluff 's Underdark settlement outpost in the late 14 th century DR .
