The Caves of Ghazz were a series of caves on the island of Saishu , off the coast of Koryo in northeast Kara-Tur .

They were a legendary site but their exact location was unknown. It was said the mirror of perfection had been hidden there since around 607 DR .

The Caves of Ghazz were one of the local legends that Dan Zor , the former king and governor of Saishu, wished to investigate. By 1357 DR , he sought adventurers who would seek out the caves for him.
