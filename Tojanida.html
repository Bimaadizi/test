Tojanidas were snapping turtle -like creatures that originated in the Elemental Plane of Water . They swam in the coastal waters of the Great Sea and off the coast of Aglarond , as well as in Lake Halruaa and Lake Ashane .

Notable Tojanidas

A tojanida was recruited by Gimlet Watersprecht as a lookout for a portal linking Lantan and the Pirate Isles .
