Trenzia was one of Halaster 's new apprentices in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR .

History

At one point before 1492 DR , Trenzia was chosen by Halaster to be one of his new apprentices. At one point, to expand her scientific and necromantic research, she asked Halaster to transform her into a flameskull and Halaster agreed, also granting her a lair inside the Arcane Chambers .

Activities

Trenzia was busy with her experiments in necromancy and electricity using copper , and was assisted by a flesh golem .

Personality

Trenzia was driven mad by her researches and sacrificed her own body to construct a device that fused necromancy and electricity.

Relationship

Trenzia had become paranoid about the other apprentices of Halaster, believing that they couldn't understand her genius.
