Rhondang was a powerful magical warhammer and the chosen weapon of Flandal Steelskin .

Description

Rhondang was made of mithril and was coated with gold . Instead of a flat back, it had an axe -blade on its back.

Powers

This warhammer had a +5 enchantment. Creatures that made use of cold-based attacks or lived in cold places were vulnerable to this weapon . On the other hand, the weapon spoke the languages of every fire -using creature, such as fire elementals .

Flandal's favored weapon was able to launch a fireball every minute, as well as burst into a flame at will.
