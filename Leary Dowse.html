Leary Dowse was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Leary was a human woman with a fair complexion and short, dark, curly hair. She wore a simple dark jerkin and a button-down tunic, with an oversized hat.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Leary Dowse was attracted by a curbside preacher of Talona , Umbrenne , spewing out his beliefs of the cycle of life and death being carried out through pestilence. Leary thought his beliefs to be nothing but madness.
