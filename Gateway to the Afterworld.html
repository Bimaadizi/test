The Gateway to the Afterworld was one of the two most significant temples of Osiris in all of Mulhorand and his preeminent temple in the town of Mishtan .

Description

This temple resembled two concentric rings of towering pillars, so large that the rooms inside of them were interconnected through graceful arches.

Activities

The clergy of the Gateway administered the maintenance of the Land of the Dead —the burial grounds of the pharaohs and their families—and the construction of tombs there. Additionally, the temple's clergy acted as the governing body of Mishtan.

Inhabitants

The Gateway to the Afterworld acted as the base for the majority of the Osiran religious orders in Mulhorand.
