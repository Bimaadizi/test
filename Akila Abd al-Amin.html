Akila Abd al-Amin was a caravan guard captain from Hiyal in Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Description

Akila was a small, wiry woman.

Activities

She commanded a group of mamluk guards called the " Faithful ." The Faithful was made up of three 10-man platoons.
