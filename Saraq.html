Saraq was a small town found within interior Zakhara and the Cities of the Heart .

Geography

Saraq was found on the eastern coast of the South Quarter of Suq Bay along the small strait that connected the Golden Gulf . The town sat within a river valley from an unnamed river southwest of Huzuz , the City of Delights.
