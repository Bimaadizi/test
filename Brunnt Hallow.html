Brunnt Hallow was a reserved man from Reithwin , once a Selûnite town in the Western Heartlands , sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Brunnt Hallow died sometime before the fall of Reithwin and its consumption by the Shadow-cursed Lands , before the 1374 DR . Brunnt was buried in Reithwin's graveyard behind the House of Healing , at the hospital's northwestern walls. His tombstone read: "Here lies Brunnt Hallow, man of few words." The grave marker remained undamaged and legible as of the 1492 DR .
