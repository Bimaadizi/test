Flostren's Memory was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Flostren's Memory was located in central Zhentil Keep within the Inner Quarter . The street ran in between the Lord's Way in the north and the wall of the Harbor District in the south. Flostren's Memory was crossed by Gold Street and Elephstron Way .

Notable Locations

The residences of Lord Taradril Aumraven , Lord Naerh , and Lord Marsh Belwintle were located along Flostren's Memory. A Zhentilar guardpost sat at the intersection with Elephestron Way.
