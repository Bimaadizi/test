Appearance

Shex is as tall as an ogre and has wings and claws. Her body constantly decomposes, slime oozing down her frame to pool at her feet. She speaks with a slurred, muddy voice .

History

Together with the Nighthaunt Ysval , Shex was a field commander for an army of undead trying to conquer Thay in 1375 DR . She was slain by the ghost Mirror at the fight of Thazar Keep . Whether she was ultimately destroyed or just sent back to her home plane is not known.
