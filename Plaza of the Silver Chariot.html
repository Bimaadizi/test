The Plaza of the Silver Chariot was a city square located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The plaza was situated in the north central section of the Foundry . The plaza sat at the junction of where the Munhani Canal joined with the Naranj Canal on the southeastern border of the Pyraculum .
