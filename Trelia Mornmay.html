Trelia Mornmay was a human druid tending to the forest Shalhoond in the mid 14 th century DR .

Description

She had watched over the Shalhoond for more than 20 years, and was well known by its inhabitants.

Personality

She loved spending her time in the calm company of the animals of the forest, but would take forceful action to protect the balance of the woods.

Possessions

She wore a robe of blending , a ring of protection and a set of bracers of defense . She wielded a staff of thunder and lightning .
