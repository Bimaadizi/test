Jhentyr Campion II was the fourth King of the Campion Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Jhentyr II inherited the throne upon the death of his father, Tarnhart I , in the Year of the Slain Mountain, 993 DR . He ruled until the Year of the Heavenly Rock, 1002 DR .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Tarnhart II .
