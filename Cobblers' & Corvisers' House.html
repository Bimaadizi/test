The Cobblers' and Corvisers' House was the guildhall of a Waterdhavian guild of the same name .

Location

This guildhall was located along Soothsayer's Way , in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This guildhall stood two stories tall.

Services

This guildhall offered clothing services, specifically the selling or repairing of footwear of all forms.

Reputation

Like other guildhalls in Waterdeep, this building was considered by locals to be a landmark.
