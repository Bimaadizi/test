Rikka was a tiefling that lived at the Emerald Grove druidic enclave after being exiled along with all the other tieflings from Elturel in the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

While guarding the Emerald Grove, Rikka wore a suit of scale mail armor and armed herself with a longbow .

History

After the Descent of Elturel into Avernus in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Rikka and the exiled Elturian tieflings led by Zevlor traveled west towards the great metropolis of Baldur's Gate . They were granted temporary refuge at Emerald Grove for some time, due to increased goblin attacks in the region.

Rikka was forced to kill for the first time defending the grove. Even though she killed a goblin that would likely cause her or her companions harm, Rikka was quite troubled about the whole ordeal.
