The Keep of Sorrows was a granite fortress, surrounded by a double ring of walls, that was said to be impregnable.

Geography

It was located in Thay to the south of the River Lapendrar , right next to a cliff that encircled central Thay called the First Escarpment , situated within the Tharch of Lapendrar , north of the tripoint where the borders of Lapendrar, Priador , and Tyraturos converged.
