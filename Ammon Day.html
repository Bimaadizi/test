Ammon Day was an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate who did business with the Counting House in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Ammon Day experienced difficulty in meeting with Rakkath Glitterbeard of the Counting House face-to-face. Ammon submitted a request to schedule an appointment to discuss a request for an appointment with Rakath via mail. It was unknown whether Ammon was successful.
