Iphigea Evermist was a duergar from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Iphigea had a black skin, typical of most duergar, with white beard and white hair gathered in a ponytail. Iphigea wore a simple set of chainmail armor .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Iphigea Evermist, along with other duergar, giant spiders , and drow , was slaughtered in the home of Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica . The murder spree was performed by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal .
