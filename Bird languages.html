Bird languages , the language of birds , avian languages or simply Avian were all terms for referring collectively to the various forms of communication practiced by mundane and magical birds .

Description

This included such things as cries, songs, and particular postures.

Speakers

Across the multiverse , there existed a wide array of creatures that could understand and speak in the languages of all birds. These included the achaierai of Acheron , avariels , avian lycanthropes , phoenixes , ratatosks , simpathetics , simurghs , and space owls . Couatls were fluent in most, though not all, avian languages.
