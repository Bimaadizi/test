Tiralaylee Tai-Muran was an elven barbarian priestess active in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Tiralaylee's personality was somewhat similar to that of her home, the Savage Frontier . She was restless, aggressive, and preferred to fight rather than negotiate.

Possessions

Tiralaylee owned a suit of banded mail , an enchanted +2 spear , a ring of human influence , and a pair of boots of the north .

History

In the 14 th century DR, Tiralaylee Tai-Muran often led raiding parties of her barbarian tribe and was known for her keep aggressive negotiating skills.
