The Tesper family ( pronounced : /ˈ t ɛ s p ɜːr / TESS-pur ) was a Waterdhavian noble family.

Activities

This family hosted many gatherings and parties, and even allowed commoners and outlanders to join in the celebrations.

Base of Operations

The family owned a villa in the Sea Ward , on the northwest corner of Mendever Street and Delzorin Street .

History

The sisters of the Tesper family grew up alongside their elf friends, and were talented in the arcane arts. However, the family frowned upon their talents, and hired an assassin to kill the sisters. The assassin was successful, and the ghosts of the sisters haunted their family's residence from then on.
