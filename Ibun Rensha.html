Ibun Rensha was a member of the Rensha family of Calimshan who conquered Loudwater and much of the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid–12 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Scourge, 1150 DR , Ibun Rensha and several of his family led a mercenary army to northwest Faerûn . They took over Loudwater and laid claim to most of the Delimbiyr Vale, beginning a period known as the Rensha Rule.
