Rosepillars was an elegantly designed residence located in the Uptown District of Ravens Bluff during the 14 th century DR . The attractive mansion, which featured rose-colored marble columns on its face, served as the city home for the noble Skyhawk family.

The Rosepillars estate was located on the southeast corner of Bouldergrinder's Way and Turnhelm Street in the city's affluent Tentowers neighborhood.
