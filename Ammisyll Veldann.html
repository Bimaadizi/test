Lady Ammisyll Veldann was the sun elven governor of Nimlith on the island of Evermeet and a member of the Council of Evermeet .

Personality

Ammisyll had extremely conservative views and opposed the monarchy on Evermeet. During the daemonfey assault on elven settlements in Faerûn in 1374 DR , she and Selsharra Durothil threatened to withdraw the support of the houses of Durothil and Veldann if Queen Amlaruil Moonflower sent forces from Evermeet to aid the Faerûnian elves.

Relationships

Ammisyll was cousin to Tarthas .
