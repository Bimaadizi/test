Ogrefire Ale was a variety of expensive magical ale found in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The enchanted drink was on the menu at the Yawning Portal tavern of Waterdeep. One serving of Ogrefire Ale was priced at ten gold coins .
