Prince Anir al-Walad al-Hadi was a member of the royal family of the Free City of Qadib in north Zakhara in 1367Â DR .

Activities

Anir was a skilled faris and spent most of his time hawking and hunting. Contrary to most of Qadib's royalty, Anir was decidedly nonmagical. This greatly disturbed the court and they urged the aging Sultan to sire more children with the hopes that they would possess some skill with magic .

Relationships

Prince Anir was the sole heir of Sultan Kamal al-Hadi .
