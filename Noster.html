Noster was a disguise prepared by the apprentices of the Red Wizard Azmyrandyr in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Improvisation

The Red Wizard Azmyrandyr had one of his apprentices rehearse Noster in anticipation of a takeover of Nimpeth for which they planned to replace a number of important citizens, almost certainly implying Noster was one such notable.
