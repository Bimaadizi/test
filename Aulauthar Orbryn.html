Lord Aulauthar Orbryn was a mage of Cormanthyr working in the 3 rd century DR .

History

In 261 DR he helped raise the mythal of Myth Drannor : he attempted to contribute one of the minor powers of the effect. Unfortunately, he became the first victim of Uldreiyn Starym , who slew him and attempted to hide in the place where his consciousness was and thus he was unable to add powers to it.
