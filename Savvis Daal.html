Savvis Daal was a Thayan knight who served her nation within their enclave in the city of Proskur during the 14 th century DR .

Savvis was charged with overseeing the subterranean cellars that held the enclave's slaves. She was served by over a dozen enforcers and handful of monstrous guardians. If she so required, Savvis could call upon the wizards and clerics for aid in case of belligerent slaves or Proskurans who began investigating their trade.
