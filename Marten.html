Marten was a toll collector who lived and worked on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He had gray-white hair that he worn nealy brushed back.

History

Marten was killed in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , when goblin cultists of the Absolute raided the tollhouse at which he worked. Marten, Vance , and their fellows were abandoned by the tollhouse's guards. They attempted to flee and hide, but were ultimately cut down.
