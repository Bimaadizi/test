Huntileir was a dwarven weaponsmith in Tilverton in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Hunteleir was 3 feet (0.91 meters) tall and 3 feet wide. He possessed extraordinarily thick muscles, particularly the biceps. He had a thin white beard around the edge of his jaw.

Possessions

He always carried at least four throwing axes , which he was very proficient with.

Activities

Around 1358 DR , Huntileir ran a weapons shop in Tilverton, making and selling axes , swords , and a variety of other implements.
