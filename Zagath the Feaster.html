Zagath the Feaster was a hungry ghoul in Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

History

The ghoul lurked inside Tyrintar's Hall , along with many other undead like Shenlazeen and Aerich the Steward . It was encountered when Uhlsdrin Nirveth came looking for the Icon of the Dwarves in 1374 DR .
