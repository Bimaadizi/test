The shield of Valkur was a blessing sometimes bestowed by Valkur , the Captain of the Waves, upon loyal followers.

Powers

If blessed by the touch of Valkur with this power, the subject would be able to breathe under water, swim proficiently, and be immune to all physical harm from water elementals , water-based magic and attacks, and normal and magical electrical attacks. The shield of Valkur blessing lasted for a full day.
