Scavenge was a hungry otyugh that foraged in the the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

History

The fellow lived in the remains of the Dlabraddath neighborhood, looking for things to eat while the Elves of Cormanthor worked to reclaim the city and fought the Red Wizards of Thay .
