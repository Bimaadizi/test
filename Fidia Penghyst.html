Fidia Penghyst was a noblewoman from Baldur's Gate who died some years before the late 15 th century DR .

History

Fidia died sometime before the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR and was buried in the Lower City Graveyard , next to the Grand Mausoleum . Her gravestone praised the woman's lanceboard paying with a single inscription: "Fidia Penghyst - A Noble Lanceboard Player to the Last." Her grave may had been accidentally desecrated, and her body risen as a zombie by Nina Dortmell , a talented child with a gift , who was trying to resurrect her recently deceased brother.
