Vuzlyn Freth was a drow in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 th century DR and a member of House Freth . He was one of Houndaer Tuin'Tarl and Syrzan 's rebel group.

History

During the Silence of Lolth in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Vuzlyn joined Houndaer's group desiring to start a slave revolt. Vuzlyn and Alton Vandree were, among others, summoned to Pharaun Mizzrym and Ryld Argith , as might be some of the males they needed to find. He was presumed killed in the suppression of the revolt.
