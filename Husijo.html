Husijo was a villager of Fochu Peninsula in Wa .

Activities

Husijo managed a small shop in the Zuke village.

History

In 1358 DR , Husijo bought from a former member of the Blue Kumi Bandits some items of property of Toshi . Later, he met Osari Minhiro and a foreign adventuring party. Minhiro recognized his ex-wife's items.
