Ulaan was and a servant in the Fortress of the Old Man . She helped wash and clothe Lewan when he was brought in by Sauk after Tarsakh 16 of 1374 DR , and eventually became close with him.

Appearance

To Lewan, she was close to his age, maybe a bit older. She had slanted eyes, honey-wax-colored skin, and long raven-eye hair.

Powers

As one of the personal Blades of Lady Talieth, Ulaan was trained in fighting.

History

Ulaan was sold to Lady Talieth in Almorel around 1364 DR . Her mother had been a servant of a merchant trading along the Golden Way . Ulaan began to get closer to Lewan on the night of Tarsakh 19 , during the assassins' vigil; she feigned fear to get into his room, and then seduced him, on the orders of Lady Talieth, who wanted to motivate him to stay in Sentinelspire. She agreed to escape with Lewan during the confusion of Tarsakh 25 , and even assisted him in combat but was captured by Chereth , who nearly slew her.
