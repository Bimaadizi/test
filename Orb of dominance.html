Orbs of dominance , were wizardly orb implements that greatly boosted the wielder's magical prowess, popularized in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

Flaming orbs helped wizards to focus their arcane energies, increasing the destructive power of their spells . Additionally, these orbs quickened the wielder's reaction and allowed them to find vulnerabilities in the spell targets.

History

In the 15 th century DR, various orbs of dominance could be found among spellcasters of the Sword Mountains , areas surrounding the city and markets of Neverwinter . One such item was among Fletcher Thane 's possessions in the late 15 th century DR, another was in the undead hands of the vampire lord Ozit who laired in the ruined Illefarn .
