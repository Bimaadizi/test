The Hospice of St. Laupsenn was a temple of Ilmater in the North Ward of Waterdeep in the late 14 th and late 15 th centuries DR .

Location

The temple was located on the south side of Undal's Lane , between Black Cat Way and Andamaar's Street .

Description

The temple had bells that had a solemn sound and they could be used to mark the time.

History

In Marpenoth / Uktar of the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , the Ilmatari were among the Waterdhavian clergies who supported Prince Haedrak Rhindaun III 's claim to Tethyr . Some of the faithful joined his forces in the Reclamation Wars .

In the late 1400s DR, Gale Dekarios was treated for the ruddy pox at the Hospice of St. Laupsenn.
