The stars of day were holy Amaunatori sun blade bastard swords seen in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The stars of day were blessed with holy power that easily destroyed the forces of evil , and blinding light and burning heat that made them fearsome in any situation. Three stars of day were said to far greater than the rest, one of which was Celestia, Brightest Star of Day .

History

The servants of Amaunator brought a number of stars of day to Eveningstar to fight against the Darkening and the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar . These relics found their way into the hands of various heroes, whether from Faerûn or Eberron .
