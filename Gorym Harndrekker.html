Gorym Harndrekker was a battle priest of Tempus and the acting Priest-General of the Abbey of the Sword as of 1372 DR . Formerly a skilled fighter, his advancing age and an encounter with wraiths greatly diminished his physical prowess. He was best known as a master military strategist.
