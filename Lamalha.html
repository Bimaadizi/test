History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Lamalha and her allies were hired by the Iron Throne to kill Abdel Adrian . The woman ambushed the Bhaalspawn outside the Nashkel mine as he and his companions emerged from a shaft after confronting Mulahey , a servant of the Iron Throne. Presumably, Lamalha did not survive the ambush.
