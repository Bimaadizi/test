Traitor Picks was a tavern in the Central Port of Skullport in the late 14 th century DR .

Structure

Traitor Picks was a one-story building in poor condition.

Interior

The tavern was noted for its disreputable atmosphere and was typically overcrowded.

Services

Traitor Picks was regarded as one of Skullport's grimiest grog-and-grub establishments. Its food wass of poor quality and offered at above-average prices.

Inhabitants

Traitor Picks was owned by Denver Gilliam , who also operated the Worm's Gullet .

Traitor Picks experienced a brief period of notoriety when Gilliam recruited a well-regarded gourmet chef from Waterdeep . The chef, Percival Gallard Woodehouse , was hired to elevate the tavernâ€™s culinary reputation. However, following an unscheduled expedition into the Underdark in the company of the notorious Volothamp Geddarm , Woodehouse returned to discover that his employment had been terminated. Gilliam had voided his contract and liquidated his possessions to cover the cost of hiring a replacement cook.

Following Woodehouse's departure, Traitor Picks has seen a rapid turnover of kitchen staff, having employed at least five different cooks in succession. In response to this instability, Gilliam has since taken a more active role in managing the establishment.
