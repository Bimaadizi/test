Meldreathe Myral was the Sage of Selgaunt , active circa 1360 DR .

History

Meldreathe Myral was known for penning a collection of thoughts and deliberations on topics including the power of rumors, both innocent and malicious. The book titled One Unwise Woman's Musings was published in the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR .
