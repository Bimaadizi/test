An egg beater was a type of cooking utensil found in the Vast in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . It utilized a gear -driven mechanism to rotate a set of "beaters" to fulfill similar tasks as whisks (such as beating eggs ).

History

Sometime in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR, the cargo ship The Gentle Lady was delivering a shipment of farmer and merchant goods to a small coastal community in the Dragon Reach . Among the goods being carried by the ship was a crate full of cookware that included four egg beaters.
