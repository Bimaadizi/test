Myrcrest was a magical bird and servant of Vangerdahast , the Royal Magician of Cormyr , in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Although resembling a raven , Myrcrest was much larger, being about 50% wider and longer in the wings than a regular specimen. Its feet were pale and fleshy and ended in 3‑inch-long (7.6‑centimeter) talons, and its eyes glowed or reflected greenish-yellow light. Stranger still, its feathers had no sheen, instead seeming dull and even absorbing light. It could be quite intimidating to those unused to it.

Personality

Myrcrest was intelligent, but could only communicate via raven-like squawking and head bobs.

Activities

Myrcrest delivered messages for Vangerdahast. These it carried in a thick leather pouch tied around its neck.

History

On the evening of Eleint 18 in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , Myrcrest carried a vital message from Vangerdahast concerning the fate of the killer of the king's envoy Grodoveth to retired War Wizard Benelaius at his cottage . He and Lindavar received it well, but Jasper was frightened of it.
