History

Garnet allowed the local merchant Barthelm Meadowbrock to use the Silver Scythe to lodge attendees of the annual meeting of the Merchants' Guild in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR . Barthelm also paid for Garnet to add a grand meeting room onto the inn The guild members arrived on Eleint 19 with the meeting itself to be held the next day .
