Ebomara was a drow cleric of Lolth in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Ebomara used her charms to seduce human and demihuman males and either convert them to Lolth worship, or slay them.

Possessions

She wore a pair of special slippers of spider climbing , that could spray web twice a day from the toes. She also had a Book of Vile Darkness and a talisman of ultimate evil .
