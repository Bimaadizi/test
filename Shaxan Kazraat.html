Shaxan Kazraat was a Netherese mummy in Anauroch .

History

Shaxan was an ancient inhabitant of Netheril buried in a tomb in Anauroch. However, its tomb was occupied by the blue dragon sorcerer Iymrith , who put Shaxan's remains away in a remote room, thus earning its rage. Shaxan waited only for someone to open its sarcophagi so it could have its vengeance.
