Pandar was an infamous highwayman of Scardale .

History

Pandar made a name for himself by escaping the elven law in the Cormanthor forest. He used the woods to hide and escaped the elves' bows and blades by wearing the Elves' Bane girdle. He eventually died from pit traps and starvation, some time before the late 1360s DR .
