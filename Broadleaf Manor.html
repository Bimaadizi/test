Broadleaf Manor was the expansive mansion of the famed adventurer, Elvira Broadleaf , located in the Temple District of Ravens Bluff during the late 14 th century DR . It was located on Rhabie Promenade in the Swordspoint neighborhood, a short ways away from the Fiddlers' Green city park.
