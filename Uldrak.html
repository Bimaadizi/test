Uldrak was a former empyrean in Avernus , first layer of the Nine Hells , in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Once Uldrak went to Avernus to kill Tiamat and gain honor for his god Surtur . However, Tiamat defeated Uldrak and cursed him to be polymorphed into a spinagon . From then on, he plotted to regain his true form. At one point he discovered he needed some blood from Tiamat but until the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , it was impossible for him obtain a sample.

Personality

After millennia isolated, Uldrak had begun to lose his mind. he often argued with himself, even in front of strangers.
