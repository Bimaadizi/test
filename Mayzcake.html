Mayzcakes were flat but fluffy mayz bread staples of Maztican cuisine.

Description

Mayzcakes were made using a corse paste of crushed mayz and pepper , hand-patted and shaped into circles, then cooked over hearthfires. They were commonly served along with hot bubbling bean stew , venison , or even fruit. Most of these dishes could be taken on the road. The cakes were a simple recipe flavored with "some pepper," of course, the light flavor was mouth-scorching spiciness for those not accustomed to Maztican pallet.

Hot spiced cocoa was sometimes served with mayzcakes wrapped around cooked eggs .

History

In the Year of Maidens, 1361 DR , Erixitl , The Chosen Daughter of Qotal , was kindly invited to share a hearty meal of mayzcakes and stew with mother Tzilla . The lone woman packed a satchel of mayzcakes and stew for Erixitl's travel the following day as she departed Ulatos in attempt to stop the looming war between Ulatos and Amn .
