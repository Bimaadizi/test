The Lake of the Leech was a small freshwater lake located in northern Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Lake of the Leech touched the northern edge of Leechhome , the only swamp in Gnarhelm. The lake was located just a few miles from Gnarhelm's northern coast on the Sea of Swords . It covered roughly 50 mi² (130 km²).
