Spider Blood was a variety of wine exported out of the Underdark and found in Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Spider Blood was a quality dark red wine.

History

As of the mid-14 th century DR, Spider Blood could be purchased in limited quantities at The Underdark Café , a new establishment run by Vincent Berelegond in the city of Ravens Bluff. Spider Blood was offered to the patrons of the Café during its grand opening for the price of thee gold coins per serving.
