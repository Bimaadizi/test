Lord Gerrin Wyvernspur was a member of the Wyvernspur noble house of Cormyr .

Background

Gerrin fought against the red dragons in the attack on Suzail in 1018 DR , and subsequently pursued Thauglor , who badly burned him, although Thauglor was defeated.
