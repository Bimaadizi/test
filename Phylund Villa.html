Phylund Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Phylund .

Location

The villa was located in the west section of Waterdeep 's North Ward on the southeast of the intersection where Julthoon Street met Copper Street .

Description

The walled compound contained three buildings arranged around a central courtyard. The buildings were between two and three storys tall.
