Suther was a house guard for the noble Seasilver family of Marsember during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He was not particularly well-spoken, and became disquieted when confronted by his superiors.

Relationships

He served under the house guard Mannus , and their superior, Sergeant Carlton .

History

When the city of Marsember was invaded by the Shadovar , on Uktar 2 of the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Suther and Mannus failed to heed the warning of the servant Odelia , allowing two shades to infiltrate the bedchambers of Lady Arietta Seasilver . They were ordered to not allow the young noblewoman to leave the family's estate, but failed in their efforts.
