Outrider's Flight was a light crossbow created to be used by halfling cavalry in Faerûn .

Powers

The crossbow possessed a +2 enchantment and magically improved the wielder's dexterity.

History

Outrider's Flight was created sometime before the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR by halfling champions known as outriders . The elite hin cavalry was celebrated defenders of their kin, and Outrider's Flight was specifically designed for mounted use. Despite its diminutive appearance, the crossbow packed a wallop. In 1374 DR, Outrider's Flight found its way to the Eyegouger Approach and was in the hands of orcish archers there until it was liberated by Kalach-Cha and their companions.
