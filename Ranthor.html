Ranthor was a human wizard and master of Shal Bal who lived in Cormyr .

Description

Being a wizard, Ranthor liked to dress in fine clothes and keep his appearance in good order; many of his robes were blue, which was his signature color.

Personality

As a teacher, Ranthor was kind and giving, always providing his students with positive encouragement and motivation. When he was a young man, Ranthor loved to dance and was always surrounded by beautiful women.

Possessions

After his death, Ranthor left Shal all his personal items, including a staff of power , a cloth of many pockets , a ring of three wishes , a wand of wonder , and his familiar , Cerulean .

History

Around 1337 DR , Ranthor took Shal Bal as an apprentice, even though she had no gold nor demonstrated any magical gift. In Flamerule 24 , 1340 DR , Ranthor traveled to Phlan to aid a fellow wizard, Denlor .

Death

While communicating with Shal through a crystal ball , Ranthor was stabbed from behind by a dark dagger wielded by an unknown killer who wore a strange serpent-headed armlet.
