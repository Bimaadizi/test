The Razorhides were a gang of street thugs in Veltalar during the late 14 th century DR .

Organization

The leader of the Razorhides was Dherk .

Activities

The Razorhides traded in traveler's dust . They were known to steal, and were rumored to kill freelance thieves, cutting out their limbs and spreading them around the docks.

Base of Operations

The Razorhides made their base of operations at an abandoned brewery in Veltalar 's wharf.
