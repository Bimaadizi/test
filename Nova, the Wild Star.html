Nova, the Wild Star was a powerful shuriken found in the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Nova was imbued with destructive wild magic , blazing and steaming as it flew through the air and inflicting random magical harm upon the foe before returning .

History

The throwing star originated in old Myth Drannor, and was gradually warped by wild magic. It was later found in a hidden Myth Drannor Cult of the Dragon base , held by the Red Wizards of Droic Orgul that sought to enslave the dracolich Karliath . The blade may have then been taken by the Cormanthor Elves when they eliminated the Red Wizards.
