The Library of Ont the Wise was a library in the T'u Lung city of Balanzia , around the Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) , that was either owned by or named for an individual known as Ont the Wise.

Location

This library was located in the western portion of Balanzia, across from the Cotnui Park and Zoo .

History

Around Shou Year 2403 (1153 DR) , this library was one of many buildings that were left intact in Balanzia as the city fell at the hands of the Shou Lung invasion of T'u Lung. Centuries later, around Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) , the building that once housed the Library of Ont the Wise still stood within the abandoned city and was recorded as such on a map made of the city in that year.
