Jacob was a senior member of the Nashers faction of the Sons of Alagondar who lived in the Blacklake District of Neverwinter during the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Jacob wrote a note to Nasher leader Karzov reporting the Dead Rats were responsible for the disappearance of Gothwan Cooper . Karzov wrote back explaining why he would not move against the Dead Rats or try to retrieve Gothwan. An adventurer seeking Gothwan later found this note, suggesting Jacob had been found dead, if not slain by the adventurer.
