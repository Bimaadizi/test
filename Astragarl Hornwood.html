Astragarl Hornwood was a renowned Mage of Elembar , who lived in the 2 nd century DR .

History

Astragarl hailed from the city of Elembar that was destroyed in the Year of the Fortress Scoured, 511 DR . The city was part of a human kingdom of Delimbiyran , located on the Sword Coast . Astragarl Hornwood became known as the Mage of Elembar by the Year of the Tusk, 112 DR . As a wizard , Astragarl Hornwood tutored at least one apprentice in 112 DR, to whom he imparted knowledge of dragons . Notably, Astragarl agreed that the great wyrms were indeed magnificent creatures, as long as one observed them on a tapestry or from the great distance of several kingdoms away.
