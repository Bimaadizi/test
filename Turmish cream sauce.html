TypeFoodstuff

Turmish cream sauce was a condiment originating in Turmish in the Vilhon Reach .

Description

It was both creamy and spicy.

History

Eckert cooked and served Turmish cream sauce on pickled asparagus as a side dish to roast beef, carrots , and onions for Talbot Uskevren , and Lommy in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
