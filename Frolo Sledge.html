Frolo Sledge was a dwarven boat operator in Cormyr during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Frolo was a stout fellow with a closely shaven scalp and medium length brown beard.

History

Frolo operated a large boat with Har Pornoth , first carrying cargo and later moving prisoners sent into Wheloon Prison . This including traversing the Starwater River , Dragonmere , and the Wyvernflow between Eveningstar and Wheloon . They did not carry anything out of Wheloon, leaving the hold empty.
