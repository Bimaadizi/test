Pork loin was a cut of pig , boar , or hog meat and a dish found in several locales around Faerûn .

Description

In the Eastern and Western Heartlands , pork loins were traditionally slowly roasted drenched in apple and cider gravy . The same recipe could also be found served in the eateries of the city of Baldur's Gate .

In Thay , pork loins were coked in green pepper sauce.

Pork loins could also be braised in mustard sauce and served with goose liver paté and crusty bread .
