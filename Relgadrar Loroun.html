Lord Relgadrar Loroun was an noble of the Loroun family in Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Manshoon tried to enslave his mind and, despite Relgadrar having powerful magic in his defense, Manshoon managed at last to enslave him.

Relationships

Relgadrar considered Melder Crownrood to be one of his greatest rivals.
