The Vishap's Spine was a small mountain range found on the island of Sahu in east Zakhara in the early 1400s DR .

Geography

The Vishap's Spine was the most dominating feature of the island, running nearly its entire length for over a hundred miles.

Inhabitants

Wyverns roosted in the cliffs of the Vishap's Spine.
