Harborwatch Tower was one of the major torchtowers of Waterdeep 's Trollwall , located in the city's Deepwater Harbor and overlooking the naval harbor.

Location

This tower was located directly south of the Watching Tower and north of the Sea Chain Gates .

Structure

Harborwatch Tower stood four stories tall and was directly connected to the western Trollwall.

Interior

Being a torchtower, Harborwatch Tower had all the features that were common among them. This included a debriefing room, a small armory, holding cells, storage rooms for the holding of evidence or confiscated items, some bedchambers, and some garderobes. Additionally, being a major torchtower, it had six small holding cells that could hold up to ten people, which were used in times of trouble.

Activities

Like all other torchtowers, this structure had three beacon-fires that were constantly kept on standby.

Inhabitants

Unlike other torchtowers, which were manned by members of the City Watch , this building was garrisoned by members of the City Navy .
