Nalander was a noble family of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Lord Arkanon Nalander schemed to place someone on the Dragon Throne other than a member of the Obarskyr family. He was backed by persons in Sembia . His fellow schemers included Lord Elbert Oldbridle .
