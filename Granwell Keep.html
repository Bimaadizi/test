Granwell Keep was a fortress of the Purple Dragons located in the Blooded Wood in the kingdom of Cormyr .

Circa 1485 DR , it was assaulted by an army of mercenary sellswords commanded by Netherese forces in search of the Abyssal Planus Mechanus Nether Scroll .
