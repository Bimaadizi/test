Location

The South Force Gate sat on the southern side of the Force Bridge that spanned the River Tesh where it met the Moonsea .

Defenses

The gate was flanked by a pair of guard towers. Ten to fifteen Zhentilar were stationed at each tower.

Activities

The South Force Gate provided access to the Foreign Quarter from the Common Quarter and Harbor District via the Force Bridge.
