Kadugan was sailor from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Kadugan was a pale and green-tint skinned wood elf with sharp chiseled facial features, long brown hair, and blue eyes. He wore dockworker's commoner clothes while aboard his vessel.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Kadugan was a lowly sailor and laborer who worked alongside Listless under Bosun Sharley , who worked her crew hard. Kadugan was tasked with unloading the boat but lamented the fact that Bosun did not land under one of the docks' unloading cranes, making unloading that much harder for Kadugan and Listless.
