A sket'kah was a specific type of serpent that dwelt in the Marsh of Chelimber .

Abilities

These serpents were venomous. Reputedly, their venom could be squeezed from them and combined with magic . When used, all one spoke was understood by all who could hear them.

History

In 1374 DR , the lizardfolk Sleyvas claimed to use the venom of the sket'kah to communicate with and understand the three adventurers, Kromlech Bruenghor , Vahn , and Adrianna . However, as he later deceived the adventurers, it is possible that the things he told them were not true.
