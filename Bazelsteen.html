Bazelsteen was a cornugon harbormaster in Avernus , first layer of the Nine Hells , in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Bazelsteen was appointed by the archdevil Zariel to direct one of her docks on the River Styx . However, by the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , he had grown frustrated at not being permitted to do his mechanical experiments with infernal war machines .
