M'dhal was a Netherese arcanist and experimentalist that specialized in protective spells .

Description

A passionate experimentalist, M'dhal was both wise and hardy.

Activities

The arcanist spent much time involved with his experiments, some of which involved attempts to control creatures from other planes. He worked within Stone Fort , which was located close to the Mines of Dekanter .

History

M'dhal was born in 2609 NY (−1250 DR) . Concentrating mostly on research and spell invention, he developed spells based on protecting the caster and their allies. Such spells were guards and wards , protection from normal missiles , protection from evil , and protection from evil, 10' radius . M'dhal died in 2941 NY (−918 DR) whilst fighting off a balor who escaped a summoning circle .
