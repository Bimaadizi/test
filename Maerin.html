Maerin was an Illuskan arcanist around the time of the Fall of Netheril .

History

In the Year of Seven Spirits, −335 DR , Maerin contacted Immar Fardelver and other dwarven artisans from Delzoun to commission the building of Gauntlgrym . The work was completed in the Year of Hollow Hills, −321 DR .

Maerin was the wizard who rediscovered Maegera after Maegera was lost in a large chamber filled with molten rock. She was put there in −22,900 DR by a group of dwarves of the great Northkingdom of Delzoun .
