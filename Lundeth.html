Lundeth was a village made up of sun-fire clay huts in the desert of Anauroch .

Geography

This village scaled the steep sides of a rock pinnacle surrounded by the Salt Flats and had a deep water well. It was a day's ride away from the southwestern edge of the Sword .

History

This village was once founded and inhabited by a Bedine tribe named the Goldor . Sometime prior to 1360 DR , the Zhentarim assaulted the village with their magical prowess and seized it as a base for their western operations.

From then on, Zhent caravans in Anauroch would often try to find their way to this village when they ran into trouble. And the Melial , a mixed community of hobgoblins and orcs , sometimes attempted raids on the village.

Eventually, after many years, once the Black Road was completed the Zhentarim abandoned this village.

Inhabitants

Around the 1360's DR , the inhabitants of this village were made up of members of the Zhentarim and the undead remains of the Goldor tribe. The latter numbered around twenty minor magelings and underpriests.

After the construction of the Black Road was finished, the Zhentarim members stationed in Lundeth left. All that remained within the village were those undead remnants of the Goldor.
