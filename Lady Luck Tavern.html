The Lady Luck Tavern was a taproom in Daggerford dedicated to Tymora and those that took risks, including soldiers, thieves, and adventurers.

Location

The tavern was located in central Daggerford where the northern arm of Rogar Alley connected to the High Road .

Interior

The two-floor tavern featured a rough pillar in the center that patrons attempted to climb, leaving a mark when they reached the top.

History

Around 1370 DR , the tavern was located in the Money Quarter , but by 1485 DR , the establishment was moved to the Caravan Quarter , just within the town's Caravan Gate .

Ownership

As of the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , the owner of the tavern was a human woman named Glenys , the great granddaughter of Darryl Orcslayer . She encouraged gambling in the tavern and even sponsored larger games each night.

Notable Patrons

The tavern was frequented by Curran Corvalin , the halfling chosen of Tymora who sponsored filling Tymora's cup , a ceremonial offering to the goddess should she want to stop by for a drink.
