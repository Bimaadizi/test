Aeon, the Blazing Reign was a powerful longbow found in the ruins of Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Aeon was linked to the dracolich Karliath , and heralded what was hoped to be a long and fiery tyranny. It landed arrows rapidly and with devastating force, sometimes setting targets alight with unnatural fire.

History

The bow originated from the Cult of the Dragon when they ritually reanimated Karliath at a hidden base in the ruins, later seized by the Red Wizards of Droic Orgul . The bow may have then found a new handler among the Cormanthor Elves sent by Phathroi .
