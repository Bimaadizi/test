The Baldur's Mouth was a print shop in Baldur's Gate that disseminated news via broadsheets and town criers. It was housed in a narrow, five-story building.

The Baldur's Mouth broadsheet was printed irregularly three to eight times during a tenday , depending on how many articles and advertisers it can come up with for an edition. It was created by Ettvard Needle , the son of a wealthy Lower City tailor.
