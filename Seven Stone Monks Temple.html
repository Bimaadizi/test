The Seven Stone Monks Temple was a monastery in T'u Lung , Kara Tur , established at some point before the early 14 th century DR .

History

The Seven Stone Monks Temple was a well-known monastic organization, already in existence some years before the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR . The monastery's inhabitants were the seven stone monks who possessed powers of divine nature. One notable creation of the seven monks was Myo-Myo's gong , an enchanted shield and a gong wielded by a famous ghost hunter Myo-Myo . The seven stone monks etched the gong with geometric patterns and imbued it with the sacred power to perform holy smite '.
