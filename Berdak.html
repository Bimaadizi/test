Berdak the Centaur was the proprietor of the Smiling Centaur tavern in Zazesspur in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Berdak was on the small side for a centaur , but he was strongly built. He was clean-shaven.

History

In the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR , then-adventuress Zaranda Star visited Berdak's tavern with her traveling companions. While there, one of her companions, Father Pelletyr died of heartstop , and Berdak was the first to reach the fallen cleric and the one to declare his death.
