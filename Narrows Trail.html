The Narrows Trail was a small trail found along the slopes of the Alimir Mountains near the Lake of Steam .

Geography

The trail originated in the north, branching from Darkhope's Road northwest of Mintar . The Narrows Trail passed south, wedged between the eastern face of the Alimir Mountains and the western shore of the Lake of Steam. The trail covered approximately 220 miles (350 kilometers) before ending at Tulmon .

Notable Locations

In addition to Tulmon, the other notable city found along the Narrows Trail was Yeshpek . The Pevadaas Trail branched from the Narrows Trail's northern section.
