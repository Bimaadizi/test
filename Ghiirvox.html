Ghiirvox was the leader of the Scoured Legion in 1479 DR .

Personality

Ghiirvox was very reserved and calculating for a tanarukk . He suspected rightly that the Grand Bloodreaver Jeervan , a follower of Garagos , would soon move to supplant him as warchief.

Description

Unusual for a tanarukk, Ghiirvox had violet-blue skin, suggesting a very particular demon among his ancestors.

History

Ghiirvox was a veteran of twelve conflicts in the Underdark . He ascended to his current position when his predecessor succumbed to Araumycos 's madness. In 1479 DR, his position was threatened by Garagos' worshipers, who were spreading in the Legion, and their leader Jeervan.
