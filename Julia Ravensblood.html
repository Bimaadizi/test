Julia Ravensblood was an adventurer in Maztica .

History

Julia was a research wizard in Murann . While she was researching a sleep spell , her lover Allon traveled to Maztica . Julia decided to meet him in Helmsport , but discovered that he had died at the hands of some Viperhand monsters. Julia wanted to explore this new world and study Hishna and Pluma magic, and hoped to kill some Viperhand monsters as well.

Personality

Julia decided to remain in Maztica in order to avenge the death of her lover. However, she became fascinated by all of the new magic she discovered. She understood that she was one of the few wizards on the entire continent.
