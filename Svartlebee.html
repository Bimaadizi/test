Svartlebee , also known as The Custodian , was a mercenary active some years before the late 15 th century DR .

History

Svartlebee was well-known for being the master of the enchanted greatsword , later called Svartlebee's Woundseeker . Sometime before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Svartlebee used the magic blade to remove individuals causing mess on requests from other and even cleaned up the crime scenes after.
