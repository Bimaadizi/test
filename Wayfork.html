Wayfork was a village located on the Sword Coast , just north of the Trollbark Forest and south of Dragonspear Castle along the Trade Way , where it splits into two roads – towards Baldur's Gate and towards Boareskyr Bridge .

Description

Wayfork was a small agrarian community of farmlands, cottages, dirt roads, and stone wells. The main landmark of the village was the Wayfork Inn , catered to traveling caravaners.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , the Wayfork Village was raided by the Red Fang marauder goblin gang, secretly allied with the Zhentarim . Hobgoblin Harnak the Butcher lead the assault and began slaughtering villages. He took a stand against a group of six adventurers inside the Wayfork Inn, where the scared townsfolk found shelter. Subsequently, the village was saved and Harnak – slain.
