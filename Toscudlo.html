Toscudlo was a Netherese arcanist , master of illusions , and spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age .

History

This mentalist developed seven spells in total. The spells they invented were illusionary wall in 2309 NY (−1550 DR) , hallucinatory terrain in 2315 NY (−1544 DR) , project image in 2338 NY (−1521 DR) , advanced illusion in 2359 NY (−1500 DR) , demand in 2384 NY (−1475 DR) , permanent illusion in 2385 NY (−1474 DR) , and finally, mass invisibility in 2396 NY (−1463 DR) .
