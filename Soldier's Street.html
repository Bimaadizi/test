Soldier's Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Soldier's Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward just west of Castle Waterdeep . The road originated on Soothsayer's Way where it branched northwest toward Castle Waterdeep to end on Watchmen's Way at the Bell Tower .
