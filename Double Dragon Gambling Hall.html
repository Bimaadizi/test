The Double Dragon Gambling Hall was a secret, invitation only, gambling den located in the Shou district of Xiousing in Marsember . The entrance to the Double Dragon was through a curtain in the Shrine of Tymora . The ground floor was reserved for common patrons while wealthy patrons were invited to game at the high stakes games on the top floor.

Patrons

The Double Dragon's patrons included Shou Purple Dragons , members of the Nine Golden Swords and many merchants.
