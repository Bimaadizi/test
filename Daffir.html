Daffir the Prescient was a human member of the College of Divination in the city of Sshamath .

History

Daffir was a human wizard diviner hailing from south Faerûn .

In 1377 DR , he was one of the members of the College of Divination sent to join the combined force of Sshamath wizards and priests of Eilistraee who planned to attack the main temple of Kiaransalee .

In the Acropolis of Thanatos Daffir found a gate and sensing his destiny inside entered into.

He emerged in the Demonweb Pits where he was killed by some monsters. Later Cavatina Xarann found his body in her escape from the Abyss .
