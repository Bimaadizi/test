Adrienne Stillwater was a female half-elf ranger and cleric of Mielikki operating in the Hullack Forest and the town of Thunderstone in Cormyr in the late 1360s DR .

Personality

Adrienne was a nature-lover, and was unbiased and restrained in her actions.

Abilities

A veteran ranger and cleric, Adrienne was stealthy and had ogres as a special foe. She commanded divine spells which aided her in her woodland travels.

Possessions

Adrienne wore +2 leather armor and fought with a flame tongue longsword and +2 dagger . She also had a shortbow with 20 flight arrows .

Activities

Basing herself in Thunderstone on occasion, Adrienne roamed eastern Cormyr around the Hullack Forest. She feared that the so-called "civilized" forces of Cormyr such as adventurers and Purple Dragons would irreparably ruin the woodland in their efforts to clear it of dangerous monsters. She tried to prevent or limit the damage to the environment where possible.

History

Adrienne spoke with the Sembite adventuring company the Wolfmasters while they were in Thunderstone in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , learning that they were venturing into the Vast Swamp . Shortly thereafter, she went into the Hullack Forest on her own mission.
