Brass Street was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Brass Street was located in the Common Quarter in the northeast section of the city near the Dragon Gate . The road ran parallel with the nearby Street of Dragons one block to the east. It originated along Fortress Street and passed south for 300 feet (91 meters) before terminating at the wall that separated the Inner Quarter from the rest of Zhentil Keep.

Notable Locations

A Zhentilar barracks and armory were located on Brass Street.
