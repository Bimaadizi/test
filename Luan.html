Luan was a news hawker who worked for the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

At the age of six, Luan was a pale-skinned human child with icy-blue eyes and hazel hair. The boy stuttered, was timid and very unsure of himself.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Luan could often be found peddling the Baldur's Mouth Gazette on the plaza outside of the Basilisk Gate in the Lower City.
