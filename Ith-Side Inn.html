The Ith-Side Inn was a riverfront inn in Darromar commonly used by adventurers .

Location

The inn sat in the southeastern corner of the Black Quarter of the city, overlooking the River Ith .

Structure

The inn had a gated courtyard encompassed by 8‑foot-tall (2.4‑meter) walls topped with broken glass to deter thieves. The windows had shutters.

Services

Rooms in the inn were cheap, and quality of the inn reflected this. The inn included a stable. The staff included serving maids, who lit lanterns in the rooms for guests.

History

In the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , future-queen Zaranda Star was in Darromar (then Ithmong) to negotiate with the city's council. She stayed at the Ith-Side Inn. While there, she was drugged by a bottle of wine .
