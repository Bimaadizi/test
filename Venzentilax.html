Venzentilax was a blue dragon living in Threskel . She was a loyal servant of Alasklerbanbastos .

Description

Venzentilax had an unusually long beard of bladelike scales beneath her chin, and her horn lacked a secondary point.

History

Venzentilax took part on Alasklerbanbastos's invasion to Soolabax . She faced Tchazzar , who killed her effortlessly.
