Lady Belinda Anteos was a member of the Waterdhavian Anteos family and the owner of the Sword Coast Traders' Bank in the town of Daggerford .

Description

Some people considered her to be responsible and dependable enough to consider the services of her bank.

History

Lady Belinda established her business in Daggerford some time around the Year of the Iron Dwarf's Vengeance, 1485 DR , thanks in part to the support of the town's leader, Duke Maldwyn Daggerford . While she swore to others that her means of transferring funds were secure and confidential, many of the local townsfolk were not receptive.
