Torntlar's Farm was a farm near Felmorel in the mid–8 th century DR .

Geography

Torntlar's Farm was separated from Felmorel by a forest through which wolves prowled; the farm stretched for six hills. The soil in at least one hill was stony.

History

Habaertus Ilynker , presumably its owner or a local farmhand, was killed by the Slayer shortly after the arrival of Elminster Aumar in Felmorel during the Year of Laughter, 761 DR .
