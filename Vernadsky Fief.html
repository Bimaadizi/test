The Vernadsky Fief was the walled estate of the Vernadsky noble family who inhabited the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

This walled compound was located almost directly in the center of Almorel along the Golden Way . Much of the estate bordered the promenade that led to Mechnik Nikitsky 's residence.
