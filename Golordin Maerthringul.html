Golordin Maerthringul was a skilled warrior inhabiting Suldolphor in the far eastern reaches of Calimshan in south Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was middle-aged as of the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

Activities

Golordin was one of two High Swords who led the Flame Guard —an elite group of warriors who protected Suldolphor's ruling olanger .

History

He served as a mercenary for many years in Tethyr and other places.

Possessions

Golordin wore an enchanted suit of armor and wielded a flame tongue sword .
