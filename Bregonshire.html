Bregonshire was an open plain located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Bregonshire comprised much of southern Stirgewick in Callidyrr, ranging from the Rushdown Wood through the peninsula of the South Great Head to the coast of the Sea of Swords . The High King's Road passed through Bregonshire's western side near the coast of the Strait of Alaron .
