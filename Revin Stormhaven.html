Revin Stormhaven was a captain of the City Watch in Waterdeep in 1379 DR .

History

In 1379 DR, Revin was investigating the kidnapping of some elves by the Unyielding Sword mercenaries hired by the slaver Lanador . He decided to hire an adventuring party that was casually involved.

Personality

Revin had worked with many adventurers and knew well how to motivate them.
