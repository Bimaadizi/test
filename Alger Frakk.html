Alger Frakk was a master blacksmith at the Smithy in Phandalin in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

His apprentice was Maza Fieldsalder , who grew into a strong and skilled smith under his tutelage.

History

Alger ran the Smithy until he passed away under suspicious circumstances in the Year of the Tyrant's Pawn, 1495 DR , leaving the business to his apprentice. There were soon rumors that Maza had killed Alger, and that perhaps she had good reason to do so.
