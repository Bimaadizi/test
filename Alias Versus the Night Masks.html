Alias Versus the Night Masks was a book that recounted the conflict between Alias of the Azure Bonds and the notorious Night Masks of Westgate of 1368 DR , penned by Harper agent Jamal the Thespian.

Content

The book recorded the events of the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , when hero Alias and her "brother," saurial paladin Dragonbait visited Westgate and became entangled in plots of the city's criminal underbelly. The duo reunited with their former companion hin " bard " Olive Ruskettle , Mintassan the Sage, and the book's author, Jamal the Thespian. Together, they ended the reign of the mysterious leader of the Night Masks, only known as the Faceless (in reality Victor Dhostar ).

The book's foreword praised the heroes, proclaiming the love for Alias as the city's hero, but conceded that true evil could not be easily defeated when it has taken root. Jamal the Thespian stated that by the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , a new Faceless rose to power, and the guild was resurgent once again. The author hopes that the book would inspire a new generation of heroes to follow in Alias' footsteps and root the Night Masks' evil out from Westgate's undercut - the Undergate .

History

The book was written and published in 1370 DR, and its copies were available for sale at Mintassan's Mysteries , a store of curios acquired from across the planes by Mintassan the Magnificent.
