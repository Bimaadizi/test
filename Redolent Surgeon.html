The Redolent Surgeon was a pirate that served aboard the Black Scourge , a skyship captained by Pirate King Bartholomew Blackdagger during the first half of the 15 th century DR . Like Blackdagger and all of his crew, the surgeon was cursed with existence as an undead monstrosity, bound to a hidden lair within Pirates' Skyhold for decades following his mortal death.

History

The undead surgeon was destroyed in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR by the Heroes of Sleeping Dragon Bridge , when the adventurers raided Pirates' Skyhold under the employ of Company Yargo .
