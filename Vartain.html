Vartain was a widely traveled riddlemaster originally from Calimport .

Description

Vartain was bald.

History

Vartain grew up on the streets of Calimport. He later became a respected riddlemaster and language scholar. He was trained in Mulhorand tradition.

In 1364 DR , Vartain was hired by Elaith Craulnober to help him seek out the Morninglark harp. They were joined by Harpers Danilo Thann and company and traveled to the High Forest , where Vartain was posed a conundrum by the riddle-loving dragon Grimnoshtadrano . Vartain overthought the riddle and answered incorrectly. His anguish at answering incorrectly later led him to go behind Danilo's back and pose Iriador Wintermist the same conundrum; she also failed to answer correctly.

Possessions

Vartain carried and was skilled with a blowpipe ( darts ).
