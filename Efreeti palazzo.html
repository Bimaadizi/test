Efreeti palazzos were the homes of noble efreeti and their slaves. These buildings served as both a palace and stronghold.

Location

Most efreeti palazzos were located within the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire .

Inhabitants

The household of an efreeti palazzo contained the ruling noble efreeti, common efreet , jann and other slaves. Often a stable was also present which housed nightmares , hell hounds , and fire falcons .
