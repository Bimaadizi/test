The Rolling Wheel was an establishment of Scornubel in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Interior

The common room of the inn held a large space for dancers, which were visible from the threshold of the entrance.

Atmosphere

At least one night in 1369 DR, the place was full of roaring men and scrawny dancers.

Services

The place had dancers.

History

While disguised as the peddler "Tarthan", Dove Falconhand determined at a glance that the place was not to her interest because it was not obviously infiltrated by drow . After meeting with the drow infiltrator Sarltan and fighting off his forces, Dove planned on heading there to draw as many witnesses as possible to the corpses of dead drow, in order to draw suspicion and thus hinder any infiltrators' plans for as long as possible.
