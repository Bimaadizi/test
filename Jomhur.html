Imam Jomhur was the High Priest of the Mosque of Brave Hajama in Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Activities

Imam Jomhur supervised around 200 priests and other laymen within the mosque.

Jomhur widely opposed the infamous group of holy slayers called the Everlasting who also worshiped Hajama .
