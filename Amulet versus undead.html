TypeMagical item

An amulet versus undead was a blessed symbol that granted the wearer the ability to turn undead .

Powers

The amulet would glow strongly when displayed in the presence of undead . After being worn for seven days, it allowed the wearer to banish undead as a cleric would, with the strength of the effect depending on the individual amulet .
