Pockall was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age .

History

Pockall invented four spells during their life. The first three spells involved invisibility, and they were: improved invisibility , which came in 2381 NY (−1478 DR) , invisibility , which came in 2385 NY (−1474 DR) , and invisibility, 10' radius , which was invented in 2388 NY (−1471 DR) . Pockall's final spell was rather different from their previous ones. This spell, charm monster , was developed in 2392 NY (−1467 DR) .
