Tributary ofThe Shining Stream

Swimstar Creek was a tributary of the Shining Stream , which in turn was a tributary of Tethyr 's River Ith . It began in the Kuldin Peaks and divided County Morninggold in half. It was fast-moving but shallow and contained a breed of luminescent fish.
