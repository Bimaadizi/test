Lex Geographica was an atlas book that included maps of the city of Phlan , drawn by a famous mathematician named Tomarus and published sometime in the mid-12 th century DR .

History

A copy of Lex Geographica was left in Mantor's Library in the city of Phlan as the city fell into ruins during a Rage of Dragons of the Year of Thunder, 1306 DR .
