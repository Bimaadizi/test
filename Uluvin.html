Uluvin was a sleepy crossroads town located in the Delimbiyr Vale in the Savage Frontier . It was a sparsely-populated settlement with a few scattered trees and dusty roads. The village was avoided by most travelers and merchants as it offered little in the way of trade beyond a few street peddlers.

Geography

The village was a cross-roads for several trails, including the Iron Road and the Secomber Trail , along with trails that led along the Unicorn Run into the High Forest and the Stronghold of the Nine .
