The Abbey of the Rose was a secret temple to Amaunator in shadovar -dominated Sembia in the mid-to-late 15 th century DR . Former, however, the site was sacred to Mask .

Location

It was located in a valley in the Thunder Peaks , near the river Draal before it joined the river Arkhen , east of the Thunder Peaks .

Defenses

The abbey was protected by the spirits of Elgrin Fau , led by Avnon Des . It was made invisible from the shadovar thanks to these spirits .

History

However, in the Year of the Awakened Sleepers, 1484 DR , the Oracle released the spirits and so Sayeed and Zeeahd invaded the Abbey with their pack of spined devils .
