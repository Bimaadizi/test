Kolapar was a town located in the nation of Estagund during the 14 th century DR .

Geography

Kolapur was located in southeast Estagund along the coast of the Great Sea . The town sat along the eastern shore of the River Gundar where it emptied into the sea.

Activities

The prominent Bihar chaka was based in Kolapar. Caravans departing the town were raided frequently by a sect of Mask worshipers.
