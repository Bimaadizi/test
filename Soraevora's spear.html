Soraevora's spear was a unique polearm , sacred to the goddess Angharradh , The Triune Goddess from the elven island of Evermeet .

Powers

Soraevora's spear was enhanced with Angharradh's divine magic, granting it a +2 enchantment to damage that guided the spear toward its target. The spear dealt piercing and additional divine damage upon each strike. Soraevora's spear's unique nature only made it usable by goodly elf favored souls .

History

This spear's existence predated the 14 th century DR , likely by many centuries. The weapon was entrusted to a favored soul of Angharradh – the sun elf named Soraevora Aeravand of Evermeet. In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Soraevora's deity sent her visions of doom and the elf embarked on the journey to the Sword Coast North to stop Zehir , a yuan-ti interloper deity. Soraevora's spear accompanied her on the perilous quest.
