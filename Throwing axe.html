Throwing axes were a light, martial melee weapon of the axe family that could be used as a ranged weapon.

Description

Throwing axes were similar to a handaxe , but lighter and balanced for throwing. Their wooden shafts were about 1.5 feet (0.46 meters) long. . Their blades were typically single-edged with a plain back.

Weaponology

Classified as a light weapon, throwing axes could be used while grappling. Some training was required to be able to hit a target with a throwing axe, thus its classification as a martial weapon. The average maximum range of a throwing axe was typically 50 feet (15 meters).
