Summarl's Scrolls and Maps was a shop within a row house in the city of Calimport .

Location

This building was located in the Shuzah Drudach , of the Jhaapir Sabban , of the Quill Ward of Calimport.

Structure

The row house that hosted Summarl's Scrolls and Maps stood three stories tall.

Services

As the name of this business implied, it sold a variety of maps and scrolls .
