Telarantra was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

She was not above gloating when killing innocent people.

Abilities

One of her spells slew a fellow War Wizard almost immediately by stealing his lifeforce. Another of her spells broke a song dragon free from a magical effect that froze it in time; the same effect, or one not long after, caused a fiery explosion at a distance.

Relationships

Besides being a War Wizard , she worked for the Rightful Conspiracy .

History

Telarantra was one of the mages that arrived in the aftermath of the song dragon Ammaratha Cyndusk 's attack on Vangerdahast at his hideout in Mouth o'Gargoyles in 1373 DR . The song dragon had been stopped with magic that froze it in time; as soon as she learned that, Telarantra immediately slew the nearest mage, and unfroze the dragon, unleashing an explosion. She expected the dragon would finish its task of slaying Vangerdahast.

However, Ammaratha Cyndusk chose to turn on her instead of slaying Vangerdahast, and quickly disemboweled her.
