Rold was a guard in Shadowdale as of the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

He had a mostache.

Personality

He had great calm and presence of mind even in the most awkward moments. He was open about finding the state of being on the offensive to be easier on his temper than being a guardsman.

Relationships

He was the uncle of Naera , a fact he preferred not to bring up around Torm . He had great respect for the adventurer.

History

He claimed to have seen something of the road Torm (of the Knights of Myth Drannor ) walked, strongly implying he had been an adventurer.

In the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , he was the one to take Torm from the arms of a wench, his niece Naera, to keep guard on the spellfire wielder Shandril Shessair , then a guest of the castle. Later, he was the one to save Shandril from a fellow guard, Culthar , then in the employ of the Cult of the Dragon . The next morning, he was relieved Shandril wasn't staying at the castle longer.

Per some early reports, Rold was also a guard during the tests that Elminster did on spellfire . Those were not repeated in more definitive reports.
