A war wizard cloak was a powerful magical item given to trusted agents of Cormyr .

Description

They were long, black weathercloaks that could cover the arms, chest, and legs of the wearer, with high collars and attachable hoods. They bore a purple dragon insignia on the left collar and another on the back of the hood. The right collar bore the design of an upraised human palm in a white circle.

Powers

The cloak provided protection against extreme cold as a ring of warmth did, and conferred infravision on the wearer. It also provided protection from normal missiles , feather fall and ironguard . It could be used to cast sending and dimension door once each per day.
