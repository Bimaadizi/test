Old Gebe was a colorful personality of uncertain age from Dlusk , Aglarond , active in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Circa the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , during the spring festivals in Dlusk , Old Gebe give free hayrides to children and told stories and sang songs about mules , including his own, Sara and Corey . He knew much of local gossip, legends, and was knowledgeable on the subject of the Unapproachable East in general.
