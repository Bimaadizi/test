An uchi-ne was a weapon unique to the continent of Kara-Tur .

Description

A uchi-ne was a compact, weighty javelin resembling a diminutive arrow . It was capable of being skillfully hurled over short distances or employed for precise thrusting and jabbing maneuvers. They were frequently carried by nobles during their travels.
