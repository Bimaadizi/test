Francis the All-Seeing was an adventurer from the city of Neverwinter , active in the mid-14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR , the adventure troupe led by Francis the All-Seeing, famously, banished the ghosts of two young children who were cursed into an endless game of tag, after being killed and buried under the street of the Merchant Quarter of Neverwinter. Francis had the children's bodies exhumed and performed a sanctified burial ceremony, laying the undead spirits to rest.
