Sharp talons of the wild were enchanted claw-like weapons, encountered in Chult in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

These fearsomely sharp claws were ensorcelled with a strong +3 enchantment and their design was based on velociraptor talons.

Notable Owners

Qawasha : a druid of the Emerald Enclave from Chult in the late 15 th century DR.
