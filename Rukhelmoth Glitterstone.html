Rukhelmoth Glitterstone , or simply Rukh was a deep gnome explorer who was held prisoner of the Cult of the Black Earth at the Temple of the Black Earth in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

In 1491 DR , while attempting to sneak into the Fane of the Eye , Rukh was captured by Black Earth cultists. They decided to sacrifice her to a black pudding living under the temple. However, some adventurers infiltrated the temple, defeated the cultists, and freed her.
