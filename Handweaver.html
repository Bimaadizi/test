The Handweaver was a mysterious wizard who played a pivotal role in the history of Westgate .

Activities

The Handweaver used his magic to reveal the true nature of Nessmara , a lamia noble who had tricked King Lyonarth . Lyonarth was enraged at the discovery and confronted Nessmara in a deadly clash that claimed both of their lives.

Relationships

The Handweaver tutored Farnath Ilistar who would later become King of Westgate.
