Enlasre was a village in the Kuong Kingdom in Malatra .

Geography

It lay on the Kuong Kingdom's trade road from the south, which from here ran north to Javuhansa on the headwaters of the Kunong River .

Activities

The village was unremarkable and no more than a stopover for travelers. Enlasre's mineral waters and hot springs were its main attraction.
