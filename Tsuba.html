Tsuba was one of the first emperors of Wa from the Goshukara line, the first whose name is remembered by history.

History

Emperor Tsuba was the one sitting on the throne of Wa in the 24 th year of the Wa calendar (the Year of Clouded Vision, âˆ’394 DR ), when mariner Cham Ko Hag arrived from Shou Lung , which was known as the "Western Lands" to Wa. Cham Ko Hag was granted an audience with Tsuba and promised to return as an emissary with gifts from the King of the Western Lands. The nations of Shou Lung and T'u Lung had not known of the island of Wa's existence until this time.
