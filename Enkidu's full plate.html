Enkidu's full plate was the suit of iron armor worn by the famous Kara-Turan warrior Enkidu .

Powers

The armor possessed a powerful +3 enchantment and offered its wearer limited protection from spells and other magical effects. Additionally, it offered complete protection from any cowardly backstabbing attacks, such as those made by rogues , assassins , or the Wu jen .

Weaker copies of the same plate mail only held a +1 enchantment and offered resistance to piercing and slashing weapons.

History

The first set of these enchanted plate mails was created for a famed and undefeated warrior Enkidu. His combat prowess was so great that no fighter was brave enough to challenge Enkidu. Instead, his enemies used the services of backstabbing assassins, ninjas , and wu-jen . Enkidu's full plate was crafted and enchanted to withstand such attacks. After his death, numerous copies and variants of the magic armor were created.

Enkidu's full plate eventually made its way to the Faerûnian realm of Calimshan , and came into the possession of the Amkethran smuggler Carras . A copy of Enkidu's armor was brought from Kara-Tur in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR and sold in Okuzo's Imports of Westgate .
