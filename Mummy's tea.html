Mummy's tea was a type of potion that healed afflictions.

Description

Mummy's tea was typically stored in slender, green bottles. It was known to have a foul odor.

Creation of this potion required boiling the used wrappings of a mummy alongside animal bones that had been naturally dried out.

Powers

Anyone who consumed this potion would experience effects similar to the spells cure blindness or deafness and cure disease .

History

Mummy's tea was first developed in Mulhorand by priests of Horus-Re . By 1281 DR , it was available for purchase from a number of vendors in the region of Icewind Dale .
