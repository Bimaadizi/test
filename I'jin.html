I'jin ( pronounced : /ˈ aɪ dʒ ɪ n / EYE-jin ) was one of the nine trickster gods of Omu in Chult . She was a primal spirit that took the form of an almiraj .

Personality

I'jin was fickle and unpredictable. She never stuck to a plan and even her favorite color would change constantly.
