King Jhanos Torlath II "the Ancient" was an exceptionally long-lasting Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty, known as the fourth Wizard-King of Amn.

Relationships

Jhanos was the second son of of King Imnel III , and his own fourth son was Rhadik II .
