The Seaspray Inn was an inn in the town of Highcastle on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 th century DR . The town was the largest port settlement near the Crossroad Keep , and as such attracted a healthy amount of naval vessels and merchant ships.

History

Circa the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the establishment was popular among locals as well as sailors whose ships docked at Highcastle. It offered food, drinks, and lodgings to locals, guests, and passing adventurers .
