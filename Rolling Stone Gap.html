Rolling Stone Gap was a mountain pass found in the Hordelands and east Faerûn .

Geography

Rolling Stone Gap sat between the northern section of the Giant's Belt Mountains and Fuirgar . The pass connected the plains of the fringes of Eastern Shaar and the Beastlands with the Raurin desert.
