Zoumdan Obarskyr , a son of King Ortolar , was the 40th Obarskyr monarch and one of the three "boy kings" of Cormyr while Rathdar Orlenthar ruled as regent. He died in the Purplethroat Plague in the same year as his older brother Artreth .
