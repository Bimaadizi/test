Gurk was a warlock that devoted his life to the Absolute , living among the Absolute'sfaithful within the Shattered Sanctum for a time during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Like other fanatics of the cult of the Absolute , Gurk was branded with a distinct brand by High Priestess Gut .

History

Gurk was among the goblins at the Shattered Sanctum celebrating their successful raid of the Waukeen's Rest inn, some time in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
