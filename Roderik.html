Roderik was a member of the Night Parade who died while fighting a group of Harpers in Calimport .

Description

Roderik appeared as a dark, featureless human whose skin seemed to absorb light and reflect shadows.

Abilities

He could walk through shadows. Additionally, what he touched would become coated in tar-like darkness that killed whoever touched it. As a member of the Inextinguishables, he was difficult to kill by normal means.

Activities

Roderik was a member of the Night Parade, in particular a member of Lord Zeal 's elite Inextinguishables force.

History

In 1359 DR , Roderik and his fellow Inextinguishables were active in Calimport, where they attacked the Harper Myrmeen Lhal and her companions. The fight went poorly, and ended when Lord Zeal killed the Inextinguishables for their failure.
