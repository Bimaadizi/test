Silverling was a species of tiny freshwater fish that was abundantly present in the Savage Frontier . These little creatures were caught in the nets along with bigger, more valuable fish, making them a cheap and simple food source.

Description

Silverlings had a very similar taste to sardines , a small type of fish found along the Sword Coast . Unlike their coastal cousins, silverlings had more of a complex smoky and mushroomy flavor.

Use

In the hamlet of Mornbryn's Shield , the local inn called the Troll in Flames served spread bread , a snack dish that accompanied alcohol. The bread was spread with silverling paste and garlic .
