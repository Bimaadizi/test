Sharath Nhalass , who was called the Doomed , was the fourteenth and final Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Two of Sharath's older brothers were killed by demons sent by Narfell , causing him to inherit the throne in −605 DR . However, Sharath met his brother's fate later that same year and the bloodline of the Nhalass Dynasty ended with his death.

Relationships

Shareth was the youngest of five sons of Darthen Nhalass III . His eldest brother died with their father while invading Narfell. His next oldest brothers were Umathar VI , Rhendar II , and Tharren .
