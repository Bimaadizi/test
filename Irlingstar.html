Irlingstar ( pronounced : /ˈ ʌr l ɪ ŋ s t ɑːr / URR-ling-star ) was a noble family of Waterdeep .

Activities

House Irlingstar built ships and ran caravans. Unbeknownst to most of the family, they also financed the Unseen . Later, they became a banking family.

Base of Operations

The members of House Irlingstar resided at the three- and four-story buildings collectively known as Irlingstar Villa in the Sea Ward on the northwest corner of Sul Street and Delzorin Street . They also had a rowhouse in the Castle Ward called Sablehearth, on Swords Street between Blackstaff Tower and the Font of Knowledge .

History

In the early 1340s DR, the patriarch's consort, Nael, was replaced by a malaugrym named Amarune . She had a daughter named Sintre , who died, leaving the family without a direct heir. In 1370 DR , Hlaavin of the Unseen discovered Amarune's true identity and began extorting her.
