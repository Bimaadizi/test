Widow-Through was a unique enchanted greataxe , once wielded by a twisted individual known as Black Donald sometime before 1312 DR .

Powers

Widow-Through was an immaculately cared-for weapon, polished and enchanted with serviceable +1 and keen enchantments that made the weapons more likely to deal devastating damage to its opponents, leaving a grieving widow behind.

History

Sometime before the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , the weapon gained notoriety in the hands of a twisted adventurer named Black Donald, who showed no regard for human life. He used the weapon to kill indiscriminately at the behest of his adventuring troupe – the Winnowing Drake Company . Donald's halfling friend, Tim Rootfield , once said that the axe was sharp enough to leave half the Faerûn widowed. Black Donald responded by saying that it was sharp enough to cut through the husbands and return to the village to get the wives on the follow-through. This comment gave the ace its name – Widow-Through .

By 1312 DR, Widow-Through belonged to Tahvo the Huntmaster from the Wandering Village of Icewind Dale . The Huntmaster offered his stock of supplies, armor, and weapons for trade with travelers and merchants who traveled between the Ten-Towns and Kuldahar .
