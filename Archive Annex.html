The Archive Annex was a library found in the city of Neverwinter during the late 15 th century DR .

Interior

The upper levels of the repository were closed off to many of its patrons, though staff were permitted to retrieve books from within and lend at their discretion.

Services

It was overseen by a stern, blue dragonborn librarian, who belittled some of the library's patrons.

History

Sometime during or before the Year of the Grinning Halfling, 1481 DR , a hill dwarf cleric of Marthammor Duin came into the Archive Annex, looking for more information about an island that appeared to them in a dream.
