Dalthar was a cultist of the Absolute that ventured along with her comrades into the ruins of Grymforge in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

She was a dwarf of few words and extremely wary of outsiders that came near her group's operations.

Possessions

Dalthar preferred to wield a crossbow when called to battle. Like others of her order, she wore an amulet bearing the unholy symbol of the Absolute.

History

She was among the duergar tasked by True Soul Nere with uncovering an entrance to a long-lost Sharran temple within Grymforge in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
