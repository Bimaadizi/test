Thauntar was a warrior in the mid-13 th century DR .

Appearance & Possessions

He wore mismatched armor, with a rusty gauntlet, and had only one eye.

History

During a conversation with Elminster in 1262 DR , he related unto Elminster that the Simbul, while deadly, was highly selective and only ever slew spies and killers from Thay , rather than all mages, and that an embassy from a city in Chessenta was expected that evening.

During a conversation with Nergal in 1372 DR , Elminster related unto Nergal that Thauntar had died, but been loved by Mystra in life.
