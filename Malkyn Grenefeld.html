Malkyn Grenefeld was a chamberlain of Hillsfar in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Malkyn was the city's chamberlain and the court's mage.

History

A member of the Brotherhood of the Cloak in Mulmaster , Malkyn abandoned her city and was chosen to join the First Lord's court.

Circa 1486 DR , she attended the First Lord's Midsummer Masquerade ball. She possibly checked out an adventuring party (who had been hired by Elanil Elassidil to infiltrate the masquerade) who had been hired as servers by Torgrave Needlebaum .

During the ball, Torin started a speech and an elf assassin tried to kill the First Lord but hit only Thessaly Malaeda . Malkyn quickly reached Torin and the two disappeared thanks to an invisibility spell and she took the Lord to a safe place.

Malkyn was totally loyal to Torin Nomerthal . She was very rigid and adhered to protocol. Malkyn was a talented mage.
