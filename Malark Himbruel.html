Malark Himbruel was a wizard and a Wearer of the Purple of the Cult of the Dragon in in the mid–14 th century DR . He also held the rank of Archmage of the Purple. He was involved in the hunt for Shandril Shessair .

History

In the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Malark tried to capture Shandril in Shadowdale and he also wanted vengeance for the death of Symgharyl Maruel , his underling. But he was ambushed and garrotted by the thief Torm , who soon after slashed his throat, ending his threat.
