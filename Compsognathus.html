Compsognathus ( pronounced : / k ɑː m p s ɑː g ˈ n ɑː θ ʌ s / kamp-sag-NA-thus ), also referred to as compy , or as a garuda chicken by the inhabitants of the Malatran Plateau , was a species of dinosaur counted among the coelurosaurs .

Description

Among dinosaurs, compsognathus were very small, being roughly the same size as a chicken .

Combat

Compsognathus hunted prey in packs. They attacked as an ambush and preferred to attack prey while it was sleeping.

Ecology

Compsognathus were carnivores and lived in packs. Their typical diet consisted of lizards and insects .

Habitats

Compsognathus lived along the River Soshenstar , and although they didn't pose a threat, their harassment of the citizens of Port Nyanzaru led to the development of a barbed metal wire known as compy wire . They also lived in the Goblin Marches and Stonelands .

Uses

Compsognathus were valued as familiars for their highly developed sight, and they could be summoned using a find familiar spell.
