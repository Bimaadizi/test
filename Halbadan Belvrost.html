Halbadan Belvrost was a fabled member of the Belvrost clan of mages. His tale was retold as of the late 14 th century DR .

Rumors & Legends

He was said to live beyond death as a whispering wraith, lurking around the mill- waiting for one who would help him recover a buried treasure he no longer had hands to dig for. Per the legend, he would not specify the nature of the treasure.
