Sumuk Island was an island within the Golden Gulf of interior Zakhara .

Geography

Sumuk Island sat approximately 40 miles (64 kilometers) offshore due west of the mainland near the city of I'tiraf and the Cities of the Pantheon region. Sumuk Island was 40 miles (64 kilometers) long and 30 miles (48 kilometers) wide. Two smaller islands lay off of its north shore. Coral reefs surrounded these unamed islands. An ancient ruined mosque was located on the southeast coast of Sumuk Island in 1367 DR .
