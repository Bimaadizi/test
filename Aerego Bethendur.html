Aerego Bethendur was a businessman in Red Larch and a member of the Believers during the Elemental Evil crisis in 1491 DR .

Personality

Aerego never asked about goods placed in his storage, even if they contained cadavers. It was only if they started to smell he would destroy them. He was a Believer, although his attendance was spotty and he was starting to wonder what exactly the mysterious priest Larrakh wanted from the village.

Activities

Aerego managed a storage place in the village.
