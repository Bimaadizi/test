The Heartseeker dagger was a unique enchanted sentient dagger , found in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

Apart from possessing a +2 enchantment, the weapon's will directed the wielder's hand towards vulnerable spots on its target.

History

In the late 15 th century DR, the Heartseeker dagger changed hands several times among heroes and villains of the Sword Mountains , the city of Neverwinter , and the Old Owl Well . It also was in possession of Kajin the pirate who laired in the Sword Mountains.
