Jester's bag of holding was an enchanted , but defective, bag of holding that produced unexpected results when used.

Powers

In appearance, jester's bag of holding was a simple bag, with its insides enchanted to contain a nondimensional space, making the bag larger on the inside. However, this particular item appeared to be malfunctioning. When an individual attempted to put something into this bag of holding it did not let anything inside. If flipped over and shaken, jester's bag of holding excreted a random item from it once per day. Some of the items expelled included arrows , gems , bottles of oil of fiery burning , mummy's tea , potions of mirrored eyes , murky potions of healing , daggers , an occasional dead cat , and many others.

History

Jester's bag of holding could be found in the elven ruined citadel of the Severed Hand in the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR .
