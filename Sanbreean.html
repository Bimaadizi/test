Sanbreean was a Red Wizard of Thay in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Sanbreean was struck down battling the War Wizards in the aftermath of the Marsember meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy in early spring of 1373 DR ; after they were discovered by the War Wizards, Sanbreean fought them in the docks of the city, only to be slain, as related by Narvo .
