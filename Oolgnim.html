Oolgnim was an oneirophagic mind flayer who fought as a gladiator in the Underdark Arena during the 1480s DR .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Oolgnim fought at Azena'ar 's Underdark Arena alongside Hessperina the medusa , tasting the thoughts and memories of those it fought. It likely perished fighting a team of surfacer fighters.
