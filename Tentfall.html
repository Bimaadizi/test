The tentfall was an experimental parachute conceived of by Nadul Da Roni while he developed other forms of powered flight.

Description

In addition to the heartland flier , air screw , and doubles wheel , Da Roni put some effort into a device that would allow a common person to fall safely from a great height. According to his claims, any person with enough canvas could make it, in the form of a 12 yards (11 meters) square and 12 yd (11 m) tall pyramidal tent -like shape. The tricky part was apparently holding onto the large ring that hung from beneath the tentfall as it drifted downwards.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue listed the design by Nadul Da Roni, though it seemed to be a non-saleable item, instead intended to instruct others in the creation of their own tentfalls.
