Shardstorm vortexes were a type of elemental of air and earth that were native to the Elemental Chaos .

Description

These elementals had swirling bodies of air, like a funnelcloud or whirlwind, with numerous green crystal shards.

Society

Diet

These elementals were scavengers, feeding off the destruction left by more powerful elementals.

Languages

These elementals could only speak the Primordial language .
