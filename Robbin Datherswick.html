Robbin Datherswick was a hin entrepreneur from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Robbin was a strongheart halfling with a light skin tone, blonde hair, and dark brown eyes.

History

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Robbin Datherswick could often be seen visiting Felogyr's Fireworks in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate. The halfling was an aspiring competitor of the fireworks seller, who considered Avery Sonshal to be a genius salesman. Robbin concluded that it was an ingenious business decision to be the only licensed fireworks vendor in the entire city. The halfling was inspired to find something unique that only he could produce, and that was hard to replicate, in order to establish his own monopoly.
