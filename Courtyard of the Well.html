The Courtyard of the Well , also known as the Court of the Well , was a courtyard in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep that was named for the well in its center. It was paved in dirt and gravel.

Geography

This courtyard was bounded by Mendever Street , Sul Street , Zarimtar Street , and Whim Street . It was situated directly south of where Cloaksweep Alley ran north off of Zarimtar Street.
