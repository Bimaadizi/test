Saer Fleiner was a middle class inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Saer Fleiner was a dwarvish woman with a clean-shaven face and dark brown hair, gathered in a braid and formed into a tight hairstyle. She had a light freckled skin and green eyes. Fleiner wore embroidered finery of green and golden colors.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, the gates between the Lower City of Baldur's Gate and its more affluent Upper City were closed, monitored by the Flaming Fist mercenaries and allowing only the wealthy and current residents of the Upper City through. Saer Fleiner was terrified of the cultist army that loomed over over city and was outraged by Baldur's Gate shutting the gates. The dwarf believed that the Upper City had more than enough space for everyone.
