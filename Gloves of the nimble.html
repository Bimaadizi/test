The gloves of the nimble were a set of quick-fingered gloves found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The gloves gave the wearer much improved quick reflexes and resistance to physical blows, as well as much greater speed of movement.

History

The gloves were found by the Elves of Cormanthor when they searched Tyrintar's Hall for Uhlsdrin Nirveth of Eilistraee's Wanderers .
