Rogering Pegleg was a drow from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Rogering Pegleg was a man with long white hair who wore light armor .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a large group of duergar , giant spiders , and drow, was slaughtered in the home of a Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica . The murder spree was performed by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal . Rogering Pegleg's slaughtered body was found in a cellar underneath Lavernica's home, near a butchered drow woman, a grotesque display – an effigy representing Sendai , a drow Bhaalspawn who died a century earlier.
