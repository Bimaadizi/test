Arcanali was a very old drow wizard in Battledale in 1357 DR .

Activities

Arcanali was responsible for brewing up drow venom for a band of drow mercenaries.

History

By the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Arcanali was a decrepit and slightly addled old woman, "retired" to the caves underneath Haptooth Hill . She brewed her poisons using local mushrooms for the wizard Dracandros who took over Hap. These interactions were not voluntary, and Arcanali considered Dracandros a "robber-baron." She was willing to help those trying to depose the Red Wizard usurper.
