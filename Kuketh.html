Kuketh was a death slaad in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR .

History

In 1491 DR , Halaster Blackcloak summoned Kuketh and ordered it using its control gem to rule over a bullywug tribe inside the Slitherswamp level and fighting the nagas of Ssethian Scourges .

Activities

When not bullying its subjects in the Blacktongue tribe, Kuketh used its time to quietly ruminate on its troubles.

Personality

Kuketh hated its situation and wanted only go back to Limbo . It was a mercurial creature and often changed its mind.
