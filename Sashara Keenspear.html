Sashara Keenspear was a famous genasi mercenary who operated as a " kraken hunter " out of Westgate in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

She was known to take an interest in the movements of the city of Xxiphu .

It was rumored that curious adventurers could find her in the private rooms of the Shell Queen or inspecting the ships in Westgate's harbor.

Possessions

She wielded a glowing, 30‑foot-long (9.1‑meter) spear inscribed with runes.

Relationships

Sashara worked and took meals with Yu Kai , a fellow famous kraken hunter.

History

As of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Sashara was regarded as perhaps the only so-called "kraken hunter" in Westgate who might have actually slain a kraken .
