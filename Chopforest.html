Chopforest was a salad dish eaten by wood elves and in Amn .

Availability

It was a traditional forest salad of the wood elves.

Chopforest was on the menu at the Battlescarred Bard inn on the Trade Way in Amn circa 1369 DR and later at the Yawning Portal in Waterdeep . Senior cook Jarandur Tallstand had adapted it to add more healthy meal options to the menu. A complimentary chopforest from Durnan was considered a good omen for one's delve. Description

The Battlescarred version was a mixed green salad with cabbage and sprouts , together with wildshoot onions and mallowfern and garnished with basil and black olives , but not so much as to overpower the other flavors.

The Yawning Portal version was similar but instead garnished with black olives, fennel , garlic , and pepper , with a tangy vinaigrette dressing.
