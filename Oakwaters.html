Oakwaters was a bathing pool open to the public located in the city of Daerlun in Sembia in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The pool was located in the north section of the city near the House of Firehair .

Description

At roughly 100 feet (30 meters) in diameter, Oakwaters was quite large and rivaled the pond found in the nearby Wild Wood in size.
