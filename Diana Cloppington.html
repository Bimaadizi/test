Diana Cloppington was an individual who worked at the Witchlight Carnival as a ticket-taker for the carousel.

Description

Due to a deal she made with a hag of the Hourglass Coven , Diana was cursed with a physical form that resembled a piebald centaur .

Personality

While she was personable and charming, Diana bore an inner sadness that became apparent over some time.

Abilities

A side effect of Diana's curse left physically incapable of discussing certain topics without retching up tree sap.

History

When she was still a human, Diana lost her beloved warhorse to some unknown fate. Hoping to be reunited with her steed, Diana ventured into the Feywild realm of Prismeer , and sought after Granny Skabatha Nightshade . Rather than bring Diana's horse back, Granny Nightshade fused Diana's body with that of her mount, transforming her into her form that so closely resembled a centaur.

After Diana ran away from Prismeer, she encountered Mister Light , one of two partners who ran the Witchlight Carnival. Mister Light felt for Diana's plight, and offered her both a job and home within the traveling Fey carnival.
