Ambletrees Arms was an inn of Athalantar in the 3 rd century DR .

Location

Ambletrees Arms was on the northern side of the Delimbiyr River .

Interior

The kitchen had stairs up and a hearth. There was a cellar in the building, and a cesspit somewhere on the property.

Services

There were drinkers, beds, and a kitchen; at least one traveler had her clothes cleaned and dried.

History

In Flamerule of the Year of Bloodflowers, 234 DR , Phaernos Bauldyn , the innkeeper, briefly hosted Elmara , a priestess of Mystra who was secretly the prince Elminster Aumar of Athalantar . She did not carry any coin, but paid her stay by magically increasing the room in the cellar and the cesspit. In exchange, she was stocked with provisions and her clothes were cleaned and dried.
