Marquox was a mind flayer prisoner of the githyanki in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR .

History

Around the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR Marquox's fellow mind flayers Tel'zact and Yeluit went inside the Crystal Labyrinth level and disappeared. Marquox followed them in but was captured by the githyanki and, very unusually for them, kept alive inside a cell.

Relationships

Marquox was a member of the illithid colony inside the Seadeeps Level with a good relationship with Tel'zact and Yeluit.
