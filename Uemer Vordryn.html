Uemer Vordryn was a king of Maurmurra in the late 9 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Curse, 882 DR , Kalgrathur Daycloaks used magic to force the king to abdicate the throne to him, and then slit his own throat, taking over Maurmurra .
