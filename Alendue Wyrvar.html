Alendue Wyrvar was a queen of Cormyr in the 13 th century DR .

Relationships

She was the wife and queen of King Palaghard II , mother of Lhannas , Salember , and Azoun III .

History

She lived from the Year of Prideful Tales, 1219 DR to the Year of Burning Steel, 1246 DR .
