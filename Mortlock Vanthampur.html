Mortlock Vanthampur ( pronounced : /ˈ m ɔːr t l ɒk ˈ v ɑː n θ ɑː m p ɛ r / MORT-lock VAN-tham-per ) was the youngest son of Thalamra Vanthampur of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Mortlock was born to Thalamra Vanthampur and her third husband in the Year of the Elven Swords Returned, 1465 DR , who on his death asked her to look after Mortlock despite his many problems.

When Mortlock was young, he was scarred across half of his face by a fire.

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , he was sent to the dungeon used by the Dead Three cultists under the bathhouse to assist them in planning attacks. After realizing he was of no use, his half-brothers conspired with several Bhaal cultists to assassinate him.
