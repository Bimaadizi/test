Goriss Steinchaser was a mage who was studying necromancy with Maferil the Gravewalker in Luskan , Sword Coast North , during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Studying necromancy in Luskan sometime prior to 1487 DR , Goriss was Maferil's third pupil. Maferil described him as an "unconventional and immature lout", but still considered him talented in the art of necromancy.
