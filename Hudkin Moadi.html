Hudkin Moadi was the halfling mayor of the city of Krenadir in Luiren in 1367 DR .

Activities

Hudkin hated adventurers of all types, and sent many to their deaths on foolhardy quests within the Mortick Swamp .

History

Hudkin wished to be an adventurer in his youth, but found the hardships of life on the road to be distasteful.
