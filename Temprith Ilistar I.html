Temprith Ilistar was the sixth King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Temprith became the next King of Westgate by seizing the throne from his nephew Farnath III upon the death of King Farnath II in the Year of the Leaning Post, 756 DR . He would only rule for a few years until he was overthrown and killed by his cousin Thartryn II in the Year of the Missing Blade, 759 DR .
