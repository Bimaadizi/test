The Beacon of Battle was a temple dedicated to Tempus within the city of Calimport .

Location

This temple was located in the Biteq Drudach , in the Qhibal Sabban , in the Shackles Ward of Calimport.

Structure

This building was constructed of red-glazed bricks, stood two stories tall, and had an outward appearance similar to what many slavers ' houses looked like in Calimshan . Swung over the main ground-floor entrance was a silver emblem of Tempus.

Activities

Beyond their religious duties, the clergy of this temple would regularly seek out suitable candidates among the slave masses of Shackles Ward for recruitment as gladiators for one of Calimport's arenas. Some slaves sought out the attention of these priests for this, as arena slaves had a slight chance of winning their freedom.
