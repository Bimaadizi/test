Uirkymbrand was a Chosen of Mystra at some point long before the 8 th century DR .

Personality

The man was self-aware about being very overbearing and not moralistic, instead being quite enthused about doing objectionable things unto others.

Description

In a vision revealed to Elminster Aumar by the goddess Mystra in 767 DR, Uirkymbrand was a bald man in red robes.

Possessions

Uirkymbrand had a crystal orb they used to scry, almost certainly a crystal ball , and he mentioned a "spell-seizing scepter".

History

One of the Chosen of Mystra, Elminster Aumar, received a vision from the goddess in the Year of the Awakening Wyrm, 767 DR . In the vision, the previous Chosen, Uirkymbrand, saw the death of a fellow Chosen, Elthaeris , as cause for rejoicing, and began considering things he would be doing to weak people.
