Friar Nolan was a priest from Caer Corwell in the Moonshae Isles in the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

The friar was a short fat man with a bald head.

Personality

Friar Nolan was a kind, benevolent shepherd of his flock. Being a believer in the gods of the Faerûnian pantheon , Friar Nolan felt the wild revelry of the Moonshae druids was blasphemous.

History

During the Festival of the Spring Equinox in the Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR , Nolan set up a white tent near the druid grove in Caer Corwell to preach the existence of gods other than the Earthmother . Despite his difference of opinion, Nolan protected the druid Robyn from Kazgaroth during the Beast's siege of Caer Corwell, suffering grievous injuries in the fight.
