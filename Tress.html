"Tress" (given name unknown) was the owner of the Dragonriders Club in Suzail , Cormyr , in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Tall and urbane, when at work, Tress dressed in leathers that resembled a dragon costume. After the Open Feast, she painted sinuous dragons on her cleavage that became visible when she leaned.

Personality

She was sympathetic to fellow servants.

Abilities

Tress was uncanny in her abilities to know who was who.

Possessions

Tress kept a chapbook by Flarm about an event from the Year of the Dark Circle, 1478 DR , which she lent to Amarune Whitewave , one of her dancers, in preparation for the Open Feast.

Activities

Though not a dancer, she was in charge of the Dragonriders Club.

Relationships

Although she was more properly called "mistress", not even the Watch called her anything other than "Tress". She was protective of her dancers and their health.

History

At the Dragonriders Club in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Tress personally ushered in Arclath Delcastle and his friends, Halance Tarandar and Belnar Buckmantle , who'd come as his guests, and saw them seated and attended to. The day after, she met with Arclath again when he came in early with the palace messenger Delnor while looking for the dancer who had entertained them the previous evening, on palace orders. Despite his attempts at bribing her, she claimed she did not know where she lived or even her name.

Despite this, she later burst into the dancer's room, with guards, to find the dancer, Amarune Whitewave , attacking Arclath Delcastle with his own dagger . He claimed quickly that it was a roleplay he'd paid for, which reduced the danger to Amarune. Tress brought them some drinks afterwards.

At a later date, Tress was present on the evening when Kathkote Dawntard 's retinue came to the Dragonriders Club looking for trouble; she ran out to find the Watch. She returned, with some improvised weapons, and the Watch in tow, which helped pacify and evict Dawntard and his cronies. After the closure of the club, Tress remained behind to clean up.

On another occasion, Tress, while working in the club, helped up Rorskryn Mreldrake , who was blackout drunk there. He briefly accused Amarune of being the Silent Shadow.

Tress later had to drag Amarune out of bed so she could perform.
