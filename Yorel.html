Yorel ( pronounced : /ˈ j ɔːr ɛ l / YORE-el ) was a veteran Zhentilar commander in Zhentil Keep in 1358 DR .

Personality

Yorel was a cautious individual well-suited to his job.

Activities

Because of his experience, Yorel was often selected to lead select patrols, bodyguard units, reconnaissance missions, and attacks near the city.
