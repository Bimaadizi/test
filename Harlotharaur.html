Harlotharaur was a black dragon wyrm in Amn .

History

Once Harlotharaur waged a campaign of destruction against humanity so Elminster defeated and bound Harlotharaur to a deep mountain cavern on the northern edge of Amn.

In 1487 DR , Elminster summoned him to Myth Drannor in order to create chaos among the Sembian mercenaries and bound Harlotharaur to that city instead.
