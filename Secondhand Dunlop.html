Secondhand Dunlop was a merchant operating in the Blacklake District of Neverwinter in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Dunlop sold supplies—possibly secondhand—to people entering the dangerous, ruined district. He had a stall near the entrance to the district in the Tatters area.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Dunlop provided a few potions of minor healing to adventurers working for Private Hawthidon Faalwun to retrieve the stolen Crown of Neverwinter . He hoped they'd get back at the Nashers for running honest merchants out of the district.
