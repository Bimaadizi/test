Thar Greenbough was a ranger living in the Rothé Valley near Neverwinter .

History

Thar was the local agent of the Emerald Enclave in the Rothé Valley during the Tyranny of Dragons ; he directed adventurers against the Cult of the Dragon in the area, including the blue dragon Venfithar .
