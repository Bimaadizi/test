Fennor was a berserker and warlord of the Uthgardt Elk tribe during the Elemental Evil crisis.

History

Fennor led a small raiding band of the Elk tribe. In 1491 DR , following visions of her daughter, Fennor led her men to a mound near the Nettlebee ranch. There Fennor, as prophesied, met the old chieftain Javor , now risen as a revenant . She offered the support of her men to him in raiding the ranch. In truth, she wanted use this occasion to raid and destroy the ranch anyway.

Personality

Fennor was a bloodthirsty leader, wanting massacres. However, she respected Javor and if asked by him Fennor could be convinced to abort the raid.
