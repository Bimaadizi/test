House Alibakkar was one of the most powerful and influential Amnian mercantile families during the 14 th , and 15 th centuries DR . They had significant holdings in both Trademeet , and the capital city of Athkatla .

Holdings

The family owned nearly every parcel of land along the Tethir Road between the cities of Trademeet and Murann .

History

House Alibakkar was founded in the Year of the Broken Helm, 1302 DR .

As of the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , the Alibakkars had been involved in a great feud with House Lurraxol for a number of generations. According some knowledgeable citizens of Trademeet, the two houses traced their lineage back to the same family.

By the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the Alibakkar family openly held the title of Namarch in the ruling Council of Five .
