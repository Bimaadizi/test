Bruc and Pishe's Emporium , previously known as Tina Turki's Good Time Bazaar , was a business located in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate , within the infamous Guild 's territory in the late 15 th century DR .

Location

Bruc and Pishe's was a small two-story building with its entrance facing the hidden entrance down into the Guild's Guildhall bar and hideout. By the late 15 th century DR, the business' sign was old, broken, and rusted with "Bruc and Pishe's Emporium" written on it as well as its previous name, "Tina Turki's Good Time Bazaar."

History

At some point before the Absolute crisis of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Bruc and Pishe's Emporium was abandoned and claimed by the Guild. During that time, the shop was used to store shipments of the Guild, as well as books, letters, and reports, including the Guild's communications with certain members of the patriar families of Baldur's Gate as well as with the Counting House .
