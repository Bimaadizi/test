Tchinggult was a large town found in northwestern Thindol on the Chultan Peninsula .

Geography

Tchinggult was located along the banks of the River Tching in the lowlands between the Sanrach Mountains and Tching Mountains .

History

Tchinggult was once a prominent waystop along the road from Narubel to Thindar . The settlement fell into a state of disrepair when trade diminished. A chaos roc began hunting in the area around the same time, compounding Tchinggult's problems.

Inhabitants

As of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Tchinggult was home to outlaws and small bands of lizardfolk . Gangs of criminals vied for control on of the streets, leading to nightly bloodshed.

Activities

Several small markets operated during the day. Items that were illegal elsewhere in Thindol could be found here.
