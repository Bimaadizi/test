The Brimstone Archon was a magma brute who was summoned at the Underdark Arena beneath Cormyr during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , the Brimstone Archon served as a summoned gladiator of sorts in the magma-filled Underdark Arena of the red dragon Aptoryx , alongside Scintilla and Finnfaran .
