Karolina Wormack was a woman in the late 15th century.

History

In 1484 DR , during the orc and drow next assault on the Silver Marches , Karolina lived with her brother Giles Wormack in Sundabar . They assisted in a speech made by knight-commander Aleina Brightlance just before the fall of the city.
