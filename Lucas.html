Lucas was a guard at the Revel's End prison on the Frozenfar in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the late 1490s DR, Lucas was one of four guards who escorted a new prisoner, the hobgoblin Gorg , to his cell, shoving him in with Holga Kilgore and Edgin Darvis .
