Shandaril's Workbook was an arcane spellbook penned by vile wizard Shandaril . It contained 9 spells ranging from common to unique or rare.

Description

Shandaril's Workbook was shaped like a crescent moon. It was five handwidths tall and three handwidths across. The front and back cover were glossy black ebony. Copper hinges held the workbook together at the curve of the crescent. The workbook contained 26 pages of thin copper sheets that contained 25 spells .

History

Shandaril hid a unique spell that she developed called Shandaril's tracer that allowed her to track down, and even teleport to, whoever carried her workbook. She deliberately allowed her workbook to be found by others so that she could hunt them down later and eliminate them as rivals while taking their magic.
