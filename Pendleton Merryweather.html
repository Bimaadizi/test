Pendleton Merryweather was a halfling rogue and adventurer in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He was an insatiable learner and became an adventurer just because it allowed him to learn so much more than if he had stayed at home.

Possessions

He wore a set of bracers of defense and wielded a shortsword of quickness . He also carried a deck of illusions .

History

He had recovered a halfling artifact for the city of Kathtyr, and subsequently was made Viscount of the People of Kathyr as reward.
