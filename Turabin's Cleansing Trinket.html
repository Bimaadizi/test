The Turabin's Cleansing Trinket was an enchanted ring , named after one of the ancient wizards of Myth Drannor . It was part of the Fading Rings set.

Description

Turabin's ring was a band of crystal that sparkled in light.

Powers

The Cleansing Trinket granted the wearer increased protection from magic and allowed them to dispel magic at will. The ring possessed nine charges of dispelling .

History

In 1369 DR , Turabin's Cleansing Trinket came to be possessed or possibly crafted by a mysterious monument, the Great Stone Head , located in the Nightingale Court in the ruins of Myth Drannor . In exchange for one of the eidolons , small totem statues depicting various gods and individuals, the monument dispensed powerful magic rings, one being the protective band of crystal, Turabin's Cleansing Trinket .
