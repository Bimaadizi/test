The Hidden Glades was a temple dedicated to Moander , the Rotting God.

Location

The temple was located within the Chondalwood near the settlement of Torsch .

Inhabitants

Dalchatha Maereegh , the Mouth of Moander during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , was in charge of the temple.
