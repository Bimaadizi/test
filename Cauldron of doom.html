The Cauldron of Doom was a unique magical cauldron of necromantic power.

Powers

The cauldron was used to animate human corpses as undead . When a human body was placed inside it, the corpse would be revived as an undead monster similar to a zombie but twice as strong and harder to hit in combat. The zombie was under the control of the one who threw it in, and with no apparent limitation on the cauldron's frequency of use, its user could raise an army of darkness in relatively little time.

History

The cauldron was fashioned in ancient times by a blacksmith under the guidance of Kazgaroth . As of 1357 DR , it was thought to be located in the Castle of Skulls in Llyrath Forest on the island of Gwynneth in Moonshae .
