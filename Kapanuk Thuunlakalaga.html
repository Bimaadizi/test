Kapanuk Talltree Thuunlakalaga was a young warrior of the Thuunlakalaga clan of goliaths that lived on Wyrmdoom Crag in the Spine of the World during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

He was a brave individual who was ready to take up arms when presented with danger.

History

Kapanuk went missing as part of a hunting party in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . After being separated from his group, Kapanuk had been abducted by duergar of the Sunblight clan and taken back to their mountainside fortress in the Spine of the World mountains.
