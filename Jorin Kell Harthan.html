Jorin Kell Harthan was a captain of the Simbul's Guard in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Jorin went to Araevin Teshurr explaining that he must go to the royal palace escorted by Jorin. The regent Phaeldara ordered Jorin to help Araevin's party to journey to Sildeyuir .

Later, he joined Araevin's party in their search of the Gatekeeper's Crystal .
