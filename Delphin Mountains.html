The Delphin Mountains were a small mountain range found in northern Tashalar on the Chultan Peninsula of southwest Faerûn .

Geography

The mountain range formed a triangular mass of peaks measuring roughly 100 miles (160 kilometers) on each side. The northern most area of the mountain range bordered the Shining Sea , forming the backbone for the Delphingulph . The southern parts reached down into the dense Black Jungles . The jungle crept passed the Delphin Mountains on their western edge through the Tashan Gap .
