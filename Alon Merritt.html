Alon Merritt was the captain of the Marigold , a vessel that sailed to and from Chult during the late 15 th century DR

Possessions

Merritt did not keep many personal effects in the captain's cabin, only weather reports and other records taken at sea.

History

During or before the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Captain Merritt's ship was taken over by a mercenary captain and his crew working for Declan Cardew . The hired killers used the Marigold to ambush a group of adventurers investigating a lost Chultan colony.
