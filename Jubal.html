Doctor Jubal was a businessman in the Moonsea in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Doctor Jubal managed the wandering Doctor Jubal's Carnival of Curiosities . He was always in his wagon counting his money or dealing with the day-to-day running of the carnival. He never asked questions of his workers.

History

Doctor Jubal had managed his carnival for almost ten years. At one point, he bought a strange mirror maze in Waterdeep . Circa 1486 DR , he hired the doppelganger Tak , promising to help her against the Great Law of Humanity in Hillsfar . However, he did not truly care about it and when Tak discovered this, she subdued Jubal and took his position. Jubal was imprisoned inside the mirror maze together with Berl Cuttler , Aelia , Weet Greenspen , and others.

Rumors

Some said that Jubal was a wizard from Hillsfar who had lost his magic or else a member of the Red Plumes .
