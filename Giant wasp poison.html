Giant wasp poison was a type of venom secreted by the giant variation of regular wasps , most commonly in Faerûn - found inhabiting the forest of Cormanthor .

Effects

Giant wasp venom was often used to coat weapons or injuring traps. When introduced into the target's bloodstream, the poison stiffened the affected creature's body, dramatically reducing their nimbleness and dexterity, eventually, leading to the victim's possible death.

Giant wasp poison was also could be used by assassins to poison foodstuffs.

Usages

Giant wasp venom was used for the final step of wands of enemy detection creation. The wands were polished using this insect substance.

History

In 1369 DR , traps of the Crypt of Kesefehon , coated in giant wasp venom, were still potent and deadly, name centuries after being set, predating the coming of the Weeping War and fall of Myth Drannor .
