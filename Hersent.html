Hersent was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Age of Discovery .

History

Hersent developed two spells during their life. The spells they invented were sepia snake sigil in 2749 NY (−1110 DR) and symbol in 2850 NY (−1009 DR) .
