Ruatheene was a wizard and a member of the Zhentarim . She led the Zhent contingent based in the valley of Arntethyl by the river Tesh .

She was a favorite of Manshoon . Though not a powerful wizard, she maintained the control thanks to the beholder Araunglauth .
