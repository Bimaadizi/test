Jonah was a news hawker who worked for the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Jonah was a dusky-skinned human child with icy-blue eyes and hazel hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Jonah could often be found peddling the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City near the Manor Gate . Jonah could not stand the broadsheet he peddled. Jonah worked for the Mouth only because his father forced him to.
