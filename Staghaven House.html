Staghaven House was the residence of the Windstag noble family in Suzail in the late 15 th century DR .

Location

The manor was close to a tower on the north end of Suzail .

Interior

The front parlor had a long table, which was used for drinking.

Defenses

The grounds were walled. A secret tunnel connected Staghaven House's grounds with their luxury stables.

History

In the aftermath of the Council of the Dragon and ensuing massacre on late Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Broryn Windstag , who had remained at home for the event, had Irlin Stonestable as a guest when his servant Aereld informed him of the chaos in the wake of the killing. Though he felt it may have been exaggerated, a soon-arriving Sacrast Handragon confirmed Aereld and explained the situation was even more dire.

Other guests arriving in that day included Traevyn Illance , who came to recruit the heirs into a plot to mind-control the King; Osbur , with a warning for the young Windstag, and a desperate Marlin Stormserpent , fearing an unknown mind-controller, actually the vampire archmage Manshoon . Illance and Marlin plotted for a way to leverage Marlin Stormserpent's blueflame ghosts to gain the King's trust.

Lord Windstag and all his servants were murdered shortly after by an unknown, sorcerous assailant, who cast a spell on the corpses that drove insane anyone who used magic to interrogate them.
