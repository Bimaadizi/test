Phaulkon ( pronounced : /ˈ f ɑː l k ʌ n / FAL-kun ) was a noble house of Cormyrean descent residing in Waterdeep .

Members

History

In the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , a Phaulkon family member from Waterdeep, Meryth Phaulkon, became romantically involved with a nobleman who revealed himself to be Georges , the leader of the reinvigorated Shadow Thieves . Her life became endangered when she convinced the guild leader to leave his criminal life behind and reveal the thieves to the Lords of Waterdeep . Meryth was kidnapped by the mysterious Xabash and held in his lair in Undermountain , until the Shadow Thieves were defeated and the noblewoman was rescued by an agent of Khelben Arunsun .
