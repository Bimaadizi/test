Laral Kroul was the main delegate for surface-dwellers in Mantol-Derith .

Personality

Laral was a cunning and mean individual. He liked to appear as a hostile stranger, but was an aggressive dealer and one of the most efficient merchants in the outpost. Nobody knew where he had all his contacts.

History

Around 1372 DR , Laral was the representative of surface-dwellers in the merchant outpost.
