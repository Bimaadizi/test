Jan Jo's tea kettle was an enchanted silver kettle that could brew three different types of curative teas depending upon what was boiled inside of it.

Powers

If just plain water was boiled within the kettle, it would come out as a mint -flavored tea that relieved imbibers of all fatigue and tiredness.

Once per day, if actual mint leaves were boiled in the water alongside honey , the kettle would produce one to four cups of tea that had the same effect as a potion of healing . However, this tea had to be consumed very soon after being poured in order for the effect to take place.
