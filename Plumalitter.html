A plumalitter , also known as a featherlitter , was a variety of litters in Maztica that had been enchanted with the pluma spell create talisman .

Description

The entire litter was weaved with feathers. The litter was roughly rectangular measuring 10 feet (3 meters) long by 6 feet (1.8 meters) wide and 1 foot (0.3 meters) thick. It could safely carry up to 750 pounds (340 kilograms).

Powers

The magic enchanting the plumalitter allowed it hover over the ground once the command word was spoken. The litter would follow whoever uttered the command word up to a speed matching a normal brisk jog. The plumalitter could alter its altitude at a rate of 30 feet (9.1 meters) per minute.
