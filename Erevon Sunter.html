Erevon Sunter was a lord in Cormyr .

Activities

Sunter commanded High Horn 's garrison.

History

In 1480 DR , Sunter saw the dismembered body of War Wizard of Cormyr Umbarl Darlhoun . Later, he asked a young war wizard for some explanations about it.

At last after hearing rumors of civil war in verge to explode he decided to retire in a isolate farm.
