A seer was a type of psion who specialized in the clairsentience discipline of psionics , honing their skills in precognitive and information uncovering powers. They were equivalent to the diviners of arcane magic .

Abilities

Seers almost exclusively utilized powers that fell within the purview of the clairsentience discipline. Their specialization in this discipline allowed seers to unlock a suite of powers that were unknown to other psions.
