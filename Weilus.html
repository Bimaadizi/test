Weilus was one of the Blades of the Old Man as of 1374 DR . On the night of Tarsakh 25 , they became aware of an intruder in Sentinelspire . While they were looking out for the intruder, they came across Lewan and Ulaan . On his orders, Dayul and Turan tried to get them to return to their room; however, this led to a fight. He took Lewan's hammer during the fight, but was killed by swarming killoren not long after.
