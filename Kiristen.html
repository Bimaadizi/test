Kiristen was Talieth 's chief maidservant. A beautiful Calishite , when Sauk came to Talieth's room to report that the Old Man of the Mountain had spoken to Lewan while he was out, she attempted to stop him, as the lady was abed together with Valmir . Sauk continued in spite of her.
