Stolyarof's table lute was a unique deep-voiced oversized lute that once belonged to Ahndri Stolyarof sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR this unique but mundane instrument was sold at the Chromatic Scale musical instrument shop located on the second floor of The Glitter Gala gem and jewelry vendor of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate . Stolyarof's table lute was among several unique instruments in Thomas C. Quirkilious 's inventory.
