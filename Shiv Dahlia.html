Shiv Dahlia was a wildheart barbarian thug who opposed the Guild crime gang of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Dahlia was a human woman with a light skin tone and strawberry-red hair. She wielded a great club in battle.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Farlin led a gang of Guild members in dealing with the Stone Lord and his men. The new kingpin secretly served the Absolute and was trying to remove the Guild's influence from across the Gate. Farlin's group faced a band of the Stone Lord's men led by the tiefling Cairos , including Shiv Dahlia. They clashed under the Wyrm's Crossing bridge in Rivington over a shipment of supplies. However, the meeting devolved into a battle and either squads' fates remained uncertain.
