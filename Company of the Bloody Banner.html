The Company of the Bloody Bandy was an adventuring company that was active in Waterdeep around 1367 DR .

Organization

The membership of the Bloody Banner was almost entirely composed of female half-elves whose alignments leaned towards evil .

Relationships

The Bloody Banner was counted among the local Waterdhavian contacts of Feldyn , the owner of the Old Monster Shop .
