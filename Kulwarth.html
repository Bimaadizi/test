Marshal Kulwarth was a scarred barbarian Zhentilar officer in the 1370s DR .

History

In 1374 DR , during the Cormanthor War , Kulwarth was the aide-de-camp of the castellan Scyllua Darkhope . With Kulwarth's help, Scyllua won a great battle against the Red Plumes .

Later, they were besieging Hillsfar when Kulwarth informed Scyllua that Fzoul Chembryl needed to see her.
