Ash mephits were a variety of mephit composed of ash.

Description

These mephits had powdery gray skin and lacked external ears. Whenever they spoke, it came out in a nasally whining tone.

Personality

Ash mephits had a perpetually depressed and gloomy disposition. They often subjected people to near-endless tales of their sorrows, boredom, and frustrations.

Abilities

Ash mephits were capable of spraying a cloud of choking ash in a 10 feet (3 meters) radius. They could gate in another of their kind once per hour. And they had an innate ability to, once per day, rally a group of good and neutrally aligned creatures into a distracting conversation, similar to the spell Leomund's lamentable belaborment .

They were immune to all forms of poison . They were impervious to harm from spells and abilities based around fire , heat, and cold. They were especially vulnerable to spells and abilities based around liquids or wind. When it came to weapons, they could only be harmed by slashing and piercing weapons that were enchanted .

Combat

Unlike some types of mephit, this variety was incapable of fighting by means of biting or clawing.

Society

Ash mephits were largely solitary creatures, though on their home plane , they lived in scattered communities. Such societies were little more than war-like hunting packs.

Relationships

These mephits regularly preyed upon the rasts of their home plane, who in turn preyed upon them.

Ash mephits sometimes acted as servants of the deity Laduguer .
