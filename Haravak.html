Haravak was a wandering half-orc ranger in Thar in 1374 DR .

Relationships

Haravak was always with his wolf companion Grùnhawr .

History

In 1374 DR, Haravak witnessed an adventuring party being abducted by some orcs , as well as large-scale orc activities around the Xûl-Jarak citadel. He hoped to sell this information to anyone interested, like the Lords of Melvaunt , the Zhentarim , or others.
