Djir Garpeth was a duergar business agent the 1480s DR .

Description

Djir had slate-gray skin, no beard, and a bald head.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Djir placed himself in a shallow cavern near Eveningstar that the Purple Dragons had recently captured from the drow . He saw the opportunity in all the heavily-armed soldiers and adventurers running about, and set about recruiting them to come fight in the nearby Underdark Arena , first to fight for the pleasure of Azena'ar , and then Aptoryx .
