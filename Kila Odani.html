Kila Odani was an inhabitant of Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Activities

Kila was in charge of the spice warehouse of the House of Al-Farif within the Warehouse District of Huzuz . She portrayed pure genius in her ability to properly handle and recognize the plethora of spices that passed through the warehouse. During the same period of time, Kila's beautiful niece worked as a record-keeper and administrator of Al-Farif's warehouse in Sikak .
