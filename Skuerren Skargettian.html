Skuerren Skargettian was a duergar tavern owner in Skullport .

History

Skuerren managed the Thrown Gauntlet , a tavern known for offering violent spectacles of armed and unarmed combat at least once a tenday. Skuerren paid gangs to "recruit" combatants in the streets, but volunteers were welcome. A hulking trollkin bouncer named Stonebreaker kept control of the situation.
