Ulcrimmon Alskayl was a hunter of the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the 14 th century DR .

Relationships

His father, Skuulaghh Alskayl , was infamous for his hedonism and had lots of children born in wedlock and lots of bastards born out of it, leaving Ulcrimmon with many siblings and step-siblings. They formed a clan of hunters in Maskyr's Eye by around 1357 DR .

Personality

He was coarse of manners and shady of character.
