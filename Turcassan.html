Turcassan was a former noble house of Cormyr . It was situated beyond the Royal Gardens in Suzail .

The house betrayed the Crown when Melineth Turcassan , in the king's absence, sold the city of Suzail to pirates , although Jotor Turcassan remained loyal to the crown.
