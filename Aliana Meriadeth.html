Aliana Meriadeth was a druid of Chauntea and a former werefox adventurer in Raven's Bluff during the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Description

She was attractive, still retaining a fox-like coloring and cast to her skin.

Personality

She genuinely cared for her friends and tried to take care of them, offering quiet wisdom when she felt it necessary.

Abilities

Amongst other mundane expertises, Aliana was a practiced cook, herbalist, and healer and was greatly acquainted with weather prediction, the lore of animals, and religious minutia.

More of note, Aliana's wounds had begun to close almost instantly, with her strength returning twice as fast during rest; this development worried her.

Possessions

She carried a spear and sling , wore leather armor , and had a potion of ESP .

Relationships

She tried to keep track of things for her fellow adventurer, the wizard Tanis Obereck , and made note that Taldar Everwind had expressed interest in her. Akira Iko , another peer and a warrior , took her aid for granted but didn't treat her as a servant. She felt very close to Serena Worthwood . Her final fellow adventurer was the dwarf Crakadan Earthdigger .

History

As a child, Aliana only remembered being raised by a werefox, eventually becoming one herself, and then breaking down in tears at the age of seventeen in a temple of Chauntea . There her curse was lifted by a friendly priest, leaving untouched only some of the elfin features that she'd developed due to the lycanthropy . She joined the temple, and later became an adventurer, eventually heading for Raven's Bluff and forming a party.

Before she was embroiled in the conspiracies of the Red Wizard Thule , her wounds had been closing instantly for a while, much like when she was a lycanthrope, which made her worry.
