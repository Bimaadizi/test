Buzzrip Blacknee was a halfling man who operated the Blacknee's Halfhappenstance inn of Halfhap alongside his wife Broonella Blacknee in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . They were assisted by their adult children Breena Blacknee , Billyup Blacknee , and Boldric Blacknee .
