Aerix Vokototh ( pronounced : /ˈ ɑː ɛ r ɪ ks ˈ v oʊ k ə t oʊ θ / AER-ix-VOH-kuh-toth ) was a young Reghedmen of the Tribe of the Wolf who lived in Icewind Dale during the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

Due to the circumstances of his birth, Aerix was not harmed by extreme cold as much as other were.

Relationships

The young boy was the son of his clan's chieftain.

History

Aerix was born on the day of Midwinter , an occasion that was blessed by the Frostmaiden Auril .

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Aerix's camp was assaulted by the Wolf King Isarr Kronenstrom and his loyal Wolf Tribe barbarians. Aerix's father and many of his clansmen were slain in the raid, though the young boy managed to survive for some time.
