Maloren Sods was a swamp located on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Maloren Sods was found on the eastern side of the island separating the coastal town of Brannoch from the rest of the island. The swamp also prevented a branch of the Snowdown Ride from reaching Brannoch, making it the only settlement on Snowdown not connected to the road. Maloren Sods covered approximately 150 mi² (390 km²) of the island.
