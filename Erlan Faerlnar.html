Erlan Faerlnar was a cleric of Chauntea with the rank of Watchful who resided in the Bounty of the Goddess temple in Voonlar around 1372 DR .

Watchful Brother Erlan was a horticulturist who specialized in soil composition, composting, and plowing and tilling techniques.
