TypeOil

Juniper oil was a type of oil in the Realms infused with the scent of juniper .

Notable Users

The beauty-conscious citizens of Waterdeep were known to use this oil to care for their hair and eyelashes, usually by dipping combs into the oils and applying it generously to their manes. The same oil was sometimes applies to the skin for its pleasant aroma.
