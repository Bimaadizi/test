Gracie was a server who worked in the City of Coin, Athkatla in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Gracie was a flirtations and good-looking human woman with light blonde hair.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Gracie was employed by The Thumb , the innkeeper of the Sea's Bounty Tavern . The establishment was located in Athkatla's Wave District . One of notable patron whom Gracie served was the notorious Red Wizards of Thay named Edwin Odesseiron .
