Brena Whitebrow was a werewolf cleric of Selûne and one of the leaders of the Fangshields . She was the alpha female and matriarch of the White Pack , and coordinated their efforts with the greater Fangshields organization.

She had a son, Brenvol , to whom she taught the responsibilities of leading their pack.
