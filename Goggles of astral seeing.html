Goggles of astral seeing enchanted goggles that extended the wearer's vision within the Astral Plane .

Description

The lenses of the goggles were made of amber .

Powers

These goggles allowed a user in the Astral Plane to see as far as 5 miles (8 kilometers) through the astral fog, bypassing most spells of invisibility .

History

Such goggles were in use at Crosswinds Keep in the Astral Plane as of 1372 DR .
