Joyous Thresher was a brand of condiment produced in and exported out of Sembia in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Joyous Thresher was among the exotic foodstuffs sold at Turnell's Provisions in the town of Tegal's Mark in Tasseldale . As an imported good, Joyous Thresher was sold at a 10% markup.
