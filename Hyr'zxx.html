Hyr'zxx was a doomsphere beholder that resided in Myth Drannor around the late 14 th century DR .

History

The undead beholder lurked in the library of the Irithlium , which was largely blocked off by collapsed rubble and other undead creatures. It may have been later found by the Elves of Cormanthor , who arrived to fight the Red Wizards of Droic Orgul .
