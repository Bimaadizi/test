Esmeltar Red was a variety of blended wine , produced in Amn . It was exported as far as the Western Heartlands , the Chionthar Valley , and the city of Baldur's Gate , as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

This blended red wine was considered to be made using some of the worst grapes in the Lands of Intrigue . It had an unmistakingly hollow flavor.
