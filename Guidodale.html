Maracrath was a village located in Impiltur in the Northeast Faerûn , off the coast of the Easting Reach and largely populated by Vaasans and Damaran folk who spoke Chardic .

Geography

Guidodale was known as one of several overnight stops for caravans that traveled along the Merchant's Run , part of the Herald's Road that stretched between Impiltur and the Cold Lands . Guidodale stood on a crossroad where the road from Timbertown to the northwest joined Merchant's Run. The town itself was located between Maracrath to the southeast and Mulltown to the northwest.

Defenses

Guidodale received protection from Ilmwatch 's garrison contingents that traveled with merchant caravans between the Cold Lands and the Easting Reach.
