Tharsult was an island in the Shining Sea and an independent nation.

Locations

Its port was Urbeth .

History

Shou efforts to establish an embassy or enclave such as a Shou Town on Tharsult were underway around 1374 DR .

By 1479 DR , the island was larger in area than it was before the Spellplague .
