Sorynth's Silverware was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Location

Sorynth's Silverware was located in the north section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on west side of the Street of the Sword . The rear of the shop faced Elsambul's Lane .
