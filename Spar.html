Spar was a squirrel that lived in or around the Emerald Grove during the late 15 th century DR .

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Spar and his fellow squirrel Corinna came upon the tiefling woman Alfira playing her lute . The pain of her singing was so painful, the pair could barely remain in her vicinity and were nearly forced away.
