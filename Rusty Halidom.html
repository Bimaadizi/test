Rusty Halidom was a nightclub in the city of Waterdeep in the 12 th century DR and was among the city's first nightclubs.

Location

This nightclub was situated on the corner of the northern entrance to Anchoret's Court , in Waterdeep's Southern Ward , on the site that would later hold the home of Kappiyan Flurmastyr .

History

On one night, prior to the 14 th century DR , two inebriated wizards got into a magic fight and ended up destroying the Rusty Halidom. All the dancers, guests, and wine that were inside the nightclub at the time were lost.

Rumors & Legends

In the years following the Rusty Halidom's destruction, rumors persisted that treasure was lost in the wreckage and was later paved over.
