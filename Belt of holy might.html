Abilities

These belts granted the same boons as a belt of priestly might and warding , except the duration of magic circle against evil or against good lasted for 110 minutes and the wearer was given the ability to cast word of recall once per day, limited to the wearer and a temple devoted to the deity of which the belt was associated.

History

Like its cousin items the belt of priestly might and belt of priestly might and warding , this belt first appeared in use after the Time of Troubles .

Creation

To create a belt of holy might , the spells barkskin , bull's strength , magic circle against evil or against good , and word of recall were cast.
