Elmarr Cardellith was a merchant lord in Sembia during the 14 th century DR .

Personality

He was quite ruthless and cruel. According to his wife, he was somewhat sexist, not considering her fit for conversing in many topics.

Possessions

He was quite rich.

Relationships

He had four daughters with Nouméa Fairbright , who married him and took his name, becoming Noumea Cardellith.

History

Elmarr Cardellith was married to Noumea for some time between 1364 DR and 1373 DR . During the span of time in which they were married, she bore him four children. Later, he attempted to have her killed; failing that, he paid her to stay away and changed churches in the meantime. He got their marriage annuled at his new church, and then remarried.
