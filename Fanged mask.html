A fanged mask was a magic half- mask adorned with sharp teeth that gave the wearer the ability to land devastating biting attacks in battle.

Description

To a person not versed in the Art , this half-mask appeared to be nothing more than one of the fanciful masks often worn by the nobility of Waterdeep at masquerade balls. This particular type of mask though had a row of sharp feline teeth at the bottom. The eyeholes of the mask had lenses, preventing the wearer from using other lensed objects at the same time.

Powers

The wearer of a fanged mask was granted an ability to land devastating biting attacks in battle. The damage of the bite bore a +1 enchantment and could leave the bitee literally stunned for 6 seconds.

Creation

The creation of a fanged mask required knowledge of crafting wondrous items and a spell of spiritual weapon .
