Silver pendant was an unassuming and duty but enchanted amulet , delicately engraved with the Harper symbol.

Powers

The wearer of this silver pendant was granted the ability to cast the guidance spell at will.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , this silver pendant was found on skeletal remains of a humanoid atop a small cliff southwest of the Emerald Grove in the Western Heartlands along the River Chionthar .
