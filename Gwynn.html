Gwynn was a Netherese arcanist and inventor .

Description

Gwynn was a prominent mage who specialized in inventive spells .

History

In −909 DR , Gwynn invented the spell feign death , which was originally known as Gwynn's feign death . About a hundred years later, the arcanist invented another spell, vampiric touch , which was also originally named after Gwynn, and was known as Gwynn's vampirism .

Circa −700 DR , Gwynn's name was shouted along with those of other prominent arcanists by Sysquemalyn when she and Candlemas tried to escape the Nine Hells .
