Feldar's Trail , also called the Mosstree Trail , was a trail that ran through the Vast , connecting the town of Mossbridges and the village of Fallentree .

From west to east, it began at Mossbridges, ran through Highbank Forest and Dark Hollow , and ended at Fallentree. On the way, it linked with the Tantras Trail , the Hlintar Ride , and Blaern's Trail .
