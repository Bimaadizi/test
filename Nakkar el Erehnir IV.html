Pasha Nakkar el Erehnir IV was the fifth bakkal of the Erehnir Dynasty of the Second Age of Calimshan .

History

Nakkar IV was born in the city of Volothamp in eastern Calimshan . He lived and reigned sometime between the 22 nd and 24 th centuries before the Dalereckoning .

Nakkar IV is most famous for having his image immortalized in stone in the cliffs of Volothamp. Each of the 30‑foot-tall (9.1‑meter) Eight Erehnirs was a different representation of the bakkal—as ruler, priest, warrior, djinni , efreeti , dao , marid , and sun god .
