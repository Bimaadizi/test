Telliam was a human mercenary who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 th century DR .

Description

Telliam was a slender woman with dark hair.

Activities

Telliam was one of the Mintarn mercenaries hired by the New Neverwinter organization to protect Neverwinter. She was an aide to Lord Dagult Neverember .

History

Around 1479 DR , Telliam contacted a group of adventurers to help her solve a series of mysterious murders in Neverwinter. When a plaguechanged aboleth of the Abolethic Sovereignty , who was the one causing people to go mad and kill one another, noticed their intervention, it launched a psionic attack against them, attempting to mind-control them. Telliam was lucky to pass out instead of being mind-controlled.

She was one of the few Neverwinter Guards who survived the aboleth's attack.
