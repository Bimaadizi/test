Feta was a xorn ruffian inhabiting the Great Dismal Delve on the Elemental Plane of Earth in 1367 DR .

Personality

Feta was extremely violent and would attack first before trying to communicate, especially if he was hungry for gold .

History

He was a runaway slave from dao masters.
