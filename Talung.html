Talung was a town in Tabot .

Geography

It stood in the middle of an open plain on the road between Chophon and Motra , and near the border with Shou Lung .

History

In Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) , several small villages south of Talung were menaced by a man-eating tiger . Though a bounty was put on its head, it cunningly escaped all the hunters. People began to fear it was a magical creature, even a spirit or god disguise and bringing divine retribution.

Inhabitants

Nako Nang , a merchant adventurer and wu jen , had a small house in Talung but was often away. A servant minded the house and was well-paid and happy.
