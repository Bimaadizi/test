Things a Knight Should Know was a book of musings penned by Myrintar Hasantar , Sage of Suzail , in the early 14 th century DR .

History

Things a Knight Should Know was published in the Year of the Mace, 1307 DR . A copy of the book was preserved within the library citadel of Candlekeep on the Sword Coast as of the late 14 th century DR.
