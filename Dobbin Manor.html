Dobbin Manor was the home of Earl Ruther Dobbin , Lord Mayor of Stagwick .

Defense

Dobbin Manor was guarded by a contingent of 50 soldiers.

History

In 1366 DR , the verbeeg runecaster Basil sneaked into the manor and stole a copy of A Full History of the Dobbins of Stagwick , by Neville Dobbin , the 35 th Earl of Stagwick.
