Hafren Ember was an elf sorceress in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Hafren had backswept red-brown hair and pale skin. She wore a bright green and yellow robe .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Hafren joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp outside the ruins of Myth Drannor . She provided training and guidance to aspiring sorcerers in the camp.
