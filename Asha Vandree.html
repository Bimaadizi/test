Asha Vandree was a drow priestess of Lolth in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Asha was ambitious enough to dream of ruling the outpost, but not so courageous as to openly challenge Ilvara Mizzrym , also knowing that for a permanent assignment she needed to also impress her superiors in Menzoberranzan .

Relationship

Asha initially considered Ilvara Mizzrym as a model to imitate, but changed her mind after the senior priestess discarded Jorlan Duskryn without hesitation.

Activities

Asha was a drow of Menzoberranzan and a member of House Vandree , living in the Velkynvelve outpost, acting as a junior priestess and second-in-command to Ilvara.
