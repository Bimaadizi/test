The Free City of Qadib , also known as the City of Wands , was located along Al-Haul River . Qadib housed more magic users than any other place in northern Zakhara , topped only by Huzuz and Rog'osto in all the Land of Fate .

Trade

The city was also known for its universities, which earned it the second title " City of Sages ", and the fabrication of Uther , a yellow-orange dye .
