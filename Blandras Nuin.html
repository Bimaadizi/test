Blandras Nuin was a textile merchant of Neverwinter who was sacrificed to Shar and impersonated in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Description

Auvrarn Labraster thought himself to be way more handsome than Blandras was.

Personality

To the Sharran priestess Meira , he was innocent and gentle.

Possessions

Of note, Blandras Nuin owned a grand home in Prendle Street .

Activities

The man was a trader in textiles.

Relationships

Blandras Nuin had six live-in servants, one of them called Alaithe .

History

The textile trader seldom traveled; when he did, it was for business that took him to Everlund and Silverymoon .

In 1369 DR , he was sacrificed by the Sharran priestess Meira, who gave his likeness to Auvrarn Labraster, a conspirer. Auvrarn killed his servant Alaithe not long after, replacing her with two other servants.
