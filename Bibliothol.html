OriginNeverwinter , Sword Coast North

Bibliothol was a substance that was invaluable in preventing tanned leathers from becoming affected by the irreversible decay known as "red rot."

Description

This paste-like consolidant was created using lanolin , animal fat, and slaked lime . The substance received a wide use among the scholars and seminarians of Neverwinter and the Sword Coast North .

History

By the late 15 th century, this paste, usually used only in academic circles, gained popularity among wearponmakers who practiced restoration and protection of old leather grips and straps.
