Malimak was a whimsical mage who lived some years before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Malimak was known for creating a wand of wonder to be presented as a prank gift to his rival. It was unknown if anything harmful came out of it, but Malimak quickly relocated soon after the wand of wonder was gifted. The wand eventually ended up in the illithid lair underneath Athkatla 's Temple District in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
