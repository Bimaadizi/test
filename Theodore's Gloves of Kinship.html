Theodore's gloves of kinship were enchanted gauntlets from the ancient elven city of Myth Drannor .

Description

Theodore's gloves of kinship were masterfully-sewn leather gauntlets.

Powers

These gauntlets possessed magics that enhanced the wearer's natural charisma, appeal, and reaction speed in battle. Additionally, they allowed the wearer to cast the charm monster spell at will. The gloves held four charges of the spell.

History

The gloves of kinship belonged to Theodore of the Myth Drannor, predating the city's fall in the Weeping War . By the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Theodore's gloves of kinship were hidden within the deeper levels of the Elven Catacombs underneath Castle Cormanthor . The gloves were locked in a room that survived centuries undisturbed. Along with the gloves of kinship , the room held several healing potions as well as a set of keys to the lower chambers of the ancient elven dungeon.
