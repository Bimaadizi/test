Jester's Chain was a unique set of enchanted chainmail that once was worn by Dekirh Fasthands , a jester - bard from the Sword Coast , who lives some time before the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

Originally, this set of magic armor looked absolutely mundane. However, Dekirh Fasthands wore it to stage and painted it in a bright jester fashion: in reds, blues, and golds .

Powers

In addition to its potent +4 defensive enchantment, Jester's Chain also offered the wearer increased resistance to slashing and crushing damage.

History

Jester's Chain was a gift to Dekirh Fasthands from his friends who were worried for the jester's well-being as he insisted on performing in some of the rowdiest taverns across the Sword Coast some time before the mid-14 th century DR. The armor was to protect the bard from things that often was thrown at him during the performances: rotten produce and knives .
