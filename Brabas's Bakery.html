Brabas's Bakery was a shop in Ravens Bluff operated by Brabas Garlandros in the 14 th century DR that sold pies and various pastries.

Location

This bakery was located in the neighborhood of Altarside , in Ravens Bluff's Temple District , standing roughly two blocks away from the Singing Sword Inn .

Services

Among the store's selection of pies was the common apple pie . The average cost of a pie at this establishment was around 1 gold piece .
