Malakos was a notable wizard inhabiting the city of Yhep along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He made potions for amorous merchants and contraceptives for the heirs of Yhep's wealthy families.

Malakos was secretly a devout follower of Iyachtu Xvim and a member of the Twisted Rune who monitored events in Yhep.
