Chalak was a human fighter serving in his tribe's army in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

A strong and dexterous man, he wielded the bow with great skill, while also serving his tribe by making both bows and arrows of excellent quality.

Possessions

He wore padded armor and a ring of warmth , while wielding an enchanted bow.

Relationships

All males in Chalak's family served alongside each other in the tribal army.
