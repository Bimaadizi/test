Olin was an ex-adventurer in Daggerford around the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR . As an adventurer he had particularly bad luck.

History

Olin's ill fortune changed when he and his fellows discovered a large amount of money. He decided to retire in the first place he came across, the town of Daggerford.

He lived in a house in the southeast section of Daggerford at the intersection of Water Street and Kaoth Alley near the River Gate .
