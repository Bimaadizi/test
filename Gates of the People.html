The Gates of the People referred to the entrances into the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz that were used by servants or commoners in 1367 DR .

Location

There were two such gates located in the southeast corner of the Palace of the Enlightened Throne on either side of Prince Tannous al-Assad 's private residence.
