Khavalanoth was a juvenile green dragon dwelling in Waterdeep circa 1372 DR .

He was the offspring of Chloracridara and Grimnoshtadrano , the Riddling Dragon of the High Forest.

He served as the mount and guardian of Maaril , the Dragon Mage, as the price of a pact between him and Grimnoshtadrano.
