The Sisterhood of the Oaks were a mercenary company based in the Chondalwood around 1358 DR .

History

They were a group of rangers who fought during the war against the arcanaloth Yrkhetep .

Membership

The Sisterhood were female trackers, archers, and scouts. They were all vegetarians, never harming fish, birds, and other "children of nature".

Contracts

The Sisterhood took 1 gold piece for a day for each member. Usually they acted as escorts or scouts, or as a special attack force.
