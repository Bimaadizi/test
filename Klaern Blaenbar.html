Klaern Blaenbar was a thief from Hastarl in the early-to-mid 3 rd century DR .

Description

He was a redhead.

Personality

Klaern didn't trust the conclusions of other people easily, and habitually didn't hide his scorn.

Activities

Klaern was a thief.

Relationships

He was not well-trusted by fellow thieves. At some point, he had a fling with Tassabra .

History

Klaern and his siblings were thieves in Hastarl through some point in Year of the Black Flame, 229 DR or Year of the Wailing Dryads, 230 DR . They had become acquaintances of local thieves Farl , Eladar the Dark, Jhardin , Rhegaer and Chaslarla , and participated in a meeting where the thieves decided to unite into a gang, the Velvet Hands to protect themselves from the Moonclaws gang. Klaern proposed himself as a leader, but was rebuffed, and swore vengeance.

Klaern, alongside his brother Othkyn, were soon killed by the Velvet Hands on the orders of Farl , their leader.

Rumors & Legends

Farl believed the Blaenbars were going to expose them to the Moonclaws, a fellow band, and had them marked for killing.
