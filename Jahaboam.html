Jahaboam was a merchant of trinkets and far-flung wares from the City of Coin, Athkatla , in Amn in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Jahaboam was a large man with pale skin.

History

Jahaboam's street stall could be found by Athkatla's docks in the Wave District in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR . At that time, Jahaboam's stock consisted of souvenirs and trinkets as well as some simple weapons and armor . The selection also included several more common potions and a certain enchanted keepsake locket, favored by Jaheira , a companion of Abdel Adrian .
