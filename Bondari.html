Bondari , also known as Bondari Quickhand or Bondari Halfman , was a novice adventurer and thief .

Possessions

The adventurer owned a standard set of leather armor and a shortsword .

History

Bondari first lived in a peaceful village in the Dales . He decided to leave to seek fame and fortune, and met his fellow adventurers, Nanoc and Tim Goldenhand in a tavern . From there, they foolishly decided to enter the Underdark , and he encountered the mad wizard Iycanth 's creations. The creatures quickly petrified the trio, turning them to stone.

Rumors

His companions mocked him for not being able to backstab a floating eyeball.
