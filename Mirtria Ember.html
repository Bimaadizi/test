Mirtria Ember was the founder and matriarch of the Neverember noble family of Waterdeep during the late years of the 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Mirtria Ember was the wife of Vers Never .

History

According to some sages, after Mirtria and Vers Never married, they founded the Neverember noble family.
