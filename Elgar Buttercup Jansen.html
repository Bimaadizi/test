Elgar Buttercup Jansen was a brother of Jan Jansen , a gnome inventor, illusionist , and a turnip aficionado, who lived in Athkatla , Amn , during the mid–14 th century DR .

History

According to Jan Jansen's highly reliable statement, his brother, Elgar Buttercup, died in a fire sometime before 1369 DR and was burned to charcoal .
