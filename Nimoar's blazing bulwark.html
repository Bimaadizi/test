Nimoar's blazing bulwark was a magical shield employed by the famed Nimoar the Reaver , War Lord of Waterdeep .

Description

The bulwark was a heavy steel shield fitted with spikes . The shield weighed about 20 pounds (9.1 kilograms) and would sell for around 12,480 gold pieces in most markets of Faerûn .

Powers

The shield itself was enchanted to act as a bashing shield, while the attached spikes were enchanted to act as a flaming weapon.

History

This weaponized shield was carried into battle by Nimoar the Reaver during the First Trollwar of the Year of Fireslaughter, 932 DR , where it served well against the regenerating giants .

After Nimoar's death, the blazing bulwark came to the Waterdhavian family of Silmerhelve , where it was passed down for generations. When heir Laerlos Silmerhelve II left Waterdeep and his family to join the Midnight Men mercenary group, he took the shiled with him. Laerlos fell in the Battle of Turnstone Pass in the Year of the Boot, 1343 DR , and the shield was buried alongside his body in the Cairn of Moonlight .
