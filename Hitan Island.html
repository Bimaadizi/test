Hitan Island was an island within the Golden Gulf of interior Zakhara .

Geography

Hitan Island sat approximately 30 miles (48 kilometers) offshore due west of the mainland of the northern section of the Cities of the Pantheon region. The elongated island measured roughly 60 miles (97 kilometers) in length and 15 miles (24 kilometers) wide. Much of the shore was rocky. There were no settlements on Hitan Island as of 1367 DR . Four smaller unamed islands lay off of Hitan Island's northeast corner.
