Amaimon ( pronounced : / ɑː ˈ m aɪ m ʌ n / a-MY-mun listen ) was a king among the azer on the Elemental Plane of Fire .

Description

Amaimon was legendary for his physical and mental prowess. He was larger, stronger, and wiser that any azer on record.

Relationships

Amaimon was one of the key allies of the good Archomental of Fire Zaaman Rul .
