A wand of color was a magical wand that allowed the user to change the color of any bolt of cloth or garment, as per the alteration spell , color .

Description

This type of wand was crafted in the shape of a finely made painter's brush. It was made with ease-of-use in mind and could be recharged quite simply, with a single casting of the spell color .
