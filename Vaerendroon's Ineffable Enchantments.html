Vaerendroon's Ineffable Enchantments was an arcane spellbook penned by Vaerendroon , a wizard known to trade other magic to acquire specific spells . It contained six spells ranging from common to unique or rare.

Description

This was a thin tome bound with red rothé hide. Six vellum pages were sewn into the tome, each containing a single spell. Each page was edged with electrum . Both the front and rear hide covers were stretched over slate with electrum corner caps. The title was branded in silver Thorass on the front cover.
