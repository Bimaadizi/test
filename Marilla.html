Gauntlet Marilla was a soldier of the Flaming Fist that served in the 15 th century DR .

History

Marilla was one of the soldiers present at the Waukeen's Rest inn when it was sacked by goblin cultists originating from the Shattered Sanctum in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . She died in the assault and her corpse lay among the many other casualties resulting from the emergent Cult of the Absolute .
