Volo's rejuvenating ring was an enchanted ring created by Elminster Aumar for his friend Volothamp Geddarm some years before the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

This small unassuming ring granted the wearer increased vitality and, once per day, the wearer could cast cure critical wounds spell .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the writer found himself swept away into a plot that crossed the merchant princess Sa'Sani , with whom he was enamored, and an interloper deity from another world called Zehir . When the god's avatar was slain by a group of adventurers who worked for Sa'Sani, Volo gifted them this magic ring as a gesture of a personal gratitude.
