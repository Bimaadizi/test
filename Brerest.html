Brerest was one of the bandits under Helm Stoneblade in the early 3 rd century DR .

Abilities

On foot, he was among the fastest in his group.

Relationships

He was a Knight of Athalantar under Helm Stoneblade , and later a bandit.

History

After a successful ambush by the bandits, slaying a dozen of the armsmen under the Magelords of Athalantar, a patrol of armsmen found the way to the bandit hideout. Brerest and fellow bandit Eladar were dispatched to run out and slay any runners back that might report the location to the main group, but they were pinned down by crossbowmen. Though Brerest managed to slay one of the attackers, he was in turn slain.
