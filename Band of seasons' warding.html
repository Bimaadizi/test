The band of seasons' warding was a magical silver ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The ring was blessed with the power of the four seasons, and gave a strong measure of protection against the elements, natural or otherwise. The wearer found themselves shrugging off the fiercest heat and cold, withstanding blasts of lightning, and even resisting the corrosion of acids .

History

The ring was found in the tunnels of the great purple worm Yegora , who lurked below the ruins of Myth Drannor while the Elves of Cormanthor and Red Wizards of Droic Orgul fought over the city above.
