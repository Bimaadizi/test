Sahvin Sel'rue was a drow scout of Q'Xorlarrin in 1484 DR .

History

Sahvin was one of the top scouts of her city.

During the War of the Silver Marches in 1485 DR , she was part of a contingent directed by Shontiq A'Lavallier that spied on Silverymoon , until the Knights in Silver attacked, killing most of the drow. Sahvin tried to escape to the camp of a nearby orc army but the Luruar army commanded by Regis , Wulfgar , and Aleina Brightlance defeated them also.
