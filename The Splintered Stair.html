The Splintered Stair was an inn located in the city of Waterdeep during the 14 th century DR .

Location

The inn was located on the east side of Gut Alley in Waterdeep's Dock Ward in between the intersections of Drawn Sword Alley and Adder Lane .

Description

A large spiraling staircase overlooked by balconies dominated the entry of the inn. The stairway was badly damaged during an epic brawl between adventurers , giving the inn its name. The rooms for rent were large, but also dark and cold.

Inhabitants

The Splintered Stair was owned by Shalanna Duthmere during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .
