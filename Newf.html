Newf was a human pirate from the isle of Brynnlaw in the Sea of Swords in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Newf was a simple-looking man with brown hair and dressed red-hued commoner's garb.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Newf could be found drinking in the Vulgar Monkey dock-side tavern. Like so many fellow pirate customers of the rowdy establishment, Newf was an eager sea-shanty singer. I'se th' b'y that Builds the Boat was his favorite.
