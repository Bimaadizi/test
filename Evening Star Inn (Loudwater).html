The Evening Star Inn was an inn in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

It stood in the High Town area of the city.

Description

It was a quite high-quality inn, with windows that could be shuttered and private rooms that could be used to host meetings.
