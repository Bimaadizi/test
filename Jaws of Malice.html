The Jaws of Malice was a crude shortbow crafted by one of the orcish tribes that tried to carve out territory in the ruins of Myth Drannor circa 1369 DR .

Description

The Jaws of Malice was bow of apparent poor craftsmanship, carved and decorated with tribal markings.

Powers

This bow's peculiar construction allowed for an easier chance of landing a critical strike on a target; however, it was hard to handle. The Jaws was also imbued with magic enhancing its damage output, similar to the ordinary +3 enchantment.

History

In 1369 DR , the Sharpstick clan of ormyrr made a home in the Deep Halls of the Dwarven Dungeons of Myth Drannor. Constantly struggling for territory, the clan was forced out by a tribe of strong orcs , who wielded the Jaws of Malice among other arms. After the orcish tribe was driven out by the Veiled Ones , ormyrr offered the adventurers Jaws of Malice , looted off one of the orc corpses who fell in the struggle.
