Mavheran Haerlskeel was the clanlord of the gnomish hold called Graevelwood , located within the Arn Forest , during the mid-14 th century DR . She was known for being an experienced trapper and scout, as were many within her clan.

Her advisor was a wizard named Halvena Fallowfield .

Outlook

Clanlord Haerlskeel had a fear that an inevitable war between the orc tribes would soon devastate their region. Due to this, she believed in keeping her people hidden from the rest of the Silver Marches .
