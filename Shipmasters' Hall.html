Shipmasters' Hall was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The inn stood three storys tall.

Location

Shipmasters' Hall was located in the southeast section of Waterdeep's Dock Ward just off of Dock Street and Cedar Street .
