Goraun was one of the apprentices of Lady Maerjanthra Shalace in the mid-14 th century DR .

Activities

Jonczer worked under Maerjanthra Shalace , the jeweler.

Relationships

His fellow apprentices included Jonczer , Rivrel , Tantheld and Rorgel .

History

Goraun was one of Maerjanthra Shalace's apprentices while she was alive. He was working under her since at least approximately 1351 DR , the birth of Narnra Shalace , who was twenty or twenty-two years old in 1373 DR . At that time, he was a witness to Maerjanthra's plot to bear Elminster Aumar a child, on which she was aided by Khelben Arunsun , who under duress from the goddess Mystra gave her a potion to override Elminster's control over his own fertility. He recounted this to Jonczer.

Shortly after Maerjanthra's death, Goraun disappeared after a break-in killed his fellow apprentice Rorgel .
