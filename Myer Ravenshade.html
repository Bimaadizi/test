Lord Myer Ravenshade was a member of the Parliament of Peers from the noble patriar Ravenshade family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , shortly before the armies of the the Absolute attacked the Gate, Lord Myer Ravenshade voted in support of crowning lowborn Lord Enver Gortash as the city's Archduke. Myer was present for Gortash's coronation that took place in the audience hall of Wyrm's Rock . Following the coronation, he proudly proclaimed his support for Gortash' accession, stating the new Archduke was there to protect them and that the nobility was safe behind the city walls.. In a conversation with Lord Raylen Jannath , Myer voiced his approval of the steel watchers and stated that as long as Baldur's Gate doesn't let refugees in, they would have enough supplies to outlast any danger.
