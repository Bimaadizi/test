Jagen Marsk was a nobleman, wizard , and magic researcher from the nation of Turmish in the Vilhon Reach , who created the rings of resistance sometime before the 14 th century DR .

History

Being a mage, Jagen of Turmish was dumbfounded by the limitations on wizards being unable to wear more than two enchanted rings at a time. He owned a variety of wondrous bands and desired to wield them simultaneously. This desire fueled the nobleman's research into combining various enchantments in one ring. His boldest attempt was combining invisibility with teleportation , but the best he could active were the rings of resistance . Jagen settled on creating rings that offered various types of magical protection, universal protection, and a sources of magical light. Jagen made a living by creating and selling such rings and financed his obsession with creating powerful combinations of powers within rings.
