Stormreaver's scale armor was an enchanted set of scale armor used in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

Stormreaver's scale armor was forged out of a mysterious metal that absorbed thunder and lightning, giving the wearer resistance and fortifying them physically.

History

In the 15 th century DR, Stormreaver's scale armor could be found worn by scoundrels along the mountain paths of the Sword Mountains , and sold in the Mercenary Arms Depot of the city of Neverwinter .
