Raman Byrrd was a human wizard and brother of Reman Byrrd , living in Ravens Bluff in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Raman was 5 ft 6 in (1.7 m) tall and weighted 145 pounds (65.8 kilograms). He had willowy blond hair and blue-green eyes. He was near indistinguishable from his twin brother Reman, and most found it very difficult to tell the two apart, as even their speech patterns were similar.

Abilities

Raman and his twin brother Reman shared an uncanny empathetic link. The twins were able to share ideas and thoughts between them without uttering a single word.

He was capable of casting the spells alarm , alter self , audible glamer , change self , clairaudience , continual light , detect evil , detect magic , feather fall , fireball , flaming sphere , gust of wind , hold person , hypnotism , invisibility , know alignment , magic missile , mirror image , spider climb and stinking cloud .

Possessions

Raman typically wore dark brown and blue clothing, to contrast with the pale colored clothes that his brother preferred. Though sometimes he swapped outfits with his brother to confuse others.

On his person, Raman Byrrd wore his magical bracers of defense and was armed with an enchanted dagger . The brothers also owned a spelljammer ship.

Relationships

He was very close with his twin brother, and the two would sometime imitate each other to confuse outsiders.

History

The most notable event in Reman and Ramen's lives was their father's mysterious disappearance that was punctuated by the transfer of ownership of a spelljammer ship. Sceptical of their father's claim of a flying ship they were shocked to receive it upon a desolate mountain peak, not wishing to sell the last item their father purred the family fortune into, they decided instead to turn the magical vessel into an amusement ride.
