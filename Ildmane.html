Ildmane was a fire giant who, together with her husband Okssort , led an attack on Triboar sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

In the late 15 th century DR , Okssort and Ildmane went in search of pieces of the Vonindod under the orders of Duke Zalto . They led a giant contingent to Triboar, the town that stood over the site of an ancient great battle between giants and dragons . However, they were defeated by a band of adventurers.
