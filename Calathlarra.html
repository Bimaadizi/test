Calathlarra was a Runemaster of the Twisted Rune in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Calthlarra was one of the most cold and emotionless leaders of the Twisted Rune.

History

She was one of the archmages who competed for possession of the Lost Spell during a spellstorm in Tarsakh of the Year of Dwarvenkind Reborn, 1488 DR . Calathlarra tried to kill Tabra but was ultimately killed by her in the fight.
