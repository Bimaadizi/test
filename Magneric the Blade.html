Magneric the Blade was a human barbarian in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Magneric had a prominent scar across his face and a head of ashy brown hair, including a large thick beard. He wore an outfit made from hides , teeth, and bits of carapace from various beasts, and carried a spiked greatclub .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR during the Elven Crusade , Magneric joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp on the outskirts of Myth Drannor . He served as a trainer for fellow warriors who passed through the camp.
