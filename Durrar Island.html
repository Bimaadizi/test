Durrar Island was a small island in the Crowded Sea within west Zakhara .

Geography

The island was located due south of the Pearl Banks and the city of Safaq on the mainland of the Pearl Cities region. Durrar Island was wider than it was long, measuring roughly 40 miles (64 kilometers) wide and 20 miles (32 kilometers) long. The entire island was surrounded by coral reefs and two small unamed islands sat off its northeast corner. There were no settlements on Durrar Island as of 1367 DR .
