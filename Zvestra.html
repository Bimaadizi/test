Lady Zvestra was the leader of the avorals and one of the Companions of the Celestial Lion Talisid of Elysium . According to the World Tree cosmology , Zvestra resided with Talisid and the other guardinal paragons , in the House of Nature .

Abilities

Beyond the powers common to all avorals, Lady Zvestra was trained as a wizard and a cleric .

History

Zvestra was the replacement for Duke Windheir , who retired from the Five Companions in the mid- to late-14 th century DR . Her tenure was very short, however, and she was soon replaced by Dutchess Sathia for unspecified reasons.
