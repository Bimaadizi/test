The Street of Dragons was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

The Street of Dragons was located in Zhentil Keep's Common Quarter in the northeast section of the city. The road took over for the Dragon Trail at the Dragon Gate , passing southwest for just 400 feet (120 meters) before terminating at the wall of the Inner Quarter . The Street of Dragons was intersected by Border Street , Fortress Street , and Beholder Street .

Notable Locations

The Eagle of the North tavern was located along the Street of Dragons. Also found along this road were a Zhentilar barracks and guardpost.
