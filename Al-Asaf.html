al-Asaf , or the Sorrowful , served as the cavalry of the efreet army in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire .

Organization

This part of the army of the efreet had around 25,000 lancers and their mounts. They were not very good soldiers and most never returned from their assignments.

Activities

Members of this organization served as cavalry for the efreet.

Base of Operations

The City of Brass served as the base of al-Asaf.
