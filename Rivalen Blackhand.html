Rivalen Blackhand was a wizard and the proprietor of Sorcerous Sundries during the late 15 th century DR . From within the store, he imported and sold arcane components , offered fortune-telling of an unreliable nature, as well as spellcasting services, particularly those related to the betterment of luck.

Rivalen was a fairly successful businessman, earning a significant and reliable income from the shop. He was aided by his apprentice, a gnome named Gilligunn .
