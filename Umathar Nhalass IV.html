Umathar Nhalass IV was the fourth Arkhan of the Nhalass Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Umathar IV reigned from −799 DR to −780 DR , inheriting the throne upon the death of his father Umathar III .

Relationships

Umathar IV died without siring any heirs, so the throne passed to his younger brother, Darthen I , upon his death.
