A Golden Axe of the Great Rift was an enchanted axe created by the gold dwarves of the Great Rift .

Powers

It was a +1 aberration bane battleaxe .

Description

These axes were decorated with dwarven runes inlaid with gold on both sides of the blade.

Usage

They were popular in areas that had tunnels to the Underdark .

Prices

Such axes sold for around 18,000 gp
