Kitcher's Inlet was a shallow bay located on the eastern coast of Chult fed by the River Olung . It was rather unremarkable compared to the other coastal inlets of the region.

History

The bay earned its name from being "discovered" by Cormyrean explorer, Ilyber Kitcher . In truth, Kitcher incorrectly navigated his way past the Bay of Chult and reached the eastern side of the peninsula. Kitcher's Inlet was a part of the Refuge Bay .

Notable Locations

Kitcher's Inlet granted ships access to the settlements of Port Castigliar and Mezro .
