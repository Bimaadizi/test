Belgos of Selgaunt was an author on or before the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Belgos was the author of Draconic Magic: A Study on the Effects on the Nondraconic Mind , a book that had been published on or before the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , where one copy of the tome was in the Chantry of St. Sammaster .
