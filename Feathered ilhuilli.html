Feathered ilhuilli and its more powerful variant, exalted feathered ilhuilli , were a variety of artifact created to be used by cleric adventurers in the Chultan city of Port Nyanzaru in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

These feathered ilhuilli were testaments of their owners' strength and the influence of their guilds. These magic items could be created by the most competent weaponscrafters of Chult with the use of soulfired obsidian , hardened bronzewood , fanged Chultan ornaments, and lakh varnish .

Powers

Feathered ilhuilli could be enchanted to house various additional magics for each owner, in addition to boosting the owner's accuracy, battle prowess, and their ability to land well-aimed devastating and lucky strikes. When a feathered ilhuilli was wielded with a feathered teotlanextli , both items boosted eachother's magical powers, increasing the wielder's damage output, healing magics, and reducing incoming damage.

History

Feathered ilhuilli were created in the late 15 th century DR and their design suggested the items' origins to be tied to the ancient Lost City of Omu .
