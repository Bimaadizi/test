The Evermelt was a subterranean hot spring found within a cave at the base of the Reghed Glacier . It was considered a holy place by the Reghedmen barbarians of nearby Icewind Dale .

Geographical Features

The warm, tranquil waters of Evermelt appeared as a serene turquoise color that glittered like gold .

Despite its mostly still waters, Evermelt had a small undercurrent that could pull individuals beneath the surface. This current led to a small tunnel that opened up into the former lair of the white dragon Ingeloakastimizilian .

Inhabitants

As of the Year of the Iron Dwarf's Vengeance, 1485 DR , the spring was guarded by members of the Tribe of the Bear , at the behest of the Ice Witch Hedrun Arnsfirth .
