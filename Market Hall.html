Market Hall was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Council of Farmer-Grocers .

Location

The Market Hall was located along Trader's Way , in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This guildhall stood two-stories tall.

Reputation

Like other guildhalls in the Castle Ward, Market Hall was considered by locals to be a landmark.
