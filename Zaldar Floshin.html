Zaldar Floshin was a half-elven member of the noble elven House Floshin , and a sea-captain in Waterdeep sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

Personality

Zaldar was said to be a rather brooding individual.

Activities

Zaldar was the captain of the Kelpie's Kiss . He had a sprite as first mate and a Harper agent as his ship's bosun.

History

Circa 1485 DR , Zaldar was hired by the Harpers to travel in search of the ship Morkoth and its hostage King Hekaton .
