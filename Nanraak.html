Nanraak , who was nicknamed the Fierce , was a goblin chieftain inhabiting the High Country of Rashemen during the late 14 th century DR .

Personality

He earned his nickname for his belligerent attitude.

Activities

Nanraak had over one hundred loyal goblin warriors under his command.
