High Morninglord Ombital Duskroon was the high priest of the Dawn Tower , a temple dedicated to Lathander , in Ordulin in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Activities

Ombital was the leader of Lathander's faithful in Ordulin. His congregation within the temple consisted of 19 other priests and 17 followers.
