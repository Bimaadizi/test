Othyl Naerimmin was a merchant of Hastarl in Athalantar in the Western Heartlands in the early 3 rd century DR .

Activities

He was a dealer in perfumes and alcohol, amongst other activities such as smuggling and currying favor. He also arranged poisonings .

History

Othyl was a guest at a revel in the home of fellow merchant Havilyn , where one Magelord of Athalantar , Neldryn Hawklyn attempted to use the Crystal Chain of Binding and one of his own spells to capture the Magister , Dorgon Heamiiolothtar , who took exception to that. Dorgon immediately began killing a number of guests he judged lacking in character, including other magelords, and also Othyl himself.
