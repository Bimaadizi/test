The heavy boltslinger was a kind of repeating heavy crossbow found in the mines of Tethyamar during the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

This crossbow was especially effective at penetrating armor, and was imbued with powerful electrical energy that shocked those it hit.

History

Using the excellent drow hand crossbows as a base, weapon designers went to work on scaling the weapon up to a more efficient crossbow. The heavy boltslinger was an experimental prototype from this effort, and was found in Ironvale Deep after the defeat of Kir'tan Melarn , an agent of House Argith as they invaded Tethyamar.
