A shade wolf was a lupine shade .

Description

Shade wolves were wolves that had been killed and raised as shades. They were not affected by un-enchanted weapons , and thus must be battled by other means.

History

Circa the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the village of Imnesvale became haunted by the Shade Lord . The evil lord used his fell magic to kill packs of wolves and invade them with his darkness. The creatures were transformed into shade wolves and used as minions in his unholy army.
