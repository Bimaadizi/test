Shadha was a holy slayer living in Hawa in 1367 DR .

Activities

Against her will, Shadha served as an assassin of the Dark Wave for the Cult of Istishia . She was under the control of Faizah 's Waters of devotion .

Relationships

Shadha was the sister of her fellow Dark Wave members Sahlah and Setara bint Aasim .
