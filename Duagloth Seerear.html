Duagloth Seerear was a drow of House Dhuurniv involved in the Zhent invasion of Shadowdale in the Year of Risen Elfkin, 1375 DR .

History

Duagloth was the sorcerer assisting Lirdnolu Dhuurniv in commanding the drow guardposts under the Twisted Tower of Ashaba .
