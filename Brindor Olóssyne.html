King Brindor Olósynne (born 998 DR , died 1128 DR ) was a half-elf and the king of Aglarond between 1065 DR and 1128 DR as the beginning of the Olósynne Dynasty .

Relationships

Brindor had a trusted advisor named Dorin , who was responsible for the Peace Talks of Ingdal's Arm held after the battle of the same name. After the founding of Aglarond, however, Dorin suffered a tragedy and became the vampire T'Laan .

History

Brindor defeated the human army of Velprin in the Battle of Ingdal's Arm in 1065 DR, creating Aglarond and becoming its first ruler. Brindor raised an army for Aglarond, and constructed the Emmech fortress (later renamed Undumor), the citadel Glarondar , and the Watchwall .

Brindor also commissioned the original palace on the site where the Palace of the Simbul stood, but this original wooden structure was burned down during a red dragon attack.

Relatives

Brindor's son and sole heir was Althon (later Althon "the Old"). He had blood ties with Aubayreer of Milvarn .
