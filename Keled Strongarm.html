Keled Strongarm was the constable of Olostin's Hold , northwest of the High Forest , in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Keled was a human man, well-trained in combat. He led nearly a couple dozen guards for the keep and could also summon the aid of a small village militia of about the same size.
