Sulima Fastwood was a half-elven ranger inhabiting the Shining South during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Sulima lived within the town of Thruldar in Estagund on the border with Luiren . She was in charge of the town's forest patrols that scoured the surrounding woodland hunting bandits and monsters.
