Lhazantal , known as the City of Smiths , was a small town in Lapaliiya in southwest Faerûn in the mid–14th century DR .

Geography

It sat beside the Lapal Way , close to one of Lapaliiya's few iron mines.

Government & Religion

Around 1373 DR , it was led by Lambrac Dinelstrand , a master smith exiled from Lantan .

The civic deity of Lhazantal was Gond, Lord of all Smiths .

Economy

The majority of Lhazantal's businesses dealt with some aspect of metalworking. The citizens manufactured a variety of farming and other tools and hardware, decent mail armor , and sturdy weapons .

Population

In addition to the human population, Lhazantal had the second-highest population of gnomes in Lapaliiya, after Zashuma .
