Nobster was an a ranger and a thug fromthe city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Nobster was a gold dwarvish woman with a light skin tone and light brown eyes. She kept her face clean-shaven. As a ranger, she wore a simple studded leather armor and a leather helmet . In battle, Nobster wielded a heavy crossbow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Nobster was a member of a gang assembled by Churg Elvek , secretly a cultist of the Absolute , who sought to strengthen its grasp of Baldur's Gate. When a famous writer Volothamp Geddarm returned to the Gate after misadventures in the Emerald Grove and coming face-to-face with the Absolute's army, Volo became a target for Churg and his gang. The master traveler was captured by Nobster's gang and tied to a stack of smokepowder barrels, ready to be "brought to justice" for being an alleged agent of the Absolute at the hands of the gang and street gawkers. When a band known as the Absolute Adversaries interrupted the street execution, Nobster, Churg Elvek, and the rest of the gang attacked the adventurers but were defeated.
