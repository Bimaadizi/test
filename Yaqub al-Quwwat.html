Sheikh Yaqub al-Quwwat was the young leader of the House of Dubb tribe in the High Desert of west Zakhara around 1367 DR .

Description

Yaqub was tall and powerful. He was a prideful man who wished he commanded an army as opposed to a tribe of desert-dwellers.

Activities

Adventure was the foremost priority for Yaqub. He would not hesitant to drop everything in favor of roaming the land on hunting trips with his favorite companions.

Relationships

Yaqub developed a severe attraction for Ajayib 's Caliph Halima al-Wahsi . As Yaqub had no wife or children, he was driven by his attraction to the Caliph. However, his kahin predicted he would die alone.
