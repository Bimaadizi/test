Akalan was the original tongue of the Arkaiuns .

Speakers

By 1367 DR , Akalan was a language used by most of the population of Dambrath .

The Arkaiuns of the outlaw Swagdar wastes spoke a Swag creole dialect of Akalan.

The language was used by traders across Durpar and Var the Golden and possibly even as far as Zakhara .
