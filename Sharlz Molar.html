Sharlz Molar was an inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Sharlz was an wood elven man with greenish tan skin. He had dark curly hair and red eyes. The elf wore embroidered finery.

History

Iza eagerly consumed the Baldur's Mouth Gazetteer, and even though she knew one should not believe every word that the broadsheet printed, Iza still avoided associating with individuals who were bad-mouthed by the publication. Unlike his friend, Sharlz insisted that everything in the Gazette was lies and encouraged anyone who was shown in the broadsheet in bad light.
