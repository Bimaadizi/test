Penfall's Pot was the enchanted helmet that belonged to a dwarven writer, scribe, and scholar Angus Hammerfall who worked under a nom de plume of Artimal Penfall sometime before 1372 DR .

Powers

Penfall's Pot slightly reduced the wearer's hearing but improved their resistance to sonic magic and its effects. It also increased the wearer's intelligence.

History

Penfall's Pot traveled the world on the head of Artimal Penfall, often helping him on his war corresponding missions. In 1372 this helmet ended up in the hands of a dwarven criminal Karragh of Daggerford , very likely acquired by some unsavory means.
