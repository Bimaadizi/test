Activities

In addition to normal temple services, it operated as a school for training professional guards. And its priests were known by other Helmites to be powerful enough to destroy a lich .

History

In 1367 DR , the Noble Hand was expecting to receive a shipment of evil artifacts from the Temple of the Vigilant Guard in the month of Kythorn . However, the Helmites and their cargo never arrived.

Inhabitants

Around 1368 DR , Noble Hand was led by High Watchful Weaponmaster Ellym "Catsjaw" Thourin . It thrived under Ellym's leadership.

Reputation

Noble Hand was considered to be not only one of the five major temples of Helm in the Vast in the 14 th century DR , it was considered the most prominent temple of Helm in all the Realms.
