Mist cheese , also called Loudwater mist and known as ethereal cheese to elves , was a rich cheese crafted in Loudwater Vale within the Savage Frontier .

Description

Mist cheese was a soft white cheese with a unique taste and succulent bite. It was translucent if sliced thinly enough, hence its names. It was said to be among the richest cheeses available in north Faer√ªn .

Production

Mist cheese was ripened in caves in the Loudwater Vale, which led to its distinct flavor and appearance. The cheese's unique properties had never been successfully duplicated anywhere else.

Prices

It was sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue . Mist cheese could be purchased only as a load weighing 1 lb (0.45 kg) for 2 gp .

History

In the Year of the Sword, 1365 DR , Sir Rivaldo was traveling in a coach along the Trade Way from the Way Inn to Waterdeep with an assortment of fine cheeses, including Loudwater mist.
