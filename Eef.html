Sharp-eye Eef was a goblin cultist and devotee the Absolute who lived in the Shattered Sanctum during late 15 th century DR .

Description

He wore simple clothing accented with a fashionable green neck-wrap and leather-and-bone bracers . He also wore a crude, leather skullcap and an amulet bearing the holy symbol of the Absolute.

He was among the goblin cutlists branded with the mark of the Absolute .

Possessions

In battle, Eef wielded a simple wooden bow .
