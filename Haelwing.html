Lord Haelwing was a nobleman of Cormyr .

Relationships

Oskrul Meddanthyr was Lord Haelwing's bodyguard wizard.

History

Before the Council of the Dragon on the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR in Suzail , Lord Haelwing got blackout drunk during mornfeast at the Old King's Favorite , leaving his bodyguard to tattle much of arcane lore to curious fellow nobles.
