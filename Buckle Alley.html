Buckle Alley was an alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Buckle Alley was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . It originated in the east on the High Road on the border with the Trades Ward where it branched west through the Sea Ward, crossing the Street of Bells , the Street of the Sword , and Warrior's Way before terminating on the Street of Silver .

History

The alley was named for guards being reminded to buckle their sword belts before stepping foot onto the alley as it was once the home of the Shadow Thieves in Waterdeep.
