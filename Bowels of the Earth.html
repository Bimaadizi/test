The Bowels of the Earth was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tavern stood two stories tall.

Location

The Bowels of the Earth was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Trades Ward on the southeast corner of where Simple's Street joined Snail Street .

Services

The tavern was a well-known spot for hiring mercenaries .

Inhabitants

The Bowels of the Earth was owned by Blazidon One-Eye in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
