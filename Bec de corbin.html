Bec de corbins were a type of polearm in the Realms that had an infamous reputation.

Description

At a glance, this weapon bore a resemblance to a lucerne hammer . Bec de corbins had 8 feet (2.4 meters) poles, with an awl spike that was shorter and broader than that of a lucerne hammer, and a hooked blade that was the weapon's namesake and comparable to a heavy military pick 's head. The other end of the weapon was backed by either a clawed or pronged hammer head like the lucerne hammer, a flat hammer head, or even an axehead.

Weaponology

These weapons were highly specialized for piercing or slicing through heavy plate armor with their crow-beak, and then using the weapon's other end to strike the opponent.
