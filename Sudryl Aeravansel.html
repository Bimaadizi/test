Sudryl Areavansel was a former High Mage in Myth Drannor in 650 DR .

History

Sudryl was born in −471 DR .

He was the Grand Mage of Cormanthor until 53 DR when he abandoned Myth Drannor in order to live in Darkwood , owing to his love for the old trees and a grudge against Coronal Eltargrim Irithyl dating back to their youth together.

Sudryl died in 642 DR .
