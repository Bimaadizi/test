Miz'ri Mizzrym was the matron mother of House Mizzrym in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

She was considered comely but perpetually wore an ugly scowl.

History

Miz'ri was the matron mother of her house by 1361 DR and through 1372 DR .

Circa 1486 DR , she dominated the city's slave trade.

Relationships

She had two twin daughters: Greyanna and Sabal . She favored Greyanna, the eldest, until Greyanna's death in 1372 DR at the hands of her brother Pharaun .
