Sorn was a fighter in Thay and an agent of the zulkir Lauzoril in 1357 DR .

History

In 1357 DR, under Lauzoril's orders, Sorn posed as a trader and went to participate in the tournament held in Mourktar in Threskel to choose a new king. He managed to finish among the tournament's finalists. His orders were to win or kill the winner.
