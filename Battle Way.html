Battle Way was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Battle Way was located in Zhentil Keep's Common Quarter in the east section of the city. The road originated on Realm Street , branching south near the eastern outer wall before connecting with Vice Street and turning southwest to terminate several blocks later.

Notable Locations

The Serpent's Eye inn and a Zhentilar guardpost were located on Vice Street.
