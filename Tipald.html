Tipald was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age .

History

Tipald developed two spells in their lifetime. The spells they developed were explosive runes in 2108 NY (−1751 DR) , and conjure elemental in 2131 NY (−1728 DR) .
