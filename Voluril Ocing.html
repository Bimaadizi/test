Voluril Ocing was an elven merchant of Marsember by day, and a jewel thief operating under the alias the Black Bandit by night.

Description

He was a slender man with light skin and long, dark hair, clad in dark clothing.

Activities

Ocing led a double life, presenting himself as an honest merchant of Marsember, whilst secretly robbing jewels from the wealthy. He was skilled enough at his second trade to avoid detection by both the Harpers and the War Wizards . Ocing's signature was a small, black doll left at the sites of his thefts.
