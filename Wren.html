A wren was a type of passerine bird.

Species

Needle

Needle wrens kept the locust population in check in the rimwood area of the forest of Cormanthor south of Shadowdale . They were received gratefully by local farmers. In the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR , a huge swarm of fyreflies caused a fire that destroyed many of the pines in which the wrens lived, causing fears among the farmers that a locust plague would result.
