The sallet was a type of helmet distinguished by its back-end, which curved out into a flange to protect the user's neck. It was primarily found all along the Sword Coast and in the nation of Tethyr .

Description

Sallets varied in their coverage, with some being more like skullcaps and others having a close-faced beak-nose like an armet .
