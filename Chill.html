The Chill was a military band of goblinoids and others. Its symbol, worn on the upper arm, was a fist surrounded by a white nimbus.

Activities

The Chill refrained from raiding nearby settlements in order to maintain good relations with the cities of the North , who would clandestinely hire the group to fight their enemies, notably trolls , orcs , and the Uthgardt .

The group charged 500 gold pieces per week.

Base of Operations

Its base was Chill Tower in the Lurkwood .

Relationships

The organization was opposed to the Bloodaxes and the Blue Sigil , and although not well-received by the orcs in the region, they were sometimes hired by them.
