The so-called From the Fields was one of the more notable dishes from the city of Velen in the nation of Tethyr .

Description

From the Fields was the name given to rice softcakes, cooked to a crispy crust, then rolled in mashed potatos , onion , and eggs , and served with chopped greens of a wild spicy onion.

History

As of the late 14 th century DR , From the Fields could be found on Blackthorn's Brew 's menu, a festhall on the Captain's Cliff in Velen. The dish was mentioned among other notable foods included in Volothamp Geddarm 's published guide to Amn and Tethyr.
