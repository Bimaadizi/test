Aglaera Rivenshaun was a traveling bard , highly-regarded in the nations of Chondath , Chessenta , and Turmish for her husky voice in the late 14 th century DR .

History

This flame-tressed entertainer witnessed a rare event in the late 14 th century DR as she traversed Chondalwood – the fabled Dancing Dryads . These hauntingly beautiful dancing forest dryads were a rare sight and were thought to be an omen of great upheaval in Faerûn .
