The Guarded Gate was the mint and treasury for the city of Ordulin in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Location

The Guarded Gate was located in the center of the city.

Description

The entire structure was designed to thwart magical scrying or entry.

Defenses

Potent golems and other magical constructions protected the Guarded Gate from intruders. A family of loyal wizards lived within the structure to monitor all activity.
