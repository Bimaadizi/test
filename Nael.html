Aldurl Nael was a Waterdhavian brass merchant in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the early spring of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Nael was in debt to the moneylender Caethur ; at a meeting with five other debtors, he protested that the business climate had been terrible, and thus was not able to make the required payment. He was soon killed by the moneylender.
