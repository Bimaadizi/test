Geezer Loryss was a vegetable vendor in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Geezer was an aging human man with graying hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Geezer ran a vegetable stall and was affected by the city's new draconian laws that confiscated most farm animals and produce entering the city in order to fight encroaching famine, according to the Flaming Fists . At one point during that tumultuous year, Geezer managed to get a shipment of cabbages into Baldur's Gate but the card ended up upturned, scattering the vegetables. The old man lamented his beloved vegetables' fate and his empty stall.
