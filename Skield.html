Skield was a drow living in Ammathtar in northeast Dambrath during the 14th century DR .

Relationships

Skield was the consort of the Duchess of the North, Tralia Falrith .

Rumors and Legends

Skield reportedly met his demise when he was sent by Duchess Falrith to face a pair of beholders in Veldorn . Others believed he just escaped while he had the change.
