Peldan was a Cormyrean soldier who settled in Mistledale upon his retirement sometime in the 11 th century DR .

History

Peldan settled in the western region of Mistledale, marking a piece of land with his helmet that would later become known as Peldan's Helm . Peldan's family grew over the years and a village sprouted up around his home. Many inhabitants of Peldan's Helm in the 14 th century DR claimed to be his ancestors.
