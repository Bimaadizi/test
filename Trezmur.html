Trezmur was a servant to House Delcastle in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He had a hard face.

History

Trezmur was assigned to standing in front of the noblewoman Marantine Delcastle 's door with a crossbow, keeping it trained on the noble heir Arclath Delcastle , every time he left his mother's chambers, as of Mirtul in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , to Arclath's perplexment.
