Winter Walk was a small street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

Winter Walk was located in western Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter . It originated on Nool Street in the west near the city's exterior wall, then passed east until it connected with Dixon Street . Silver Street intersected Winter Walk near its origin.

Description

The road was quite small at 300 feet (91 meters) in length.
