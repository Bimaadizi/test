Nenon was the Flaming Fist commander of Fort Beluarian in Chult during the late 14 th century DR . She was an officer within the Flaming Fist , serving under Grand Duke Eltan .

History

After 1362 DR , Nenon was hired to drive the Batiri away.

Activities

Nenon permitted truly desperate explorers to remain one or two days inside the Fort.
