Valana was an elven sage who specialized in planar mechanics. She visited the the town of Eveningstar to lend her expertise in the 15 th century DR .

Description

Valana had pale skin and wore her white hair tied back in a braid.

Personality

Valana was an absent-minded and somewhat amoral arcanist.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Valana offered her magical expertise to the War Wizards there as the conflict moved to the Demonweb Pits . Taking inspiration from the demonic rituals and blood sacrifices of the drow, she devised a particular enchantment that would allow someone to weaken the Rift Between Worlds using a small sacrifice of vitality, preventing it from destroying the world of Eberron . This mission also pitted the heroes against the draegloth Khaszlokhar , who guarded one of the Orbs of Lolth . While it took some effort for her to explain the details, the attack went off as planned, saving Eberron and weakening Lolth's hold on the Thread of the Weave .
