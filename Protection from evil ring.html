Protection from evil rings were enchanted rings , imbued with protective magics.

Description

Protection from evil rings were simple bands of silver with three large imbedded gemstones .

Powers

Each of these rings gave its wearer the ability to cast the protection from evil spell twice per day.

History

Several of these rings could be found in the Ten Towns and wider Icewind Dale circa the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR .
