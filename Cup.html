Description

Cups were simple handheld containers, resembling bowls . They could be made of many materials, like glass , tin , silver , or porcelain . Collapsible leather cups were common in travel mess kits , alongside other basic utensils. Leather dice cups were used widely with games of chance like table dice for shaking the dice .
