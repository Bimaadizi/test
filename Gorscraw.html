Gorscraws were ground birds .

Description

Gorscraws were plump.

Behavior

Such birds were largely considered dim-witted. They nested on the ground.

Ecology

Gorscraws were considered good eating. They inhabited the Heartlands and the Sword Coast .

History

The name of gorscraws originated in Tethyr .

In the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Storm Silverhand was plucking a gorscraw at her home in the Dalelands when Shandril Shessair and Narm Tamaraith arrived at her home.
