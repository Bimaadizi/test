Imaskari Shields were tower shields enchanted to grant minor spell resistance. They were not created by the Imaskari ; instead, they were used to fight them.

Powers

Imaskari shields carried a minor enchantment of resistance against all magic, unlikely to block even half of the feeblest spells.
