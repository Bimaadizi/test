Personality

Lady Shareen was extremely snobbish, acting dismissively to those she believed those that were lacking in social status.

Activities

She and her husband were regular patrons of Waukeen's Promenade in the Center District of Athkatla. They sat atop a large wooden platform surveying the wonders of the marketplace without resorting to speak to the lesser people that conducted business within.

Relationships

Lady Shareen was married to Lord Dermod Ophal .

History

Both Lady and Lord Ophal were active in Waukeen's Promenade around the time a great magical battle between the Cowled Wizards and some unlicensed spellcasters destroyed a section of the amphitheater, in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
