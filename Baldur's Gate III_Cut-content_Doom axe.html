A doom axe was an enchanted greataxe that instilled terror in any creature it struck.

Powers

Whenever a doom axe struck a creature, they became filled with incredible sense of dread. During that time, they became unable to to heal or regenerate . Undead creatures were especially weakened, and became less capable in battle.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , one of these axes remained in possession of the goblin trader Grat .
