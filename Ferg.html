Ferg was a priest of Ilmater from Kuldahar in the Spine of the World .

Description

The priest wore gray robes and had light brown hair.

Relationships

Ferg had a twin brother Gus and helped Sister Calliana at the House of the Bleeding Rose .

History

Around Eleint , 1281 DR , Ferg and his brother Gus helped Sister Calliana in the temple after Mother Egenia disappeared after seeking missing townsfolk of Kuldahar.
