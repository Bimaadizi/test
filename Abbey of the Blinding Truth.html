The Abbey of the Blinding Truth was a temple devoted to the Maimed God Tyr , located in Westgate during the 14 th century DR . It was constructed in the King's March , sometime around the year 1372 DR to oppose the Night Masks criminal organization that ran rampant throughout the city.

Activities

The militant High Lord Abbot Grigor Khazaar led the clerics , monks , and paladins of Tyr in an active campaign against the Night Masks and their leadership. In the year after the abbey's founding, they regularly clashed with the criminal guild and greatly weakened their operations.

Councilor Seirkas Undo was seen attending services within the chapel of the Tyr's abbey.
