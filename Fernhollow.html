Fernhollow was a member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Fist Fernhollow was a hin woman with olive skin, and hazel eyes. She wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist: a helmet , a set of chainmail , and a tabard adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. She wielded a longsword .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Fernhollow was stationed in the Outer City of Baldur's Gate, within the Wyrm's Crossing fortress.
