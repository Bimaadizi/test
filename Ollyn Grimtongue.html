Ollyn Grimtongue was a dwarven bard of Citadel Felbarr during the late 14 th century DR .

History

Some time before 1374 DR , he was named "Dirgekeeper" of the Citadel, by King Emerus Warcrown . With this unofficial title, Ollyn was solely entrusted with the duty of contributing new lines to the epic ballad The Dirge of Delzoun . Over a century later he still held this position of authority and was working on a stanza to honor the memory of his recently departed king.
