The Eye of the Beholder was jewelry shop located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The store was located in south Zhentil Keep in the western section of the Foreign Quarter on Twilight Street and Center Street .

Activities

In addition to providing gem-cutting services, the owner of the shop was quite skilled with working various precious metals as well.

Inhabitants

The Eye of the Beholder was owned by a dwarf named Artagalor Goldbeard in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
