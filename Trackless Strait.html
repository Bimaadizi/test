The Trackless Strait was a channel of the Trackless Sea that separated the islands of Moray and Norland in the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Approximately 10 miles (16 kilometers) of ocean within the Trackless Strait separated the two islands. The strait served as the western boundary between the Ffolk in the south and the Northlanders in the north. The strait connected the larger Trackless Sea with the Sea of Moonshae .
