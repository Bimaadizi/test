The Serpent's Eye was an inn found in Zhentil Keep in 1358 DR .

Location

The inn was located along Beholder Street in Zhentil Keep's Common Quarter in the southeast part of the city.

Description

This huge inn leaked during inclement weather and was drafty throughout the year. A large gambling room was located in the basement. The naug-orls oversaw all gambling here. The Serpent's Eye rented 37 rooms which were rarely empty despite the inn's shabby appearance.

Activities

The Serpent's Eye was a drafty leaking ruin of an inn. The inn's prices were moderate but a bit too steep for the poor accommodations.
