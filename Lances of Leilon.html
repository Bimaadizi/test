The Lances of Leilon was a group of soldiers that defended the town of Leilon .

Organization

This force of about 200 men was always on patrol trying to minimize orcs and goblins in the area.

Possessions

The Lances of Leilon were skilled at firing crossbows from horseback. While clad in chain mail , each lancer carried a shield . In battle, they fought with lances , light crossbows , swords , axes , and daggers .
