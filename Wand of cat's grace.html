Cat's grace wands were enchanted wands that held multiple charges of beneficial spells unleashed upon command.

Description

Cat's grace wands were 1 foot and 3 inches (0.38 meters) items of angular design. They were usually made using bone, ivory , or wood . The total number of charges varied from wand to wand.

Powers

Each of these mages' accessories held several charges of the cat's grace spell. The magics held within the wand could be discharged at will using a command word. Upon expenditure of a wand's last charge, it disintegrated.
