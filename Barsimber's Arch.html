Barsimber's Arch was a gateway in the palace of Silverymoon during the 14 th century DR .

Location

The arch was within the palace of Silverymoon .

History

During the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , there were guards posted at Barsimber's Arch in the palace. They helped escort guests at times.
