Lake Muskar was the easternmost of the lakes forming the Teardrops in Narfell .

Geography

Lake Muskar was the easternmost lake of the Teardrops, connected to the Muirenon Lake by a winding river, and fed by waters flowing westward from the Icerim Mountains .

Inhabitants

The Ragnor tribe lived along the northern Teardrop lakes of Muirenon and Muskar.
