Captains' Close , sometimes written as Captain's Close , was a park in Luskan 's South Bank .

Description

The walled park was home to deer . It was one of the nicer parts of the city.

Geography

The park was located between Aldever's Street to the west and Reavers' Run to the east. It was located just north of the Luskan slums.

The residences of two of the five High Captains of Luskan were located within Captains' Close: Taerl's Fortress in the northwest and Suljack's Lodge in the southeast. Aldever's Street separated the park from a third Captain's residence, Ten Oaks , and from the Ruins of Illusk .

History

As of the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , High Captains Taerl and Suljack both enjoyed hunting within the park, often using only knives and javelins and accompanied by their female admirers.
