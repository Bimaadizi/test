A daisy , also known as woundwort was a flowering plant very similar in appearance to chamomile .

Use

Daisy plants could be used to create healing poultices.

Habitat

Daisy flowers were known to grow in the Savage Frontier region, where they were often used to crate curative poultices. The areas of Dunwood were known to have fields of flowering daisies.

Religion

The appearance of daisies could indicate the favor or encouragement of Ilmater , the deity of suffering and martyrdom.

Finder Wyvernspur sometimes manifested himself to his most loyal followers as a daisy flower, sprouting to life, maturing, blossoming, and dying, all within a few moments.
