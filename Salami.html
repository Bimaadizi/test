Salami was a type of preserved sausage found in the Western Heartlands , the Chionthar Valley , and the city of Baldur's Gate .

Description

Salami sausages were popular among adventurers due to their delicious taste and shelf-life. Some of these big sausages were so well-preserved and hardened they could be used as bludgeoning weapons, tough enough to knock a creature out cold.
