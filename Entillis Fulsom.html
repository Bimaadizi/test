Entillis Fulsom was an elf and a member of the Harpers living in Baldur's Gate .

Relationships

Entillis was a friend of Gorion , Jaheira , and Khalid .

History

When Gorion's Ward arrived in Baldur's Gate, Entillis told him important information about the Iron Throne 's enemies and about possible allies for the young adventurer.
