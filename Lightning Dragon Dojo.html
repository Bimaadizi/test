The Lightning Dragon Dojo was a martial arts academy founded by the Shou Duc-Sum Li . The school was located in the Shou district of Xiousing in Marsember and was named after Master Li's fighting style. When Li became too old to spar, his son Chang Li took over operation of the dojo.
