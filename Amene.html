Amene was a kahin and member of the House of Hotek tribe in the Haunted Lands of interior Zakhara around 1367 DR .

Activities

Amene served as Sheikh Ibrin 's vizier. She convinced him of the erroneous ways of his past when he traded with Hiyal , convincing him of the need to destroy the city. She was always at the sheik's side offering advice and comfort.

Relationships

Many whispered that Amene would one day become Ibrin's wife. Ibrin's first wife died shortly after Amene became part of the tribe.
