Umar , also called Umar the Patrician , was the governor and commander of the military forces of Port Ghaast in 1359 DR .

Personality

Umar was a competent and intelligent commander. He was easily frustrated by his troops' discipline or lack thereof.

Activities

Even though Umar knew that King Tharmakkas IV was a fool, he refused several treasonous invitations from prominent citizens of Murghôm . However, he did not report their activity to the king either.
