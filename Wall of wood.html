The wall of wood was a magical large shield found in the Cormyrean village of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The shield was a large and solid piece of wood, carved with swirling designs.

Powers

The wall of wood was exceptionally tough and sturdy, and did well at protecting the bearer. It also greatly amplified the positive energy and other sources of healing that the bearer both produced and received.

History

The wood was harvested from the King's Forest and made into a strong shield, aided further by overt magical enchantments. It was later recovered from Uthe Lodge by adventurers after defeating the wood woads Prinus , Robur , and Falcata , who caused problems during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar .
