Orgallryd Avithoul was a drow slavemaster of House Avithoul from Sschindylryn during the 1480s DR .

Description

Orgallryd had his hair tied back and wore a chain shirt .

Abilities

Orgallryd could cast disintegrate , greater shout , Otiluke's freezing sphere , power word blind , power word stun , symbol of fear , and symbol of weakness .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Orgallryd oversaw the operations near the House Avithoul estate at the adjacent Slave Quarters .
