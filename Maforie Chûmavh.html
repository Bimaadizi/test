Maforie Chûmavh was a drow slave in Maerimydra .

History

Maforie lived all her life in slavery to the fire giant rulers of her city.

As of 1491 DR , Maforie was at the entrance to the Undying Temple .

Personality

Unlike most drow females, Maforie was shy and quiet, but still distrustful of others.

Relationship

Unknown to herself, Maforie was the last heiress of House Chûmavh .
