Fenerus Stormcastle ( pronounced : /ˈ f ɛ n ɛ r ʌ s / FEN-er-us ) was a retired brigand-turned-spy who operated under the guise of a lamplighter in Waterdeep during the early 1490s DR .

Activities

Beyond his work as a lamplighter, Fenerus spied on Waterdeep for agents of Luskan , who were in fact secret drow operatives of Bregan D'aerthe . He provided information about current political decisions made in Waterdeep to his contacts in the City of Sails.

History

Fenerus was the founder of the infamous mounted bandit group known as the Blood Hawk Posse . He was ousted as the head of the gang after a failed raid and decided to retire in Waterdeep, where he unknowingly became the primary spy of Bregan D'aerthe.

In 1492 DR , while under pressure from the Lords' Alliance , the Lords of Waterdeep ordered the City Watch to arrest Fenerus for his past crimes.

If Jarlaxle managed to obtain the Stone of Golorr , he entrusted the Stone to Fenerus. He hid the artifact before being arrested but to reveal the location Fenerus wanted to be released and have immunity for his past crimes.
