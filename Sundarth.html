Sundarth was a Zhentarim mage of the mid-14 th century DR .

Personality

Manshoon always considered Sundarth more ambitious than thoughtful. Sundarth was excited to report to him.

Abilities

Sundarth was a mage .

Activities

Sundarth formed part of a squad of Zhentarim mages.

Relationships

Other mages in the same squad to which Sundarth belonged were Chsalbreian and Arluth .
