Far Torel was the site of an armed camp in the early 3 rd century DR .

Geography

It was on the northern edge of Athalantar , along the border with the High Forest .

History

An army was massed there by the Magelords of Athalantar in Flamerule of the Year of Bloodflowers, 234 DR , almost certainly preparing to move into the High Forest. However, they were stopped by the instant corrosion of all their metal implements, due to a spell cast by an adventuring priestess of Mystra , Elmara ; she was secretly the disguised Prince of Athalantar , Elminster Aumar .
