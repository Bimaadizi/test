Adama's Wrath was the highest peak of the mighty Dustwall Mountains in the Shining South .

Description

Though the Dustwall Mountains averaged 15,000 ft (4,600 m) in height, Adama's Wrath towered over the rest of the range at 22,000 ft (6,700 m).
