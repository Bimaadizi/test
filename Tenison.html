Tenison was a wizard in Daggerford around 1370 DR .

Activities

Tenison had always been fascinated by fire, ever since he was a child. At the age of three, he burned down his family's entire farm.

Later he become an elementalist , specializing in the use of para-elementals of smoke and magma and the quasi-elementals of ash and radiance .

He lived in a house in the southeast section of Daggerford located at the intersection of Water Street and Hill Road near the River Gate .
