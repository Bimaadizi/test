The Council of Tribal Chieftains was a political council from Zagrus in the Whamite Isles . They represented Zagrus in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
