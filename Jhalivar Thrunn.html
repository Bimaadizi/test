Jhalivar Thrunn was a traveling writer who penned the Trail Tales of the North in 1197 DR .

History

Jhalivar Thrunn compiled the musings and tales from his travels across North Faerûn in the Year of the Sundered Shields, 1197 DR . By the 14 th century DR , Jhalivar's book found a home in the citadel-library of Candlekeep on the Sword Coast .
