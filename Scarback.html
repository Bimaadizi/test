Scarback was a young tyrannosaurus who competed in the dinosaur races of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 th century DR .

History

As of the start of the death curse , the young Scarback was on the verge of retirement. Going into its purported final race, bookies were giving it only 1:2 odds of winning. Scarback was present at the races on the day of the inaugural Na N'buso's Haka .
