Undying Gaze was the shield that belonged to King Draxius Obarskyr the Never-Dying. He ruled Cormyr from 480 DR to 875 DR .

Description

The shield was studded with eyes.

History

The shield became the namesake for one of Cormyr's official naval vessels some time before the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR
