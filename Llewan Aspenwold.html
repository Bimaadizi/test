Llewan Aspenwold was a priestess of Mystra in the Harrowdale region of the Dalelands around the 14 th century DR .

Personality

Llewan had a great fascination with magic tricks and flashy arcane spells , such as color spray or prestidigitation . She would continue to exhibit this fascination after death.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Llewan opened the House of Mystra in Harrowdale. However, she made very few converts and most Dalesfolk simply ignored her. Llewan wondered to do something great to arrite attention.

In the Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR , Llewan died within her temple as it was destroyed by the blue fires of the Spellplague . Her temple would go on to be left abandoned, leaving the restless spirit of Llewan to haunt it as a ghost .
