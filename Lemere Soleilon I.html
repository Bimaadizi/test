Lemere Soleilon I , who was also called the Merchant King , was the second King of the Soleilon Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Lemere I inherited the throne when his father, King Gen Soleilon , died in −91 DR . He ruled until his death in −74 DR .

Relationships

His firstborn son and heir was Lemere II .
