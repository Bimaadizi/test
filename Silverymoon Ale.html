Silverymoon Ale was a variety of hearty bodied ale from just outside of the northern city of Silverymoon , served at the Silvermoon Playhouse of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Despite the name "Silverymoon Ale," the drink was not produced in the Gem of the North. In reality, it was brewed by a small dwarven community near Silverymoon.
