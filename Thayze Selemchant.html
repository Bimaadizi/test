Thayze Selemchant was the first Meisarch of Amn and Lord of House Selemchant .

He founded the Council of Six in 1333 DR , but then became petty and jaded. He was also secretly a high-ranked member of the Cowled Wizards . He was blackmailing two other council members but his membership of the Cowled Wizards was revealed and he died. There was a rumor that he survived as a ghost .

In 1345 DR , Tessarch Quorl Vemmil attempted to frame Thayze for a plague in Keczulla but Thayze's intelligence agents revealed the plot, and Quorl and his priests were forced to Keczulla where they perished. Thayze survived this attempt against him.
