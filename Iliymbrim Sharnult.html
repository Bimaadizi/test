Illiymbrim Sharnult was a mage at some point before the 8 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Laughter, 761 DR , the sage Beldrune , working at the Moonshorn Tower , opined about a certain Olbert 's triple-delayed blast fireball spell. Beldrune noted that Olbert had created that spell by combining the work of Illiymbrim Sharnult, Lhabbartan , and a third person whose name he could not recall at the moment. This strongly implied that Illiymbrim Sharnult predated Olbert.
