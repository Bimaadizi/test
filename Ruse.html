Ruse was a bandit berserker in the Moonsea in 1489 DR .

History

Ruse was one of the two lieutenants of the Gray Patriots bandit group under Sahnd Krulek . During a meeting with another bandit group, the Claws of Tiamat , an adventuring party hired to clear the Iron Route of outlaws caused havoc. Ruse was killed in fighting.
