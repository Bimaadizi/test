The Keffinspires was a district within the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire .

Description

This quarter of the efreet city housed the golden towers of the azer , many of which were connected to each other by suspended walkways.

Trade

Most of the trade within this district was regulated by the Azer Steel Guild , whose offices were located in the golden towers. They had nearly exclusive rights to oversee the manufacture and sale of armor and weapons .

Inhabitants

The Keffinspires was primarily populated by azer and some salamanders .
