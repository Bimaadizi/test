Belevan Crell , who was also later called Belevan the Unliving , was a Nentyarch of Narfell during the time of the Crell Dynasty .

Activities

Belevan ruled from −843 DR to −633 DR , far longer than any of his predecessors thanks to his successful transformation into a lich . Belevan's rule, and that of the entire Crell Dynasty, came to an end in -633 DR when he was overthrown by Ulthas and Ulthar Orgolath , a pair of brothers known as the "Demon Twins."

Relationships

Belevan was the son and sole heir of the previous Nentyarch, Arthauld Crell . He was born during the first year of Arthauld's reign in the −891 DR .
