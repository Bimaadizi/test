Captain Flinn was a sailor on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR .

History

Before he became a sailor, Flinn adventured with Daeghun Farlong , exploring dungeons. Once he got tired of adventuring, however, Flinn bought a ship and named it Double Eagle .

In 1374 DR , Flinn became stranded at in Highcliff like all the others sailors because lizardmen were attacking the boats. Once the Kalach-Cha dealt with the lizardmen, the villagers repaired the Double Eagle , allowing Flinn and the Kalach-Cha to sail to Neverwinter .
