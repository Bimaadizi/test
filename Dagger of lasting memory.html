The dagger of lasting memory was a unique enchanted dagger crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Shevarith Kendia in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

Silverhand crafted this dagger to represent the shocking impression that Shevarith's seemingly simple yet devastating spells left on her foes and allies alike.

Powers

The dagger of lasting memory had the effect of the weird spell on those struck by it, causing them lasting psychic injuries.
