Malaritheos was a red dragon who lived in the Moonsea region in 1373 DR .

Activities

Malaritheos laired with his mate, Zathantramyx , in a dormant volcano near Zhentil Keep . They were served by a frost giant tribe.

History

In 1368 DR Malaritheos and Zathantramyx joined the monstrous army that raided Zhentil Keep. In later years they were busy raising three young dragon children.
