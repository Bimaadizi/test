A ring of flying was a type of magical ring that allowed the wearer to fly.

Description

These rings were often made of platinum and edged with brass . They were covered with tiny runes imbuing the ring with magic.

Powers

The ring was activated with concentration and the result was identical to the fly spell. It allowed the wearer and up to 600 pounds (270 kilograms) additional weight to fly. The wearer could use the ring up to two hours a day. The ring also allowed the wearer to cast one feather fall spell per day.
