A helm of seabreathing was a magical helmet that granted the wearer the power of underwater breathing.

Powers

This item was essentially a less useful version of a helm of underwater action . While it granted the ability of underwater respiration no matter how deep, it had no effect on the wearer's vision or movement while in that environment.
