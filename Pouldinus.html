Pouldinus was an archmage from the ancient Netherese Empire , who lived over 3,000 years before Dale Reckoning .

History

Sometime around -3000 DR Pouldinus created a powerful enchanted bastard sword for his friend Kalamnin , who was a devote follower of Amaunator . The sword was named Amaunator's Legacy and survived well into the late 13 th century DR .
