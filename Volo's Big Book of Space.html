Volo's Big Book of Space was a book written by Volothamp Geddarm in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . It covered spelljamming and spelljammers and navigation in space .

It recommended that the best use of a planetary locator was to identify landmarks within a system that could guide a spelljamming traveler to a civilized place where they could buy more reliable star charts .
