The robes of vermin were cursed items that caused a multitude of bites on the wearer.

Description

A robe of vermin would appear to be a powerful magical robe, and spells like detect magic would mark it as a robe of protection .

Powers

Once somebody put on the robe it would act just like a lesser robe of protection , until the moment the bearer entered combat where upon a great multitude of in insects would magically infest the robe and begin biting the wearer. Once the vermin had manifested the wearer could do naught but scratch and squirm, any attempts to cast a spell would fail half the time. The robe could not be removed unless a remove curse or similar spell was cast upon the wearer/robe.
