Inwhar III was the emperor of Agenais in the Whamite Isles . Along with the empress Zelina , Inwhar represented Agenais in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
