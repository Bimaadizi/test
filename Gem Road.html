The Gem Road was a major highway in Amn that served as the main route between Esmeltaran and Keczulla .

Geography

The road wound along the shore of Lake Esmel and the bank of the Splendarrllur river for much of its length, and passed near the rumored location of Myth Lharast .

Defenses

The Gem Road was heavily patrolled by a Council Army garrison based out of Keczulla that also provided escorts for caravans. During the 14 th century DR , this detachment was commanded by Colonel Gladdos Wintosnip , who ensured that his soldiers did their duty and weren't bought off by rich merchants seeking extra services.

History

The Gem Road was built as a response to the increasing wealth and prosperity of Keczulla following the discovery of the Tanislove 's new gem mines in 1355 DR . It was planned and sponsored by the Council of Six and Lord Pulth Tanislove , and construction began in 1359 DR . Although Pulth died in 1361 DR , his son Pehllus oversaw its completion in 1366 DR . The road quickly became a popular route between the two cities and increased the prosperity of both, and brought a new wave of settlers to the previously quiet countryside around Lake Esmel.
