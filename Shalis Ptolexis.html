Shalis Ptolexis was a human woman of Mulhorandi descent who was married to the patriarch of House Darkdance of Westgate , Neveren Darkdance . She was also the mother of the powerful mage Myrin Darkdance . Shalis was a priestess of Sharess and wrote a book called Wanderings in Love's Name , which was published in 1354 DR .
