A Forest Monarch was a special kind of tree in the Feywild . More than mere trees, they were emblems of the Feywild , vessels of life force, and primeval spirits.

Description

One of those trees had a trunk over 100 feet (30 meters) in diameter; after petrifying itself, its bare branches had transformed into a vaulted cyst atop the tree.

History

One forest monarch captured the Traitor while the latter fled from Stardeep after the Spellplague , around the year 1385 DR . The tree petrified the Traitor, sacrificing itself as well in the process.
