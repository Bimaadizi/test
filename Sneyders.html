Sneyders was a Netherese fighter who worked as a confectioner at Laclos's Cremes and Candies in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Sneyders was an extremely capable creator of desserts and treats, and alongside Kiesewetter made much of what was sold at Laclos 's chocolate factory. He was extremely well paid by her to ensure his continued loyalty.
