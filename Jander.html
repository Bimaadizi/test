TypeHamlet

Description

Jander was a small, forlorn hamlet.

Geography

It sat near the northern edge of Athalantar, north-by-northeast of the site of the future Secomber .

Landmarks

The Woodsedge Inn was due north of Jander.

History

Jander was flourishing in some ways by 180 DR .

In the Year of the Battle Horns, 216 DR , locally named the Year of Frosts, King Uthgrael Aumar perished near Jander in battle against orcs .

By 240 DR , the mage Nasarn had a country house near Jander.

It was long-gone by the 14 th century DR .
