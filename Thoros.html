Ranger Thoros was a scout for the Company Yargo mercenary group that operated throughout the Realms during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He was a burly man whose face was decorated with particularly stylish face tattoo.

History

Thoros was a member of the expedition that ventured up to Pirates' Skyhold aboard the Buccaneer's Tear in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR . He and his fellow scout Shaben Shortcloak braved the perilous Vilebog swamp and made it past the lizardfolk denizens en route to Skull Fortress , but the two became separated and Thoros believed Shaben required rescuing.

He worked with the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge to "evict" many of the lizardfolk that dwelled within sunken village surrounding the old fort, in order to save his friend and fellow scout.
