The Lord's Way was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

Located in the Inner Quarter in central Zhentil Keep, the Lord's Way originated near the Black Altar in the west and passed east through the quarter passed the Tower High to end at the wall of the Common Quarter . Along the way the Lord's Way was intersected by Raven Street , the Street of the Gods , the Dark Road , and Flostren's Memory .
