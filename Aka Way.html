Aka Way was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Aka Way was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . The road originated on the western side of Bazaar Street branching southwest and continuing briefly before ending on Tharleon Street . Marlar's Lane connected with Aka Way.
