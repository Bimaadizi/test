The bottomless flask of whisky was an enchanted bronze flask that produced whisky. The elven brigand Kiril Duskmourn had one.

Description

The flask was forged of bronze , with a greenish blue patina of verdigris covering a deranged face. Kiril Duskmourn believed it may have been forged by elves outside of her lineage ; she knew the face to be that of a god of the vine, though she didn't remember their name and didn't care.

Powers

The flask produced whiskey whenever prompted. To Kiril Duskmourn's recollection, it had never failed to provide alcohol.
