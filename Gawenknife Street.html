Gawenknife Street was a large road that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

Gawenknife Street was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . It was easily the largest road in this section of the Field Ward, spanning east to west in between the High Road and an unnamed courtyard near Endshift Street . Other roads that intersected Gawenknife Street included Logan's Walk , the Semize , and Chop Street .
