Katl was an Eagle Knight in Maztica .

History

In late 1361 DR , Katl was one of the many officers in the assorted column of refugee led by Cordell . When the survivors arrived in Ulatos and discovered that the place was under the control of Don Vaez , an old rival of Cordell, Katl tried to kill Don using his eagle form but was defeated and severely injured.

Put in prison with all the officers of the Golden Legion , he was near death but Cordell offered Don Vaez all his gold in order to have a cleric save him.
