Barking Seal was the village chieftain of the Ice Hunters that dwelled on the isle of Oyaviggaton as of the 1480s DR .

Relationships

He had a strong bond with his village's shaman Bonecarver . Together the two did all the speaking to outsiders for their people.

If any outlander refused to leave their home, Barking Seal would propose the village's champion Orcaheart . If Orcaheart won, the Ice Hunters would receive any of the visitors' more finely-crafted weapons.

History

Some time in the 1480s DR, Barking Seal and Bonecarver greeted a band of adventurers from the Sword Coast at their village.
