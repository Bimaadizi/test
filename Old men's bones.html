Description

An old men's bones set consisted of tiny bones that were poured into a pile. The object of the game was to remove the bones one at a time from the pile without disturbing the other bones in the pile.

Cheaper sets were made from the bones of birds; however, sets constructed from the slivered bones of wyverns , dragons , or other dangerous creatures also existed. Aurora's Emporium promised that none of their sets were made from the bones of sentient humanoids .

The bones were usually stored in a leather or metal tube canister.

History

The game originated in Phlan and the Moonsea region.
