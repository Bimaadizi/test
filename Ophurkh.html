Ophurkh was a quasit servant of Baphomet during the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , Ophurkh was one of the many demons in Avernus when Elturel fell into the Nine Hells . He was one of the first to enter the fallen city, acting as scout. Ophurkh managed to infiltrate the Cathedral to Lathander and there waiting the arrival of more demons.
