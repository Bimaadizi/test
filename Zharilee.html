Zharilee was a sun elf citizen of Evereska that served as a commander of the city's Long Watch as of the late 14 th century DR .

Description

The gold elf leader was stunningly beautiful, even for a member of the Ar-tel-quessir.

Activities

Zharilee worked alongside the Cloudtop Circle of elven high mages , coordinating the defense of the valley and lands beyond Evereska.

Relationships

She had a sister named Gvendor , who also lived in the city.

History

Zharilee was present in Evereska during a series of skirmishes that broke out between her people and the phaerimm that had recently been released from within the Sharn Wall beneath the Shaeradim in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR .
