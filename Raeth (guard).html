Raeth was a guard in Shadowdale in the mid-14 th century DR .

Personality

He was a late riser.

Relationships

He was teased at times for being a late riser.

History

As of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , he was a young, new guard in the Twisted Tower of Ashaba of Shadowdale , employed at least since some time before the night when Culthar , another guard, was caught trying to kidnap Shandril Shessair .
