Bindle "Eye-nock" Tempertuppin was the inventor of Tempertuppin's Ever-Throwers , active sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Bindle created his enchanted slings sometime before the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . The muses came to the man after he witnessed a fire giant using a giant-sized sling, big enough to launch a donkey 300 paces. However, some questioned the truthfulness of Bindle's story.
