Daardendrien Nijana was a dragonborn warrior member of Clan Daardendrien , and a member of the Lance Defenders of Djerad Kethendi in 1487 DR .

Abilities

She was capable of breathing acid .

Relationships

Nijana was the daughter of Daardendrien Turan and the sister of Medrash .

History

Nijana was one of the survivors of the Kethendan forward regiment of the Lance Defenders, which was devastated during the Untheran siege of Djerad Kethendi, the first important battle of the First Tymanther-Unther War .

On Hammer 8 of 1487 DR, Nijana led a group of survivors of that battle to safety, after they freed themselves from chains , taking advantage of a storm that interrupted Gilgeam 's speech on the eve of the battle of Djerad Thymar .
