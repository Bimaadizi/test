Sudrav was a small mining town found in the farthest southern reaches of Arcata in Damara .

Geography

Sudrav was nestled with the Earthwood forest at the foot of the Earthspur Mountains .

Inhabitants

Disgusted by the politics of Damara, inhabitants despised Duke William and were not afraid to burn his effigies. Inhabitants of Sudrav considered themselves more politically aligned with Impiltur . They even went so far as to secretly ask Impiltur to annex their town. Though Imphras II refused the request, he did promise aid if needed.
