Shipwright's Square was a small courtyard that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Shipwright's Square was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward . It was bordered to the southwest and southeast by Aline's Way , to the northeast by Keel Alley , and to the northwest by Ship Street . The largest entrance into the square branched southwets off of Keel Alley.
