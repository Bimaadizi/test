Gudea was the ruler of Ssintar in Unther in 1357 DR .

Activities

Gudea was a supporter of Gilgeam . However, like many, he hid from the king when the situation was desperate. He was bargaining with Furifax and the cult of Tiamat in order to survive.

Personality

Gudea was a natural pessimist, always figuring the worst scenarios. He had no ambitions to take power.
