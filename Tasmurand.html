Tasmurand was an assassin in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

During his last job, he was dressed in all black. His clothes included a belt and boots.

Possessions

Besides an assortment of knives, Tasmurand was carrying, to Joysil Ambrur 's knowledge, three magical devices of note: two daggers and a small vial. The enchantment in at least one of those daggers was meant to strip away magical defenses.

Abilities

He was a deft, agile killer.

History

After Harnrim Starangh was guest to Lady Joysil Ambrur of Marsember one day in the early spring of 1373 DR , he sent a number of assassins at her by evening; Tasmurand himself was one of those assassins. She slew most of her slayers with spells, protected by her magic; he was the last one left alive. Before slaying him, she turned into her normal form as a song dragon , and killed him in that shape.
