Watanabe Kanzo was a samurai retainer of the Niwa clan who ran a posthouse in Kozakura in 1357 DR .

Activities

In a posthouse in the center of an imperial shoen , he led a company of twenty samurai and one hundred bushi . It was clear that all these men were not needed to run the place; instead, they were a show of force by Niwa Hirotada .

Personality

Watanabe was one of the most loyal followers of the Niwa clan.
