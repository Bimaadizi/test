Po'ioa'nam was a shalarin innkeeper in Myth Nantar .

History

Po'ioa'nam was a rogue warrior who, around 1349 DR , managed to penetrate the Great Barrier with his wife Tu'ara'thal after fleeing a group of merrow who had killed all his companions. Qos accepted Po'ioa'nam and his wife and permitted them to settle in the city. There they would go on to operate the Resonant Horn Inn .

During the city's invasion at the end of the Twelfth Serôs War , Po'iao'nam fought against Iakhovas 's troops.

Personality

Po'ioa'nam was happy to not fight again and was very proud to his daughter Ka'ioa'Tara .
