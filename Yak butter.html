Yak butter was a soft cream cheese made from the milk of a yak . It was made in the Spine of the World , Khazari , the Hordelands and the Land of the Yak-Men .

Description

It was a mild and delicate cheese but had a more characterful flavor than a typical cream cheese . The best yak butter was made in the spring after the first major thaw.

Usages

Yak butter was used as an ingredient in butter tea .

Prices

As of 1368 DR , a 1 pound (0.45 kilograms) tub was available for around four silver pieces , whereas a 100 pounds (45 kilograms) tub was about 35 gold pieces .
