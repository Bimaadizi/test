Paddo Garrard was a builder and city wall repairman from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Paddo was a human man with tan light skin, and icy blue eyes. He wore a simple blue worksman's tunic and a cloth skullcap.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Paddo Garrard could be found in the Lower City, working on the city walls alongside Tamrin and Buntter .
