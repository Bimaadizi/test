The Draakhorn was an artifact that was a gift from Tiamat to dragonkind during the war between dragons and giants .

Powers

The Draakhorn alerted all chromatic dragons within 2,000 miles (3,200 kilometers). However, the Cult of the Dragon corrupted this power in order to call them to the Well of Dragons . Additionally, it discomfited all normal animals within a few miles when blown.

History

The Draakhorn was made from a horn of Ephelomon , once a red dragon consort of Tiamat.
