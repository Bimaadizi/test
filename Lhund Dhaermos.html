Lhund Dhaermos was a fence inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Lhund operated out of the Dhaermos Warehouse located on Belnimbra's Street in Waterdeep's Dock Ward . He specialized in exotic plants and animals, and slaves.
