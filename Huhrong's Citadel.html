The Huhrong's Citadel , which was also called the Iron Lord's castle , was a fortress and seat of government in the capital city of Immilmar in Rashemen in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

Location

The keep stood above Immilmar at the city's center.

Description

This foreboding castle was made from stone and iron . Powerful water and earth spirits were believed to have played a part in its construction. The keep was constructed on an ancient ruin from Narfell .

Inhabitants

The Huhrong's Citadel was the ancestral home of Rashemen's Iron Lord, or Huhrong , who, in 1367 DR, was Yvarrg Bearkiller . In addition to the Iron Lord's family, his trusted advisors and commanders also inhabited the citadel. A trio of hathran wychlaran were also found within.
