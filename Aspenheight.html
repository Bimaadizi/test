Aspenheight was a mountain range located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The mountain range was located in northwestern Gwynneth along the coast of the Strait of the Leviathan . Aspenheight formed part of the western border of Myrloch Vale . It was separated from the Highlands mountain range by the Aspenheight Gap . Several unnamed rivers flowed into the Strait of the Leviathan from the mountains.
