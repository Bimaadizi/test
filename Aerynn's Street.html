Aerynn's Street was a street found in the coastal town of Aithelar in Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles in Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR .

Location

Aerynn's Street connected and ran perpendicular to Bottle Lane and Callidyrr Ride near the center of Aithelar. It intersected Aithe High Street midway along its length.
