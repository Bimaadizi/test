Lylar Emveolstone II was a nobleman of Waterdeep , heading House Emveolstone in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

One Waterdhavian socialite described him as a "vision of manliness".

Possessions

He presumably dwelt at Emveolstone Villa .

Activities

He was involved with House Emveolstone 's business, namely ironmongery and curio trading.

Relationships

Lord Emveolstone had a consort, Dalene Emveolstone , and a son, Alars Emveolstone . His brother Challas Emveolstone was the present regent from at least 1358 to 1372.

History

Lhaeo , then king of Tethyr , realized while going over numerous records by 1367 DR that Lady Evelyn Emveolstone was responsible for a number of murders in past decades, and told as much to Khelben by missive. Khelben apprised Lord Lylar of the matter, who agreed to let the matter rest.

Lord Lylar Emveolstone II was spotted at a ball in Waterdeep by Simylra Lavartil in 1369 DR .
