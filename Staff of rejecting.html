A staff of rejecting was a magic staff that could be found in Waterdeep as of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .

Description

This staff was made from ivory and weighed just 1 pound (0.45 kilograms).

Powers

The magic of the staff allowed the wielder to cast Trobriand's baleful teleport when expending two charges. These items were keyed to a specific location in Undermountain called Trobriand's Graveyard and could not but used to send a target to any other location.

History

The wizard Trobriand created several of these staves.
