The Jhansczil ( pronounced : /ˈ dʒ ɑː n s z ɪ l / JAN-ss-zil ) family was a significant member of the nobility of Waterdeep , first gaining nobility status in the Year of the Cockatrice, 1248 DR . Like House Amcathra , House Roaringhorn , and House Ilzimmer , House Jhansczil's influence also reached out to the town of Amphail .

Organization

As of 1372 DR , the patriarch of the Jhansczil family was Lord Harkas Jhansczil with his consort Ariel and their eldest son as the designated heir, Arrikes . During this period, there were twenty-nine living members of the Jhansczil noble family.

Activities

The Jhansczil family thrived thanks to trading, mercenary fighting, and horse breeding.

Base of Operations

The family villa and main base was located in the Sea Ward .
