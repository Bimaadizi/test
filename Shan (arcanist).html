Shan was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Shadowed Age .

History

Shan developed three spells of the inventive during their life: Shan's web in 3205 NY (−654 DR) , Shan's tallow in 3215 NY (−644 DR) , and finally, Shan's forcewall in 3235 NY (−624 DR) .
