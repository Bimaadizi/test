Bitter Divorce was a magical wand created by Auntie Ethel .

Description

It was formed from twisted bits of tree root along wits bits of hair and flesh. The name "Connor" was inscribed along the shaft.

Powers

The wand could be used to bring back the recently deceased as a form of mindless, zombified undead .

History

This wand was crafted by Ethel in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , with the aim of raising a man named Connor Vinderblad . Ethel planned to offer up his undead corpse to his wife Mayrina , fulfilling their deal to "bring back" Connor in exchange for their unborn baby.
