Windon Lumbus was a halfling boy in the Moonsea in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Windon lived with his family at the Lumbus farm, near the village of The Stop .

History

Circa 1486 DR , a group of goblins wearing strange robes attacked the Lumbus farm, killing all the Lumbuses while they slept. Windon however managed to escape and hid himself in the barn among the surviving goats.

Personality

Windon trusted halflings and humans but was shy with other races. He knew much about goblins, as his uncle had told him many tales about them.
