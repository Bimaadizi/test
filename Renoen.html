Renoen , also known as Renoen the Fair , was a councilor that served in the Dale Council in High Dale in the late 14 th century DR . She was known in Highcastle for her remarkable beauty and charm. Furthermore, she was also well recognized as being the only wizard of any real significance within the dale.
