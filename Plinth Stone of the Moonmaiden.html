The Plinth Stone of the Moonmaiden was one of the earliest altars used by the faithful of Selûne on the slopes of Mount Waterdeep .

Description

The Plinth Stone was a massive block of tarnished silver . On its surface was carved a symbol of the Moonmaiden.

History

The Plinth Stone was stolen by the church of Shar , and hidden in their base of Vanrakdoom . Defaced by carving a Holy symbol of Shar on top of that of the moon goddess, it served as a bier for Gorothir 's sarcophagus.
