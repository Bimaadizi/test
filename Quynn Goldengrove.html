Quynn Goldengrove was a prince of the Suldusk tribes of wild elves in the Forest of Tethir who married a half-elf woman from Clan Ithal .

Relationships

Prince Quynn married Varytha Ithal , the daughter of human chieftain Corin Ithal and this elven wife Shyllisyrr Ithal .

History

In −189 DR , Shyllisyrr's accidental death lead to fragile relations between the Tethyrian clans the tribes of the Forest of Tethir, which lasted for over a decade. Quynn's decision to marry a woman from Clan Ithal played an important role in easing tensions between the two peoples and restoring peace.
