Edwin's amulet was a unique enchanted amulet that belonged to Edwin Odesseiron during the mid–14 th century DR .

Powers

Edwin's amulet was a chain inlaid with his family stone. The amulet's enchantments allowed the wearer to memorize three additional wizard spells.

History

Edwin Odesseiron was inseparable with his family jewel, wearing it through his hunt on behalf of the Red Wizards for the Rashemi Wychlaran , Dynaheir some time before 1368 DR . As well as during his time living in Athkatla of Amn after a fallout with the Thayan Red Wizards and the search for the Nether Scrolls in the city's graveyard district.
