Location

The Tower was located in the southwest corner of the City of the Gods in the northcentral part of the city.

History

As its name implied, the Tower of Anhur was once the home of an incarnation of Anhur who held the title of God-King in Mulhorand. It was constructed by slaves thousands of years ago as of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .
