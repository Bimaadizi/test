The Peacock Resplendent was a large inn in the city of Innarlith .

Location

It was located on Ransar's Ride near the border between the second and third quarters of the city. The Palace of Many Spires was also located nearby.

Structure

It was a sprawling complex.

Interior

It included rooms that could be used for private gatherings and feasts. One of these rooms was huge and had about a hundred candelabra in it.

Atmosphere

Senators and elite of Innarlith often liked to use this inn for gatherings.

History

A number of senators met here in 1364 DR after the funeral for Senator Khonsu . At the meeting they made plans to make Willem Korvan , Khonsu's assassin, into a new senator for the city-state.
