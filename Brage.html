Possessions

Most notable among Brage's possessions was a cursed berserking greatsword that caused him to lost control in battle.

Relationships

Before he became wanted by the authorities, Brage was highly respected among the citizens of Nashkel.

He had a cousin named Laryssa who cared deeply about his well-being.

History

At some point in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , he come into possession of the cursed sword that sent him on a murderous rampage. He fled town west in the direction of an active archaeological dig site.

To bring Brage to justice, Nashkel officials placed a bounty on Brage's head, which was to be collected by the officer Oublek . At the same time, Laryssa sought to bring him back alive to the Helmite cleric Nalin so that he might recuperate from his madness.
