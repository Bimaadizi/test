The Sholapur chaka was a prominent chaka within the Shining Lands in 1367 DR .

Organization

The Sholapurs owned most of Ship Isle off the coast of Var the Golden near Myrmyr . Their shipyards produced the finest merchant vessels in all of the Shining Lands, making them an extremely wealthy chaka.

Base of Operations

The Sholapur chaka was based out of Myrmyr in eastern Var the Golden.
