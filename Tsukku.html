Tsukku was a samurai in the town of Aru in Wa in the 1770s on the Wa Calendar .

Activities

He was a lieutenant of Buntaro Ostato and served Benju Matsutomo , daimyo of Aru Province .

Personality

Tsukku was young but a very proud samurai. He took any kind of offense very seriously, even challenging an offender to a duel, but he would not go against orders and would tolerate any rudeness if he had to.

Description

He wore a brown kimono and ring mail , and was armed with katana and wakizashi .

Abilities

He was adept in horsemanship , calligraphy, painting, and poetry.

History

One night in high summer in Wa Year 1775 (1357 DR) , Benju ordered Tsukku to meet a group of adventurers who were staying at The Waiting Stone , a local ryokan (inn). He was to invite them to a midnight meeting with the daimyo at his donjon , and escort them there, and not take no for an answer. Tsukku privately felt the adventurers were most likely rogues and ronin. He met them with a group of seven other samurai, and later escorted them back.
