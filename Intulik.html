Intulik of the Blood was an ambitious and ruthless witch doctor and a politician from Chult , active before 1372 DR .

Possessions

Intulik used his magical abilities to fuse the spirits of seventeen tigers and imbue them into a helmet , which he would later name the Spirit of the Predator .

History

Intulik of the Blood used his magics and political prowess to gain power sometime before 1372 DR. He crafted an enchanted helmet, the Spirit of the Predator which he subsequently used to dispose of political opposition. Eventually, Intulik of the Blood rose to power over non- Mezroan Chultan tribes. However, the subjection was short-lived. Intulik's career was ended via the sword of a bounty hunter called Tatchalik who claimed the Spirit of the Predator and left Chult for Waterdeep .
