Hemmerem's Stables was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The three buildings that composed the stables were one and two stories tall.

Location

Hemmerem's Stables was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter . The front of the stables sat along the west side of Slop Street while the rear of the buildings faced Kolat Towers and Weeping Maiden's Run . The southern building sat on the west end of Grocer's Lane .

Inhabitants

The stables were owned and run by Hemmerem in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
