Fist Starrlet was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Starrlet was a human woman with soft features, sun-kissed skin, and grayish blue eyes. She wore a Fist uniform helmet that hid her gray hair, and wore a set of chainmail armor decorated with a tabard embroidered with the Flaming Fist insignia. Starrlet wielded a halberd when on duty.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Starrlet was tasked with patrolling the Lower City, along Wendserpent Street and near the Manor Gate , accompanied by a group of fellow Fists and a steel watcher .
