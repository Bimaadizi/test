A. Grekov was a Raumviran innkeeper who lived in Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Activities

Grekov owned and operated the Inn of the Crooked Staff . His inn was located near the center of town next to the Raumkremyl .
