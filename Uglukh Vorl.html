Uglukh Vorl was a half-orc barkeep and proprietor of the Bloody Fist tavern in the city of Waterdeep in the 14 th century DR .

Personality

Uglukh was well known to yawn in the face of combat.

Possessions

The personal weapon of Uglukh was a greataxe , whose central shaft ended in a spearhead , and was laced with sleep poison .
