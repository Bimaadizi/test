Moradin's Will was a unique enchanted mace , imbued with divine magics of Moradin , in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

This Moradite bludgeon possessed a +2 offensive enchantment and it aided the wielder in recovering from various afflictions.

History

In the 15 th century DR, Moradin's Will belonged to a pirate from the Sword Mountains named Kajin .
