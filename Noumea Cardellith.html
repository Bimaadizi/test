Nouméa Cardellith (maiden name Nouméa Fairbright ) was a Sembian wizard and an apprentice of Elminster Aumar in the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

During her encounter with Elminster, she was wearing, almost certainly as part of a complete attire, golden slippers with spike heels.

Personality

She was practical and not reticent to respond to absurd situations.

Abilities

Great Lady Calabrista had taught her a number of things including the Art .

Possessions

She carried early on a wand of ever-searing flames .

Relationships

She had been a student of Great Lady Calabrista before meeting Elminster.

History

She met Elminster as one of Great Lady Calabrista's best students on Eleasias 4 of the Year of the Wave, 1364 DR . During the meeting, she attacked a persistent Red Wizard with a "wand of ever-searing flames" which made Elminster quite interested.
