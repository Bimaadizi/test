Mal Tamesis was a small lake found on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Mal Tamesis was found near the center of Myrloch Vale just northwest of the Myrloch . Nearly half of Mal Tamesis' shoreline was covered by a small unnamed forest, while the remaining shore faced the open plains of the vale.
