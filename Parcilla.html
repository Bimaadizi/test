Parcilla was an employee of Kalas Winters , who worked as a waitress at the Wolf's Pelt Inn in the Icewind Dale community of Targos circa Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Description

Parcilla was a 20-year-old woman with short blonde hair and green eyes. She was 5 feet (1.5 meters) tall and weighed 107 pounds (48.5 kilograms).

Personality

Parcilla did not perform her duties well—her thoughts was often elsewhere. She felt she deserved a better lot in her miserable life than being stuck waiting tables. The guests got the impression that she looked down on them with an air of superiority.

Parcilla acted coldly toward and often snapped back at anyone who tried to flirt with her.

Possessions

Parcilla wore a small gold ring on her left hand that was a ring of protection +1 . It was an engagement ring given to her by her fiance and she felt it kept her safe. She also carried a kitchen knife with her in case of trouble.

History

At some point in her past, Parcilla was engaged, but her fiance went missing in Maer Dualdon after a sudden storm.

Around Tarsakh in 1369 DR, Parcilla worked as a waitress in Wolf's Pelt Inn.
