TypeBeverage
OriginRashemen , Unapproachable East

Kvas was a type of beverage found in Rashemen . There it was not uncommon to find people trading gossip in village courtyards over shared mugs of steaming kvas.

History

In the Year of Maidens, 1361 DR , the Rashemaar berserker Fyodor found himself in Trollbridge , a village in the Savage Frontier , he contemplated how life was so different and grim there compared to his home back in Rashemen. Among the things that stood out to him was the lack of courtyard gossip over kvas.
