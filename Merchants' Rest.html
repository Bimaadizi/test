Merchants' Rest , which was also known as the Coinscoffin , was a tomb located within the City of the Dead in Waterdeep .

Location

Merchants' Rest was located in the central section of the City of the Dead near the entryway.

History

Despite its name, Merchants' Rest was reserved for anyone, whether they were a merchant or not, who prepaid to be interred within.

Rumors & Legends

A portal created by Anacaster was located within the tomb. The portal lead to a wooded region with burial plots marked neatly on the ground.
