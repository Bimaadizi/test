Tobiworth Hedgeblossom was one of the leaders of the Social Justice League , one of the factions active in the city of Zazesspur during the Tethyrian Interregnum period.

Description

Tobiworth was a diminutive halfling , but—although his voice was high-pitched—he spoke with great authority. He was called Toby by his followers.

Activities

Toby spent a large amount of his time preaching through a speaking-tube from the streets of Zazesspur a message that the rich were responsible for the troubles affecting Tethyr .

Relationships

Toby had a brother named Putomas , or Poot. Together, the two were known as the Hedgeblossom brothers and led the Social Justice League.

The League was strongly opposed to the Hairheads , another major faction in Zazesspur the time, because that group preached violence against non humans .

History

In the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR , when Zaranda Star visited her friend Artalos the armorer in Zazesspur, she heard Toby give a speech on one occasion and later watched as a gang of Hedgeblossom followers confronted a gang of Hairheads in the streets with crude weapons.
