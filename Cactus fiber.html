Cactus fiber was a material produced on the continent of Maztica .

Usages

Most commonly, cactus fiber was accosted with the nations of Maztica that followed the teachings of Qotal , the Feathered Dragon. Cactus fiber was pressed into a crude type of fabric and thinner parchment -like material. In the land of Payit , rolled sheets of cactus fiber were used for writing messages to be carried between the cities of Ulatos and Tulom-Itzi .

Plumaweavers of Maztica used cotton and cactus fiber for weaving of their pluma magic.
