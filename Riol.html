Riol was a soldier of Hastarl in Athalantar in the mid–3 rd century DR .

Relationships

He worked under First Blade Sauvar .

History

During the assault on Athalgard in the Year of the Chosen, 240 DR , Riol was slain by Tharl Bloodbar , a Knight of Athalantar turned to banditry who was participating in the siege, almost as soon as the Magelords of Athalantar struck the alarm.
