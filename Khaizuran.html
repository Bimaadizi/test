Khaizuran was a tasked warmonger genie who served the Brotherhood of the True Flame in the fortress of Krak al-Niraan in 1367 DR .

Activities

Simply put, Khaizuran was the tactical genius behind all of the raids sent forth from the keep. He also coordinated an elaborate system of supply lines bringing goods into the keep from all corners.

History

Khaizuran led a savage attack against the Free City of Liham years ago.

Possessions

Khaizuran wielded a scimitar of wounding called Tears of Eternity .
