Kelan was a guard in Shadowdale in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

He had a sharp voice, but could speak softly.

History

In the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Kelan was amongst the guards with whom Rold palavered in the morning after Culthar revealed himself to be an agent of the Cult of the Dragon . He had some concerns.
