Czszudleaux was a powerful gnome illusionist inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Considered to be a master of illusion magic , Czszudleaux used his skills to create an annual display of elaborate illusions as a form of entertainment for the citizens of Daggerford for a modest fee of 2 sp per person. His home was located in the northwest corner of Daggerford along Fields Lane .
