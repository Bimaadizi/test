Bhylqex was a mutant ixzan cleric and a member of the Xendreth , the ruling council of of Malydren in 1370 DR .

Personality

Bhylqex was known for her violent temperament, larger-than-normal size, and unique mutant ability to shoot barbed spikes from her tail like a manticore .
