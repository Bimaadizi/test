Rondeval was a legendary stage performer of Eskorn , Laerakond .

History

Several centuries before the Spellplague , Rondeval was a stage performer renowned for his wit, charm, and womanizing ways. Rondeval eventually founded an inn in a crossroads that in time became a trade-moot.

Legacy

The town of Rondeval , that grow up around his inn, was named after him.
