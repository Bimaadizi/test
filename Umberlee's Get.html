The Umberlee's Get was the personal caravel of Jezgar Skentzin , a greater seawolf pirate in the 14 th century DR .

Description

The ship had a crew of 17 seawolves that had been infected by a seawolf that they had brought aboard and been attacked by, and was armed with two arbalests . Whenever the crew took losses, they bit the new recruit and spread the seawolf infection.
