TypeHarbor

Clearwater Harbor was the inner harbor of the Fire River , around which the Crow's End was built.

Description

The Clearwater Harbor was guarded by the Ladyrock island in the middle of the Fire River. Clearwater Harbor and the Crow's End neighborhood were sandwiched between the Burnt Gables on the east and the Anvil to the west.

Clearwater Harbor was fed by the De Villars Creek from the north, cutting across the city of Ravens Bluff and connected to Lake Christina . Many roads and streets touched Clearwater Harbor, including Iymiril Lane , Sindle Street , and the White Wyrm Way in Burnt Gables, Black Visor Street , Cove Street , Red Wyrm Ride , Crescentcoat Court , Unicorn Lane , O'Kane Court , and Maclntyre Path in the Anvil.
