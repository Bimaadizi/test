TypeMagical sword

Telvaran's Enchanted Blade was a famous lost magical sword as of the mid–14 th century DR .

Powers

It possessed all the same powers as a rod of lordly might , and others that were not recorded.

History

In Tsurlagol in the Vast in the late 1350s DR, an unnamed female human adventurer presented a magical sword to the sage Othiiyr Velthrann for identification. He found it to Telvaran's Enchanted Blade . But the client teleported away without even paying, so Othiiyr revealed the finding to others—when a client didn't pay, it was permitted to break the traditional bond of secrecy meant to exist between sages and clients. Its location and ownership remained unknown.
