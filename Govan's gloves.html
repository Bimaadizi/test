Govan's gloves were enchanted gloves that belonged to druid Govan Irfandel who was renown for erecting the Stone Circle of Govan of the Brush of Barbs circa 1072 DR .

Powers

Govan's gloves were enchanted to allow the wearer to cast the one with the land spell once per day, enhancing the user's connection to animals and plant life. Due to the nature of the magic imbued within these gloves, they could only be used by druids and rangers .

History

Govan Irfandel was said to have created these magic gloves to enhance his natural affinity with the land and its inhabitants. With these powers he enlisted the aid of trees , thickets, and vines of the Brush of Barbs to move the rocks into what was later became known as the Stone Circle of Govan around 1072 DR.

By 1372 DR , Govan's gloves could be found in one of the piles of junk and "antiques" within Torleth's Treasures of Gillian's Hill , being sold for 300 gold pieces.
