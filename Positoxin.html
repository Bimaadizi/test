TypePoison

Positoxins were special alchemical mixtures distilled with holy water and laced with positive energy . They effected corporeal undead the way poisons effected the living.

Positoxins could break down an undead's physical body, potentially permanently. Such substances also disrupted the negative energy that animated the creatures. Positoxins were harmless to all other creatures (including incorporeal undead), and as supernatural substances, an antimagic field or similar area rendered them ineffective.

Spells such as detect poison , delay poison , and neutralize poison effected positoxins as if they were poisons.
