Chalaratha's Fragrances was a perfume shop and factory located in the city of Westgate in 1358 DR .

Inhabitants

The shop was owned and operated by the skilled wizard Chalaratha .

Location

Chalaratha's Fragrances was found in the southwest section of the city on South Ssemm Spur .
