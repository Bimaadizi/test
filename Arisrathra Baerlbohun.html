Arisrathra Baerlbohun was the daughter of Unsible Baerlbohun , the "Mad Toymaker," who rose to wealth and prominence in Athkatla in the 14 th century DR .

Personality

Arisrathra was a revelry-lowing and wild daughter of a gaudy-rich and decadent merchant Baerlbohun family. Some described her as a "winking" woman who was renowned for being "ready and willing for all foolery" among men of Athkatla. She spent her time as an eager revel-companion and social butterfly of the city's affluent Gem District .

History

By the late 14 th century DR , Arisrathra was the most well-known member of the family, apart from her "Mad Toymaker" father. She had three large, lazy, lumbering brothers, Purdynkul Baerlbohun being the oldest.
