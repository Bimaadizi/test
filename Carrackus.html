Carrackus was a male scrag and a pirate on the Sword Coast under the command of Pinochet .

History

Carrackus was present in 1356 DR when the Sea Sprite , sailed by Captain Deudermont and the Companions of the Hall , defeated Pinochet's fleet, but Carrackus managed to escape.

Carrackus later became captain of his own pirate ship. However, in 1364 DR , he was pursued by the Sea Sprite and defeated by Drizzt Do'Urden and Guenhwyvar . He was torn to pieces and the Sea Sprite 's crew fished his remains out of the sea so they could be burned and permanently destroyed
