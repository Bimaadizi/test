Smolyn was a Netherese arcanist and prolific spell inventor who developed spells that affected one's vision.

History

Smolyn invented eight spells during their life. The spells they developed were blindness and foresight in 1895 NY (−1964 DR) , true seeing in 1901 NY (−1958 DR) , blur in 1917 NY (−1942 DR) , veil in 1925 NY (−1934 DR) , eyebite in 1935 NY (−1924 DR) , false vision in 1940 NY (−1919 DR) , and finally, mirror image in 1943 NY (−1916 DR) .
