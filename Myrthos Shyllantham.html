Myrthos Shyllantham was a member of the Harpers who petitioned High Lady Alustriel Silverhand to restore the Fochlucan bard college.

History

Around 1370 DR , Shyllantham saw the rise in influence of the New Olamn bards' college in Waterdeep and wanted to spread the knowledge to Silverymoon.
