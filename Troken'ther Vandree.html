Troken'ther Vandree was the matron mother of House Vandree in Menzoberranzan around 1358 DR .

Personality

The drow elf matron was known for being inefficient in challenging other houses, such as House Barrison Del'Armgo .

History

Troken'ther was killed by her daughter Fiirnel'ther Vandree in 1388 DR .
