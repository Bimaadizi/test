Incense of meditation was enchanted to focus a cleric 's spells to their maximum effect.

Description

This item was formed into small rectangular blocks that gave off a sweet smell when burned. They appeared identical to incense of obsession .

Powers

A cleric or priest who lit incense of mediation while praying for a full eight hours would find that any spell they cast would experience maximum results. The effects of this incense lasted for a full day.

Notable Owners

Beldora Thiiruin , the owner of the Maid of the Moors in Mornbryn's Shield , carried some incense of meditation .
