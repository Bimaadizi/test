Abilities

Tipsy was an exceptionally dexterous and uncannily lucky halfling. He was a talented rogue but laziness was his most prominent trait. Many considered Tipsy to be selfish, unreliable, and untrustworthy, especially in a crisis.

Possessions

Tipsy Toehammer had a special throwing dagger harness draped across his chest for ease of access to his main weapons. Along with the daggers, the halfling had a ring of invisibility handy.

History

Ever since his childhood, Tipsy was a lazy person. His natural size and aptitudes guided him toward the life of thievery. Tipsy's uncanny luck certainly helped him to make a name for himself. Many claimed that the halfling's luck could even defy gods .
