The Wemic and Wineskin was a bustling tavern located in the town of Tulmon on the Lake of Steam in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tavern was well lit and usually so busy that it felt like a marketplace inside.

Activities

The Wemic and Wineskin was a popular spot among Tulmon's younger fashionable crowd. The tavern offered a wide variety of beverages, but the only fare to be had was spiced buns.
