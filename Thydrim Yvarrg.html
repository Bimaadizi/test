Thydrim Yvarrg , who was nicknamed the Bearkiller , was the Iron Lord, or Huhrong , of Immilmar in Rashemen during the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Thydrim was slain by a Thayan assassin in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR . Rather than raising him from the dead, the wychlaran opted to install Volas Dyervolk as the new Iron Lord as they believed he would be easier to control.

Relationships

Thydrim was married to Nytharri . They had several children, the eldest of whom was Fyldrin .
