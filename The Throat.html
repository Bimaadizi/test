The Throat was the name given to a small bay found in northern Var in the Shining Lands .

Geography

The Throat sat in the northwest corner of Var. It offered natural shelter for several notable towns, including Var's capital city Pyratar . It was roughly enclosed by the islands of Vathal and Mordyr .
