Cathal Sunspear was the counter clerk for the Aurora's Realms Shop, Waterdeep Way Catalogue Counter in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

She was the daughter of a wealthy family of Tethyr , the Sunspears who ruled County Bardshyr .

She moved to Waterdeep and was working for Aurora's by the mid-1360s DR.

Activities

As well as her work at the shop, Cathal was an investor. She paid close attention to the backroom deals of the city's noble families and guilds.

Description

She was middle-aged.

Personality

She was calm and cultured.
