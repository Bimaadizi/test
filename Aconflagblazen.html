Aconflagblazen was a red dragon who was known as the Steam Snake.

Activities

She was known to lair in the sea caves of Velmhold .

History

Aconflagblazen seized control of the port known as Velmhold, which was previously held by humanoid tribes for many centuries. Prior to being owned by such tribes, Aconflagblazen's home had been occupied by the dwarves of Clan Gemcrypt and elves from the Forest of Tethir . After much time, an unknown dragon slayer killed her. The name of Aconflagblazen's lair had changed over the centuries, and as of 1352 DR , was known as the Cloister of St. Ramedar , where Ilmatari monks lived.
