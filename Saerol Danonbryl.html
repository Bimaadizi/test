Saerol Danonbryl was a member of the Zhentarim in Mulmaster .

History

In 1491 DR , Saerol was ordered by his master to kill the Lantanese duelist Audrec Se Tourne , who was extorting a noble of the city who was allied with the Zhentarim, during one of the gladiator matches in the Black Blade and Bloody Boar tavern.
