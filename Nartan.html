Nartan was a successful dwarven shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He owned Nartan's Fine Foods , an expensive dry goods store located in central Daggerford along the High Road . Nartan imported rare items from as far away as Calimshan and even Chult .
