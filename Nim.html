Nim was a nimblewright at the House of Inspired Hands in Waterdeep in the early 1490s DR .

Activities

Nim was an avid and capable inventor. Some of its inventions included a partially functional mechanical sparrow , as well as a nimblewright detector . It was also capable of constructing other nimblewrights.

History

Nim was built by a Lantanese wizard who, during a visit to Waterdeep, decided to donate him to the local church of Gond . Out of loneliness and boredom, Nim started creating his own inventions.

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Nim created a nimblewright that went rogue and escaped from the temple. The rogue nimblewright was befriended and then recruited by Yalah Gralhund as a servant. It was given a necklace of fireballs and tasked with assassinating the gnome Dalakhar and obtaining the Stone of Golorr , triggering the detonation of a fireball in Trollskull Alley . When informed about those events, the cleric of Gond Valetta decided to strip Nim of all his construction materials.
