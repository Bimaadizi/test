Ulmar , who was nicknamed the Watchful , was a fence of stolen goods inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Ulmar operated out of Crommor's Warehouse located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward on the Reach . Specifically, he dealt in stolen ships, shipboard items, and cargo.
