Force was a magical quality that could be added to any weapon, even ammunition such as arrows .

Use

It allowed the wielder to make destructive blasts of magical force with each attack. The weapon could even shatter through a shield spell or bracers of armor . Incorporeal creatures could also be hurt by force weapons.

Creation

Applying the force property required the spell wall of force .
