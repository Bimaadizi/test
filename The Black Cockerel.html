The Black Cockerel was an aging inn located in the city of Ordulin in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .

Inhabitants

Known for its sloped floors, ragged carpets, and dusty tapestries, the Black Cockerel was a huge establishment that offered great accommodations to anyone with the coin to pay.
