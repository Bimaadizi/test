Shalais Wyrmwood was a young noble in Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Rune Lords Triumphant, 1487 DR , Shalais was among the nobles who gathered to discuss the threats facing the kingdom. She looked excited for the dangerous times she lived in.
