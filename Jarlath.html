Jarlath was a laborer, packer, and a delivery man of the Baldur's Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Jarlath was a pale-skinned gold dwarven man with deep brown eyes and a goatee in place of a more traditional beard and slicked back hair. He had an extremely thick neck and muscled body.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Jarlath was employed by Baldur's Mouth and could be found outside the broadsheet publisher's office packing up deliveries of fresh issues. The man was a busy diligent worker who lamented dramatically increased work load after a gift of an enchanted printing press to the broadsheet's editor-in-chief.
