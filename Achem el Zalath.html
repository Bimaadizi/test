Achem el Zalath was a Calishite wizard inhabiting Almraiven in eastern Calimshan during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

He was a member of the Guild Arcane in Almraiven.

History

Achem was a student of magic who graduated from Blackstaff Tower in Waterdeep .
