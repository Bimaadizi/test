Ramshorn was a small village located on Oman's Isle within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The village was located along the southeastern coast of Oman's Isle along the Strait of Oman . Ramshorn sat on the southerwest edge of an unnamed marsh.

History

The village was reduced to ruins after giants passed through the region following the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR .
