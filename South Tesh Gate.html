Location

The South Tesh Gate sat on the southern side of the Tesh Bridge that spanned the River Tesh where it met the Moonsea .

Defenses

The gate was flanked by a pair of guard towers. Furthermore, a Zhentilar guardpost was located just inside the gate in the Foreign Quarter.

Activities

The South Tesh Gate provided access to both the Foreign Quarter via the Tesh Bridge from the Common Quarter and Harbor District in southeastern Zhentil Keep.
