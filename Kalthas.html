Lord Kalthas was a nobleman of Zhentil Keep in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

At the time of the meeting with Manshoon , he was wearing a fur-trimmed doublet and was styled with a moustache, which was of sand color.

Personality

During his meeting with Manshoon in 1357Â DR , he showed himself as boastful and confident.

Activities

He was the general of the armies of Zhentil Keep north of the Moonsea by the mid-14 th century DR.

History

During a meeting of the High Lords of Zhentil Keep with Manshoon in Mirtul 21 of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Lord Kalthas boasted of the strength of Zhentil Keep, and announced the defeat of Arkhigoul , a lich, arguing it would mean they could relax their forces in Thar . The meeting was interrupted by the sudden departure of Manshoon.

Rumors & Legends

Per agents of the High Imperceptor of Bane relating those events, he was a fool that started telling all grandly nearly from the moment the meeting started.
