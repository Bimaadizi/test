Ajhalanda was a sorceress in service to the Coronal Eltargrim Irithyl in the 3 rd century DR .

History

In 241 DR , she accompanied Coronal Eltargrim Irithyl into the Vault of Ages alongside a number of other sorceresses.

In 261 DR , she helped during the laying of the mythal by weaving spells that listened for the names and titles of Mythanthor .
