House of the Waves was a temple dedicated to Trishina in the aquatic elf city of Myth Nantar .

Location

This temple stood along Chamal Avenue , in the Elves Quarter of Myth Nantar. Like all temples in Myth Nantar, there was a 20 feet (6.1 meters) perimeter around the building that was free of coral . The coral that grew around this boundary, which was clearly defined as if moving around a force field, was always as smooth as silk .

Structure

This temple stood two stories tall.
