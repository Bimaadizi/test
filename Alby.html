Father Alby was a priest of Kelemvor who watched over the temple in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR . He was assisted by a younger cleric , Nerys Kathon .

Activities

As a faithful of Kelemvor, Alby offered coins as a form of compensation to those who performed favors for the Baldurian church .

Description

Alby was a reasonable and determined man capable of sensible compromise. He did whatever he had to in order to maintain and protect his temple.

History

Some time in the 1480s DR, he hired the Heroes of Baldur's Gate to investigate some goings-on at his temple.

In 1492 DR , the priest joined the Cult of the Absolute .
