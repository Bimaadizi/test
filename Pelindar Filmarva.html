Lord Pelindar Filmarva was an inhabitant of the mining town of Leilon during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Pelindar was the "Lord of Leilon" circa 1357 DR . He was a close ally with Waterdeep and trusted by the Lords' Alliance .
