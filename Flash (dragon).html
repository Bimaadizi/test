Flash was a young red dragon that dwelled within Arkhan's Tower on the plane of Avernus in the Nine Hells , circa the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR .

Flash resided within the tower along with three other young chromatic dragons , Slarkas , Vermilius and Vistalancer , and the adult white dragon Obatala . Collectively they protected the master of the tower, the servant of Tiamat , Arkhan the Cruel .
