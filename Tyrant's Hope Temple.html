The Tyrant's Hope Temple was a temple dedicated to Bane located in the village of Kzelter during the late 14 th century DR .

History

The temple was built circa 1369 DR over the ruins of an old temple to Oghma , after the conquest of the village by the Knights of the Black Gauntlet .

Inhabitants

The temple was inhabited by Neth Arrovar , the resident cleric. Neth didn't spend all his nights there, having claimed the best home of the village as his residence instead.
