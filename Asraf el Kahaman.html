Asraf yn Malik el Kahaman yi Manshaka was one of the most prominent members of the Moonstars in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .

Personality

For unknown reason he remain loyal to Khelben Arunsun even after his transformation.

History

Asraf was initially a blood warrior of Manshaka ; after he entered in the Harpers as deep cover agent. He sided with Khelben in during the archmage's trial and entered in the Moonstar. At one point Asraf was assaulted and bitten by a vampire but even after his transformation in a night predator he remained loyal to Khelben and continued to be his agent in the Vilhon Reach region.
