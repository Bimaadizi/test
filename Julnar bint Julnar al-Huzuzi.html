Julnar bint Julnar al-Huzuzi supervised Huzuz 's Foreign Bureau in 1367 DR .

Activities

Julnar reported to Ambassador Jiraad , but she directly managed the bureaus of local relations, Zakharan relations, and distant relations. She was a competent ambassador and bureaucrat.
