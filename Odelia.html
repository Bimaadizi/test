Odelia was the lady-in-waiting who served Lady Arietta of House Seasilver during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

She was a dutiful attendant, seeing to all of Lady Arietta's needs without complaint.

History

When Marsember was assaulted by the shades of Netheril , on Uktar 2 of the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Odelia was directed to stay behind at the family's city estate while the nobles and their soldiers fled to Elversult . While Arietta tried to smuggle Odelia aboard the family's ship within one of her larger trunks, they did not act swiftly enough. Odelia was slain by a shade that made its way into Lady Arietta's chambers within the Seasilver estate.
