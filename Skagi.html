Skagi was a shadar-kai warrior living in the Shadowfell side of Ikemmu in the late 15 th century DR .

Relations

He was Cree 's brother and was a close friend of Ashok .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Skagi traveled with Cree, Ashok, and Vedoran on a quest to rescue the witch Ilvani from a shadar-kai enclave in the Shadowfell.

In the Year of Deep Water Drifting, 1480 DR , he journeyed with a caravan to Rashemen to discover the cause of the visions that were haunting Ilvani.

Description

Skagi had muscular arms and long grey hair. His torso was covered in green tattoos.

Equipment

Skagi carried a falchion .
