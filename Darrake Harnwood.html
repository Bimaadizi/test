Darrake Harnwood was a War Wizard of Cormyr until the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

For whatever reason, Harnwood was known by name to Marlin Stormserpent .

History

When in Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Marlin Stormserpent gathered a group to covertly appropriate the Wyverntongue Chalice from Castle Obarskyr in Suzail , a group of his hirelings arrived late after having had to slay a War Wizard who was following them. Stormserpent asked if it was Darrake Harnwood, and the killer agreed. They also said that he'd been disposed of at some jakes.
