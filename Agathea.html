Agathea was a powerful night hag who dwelled in the Underdark in the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR , Agathea lived in her hut in the Underdark near Sschindylryn , not far from Nessaleesa's Garden and the Great Waterfall . She shared her space with a number of night hag sisters, inviting travelers in for dinner.
