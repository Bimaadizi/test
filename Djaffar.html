Djaffar , or Djassar , was the leader of the Hammers , the Marchion of Mirabar 's personal defence.

Description

Djaffar wore a plumed open-faced helmet.

Relationships

Djaffar was known to eye of Shoudra Stargleam in a romantic fashion, though it was not known if Shoudra felt the same way.
