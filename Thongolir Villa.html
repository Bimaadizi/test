Thongolir Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Thongolir .

Location

The Thongolir Villa was located in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep , on the northeast corner of Feather Street and Rough Road . It stood across from the Eagleshield Villa .

Structure

This building complex consisted of two high-quality buildings, one that was two stories tall and another that stood a single story tall, one being the mansion proper and the other a guesthouse.

Like many Waterdhavian villas, it was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden.
