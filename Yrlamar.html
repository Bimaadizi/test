Yrlamar was a wizard who, in his old age, found himself imprisoned in the dungeons underneath the Fortress of the Five Vultures in Calaunt . Deprived of his spellbook , he was unable to escape. Yrlamar was chained next to the wizard Thogaf , and spent a lot of time training his much younger colleague in the art of magic .
