Vivienne was a woman who lived on the Sword Coast during in the mid—14 th century DR .

Relationships

She had a husband who worked as a farmer.

History

Vivienne's life was adversely affected by the iron crisis that plagued the Sword Coast in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR . When her husband was riding his horse -drawn plow, the iron deteriorated and he was crushed beneath the plow and dragged along for 20 yd (18 m) by the panicked beasts. He remained in a state that was incurable by the priests of Baldur's Gate , and Vivienne's life was left in ruin.

She traveled to the Blushing Mermaid inn, seeking adventurers or anyone that would help end the calamity that befell the land she called home.
