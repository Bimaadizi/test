Yathchol ( pronounced : /ˈ j ɑː θ k oʊ l / YATH-kohl ) was a village in the Middledark of the Northdark Underdark . It was inhabited by chitines who had been former slaves of the drow . It was located relatively close to the drow city of Ched Nasad .
