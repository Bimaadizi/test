TypeMagical Harp

A harp of healing was a variety of harp imbued with restorative magic .

Powers

This harp would gradually heal any wounded creature within 10 feet (3 meters) of it when played. This could be done for eight consecutive hours before a bard began to seriously risk misplaying it.

If the playing of the harp ceased for whatever reason, it would not continue healing affected creatures until they became wounded again.
