Marimo were a kind of moss that grew into the shape of a ball. They were found at the bottoms of certain lakes, including Lake Glorfindar .

Usage

The marimo of Lake Glorfindar were sometimes inadvertently caught by fishers. Bright lumens or flakes of blacklead were often found within cut open specimens.
