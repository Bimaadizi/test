Candalo Windroot was a elven druid in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Candalo had short black hair, and wore a green and brown leaf-themed robe while carrying a long wooden staff .

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Candalo joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their base camp at the ruins of Myth Drannor . He served as a trainer for prospective druids at the camp.
