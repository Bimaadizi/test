Angus Materi was an important member of the Zhentarim and the leader of the Outriders of Darkhold , an elite cavalry troop of vanguard raiders based in Darkhold .

Personality

Angus was among the most easily encountered of the Zhent secondary leaders. He had an intense hatred for king Azoun IV of Cormyr and attacked all Purple Dragons on sight.

Relationships

The fighter reported all activity in the area surrounding Darkhold to the Pereghost .
