Nako Nang was a wu jen adventurer and merchant in Tabot circa Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) .

Activities

Nako had a small house in the town of Talung but was most often out with his caravans. A servant minded the house while he was away, and was well-paid and happy.

He traded furs and ice in exchange for timber and steel , and sometimes new spells . Loving life on the caravans, he had journeyed all over Tabot and much of T'u Lung . Nako was an able guide and a true adventurer, telling many stories of the legends of Tabot and his own deeds. He respected Tabot's High Lord of Oceans and the theocratic government.
