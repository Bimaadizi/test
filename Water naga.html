Water nagas lived within rivers and lakes. They preferred solitude and isolation.

Description

Water nagas were about 10​ to ​20 feet (3​ to ​6.1 meters) long and weighed 200‒500 lb (91‒230 kg). They had scales that ranged in color from emerald green to turquoise. Along with these were scales in reticulated patterns that ranged from light olive and dark emerald greens to dark browns and greys. Their bodies were lined with frilled spines that stood on edge when excited or agitated.

Behavior

Water nagas were known to be very curious creatures with a neutral attitude towards life. However, they also had bad tempers and were quite mischievous. They rarely attacked other creatures unless threatened.

Biology

The fangs of a water naga were lined with some form of poison .

Abilities

These creatures had an innate ability to cast a variety of spells , except for those involving fire .

History

Water nagas were the first species of naga created by the sarrukh , specially bred for traveling across the vast land and waters of Abeir-Toril .

Sometime after the fall of Mhairshaulk , a group of water nagas attempted to reverse the diverse subraces of naga that had developed in its wake, but their efforts resulted in the creation of the nagahydras .

Society

Homelands

These creatures were typically found inhabiting the deep depths of clear freshwater pools, lakes, and rivers.

Some were known to inhabit the River Ashaba of Cormanthor , and many others were found in the Nagaflow and Nagawater in Southern Faerun.

In the Zakhara region's Crowded Sea , they could be found on the archipelago of Jazayir al-Sartan .

Language

They referred to themselves as "Naja'ssara" and to all other beings as "Dista'ssara". The latter term meant "hands of the embodiment", and they even used this term to describe other species of Naga that they didn't consider real nagas, like banelar nagas .

Religion

It was said that water nagas were among the followers of Dagon .
