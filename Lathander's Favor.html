Lathander's Favor was a type of enchanted amulet created by the Church of Lathander and sold in the Morninglord's temples.

Powers

Each of these amulets was ensorcelled with Lathander's divine magics allowing the wearer to receive the deity's blessing once per day.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , Lathander's Favors were sold in the Morningstar Haven , temple of Lathander in Westgate .
