Khostal Hannas was a shopkeeper inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Khostal owned a shop called Fine Nuts that was located in the southeast section of the Dock Ward on Spices Street just to the west of where it was intersected by Wharf Street . He sold nuts from all across Toril .
