Enda Yate was a halfling rogue in Waterdeep in 1479 DR .

History

In 1479 DR, having heard about a ruby -eyed-statue somewhere inside Undermountain , Enda agreed to join the doomed Rutherford Urmbrusk 's party. When Rutherford disappeared, the halfling abandoned her companions in search of the statue, discovering the statue's general location. So Enda went back to the surface and recruited a large number of ruffians to help her in her treasure hunt.

So, leading a large group, Enda ventured back into the dungeon. However they met a large tribe of lizardfolk followers of Sess'innek and in the fighting most of the Enda's fellows were killed or captured.

Enda herself and a few of her ruffians hid in a room, praying for their lives with only Enda without any great injures.
