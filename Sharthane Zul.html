Sharthane Zul was the human high priest of Cyric who presided over the House of Cyric in Bezantur in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . As the most influential member of the church of Cyric in Thay , he was one of the most powerful individuals in that realm as of the late 1360s DR. His influence was said to rival that of the zulkirs .

Personality

He was a masterful politician as well as suspected of being both a sadist—amusing himself with a private underground torture chamber—and a masochist—known to willingly give up his life energies in exchange for sexual favors from a succubus .

Activities

Sharthane presided over the largest congregation of Cyric-worshipers in all of Thay at the House of Cyric. He lived on the temple grounds in a personal mansion, which was opulent enough to have been the home of a king. He had hundreds of personal slaves .

He was known to traffic with tanar'ri for information and entertainment.

History

During the ongoing Thayan civil conflicts of the mid-to-late 14 th century DR, Sharthane was careful to maintain neutrality lest his political power be tested by the Red Wizards .
