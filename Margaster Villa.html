Margaster Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Margaster .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's North Ward within a neighborhood enclosed by Suldown Street and Vhezoar Street . Margaster Villa sat on Stabbed Sailor Alley and Shattercrock Alley within the neighborhood.

Description

The walled compound contained four buildings arranged around a central garden. All four buildings stood two storys tall.
