Ashtukzu II was the third King of the Mulan Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Ashtukzu II was the son and rightful heir of the throne, but his cousin Thunn ousted his father, King Ashtukzu I , and claimed the throne for himself. Ashtukzu II avenged his father in the Year of the Slaked Blade, 4 DR , when he overthrew his cousin and became king. He reigned until the Year of Dark Venom, 37 DR .
