Aurora's Realms Shop, High Road Catalogue Counter was an outlet of the Aurora's Emporium chain in the North Ward of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . It was one of six such outlets in the city.

Location

It stood on the High Road , next door to the House of Healing .

Description

It was situated within the ground floor of an old house, grand but narrow, where the upper floors had been converted to luxury apartments, though it was later said to have only one story.

Services

This shop sold the various goods found in Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue . Perhaps the most lavish of the six outlets in the City of Splendors, it kept a great many rare and pricey goods on site in the store in order to entice wealthy noble customers.

Staff

In the mid-1360s DR , the beautiful Phandalue Tarinthil served as a counter clerk, known for her sharp tongue, while the sinister Quirtan Ondever acted as the service-mage. There were also four guards who worked shifts of two on and two off.
