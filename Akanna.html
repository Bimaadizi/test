Relationships

Akanna was aided by a number of followers, similarly devoted to the Dark Sun. Most notable among them was the Half - green dragon Blackguard named Ziatar .

History

In the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR , Akanna and her fellow Cyricists took control of Bhaal's temple in the Forest of Wyrms . While they slew many of the Bhaalists they spared the life of High Priestess Madele and imprisoned her instead.

Some eight years later , Akanna's mind was taken over by the illithid Darskhelin . The being used Akanna to perform a lengthy ritual that brought forth an enormous neothelid within the Cyricists' sanctuary.
