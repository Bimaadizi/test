Frouth was an orc reaver in 1484 DR .

History

In 1484 DR, during the War of the Silver Marches , Frouth was a member of the orc army that besieged Mithral Hall . He accompanied Szordrin Xorlarrin to secretly infiltrate the Keep as added muscle. However, he was discovered and defeated by an adventuring party.
