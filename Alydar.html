Alydar was an elf fighter and adventurer who was active in the city of Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Possessions

Alydar dressed in very refined clothing.

History

In the late 14 th century DR, Vernon Condor conducted a census of adventurers in the city of Ravens Bluff, which was later publicized in an issue of the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter broadsheet . Within that census Alydar was included in a list of the city's most skilled active adventurers.

Sometime later, a group of adventurers were hired by Davy Jones to hunt down a mammoth and stopped by Flirin's Sushi Bar . There they found both Alydar and Charles Oliver O'Kane waiting in line for the restaurant.
