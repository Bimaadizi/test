Mae'Var was the leader of a guildhall of the Shadow Thieves in Athkatla in Amn in the late 1360s DR .

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , he employed Gorion's Ward in some missions until he ordered him to kill his superior Renal Bloodscalp in order to ascend in the Shadow Thieves' ranks. Eventually, Renal sent Gorion's Ward back to kill him.
