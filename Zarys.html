Zarys was the leader of the Zhentarim operating out of a hideout beneath the Waukeen's Rest inn during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Zarys oversaw her group as they smuggled and sold illicit goods from their hideout. She allowed them to dabble in other income streams such as kidnapping and potential ransom of captives.

Relationships

She was brought into the Zhentarim by her fellow agent Rugan , but eventually rose above him within the organization's hierarchy.

History

When Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard was abducted from Waukeen's Rest in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Zarys ordered her men to prepare for the demolition of their underground hideout. She knew that Baldurian authorities would have to blame someone for the Duke's disappearance and would not allow Zhentarim operations to be compromised.

At the same time she was coordinating the special delivery of a sealed strongbox to some unidentified noble in Baldur's Gate . For the survival of her group it was imperative that the packed not be opened and its contents removed.
