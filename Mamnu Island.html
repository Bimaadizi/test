Mamnu Island was a large rocky island within the Great Sea off the coast of west Zakhara .

Geography

Mamnu Island was located off the southwest coast of the Pearl Cities region of Zakhara. The island was separated from Al-Suqut Mountains , that run up the coast of the mainland in this region, by a narrow stretch of sea just a few miles wide. Mamnu Island was 150 miles (240 kilometers) long and 40 miles (64 kilometers) wide. An unnamed mountain range dominated the central spine of the island, while rocky beaches formed along the shore. The island was uninhabited as of 1367 DR .
