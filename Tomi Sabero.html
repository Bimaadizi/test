Tomi Sabero was an explorer from the city of Shou'Kuan in Ch'ing Tung Province in Shou Lung in the mid–14 th century DR .

History

In late Shou Year 2608 (1358 DR) or early Shou Year 2609 (1359 DR) ], he went to explore the Shao Mountains . There, Tomi Sabero was bitten by mosquitos that infected him with the Nyamuk Plague and he died from the illness. Later explorers may have stumbled across his body in the weeds.

Possessions

He was found with an enchanted uchi-ne +1 , an extensive and accurate map of the Shao Mountains region with friendly villages noted, and 11 tael and 5 ch'ien in coin.
