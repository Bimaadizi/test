Relationships

Lilith was the favored grandchild of the Lurraxol matriarch, Lady Ximora . Her grandmother served as a mentor of sorts.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Lady Lilith became involved in a fierce competition with Skarmaen Alibakkar over the Mantle of Waukeen . She sought the relic to prove that her ancestors founded the city of Trademeet so many years before.

A couple years later, in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Lady Ximora sought to reopen trade between Trademeet and the rebellious city of Riatavin . To this end she had Lilith attempt to convince High Merchant Logan Coprith to reopen the trade route, albeit unsuccessfully.
