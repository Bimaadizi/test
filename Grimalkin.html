Grimalkin was a cat belonging to Benelaius , a retired War Wizard , at his cottage outside the town of Ghars in Cormyr in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Abilities

Along with Razor , Grimalkin was one of almost a hundred cats in Benalaius's possession. They served him as companions, guardians, and agents, and were good judges of character. He could communicate silently with them.

Personality

He regularly lay on Benelaius's lap and liked being rubbed behind the ears. He also tended to wake his servant Jasper in the mornings by licking his face.

Description

He had soft fur.

History

By occupying Benelaius's lap after midnight on Eleint 20 of the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , Grimalkin was one of a number of cats present at the meeting of townsfolk he called to reveal the murderer of Dovo and Grodoveth , as well as an Iron Throne spy ring.
