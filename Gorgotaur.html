The Gorgotaur was a tauric gorgon who led the Cult of the Labyrinth in the Northdark in the late 14 th century DR . He claimed to be a child of the demon lord Baphomet .

Personality

He was a notably ruthless leader who pushed his cultists past the brink of exhaustion.

Activities

Under the Gorgotaur's leadership, the Cult of the Labyrinth launched numerous raids on drow and duergar merchants traveling between Menzoberranzan and Gracklstugh .
