Kella the Fair-Dealing was a merchant who lived on the Sword Coast North during the late 15 th century DR .

History

During the Siege of Helm's Hold , circa the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Kella set up shop at a small camp located just outside of Helm's Hold on the outskirts of Watcher's Forest . She was accompanied by the ranger Dassar Truebow , the hedge wizard named Nottin , and Meryth Heartwood .
