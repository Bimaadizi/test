Selgoth Zun was a Red Wizard of Thay that joined Droic Orgul 's raid on Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Abilities

Selgoth knew spells like ball lightning , cyclonic blast , and necrotic ray .

History

Selgoth was found near the Irithlium in Myth Drannor, using a ritual circle of power drawn on the ground to enhance his defenses and spellcasting.
