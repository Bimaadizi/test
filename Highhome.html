Highhome was a dwarven fastness in the Cambro Mountains on Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles .

Description

This dwarven stronghold was naturally protected by the surrounding mountains.

History

Highhome was founded by dwarves circa –7000 DR by the settlers of Dwarvenhome .

Some time prior to –201 DR , the Sword of Cymrych Hugh was forged by Highhome dwarves.

Following the return of Sarifal in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the dwarves of Gwynneth would eventually be driven from their homes by evil fey , notably fomorians and spriggans . By the 15 th century DR , there were only a handful of defenders (known as cragwardens ) left to protect Highhome. Around the same time, a ki-rin named Daiwon fought off a red dragon and took refuge in Highhome.
