Lord Gould Wyvernspur was a member of the noble House Wyvernspur of Cormyr who lived in the late 10 th and early 11 th centuries DR .

History

Gould Wyvernspur was born in the Year of the Crooked Finger, 956 DR to Paton Wyvernspur and Jasanna Larkspar . Gould's only sibling was his sister Andra Wyvernspur . Later in life, he wed Amalee Winter and fathered two children. The older son, Lord Gerrin Wyvernspur , was born in Year of the Orator, 980 DR , eventually becoming a hero fighting red dragons in the attack on Suzail in the Year of the Dracorage, 1018 DR . Gould's and Amalee's younger son was Finder Wyvernspur who would eventually become a renowned bard , one of the founding members of the Harpers , and eventually, a deity .
