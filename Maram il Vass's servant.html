An unnamed servant attended the ultroloth Maram il Vass in the Deep Wastes in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

The long years of servitude had completely conditioned him into unquestioningly serving the yugoloth. He had even lost his name.

History

Circa 1486 DR , the servant welcomed the guests of his master at the door of his lair.
