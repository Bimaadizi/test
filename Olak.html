Sentinel Olak was a goblin warrior and lookout that helped guard the Shattered Sanctum , one of the outposts of the cult of the Absolute , during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Olak wore a suit of modified chainmail armor, adorned with leather scraps and bones. He also bore the mark of the Absolute .

Personality

Rather than attack non- goblins on sight, Olak preferred to berate and threaten anyone that dared approach him.

Relationships

He had a "pet" worg named Klaw .

History

Unfortunately, Olak did not participate in the celebration following his band's raid on the Waukeen's Rest . He had guard duty at the outpost just outside the Shattered Sanctum.
