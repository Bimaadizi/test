Wormblod was the chieftain of the Great Worm Tribe sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR , Wormblod led a group of his berserkers , an enslaved white dragon , and a shaman to try to break inside the Eye of the All-Father temple with the objective of find a missing member of the tribe, Noori . In truth, Wormblod only wanted the treasures inside.

Activities

Wormblod only occasionally led his warriors to defend their territory. However, every time he acted he wanted war and treasure.
