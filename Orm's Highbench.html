Orm's Highbench was the headquarters of a trading company in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Orm's Highbench was located along the major intersection Waymoot , in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This building stood four stories tall.

Services

One could hire the services of mercenaries at this establishment.
