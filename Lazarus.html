Lazarus was a drow psionicist from the Underdark , active in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Lazarus the Drow had dark, purple-hued skin, dark eyes, and dark hair, virtually unheard of among drow elves . He wore a hefty amulet around his neck.

Abilities

Lazarus possessed a powerful gift for psionics . All typical psionic powers used by this man were doubled in power and his presence rendered Lazarus's allies immune to psionics.
