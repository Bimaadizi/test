Andreth Thalendur was a War Wizard who served the Royal Court of Cormyr in Suzail under the direct command of the Royal Magician Vangerdahast Aeiulvana in the 14 th century DR .

History

As of the Year of the Spur, 1348 DR , Andreth Thalendur was placed in charge of enchanting speaking-stones that were in wide use among Coryrean nobles, court, and the War Wizards. Andreth found himself abusing access to the speaking-stones of his creation. However, he always claimed to have been trained to hear the words of the nobles but not listen to them.
