Westband was a soldier who served as a spycatcher of the Neverwinter Guard in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

He was tasked with apprehending, and specifically not killing, spies that worked against Neverwinter and its interests.

History

As of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Spycatcher Westband was operating out of the Broken Crown inn located on the High Road near Cragmire Keep . He worked with passing adventurers to catch members of the Blackdagger Bandits that were operating as spies in the region and retrieve any information they carried.
