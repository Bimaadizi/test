Narth was an elderly farmer that lived in Phandalin .

Description

Narth often went to the Stonehill Inn , Phandalin's inn at the centre of town.

History

In 1491 DR , Narth found out that the overseer of the Shrine of Luck , Sister Garaele , recently left Phandalin. On her travels, she became injured and made her way back to town.
