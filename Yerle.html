Yerle was a guardswoman who worked for the Zhentarim during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Yerle had fair skin and wore her hair in curled braids along the top, with sheared sides. She wore a suit of simple, studded leather armor and leather gloves.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Yerle was tasked with protecting Roah Moonglow during her dealings with the cult of the Absolute at the Shattered Sanctum . The Zhentarim and the cult were trading in slaves and dangerous amounts of smokepowder . Yerlo and her fellow guard Erna watched over a storerom in the sanctum, that held barrels upon barrels of the highly-volatile substance.
