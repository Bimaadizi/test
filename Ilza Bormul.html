Baronne Ilza Bormul was a renowned member of the noble patriar Bormul family of Baldur's Gate many years before the late 15 th century DR .

History

Baronne Ilza Bormul was a historic individual, a patriar who lived many years before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . Ilza owned slave-labor mines in the mountains of Amn , importing ingots of precious metals to enrich herself and the city of Baldur's Gate. The bloody nature of Ilza Bormul's ingots was often not mentioned in classless and history books , however, her cruel imports were named on page thirty seven of Baldur's Gate and the Dialectics of Plunder , a book sold in the Gate in the late 15 th century DR.
