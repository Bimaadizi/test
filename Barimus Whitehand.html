Barimus Whitehand was a law enforcement officer in the town of Voonlar sometime during the term of Bron Buorstag Hlammythyl , i.e. , from about 1355 DR until at least 1372 DR .

Personality

He did not pursue violence for violence's sake, but liked the peace and orderly calm that resulted from force applied appropriately.

Abilities

He was proficient with a greatsword , but had no other significant abilities.

Possessions

He wore chain mail but he could don plate mail stored in a few caches around town if needed. He carried a two-handed sword, club , and dagger .

History

Barimus joined in a fight on the Bron's side one night and was offered the position of deputy on the spot. At some point in his career he received some training from the Zhentilar .
