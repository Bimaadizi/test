Tersonm was a powerful wizard inhabiting Westgate in 1358 DR .

Description

Tersonm was cold and formal.

Activities

Tersonm sold potions and scrolls and tutored students on occasion if the price was right. He enjoyed placing magical traps on his possessions. He lived in the south section of the city along a branch of Southgate Market Street .
