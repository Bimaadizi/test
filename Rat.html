A rat was a long-tailed rodent, a species of animal . Rats with brown fur were favored by the Mulhorandi god Geb , while those with black fur were sacred to Loviatar . Rats of any variety were seen as signs of good omen to worshipers of Beshaba , Talona , and Yurtrus .

Behavior

Rats were generally cowardly creatures, but would become fierce if cornered or trapped. They feared fire, but if hungry enough could be driven to ignore this base instinct.

Species

Ecology

Thomas C. Quirkilious of the Chromatic Scale instrument shop in Baldur's Gate experimented with using ratgut to make lute strings. The experiment was a failure, as it proved difficult to process them into strings and the resulting fiber squeaked when played.

Habitats

Rats thrived in urban environments where they could easily feed off the leftover food and waste of the community. Deep in the Underdark near Menzoberranzan , scurry rats were hunted by riding lizards of the drow .

Beyond the Prime Material plane , rats could be found in the Domains of Dread .

Summoning

For those not well-versed in sea-based magics, the summon monster I spell aboard a ship would conjure between 2 and 8 shipboard rats instead of the usual land-bound summoned creatures. A swarm of rats could be summoned with the spell summon swarm .
