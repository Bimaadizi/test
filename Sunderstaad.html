Sunderstaad was a small town located in the kingdom of Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Sunderstaad was located on the coast of the Sea of Swords in northeast Gnarhelm near Lake Sunder . It was the northernmost settlement found in Gnarhelm. The town was situated on a small unnamed bay.
