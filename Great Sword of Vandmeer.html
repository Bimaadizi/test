The Great Sword of Vandmeer was a magical sword from the Whamite Isles in the Sea of Fallen Stars . Those with fighting ability could wield it, granting them impressive attack power in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Reputation

The Great Sword bore the name of the Whamite nation Vandmeer . The particulars of its relationship to Vandmeer were unknown.
