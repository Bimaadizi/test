The Riverbank Theater was a large playhouse located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The guildhall was located in south Zhentil Keep in the central section of the Foreign Quarter just north of Zhent Avenue .

Description

The acoustics within the theater were perfect for projecting sound. The Riverbank Theater could seat 2,500 patrons at a time. Globes of colored glass enchanted with continual light illuminated the plush interior.

Activities

The theater put on nightly performance by bards, jugglers, dancers, jesters, etc. A popular play featured here was Cyric the Duck Sun .

History

Before the destruction of the city in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , the structure was used to house humanoid armies employed by Zhentil Keep. After the city's destruction it lay abandoned for a brief period of time before being taken over by a group of bards and performers who could not escape the city. Upon hearing of the bards' occupation of the building, Lord Orgauth hired artists to restore the building so it could put on proper shows.
