Hruk was a hill giant sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Chief Guh took Hruk from his wife Moog as her own husband, like many others.

In Grudd Haug , Hruk was at the main entrance watching the door. Secretly he wanted to go back to Moog but did not dare defy Guh.
