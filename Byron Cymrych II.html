Byron Cymrych II , born Merrick Cymrych , was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the late 9 th century DR . He inherited a High Kingship with severely weakened authority and respect owing to the loss of the Crown of the High King by his grandfather .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High King Conn Cymrych . His successor was his second son, Tanner Cymrych .

History

Byron II assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Thistle, 885 DR , following the death of his father amidst the invasion of the Moonshaes by the Northmen . He changed his name from "Merrick" to "Byron II" in honor of his great-great-grandfather .

He ruled for seventeen years before dying of illness in the Year of the Queen's Tears, 902 DR .
