Jasrya Ilviiri was a tall female fey'ri who served Sarya Dlardrageth . Her skin was covered with diamond-shaped scales and she had marilith heritage. She was given control of the houses of Ilviiri , Aelorothi and Dhaorothi against the wood elves of the High Forest , along with Sarya's son, Xhalph .
