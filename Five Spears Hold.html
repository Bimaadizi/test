Five Spears Hold was a mining town west of the Cloven Mountains and east of the Omlarandin Mountains .

Inhabitants

Five Spears Hold was garrisoned with troops meant to protect the southern borders of Erlkazar . The leader of the barony was also the commander of the garrisons.
