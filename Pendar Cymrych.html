Pendar Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the early-to-mid 8 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the son of his predecessor, High King Alec II . His successor was his first son, Declan Cymrych .

History

Pendar assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Gliding Man, 738 DR , following the death of his father.

In the seventh year of his reign , the first raid in a centuries-long practice by the fire giants of Tuern occurred when their longships arrived in Gundarlun and sacked the city of Gundbarg .

Pendar ruled for eleven years before being killed by wolves in the Year of the Glass Eye, 749 DR .
