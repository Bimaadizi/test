Pendarn Mirandor was one of the early monarchs of Impiltur . He ruled as the 17 th member of the Mirandor Dynasty for over a decade during the late 2 nd century DR .

Relationships

He was the second son of Inrath Mirandor III , brother of Inrath IV , and stepbrother of Doraven "the Bloody" .

His only son, Ellarath served as his successor.

History

Some time during or before the Year of the Great Dwarven Gate, 162 DR , Pendarn returned to his native Impiltur from Cormyr , following the death of his elder brother Inrath IV. His return home was largely in response to the bloody coup initiated by their stepbrother, Doraven , who had slain Inrath and seized the throne of Impiltur for himself. Pendarn slew Doraven in turn, and reclaimed the monarchy in his brother's name.

Pendarn ruled Impiltur for over a decade and a half, until the Year of the Jealous Spouse, 178 DR .
