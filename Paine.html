Paine was an inhabitant of Daggerford around 1370 DR .

History

Once Paine was a Zhent executioner until she escaped to the North . She was paranoid about new settlers in Daggerford, afraid of possible Zhent assassins. She was right: the Zhentarim searched for her in order to kill her.

Her home was located in the northwest corner of Daggerford along Fields Lane .
