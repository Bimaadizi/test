Maid with Duster was a portrait painting created in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The portrait depicted a young maid dressed in a frilly blue blouse and white kerchief. She was painted holding a feather duster as if the woman was presenting it proudly to the observer.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , copies of the Maid with Duster painting were found in certain homes and establishments of the city of Baldur's Gate , the Western Heartlands , and within the ruined apothecary shop in the blighted village of Moonhaven .
