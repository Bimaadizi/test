Vinnia was a Night Blade who resided in the ruined Temple of Bhaal in the Undercity of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Vinnia was a light brown-haired and pale-skinned human woman armed with a crossbow . Bhaalist tattoos that decorated her neck and chest were visible from under the assassin 's garb.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Night Blade Vinnia was among the Cultists of Bhaal led by Cirian on a mission of venerating their evil deity through the slaughtering of innocents in the Bloomridge Park of the Lower City . It was unknown if the slaughter was commenced or if the Bhaalists were stopped and slain by the Absolute Adversaries .
