Renault Braveblood was a human assassin adventurer active in the 14 th century DR .

History

By the age of nineteen, Renault followed his mother's footsteps, who was a renowned assassin whose name was only known to the most wealthy houses of Faerûn . Like many young professionals, Renault faced the problem of not being able to get a job without experience and not being able to get experience without a job. With his mother's advice, Renault took on an adventuring career to learn to deliver death to his foes while surviving dungeon delves.

Sometime in the 14 th century DR, Greenwood was among adventurers interested in being sent into a mysterious dungeon by an unnamed sorceress to retrieve the equally mysterious magical orb from its depths.
