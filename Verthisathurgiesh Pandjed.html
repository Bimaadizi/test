Verthisathurgiesh Pandjed was the dragonborn patriarch of Clan Verthisathurgiesh in the late years of the 15th century DR , until his death in 1486 DR . He was a direct descendant of Khorsaya .

Description

According to Anala , Dumuzi , and Tarhun , Pandjed was a look-alike of his son, Mehen .

Personality

Pandjed was an aggressive individual, prone to fits of rage. Many compared him with the dragon tyrants of Abeir.

Abilities

Pandjed could breathe lightning.

Relationships

Pandjed was the elder brother of Anala, and the father of Mehen.

History

Around 1450 DR , Pandjed exiled his scion, Mehen, because he had a gay relationship with his clanmate, Verthisathurgiesh Arjhani , and didn't want to marry Kepeshkmolik Uadjit .

The situation worsened when Pandjed began to exile from the clan nearly anyone who annoyed him, greatly damaging his clan's reputation in Thymari society. He also brutalized Dumuzi, just because he reminded him of his rebellious son.

In 1486 DR , Pandjed died of a heart attack. Anala had a hand in her brother's demise.
