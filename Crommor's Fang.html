Crommor's Fang was a rocky island in the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Geography

It was tall, rocky, and had few trees, sitting half the Sea of Fallen Stars from Veltalar .

History

The Simbul liked to hurl her most destructive magics and stargaze at Crommor's fang. On the Year of the Black Wind, 1262 DR , she spell-battled Elminster Aumar there.
