Big Huido was a smuggler ringleader who worked with the infamous Guild in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He was a hin with a close-cut blond hair and brown eyes. The halfling's face was streaked with numerous long scars. Huido armed himself with two simple daggers .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Big Huido worked with a gand of smugglers: Galem Meats , Lo Blank , and Jezzy Topps , transporting weapons , drugs , and firewine into Baldur's Gate. The shipments were well defended and all were released into the hands of the members of the Guild only.
