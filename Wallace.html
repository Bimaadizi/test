Description

While he was an honest man, Wallace's judgement in business matters was not always the best. He had pale skin and dark brown hair.

Activities

Among his wares were various projectiles, some scant weaponry, and potions , including potions of love .

Relationships

Wallace was good friends with Mazzy Fentan , her sister Pala , and the rest of the Fentan family, having known them for decades.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Wallace started doing business with a new supplier, Barl . Unbeknownst to Wallace, Barl was a worshiper of the deity Talona . This resulted in Danno Fairfoot purchasing of a poisoned potion of love for his betrothedt Pala Fentan, which rendered her comatose. Soon after, Mazzy was asked for aid in investigating her sister's condition.
