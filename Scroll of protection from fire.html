A scroll of protection from fire was an enchanted scroll that offered protection from fire.

Powers

After reading this scroll a 30 ft (9.1 m) sphere of protection was centered on the reader. The sphere protected anyone inside from all forms of magical and nonmagical fire. The sphere lasted for 50–90 minutes.

Notable Owners

Usamah , a corsair sailing the Great Sea near Zakhara , carried a scroll of protection from fire .
