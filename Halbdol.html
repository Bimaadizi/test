Halbdol was a special paste used in the Cold Lands , specifically in Narfell .

Description

Halbdol was a thick black paste that gave off a flowery smell with a hint of mint .

Uses

This paste was spread over the eyelids and around the nose and mouth, making it easier to breathe in the harsh cold climate. It could even keep eyes from freezing.
