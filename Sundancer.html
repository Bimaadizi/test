Sundancer was an enchanted scimitar stored within the Gallery of Antiquities in the Jade Palace of the Necromancers in Ysawis .

Description

The hilt of this sword was made from pure ivory . Its crossguard was a pair of entwined biting snakes .

Powers

Sundancer was a scimitar of speed , granting unbelievable quickness to the wielder's strikes.
