The Western Tower , also called the Vine Tower , was a fortification in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . It was the headquarters of the Gauntlet of the Western Marches .

Location

By its name, it stood in the west of Loudwater, near the Vine Gate.

Inhabitants

Around 1372 DR , the Western Tower was the base of Gauntlet Jaida Zerezeal , who was soon replaced by Gauntlet Isyan Kiy'sisnos . Gauntlet Harazos Thelbrimm was briefly based there as well in Eleasis / Eleint of that year.

Structure

It had a main hall where the Gauntlet and members of the Loudwater Guard had their meals together, often the strong ale , rough bread , and meat still on the bone favored by human warriors.
