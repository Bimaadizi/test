Preeta Kreepa was a twisted wizard in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR .

Activities

Preeta lived in hiding in a stone hovel inside the hobgoblin settlement in the Sargauth Level ruled by Azrok . She was busy raising drow corpses using the animate dead spell in order to create an army for Azrok.

History

Once Preeta was a wizard assistant of Arcturia , who nevertheless performed horrible experiments upon her. So she escaped and settled inside the hobgoblin settlement in the Sargauth Level; the fearful hobgoblins left her alone.

In 1492 DR , however, the menace of the drow of House Auvryndar forced Azrok to ask Preeta for help and she agreed.

Powers

Preeta had a mouth twice as large as normal size full of sharp teeth, and two beholder eyestalks sprouting from her eye sockets. The eyestalks emitted a fear ray and a paralyzing ray.

Possessions

She wore the skin of a kuo-toa as a cloak.
