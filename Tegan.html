Tegan was an escaped slave in the Deep Wastes in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Tegan and her brother Drystan were two farmers in the Dalelands until, circa 1481 DR , they were captured in an orc raid. They were sold to the yugoloth Maram il Vass as a slave in its lair in the Deep Wastes. Circa 1486 DR , they managed to escape but were in very poor condition until they met an adventuring party that saved Tegan.

Personality

Tegan had a desperate need for help but her time with a yugoloth caused her to not trust anyone. Tegan was proud and stubborn.
