Ilmater's Aid was an enchanted amulet associated with the Ilmatari faith.

Description

The amulet appeared to shine brighter whenever the wearer shed some of their blood.

Powers

Ilmater's Aid bolstered the spellcasting ability of the wearer whenever they suffered physical or psychic harm. When they were badly injured or weakened, any non- cantrip spells they cast that damaged others were bolstered with additional force energy .

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , this amulet could be found within the Shattered Sanctum .
