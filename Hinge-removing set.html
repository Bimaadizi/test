Hinge-removing sets were a type of tool in the Realms used to remove hinges from doors or sealed containers.

Description

These sets contained a variety of small metal tools used for various applications.

History

A set of these items were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue for 30 gold pieces .
