House of Despana insignias were small enchanted items, imbued with magics and symbolizing the authority and power of the drow House Despana .

Description

An insignia of House Despana was a small trinket hoop made out of adamantine metal and obsidian . The insignia resembled a sun with an arrow attached to it. This arrow and hexagon glyph was the emblem of House Despana. Pink ribbon was tied through the hoop.

Powers

The insignia could produce three spells : blur , Melf's acid arrow , and Otiluke's resilient sphere , once per day each.

History

Malavon Despana created the House of Despana insignias for his brother, Ilmryn Despana , long before the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR . It was said that when the Church of Vhaeraun attacked the Lolthites , Malavon and Ilmryn found eachother on the opposite sides of the conflict and clashed. Ilmryn Despana was the Weapons Master of his house at the time, and was close to winning the brothers' duel, however, Malavon uttered a command word that awoken a secret magic hidden within the insignia . Immediately, Ilmryn was enveloped by a magical barrier that protected him from all harm but trapped him within. When the spell expired, Ilmryn emerged from the protective shell only to face more than a dozen of powerful tanar'ri . Ilmryn did not survive the battle and died screaming curses onto his brother's name.
