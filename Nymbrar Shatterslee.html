Nymbrar "Nighteye" Shatterslee was a traveling minstrel responsible for the composition and performance of Crying To My Harp , a collection of satirical ballads criticizing the Waterdhavian nobility that proved to be extremely successful when performed in Waterdeep . After enraging the targets of the criticism and attracting the attention of thugs, Nymbrar left the city.
