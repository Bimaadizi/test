Ruckert was a secret cleric of Ghaunadaur in Daggerford around 1370 DR .

Activities

In public, he proclaimed his faith was to Ibrandul and nobody discovered his secret patron. His home was located in between Tymora Alley and Wall Street in north Daggerford.
