Sarthryn was a city purported to export swords in the mid-14 th century DR .

Rumors & Legends

In the Year of the Chosen, 240 DR , the Knights of Athalantar under Helm Stoneblade passed themselves off as traders in a plaza of Hastarl in preparation for a sneak attack on Athalgard coordinated under Elminster Aumar , prince of Athalantar . One of them pretended to be selling swords from Sarthryn while in the plaza.
