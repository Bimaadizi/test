Shoal was a nereid who dwelled on the Sword Coast for a time during the mid–14 th century DR .

Abilities

Shoal was capable of killing someone with a mere kiss.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Shoal was enslaved by the ogre mage Droth . He had her draw in and attract passers so that he could rob and slay them.
