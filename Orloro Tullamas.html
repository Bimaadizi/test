Orloro Tullamas was a plump merchant inhabiting Tulmon along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Orloro had a commanding voice and enjoyed chuckling.

Activities

His store, Tullamas Sales on Shandlas Street , focused on the sale of dry goods, carrying items from "gloves to grain and candles to carts."

Relationships

Orloro was the nemesis of Horomel Ibrin , believing the younger merchants of Tulmon should focus on their own profits and steer clear of larger politics.
