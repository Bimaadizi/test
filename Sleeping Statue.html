The Sleeping Statue was a large stone carving found within the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 th century DR .

Description

The statue was carved into an open clifface on the Great Chamber level of the temple. The Sleeping Statue was massive, measuring 80 feet (24 meters) long by 40 feet (12 meters) wide.

History

The Sleeping Statue was a representation of the Enlightened One sleeping in a pose.
