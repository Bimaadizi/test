Skarn Brassaxe was a dwarven commander that committed his forces to the Army of the North , in order to combat the threat of the Shadovar during the late 14 th century DR .

History

In Mirtul of the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Skarn, his dwarves, and the rest of the Army of the North teleported from the Forest of Wyrms to the Shaeradim , just outside the Shadovar -crafted shadowshell . As the allied forces set up camp, Skarn and the army's other leaders were subjected to some enchantment that left them bickering with one another to the point of raising arms.
