The Lesser Hall of Perfect Understanding was a large dormitory cave found within the Horseshoe Temple Oasis in the Quoya Desert of the Hordelands during the mid 14 th century DR .

Description

The cave was located on the far left side of the great chamber level of the temple. The dorms measured 35 feet (11 meters) wide by 40 feet (12 meters) long. Three passages served as entryways into the cave.

History

During the temple's heyday, the hall was used as personal quarters for all of the abbots who lived within.

Inhabitants

The Lesser Hall of Perfect Understanding was occupied by an oni named Halbasho the Smart in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR .
