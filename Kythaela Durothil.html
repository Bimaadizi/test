Kythaela Durothil was a renowned elven weaponsmith and armorer living on Evermeet .

History

Kythaela spent nearly 150 years forging and crafting the two-handed sword Tahlshara . It was widely rumored that she clung to life for more than a century beyond her allotted time to complete the sword which was viewed as a masterpiece.
