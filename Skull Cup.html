The Skull Cup was an inn located in the northwestern Ring Caverns of Oryndoll . The inn catered to non- illithids . Drinks were served in goblets that were shaped from the skulls of humans , demihumans , and other humanoids . The safety of guests was not guaranteed and the patrons were also watched at all times by vigileators (security officials) and their agents.
