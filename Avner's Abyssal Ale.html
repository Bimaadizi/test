Avner's Abyssal Ale was a type of alcoholic drink found in the city of Zelatar , on the Abyssal plane of Azzagrat .

Description

This intoxicating drink was a heavy dark type of porter beer .

History

As of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Avner's Abyssal Ale could be purchased from demonic breweries and taverns in Zelatar. Unlike many of the drinks favored by the fiends who inhabited the realm, Avner's was suitable for consumption by inhabitants of the Prime Material plane . Further from the Abyss, Avner's Abyssal Ale was served at the Wandering Treant inn on the plane of Bytopia .
