Ergrith Klavulgrun was the Magister from 276 DR to 278 DR . After he made the pronouncement that kings should kneel to mages, rather than the other way around, he spent the rest of his tenure in a long battle against a force sponsored by nobles of Calimshan .

Personality

He was proud, blustering, and cruel.

Abilities

He was known to shapeshift, and to have at least one spell that transfixed his foes, leaving them in great pain. As soon as he became the Magister, he employed a spell to petrify the arms of a rival, shatter them, and finally let him bleed to death. He crushed another rival's head with a spell of battering ram force, closing a door through his skull.

History

After accepting the office that Azuth offered him, Ergrith immediately set out to slay three mages he'd been scrying on, taking their magic for himself. He spent one winter to master it, and then immediately sought more magic come spring. After a Tashlutan lord forbade him from slaying a court mage, Ergrith sought to terrorize noblemen into kneeling before mages, slaying more than a dozen lords of independent cities in the Vilhon Reach and in Tashalar , earning himself the nickname. Calishite rulers sponsored a force of wyvern -riding mages armed with as much magic as could be scrounged to take him on. Ergrith slew most of them in a month-long chase involving shapechanging and ambushes.

At one point, he transformed into a tombstone at a private mausoleum in Tashluta , from where he led twelve of his pursuers on a chase north across the lands bordering the Sword Coast . The pursuers attacked a black dragon whom they took to be him in disguise; he transformed himself to pretend to be amongst their dead, as they destroyed the wyrm. Afterwards, he got each of them alone and slew them, the last one near Neverwinter . He taunted and executed that last foe with such cruelty that a nearby Adriyana Voudsarr took her own shape again and challenged him to a duel.

Her attack was furious, and the spells left to him, few. When she turned one of his slaying spells back upon him, he died.
