Fleer was a fisherwoman from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Fleer was a human woman with a bronze skin tone, light brown hair and eyes. She wore a blue commoner's tunic and a workman's trousers.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Absolute crisis ravaged the Gate, Fleer's fishing was damaged further by pollution of the Gray Harbor . Fishers in the Lower City kept pulling up nets filled with fish covered in black oily goo. In reality, the fish was smothered in oil pollution left behind by a submersible used by the Cult of Bane to transport Lord Gortash 's prisoners into the drowned Iron Throne headquarters prison at the bottom of Gray Harbor.
