The River Chun was a small waterway found deep in the Black Jungles of Tashalar on the Chultan Peninsula of southwest Faerûn .

Geography

The river originated near the heart of the Black Jungles, flowing south down the center of the jungles for 100 miles (160 kilometers) before emptying into the Great Sea in an unnamed bay.

Inhabitants

Yuan-ti from House Jennestas patrolled the banks of the River Chun.
