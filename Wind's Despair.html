The Wind's Despair was the personal caravel of Quelzur Naismen , a piratical exiled Red Wizard of Thay in the 14 th century DR .

Description

The vessel had an average crew of 17 sailors and two medium ballistae . Although not a good captain, Quelzur's survival was guaranteed by his possession of a censer for summoning air elementals , which he used to fill the sails and ensure that the Wind's Despair always sailed at full speed, no matter the weather conditions.
