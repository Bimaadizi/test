Stench Alley was a small alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 th century DR .

Location

Stench Alley was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's Field Ward . It branched south off of Chop Street , branching several more times to connect with Logan's Walk and Tanner Alley .
