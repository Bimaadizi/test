Dhunrune was a small fishing village on the island of Snowdown .

Geography

This village was located across from the Kingsbay (about midway between the towns of Horstall and Kingsbay on Gwynneth ).

Inhabitants

In 1359 DR , Thauntiver the leader of The Broken Ring was living here, posing as a mere boat builder.
