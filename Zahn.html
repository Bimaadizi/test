Zahn was a Netherese arcanist and prolific spell inventor who lived during the Age of Discovery .

History

The mentalist developed seven spells, one per year. Many of their spells revolved around the senses. The spells they invented were clairaudience in 2931 NY (−928 DR) , mirage arcana in 2932 NY (−927 DR) , clairvoyance in 2933 NY (−926 DR) , find familiar in 2934 NY (−925 DR) , ESP in 2935 NY (−924 DR) , locate object in 2936 NY (−923 DR) , and finally, vision in 2937 NY (−922 DR) .
