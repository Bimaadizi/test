Beruel's Retort were a pair of enchanted dwarven throwing axes that could as easily be wielded in melee as they were effective at a great range.

Description

The Beruel's Retort axes were short-hafted weapons of steel and silver . These axes were designed to penetrate thick orcish skulls.

Powers

These thrown axes were silvered and possessed a +1 enchantment .

History

Circa 1368 DR , these axes were available from Taerom Fuiruim , the smith from the Thunderhammer Smithy in Beregost .
