Homeland(s)Lake Miir , Netheril
Skin color(s)Blue scales

Brench were a species of blue-scaled fish that inhabited Lake Miir .

History

Back during the days of Netheril , the small-net fishermen of Asram 's port city Miirsar went out on Lake Miir on a daily basis to fish for brench.
