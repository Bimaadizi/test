The Jansen Adventure Wear , or the Jansen-Brand Metal-Repellent Adventure Wear , was a unique set of armor that was invented and worn by the gnome inventor Jan Jansen .

Description

The Adventure Wear was made from a flexible yet strong material that was not native to Amn . Its appearance was objectively stylish.

It could be cleaned by dry-clean methods only.

Powers

The armor protected Jan from projectiles and partially limited the physical harm inflicted upon him. It did not restrict his spellcasting abilities in any way.

History

As of the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the patent for the Adventure Wear was still pending.
