Fiendish Alchemy was a book on the topic of the art of alchemy and the Part XIV of the Basics of Alchemy series penned by Haskinn Xhesilaphin , a dragonborn alchemist sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

Content

Most notably, Fiendish Alchemy reatured a chapter called Fiendish Flora , which listed plantlfe found on the layer of Avernus in the Nine Hells . Most of them were stated to be born from the vital fluids spilled in the Blood War .
