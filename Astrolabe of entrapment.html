Astrolabes of entrapment were rare magical items that could be found in Zakhara , the Land of Fate .

Description

These spherical astrolabes were usually made of metal. Inscriptions of planet and constellation locations were found on the exterior.

Powers

Those proficient with the use of regular astrolabes could use this to determine the time, date, and latitude.

The main power of this item was its ability to trap genies . Each of these items contained twelve genie prisons arranged inside of the metallic sphere. The currently aligned prison deactivated at the end of each calendar month. This lasted for the next eleven months. During this time the genie was trapped with no chance of release.

Only a powerful diviner was able to determine the exact type of genie trapped within each prison.
