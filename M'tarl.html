M'tarl was a drow lieutenant within Jarlaxle's Bregan D'aerthe .

History

In 1358 DR , while traveling through the deadly streets of Menzoberranzan seeking valuable information to help the organization's cause, Jarlaxle and M'tarl encountered Gromph Baenre .

To have a private conversation with Jarlaxle, Gromph transported M'tarl to an extra-dimensional realm. After their meeting ended, Gromph told Jarlaxle his lieutenant perished.
