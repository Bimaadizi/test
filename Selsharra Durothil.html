Selsharra Durothil was a sun elf , matron of the powerful Durothil clan, and a member of the Council of Evermeet .

During the daemonfey assault on elven settlements in Faerûn in 1374 DR , she and Ammisyll Veldann threatened to withdraw the support of the houses of Durothil and Veldann if Amlaruil Moonflower sent forces from Evermeet to aid the Faerûnian elves.
