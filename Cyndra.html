Queen Cyndra was a windsoul genasi and the ruler of Akanûl in the early years of the 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Personal

Cyndra was the daughter of Zavon , and she was also the mother of Arathane , who she bore late in life.

Political

Cyndra was considered a fair and just ruler by her subjects, and was well liked by both the nobility and the commoners of Akanûl.

History

Around the Year of Ten Terrors, 1429 DR , Cyndra became queen of Akanûl after succeeding her father, the late King Zavon. Cyndra led the survivors of Brassune to the city of Airspur , displacing its orc and half-orc inhabitants, and rebuilding the city into the new home of the displaced genasi . Cyndra also enacted laws and edicts that gained her the approval and support of the nobility, such as the Covenant of Stewardship and the office of the Four Stewards . It was considered that her rule was a renaissance of sorts for the genasi kingdom.

Queen Cyndra died of illness in 1469 DR , and was succeeded as queen by her daughter, Arathane.
