Kylie was a lightfoot halfling rogue of the adventuring company - the Lady's Lament from Waterdeep , active circa 1310 DR .

Description

Kylie was a cheerful halfling who used daggers and shortbow in battle.

History

Several years before the Year of Storms, 1310 DR , Kylie joined the Lady's Lament and quickly befriended his comrades. The halfling wanted to put a smile on the faces of his new friends via jokes and pranks. He understood well that "misery likes company."

In 1310 DR, the Lady's Lament, along with many other mercenaries, traveled from Luskan to the besieged city of Targos in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale .
