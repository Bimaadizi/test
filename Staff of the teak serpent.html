The staff of the teak serpent was an improved version of the staff of the serpent that could be found in the Cities of the Ancients region of east Zakhara .

Description

A staff of the teak serpent was 6 feet (1.8 meters) long.

Powers

This staff could be wielded as a potent magical weapon during combat.

If wielded by a priest , the staff would change into a giant teak serpent when thrown to the ground. The teak serpent measured 35 feet (11 meters) in length and was under the complete control of the wielder. However, the staff would be destroyed if the teak serpent was slain.
