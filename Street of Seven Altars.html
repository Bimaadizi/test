The Street of Seven Altars was a street running diagonally, northeast-by-southwest, through the Temple District in the city of Athkatla . Like the names of many streets in the temple district, the name "Seven Altars" was a remnant of its noted temples that dated back to before the 14 th century DR .

Geography

The southwest end of the street connected to Aundle's Walk , at the corner tavern Harfin Draether , before intersecting with Godsmoot a couple of buildings west.
