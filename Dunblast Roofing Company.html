Dunblast Roofing Company was a carpentry business in the city of Waterdeep that was owned by Elemos Dunblast in the 14 th century DR .

Location

This shop was located along the corner of Ironpost Street and Zendulth Street , in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep. This lasted until the late 14 th century DR, at which point the business moved from Ironpost Street to Coffinmarch .

Structure

This shop's building stood two stories tall.

Services

The Dunblast Roofing Company that offered carpentry and roofing services to customers.
