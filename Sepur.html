Sepur was the tenth High Mage of Silverymoon .

Activities

Sepur briefly held the position of High Mage from the Year of the Long Watch, 1230 DR , to the Year of the Weeping Wives, 1232 DR , before abandoning his position. His unplanned departure left dozens of mages dead within the city during the ensuing power struggle.

He was slain by an unknown assailant after he left Silverymoon.

History

Sepur was the apprentice of High Mage Orjalun who later appointed him as High Mage upon his retirement.
