A mirror-moth was a type of glowing moth found in the valley of Synnoria on the island of Gwynneth on the Moonshae Isles .

Ecology

Mirror-moths were found in the sweet-smelling forests of Synnoria. Groups of them could be seen trailing behind nearly all of the valley's fist-sized fireflies .
