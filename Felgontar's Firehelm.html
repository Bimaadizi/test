The Firehelm was a dining house in Marsember in Cormyr in the late 14 th century DR .

Location

The tavern was at the intersection of Gelguld Lane and Tarnsar Lane .

Services

The large dining house was popular, but respectable.

Rumors & Legends

In early spring of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , when Aumun Tholant Bezrar and Malakar Surth threatened their fellow merchant Durexter Dagohnlar and his wife, Starmara Dagohnlar , Malakar threatened cutting off Starmara's toes and giving them to Ponczer at the Firehelm to cook something with them.
