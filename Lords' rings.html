A Lords' ring was an enchanted ring given to all Lords of Waterdeep .

Powers

A Lords' ring provided the wearer with the magical benefits of a periapt of proof against poison . The ring could also be used to issue a sending to Khelben Arunsun or Piergeiron Paladinson , and safely teleport the wearer to a predetermined familiar location at will.
