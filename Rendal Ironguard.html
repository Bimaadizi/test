Rendal Ironguard was a Harper spy and the cook at Warmfires House in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was grim-faced.

Activities

He was a cook, and a Harper .

Relationships

He saluted Storm Silverhand in the manner of a fellow Harper and followed her orders, rendering it a foregone conclusion he was in fact a Harper.
