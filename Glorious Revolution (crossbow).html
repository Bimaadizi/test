Glorious Revolutions were enchanted heavy crossbows that were used by the leaders of the Glorious Revolution in the early 13 th century DR .

Powers

These crossbows possessed a +2 enchantment that made the weapon deadlier and guided its bolts toward the target. It was especially effective at hitting evil creatures. Its magics also increased the wielder's spell resistance.

History

These ranged weapons were used by the leaders of the Glorious Revolution, a popular uprising against the tyrannical Duke Bogob in the Year of the Falling Moon, 1217 DR . While the uprising failed, one such crossbow survived and was found on a corpse close to a glow spider nest in the Glowstone Caverns , not far from Port Llast in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR .
