A potion of speed , sometimes called a potion of haste , was a very rare potion that put its imbiber under the effect of the haste spell.

Description

The effect of these potions lasted for a minute each, doubling the imbiber's speed, and raising their durability . Because of that, potions of speed were commonly used by scouts and heralds . The liquid was yellow and had black stripes; the fluid seemed to swirl on its own.

Creation

One possible ingredient for creating these potions was sweat from a fast horse .
