Skuulaghh Alskayl was a smuggler and thief from the village of Maskyr's Eye in the Vast in the 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Infamous for his hedonism, Skuulaghh had lots of children born in wedlock and lots of bastards born out of it. One of his sons was Ulcrimmon Alskayl . His offspring formed a clan of hunters in Maskyr's Eye in the mid-1300s DR.

Rumors & Legends

Before his passing, Skuulaghh had supposedly gathered a great fortune, but his children were yet to find it as of around 1357 DR .
