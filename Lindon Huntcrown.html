Lindon Huntcrown was a member of the Huntcrown noble family of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR . He was the short-lived husband of Queen Raedra Obarskyr but was exposed as an agent of Netheril . He was publicly executed when she discovered his hidden correspondences, bringing significant shame and disbelief to his parents.
