Caravan Court was a large courtyard that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Caravan Court was located in the northeast section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . The northern side of the courtyard bordered the Trades Ward . Streets connecting with Caravan Court included Quarrel's Flight , Sahtyra's Lane , Ruid's Stroll , the Rising Ride , Belzer's Walk , and Caravan Street .

Activities

The court was a popular spot for old men to gather and watch others perform hard labor and sweat. Many brought their own stools or crates for the occasion.
