Launcel was a soldier in the Purple Dragons based in Castle Obarskyr in Suzail , Cormyr , during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Launcel was called upon by King Foril Obarskyr I in the wake of the massacre during the Council of the Dragon of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR . When the former Royal Magician , Vangerdahast , revealed the place where Vainrence and current Royal Magician Ganrahast were imprisoned, Foril ordered Glathra Barcantle , a high-ranked War Wizard , to take all of his Purple Dragons to save the trapped wizards, leaving him guarded by Launcel and Tarimmon alone.
