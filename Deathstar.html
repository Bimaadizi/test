Deathstar was a hobgoblin member of the Lostafinga tribe. He served under Lieutenant Julla and occupied a castle in the southern end of Kryptgarden Forest on the Sword Coast North in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR . While the other hobgoblins slept, he played a game involving sticks, bones, and stones with Lucky and Jurmane . Deathstar was proficient with the halberd and morning star .
