Lallara Mediocros was a human abjurer and archmage and the Zulkir of Abjuration within the ranks of the Red Wizards of Thay in the 14 th and 15 th centuries DR .

Description

Lallara was seemingly pale and vital but her age showed in the lines at the corners of her eyes, mouth, and soft flesh beneath her chin. She wore several rings on her fingers.

Personality

A particularly decadent and extremely chaotic woman, she counted herself among the (albeit unreliable) allies of Szass Tam . Lallara was a sort of creative artist in the field of torture and enjoyed practicing on her slaves. She also collected unusual magic items .
