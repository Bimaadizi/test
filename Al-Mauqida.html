Al-Mauqida , or the Hearth , was the bazaar in the city of Liham in north Zakhara .

Description

The Hearth was named for the multitude of red clothing worn by the roaming citizens of Liham. The marketplace was often clogged with camels and caravans, creating a congested bustle of activity.
