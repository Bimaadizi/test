Trasaal the Gold Mage was a powerful transmuter armathor during the Fall of Myth Drannor .

History

Trasaal was born in 429 DR . At the start of the Weeping War , Trasaal was the human representative in the Council of Twelve .

In Eleint 9 of 713 DR , based on a divination , Trasaal led Akh'Faer troops to attack an Army of Darkness contingent at Iolrath 's tomb. They managed to destroy the fiend troops but Trasaal and the Akh'Faer leading officer Kyrtaar Ammath were killed.
