Maelstrom's Punch was the specialty alcoholic drink from The Track in the city of Westgate .

Description

Maelstrom's Punch was a quite mundane and average libation brewed in-house by The Track's employees. What made it standout among many other swirls was the sorcerer who placed minor enchantments on each batch that highbred the drinker's senses.

History

By the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Maelstrom's Punch was created in and popularized by the Track, the underground racing rink, and gambling establishment on the Market Triangle of Westgate.
