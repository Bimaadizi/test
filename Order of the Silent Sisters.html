The Order of the Silent Sisters were a Cormyrean adventuring company that operated in the mid-14 th century DR . In Ches of the Year of the Wyvern, 1363 DR , they vanished without a trace in the Vast Swamp . Their service was commemorated by a memorial plaque in the office of Registrant General Lord Partic Thistle in the Royal Court of Suzail .
