Doughty was a knight of the Knights in Silver in the late 15th century DR .

History

In 1484 DR , during an assault by orc and drow on the Silver Marches , Doughty and Plenerond Silverbell were part of a contingent of Knights under Aleina Brightlance who helped in the defense of Sundabar .
