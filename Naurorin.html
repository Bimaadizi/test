Naurorin was one of the three war-leaders of the Tornskulls tribe of orcs that lived within the Dead Orc Pass during the 14 th century DR .

Abilities

Naurorin was supremely fast in battle and as such, led the tribe's raids during daytime hours.

Relationships

Naurorin enjoyed a strong rivalry with his fellow war-leader Koront .

History

Naurorin, Koront and Baerzel came into power after the death of the tribe's previous leader, Chief Glorlarkh died around the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
