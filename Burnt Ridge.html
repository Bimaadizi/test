Burnt Ridge was an area of burnt woodland outside the city of Myth Drannor that was used by its wizards to practice hurling their spells.

History

In 714 DR , it served as a last stand for the city's wizards who were overcome by the invading Army of Darkness , that would later bring about the fall of Myth Drannor .
