Five was a goblin child who lived in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Five wore a simple blue loincloth, a bone necklace, and leather wraps around his hands and arms.

Possessions

He carried with him a simple dagger .

History

Four was present when his friend Eight 's parents were killed during the cult of the Absolute 's raid on Waukeen's Rest in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
