Tyraturos was large, centrally-located tharch of Thay , that controlled and regulated much of the nation's overland trade.

History

During the rule of Tharchion Dimon , in the mid-14 th century DR , the tax stations along Tyratauros' trade routes collected reasonable tolls from Thayan caravans. The roads were well-patrolled and kept safe from possible dangers.
