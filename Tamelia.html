Tamelia was a wizard in the employ of the Zhentarim that regularly visited Voonlar , about every two or three tendays . She was most often seen in the company of deputy Marsara Storntil and this led to secret speculations by the town gossips about Marsara's love life. The deputy was apparently defensive about Tamelia because one farm worker, who imagined himself a potential suitor and had found courage by consuming alcoholic beverages, asked her bluntly about the rumors. Marsara proceeded to beat him so severely that she and Tamelia were never bothered by such rumors again.
