Description

This gemstone was transparent, colorless, and soft enough to carve without special tools or exceptional skill, but too soft for intricate carving because it tended to split. A typical specimen had a base value of 10 gp .

Powers

The power of this stone was its antipathy to magic . Spells were slightly easier to resist, and magically-induced injury was slightly lessened within a 10 ft (3 m) radius around an augelite gem.
