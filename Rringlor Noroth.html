Ringlor Noroth , also known as the City of Soaring Shadows , was the largest settlement of cloakers in the Upperdark .

Geography

It was located in the Underdark beneath the Marching Mountains in Calimshan . The city was founded around an ancient and ruined bridge known as the Shadowspan , which connected the Lake of Radiant Mists to a mysterious lake located in the Shadowfell . The bridge was said to predate the dwarves of Old Shanatar , and had long since collapsed.

Government

The city was ruled by a council of twelve cloaker lords known as the Shadoworb Conclave who took directions from a mysterious entity from the Shadowfell known as the "Emissary".

The cloakers of Rringlor Noroth ascribed to a rigid caste system.

History

Ringlor Noroth was founded when its twelve cloaker lords brought its population of cloakers together over ten thousand years prior the 14 th century DR . If dwarven legends of its ancient history were to be believed, it had not changed at all in that time. However, sometime in the 12 th century DR, the Shadoworb Conclave began a project to rebuild the Shadowspan in order to reconnect the lake on the Material Plane to its sister lake in the Shadowfell.
