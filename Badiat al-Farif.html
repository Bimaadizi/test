Badiat al-Farif was a member of the court of Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Activities

Concerned because the Grand Caliph was unable to produce a male heir, she inquired about the issue at the Mosque of Zann the Wise , tasking them to try to find a solution to the problem. Members of the Readers spent many months searching through texts, hoping to experience an insightful vision that would shed some light on the problem.
