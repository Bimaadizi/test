Jemuril's residence was a the home of a dwarf warrior named Jemuril located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The home stood two stories tall.

Location

Jemuril's residence was located in the far southeast corner of Waterdeep's Dock Ward on the east side of Tower Trail .
