Dungrunglung was a settlement of grungs located in the jungles of Chult in the late 15 th century DR . Dungrunglung was surrounded by a tall maze of magical, twisted vines. The interior consisted of a large pond with narrow edges dotted with tents. In the center of the lake there was a giant shrine dedicated to the goddess Nangnang .
