Hadra bint Aswiyah was a princess of of Muluk in 1367 DR .

Activities

In addition to being the royal heir of Muluk, Hadra was an accomplished sha'ir who held the rank of Sorcerer-General in Muluk's army. She led successful attacks against both Umara and Qadib during her time in the army.

She commanded the cavalry of Muluk during battles.

Relationships

Hadra was the only daughter of Muluk's Caliph, Aswiyah al-Muftahir .
