Samia al-Kasir was an elven wind mage within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Activities

Samia was a former apprentice of the Grand Vizier, Alyana al-Azzazi , and served as her assistant.
