Lanternmaker Zorth Ulmaril's shop was located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood two storys tall.

Location

The shop was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward on Presper Street just south of the end of Bitter Alley .

Inhabitants

The shop was owned by its namesake, Zorth Ulmaril , in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR .
