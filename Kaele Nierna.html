Kaele Nierna was a human bard and wandering minstrel sometime in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

She had voluminous flamed-tressed hair that spilled down her back.

Personality

She was less serious and more outgoing and flirtatious compared to her twin sister Keira .

Possessions

She wore a set of leather armor , and had a harp , a blowgun , and a set of daggers .

Relationships

She worked alongside her twin sister as wandering minstrels. She had two animal companions: a wolf called Quicksilver , and Graywing , a hawk .

History

Once Kaele and her sister played for the wizard Daltim Flamefist , and managed to dazzle him so well that he, afterwards, would refer to them as the sirens.
