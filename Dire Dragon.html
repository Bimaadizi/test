Uthagrimnoshaarl , also known as the Dire Dragon , was a shadow dracolich who acted as the guardian of the Well of Dragons in Skull Gorge .

Description

Uthagrimnoshaarl was a special kind of undead , a dracolich.

History

Around the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the Cult of the Dragon approached him with a proposal of an alliance: The Cult's wizards would release the Dire Dragon from his role of guardian if, in exchange, he would permit the Cult to build a fortress inside the Well.

He annihilated the village of Ladydove , with only Yajandra Dlathaero 's house surviving.

Sometime after 1370 DR, he was destroyed.
