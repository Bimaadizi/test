The cold of night was a magical orb found in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The pale green crystal orb was smooth and cool to the touch. It was wrapped in a wooden lattice, and emanated a cold moonlight glow.

Powers

The orb channeled cold and freezing energies, improving related spells, as well as making the bearer immune to death magics that would instantly snuff out their life.

History

The orb could have been found in Darstil's Tower in Wheloon Prison , after the mad wizard Darstil Vurusk was slain by the Riverside Rats .
