Luiren's Best was a stout ale brewed in Luiren by the hin Smokardin clan . It was also sold through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .

Description

Luiren's Best was a heavy stout beer. It was jet-black and had a thick consistency. Luiren's Best had a sweet aftertaste. It was the favored beer in Luiren, Halruaa , and the Shining South . Sembia was able to acquire limited quantities on occasion.

The Jovial Juggler of Beregost served Luiren's Best, priced at a single gold coin per serving.
