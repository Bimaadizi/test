Dracowyr was an earth mote that floated five miles above the Great Wild Wood in Murghôm . Near the center of the mote was a castle-like building carved into the landscape. Dracowyr was used as a neutral ground where the Princes of Murghôm and other foreigner dragons celebrated meetings and other events relevant to dragonkind .

As of 1479 DR , the mote was guarded by a dragonborn named Ananta .

History

In 1479 DR, Dracowyr became the neutral ground of Brimstone 's xorvintaal . Dragons reunited there to discuss the rules or to consult Brimstone in affairs related to the game.
