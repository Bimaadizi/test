Harskur was one of the Magelords of Athalantar in the early 3 rd century DR .

Description

Harskur was fat and old.

Relationships

Being one of the Magelords of Athalantar , Harskur had at least one apprentice, Saphardin Olen .

History

One of Harskur's apprentices, Saphardin Olen, died in the Year of the Loremasters, 228 DR .
