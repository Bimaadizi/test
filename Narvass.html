Narvass was a devoted member of the cult of the Absolute who lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR . Along with the human Kagran , Narvass was one of the few non- goblinoid cultists granted permission to stay within the Shattered Sanctum .

Possessions

Like many of the Absolute 's other devotees, Narvass wore an amulet bearing the Absolute's holy symbol .

He also carried a crossbow strapped to his back.

History

Narvass was present when the warlord Dror Ragzlin attempted to commune with a dead mind flayer whose body was recovered from a crashed nautiloid that fell near the River Chionthar in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
