The Skull-King's flail was the unique enchanted Abyssal flail that belonged to Quah-Nomag the Skull-King, infamous priest of Orcus .

Powers

This dreaded light flail was imbued with a potent +4 enchantment. Its hammer was forged to deal additional bludgeoning and negative energy damage. Additionally, the Skull-King's flail allowed its wielder to cast the inflict serious wounds spell once per day. The weapons held nine charges of that spell.

History

Quah-Nomag carried the Skull-King's flail into the battle between his master Orcus and the city of Zhentil Keep in 1373 DR . This vicious flail took the lives of many Zhent civilians and defenders until the onslaught was stopped by the Hero of Daggerford . Quah-Nomag and his demonic troops were broken by the adventurers, and soon after he was slain by the heroes during Orcus' last stand against Fzoul Chembryl . With the demons sent back to the Abyss, the Skull-King's flail was left behind on the battlefield in the Temple of Bane .
