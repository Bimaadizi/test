Berthot was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor that lived during the Age of Discovery .

History

Berthot developed three spells during their life. The spells they invented were fumble in 2710 NY (−1149 DR) , screen in 2730 NY (−1129 DR) , and finally, chaos in 2754 NY (−1105 DR) .
