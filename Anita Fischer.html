Anita Fischer was a commoner from the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Anita was a human woman with a tan skin, straight brown hair, and hazel eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Anita and Elias Fischer suffered a terrible loss. The couple's seven-year-old daughter Lily was murdered, leaving her parents grief-stricken. Anita and Elias planned on burying their daughter in the Lower City Graveyard , but the undertaker carpenter, Ulkov Sully , demanded a priority fee for a coffin due to a massive backlog of funerals caused by the skyrocketing numbers of murders in the Gate. Without paying the fee, something the Fischers could not do, it would've taken a month of wait time. Anita and her husband tried their best to get Lily buried via negotiations and demands from the Valmorba and her mortuary.
