Murbryn was a warrior for the Cult of the Dragon in the mid-14 th century DR .

Relationships

Thoadrin and Curthryn were aware of his name.

History

In late 1357 DR , Thoadrin led a force of Cult of the Dragon warriors to harry Orthil Voldovan 's caravan, aiming to capture one of her passengers, Shandril Shessair . During one of their ambushes, his underling Curthryn related that Murbryn may have been one of the dead.
