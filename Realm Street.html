Realm Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Realm Street was located in east Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter . The road originated near the outer eastern wall at Border Street and moved west through the city to terminate at the wall of the Inner Quarter . Realm Street was intersected by Black Street , Beholder Street , Star Street , Battle Way , and the Street of Sorrow .

Notable Locations

Prominent locations along Beholder Street included Joseph's Locks and several barracks for the Zhentilar .
