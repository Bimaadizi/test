Seiji Kubahachi was a head of the Kubahachi clan and a daimyo of Aru Province in Wa during the late 1600s on the Wa Calendar .

History

Seiji Kubahachi became daimyo of Aru Province in Wa Year 1652 (1234 DR) , following the removal of Koji Matsutomo . Seiji governed the province ably.
