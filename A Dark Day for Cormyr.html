A Dark Day for Cormyr was a book penned by Bardraskur , Bard of Immersea in the mid-13 th century DR .

History

The work was published in the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR . Its flowery musings and warnings eerily foreshadowed the events that took place a century later, when the mythical Lost Spell was unearthed in Cormyr in the Year of Dwarvenkind Reborn, 1488 DR , unleashing a magical spellstorm.
