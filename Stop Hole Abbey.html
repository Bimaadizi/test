Stop Hole Abbey was a curio store filled to the brim with artifacts, collectibles, and rarities in Daggerford circa 1372 DR .

Location

Stop Hole Abbey was located in the market square of Daggerford , in front of the stables at the city's southern wall.

History

At some point before 1372 DR, Vladmyr came across a long-abandoned abbey building that once housed members of the Old Order . Vladmyr restored the old gothic building to serve as his store.

During the temporary reign of the corrupt baron Matagar Bugo , following the Duke's disappearance, none of the items in the store were for sale, with some more interesting items reserved for undisclosed clients.
