The gloves of soul catching was a unique set of monk gloves once used by Autumnal Baskin , a professional meditator and a bare-knuckle champion, sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The gloves of soul catching could be worn by anyone, but they were most useful to a monk or a similar hand-to-hand combatant. The wearer's unarmed attacks received a magical force damage boost; additionally, once per six seconds, one successful unarmed attack restored the wearer's health. However, the wearer could choose to dismiss healing magic and receive a boost of another nature instead – an advantage in aiming and landing hits, as well as a slight boost to their reactions and protections for the next six seconds. Lastly, the gloves significantly boosted constitution while the gloves were worn.

History

The gloves' of soul catching original owner was a well-known negotiator and a mêlée fighter – Autumnal Baskin, sometime before the late 15 th century DR. By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the gloves were in the hands of Hope , a dwarven cleric , imprisoned in the so-called House of Hope in Avernus by Raphael , a cambion spawn of Mephistopheles .
