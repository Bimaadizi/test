The theurgy of autumn was a magical ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The ring balanced the power of the sun and moon , channeling the full energies of autumn . It helped the wearer survive all sorts of danger, and greatly aided in corrosive and acidic spellcasting.

History

The ring was found in the Temple of Labelas Enoreth when Neremyn Proris and the Elves of Cormanthor searched for a way to defeat the lich Droic Orgul .
