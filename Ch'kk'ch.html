Ch'kk'ch was the daughter of Ma Festina and an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Like her mother, Ch'kk'ch was a human girl with a light skin tone. But unlike Ma, she had dark red hair, and her eyes were blue.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldur's Gate, Festina brought her daughter to a performance of some showy magics by three spellslingers, on the Murl's Rest plaza in front of Sorcerous Sundries . The mother was delighted by her daughter's joy seeing the magical show. It was the most fun the girl had since her birthday.
