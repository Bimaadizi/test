Force bullets were a type of enchanted sling missile designed to restrain rather than to harm.

Powers

Force bullets possessed an enchantment that made them easier to strike their targets and slightly boosted their damage output. However, their magic's value become apparent once a force bullet hits its target. Upon collision, the bullets released the Otiluke's resilient sphere , enclosing the target within for 42 seconds.

History

As of the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , force bullets were popular weapons among the clergies of Helm , Eldath , and Ilmater . Grand Syndar Quinn Silverfinger , a cleric of Waukeen was amongst the users during his tenure at The Golden Lodge of Lonelywood in the late 13 th and early 14 th centuries DR .
