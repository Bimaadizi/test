Stunning arrows were a type of enchanted bolt ammunition for crossbows that would stun any creature it struck.

Powers

Stunning arrows were enchanted as a part of their creation. Each arrow possessed a +1 enchantment to damage dealt and accuracy. Additionally, these arrows had a coin-toss chance of stunning the target, leaving them briefly vulnerable in the heat of battle.

History

Circa the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , stunning arrows were used by orcs and goblins who joined the Legion of the Chimera to bring destruction to the Ten Towns and Kuldahar of Icewind Dale .
