Marath Haldewn was the leader of a circle of Chauntean druids active on the Moonshae Isles during the middle of the 14 th century DR .

History

Marath was the last worshiper of Chauntea to watch over Earthmother's Weapons , a book containing several spells unique to her faith. She protected the book while her order passed knowledge of its spells by oral tradition.

During the Year of Many Bones, 1278 DR , Marath was attacked and brutally murdered by a force of Malarite berserkers who found her hidden tree-home. While she managed to dispatch half of her assailants, she was ultimately overpowered and offered as a sacrifice to the Beastlord.
