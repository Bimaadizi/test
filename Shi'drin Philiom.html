Shi'drin Philiom was a fanatic priest of Ghaunadaur in Llurth Dreir .

Activities

Shi'drin was a member of House Philiom in Llurth Dreir and the second-in-command of Molvayas 's group.

History

In 1379 DR , he questioned Kâras about Molvayas's disappearance but Kâras said he was killed by a priest of House Abbylan . Shi'drin didn't question any further, happy to become the new Eater of Filth of the House.
