The Temple of Loviatar was a temple dedicated to Loviatar , the Maiden of Pain, in the city of Eltabbar in Thay during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The temple was located in the northwest section of the city along the banks of the River Eltar where it entered Eltabbar. The temple sat just south of the Temple of Cyric .
