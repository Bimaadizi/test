Vane was a member in the Golden Legion during the mid 14 th century DR , and a companion of the officer Alvarro .

History

Vane took part in the Maztican expedition as one of the cavalry.

He was at his superior's side during the battle at Palul and was taking care of the horses when Alvarro tried to rape Erixitl . Vane was killed from behind by the furious Halloran , who went to save his lover.
