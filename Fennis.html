Fennis was a tasked winemaker genie who served the Brotherhood of the True Flame in the fortress of Krak al-Niraan in 1367 DR .

Activities

When he wasn't lounging in a drunken stupor, Fennis spent his time harvesting grapes and making wine for the keep. Fennis was assisted by a trio of copper automatons in his work.

Possessions

Fennis had three potions of plant control that he kept hidden within an empty wine urn.
