Jugglables were small items in the Realms popular among bards who would juggle with them.

Description

Jugglables included leather balls , small knives , bean-bags , wooden plates , etc.

Availability

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold jugglables for 7 copper pieces –2 gold pieces depending on the nature of the set.
