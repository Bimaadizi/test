Shira was a great black wolf, companion to Thanial Selwander . The first night that Marrec spent with them as Thanial's apprentice, she sniffed him suspiciously. She participated in the fighting during the raid on the village by the Durang ogres . After Marrec accidentally petrified Thanial during the fight, she fled.
