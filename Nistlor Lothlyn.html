Nistlor "The Undying" Lothlyn was a smoothspoken wizard and proprietor of the House of the Wise Unicorn who turned to necromancy and even lichdom . Having already mastered the summoning of lesser undead he hoarded potions of longevity , purchasing them at every opportunity. Nistlor had three apprentices who helped run the Unicorn and assist with his acquiring of spell components.
