Tallos Talanthil IV , who was also called the Artificer , was the ninth Sorcerer - Arkhan of the Talanthil Dynasty who ruled the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Tallos IV reigned from −168 DR to −154 DR . He inherited the throne when his father Mornarth III , died. Tallos IV died in −154 DR during an assault on Dun-Tharos in Narfell .

Relationships

Tallos IV's younger brother was Vayloss IV
