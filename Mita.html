RaceRaccoon familiar

Mita was the raccoon familiar of No Ziar , a notorious wu jen who dwelled in the Vorkani Mountains and in the settlement of Pan in Koryo around 1357 DR .

Mita could swell to twice its natural size in a fight. It enjoyed eating fresh fish, namely the people that No Ziar transformed into fish.
