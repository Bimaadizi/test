Chime lamps were decorative sonorous lamps from Evermeet .

Description

Typically made of brass , a chime lamp had attached to it a vented wheel that was set in motion by the rising hot air from the flame. The wheel had blades that struck an array of chimes made of crystal or brass , producing a soothing sound. The lamp's light shone with a rosy glow.

Availability

Chime lamps imported from Evermeet were available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .
