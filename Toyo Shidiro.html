Toyo Shidiro was a sailor in 1358 DR .

Activities

Toyo Shidiro was a Kozakuran sailor in service to the mercantile Ko clan , acting as second-in-command of the Victorious Morning ship under Captain Hu .

History

In 1358 DR, he was ordered to take Ko La Ko and an adventuring party hired by Ko Ho San to H'sin-to , at the mouth of the Fenghsintzu River in T'u Lung . Eventually, he started a mutiny but was defeated by the adventurers.

Personality

Toyo Shidiro had a bad treatment from the Ko clan and secretly wanted revenge against them.
