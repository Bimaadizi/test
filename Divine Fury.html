Divine Fury was the name given to a number of magical katanas that appeared in northwest Faerûn circa 1372 DR .

History

Although unconfirmed, in the account of Kaa Mihnd , these swords were forged from fallen stars that crashed into the square of a poor fishing village somewhere. Kaa Mihnd and the other villagers used these swords to rise up against their rich overlord and reclaim their ancestral land.

Description

These katanas bore either a wide guard or a brown cloth wrapped around the lower blade.

Powers

A Divine Fury was a +3 katana that inflicted electrical burns and could stun an opponent for a dozen seconds.
