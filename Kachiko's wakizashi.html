Kachiko's wakizashi was a unique enchanted blade from somewhere in Kara-Tur .

Powers

This wakizashi possessed a powerful +3 enchantment that improved the wielder's aim and dealt more powerful damage than an ordinary blade. Upon a successful slash, Kachiko's wakizashi had a chance of temporarily stupefying the opponent, effectively reducing their perceptiveness with each landed attack.

History

Little was recorded of the weapon's history. The only thing known for sure was its owner – a female warrior named Kachiko who wielded this wakizashi somewhere in Kara-Tur, sometime before the 14 th century DR .

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Kachiko's wakizashi , along with several other magic items from Icewind Dale, ended up in the hands of the merchant named Joluv who traveled from Icewind Dale to the City of Coin, Athkatla , to sell the items to wealthy Amnian adventurers. Joluv peeled his wars from the Copper Coronet in Athkatla's River District .
