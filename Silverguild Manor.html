Silverguild Manor was part of the Prosperity House complex located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The manor was located in south Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the Foreign Quarter on Zhent Avenue .

Inhabitants

Silverguild Manor was the home of Malvred Silverguild and his family. The family was served by a dozen servants and a group of fourteen mercenaries served as his personal guard.
