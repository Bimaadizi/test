Penguins were aquatic flightless birds. They lived on the floes of the Sea of Moving Ice and in Alpuk .

Abilities

An average penguin could hold its breath for about 6 minutes.

Ecology

The penguins of the Sea of Moving Ice were preyed upon by polar bears and walruses . Those in Alpuk were hunted by Iulutiuns , who ate them raw and considered them among their favorite birds as food. They were also known to be preyed upon by killer whales .
