The Five Lions was an inn in the realm of Turmish in the Vilhon Reach in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Location

An isolated inn, it stood on a side-trail a little way beyond the lip of the Allascar valley and on the steep slope down to the great Faraway forest.

Description

It was considered 'quaint'.

Inhabitants

As well as being an inn, it was the base of operations and the namesake of an adventuring company , the Five Lions . They provided training to young adventurers .

History

During the Ten Black Days of Eleint in the Year of the Bright Blade, 1347 DR , several members of the royal family escaped Tethyr and somehow made it to The Five Lions inn. However, they were followed by rebels, who killed them in the woods.

Rumors & Legends

These events gave rise to a rumor that, within the cellars of The Five Lions inn, there was a permanent gate of great age that connected to some hidden location in Amn . It was supposed both the fleeing royals and the pursuing rebels had found and gained access to this gate .
