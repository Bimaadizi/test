Cowper was a Netherese arcanist who worked at the Mythallar Housing of Eileanar shortly before its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Cowper worked variously alongside Dewez , Haym , and Salix as they guarded and monitored the mythallars on their regular shifts at the Housing, with Cowper appearing whenever assistance was needed.
