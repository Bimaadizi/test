Huntinghorn was a Herald of Tavaray .

Description

The man had hairy, stubby-fingered hands, an elegant, short beard that curved over his chin, and dressed in black.

Possessions

Myrjala Talithyn's disguise had his cloak deviced with crossed trumpets in gold trim.

Activities

To the judgement of Ubriien Orlyn , Mage Royal of Athalantar , he was most likely an envoy of Lord Elember .

Relationships

He was a herald of Tavaray .

Rumors & Legends

The sorceress Myrjala Talithyn disguised herself as Huntinghorn, Herald of Tavaray, to enter Morlin Castle in Eleint 11 of the Year of the Flaming Forests, 224 DR . The deception didn't last long, during which not much was revealed of the real Huntinghorn.
