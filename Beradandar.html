Beradandar was a city of the Kuong Kingdom in Malatra and once its temporary capital.

Geography

It lay between the cities of Parmahana and Ranguri , far into the jungle.

History

After deserting their former capital at Parmahana some time in the early 8 th century DR , the Kuong people made an exodus through the jungle. They made a temporary capital at Beradandar, before moving on to the permanent capital at Ranguri.
