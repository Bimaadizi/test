Velzraedo Hlaklavarr was an envoy from Calimshan in the late mid-14 th century DR .

Description

He was almost as fat as Mirt . Velzraedo was thickly perfumed and dressed mostly in purple silks , with gilt weave boots and a turban .

Personality

He was quite effusive with words and affected an entitled manner.

Relationships

He employed six bodyguards, and considered himself the First Finger of the Masked Vizier .

Possessions

Besides the purple silks, turban and gilt weave boots, he carried a number of silk daggers on his belt .

History

He got in a confrontation with Mirt over seating in the Brave Bustard tavern in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , and was promptly knocked out before his bodyguards could react. On their way out, the bodyguards quickly got into trouble all on their own: per Beldrigarr Stoneshield , he was being sought for using counterfeit currency to pay at the Sunset Sail .
