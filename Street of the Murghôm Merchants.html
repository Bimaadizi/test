The Street of the Murghôm Merchants was a street found in the city of Dhaztanar in Semphar during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Street of the Murghôm Merchants was located in the Furdah Rustauk District in southern Dhaztanar.

Activities

The street provided access to the Furdah Rustauk District, and eventually the rest of Dhaztanar, for anyone entering by the city's southern Little Gate .
