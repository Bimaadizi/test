Lowhill was a small halfling town a mile outside of Caer Corwell on the isle of Gwynneth .

Inhabitants

A notable halfling from the community was Pawldo , a talented archer and swordsman who traveled with Tristan Kendrick and who also sold him the moorhound Canthus .
