Faraneyth's Redemption was the unique enchanted belt that once belonged to Faraneyth , a cursed paladin who lived some years before the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The belt possessed a powerful protective +4 enchantment and granter the wearer magical regeneration and posted their will.

History

Faraneyth was a devotee paladin of Torm until one day, he was driven mad by a cursed set of armor, and drove him to murder. He was banished for the order in shame. Faraneyth took on a penance of a traveling preacher, praising his deity actors the Realms. One day, the disgraced paladin came across an evil cult that took over a temple. He encouraged locals to take up arms against valiantly stood against evil, being gravely injured in the process. As he laid dying, a giant eagle appeared to him, healing the wounds and returning Faraneyth divine Tormish powers. His belt, which was used to stop the bleeding, received a portion of benevolent godly powers, becoming Faraneyth's Redemption .

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Faraneyth's Redemption was found in the hands of Mother Feodorra , a charm and magic item peddler from the Rashemi city of Mulsantir .
