A staff of teleportation was a staff with the power to teleport small objects. One of them was in the Mobius Tower .

Powers

These staves could teleport objects of up to 20 lb (9.1 kg), simply by touching the staff to the object while picturing where it would go.

Notable Staves

An efreet guarded one that hung on a wall in the Mobius Tower as of 1356 DR . That particular staff had only five charges left.
