Mugwort was a common fast-growing weed often disliked by farmers and gardeners, but useful to druids and potion -makers. Mugwort plants often grew back within mere days after being cut.

Availability

In the Western Heartlands , mugwort was a common plant. It was used by the druids of the Emerald Grove to brew a powerful anti-toxin called the Elixir of Silvanus . The recipe for which could be found within the Flora of the Dalelands book on botany.

Mugwort roots were dried and crushed and alchemically processed into salts . This substance, in turn, could be mixed with certain types of alchemical suspension to create an effective variety of antidote potion .
