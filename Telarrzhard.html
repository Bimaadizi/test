Urvraunt was a Netherese enclave that was destroyed during the Fall, notable for its ultimate distance from Netheril.

Geography

When the enclave crashed, it came to a rest in the northern Wu Pi Te Shao Mountains above the Utter East , just west of the Yehimal Mountains and northeast of the Kingdom of Nix .

History

After crashing during Karsus's Folly in −339 DR , the enclave was ruined but remained partially intact. Due it its immensely remote location, it stayed completely unknown to the wider world through the 15 th century DR .
