Galath was a bandit captain who raided Mistledale during the 10 th century DR .

Activities

Galath conducted raids from his fortress, Galath's Roost , located in the eastern reaches of Mistledale. He was killed, along with most of his men, when forces from Sembia retaliated.

Rumors & Legends

Galath's ghost was rumored to haunt his fortress long after his death.
