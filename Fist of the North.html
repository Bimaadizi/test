The Fist of the North was a legendary enchanted bludgeon, used in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

The Fist of the North possessed a potent +3 enchantment that increased the weapon's damage and guided the wielder's hand to ward the targets. The mace 's magic made it deadly when used against orcs and ogres , dealing additional damage to them in addition to being scorched by radiant energy .
