Grand Ritualist Kathul Sep was a Red Wizard necromancer that joined Droic Orgul 's raid on Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

History

Kathul was a senior Red Wizard charged with subverting the partway-reborn dracolich Karliath , found in an old Cult of the Dragon base in the depths of Myth Drannor. She was personally accompanied by a team of three Red Wizards and a good supply of undead servants, but before she could finish leading the great necromantic ritual she fell in battle to the attacking Elves of Cormanthor sent by the guardnorn Phathroi .
