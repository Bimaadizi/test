Saldruth's viper venom was a poison extracted from the fangs of the Saldruth's viper , a snake found in the Nelanther Isles . It was harvested by pirates and sold to merchants in Skullport and Waterdeep .

Description

This viscous, white liquid tasted slightly sweet. It could be mixed with water or alcohol and ingested, or applied to a weapon and introduced into the bloodstream by injury. A single dose typically cost 300 gp.

Effects

Unless the victim was able to resist, this poison initially sapped a victims health and eventually damaged their constitution.

Notable Users

Elsk Thavarask , the proprietor of the Missing Minotaur tavern in Everlund , circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , was known to use this substance to subdue excessively rowdy customers on occasion. He was also known to sell it to people he trusted.
