Distinctions

Oregano was a fragrant plant of wide green leaves and light purple flowers used as a seasoning.

Availability

Oregano was sold as a common seasoning through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue , priced at two silver pieces per ounce. The same herbs were also sold on the streets of the City of Splendors, Waterdeep .

Oregano was grown in the lush herb gardens of the Sarahin fortress in the Haunted Lands of Zakhara. The aromas of mint , sage , rosemary , dill , parsley , cilantro , thyme , and oregano all clashed with each other and these were ready to be used in the fortress's nearby kitchens.

In the jungles of the Malatran Plateau in Kara-Tur oregano grew near Koshivan and Rudran settlements. The herb was also a known food consumed by the Ancient Nubari . One of the ancient recipes using oregano was discovered and turned into the Zucchini Saiyama style dish.

Usages

Some believed that oregano possessed some medicinal properties and received use as a germicide and a pain killer.
