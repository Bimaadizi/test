Tam was a junior priestess of Selûne at Moonshadow Hall in Yhaunn in the 14 th century DR .

Personality

As an inexperienced and young priestess, Tam was quite unnerved by the events at Moonshadow Hall, and rather easily cowed and led by more authoritative people.

History

In the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Tam assisted the senior priestess Velsinore in her duties at Moonshadow Hall. In Eleasis of that year, she met the returned Feena Archwood and helped Velsinore administer the temple after Moonmistress Dhauna Myritar 's transformation into a werewolf and subsequent death.
