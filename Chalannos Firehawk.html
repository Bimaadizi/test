Chalannos "the Masked" Firehawk was a retired adventurer living in the city of Wheloon in the mid-14 th century DR .

Activity

After having retired as an adventurer, Chalannos took up the life of an instructor for other warriors. He was considered to be an expert trainer, able to teach his students how to use any particular weapon. His fees were several gold pieces , although he would also accept payment in the form of any magical item that he could make use of.

History

Chalannos had retired to a cottage that stood on a lane on the northeastern edge of Wheloon by 1358 DR .
