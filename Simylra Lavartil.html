Simylra Lacartil was an Amnian socialite in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

During one particular ball in 1369 DR, fur-covered breastworks dusted with diamond were part of her fittings; these breastworks were tight enough to be in jeopardy during particularly vigorous motion. She was overly plump.

Relationships

Her cousin was Cathlona Tabbartan .

History

Originally from Amn , Simylra Lavartil was a participant in a nobles' ball in Waterdeep during 1369 DR , where she was teaching her cousin the who's who of Waterdeep. The ball was crashed by the drow Qilué Veladorn , who was disguised as a drow princess and assumed to be a human disguised as a drow. She was put off because of the newcomer's daring costume.
