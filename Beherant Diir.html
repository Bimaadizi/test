Description

He was a rather portly individual.

Possessions

Beherant sold a variety of armor , including shields of many different designs and colors.

Relationships

He worked alongside several other merchants including Elence Fielding and Min Minling .

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Beherant and his fellow merchants in town were infiltrated by a book-and-potion peddler named Fael , who was in fact an agent of the Twisted Rune . When Imnesvale was threatened by mysterious disappearinces and killings, Beherant Diir blamed them on a group of ogres who camped nearby. These killings also negatively affected Beherant's business.
