Meldrethe Klaeverdar was a member of the Klaeverdar family in the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

She liked to "ride the storms", that is, flying into storms and hurling lightning bolts while protected by spells of her own devising. She was also known to enjoy the act of spellcasting, and to have devised a number of bespoke spells.
