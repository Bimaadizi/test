Kharkos was a young warrior inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Kharkos was eager to form his own adventuring company, gathering at least four other would-be adventurers before setting out for glory and riches.

Possessions

He wore an enchanted suit of chainmail and carried a shield and longsword .
