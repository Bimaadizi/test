Oldturret Farm was a farm belonging to the Klaeverdar clan as of the late mid-14 th century DR .

Location

The farm was northwest of Elturel .

History

Baerbuckle Klaeverdar headed one of the Klaeverdar branches from that farm; Gueladdeth Klaeverdar , the clan matriarch, dwelt there in 1370Â DR .

Rumors & Legends

The farm's namesake was an old keep, supposedly destroyed when a matriarch of a previous generation, Jaedreene Klaeverdar , fought through a whole night with a small army of invaders, the "evil ones out of Dragonspear ".
