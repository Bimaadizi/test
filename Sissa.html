Sissa was an Ilmatari acolyte that maintained the Shrine of the Suffering .

Personality

The residents of Baldur's Gate very much cherished Sissa, their sibling, and father, Brother Hodges .

History

In 1482 DR , Sissa worked with her father in Baldur's Gate . During this time, neither Sissa nor Hanse were old enough to marry, but were in 1492 DR .
