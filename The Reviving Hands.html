The Reviving Hands was a pair of enchanted gauntlets that boosted the wearer's healing magics.

Description

These gilded gauntlets' lining had a message stitched into it: "Yes all right, all right, stop moaning and get up."

Powers

When the wearer of The Reviving Hands cast any type of healing spell , the target also received the effects of blade ward . When the wearer revived a target creature, they also were granted a death ward . The gauntlets also slightly boosted the wearer's physical strength and allowed them to cast the revivify spell once a day.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a pair of the Reviving Hands was available for sale at the Stormshore Tabernacle of Baldur's Gate .
