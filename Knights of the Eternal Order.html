The Knights of the Eternal Order was a knightly order in service of the god of the dead, Kelemvor , during the late 14 th century DR . Kelemvor's church gathered the knights together to hunt down and destroy powerful undead from a fortress outside of Iriaebor .

Among their members were paladins, and some doomguides .
