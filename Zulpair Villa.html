Zulpair Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Zulpair .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward on Delzorin Street in between Diamond Street and Flint Street .

Description

The walled compound contained three separate buildings with the largest standing in the southwest corner. Each building was three stories tall.
