Pappilon was an adventuring human , active in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Pappilon was a human man with a light skin tone and eflin features. He kepts his head clean-shaven and wore light and unarmored garb.

History

The Masked Lords determined that the danger was coming from underneath the Yawning Portal , and Durnan , the proprietor of the famed establishment, put out a call for adventurers willing to traverse the dungeon's depths and route the evil within.

Pappilon was among the heroes who answered the call, eager to spread the faith of his goddess and to practice his healing arts. Survivors chased the assassins down into the Undermountain, while those killed were resurrected by the city's clergy , including Pappilon.
