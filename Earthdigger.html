Earthdigger was a wizard member of the Flaming Fist who served the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Fist Earthdigger was a fair-skinned human man with blue eyes, mousy-blonde facial hair, and disheveled haircut. He wore a dark red robe of a Flaming Fist mage and was armed with a quarterstaff .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Earthdigger was stationed in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, within the Wyrm's Crossing fortress.
