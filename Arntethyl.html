Arntethyl was a high mountain valley where the River Tesh was born and cascaded to the east, across the Dalelands and into the Moonsea .

Description

River Tesh moved across downslope across many gorges of the Desertsmouth Mountains and formed the river's Tesh Valley .

Arntethyl held a supply encampment of the Zhentarim that supported the Black Network's activities in the great dessert of Anauroch .

History

The encampment also held Zhentish troops that could be used as reinforcements across the region. The valley held at least a thousand Zhentish warriors and twenty wizards circa the Year of the Turret, 1360 DR . During that time, the troops were led by the mage named Ruatheene , a Manshoon 's favored, and her massive and powerful beholder Araunglauth . The beholder called the valley of Arntethyl its home for seventy years prior.
