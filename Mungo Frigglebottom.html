Mungo Frigglebottom was an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Frigglebottom was a customer of the Sword Coast Couriers , a mail and parcel delivery service that serviced Baldur's Gate. When the Couriers started experiencing mail disappearances along with its pigeons , Frigglebottom was among the people affected. The Sword Coast Couriers was delivering tuition fee notices from Mungo's alma mater , the magely Eltorchul Academy of Waterdeep . The Mungo preferred these letters "stay lost."
