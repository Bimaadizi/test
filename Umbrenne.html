Umbrenne was a Talonite preacher from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Umbrenne was a midle-aged in appearance wood elf with sickly green skin, long straight graying hair, and brown eyes. He was dressed in simple burlap priestly robe .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Umbrenne could be found preaching to commoners and refugees in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, not far from the Basilisk Gate . He spent his days evangelizing over the cycle of life and death through pestilence as taught by the Mother of all Plagues.
