The Schooner of the Seas was a ship and temple dedicated to Valkur , the Captain of the Waves.

History

Originally constructed in Almraiven , the Schooner of the Seas was the fastest known ship to sail on Toril . It featured an advanced design that left even the masters in Lantan befuddled. The ship's journeys reached exotic places such as Zakhara , Maztica , Nimbral , and Kara-Tur . It was based in Athkatla and commonly seen between Waterdeep and Calimport .

Rumors & Legends

Some believed that Gond built the Schooner of the Seas for Valkur.
