The Quaffing Quaggoth was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tavern stood one story tall.

Location

The Quaffing Quaggoth was located in the far southwest corner section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward on the west side of Lackpurse Lane just south of the Reach .

Inhabitants

The Quaffing Quaggoth's bartender in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , was a dwarf named Baklin .
