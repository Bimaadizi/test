Nettle beer was a variety of beer brewed from the Malatran jungle nettle plant on the Malatran Plateau of Kara-Tur .

Description

Nettle beer was stored in wineskins and was considered a valuable commodity in the Living Jungle. Containers of nettle beer as well as harvested nettle plants were available for purchase or barter from the local merchants, including Abu , a hedgehog katanga merchant in the late 14 th century DR .
