The thunderstorm crescent was a magical sickle found in Eveningstar during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The oddly shaped handle connected to a wicked metal blade that crackled with electricity.

Powers

The crescent possessed a powerful offensive enchantment that guided the weapon to its target and increased its damage. It also allowed the weapon to damage creatures unaffected by mundane weapons and it shocked targets struck by the blade. Additionally, the thunderstorm crescent improved the power of the wielder's lightning-related spells .

History

The sickle was recovered from the Eveningstar War Hospital following an outbreak of red musk zombies caused by the ranger Peron Uthe , who had been infected by the maddened druid Halsaime of Thorngap .
