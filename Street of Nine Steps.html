The Street of Nine Steps was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Street of Nine Steps was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . The road branched east from Caravan Street just south of Tilman's Lane , turned south, east, then north through a small neighborhood, and eventually ended on Wall Street near the Trollwall .

Notable Locations

The Old Monster Shop was located on the eastern end of the Street of Nine Steps.
