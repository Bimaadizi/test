The Crown of the Lost King was a unique antique enchanted crown from the mega-dungeon of Undermountain .

Powers

The crown possessed the challenger's might enchantment that greatly boosted the wearer's might, accuracy, defense, and luck and made their deadly hits land much easier.

History

The Crown of the Lost King was a mysterious item from the time of distant antiquity and all history and lore associated with the artifact were long lost.
