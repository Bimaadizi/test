Description

Porios was something of a pragmatist rather than idealist.

History

During the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Porios was under the employ of the Bhaalspawn Sarevok Anchev during his attempted coup in Baldur's Gate . After Sarevok's death, Porios stole his sword of chaos from the Ducal Palace under the direction of the Bhaalspawn's loyal disciple Korlasz .

Soon after, Porios and his crew were caught by the Hero of Baldur's Gate .
