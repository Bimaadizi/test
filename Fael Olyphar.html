Fael Olyphar was one of the common drow families of Menzoberranzan in the Northdark in the late 14 th and 15 th centuries DR .

History

The Fael Olyphar family was founded sometime before the Spellplague years of the late 14 th and 15 th centuries DR. Their estate stood in the Eastmyr neighborhood of Menzoberranzan, amongst other less successful drow families: Asbodela , Dlaen Dei'Amatar , Ilystryph , Uluar , and Zalyzryn .
