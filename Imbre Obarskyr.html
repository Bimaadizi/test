King Imbre was the eighth Obarskyr monarch and King of Cormyr for four years. He was the second son of Embrus Obarskyr and began his reign after the death of his brother Kaspler "the Learned" in 90 DR . He passed in 94 DR and his legitimized bastard son Sacrast inherited the throne.
