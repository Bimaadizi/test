Squint was an enchanted spyglass found in Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Squint was a small collapsible tube with carved gem lenses . It was bound in golden metal and wrapped in a bright purple ribbon, forming a trellis pattern.

Powers

Apart from the ingenuity of lenses allowing the user to see distant objects up close, Squint possessed an enchantment that allowed the viewer to place the eyebite spell on the target as well as disarm them for a brief time.
