Menold was a master weaponmaker of Waterdeep , who lived many years before the late 14 th century DR .

History

Menold was the creator of the Briarspikes , a variety of sophisticatedly crafted enchanted spears . Menold's fate was unknown as he disappeared following the fall of his tower in an orcish raid. Some speculated that Menold attempted to barter his freedom by offering a weapon smith's services to the orcs, but that would've made him an even more valuable asset to keep.
