History

Taver's Talons were a properly chartered adventuring band during the mid-900s DR ; there, they operated in an area close to Buckralam's Starn. They rode regularly there, until the Year of High Treachery, 752 DR or the next year, when they were killed by a dragon . The dragon so mangled their remains that they could not be identified properly.
