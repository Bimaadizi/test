Mina was a small town found along the coast of the Great Sea in north Zakhara .

Geography

Mina was located approximately 100 miles (160 kilometers) southwest of mighty Qudra . A pair of unnamed rivers, and a standing lake, provided the town with fresh water throughout the year and contributed to the large area of cultivated land in the region.
