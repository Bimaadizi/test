A'lai Aivenmore was one of the eight Great Readers of the Avowed that served Candlekeep during the late 15 th century DR .

Abilities

Great Reader Aivenmore was an academic authority on deities and the nature of divinity.

Activities

As one of the great Great Readers, A'lai was partly responsible for the daily operations of Candlekeep.

She was a devout follower of Oghma , the God of Knowledge.
