Mawser was a Tymora -worshiping man from the Dalelands that fought in the famous second battle of Shadowdale during the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

He was a thin man that held the devout belief that the Goddess of Luck watched over his safety.

History

During the Zhentilar invasion Shadowdale in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , Mawser volunteered to set off a falling-tree trap that would take out many Zhent soldiers and block the incoming road from Voonlar . Under an invisibility spell provided by the mage Elminster , Mawser jumped off a nearly-felled tree, setting others in motion in a cascade that crashed down upon the Zhentilar.

Unfortunately Mawser's luck soon ran out, as he was shot through the neck by a Zhentish arrow.
