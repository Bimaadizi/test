Draevyn Thornbolt was an albino wizard in Neverwinter sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

Activities

Draevyn was a captain on the employ of Dagult Neverember , captaining the Seabreaker . He had an affable half-orc brute as a first mate. He always dressed impeccably.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR , Draevyn was hired to accompany an adventuring party to Svardborg .
