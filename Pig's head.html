Pig's Head was an Amnian delicacy prepared out of a whole pig or hog's head.

Description

The traditional Amnian cooking method included the head being baked in quality oils and dusted with various spices .

History

In 1492 DR , the goblin followers of the Absolute outside of the Emerald Grove enjoyed this particular sophisticated dish in their camp.
