The Shinglefell Gnome Burrow was a community of gnomes located somewhere in Faerûn . It was long lost by the mid–14 th century DR .

A notable inhabitant of Shinglefell was the mighty gnome illusionist Fitzmilliyun Sparkledrim .

Shinglefell was among the gnome settlements linked through the magical transportation network known as the Trail of Mists .
