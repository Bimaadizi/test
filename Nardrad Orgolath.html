Nardrad Orgolath was a tiefling Nentyarch of the Orgolath Dynasty of Narfell .

Activities

Nardrad ruled as Nentyarch from −434 DR to −399 DR .

Relationships

He was the sole son and heir of Morthass Orgolath and inherited the throne upon his father's death. Nardrad had a single human son named Orlathaun .
