Laylee was a kobold from the Dragon's Rest cloister in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Laylee served as a helper for Myla , a kobold tinkerer.

Personality

Laylee had an inquisitive nature and a flair for tools and building.

History

Laylee once handled Myla's alchemical fire and caused an incident in the cloister kitchen . Myla prohibited her from using it again.

Circa the Year of the Grinning Halfling, 1481 DR , Laylee shared with three other kobolds a small monastic cell in the Dragon's Rest cloister cut into the cliff face. Each of the four slept on individual hammocks. Just like all the other kobolds, she worked at night because of their sunlight sensitivity.
