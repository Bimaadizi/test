Hedrick "the Hammer" Torlund was, along with his wife Rose one of the innkeepers of the Rose and Hammer located the town of Deadsnows in the country of Luruar . Together, they cultivated a warm and friendly atmosphere for their patrons.

Hendrick retired from a life of fighting before joining field of hospitality.
