The Soul of the Earth was an ancient and magical stone that allowed the user to scry on anyone who left behind an arrow or other projectile.

Description

It was a polished piece of volcanic rock .

Powers

The Soul of the Earth granted its wielder a vision of whoever had fired a given projectile, affixing their image in the wielder's mind and making them easier to track.
