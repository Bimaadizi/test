Dalakhar was a thief and spy in Waterdeep working for Lord Dagult Neverember in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In 1492 DR , Dalakhar was spying upon Renaer Neverember , the estranged son of his master. At one point, however, Lord Dagult ordered many of his agents, including Dalakhar, to obtain the stone of Golorr from the lair of the Xanathar . The rock gnome managed to steal the stone, ruining a meeting between Xanathar and the Zhentarim .

Later, Dalakhar was targeted by agents of the Zhentarim, the Xanathar's Thieves' Guild , Bregan D'aerthe , and others, making it impossible for him to get out of Waterdeep as planned. For the time being, Dalakhar decided to entrust the stone to some adventurers who had saved Renaer. However, Dalakhar was ambushed by some Zhentarim led by Urstul Floxin and hit by a fireball launched by the nimblewright of House Gralhund , killing the rock gnome along with ten more people.

Personality

Dalakhar was very loyal to his master, believing Dagult to be the rightful Open Lord of Waterdeep.
