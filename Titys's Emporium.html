Titys's Emporium was a well-stocked general store located in Hillsfar . The Emporium was a favorite stop for adventurers as the shopkeeper purchased anything brought to him with no questions asked.

Location

Titys's Emporium was located in the southeast corner of the city.

Inhabitants

The store was owned by an old man named Titys .
