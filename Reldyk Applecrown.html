Reldyk Applecrown was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR . The Chosen of Mystra , Elminster Aumar , later borrowed his body.

Appearance

He was young and pleasant-looking, though not overly handsome.

Personality

He didn't have much of a personality left after the fight against Xarlandralath .

Abilities

Reldyk Applecrown was one of the lesser War Wizards.

Activities

He was a War Wizard .

Relationships

His comatose body was later yielded up to Elminster Aumar .

History

Reldyk Applecrown had taken some damage during the fight against Xarlandralath , but the worst of his injuries was mental after he'd been caught in some magical backlash, which had left him comatose and experiencing occassional seizures. It was in this state that Vangerdahast offered his body to Elminster, the Chosen of Mystra , who was in turn without a body after a beholder attack. With some magic, Elminster took possession of Reldyk Applecrown's body.

Hosting Elminster

Elminster used Reldyk Applecrown's body to accompany Lady Darlethra Greatgaunt , actually a disguised Storm Silverhand , the Marchioness Immerdusk , in her walks to and fro all over the city. He later used the same body still to pursue after Morligul Downdagger after he kidnapped one of the Lords of Waterdeep , his friend Mirt , believing him to be actually Rauligus Helderstone , whom he was impersonating at the time.

He continued to wear that body for a bit, until while trying to lure the blueflame ghosts to the Bold Blazon , he got caught in the crossfire after the War Wizards turned up. He bodily blocked one of Glathra Barcantle 's spells on Storm Silverhand , getting Reldyk Applecrown's body completely annihilated in the process.
