Abilities

Daeraude was an effective infiltrator, whether through magic or mundane disguises.

Activities

Daeraude could be counted on to be at Blackmanacles during the night.

Relationships

Daeraude was an acquaintance of Yamaerthe and worked for Sarltan .

History

After meeting with the drow trader Iylinvyx , who wanted to trade her Glowcap mushrooms and deep wines for Calishite silks and other sundries, Yamaerthe directed her to speak with Daeraude at Blackmanacles if she sought Sarltan, whom she was to talk to before trading anything.
