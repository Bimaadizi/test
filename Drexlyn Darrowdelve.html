Drexlyn Darrowdelve was a dwarf vampire cleric of Abbathor inside Undermountain in 1375 DR .

Activities

Drexlyn and his retinue had occupied some platforms inside the Belkram's Fall citadel.

Personality

Drexlyn was insane in his hatred for drow and anyone related to them, for desecrating the citadel.
