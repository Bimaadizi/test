A mask of the beast was a magical mask that allowed the wearer to better interact with beasts .

Description

Masks of the beast were shaped in the likeness of a beast's visage and were often ornately decorated.

Powers

The mask granted its wearer the benefits of the animal friendship spell up to three times a day. It then recharged its magic at every dawn. It was said that powerful druids and sorcerers could use a mask of the beast to exert influence over large groups of animals.
