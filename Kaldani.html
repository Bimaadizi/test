Kaldani was a tiefling that lived in Elturel during the late 15 th century DR , before she was forced out of the city alongside the others of her kind.

Relationships

She held archdruid Halsin in high esteem, but did not care for the mercenary leader Aradin that the elven druid hired as a guide.

History

When the city of Elturel was pulled down to the Nine Hells in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Kaldani and the other tieflings were kicked out of the city. Led by Zevlor , the stalwart former member of the Hellriders , the group traveled west for a time, before settling at the Emerald Grove druidic sanctuary. Like many of them, Kaldani became very worried when the grove's leader Halsin went missing, fearing for their safety in his absence.
