Pip was the butler at the Balthis Estate in the City of Coin, Athkatla in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Pip was a halfling man of refined etiquette with hair the color of hay.

History

By the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Pip had been empty by Acton Balthis for some time, residing in the nobleman's home in Athkatla's Bridge District . Pip greeted all visitors who entered the Balthis Estate and turned away unwanted guests, claiming Acton was not in and had no time for walk-in appointments. If Pip's requests were unheeded, the estate's bodyguards were sure to escort unwanted visitors out.
