The Saddle of the Plains was a quality inn located in the city of Lheshayl on the Shining Plains .

Services

The Saddle of the Plains offered typical guest services of food and lodgings. Along with the Bit & Bridle , the Saddle of the Plains was considered a moderately priced establishment.

History

At some point before the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Adamar Session , Bard of Starmantle , visited the Saddle of the Plains inn during his twelve-year adventuring trek across the Reach. The establishment was also noted in the bard's guide to the Vilhon Reach .
