Bagshaw was a Netherese arcanist who worked at the Mythomian Warehouse in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Bagshaw worked for Adrian Freeman at the warehouse alongside Debay , maintaining the portals to the Paraelemental Plane of Ice and shovelling excess ice outside.
