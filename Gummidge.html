Fist Gummidge was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Gummidge had a fair complexion with light brown hair and beard. He wore a set of leather armor as part of the Flaming Fist uniform and was armed with a deadly heavy crossbow .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Gummidge was tasked with patrolling the Lower City, near the Basilisk Gate . He and his fellow patrolmen had no qualms with shutting protesters up with force.
