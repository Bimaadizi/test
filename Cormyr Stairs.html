The Cormyr Stairs was one of three major pedestrian stairs that connected the Lower City and the Old Town in the Cormyrean city of Tilverton .

Geography

The Cormyr Stairs were the stairs in the western side of town, just by the city gate that led towards wider Cormyr and the Gnoll Pass . The stair climb was the only way into the wealthier and less crowded Old Town apart from passing the Upper City Gate on the southernmost side of Tilverton.
