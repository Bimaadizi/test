Thaalis was a lizardfolk cleric of Semuanya . She resided in the mines below Baeniff Tor in the Evermoors where she sacrificed victims to the giant crocodile , Kheshebik , who she and the Khoroshen tribe of lizardfolk believed to be an avatar of the deity Semuanya .

Appearance

Thaalis' scales were green and yellow. She had a large scar on her face and the end of her tail was missing.

Possessions

Thaalis wore crocodile hide armor reinforced with parts of chain mail and plate mail . She wielded a club adorned with the vertebrae from crocodiles.
