Gandios Laffabar was the owner and proprietor of the Giant's Folly festhall that operated Reddansyr during the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Personality

He was a man that could readily tell a grand story to impressionable adventurers, often leading others on wild-goose-chases that may or may not have led to them in peril.

Relationships

The successful business owner was inadvertently at odds with Patriarch Thyrius Jorman , head of the local temple of Oghma . The knowledge-hoarding Oghmite priests resented the free availability of information that could be found having some drinks at the Giant's Folly.

History

Gandios famously lost a wager with a fire giant involving the transportation of a boat inland to Reddansyr from Westgate . Not one to be defeated, Gandios made another bet that won him the boat, and with it a great story and name for his future establishment.

Business was so successful as of the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR , he thought about bringing over a second boat from Westgate to Reddansyr.
