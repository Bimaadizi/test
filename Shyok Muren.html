The Shyok Muren was a small river found in Khazari .

Geography

The Shyok Muren originated within a branch of the Katakoro Shan mountains in southeastern Khazari. It flowed northwest down from the mountains through central Khazari, passing by the capital of Skarou before joining with the mighty Jumpa River .

Description

The Shyok Muren was 75 mi (120 km) long.
