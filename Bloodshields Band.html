The Bloodshields Band was an adventuring party of the late 15 th century DR .

Organization

The band was made up of veteran adventurers. Their leader was Gaerond of the Scars.

History

They were hired by a wealthy Sembian to find the wizard Elminster Aumar in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR ; they made it to Shadowdale by early spring. At the Old Skull Inn , they took on the young Thal as a guide, who offered to lead them to where Elminster Aumar was said to reside. He led them instead to an ambush by doppelgangers , being one himself, and the whole band died.
