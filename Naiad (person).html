Naiad was one of the students of the Neverwinter Academy in 1372 DR during the Wailing Death epidemic.

Personality

Naiad adored Aribeth de Tylmarande and was impressed with the paladin 's skills and stealth with which she was searching for the Wailing Death cure.

History

Naiad was one of the graduating class of the Neverwinter Academy in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , alongside the Hero of Neverwinter . Shortly before the graduation commenced the Academy was attacked by the cult that sought to destroy Neverwinter.
