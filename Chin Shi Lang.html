Chin Shi Lang was a human monk who practiced his martial arts in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Once named Kempuent of Cormyr , he fled from his homelands as a young man, all the way to Shou Lung . There he found meaning under the supervision of a martial arts master. As Chin Shi Lang he learned to fight without armor or weapons .

Possessions

As an ascetic monk, he wore nought but a simple robe and plain bead belt .
