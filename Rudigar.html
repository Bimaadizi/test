Rudigar was a young man living in the village of Tweyar in Tethyr in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR who joined the Tweyar Self-Defense Force .

Relationships

Rudigar was a close friend of Ernico , another member of the Defense Force.

History

In the month of Flamerule , in the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR , when the village hetman, Osbard , ordered Zaranda arrested for treason, Rudigar and Ernico were also present.
