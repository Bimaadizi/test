Iogar was a Zealot of the Absolute , stationed in the Moonrise Towers during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Iogar wore the uniform armor of a Zealot of the Absolute – a fur-cloaked night-blue armored garb and a metal helmet . He had pleasant facial features punctuated by facial tattoos and hazel eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Iogar was among those who joined the new religion that sprang in the Western Heartlands . He was stationed within the Moonrise Towers – the fortress controlled by the Absolute's warlord – General Ketheric Thorm . Iogar directs new arrivals to the Absolutist army cams as the Moonrise Towers were fully populated. Like many other members of the cult, Zealot Iogar's fate remained unknown as he could've perished in battle when the High Harper Jaheira and her True Soul allies stormed the Moonrise.
