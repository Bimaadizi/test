Patchouli was a species of aromatic flowering plant found in the jungles of the Malatran Plateau and several other regions.

Availability

Usages

Dried patchouli was sold as an aromatic substance by the Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue . It was listed in the Priest's Alcove section of the catalogue and was sold at two ounces for a single silver coin . The same catalogue offered exotic scented oils , including patchouli lamp oil, and scented patchouli beeswax .
