The Nebelungen were mysterious individuals from Isle Broddick in the Whamite Isles . They represented Broddick in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles at some point prior to the mass death of the population in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Centuries prior, the Nebelungen created the Nebelshield , a magic shield which was most powerful when wielded by them.
