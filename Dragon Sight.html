Dragon Sight was an enchanted helmet that granted the wearer a temporary simulacrum of a dragon 's magic sight.

Description

The helmet's main feature was a small dragon skull attached to its top, giving it a fearsome appearance. Each of the skull's eye sockets held a menacingly glowing gemstone .

Powers

The wearer could tap into the helmet's magic to cast the see invisibility spell once per day. However, Dragon Sight's menacing appearance made it harder for the wearer to appear likable and personable.

History

Dragon Sight was among many exotic magical items that belonged to Bashshar al Rachid , a famous collector and merchant, and the wealthiest djinni on Toril as of the early 14 th century DR . The djinni was confined to the avarine decanter that was in possession of a shifty drow merchant, Nym of Icewind Dale .
