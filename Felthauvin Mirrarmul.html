Felthauvin Mirrarmul was a weaver inhabiting Waterdeep , the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Felthauvin was the guildmaster for the League of Basketmakers & Wickerworkers circa 1357 DR .
