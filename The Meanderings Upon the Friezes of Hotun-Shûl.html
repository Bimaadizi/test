The Meanderings Upon the Friezes of Hotun-Shûl was a book penned by Skorm Caldril and translated by Triel of Baldur's Gate sometime before the late 14 th century DR .

History

A copy of The Meanderings Upon the Friezes of Hotun-Shûl belonged to Manshoon XII , a clone of Manshoon , in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR . The work was dedicated to the architecture of Hotun-Shûl , an underground giant temple beneath the Ice Spires , dedicated to Annam All-Father . The book was stored in the mage 's library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep .
