Nyth was a small city in Thesk .

Government

It was once controlled by the independent city-state of Telflamm , but it was a part of Thesk by 1372 DR .

Economics

It traded with its fellow cities of Thesk and with Telflamm.

History

The city took its name from a creature called a nyth that once haunted the western fringe of the Forest of Lethyr . A cabal of wizards defeated the nyth in a battle that ended with the deaths of all involved. As a result of this, wizards in Nyth were respected but considered to carry great misfortune.
