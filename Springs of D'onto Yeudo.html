The springs of D'onto Yeudo was a mythical location said to lie on the island of Saishu , off the coast of Koryo in northeast Kara-Tur .

The springs of D'onto Yeudo were one of the local legends that Dan Zor , the former king and governor of Saishu, wished to investigate. By 1357 DR , he sought adventurers who could find the mythical springs for him.
