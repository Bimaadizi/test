Tracker Tozad was a goblin cultist and novice of the Absolute in late 15 th century DR .

Description

Tozad had green-brown skin and brown hair combed over to one side. He wore patches of bone-and- leather armor, fastened together with straps.

He was among the goblin cutlists branded with the mark of the Absolute .

Possessions

He fought with a crude, goblin-made bow .

History

As of the the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Tracker Tozad remained stationed at the Shattered Sanctum .
