Spices Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Spices Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's Dock Ward . It originated on the western side of the Way of the Dragon , branching west the turning southwest near its midpoint to end on Dock Street on the waterfront. Other roads connecting with Spices Street included Wharf Street and Fishgut Alley .
