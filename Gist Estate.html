The Gist Estate was home of the patriar Gist family of dye merchants of Baldur's Gate , located in the Upper City Manorborn neighborhood.

Location

The Gist Estate was located roughly in the middle of the westernmost part of the Upper City, just three blocks away from the Manor Gate , and there blocks away from the Sea Gate . A small quality establishment, Drakon Tavern , was located immediately next to the Gist family manse.
