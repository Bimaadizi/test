An octopus was a breed of multi-tentacled cephalopod that lived within the ocean.

Habitats

Octopuses were lived in the Trackless Sea off the coast of Lantan .

Combat

Generally, regular octopuses were not dangerous unless provoked, and in this case, would usually try to escape.

Species

Ecology

Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue sold dried octopus tentacles for 4 gp a piece.

Summoning

Summon nature's ally I could be used to summon an octopus. A fiendish octopus could be summoned with the summon monster I spell , while 3 fiendish octopuses could be called forth with summon monster II .
