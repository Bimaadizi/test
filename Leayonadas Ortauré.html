Leayonadas Ortauré was an elven inhabitant of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

One of the conspirators against Coronal Eltargrim named him as amongst those slain by Elminster Aumar , and claimed to be their relative, almost certainly their parent. Elminster was informed prior that the Ortaure heir was slain by the pool, during his encounter with Symrustar Auglamyr, by an elf who held him responsible.
