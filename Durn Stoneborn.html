Durn Stoneborn was a passenger aboard the Vigilant in 1374 DR .

Description

Durn was a solemn, heavily armed, and armored duergar . He was not keen on conversing and uttered not a word during the voyage on the Vigilant . Durn used his trusty longsword in battle.

History

In the 1374Â DR , Durn Stoneborn was among the passengers aboard Captain Lastri Kassireh 's Vigilant that traveled from the Sword Coast to the nation of Samarach on the Chultan Peninsula . Several passengers were onboard the ship, including renowned writer Volothamp Geddarm . The voyage was cursed with bad luck and culminated in a deadly storm. The Vigilant's shipwreck was washed ashore on Omgar's Teeth , not far from the city of Samargol . Durn was among several survivors of the crash and faced a scavenging band of Batiri goblins on the beach shortly after the disaster.
