The burning manacles were a set of manacles found in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The shackles were closely tied to fire magic, and greatly improved the power of fiery spells cast while wearing them. Only those who could endure the pain were deserving of the power.

History

The manacles could have been found in the Temple of Shar in Wheloon Prison , after the Night Brigade gang was destroyed by the Riverside Rats .
