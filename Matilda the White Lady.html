Matilda the White Lady was one of the retired adventurers inhabiting Nellie Thursday Home for Experienced Adventurers sometime in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Matilda was a 6 feet (1.8 meters) tall tough woman of 130 pounds (59 kilograms). Her hair was silvered with age and eyes - blue. The White Lady had a blustering personality in contrast to her subdued and quiet husband.

Possessions

In her long life, the White Lady accumulated some impressive magic items: an enchanted +2 white chain mail , a magical +1 shield , and wielded a scimitar of speed . She carried potions of extra healing , potions of hill giant strength , and potions of speed , all of some dubious potency due to being created forty years prior.

The White Lady was an avid reader of the Good Castlekeeping monthly periodical.

Abilities

Matilda was fluent in several languages: Common , Elvish , and Orcish . As a fighter , she specialized in battling with scimitars and was trained to wield flails , lances , spears , short swords , and more exotic bastard swords . The White Lady had impressive endurance, could battle blinded, and was a capable horseback rider, swimmer, and a talented needle-point seamstress.

History

Matilda the White Lady and her husband of forty years, Henry the Shadow Master , retired to Nellie Thursday Home for Experienced Adventurers together. Nellie Thursday became Matilda's good friend for the entire thirty years the couple spent at the kindly dwarf 's retirement community. After retirement, Matilda managed to achieve a great feat – convincing Henry to stop smoking. The two enjoyed the time at Nellie Thursday, its elegant lodgings, and even a training area where the eighty-nine-year-old adventuress worked out to maintain her physique.

Matilda the White Lady and her husband joined several other aging adventurers on a quest that ended up dragging them across several worlds, including Olympus , where they met the deity Hermes ; the jungles of Quorsit ; and the demi-plane of Kansas , where she faced a powerful fighter Maralix in a duel and survived.
