Sancha was a penpal of a noblewoman from Daggerford , Alicine Bricket in the 14 th century DR .

History

Even though Sancha lived in a town of Bowshot on the Sword Coast , she had been exchanging letters with Alicine Bricket for the majority of their lives since childhood. In 1372 DR , Sancha was expected to visit her old friend in the Bricket Homestead to the west of Daggerford. She was invited to a celebration of Alicine's and Muriel Bricket 's much hated brother, Elveronne Bricket .
