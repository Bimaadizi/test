Nad Ho was a town in Tabot .

Geography

It stood at the southern foot of the Kun-Yen Shan in northwestern Tabot, amidst the forests there, and south of the high mountain lake named Nad Ho Ting . A road ran east to Fij .

History

In Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) , korobokuru were sighted in the mountains west of Nad Ho. The dzong-pon (sheriff) of the district proposed an expedition to their lands to locate their mines and open trade with them.
