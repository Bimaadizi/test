Leonid the Reacher was a mage who was studying necromancy with Maferil the Gravewalker in Luskan , Sword Coast North , during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Studiyng necromancy in Luskan sometime prior to 1487 DR , Leonid was Maferil's first and most promising ward.
