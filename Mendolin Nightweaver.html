Mendolin Nightweaver was an elvish specialty priest of Mystra in the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

Mendolin was not a confident man and he had not reached his full potential as a cleric .

Possessions

The elf wore a set of mundane splint armor and carried a shield . He was armed with an enchanted +1 silver dagger .

History

In the mid–14 th century DR, Mendolin Nightweaver sought to establish a temple to Mystra in the city of Loudwater in the Savage Frontier .
