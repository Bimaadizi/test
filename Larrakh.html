Larrakh was a member of the Cult of the Black Earth in Red Larch who was in contact with the Believers during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Personality

The priest hoped to redeem himself in the eyes of his cultist masters by conquering Red Larch. His plot was to transform the Believers into a nefarious organization and afterward unveil and defeat them, becoming a hero in the eyes of the citizens.

Relationships

Larrakh was a Black Earth priest in disgrace for failing some missions.

History

Around 1491 DR , he went to Red Larch, where he began to influence the Believers into becoming more dark and evil. He was defeated by some adventurers who were investigating in the village.
