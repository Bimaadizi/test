Khammeral's Coins was a currency exchange located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building had a single floor.

Location

The business was located in the Trades Ward on the eastern side of the High Road just north of the intersection with Selduth Street and due west of the City of the Dead .
