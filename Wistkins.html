Wistkins was a resident of the village Volkumburgh in the 14 th century DR .

Abilities

The man was exceptionally nimble.

Possessions

Wistkins possessed a ring of invisibility , some potions of healing , and a potion of flying .

Activities

Wistkins worked as a miner in the village and spent much of his time searching for the fabled " Volki's treasure."

History

In 1358 DR , in the month of Uktar some time prior to the Feast of the Moon , a group of adventurers came into the village, having been hired by the wizard Amelior Amanitas to search for fragments of the Awtawmatawn . While there they investigated the hut of Wistkins, finding it to be cold and abandoned. All they could find within it was a crude map of the Thunder Peaks he had drawn. This map had the village of Volkumburgh marked, as well as the ruins of the College of Rune Magics and the College of Shaping Magics .

A few years after this he was recruited by a Cult of the Dragon cell in Sembia to spy on a cell operating in the Thunder Peaks.
