Manycats Alley was a small back alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Manycats Alley was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward and the western section of the North Ward . The alley ran east to west in between Copper Street and Sul Street . Manycats Alley was also intersected by Shield Street .

Description

Manycats Alley was notable for the presence of a large number of roaming cats , which gave the alley its name, and a wide assortment of carved stone heads that decorated the buildings lining the alley. The carved heads were so life-like that many locals believed them to be the petrified remains of once living people.
