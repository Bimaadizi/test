Ahmad ( pronounced : / ɑː ˈ m ɑː d / ah-MAHD or : /ˈ ɑː m ɑː d / AH-mahd ) was a gnome innkeeper inhabiting Dihliz in 1367 DR .

Personality

Ahmad was an obsessively tidy individual—a trait that was reflected in the way he kept his inn, the Jasmine Dream .

Activities

Ahmad provided safe and tidy rooms to travelers.
