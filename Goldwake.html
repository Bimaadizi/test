Goldwake was a sea captain and a worshiper of Umberlee who prayed at the Water Queen's House in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Goldwake was a human woman with a light skin tone and hazel eyes. She wore a set of heavy splint mail and a helmet .

History

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Captain Goldwake frequented the Water Queen's House, uttering prayers to her goddess. She was at the temple shortly after one of its priestesses , Holli Dylford , drowned whilst swimming in the Gray Harbor . Goldwake wanted to appease the Bitch Queen before a voyage but found all the priestesses preoccupied with the last rights ceremony for Holli.
