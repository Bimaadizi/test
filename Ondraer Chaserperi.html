Ondraer Chaserperi was an entrepreneurial member of the Chaserperi family in the late mid-14 th century.

Activities

He traded in objects made of ivory ; his most notable wares were coffers, dice , and "smooth-sweep" style furniture. He was also among the best known members of the Chaserperi .
