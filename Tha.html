Tha was one of the phaerimms that dwelled behind the Sharn Wall beneath the Greycloak Hills for over a thousand years, before being released from imprisonment in the early days of the Return of the Archwizards saga.

Personality

Like the rest of the War Gather, Tha was contentious with the other phaerimm. It was against the nature of the phaerimm to remain in such close proximity to others of its kind for an extended period of time.

History

After being released in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , Tha and the other phaerimm in the Greycloak Hills War Gather captured a group of Evereskan elves that ventured underground to repair the breach in the Sharn Wall. Along with Zay , Tha demeaned and tortured the elves in order to break into Evereska and consume its mythal , killing many of them in the process.

Soon after, a mixed band of human and elves led by Melegaunt Tanthul ventured into the phaerimm's lair to rescue the captives. The elven warrior Galaeron Nihmedu killed Tha by slicing its face open at the jaw with one of the Vaasan darksword s.
