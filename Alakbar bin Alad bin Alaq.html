Alakbar bin Alad bin Alaq , or Alakbar for short, was the only male heir of the Padishah of Medina al-Afyal in 1367 DR .

Relationships

Alakbar was the firstborn son of Alad bin Alaq bin Alonka , the Padishah of Medina al-Afyal. He was a member of the House of Alon , the historical ruling family.

Activities

Alakbar left for a voyage over the Crowded Sea in 1363 DR and was not seen since.
