The Tuutsaas Chain was a mountain range located in the Alpuk region within the Great Glacier .

Geography

The Tuutsaas Chain was the western border of Alpuk, diving the Great Glacier and the Tortured Lands . Pingos were found in great numbers below the eastern slopes of the mountain range.

Flora & Fauna

Remorhaz have created an extensive network of tunnels below the mountain range. The southern reaches were home to white dragons . A tribe of gargantua yeti lived amond the most eastermost peaks.
