Sprangalar's Swordcut , which was also simply called the Swordcut , was a small alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Sprangalar's Swordcut was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's North Ward . The alley branched south from the Street of the Manticore , connecting with Stabbed Sailor Alley after a short distance.

History

Sprangalar's Swordcut was the site of a bloody confrontation between citizens and the Cult of the Dancing Bear .
