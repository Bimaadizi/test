Gavilon Jostins was the ex-adventurer mayor of the city of Elbulder as of 1372 DR.

Background

Gavilon Jostins became the mayor soon after Aldorn Thuragar became the ruler of Sespech . When Chondathan soldier attacked Elbulder with the intention of capturing it, Gavilon dispatched them with fireballs and lightning bolts . It was after this amazing display that the citizens of Elbulder came to agree that having wizards on hand might come in hand now and again.
