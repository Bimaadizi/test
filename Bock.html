Bock was the Guildmaster of the Purple Masks Guild , a thieves' guild located in Iriaebor in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Description

He was an enormous man with several chins, dressed in maroon and silver silk, with a pudgy ring-covered hand.

Personality

Despite his appearance, he was an intelligent man.

Relationships

By the early 1360s, Bock had been asking Jewel , the head of an independent thief family, to marry him for at least twenty years; she kept saying maybe.

History

In 1363 DR , he helped the Fellowship of the Dreaming Dragon get information about Ravendas , and he also helped them get into the dungeons of the High Tower of Iriaebor to try and free prisoners that Ravendas was holding captive.
