Dandifox was a famous hero among halflings who lived some years before the 14 th century DR .

History

Dandifox gained fame and hero's status by collecting a "tithe" from wealthy travelers and caravans whom he ambushed from trees and rubbery alongside roads in Faerûn . Dandifox then distributed collected loot among poor locals. Eventually, Dandifox died on a guardsman's spear sometime before the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
