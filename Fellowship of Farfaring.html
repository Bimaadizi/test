The Fellowship of Farfaring was an adventuring organization that consisted of multiple chapters in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The Waterdeep chapter of the Fellowship of Farfaring was opened in the late 15 th century DR. The organization advertised itself via street criers in the city's Dock Ward , offering its new members blazing new trails, seeing Toril , and getting rich doing so.
