Brus was a commoner child messenger and a nephew of the Shadow Thief named Gaelan Bayle from Athkatla in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Brus was an affable young boy with brown skin and light brown hair.

History

By the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR helped his uncle as a messenger and informant who led a group of Athkatlan youths, gathering rumors and kept an ear close to the gourd on various profitable opportunities around the City of Coin. Young Brus might've helped Abdel Adrian , serving as a guide and pointing him towards various well-paying individuals in Athkatla when Gaelan Bayle, along with the Shadow Thieves offered to sell him information that would help rescue his sister Imoen .
