The Shield of Balduran was the shield wielded by the legendary explorer Balduran .

Powers

This +3 shield made the wielder feel weak, but possessed the powerful ability to reflect the eye rays of beholders .

History

Along with his sword and armor , Balduran's shield was stolen from a museum in the city of Baldur's Gate . At some point it came into the inventory of Deidre , a merchant from Aurora's Magic Emporium , who did business from the Adventurer Mart located in Amnian the city of Athkatla .
