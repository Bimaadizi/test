Thuvauvyn Nathalanorn was a baelnorn lich in Myth Drannor .

Activities

Thuvauvyn was the guardian of House Nathalanorn 's crypt.

History

In 1487 DR , Thuvauvyn appeared to stop the shadovar agent Helgore Ulitarathulm . However, after a long and fierce battle, Helgore prevailed, destroying Thuvauvyn.
