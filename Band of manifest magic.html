The band of manifest magic was a magical copper ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The ring gave spellcasters more energy to use, and particularly empowered spells in the fields of evocation , conjuration , and transmutation .

History

The ring was found in the tunnels of the purple worm Yegora , who lurked below the ruins of Myth Drannor around the time the Elves of Cormanthor and Red Wizards of Droic Orgul fought over the city.
