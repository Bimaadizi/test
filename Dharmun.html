Dharmun was a woodcutter who worked on the project to build a canal that connected the Lake of Steam to the Nagaflow in the 1360s DR .

Possessions

He had a woodcutter's axe.

History

He worked as a woodcutter for the Innarlan canal project in the mid-1360s DR. On the night of Eleasis 10 , the Year of the Sword, 1365 DR , a black firedrake that had been sent to attack the canal site by the Red Wizard Marek Rymüt went into Dharmun's tent and snatched his teammate away. Dharmun ran away to get help. He, Ivar Devorast , and Hrothgar Deepcarver went in pursuit of the drake. They found it on the Naga Plains , but in the ensuing fight, Dharmun was melted to death by the drake's fire/acid breath weapon. Afterward, Devorast and Hrothgar succeeded in killing the drake.
