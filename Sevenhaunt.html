Sevenhaunt was the Rengarth father of Sunbright Steelshanks circa −700 DR .

Personality

The shaman was a powerful leader of the Raven Tribe .

Possessions

Sevenhaunt carried the greatsword Harvester of Blood into battle.

Relations

Sevenhaunt and Monkberry sired the fighter Sunbright Steelshanks .

History

Later in the Shadowed Age, Sevenhaunt became ill with a wasting disease and died. His sword was passed down to his son by his wife.
