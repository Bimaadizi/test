Thamalon's Own was a pear wine created by Thamalon Uskevren , of the noble Uskevren family of Selgaunt , Sembia , in the 14 th century DR .

Description

Thamalon's Own was the color of straw.

History

By the mid-14 th century DR, Thamalon's love for winemaking paid off. He created Storm Ruby , Usk Fine Old , and Thamalon's Own wines. In the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , Thamalon Uskevren sought to trade with Nuldrevyn Talendar of House Talendar , discussing a deal to ship Thamalon's Own and other Uskevren wines to Southern Amn , Tethyr , Calimshan , along with other Sembian trade goods. After tasting Storm Ruby, Nuldrevyn Talendar appreciated its excellent taste.
