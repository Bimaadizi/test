Mad Melyrk's was an outfitting supply store in Thentia in 1367 DR .

Location

The shop was on a main intersection in southern Thentia, just down the road from the docks.

Services

Mad Melyrk's offered a wide range of new and used equipment at bargain prices, buying, selling and trading as needed. The proprietor, Melyrk Rubyripper , was exceptionally good at determining the value of goods, and enjoyed haggling for fun.
