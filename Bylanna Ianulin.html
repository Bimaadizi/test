Magistrate Bylanna Ianulin was an official of the Amnian government who presided over the city of Athkatla during the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Personality

While she often offered sympathy to those who could not find justice, she was dedicated to upholding the letter of the law.

Activities

As magistrate, she oversaw city-wide investigations and made the decisions as to when individuals or groups would be charged with crimes.

Magistrate Ianulin operated under the Tessarch of the Council of Six . She in turn was served by Chief Inspector Brega and the city's other investigators.

Despite her authority and rank, she was powerless to interfere with the actions of the Cowled Wizards .
