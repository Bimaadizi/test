Thoran was a shadar-kai warrior in the ruin of Spellgard .

History

Thoran was once the apprentice of a cabal of Shadowfell sages until he was exiled for stealing forbidden tomes. In his exile, he started studying the secrets of Netheril , becoming an expert on Netherese spells to bind an arcane spirit. After reading " The Analects of Kuryon ", Thoran understood that his perfect victim would be Lady Saharel .

In 1479 DR , Thoran and an army of minions took control of the central tower of Spellgard with the intent to find and enslave the Lady.
