Namaxil was a Jaguar Knight in Maztica .

History

Naxamil was born in Qoral . When the village grew into a large city, thanks to the estate of Governor Cordell , he became one of the head workers involved in building warehouses.

However, in 1370 DR Naxamil and two friends started a terrorist campaign trying to destroy the new settlement. They burned warehouses and freed some Payit slaves. One night, one of Namaxil's group tried to kill Cordell. The assassin failed but managed to escape.
