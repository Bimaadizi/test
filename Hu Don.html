Hu Don was a bushi in T'u Lung in 1358 DR .

History

Hu Don was a bushi in the town of Pe Nok in T'u Lung. In 1358 DR, he became the leader of the local Dragon Claw followers, destroying local martial schools and shrines. Later, Hu Don was confronted and killed by the wu jen Ko La Ko and an adventuring party hired by Ko Ho San .
