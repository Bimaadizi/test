Albie Grizlow was a teenager cattle rancher in Uluvin , who lived there around the time of the War of the Silver Marches .

History

Albie and his friend Leshonna Daar escaped after their village was invaded by a group of hill giants and goblins . They tried to recruit a traveling adventuring party to save Uluvin
