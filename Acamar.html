Acamar was a star-like entity from the Far Realm who had taken a place among stars of Realmspace . Unlike normal stars, Acamar didn't have a fixed place in the sky but instead danced and wavered across Realmspace.

Description

Acamar was a dead star of utterly black inky nothingness that ate other celestial bodies that drew too close to it.

Acamar's hunger was endless, and when its influence on Toril was at its peak, it sent its agents, the Maws of Acamar , to devour everything they encountered in their path to sate the hunger of the dead star.

Acamar was usually sought out by some warlocks to make a pact with it. Neogi were one race that had made a pact with it and the other Far Realm-infested stars.

History

Acamar was one of the stars the warlock Japheth Donard sought out in 1396 DR , after losing his pact with the archfey Neifion .

In 1492 DR , Acamar, posing as an imaginary lover, taunted Skrianna Shadowdusk .
