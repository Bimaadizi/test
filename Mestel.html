Mestel was an old and powerful merchant organization and a noble family from the city of Arrabar in the Vilhon Reach who controlled a significant share of all the trade that moved through Chondath .

Organization

House Mestel was one of the oldest and most powerful merchant families of Arrabar. The oldest families held nine-tenth of the city's wealth while the younger merchant houses, such as House Matrell , fought for the one-tenth share of the market. The estate of the Mestel family was a sprawling high-spired mansion surrounded by a grove of suth trees planted along the estate's perimeter. Like all other mercantile houses of Arrabar, Mestels housed a sizable mercenary and guard force.
