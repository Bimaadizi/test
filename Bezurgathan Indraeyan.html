High Lord Doom Bezurgathan Indraeyan was the ranking priest of Kelemvor , the Lord of the Dead, within the Tower of Skulls in Ormath during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Bezurgathan commanded the other priests within the abbey, assembling them if needed into a formidable defensive force if the abbey was ever threatened.
