Master's Robes were enchanted magely robes created for the members of the Michell Academy near the town of Berdusk .

Powers

Master's Robes boosted the wearers's spellcasting abilities and enhanced their intelligence and charisma. Due to the nature of these enchantments, the robes could only be used by wizards and sorcerers .

History

One set of these robes was worn by Jamal Briars , a professor of the Michell Academy. The school was destroyed by the local temple dedicated to Loviatar long before the early 14 th century DR. It was unknown what came to be to most of these robes, however, one set of crimson garments ended in the hands of Marcos Flass , a disgraced Red Wizards of Thay , who pursued his golemcraft research in the mines of Termalaine in Icewind Dale in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR .
