The swailing blade was a magical flaming scimitar found in Thorngap during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

The long, curved scimitar visibly glowed and shimmered with heat, igniting the air around it.

Powers

The swailing blade burned and incinerated those it struck with varying degrees of ferocity. It also passively improved the firepower of magical flames produced by the wielder.

History

The blade was recovered from the stash of the druid Halsaime after he was defeated within the Druid's Deep of Thorngap.
