King Caladorn the Wise was a ruler of the realm of Ulgarth in southeast Faerûn before the 14th century DR .

King Caladorn decreed that priests could not judge a person's worldly behavior. As a result, priests could not serve as either king's justices or baron's justices in the kingdom.

Legacy

The lieko , the gold coin currency of the realm used circa 1367 DR , bore an image of King Caladorn the Wise on the front, with a mounted knight on the back.
