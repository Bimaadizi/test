Alaundo's Loop was a mysterious magical item apparently associated with the great prophet Alaundo the Seer .

Description

It took the form of a golden crystal shaped like a twisted curl that was constantly turning in on itself.

History

By 1374 DR , Alaundo's Loop was in the possession of Khelben Arunsun and kept within Blackstaff Tower , where it rested on a white velvet cushion on display between the ground and second floors.

Powers

Deep inside, it was said to conceal eternity itself.
