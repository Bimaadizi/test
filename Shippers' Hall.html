The Shippers' Hall was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Fellowship of Salters, Packers, and Joiners .

Location

This building was located along Oar Alley , in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep . Situated behind it was the Shippers' Storage (254 on the map), the guild's warehouse.

Structure

This building stood two stories tall.

Reputation

Like other guildhalls, the Shippers' Hall was considered by locals to be a landmark.
