Alatian's Spell Shroud was an eerily-glowing piece of enchanted clothing that belonged to Alatian , a servant and an apprentice of a lich named Estera .

Powers

This set of magical clothing possessed a +1 enchantment to the wearer's defense and it emitted dim blue lights that illuminated 10 feet (3 meters) around it. When the wearer of Alatian's Spell Shroud was struck, the item unleashed a burst of dispelling magic on the attacker. The wearer also received access to the spell sequencer spell stored within the robe. This spell was accessible once per day.

History

Sometime before 1372 DR , these magical robes were gifted to Alatian by his undead master. The lich's apprentice was often sent on accusation mission of various items from the land the living by Estera. During these expeditions, those who witnessed Alatian creeping through graveyards late at night, assumed that the eery glow radiating from the Spell Shroud was a restless ghost . The Shroud remained in Alatian's possession until he himself ascended into lichdom.

Alatian's Spell Shroud eventually ended up in possession of Boonmoppet 's master. The wizard , and later a lich, was killed before 1372 DR , leaving his tower and his magical goods in the hands of the lonely imp familiar.
