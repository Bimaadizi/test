Esmerella was a lycanthrope from Moray in the Moonshae Isles during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Originally a member of the Black Blood tribe , Esmerella came to live in Caer Moray within the community of mostly female lycanthropes led by Lady Amaranth , who taught her followers to be peaceful. Esmerella served as the midwife of this community.

Following the revival of the god Malar in the late 1470s DR, the community at Caer Moray was destroyed in the Battle of Caer Moray and Lady Amaranth left it behind.
