Amraith was a moon elf fighter adventurer, a member of the Hands of Fury , active in the early 14 th century DR .

Description

Amraith was an ousted elven nobleman turned adventurer, thanks to his skills in wielding weapons and ambidexterity . He was a man of cool demeanor and efficiency. Amraith's elven nature granted him speed that exceeded that of his teammates. His quick mind often resulted in won arguments.

Possessions

Amraith was armed with a shortbow , but when faced with melee, he wielded a duo of a short sword and a dagger .

History

Amraith joined his adventuring group sometime before the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , when he, along with the Hands of Fury, traveled aboard a pirate ship to the city of Luskan , ending up stranded there after the further marine plans fell apart. Seeing no other opportunities for profit, the troupe answered a call for mercenaries coming from the far northern town of Targos , besieged by the Legion of the Chimera .
