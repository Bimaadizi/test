The Kalian , which literally meant the Pipe or the smoking pipe , was a market located in the City of Brass in 1367 DR .

The Kalian was found in the Rookery ward of the city.

Activities

The Kalian was one of the largest markets within the city. Smoke vendors abounded here, selling incense, tobacco, smoked foods, and other aromatic luxuries.
