The Red Dragon Trading Post , also known as the Red Dragon Trading Lodge , was Luskan 's most successful overland trading company.

Location

The post was located in the North Bank of Luskan near the Throat water tower in the Mirabar District . The lodge could be reached by the long span of Harbor Cross from Blood Island .

Inhabitants

Although not as aggressive as Luskan's pirates, Red Dragon employees were always alert and well-armed. Due to staff shortages, they were usually looking to hire, at a rate of four gold pieces per day, with food, water and one potion of healing supplied per trip.
