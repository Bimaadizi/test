Ressamon was a mind flayer that joined the armies of the phaerimm during their return to the surface of Faerûn in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Ressamon was among the phaerimm's forces that took over the depths of the Irithlium in ruined Myth Drannor . On Mirtul 19 that year, the Vaasan warrior Vala Thorsdotter ventured into the Irithlium's dungeons by the grace of the baelnorn Corineus Drannaeken and slew all six of the phaerimm within, many of their bugbear and beholder minions, and decapitated the unfortunate Ressamon.
