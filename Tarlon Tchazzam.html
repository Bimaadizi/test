Tarlon Tchazzam was heir to the noble Tchazzam family of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Tarlon had two older siblings: Ulboth Tchazzam , the patriarch of the Tchazzam family in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR, and Carina Tchazzam , the previous heir after the disappearance of Tarlon's nephew, Corahk Tchazzam .

History

Tarlon became heir to the family in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , when his sister's pet cat suddenly killed her after being transformed into a beast of Xvim . Upon being named as the new heir, Tarlon decreed that cats were not permitted on his family's properties.
