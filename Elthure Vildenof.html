Elthure Vildenof was a former aspiring Red Wizard and later member of the Stalwart Lions as of 1372 DR .

Description

Though much older, he appeared to be in his late fifties, with a gray beard to his midsection and spectacles. He measured 5'8" and weighed 160 pounds (73 kilograms).

Personality

He felt the spectacles made him look distinguished.

Abilities

He was familiar with the construction of most categories of enchanted devices.

Possessions

Of note, Elthure owned a dusty rose ioun stone , a necklace of fireballs , and three rooms in the upper floor of Crosswinds Keep , where he kept a spartan bedroom, a magical laboratory, and a library. His only creature comfort or extravagance was an ivory-colored quilt with a subtle pattern of lighter starbursts.

Activities

Elthure mostly dedicated himself to magic, and he was available for consulting on others.

Relationships

He was a member of the Stalwart Lions.

History

Elthure had aspired to becoming a Red Wizard, but turned his back on the order and suffered a number of assassination attempts as a result. He continued to suffer these attempts until he joined the rest of the Stalwart Lions in the astral, at which point they ceased and he could resume his quest for knowledge. As of 1372 DR , he was still in Crosswinds Keep.
