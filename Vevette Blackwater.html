Vevette Blackwater ( pronounced : / v ɛ ˈ v ɛ t / veh-VET ) was a high-ranking member of Manshoon 's Zhentarim in Waterdeep during the early 1490s DR .

Activities

Vevette resided inside Kolat Tower with her master Manshoon.

History

In 1492 DR , if Manshoon managed to obtain the Stone of Golorr , after the Xanathar's Thieves' Guild and some adventurers discovered the Stone' s hiding place devised by the dragonborn butcher Thrakkus , Vevette was instructed to deliver the Stone to the fellow Zhent Agorn Fuoco and she managed to deliver it during a performance at the Trades Ward theater called the Brizzenbright .

Relationship

Vevette was one of the lieutenants of Manshoon.
