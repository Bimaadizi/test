Jherald was a local businessman in Blackfeather Bridge in Featherdale who owned a small general store as of the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

Activities

Jherald's store was well stocked with an impressive variety of goods. Well aware that his was the only general store for a good distance in any direction, Jherald raised the prices of his wares to about one and a half times their value in Ordulin . Customers in dire need of supplies were forced to either pay the prices or do without whatever it was that they needed.
