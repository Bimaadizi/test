Grout was an inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Grout was a forest gnome man with a sun-kissed complexion, large green eyes, and wavy blonde hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Grout could be found in the docks of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, where commoners could buy fresh fish , some said the best place to shop for the catch of the day. The docks were attacked by a band of sahuagin when the area was visited by the Absolute Adversaries , if was unknown if Grout survived the attack but he thought sahuagin to be disgusting creatures.
