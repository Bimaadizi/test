Severious was a magical and powerful longsword .

Description

Severious was owned by the adventuring fighter and member of Waterdeep 's City Watch , Beohram . This unique weapon was found among his remains in the lower reaches of the drow above Xanathar 's sanctum. The powerful longsword had a +5 enchantment.
