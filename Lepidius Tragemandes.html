Lepidius Tragemandes was a traveling con artist.

Description

A fair-skinned man, Lepidius was bald save for a slight crest of graying hair. He wore a red and white checkered shirt, and several items of jewelry.

Personality

Lepidius was a committed coward, and tried to avoid danger.

Possessions

He owned a caravan wagon, as well as loaded die , marked playing cards, and a blackjack .

Activities

Lepidius traveled extensively, and was known to appear in the unlikeliest of places. He referred to himself as an "impresario, orator, and medicine man," but was also worked as an information broker.
