Mara was the chef of the Bubbling Cauldron tavern in the village of Hilltop .

History

In 1372 DR after a kobold attack on Hilltop, Mara was taken hostage in the tavern's kitchen by a small group of kobolds led by Hurc , who had been separated from the main attack force.
