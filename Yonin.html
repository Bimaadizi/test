Yonin was a head ninja and also a zussho in Kozakura in 1357 DR .

Activities

Yonin led the ninja village of Susa . He was also the zussho of the local shoen .

Personality

Yonin, like all ninja of Susa, was very loyal to the Niwa clan .

Relationship

Yonin married a daughter of Niwa Hirotada .
