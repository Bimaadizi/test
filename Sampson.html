Sampson was the blacksmith in the town of Moonhaven sometime during or before the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Sampson had one known apprentice, Dida , who regularly burned her arms while working the forge and bellows .

History

At some point, Sampson developed gout , likely from having a poor diet. The town's healer treated the malady with a tincture of autumn crocus and recommended the blacksmith refrain from eating red meat and ale .
