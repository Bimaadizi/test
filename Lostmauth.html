Lostmauth was a red dragon living on the isle of Tuern in the Trackless Sea .

History

The Cult of the Dragon sent their leader, Severin himself, to bargain with Lostmauth for the red dragon mask . Severin also convinced the mighty dragon to lay waste to the city of Neverwinter , but Lostmauth was slain by adventurers before he could do so.
