Chalaratha , who was nicknamed the Dancing Maiden , was a skilled half-elven wizard inhabiting Westgate in 1358 DR .

Description

She was described as being breathtakingly beautiful.

Activities

In addition to her studies in arcane magic , Chalaratha owned and operated a perfume business called Chalaratha's Fragrances located on South Ssemm Spur .
