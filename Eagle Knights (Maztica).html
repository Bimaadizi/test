Organization

The lower echelon soldiers were known as Eagle Warriors and the leaders were called Eagle Knights. They practiced pluma magic and some were able to shapechange into eagles .

Possessions

All warriors of the Eagle Knights fought with a maca and a bow . High-level knights wore eagle cloaks , which were mantles made of eagle feathers.

Relationships

They were usually the scouts of the Maztican armies.

History

In the Year of the Wave, 1364 DR , a group of 60 Eagle Knights joined the Flaming Fist expedition to explore Anchorome as they departed Helmsport heading north.
