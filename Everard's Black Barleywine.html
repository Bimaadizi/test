Everard's Black Barleywine was a type of magical wine , available in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The enchanted libation was on offer at the Yawning Portal tavern in Waterdeep, the City of Splendors. Bottles of Everard's Black Barleywine were stashed away in Durnan 's cabinet. One serving of the beverage was priced at ten gold coins .
