Stuckley was a Netherese arcanist who owned and operated Gallery Steamers in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Stuckley oversaw the construction of flying ships that were used for transportation in Netheril . His immediate underlings were Rousselet and Flue , who managed the company at large.
