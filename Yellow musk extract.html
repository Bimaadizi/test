NicknamesDrug

Yellow musk extract was a drug that caused deep sleep for a day or more. The substance was extremely expensive and so strong that just a whiff would make a person light-headed.

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Jack Ravenwild purchased some yellow musk extract in Ravens Bluff and used it to knock out the slaver Cailek Balathorp , known as Fetterfist.
