Atabeg al-Mazdaghani was a noble marid who lived under the waters of the island of Al-Zira in the Djinni's Claws in 1367 DR .

Activities

The noble marid aggressively confronted any ship daring to drop anchor in his waters. He was fond of making sailors take vows of servitude. He usually put them to work underwater and granted them a water breathing ability.
