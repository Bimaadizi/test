The Bride was an ancient titaness who was the consort of the primordial Cirotralech , and that was sealed alongside him in Fimbrul , Laerakond .

Description

The Bride had three heads and three sets of arms. The hair in each of her heads was colored with different shades of blue.

History

In 1480 DR , the primordial cult known as the Sons of Winter was able to release the Bride from her slumber. While a band of adventurers was able to destroy the cult, the Bride fled the battlefield and began her preparations for the eventual revival of Cirotralech.
