Thunderstrike was a magical rapier found in the mines of Tethyamar during the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The rapier possessed a strong electrical shock that made it more lethal and more effective at finding weaknesses in the targets defenses.

History

Thunderstrike was found after Eluf the Steadfast sent a team to fight the drow of House Argith and rescue dwarven slaves in Tethyamar.
