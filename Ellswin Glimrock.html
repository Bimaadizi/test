Ellswin Glimrock was a master artisan refugee from the city of Elturel who found new home in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Absolute crisis followed the calamity of the Descent in which Elturel was briefly plunged into the Nine Hells . Ellswin Glimrock was among the many refugees forced out of Elturel by one of the two threats, despite being a master artisan of some small renown. Seeking employ in the new home, Ellswin sent a letter of introduction and inquiry to Madame Omotola of The Glitter Gala in the Lower City, praising her dazzling shop and offering services as a resident jeweler.
