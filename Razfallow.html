History

Born from a human father and an orc mother, Razfallow grew up to train at the Academy of Stealth in Calimshan. During this time, he attained leadership of his own team and trained Daryth . He would have promoted Daryth to be his lieutenant, but the student left the academy in fear.

In the Year of the Bloodbird, 1346 DR , he was hired by the evil wizard Cyndre in his attempt to take over the Moonshae Islands after joining the king's council of sorcerers. He slew many members of the royal houses as well as many druids of the Isles.

He led a team of assassins to kill Tristan Kendrick , but his father, King Bryon sacrificed himself to save the prince. Daryth came to assist the prince and recognized his former trainer. Razfellow was able to escape and return to Cyndre.

He later accompanied the wizard Kryphon to Duncastle , the forest stronghold of Hugh O'Roarke . Under the effects of magical invisibility and silence , he disposed of the clerical and magical defenses of the stronghold of Hugh O'Roarke. However, Razfallow was killed by Daryth in the streets of Duncastle.
