The Caliph's Bridge was located in the city of Dhaztanar in Semphar during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Caliph's Bridge was located within the Shari-Souk District in central Dhaztanar. It connected to the Silk Road as it passed through Dhaztanar. The bridge spanned one of many arms of the Tarkhold River as it passed through the district from the east.
