Tymora Alley was a small road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

Tymora Alley branched south off of Wall Street at the midway point between the northern Farmer's Gate and Delfen Ondabarl 's tower. Tymora Alley cut south then east before terminating at the landrise that separated the ducal estate from the rest of Daggerford.
