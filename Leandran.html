Leandran was a male drow sorcerer and the Master of Abjuration at Sorcere .

History

He was killed by duergar stonefire pots during the Siege of Menzoberranzan sometime between the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , and the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR . The magic fire hit the sorcerer directly, and left behind nothing but the stench of charred elven meat.
