Whitefish Bay was a prominent bay located on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Whitefish Bay was located near the center of Alaron, on the island's western shore of the Sea of Swords .

Description

The bay helped to naturally pinch the land in that region of Alaraon, and, along with the Fairheight Range , formed part of the border with Gnarhelm .
