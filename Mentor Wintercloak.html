Mentor Wintercloak was a mysterious human archmage who lived for some time in Myth Drannor in Cormanthyr .

Relationships

He was a close ally of Alais Dree .

History

Mentor was born in the Year of Old Danger, 219 DR . He lived for many years in the city of Myth Drannor whilst being perfectly disguised as an elf . He only revealed himself in the Year of Soaring Stars, 261 DR , for the creation of the mythal of Myth Drannor , offering his help in the creation.

As a member of the Incanists' Society , Mentor helped found and direct the Incanistaeum magic school. In the Year of Austere Ceremonies, 667 DR , he was the only human member of the early Council of Twelve .

From 426 DR to 440 DR Mentor tutored the young Khelben Arunsun (at that time still unnamed). In the Year of the Hunting Ghosts, 659 DR , Mentor also tutored the young Uthgardt mage Tulrun . During this period, Mentor was believed to have collected new spells created by his apprentices in a tome titled The Wizards' Workbook , which found its way into the possession of the wizard Flamsterd of the Moonshaes , who scribed a copy for his friend Elminster .

However, in the Year of the Howling, 682 DR , Mentor, together with the Masked and their four gnomes apprentices, left the city.

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , he was among the many powerful mages alongside Tulrun as his former student Khleben Arunsun brought them together to cleanse part of the High Moor and restore Faer'tel'miir , a lost city of Miyeritar .
