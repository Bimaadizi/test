Vayloss Talanthil I , who was called the Fireblood , was the first Sorcerer - Arkhan of the Talanthil Dynasty to rule the nation of Raumathar .

Activities

Vayloss was the patriarch of the Talanthil family when the last few Arkhans of the Nhalass Dynasty were killed by demons sent by Narfell in −605 DR . Since the last Nhalass died without an heir, Vayloss was proclaimed the new Arkhan with overwhelming support of the public. He ruled Raumathar until −559 DR .

Relationships

Vayloss I's firstborn son was Corlarth .
