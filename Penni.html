Penni was a wealthy inhabitants of Baldur's Gate in the 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Penni was accounted with the leven Lady Whitburn . Penni was known for wearing fanciful hats she purchased in the Lower City of the Gate. During the Absolute crisis , Lady Whitburn and her husband traveled to the Lower City, searching for the hat shop frequented by Penni..
