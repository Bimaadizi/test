Beldara Laerune was a wayward Harper .

Activities

Beldara was quite famous for her travels, like the one from Icewind Dale to Halruaa and the one from Nimbral to Sossal . She usually preferred to travel with elves or dwarves rather than with humans . She was a terrible cook but could easily read the surrounding area.

History

Beldara left her home city of Melvaunt when the Zhentarim rose to power in Zhentil Keep , hoping to find less oppressive areas where she could find inspiration for her music, adventures, and treasures. Beldara found a lot of all three.

In 1368 DR , she went missing and in Sembia some said she went inside a gate in Myth Drannor .
