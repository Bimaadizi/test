Theleda Rost was a human adventurer and a former member of the Company of the White Star in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

When the company disbanded, she settled in Waterdeep and used her treasures to run her own tavern, and provision caravans and merchant ships.

Theleda was murdered by one of her business rivals in the summer of the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR .

Abilities

She was an expert with traps and locks.

Description

Theleda had a pointed chin and a heart-shaped face.

Relationships

Theleda's daughter was Maresa Rost .
