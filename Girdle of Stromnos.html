The girdle of Stromnos was an enchanted belt created by Presdon of the Vast .

Description

The belt was a plain in appearance but distinctly wide girdle.

Powers

The girdle of Stromnos possessed a simple enchantment that significantly enhanced its wearer's strength, making them as strong as a hill giant .

History

The girdle of Stromnos was created by Presdon of the Vast several centuries before 1281 DR . The enchanter once encountered a hungry hill giant named Stromnos while gathering spell components . Presdon survived the encounter and named the belt after Stromnos. The girdle then was passed along to the enchanter's bodyguard Dendes the Wry . The belt was lost some time late outside Ordulin as Dendes was filled in the line of duty.

For the several centuries , the girdle of Stromnos was though lost, until it reappeared in the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR . The belt was worn by Flozem , one of the two bodyguards employed by Marketh of the Dorn's Deep in the Spine of the World .
