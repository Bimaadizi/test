Foley was an employee at the Cutlass in Luskan during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Around 1490 DR , the bartender of the Cutlass, Leoril , was arrested by members of the Order of the Gilded Eye , and Foley was made to stand in for him, during which time he encountered the Champion of the Burning Dawn .
