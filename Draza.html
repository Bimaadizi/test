Draza was a citizen of Port Nyanzaru in Chult during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

He had a husband named Belym .

History

Around the same time as the death curse , Draza was arrested for theft and sentenced to Executioner's Run . He and Belym claimed that the arrest was a case of mistaken identity, but were unable to prove his innocence before the time he was to be thrown into the Run with a pair of velociraptors .
