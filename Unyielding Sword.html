The Unyielding Sword was a small mercenary company in Skullport in 1379 DR .

Activities

The Unyielding Sword often procured slaves for the slaver Lanador .

History

In 1379 DR, Lanador hired Eldreth and his men to kidnap ten elves from Waterdeep 's streets. Unfortunately, unknown to them, one of them was one of the Agents of the Eye . So the Xanathar supported an adventuring party to enter the Lanador's lair. Many of the Unyielding Sword were there and were killed in the fighting.
