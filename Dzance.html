Dzance was a ranger of the North who retired in Silverymoon after his years spent adventuring across the Realms.

History

At some point in his adventuring career, Dzance explored the depths of the ancient dwarven city of Gauntlgrym . While exploring the former capital of Delzoun , he recovered a magical shield within a chest that came to be called Dzance's guardian .

When the veteran ranger finally retired in the "Gem of the North", Dzance gifted his namesake shield to the young knight of the city, Belpir , before 1357 DR .
