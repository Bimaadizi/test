The belt of the Beestinger was an enchanted monk 's belt that belonged to the famed hin adventurer and a member of Acquisitions Incorporated Rosie Beestinger in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

Rosie's belt of the Beestinger had an empowering effect on its wielder and helped their blows land unerringly.

History

The belt of the Beestinger was in Rosie's possession in the Year of the Duplicitous Courtier, 1496 DR .
