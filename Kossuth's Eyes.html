Kossuth's Eyes were a pair of dormant volcanoes in the Small Teeth mountains.

Description

These two volcanoes were located on the far eastern end of the mountain range.

History

The volcanoes were last active during the founding of the dwarven kingdom of Shanatar . However, just two miles east of the Eyes was the site of another more recent volcanic eruption that buried the city of Xandar during the time of the Korrunhel Dynasty of Tethyr in 260 DR .
