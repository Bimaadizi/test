Willow was a child customer of Felogyr's Fireworks , enamored with fireworks and their scent, in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Willow was a human child with long white dreadlocked hair, hazel eyes, and chestnut skin. She wore a commoner's tunic .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Willow frequented Felogyr's Fireworks, being lured in by the seductive smell of smokepowder . When she had pocket coin , Willow purchased fireworks to launch outside with fellow kids. Felogyr's Fireworks's proprietor, Avery Sonshal, sold the child the fireworks, but not before making sure she remembered the safety rules and after reciting Felogyr's Fireworks's legal disclaimer of not accepting any "liability for singed nails, toasted eyelashes, loss of limbs, scalded torsos, or blown off heads."
