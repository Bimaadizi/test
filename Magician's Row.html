History

Circa 1357 DR , Norweg and Panese separately stole two minor magic items of historical importance from the city vaults at approximately the same time. Though the thieves were unidentified, clues at the crime scenes, suspicions, and divinations tracked them to Magician's Row. The City Guard serving Regent of the Exchequer Vernon Condor only had time to search two of the five houses before they earned their boss's ire, so they tried to deduce the culprits from what they knew of the five wizards on Magician's Row.
