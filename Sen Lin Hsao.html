Sen Lin Hsao was a korobokuru shukenja in Shou Lung around Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) .

Activities

Lin lived alone in a small hut in a forest somewhere. Often often korobokuru and sometimes even wandering spirit folk asked her counsel about all kinds of issues, including business enterprises and marriages.

Possessions

Sen possessed the Mantle of Forest Spirits , a mantle woven from feathers, down, with every animal of the forest having contributed a tiny piece. The mantle permitted her to ask for omens and advice from any of the forest's creatures.
