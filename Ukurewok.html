Ukurewok was a small village in the Nakvaligach region within the Great Glacier .

Geography

Ukurewok was located in central Nakvaligach near the Shakkak Pit .

Inhabitants

Ukurewok was uninhabited. The previous residents left the village as a result of a kaiurit . Only ice toads and vermin remained in the abandoned village.
