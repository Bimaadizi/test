Farida bint Thuriya was a moralist and an ambassador of the Pantheist League .

Personality

Farida was known to be a very passionate and holy crusader for the Pantheist League.

History

In 1367 DR , Farida was a member of the royal court of Rog'osto 's khedive, Samia al-Sa'id , acting as a representative of the Pantheist League. During this time she was known to pay adventurers for whatever treasures they found out in the uncharted wilderness of the Ruined Kingdoms , after which she would arrange to have them smuggled out of Rog'osto.

Relationships

Farida was distrustful of Rog'osto's laymen mages and weak priests , believing that they inherent power within the city should not rest in their hands.
