Valero Brokengulf was a member of House Brokengulf in 1489 DR .

Personality

Valero had a deeply hatred of Claugiyliamatar .

History

Valero always wanted to destroy his old family's enemy, Claugiyliamatar. In 1489 DR, rumors arose that the Cult of the Dragon wanted to transform her into a dracolich at last. With the support of House Silmerhelve , Valero hired an adventuring party to defeat the great dragon.
