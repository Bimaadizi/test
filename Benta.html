Booyagh Benta was a cultist of the Absolute who served her goddess in the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

She wore the crudely-crafted leather -and-cloth straps, and signature headdress that were often worn by goblin spellcasters.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Booyagh Benta remained among the Absolute's devotees who were readied to assail the Emerald Grove , under the command of their general, True Soul Minthara Baenre .
