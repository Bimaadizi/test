Mushir Island was an island within the Crowded Sea of interior Zakhara .

Geography

Mushir Island was located southwest of the Cities of the Pantheon region of Zakhara. It was one of the southern islands that composed Al-Faraq Islands . Mushir Island was approximately 30 miles (48 kilometers) long and 15 miles (24 kilometers) wide. The eastern coast of the island was formed from rocky beaches while the western side was sandy. Submerged rocks lined the eastern coast and the western coast of the island was filled with coral reefs. A circular elevation was located in the southern section of the island. The island was uninhabited as of 1367 DR .
