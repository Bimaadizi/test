Dannal Harftevor was a half-elf male cavalry officer of the Amnian Council Army in Eshpurta , in the early Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR .

History

Originally from Tethyr , Dannal joined the Council Army Cavalry and rose to the rank of lieutenant. Due to the treatment of arcane spellcasters in Amn, Dannal kept his wizardly skills a closely guarded secret.
