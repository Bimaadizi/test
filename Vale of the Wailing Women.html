The Vale of the Wailing Women was a valley in the Small Teeth mountains of southern Amn .

Geography

The valley cut through the Small Teeth between the South Road and the Tethir Road . To the northwest was Lost Xandar and Kossuth's Eyes , and to the east was Hillfort Ishla and Arellaxerrontoal's lair . The southern end of the Vale held the Black Spires of the Maiden , a great temple of Loviatar .

History

The Vale was named for the women of Amn who watched their husbands march to war under Prince Imnel Torlath I during the Ogre Wars in the Year of Forestsfrost, 479 DR .
