Krogynth was a rare grog , or moonshine -like alcoholic beverage that originated from the Moonshae Isles .

Description

Krogynth was a strong distilled alcoholic drink of greenish color due to being made using a fermented green mold. The drink was not known for its sophistication and was carried around in buckets and served in giant-sized mugs . The resulting conception had a mild aroma, vaguely reminiscent of licorice root. Its taste was quite unique and abrasive. Those unaccustomed to the beverage said it tasted like " currants mixed with earwax."
