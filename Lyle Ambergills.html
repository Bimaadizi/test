Lyle Ambergills was the first mate aboard the Osprey during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He had brown eyes and brown hair, though he was almost bald. He grew a long handlebar mustache. He stood 5 ft 9 in (180 cm) tall. Lyle was incredibly strong despite his average height.

Activities

Lyle was a competent ship master and, despite wanting to captain a crew of his own, remained loyal to his captain Raider Mc Allister . He usually handled the recruitment of new sailors aboard the Osprey .

Possessions

Lyle wore a pair of bracers of defense and wielded an enchanted bastard sword . He never wore armor .
