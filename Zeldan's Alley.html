Zeldan's Alley was a small backalley road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Zeldan's Alley was located on the eastern side of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . It ran in between Warrior's Way and the Street of the Sword , passing north to south through a small neighborhood. A public well was located near the center of Zeldan's Alley.
