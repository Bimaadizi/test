Mintassan the Magnificent (also known as the Sage or the Mad ) was a planewalker and sage of the planes who lived in the city of Westgate . His sigil was a stylized sideview of a four-legged beast that vaguely resembled the letter 'M'.

Description

Mintassan was a middle-aged human man with dark hair and the eyes of brightest sapphire blue. He always was adorned in wizardly garb, his fingers decorated with numerous rings .

Personality

Whimsical and impulsive, Mintassan loved adventuring.

Possessions

He was known to have several magical items, including a staff of the undead .

Relationships

He was romantically linked with Alias .

History

Mintassan's townhouse was located in the southern section of Westgate on a branch of Southgate Market Street . The sign out front read " Mintassan's Mysteries : Curios from Very Faraway Places". It was known to be heavily warded.
