Andover Heights was a small mountain range located on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The mountain range stretched for roughly 60 miles (97 kilometers), running north to south along Snowdown's western coast with the Straight of Alaron . An unnamed forest and several lakes, including the Harloch , Mal Feargal , and Mal Sul , were found along the base of the eastern slopes. The inhabitants of the island also constructed several mines within Andover Heights.
