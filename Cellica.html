Cellica was a halfling thief who lived in Waterdeep with her adoptive brother Kalen Dren in the late 15 th century DR .

History

As a youth in Luskan , Cellica worked as a thief for the same organization as Kalen. The two became very close and considered each other brother and sister. When Kalen met Gedrin Shadowbane and joined the Eye of Justice , Cellica followed him to Waterdeep, where they lived together in a rowhouse. While Kalen performed his duties in the city guard, Cellica stayed home and tended house.

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Kalen attempted to stop a plot by the high priest of Beshaba to kidnap a powerful spellcarred wizard named Myrin Darkdance . A dwarf monk named Rath who was working for the high priest entered their house and stabbed Cellica to death.

Abilities

Although Cellica was not a spell caster, she had the power to use her voice to command others when she wanted to.
