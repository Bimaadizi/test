The Field of Megiddo , also known as the Battlefield of Megiddo , was a plain on the Avernus layer of Baator . It was one of the many battlefields of the Blood War .

Geography

This battlefield was located somewhere close to Ankhwugaht Prime , a small divine realm in Avernus belonging to the Mulhorandi god Set .

History

Sometime in the late 14 th century DR , a group of adventurers from Ravens Bluff found themselves in the plane of Baator, at a spot overlooking the Field of Megiddo, after being swept away by psychic winds in the Astral Plane and dragged through an anomaly known as Nelseddi's Maelstrom . At that time, a group of blue dragons were in flight from Megiddo, having taken a severe beating from tanar'ri , and they quickly sprung an attack upon the Ravenians. When the adventurers eventually managed to return to the Prime Material plane , they startled their benefactor Simon Regulus , who had just managed to find the Field of Megiddo in an ornate mirror he was scrying with.

Rumors & Legends

According to some legends, the Field of Megiddo was destined to one day be the site of the final battle of the Blood War.
