Waukeen's Wares was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood two stories tall.

Location

Waukeen's Wares was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward within the Adventurers' Quarter . The shop was located on the western end of Grocer's Lane where it was joined by Snake Alley .
