Description

The tavern was smoky, stale and dim, with light provided by a few high windows and oil lamps , and fitted with wonky and warped furnishings. It was reasonably popular at night, but still very quiet.

Location

The Drink was hidden in the dark back alleys of Tilverton.

Inhabitants

The Drink was attended by the barfly Ponter and the professional thief and underworld agent Orrag .
