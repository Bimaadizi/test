The Medusa's Glare was a sculptor's shop in the city of Waterdeep in the 14 th century DR .

Location

This shop stood at the southern end of Slop Street , near the Jade Dancer , in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

Interior

Inside the building was a secret room containing a portal to Harpers' Hold .

Activities

Owned by a member of the Harpers , the Medusa's Glare acted as a safe-house for that organization in addition to operating as a shop.
