Darantha was the personal maid of Lady Marantine Delcastle in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Darantha was a head maid in Delcastle Manor .

Relationships

Darantha served as the personal maid of Marantine Delcastle .

History

As of Mirtul in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , it was an established ritual in Delcastle Manor that anyone of the House should introduce themselves to Marantine upon entering, or to Darantha, if Marantine would not want to be disturbed.
