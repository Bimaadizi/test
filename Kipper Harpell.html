Kipper Harpell was a member of the Harpell family and their oldest member in the late 15th century, and he was known to be well versed in transportation magic.

History

In 1479 DR , Kipper was the oldest sage of the clan. Despite not liking to leave his house, he was, like all his family, involved in the war started by the drow with the Darkening .
