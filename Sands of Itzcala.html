The Sands of Itzcala was a massive desert in northern Maztica .

Geography

The Sands of Itzcala covered most of northern Maztica. Directly south of it were the nations of Kultaka , Huacli , and the Valley of Nexal . It stretched from the eastern coast to the western coast, and northwest of it was the Pasocada Basin .

Inhabitants

The Sands of Itzcala was a harsh land, inhabited only by the Dog People , who lived a semi-nomadic existence in small tribes. They understood their land better than anyone else, and relied on it for protection against foreign enemies.
