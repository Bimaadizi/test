History

In the Year of the Wyvern, 1363 DR , Frey and her 3 rd company of Waterdhavian guardsmen were patrolling the lands south of the city when they heard the call to arms raised by the militia of Daggerford . They fought alongside their countrymen several battles in the Second Dragonspear War , which culminated at Dragonspear Castle .
