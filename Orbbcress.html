Orbbcress (or Spiderweb ) was a shield belonging to Tiago Baenre .

Description

The shield had grips formed like black spiders with blue sapphire eyes. Like Tiago's sword Vidrinath , it was formed of translucent material that was spotted with diamond stones. It was made of glassteel .

Abilities

The shield could defeat some magical energy. When Tiago willed it, weapons stuck to his shield as if it were a spiderweb. It could also magically enlarge, spinning around to cover a larger area.

Tiago was able to attach the shield to a surface and hang from it (although perhaps only for a short time).

History

The legendary weaponsmith Gol'fanin forged Orbbcress in Gauntlgrym for Tiago in 1463 DR .

Following a duel with Drizzt Do'Urden that resulted in Tiago's death, Drizzt obtained Tiago's sword, Vidrinath , and shield, Orbbcress . Catti-brie combined both Bruenor 's foaming-mug shield with Orbbcress using the magic of a primordial , with the resulting shield keeping the ability to produce dwarven holy water and Orbbcress' s magical enchantments.
