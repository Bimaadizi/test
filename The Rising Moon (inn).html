The Rising Moon was an inn found in Highmoon , Deepingdale , during late 14 th century DR . It offered modest accommodations and rooms for travelers on a budget, such as merchants , adventurers , and mercenaries .

A room at the Rising Moon inn cost 6 sp per night.
