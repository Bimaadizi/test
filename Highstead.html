King Highstead was the ruler of the Onusclan tribe of orcs from the Orsraun Mountains in the late 14 th century DR .

History

King Highstead's reign was in full power circa the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR . Under his rule, Onusclan orcs raided neighboring territories of the southern Ancient Ax goblin clan and the eastern Tattered Cloth kobolds . Due to regular raids, populations of all three factions of the Orsrauns were kept in check and away from becoming a problem for Turmish . However, King Highstead's orcs trespassed in the Orbrekh , the territory of a group of giants calling themselves the community of the high brows , the giants decimated Onusclan.
