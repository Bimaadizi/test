Fated Winds was a trading cartel active in Neverwinter in the late 14 th century DR .

Organization

The Fated Winds coster was run by Jordan Wright circa 1374 DR . It was one of three major trade cartels granted offices within the Temple of Waukeen following the Shadow War in Neverwinter's Tyr's Eye district.

History

Before the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Fated Winds had an unsavory reputation as there were many rumors that claimed that the coster had dealings with the Zhentarim .
