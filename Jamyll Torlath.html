Jamyll Torlath was a royal daughter of the Amnian Torlath dynasty during the 6 th century DR .

Relationships

Jamyll was the child of Imnel Torlath I , the second king of Amn, and brother of Rhadik II . Her distant descendants were the Wavegold family who claimed the throne of Amn in 1095 DR starting with Erdhinn Wavegold I .
