LocationCastle Obarskyr , Suzail

The Warhorn Room was a room within Castle Obarskyr in Suzail .

Location

The room was somewhere within Castle Obarskyr .

Interior

The Royal Gorget of Battle had been on display there.

History

The Royal Gorget of Battle was held there until the fugitive wizard Elminster Aumar stole it, at some point close to Mirtul 5 of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR . The theft was discovered by Ganrahast Aeiulvana .
