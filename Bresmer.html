Bresmer was a seneschal of Langalos during the mid–8 th century DR .

Personality

He murmured more than he talked.

Description

The man was carefully expressionless.

Relationships

He served under High Duke Horostos of Langalos.

History

Bresmer reported to High Duke Horostos after the death of the adventurers of the Frostfire Banner , who had been seeking the Slayer in the Dead Place just west of Starmantle , that one of their horses had been found washed up in the river. He then reported, to the duke's displeasure, that no one in Reth or Westgate was forthcoming to take on the assignment to find the mysterious Slayer. And then, to even more of the duke's displeasure, he reported the Harpers had taken an interest in the matter, and would be reporting to him before the end of the season. He then helped the Duke come to terms with the fact he was in a bad situation and the best he could do was wait.
