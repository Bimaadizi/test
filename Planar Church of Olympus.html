TypeChurch

The Planar Church of Olympus was a religious organization present across various crystal spheres that was dedicated to the veneration of the Olympian Glades of Arborea , the pinnacle of chaotic goodness in the Outer Planes , as well as all of the deities that resided within it.

Dogma

This church solely venerated deities that resided within the plane of Arborea, regardless of their attributes, positions, or portfolio. This included almost all members of the Olympian pantheon , most members of the Seldarine , and some other varied Arborean powers.

Bases

Notable Locations

Within Realmspace , the Church had a temple and sect within the city of Bral , the sole city on the Rock of Bral .

Equipment, Spells, and Relics

Like any planar church, the priests of this faith counted as polygots . And due to venerating such a wide range of deities, the priests of this Church found themselves having access to their divine magic within the majority of crystal spheres.

Relationships

The members of this faith were generally accepting of those who had an Olympian power as their patron and were willing to grant them aid. However, priests of individual Olympian powers often found it hard to accept the Church's idea of worshiping the entire plane of Arborea. Priests of individual Arborean powers were also liable to commune with their deity to discern whether or not the Church was an acceptable form of veneration for them.

In terms of other planar churches, this Church had a good natured rivalry with the Planar Church of the Seven Heavens . With members of both clergies sometimes cooperating against evil causes.
