Jamaldro was the cellarer of Castle Obarskyr in Suzail in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Jamaldro was the cellarer.

Relationships

A number of nobles knew his name.

History

Jamaldro was working during the Open Feast held at Castle Obarskyr in Mirtul 26 of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR . His habit of setting out a specific vintage for War Wizards too early, however, gave Marlin Stormserpent a chance he seized to have a number of War Wizards drugged.
