Demra Samdro's Best Beds was a small shop of a maker and seller of grand beds that could be assembled after delivery. The shop was located on Saerdoun Street in the North Ward of Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

Location

Demra Samdro's Best Beds shared a three-story-and-an-attic building with a number of other businesses. The building stood on the southern side of Saerdoun Street, three doors west from the intersection with Thunderstaff Way . Other tenants of the office building were Erro Alhandrar's Nets , Tantra Jaressra's Fine Gowns , Laskalan's Lamps and Lanterns , and Vressa's Sweep Begone , as well as Tiger's Eye at one point.

History

The Best Beds shop was established sometime before the building changed management in the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , now in the hands of the gnome named Hardyn Glorjarn , who took over the building's attic. The shop's owner and master crafter was a dwarvish exile who operated under the alias of Demra Samdro .
