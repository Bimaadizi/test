Fabbra was a Netherese arcanist who worked at Ioulaum Industries in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Fabbra worked under Ghazzalee , managing the sales of the mythallar business. She investigated potential customers and ensured that they were both capable of paying, and weren't a threat to Netheril .
