Shadow snakes were large, venomous snakes that lived in the jungles of southern Faerûn . They may have had some connection to the Shadowfell . Yuan-ti were said to keep shadow snakes as pets.

Description

Shadow snakes were twice as long as a human and had greyish-black skin that was shadowy in substance.

Combat

Shadow snakes were ambush predators that surprised prey by stepping through shadows. The bite of this venomous snake could turn a victim's limb into a shadowy substance.

History

Shadow snakes were thought to have been the creation of the god Zehir . For this reason, they were greatly revered by the yuan-ti.
