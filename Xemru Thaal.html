Xemru Thaal was a high priest of Savras and leader of the House of the All-Seeing Orb in Tashluta during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Sometime during the the Year of the Scarlet Witch, 1491 DR , or early the following year, Xemru deposited the tome titled The Canopic Being within the shelves of Candlekeep , along with a hand-written note. Shortly thereafter he surrendered himself to the oracle of Tashluta, Valin Sarnaster , helping her achieve immortality as a mummy lord . He became the first of her canopic golem , powerful constructs that protected the demiplane in which she resided .
