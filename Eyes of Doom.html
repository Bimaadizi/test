Eyes of Doom were enchanted helmets used by Bane -worshiping Zhentarim agents in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

The name of the Eyes of Doom was in reference to two lenses that were part of the item's design and were mounted over the wearer's eyes. Those to observed a person wearing Eyes of Doom saw two swirling infinite voids through the helmet's opening. A prominent holy symbol of Bane was inscribed upon these items.

Powers

The wearer was able to cast the doom spell once per day by simply meeting their target's gaze with the helmet's void eyes.

History

Before Bane's demise during the Time of Troubles , Eyes of Doom were worn by Bane-worshiping Zhents. After the Zhentarim adopted first Cyric and the Bane's son Iyachtu Xvim , the helmets quickly lost favor and were sold, entering the general population. With Bane's return in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , the Zhentarim embarked on a mission to reclaim Eyes of Doom .

As of the late 14 th center DR, Eyes of Doom were spread as far as the Nelanther Isles off the Sword Coast .
