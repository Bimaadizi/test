Sulfur , also known as brimstone , was an alchemical substance used in potion -making. It had a potent rotten egg smell, often associated with fiends of the Abyss .

Description

Sulfur was a naturally occurring substance that could be found and, more often smelled, in the vicinity of volcanoes, swamps, and hot springs.
