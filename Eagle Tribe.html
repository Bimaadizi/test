The Eagle Tribe were a roaming tribe in the furthest western region of the Shaar in the mid–14th century DR . Their hunting lands were bordered on the west and east by the Shining Sea and the Lake Lhespen .

The tribesmen revered the giant eagles of the region and had cultivated a leaping attack that mimicked their movement. Like their namesake, they were known for their keen eyesight.
