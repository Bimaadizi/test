Shur was a powerful human mage who actively opposed violence against nature.

Description

Shur had a stern and wrinkled face, framed by long locks of gray and white-streaked hair. She was dressed in flowing red robes, covered with a black cloak.

Personality

A self-appointed protector of the wild, in particular trees , Shur was abhorred by the destruction of nature. She considered such acts evil, and used her considerable magical talents to both find and stop them.

Possessions

Among Shur's possessions were a cloak of protection and a ring of mind shielding , as well as a gem of insight .
