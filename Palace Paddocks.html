The Palace Paddocks was a horse enclosure located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The enclosure stood two stories tall.

Location

The Palace Paddocks were located in the western section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward just north of Piergeiron's Palace and south of Fetlock Court .
