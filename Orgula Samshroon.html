Orgula Samshroon was a clerk inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

She was a stout and middle-aged human woman.

Personality

She had a motherly or matronly manner to her and was quite easy going.

Abilities

She had an excellent memory, never forgetting a face and able to recall details of a person's appearance and attire.
