Mixed coffee was a common type of a kaeth beverage served in the city of Baldur's Gate , the Western Heartlands , and the West Faerûn in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The drink was a blend of a bitter but enlivening kaeth and a soothing milk tea . This drink was a popular libation sold at grand city plazas during musical performances.
