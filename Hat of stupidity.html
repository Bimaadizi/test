A hat of stupidity was a magical hat that cursed its wearer to become quite dumb.

Description

A hat of stupidity looked just like any other magical hat. Any and all attempts to divine its true nature would fail, still seeming like a beneficial magic hat.

Powers

Once placed upon a head, the hat would curse the wearer with stupidity such that they thought themselves smart, and preferred to wear the hat while thinking, casting spells , or doing any other sort of mental work. Only with the use of a remove curse spell could the wearer ever be free from the hat's effect.

History

In the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR a coach traveling from The Way Inn to Waterdeep carried, amongst varied and sundry contents, a hat of stupidity .
