Lana Mara was a high-ranking officer of Muktar in the Turmish in 1358 DR .

Activities

Lana was a member of the Champions of Muktar . She was also a Deputy Brigade Commander in the Muktar's army.

History

Lana was the latest, and possible greatest, apprentice of the famed illusionist Yhelva the Wicked . In 1358 DR Lana and his fellow Champions arranged the trap inside Fort Blood planned by the self-styled king of Muktar , Hetep , to eliminate an adventuring party well-known in Golconda . Later she took part in the Battle of Wilma Pass .
