The bauble of draconic resistance was a powerful magical item, a small orb found in Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Drawn from the rituals of the Cult of the Dragon and the dracolich Karliath , the orb gave the bearer the agility and resilience of a powerful dragon . It sheltered them from magical and physical strikes, and let them dodge threats all the better.

History

The bauble was found in the Cult of the Dragon base hidden in Myth Drannor, later attacked by the Cormanthor Elves when they attacked the Red Wizards there.
