Enollar was an exceptionally talented and extremely intelligent Netherese arcanist that developed multiple spells .

Description

Enollar was an exceptional student who travelled to multiple Netherese enclaves to further his knowledge.

History

Enollar was born in 2199 NY (−1660 DR) and became a dockworker during his younger years. However, Enollar was destined for greater things. Thus, at 18 years old, he boarded a ship to Seventon to attend Alimon's Mental Academy , where he became the top student. After the young talent left the academy, he visited multiple enclaves before settling in Harborage . Back at home, he invented several spells, such as misdirection , delude , sequester , mislead , remove curse , and sending . The spellcaster died in 2298 NY (−1561 DR) .
