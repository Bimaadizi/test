Ortauré was an elven house of Cormanthor .

History

Lord Ortauré entered into a conspiracy against Coronal Eltargrim Irithyl while at Castle Maendellyn in 241 DR after the death of a heir of the house by the Narnpool during the revel in which the new human armathor Elminster Aumar substituted for the Coronal, believing him to be the killer.
