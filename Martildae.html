Martildae was a wererat member of the Riverside Rats gang, who dwelt in the Wheloon Prison of Cormyr during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

As a wererat, Martildae had white fur.

History

Martildae lived in Wheloon Prison with her fellow wererats of the Riverside Rats gang. When they were forced from their hideout by the Night Brigade under Storna Harnwood , Ryndalla Cuttail organized a counter-attack on the brutal arena master Agarta the Breaker . Martildae helped guide the Rat's help into Agarta's arenas , and handed them a magical talisman that allowed the allies to shapeshift into rats . This allowed them to sneak into the Night Brigade areas and take them by surprise.
