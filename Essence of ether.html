Essence of ether , was a somnolent drug and an illicit substance, inhaled to induce drowsiness and sleep.

History

In the late 15 th century DR , members of the Guild worked with smugglers who brought drugs , weapons , firewine , and other substances into Baldur's Gate . Bowls of 0.8 pounds (0.36 kilograms) essence of ether were among such illicit goods.
