The Semphari was a magnificent light horse breed found in the lands of Semphar and Murghôm . They were nearly exclusive to those realms in the east and beyond within the Hordelands .

These noble horses were bred for generations by the nations' aristocracy. While they were not well-known throughout foreign lands, Semphari steeds commanded a steep price in the range of 2,000 gp .

Description

The beautiful steed had a light, lean-muscled body with long, slender legs. Their efficient builds granted them a significant amount of stamina. The manes and tails of the Semphari were quite long.

Semphari were exceptionally sure-footed, graceful in their movement and quite intelligent in nature.
