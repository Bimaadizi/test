The First Lancers , also called the Blazes , was part of the standing army of the efreet in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire .

Organization

Noble efreet led these medium cavalry who numbered 1,000 strong. Nightmares served as their mounts.

Activities

The First Lancers were used against fallen nobles or to quell small rebellions. Their noble efreeti commanders enjoyed lording their position of power over others.

Base of Operations

The City of Brass served as the base of the First Lancers.
