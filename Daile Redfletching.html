Daile Redfletching was a human ranger of the Moonsea in the late 14 th century DR .

Relationships

Daile was the daughter of the ranger-thief Ren o' the Blade and the druid priestess Ciela , who died when Daile was young.

Description

She had red-gold hair cut short and her father's blue eyes.

Possessions

Ren carved for Daile a longbow and polished the wood with the ioun stones from the hilts of his magical daggers Left and Right . The longbow was imbued with the magical power to shoot arrows with great accuracy and distance. The bow had a deep red-brownish color and a highly polished surface, and emitted a low hum when Daile wielded it and only she could use it. The ioun stones magically embedded themselves within Daile's bow when Left and Right were passed on to her after Ren's death.
