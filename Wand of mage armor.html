Wands of mage armor were enchanted wands that held multiple charges of defensive spells unleashed upon command.

Description

Wands of mage armor were slender 1 foot and 3 inches (0.38 meters) wands with sharp point and angular design. They were usually made using bone, ivory , or wood , yet fragile and kept in cases. The total number of charges varied per item, between ten and could count as as much as fifty.

Powers

Each of these wands held several charges of the mage armor spell that could be discharged at will using a wand's command word. Upon expenditure of a wand's last charge, it disintegrated.
