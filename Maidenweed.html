Maidenweed was an alchemical draught used to prevent pregnancies.

Powers

When drank, it prevented pregnancy in the drinker for about a month.

History

The synthesis of this concoction required a certain aptitude with herbal lore. Hedge wizards often traded in it. It sold for about 250 gold pieces .
