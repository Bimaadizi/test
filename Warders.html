The warders were a loose military order based in or around the halfling cities of Luiren .

Organization

A particularly large troop of warders found in Beluir was composed of 400 halflings who were all elite specialists with the sling .

Tactics

While using stealth to their advantage, the warders preferred to attack any threat to Beluir with a hail of stones from multiple directions. The enemy was often so confused that they did not realize where they were being attacked from.
