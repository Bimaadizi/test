Fist Murphy was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Murphy had a fair complexion and pale blue eyes. He wore a set of chain armor as part of her Flaming Fist uniform and was armed with a simple spear .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Murphy was stationed in the Basilisk Gate 's barracks and prison, serving under Blaze Liara Portyr and Manip Seyama . During the war with The Absolute , Fist Murphy was cause stealing a purse of gold from the evidence locker in the Basilisk Gate. Murphy swore to Blaze Liara Portyr that he made a mistake and would never do such a things again. As a punishment, Portyr ordered Murphy to stand balancing on one foot until she believed his promise. Murphy was trying his best to do just that.
