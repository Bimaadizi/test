Silbran was the 32nd Obarskyr monarch and a queen of Cormyr . She took the crown after the death of her husband, Ulbaeram Obarskyr , so that she could protect her son Raerboth from his wicked aunt Gorauna . When her son came of age, the crown passed to him.
