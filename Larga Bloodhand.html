Larga Bloodhand was a orog warlord in Thar near Phlan in 1489 DR .

History

In 1489 DR, she went with her bodyguards to meet the black dragon Throstulgrael , who had recently settled near Twilight Marsh . She hoped that the dragon would sponsored her in her rise to power, but one of her bodyguards inadvertently insulted the dragon and they became his captives. Eventually, she was freed by an adventuring party sent to recover some items from the dragon's lair.
