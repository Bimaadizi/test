Milver's Silver Star was a brand of condiment produced in and exported out of Sembia in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Milver's was among the exotic foodstuffs sold at Turnell's Provisions in the town of Tegal's Mark in Tasseldale . As an imported good, Milver's Silver Star was sold at a ten percent markup.
