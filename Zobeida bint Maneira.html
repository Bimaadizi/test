Zobeida bint Maneira was a sorcerer within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz , the City of Delights, in 1367 DR .

Activities

Zobeida served as an assistant of the Grand Vizier, Alyana al-Azzazi . She was particular skilled with warding magic.
