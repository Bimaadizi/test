Coletti was a Netherese arcanist who taught at the Institute for Sorcerous Imagery in Eileanar until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Coletti taught alongside the other six resident teachers, made up of Driander , Dillencraft , Galanti , Maleville , Holstmann , and Sylberg .
