The Dragon Throne was the throne of the king of Cormyr .

Description

The Dragon Throne, as its name suggests, was carved in the likeness of a great dragon . The throne had a high back that was taller than any human . It was crafted from a dark material.

It sat upon a raised dais on the northern wall of the Chamber of the Purple Dragon in the Royal Court of Cormyr in Suzail .

History

Shortly after Cormyr was founded in the Year of Opening Doors, 26 DR , the first king, Faerlthann Obarskyr , renovated a large meeting hall constructed by his father, Ondeth , and made it into his throne room at the Royal Court. He commissioned the construction of the Dragon Throne, which remained the throne of Cormyr for over a thousand years.

Only the king was permitted to sit upon the Dragon Throne.
