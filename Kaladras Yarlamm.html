Kaladras Yarlamm was a Red Wizard of the 14 th century DR who survived combat with the Simbul , only to be summoned by her and die in combat with devils in that year.

Abilities

He knew of a method to convert flames to acid .

Possessions

He had wands .

History

Kaladras Yarlamm once fought the Simbul and survived; unknown to him, however, she'd thrust some of her blood into his body, where it had lodged, making him vulnerable to her blood ring spell later on. Decades later, she used the spell in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , calling him to her service in Avernus , where he was killed before he could defend her.
