Haldan was a thief in Bloodstone Village in 1357 DR .

Activities

Haldan was the main source of gossip in the village (often inaccurate). He liked to stay at the Inn of the Clowns .

History

In 1356 DR , Haldan collaborated with a bandit army for some money. In 1357 DR, he was not impressed by Gareth Dragonsbane 's party.

Personality

Haldan wanted to always be with the winning side. He did not believe in Gareth Dragonsbane 's chances of victory against the bandit army, so he agreed to work for them if they would pay him.
