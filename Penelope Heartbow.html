Penelope Heartbow was a low-ranking priest of Chauntea who served the House of Bountiful Harvest temple dedicated to the The Golden Goddess in Ravens Bluff 's Temple District of Ravens Bluff in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Penelope was one of the three lowest-ranking serving priests at the House of Bountiful Harvest under the leadership of High Harvestmistress Arabella Ieruin of Chauntea by the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR . The other two were Melissa Drover and Carmon Tessler .
