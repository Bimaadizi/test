Geremis Dyrr was an old member of House Agrach Dyrr of Menzoberranzan in 1373 DR .

History

Geremis graduated from Sorcere at an unknown time and became an apprentice of the Lichdrow Dyrr .

During the siege of Dyrr's compound for their betrayal of Menzoberranzan during the Siege of Menzoberranzan , Geremis, bald and aging, was called back to his house together with all of Dyrr's other wizards to help raise magical defenses.
