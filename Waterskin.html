A waterskin was a bag or pouch of skin that was used to hold water , particularly in hot regions.

Description

Waterskins were often shaped like kidneys and made of goatskin , but they could also come in a variety of other styles. They were sold in Maztica , Faerûn , and Zakhara , as well as other places, where they were often sold in explorer's kits .

Powers

Magic waterskins existed throughout the Realms , particularly in Maztica. Golden water of Zakhara could be used to repeatedly replenish waterskins.
