Cthylarr , also known as Cthylarr the Illithilich , was an undead mind flayer from Cyrog as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Cthylarr was an illithilich of epic power.

History

When Orcus arrived in Cyrog in the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Cthylarr ended up serving him. The illithilich travelled with its patron to Castle Never , where it sought the phylactery of the dracolich Azharzel .
