Artagalor Goldbeard was an dwarf jeweler and shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the lateâ€“14 th century DR .

Description

As his name implied, Artagalor's beard was brilliant gold.

Activities

He owned the Eye of the Beholder , a jewelry shop located in the Foreign Quarter on Twilight Street . He was a dextrous and skilled craftsman who worked with jewels and precious metals.

Artagalor stayed in Zhentil Keep following the city's destruction in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR because his god, Moradin , commanded it of him. Moradin appeared to him saying "this city needs a few shining lights amongst the filth."
