Lawrence was a giant, particularly intelligent badger that formed a bond with the druid Lanseril Snowmantle and lived with him in Snowmantle during the 14 th century DR .

History

When Lanseril was imprisoned by the Zhentarim in the year 1369 DR , Lawrence went to live with the half-elf 's uncle Willard Gooseford at the village's winery. Lawrence allowed the imprisoned druid to maintain communication with the outside world, by relaying messages between him and his elderly uncle.

He was spotted by the Priestess of Bane Glorganna who, understanding Lanseril's affinity with the natural world, ordered the Zhentilar soldiers to kill Lawrence on sight.
