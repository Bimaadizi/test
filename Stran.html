Stran was a shady-looking black marker merchant who operated out of Black Stone Inn in Daggerford circa 1370 DR .

Description

Stran wore a dark leather armor that made his already pale skin appear almost ghostly. He kept his black hair in a ponytail and sported a big bold spot.

Possessions

Stran was selling items unavailable anywhere else in Daggerford like light and flexible armors for the roguish types, a wide selection of traps and lockpicks, as well as Zakharan Knockout drops .

History

Stran could be found at the back dark corner of the Black Stone Inn , an inn of ill repute known for loud drunks, and rowdy sailors circa 1370 DR peddling his illicit stock.
