History

In 1372 DR, during Zhentarim's plot to covertly take over the city of Daggerford via a puppet ruler, Teshlang's cell operated out of the city's sewers . The Black Network cell Teshlang was a part of established a secret route to finance and equip an army of orcs and even made a deal with a devil , the ancient enemy of Daggerford. The sewer cell was eventually discovered by the Hero of Daggerford . All members, apart from Teshlang, who was absent at the time, were slain.
