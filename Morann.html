Morann was a Griefguard in the House of Grief temple that hid the Cloister of Sombre Embrace in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Morann was a stout dwarf with brown beard and red hair. He wore a set of padded armor and was armed with a scimitar and a shield .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Morann served the Mother Superior as one of her Griefguard at the gates of the House of Grief. Morann, along with the entirety of the cloister may have confronted its member Shadowheart were she to turn away from Shar .
