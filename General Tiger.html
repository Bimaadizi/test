General Tiger was a high-ranking member of the Celestial Bureaucracy .

History

General Tiger was a member of the Animal Kings' Court , of the Ministry of Animal Affairs of the Celestial Bureaucracy, overseeing the Qui sub-committee that governed the affairs of the animal population of the Fochu Peninsula .

In 1358 DR , he was summoned by the ch'i-lin officer Chanii in order to expel Osari Minhiro and an adventuring party he had hired from Qui . He easily sent them into unconsciousness but insulted Chanii for asking his help.
