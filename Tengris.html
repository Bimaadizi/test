Tengris was a Tuigan deity of the Sun .

History

According to legends told by the Tuigan, Tengris was pursued by the moon goddess Becal after she scorned her nine suitors.

Worshipers

It was a custom among the nomadic people of the Hordelands to not dry clothes in the sun, believing that doing so would offend Tengris.
