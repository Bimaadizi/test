Yochit was a traditional Thymari salad dish, made of a variety of herbs , spices , nut oil , and ants ' eggs .

Locations

The yochit was a traditional delicacy of Tymanther . The ants used for the dish were of a variant unique to Djerad Thymar and nearby lands, making the yochit a dish pretty difficult to get in other regions.
