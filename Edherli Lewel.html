Edherli Lewel was a merchant just before the Tyranny of Dragons .

Personality

The merchant was an impatient individual but took great care with her wagon and horse.

History

Around 1489 DR , Edherli was traveling from Chult to Waterdeep to sell exotic woods. She attached her wagon to a great caravan, which unknown to her was full of Cult of the Dragon agents transporting treasures for the Hoard of the Dragon Queen .
