Maraeda was a location somewhere near Moonshorn Tower .

Description

There was a well at Maraeda, known as Gullet Well , or the Well of the Bonedance.

History

The Gullet Well at Maraeda ran dry in the Year of Laughter, 761 DR , which led to the death of many a horse . The sage Tabarast of Moonshorn Tower reported that this had been taken as a divine sign.
