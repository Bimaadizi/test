Lymmura Auldarhk was a devotee of Sune in Red Larch during the Elemental Evil crisis.

Personality

Lymmura was a sympathetic ear and guide to all the citizens. She was widely loved and trusted in the village.

Activities

Lymmura Auldarhk, along with Imdarr Relvaunder , was the caretaker of the Allfaiths Shrine . She officiated at many marriages in the village.
