The House of Morning was a temple of Lathander , the only shrine of significance in Feather Falls , located within Featherdale in the late 14 th century DR . It was run by Morninglord Jallian Horgontivar , who himself was supported by a clergy of 16 clerics and acolytes devoted to the deity of "dawn" and " light ".

Activity

The temple rented out spartan rooms at the cost of 1 gp per head per night. They also issued loans to local businesses.
