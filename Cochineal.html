Homeland(s)Maztica

Cochineals were tiny parasitic insects , native to Maztica , notable for their red color.

Usages

Cochineals were crushed into a red dye , most commonly used in the production of makeup for reddening lips and cheeks, as well as fabric dyes.

History

By the late 14 th century DR , powdered cochineal was imported from Maztica and sold all around the Realms via Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue . A vial of 8 ounces (230 grams) was sold for 4 silver pieces .
