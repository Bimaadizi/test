Fara Icehewer was a ranger of the Sky Pony tribe of Uthgardt barbarians that lived in a camp in the Nether Mountains circa 1372 DR . This band snubbed the traditions of the Uthgardt and acted as plundering bandits under the leadership of Wolmad Eaglewing .

Despite her identity with the tribe, Fara led a faction within the Sky Ponies that opposed the needless violence and greed.
