Acciajuoli was a Netherese arcanist and co-owner of Transgate Industries , who lived in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

After the death of her parents Ocelot and Ytartia , Acciajuoli operated Transgate Industries alongside Shander and Thamer . The three siblings managed to do extremely well and greatly increased the success of the company. Acciajuoli also gave money to her husband Benante , which allowed him to pursue his passion project of Benante's Butchery .
