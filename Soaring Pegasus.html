The Soaring Pegasus was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tavern stood two stories tall.

Location

The Soaring Pegasus was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward on the west side of the intersection of where Sail Street joined Dock Street .
