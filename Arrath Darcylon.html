Arrath Darcylon , known as the Wandering Bard , was an itinerant writer active in Faerûn sometime between the 8 th and mid-to-late 14 th centuries DR .

Arrath once wrote of encountering a Triadic knight dedicated to Ilmater and marveling at her resilience.
