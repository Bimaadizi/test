Wyvern Road was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

Wyvern Road originated at Zhentil Keep's Trail Gate , taking over for the Zhentilar Trail inside the city walls. The road terminated at the unnamed gate that allowed passage into the Inner Quarter . Wyvern Road was located in northwest Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter . Wyvern Road was part of a large convergence of other roads in Zhentil Keep that formed a large intersection just east of the Trail Gate. The other roads were Border Street , Fortress Street , Cobble Road , and Nool Street .

Description

The road ran roughly west to east.
