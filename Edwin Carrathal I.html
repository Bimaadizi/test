Edwin Carrathal I was High King of Callidyrr at the turn of the 12 th century DR . Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles , he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.

Relationships

He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty . He was the third son of Queen Yolanda and King Bertrand , and his eventual successor was his first daughter, Bethany . Her first son and heir was named in Edwin's honor .

History

Edwin I assumed the throne in the Year of the Dawndance, 1095 DR , following the death of his father.

He ruled for thirty-two years before he died of natural causes in the Year of the Luminar Procession, 1127 DR .
