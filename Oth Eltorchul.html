Oth Eltorchul was a young wizard and noble inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Oth worked within the Eltorchul Academy in Waterdeep's Sea Ward until a tragic accident in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , damaged his mind. Oth was attempting to create a dream sphere using the Mhaorkiira Hadryad when the accident occurred.

Relationships

His father was Thesp Eltorchul and his younger sister was Fea Eltorchul . Oth was the heir apparent of the Eltorchul noble family before his accident.
