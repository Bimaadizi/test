Apryl Wavesilver was a noblewoman of the Waterdhavian noble house Wavesilver during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

She lived within Wavesilver Villa located at the corner of Ivory Street and Flint Street in the central section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward .

Relationships

Apryl was the consort of Lord Bleskos Wavesilver .
