Saerghon Alir , also known as Saerghon the Magnificent , was a wizard and the service-mage for the Aurora's Realms Shop, Singing Dolphin Catalogue Counter in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Personality

As his title attested, Saerghon the Magnificent was a pompous and arrogant man with many affected mannerisms and flourishes and who always made a grand entrance. He considered himself the greatest mage in Waterdeep.

Activities

For all that, he was simply the service-mage at an outlet of Aurora's Emporium , usually only transporting goods via teleport spells from the central warehouse.

What's more, he apparently hadn't even opened, let alone read, all the books and scrolls he'd bought or obtained over much of his life, and though he was a wizard of some power, he had not mastered half the magic in his possession.

Possessions

Instead, for his protection, he relied on a couple of enchanted rings: a ring of spell turning and a ring of spell storing , which contained Evard's black tentacles , feeblemind , fly , invisibility , and wizard eye . He wore many other shiny rings, all with gems , on all his fingers.
