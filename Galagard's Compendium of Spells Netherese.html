Galagard's Compendium of Spells Netherese was a spellbook featuring in a vision sent by Mystra .

History

In the Year of the Awakening Wyrm, 767 DR , Elminster Aumar , a Chosen of Mystra , was shown a number of visions and temptations by Mystra . One of them was a room full of spellbooks, including one especially thick volume labeled Galagard's Compendium of Spells Netherese . It was unclear whether these specific books actually existed outside of the vision.
