Tholli Ironweaver was a retired Rashemaar warrior and a superb weaponsmith and armorer inhabiting Immilmar in Rashemen in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

Activities

Tholli was one of the most skilled weaponsmiths throughout Rashemen. In particular, his broadswords and chainmail were used all over the country and fetched impressive prices. Tholli was often commissioned by wychlaran to forge weapons that eventually would be enchanted. His shop was located in the north section of Immilmar.
