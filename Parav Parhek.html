Parav Parhek was an Amnian son of the Parhek dynasty.

Relationships

Parav was the youngest child of Prince Malek the Coinmage by a different mother than the twins Rhinn and Savra .

History

Parav grew up in Waterdeep as part of the exiled Parhek family. They were located by his cousin Prince Rohav , and Parav was killed in 1305 DR by his agents.
