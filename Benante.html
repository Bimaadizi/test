Benante the Butcher was a Netherese fighter who owned Benante's Butchery in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

Activities

Benante painlessly killed the livestock using a portal to the Negative Energy plane , before having Franchvail cleanse the carcasses. His wife, Acciajuoli , was quite wealthy and financially supported his business, allowing him to focus on making the best possible product and not worry about making sales.
