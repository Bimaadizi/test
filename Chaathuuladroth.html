Chaathuuladroth was a green dragon inhabiting the region of Okoth in the Year of the Zombie Lords, 681 DR .

Activities

For unknown reasons, the young Chaathuuladroth destroyed Nezram's tower and looted it for treasure, killing or driving off most of the wizard's descendants; the powerful Nezram himself was not living in the tower at this time. Chaathuuladroth then carried off most of Nezram's treasures away somewhere to the west.
