Beastmaster's Chain was an enchanted amulet that allowed the wearer to befriend beasts .

Description

Beastmaster's amulet was decorated with a green gem set into the hand-shaped body. The gem was shaped like a cat 's slivered pupil.

Powers

Beastmaster's Chain allowed the wearer to cast the animal friendship spell.

History

In 1492 DR , Beastmaster's Chain was kept in worg pens of the Cult of the Absolute inside the Shattered Sanctum . The goblins of the cult used the amulet to train worgs they used in raids on settlements of the Western Heartlands .
