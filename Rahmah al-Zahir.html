Sitt Rahmah al-Zahir was the Grand Caliph 's favorite maternal aunt in Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

Rahmah was a member of the Magical Legion but spent too much time in politics to fully develop her power.

Her magical training was just an elaborate ruse to disguise her true calling as the Grandmother of the Soft Whisper . Rahmah and the Soft Whisper made sure that other Zakharan rulers who did not work to further the Grand Caliph's policies were visited by Holy slayers .

Possessions

Much of her outward magical prowess was courtesy of a ring of spell storing supplied by the Grand Vizier .

Relationships

Rahmah was lifelong friends with the Grand Vizier Alyana al-Azzazi.

History

Rahmah studied at the magical university at Qadib .
