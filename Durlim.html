Durlim was one of the bandits under the Iron Fox in the mid–8 th century DR .

Possessions

The men of the Iron Fox wore light armor and brought no lances or horses.

History

When Elminster Aumar , a Chosen of Mystra , carried out a scouring of the bandits in Buckralam's Starn , Durlim remained with his boss, the Iron Fox, until moments before his death at Fox Tower . They were ambushed at the mess hall, where Elminster almost struck him down with a crossbow quarrel to the face moments after he was ordered to the gallery; as a veteran, Durlim managed to block the quarrel with his gauntlet. He was then sent down the gallery. The Iron Fox died while trying to regroup with the man, without learning what became of him.
