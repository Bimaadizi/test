Tamaeril Bladesemmer was a noble and Masked Lord of Waterdeep in the mid-14 th century DR .

Appearance

As of 1355 DR , she was gray-haired.

Possessions

She carried a dagger and an enchanted amulet.

Abilities

Even in the instants before her death, she employed spells that allowed her to open a door, drop a drinking vessel on a foe's head, a cantrip to pull on the rug beneath another's feet, and finally, she cast a spell for a message of hers to be sent directly to the mind of Mirt the Moneylender , almost certainly sending .

Relationships

She was a widow, who owned a dog named Borgul , a songbird, and at least one cat. Elminster Aumar had tutored her at one point.

History

Tamaeril Bladesemmer had been a Masked Lord for nine years, and had adventured with Mirt at some point in the past as of early Flamerule in 1355 DR . She was the oldest of all the Lords, indicating she was older than Mirt or Durnan . One night in that month she was attacked by Amril Zoar , who also killed her dog and her songbird. She managed to cast a spell warning Mirt that her slayer was a mage who claimed to be after the Lords of Waterdeep.
