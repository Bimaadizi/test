Ladyhouse Falls was a waterfall with an eponymous temple to Mystra in the 3 rd century DR .

Description

The roar of water in the falls was audible from a distance.

Geography

Ladyhouse Falls was fed by the River Dessarin . It was broadly considered the southern border of the Mlembryn lands.

History

By the 14 th century DR , the tributary of the Dessarin River that fed Ladyhouse Falls had vanished.
