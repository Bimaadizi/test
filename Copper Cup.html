The Copper Cup was a festhall located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building compound was four and five stories tall.

Location

The Copper Cup was located in the southeast corner of Waterdeep's Dock Ward on Smuggler's Run and bordering the Southyard near the High Road .

Activities

The Copper Cup hosted annual guild-sponsored galas celebrating Fleetswake during the last days of Ches .
