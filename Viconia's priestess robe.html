Viconia's priestess robe was a powerfully enchanted Sharran robe worn by Mother Superior Viconia De Vir of the Cloister of Sombre Embrace in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Viconia's robes were black like Shar 's heart but decorated with silver metallic motifs, including a stylized shape of a spider on her open décolleté and dark golden patterns on the fabric.

Powers

The robes hummed with dark enchantments that dulled the senses, thoughts, and feelings of those around them. If one listened to the hum for a prolonged period of time, one could almost hear the chittering of a thousand spiders being devoured by a hungry darkness. It possessed a +1 protective enchantment, and Shar's umbrae magic wrapped the wearer in darkness, making it easier for them to dissolve into the shadows. It also possessed a permanent Sharran veil enchantment that functioned similarly to the shield of faith spell .

History

Viconia De Vir came to possess her unique robes sometime before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , when she was always seen wearing the Sharran garb whilst surrounded by the faithful of the Cloister of Sombre Embrace.
