Prestar's Furniture was a furniture shop in the city of Waterdeep in the 14 th century DR , up until around 1368 DR . It continued to be in a ruined state by 1372 DR .

Location

This shop was located along Carters' Way , in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This building, at least in its ruined state, stood a single story tall.

Services

When it was operational, this shop sold a variety of furniture.
