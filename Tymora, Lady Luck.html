Tymora, Lady Luck was a Tymoran book dedicated to Tyche 's Fair-Haired Daughter, the deity of luck, penned sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

Content

One of the book's chapters listed oaths and sayings that invoked the goddess' favor. Come of them were:﻿﻿﻿ "By Lady Lucks's smile," "turntable Tymora, I invoke thee,"﻿ "by the rolling of my dice , please let these results be nice," "spin, spin, oh fickle fate wheel!" and "Lady of the Diamond-Glitter Dice, Mistress of Good Fortune, Sovereign of Luck, please have mercy on me, for fuck's sake."

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a copy of Tymora, Lady Luck could be found in the library of the Stormshore Tabernacle , a non-denominational shrine in the city of Baldur's Gate .
