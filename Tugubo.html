Tugubo was a craftsman skilled in making lances in the nation of T'u Lung around Shou Year 2310 (1060 DR) .

History

Tugubo lived in the great city of Balanzia , a century prior to its destruction. There they operated a shop in the western reaches of the city, just across the road from the home of the wu jen Nendra .
