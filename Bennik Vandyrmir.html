Bennik Vandyrmir was an employee of Sa'Sani 's Mercantile House that operated in the village of West Harbor in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Bennik was a serious man with short blonde hair, pale skin, blue eyes, and chiseled face.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Bennik was one of the local merchants who operated on the Sword Coast North . When merchant princess Sa'Sani expanded her trading coaster, Bennik Vandyrmir was among her hires.
