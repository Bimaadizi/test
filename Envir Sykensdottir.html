Envir Sykensdottir was a member of the Uthgardt Gray Wolf tribe during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Envir was the daughter of Syken Nightblaze , former Great Chief of the Gray Wolf tribe.

History

Sometime around the Year of the Iron Dwarf's Vengeance, 1485 DR , Great Chief Nightblaze was slain by adventurers that hailed from the city of Neverwinter . Envir planned her revenge and sought to assault the city and regain the tribal authority that was taken from her father.
