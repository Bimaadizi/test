Osk Thunderhale was a sea captain sometime after the War of the Silver Marches .

Relationship

Osk was at the employ of Lord Dagult Neverember .

Activities

Osk sailed with his ship the " Moon Maiden " from Waterdeep to Luskan and back. Often he was at the Hanging Lantern festhall in Dock Ward of Waterdeep . He was a skilled fiddler and loved playing his fiddle to amuse his crew and the others festhall patrons'.

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR Osk saw on sea debris of the " Dancing Wave " ship and a giant warship nearby. Possibly he was contacted by an adventuring party hired by Duvessa Shane .
