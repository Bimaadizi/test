History

Tarth was a guard for Orthil Voldovan's caravan in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , when it was joined by Shandril Shessair and Narm Tamaraith . During the ride from Scornubel to Triel , he was briefly assigned to helping Mulgar quiet a horse somehow or else kill it. He was also assigned briefly to keep watch on Shandril, where he helped quiet Sarlor , a fellow guard, after the latter's outburst at Shandril.
