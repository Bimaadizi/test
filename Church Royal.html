The Churches Royal were the few religious organizations officially supported by the monarch of Cormyr or by members of their immediate family.

Members

Recorded Churches Royal included the Church of Tempus and Church of Tyr , which were supported by King Azoun Obarskyr IV .

The nomination of the Church of Chauntea as a Church Royal by Crown Princess Tanalasta Obarskyr moments before her death in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , created such an uproar that no churches were granted that privilege ever since.
