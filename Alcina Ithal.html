Alcina Ithal was the second queen of Tethyr .

Relationships

Queen Alcina was the consort of King Silvam of the Ithal Dynasty . They were the parents of at least three children. Their second son was named Nishan , and their third son was named Tyrom . Their first son did not survive to take the throne.

History

Alcina gave birth to Nishan in −179 DR . He would grow up to succeed her husband the king.
