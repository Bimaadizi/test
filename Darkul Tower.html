Darkul Tower was the residence of Nusair Darkul in Bezantur . The tower had at least one bedroom, a room devoted to a crystal ball , and a library. The latter was perhaps the largest room in the tower, with a fireplace, a rug, an oaken table, and red tapestries covering every wall that had no bookshelves.
