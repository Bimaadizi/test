The pendant of hardy presence was a magical pendant found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Description

The pendant was a gaudy design that resembled dwarven craftsmanship, and had a strong presence no matter what.

Powers

The pendant of hard presence gave the wearer incredible resilience and vitality, allowing them to shrug off otherwise lethal injuries and actively regenerating their wounds. It also made them generally much better at threatening and intimidating everything around them.

History

The pendant may have been found when the Elves of Cormanthor raided Castle Cormanthor and destroyed the lich Droic Orgul .
