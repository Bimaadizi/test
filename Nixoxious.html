Nixoxious was a kraken priestess and a member of the Cult of Talos in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Nixoxious was a tiefling who wore blue robes.

History

Circa 1491 DR , Nixoxious led a group of berserkers from the Cult of Talos in an attack on the recently resettled town of Leilon . Their assault sent much of the town's inhabitants fleeing north along the High Road . The cultists captured a number of fishers and soldiers to use as human sacrifices to water elementals that Nixoxious attempted to ritually conjure to destroy the town. However, a group of adventurers managed to defeat her after she began the ritual.
