The Dolphin Bridge was one of the three emblematic bridges of Neverwinter .

Description

The bridge was carved in the likeness of a dolphin .

Location

The Dolphin Bridge, alongside the other two bridges, radiated out of Castle Never , connecting it with the rest of the city. In the Dolphin's case, it connected the castle with the House of Knowledge .

History

During the Ruining of 1451 DR , the Dolphin Bridge was destroyed by the cataclysm.
