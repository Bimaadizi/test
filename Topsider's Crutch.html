Topsider's Crutch , also referred to as rings of infravision were enchanted ring 's that granted their wearers the ability to see in little or no light.

Powers

Topsider's Crutch gave its wearer the ability to see in the dark at the distance of 60 feet (18 meters), similarly to the effects of the infravision . The distance increased at night up to 300 feet (91 meters).

History

In the 14 th century DR , Topsider's Crutch rings were used by daring merchants that attempted to trade with the drow of the Great Rift .
