The gloves of the strong was a set of gloves found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The gloves gave the wearer physical strength, mental fortitude, and resistance to blows, as well as much greater speed of movement.

History

The gloves were found by the Elves of Cormanthor when they investigated the House of Gems , searching for the phylactery of Droic Orgul .
