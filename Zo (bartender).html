Zo was the proprietor of the Lord Cyric's Bane tavern in Priapurl on the Dragon Coast in 1372 DR .

Personality

Formerly a priest of Bane , Zo claimed he became disinterested in the politics of the Zhentarim and vowed to become an honest citizen instead.
