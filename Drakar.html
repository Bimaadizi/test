Drakar was a human man. He served as a cook to both the Red Brigade mercenaries and later the Vipers bandits .

History

Drakar was once the personal cook to Captain Cedric Ravajar of the Red Brigade during the 1360s DR . When Cedric disbanded the group in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Drakar followed him, and joined the new Vipers bandits. He cooked for them at the Vipers' Nest .

Description

Drakar was 6 feet (1.8 meters) tall and weighed 250 pounds (113 kilograms). In his younger days, he had a muscular and athletic build, but he let himself go, fattening as he became a gourmand. He had black eyes, long, blond hair that was unclean and unkempt, and a scruffy beard as he went weeks between shaving.
