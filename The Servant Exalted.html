The Servant Exalted was an upscale tavern in Suzail , Cormyr , in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , prior to the Council of the Dragon , the lords Broryn Windstag , Kathkote Dawntard , and Delasko Sornstern had offered much money at taverns frequented by servants of noblemen, including the Servant Exalted, for any enchanted largish hand axes belonging to a noble under the assumption such weapons were likely to be blueflame items .
