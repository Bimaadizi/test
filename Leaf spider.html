Leaf spiders were a species of venomous giant spiders found in the jungles of the Malatran Plateau .

Description

Leaf spiders were huge arachnids that reached 6 feet (1.8 meters) in length. They were hard to notice among the trees and used their natural leaf-like camouflage to sneak on and attack prey.

Abilities

Venomous, like many other species of giant arachnids, leaf spiders injected poison into their opponents with each successful bite, and each bite drained the opponents' strength. The effects were not permanent, and most could restore the damage done to their muscles with enough rest.

Ecology

Leaf spiders could be found inhabiting the canopies of the iticala trees, feeding on the oily sap secreted high above the ground. Leaf spiders' flesh was edible and delicious, also possessing some curative properties.

Some leaf spiders were known to be trained as exotic mounts. Notably, some of the creature markets in Neverwinter sold them to adventurers with enough coin in the late 15 th century DR .
