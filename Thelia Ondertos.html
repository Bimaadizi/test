Thelia Ondertos was a human woman who owned the Leaping Hart Tavern as of 1479 DR . She lived in the city of Harglast , in the Windrise Ports of Laerakond .

History

Thelia was an affable middle-aged woman who engaged in idle chit-chat for free, but required her patrons buy a round or two before she opened up to real gossip with strangers. She was good friends with Halagothra the Healer from Tarmalune .
