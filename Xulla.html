Xulla was a beholder member of the Zhentarim .

Relationships

Xulla was the longest and most loyal beholder ally of Fzoul Chembryl .

History

It was one of the many beholder exiled from Ooltul The beholder was chosen as a replacement for Xantriph as Voice of Bane in the Temple in the Sky but was not so efficient.

In later years, Xulla was nominated as Caravan Leader of the Zhentarim and responsible for the mercantile part of the organization.
