Thard was an human barbarian of the Sky Pony tribe and a member of the adventuring group, the Moor Runners .

Description

Thard was a hulking man who wore leather armor and wielded a greataxe. His greataxe was etched with the blades of Tempus emblazoned on a swift steed. His voice was rough and deep.

Relationships

Thard was the companion and lover of Inri .

History

Inri was a member of the Moor Runners for years. In 1364 DR he was killed by the Red dragon Tharas'kalagram 's fiery breath.
