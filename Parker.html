Parker was a druid and slave of Baeloth Barrityl .

Description

Parker used his druidic powers to gather groups of spiders and giant spiders . The druid slave was used in the Black Pits arena to fight contestants. He fought with a scimitar and had access to a few spells .

Possessions

The druid owned a fair amount of magical equipment: the Protector of the Second set of armor , the Edventar's Gift ring , a periapt of proof against poison , and a golden girdle of Urnst .

History

He was killed by the Champion of Baeloth's Pits and their party in the Black Pits arena.
