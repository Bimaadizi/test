TypeRune

Sleep was a type of giant rune that functioned similarly to the sleep spell .

History

In 1366 DR , seeking a way to delay the interference of Morten and the earls of Hartsvale in his group's rescue of Princess Brianna , the runecaster Basil suggested and began work on a sleep rune. However, he never got the chance to utilize the rune, as Morten and the earls had taken a different path.
