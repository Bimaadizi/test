Ithrythra Mornmist was an inhabitant of Myth Drannor in the 3 rd century DR .

Appearance

During her meeting with her friends at Satyrdance Pool , she wore spike-heeled, thigh-high silver boots, a lacy gown, and an undergown. Her hair had a sideswirl around a hand lyre, the symbol of her house.

Abilities

She helped weave a spell to turn wine into water.

Activities

She grew mushrooms in her cellar.

History

After Elminster Aumar was made armathor, Ithrythra shared her concerns for the stability of Cormanthyr with her friends at Satyrdance Pool . They agreed to try and nudge their husbands towards keeping Cormanthor together. Days after, she was confronted by Elandorr Waelvor , who insinuated he might have to do to her the same as he did to Symrustar Auglamyr .

In 261 DR , she attended to her husband, Nelaeryn Mornmist , when he was affected by the laying of the mythal , and then struck by the effects of the fighting during its laying.
