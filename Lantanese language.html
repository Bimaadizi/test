Lantanese , also known as Lantanna , was the official language of Lantan .

Speakers

Other regions where Lantanese was commonly spoken included the Nelanther Isles and Tethyr .

Script

Literate Lantanese speakers used the Draconic alphabet . Scholars suspected that the Lantanna people learned of this language through trade with early Halruaan or Nimbrali traders.

Related Languages

Some scholars classified Lantanna as an Imaskari language , but this was far from accepted by everyone.

Examples

Common female names in Lantanese were Avilda, Bersace, Charissa, Melsany, Phaerilda, Ravace, and Umbrasy. Male names might be Eberc, Fodoric, Koger, Lambrac, Midoc, Norbert, Samber, or Tibidoc. Common Lantanese surnames included Angalstrand, Decirc, Lamstrand, Se Korc, Se Langstra, and Se Milderic.
