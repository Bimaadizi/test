The Tower of the Talon was a fortress inhabited by a cell of the Cult of the Dragon circa 1479 DR . It was named in honor of Anabraxis the Black Talon. The tower was shaped to look like a huge dragon fang with two tips. There were numerous balconies in the tower for the convenience of dragons. The tower was organized as a military organization and had a large armory and barracks.
