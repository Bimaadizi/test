Amandrala of Calimport was one of the escort workers employed by the Blackthorn's Brew festhall in the coastal city of Velen in Tethyr in the late 14 th century DR .

History

By the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , Amandrala was famous for her grace and beauty. She was said to leave men "dry-mouthed" and the festhall's taproom frozen in silence as she glided across the room. Her beauty was mentioned in one of the guides penned by famed Volothamp Geddarm that covered the eateries of the Lands of Intrigue .
