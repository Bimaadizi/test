The Dacha of the Fish was an establishment located in Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Activities

The building was located in northeastern Almorel behind the Gospoda . It was responsible for taking care of the widows of fishermen.
