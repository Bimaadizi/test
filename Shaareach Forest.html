The Shaareach Forest was a small forest found in the southern Shaar near the border of Lapaliiya .

Geography

The forest was located in between the Channathwood in the east and the Misty Vale in the west. It was bordered to the north by the plains of the Shaareach and to the south by the River Channath and River Talar . The River Shaar flowed through the middles of the Shaareach Forest.
