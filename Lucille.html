Lucille was a powerful pit fiend , as well as the most trusted and loyal of Zariel 's generals.

Description

She was a fearsome and terrible sight to behold on the battlefield.

Possessions

Lucille wore a frightening helm of devil command and carried a massive battle standard of infernal power while commanding her troops.

Relationships

Among the forces she led were a squad of a dozen spined devils .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Lucille led Zariel's forces in the conflict to control the city of Elturel , after it was pulled down into Avernus atop the River Styx . Her forces engaged a horde of demons that erupted up from the river, and poured over into the ravaged streets of the fallen city. During the melee, she appeared to grant some mercy upon a group of adventurers who aided her by helping slay a group of hezrous .
