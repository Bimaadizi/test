Minda Obarskyr (born Minda Bleth ) was the sister of Mondar Bleth and settled on the land that would become known as Suzail in 15 DR . She had a secret relationship with Ondeth Obarskyr after his wife had left him that was eventually exposed.

Appearance

Minda had very dark hair and a pretty face. She was quite tall.

Relatives

Minda had a brother, Mondar Bleth , who was killed by elves in 16 DR . Her nephew was Mondar's youngest son, Arphoind Bleth .

History

Minda came to the new settlement on the future site of Suzail in 15 DR . She began a secret relationship with Ondeth Obarskyr , and this saved her life in 16 DR. She was in bed with him instead of at home when the elves raided her farmstead and killed its occupants, including her brother Mondar. She later married Ondeth.
