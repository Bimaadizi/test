Boroland Nevilard was a wandering bard of the late mid-13 th century DR .

Activities

Boroland Nevilard passed through Innarlith once a season on the way between the Border Kingdoms and the Vilhon Reach , never spending more than a few days at a time, but spending them with Talatha Vaerovree , a local sorceress.

Relationships

He was a fling of Talatha Vaerovree's in the late 14 th century DR.

History

His visits to Talatha began when he mistook her home for an abandoned ruin, and caught sight of her bathing in a rainwater basin. She approached him then.

Rumors & Legends

Talatha Vaerovree suspected him of being a spy and information broker for wizards in the Vilhon.
