The Palace of the Karanoks was the home of the ruler, typically a member of House Karanok , of the city of Luthcheq in Chessenta during the midâ€“14 th century DR .

Location

The Palace of the Karanoks was located in eastern Luthcheq along the exterior wall of the city near the east gate.

Description

The entire structure was separated from the rest of the city by a wall and was accessed via a single gate near its center.

Inhabitants

Shala Karanok ruled Luthcheq and lived within the Palace in the late 15 th century DR.
