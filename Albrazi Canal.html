The Albrazi Canal was a prominent canal located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The canal branched west from the Great Canal in the Pyraculum near the center of the city, turned southwest at the borders of the Rookery and Iskalat , and continued through Iskalat until it emptied into the north side of the harbor. It passed under the Street of the Craftsmen and Street of the Vine along the way.

Description

As with all canals within the City of Brass, the Albrazi Canal was filled with lava from the Sea of Fire as well as oil which could be set alight.
