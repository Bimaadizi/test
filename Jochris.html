Jochris was the headmaster of the Neverwinter Academy of Magic around 1374 DR .

History

After Qara 's fight with Ashni , with the Kalach-Cha and companions, Jochris would approach Qara. Seeing his students being hurt by Qara, Jochris summoned Sydney Natale to his quarters, as he wanted revenge on Qara. After a while, Jochris gave Sydney some thread from Qara's robe and some of her hair, as well as his blood, to power a ritual. Sydney summoned an animus elemental , which would track Qara and attack her.
