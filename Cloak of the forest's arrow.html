The cloak of the forest's arrow was a woodsman's cloak found during the reclamation of Myth Drannor in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

It was a dark gray cloak decorated with bright flowers.

Powers

The cloak aided archers in particular, helping them shoot faster and knock their foes off-balance.

History

The cloak was once used by the elven rangers of pre- Weeping War Myth Drannor as they patrolled the Cormanthor for threats. It was eventually found after the Elves of Cormanthor investigated the abandoned Throne of Thought in the ruins.
