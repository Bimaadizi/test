Thaxll'ssillyia was a shadow dragon brought from the Plane of Shadow by the Shade Lord during his attempt to extend his influence onto the Material Plane .

History

Prior to 1369 DR , Thaxll'ssillyia was left to guard the entrance into the Shade Lord's inner lair in the old temple to Amaunator near the Umar Hills .

Gorion's Ward and party may have been given a wardstone by the prophetess, Amauna to bypass the dragon. Thaxll'ssillyia's hide, being that of a shadow dragon, could be made into a powerful set of light armor .
