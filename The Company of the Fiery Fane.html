The Company of the Fiery Fane was an adventuring company in the early 14 th century DR .

The group was founded by Szuszalan, the Warrior Maid, who was formerly of the famous all-female adventuring company known as the Circle of Steel . She wrote of their exploits in her biography, Walking on a Swordblade , published in the Year of the Dragon, 1352 DR .
