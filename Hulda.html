Hulda was a stone giant who helped Blagothkus in managing the flying Skyreach Castle .

Personality

Hulda was very concerned about Blagothkus' plan to ally with dragons and the Cult of the Dragon . As a companion of Wigluf, she supported him in all his decisions.

History

Hulda and her companion Wigluf were guests at Skyreach Castle. Hulda protected Wigluf when he tried to use a magic cauldron to interrogate the giant pantheon about Blagothkus's plan.
