Embro Cymrych I was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the early 7 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High Queen Miranda the Fair . His successor was his son, Embro II .

History

Embro I assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of Old Bones, 609 DR , following the death of his mother.

He ruled for only five years before succumbing to injuries he received in a horse -riding accident in the Year of the Shattered Scepter, 614 DR .
