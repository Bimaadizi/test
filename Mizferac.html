Personality

Mizferac was described as stupid by the imp who'd provided his name to Cadderly.

History

Mizferac was summoned by Cadderly in the Year of the Staff, 1366 DR to help determine the whereabouts of Jarlaxle . He did so on a regular basis for a tenday until he brought news of Jarlaxle, Artemis Entreri , and Crenshinibon coming to see the priest. Immediately after, he was banished for a hundred years by Cadderly after threatening Cadderly's family.

However, he was later summoned again by Cadderly, rescinding the banishment.
