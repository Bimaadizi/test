Jevan was a necromancer in the service of House Szind during the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the 1480s DR .

Description

Jevan had his white hair styled in a mohawk and had facial tattoos.

Abilities

Jevan was an enthusastic necromancer and knew powerful spells including acidball , ball lightning , black dragon bolt , horrid wilting , mass hold person , necrotic ray , and negative energy burst .

History

During the Battle for Eveningstar , Jevan was stationed with drow forces from House Avithoul and House Dun'Robar in the razed ruins of western Eveningstar . When the Cormyrean forces counter-attacked, they found Jevan beneath the ruins of the House of the Morning . Despite the arrogant necromancer's pet zombies , he too was ultimately slain in the course of the battle.
