The Tam family of Thay was nearly as old as the nation itself, and greatly influenced its course of growth and direction throughout the years. Their seat of power was known as the Necropolis .

Activities

Since the 11 th century DR , the Tam family has overseen operations of the undead servants who moved goods across the long portage . They profited well from transporting shipments arriving from Lake Mulsantir along the River Thay , up the First Escarpment and unloading them on vessels that sailed the waterways atop the Thayan Plateau .

History

Throughout the family's history in the magocratic nation they have had only three heads of household. The most recent leader of the family was Szass Tam , who has existed on Toril since the early 12 th century. He has accomplished more than anyone within the family, and perhaps all of Thay, having incited the War of the Zulkirs and ascended as the nation's sole ruler and High Regent for over a century.
