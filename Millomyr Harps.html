Millomyr Harps was an instrument shop specializing in harps in the city of Waterdeep in the 14 th century DR .

Location

This shop stood on the eastern side of Golden Serpent Street , in the North Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This building stood two stories tall.

Structure

Interior

Inside the building was a secret room that acted as the exit point to one of the teleporters in Harpers' Hold .

Activities

Owned by a member of the Harpers , Millomyr Harps acted as a safe-house for that organization in addition to operating as a shop.
