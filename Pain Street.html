Pain Street was a small road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

Pain Street begun and ended on Cobble Road , turning along its midpoint to reconnect with the larger road. Pain Street was located in west Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter . Fire Way connected with the eastern arm of Pain Street.

Notable Locations

The most prominent buildings found along Pain Street were the Tower of Pain Exalted , a temple to Loviatar , and the Whipwalker's Cloak , a tavern that mostly catered to Loviatar's faithful.
