The Starwater bridge was the bridge in Eveningstar where the High Road (called the Main Road in Eveningstar ) crossed the Starwater River .

Description

The bridge was wide and made of stone. It was located east of the town, near the Welcoming Hand Inn .

On summer evenings, many caravan masters could be found smoking pipes or fishing from the top of the bridge.
