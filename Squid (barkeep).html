Squid was a half-elven barkeep at the Stable Quiver .

Description

Squid's namesake was a squid -shaped birthmark on his face.

Relationships

Squid was acquainted with Oppal De Scart , a doppelganger agent of Acquisitions Incorporated , and was aware of her disregard of the law. He did, however, recognize that she would never commit murder.
