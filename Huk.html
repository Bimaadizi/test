Huk was a goblin warrior that served Dror Ragzlin and the Cult of the Absolute in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

He followed Dror Ragzlin's every word and did not like others interrupting the boss' speeches.

History

Huk was present when the boss Dror Ragzlin attempted to speak with a dead mind flayer brought into their sanctuary in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . The mighty hobgoblin leader was attempting to extract information crucial to the Absolute and her plans for the Sword Coast .
