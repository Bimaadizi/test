The Company of the Red Raven were a Cormyrean adventuring company that operated in the mid-14 th century DR . On 12 Ches of the Year of the Harp, 1355 DR , they were burned to death by the Great Red Wyrm Uryiax . Their service was commemorated by a memorial plaque in the office of Registrant General Lord Partic Thistle in the Royal Court of Suzail .
