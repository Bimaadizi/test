A wand of sleep was a magic item that forced targets to fall asleep.

Description

These wands were made from green willow vines from the Ganathwood . They became as hard as stone once enchanted.

Powers

Once the command word was uttered, the wand would emit a beam of energy 80 feet (24 meters) long that was 10 feet (3 meters) wide at the beginning and widened to 30 feet (9.1 meters) at the end. Anything caught within this beam would fall asleep for 3–12 minutes.
