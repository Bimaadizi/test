The Cavern of the Everlasting was located beneath the fortress of Sarahin in the Haunted Lands .

Description

The Cavern of the Everlasting was a natural cavern. The cavern housed five statues depicting heroes of the Everlasting . Tables and benches were used during meetings.

Activities

This location was often used when the holy slayers of the Everlasting wished to hold a meeting during the heat of the day. Located underground, this cool chamber was a comfortable solution.

Festivals or celebrations, usually as the result of the death of an enemy, were held here.
