Laeral's anklets were a unique set of enchanted jewelry that surrounded the wearer with a field of force.

Description

Tiny padlocks adorned the anklets .

Powers

The anklets were imbued with the everbright armor quality , ensuring they would never tarnish while also granting the wearer some resistance to acid . The anklets protected the wearer from harm by creating an invisible field of force around them. The magic only protected the wearer if both anklets were worn at the same time.

Notable Owners

This item was created and worn by Laeral Silverhand .

Rumors & Legends

The presence of the padlocks lead many to suspect that Laeral was Khelben Arunsun 's unwilling prisoner. Interestingly, Laeral never denied the rumors, leading many who sought her favor to challenge Khelben.
