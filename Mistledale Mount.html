RegionShadowdale , Dalelands

Mistledale Mount was a large hill southwest of the village of Shadowdale . The mountain received its name from the local legend that on a clear day you could see the smoke from fires in distant Mistledale .

Description

Sporadic clearings of trees for lumber had taken place on Mistledale Mount.
