Anariel was an individual who gave the elven longbow Taulmaril and a quiver of Anariel to Clan Battlehammer . She had the title "Sister of Faerûn ".

History

Anariel once gifted the bow Taulmaril and a quiver of endless arrows to Clan Battlehammer at Mithril Hall .

In 1356 DR , the Companions of the Hall explored the ruins of Mithril Hall and Regis discovered Taulmaril and the quiver in a crack in a stone wall. They gave both quiver and bow to Catti-brie , and she went on to use both in many battles. The quiver was named the "quiver of Anariel" after Anariel.
