Raanaghaun Cormrael was a member of the Dark Diviners of Windsong Tower in the 7 th century DR .

History

He was named for a famous Netherese priest of Shar , long since deceased.

After the theft of the Leaves of One Night and the Book of the Black from the Dark Diviners in the Year of the Sundered Crypt, 684 DR , he left the tower and returned to the Fane of Shadows to learn more about the latter tome. He never returned.

Relations

He was the brother of Shar Cormrael .
