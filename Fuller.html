Fuller was one of the four Watchers of Candlekeep , as of the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .

Abilities

He was particularly fond of mead and known to over-indulge during celebrations in the Watchers' barracks.

Possessions

Among his possessions, Fuller owned an enchanted dagger that once belonged to his father. According to Fuller it was once used to kill a hobgoblin in a single strike.

Relationships

Fuller served alongside his fellow Watchers Hull , the Keeper of the Portal, and two other individuals.
