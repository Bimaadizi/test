The amulet of true strikes was an enchanted amulet worn by rangers around Waterdeep as of the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

The wearer of an amulet of true strikes was more deadly in battle. When the amulet activated, the wearer's attacks struck truer and more powerfully, akin to being under the effect of a true strike spell. However, the amulet did not always activate reliably.
