The Sly Fox was an inn located in Zelbross .

Services

It provided good services such as soft beds, hot turnip pie, and a hearth, as well as stores.

Description

The inn was rather popular, as Zelbross had a good view of the Southwood across the river, and had a sign carved in the shape of a fox 's head.

History

It was later destroyed, either pillaged by goblins or burned down by brigands, the sources are not particularly clear. Later, a boy known as Orok used the ruin of the inn as a hideout.
