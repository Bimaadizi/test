Umber hulk plate was a type of heavy armor crafted using umber hulk chitin and imbued with the creature's natural magic.

Powers

These plate mails were created through reasonably easy shaping and tampering of umber hulk's shell to a humanoid form. Duergar smiths learned the process of curing and reinforcing these dark sets of plates. Umber hulk plates possessed a +2 enchantment but were somewhat vulnerable to slashing weapons.

History

In the Year of the Cold Soul, 1281 DR , umber hulk plates were crafted by a svirfneblin named Dirty Llew who inhabited a deep gnome camp in the depths of the Lower Dorn's Deep in the Spine of the World .
