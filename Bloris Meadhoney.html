Bloris Meadhoney was an employee of the Counting House and columnist for Baldur's Mouth in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Bloris Meadhoney penned articles for the Baldur's Mouth Gazette circa Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . The "Money-Saving Advice" column was published during the Absolute crisis in Baldur's Gate .
