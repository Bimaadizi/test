Sudbury was a dwarven advisor to Dagult Neverember in Neverwinter during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Sudbury helped to coordinate the efforts of adventurers in accordance with Lord Neverember's goals for Neverwinter, such as by directing them to job boards and bounties posted by Neverember.

Relationships

Sudbury was a member of the Harpers . He worked alongside the likes of Shay Morningstar , Drijm Stoneaxe , and Silwen Wiltfen in his service to Lord Neverember.

He was familiar with the kobold Rhix .
