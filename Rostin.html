Rostin was a scrivener and spy for the Zhentarim in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Abilities

He was most notable for his literacy.

Activities

He was a scrivener penning letters, contracts, and such, who walked regularly between Ashabenford , Tilverton , and Shadowdale . He was also a Zhentarim spy.

Relationships

Rostin's superior among the Zhentarim was Samshin .

History

Rostin was one of the Zhentarim that dropped in on Maervidal Iloster , a fellow scribe of his, to invite him to the party at Warmfires House in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .
