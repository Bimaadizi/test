Duver Rillyn was a member of the Rillyn patriar family of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

At some point in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Duver Rillyn became afflicted by lycanthropy , the affliction that led him into the service of the Kozakuran vampire Cazador Szarr , the lord of the Szarr Palace in Baldur's Gate. Along with Gheris Hhune , Blaise Hhune , and other werewolves , Duver massacred a party full of influential guests at the Szarr Palace, shortly before Cazador attempted to perform the ascension ritual.
