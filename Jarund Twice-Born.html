Jarund Twice-Born was a Reghedmen human warrior and the son of King Jarund Elkhardt from the Tribe of the Elk in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Narthex Murders, 1482 DR , Jarund Twice-Born was slain by a bear . Since then, some of the tribe's members nicknamed his father, Jarund Elkhardt, "the Elder", but never mentioned it in his presence. The king struggled to name an heir to his rule.
