Ruke Diggot was a human scholar from Mistledale . He was tall with curly red hair. During a trip to the forest of Cormanthor to study butterflies, he met the half-elf ranger Lyra Sunrose and they married a few months later, having eleven children together, all of whom still lived in Cormanthor by the 1360s DR

Ruke was killed by a giant frog when it jumped out from an alder thicket and swallowed him whole.
