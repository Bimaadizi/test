The Sons of Winter was a primordial cult dedicated to Cirotralech that operated out of the Fimbrul in Laerakond .

History

In 1480 DR , the Sons of Winter were founded by the scholar Rathloff , who had been corrupted by Pyrultimus , in order to awaken the primordial Cirotralech.

To fulfill their goals, Rathloff and his followers raided the ruins of Darkturret in Gontal , searching for powerful magic items . This alerted Queen Imreera Hethkantan of their existence, and the Queen quickly assembled a group of adventurers to deal with the cultists.

The adventurers faced the Sons in Fimbrul, where they were conducting foul rituals to unleash Cirotralech, having previously released the Bride , Cirotralech's consort. The adventurers were able to stop the ritual and kill all the Sons, but is uncertain whether or not they could stop them unleashing Cirotralech.

Membership

The Sons of Winter was composed of madmen who believed in the primordials.
