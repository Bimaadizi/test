Krak al-Sajjaan was a qal'at found in the Cities of the Ancients region of east Zakhara in 1367 DR .

Geography

This fortress was found toward the southern region of al-Sayaj Mountains . It stood approximately 60 mi (97 km) slightly northwest of Kadarasto on the outskirts of the jungle.

Activities

Krak al-Sajjaan had a commanding view of the grasslands around the southern arm of the Nogaro River . It was unoccupied as of 1367 DR.
