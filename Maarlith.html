Maarlith was a small town located in the nation of Dambrath in the Shining South during the 14 th century DR .

Geography

Maarlith was located in central Dambrath in the southern reaches of the Rialuil Plain on the northern coast of the Bay of Dancing Dolphins . The town lay at the end of the Trader's Road southwest of Cathyr .
