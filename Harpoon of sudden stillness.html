The harpoon of sudden stillness was a unique enchanted harpoon crafted by Laeral Silverhand for Joppa in the late 15 th century DR . It was one of many pieces of so-called " Silverhand gear " crafted for adventurers who helped to protect Waterdeep .

Description

Silverhand crafted this broad-bladed spear to react to and recognize Joppa's commanding presence.

Powers

The harpoon of sudden stillness could be hurled at a foe, which it would attempt to impale, paralyze, and then bring to Joppa's side. This left the foe stunned and unable to move.
