The Hag language , widely referred to as Annis by scholars across multiple Prime Material worlds, was a shared language among hags . Its alternative name stemmed from a belief that it had first originated among annis hags before becoming widely adopted by the other hag subraces.

History

Some scholars believed that hags were descended from ogres , or at the very least that annis hags and ogre mages were somehow related. As a consequence, it was believed by some scholars that Annis was an offshoot of Ogrish and that those knowledge in the latter could potentially understand bits and pieces of conversations conducted in Annis.
