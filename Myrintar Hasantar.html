Myrintar Hasantar was a writer and a Sage of Suzail , the capital city of Cormyr , who was active in the late 12 th and early 14 th centuries DR .

History

Myrintar penned Things a Knight Should Know in the Year of the Mace, 1307 DR . The book was dedicated to the sage's musings on the topics of adventuring and knighthood .
