Reptilian scale mail was a set of enchanted scale armor made out of an unfortunate lizardfolk 's hide.

Powers

Reptilian scale mail was a set of tanned and cured scale armor crafted out of a lizardfolk's hide. The armor was masterfully made and gave the wearer improved defense only slightly hindering their ability to cast spells .

History

A set of reptilian scale mail could be found in a locked and trapped chest within the Dwarven Dungeons ' Main Halls in 1369 DR . The armor was crafted by one of the tribes of orcs or orogs who feuded with the sorcerer Malgi Hi 's lizardfolk tribe in the ruins of Myth Drannor .
