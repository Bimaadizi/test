Transtra was one of the leaders of the Iron Ring and a slaver of Skullport around 1370 DR .

History

Transtra was a powerful crime lord and slaver in Skullport who associated herself with the Iron Ring Consortium. Very few knew about her true nature as a lamia noble , as she used many disguises and illusions. Very secretly—and under a geas effect—she was being tutored in the magical arts by Halaster himself.

Relationship

Transtra's only companion was her pet behir Uliss , that she had come across in Undermountain and charmed to obey her.

She was also a business associate of, and informant for, Mirt the Moneylender.

Business

Transtra was the owner of the House of the Long Slow Kiss , a 4-story festhall in Skullport. She also owned at least one warehouse, and had business dealings in smuggling, gambling, thievery, slavery, and moneylending. She was also known to hire adventurers to investigate rumors in Undermountain . She also owned a warehouse in the Lower Port .
