The Bugbear's Cave was a tavern in Hillsfar famous for its terrible food, Moonsea -watered ale , and excellent gossip.

Services

Despite the poor quality of the food and drink, both were quite expensive. It was generally recommended to sate your appetite elsewhere before coming to the Bugbear's Cave to gather useful information.
