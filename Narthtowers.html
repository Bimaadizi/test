Narthtowers was the abandoned wizard tower in the foothills of the northern border of Halruaa .

History

The tower was once owned by the Halruaan wizard Thongameir Halargoth , known also as " Stormspells ". After his death, no one in the region had figured out how to clear out the tower of Thongameir's vast collection of murderous version of a wide varieties of plantlife.
