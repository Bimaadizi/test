Location

It stood in an older part of Loudwater, on the bank of the Delimbiyr River and reached via a U-shaped passage with arches at its entrances, not far from Enalin's Shop of Adventurers' Supplies .

Description

A dusty and long-abandoned space, it was home to skeletal fighters , zombies , ghouls , wights , and wraiths .

History

In autumn in the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , undead emerged from the Mausoleum at nights and plagued the surrounding streets until adventurers , possibly the returning Heroes of Ascore , eliminated them. The thankful citizens returned to the area and gifted the heroes with a cloak of protection +2 .
