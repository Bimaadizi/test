Vingdavalac was a bronze dragon and a member of the Talons of Justice during the Rage of Dragons of 1373 DR .

History

In 1373 DR, during the final battle against Sammaster , Vingdavalac was one the Talons that were sent to attack Sammaster's citadel. He was one of the few Talons who survived the battle.
