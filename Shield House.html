Shield House was a fortified compound in the hinterlands of Cormyr , used by the Heralds of Faerûn .

Location

The House lay in the East Reaches of Cormyr northeast of Castle Crag and the Gnoll Peaks , just west of the joining of the Immerflow 's headwaters.

Inhabitants

In the 14 th century DR , the House was held by Naerlyn Phalphar of the Herald's Green Shield office.
