Iyrindyl was a Sharran faithful during the mid-8 th century DR .

Description

He was a bald man with purple and black robes. Also, he was the youngest of his peers.

Abilities

As a dreadspells, he wielded borrowed arcane power.

History

While the Sharran woodsman Uldus Blackram was leading the Sharran dreadspells to ruins within the Dead Place during the Chaos of the Art of the Year of the Awakening Wyrm, 767 DR , with a noose tied to his throat, they came across a hostile elven mage, Ilbryn Starym . Iyrindyl was struck down by a tree that fell due to the wild magic raised during the ensuing spell-battle; Femter Deldrannus , a fellow Dreadspell, confirmed he was dead after the fight.
