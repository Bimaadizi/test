Hardangheight was a large mountain range located in the kingdom of Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

Hardangheight was one of two mountain ranges found in Gnarhelm, and it was by the the largest. Hardangheight covered approximately 1,250 mi² (3,240 km²) of Gnarhelm and was the dominant land feature in the western region of the kingdom. The mountain touched the waters of the Sea of Moonshae in the north, and reached south to the shores of the Vasty Drink . Its eastern slopes plunged into Salmon Bay . The Ironflow flowed south from Hardangheight's southern slopes.
