Bryntarth I was the 56th Obarskyr monarch and king of Cormyr from Year of the Stricken Star, 875 DR until his death in the Year of the Calling Shrike, 897 DR . He was born in Year of the False Smile, 852 DR , the only son and heir of King Draxius the Neverdying . His first born son Galaghard III ascended the throne after him.
