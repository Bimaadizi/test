Orielen , also spelled Orielden , was a human hexblade and a Knight of Shar who served the Dark Lady 's temple, the House of Night , in Shadowdale in the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

As part of his duties as the House of Night's head guardsman, Orielen kept a watchful eye over the two mezzoloths that stood as sentinels at the temple's front entrance.

Relationships

Orielen served under High Priestess Esvele Greycastle . He led a small number of guardsmen, and was aided in his duties by two acolytes of the Sharran church, Telkin and Jorra .

History

He was involved in the Zhent invasion of Shadowdale in the Year of Risen Elfkin, 1375 DR .
