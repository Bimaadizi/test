Kaitlin's weed was a plant found growing within the prairie lands surrounding the Hill of Lost Souls , located slightly southwest of the southern border of the great desert Anauroch .

Uses

Often confused with another weed called dragon's breath , which was poisonous, Kaitlin's weed was used as a natural balm treatment for common sunburn.
