Velkora Ashenwell was a dwarf mason and acolyte of Moradin in Elturel in the late 15 th century DR .

History

After Elturel was transported to Avernus , first layer of the Nine Hells , in the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , many quakes struck the city and three dwarves—Velkora Ashenwell, Kartra Boulderstern , and Strovin Ironfist —became trapped in the rubble of a collapsed building. If they were lucky, they may have been rescued by passing adventurers.
