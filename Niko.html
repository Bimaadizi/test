Niko was a woman living the in Chu' Yuan province of Shou Lung in the mid-14 th century DR .

History

Niko lived in village of Tangan , in Keluh Valley , with her mother Shanshu .

In 1358 DR , she went to hunt with eight male friends. While out hunting they were all captured and killed by bakemono followers of the Earth Spider , with only Niko's life spared.

Niko agreed to become the Earth Spider's lieutenant in the mortal plane, leading the bakemono and goblin spider , but in actuality was playing a ruse in order to gain time to escape. Niko' s chance arrived when an adventuring party , hired by the Emperor to find General Goyat Nagumo , arrived in the region. She helped the adventurers to escape from Earth Spider's minions and eventually, with their support, all made it back safely to Tangan.
