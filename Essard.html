Essard was a guest at the Open Feast of 1479 DR held at Castle Obarskyr in Suzail , Cormyr .

Relationships

Per another guest, Essard had servants, some of which may have kin who worked at the palace.

History

At the Open Feast held at Castle Obarskyr in Mirtul 26 of the 1479 DR , Essard asked why there still was a haunted wing at the Royal Palace of Suzail , and why it hadn't been un-haunted with spells or faith. It was explained to them that it had been tried, but it just wouldn't take.
