Larrin was a thief in Athalantar and a member of the Velvet Hands in the early 3 rd century DR .

Relationships

He became a member of the Velvet Hands on Year of the Black Flame, 229 DR .

History

Larrin joined the Velvet Hands bands in 229 DR , becoming a founding member. Soon after, the Velvet Hands were targetted by an established band, the Moonclaws , killing a number of them. Larrin was involved on the ambush that the Velvet Hands put for the Moonclaws at the city gates in response. While fleeing from the ambush, the Velvet Hands were pursued by a gargoyle , which managed to slay him.

Rumors & Legends

Larrin thought Tassabra was the lover of Klaern Blaenbar . He was mistaken as to that.
