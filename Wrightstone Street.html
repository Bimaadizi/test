Wrightstone Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Wrightstone Street was located in the north section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . It ran east to west, originating on Shield Street and terminating at the junction of the Street of Glances and Staghunter's Way . Wrightstone Street was also intersected by Sul Street , Mendever Street , and Ivory Street .
