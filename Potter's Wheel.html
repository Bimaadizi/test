The Potter's Wheel was a small shop located in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The shop was located in the far northwest corner of Daggerford on Wall Street next to Wayfel's Smithy .

Activities

The shop was rather unimpressive. This was not an accident since the shop owner, Marcus , was an agent of the duke and couldn't care less about the success of the business.
