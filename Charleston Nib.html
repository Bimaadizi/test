Charleston Nib was an archaeologist who lived on the Sword Coast , for at least a short time, during the mid–14 th century DR . He believed in scholarly pursuits and believed his findings were best suited for display in Faerûnian museums.

Relationships

While Charleston seemed blissfully unaware, his "partner" Gallor was not happy with the altruistic nature of their operations.

History

In the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Charleston and his dig team were excavating the ruined village of a "primitive" race that had gone extinct ages before. While Charleston was confident about the purposes of the various rooms they dug out, little else was known about the extinct society. They were known to worship the Bedine / Netherese deity of Kozah .
