Gordis was one of the favorite hunting hounds of the Durinbold patriar family of Baldur's Gate , who died sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

Possessions

Gordis's sarcophagus was filled to the brim with toy balls and a single potion of speed .

History

By the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Chief Houndmaster Pol was long dead and interred within the Durinbold Mausoleum of the Lower City Graveyard , surrounded by the interred bodies of his favorite beasts, Gordis, Rocky , and Zina . The trainer and his three dogs ended up as ghosts that would attack anyone who dared to invade and disturb the Durinbold family tombs. Gordis's sarcophagus was inscribed with: "Fearless Gordis, who would chase any prey and helped bring in our greatest trophies."
