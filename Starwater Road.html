Starwater Road , known to locals as the Suzail Road or the Southrun , was central road in Cormyr that bisected the King's Forest .

Description

The road began in Immersea along the Wyvernwater , passing west through the King's Forest and crossing Starwater River before turning north to pass through Dhedluk and then terminating at Eveningstar where it touched the East Way .
