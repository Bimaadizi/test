Lactin was a human warrior from Maztica , and fought for his tribe as a jaguar warrior in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

He was raised to become a Jaguar Knight , and was capable of shapeshifting into a jaguar , but was also quite skilled at making stone-weaponry like the maca .

Personality

A fierce warrior, he observed the customs of his tribe carefully, and feared no kind of battle.

Possessions

Clad in armor made from jaguar skin, he wielded his maca and shield with great skill.
