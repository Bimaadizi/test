Pearl Citadel was the headquarters of the military forces of Sambral , one of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond .

Description

The Pearl Citadel was a titanic construction. Its most striking feature was the soaring Nacre Tower, built at the tip of the promontory and facing out over the sea. It was made out of mother-of-pearl, gleaming in iridescent beauty, especially at sunset.

The keep served as the headquarters of the Sentinels , the military forces of Sambral.

History

The keep was built millennia ago by the Dawn Titans , and was infused with their power to resist even the fiercest dragon attack.
