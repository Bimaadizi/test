St. Ulrach the Twice Martyred was a saint of the faith of Ilmater , the Crying God, who lived in the Delimbiyr Vale in the midâ€“14 th century DR .

History

He built the monastery that would be named for him, the Monastery of St. Ulrach .

Ulrach earned his appellation when he died, for the second time, in defense of the Delimbiyr Vale against marauding hobgoblins commanded by a cornugon circa 1363 DR .

With his sacrifice and sainthood, Ulrach's small monastic order swelled. A statue of him was erected in Tragedy's Grotto , a shrine to Ilmater in Loudwater .
