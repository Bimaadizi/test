The Men of Hammer Hall was an adventuring company hailing from Waterdeep that operated in the North during the midâ€”14 th century DR . They operated out of a wooden cabin, of their own construction, that they called Hammer Hall .

Activities

For several years the adventurers led expeditions into the nearby dwarven settlement that fell into ruin, the Halls of the Hammer .

Between their forays underground, the company regrouped and rested in their outpost. On a number of occasions they fought off fearsome creatures, such as trolls , orcs , and even bugbears .

History

Sometime before the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the Men of Hammer Hall ventured north and abandoned their namesake home.
