Ster Longhorn was a minotaur chieftain inside Undermountain in 1375 DR .

Activities

Ster lead his tribe inside the Maze level of the dungeon. He was allied with the drow of House Tanor'Thal in their war with the doppelgangers . He led his tribe in fishing, herding roth√© , and occasionally mining raw crystals from a nearby glowing cave.

History

Ster gained control of the tribe after his father became weak. From that point, however, he was busy defending his tribe against all aggressors.

Personality

Ster was smarter than most of his fellows, despite being cruel and murderous like them. He believed he had to prove himself worthy of the title of chieftain by fighting strong opponents. Ster used his magic Kyo crystals only if the enemy first used magic powers.
