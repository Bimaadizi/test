Rings of the wise were enchanted rings that fortified the wearer's wisdom.

Description

Rings of the wise were simple bands of precious metal with several impeded gemstones .

Powers

The rings possessed a simple enchantment that slightly improved the wearer's sagacity.

History

A ring of the wise was worn by a goblin commander Ghotrag who participated in the siege of Targos in Mirtul of the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR .
