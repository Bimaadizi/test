Activities

Bresk Thrattul once ran a clothing business named Five Fine Cloaks on Telvurr Street in Arabel.

History

After being condemned as a thief, Bresk began going by the name "Culdath".

During Gondegal's war, Culdath was declared an outlaw in a proclamation penned by the Sage Royal Alaphondar on Ches 11 , 1352 DR, and was wanted dead or alive by the crown.
