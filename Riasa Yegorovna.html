Riasa Yegorovna (pronounced REE-suh YAY-gore-OVE-nuh) was a girl in Mulmaster .

Personality

Riasa was an easily excitable, but a little unfocused girl. She was easily distracted and her attention easily drifted. She was a little clumsy, though stronger than she looked.

Family

Riasa was the daughter of Blade Pankin Yegorovna .

History

In 1491 DR , she became the disciple of Thylius Caramitru , a minor noble who claimed to have learned at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose the new meditation technique of the Yellow Breath . They gave a demonstration during a party hosted by Astrid Saj and Rosealine Culkin to honor Aleyd Burral , the Resurrected Hero of Phlan .
