Inrath Mirandor IV was the 15 th king of the Mirandor Dynasty of Impiltur . His reign was short-lived, lasting only two years during the mid–2 nd century DR .

Description

Inrath IV was significantly affected by the plague that claimed the life of his mother. This was possibly connected to his nickname of Inrath "Lackcrown" .

Relationships

He was the eldest son of Inrath Mirandor III . Inrath IV had two younger brothers, Pendarn and Doraven , the latter of whom was born to a different mother.

History

Inrath IV ruled over Impiltur for a mere two years before he was forcefully deposed by Doraven in the Year of the Lost Profit, 159 DR . While he managed to flee the royal city of Lyrabar , he was eventually hunted down and killed by his stepbrother.
