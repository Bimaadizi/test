Lost and Away was a traditional elfin fireside ballad sang by the Fair Folk of Faerûn .

Contents

History

This folksong was known to adventuress and a Harper Sharanralee of the 14 th century DR . This ballad in full was likely included in Sharanralee's recorded work – Ballads and Lore of One Dusty Road , that can be found in the library fortress of Candlekeep . The song's lyrics and sheet music were collected in the book simply titled Sword Coast Songbook , compiled by Silke Rosena sometime in the 14 th century DR, and made available in the city of Baldur's Gate as of the late 15 th century DR.
