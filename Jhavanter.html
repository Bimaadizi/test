Jhavanter was a wizard and the teacher of Garthond Shessair .

Possessions

Jhavanter dwelt at the Tower Tranquil .

Relationships

Garthond Shessair apprenticed under Jhavanter in his youth.

History

He was an enemy of the Cult of the Dragon . They had fought in Sembia and nearby locations multiple times.

The cult eventually destroyed him in battle and seized his tower.
