Ompahr Daergech was an extremely elderly gnome high priest of Baravar Cloakshadow in the Warrens beneath Waterdeep . In his youth, he studied sorcery with Ahghairon in Silverymoon . He was a friend of Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun and held a magical key on his behalf.
