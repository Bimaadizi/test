Imperceptor Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 th century DR .

Location

Located in the Inner Quarter in central Zhentil Keep, Imperceptor Street cut south from Elephstron Way near the Black Altar to end at the wall of the Common Quarter .

Notable Locations

A Zhentilar guardpost and barracks were located on Imperceptor Street, as was Edwin's Mercantile Traders .
