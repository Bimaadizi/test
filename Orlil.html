Orlil was a small island located off of the southeastern coast of Lantan proper on the Trackless Sea .

Geography

At its nearest point, Orlil only sat a few miles off of Lantan's coast. It was situated along the eastern edge of the Sambar Run that separated Lantan from Suj .

Description

Orlil was a small rocky island.
