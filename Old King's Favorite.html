The Old King's Favorite was a resplendent and glittering lounge that stood on the grand Promenade of the Cormyrean capital city of Suzail in 15 th century DR.

Location

The club stood on the Promenade, just west of Castle Obarskyr . The Court Close hooked around the Old King's Favorite's city block, starting to the west and crossing the Promenade to the east.

Services
