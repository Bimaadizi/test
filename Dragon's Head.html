The Dragon's Head was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The tavern stood two stories tall.

Location

The Dragon's Head was located in the far southwest section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward across from Ahghairon's Tower where Waterdeep Way and the Street of Silks joined. The rear of the tavern sat on both Cage Street and Shadow Alley .
