The Ring of Exalted Marrow was an enchanted ring imbued with powerful necromantic magics.

Description

The ring appeared to be a twisted band of metal that wrapped itself over the wearer's finger, topped with a likeness of a skull with two gem -inlaid eyes. When someone looked through the ring's loop, they could not see its other side – there was nothing but blackness.

Powers

The wearer of the ring could tap into the exhort the risen enchantment once per day, which acted like the command undead spell . Another magic of the ring that could be activated once per day was its ghoulish touch ; when activated, it allowed the wearer to slash at their opponents with deadly necrotic ghoul claws that were capable of paralyzing the targets.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Ring of Exalted Marrow was stored in General Ketheric Thorm 's chest atop of the Moonrise Towers in the Western Heartlands .
