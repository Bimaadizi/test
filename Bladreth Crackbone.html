Battlemaster Bladreth Crackbone was the High Priest of Tempus in Battlehall in the Foreign Quarter of Zhentil Keep in the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Activities

Bladreth ran the operations within Battlehall and was assisted by eight other priests . He saw a surge in followers flocking to Tempus' banner following the destruction of Zhentil Keep in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .
