Lulaku was a hunting spider katanga who served the Mother of a Thousand Young within the Fire Mountain of the Malatran Plateau in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Lulaku was a spider katanga who wielded daggers in combat.

Abilities

When faced to battle, Lulaku promptly tried to have her daggers enchanted by Fusamu , a spider katanga sorceress . When faced with overwhelming odds, Lulaku was likely to try and escape with her life.

Possessions

Lulaku was armed with bone daggers, a longspear , but her venomous bite was the katanga's most dangerous weapon.

History

In the late 14 th century DR , the Tribe of the Wise Ones became aware of a looming threat. The leader of the dreaded spider katanga , the Mother of a Thousand Young, was swollen with a thousand children ready to overflow the jungles. In preparation for the birthing, she initiated a ritual that sent deadly poisons into the rivers cascading off the Fire Mountain. Lulaku was posted to guard a stockade of captured heroes of the Living Jungle, enemies of the Mother of a Thousand Young. She was accompanied by Turanu and Fusamu. The spider katanga were guarding several Nubari humans and lizardfolk . Lulaku was loyal to Talunuku , a spider katanga who was secretly responsible for the poison and sought to become the new Mother of a Thousand Young.

Lulaku's fate was unknown; however, she and other guards were faced with heroes of the Living Jungle that were sent to rescue the prisoners and stop the Mother of a Thousand Young, likely resulting in her death.
