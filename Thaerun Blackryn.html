Thaerun Blackryn was a member of Zhentil Keep 's Council of Lords in 1368 DR .

Personality

Thaerun look more like Lord Chess ; he was a cunning man but short-sight in his decisions, always fearing retribution from more ambitious and powerful Lords.

History

Thaerun was an able merchant, from one of the most ancient families in the Council with one of his ancestors being a wizard companion of Zhentar . Thanks to his Council dealings he gained consistent wealth. He usually voted with the majority.
