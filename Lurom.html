Lurom was one of the Blades of the Old Man as of 1374 DR . On the night of Tarsakh 25 , he was on guard duty at the Fortress of the Old Man together with Ferluk . They were slain by the ranger Berun , unable to let out a signal. His corpse was later used to cross the Gallery of Stone Faces .
