The Knights of the Fire Drake was a military order of crusaders who worshiped Kossuth , the Tyrant Among Fire Elementals.

Activities

Members of the Knights of the Fire Drake protected the various sites that were holy to the Church of Kossuth , lead holy campaigns and personally protected the Eternal Flame .
