C. Balderk was an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate who died some years before the late 15 th century DR .

History

Balderk died at sea, sometime before the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , and was buried in the Lower City Graveyard , next to the Durinbold Mausoleum . The gravestone was etched with a single inscription: "C Balderk. Taken by Umberlee's rage. Recovered by Her grace." Balderk's grave may had been accidentally desecrated, and the body risen as a zombie by Nina Dortmell , a talented child with the gift , who was trying to resurrect her recently deceased brother.
