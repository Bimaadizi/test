In Faerûn , andar was the name for andalusite , a semi-precious stone usually found in streambeds as tumbled pebbles or crystals with an average diameter of 1 ⁄ 2 in (13 mm).

Description

Andar was a durable gemstone that could be facet-cut by a skilled lapidarist to produce translucent gems with a green-red or brown-red flash. A typical stone had a base value of 50 gp .

Powers

Combining powdered andar with a dragon scale ( gold , silver , or amethyst ) and boiling produced an alternate ingredient that could be added to the oily base of a potion of treasure finding .
