Mattrim "Threestrings" Mereg ( pronounced : /ˈ m ɑː t t r ɪ m ˈ m ɛər ɛ g g / MAT-trim MAIR-egg ) was a bard and Harper spy at the Yawning Portal in 1492 DR .

Activities

Mattrim hid his true elevated skills as a musician to maintain a low profile. He resided in the inn and used all his afternoons and evenings to spy upon Zhentarim agents and collect information about others possible troublemakers.

History

In 1492 DR , Mattrim befriended the barmaid Bonnie and, after discovering her secret, decided to help her.

Some time later, Mattrim hired adventurers to take a pouch of gold and deliver it to Cal'al Claddani , a female half-drow located in Skullport , in exchange for her saving a wounded Harper agent.
