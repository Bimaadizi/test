Mere was a servant of the Knights of the Black Sword who lived in Caer-Dineval as of the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The young tiefling wanted free from their life as a servant but was incredibly fearful of their captor. Mere garnered a fair amount of knowledge about the cultish Knights, including their ties to Levistus , the layout of their personal keep , as well as the group's hatred of the local duergar .

History

Circa the Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR , Mere was living in Bryn Shander when they were "found" by the fiendish cult leader Kadroth . The elder tiefling brought the young beggar to Caer-Dineval to act as his group's personal servant.
