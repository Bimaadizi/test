A whipsting was a special type of sword originating from southeast Faerûn , in a land south of Unther .

Description

It was very slender, enabling it to be used as a whip as well as a sword.

Notable Users

The famed mercenary Gayrlana , "Lady Bloodsword", specialized in this exotic weapon by 1356 DR .
