Dragonheir Clans was an Abeiran book that chronicled the story of the Dragonheirs of the Sword Lands , and recorded the genealogy of every Dragonheir clan that had existed.

History

In 1479 DR , the book came into possession of the adventuress Jada Marlserpent , the last survivor of Clan Marlserpent . Before that, the book was owned by a merchant of Harimmon , Shander Harimdor .
