Red Mazzie was the Court Wizard of Damara in the late 15th century DR . He was said to be most talented wizard in the kingdom.

History

In 1484 DR , in order to gain the favor of King Yarin Frostmantle , Red Mazzie captured the copper dragon sisters Tazmikella and Ilnezhara . However they were freed by Jarlaxle and Red Mazzie was without his prize when Captain Dreylil Andrus arrived to check his claim.

When King Frostmantle was assassinated, Red Mazzie devised a plan to cover it up as a natural death in order to ensure a peaceful transition of power to Frostmantle's widow, Queen Concettina Delcasio .
