The Ironseth family was a human Amnian mercantile family of Amnwater during the 14 th century DR .

Activities

The Ironseths focused on horse training and the hiring and training of mercenaries , and owned more than half of the land around Amnwater. Together with the Wheelarits and Yhliivasts , they managed to corner the economic and political power in Amnwater around 1320 DR and proceeded to effectively operate the town as an oligarchy.
