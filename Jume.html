Jume was a wizard of Baldur's Gate who lived several hundred years before the Time of Troubles , presumably in the 11th century DR . She was renowned for her contributions to the Art in the field of illusion spells , despite not being an illusionist herself.

Personality

She was known as having a good-humored nature and for being clever of wit, specifically in how she crafted illusions.

Works

Her personal spellbook , the arcanabula of Jume , was still educating arcane spellcasters as of 1359 DR . Among several, more common spells, it contained four that could be attributed to her creation.
