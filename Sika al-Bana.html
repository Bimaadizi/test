Sika al-Bana was a halfling who worked and cared for Hama Sayif within the Grand Bazaar of Huzuz in 1367 DR .

Activities

Sika played his pipes while Hama performed his splendid dances. He also took care off all haggling for Hama's customers.

Relationships

Sika's mate was Bara Minora .

Sika had been with Hama ever since he saved the lives of he and his mate in Al-Akara Mountains .
