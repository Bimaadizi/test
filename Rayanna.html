Rayanna the Rose was the leader of the Red Ravens in 1374 DR .

History

Rayanna led an adventuring party that was composed of one hundred members. They were hired by the Cormyrean Crown in 1364 DR to clear the Stonelands of evil humanoids and they managed the mission.
