The Kissing Wench was a festhall in Hastarl , Athalantar , during the 3 rd century DR .

Interior

The main room had a number of corners, some of them dark. There were also stalls with beds and curtains that could be drawn, to give lovers privacy.

Services

Drinks were served there. Using the stalls, with their privacy curtains, normally cost six gold falcons .

History

In the Year of the Black Flame, 229 DR , two regulars at the tavern, the thieves Farl and Eladar the Dark (who was secretly Elminster Aumar , prince of Athalantar ) had an altercation with local nobleman Jansibal Otharr , who nearly had his bodyguards kill them. The confrontation was headed off by the intervention of Thelorn Selemban , a rival nobleman, and the swordmaster Adarbron . However, the two took revenge on Jansibal for almost having them killed by knocking him out in the privacy stall he was using while entertained by local coinlass Shandathe Llaerin , and then doing the same with his rival, leaving them in the same stall.
