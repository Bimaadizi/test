Victoria's leather corset was a unique enchanted elven leather armor that once belonged to an Akh'Velahr war party leader, Victoria , who died in the Weeping War .

Description

Victoria's leather corset was a tight-fitting leather sheath.

Powers

This leather corset was enhanced with elven magics with a +2 enchantment. It also dramatically increased the wearer's charisma at the cost of somewhat reduced fortitude. Additionally, the leathers allowed the wearer to cast dominate person at will. Victoria's leather corset held six charges of the spell.

History

During the Weeping War, Victoria led her elven war party against the invading Army of Darkness and met her demise in the Elven Catacombs . In 1369 DR , the drow of House Freth were smuggling elven artifacts from the catacombs under the city and Victoria's gear was among the items that were collected and prepared for transport. Victoria's leather corset never reached the Underdark as a group of adventurers from Phlan destroyed drow operations in the Elven Catacombs.
