Mahmud was a carpet merchant living in Hawa in 1367 DR .

Activities

Mahmud spent much of his time at a local tavern when he was not selling carpets in the market.

Rumors and Legends

Grima al-Auni suspected Mahmud of being the leader of a slave ring operating in Hawa.
