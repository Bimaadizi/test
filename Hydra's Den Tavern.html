The Hydra's Den was a busy pub that stood in the south-western part of Hillsfar , not far from the Vault of Swords , a local temple of Tempus .

Services

The Hydra's Den offered several types of meals, catering to people of varied means. An average meal could cost up to 4 gold coins . Among libations severed, the tavern offered pink lemonade , beer , wine , and whiskey .

History

As of the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , the tavern was a crowded establishment swirling with gossip. The taproom had individual tables, booths, and seats at the bar. The tavern survived well into the late 15 th century DR and was considered one of the most popular drinking spots in Hillsfar.
