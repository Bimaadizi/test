Voosbur was a myconid adult in Gracklstugh in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Voosbur lived in Neverlight Grove with all its "troupe". Directed by Zuggtmoy 's will, it led its troupe to Gracklstugh to collect bigwig and pygmywort mushrooms.
