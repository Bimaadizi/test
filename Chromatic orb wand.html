Chromatic orb wands were enchanted wands that held multiple charges of destructive spells unleashed upon command.

Description

Chromatic orb wands were on average 1 foot and 3 inches (0.38 meters) long scepter-shaped items topped with a glowing orb. They were usually made using ivory , or wood .

Powers

Each of these mages' accessories held several charges of the chromatic orb spell. The magics held within it could be discharged at will using a command word. Upon expenditure of a wand's last charge, it disintegrated.

History

Chromatic orb wands were rare and circa the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , sometimes appeared in the hands of spellcasters of Icewind Dale in Northwest Faerûn .
