Reinhar III was a king of the Arkaiuns of Dambrath .

Relationships

The man who would rule as Reinhar III was the only son of Reinhar II before him. He had at least one sister and two sons.

History

Reinhar III was born in the Year of the Simpering Courtier, 547 DR . He took the throne of his father in 591 DR .

His reign ended violently in 600 DR when he and both of his sons were killed by giants while on a hunting trip in the Gnollwatch Mountains . This triggered a short war of succession. At the end of it, his nephew succeeded him as king.
