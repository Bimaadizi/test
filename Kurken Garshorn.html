Kurken Garshorn was an exiled duergar merchant in Mantol-Derith .

Personality

Kurken's status as an exile caused him serious trouble with other duergar. He believed that Mantol-Derith was the only place where he could live in peace.

History

Around 1479 DR , Kurken sold armor in Mantol-Derith.
