House Arnise , also referred to as De'Arnise , was a noble family of warriors from the nation of Amn , during the 14 th century DR . Unlike the many mercantile families that were so common in the nation, the Arnise family were well-renowned for their trade as warriors and soldiers-for-hire.

They were an honest, and somewhat simple noble family, who were fair and hospitable of the farmers that looked to them for protection and leadership.

Their familial hold was located west of the Trade Way in Zehoarastria .
