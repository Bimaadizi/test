Ussilbran Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Ussilbran Street was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's North Ward . The road ran north to south, originating in the north at the intersection of Saerdoun Street and Lion Street , and passing south to end on Tarsar's Street . Ussilbran Street was intersected by Sulmor Street , Endcliff Lane , and Hassantyr's Street
