The Watchfire House was a mansion located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR .

Location

The mansion was located near the center of the city within the Plume district just south of the Street of Stelae and the Gate of the Fallen .

Inhabitants

The Watchfire House was the home of the City of Brass's Minster of Security.
