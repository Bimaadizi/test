Ss'essethen'aa was a city of twisting tunnels inhabited by yuan-ti clerics of Sseth and ophidian laborers.

Description

It had many gardens, chapels, and shrines on terraces built along the hill. At the very top of the tor lay a temple to Sseth.

Geography

Ss'esseten'aa sat on the largest tor of the Serpent Hills .

Geographical Features

Many subterranean tunnels linked it to the other cities of the Serpent Hills.

Notable Locations

A two-way portal to the Elemental Plane of Earth in which the ophidians mined for gems.
