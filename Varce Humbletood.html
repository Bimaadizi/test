Varce Humbletood was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Varce was a human with light skin tone, aquamarine eyes, and light brown hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Varce Humbletood was a customer of the Facemaker Fashion boutique. On the day the shop's proprietor was attacked by the Bhaalist assassins, Varce impatiently waited for his hair appointment in a queue outside of the shop.
