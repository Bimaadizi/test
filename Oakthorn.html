TypeSickle

Oakthorn was a unique weapon that was the favored weapon of Sheela Peryroyl , the halfling deity of agriculture and love.

Description

Oakthorn was a sickle , though its level of enchantment, if it was magical at all, was unknown. Unlike other deities of Yondalla's Children , Sheela Peryroyl did not appear to use her favored weapon in combat. Instead, she would would conjure a highly enchanted quarterstaff or use an array of spells .
