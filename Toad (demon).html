Toad was a hezrou servant of the demon lord Graz'zt in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Graz'zt sent Toad to serve the cambion siblings Gattash and Rashara , who had gained control of a kuo-toa tribe in the Deep Wastes , by the Waydown River . However, Gattash and Rashara bullied Toad. He was ordered to remain in his lair until the cambions needed him.

Personality

Toad was very stupid but believed himself to be smart. He knew that the cambions were bullying him and wanted to rebel but was alone and knew he needed allies first.
