XYZ balm was an alchemical balm sold by hedge wizards .

Powers

It was a general purpose ointment used to cure burns, scrapes, and poisons such as poison ivy . It wasn't magical and couldn't heal those outright, instead speeding up the body's recovery.

History

Despite being nonmagical, it still required water that had passed down a unicorn's horn for its synthesis. The synthesis of this reagent was extremely delicate, requiring both chemical and herbal knowledge in order to brew correctly. Hedge wizards often traded in it. It sold for about 500 gold pieces .

Reputation

It was popular with thieves.
