Cressida Flint was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Cressida was a human woman with a tan skin tone, straight and long dark brown hair, and dark brown eyes. She wore a colorful striped orange and red tunic .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Cressida Flint could often be found pacing down the streets of the Lower City, consumed by her thoughts. She reacted hostilely to anyone who dared to interfere with her busy day. She was one of the customers interested in the Honest Vendor 's not-so-magical protective trinkets.
