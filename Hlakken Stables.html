The Hlakken Stables were a stables in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

These stables were located on Coachlamp Lane , in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

Hlakken's stables were a two-story building.

Services

Mounts and gear related to them, such as bits, bridles, and saddles , could be purchased here.

Staff

The only known worker of these stables was Surrolph Hlakken , who acted as a horse merchant.
