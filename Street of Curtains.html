The Street of Curtains was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Street of Curtains was located in the northeast section of Waterdeep's Dock Ward . It originated in the west on Snail Street and branched east through Book Street to end on the northern end of Zastrow Street just off of the Way of the Dragon .
