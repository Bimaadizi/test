Ambrose was a human fighter , a cavalry commander in his own right and part of Gabriels retinue.

Description

Whenever he was not travelling around as part of Gabriel's retinue, he was training king Halvor IIs army up for war. He was appointed to lead the army once war was declared, and spent much time drawing up battle plans and defences for his kings ambition.

Possessions

Ambrose rode astride his heavy warhose wielding his enchanted chained morningstar and a large shield , he wore an enchanted chainmail , a ring of protection and a girdle of fire giant strength .

Relationships

He was a personal friend of the paladin Gabriel, and his fellow retinue member Barent
