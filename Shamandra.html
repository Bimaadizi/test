Shamandra was the third largest of the most southernly islands in the Archipelago of Altumbel on the Sea of Fallen Stars .

Description

Jonsra Burunmere , a female Chondathan human held the title of the Arch Sorceress Supreme of the island realm. Under her rule, Shamandra was a tiny farming realm due west of the city of Delthuntle . It was in an alliance with the nation of Aglarond .

History

In the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , Shamandra suffered from a devious attack by the Red Wizards of Thay . It was repulsed with a personal aid from the Simbul herself.
