Reagan Splint was an inhabitants of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Reagan was a large and muscular half-orcish woman. She had gray skin and fur and styles her hair into a red mohawk.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate , displacing countless refugees in the process along the way and provoking fear and paranoia inside the city walls. Reagan was outraged by the number of public executions that took place on the plaza outside of the Basilisk Gate . She wrote to the Baldur's Mouth Gazette about the issue every tenday and every tenday, the broadsheet ignored the half-orc's calls. During that time, Reagan spent some time renting lodgings at Nortale's Hostel until an incident that had her removed from the reestablishment.
