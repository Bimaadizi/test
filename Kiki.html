Kiki was an elf wizard and a student of the Neverwinter Academy in Neverwinter during the late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Like almost all the other students of the Neverwinter Academy, Kiki was fond of Lady Aribeth and was grateful that she didn't blame all the wizards for the spreading of the Wailing Death and events in Neverwinter.

Relationships

During the graduation ceremonies in 1372 DR, Kiki was chatting with fellow students Deelin and Fonlim .

History

In the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Kiki finished her studies and was one of the eight students, among whom was the future Hero of Neverwinter , present at the graduation ceremony that year. During the ceremony led by Lady Aribeth, the Academy was attacked. She fought against the mysterious mages in the Graduation Chamber. It is not known if Kiki survived the attack.
