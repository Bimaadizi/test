The Quomec Ziggurat , also sometimes called simply Quomec , was a large ziggurat that sat in the jungles of northern Chult .

Structure

The ziggurat stood twenty-eight levels tall. The exterior was inscribed with indecipherable glyphs, with the inscriptions near the base seeming to show a massive sarcophagus writhing with animated vines. At the top of the ziggurat sat a temple sealed behind enormous bronze doors.

History

As of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , local tribes claimed that the ziggurat was the crypt of an emperor known as Lord Quomec , although that name had not been recorded in any texts.
