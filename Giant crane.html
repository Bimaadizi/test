Giant cranes were a monstrously sized species of crane .

Behavior

These creatures had an average animal degree of intelligence. Though generally non-aggressive, giant cranes were very protective of their mates, young, and nesting areas.

Ecology

Diet

These creatures were a carnivorous species, preying upon large fish .

Habitats

Giant cranes were generally found in the same habitats of similar wading birds, such as ordinary cranes, herons , and bitterns. These habitats included marshes, swamps, lake verges, and along the banks of watercourses.

Giant cranes could be found in Kara-Tur 's temperate plains, as well as in its tropical and sub-tropical swamps.
