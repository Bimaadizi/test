Shadow Ridge was a rise of ground on the west side of the Ashaba in Shadowdale .

Geography

The ridge was north of the Shrine to Mystra and east of Luth Mlennon's farm. Shadow Ridge had a large amount of brambles, yew , grape , and raspberry bushes that covered it.

Inhabitants

Children occasionally swam the river to pick berries on Shadow Ridge.
