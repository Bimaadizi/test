A House of Joy was the shrine built to Lliira in the small town of Asbravn . Both the town and shrine were situated within an isolated area of the Sunset Vale , where the Dusk Road met the Uldoon Trail .

It was one of several smaller shrines, including those to Lathander and Waukeen , built around the rundown yet still-running House of the Suffering God , dedicated to Ilmater .
