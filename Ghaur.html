Ghaur was a goblin of the Spine of the World .

Notably, he wielded the enchanted sword Namarra , the Sword That Never Sleeps, which the goblins had looted in 753 DR .

Ghaur was killed by adventurers of the Company of the Gryphon . One of those adventurers, a warrior named Flar , claimed Ghaur's sword.
