Fist Olloa was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Olloa had a tanned complexion with black hair gathered in a bun. She wore a set of leather armor as part of her Flaming Fist uniform and was armed with a deadly heavy crossbow . Olloa had a strict and somewhat cruel personality.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Olloa was tasked with patrolling the Lower City, near the Basilisk Gate . She and her fellow patrolmen had no qualms with shutting protesters up with force.
