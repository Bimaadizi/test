Personality

Vail had something of a silver tongue, readily offering praise and flattery to others.

Possessions

During his travels, Vail managed to track down some artifacts belonging to the famous explorer Balduran , acquiring both the helm and the cloak of Balduran .

Relationships

Vail had a lover by the name of Quenash , who worked as a courtesan in Baldur's Gate.

History

Some time during or before the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , Vail was hired by the mage Degrodel to raid the tower of Ramazith Flamesinger . He gathered together some allies: the warrior Rance and the conjurer Faizah Tin , along with two newcomers to their group, Cailan and Achen Dell .

However, Ramazith set a basilisk loose upon the mercenaries, which then proceeded to turn each of the intruders to stone . Vail and his companions remained as statues, before they were sold to the nobleman art collector Felonius Gist . They remained at his estate in Baldur's Gate.
