Reliath Crescentia was a female wood elf lurk and a young member of the Kaliesh'erai , an association of psionic elves in Evereska .

In the mid-1370s DR, Reliath robbed the Hall of the Kaliesh'erai of a number of powerful psionic items . The theft triggered huge debates among the remaining members, which came to focus on the future of the Kaliesh'erai and led to a schism between older conservative and younger expansionist elements.
