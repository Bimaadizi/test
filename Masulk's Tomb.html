Masulk's Tomb was the burial place of Masulk the Magnificent located in the Far Forest in the Savage Frontier .

Description

The tomb was hidden by trees, shrubs and climbing plants, so it was hard to locate.

History

Masulk was a vizar stricken by a terrible and incurable disease. After he died, Masulk's servants dug the tomb as ordered by the master himself, where his flying carpet came to rest. Masulk wanted to be buried with no coins, but with all the magic he'd accumulated during his lifetime.
