Hammergrim was the home plane of Laduguer and Deep Duerra in the World Tree cosmology . The evil gnomish god Urdlen also burrowed its tunnels through the foundations of the plane.

History

In 1383 DR , Laduguer , along with Deep Duerra , was destroyed and Hammergrim dispersed into the Astral Plane .
