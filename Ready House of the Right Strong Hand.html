The Ready House of the Right Strong Hand was the local temple of Helm in the city of Berdusk .

The temple was overseen by Vigilant Godseye Tathlosar Brimmerbold , who was aided by three dozen lesser priests .

History

As of the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , the temple had over one hundred followers among the local Berduskan populace.
