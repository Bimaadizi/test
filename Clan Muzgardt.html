Clan Muzgardt was a duergar clan based in the Underdark city of Gracklstugh .

Organization

Circa 1489 DR , the matriarch of the Muzgardt clan was the cunning Grandolpha Muzgardt .

Activities

The clan specialized in brewing. Their best draught was Darklake Stout , an ale made from mushrooms that only a duergar could truly enjoy. Their main brewery was located in the Darklake District in Gracklstugh.

Their reach extended far in the Underdark, with their ale being sold in Mantol Derith ―managed by the merchant Krimgol Muzgardt ―and in the Sunblight kingdom beneath the Spine of the World .

History

In the Year of the Star Walker's Return, 1490 DR , Grandolpha Muzgart was invited to the Sunblight stronghold in Icewind Dale by Xardorok Sunblight . She started plotting with his disenfranchised advisors and warriors to overthrow their kings.
