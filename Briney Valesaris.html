Briney Valesaris was a half-elf buccaneer who captained the ship known as the Cambacks .

Activities

He joined every battle he could find, always siding against the attackers, and pursued Thayvian ships even into the Pirate Isles .

History

Briney had stolen his ship from the Thayan merchant-mage Haldracon . He'd since destroyed and sunk many Thayvian ships.
