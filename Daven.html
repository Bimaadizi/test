Daven was a man who lived in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

He had a lover and partner named Flora . While Daven was apparently aware Flora was pregnant, he did not know their son, Daven Junior , had been born.

History

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Daven was away from Flora with work. While they were separated, Flora gave birth to their son, whom she named after his father. Flora wrote to Daven requesting he come home on leave immediately. Unfortunately, they letter never arrived, as the courier carrying it was slain while traveling along the Risen Road .
