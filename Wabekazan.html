Wabekazan was a volcanic island located in Kozakura .

Description

Wabekazan was volcanic cone of jagged basalt . It was an active volcano, spewing lava at irregular times. The slopes of the volcano were hot and covered in lava flows and poisonous gas.

Geography

Wabekazan was found to the southeast of Mikedono .

History

Wabekazan rose suddenly from the seabed circa Kozakuran Year 1331 (1257 DR) .
