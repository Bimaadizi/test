Tristram Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles for a year in the late 5 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High King Kemble . His successor was his own first son, Tuathal Cymrych .

History

Tristram assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Blighted Vine, 482 DR , after the death of his father.

He ruled for just a year before dying of disease in the Year of the Soaring Galleon, 483 DR .
