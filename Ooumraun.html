Ooumraun was the second ruler of the phaerimm .

History

He was a colleague of Phaerl and developed many new spells. After Phaerl's death to the sharn , Ooumraun became prominent. Many believed that he knew many other, very powerful new spells that had he taught to no one, using them against his many enemies over the years.
