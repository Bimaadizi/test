Saer Grotpoll was a high elven commoner in the late 15 th century DR who lived in Baldur's Gate 's Rivington district.

Description

Grotpoll was an elven man with a light skin tone, long black hair, and blue eyes. Despite his attitude towards aliens, Saer Grotpoll had an accent recognizable by citizens of the Gate as that of a man from Rivington, rather than the Gate proper.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldur's Gate , displacing countless refugees along the way in the process. Some of the Baldurians did not take kindly to the refugees flooding the outskirts of the city, while others took to protesting. Grotpoll was the main agitator among the protestors in Rivington, who thought that the refugees were vermin that takes homes and goods intended for Baldurians.
