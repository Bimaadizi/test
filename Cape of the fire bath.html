Capes of the fire bath were common enchanted cloaks worn by worshipers of Kossuth .

Description

These capes were dyed in brilliant red color and decorated with shimmering embroidered flames.

Powers

Capes of the fire bath shielded their wearers from extreme heat, magical flames of spells, and even fiery dragon breath . Additionally, these cloaks improved the wearers' reaction and defenses against physical and magical threats.

History

These items were created for the use of influential clerics and worshipers of Kossuth, however, by the late 14 th century DR , these flaming capes entered a wide rotation among spellcasters of the Realms. In 1372 DR , these capes could be purchased from the Sorceller's Encapsulate of Daggerford. The same year, several of powerful magical items were brought to Toril by a planeswalking wizard Vejoni H. Wyzz who pitched his extraplanar tent just outside the town of Liam's Hold . A cape of the fire bath was found in his collection.
