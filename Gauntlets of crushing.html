TypeGauntlets

The gauntlets of crushing were a pair of gloves that greatly increased the combat prowess of an individual when fighting with their fists.

Description

These gauntlets varied in appearance from quite thin, to bulky yet comfortable. The larger variety did not interfere with any delicate or intricate work of the wearer.

Powers

When worn, the gauntlets allowed someone to punch with much greater accuracy and damaging force.

Notable Owners

A famous fighter from the east named Floating Bear wore a pair of these gauntlets when he traveled through the Sword Coast .

Another pair was owned by King Ixilthetocal , who ruled the sahuagin of the City of Caverns .
