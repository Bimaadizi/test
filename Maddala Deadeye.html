Maddala Deadeye was a blind human rogue who died in the 15 th century DR , killed by the Cult of the Absolute .

Description

Maddala was had milky gray eyes, bronze skin, and pale blonde hair. Their face and neck were decorated with black ink tattoos .

History

Maddala Deadeye met a True Soul of the Absolute when the cultist visited their home village. Maddala was offered to join with the Absolutists but refused. As punishment, the True Soul cured Maddala's blindness so they could watch their home being burned into ash and only then Maddala met their death. Their soul yearned for revenge, and Maddala ended up among the dozen individuals plucked from the Fugue Plane , chosen by the entity called Withers to aid in his campaign against the Dead Three and their Cult of the Absolute.
