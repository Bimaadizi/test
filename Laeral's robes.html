Laeral's robes was a unique enchanted robe that combined the magical effects of several items.

Description

A plethora of magical patterns resembling eyes adorned the robes.

Powers

Laeral's robes combined the effects of a robe of eyes , a cloak of resistance , and Heward's handy haversack .

Notable Owners

This item was created and worn by Laeral Silverhand .
