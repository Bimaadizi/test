Melinda Bothgan was a bard in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

Melinda had light skin, brown eyes, a bob of black hair, and wore a flashy white, gold, and purple outfit.

History

Around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Melinda arrived in the village and helped train prospective bards.
