Fidilee Fallowfield was a halfling resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr during the mid-1480s DR .

Description

Fidilee had white hair tied back into a bun. She wore a black and white dress.

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Fidilee met adventurers and exhorted them to save the town.
