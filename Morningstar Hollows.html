Morningstar Hollows once was a small fishing village, abandoned due to the continual flooding of the Alaoreum River . The boggy village bacame home to a tribe of lizardfolk .

Description

After the village was swallowed by Alaoreum River's bogs, the nature flourished growing huge river pines , oaks , elms , walnut and pecan trees.

Trade

The lizardfolk of Morningstar Hollows survived by fishing and hunting.

History

The flooding of the Alaoreum River took several seasons. After inhabitants of Morningstar Hollows moved to nearby Velorn's Valor , the abandoned village was taken over by bogs and swamps. By the late 14 th century DR , the village was believed to be inhabited by a three dozen lizardfolk.

In the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR , the Morningstar Hollows were taken by the undead armies of mohrg Borran Klosk of Turmish. The undead warlord used his necromancy to fill his army with long dead folk, murdered by the Emerald Enclave .

By the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , the lizardfolk of Morningstar Hollows was gone, driven away by a slew of adventurers seaking fame and fortune. The lizardfolk moved on to the Wetwoods while a gang of bandits took over the village, ambushing scar pilgrims.
