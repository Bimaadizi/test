Tim was a halfling thief and slave of Baeloth Barrityl .

Description

The halfling was proficient at using daggers , as well as a shortbow and arrows . Tim wore leather armor , could set traps, and poisoned his weapons. He was the only evil member of his party, which also contained a gnoll , Bort .

History

Tim and his party were taken from a "shabby saloon" to the Black Pits . They were killed by the Champion of Baeloth's Pits and their party in the Black Pits arena.
