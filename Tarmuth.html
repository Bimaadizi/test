Tarmuth was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He was young.

History

When the Hall of Justice became filled with deadly smoke on the day of the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Tarmuth was one of the mages Glathra Barcantle commanded to rectify the situation. She told him to go after King Foril Obarskyr I 's bodyguard and warn all of them to put on night helms . He immediately ran off to do that.
