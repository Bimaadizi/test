Belyssa Erstros was a whaler from Tarmalune and the captain of the ship Devastating in 1479 DR .

Description

Belyssa was described as an amazon of a woman. She was tall, with tattooed strong arms and a fiery red head.

She was ratter infamous for her attraction to males smaller than her, such as dwarves .

Activities

Belyssa was a whaler operating out of Tarmalune. In 1479 DR, she obtained a good fortune and became an influential person across the Windrise Ports . Thanks to her newfound fortune, she became the captain of her own ship, the Devastating .
