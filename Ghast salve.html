TypeOintment

Ghast salve was an enchanted paste that offered protection from paralysis.

Descripion

Ghast salve was a sickly verdant ointment that smelled quite pungent.

Powers

This salve offered total immunity from the paralysis associated with a ghast 's touch when spread over the skin and granted the same paralyzing touch to the wearer if they touched others. The salve also made the wearer immune to the effects of the ghast's nauseating smell while also making them exude the same stench to others. Ghouls and ghasts viewed the wearer as one of their own while wearing this salve. The effects of ghast salve lasted for one hour after applied.

Anyone wearing ghast salve could be burned if holy water was splashed upon them.

Notable Owners

Pizentios , a necromancer from the isle of Sahu , carried some ghast salve .
