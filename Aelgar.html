Aelgar the Fierce was a barbarian who took up residence in the city of Neverwinter for a time during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Aelgar venerated Uthgar , the deity of the Uthgardt people.

History

After the Siege of Neverwinter , in the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR , Aelgar stationed himself at the Neverwinter Guard post in the city's Tower District . While there he led the city's defenders against waves of attacks made upon them by the Many-Arrows orcs . Among the marauding assailants was the enormous ogre , Mog the Crusher .
