The Harbreeze Bakery was a beloved eatery of Baldur's Gate , located within the marketplace of the Upper City during the late 15 th century DR . It was run by the red-headed Ellyn Harbreeze , a woman who garnered as much knowledge as some information brokers.

Services

The bakery offered such favorite delicacies as cinnamon cookies and full loaves of sugarbread highlighted by the flavors of foreign teas and spices . Their real commodity however was in information, specifically that regarding the social lives of the bakery's clientele.

It was one of the favorite mid-day stops for gossipy Baldurian patriar .
