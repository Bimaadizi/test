Selmalyr was an ancient sea elf kingdom in Serôs , the realms beneath the Sea of Fallen Stars , that gave rise to the Aryselmalyr Empire in −10,933 TS (−11,003 DR) or 1 AC (the first year in the Aryselmalyn calendar ).

Geography

This kingdom was located in the Selmal Basin portion of the Inner Sea, what was known to surface worlders as the Vilhon Reach.

History

Circa −7630 TS (−7700 DR) , a number of rogue marids of the Calim Empire trespassed into Selmalyr, gradually sparking a war between them and the sea elves of the Selmal Basin.
