Surtlan's Metalwares was a shop in the city of Waterdeep , owned by Baerhar Surtlan in the 14 th century DR .

Location

This shop was located along River Street , in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.

Structure

This shop stood a single story tall.

Services

Surtlan's Metalwares sold metal products and equipment.
