A siangham , also known as a siangkam , was an exotic light melee weapon used mainly by monks .

Description

A siangham looked like a heavy metal arrow with a hilt or handle where the feathers would be. It had a piercing tip. An average siangham cost 3 gp , weighed 1 lb (450 g) and was about 1 foot (30 centimeters) in length. It took special training to use a siangham in melee combat, therefore it was considered an exotic weapon.

In Kara-Tur, it was primarily used by wu jen .
