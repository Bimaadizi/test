Traxigor was an archmage polymorphed into an otter during the late 15 th century DR . His stone tower floated above the Sea of Swords .

Relationships

He was an acquaintance of the archmage Sylvira Savikas . Traxigor took care of the hollyphant Lulu for her.

History

At one point, Traxigor accidentally polymorphed himself into an otter but eventually decided he preferred his new body to the old one and made his condition permanent with a wish spell .

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Sylvira asked Traxigor's help to send an adventuring party to Avernus and he agreed.
