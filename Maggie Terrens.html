Maggie Terrens , sister to Marcus Terrens , was a child who lived in the town of Moonhaven in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Sometime before the Moonhaven was attacked and ruined by the goblin raiders of the Cult of the Absolute in the Year of the Scarlet Witch, 1491 DR , Maggie was among the four children who had gone missing from Moonhaven. Their names: Maggie Terrens, Marcus Terrens, Mathen Deetch , and Rochelle Kirk . The missing children's notice was still hanging on a wall as the village laid in ruins in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
