The Downed Falcon was a tavern in Suzail in Cormyr in the late 14 th century DR .

History

In early spring of Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Caladnei pointed out the Downed Falcon to her newest spy, the thief Narnra Shalace from Waterdeep , as a regular watering hole for the War Wizards Bowsar Ostramarr and Bathtar Flamegallow , whom she was going to spy on for her first assignment.
