Stormstar's Ride was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Stormstar's Ride was located in the northwest section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward near the Trolltower . It branched south and southwest from the Street of Lances to end on the Street of Glances where Staghunter's Way also merged. Stormstar's Ride was also intersected by Aureenar Street and Phastal Street .
