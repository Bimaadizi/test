Agundar Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Agundar .

Location

The Agundar Villa was situated on the corner of Copper Street and Hassantyr's Street , in the North Ward of Waterdeep .

Structure

This building complex consisted of two high quality buildings, one that was three stories tall and another that stood a single story tall, one being the mansion proper and the other a guesthouse. Like many Waterdhavian villas, it was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden.
