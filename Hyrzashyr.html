The Hyrzashyr was a short river in Amn .

Geography

The Hyrzashyr flowed north from the Tejarn Hills into Lake Esmel .

History

The river received its name during the time of the halfling kingdom of Meiritin .

Notable Locations

The fishing village of Zinner was found along the banks of this river.
