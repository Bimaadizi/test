Nezram's tower was once the home of the powerful Mulhorandi wizard named Nezram .

Location

The tower was found along the outskirts of Azun on the western shore of the Lake of Salt .

History

Nezram's tower was destroyed by the green dragon Chaathuuladroth in the Year of the Zombie Lords, 681 DR . The treasure contained within the tower was looted both by the dragon and later by Nezram's descendants. However, the lure of potential hidden powerful magic items lured many adventurers to explore the ruins centuries after its destruction.
