Tornamn was a royal house of Tethyr . It was the last of the Shoon Traitorum vassals of the Shoon Imperium .

History

The family came to the throne by the daughter of King Karaj Tiiraklar I . Though ostensibly vassals of the Shoon, the Tornamn kings were largely loyal to Tethyr first. A daughter of the family later wed with Strohm I , founding the Strohm Dynasty .
