Leoril was an employee at the Cutlass in Luskan during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Around 1490 DR , Leoril was arrested while working the bar at the Cutlass by members of the Order of the Gilded Eye , under allegations of smuggling wine past the blockade of the gate into the city.
