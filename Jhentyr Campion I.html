Jhentyr Campion I was the second King of the Campion Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Jhentyr I inherited the throne two years after his father, Altarl , died in the Year of the Sky Riders, 936 DR . Jhentyr I ruled until the Year of Perilous Halls, 954 DR .

Relationships

His nephew, who would inherit the throne, was Tarnhart I .
