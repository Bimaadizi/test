Magasorium plants were very common herbs found in the Malatran jungles.

Description

Magasorium plant was a small flowering plant, easily recognizable for its wide flat green leaves with black ribbing. Due to its size, magasorium was often hard to find. When crushed, leaves of magasorium plants released juice that could be used as an effective insect repellent . A single plant could be used to create six doses of this repellent. A single topical application lasted for around four hours; however, it could be washed off or diluted with excessive sweating. Burning the plant was also an effective way of keeping jungle insects at bay.

History

In the 14 th century DR , magasorium leaves were known to be gifted to goodly travelers by the lesser nature spirits of a Malatran vanilla grove Timon and Pumba .
