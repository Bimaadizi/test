Pankin Yegorovna (pronounced PAN-kin YAY-gore-OVE-nuh) was one of the Blades of Mulmaster .

Relationship

Pankin had a daughter, Riasa Yegorovna .

Activities

Pankin was one of the Blades and also owned many trading vessels.

History

At one point, his wife died and he had the responsibility of caring for his daughter Riasa.

In 1491 DR , however, she became a disciple of Thylius Caramitru . Riasa and Thylius performed a demonstration of Yellow Breath at a party held for Aleyd Burral . After Pankin was informed of his daughter's activities, he decided to lock her in the Tower of Blades .
