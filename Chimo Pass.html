Chimo Pass was a pass located in Kozakura .

Description

Chimo Pass was located in Fukudo Province on the island of Shinkoku . The Chimo Pass, the lowest and most accessible passage over the Dragon's Spine , was a recent addition to the Imperial Post Road as of Kozakuran Year 1431 (1357 DR) . At the pass's peak, bamboo and fern forests merged with cedar trees near the town of Chimotoge , which was a vital junction for various smaller mountain trails.
