Kitashi was a yakuza in Kozakura in 1357 DR .

History

Kitashi was a yakuza spy in a town in Kozakura, where resided Ko Mei Kho . In 1357 rumors said that Ko clan was involved in a great enterprise and Kitashi went to spy Ko Mei Kho, discovering that he had just hired an adventuring party to solve a matter in far Akari island.

Personality

Kitashi was a very able spy. She not want die for her mission and if captured told all her known, hoping for a next occasion to escape.
