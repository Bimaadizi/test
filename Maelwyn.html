Maelwyn was a cavalier inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Maelwyn had decent luck working as an adventurer , but preferred the solitude of court life in Daggerford. His home was located on the Farmer's Road near the rear of the Grand Playhouse .

Relationships

His father was a respected veteran of the militia.
