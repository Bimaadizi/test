Picklock Alley was a small backalley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Picklock Alley was connected to Gut Alley in Waterdeep's Dock Ward , branching west just south of the intersection with Adder Lane and continuing to a dead end after several buildings. Julbuck Alley branched south off of Picklock Alley.
