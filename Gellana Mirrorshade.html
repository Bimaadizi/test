Gellana Mirrorshade was a cleric of Garl Glittergold in the Friendly Arm Inn in 1359 DR .

Activities

Gellana managed the local temple to Garl and helped her husband Bentley Mirrorshade to run the inn. She also maintained the inn's garden.

Personality

Gellana always tried to see the larger picture involving people while Bentley unveiled their schemes. She had built many walls to protect the inn from any attack.

Relationship

Gellana was considered a model figure to inspire all young female gnomes in the Realms .
