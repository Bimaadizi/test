Rods of beguiling were enchanted rods that could calm and charm even the angriest creatures.

Description

Rods of beguiling were beautifully crafted oaken devices sometimes decorated with a couple of gemstones .

Powers

These rods allowed the wielder to cast charm person and dominate person spells. An average rod of beguiling held fifty charges and each use of the charm person spell exhausted one charge, while dominate person - two.

Notable Owners

History

In the late 14 th century DR , these rods could be found throughout the Sword Coast region, notably in the cities of Neverwinter and sold by an imp Boonmoppet in Daggerford 's Western Farmlands .
