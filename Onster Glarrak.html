Onster Glarrak was a member of the Council of Tarmalune as of 1479 DR .

Description

Onster was described as a fat and unlovely human .

Activities

Onster was a wealthy investor who was well-informed of many things in Tarmalune. He was one of the loudest voices in the Council.
