Jhandess Millomyr was a Tethyrian bard inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Jhandess was a harp -maker who owned her own shop called Millomyr Harps located on Golden Serpent Street in Waterdeep's North Ward . She was affiliated with the New Olamn bardic college.
