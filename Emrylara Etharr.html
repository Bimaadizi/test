Emrylara Etharr was a noblewoman of the Rallyhorn family in Cormyr in the 2 nd century DR . She married Aulard Etharr , the grandson of the Royal Magician of Cormyr, Baerauble Etharr .

Description

Emrylara was a tall woman with porcelain-white skin, green eyes, and hair black as a raven. She typically wore dark colored gowns and moved about silently in soft shoes.

Personality

She was quiet and had a serious demeanor. She admired thoughtful wisdom and shunned suitors that tried to impress her with showy, loud, or exuberant courtship rituals and clichés.

Relationships

She was the daughter of Lord Theldrin Rallyhorn , a close and trusted advisor to the Obarskyrs . Emrylara greatly admired High Mage Baerauble and was attracted to his grandson, Aulard, who was "an echo of his grandsire" in look, dress, and manner.

History

She bore two children before she died at age thirty one. Her firstborn was a son, Obrynn . Her daughter, Narnytha, died in childhood. When Emrylara passed, she was entombed in a vault down a short passage from Theldrin's tomb beneath Castle Obarskyr . Obrynn was afforded the same honor when he died.

Rumors & Legends

Her tomb was said to be filled with art and objects of great beauty, but nothing extremely valuable. Her ghost could be seen floating down the passage toward Theldrin's tomb and attempting, but failing, to gain entrance.
