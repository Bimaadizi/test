Lackpurse Lane was a prominent border road located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Lackpurse Lane served as part of the border between the Dock Ward and Castle Ward . It originated on Belnimbra's Street in the east, branching northwest before passing due west past Dretch Lane before turning south to end on Sail Street near the waterfront. The road was intersected by the Reach , Net Street , Eel Street , Sakiir's Street , Ward's Way , and Cook Street .

Notable Locations

The Grey Griffon tavern was located on Lackpurse Lane on the west side of the intersection with Eel Street.
