Datchas was the common name for datolite in the Realms . The pink variety was also known as sugar stone .

Description

Datchas could be found in small stones, which were typically cut into faceted gems , or large warty nodules up to 10 inches (0.25 meters) in diameter which were either sliced and polished for use as inlays or cut cabochon . The pale yellowish green hue was the most prized for faceted gems; the orange hue was in highest demand for inlays. Other colors, caused by impurities of copper and other minerals, were white to pink to red, and reddish brown. A typical faceted stone had a base value of 50 gp.

Powers

A person under the spider climb enchantment could double the duration of the effect by consuming crushed datchas powder mixed with any non-alcoholic liquid.
