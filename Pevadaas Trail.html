The Pevadaas Trail was a small trail found within the Alimir Mountains in Calimshan near the Lake of Steam .

Geography

The trail branched from the Narrows Trail , cutting through the northern half of the mountain range and passing from the northeast near the shore of the Lake of Steam to the southwest toward the Spider Swamp . The Pevadaas Trail was roughly 60 miles (97 kilometers) long.

Notable Locations

The Monastery of St. Fanal was located along the Pevadaas Trail on the northwestern edge of the Alimir Mountains.
