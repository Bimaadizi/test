History

According to Jan Jansen's highly trustworthy story, he was swallowed by Lolth's avatar sometime before the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR . He was stuck in the deity's stomach for days alongside a miserable drow named Biffle Chump. Jansen claimed he had to eat Biffle Chump to survive, and he tasted like chicken .

It was likely that Biffle Chump never existed in the first place.
