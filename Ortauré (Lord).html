Lord Ortauré was an elven inhabitant of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

Lord Ortauré participated in a covert meeting to conspire against Coronal Eltargrim in 241 DR . He saw the slaying of the Coronal that they planned as an opportunity to avenge his son, who had taken part in the hunt for Elminster .
