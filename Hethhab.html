Hethhab was the current incarnation of Anhur in Mulhorand in 1357 DR .

Activities

Hethhab was the protector of the eastern sections of Mulhorand. He wandered with six disguised divine minions of Anhur.

History

Despite being the incarnation of a god, Hethhab kept this secret in order to avoid a personality cult centered on him. He became famous as a slayer of monsters and so the vizier Rezim appointed him as Defender of the Eastern Way, a very dangerous position. The fact that Hethhab was still alive concerned Rezim, who did not knew his true identity.
