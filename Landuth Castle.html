Landuth Castle was a small, fortified though tattered, estate belonging to the minor Cormyrian noble house Tammarast in the late 14 th Century DR .

Location

This estate was located on the very edge of the Vast Swamp , on the southern edge of Landuth , the Tammarast family's land holdings in the East Reaches .

Activities

Visitors to the estate were often presented with beer and mint wine by Lord Ingleiyr Tammarast .

History

The castle and the holding attached to it had entered a deteriorated state long before Ingleiyr Tammarast was made Lord of Landuth circa the 1370s DR.
