Personality

Kralgar put little faith in the power of magic .

Activities

He endlessly struggled to fulfill his goal of conquering and ruling one of the great cities of the North .

Kralgar wanted most to be enthroned in Silverymoon , and would almost be content with Everlund , if the city were more significant and defensable. He would likely settle for Neverwinter , Mirabar , Sundabar or Everlund .

Possessions

Despite his beliefs, Kralgar acquired quite a number of magical items in his quest for power.
