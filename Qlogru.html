Qlogru was a mind flayer wizard during the mid-1480s DR .

Abilities

In addition to typical illithid abilities, they were a powerful arcane spellcaster, and were served by various creatures including water elementals and skeletons .

History

Qlogru lurked near the Cave of the Elements in the Underdark around the time of the invasion of Eveningstar .
