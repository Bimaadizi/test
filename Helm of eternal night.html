The helm of eternal night was a magical helmet found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The helm was blessed with the power of sacred moonlight when it was first created. It protected against all kinds of magical assaults, and was most effective against freezing cold and raw arcane force.

History

The helm of eternal night may have been found by the Elves of Cormanthor when they raided an old Temple to Moander while reclaiming the city.
