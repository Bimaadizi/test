Jelethar Orgolath , who was also called the Blackscale , was a tiefling Nentyarch of the Orgolath Dynasty of Narfell .

Activities

Jelethar's reign as Nentyarch was brief, lasting only from −482 DR to −475 DR . He disappeared while on an expedition to the Sunrise Mountains and was presumed dead.

Relationships

Jelethar was the firstborn son of Horthaun , and inherited the throne upon his father's death in −482 DR. Since he had no children of his own, the throne passed to Jelethar's younger brother Morthass upon his death.
