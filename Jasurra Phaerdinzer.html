Jasurra Phaerdinzer was the matriarch of the Phaerdinzer clan as of the late mid-14 th century DR .

Activities

Retired shopkeepers, asurra and her husband knew to scry on other wealthy people, the "Faceless, Heartless Ones" and invest accordingly. With that money, they supported the ventures of the other Phaerdinzers .

They enjoyed floating naked everywhere in their manor.

Relationships

Jasurra dwelt in Tallstones manor with her husband, Othlo Phaerdinzer .
