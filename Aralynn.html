Aralynn the Pious was a priestess who lived in Neverwinter during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

She spent much of her time at the Halls of Justice teaching devout citizens in the city how to invoke the blessings of their deities.

History

Circa the Year of Deep Water Drifting, 1480 DR , Aralynn and Sergeant Knox of the Neverwinter Guard began investigating the spectral image of Dyrl Godsword , which had been appearing in the city and the Neverwinter Wood .
