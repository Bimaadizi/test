Mulad was a sha'ir and the middle son of Al'ia bint Hazir and Sultan Yusef al-Wadi of Gana in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara .

Personality

Mulad was quite rebellious. He strove to prove to his older brother, Yusef , that the powers of magic far outweighed those of the sword .

Description

He had a mole under his right eye in the shape of a butterfly.

History

As with all of his siblings, Mulad's parentage was kept secret from the population of Gana. He was raised in a monastery high in Al-Yabki Mountains .
