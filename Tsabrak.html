Tsabrak was a drider in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 th century DR . He was one of Houndaer Tuin'Tarl and Syrzan 's rebel group.

History

Tsabrak was part of a drider colony living in tunnels near Menzoberranzan.

During the Silence of Lolth in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , Tsabrak and others driders joined Houndaer's group in the hopes of a better future for their fellows. He was presumed killed in the suppression of the revolt.
