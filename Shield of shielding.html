Shields of shielding were enchanted shields that were imbued with protective magics.

Powers

A shield of shielding allowed the wielder to cast the shield spell once per day.

History

It was believed that the shields of shielding were named in the style of Extremely Unimaginative Jimmy , the man credited with creating the words of "waterfall" and "fireplace." During the Absolute crisis in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , a shield of shielding was among offerings made for the deity Helm at the Stormshore Tabernacle in Baldur's Gate . The magic item was sealed away in the temple's basement vault. Anyone daring enough to steal a shield of shielding would end up being cursed by the gods and attacked by Helm's servant devas .
