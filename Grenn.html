Grenn was a Harbormaster of the city of Westgate in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Grenn was a pale middle-aged human man with brown eyes, gray hair, and a big and bushy gray moustache. He wore a green turban on his head.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Grenn took the Harbormaster's title from Harbormaster Lanley , who had fallen ill. Grenn diligently did his job, despite being overwhelmed by the amount of work.
