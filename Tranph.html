Sergeant-Major Tranph was an officer in the Golden Legion .

History

Tranph was a veteran of the Legion.

In 1361 DR , after the victory at Ulatos , Cordell assigned Tranph to oversee Helmsport 's garrison whent he went to Nexal with most of the Legion.

After the arrival of Don Vaez , Tranph and his men were put in prison.
