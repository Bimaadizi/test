Brinna Brightsong was a halfling bard who lived and performed in Triel during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

She had a prominent nose, hazel eyes, and light blonde hair.

Activities

She was known for performing some rather bawdy and ultimately controversial songs.

History

Brinna met her untimely end in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . She was killed while performing at a tavern in Triel, by a cultist of the Absolute . She was among the dozen individuals plucked from the Fugue Plane , chosen by the entity Withers to aid in his campaign against the Dead Three .
