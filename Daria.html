Daria the Hammer was a mercenary recruiter in Waterdeep .

Activities

Daria was a talented merchant but was also the best-known mercenary recruiter in the City of Splendors. She often worked with Blazidon One-Eye and Filiare if she needed to recruit special units. Usually she recruited and led her company in battle. She was a master tactician.
