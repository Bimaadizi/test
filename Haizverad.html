Haizverad was a dragonborn of Abeir , and the ancestor of Clan Shestandeliath .

History

Haizverad was a slave of the dragon Versveshardinazar , who lived in the Dead Stone Mountains of Shyr . One day, while mining the remains of the primordial Petron in search of good materials to create magic items for his master, Haizverad found a powerful, yet small bit of Petron's lungs. Haizverad secreted it away and fashioned with it the powerful artifact known as the Breath of Petron , that was passed on to his descendants.

Eventually, Haizverad became the posthumous patriarch of Clan Shestandeliath.
