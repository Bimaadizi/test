Snicker-Snack was an enchanted greatsword in the possession of Iggwilv .

Description

It was gold with an ornate hilt and crossguard, purple surfaces on the blade, and a rose-like shape on the hilt.

Powers

This greatsword had a +3 enchantment . In addition, it carried the enchantment of a vorpal blade.
