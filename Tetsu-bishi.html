Tetsu-bishi were weapons unique to the continent of Kara-Tur .

Description

Tetsu-bishi were small caltrops used by ninjas . Each tetsu-bishi was designed as a spiked ball or four-sided diamond about 2 inches (5.1 centimeters) wide, ensuring that, no matter how it landed, at least one spike pointed upwards. These devices were strategically deployed on the ground to create a highly treacherous walking surface, effectively impeding or slowing the movement of both individuals and animals. Their design rendered them ineffective when thrown directly at opponents. Instead, tetsu-bishi were cast onto the ground near pursuers, allowing the ninja to swiftly escape.
