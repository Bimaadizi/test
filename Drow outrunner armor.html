Drow outrunner armor was a kind of leather armor used by drow scouts in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

The armor was light and didn't provide full body coverage, but was bedecked in straps, pouches , and bandoliers .

Powers

The protections imbued in the armor made it exceptionally good at keeping the wearer alive in the face of all sorts of threats, especially magical attacks. It also made them immune to most natural poisons , and resistant to magical ones.

History

Favored by drow scouts, suits of outrunner armor were found in Mistress Laerion'vyr 's drow outpost in the Stonelands .
