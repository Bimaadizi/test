Sung was a Prince of Shou Lung who earned the disfavor of the Emperor.

History

Prince Sung was banished to the Valley of Exiles when he attempted to elope with one of the Emperor's concubines. Only the fact that the Emperor favored him spared Sung from certain execution for his brazen act.

He spent his time in the Valley of Exiles learning what he could about the Emperor and his enemies with the vain hope to overthrow him one day.
