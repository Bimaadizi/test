Sharp-eye Sluck was a goblin archer, as well as a member of the Cult of the Absolute , who lived in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

She wore a crude cuirass of goblin-made scale armor over a simple leather tunic and cloth pants.

Possessions

The shaft of her crude bow was crafted from some manner of bone.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Sluck was among the goblins celebrating the cult's raid on the nearby Waukeen's Rest inn.
