Shangtou was a small farming town in the Ch'ing Tung Province of Shou Lung .

Geography

It stood in the southwest of the province, amongst central hills. A trail led southwest to the city of To'Ming and the Silver Road .

Trade

Focused on farming, Shangtou was well known for producing quality tea and rice .

Notable Locations

A popular martial arts school run by the kensai Loy Ho-dun lay amongst the hills north of Shangtou, though he was hardly ever there to actually teach.
