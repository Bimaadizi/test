The Old lodge was a unique lodge of Rashemaar berserkers based in the town of Taporan as of the mid–14 th century DR .

Base of Operations

The Old lodge's longhouse hugged the waterfront of the River Tir .

Activities

Unlike other berserker lodges, the Old Lodge was heavily involved with running the town of Taporan in the name of the Iron Lord .

Organization

Members of the Old Lodge were extremely skilled in their craft, having mastered the fighting techniques of at least three other berserker lodges before being recruited to join their ranks. The Master of the Old Lodge as of mid–14 th century DR was Jhorrgel the Black .
