Eel Street was a small street found in the coastal town of Aithelar in Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles in Year of the Saddle, 1345 DR .

Location

Rope End Lane passed between the eastern outer edge of Aithelar and ended at Aithe High Street near the center of town.
