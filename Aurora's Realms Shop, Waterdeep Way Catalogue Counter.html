Aurora's Realms Shop, Waterdeep Way Catalogue Counter was an outlet of the Aurora's Emporium chain in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep in the mid–14 th century DR . It was one of six such outlets in the city.

Location

The shop was located on Waterdeep Way very close to its junction with the High Road . It was on the north side of the road next door to the Jade Jug .

Services

This shop sold the various items found in Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue . It also sold a number of magic items , but with expected delivery times of 10–30 days. Home deliveries could be made within the Castle Ward, using the shop's own coach.

Staff

In the mid-1360s DR , the calm and cultured Cathal Sunspear worked here as a counter clerk and the handsome Xanatrar Hillhorn was the service-mage with an eye for ladies. There were also four guards who worked shifts of two and two, plus a team of six delivery people.
