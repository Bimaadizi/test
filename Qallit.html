Qallit was a sahuagin malenti living in the Great Sea near a zaratan around the Corsair Domains in 1367 DR .

Activities

Qallit guarded the island formed by the zaratan's shell from intruders, questioning any who approached. They tried to enlist aid when they were able to help cleanse the isle of a self-proclaimed caliph named Nuri al-Zaratani
