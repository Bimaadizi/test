Loremaster Gustyl "the Curious" was a priest of Oghma . He took on Raegar Stoneblade to unofficially work for him, spying on wizards in Waterdeep , although he forbade Raegar from spying on Maaril the Dragonmage , or anyone from the Wands clan or Blackstaff Tower . Gustly's assistant was Phanar Manthar . Gustyl died suddenly in 1371 DR .
