Mucknuggle was an elderly duergar who worked in Mithral Hall , during the reign of Haerinvureem .

Description

The dwarf had a long gray beard.

Relationships

Mucknuggle was a member of Clan Bukbukken under the command of the shadow dragon Shimmergloom .

Possessions

When on patrol, the guard wore mithral chainmail and fought with a broadsword and shield .

History

In 1356 DR , Mucknuggle encountered Drizzt Do'Urden and Artemis Entreri in the lower tunnels of Mithral Hall. In a later skirmish, Mucknuggle was killed when the assassin put a dagger through his eye when Drizzt showed the dwarf mercy.
