Flaheerta was an inhabitant of the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Flaheerta was a half-elven woman with skin the hue of wood and bronze. She had light hazel eyes and dark brown hair gathered in a bun. She wore red wizardly robes .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Flaheerta could be found in the docks of the Lower City of Baldur's Gate, where commoners could buy fresh fish . The docks were attacked by a band of sahuagin when the area was visited by the Absolute Adversaries , if was unknown if Flaheerta survived the attack.
