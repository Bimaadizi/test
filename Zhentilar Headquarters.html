The Zhentilar Headquarters was the base of operations for the Zhentilar in Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Location

The Headquarters was located in south Zhentil Keep in the central section of the Foreign Quarter along Zhent Avenue .

Interior

A massive armory was located beneath the building. This area also contained cells reserved for soldiers who broke their oaths and citizens suspected of treason.

Inhabitants

Captain Ulgrym commanded the soldiers stationed here as of the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Defenses

750 Zhentilar soldiers were stationed within the headquarters or at a nearby barracks.

History

The building served as an armory before the destruction of the city in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR .
