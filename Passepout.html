Passepout was one of Volothamp Geddarm 's companions during his adventures.

Description

Slightly rotund, the only thing larger than Passespout's appetite was his belief that women were enamored with him. This belief was usually met with humorous results.

Relationships

He was the son of the "famous thespians " Idle and Catinflas .
