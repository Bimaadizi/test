Zelderan Guthel was a grocer inhabiting Waterdeep , the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Zelderan was the guildmaster for the Council of Farmer-Grocers circa 1357 DR . He held the title of "Master Provisioner" within the guild and worked out of the Market Hall , which was their headquarters on Trader's Way in the Castle Ward .
