Gandrel's Aspiration was a unique enchanted heavy crossbow that belonged to the Gur monster hunter named Gandrel in the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

Gandrel's Aspiration possessed a +1 enchantment that guided the bolts towards their targets and increased their damage. The crossbow also possessed magic that made it especially effective against monstrosities .

History

Gandrel named his heavy crossbow after the incident he witnessed at seven years old – a vampire spawn being burned at the stake. In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Gur carried this crossbow while hunting for the vampire spawn named Astarion Ancunín across the Western Heartlands and the city of Baldur's Gate .
