The Parched Mamluk Society , also known as the Parched , was a military organization in Zakhara .

Activities

The Parched was one of the smallest mamluk organizations in Zakhara. They were dedicated to a single task: to ensure the security of Talab 's water supply. They were in charge of maintaining the system of aqueducts that brought the water from the distant Al-Sayaj Mountains along Al-Muti River .

Organization

The Parched were led by Akbara Abd al-Yahruq , the Most Respected Warrior. There was 900 mamluks in the Parched in 1367 DR .

Base of Operations

Talab was the center of operations for the Parched.
