Clan Dankil was a Duergar clan active in Waterdeep during the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

The Dankil clan was known among the dwarven community of Waterdeep to be at odds with the Stonebridge clan because of trade disputes spanning generations. Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Stonebridges freed ghouls from the City of the Dead in an attempt to get revenge at the Dankils.
