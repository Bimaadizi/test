Quietstrike was a unique enchanted sentient short sword located in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Powers

In addition to making it easier for its wielder to strike and harm an opponent, Quietstrike could detect secret doors and detect shifting walls and rooms .

Notable Owners

The sword was carried by Fulbar Hardcheese , a halfling thief and owner of the Happy Cow .
