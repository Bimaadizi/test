The Uldoon Trail was a road running through the Greenfields of the Sword Coast and Western Heartlands . It stretched eastward from Nashkel , through Greenest and Berdusk , before culminating in Asbravn .

Description

The Uldoon Trail was the most direct connection between the roads heading east and the Coast Way running south, which made it a popular caravan route. It also intersected with the Dusk Road at Asbravn, Skuldask Road just south of Berdusk, and crossed the River Chionthar .

By 1489 DR , Greenest was the only sizable town along the Uldoon Trail.

History

The Trail was named after the evil wizard Uldoon , who ruled the realm of Torsil in the lands between the Wood of Sharp Teeth and the Cloud Peaks around the 4 th century DR . Uldoon was eventually slain by an unnamed paladin wielding the holy sword Dornavver , a tale recounted in the popular ballad " Uldoon's Doom ".
