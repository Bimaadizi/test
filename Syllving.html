Fist Syllving was a healer of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Syllving was a gold dwarf woman with a clean-shaven face, light skin, light brown eyes, and strawberry blonde hair. She wore a set of splint mail armor decorated with a tabard embroidered with the Flaming Fist insignia. Fist Syllving wielded a warhammer and defended herself with a shield when on duty.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Syllving was ordered to lend her healing magics to the Fists patrolling the Lower City, along the Wendserpent Street , and near the Manor Gate , accompanied by a group of fellow Fists and a steel watcher .
