Spear of the mammoth was an enchanted spear created by Graendel Granitefist of Graendel's Fine Dwarven Craft in Targos in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

Spear of the mammoth was created from a magic +2 spear and a woolly mammoth 's tusk. The weapon was imbued with the namesake animal's vigor and kinetic force. The finished spear possessed a potent +3 enchantment that guided the wielder's hand towards the target and made the weapon's damage more deadly. Additionally, it boosted the wielder's constitution and allowed the wielder to knock their opponents prone as if they were stricken by a charging mammoth.

History

Graendel Granitefist, in his old age, sought to create a collection of masterwork weapons and armor that would immortalize his dwarven craft. One of these items, forged in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , was a spear of the mammoth .
