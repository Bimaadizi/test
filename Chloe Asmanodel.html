Chloe Asmanodel was a human bard working for the Harpers in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Chloe was part of Harpers' operations against the Zhentarim , Red Wizards of Thay , Many-Arrows , and the Frogmire Coven . She frequently took the role of the squad healer .

Throughout her missions, she partnered with the other agents, including Abelio Mac Gabhann , Drakmau Rockbiter , Grabbleshanks , Lightning-Dancer , Sedonna Sparklebang , Ayur , Adnan Zarehm , Eileen Rumrunner . and Nurith .
