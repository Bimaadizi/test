Bao'ek'nuk was a bugbear that lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

While he made quite the formidable mercenary, Bao'ek'nuk was not known for skills as a conversationalist.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Bao'ek'nuk was hired by the forces of Dror Ragzlin and the cult of the Absolute . He aided in the protection of their makeshift fortress referred to as the Shattered Sanctum .
