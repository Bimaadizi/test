Thloram Flambaertyn was an adventurer of the Brightstar Sash in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He was an older man, with a tufted beard.

Personality

He was easy on the jokes.

Abilities

He made an amazing hotspice stew .

Activities

He was an adventurer.

Relationships

He was a member of the Brightstar Sash .

History

Thloram was already a member of the Brightstar Sash when they recruited the sorceress Caladnei , and he cautioned against letting her join too swiftly, as she was young. She was ultimately allowed to join, and during one of their forays together, the Sash recovered a treasure of at least 6,000 gp . Thloram was also the one to introduce Caladnei to portals .

When Vonda and Rimardo attempted to rob a nobleman's house at Waterdeep only to be captured and slain, Thloram sough out Caladnei and warned her to escape. In their getaway, Thloram had to fight a large, enraged mob.

Thloram Flambaertyn died after a fall in the Great Rift at some point before 1373 DR , and was buried there. With his death, only Marcon remained of the Brightstar Sash at the time Caladnei had joined.
