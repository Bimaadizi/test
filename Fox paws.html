The fox paws were enchanted boots designed to provide both defense and agility. They were sized for a halfling .

Description

The fox paws were metal, and equivalent to the boots in a set of plate mail .

Powers

A wearer of the fox paws was said to be as swift and sure-footed as a fox , allowing them to move about the battlefield at surprising speed and making them harder to hit.
