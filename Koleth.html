Activities

Koleth ran the Tesh-Wave tavern located on the waterfront of Zhentil Keep's Foreign Quarter on the south bank of the River Tesh . He weathered the destruction of Zhentil Keep in the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR , by boarding up the tavern and waiting in the basement until the violence ceased.
