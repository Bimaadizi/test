Flaming orbs , were wizardly crystal orb implements that hid raging inferno inside them, popularized in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

Flaming orbs served the purpose of enhancing a wizard's fire-related magics, making the flames created hotted and damage more severe.

History

In the 15 th century DR, various flaming orbs could be found among spellcasters of the Sword Mountains , areas surrounding the city and markets of Neverwinter .
