Lord Urddusk was the senior lord of house Urddusk of Cormanthyr in the 3 rd century DR .

History

In 261 DR , Lord Urddusk, alongside Lord Haladavar and Lord Malgath solicited the Coronal Eltargrim at his court to reconsider the Opening. They made their argument at swordpoint, which Lord Eltargrim later classed as an "error in judgement". He gained their patience by unveiling the project to raise a mythal .
