Mad Aja's Magic for Fools & Simpletons was a shop located in the Grand Bazaar of Huzuz .

Services/Merchandise

This shop offered rare and magical items for purchase to those who "looked like they needed to be able to do anything." In reality, many of the nicer looking magical items were cursed.

Inhabitants

Aja Pelima , or Mad Aja, ran this shop. He often used displays of magic as a sales pitch to attract customers.
