The Temples District was the largest and perhaps the oldest and most important regions in the entirety of the Upper City of Baldur's Gate . It featured some of the city's greatest buildings and landmarks that were famous across the Sword Coast . It housed the grand Ducal Palace , which served as the seat of power for the ruling Grand Dukes , the vast Gondar temple-workshop known as High House of Wonders , along with the museum that proudly displayed the creations of Baldurian Gondsmen , the Hall of Wonders .
