Batu , who was nicknamed the Lame , was a civilized nomad who lived in Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR .

Activities

Batu lived in a large dwelling located just outside of northeast Almorel. He earned a living by offering his services as a skilled guide for the region.
