Pavel was a solider of the Flaming Fist of that fought for the city of Baldur's Gate and the surrounding lands during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Pavel had dark skin and short gray hair with accompanying beard. He wore leather armor emblazoned with the logo of the Flaming Fist.

History

He was among the Flaming Fist soldiers present at the Waukeen's Rest inn when it put to the torch by goblin and drow cultists of the Absolute , the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . Pavel joined fellow soldiers Ito , Aelis , and Gauntlet Yeva in their attempt to break through the front entrance of the inn, to rescue Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard who was believed to be trapped within.
