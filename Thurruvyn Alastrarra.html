Thurruvyn Alastrarra was an ancestor of the Alastrarra house of Cormanthyr by the 3 rd century DR .

Legacy

On the year 241 DR , Naeryndam Alastrarra named Thurruvyn's honor as a reason to respect Elminster 's claim to the protection of Coronal Eltargrim 's law, opposing the wishes of his house in doing so.
