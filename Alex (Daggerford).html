Alex was one of the inhabitants of Daggerford who died and was buried long before 1372 DR .

History

After his death, Alex was buried in the Daggerford Graveyard just outside the walls of the city in the Eastern Farmlands . His humble headstone was located in the south-eastern part of the graveyard. Even though the engraving on his headstone suggester otherwise, Alex was not back as of 1372 DR.
