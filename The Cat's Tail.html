The Cat's Tail was an alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

The Cat's Tail was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . The alley branched off of the southern end of Cat Alley in between the Street of Bells , the High Road , and Waterdeep Way .
