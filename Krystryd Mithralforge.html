Krystryd Mithralforge was the dwarf blacksmith of Quaervarr in 1484 DR .

History

After Highharvestide of 1484 DR, a supposed phantom started appearing in the village. One night, Krystryd saw the phantom and was greatly scared. However, she was more worried about rumors she heard from her kin in Mithral Hall about orc movements.
