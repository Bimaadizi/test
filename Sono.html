Sono was a servant in the town of Aru in Wa .

Personality

Sono was totally loyal to his daimyo but also in love with Buntaro Ostato .

Activities

Sono was a servant in the house of the daimyo Benju Matsutomo . She had talent playing the samisen and often played for Benju's guests.
