The Wavesilver ( pronounced : /ˈ w eɪ v s ɪ l v ər / WAYV-sil-vər ) family were members of the nobility in Waterdeep circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR . They were primarily in the merchant shipping business.

Organization

The Wavesilvers were a wealthy family that made their fortune shipping goods in and out of Waterdeep. From at least the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , to at least the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , the family was led by Lord Bleskos with his consort Apryl . The designated heir was their second son, Aristed Wavesilver .

Base of Operations

The Wavesilver Villa was located in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep on the southwest corner of Ivory Street and Flint Street . Across Ivory Street to the north was the Blue Alley , a few doors to the east was the abode of Myrna Cassalanter , and the Tower of Luck was a short walk to the west. Just to the south, down Flint Street, was the House of Wonder .

History

The Wavesilvers were of Chondathan descent and the family was ennobled in the Year of the Wandering Winds, 1251 DR .
