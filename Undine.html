Undine was a Netherese arcanist and spell inventor who lived during the Age of Discovery .

History

The variator developed five spells during their life, all within a four-year period. The spells they invented were avoidance and hold portal in 2938 NY (−921 DR) , rope trick in 2939 NY (−920 DR) , phase door in 2940 NY (−919 DR) , and finally, deeppockets in 2941 NY (−918 DR) .
