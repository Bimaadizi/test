Duskview House was an inn outside Triel during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

Dusvkiew house was located atop a knoll, overlooking Dusk Road .

Interior

The place was newly built as of 1357 DR , and had a lounge that was downright palatial. The lounge's ceiling was whitedaub, with a dragon motif , and had holes, most likely for peeping.

Services

According to Arauntar , the house was highcoin, that is, expensive, and was likely to sell foodstuffs. The stewards there served alcohol, amongst other amenities. Orthil thought people were almost certainly staying there, strongly implying the place was an inn.

History

The place was newly built as of 1357 DR .

Late that year, Orthil Voldovan 's caravan stopped there, looking for reinforcements after suffering a series of ambushes on its way from Scornubel to Waterdeep . While he was there, Voldovan was slain and replaced by Thavaun , a Red Wizard .

Rumors & Legends

It was said that the inn was raised by Thayan mages.
