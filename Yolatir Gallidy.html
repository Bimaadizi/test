Lady Yolatir Gallidy was the ruler of Two Stars as of 1372 DR . She ruled with a light touch.

Personality

In her opinion, just about anything was okay so long as no one got hurt. She was stubbornly good-hearted, but she also had proven unable to resist allowing a Thayan enclave in her city.

Activities

In 1372 DR , she was struggling with an incursion of the Shadowmasters of Telflamm . She taxed only wares entering the town. She continued to rule the town in 1373 DR .

Relationships

Lady Yolatir Gallidy was the latest in a long line of Gallidys, which started with Prasso Gallidy , the town's founder, legendary in 1372. Her cousin, Shanton Gallidy , had also created the largest temple to Shaundakul that year. Per Elowen , she was a favourite of the guilds.
