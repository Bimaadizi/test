Mairara was a Zhentarim apprentice in the mid-14 th century DR .

Description

She kept her glossy black hair long. Her hands were clawed.

Personality

Mairara was treacherous, ambitious, and quite arrogant, doubting the power of spellfire and of her master's servants both. However, she wanted to prove herself, and expected no more mercy than she gave. For whatever reason, she misliked the idea of lovers being driven against each other, or perhaps, her master's more bloodthirsty side.

Abilities

She knew at least one spell of lightning that struck many from a distance. She also knew a spell that converted a dead gargoyle into two fresh ones. These gargoyles were under her personal control.

Relationships

She was apprenticed to Gathlarue . The two were romantically involved, and had engaged in sadomasochist play.

History

As of late 1357 DR , Mairara was with Gathlarue and Tespril at Marsember with sixteen Zhentilar as an escort when they set out to capture Shandril Shessair . The group scried on Shandril all the way, until they moved personally at Irondrake Rock .

During the battle, Mairara employed a spell that converted a shattered gargoyle into two gargoyles, both of which she controlled. She was annihilated with spellfire during the battle.
