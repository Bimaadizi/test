Cirid was a human Nentyar hunter in the service of the Nentyarch in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

She dressed in a green gown, and carried a greatsword in a white sheath at her waist.

History

Though the Nentyarch had assigned her to accompany Marrec and Elowen 's party in their quest to confront the Rotting Man in the Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR , Fallon 's betrayal forced the party to depart posthaste, without her.
