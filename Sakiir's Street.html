Sakiir's Street was a road located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Sakiir's Street was found in southern Waterdeep within the Dock Ward and Castle Ward . It originated on Soothsayer's Way just south of the feet of Mount Waterdeep . The road passed south to end at Fishgut Court and Sail Street . Along its path Sakiir's Street was intersected by Lackpurse Lane , Rainrun Street , Belnimbra's Street , and the Slide .
