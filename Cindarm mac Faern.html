Cindarm mac Faern was a shield dwarf man of the dwarven city of Earthfast in the Earthfast Mountains of north Faerûn , in the Earthroot region of the Underdark

History

Cindarm was the son of Faern , and the grandnephew of Torg mac Cei , the ironlord of Earthfast.

In the late 1360s DR , Cindarm led veteran Clangeddite warriors in defense of Alagh Rorncaurak , the temple-cavern of Clangeddin Silverbeard , against overwhelming numbers of orcs .
