An garnok was a type of blowpipe used by Angulutiun and Iulutiun hunters.

Description

A garnok was a rod made from bone that was about 1 foot (30 centimeters) in length. It featured a leather thong at one end and a small cup at the other. It was employed for propelling small bone darts, typically measuring 3 inches (0.076 meters) or less in length.
