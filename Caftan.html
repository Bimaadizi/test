A caftan was a long ankle-length garment worn in Zakhara .

Description

The robe was usually made with cotton, but finer versions could be crafted from silk or brocade. When worn, a sash was used as a belt around the waist. Nearly every woman in Calimshan would dress in a caftan. A typical caftan had a base value of 1 gp, but more elaborate ones could cost up to 20 gp.
