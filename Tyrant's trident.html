Tyrant's trident was a trident that belonged to Tarnheel Embuirhan .

Description

The trident was named for its owner, Tarnheel "Tyrant" Embuirhan . It had a +3 enchantment.

History

In 1358 DR , the Zhentarim tried to dominate the Lords' Alliance and gain control of the Sword Coast . Part of this involved turning Mintarn against Waterdeep . The Heroes of Ascore foiled the Zhentarim plot, and Mintarn's ruler was grateful. In return, he bestowed upon them his enchanted trident, as well as several coins.
