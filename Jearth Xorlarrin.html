Jearth Xorlarrin was the weapons master of House Xorlarrin . He was one of the most powerful weapons masters in the drow city of Menzoberranzan in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Jearth accompanied the expedition led by Ravel Xorlarrin to Gauntlgrym in the Year of the Reborn Hero, 1463 DR .

In the Year of the Awakened Sleepers, 1484 DR , he was killed by the dwarves .

Relationships

Ravel considered Jearth his closest ally.
