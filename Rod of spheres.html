A rod of spheres was a magical rod that could create a magical sphere that floated above the ground and could be used to transport items.

Powers

The rod allowed its wielder to create up to three spheres per day. These conjurations were four feet (1.2m) in diameter, could be used to carry items up to 200lbs (91kg) and floated a few feet off the ground, following their creator for up to two hours until they disappeared or were attacked. When the sphere was dissipated, the items within a destroyed sphere would float to the ground just like they were affected by a feather fall spell .

Creation

The first rod of spheres was believed to have been created by the archmage Elminster , though many have been created since. The spells Feather fall and Tenser's floating disk were used in its crafting.
