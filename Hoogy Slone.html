Hoogy Slone was a human inhabitant of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Hoogy was a dark-skinned human man with straight black hair he wore in a ponytail. He wore tan-colored finery.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Hoogy could be found on a plaza in the Lower City near the Basilisk Gate .
