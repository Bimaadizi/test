A Tenday's Prayers to Gond was customary prayer dedicated to the deity Gond Wonderbringer, adopted sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

Content

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , A Tenday's Prayers to Gond was used by the Gondar of the city of Baldur's Gate and the workers of the Steel Watch Foundry .
