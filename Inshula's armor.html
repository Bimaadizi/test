Inshula's armor was a unique set of light armor worn by Inshula sar Mashewe , a mercenary hunter from the nation of Tashalar active in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

This light armor was custom-tailored to fit Inshula's lanky, muscular form. It was enchanted to improve the wearer's defense and was especially effective against beast attacks.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , the armor accompanied its owner on her travels from Tashluta to the jungles of Samarach and whilst working with Sword Coast adventurers for the merchant Sa'Sani of Samargol .
