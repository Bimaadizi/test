Seven Songs Importing was a shop in Athkatla 's rich Temple District in the 14 th century DR .

Location

Seven Songs Importing stood across from the famed Crown Aflame theater of Athkatla on the crossing of the Street of Seven Altars , Oblade Street , and Aundle's Walk . The shop's next-door neighbor to the west was the home of Marimmal Theer , an actor and musician of the late 14 th century DR.

Activities

The shop dealt in little physical goods, instead, it procured a variety of rare and exotic goods to order.
