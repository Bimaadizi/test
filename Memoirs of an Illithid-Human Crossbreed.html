Memoirs of an Illithid-Human Crossbreed was a book written by Naneetha Shinalstar , War-Mage of Saelmur on the Lake of Steam , and published in the Year of the Harp, 1355 DR .

Contents

It was a biography of Naneetha's life and exploits, including her love of the realm of Turmish .

History

The original was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep around 1372 DR .
