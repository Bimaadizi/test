The Fall of Stars was a tavern found in Myth Drannor during the city's glory days, before the 8 th century DR .

Location

It was located 75 ft (23 m) above ground level in the Sheshyrinnam district of the city, just east of the Westfields .

Structure

This two-story tavern was constructed within the boughs of an enormous shadowtop tree. It was named for the means by which patrons could enter the establishment. Within the floor of Stars' taproom was a 20 ft (6.1 m) wide hole, beneath which was a magical elevator that allowed people to ascend within.
