The Fair Venture was a caravel that operated in the water around Sembia during the mid-14 th century DR . It was owned by the merchant Golthond of Thesk .

History

In Flamerule of 1357 DR , after leaving port in Sembia the Fair Venture went missing while passing by the Pirate Isles . In response, Golthond sought to hire a group of mercenaries to clear the islands of the raiding sea-bandits.
