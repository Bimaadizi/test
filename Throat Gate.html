The Throat Gate was the eastern entry point into lower half of the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid–14 th century DR .

Location

The Throat Trail Gate sat on the eastern side of the Foreign Quarter on the southern bank of the River Tesh . It was situated on the Throat where the road entered the Foreign Quarter to connect with Bridge Street .

Defenses

The gate was flanked by a pair of guard towers. Ten to fifteen Zhentilar were stationed at each tower.

Activities

The Throat Gate provided access to the Foreign Quarter from the east for land-based traffic, mostly from Yûlash .
