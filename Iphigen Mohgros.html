Iphigen Mohgros was a theologian and a writer, who authored Judge of the Damned sometime in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

Iphigen penned his book on the strange occurrences of mortals who reached apotheosis, their mortal lives and divine existence, including the Lords of Death Kelemvor , sometime after the Year of the Banner, 1368 DR and before the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR . The book remained in circulation as late as the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , with a copy stored in the library of the Stormshore Tabernacle of Baldur's Gate .
