Amarok the Wolf was a powerful primal spirit venerated not unlike a deity in parts of Toril . He was a source of primal magic for his followers.

History

As of the late 15 th century DR , Amarok was worshiped in the realm of Sarifal on the Moonshae Isles alongside many other powerful spirits and archfey .
