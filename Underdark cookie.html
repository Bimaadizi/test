Underdark cookies were dark, rich, and decadent sweet treats from the city of Neverwinter .

Powers

These cookies were known not only to satisfy one's sweet tooth, but also gave a small boost to vitality.

History

In the mid-to-late-15 th century DR , underdark cookies became available during the extended celebration of Liar's Night , extended into the grand Masquerade of Liars by the order of Lord Neverember.
