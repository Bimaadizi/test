Disruption was a weapon quality that could be added to magical weapons. It could destroy undead outright.

Powers

Whenever an undead foe was struck with a disruption weapon, it had to successfully resist the effect with an effort of will or be destroyed instantly.

Creation

One who would enchant a disruption weapon had to have the heal spell available. The property could only be used on a bludgeoning weapon, such as a club or mace .

Uses

Some veteran Thayan gladiators had their horns, claws, and other natural weapons enhanced as weapons of disruption .
