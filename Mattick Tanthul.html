Prince Mattick Tanthul of Thultanthar , the City of Shade, was the twin brother of Prince Vattick Tanthul .

Relationships

He was two minutes younger than his brother. Both had black hair and eyes and were perfect replicas of one another—only their parents could distinguish one from the other. Mattick and Vattick used this to perpetrate innumerable conspiracies and jokes, some benign and others much less so, on the royal household since their early childhood. They were inseparable and were rarely found at great distance from one another. Mattick and Vattick divided their time between research at the academy of alchemy and helping Brennus Tanthul seek out clues to the locations of old Netherese sites.

Personality

Mattick and Vattick sided with Brennus and Hadrhune in supporting the swift destruction off all threats to Shade´s rule.

Lore

Prince Mattick's personal sigil was a pair of interlocking gold rings, outlined in purple on a charcoal gray field.
