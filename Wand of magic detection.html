A wand of magic detection was a magical wand that had the power to detect the typical magical auras emanated by spells and magic items.

Powers

The wielder of a wand of magic detection could activate it to cast the detect magic spell up to three times a day. The wand gradually recovered its power at each successive dawn. Cheaper variations of the wand could be used an unlimited number of times per day, but expired after 50 uses total

Notable Owners

The githzerai zerth Yrlakka owned a wand of magic detection .
