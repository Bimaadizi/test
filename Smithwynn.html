Fist Smithwynn was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Smithwynn was a human man with a clean-shaven face, blue eyes, and a light skin tone. He wore a set of chainmail armor as a part of her Flaming Fist uniform decorated with a tabard that bore the company's banner, and was armed with a spear and a shield .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Fist Smithwynn was stationed within the barracks of the Heapside district of Baldur's Gate, where he, a steel watcher , and several other Flaming Fists guarded a small dungeon prison.
