The Order of the Hammer were a Cormyrean adventuring company who venerated Torm , that operated in the mid-14 th century DR . On 5 Alturiak of the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR , they perished on a mission on behalf of Cormyr . Their service was commemorated by a memorial plaque in the office of Registrant General Lord Partic Thistle in the Royal Court of Suzail .
