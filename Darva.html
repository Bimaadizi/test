Darva was a member of the ruling triumvirate in Fireshear in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR , sometime after the War of the Silver Marches , Darva was the leader of the local branch of the Silver Triangle , a merchant company based in Neverwinter . Triumvirate members served until they died or resigned.

Darva was one of the leaders of the town when it was attacked by a marauding party of twenty frost giants , who arrived in a frost giant greatship .

Personality

Along with the other members of the ruling triumvirate, Darva was known to accept bribes, exchanging ore and coin for more and better supplies, whose distribution they controlled.
