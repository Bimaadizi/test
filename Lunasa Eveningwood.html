Lunasa Eveningwood was an elf bard in Cormyr during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Lunasa had bright orange backswept hair and gray eyes.

History

Lunasa camped at the ruins of Beckside Castle on the High Road around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar . She sang with her companion Merlin Fastfoot , including Here's a Health to the Company , The Mermaid , and The Crafty Maid's Policy .
