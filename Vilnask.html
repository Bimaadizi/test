Vilnask was a small mining town and a caravan stop on the High Road on the Sword Coast North in the mid–14 th century DR .

Geography

Vilnask stood between the city of Neverwinter and Leilon along the High Road.

Trade

The town made business by mining raw silver , iron , and mithral as well as providing smithing services. Additionally, like other small towns, Vilnask also had an inn , jeweler, and an arms shop.

History

Founded in the early 14 th century DR , after a great supply of valuable ores were discovered in a cave south of Neverwinter by an adventurer for whom the town was named after. Following the discovery, for the next several winters, miners, smiths, and merchants from nearby regions came to settle in Vilnask where these precious metals were found.

By the mid–14 th century DR , Vilnask was known for the fine metal goods it produced.
