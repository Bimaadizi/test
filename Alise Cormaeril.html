Lady Alise Cormaeril was a member of the noble Cormaeril family of Cormyr in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Lady Alise attended King Azoun Obarskyr IV 's birthday feast, hosted by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Keep . She was accompanied there by Lord Barrit Cormaeril .
