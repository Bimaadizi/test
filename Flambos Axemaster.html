Flambos Axemaster was a ranger and follower of Mielikki inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Flambos enjoyed exploring the various taverns and inns , both lavish and squalid, within the entire expanse of Waterdeep. He roamed the High Moor , ridding the area of wicked creatures until he required supplies or healing, at which point he returned to the City of Splendors. He would offer his sword or services as a guide to kindly travelers for 1 sp per day and a share of any treasure acquired during their adventures. Flambos tithed a fair amount of treasure to the shrine of Mielikki in Waterdeep.

Possessions

He wielded a longsword , battleaxe , and longbow .
