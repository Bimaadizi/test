Clever Nala was a dragonborn of Abeir , and an ancestor of Clan Verthisathurgiesh .

History

Nala was a slave of the dragon Morthongiarimyth .

One day, Nala freed some dragonborn families from slavery, including her own, by using light tricks to scare Morthongiarimyth until he went mad.

Eventually, Nala became one of the most revered ancestors of Clan Verthisathurgiesh.
