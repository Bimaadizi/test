The cryohydra ( pl : cryohydrae or : cryohydras ) was a relative of the standard hydra that was able to breathe frost from its heads.

Ecology

Habitats

Cryohydras were known to inhabit the Umber Marshes in Aglarond and the arctic plains of the Hordelands .

A mated pair of cryohydras preyed on the people of Pilver's Creek , on the coast of the Moonsea , sometime in the mid-14 th century DR .
