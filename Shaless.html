Shaless was a woman of the city of Tsurlagol in the Vast . She was an adventurer .

In Nightal of the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , she made a triumphant return to The Drunken Dragon , favorite tavern in Tsurlagol, and there displayed a selection of curious harps and beautiful, magical blades . According to her, she had explored the ruins of Myth Drannor , battled devils , and pulled the treasures from their claws.

Maerhult , a bard also visiting the tavern, examined two of her harps and pronounced them to be genuine works of old Myth Drannor, and to be rare magical items.
