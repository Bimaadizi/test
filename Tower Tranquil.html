Tower Tranquil was a mage's tower in the Dalelands . It stood just north of Oversember Vale .

Location

Tower Tranquil stood atop the gateway to the cavern of the dracolich Rauglothgor . A gate once linked the tower to a cavern in Myth Drannor .

History

Tower Tranquil was built by the mage Jhavanter of Highmoon .

Shandril Shessair destroyed the tower in 1356 DR with spellfire .
