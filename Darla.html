Darla was a young thief in Neverwinter .

Activities

Darla was one the leaders of the Little Rats , a gang of children affiliated with the Dead Rats gang who acted as spies and messengers.

History

In 1479 DR , she approached an adventuring party that was investigating the kidnapping cases. She offered vital information that connected an Ashmadai cell to Elden Vargas .
