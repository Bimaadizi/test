Hafiz bin Watat was a gnome wizard who lived in the Sword Coast during the mid–14 th century DR . In addition to being a spellcaster , he was also a seer , scientist, and astrologer .

When Hafiz met Gorion's Ward in 1368 DR , he said that he had met them previously in their dreams. He also offered them some cryptic advice about a future menace .
