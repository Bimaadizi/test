Salene Freig was a broadsheet delivery clerk employed by Baldur's Mouth in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Salene was a human woman with a light tan complexion. The woman had long, dark brown hair, and eyes of the same color. She wore simple garb of a common delivery woman.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Salene worked on managing the delivery of freshly printed Baldur's Mouth Gazette bundles. During the Absolute crisis , she could be often be found within Baldur's Mouth's offices in Heapside of the Lower City .
