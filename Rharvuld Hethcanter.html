Rharvuld Hethcanter was a renowned furniture maker from Immersea and the father of Paresper Hethcanter , active in the late 15 th century DR .

History

Rharvuld's superb skills made him a highly sought-after furniture maker by the late 15 th century DR. His wardrobes were all the rage among wealthy noblewomen of Suzail , the capital of Cormyr. Rharvuld Hethcanter had some undisclosed connection to the inquisitive War Wizards , but the furniture maker kept mum on the subject.
