Gha'an was a githyanki child from Crèche Y'llek on Toril in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Gha'an had a shaved head and was dressed in leather pants and pauldrons of githyanki design.

History

Githyanki of what would become Crèche Y'llek, attacked and claimed the Rosymorn Monastery of Lathander in the Western Heartlands sometime in the late 15 th century DR . Gha'an was hatched in the Crèche and, along with other gith children, attended military and combat training around the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis.
