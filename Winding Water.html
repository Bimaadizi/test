The Winding Water was a river located along the Sword Coast , roughly 250 miles north of Baldur's Gate and the River Chionthar . It flowed from several tributaries, some as far away as the Marsh of Chelimber , and eventually flowed into the Sea of Swords south of the Troll Hills .

Notable locations along the river were Boareskyr Bridge , Trollclaw Ford , and Serpent's Cowl . The river also formed the northern boundary of the Fields of the Dead .
