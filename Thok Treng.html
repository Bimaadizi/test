Thok Treng was a town in Laothan in Malatra .

Geography

It lay on Laothan's northern coast, on the shore of the Eastern Sea .

Society

This town was where the House of Thok was born —the family of the monarch, Thok Lian —and it remained full of its supporters.

Economy

In the mid–14 th century DR , Thok Treng enjoyed some small trade concessions, thanks to its connection to the reigning Thok clan, helping it prosper.

History

At that time, ninja in service to Prince Nuyen Phi of Sayan were assassinating certain persons who might prove problematic to his plans. They made the murders look like accidents, so the authorities were yet to grow suspicious.
