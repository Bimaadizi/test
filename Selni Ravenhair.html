Selni Ravenhair was one of the owners of Jendalar's Fine Fruits in Archenbridge during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Selni was the consort and business partner of Alduvar Snowbrand . She was one of the very few people to know of Alduvar's identity as the Blue Sword of Archendale , and often took care of the shop without him.
