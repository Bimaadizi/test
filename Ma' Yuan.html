Ma' Yuan Province (also spelled Mai Yuan Province ) was a province of the empire of Shou Lung in Kara-Tur by the 14th century DR . It was also called the Plain of the Horse . In ancient times, it was a province of Anok-Imaskar and later an independent realm in its own right, called simply Mai Yuan .

History

Ancient History

By around −2300 DR , the land was a part of the empire of Anok-Imaskar . The empire collapsed in −1943 DR , fracturing into a number of warring states, among them the new independent realm called Mai Yuan.
