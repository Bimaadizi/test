Galfriel Jegg'Dralnoc was a priestess of Lolth , the last of House Jegg'Dralnoc , who wandered the Underdark during the 15 th century DR .

Description

Galfriel had long thick white hair, hanging loose with some tied back in a ponytail. She wore a fashionable chain shirt that bared her midriff along with a skirt and armored leggings.

Abilities

As a proficient priestess, Galfriel could cast fear , cometfall , and divine punishment .

History

During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar , Galfriel was left exiled in the Underdark, as House Szind had destroyed her home and family. She beseeched Lolth at a small shrine near the Boneyard , praying for guidance in winning a victory over the hated Szind.
