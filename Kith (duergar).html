Stonemason Kith was a craftsman of Clan Flameshade that took up residence in Grymforge for a time during the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Kith was a compassionate dwarf —at least compared to other duergar from his clan—that was wholly dedicated to his craft as a stone mason.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the duergar of Clan Flameshade began an excavation of Grymforge with the aim of staking claim on an adamantine forge . While his fellow clansman followed the orders Nere and the cult of the Absolute , Kith spent his time studying the ruined architecture of the underground fortress, hoping to learn more about its history and downfall.
