Gost Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Gost .

Location

The villa was located in the southwest section of Waterdeep 's North Ward . It sat on the southwest corner of Gelthoon Street where it met Golden Serpent Street .

Description

Two buildings faced each other from opposite sides of a garden within the walled compound. The buildings were three and four stories tall.
