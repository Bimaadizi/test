Rowland Cymrych , known as " the Boy King " was High King of the Moonshae Isles for a brief time in the mid–7 th century DR .

Relationships

He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the son of his predecessor, High King Alec I . His successor was his uncle, Samuel .

History

Rowland assumed the throne of the High King in the Year of the Fanged Beast, 640 DR , following the death of his father. He was fourteen years old.

He ruled for only two years before dying of illness in the Year of the Sifting Sands, 642 DR .
