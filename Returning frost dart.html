Returning frost darts were enchanted thrown weapons that flew back to the thrower's hand after reaching their targets.

Powers

These weapons were small enough to conceal them easily. Each dart bore markings of engraved words and lines of power. When they reached the target, returning frost darts had a chance of dealing magical cold damage and returned to the thrower's hand.

History

In the Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , returning frost dart were used by Aurilite Stormsisters and postulants who joined the Legion of the Chimera to bring destruction on the Ten Towns and Kuldahar of Icewind Dale .
