Storna Harnwood was a priest of Shar and leader of the Night Brigade gang in Wheloon Prison in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Storna had pale skin, gray eyes, and long black hair.

Personality

Storna was wholly devoted to Shar and the tenets of the faith. She was cold, dispassionate, and ruthless in her goals. While a powerful and wealthy gang leader, she considered the material boons secondary to the glorification of Shar.

Possessions

Storna wore a suit of black half-plate and carried a round small shield and ornate mace . She accessorized with a special pendant and a circlet of Shar .

History

Storna led the Night Brigade from strength to strength through ruthlessness and guile. After years of work, their power was solidified when they attacked and nearly destroyed the far less militant Riverside Rats gang of wererats , taking their hideout and transforming it into a Temple of Shar . The few survivors escaped through the Wererat's Entrance to the exterior of the prison, where they recruited some opportunistic allies to lead the counterattack. After the slaying of her lieutenants Master Thief Karissa , Battlemaster Agarta , and her Grand Arcanist Darstil Vurusk , Garmor the Grim set a mission to finish off Storna and destroy the gang once and for all.
