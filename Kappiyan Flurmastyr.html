Kappiyan Flurmastyr ( pronounced : /ˈ k ɑː p p i j ɑː n ˈ f l ʌr m æs t ʌr / KAPP-ee-yan FLURR-mass-turr ) was a wizard and loremaster in Waterdeep in 1372 DR .

Activities

Together with his wife, Kappiyan sold potions in order to fund his Mystra -blessed magic research. However, he did not like repeat customers who interrupted his studies more than once in a few months.

Relationships

Circa 1372 DR, Kappiyan married his long-time apprentice Shalara Malarrkin
