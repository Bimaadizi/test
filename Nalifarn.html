Nalifarn was an durzagon alchemist and chieftain around 1485 DR .

History

Nalifarn led a clan of duergar . In 1485 DR, he was approached by the pit fiend Baazka , who offered an alliance with his Red Wizard masters. Nalifarn accepted and, with the Red Wizards' help, took control of the Firehammer Hold . In exchange, the durzagon started manufacturing weapons for the Thayans . Nalifarn planned to make the Hold a permanent duergar outpost.

Later, he was killed by an adventuring party hired to search for Jekk Ironfist .
