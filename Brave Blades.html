The Brave Blades were an adventuring band in the early 3 rd century DR .

Organization

The leader was Tarthe Maermir .

Activities

The Brave Blades adventured mostly in the lands north and west of Athalantar .

History

The Brave Blades were an adventuring band in the early 3 rd century DR . In the Year of the Leaping Centaur, 232 DR , their wizard Lhangaern , accidentally killed himself with a potion he brewed; he was replaced by a priestess, Elmara (secretly Prince Elminster Aumar of Athalantar ). On the Year of Much Ale, 233 DR , they fought and slew the Bright Shields band of adventurers in Baerlith , several of their numbers dying in the confrontation.

Later, they adventured into the Floating Tower . Everyone in the band made it to Ondil's Tomb , but only Elmara survived.
