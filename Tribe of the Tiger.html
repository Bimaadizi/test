The Tribe of the Tiger was a nomadic group of Reghedman barbarians living in the tundra of Icewind Dale .

Like their fellow Tribes of the Bear and Elk , the Tribe of the Tiger lived in relative peace with the folks of the Ten Towns .

History

By the time of the War of the Silver Marches in the late 15 th century DR , the Aurilian Bjornhild Solvigsdottir had become queen of the Tiger tribe. She assumed the title after her husband, King Korold , was slain by a frost giant .
