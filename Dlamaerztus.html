Dlamaerztus was a Red Wizard of Thay in the mid-14 th century DR .

Relationships

He was part of a cabal together with Thaltar Glaervar , Norlarram , Quaerlesz , Iyrtaryld , Olorus , and six other Red Wizards .

History

In 1369 DR , Dlamaerztus was attending a meeting with several of his fellow Red Wizards, where Iyrtaryld demonstrated an illusion of his newest magical development, the Hungry Mouth . While he got some feedback on it, the table at which they sat was hurled upwards by a colossal entity made of stone which smashed Dlamaerztus, killing him instantly. That was the Simbul 's doing.
