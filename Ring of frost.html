A ring of frost was a magical ring capable of producing frost.

Powers

A ring of frost functioned like a wand of frost , though it had an unlimited number of charges.

History

Circa 1369 DR , Ascalan , a retired wizard and fishmonger of The Fish House in Wheloon , Cormyr , owned a ring of frost . He used it to refrigerate fish and create blocks of ice for sale.
