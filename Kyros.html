Kyros was one of ten guards stationed at the enclave run by the young Red Wizard Hinnar .

Abilities

Kyros was tough, strong, and dexterous.

Possessions

Like the other guards at the enclave, Kyros was trained in the expert use of the bastard sword and possessed a finely crafted example. Kyros also carried a longspear and a composite longbow and was trained in ranged combat at short range.

Activities

Kyros worked in alternating shifts guarding the six watch platforms, observing the sales booth, or patroling the compound grounds.

History

When adventures came to the enclave, seeking information about the kidnapping of Talf and Elonn , Kyros was completely ignorant of the fact that the two young men had been taken by the wizard Kizzaf .
