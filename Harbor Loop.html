The Harbor Loop was a small rounded court found in the city of Westgate .

Location

The Harbor Loop was found in the eastern section of the city. It began and ended along Silverpiece Way , looping toward Westgate's harbor. The small street known as the Wizard's Cut connected to the middle of the Harbor Loop. Several prominent families, including the Cormaeril 's and Ssemm 's, owned docks touching the street.
