The winter solstice was a magical ring found in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

The ring embodied the merciless power of the moon and channeled the deadly might of winter and the solstice . It helped the wearer dodge and resist all sorts of threats, and gave them a certain remorseless cunning that worked well in both combat trickery and conversational deception. It carried a biting cold that inflicted itself upon enemies, and at times a freezing gale engulfed the battlefield to destroy whole groups of foes.

History

The ring was found in the old House of Gems when the Elves of Cormanthor searched for the phylactery of the lich Droic Orgul .
