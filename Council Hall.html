Council Hall was the monthly meeting place of the Royal Council of Aglarond , advisers to the Simbul , around 1368 DR .

Location

This building was situated at an elevated position in Velprintalar , overlooking the rest of the city.

Description

Council Hall was built in a typical Aglarondan style, being constructed of weathered wood and featuring steep roofs, chimneys and cupolas. Originally built to house but a singular meeting room, the hall was expanded over the years until it contained many guest rooms, kitchens, and other rooms in addition to the meeting rooms.
