Xulbraddon Ahmaer was a powerful wizard who lived in Elversult in 1358 DR .

Description

This mysterious wizard was bald and his dusky skin hinted at his southern heritage.

Activities

Many of the spells Xulbraddon cast where unknown in the Inner Sea lands . He was also known to have dealings with elementals .
