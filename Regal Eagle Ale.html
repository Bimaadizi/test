Regal Eagle Ale was a variety of magical ale available in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 th century DR .

History

The enchanted beverage was on Yawning Portal tavern's menu in Waterdeep with its bottles stashed away in Durnan 's cabinet. One serving of Regal Eagle Ale was priced at ten gold coins .
