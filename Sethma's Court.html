Sethma's Court was a cobbled courtyard that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Sethma's Court was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Southern Ward . The courtyard sat directly southwest of Tulmaster's Street .

History

The court was named for Sethma , an old lady who lived here during the early 14 th century DR. For unknown reasons Sethma's Court was a gathering place for all kinds of birds flying over the city.
