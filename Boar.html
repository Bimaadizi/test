Boars were wild relatives of the domestic pig . They were favored animals of the deities Gruumsh , Gwaeron Windstrom , Ilneval , Luthic , Rillifane Rallathil , Shargaas , and Yurtrus .

Description

Boars were covered in thick fur, often brown or grayish in color. The exact size of a boar varied from species to species, from roughly human size to roughly the mass of an ogre or similarly sized creature. All varieties of boars were armed with sharp tusks that jutted out from beneath their snout and which were capable of causing serious injury. Boars also had a highly developed sense of smell.

Behavior

Though not predatory by nature, boars were ill-tempered and territorial, a combination made worse by boars' powerful musculature and sharp tusks.

Ecology

Boars usually traveled in groups known as sounders .

Habitats

Boars were found in most locales across the world, from chilly tundras to hot jungles.

In north Faerûn , wild boars inhabited the medium woods of Cormanthor , Daggerdale , and Shadowdale . Particularly the Border Forest and Vale of Lost Voices . They could also be found in the Marsh of Chelimber , the forests of Mistledale , around the Pool of Yeven , and in the Ardeep Forest . They were found throughout the Savage Frontier .

In interior Faerûn found in Cormyr , while in west Faerûn they could be found in Tethyr and in Amn , where they were so common along the Trade Way near the Battlescarred Bard inn that some believed a local landowner must have been using a deepspawn to create them. They were also particularly common throughout the forests of the Vast . In the Unapproachable East , they could be found in the Forest of Lethyr and Yuirwood . In the Hordelands , they could be found on the Katakoro Plateau High Meadows , and Central Steppe . In the land of Anauroch , they could be found in the fertile reaches of the Plain of Standing Stones as well as the bordering Desertsmouth Mountains .

Beyond Faerûn they could be found on the island of Evermeet , where they were non-hostile towards elves and occasionally hunted by gold elves . On the continent of Laerakond , boars could be found in Eskorn , Fimbrul , and Relmaur . In Zakhara they could be found in the land's hills and badlands. White-furred arctic boars were known to inhabit Icewind Dale and the Frozenfar .

Beyond the Prime Material plane , boars could be found in the Feywild , on Yggdrasil , the Cavern of Rest . They were particularly known to act as guards in this divine realm of Marthammor Duin .

Usages

History

A petrified boar was kept on the Arcane Chambers level of Undermountain by Halaster .

Members of the church of Silvanus often encouraged the hunting and eating of boar under the belief that the nobles who owned forests which were good for hunting would take efforts to preserve or expand those woodlands.
