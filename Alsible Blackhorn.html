Alsible Blackhorn was a cobbler that lived in the Trades Ward of the city of Waterdeep sometime prior to the 14 th century DR .

History

Alsible died sometime long before 1363 DR . Having some notoriety, the alleyway Alsible lived on would go on to be named after them, becoming known as Blackhorn Alley .
