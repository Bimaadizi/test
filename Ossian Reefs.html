The Ossian Reefs were a coral reef formation found off the coast of the isle of Moray within the Moonshae Isles .

Geography

The Ossian Reefs stretched along the entire western coast of Moray, from the Last Head cape in the south all the way to the rocky Trollclaw Range in the north near the Trackless Strait . The reefs were approximately 100 miles (160 kilometers) long.
