Hajima sitt-Nasir was a Zakharan elf leader of the Sirocco riders in the mid–14 th century DR .

Description

Hajima was a woman known for her curiosity and charismatic leadership. She appreciated luxury items that could make the lives of her clan better and always sought news from outside of the Great Desert.

Possessions

Hajima wielded an enchanted +4 scimitar and well as a light lance and a jambiya .

History

In the mid-14 th century DR, Hajima sitt-Nasir led the Sirocco, a mercenary rider, fighter, and scout group composed of humans , dwarves , and elves from Zakhara. The Sirocco ended up taking missions in the Shaar and in the Old Empires regions but eventually moved to the Great Desert of Anauroch , where they, under Hajima's leadership, offering protection services to the caravans moving across the hot sands.
