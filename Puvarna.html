The Puvarna was a holy book containing the teachings of H'Dang Li ( Shou Year 2311 (1061 DR) to c. Shou Year 2400 (1150 DR) ), the first High Lord of Oceans of Tabot , on the Tabotan form of the Way of Enlightenment . He wrote it in his own hand. It was kept in the libraries of U'Chan Gompa in Tabot by Shou Year 2607 (1357 DR) .
