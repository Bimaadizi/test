The Lamp of Learning was a magical academy that operated in Baldur's Gate during the late 15 th century DR . It was located in the walled-off district of Little Calimshan , in the Gate's impoverished Outer City .

The school offered training for arcane spellcasters , in the tradition of Calimshan , a nation in the Lands of Intrigue . They only admitted students of Calishite heritage or outsiders of exceptional skill and talent.
