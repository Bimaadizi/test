The Hanging Lantern was a festhall located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The building stood an impressive six storys tall.

Location

The Hanging Lantern was located in Waterdeep's Dock Ward in a small neighborhood bordered by Ship Street to the west, Pressbow Lane to the east, and Oar Alley to the south.

History

Volothamp Geddarm revealed that the escorts within the Hanging Lantern were actually dopplegangers working for the Unseen .

Inhabitants

The owner of the festhall in the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , was Lady Cyrtue .
