Garri Orne was a wood elven inhabitant of the Lower City on Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Garri was a wood elven man with a pale greenish skin tone and blue eyes. He had short blonde curly hair.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Garri Orne was worried over the failing sources of drinking water across the Lower City. Like other citizens, the elf thought the Upper City cared naught for the common folk. Garri filed five complaints with the city government yet no drinking water supply repairs were done.
