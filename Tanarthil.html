Tanarthil of the Towers was a wizard in the mid–14 th century DR .

Personality

He was a brave man, willing to fight even when it meant his own death.

Possessions

He had a staff . Two of its powers were one that created a storm of flames, and another that created a cone of rending, howling force.

History

Tanarthil came across an Eilistraean rite at some point in or prior to the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , and judging them as engaging in some foul purpose, attempted to eliminate the dancing drow by using his staff. In response, the drow priestess leading the rite, Qilué Veladorn , blocked his first spell, turned the second one into the sign of Mystra , and then had him dragged back to Waterdeep by his staff. She had some trouble convincing her fellow priestesses not to kill him.

She would remind her fellow priestesses of the event during a later intrusion.
