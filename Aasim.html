Aasim was an elven sha'ir living on Masud Jazayir in the Crowded Sea in 1367 DR .

Activities

Aasim was responsible for inspecting all of the cargo at the wharf in Bandar al-Sa'adat .

Relationships

He was the brother-in-law of Mahmud Ben Aziz .
