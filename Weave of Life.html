The Weave of Life was a shop in the Grand Souk marketplace of Port Nyanzaru , Chult , as of the late 15 th century DR . It was owned and operated by Klevin Van'Sheran , a Tethyrian man obsessed with Chultan culture.

Description

The storefront was a large stand with a sign displaying the shop's name in both the Chultan and Common tongues. Klevin stood behind the main counter, and greeted customers first in Chultan and then in Common.

Services

The shop sold a variety of decorative Chultan clothing —lightweight and colorfully patterned shirts , kilts , pants , and sarongs —and a wide range of trade goods, including trinkets, ivory sculptures, jewelry , and scarves . The entire inventory evoked local Chultan flavor and had clearly been curated by someone with a love for all things Chult. The shop also sold pamphlets authored by Klevin that discussed topics related to local traditions and customs.

History

Klevin had arrived in Chult as an agent of the Lords' Alliance , but he was generally more interested in his business than in the goals of his faction and would take the opportunity to try to sell his wares to any fellow operatives or adventurers before engaging in Alliance business.

The Weave of Life was a thriving business by 1490 DR . The most notable item for sale at this time was a life-size ivory carving of a Batiri battlestack .
