Jerrod's mace was a unique enchanted bludgeon from the town of Easthaven in Icewind Dale .

Powers

The mace possessed a +2 enchantment that guided the wielder's aim towards the enemy and landed deadlier strikes. It was specifically ensorcelled to be devastating when used against demons and devils .

History

As it's named suggested, Jerrod's mace once belonged to Jerrod , a historic individual. The barbarian shaman of Tempus wielded his mace against the powerful devil known as Belhifet ages before the 13 th century DR . The hero sacrificed his life in services of town of Easthaven in Icewind Dale.

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Jerrod's mace , along with several other magic items from Icewind Dale, ended up in the hands of the merchant named Joluv who traveled from Icewind Dale to the City of Coin, Athkatla , to sell the items to wealthy Amnian adventurers. Joluv peddled his wares within the Copper Coronet in Athkatla's River District .
