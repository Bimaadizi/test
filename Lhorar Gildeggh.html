Lhorar Gildeggh was a guildmaster inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, in the mid-to-late 13 th century DR . He was the guildmaster of the Order of Master Shipwrights .

Activities

In the Year of the Black Wind, 1262 DR , Lhorar Gildeggh was one of two guildmasters to survive the brutal Guildwars that occurred in Waterdeep several years after Ahghairon 's death. Lhorar and his fellow surviving guildmaster, Ehlemm Zoar , declare themselves the "Two Lords Magister" of the city.
