Carngaur was a caravan guard in the Sword Coast during the mid-14 th century DR .

Relationships

He was one of Orthil Voldovan 's men.

History

During Orthil Voldovan's caravan run from Scornubel to Waterdeep in late 1357 DR , Carngaur was one of the caravan guards. During an ambush by the Cult of the Dragon , he was struck with a lance, and later died; a poisoned lance was suspected.
