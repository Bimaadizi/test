Rana Leafheart was a fey eladrin druid who worked as a hireling in the 14 th century DR .

Description

As a summer-attuned eladrin, Rana had bright orange-red skin, darker red hair, and glowing orange eyes.

History

Born after the Fall of Myth Drannor , Leafheart was raised by her mother and told many stories about the legendary city. Around the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , Rana worked as a hireling to assist other adventurers as they went to the ruins of Myth Drannor and helped the Elves of Cormanthor reclaimed the city.
