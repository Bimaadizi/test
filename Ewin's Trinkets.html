Ewin's Trinkets , originally known as Earvin's Treasures , was a shop in Bremen , of the Ten Towns in Icewind Dale , that bought and sold items that were found by the treasure-hunters that came to town. Around 1485 DR , it was owned and run by the halfling Ewin , who purchased it from its previous owner Earvin Fastul .

Services

Under the ownership of its founder, the shop sold small items of a magical nature. Once it changed hands, Ewin's Trinkets sold locally found treasures, gems , and jewelry.

History

The shop was founded by a wizard of the Arcane Brotherhood , Earvin Fastul, who fled Luskan following the destruction of the Host Tower of the Arcane .
