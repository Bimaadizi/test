Klavikus was a balor in the service of Zhengyi the Witch-King in Vaasa in 1359 DR .

Personality

Klavikus wanted to rule the Abyss, and he was mighty and shrewd enough that Orcus himself tricked him into servitude to curb his ambitions. He preferred to enter fights with overwhelming odds on his side.

He loved reading and could be bribed with a good book.

History

Klavikus was a powerful and ambitious demon at the court of the demon lord Orcus . Sensing him a threat, Orcus sent him to fulfill trivial duties on the Prime Material plane for 500 years starting in the Year of the Children, 971 DR . As of the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR , with 112 years to go, Orcus himself had commanded Klavikus to guard the interior of Castle Perilous .

Klavikus used all his centuries of servitude to Zhengyi to read all the books in the lich 's library. At some point, he started to send some of the tomes he had finished reading to his original house in the Abyss .
