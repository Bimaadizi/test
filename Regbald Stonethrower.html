Regbald Stonethrower was a dwarven warrior of the Frozenfar around 1369 DR .

History

In Tarsakh , 1369 DR , while traveling through the Spine of the World pass into Icewind Dale Regbald was killed fighting hobgoblin raiders and their goblin scouts who ambushed merchant caravans.
