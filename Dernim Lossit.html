Dernim Lossit was a member of the Sembian High Council in Sembia in the late 14 th century DR .

Personality

Dernim was a compromising individual by nature.

Activities

Dernim Lossit was the Highspeaker of the Council, the person that acted as mediator during the council's debate.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , after the death of Kendrick Selkirk , Dernim Lossit summoned the Council on Marpenoth 10 in order to elect the new Overmaster. The two main candidates were Countess Mirabeta Selkirk and Endren Corrinthal of Saerb . After the Council collapsed in chaos over the accusation that Endren had killed Kendrick, many drew their swords and started to fight, and Zarin Terb and Inmin Dossir were killed. Lossit agreed to hold a vote for a temporary Overmaster and Mirabeta was elected. She ordered the arrest of Endren.
