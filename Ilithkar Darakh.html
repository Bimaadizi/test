Ilithkar Darakh was the fifth Nentyarch of the Darakh Dynasty to rule Narfell .

Activities

Ilithkar assumed the title of Nentyarch upon the death of the previous ruler, Garthelaun the Goreclaw , in −235 DR . He ruled until −181 DR when he was overthrown and slain by his cousin Rheligaun the Horned .

Relationships

Ilithkar was the half-fiend offspring of a human woman and the demon lord Orcus .
