The Hunt Trail ran through the Vast , connecting the towns of Ylraphon and Kurth .

From west to east, it began at Ylraphon, passed the Flooded Forest , ran through the village of Tavilar and the hills of Beluar's Hunt and ended at Kurth. On the way, it joined the Pass Trail and the North Road .

The minstrel Lieshann , traveling from Tavilar to Kurth, once took a wrong turn onto the trail to the Mage's Tower , having mistaken it for the Hunt Trail.
