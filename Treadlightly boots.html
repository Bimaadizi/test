Treadlightly boots were mundane but masterfully crafted boots used by some inhabitants of the Ten-Towns in Icewind Dale in the early 14 th century DR .

Powers

Treadlightly boots were non-magical items of clothing crafted out of soft doeskin with their soles padded to make the wearer move more silently.

History

Circa Year of the Griffon, 1312 DR , a pair of treadlightly boots was sold by Elytharra Dinnesmore , the enchantress wife of Speaker Ulbrec Dinnesmore of Targos .
