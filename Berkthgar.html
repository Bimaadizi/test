Berkthgar was a Reghedman barbarian from the Tribe of the Elk of Icewind Dale who served as the chieftain of Settlestone in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The barbarian chieftain had wild brown hair, which fell across his strong shoulders. Berkthgar had muscles the size of dwarf thighs and stood 6 foot 6 inches (1.98 meters). To add to his imposing presence, the huge human had strikingly blue eyes.

Personality

As leader of Settlestone, Berkthgar lived in the shadow of Wulfgar and wanted Aegis-fang to demonstrate to his people he was the rightful chieftain of the Tribe of the Elk. He was set in his ways and viewed the dwarves and the residents of the Ten Towns as enemies. He was also "stymied by strong women," namely Catti-brie

Possessions

When in battle, Berkthgar used a great flamberge called Bankenfuere the Northern Fury and wore a fur covered jerkin . The huge 4-foot-long (1.2 meters) sword rested in a leather sheath over his right shoulder.

History

Berkthgar was the son of Beothgin .

In the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR , Berkthgar was an emissary between Settlestone and Mithral Hall who interacted with King Bruenor . In the Year of Shadows, 1358 DR , Drizzt Do'Urden and Catti-brie traveled to Settlestone to resolve a disagreement Berkthgar had with Bruenor Battlehammer over Wulfgar's warhammer , Aegis-fang.
