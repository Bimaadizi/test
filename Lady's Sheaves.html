The Lady's Sheaves was a Chauntean temple in the city-state of Saelmur .

Location

This temple situated close to the Central Marketplace of Saelmur.

Inhabitants

In the mid-to-late 14 th century DR , the clergy of the Lady's Sheaves was led by Thera Goldencorn .
