The Forgotten Pearl was a welcoming and lively inn and tavern in the city of Rassatan that specialized in seafood and a strong mixed drink called " healer ".

Services

The Forgotten Pearl offered drinks and food to its guests as well as lodgings. The establishment was among the very few places open to non-citizens of Samarach. The tavern's taproom was constructed in a traditional way for drinking establishments in Samarach. The taproom was built in the shape of a cross. Tables, booths, and places to mingle were placed at each end of the structure. At the center stood the bar from where the innkeeper did business. The Forgotten Pearl employed musicians and dancers who entertained its guests. The inn's specialty was seafood, evident by a faint scent of fresh catch wafting from the establishment open doors.

History

In the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , a local street musician, Redfeyer , visited the Forgotten Pearl to drink his sadness away after surviving a trip to the Singing Caverns east of Rassatan. He had lost his prized singing pendant .
