Sephek Kaltro ( pronounced : /ˈ s ɛ f ɛ k ˈ k ɔː l t r oʊ / SEH-fek KAWL-troh ) was a sailor who lived during the late 15 th century DR , who enjoyed a second existence as the undead host to a spirit in the service of Auril .

Description

As it was his presumed age at the time of his death, Sephek appeared as a man in his thirties. He had tan skin and dark hair he often wore pulled back in a ponytail.

Fitting for a servant of the Frostmaiden, Sephek's eyes were said to have been as blue as a lake of ice.

Abilities

Due to his supernatural existence, Sephek was able to teleport through mist up to three times per day.

He did not give in to any form of fear, and was immune to both charm magic and harmful coldness. In fact he thrived in extreme cold, gaining a form of regeneration while in sub-zero temperatures.

Possessions

Unlike the warm-blooded mortals who lived in the Far North , the undead Sephek did not have to bundle himself in cold-weather clothing. The clothing he did wear was reminiscent of the styles found across the Sword Coast .

He wielded weapons made of pure ice, which he could summon as he willed.

Activities

During undeath, Sephek masqueraded as a mere bodyguard in service to the corrupt caravan-master Torga Icevein . In truth, he served as Auril's agent of death. He collected the lives of those individuals who sought to cheat their way out of the sacrificial lottery that was established by the leaders of the Ten Towns .

History

While he was a mortal man, Sephek earned his living as a seaman. When his ship crashed near the isle of Solstice , in the Sea of Moving Ice , Sephek was left stranded and nearly froze to death. During his final moments, his body was possessed by the spirit of one of Auril's deceased frost druids .
