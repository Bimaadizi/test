Sweelfire Ice was a chilled dessert dish from the city of Velen in the nation of Tethyr .

Description

A simple delight of a cupful of ice, topped with cream and then topped with sweet tamarind sauce.

History

As of the late 14 th century DR , Sweelfire Ice could be found on Blackthorn's Brew 's menu, a festhall on the Captain's Cliff in Velen. The dish was mentioned among other notable foods included in Volothamp Geddarm's published guide to Amn and Tethyr.
