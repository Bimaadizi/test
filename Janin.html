Janin was a Netherese arcanist who performed research at the Shadow Consortium of Eileanar before its destruction in 3520 NY (−339 DR) .

History

Following the disappearance of the institute's founder, Shadow , Janin performed experimental research into the planes as part of Alkindus 's project. Focusing on the unlikely possibilities of semiplanes and antiplanes , they worked alongside Pringle , Vanderanus , and Shiris Blamira .
