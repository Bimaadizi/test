Randelo was a Watch captain and Purple Dragon knight in Suzail during the late 15 th century DR .

History

Randelo questioned the nobleman Arclath Delcastle and the coinlass Amarune Whitewave in the aftermath of the murder near her home, one morn in Mirtul of the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR . Luckily, despite his suspicions, a watchman had been following them all night, and vouched for the fact they had taken no part in the murder.
