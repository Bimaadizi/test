Tia Ingelri was a skilled human blacksmith for hire from the city of Neverwinter , active in the late 15 th century DR .

History

By the late 15 th century DR, Tia Ingelri was already an experienced blacksmith with many years of experience under her belt . Despite her long career, she remained passionate and driven to tackle more challenging projects, leading her to offer services to the adventurers and defenders of Neverwinter.
