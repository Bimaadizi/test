Ailon Finch was a cloth merchant in Ylraphon . He was slain by a black dragon in the Rage of Dragons of 1373 DR .

Description

He was balding and heavyset. His voice was a little strangled the night he died, probably because of too tight a cuirass .

Possessions

The merchant wore a cuirass two sizes too small on the night he died.

History

On Hammer 23 , Ailon Finch was abruptly woken up after Dorn Graybrook roused the local council of merchants to warn them of the signs of a Rage of Dragons . He was magically blinded and then slain that night by a black dragon.
