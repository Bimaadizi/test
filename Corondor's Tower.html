Corondor's Tower was a tower located in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan near the Arnaden .

Location

The tower was located along the perimeter wall in northwest Suldolphor north of the Golden Gate .

Description

With its own granary, fortified walls, smithy, dungeon, and stables, Elaph's Tower was a fortress in its own right.

Inhabitants

Corondor's Tower served as a barracks for Suldolphor's elite Flame Guard .
