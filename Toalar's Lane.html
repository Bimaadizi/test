Toalar's Lane was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Toalar's Lane was located in the far northwest section of Waterdeep's Castle Ward . It originated on Julthoon Street just west of the intersection with Calamastyr Lane and south of the Field of Triumph . The road branched southwest then turned north to end on Calamastyr Lane facing the Spires of the Morning .
