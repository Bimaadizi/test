Scar Keep was a military outpost located in the Chasm of Neverwinter . It was used as the headquarters of Scar Company .

Structure

Scar Keep was made up of the ruins of a former Graycloak military outpost. The original construction had been warded to protect it from enemy spells and siege engines , and these enchantments had the effect of preserving the building mostly intact when it plunged into the Chasm.

History

Scar Keep fell into the Chasm when Mount Hotenow erupted in the Year of Knowledge Unearthed, 1451 DR . After the Wall of Neverwinter was built, Scar Company used the Keep to protect Neverwinter from the horrors of the Chasm.
