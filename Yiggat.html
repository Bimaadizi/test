Yiggat was a small village in the Angalpuk region within the Great Glacier .

Geography

Yiggat was located in western Angalpuk, near the Opoboquo Valley .

Inhabitants

A disease decimated the villages communal caribou herd. This has forced the villagers to begin planning raids on ikili as well as raiding the communal herds of nearby villages.
