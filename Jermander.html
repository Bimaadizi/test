Jermander was a tiefling swordsman and a member of the Bounty Hunters of Cavus Dun in the mid-to-late 15 th century DR .

History

Jermander was a swordsman and the leader of the Cavus Dun squad tasked by Effron with finding Dahlia Sin'felle and Drizzt Do'Urden . However, in the first skirmish against the pair of elves, most of his squad were killed by Dahlia and Drizzt. Jermander perished in single combat against Artemis Entreri , who came to Dahlia's rescue at the last second.
