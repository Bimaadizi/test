Teratosaurus ( pronounced : / t ɛ r ˈ æ t oʊ ˈ s ɑːr ʌ s / ter-@t-o-SAR-us ) was a species of dinosaur .

Description

Teratosaurs resembled many other carnosaurs, such as the tyrannosaurus , but were much heavier.

Behaviour

Teratosaurs were known for being aggressive.

Abilities

Teratosaurs were excellent runners.

Ecology

Teratosaurs were a carnivorous and solitary species.

Habitats

Teratosaurs were most often encountered in forests and plains. They were particularly known to inhabit the land of Chult .
