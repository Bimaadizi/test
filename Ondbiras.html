Ondbiras was an Imaskari artificer and the master of Yaravindar Ipurnos .

History

Ondbiras was the master of Yaravindar Ipurnos . During the fighting between the manifestations of the Mulhorandi deities and the defenders of Inupras , Ondbiras cast a spell that sent the pupil into a safe house in the Glittering Spires . Ondbiras was never seen again, and presumed dead by their student.
