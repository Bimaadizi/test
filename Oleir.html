Oleir was a Shadowdale forester and Zhentarim spy in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

The blonde man was immense and broad-shouldered, with mighty hands.

Personality

Oleir was a vicious person, but extremely dull.

Abilities

He was a bear trapper, among other activities, who could move silently. He was also notably strong.

Relationships

Samshin was his superior. Maervidal Iloster , a local scribe and fellow Zhentarim spy, feared him.

History

One day, he was one of the people who passed by Maervidal Iloster's workplace, Crown & Raven Scriveners , to remind him he should attend the revel that evening on Warmfires House .
