Lady Cathone "Cat" Ravenhill was a young noble lady of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

Cat was a spirited and merry young woman.

Relationships

Cathone was a constant companion and lover of Lady Narnathra Barelder , along with Lady Laeyalane Tantorn .
