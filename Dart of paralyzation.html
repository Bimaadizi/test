Darts of paralyzation were enchanted darts that could inflict paralysis on a target.

Powers

A creature struck by one of these darts was at risk of being paralyzed for ten minutes. They did not lose their magic if they missed their intended target, and so could be retrieved and used again.

History

As of the Year of the Serpent, 1359 DR , the sage Flamsterd was known to carry half a dozen darts of paralyzation when he traveled.
