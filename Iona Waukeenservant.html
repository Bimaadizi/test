Iona Waukeenservant was a Sembite adventurer and co-leader of the Company of Night in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

History

In Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , Iona and Idalia Golddust attended a party at Thistleflame Keep , held by Lord Partic Thistle for King Azoun IV 's birthday. They were interviewed as suspects after Lord Thistle appeared to be murdered at the party, but were not involved and knew nothing of it.
