Jephford was a soldier of Cormyr in the late 15 th century DR .

Relationships

Glathra Barcantle knew his name.

Activities

He was a member of the Purple Dragons , and a soldier.

History

In the aftermath of the massacre at the Council of the Dragon of Cormyr in late Mirtul of the 1479 DR , Jephford was part of a group including the War Wizard Glathra Barcantle , Lord Warder Vainrence , a Highknight named Eldur Hawkmantle , Jephford himself, and three other Purple Dragons. Jephford was one of these Purple Dragons; after Glathra revealed they were seeking out the sages Orkrash , Loagranboydar , Garendor , Argrant , and Wyshbryn , and that they were doing other tasks as well, Jephford blurted out she must be looking for nobles born with a talent for the Art so as to determine who was responsible for the blueflame ghost featuring in the massacre. Glathra all but confirmed his conclusion.

After the five sages were discovered slain, Vainrence cast a spell to determine how they were killed. A lurking contingency exploded the room in response. Only one of the Purple Dragons survived.
