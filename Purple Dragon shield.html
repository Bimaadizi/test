The Purple Dragon shield was a lost and powerful, possibly unique enchanted tower shield adorned with the Purple Dragons heraldry that was hidden in the Haunted Halls of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the 15 th century DR .

Powers

The Purple Dragon shield was made out of plane -forged steel . It carried a collection of powerful enchantments , including magics that boosted the carrier's defense and gave them protection from acid , cold, electricity, fire , and sonics. It also greatly increased their constitution and charisma, and it could be used as a powerful magic bashing weapon that dealt extra damage. Lastly, the Purple Dragon shield was a vorpal weapon and a bash with it could easily decapitate the opponent.

History

Sometime long before the 15 th century DR, the person who wielded this shield descended into the depths of the Haunted Halls of Eveningstar, along with eleven other Purple Dragons. The raid was a failure, the shield was lost, and the owner, whose name was lost to time, was never seen again.
