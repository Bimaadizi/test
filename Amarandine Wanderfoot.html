Amarandine Wanderfoot was a retired adventurer who served as the Chief Speaker of Helm's Hold during the late 15 th century DR .

Description

At the time of her political career, Amarandine was described as rather matronly.

Relationships

She maintained a good relationship with the city's Holy Watcher, the Helmite head of the city's Cathedral of Helm .

History

Having been an adventurer in her younger years, Amarandine enjoyed success as an elected politician later in life. By the Year of the Warrior Princess, 1489 DR she had been elected as Chief Speaker of the city council in Helm's Hold.
