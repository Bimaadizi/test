A cube of force was a magical item used to create barriers.

Description

A cube of force measured 1 inch (2.5 centimeters) across each face. Each side had a unique design. They were made of either bone, ivory, or metal.

Powers

A cube was activated when an individual pressed on one side of the cube. Each cube held 36 magical charges and one was discharged when a side was pressed. When pressed, a magical barrier 15 feet (4.6 meters) wide per side was created, centered on the individual, with each side of the cube creating a barrier with different properties. The barrier remained in place for up to a minute or until deactivated.
