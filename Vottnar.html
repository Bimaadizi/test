Guardian Vottnar was the cleric of The Watcher, in charge of the Temple of Helm in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Vottnar was a plate armor -clad human male with pale skin. He wore an open-faced horned helmet and was armed with a mace and a shield .

History

In the late 14 th century DR, Guardian Vottnar could be found running a small temple dedicated to Helm in Athkatla 's Bridge District . He offered services of divine magic and magic potions in exchange for a donation to the Church of Helm . Vottnar also had a small selection of magic scrolls of protection and wooden stakes .
