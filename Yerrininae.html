Yerrininae was the leader of the driders in service to the drow House Xorlarrin .

History

In Menzoberranzan , Yerrininae served Gromph Baenre , who frequently ordered him to guard Ravel Xorlarrin while the younger drow collected spell components.

In 1463 DR , Yerrininae led a group of driders alongside members of House Xorlarrin when they captured Gauntlgrym in preparation for the settlement of Q'Xorlarrin in 1484 DR .

In 1484 DR , when Ambergris was captured, Yerrininae took her mace, Skullcrusher . Yerrininae was killed after a battle with Wulfgar .

Relationships

Yerrininae's consort was Flavvar , who was killed by Dahlia Sin'felle in 1484 DR .
