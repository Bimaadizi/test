Talli Armbrust was the wife of Tym Armbrust , a down-on-his-luck tailor, and mother of Ayo Armbrust from Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Talli was a human woman with a dusky skin tone, long curly brown hair, and dark brown eyes.

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Talli's health and wellbeing were threatened by Tym's debt to the notorious Guild of Baldur's Gate. When Tym was unable to pay, Nine-Fingers Keene implicitly threatened the tailor's family in a letter and notified him of collectors coming to collect the late fee. The family's struggle was noticeable to Talli's friends, Sabine and Freddie , who visited the Armbrusts. They offered to help the family out by taking in little Ayo to give Talli and Tym some privacy and a way to relax.
