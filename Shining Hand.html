The Shining Hand was a monastic order based in Amn , dedicated to Azuth .

Activities

The monks of the Shining Hand mixed their monastic practices and their faith in Azuth with the study of magic , such that they could also be wizards .

Possessions

The Vaasan monks of the order made a unique variety of enchanted robes that possessed powerful magic.

History

The Shining Hand was one of the longest-running monastic orders in Amn.

However, Amn's persecution of wizards and their practices caused many monks of the order to go underground or even to leave the country.
