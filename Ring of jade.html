Rings of jade were common minor magic items found across the Sword Coast and exported out of Kara-Tur in the late 14 th century DR .

Powers

These jade rings were imbued with simple enchantments that made them glow with soft green light, illuminating an area of 15 feet (4.6 meters) around them.

History

By the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR , these common pieces of enchanted jade were found across many pockets, fingers, and merchant stalls of the Sword Coast. They were especially popular among adventurers, freeing their hand for weapons and tools instead of holding lanterns and torches . As of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , rings of jade were sold at Okuzo's Imports , a shop that dealt with exotic Kara-Turan goods, located on the Harbor Loop in Westgate .
