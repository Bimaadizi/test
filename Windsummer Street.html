Windsummer Street was one of the roads in the city of Urmlaspyr in Sembia .

History

In the mid-14 th century DR , the adventuring wizard named Pendara the Slim was one of the inhabitants of Windsummer Street in Urmlaspyr. In the Year of the Dragon, 1352 DR , she rode an untamed griffon and crashed it in front of her house.
