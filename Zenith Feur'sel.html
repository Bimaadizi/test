Zenith Feur'sel was a member of the Greycloaks of Evereska who ventured out from his home city in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

He had light skin, a prominent chin, and wavy chestnut hair.

Abilities

In his studies as a cleric , Zenith chose to focus on the light domain of divine magic .

History

After serving with his fellow Greycloaks of Evereska, Zenith's wanderlust saw him leave his home city and travel the breadth of Faerûn . His journey was ultimately cut short however in in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . He was killed by a group of raiders aligned with the cultist of the Absolute . Zenith was among the dozen individuals chosen by the entity Withers to aid in his campaign against the Dead Three . It may be implied that Zenith was the son of another elven Greycloak of Evereska, Xan , having his father noted as being a dour mannered man who (rightfully) claimed that Zenith was 'doomed'.
