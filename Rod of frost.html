Rods of frost were enchanted rods that allowed the wielder to fire rays of freezing cold.

Description

Rods of frost were 2‑foot-long (0.61‑meter) transparent glassteel rods bound with iron bands and weighing 5 pounds (2,300 grams).

Powers

When activated, these rods emitted a 30‑foot-long (9.1‑meter) ray of frost . The magical cold of the ray extinguished all flames it touched and froze the entire affected area. The ray of frost could be blasted three times per day.

Creation

A skilled crafter of wondrous items could create such rods with the use ray of frost spell. The process usually took two days and cost 700 gp .

History

In the late 14 th century DR , rods of frost could be found throughout the Sword Coast region, notably in the city of Neverwinter
