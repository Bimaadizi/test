Ladymist Talons were enchanted kukris associated with the Church of Akadi .

Description

Notably, Ladymist Talons lacked the traditional markings used by the worshipers of Akadi .

Powers

These kukris possessed a +1 enchantment and made the wielder completely unaffected by the cone of cold spell. Additionally, the wielder could cast the stinking cloud spell using the weapon. Each Ladymist Talon could hold up to ten charges of the spell.

History

Despite the fact that many sages presumed the Ladymist Talons had an association with the greater power of the Elemental Plane of Air , Akadi, it was never proven. This presumption was fueled by the fact that the weapons possessed magical powers appropriate for the goddess's domain. The most common school of thought was that, indeed, Ladymist Talons were from Akadi's realm but they drew power from somewhere else entirely.
