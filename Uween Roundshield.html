Uween Roundshield was a dwarf blacksmith in Citadel Felbarr in the 15 th century DR . She was the mother to Reginald Roundshield , the reincarnated Bruenor Battlehammer , whom she had with Reginald Roundshield Sr. in 1463 DR .
