Joel Firris Baldidims was a scholar and writer who committed his work to the theme of love, active sometime in or before the late 15 th century DR .

History

Joel Firris Baldidims' work was notable for his disdain for many poets comparing women to flowers or "features of geography." By his own words, these euphemism were so tired, Joel was ready to "start knocking on poets' doors with a sledgehammer ."
