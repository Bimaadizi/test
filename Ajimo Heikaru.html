Ajimo Heikaru was a Wererat samurai in Ito-jo in 1360 DR .

History

Heikaru was the first son of the warlord of ito family but against his family's wishes he fall in love with Emi Towiki a kitchen worker. Distracted by his love he was negligent in rallying the clan’s army when needed in 647 DR . So the curse transformed him in a Wererat condemned to guard his lover room in caves deep the castle for eternity. At last in 1360 DR, an adventuring party hired by Michimori Uemon to recover the Seven Swords fought and destroyed him.
