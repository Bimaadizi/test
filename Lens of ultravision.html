A lens of ultravision was a type of enchanted lens .

Description

These lenses were fashioned from flawless, pale aquamarine gems . They had many facets and a prismatic quality to them. They were roughly 1 ft 05. in (0.43 m) in diameter.

Powers

When held up to the eye, the wielder of a lens of ultravision could sense radiation in the ultraviolet spectrum . This allowed them to see within areas of total darkness, as though they were bathed in the bright light of selûne . Anything within 120 feet (37 meters) of them could be seen clearly, while general outlines could be distinguished from as far away as 240 feet (73 meters) away.

History

Circa 1357 DR , the leader of a band of bugbears in the Delimbiyr Vale region that had been driven from the Misty Forest possessed one of these.
