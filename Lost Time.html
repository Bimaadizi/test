Lost Time was a potion formulated by the hag Ethel , that lived within the Gnarled Teahouse in the Putrid Bog of the Western Heartlands .

Description

The potion was entirely clear and kept within an old, cracked jug.

Powers

Lost Time caused the drinker to act much slower than usual, lessening their reflexes and ability to move out of harm's way. These effects lasted for a duration of five minutes.

History

The only known jug of Lost Time could be found within Ethel's Ancient Abode as of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
