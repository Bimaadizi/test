Rockgrit was a strong gnomish pale ale from the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast .

Description

Rockgrit a highly-alcoholic ale, on par with hard liquor, that was distilled from potatoes . It was sometimes served with two highsunfeasts – Brick , or Rosto .

History

Stint was a staple of a gnomish out-of-the way café in Ravens Bluff called Le Po Teete Café in the late 14 th century DR . It served only gnomish pale ales, with Greentart and Stint being only alternatives.
