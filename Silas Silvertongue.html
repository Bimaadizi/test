Silas Silvertongue was a red cap boatswain of the Blushing Mermaid in the city of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the time Captain Grisly was replaced by the green hag Auntie Ethel as the owner of Blushing Mermaid, Silas Silvertongue resided at the Mermaid, alongside other hag's crewmen, spending their days drinking and partying. Silas was masquerading as a human sailor, keeping his fey nature a secret. It was unclear if the Silas Silvertongue identity was stolen from a slain sailor or was a fabrication.
