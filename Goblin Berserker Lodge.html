The Goblin Berserker Lodge was the base of operations for the Goblin Berserkers found in the capital city of Immilmar in Rashemen in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

Location

The lodge was located on the north side of the city.

Description

The longhouse's small stature, especially when compared with the nearby Owlbear Berserker Lodge , accurately reflected the quantity of Goblin berskers.
