Snake soup was a variety of soup , prepared using snake meat, that was most common in the city of Crimmor in Amn .

Description

Hot snake soup was a common delicacy in Crimmor due to a high population of snakes that fed on its abundant rats . If the meat in a soup or stew was hard to identify â€“ it was likely snake. A common practice among Crimmor eateries was hiding snake heads in soups served to out-of-towners.

Cold snake soup was a part of elvish cuisine. It was prepared using four types of beheaded snakes and cooked in water until the skin was easily peeled.
