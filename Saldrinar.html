Saldrinar , who was nicknamed of the Seven Spells , was a Netherese wizard and the first human King to rule the city-state of Westgate .

Activities

Saldrinar claimed the throne for himself when he slew the previous King, a topaz dragon named Kisonraathiisar in −349 DR . He ruled until his death in −307 DR .

Relationships

His apprentice, Mather "Wyrmshroud" would inherit the throne upon his death.
