High Captain Orbachnar was the ranking priest of Valkur in the Temple of Endless Waves and Wind on the Sea of Fallen Stars in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR .

Activities

Orbachnar apparently had a degree of control over the massive zaratan upon which the temple was constructed as the creature seemed perfectly content to follow his directions.
