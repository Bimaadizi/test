Aldric was the abbot of the Abbey of St. Sollars of the Barony of Bloodstone in 1357 DR .

Activities

Aldric was the spiritual leader of the Barony.

History

In 1357 DR, Aldric opposed the idea of hiring Gareth Dragonsbane and his party.

Some time after the adventurers defeated the bandit army minions of Orcus infected Aldric with vampirism , driving him mad. He so said to all that he was sick and never exit more in daytime. At same time he summoned rats and worgs that spread chaos near the abbey. At last he tried to escape from the village in gaseous form .

Personality

Aldric was very fatalistic, disapproving of going against destiny. He was a powerful opponent.
