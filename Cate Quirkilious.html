Cate Quirkilious was a daughter of Thomas C. Quirkilious , the dragonborn proprietor of the Chromatic Scale music shop in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Cate departed Baldur's Gate for Calimshan . The city of Calimport dazzled the woman and its music tickled her ears. There, she encountered a curious snakeskin musical instrument called slitherhorn . In her letter to Thomas, she promised to bring the instrument home and play it for him.
