The Order of the Forgotten Page were a group of clerics who worshiped Azuth who performed a great service in retrieving magical knowledge.

Activities

Members of this order were voted on and invited to join after they recovered a great piece of lost magical knowledge. Once accepted, they were permitted to wear special silver trim on the collar of their priestly vestments to denote their inclusion within the Order of the Forgotten Page.
