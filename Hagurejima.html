Hagurejima was an island located in Kozakura .

Description

Hagurejima was located to the northwest of the island of Kabudono . It was characterized by steep slopes and bamboo -covered mountain summits. Its western coast had shallow, sandy beaches, while the eastern shore had a multitude of inlets and coves. Notorious as a mooring spot for wako pirates, it frequently fell victim to raids by Kozakuran war vessels.
