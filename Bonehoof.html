Bonehoof was an undead skeleton minotaur in Myth Drannor during the late 14 th century DR .

History

The creature remained locked inside Tyrintar's Hall , along with countless other undead. It was encountered when Uhlsdrin Nirveth came looking for the Icon of the Dwarves in 1374 DR .
