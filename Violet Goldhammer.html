History

Violet perished sometime before the fall of Reithwin and it being consumed by the Shadow-cursed Lands before the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR . She was buried in Reithwin's graveyard by the northeastern wall of the House of Healing . Her tombstone read: "Here rests Violet Goldhammer, beloved singer. May her voice live on in our memories." Violet's grave marker remained relatively undamaged and legible as of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR .
