Althune Dembrar was the head cleric of Deneir in Berdusk in 1359 DR .

Activities

An expert in symbols, Althune managed the local temple to Deneir. She resided inside Twilight Hall .

Relationship

Althune was a strong ally of the Harpers and acted as their eyes and ears.

Rumors & Legends

Some gossiped about Althune Dembrar having a romantic encounter with the unnamed Baron of Gateford , however these scandalous rumors were never confirmed.
