The Sand Kings were a rebel Bedine group in Anauroch around 1479 DR . The Sand Kings opposed the shadovar 's domination of Anauroch.

Activities

Usually the Sand Kings used hit-and-run skirmishes against shadovar patrols and supply convoys but sometimes they devised more complex plots.

History

In 1479 DR, a Sand Kings group led by the mystic Ashurta tried to stop a shadovar plot to gain control of the Spiral Gate portal nexus .
