Hunter's Square was a courtyard in Port Nyanzaru , Chult where adventurers and members of the Hunters' Guild of Chult congregated to plan their hunts and trade for lures and equipment.

Geography

The square was located south of the Bazaar and just inside Port Nyanzaru's main gate.

History

During the events of the death curse in the late 15 th century DR , the famed writer and traveler Volo visited Hunter's Square and became enamored with the hunters' tales of dangerous Chultan beasts, particularly the King of Spines .
