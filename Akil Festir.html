Akil Festir was a powerful and ambitious wind mage who resided in Huzuz , the City of Delights.

Personality

His delusions of grandeur often exceeded his abilities to bring them to fruition.

History

Akril was credited with founding the Exterminators , an adventuring company specializing in ridding Huzuz of elemental vermin .
