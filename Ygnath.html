Ygnath was a follower of Bane in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Twelve Warnings, 1494 DR , Ygnath was a member of a Dead Three cult in Baldur's Gate. He was busy torturing Klim Jhasso for fun inside their lair while Kazzira watched.
