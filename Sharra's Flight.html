Sharra's Flight was a small back alley and courtyard that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Sharra's Flight was located in the central section of Waterdeep's Sea Ward . The alley branched east off of the Street of Whispers at several locations, passing through a small neighborhood and connecting with the southern side of Moonstar Alley . An entrance to the Waterdeep's sewers was also located here.

History

The alley was named for Sharra of the Invisble Dragon .
