Rairdrun Hill was the name of a hill near the Dragon Coast in the mid–8 th century DR .

History

In the Year of the Awakening Wyrm, 767 DR , the stretch between Rairdrun Hill and Oggle's Stream was known as the Dead Place , because of a prowling phantom known as the Slayer , which had killed almost everything larger than a mouse .
