The Thornwash , also called the Thornwash River , was a small river located in the Vilhon Reach and Lake of Steam region .

Geography

The Thornwash originated in the western branch of the Cloven Mountains as well as in the heart of the Winterwood . It flowed south for approximately 150 mi (240 km) before emptying into the Lake of Steam .
