The Seven Masks Theater was a theater in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep that catered to the masses typically found in the neighborhood. Ship captains and sailors were admitted for free. In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the owner was a man from Shou Lung named Rongquan Mystere but in reality it was the famed rogue Jarlaxle in disguise.

Structure

The building was approximately 105 feet (32 meters) wide by 130 feet (40 meters) deep. The auditorium had a 20‑foot (6.1‑meter) ceiling with bare rafters. The lobby, ticket booths, offices, dressing rooms, etc. had 10‑foot (3‑meter) ceilings. Passageways between rooms were 8 feet (2.4 meters) high with 7‑foot (2.1‑meter) doorways.
