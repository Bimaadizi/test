History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the place was visited by Dove Falconhand in the guise of an old peddler called Tarthan. He was the only customer, the barkeep was rather clueless about recent events, and kept polishing glasses that were already clean. Tarthan asked about drow infiltrators, and received little answer, but the barkeep acted swiftly after he left, sending a man to tail him.
