The Garb of the Land and Sky was a unique set of monk robes that one belonged to the famous monk Kushigo sometime before the late 15 th century DR .

Powers

Even though this garb could be worn by anyone, only monks could utilize all of its enchantments. Its mountainous poise enchantment allowed the monk receive effects of the blade ward spell when they used the patient defense technique. The breezeswift speed enchantment allowed the monk to briefly boost their protection and attack after using the step of the wind technique. Once the monk used the step of the wind technique, their defense received a magical boost similar to that of a +1 enchantment and their blows were guided to the targets, dealing additional force damage.

History

Once, long before the late 15 th century DR, the Garb of the Land and Sky was worn by Kushigo, a warrior who gave up his heavy shell for the freedom of movement after suffering a great loss. In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the Garb of the Land and Sky could be found in Danthelon's Dancing Axe 's inventory, among mostly mundane items in stock.
