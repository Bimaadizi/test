Marrakt was an orc messenger in Zhent -occupied Daggerdale in 1367 DR .

Activities

An orc without a tribe, Marrakt acted as a lackey and messenger for Constable Tren Noemfor .

History

In 1367 DR, Constable Tren sent Marrakt escorted by two mercenaries to invite a just-arrived-in-town adventuring party to meet him.

Personality

Marrakt did not want to be part of any fight; at the first sign of trouble, he escaped.
