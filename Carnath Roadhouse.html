The Carnath Roadhouse was a compound and small supply depot found near the Mere of Dead Men along the High Road on the Sword Coast between Waterdeep in the south and Neverwinter in the north, in the late 15 th century DR .

In the 1480s DR, it was used by the Cult of the Dragon as a waypoint to transfer stolen goods to a secret location in the Mere, that is, Castle Naerytar , via an underground tunnel leading from the a trap door in the floor of the strong room.
