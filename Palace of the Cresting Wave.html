The Palace of the Cresting Wave was a temple to Istishia in the city of Calimport .

Location

The temple was located in Markhib Drudach of Aktorral Sabban within Dock Ward .

Structure

The building was an elaborate and ornate seven-story structure towering over most of the other buildings in the waterfront district.
