Avgarioletta was a Dark Breaker of Umberlee , a member of the Bitch Queen's clergy under Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queen's House in Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

Avgarioletta was a human woman with strawberry blonde hair and a tan skin tone. She had watery blue eyes and had several tiny moles on her face, including a beauty mark under her heft eye.

Posessions

Avgarioletta's Dark Breaker uniform was a set of skin-tight heavy chainmail dress. The armor was decorated with golden trim and had decorated metallic pauldrons. Her weapon of choice was heavy crossbow .

History

Circa the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , during the Absolute crisis , Dark Breaker Avgarioletta was among the church's members who performed the faith's ceremonies and sang hymns in praise of the Bitch Queen. She was alongside Flood Tide Allandra Grey and other priestesses during Holli Dylford 's last rites.
