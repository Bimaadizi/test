Al-Malja River was found in the far southern corner of west Zakhara .

Geography

Al-Malja River flowed east down from Al-Suqut Mountains through the Pearl Cities region. It emptied into the Crowded Sea at Ajayib , the City of Wonders.

Description

Though small in size, Al-Malja, and the fertile lands hugging its banks, were vital for the city of Ajayib.
