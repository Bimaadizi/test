Lone wolves were specialty priests devoted to Fenmarel Mestarine , the Lone Wolf.

Culture

Lone wolves were elves or half-elves , with the majority being green elves .

Abilities

Their survival skills in the wild rivaled those of rangers .

In addition to their normal spells , lone wolves could cast entangle , pass without trace , blur , obscurement , barkskin , spike growth , tree shape , plant door , hallucinatory forest , charm monster , commune with nature , wall of thorns , transport via plants , creeping doom , and acid storm once per day.

Possessions

Lone wolves used a variety of weaponry including blowguns , clubs , daggers , darts , hand axes , knives , slings , quarterstaves , short bows , and staff-slings . For armor , they wore leather armor or hide armor and some carried a wooden shield .
