Gyudd's Distillery was a business located within the subterranean city of Skullport that specialized in the creation of certain spirits. It was run by the shield dwarf Gyudd , after whom it was named.

The establishment could be found within the lowest level of the city, known as the Dredge and was so tall that it extended beyond the Venter .

Services

As of the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , the distillery produced Goat's Head ale , a sherry called Amberjack , and the absolutely disgusting beverage known as Wyrmwizz .
