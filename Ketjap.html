Ketjap was a condiment found in the Shining Lands in 1367 DR . It was available for purchase through Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue .

Description

Ketjap was made by combining mingari from Ulgarth with rare fruits from Maztica . This thick and spicy sauce had a unique flavor, making it a prized commodity in the Shining South . It was a good condiment for otherwise ordinary grilled or roasted meats.
