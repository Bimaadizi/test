Kalifen Stonefist was a traveling monk , cleric , and a philosopher active in the 14 th century DR .

Personality

His training gifted Kalifen with an introspective personality and a philosophical mind. He was a shy and donnish man.

History

By the age of eighteen, Kalifen had already spent a significant portion of his life traveling the world after departing his home, S'tung Monastery in the Hills of Quai , in Bashan Do Province in T'u Lung . Throughout his life, Kalifen learned several types of martial arts, turning his body into a deadly weapon. One of the requirements of his faith, his religious duty, was roaming the world in order to learn the harsh realities of life.

Sometime in the 14 th century DR, at the age of fifty-four, Kalifen Stonefist was among the ambitious adventurers who were interested in being sent into a mysterious dungeon by an unnamed sorceress to retrieve the equally mysterious magical orb from its depths.
