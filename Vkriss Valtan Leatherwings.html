Vkriss Valtan Leatherwings was a dragonborn of Tymanther and a member of Clan Vkriss in 1479 DR .

Personality

Valtan was a cunning and cutthroat businessman.

History

At some point, Valtan lost his wife in a tragic "accident". Two days later, nearly all the prominent members of Clan Gethmara were killed in a gruesome massacre.

In 1479 DR, Valtan was the guildmaster of Djerad Thymar's weaver's guild.
