High Roll was a cheerful pony that belonged to traveling "bard" Olive Ruskettle circa 1357 DR .

History

In 1357 DR , companions of the hero Alias purchased pony High Roll, along with other horses from Dimswart Manor in Cormyr .

The pony was acquired especially for Olive Ruskettle, her being a halfling of smaller-than-average size. She rode the pony to Suzail , the Shadow Gap , Shadowdale , and Yulash , where the heroes were forced to abandon their mounts while pursuing the Abomination of Moander through Cormanthor .
