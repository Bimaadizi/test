Felibarr Blacklance was a bounty hunter and assassin who inhabited the city of Waterdeep during the 1350's DR .

History

Felibarr followed a particularly wicked mage named Melinter . He would meet his master, along with other apprentices and members of their gang, in a small courtyard in Waterdeep's Dock Ward . The courtyard would later become known as Melinter's Court because of these meetings.

Felibarr was a skilled wizard who developed the blacklance spell .

He also spent alot of time at the Blushing Mermaid tavern in Waterdeep.
