Bel Morgahl was a captain of the Neverwinter Guard who served her city during the late 15 th century DR . She led the guards stationed at one of the city's districts other than Blacklake .

Description

She was rather tall and burly for a dragonborn .

Personality

Captain Morgahl was a corrupt member of the City Guard, and had little patience for festidious guards that followed the letter of the law to each exact detail.

Possessions

She wielded a longsword whenever she engaged in combat.

History

At some point during or before the Year of the Grinning Halfling, 1481 DR , Captain Morgahl began harassing Rubalta , the head of the Bahamutan temple in Neverwinter . She then got into something of a confrontation with a fellow guardsman, a dwarf cleric of Marthammor Duin .
