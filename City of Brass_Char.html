The Char was a district in the City of Brass dedicated to metalworking businesses.

Description

This walled section held slave barracks, tenements, and several smelters. As well as a massive forge known as "Firespring" that was used to construct fire archons to act as soldiers. The smell of molten metal was prevalent throughout the district.

Notable Locations

Inhabitants

As property of the Sultan , the slaves here worked for the smelters, smithies, and assorted crafts shops.
