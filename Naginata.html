A naginata ( pronounced : / n ɑː g i n ɑː t ɑː / nah-gee-nah-tah ), also known as a footman's glaive , was a common pole arm weapon and form of glaive used in the continent of Kara-Tur .

Description

A naginata was a sword-like blade mounted on a shaft between 6‒8 ft (1.8‒2.4 m) in length.

Weaponology

There existed one unique form of martial art , titled the Mad Monkey school, that was centered around the naginata.
