Orim Redbeard was an infamous pirate captain of the Black Dragon from Konigheim in the Utter East in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Orim was a giant, fat, heavily muscled human with a red beard, hence his nickname. In truth, he had a white beard that he dyed with a mixture of rust and milk .

Personality

Orim was confident in his skills and had a cruel side.

Relationships

Orim hated the Kissing Shark and its captain, Blackfingers Ralingor , and his navigator Drethil .

History

In the Year of the Unstrung Harp, 1371 DR , and his crew managed to the destroy the Kissing Shark in a blast of smoke powder near the Tenteeth Point thanks to his hired sorcerer. However, when he heard that some of the surviving Sharkers boarded the Morning Bird , he and his crew hunted that ship in order to find if his hated enemies had survived. Orim's ship attacked the Morning Bird but after Belmer Droon almost managed to kill the mage, Orim asked to parley. However, Kurthe Lornar , still loyal to his old captain, tried to attack Orim, but Belgin Dree managed to stop Kurthe. Orim Redbeard realized that he pursued the wrong ship, and left the Sharkers in peace.
