Rye was a widespread grain-producing grass found in Faerûn and other realms. It was often ground into flour in a gristmill and used for making bread and alcohol .

Availability

Ecology

Rye plants were affected by the wheat rust blight, which destroyed the plants and their grains during the time of Netheril. The spreading disease was cured in 3165 NY (−694 DR) by the arcanist Candlemas , who used a complex magical ritual to produce a cure and save the crops.
