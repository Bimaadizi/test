Sulaspra Delzuld was a woman of the Delzuld noble family of Cormyr in the mid–13th century DR , living 1241 DR to 1276 DR .

She married Gaspar Ammaeth , and with him was the mother of Elduss Ammaeth and the grandmother of Duke Bhereu and Baron Thomdor .
