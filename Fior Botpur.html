Fior Botpur was one of the elected Three Lords of Hlath in Chondath in 1358 DR .

Activities

Lord Botpur ruled the city together with Lord Warkas and Lord Nybahn .

History

In 1358 DR, during an absence of both his colleagues, it was reported to Lord Botpur that a barbarian army from Kara-Tur threatened the city. Quickly he gathered all the prominent citizens to discuss the matter. Botpur wanted to hire an adventuring party for an exploration mission.
