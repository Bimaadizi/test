A breastplate was one of the better forms of medium armor .

Description

The armor covered the wearer's front and back with a metal cuirass , and included a helmet and greaves . A light suit of studded leather beneath the breastplate finished the armor suit, protecting the wearer's limbs without restricting movement much.
