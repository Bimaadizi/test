A potion of extra-healing or potion of cure critical wounds was a magical liquid that provided a great deal of healing magic when consumed.

Powers

These potions could be consumed as a whole or in thirds, but drinking the entire potion at once provided slightly more healing magic.

Notable Owners

Tessaril Winter , the warrior and wizard of Eveningstar , carried some potions of extra-healing .
