Denderida was a drow trader and member of House Hunzrin in the late 15 th century DR .

Activities

Denderida was one of the best surface traders of her House, having centuries of experience, and for this she was greatly respected, despite not being a noble.

History

In the Year of the Nether Mountain Scrolls, 1486 DR , Denderida arranged a meeting between a clan of spriggan followers of Malcanthet in Damara and the First Priestess of her House, Charri Hunzrin .
