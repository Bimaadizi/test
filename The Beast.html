The Beast was a savage god of Zakhara worshiped among the Hill Tribes south of the Free Cities .

Shamans of the Beast whipped their followers into a fervor when assaulting targets, often resulting in massive bloodshed for both sides.

Description

The Beast was said to be a drooling half-man with the eyes and fangs of a cat.

Rumors & Legends

The Beast was believed to hunt the hills in search of lost or stolen children whom he could reward with death.
