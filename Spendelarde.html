Spendelarde was a wizard and spell developer.

Magic

He was noted as the inventor of several utilitarian spells, including the necromancy spell Spendelarde's chaser .

Possessions

He once owned the magical staff Spendelarde's Protector , which he carried with him when traveling.

History

Spendelarde studied magic under Mentor Wintercloak , one of the Seven Wizards of Myth Drannor .

Activities

Although Spendelarde did not travel often, he occasionally went on long and dangerous treks to distant locales.
