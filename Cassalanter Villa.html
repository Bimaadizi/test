Cassalanter Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Cassalanter .

Location

The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeep 's Sea Ward on the southwest corner of where Diamond Street and Delzorin Street joined.

Description

The walled compound contained four separate buildings and several gardens. Each building stood between three and four stories tall.
