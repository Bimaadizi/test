Alomystia was a ranger of the Reaching Woods in the Western Heartlands in the 13 th century DR .

In the mid-1200s DR, Alomystia was in the possession of the Walking Tower, believed to be a relic of ancient Netheril . It took the form of a massive statue, standing many stories tall, and it wandered the Reaching Woods seemingly at random or as if seeking something. Alomystia's fate is unknown.
