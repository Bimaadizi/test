Magmahammer , or Magmammer , was a mattock that was the favoured weapon of Dumathoin , the dwarven deity of mining, exploration, and the riches of the earth.

Description

Magmahammer was composed of solid magma.

Powers

This weapon had a mighty +5 enchantment. It had the combined powers of a maul of the titans and a mattock of the titans .
