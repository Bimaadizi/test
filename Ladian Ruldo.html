Ladian Ruldo was the stablemaster of the village of Eveningstar during the late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Ladian owned and operated the stables of Eveningstar found within the Market Square . He had a second stable located in the southeast section of the city along the Starwater River . He rented stalls on a regular basis to Dunman Kiriag who used them for guests of the Lonesome Tankard .
