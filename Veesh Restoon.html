Veesh Restoon was a member of the Xanathar Thieves' Guild in Waterdeep around 1370 DR .

Activities

Veesh's role on her team was as sneak attack specialist. She would wait in a pre-established place where the victims were funneled and would strike with a poisoned dagger. She was obsessive about cleaning and avoided entering sewers whenever possible.
