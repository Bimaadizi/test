Theln Lane was an alleyway of hard-packed dirt and gravel in the northern end of the Trades Ward of Waterdeep .

Geography

This bare, short cut-through passage, ran southwest off of Andamaar's Street and terminated on Irimar's Walk .

Notable Inhabitants

A number of hard-currency girls lived on the upper floors of buildings on this alley. They were known to drop rope ladders down from their balconeys to awaiting clients.
