Luddrick Cromps was a client of the Sharran House of Grief and an inhabitant of Baldur's Gate in the late 15 th century DR .

History

In the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR , Luddrick sought services of the House of Grief aid. The Mapping of the Heart revealed Luddrick's "jealous heart." The ritual was paid for in full and was to be proceeded with the Unburdening .
