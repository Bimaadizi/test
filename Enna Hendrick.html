Enna Hendrick was a farmer who resided in the village of Imnesvale in the Umar Hills of Amn in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Enna was a woman of humble means. She wore commoners' garb. Enna was a human with a bronze skin.

History

In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR , the village of Imnesvale faced a slew of disappearances and locals did not hesitate to blame the legendary Umar Witch . Enna and her husband Erlin Hendrick fled their distant farm in fear of the dreaded witch and stayed within the Imnesvale. They brought their seven favorite chickens over to the village. The chickens were not pets but rather the couple's future meals. Curiously enough, one of the chickens they brought over to Imnesvale had swallowed a precious beljuril gemstone that was still inside it.
