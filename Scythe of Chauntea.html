Scythes of Chauntea were enchanted scythes modeled after the deity Chauntea 's favorite weapon, A Shock of Grain .

Powers

Scythes of Chauntea possessed an impressively powerful +7 enchantment that made their blade deadlier and guided the wielder's hand towards the target with supernatural ease.

History

As of the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR , scythes of Chauntea could be purchased from the cleric of the Three named Katya at the Temple of the Three in Mulsantir .
