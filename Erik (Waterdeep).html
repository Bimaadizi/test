Erik was a silversmith inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Erik stood several inches over 6 feet (1.8 meters) tall.

Activities

Similar to a dwarf , Erik was usually extremely focused on his work.

History

His height helped him bully other kids growing up, but he later poured all of that anger into working metal.
