The Sanctorium was the grand library of Myth Drannor , located in the city during its glory days before the 8 th century DR .

Location

It was located in the district of Sheshyrinnam , on the corner of Moonopal Way and Elbansyr's Ride .

Structure

The above-ground structure of the Sanctorium was a white marble building surrounded by wide-set stone stairs. Its outer walls were decorated with elaborate frescoes and sculptures that depicted the daily life found in Myth Drannor. Beneath the library hall were five subterranean levels that housed its grand collection of documents.

Interior

Within the marble structure of the Sanctorium was a vast hall that featured statues that depicted elves , humans , dwarves and the other races that took up residence within Myth Drannor.

Services

A single figure sat at a lone desk within the hall to greet visitors and retrieve books and documents from within the Sanctorium's vaults. The true breadth of information found within the vaults was unknown to all. While petitioners were seldom denied a specific tome, no one was fully aware of everything it had to offer.

The only other people allowed within the vaults were the city's high mages and those spellcasters who had graduated from Windsong Tower .
