Lord Vondryx was an individual who recruited mercenaries from the city of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 th century DR .

History

Lord Vondryx placed two noticed in the Classified sections of the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter broadsheet in Uktar - Nightal and Marpenoth of the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR . Vondryx sought mercenaries and adventurers to partake in the Blood War with great compensation, exceptional death benefits, and an opportunity to relocate to the Abyss (transport not provided).
