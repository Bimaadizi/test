Namra Dunseltree was the wife of the merchant Inder Dunseltree in the mid-14 th century DR . She was replaced by drow on or before the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR .

Description

She was a quite heavyset woman, who used a walking stick.

Possessions

She owned a walking stick was shod with iron, and struck quite painfully.

Activities

After being kidnapped by drow , she was shipped off to Chult , where she would have been subject to hard labor.

Relationships

She was married to Inder Dunseltree .

History

In 1369 DR , Namra and her husband had been replaced by drow infiltrators, replaced by spell-changed imposters. To the best of their imposters' knowledge, the two merchants had been sent off to Chult and sold as slaves.
