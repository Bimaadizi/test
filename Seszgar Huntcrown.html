Seszgar Huntcrown was a heir of House Huntcrown in the late 15 th century DR .

Description

When he was decapitated, his features got frozen in terror. His usual face was a sneer. At the time of his death, he wore a jerkin that had horns for shoulders, black musterdelvys, and luster-stars of white all over. His hair was fair, his eyes green, and a somewhat sharp nose.

Personality

He was generally known as a sneering braggart.

Abilities

Seszgar was purportedly confident in his fencing skills.

Relationships

Marlin Stormserpent hated him until the day he died. Seszgar was the heir of House Huntcrown .

History

In his childhood, the Huntcrown heir had bullied a young Marlin Stormserpent, who bore a grudge against him to the end.

In 1479 DR , Seszgar was slain by the Blueflame ghosts Relve Langral and Treth Halonter , sent by Marlin Stormserpent against him. The ghosts found and slew him at the Bold Archer , a club in Suzail , alongside his retinue and some other nobles. They carried out his head in a bundle, which they presented to Marlin Stormserpent after the fact.

Rumors & Legends

While Ganrahast and Vainrence were briefly informed that Seszgar Huncrown was plotting with Rothglar Illance and Harmond Hawklin to unleash a "ball of Spellplague " in the Council of the Dragon of 1479 DR , this was a plot by Marlin Stormserpent.
