The Sea Ward of Waterdeep was the wealthiest ward in Waterdeep and located in the northwest of the city.

Geography

The ward was delimited by the Trollwall on the north and west, by Julthoon Street to the south, and by Shield Street to the east, extending to the High Road north of Vondil Street .

The ward contained the Field of Triumph , Heroes' Garden and the Sea's Edge Beach . It also contained some of the city's largest temples.

History

The Sea Ward was officially included as one of the official wards of Waterdeep in the Year of the Crumbling Keep, 1276 DR , together with the North Ward .
