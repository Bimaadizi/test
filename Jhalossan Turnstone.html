Jhalossan Turnstone was a plumber inhabiting Waterdeep , City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Activities

Jhalossan worked for the Cellarers' & Plumbers' Guild during the mid-to-late 14 th century DR where he served as the guild's main point of contact. He worked out of the Old Guildhall –their headquarters in the Dock Ward –as well as Turnstone Plumbing and Pipefitting on Belnimbra's Street .

Relationships

His father, Hilithiimm Turnstone , was the guildmaster for the Cellarers' & Plumbers' Guild.
