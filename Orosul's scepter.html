Orosul's scepters were Netherese power scepters capable of healing wounds and charging depleted magic items .

Description

Orosul's scepters were metal bars around 8 inches (20 centimeters) in length, terminated by small metal balls faceted like gems. Each scepter could be split in two, and the halves could subsequently be fused together again by having them touch each other.

History

Because their original name was lost to the ages, these scepters were named after Orosul , a wizard who owned half a scepter and extensively studied it during the 14 th century DR .

Rumors

A half of a scepter was said to be hidden under a cobblestone in Archenbridge , within sight of Orosul's Tower . The other half was for a time in possession of the dracolich Rauglothgor , before being brought to Zhentil Keep .
