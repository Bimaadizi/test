Lady Terissa Ketal was the Middle Magistrate of the city of Bral in the late 14 th century DR .

Description

Middle Magistrate Ketal was a human woman who was in her 50s during the reign of Prince Andru .

Personality

Lady Terissa was known as a strict judge who held grudges. She had a reputation for bringing down guilty sentences with a punishment of death by jettisoning into Wildspace , which was the most common method of execution on the Rock of Bral .
