Laszik Silvermind was a renowned psion mystic and devotee of the god Auppenser who reformed the empire of Jhaamdath during the 6 th millennium before Dalereckoning .

History

In −5730 DR , the Lord of Reason used Laszik Silvermind to speak to the Jhaamdathan people and reveal a grand plan by which the empire would grow and prosper. His first divine precept was the abolition of slavery, which was swiftly enacted all around the empire.

Laszik went on to fashion a new and powerful psionic artifact in −5725 DR . The udoxias , as it was called, permitted all psionic beings in mental contact with it to access one of the many psionic powers stored inside. Installed in the capital city, Naarkolyth , its effect reached for miles around, drawing people from far and wide to learn the novel mind magic.

As psionic training and knowledge flourished in Naarkolyth, Laszik established the Udoclian in −5710 DR . This university taught and promoted the Invisible Art.

In −5700 DR , in accordance with Auppenser's plan, Jhaamdath replaced its former oligarchy with a psiocratic system of government.

Finally, in −5690 DR , now of advanced years, Laszik Silvermind disappeared inexplicably. The faithful believed that the mystic had ascended in mind and body to serve the Lord of Reason directly by his side.
