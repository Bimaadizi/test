The crimson brooch was an enchanted piece of jewelry from the ruins of Myth Drannor .

Description

This amulet was a small piece of blood-red jewelry.

Powers

The crimson brooch possessed an enchantment that increased the wearer's resistance to magic spells.

History

In 1369 DR , the crimson brooch could be found in one of the ancient coffins in the tombs of the Deep Halls of Myth Drannor's Dwarven Dungeons .
