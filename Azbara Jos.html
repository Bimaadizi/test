Azbara Jos was a Red Wizard of Thay and a member of the faction loyal to Rath Modar .

History

Azbara acted as Modar's envoy to the raiding armies of the reformed Cult of the Dragon . He traveled in a caravan to Baldur's Gate that was smuggling goods for the Cult's hoard.

In the end, Azbara arrived at Skyreach Castle in the Greypeak Mountains , where the hoard was kept.
