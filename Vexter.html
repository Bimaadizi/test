Vexter was a human illusionist who gallivanted across the planes in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

He had long, greying hair, a receding hairline, and a well-trimmed beard.

Possessions

He wielded a staff of striking and a wand of negation , wearing a cloak of protection and a pair of bracers of defense .

Activities

Vexter became an illusionist of repute after several adventures on other planes.

Relationships

At some point, after he was done being an apprentice, he met the halfling homebody Thwitle Phaslebum , whom he befriended and took along on his travels. The bard Lanasetas Neasfilarion joined in his planeswalking adventures once he had his fill of being a minstrel for a cantankerous old lord. Another travelling companion he made was the druid Boeddu , who brought along a donkey named Schmek .

History

Born to peasant parents in the village of Amphail , a bad winter and the resulting bad harvest forced the family to move to Waterdeep , where he became apprenticed to Delfen Yellowknife Ondabarl .
