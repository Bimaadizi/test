A potion of mind reading was a potion that temporarily empowered its drinker with the power to read minds.

Description

A typical potion of mind reading was a dense, purple liquid. Floating within the liquid, an ovoid pink cloud could be seen.

Powers

The potion gave its drinker the effects of the detect thoughts spell .

Creation

One of the recipes of potions of mind reading required a potion of an alchemical suspension of mergrass mixed with certain alchemical sublimates.
