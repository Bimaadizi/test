Zora Tiega Gos ( pronounced : /ˈ z ɔːr ə ˈ t i ɛ g ə ˈ g ɑː s / ZOR-uh TEE-eg-uh GAHSS ) was a noble in Mulmaster of the Gos family.

History

In 1491 DR , for her birthday, Faltyn commissioned his nephew Andrik Gos to create a painting of her and hired Marim Tem as a musician, and hosted a party at the popular dance hall the Wave and Wink . However, during the party, Tiega was a victim of a mysterious arson attack by Kata Brizik , so Faltyn decided to hire an adventuring party to uncover the truth.

Personality

Tiega was down to earth and friendly, in total opposition to her husband. She did not care for status. Tiega's interests were art, knowledge, and living life to its fullest, despite these not being typical characteristics of the Gos family.

Relations

Tiega was the wife of Faltyn Gos .
