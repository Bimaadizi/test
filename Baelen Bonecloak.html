Baelen Bonecloak was a shopkeep from Baldur's Gate and the co-proprietor of the shop that bore his family's name, along with his wife Derryth as of the late 15 th century DR .

Personality

For much of his life Baelan was a troubled and brutish man. He was an alocoholic and nearly ruined his family's business by spending their money on drink.

At some point Baelen became feebleminded and underwent a rapid change in personality. While could hardly speak in full sentences, and had trouble with complex directions, he became a much kinder man.

Abilities

Despite his condition and change in capabilities, Baelan was still capable of casting spells from scrolls .

Relationships

Baelen and Derryth were married for over 70 years, most of which he spent acting in a horrid manner. He was abusive towards Derryth and for some reason or another, the pair stuck together.

History

Facing financial troubles, Derryth took Baelen down with her into the Underdark in the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR . They hoped to recover some specimens of the noblestalk mushrooms. While Baelen became close to plucking one form the ground, he lost his pack, somehow injured himself, and became trapped behind a large grouping of bibberbangs , unable to maneuver his way out.
