Shando Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep .

Location

Shando Street was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's North Ward . Shando Street ran diagonally between Tarnath Street to the northwest and Golden Serpent Street to the southeast. It was also intersected by Suldown Street , Aveen Street , and the Magecourt .
