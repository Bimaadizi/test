Nuur Throth was a cave located in Lowerdark .

Description

Nuur Throth was a very unusual cave. In the upper parts of the cave, sheets of wizard fire constantly danced about. Throughout the cave were clusters of unusual stones which were the covers for deep wells. Around these clusters grew grooves of sussur trees.

Located in the center of the cavern was a large earth node made from a dense iron ore that was black in color. Anyone casting the node door spell was able to travel along this network of linked earth nodes.

Geography

Nuur Throth was located beneath the Sword Mountains in the western Northdark .
