Sheeryl Soulsearcher was an elven diviner and part-time adventurer in the mid-to-late 14 th century DR .

Description

Frail even for an elf, she was not strong enough to be a full-time adventurer. Instead, she became a font of information, using her abilities as a diviner to obtain information about items, ruins, and the like that were otherwise inaccessible without strong divination magic .

Possessions

While seldom participating in adventures directly, she nonetheless still wore a pair of bracers of defense , a cloak of protection , and a robe of stars . She also owned and used powder of magical detection .
